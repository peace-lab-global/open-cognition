# 克莱因自然决策进阶阅读

## 入门
1. Klein, G. (1998). *Sources of Power: How People Make Decisions*. MIT Press. — 叙事驱动，易读，NDM入门首选
2. Klein, G. & Kahneman, D. (2009). "Conditions for Intuitive Expertise." *American Psychologist* 64(6). — 直觉可靠性的条件共识

## 原典
- Klein, G. (1993). "A Recognition-Primed Decision (RPD) Model of Rapid Decision Making." In *Decision Making in Action*. Ablex.
- Klein, G. (1998). *Sources of Power*. MIT Press.
- Klein, G. (2003). *The Power of Intuition*. Currency/Doubleday.
- Klein, G. (2013). *Seeing What Others Don't*. PublicAffairs.

## 二手研究
- Kahneman, D. (2011). *Thinking, Fast and Slow*. — 第21-22章讨论Klein的RPD与直觉
- Lipshitz, R., et al. (2001). "Naturalistic Decision Making and Organizations." *Journal of Management Studies*.
- Zsambok, C. E. & Klein, G. (Eds.). (1997). *Naturalistic Decision Making*. Lawrence Erlbaum.
- Salas, E. & Klein, G. (Eds.). (2001). *Linking Expertise and Naturalistic Decision Making*. Lawrence Erlbaum.
