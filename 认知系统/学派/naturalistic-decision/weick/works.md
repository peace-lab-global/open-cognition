# 维克作品总览

## 核心学术论文
| 年份 | 论文 | 期刊/出处 | 核心贡献 |
|------|------|-----------|----------|
| 1969 | "The Social Psychology of Organizing" (书) | Addison-Wesley | 组织化概念首提 |
| 1979 | "Cognitive Processes in Organizations" | *Research in Organizational Behavior* | 组织认知视角 |
| 1988 | "Enacted Sensemaking in Crisis Situations" | *Journal of Management Studies* | 制定与危机 |
| 1993 | "The Collapse of Sensemaking in Organizations: The Mann Gulch Disaster" | *Administrative Science Quarterly* 38(4) | Mann Gulch案例，意义崩溃 |
| 1995 | *Sensemaking in Organizations* (书) | Sage | 意义建构理论系统化 |
| 1999 | "Organizing for High Reliability" (与Sutcliffe) | *Research in Organizational Behavior* | HRO五特征 |
| 2001 | "Leadership as Sensemaking" | *The Leadership Quarterly* | 领导力与意义建构 |
| 2005 | "Organizing and the Process of Sensemaking" | *Organization Science* 16(4) | 意义建构七特征 |
| 2006 | "Faith, Evidence, and Action" | *Organization Studies* | 不确定条件下的行动逻辑 |

**进阶**: *Sensemaking in Organizations* (1995) → "Organizing and the Process of Sensemaking" (2005)
**深潜**: *The Social Psychology of Organizing* (1979) 完整 → HRO相关论文集
