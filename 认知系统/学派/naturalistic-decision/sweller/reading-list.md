# 斯威勒认知负荷理论进阶阅读

## 入门
1. Sweller, J. (1988). "Cognitive Load During Problem Solving: Effects on Learning." *Cognitive Science*, 12(2), 257-285. — CLT奠基论文
2. Sweller, J. (2011). *Cognitive Load Theory*, 第1-3章. Springer. — 理论框架入门
3. Clark, R. C., Nguyen, F. & Sweller, J. (2012). *Efficiency in Learning*. — 面向实践者的应用指南

## 原典
- Sweller, J. (1988). "Cognitive Load During Problem Solving." *Cognitive Science*, 12(2).
- Sweller, J., Van Merrienboer, J. & Paas, F. (1998). "Cognitive Architecture and Instructional Design." *Educational Psychology Review*, 10(3).
- Sweller, J. (2011). *Cognitive Load Theory*. Springer.
- Sweller, J., Ayres, P. & Kalyuga, S. (2011). *Cognitive Load Theory*. Springer.

## 二手研究
- Mayer, R. E. (2020). *Multimedia Learning* (3rd ed.). Cambridge University Press. — 多媒体学习CLT应用
- Van Merrienboer, J. & Kirschner, P. (2018). *Ten Steps to Complex Learning*. Routledge.
- Geary, D. C. (2011). "An Evolutionarily Informed Education Science." *Educational Psychologist*, 43(4).
- Kirschner, P., Sweller, J. & Clark, R. (2006). "Why Minimal Guidance During Instruction Does Not Work." *Educational Psychologist*, 41(2).
