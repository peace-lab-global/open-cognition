# 托马斯·谢里丹 · Thomas Sheridan

> "The issue is not whether to automate, but how to automate — at what level and with what human involvement."
> "问题不在于是否自动化，而在于如何自动化——在何种层级、以何种人类参与方式。"

## 基本信息 / Basic Information

托马斯·B·谢里丹（Thomas B. Sheridan, 1929–2024）是美国人因工程师、控制理论家、麻省理工学院（MIT）机械工程系教授，也是人机系统研究最重要的奠基者之一。他在长达半个多世纪的学术生涯中，系统建构了监督控制理论（Supervisory Control Theory），提出了自动化十级模型（10 Levels of Automation），并开创了远程机器人学（Telerobotics）这一全新学科领域。他的工作从根本上改变了人们对人类在自动化系统中角色的理解——人不再是直接操作者，而是监督者、决策者和异常干预者。

Thomas B. Sheridan (1929–2024) was an American human factors engineer, control theorist, and professor of mechanical engineering at MIT. He is widely regarded as one of the most important founders of human-machine systems research. Over a career spanning more than half a century, he systematically developed Supervisory Control Theory, proposed the 10 Levels of Automation model, and pioneered the entirely new field of telerobotics. His work fundamentally transformed the understanding of human roles in automated systems — from direct operators to supervisors, decision-makers, and interveners in anomalous situations.

谢里登的学术影响远超工程领域。他与 Raja Parasuraman 和 Christopher Wickens 的合作论文（2000年）成为人机自动化交互研究被引用最多的文献之一。他的思想直接影响了航空自动化、核能控制、远程手术、空间机器人、自动驾驶以及当代 AI 代理监督等领域的设计哲学。

Sheridan's academic influence extends well beyond engineering. His collaborative paper with Raja Parasuraman and Christopher Wickens (2000) became one of the most cited works in human-automation interaction research. His ideas directly shaped design philosophies in aviation automation, nuclear power control, remote surgery, space robotics, autonomous driving, and the contemporary supervision of AI agents.

## 核心命题 / Core Theses

**1. 监督控制范式 / Supervisory Control Paradigm**
在高度自动化系统中，人类的角色从直接操纵转变为监督控制：设定目标、分配任务、监控系统状态、在异常情况下介入干预。这一范式将维纳的反馈控制思想扩展为层级化的人机协作结构。
In highly automated systems, the human role shifts from direct manipulation to supervisory control: setting goals, allocating tasks, monitoring system states, and intervening in anomalous situations. This paradigm extends Wiener's feedback control ideas into a hierarchical human-machine collaboration structure.

**2. 自动化连续光谱 / The Automation Continuum**
自动化不是一个二选一的状态（自动/手动），而是从完全人工到完全自主的连续光谱，可以用十个层级来精确描述。每个层级对应不同的人机功能分配模式。
Automation is not a binary state (automated/manual) but a continuous spectrum from fully manual to fully autonomous, precisely describable in ten levels. Each level corresponds to a different human-machine function allocation pattern.

**3. 适当自动化原则 / The Appropriate Automation Principle**
自动化设计的目标不是追求最高层级的自动化，而是根据任务特性、人类认知能力和系统可靠性要求，选择"适当"的自动化层级。过度自动化与自动化不足同样危险。
The goal of automation design is not to pursue the highest level of automation, but to select the "appropriate" level based on task characteristics, human cognitive capabilities, and system reliability requirements. Over-automation is as dangerous as under-automation.

**4. 通信带宽约束 / Communication Bandwidth Constraint**
人类与计算机系统之间的信息传输带宽是监督控制的根本约束。感知延迟、力反馈质量和信息呈现方式直接决定了远程操作的效能和安全性。
The information transmission bandwidth between humans and computer systems is the fundamental constraint of supervisory control. Perceptual delay, force feedback quality, and information presentation modes directly determine the effectiveness and safety of teleoperation.

**5. 人类-机器功能分配 / Human-Machine Function Allocation**
在设计自动化系统时，必须系统性地回答"哪些功能由人执行、哪些由机器执行"的问题。这一分配不是静态的，而应随情境动态调整。
When designing automated systems, one must systematically address "which functions should be performed by humans and which by machines." This allocation is not static but should adapt dynamically to situational demands.

**6. 联合认知系统 / Joint Cognitive Systems**
人与自动化系统构成一个统一的认知整体——联合认知系统。系统的认知特性不能简单还原为人或机器各自的属性，而是产生于二者的交互之中。
Humans and automated systems form a unified cognitive whole — a joint cognitive system. The cognitive properties of the system cannot be reduced to the attributes of human or machine alone, but emerge from their interaction.

## 思想脉络 / Intellectual Development

### 早期 / Early Period (1950s–1970s)

谢里登的学术生涯始于经典控制理论。他在 MIT 接受了严格的工程学训练，深受诺伯特·维纳（Norbert Wiener）控制论思想的影响。在这一时期，他将反馈控制原理应用于人机系统，开始思考人类操作员在复杂控制系统中的角色问题。

Sheridan's academic career began in classical control theory. He received rigorous engineering training at MIT and was deeply influenced by Norbert Wiener's cybernetics. During this period, he applied feedback control principles to human-machine systems, beginning to think about the role of human operators in complex control systems.

1960年代，谢里登开始在MIT人因实验室研究远程操作（teleoperation）问题。他关注到，在远程控制系统中，通信链路的延迟和带宽限制会严重影响操作效能。这一问题驱动他开始思考人与机器之间信息传递的本质——不仅是数据层面的传输，更是认知层面的监督与控制。这一阶段的工作为后来的监督控制理论奠定了实验基础。

In the 1960s, Sheridan began studying teleoperation problems at the MIT Human Factors Laboratory. He observed that communication link delays and bandwidth limitations in remote control systems severely impacted operational effectiveness. This problem drove him to consider the nature of information transfer between humans and machines — not merely at the data level, but at the cognitive level of supervision and control. This phase of work laid the experimental foundations for later supervisory control theory.

### 中期 / Middle Period (1978–1992)

1978年，谢里登与 William Verplank 合作发表了开创性论文《Human and Computer Control of Undersea Teleoperators》，首次系统地提出了自动化十级模型。这一模型将人机交互从"全手动"到"全自动"划分为十个渐次递进的层级，每个层级明确定义了人类与计算机在决策和执行中的分工。

In 1978, Sheridan and William Verplank published the groundbreaking paper "Human and Computer Control of Undersea Teleoperators," which systematically proposed the 10 Levels of Automation for the first time. This model divided human-machine interaction from "fully manual" to "fully automatic" into ten progressive levels, each clearly defining the division of decision-making and execution between humans and computers.

1992年，谢里登出版了里程碑式专著《Telerobotics, Automation, and Human Supervisory Control》（MIT Press）。该书将远程机器人学、自动化理论和监督控制理论统一在一个连贯的理论框架中，系统论述了从感知延迟、力反馈到任务分配、人机界面设计的各个层面。这部著作标志着监督控制理论从工程直觉上升为系统理论。

In 1992, Sheridan published the landmark monograph *Telerobotics, Automation, and Human Supervisory Control* (MIT Press). The book unified telerobotics, automation theory, and supervisory control theory into a coherent theoretical framework, systematically addressing aspects from perceptual delay and force feedback to task allocation and human-machine interface design. This work marked the elevation of supervisory control theory from engineering intuition to systematic theory.

### 晚期 / Late Period (2000–2024)

进入21世纪，谢里登的学术影响力达到了新的高度。2000年，他与 Raja Parasuraman 和 Christopher Wickens 合作发表了具有划时代意义的论文《A Model for Types and Levels of Human Interaction with Automation》。这篇论文将谢里登的十级模型与 Parasuraman 的四阶段模型（信息获取、信息分析、决策与行动选择、行动执行）整合为一个二维框架，为自动化系统设计提供了更精细的分析工具。

Entering the 21st century, Sheridan's academic influence reached new heights. In 2000, he collaborated with Raja Parasuraman and Christopher Wickens on the epoch-making paper "A Model for Types and Levels of Human Interaction with Automation." This paper integrated Sheridan's 10-level model with Parasuraman's four-stage model (information acquisition, information analysis, decision and action selection, action implementation) into a two-dimensional framework, providing a more refined analytical tool for automated system design.

2002年的《Humans and Automation: System Design and Research Issues》进一步扩展了理论视野，将人因工程、认知心理学和系统工程的研究成果整合到人机自动化的综合框架中。在晚年，谢里登持续关注 AI 代理、自主系统等新兴领域中的监督控制问题，其理论框架被广泛应用于自动驾驶、无人机操作和智能代理监督等领域。

The 2002 book *Humans and Automation: System Design and Research Issues* further expanded the theoretical horizon, integrating findings from human factors engineering, cognitive psychology, and systems engineering into a comprehensive framework for human-automation interaction. In his later years, Sheridan continued to engage with supervisory control issues in emerging fields such as AI agents and autonomous systems, with his theoretical frameworks widely applied to autonomous driving, drone operation, and intelligent agent supervision.

## 重要概念 / Key Concepts

1. **[监督控制 / Supervisory Control](concepts/supervisory-control.md)** — 人类设定目标与约束、计算机执行子任务、人在异常时介入的层级化控制范式。
2. **[人机模型 / Human-Machine Model](concepts/human-machine-model.md)** — 描述人与机器在自动化系统中功能分配的建模框架。
3. **[远程操作 / Teleoperation](concepts/teleoperation.md)** — 通过通信链路远程控制机械装置，涉及感知延迟、力反馈和带宽约束。
4. **[人机交互 / Human-Robot Interaction](concepts/human-robot-interaction.md)** — 人与机器人之间在感知、决策和行动层面的动态协作。
5. **[远程机器人学 / Telerobotics](concepts/telerobotics.md)** — 远程操作与自主机器人技术的交叉学科。
6. **自动化十级 / 10 Levels of Automation** — 从完全手动到完全自主的连续光谱模型。
7. **人机界面带宽 / Human-Machine Interface Bandwidth** — 人与计算机之间信息传输速率的概念。
8. **适当自动化 / Appropriate Automation** — 根据任务和认知特性选择最优自动化层级的设计原则。
9. **联合认知系统 / Joint Cognitive Systems** — 人与自动化构成统一认知整体的理论框架。
10. **自适应自动化 / Adaptive Automation** — 根据实时情境动态调整自动化层级的方法。

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

**1. 远程手术 / Remote Surgery**
外科医生通过达芬奇（da Vinci）等机器人系统进行远程操作，是谢里登远程操作理论的直接应用。感知延迟、力反馈质量和视觉呈现带宽仍然是核心技术挑战。
Surgeons operating through robotic systems such as da Vinci exemplify the direct application of Sheridan's teleoperation theory. Perceptual delay, force feedback quality, and visual presentation bandwidth remain core technical challenges.

**2. 空间机器人 / Space Robotics**
NASA空间站机械臂（如Canadarm2）的地面操控是监督控制的经典案例。地面操作员在通信延迟条件下监督自主执行子任务的机器人。
Ground operation of NASA space station robotic arms (such as Canadarm2) represents a classic case of supervisory control. Ground operators supervise robots executing sub-tasks autonomously under communication delay conditions.

**3. 自动驾驶 / Autonomous Driving**
SAE J3016自动驾驶分级标准（L0–L5）与谢里登的自动化十级有着清晰的对应关系。L2–L3级别的"人在回路"监督问题是当前自动驾驶安全的焦点。
The SAE J3016 autonomous driving classification (L0–L5) has clear correspondences with Sheridan's 10 levels of automation. The "human-in-the-loop" supervision problem at L2–L3 is the current focus of autonomous driving safety.

**4. 无人机操作 / Drone Operations**
单操作者同时监控多架无人机的监督控制问题直接涉及谢里登关于人机界面带宽的理论。注意力分配和任务切换是核心挑战。
The supervisory control problem of a single operator monitoring multiple drones simultaneously directly involves Sheridan's theory of human-machine interface bandwidth. Attention allocation and task switching are the core challenges.

**5. AI代理监督 / AI Agent Supervision**
对自主AI代理（如大语言模型Agent）的监督控制是谢里登范式在智能时代的新应用。如何设定AI的行为边界、监控其决策过程、在异常时介入，正是监督控制理论的核心问题。
Supervisory control of autonomous AI agents (such as LLM-based agents) is a new application of Sheridan's paradigm in the intelligent era. How to set behavioral boundaries for AI, monitor its decision processes, and intervene during anomalies are precisely the core questions of supervisory control theory.

**6. 核能控制 / Nuclear Power Control**
核电站控制室的设计深刻体现了监督控制原则：操作员监督高度自动化的反应堆系统，仅在异常工况下介入。
Nuclear power plant control room design deeply embodies supervisory control principles: operators supervise highly automated reactor systems, intervening only under abnormal conditions.

**7. 工业自动化 / Industrial Automation**
现代工厂的人机协作（如协作机器人cobots）需要在谢里登的自动化光谱上找到适当的位置，平衡效率与人类监督。
Human-machine collaboration in modern factories (such as collaborative robots/cobots) needs to find the appropriate position on Sheridan's automation spectrum, balancing efficiency with human supervision.

## 常见误读 / Common Misreadings

**1. 自动化越高越好 / Higher automation is always better**
❌ 十级模型意味着自动化层级越高，系统越好。
❌ The 10-level model implies that higher automation levels mean better systems.
✅ 模型是描述性和分析性的工具，不是规范性方案。谢里登的核心观点是选择"适当"的自动化层级，过度自动化（如让驾驶员完全信任自动驾驶）可能带来灾难性后果。
✅ The model is a descriptive and analytical tool, not a prescriptive prescription. Sheridan's core argument is selecting the "appropriate" automation level; over-automation (such as drivers fully trusting autonomous driving) can lead to catastrophic consequences.

**2. 监督控制意味着人总在回路中 / Supervisory control means humans are always in the loop**
❌ 监督控制意味着人类始终处于控制回路之中。
❌ Supervisory control means humans are always in the control loop.
✅ 在高层级自动化中，人类可能完全脱离执行回路，只在战略层面进行监督。这正是 Bainbridge 指出的"自动化悖论"的来源——系统越自动化，人类越难在关键时刻有效介入。
✅ In high-level automation, humans may be entirely removed from the execution loop, supervising only at a strategic level. This is precisely the source of the "ironies of automation" identified by Bainbridge — the more automated the system, the harder it is for humans to intervene effectively at critical moments.

**3. 十级模型与四阶段模型是竞争关系 / The 10-level and 4-stage models compete**
❌ 谢里登的十级模型与 Parasuraman 的四阶段模型是互相替代的竞争关系。
❌ Sheridan's 10-level model and Parasuraman's 4-stage model are competing alternatives.
✅ 十级模型是单维度光谱（自动化程度），四阶段模型是多维框架（认知功能阶段）。Parasuraman 在每个功能阶段内应用了十级的概念，二者是正交整合关系——四阶段回答"在哪一环节"，十级回答"自动化到什么程度"。
✅ The 10-level model is a single-dimension spectrum (degree of automation), while the 4-stage model is a multi-dimensional framework (cognitive function stages). Parasuraman applied the 10-level concept within each function stage — they are orthogonally integrated: the 4-stage answers "at which stage," the 10-level answers "to what degree."

**4. 谢里登仅是工程师 / Sheridan was merely an engineer**
❌ 谢里登的贡献仅限于工程技术领域，与认知科学和哲学无关。
❌ Sheridan's contributions are limited to engineering technology and unrelated to cognitive science or philosophy.
✅ 谢里登的监督控制范式深刻触及了人类认知架构的本质问题——注意力分配、情境意识、认知负荷管理。他与 Hollnagel 共同发展的联合认知系统概念具有深刻的哲学意涵，挑战了主客二分的传统认识论。
✅ Sheridan's supervisory control paradigm deeply addresses fundamental questions of human cognitive architecture — attention allocation, situational awareness, cognitive load management. The joint cognitive systems concept he developed with Hollnagel carries profound philosophical implications, challenging the traditional subject-object epistemological dichotomy.

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

谢里登的监督控制理论与东方思想传统之间存在深刻的呼应关系。

Sheridan's supervisory control theory resonates profoundly with Eastern intellectual traditions.

**道家"无为而治" / Daoist Wu Wei (Governance through Non-Action)**
监督控制的最高境界——人类设定目标后退居监督角色，让系统自主运行——与道家"无为而无不为"的治理哲学高度契合。十级模型的最高层级（系统自主决策与行动）可以理解为技术层面的"无为"——不是不行动，而是通过适当的结构和约束让系统自行演化。
The highest aspiration of supervisory control — humans setting goals and then retreating to a supervisory role while the system operates autonomously — closely parallels the Daoist governance philosophy of "wu wei er wu bu wei" (acting through non-action). The highest level of the 10-level model (autonomous system decision-making and action) can be understood as "wu wei" at the technological level — not inaction, but allowing the system to evolve through appropriate structures and constraints.

**儒家"中庸"思想 / Confucian Doctrine of the Mean**
谢里登的"适当自动化"原则与儒家"中庸"之道形成有趣对话。中庸不是折中，而是根据具体情境找到最恰当的平衡点。同样，适当自动化不是选择中间层级，而是根据任务特性和人类能力动态确定最优分配。
Sheridan's "appropriate automation" principle forms an interesting dialogue with the Confucian "Doctrine of the Mean." The Mean is not compromise but finding the most appropriate balance point according to specific circumstances. Similarly, appropriate automation means not choosing a middle level, but dynamically determining optimal allocation based on task characteristics and human capabilities.

**佛教"正念"传统 / Buddhist Mindfulness Tradition**
监督控制中对"情境意识"（situational awareness）的强调与佛教正念（mindfulness）传统形成对话。操作员需要维持对系统状态的非执着但清醒的觉知——这正是正念的认知科学表达。
The emphasis on "situational awareness" in supervisory control forms a dialogue with the Buddhist mindfulness tradition. Operators must maintain a non-attached yet alert awareness of system states — which is essentially the cognitive scientific expression of mindfulness.
