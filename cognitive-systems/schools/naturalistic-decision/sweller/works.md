# 斯威勒作品总览

## 核心学术论文
| 年份 | 论文 | 期刊/出处 | 核心贡献 |
|------|------|-----------|----------|
| 1988 | "Cognitive Load During Problem Solving: Effects on Learning" | *Cognitive Science* 12(2) | CLT奠基论文 |
| 1990 | "The Use of Worked Examples as a Substitute for Problem Solving" | *Cognition and Instruction* | 样例学习效应 |
| 1994 | "Cognitive Load Theory, Learning Difficulty, and Instructional Design" | *Learning and Instruction* 4(4) | CLT框架系统阐述 |
| 1998 | "Cognitive Architecture and Instructional Design" (与Van Merrienboer, Paas) | *Educational Psychology Review* 10(3) | CLT与教学设计完整联结 |
| 2002 | "When Redundant Instructional Materials Can Impair Learning" | *Journal of Educational Psychology* | 冗余效应实证 |
| 2006 | "The Worked Example Effect and Human Cognition" | *Learning and Instruction* | 样例效应的认知机制 |
| 2010 | "Element Interactivity and Intrinsic, Extraneous, and Germane Cognitive Load" | *Educational Psychology Review* 22(2) | 三类负荷的严格界定 |
| 2019 | "Cognitive Architecture and Instructional Design: 20 Years Later" (与Van Merrienboer) | *Educational Psychology Review* | CLT二十年回顾 |
