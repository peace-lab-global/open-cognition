# 冲突原则 / Conflict Not Linkage

## 概念定义 / Definition

爱森斯坦的核心论点：蒙太奇的力量来自镜头之间的**对立和冲突**，而非简单的前后衔接。他区分了"链接"（linkage）和"碰撞"（collision）：链接是将镜头平滑地串联在一起（如经典好莱坞的连续性剪辑），而碰撞是让镜头之间产生张力和冲突——正是冲突创造了新意义。

Eisenstein's core argument: montage's power comes from **opposition and collision** between shots, not simple sequential linkage. He distinguished "linkage" (smoothly connecting shots, like classical Hollywood continuity editing) from "collision" (creating tension between shots) — collision creates new meaning.

## 冲突的五个维度 / Five Dimensions of Conflict

1. **图形冲突**：线条方向、形状、构图的对立（如向上 vs 向下、圆形 vs 方形）
2. **平面冲突**：景深层次的冲突（前景 vs 后景）
3. **体积冲突**：物体大小的突然变化（特写 vs 远景的碰撞）
4. **空间冲突**：空间方向的不一致（左 vs 右、内 vs 外）
5. **光线冲突**：明暗的对立（高调 vs 低调照明）

Graphic conflict (line direction, shape, composition opposition); plane conflict (depth layer conflicts); volume conflict (sudden size changes); spatial conflict (directional inconsistencies); light conflict (brightness opposition).

## 对经典好莱坞剪辑的批判 / Critique of Classical Hollywood Editing

爱森斯坦批评经典好莱坞的"不可见剪辑"——它的目标是让剪辑不被观众注意到，维持"现实的幻觉"。在爱森斯坦看来，这种剪辑是保守的——它不挑战观众的认知，不产生新的思想。蒙太奇应当是**可见的**——观众应该感受到镜头之间的碰撞。

Eisenstein criticized classical Hollywood's "invisible editing" — its goal is making editing unnoticed, maintaining "reality illusion." For Eisenstein, this editing is conservative — it doesn't challenge audience cognition or produce new ideas. Montage should be **visible** — audiences should feel shot-to-shot collision.

## 镜头内蒙太奇 / Intra-Frame Montage

爱森斯坦后来发展了"镜头内蒙太奇"概念——即使在一个连续镜头内部，如果存在构图、运动、光影的冲突，同样产生蒙太奇效果。这意味着长镜头不一定与蒙太奇对立——关键在于冲突是否存在。

Eisenstein later developed "intra-frame montage" — even within a continuous shot, if composition, movement, or lighting conflict exists, montage effects are produced. This means long shots aren't necessarily opposed to montage — what matters is whether conflict exists.
