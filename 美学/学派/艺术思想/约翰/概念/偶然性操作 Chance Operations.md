# 偶然性操作 / Chance Operations

## 概念定义 / Definition

"偶然性操作"是凯奇从1950年代初开始使用的创作方法——用随机过程（《易经》占卜、掷骰子、星图投影等）来替代作曲家的主观意志做出创作决定。凯奇认为，西方艺术传统中过度依赖的"自我表达"和"情感传达"实际上是"自我"（ego）的障碍——偶然性操作可以帮助艺术家超越自己的偏好、习惯和偏见。

"Chance Operations" is Cage's compositional method from early 1950s — using random processes (I Ching divination, dice throws, star chart projections) to replace composer's subjective will in creative decisions. Cage believed Western art tradition's over-reliance on "self-expression" and "emotional communication" is actually "ego's" obstacle — chance operations help artists transcend preferences, habits, and biases.

## 《易经》作为创作工具 / I Ching as Creative Tool

凯奇1950年从钢琴家大卫·都铎那里获得了一本《易经》（中文版附英文翻译）。他将《易经》的64卦系统发展为创作工具：
- 将音乐参数（音高、时值、力度、音色）映射到64卦
- 用投掷硬币的方法确定卦象
- 由卦象决定作品的各个要素

这种方法的关键不是"随机"本身，而是"消解意志"——让作品脱离创作者的控制，让声音"自由发生"。

Cage received an I Ching copy in 1950. He developed the 64-hexagram system as a creative tool: mapping musical parameters to hexagrams; using coin tosses to determine hexagrams; letting hexagrams determine compositional elements. The key isn't "randomness" itself but "dissolving will" — letting works escape creator's control, letting sounds "freely occur."

## 与杜尚的关系 / Relation to Duchamp

凯奇的偶然性操作与杜尚的"现成品"（readymade）有深层联系：
- 杜尚选择现成品时声称是"无关审美"的——消解了艺术家的审美判断
- 凯奇用偶然操作消解了作曲家的创造意志
- 两者都质疑了"艺术家作为创造者"的传统观念

Cage's chance operations connect deeply with Duchamp's "readymades": Duchamp claimed "indifference" to 美学 in selecting readymades — dissolving artist's aesthetic judgment; Cage used chance to dissolve composer's creative will; both questioned "artist as creator" traditional conception.

## 批评与争论 / Criticisms and Debates

- **阿多诺**的追随者认为偶然操作是"逃避责任"——艺术家放弃了批判性介入
- **布列兹**（Pierre Boulez）早期与凯奇通信密切，后来决裂——他认为完全的偶然性是"不负责任的放弃"
- **凯奇的回应**：偶然操作不是"放弃"，而是"更大的接受"——接受世界的本来面目比强加个人意志更困难

Adorno's followers saw chance operations as "evading responsibility"; Boulez broke with Cage, seeing total chance as "irresponsible abandonment"; Cage's response: chance is not "abandonment" but "greater acceptance" — accepting the world as it is requires more than imposing personal will.
