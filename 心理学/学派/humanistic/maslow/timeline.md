# 马斯洛 · Abraham Maslow - 生平年表 / Life Timeline

## 早年与教育 / Early Life and Education

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|:---|:---|
| 1908 | 4月1日出生于纽约布鲁克林，父母为俄国犹太移民。是七个孩子中的长子。/ Born April 1 in Brooklyn, New York, to Russian-Jewish immigrant parents. Eldest of seven children. |
| 1926 | 进入纽约市立学院（City College of New York）学习法律，但不久转学心理学。/ Enrolled at City College of New York to study law, soon switched to psychology. |
| 1928 | 与表妹Bertha Goodman结婚（后育有两个孩子）。转入威斯康星大学。/ Married cousin Bertha Goodman (two children). Transferred to University of Wisconsin. |
| 1930 | 获威斯康星大学心理学学士学位。/ Received B.A. in Psychology from University of Wisconsin. |
| 1931 | 获威斯康星大学心理学硕士学位。/ Received M.A. in Psychology from University of Wisconsin. |
| 1934 | 在哈里·哈洛（Harry Harlow）指导下获心理学博士学位。博士论文研究灵长类动物的支配行为和性行为。/ Received Ph.D. in Psychology under Harry Harlow. Dissertation on primate dominance and sexual behavior. |

## 早期学术生涯 / Early Academic Career

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|:---|:---|
| 1935 | 在哥伦比亚大学担任爱德华·桑代克（Edward Thorndike）的研究助理，进行智力测试相关研究。/ Research assistant to Edward Thorndike at Columbia University, working on intelligence testing. |
| 1937 | 任教于布鲁克林学院（Brooklyn College），开始了长达14年的教学生涯。与阿尔弗雷德·阿德勒（Alfred Adler）、埃里希·弗洛姆（Erich Fromm）、卡伦·霍妮（Karen Horney）、马克斯·韦特海默（Max Wertheimer）和鲁思·本尼迪克特（Ruth Benedict）等人建立了重要学术联系。/ Appointed at Brooklyn College, beginning 14-year tenure. Formed important intellectual relationships with Alfred Adler, Erich Fromm, Karen Horney, Max Wertheimer, and Ruth Benedict. |
| 1943 | 发表《人类动机理论》（A Theory of Human Motivation），首次系统提出需求层次理论。/ Published "A Theory of Human Motivation," first systematic proposal of the hierarchy of needs. |

## 人本主义革命 / The Humanistic Revolution

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|:---|:---|
| 1950 | 发表"Self-Actualizing People: A Study of Psychological Health"，首次系统描述自我实现者的特质。/ Published first systematic description of self-actualizer characteristics. |
| 1951 | 受聘为布兰迪斯大学（Brandeis University）心理学系主任，在此建立了全国领先的人本主义心理学研究中心。/ Appointed Chair of Psychology Department at Brandeis University, establishing a leading humanistic psychology research center. |
| 1954 | 出版《动机与人格》（Motivation and Personality），系统阐述需求层次理论和自我实现概念，标志人本主义心理学的理论奠基。/ Published *Motivation and Personality*, systematically formulating hierarchy of needs and self-actualization — the theoretical foundation of humanistic psychology. |
| 1961 | 与Anthony Sutich共同创立《人本主义心理学杂志》（Journal of Humanistic Psychology）。/ Co-founded *Journal of Humanistic Psychology* with Anthony Sutich. |
| 1962 | 出版《存在心理学探索》（Toward a Psychology of Being），将理论焦点扩展到存在性价值、高峰体验和B-认知。在Non-Linear Systems公司度过夏天，研究人本主义管理学。/ Published *Toward a Psychology of Being*, expanding to Being-values, peak experiences, and B-cognition. Spent summer at Non-Linear Systems studying humanistic management. |
| 1963 | 当选美国人本主义心理学会（American Association of Humanistic Psychology）首任主席。/ Elected first president of American Association of Humanistic Psychology. |

## 晚期与超越 / Later Years and Transcendence

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|:---|:---|
| 1964 | 出版《宗教、价值与高峰体验》（Religions, Values, and Peak-Experiences），系统探讨超越性体验与宗教的关系。/ Published *Religions, Values, and Peak-Experiences*, systematically exploring transcendent experience and religion. |
| 1965 | 出版《尤赛奇管理》（Eupsychian Management），将人本主义心理学应用于管理学。/ Published *Eupsychian Management*, applying humanistic psychology to management. |
| 1966 | 出版《科学心理学》（The Psychology of Science），对科学方法论进行人本主义批判。/ Published *The Psychology of Science*, humanistic critique of scientific methodology. |
| 1967 | 当选美国心理学会（APA）主席（任期1968年），但健康问题日益严重。/ Elected president of American Psychological Association (term 1968), but health problems worsening. |
| 1968 | 因严重心脏病发作从布兰迪斯大学退休。迁居加利福尼亚。/ Retired from Brandeis University due to severe heart attack. Moved to California. |
| 1968–1969 | 在加利福尼亚索尔克研究所（Salk Institute）驻留研究。提出自我超越概念和Z理论。开始整合人本主义心理学与超个人心理学。/ Resident at Salk Institute in California. Proposed self-transcendence concept and Theory Z. Began integrating humanistic and transpersonal psychology. |
| 1969 | 在《超个人心理学杂志》（Journal of Transpersonal Psychology）发表"Theory Z"论文。与Stanislav Grof等人共同推动超个人心理学运动。/ Published "Theory Z" in *Journal of Transpersonal Psychology*. Helped launch transpersonal psychology movement with Stanislav Grof and others. |
| 1970 | 6月8日在加利福尼亚因心脏病发作去世，享年62岁。/ Died June 8 in California from a heart attack, aged 62. |

## 身后影响 / Posthumous Legacy

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|:---|:---|
| 1971 | 遗著《人性能达的境界》（The Farther Reaches of Human Nature）出版，由遗孀Bertha Goodman编辑。/ Posthumous *The Farther Reaches of Human Nature* published, edited by widow Bertha Goodman. |
| 1979 | Edward Hoffman出版传记《做人的权利：马斯洛传》（The Right to Be Human）。/ Hoffman published biography *The Right to Be Human*. |
| 1998 | Martin Seligman在APA年会上正式提出"积极心理学"（Positive Psychology），明确将马斯洛视为思想先驱。/ Seligman formally proposed "Positive Psychology" at APA convention, explicitly naming Maslow as intellectual forerunner. |
| 2002 | *Review of General Psychology*调查将马斯洛列为20世纪第10位最具影响力的心理学家。/ Survey ranked Maslow as 10th most cited psychologist of the 20th century. |
| 2020 | Scott Barry Kaufman出版《超越：自我实现的新科学》（Transcend），以当代实证方法重新阐释马斯洛的需求层次理论。/ Kaufman published *Transcend*, re-interpreting Maslow's hierarchy with contemporary empirical methods. |
