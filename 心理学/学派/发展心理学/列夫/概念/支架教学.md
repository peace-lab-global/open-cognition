# 支架教学 · Scaffolding

## 定义 / Definition

支架教学（Scaffolding）是一种教学策略，指教师或有能力的他者在学习者的最近发展区（ZPD）内提供临时性、结构化的支持，帮助学习者完成其独立无法完成的任务，并随着学习者能力的增长逐步撤除支持（"撤架"，fading），最终使学习者能够独立完成任务。

Scaffolding is an instructional strategy in which a teacher or more capable other provides temporary, structured support within the learner's Zone of Proximal Development (ZPD), helping the learner accomplish tasks they cannot complete independently, and gradually removing the support ("fading") as the learner's competence grows, ultimately enabling independent task completion.

> **重要澄清 / Important Clarification**："支架"这一术语并非维果茨基本人提出，而是由布鲁纳（Jerome Bruner）、伍德（David Wood）和罗斯（Gail Ross）于1976年首次引入教育心理学文献。它是布鲁纳等人对维果茨基ZPD概念的教育学演绎和操作化。
>
> The term "scaffolding" was not coined by Vygotsky himself but was introduced into educational psychology literature by Jerome Bruner, David Wood, and Gail Ross in 1976. It is Bruner et al.'s pedagogical elaboration and operationalization of Vygotsky's ZPD concept.

"支架"这一隐喻来自建筑行业——脚手架在建筑施工期间为建筑物提供临时支撑，建筑完工后即被拆除。同样，教学支架在学习过程中为学习者提供临时认知支持，当学习者掌握了相关技能后，支架即被撤除。

The "scaffolding" metaphor comes from the construction industry — scaffolding provides temporary support for buildings during construction and is removed when the building is complete. Similarly, instructional scaffolding provides temporary cognitive support during the learning process and is removed once the learner has mastered the relevant skills.

## 核心机制 / Core Mechanism

### 支架教学的六个功能 / Six Functions of Scaffolding

Wood, Bruner & Ross (1976) 提出了支架教学的六个核心功能：

Wood, Bruner & Ross (1976) proposed six core functions of scaffolding:

1. **招募 / Recruitment**：吸引学习者的兴趣，使其投入到任务中。/ Engaging the learner's interest and commitment to the task.
2. **简化 / Reduction in Degrees of Freedom**：简化任务要求，减少学习者需要同时处理的因素。/ Simplifying task requirements, reducing the number of factors the learner must handle simultaneously.
3. **维持方向 / Direction Maintenance**：保持学习者对目标的关注，防止偏离。/ Keeping the learner focused on the goal, preventing drift.
4. **标记关键特征 / Marking Critical Features**：标示任务中最重要的元素，引导学习者注意。/ Highlighting the most important elements of the task, directing learner attention.
5. **控制挫折 / Frustration Control**：管理学习者的挫折感，保持动机。/ Managing learner frustration, maintaining motivation.
6. **示范 / Demonstration**：展示解决问题的方法和策略。/ Modeling problem-solving methods and strategies.

### 支架的类型 / Types of Scaffolds

#### 硬支架 / Hard Scaffolds（预设支架）
在教学设计阶段预先设定的支持结构：
Support structures pre-designed during instructional planning:

- **工作表与模板 / Worksheets and Templates**：结构化的学习材料。
- **提示卡 / Prompt Cards**：引导思考的提示性问题。
- **概念图 / Concept Maps**：可视化的知识结构框架。
- **词汇表 / Glossaries**：关键术语的定义和解释。

#### 软支架 / Soft Scaffolds（即时支架）
在教学过程中根据学习者的即时需要提供的支持：
Support provided responsively during the instructional process based on immediate learner needs:

- **提问 / Questioning**：根据学习者的困惑提出引导性问题。
- **反馈 / Feedback**：针对学习者表现给予即时反馈。
- **重新解释 / Re-explanation**：用不同方式重新解释概念。
- **暗示与线索 / Hints and Cues**：提供部分信息引导学习者思考。

#### 同伴支架 / Peer Scaffolding
能力较强的同伴为能力较弱的同伴提供的支持：
Support provided by more capable peers for less capable peers:

- **合作问题解决 / Collaborative Problem-Solving**
- **同伴辅导 / Peer Tutoring**
- **合作写作 / Collaborative Writing**

### 撤架过程 / The Fading Process

撤架（fading）是支架教学的关键环节——如果支架不被撤除，学习者将产生依赖，无法实现独立学习。撤架应遵循以下原则：

Fading is the crucial element of scaffolding — if scaffolds are not removed, learners develop dependency and cannot achieve independent learning. Fading should follow these principles:

1. **渐进性 / Gradual**：支持应逐步减少，而非突然撤除。/ Support should decrease gradually, not be suddenly removed.
2. **响应性 / Responsive**：撤架的速度应根据学习者的进步而调整。/ The pace of fading should be adjusted based on learner progress.
3. **条件性 / Conditional**：只有当学习者展现出独立完成任务的能力时，才撤除相应支架。/ Scaffolds should be removed only when the learner demonstrates ability to complete the task independently.

## 发展脉络 / Development

### 起源 / Origins (1976)

"支架"一词最早出现在Wood, Bruner & Ross (1976) 的论文 "The Role of Tutoring in Problem Solving" 中。该研究观察了母亲如何帮助3-5岁儿童完成积木搭建任务，识别出上述六个支架功能。

The term "scaffolding" first appeared in Wood, Bruner & Ross's (1976) paper "The Role of Tutoring in Problem Solving." The study observed how mothers helped 3-5 year old children complete block-building tasks, identifying the six scaffolding functions described above.

### 教育学的拓展 / Pedagogical Expansion (1980s-1990s)

1980年代，随着维果茨基理论在西方的广泛传播，支架教学概念被拓展到各种教育情境：

In the 1980s, as Vygotsky's theory spread widely in the West, scaffolding was extended to diverse educational contexts:

- **写作教学 / Writing Instruction**：Applebee & Langer (1983) 将支架概念应用于写作教学。
- **阅读教学 / Reading Instruction**：Palincsar & Brown (1984) 的"互惠教学法"（Reciprocal Teaching）是支架教学在阅读领域的重要应用。
- **语言教学 / Language Teaching**：Gibbons (2002) 发展了课堂语言教学中的"高层支架"（high-challenge, high-support）方法。

### 技术与支架 / Technology and Scaffolding (2000s-present)

21世纪以来，数字技术为支架教学提供了新的可能性：

Since the 21st century, digital technology has opened new possibilities for scaffolding:

- **计算机支架 / Computer-Based Scaffolding**：智能辅导系统（Intelligent Tutoring Systems）根据学习者表现自动调整支持水平。
- **自适应学习 / Adaptive Learning**：人工智能驱动的个性化学习路径设计。
- **MOOCs中的支架 / Scaffolding in MOOCs**：大规模在线课程中的同伴支架和自动化反馈。

## 临床应用 / Clinical Applications

### 1. 课堂教学 / Classroom Instruction

**示例：写作教学中的支架 / Example: Scaffolding in Writing Instruction**

| 阶段 / Stage | 支架水平 / Scaffold Level | 活动 / Activity |
|--------------|--------------------------|----------------|
| 示范 / Modeling | 高 / High | 教师展示完整写作过程 / Teacher demonstrates complete writing process |
| 引导实践 / Guided Practice | 中高 / Medium-High | 师生共同撰写 / Teacher and students co-write |
| 合作写作 / Collaborative Writing | 中 / Medium | 同伴互助写作 / Peer-assisted writing |
| 独立写作 / Independent Writing | 低 / Low | 学生独立完成 / Student writes independently |

### 2. 语言教学 / Language Teaching

在第二语言教学中，支架教学的具体策略包括：

In second language teaching, specific scaffolding strategies include:

- **语言框架 / Language Frames**：提供句型模板（如"I think ___ because ___"）。
- **视觉辅助 / Visual Aids**：图片、图表、手势等非语言支持。
- **预教词汇 / Pre-teaching Vocabulary**：在阅读前教授关键术语。
- **思维出声 / Think-Aloud**：教师展示阅读和思考过程。

### 3. 特殊教育 / Special Education

对于学习障碍学生，支架教学需要更精细的分解和更缓慢的撤架：

For students with learning disabilities, scaffolding requires finer task decomposition and slower fading:

- **任务分解 / Task Analysis**：将复杂任务分解为小步骤。
- **多重示范 / Multiple Models**：通过多种方式（视觉、听觉、动手）示范。
- **频繁检查 / Frequent Check-ins**：更频繁地评估学习者的理解水平。
- **延长支架时间 / Extended Scaffolding Period**：给予更多时间在有支持下练习。

### 4. 临床督导 / Clinical Supervision

在心理治疗培训中，督导过程本质上是支架教学：

In psychotherapy training, the supervision process is essentially scaffolding:

- 督导者根据受训者的水平调整指导的详细程度。
- 从全面指导逐步过渡到最少干预。
- 受训者最终能够独立进行临床工作。

## 与其他概念的关联 / Related Concepts

### 维果茨基体系内 / Within Vygotsky's System

- **最近发展区 / Zone of Proximal Development**：支架教学发生在ZPD内。→ [zone-proximal-development.md](zone-proximal-development.md)
- **中介 / Mediation**：支架本质上是一种中介活动——教师通过语言、示范等文化工具中介学习者的认知过程。→ [mediation.md](mediation.md)
- **内化 / Internalization**：支架的最终目标是促进学习者的内化——将外部支持转化为内部能力。

### 相关理论 / Related Theories

- **Bruner 的螺旋课程 / Bruner's Spiral Curriculum**：课程内容在不同水平上反复呈现，每次增加深度。
- **Palincsar & Brown 的互惠教学法 / Reciprocal Teaching**：在阅读教学中运用预测、提问、澄清和总结四种策略。
- **Collins, Brown & Holum 的认知学徒制 / Cognitive Apprenticeship**：通过示范、辅导、脚手架和反思促进专业知识传递。
- **Belland 的基于问题的学习支架 / Problem-Based Learning Scaffolds**：在PBL情境中设计结构化支持。

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

### 与儒家"启发式"教学的对话 / Dialogue with Confucian "Heuristic" Teaching

孔子的"启发"概念与支架教学有着深层的对应关系。孔子提出"不愤不启，不悱不发。举一隅不以三隅反，则不复也"——教师在学习者"愤"（困惑但渴望理解）和"悱"（有话要说但表达不清）的时候提供启发，而非提前灌输。这精确对应了支架教学中"在学习者ZPD内提供适时支持"的原则。

Confucius's concept of "inspiration" (启发) has a deep correspondence with scaffolding. Confucius stated: "I do not enlighten those who are not eager to learn, nor do I instruct those who are not struggling to express themselves. If I hold up one corner and they cannot return with the other three, I do not repeat" — the teacher provides inspiration when the learner is in states of "frustrated eagerness" (愤) or "struggling expression" (悱), rather than pre-emptively indoctrinating. This precisely corresponds to scaffolding's principle of "providing timely support within the learner's ZPD."

孔子的"举一反三"理念还体现了支架教学的核心目标——培养学习者的独立思考和迁移能力，而非仅仅完成特定任务。

Confucius's principle of "from one corner deducing three" (举一反三) also embodies scaffolding's core goal — developing learners' independent thinking and transfer abilities, not merely completing specific tasks.

### 与禅宗"师徒制"的对话 / Dialogue with Chan Buddhism's Master-Disciple System

禅宗的师徒传承制度与支架教学有着结构性的平行关系。禅师根据弟子的"根器"（准备程度）和"时机"（教学时机）给予不同形式的指导——有时是直接教导（相当于"示范"），有时是公案（相当于"引导性问题"），有时是棒喝（相当于"标记关键特征"）。弟子开悟后，禅师即"撤架"，让弟子独立修行和传法。

The Chan Buddhist master-disciple transmission system has structural parallels with scaffolding. Chan masters give different forms of instruction based on the disciple's "capacity" (readiness) and "timing" (teachable moment) — sometimes direct teaching (equivalent to "demonstration"), sometimes koans (equivalent to "guiding questions"), sometimes stick and shout (equivalent to "marking critical features"). After the disciple achieves enlightenment, the master "fades," allowing the disciple to practice and teach independently.
