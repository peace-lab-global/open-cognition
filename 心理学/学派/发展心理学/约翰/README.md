# 约翰·鲍尔比 · John Bowlby

## 基本信息 / Basic Information

**全名 / Full Name**：爱德华·约翰·莫斯特·鲍尔比（Edward John Mostyn Bowlby）
**生卒 / Lifespan**：1907年2月26日（伦敦）– 1990年9月2日（苏格兰斯凯岛）/ 26 February 1907 (London) – 2 September 1990 (Isle of Skye, Scotland)
**国籍 / Nationality**：英国 / British
**身份 / Roles**：精神分析师、精神病学家、发展心理学家 / Psychoanalyst, psychiatrist, developmental psychologist
**主要职位 / Key Positions**：
- 伦敦塔维斯托克诊所（Tavistock Clinic）儿童与家庭部门主任 / Director, Department for Children and Families, Tavistock Clinic, London
- 伦敦大学学院（UCL）客座教授 / Visiting Professor, University College London
- 英国精神分析学会（British Psychoanalytical Society）正式会员 / Full member, British Psychoanalytical Society

**学术师承 / Academic Lineage**：
- 精神分析训练师：梅兰妮·克莱因（Melanie Klein）/ Psychoanalytic supervisor: Melanie Klein
- 重要合作者：玛丽·安斯沃思（Mary Ainsworth）、詹姆斯·罗伯逊（James Robertson）/ Key collaborators: Mary Ainsworth, James Robertson

## 核心命题 / Core Propositions

**命题一 / Proposition 1**：
依恋行为是人类的基本行为系统，与觅食和性行为同等重要——它不是衍生于喂养的次级驱力，而是一种独立的、进化而来的生存机制。
Attachment behaviour constitutes a fundamental behavioural system in humans, as primary as feeding and sexuality — it is not a secondary drive derived from feeding, but an independent, evolutionarily derived survival mechanism.

**命题二 / Proposition 2**：
婴儿与主要照顾者（通常是母亲）之间的情感纽带质量，直接塑造了个体的人格结构与终身关系模式。
The quality of the emotional bond between infant and primary caregiver (typically the mother) directly shapes the individual's personality structure and lifelong relationship patterns.

**命题三 / Proposition 3**：
每个个体基于早期依恋经验建构"内部工作模型"（internal working models）——关于自我、他人和关系的心理表征——这些模型在无意识层面运作，影响个体对世界的预期和回应方式。
Each individual constructs "internal working models" — mental representations of self, others, and relationships — based on early attachment experiences. These models operate at unconscious levels, shaping how the individual anticipates and responds to the world.

**命题四 / Proposition 4**：
可靠的照顾者为儿童提供"安全基地"（secure base），使其得以自信地探索外部世界，并在遭遇威胁时返回寻求安慰——安全基地功能是健康心理发展的必要条件。
A reliable caregiver provides the child with a "secure base" from which to confidently explore the external world and to which to return for comfort when threatened — the secure base function is a necessary condition for healthy psychological development.

**命题五 / Proposition 5**：
早期与依恋对象的分离和丧失会引发可预测的、阶段性的痛苦反应（抗议—绝望—脱离），持续或反复的分离可能对心理发展造成深远损害。
Early separation from and loss of attachment figures elicits predictable, phased distress responses (protest–despair–detachment); prolonged or repeated separations may inflict profound damage on psychological development.

**命题六 / Proposition 6**：
依恋需要贯穿整个生命周期——成人同样需要安全基地来维持心理健康，心理治疗的核心功能之一就是为来访者提供安全基地体验。
Attachment needs persist throughout the lifespan — adults likewise require a secure base to maintain psychological health, and one of the core functions of psychotherapy is to provide a secure base experience for the client.

> "The propensity to make strong emotional bonds to particular individuals is a basic component of human nature."
> （与特定个体建立强烈情感纽带的倾向是人性的基本组成部分。）
> — John Bowlby, *A Secure Base* (1988)

## 思想脉络 / Intellectual Trajectory

### 第一阶段：个人经验与临床觉醒（1907–1950）/ Phase 1: Personal Experience and Clinical Awakening (1907–1950)

鲍尔比出生于伦敦一个上层中产阶级家庭。父亲安东尼·鲍尔比爵士（Sir Anthony Bowlby）是国王的外科医生，母亲玛丽安（Marian）信奉"每天与孩子接触一小时即可"的育儿理念。鲍尔比主要由保姆（nanny）照料，六岁时心爱的保姆离开，他经历了深刻的丧失感——这一童年经验被传记作者约翰·霍姆斯（John Holmes）视为他终生关注分离与丧失的个人根源。

Bowlby was born into an upper-middle-class family in London. His father, Sir Anthony Bowlby, was surgeon to the King; his mother, Marian, adhered to the view that one hour of daily contact with children sufficed. Bowlby was raised primarily by a nanny, and when she left at age six, he experienced a profound sense of loss — a childhood experience that biographer John Holmes identified as the personal root of his lifelong preoccupation with separation and loss.

他先后在剑桥大学三一学院学习医学和自然科学，后进入伦敦大学学院医院学习精神病学，并在塔维斯托克诊所接受精神分析训练。他的精神分析训练师是梅兰妮·克莱因，但鲍尔比逐渐对克莱因学派过度强调内部幻想（phantasy）而忽视真实养育环境的做法产生不满。1944年，他发表了开创性论文《四十四名少年小偷》（*Forty-Four Juvenile Thieves*），首次系统呈现了早期母子分离与反社会行为（特别是"无情型人格"affectionless character）之间的统计关联。

He studied medicine and natural sciences at Trinity College, Cambridge, then trained in psychiatry at University College Hospital and psychoanalysis at the Tavistock Clinic. His psychoanalytic supervisor was Melanie Klein, but Bowlby grew increasingly dissatisfied with the Kleinian school's overemphasis on internal phantasy at the expense of real caregiving environments. In 1944 he published his landmark paper *Forty-Four Juvenile Thieves*, presenting for the first time systematic evidence of the statistical association between early mother-child separation and antisocial behaviour, particularly the "affectionless character."

### 第二阶段：依恋理论的系统建构（1950–1970）/ Phase 2: Systematic Construction of Attachment Theory (1950–1970)

1951年，鲍尔比受世界卫生组织（WHO）委托撰写了关于无家可归儿童心理健康的报告《母性照顾与心理健康》（*Maternal Care and Mental Health*），提出了"母性剥夺"（maternal deprivation）假说——认为婴幼儿期与母亲（或母亲替代者）的分离会对心理发展造成严重损害。这份报告引发了广泛的社会政策讨论，也招致了来自精神分析界和学术心理学界的批评。

In 1951, Bowlby was commissioned by the World Health Organization to write a report on the mental health of homeless children, producing *Maternal Care and Mental Health*, which advanced the "maternal deprivation" hypothesis — arguing that separation from the mother (or mother-substitute) during infancy could inflict serious damage on psychological development. This report provoked wide social policy discussion and drew criticism from both psychoanalytic and academic 心理学 circles.

面对批评，鲍尔比转向多学科整合，从以下领域汲取理论资源来重新构建依恋理论：

In response to criticism, Bowlby turned to multidisciplinary integration, drawing theoretical resources from the following fields to reconstruct attachment theory:

- **行为学/动物行为学（Ethology）**：洛伦茨的印记（imprinting）研究和廷伯根（Tinbergen）的本能行为理论，使鲍尔比将依恋理解为一种先天的、适应性的行为系统 / Lorenz's imprinting 研究 and Tinbergen's instinct theory led Bowlby to reconceptualize attachment as an innate, adaptive behavioural system
- **控制论（Cybernetics）**：反馈环路概念帮助他解释依恋行为的动态调节机制 / The concept of feedback loops helped explain the dynamic regulation of attachment behaviour
- **认知心理学（Cognitive Psychology）**：内部表征和信息加工模型为"内部工作模型"概念提供了框架 / Internal representation and information-processing models provided the framework for the "internal working models" concept
- **精神分析（Psychoanalysis）**：保留了客体关系理论对早期关系的重视，但扬弃了驱力理论 / Retained object relations' emphasis on early relationships while discarding drive theory

1958年发表的论文《儿童对母亲的情感纽带本质》（*The Nature of the Child's Tie to His Mother*）标志着依恋理论的首次系统阐述。1969年，《依恋与丧失》第一卷出版，依恋理论的完整理论框架正式确立。

The 1958 paper *The Nature of the Child's Tie to His Mother* marked the first systematic exposition of attachment theory. In 1969, *Attachment and Loss* Volume 1 was published, formally establishing the complete theoretical framework.

### 第三阶段：实证验证与理论深化（1970–1990）/ Phase 3: Empirical Validation and Theoretical Deepening (1970–1990)

鲍尔比的理论构想被他的合作者玛丽·安斯沃思（Mary Ainsworth）通过"陌生情境"（Strange Situation）实验范式所实证验证。安斯沃思识别出三种主要依恋类型：安全型（secure）、回避型（avoidant）、焦虑-矛盾型（anxious-ambivalent），后由玛丽·梅因（Mary Main）补充第四种——混乱型（disorganized）。

Bowlby's theoretical构想 was empirically validated by his collaborator Mary Ainsworth through the "Strange Situation" experimental paradigm. Ainsworth identified three primary attachment patterns: secure, avoidant, and anxious-ambivalent, later supplemented by Mary Main with a fourth — disorganized.

在生命的最后二十年，鲍尔比致力于将依恋理论应用于临床实践。1988年出版的《安全基地》（*A Secure Base*）总结了依恋理论在心理治疗中的应用——治疗师作为"安全基地"，帮助来访者探索痛苦的内在经验。这一视角深刻影响了后来的情绪聚焦治疗（EFT）、心智化治疗（MBT）和慈悲聚焦治疗（CFT）等治疗流派。

In the final two decades of his life, Bowlby devoted himself to clinical applications of attachment theory. *A Secure Base* (1988) synthesized the therapeutic implications — the therapist as "secure base" helping clients explore painful inner experiences. This perspective profoundly influenced later therapeutic modalities including Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT), Mentalization-Based Treatment (MBT), and Compassion-Focused Therapy (CFT).

## 重要概念 / Key Concepts

1. **依恋行为系统（Attachment Behavioural System）** — 一种进化而来的、以维持与照顾者接近为目标的行为组织系统 → [concepts/attachment-theory](concepts/依恋.md)
2. **安全基地（Secure Base）** — 可靠的照顾者为儿童提供的心理与物理"基地"，支撑探索行为 → [concepts/secure-base](concepts/secure-base.md)
3. **内部工作模型（Internal Working Models）** — 基于依恋经验建构的关于自我与他人的无意识心理表征 → [concepts/internal-working-models](concepts/internal-working-models.md)
4. **分离焦虑（Separation Anxiety）** — 与依恋对象分离时引发的阶段性痛苦反应 → [concepts/separation-anxiety](concepts/separation-anxiety.md)
5. **母性剥夺（Maternal Deprivation）** — 婴幼儿期缺乏母亲或母亲替代者的敏感照顾所导致的发展损害 → [concepts/maternal-deprivation](concepts/maternal-deprivation.md)
6. **依恋类型（Attachment Patterns）** — 安斯沃思在鲍尔比理论基础上识别的安全型、回避型、焦虑-矛盾型、混乱型四种模式
7. **哀伤与丧失（Grief and Loss）** — 依恋对象丧失后的心理反应过程，包括正常哀伤与病理性哀伤的区分
8. **安全基地行为（Secure Base Behaviour）** — 儿童在安全基地与探索行为之间的动态平衡模式
9. **敏感性假说（Sensitivity Hypothesis）** — 照顾者的敏感回应是安全依恋形成的关键因素
10. **终身依恋（Attachment Across the Lifespan）** — 依恋需要并非仅限于婴儿期，而是贯穿整个生命周期的基本人类需要

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

1. **心理治疗 / Psychotherapy**：依恋理论已成为多种循证治疗模型的理论基石——情绪聚焦治疗（EFT，Sue Johnson）、心智化治疗（MBT，Peter Fonagy）、慈悲聚焦治疗（CFT，Paul Gilbert）、图式治疗（Schema Therapy，Jeffrey Young）。治疗关系本身被视为一种"矫正性依恋体验"。 / Attachment theory has become the theoretical foundation for multiple evidence-based treatment models — EFT, MBT, CFT, Schema Therapy. The therapeutic relationship itself is understood as a "corrective attachment experience."

2. **儿童发展与评估 / Child Development and Assessment**：陌生情境实验（Strange Situation）、成人依恋访谈（AAI）、依恋Q分类（Attachment Q-Sort）等工具广泛应用于研究和临床评估。 / The Strange Situation, Adult Attachment Interview (AAI), and Attachment Q-Sort are widely used in 研究 and clinical assessment.

3. **教育心理学 / Educational Psychology**：安全依恋与学业成就、社交能力、情绪调节的正相关已被大量元分析研究证实。"安全基地"理念影响了学校心理咨询和师生关系建设实践。 / The positive correlation between secure attachment and academic achievement, social competence, and emotional regulation has been confirmed by numerous meta-analyses. The "secure base" concept has influenced school counselling and teacher-student relationship practices.

4. **社会政策 / Social Policy**：鲍尔比关于住院儿童分离反应的研究直接推动了英国NHS医院允许父母陪护住院儿童的政策改革。他的工作也影响了寄养制度、收养程序和儿童福利立法。 / Bowlby's 研究 on separation responses in hospitalized children directly drove NHS policy reform allowing parents to stay with hospitalized children. His work also influenced foster care systems, adoption procedures, and child welfare legislation.

5. **神经科学 / Neuroscience**：依恋理论获得了日益丰富的神经科学实证支持——催产素（oxytocin）系统在依恋纽带形成中的作用、杏仁核（amygdala）在威胁检测与依恋激活中的角色、前额叶皮层（prefrontal cortex）在情绪调节与安全基地功能中的参与。Stephen Porges的多迷走神经理论（Polyvagal Theory）进一步扩展了依恋的神经生理学解释。 / Attachment theory has received increasingly rich neuroscientific support — the role of the oxytocin system in bond formation, the amygdala in threat detection and attachment activation, and the prefrontal cortex in emotion regulation and secure base function. Stephen Porges' Polyvagal Theory further extends the neurophysiological account of attachment.

6. **组织心理学 / Organizational Psychology**：依恋理论被应用于领导力研究和团队动力学——领导者的"安全基地"功能、组织中的"依恋风格"与员工敬业度的关系。 / Attachment theory has been applied to leadership 研究 and team dynamics — the leader's "secure base" function, the relationship between organizational "attachment styles" and employee engagement.

7. **数字时代依恋 / Attachment in the Digital Age**：当代研究者探索数字设备（智能手机、社交媒体）是否成为新的"依恋对象"，以及虚拟连接对安全基地功能的影响。 / Contemporary researchers explore whether digital devices (smartphones, social media) have become new "attachment objects" and the impact of virtual connection on secure base functions.

8. **跨文化依恋研究 / Cross-Cultural Attachment Research**：依恋类型的文化分布差异（如德国回避型比例较高、日本焦虑型比例较高）引发了关于依恋理论文化普适性的深入讨论。 / Cultural variations in attachment pattern distribution (e.g., higher avoidant rates in Germany, higher anxious rates in Japan) have prompted deeper discussion of the cultural universality of attachment theory.

## 常见误读 / Common Misreadings

**误读一 / Misreading 1**：
❌ "鲍尔比说母亲必须全职在家照顾孩子。" / "Bowlby said mothers must stay home full-time to care for children."
✅ 鲍尔比强调的是"敏感、可及的照顾"的**质量**，而非照顾者的性别、身份或是否全职。他明确指出父亲、祖父母或专业照顾者同样可以担任安全基地角色。他的核心关切是照顾的**连续性和情感质量**，而非照顾者的特定身份。 / Bowlby emphasized the **quality** of "sensitive, available caregiving," not the gender, identity, or full-time status of the caregiver. He explicitly stated that fathers, grandparents, or professional caregivers can equally serve the secure base role. His core concern was the **continuity and emotional quality** of care, not the specific identity of the caregiver.

**误读二 / Misreading 2**：
❌ "依恋类型在婴儿期确定后就永远固定不变。" / "Attachment patterns, once established in infancy, are permanently fixed."
✅ 依恋类型具有相当的稳定性，但绝非不可改变。后续的关系经验——包括心理治疗、稳定的伴侣关系、反思性功能的提升——都可以修正早期的不安全依恋模式。"习得的安全感"（earned security）概念表明，即使童年经历不安全的依恋，成人仍可以发展出安全的依恋状态。 / Attachment patterns show considerable stability but are by no means immutable. Subsequent relational experiences — including psychotherapy, stable partnerships, enhanced reflective functioning — can modify early insecure patterns. The concept of "earned security" demonstrates that adults can develop secure attachment states even after insecure childhood experiences.

**误读三 / Misreading 3**：
❌ "依恋理论只适用于婴儿与母亲之间的关系。" / "Attachment theory applies only to the infant-mother relationship."
✅ 鲍尔比本人明确指出依恋行为贯穿整个生命周期。玛丽·梅因的成人依恋访谈（AAI）和辛迪·哈赞（Cindy Hazan）与菲利普·谢弗（Phillip Shaver）的成人浪漫依恋研究已将依恋理论扩展到友谊、浪漫关系、治疗关系乃至人与宠物、人与地方的联结。 / Bowlby himself explicitly stated that attachment behaviour persists throughout the lifespan. Mary Main's Adult Attachment Interview (AAI) and Cindy Hazan & Phillip Shaver's adult romantic attachment 研究 have extended attachment theory to friendships, romantic relationships, therapeutic relationships, and even human-pet and human-place bonds.

**误读四 / Misreading 4**：
❌ "鲍尔比是生物决定论者，认为一切都是基因决定的。" / "Bowlby was a biological determinist who believed everything is genetically determined."
✅ 鲍尔比的理论是**基因-环境交互作用**的典范——依恋行为系统具有生物基础，但其具体表达模式（安全或不安全）完全取决于真实的养育环境经验。他反复强调"真实的环境事件"（real environmental events）而非内部幻想对发展的决定性影响。 / Bowlby's theory is a paradigm of **gene-environment interaction** — the attachment behavioural system has a biological foundation, but its specific expression pattern (secure or insecure) depends entirely on actual caregiving environmental experience. He repeatedly emphasized the decisive developmental impact of "real environmental events" rather than internal phantasy.

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

### 与唯识学的对话 / Dialogue with Yogacara (Consciousness-Only) Buddhism

鲍尔比的"内部工作模型"概念与唯识学的"阿赖耶识"（alaya-vijnana, storehouse consciousness）之间存在深层共鸣。两者都描述了早期经验如何在无意识层面形成持久的心智结构，并持续影响个体对现实的感知和回应方式。唯识学的"种子"（bija）概念——过去的行为经验在阿赖耶识中留下"种子"，在因缘和合时"现行"——与内部工作模型中"早期依恋经验形成心理表征，在无意识层面组织后续关系经验"的机制高度类似。

Bowlby's "internal working models" resonate deeply with Yogacara Buddhism's "alaya-vijnana" (storehouse consciousness). Both describe how early experience forms enduring mental structures at unconscious levels, continuously shaping perception and response. The Yogacara concept of "bija" (seeds) — past behavioural experiences leaving "seeds" in the alaya-vijnana that "manifest" when conditions converge — closely parallels the mechanism by which early attachment experiences form mental representations that unconsciously organize subsequent relational experience.

关键差异在于：鲍尔比的模型强调具体的关系经验（特别是早期母婴互动）作为心理结构的来源，而唯识学将种子理解为跨越多世的业力积累。但两者都为"早期经验如何塑造当下感知"提供了系统解释，也为心理改变提供了路径——鲍尔比通过新的关系经验修正工作模型，唯识学通过修行转化种子。

A key difference: Bowlby's model emphasizes specific relational experiences (especially early caregiver-infant interactions) as the source of mental structures, while Yogacara understands seeds as accumulating across multiple lifetimes. Yet both provide systematic accounts of how early experience shapes present perception, and both offer pathways for psychological change — Bowlby through new relational experiences modifying working models, Yogacara through practice transforming seeds.

### 与庄子哲学的对话 / Dialogue with Zhuangzi

庄子的"逍遥游"所描述的自由状态——不受内在执念和外在束缚限制的存在方式——可以与安全依恋个体的心理自由相比较。安全依恋的儿童从安全基地出发自由探索世界，正如庄子笔下的"至人"不被固定的自我认同和关系期待所束缚。

The state of freedom described in Zhuangzi's "Free and Easy Wandering" (xiaoyao you) — existence unbound by internal fixations and external constraints — can be compared with the psychological freedom of the securely attached individual. The securely attached child freely explores the world from a secure base, just as Zhuangzi's "perfected person" (zhiren) remains unbound by fixed self-identity and relational expectations.

然而，两者的出发点截然不同：鲍尔比认为安全的关系联结是自由的前提——自由来自"有所依"（有安全基地可回归）；而庄子暗示最高的自由来自超越一切依附——"无待"（无所依赖）。这一对比触及了东西方思想中关于"关系性自由"与"超越性自由"的深层张力。

Yet their starting points diverge sharply: Bowlby holds that secure relational connection is the precondition of freedom — freedom comes from "having something to depend on" (a secure base to return to); Zhuangzi suggests that the highest freedom comes from transcending all attachment — "having nothing to depend on" (wu dai). This contrast touches a deep tension between "relational freedom" and "transcendent freedom" in Eastern and Western thought.

### 与佛教心理学的对话 / Dialogue with Buddhist Psychology

佛教心理学对"执着"（upadana, clinging/attachment）的理解与鲍尔比对不安全依恋的分析形成有趣的对照。佛教将执着视为苦的根源，提倡通过觉察和不执来超越对特定对象的依赖。鲍尔比则区分了安全的依附（健康的联结）和不安全的依附（焦虑的执着）——他的目标不是消除依附，而是将不安全的依附转化为安全的联结。

Buddhist psychology's understanding of "upadana" (clinging/attachment) forms an illuminating contrast with Bowlby's analysis of insecure attachment. Buddhism regards clinging as the root of suffering, advocating transcendence through awareness and non-attachment. Bowlby distinguishes secure attachment (healthy connection) from insecure attachment (anxious clinging) — his goal is not to eliminate attachment but to transform insecure attachment into secure connection.

这一对话提示了一个综合的可能性：心理健康可能需要同时具备安全联结的能力（鲍尔比的贡献）和适度放下的智慧（佛教的贡献）——在"有所依"与"无所执"之间找到动态平衡。

This dialogue suggests a potential synthesis: psychological health may require both the capacity for secure connection (Bowlby's contribution) and the wisdom of appropriate letting-go (Buddhism's contribution) — finding a dynamic balance between "having something to depend on" and "clinging to nothing."

## 跨学科关联 / Interdisciplinary Connections

- **神经科学 / Neuroscience**：催产素、杏仁核、前额叶皮层、镜像神经元系统在依恋行为中的作用；Porges的多迷走神经理论 / Oxytocin, amygdala, prefrontal cortex, mirror neuron systems in attachment behaviour; Porges' Polyvagal Theory
- **精神分析 / Psychoanalysis**：与温尼科特"足够好的母亲"、科胡特"镜映"（mirroring）、比昂"容纳"（containment）的理论对话 / Theoretical dialogue with Winnicott's "good-enough mother," Kohut's "mirroring," Bion's "containment"
- **发展心理学 / Developmental Psychology**：与皮亚杰认知发展理论、埃里克森心理社会发展理论的交叉 / Intersection with Piaget's cognitive development theory and Erikson's psychosocial development theory
- **进化心理学 / Evolutionary Psychology**：依恋理论作为进化心理学的先驱——将人类情感理解为适应性机制 / Attachment theory as precursor to evolutionary 心理学 — understanding human emotions as adaptive mechanisms
- **社会学 / Sociology**：依恋理论对社区凝聚力、社会资本研究的启示——安全依恋的个体更可能建立信任关系和参与社区活动 / Attachment theory's implications for community cohesion and social capital 研究 — securely attached individuals are more likely to build trust relationships and participate in community activities
- **教育学 / Education**：依恋理论对师生关系、课堂管理、学校心理健康服务的指导意义 / Attachment theory's guidance for teacher-student relationships, classroom management, and school mental health services
- **存在主义心理学 / Existential Psychology**：依恋丧失与存在焦虑的关联——鲍尔比的丧失理论与罗洛·梅（Rollo May）、欧文·亚隆（Irvin Yalom）的存在焦虑概念的对话 / Connection between attachment loss and existential anxiety — Bowlby's loss theory in dialogue with Rollo May's and Irvin Yalom's existential anxiety concepts
