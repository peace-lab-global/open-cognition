# 客体关系理论 · Object Relations Theory

## 定义 / Definition

客体关系理论是梅兰妮·克莱因最具影响力的理论框架——它主张人类心理的基本单位不是弗洛伊德所说的"驱力"（drive），而是"关系"（relation）。婴儿从出生起就处于与客体（主要是母亲/照顾者）的关系中，这些早期关系被内化为"内在客体关系"，构成个体心理生活的核心结构。

Object relations theory is Melanie Klein's most influential theoretical framework—it argues that the basic unit of human psychology is not the "drive" as Freud proposed, but the "relation." From birth, the infant exists in relation to objects (primarily the mother/caregiver), and these early relations are internalized as "internal object relations," forming the core structure of individual psychological life.

在克莱因的理论中，"客体"（object）不是指物理世界中的物体，而是指心理世界中的"人"或"人的部分"——最初是母亲的乳房，后来是完整的母亲，再后来是父亲和其他照顾者。"客体关系"就是个体与这些内在和外在客体之间的关系模式。

In Klein's theory, "object" does not refer to physical things but to "people" or "parts of people" in the psychological world—initially the mother's breast, later the whole mother, then the father and other caregivers. "Object relations" are the patterns of relationship between the individual and these internal and external objects.

## 核心机制 / Core Mechanisms

### 内射与投射 / Introjection and Projection

客体关系的形成依赖两个基本机制：**内射**（introjection）和**投射**（projection）。内射是个体将外部客体的特征纳入自我内部的过程——例如，婴儿将满足自己的"好乳房"内射为内在的好对象。投射是个体将自我的某些部分或特征归因于外部客体——例如，婴儿将自己的攻击性投射到"坏乳房"上。

The formation of object relations depends on two fundamental mechanisms: **introjection** and **projection**. Introjection is the process by which the individual incorporates characteristics of external objects into the self—for example, the infant introjects the satisfying "good breast" as an internal good object. Projection is the process by which the individual attributes parts or characteristics of the self to external objects—for example, the infant projects its own aggression onto the "bad breast."

这两个机制相互作用，构成了内在客体世界的形成基础。内射建立内在好对象（提供安全感和内在资源），投射处理内在坏对象（将威胁外化）。当两者达到平衡时，心理发展健康进行；当投射过度或内射不足时，心理发展受阻。

These two mechanisms interact to form the foundation of the internal object world. Introjection builds internal good objects (providing security and internal resources), while projection deals with internal bad objects (externalizing threats). When the two are in balance, psychological development proceeds healthily; when projection is excessive or introjection insufficient, psychological development is impeded.

### 幻想的核心地位 / The Centrality of Fantasy

克莱因认为，婴儿从出生起就具有活跃的幻想生活（phantasy life）。"幻想"（phantasy）在克莱因理论中有特殊含义——它不是有意识的想象，而是潜意识的心理活动，是驱力在心理层面的表达。婴儿的每一个本能冲动都伴随着一个幻想——例如，饥饿的本能伴随着"吞噬好乳房"的幻想或"被坏乳房吞噬"的幻想。

Klein believed that infants have an active phantasy life from birth. "Phantasy" has a special meaning in Kleinian theory—it is not conscious imagination but unconscious mental activity, the expression of drives at the psychological level. Every instinctual impulse in the infant is accompanied by a phantasy—for example, the instinct of hunger is accompanied by the phantasy of "devouring the good breast" or "being devoured by the bad breast."

幻想是客体关系的心理媒介——正是通过幻想，外在的现实关系被转化为内在的心理结构。因此，克莱因的客体关系理论既不是纯粹的外部环境决定论（如行为主义），也不是纯粹的内在驱力决定论（如经典弗洛伊德主义），而是两者的辩证统一。

Phantasy is the psychological medium of object relations—it is through phantasy that external real relations are transformed into internal psychological structures. Thus, Klein's object relations theory is neither purely external environmental determinism (as in behaviorism) nor purely internal drive determinism (as in classical Freudianism), but a dialectical unity of both.

## 发展脉络 / Developmental Trajectory

### 从弗洛伊德到克莱因 / From Freud to Klein

弗洛伊德的精神分析以"驱力"（Trieb）为核心——他认为心理生活的基本动力来自生本能（Eros）和死本能（Thanatos）。客体在弗洛伊德理论中是次要的——它是驱力满足的工具。克莱因保留了弗洛伊德的本能理论（特别是死本能的概念），但将焦点从驱力转向了关系。

Freud's psychoanalysis centered on "drive" (Trieb)—he believed the basic dynamic of psychological life comes from the life instinct (Eros) and death instinct (Thanatos). Objects were secondary in Freud's theory—they were instruments of drive satisfaction. Klein retained Freud's instinct theory (especially the concept of the death instinct) but shifted the focus from drives to relations.

这一转向的意义在于：如果心理生活的基本单位是关系而非驱力，那么心理病理的根源就不是驱力的压抑，而是关系的扭曲。治疗的目标也因此改变——不是释放被压抑的驱力，而是修复受损的客体关系。

The significance of this shift is: if the basic unit of psychological life is relation rather than drive, then the root of psychopathology is not drive repression but relational distortion. The goal of treatment also changes—not to release repressed drives but to repair damaged object relations.

### 克莱因学派的发展 / Development of the Kleinian School

克莱因的理论在1940年代引发了英国精神分析学会的"大论争"（Controversial Discussions, 1942-1944）。安娜·弗洛伊德学派批评克莱因偏离了弗洛伊德的驱力理论，而独立学派（温尼科特等）则试图在两者之间找到中间立场。最终，英国精神分析学会分为三个团体：克莱因学派、安娜·弗洛伊德学派和独立学派。

Klein's theories provoked the "Controversial Discussions" (1942-1944) in the British Psychoanalytical Society. The Anna Freud school criticized Klein for departing from Freud's drive theory, while the Independent school (Winnicott and others) tried to find middle ground. Eventually, the British Psychoanalytical Society split into three groups: the Kleinian school, the Anna Freud school, and the Independent school.

### 当代发展 / Contemporary Developments

克莱因的客体关系理论在当代持续发展。**关系精神分析**（Relational Psychoanalysis）继承并修正了克莱因的理论，强调主体间性（intersubjectivity）和双向性（mutuality）。**神经精神分析**（Neuropsychoanalysis）试图将客体关系理论与神经科学整合。

Klein's object relations theory continues to develop. **Relational Psychoanalysis** has inherited and revised Klein's theory, emphasizing intersubjectivity and mutuality. **Neuropsychoanalysis** attempts to integrate object relations theory with neuroscience.

## 临床应用 / Clinical Applications

在临床实践中，客体关系理论指导治疗师关注来访者的**内在客体关系模式**——这些模式如何在治疗关系中"活现"（enactment），以及如何通过治疗关系的修复来改变这些模式。

In clinical practice, object relations theory guides therapists to attend to the patient's **internal object relation patterns**—how these patterns are "enacted" in the therapeutic relationship, and how they can be modified through the repair of the therapeutic relationship.

关键的治疗技术包括：分析移情和反移情中的客体关系模式；识别投射性认同的过程；帮助来访者从偏执-分裂位态向抑郁位态移动；以及提供一个"足够好"的容器来接纳和转化来访者的痛苦体验。

Key therapeutic techniques include: analyzing object relation patterns in transference and countertransference; identifying processes of projective identification; helping the patient move from paranoid-schizoid to depressive position; and providing a "good enough" container to accept and transform the patient's painful experiences.

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

客体关系理论与佛教"缘起"（pratītyasamutpāda）思想有深层共鸣。缘起论认为一切现象都是在关系中产生的——没有独立的自我，只有在关系中存在的自我。这与客体关系理论的核心主张——自我从出生起就在关系中形成——形成了有趣的平行。

Object relations theory resonates deeply with the Buddhist concept of "dependent origination" (pratītyasamutpāda). Dependent origination holds that all phenomena arise in relation—there is no independent self, only a self that exists in relation. This forms an interesting parallel with the core claim of object relations theory—that the self is formed in relations from birth.

儒家的"仁"（rén）概念也可与客体关系理论对话。"仁"是人与人之间的关系性美德——它暗示人的本质存在于关系中，而非孤立的个体性中。这与客体关系理论强调关系是心理生活的基本单位相呼应。

The Confucian concept of "rén" (humaneness) can also dialogue with object relations theory. "Rén" is a relational virtue between people—it implies that human essence exists in relations rather than in isolated individuality. This echoes the emphasis in object relations theory that relation is the basic unit of psychological life.
