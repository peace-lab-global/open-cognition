# 克莱因主要著作年表 · Chronological Table of Klein's Major Works

## 早期论文 / Early Papers (1919-1929)

| 年份 / Year | 标题 / Title | 发表 / Published In | 核心内容 / Core Content |
|------|------|------|------|
| 1919 | "Der Familienroman in statu nascendi" | *Internationale Zeitschrift für Psychoanalyse* | 克莱因的第一篇论文，基于对自己儿子的观察 (Klein's first paper, based on observations of her own son) |
| 1921 | "The Development of a Child" | *International Journal of Psycho-Analysis* | 儿童心理发展的早期观察 (Early observations of child psychological development) |
| 1923 | "The Role of the School in the Libidinal Development of the Child" | *International Journal of Psycho-Analysis* | 学校环境与力比多发展 (School environment and libidinal development) |
| 1924 | "The Technical Handling of Transference in the Analysis of Young Children" | *International Journal of Psycho-Analysis* | 儿童分析中的移情处理 (Handling of transference in child analysis) |
| 1925 | "A Contribution to the Psychogenesis of Manic-Depressive States" | *International Journal of Psycho-Analysis* | 躁狂-抑郁状态的起源研究 (Study of the psychogenesis of manic-depressive states) |
| 1927 | "Symposium on Child Analysis" | *International Journal of Psycho-Analysis* | 与安娜·弗洛伊德的首次公开分歧 (First public disagreement with Anna Freud) |
| 1928 | "Early Stages of the Oedipus Conflict" | *Writings* 1 | 早期俄狄浦斯冲突的理论 (Theory of early Oedipus conflict) |
| 1929 | "Personification in the Play of Children" | *International Journal of Psycho-Analysis* | 儿童游戏中的人格化现象 (Personification in children's play) |

## 核心理论期 / Core Theory Period (1930-1949)

| 年份 / Year | 标题 / Title | 发表 / Published In | 核心内容 / Core Content |
|------|------|------|------|
| 1930 | "The Importance of Symbol-Formation in the Development of the Ego" | *International Journal of Psycho-Analysis* | 符号形成与自我发展的关系 (Relationship between symbol-formation and ego development) |
| 1932 | *The Psycho-Analysis of Children* | Hogarth Press, London | **核心著作**：系统阐述游戏技术和儿童精神分析方法 (Seminal work: systematic articulation of play technique and child psychoanalysis method) |
| 1934 | "A Contribution to the Psychogenesis of Manic-Depressive States" (revised) | *Contributions to Psycho-Analysis* | 修订后的躁狂-抑郁理论 (Revised manic-depressive theory) |
| 1935 | "The Psycho-Genesis of Manic Depressive States" | *International Journal of Psycho-Analysis* | 抑郁位态的首次系统阐述 (First systematic articulation of the depressive position) |
| 1936 | "The Weaning Situation" | *Contributions to Psycho-Analysis* | 断奶情境的心理意义 (Psychological significance of the weaning situation) |
| 1937 | *Love, Guilt and Reparation* | Hogarth Press, London | **重要著作**：爱、内疚与修复的关系 (Important work: relationship between love, guilt, and reparation) |
| 1940 | "Mourning and Its Relation to Manic-Depressive States" | *International Journal of Psycho-Analysis* | 哀悼过程与抑郁位态的关系 (Relationship between mourning process and depressive position) |
| 1945 | "The Oedipus Complex in the Light of Early Anxieties" | *International Journal of Psycho-Analysis* | 早期焦虑视角下的俄狄浦斯情结 (Oedipus complex from the perspective of early anxieties) |
| 1946 | "Notes on Some Schizoid Mechanisms" | *International Journal of Psycho-Analysis* | **里程碑论文**：首次提出偏执-分裂位态和投射性认同 (Landmark paper: first formulation of paranoid-schizoid position and projective identification) |
| 1948 | "On the Theory of Anxiety and Guilt" | *International Journal of Psycho-Analysis* | 焦虑与内疚的理论 (Theory of anxiety and guilt) |

## 晚期著作 / Late Works (1950-1961)

| 年份 / Year | 标题 / Title | 发表 / Published In | 核心内容 / Core Content |
|------|------|------|------|
| 1952 | *Developments in Psycho-Analysis* (with others) | Hogarth Press | 与同事合著的克莱因学派发展 (Co-authored development of the Kleinian school) |
| 1952 | "The Mutual Influences in the Development of Ego and Id" | *Psychoanalytic Study of the Child* | 自我与本我的相互影响 (Mutual influences of ego and id) |
| 1955 | "The Psycho-Analytic Play Technique: Its History and Significance" | *International Journal of Psycho-Analysis* | 游戏技术的历史和意义 (History and significance of play technique) |
| 1955 | "On Identification" | *New Directions in Psycho-Analysis* | 认同机制的深入研究 (Advanced study of identification mechanisms) |
| 1957 | *Envy and Gratitude* | Tavistock Publications, London | **最后重要著作**：嫉羡与感恩的发展动力学 (Final major work: developmental dynamics of envy and gratitude) |
| 1958 | "On the Development of Libido Theory" | *International Journal of Psycho-Analysis* | 力比多理论的发展 (Development of libido theory) |
| 1959 | "Our Adult World and Its Roots in Infancy" | *Human Relations* | 婴儿期对成人世界的影响 (Influence of infancy on the adult world) |
| 1961 | *Narrative of a Child Analysis* | Hogarth Press, London (遗著 / posthumous) | 一个十岁男孩理查德的详细分析记录 (Detailed record of analysis of a ten-year-old boy, Richard) |
| 1963 | "On the Sense of Loneliness" | *International Journal of Psycho-Analysis* (遗著 / posthumous) | 孤独感的起源和本质 (Origins and nature of loneliness) |
