# 交互决定论 · Reciprocal Determinism

## 定义 / Definition

**交互决定论**（Reciprocal Determinism）是班杜拉社会认知理论的核心架构原则——它主张人的行为（Behavior, B）不是由个体因素（Person, P）或环境因素（Environment, E）单方面决定的，而是由三者之间的**双向因果关系**共同决定的。在这个三元模型中，每个因素既是其他两个因素的原因，也是它们的结果。

**Reciprocal determinism** is the core architectural principle of Bandura's social cognitive theory — it asserts that human behavior (Behavior, B) is not unilaterally determined by personal factors (Person, P) or environmental factors (Environment, E), but by the **bidirectional causal relationships** among all three. In this triadic model, each factor is simultaneously cause and consequence of the other two.

```
    P (个体/Person)
   ↗        ↖
  ↙            ↘
B (行为/Behavior) ↔ E (环境/Environment)
```

这一原则同时拒绝了两种简化论：（1）行为主义的环境决定论——认为行为完全由环境刺激和强化历史决定；（2）特质论的个体决定论——认为行为完全由内在人格特质决定。

This principle simultaneously rejects two reductionisms: (1) behaviorist environmental determinism — that behavior is entirely determined by environmental stimuli and reinforcement history; (2) trait-theory personal determinism — that behavior is entirely determined by internal personality traits.

## 核心机制 / Core Mechanisms

### 三因素的内涵 / Content of the Three Factors

**个体因素（P）/ Personal Factors**：
包括认知能力（智力、记忆力、注意力）、情感状态（焦虑、抑郁、积极情绪）、生理特征（身高、体能、健康状况）、信念系统（自我效能感、价值观、目标）和人格特质。

Cognitive abilities (intelligence, memory, attention), affective states (anxiety, depression, positive emotions), physiological characteristics (height, fitness, health status), belief systems (self-efficacy, values, goals), and personality traits.

**行为（B）/ Behavior**：
个体的实际行动——包括行为的选择、频率、强度、持续时间和质量。行为不仅是其他因素的结果，也是主动塑造环境和改变个体认知的力量。

The individual's actual actions — including behavioral choice, frequency, intensity, duration, and quality. Behavior is not only the result of other factors but also a force that actively shapes environment and alters individual cognition.

**环境（E）/ Environment**：
包括物理环境（家庭、学校、工作场所、社区）和社会环境（家庭关系、同伴群体、文化规范、社会制度、媒体）。环境不仅包括客观存在的条件，还包括个体**感知到**的环境——同样的客观环境对不同个体的意义可能不同。

Physical environment (home, school, workplace, community) and social environment (family relationships, peer groups, cultural norms, social institutions, media). Environment includes not only objectively existing conditions but also the **perceived** environment — the same objective environment may have different meanings for different individuals.

### 双向因果关系 / Bidirectional Causation

交互决定论的关键特征是因果关系的**双向性**——每个箭头都可以指向任一方向：

The key feature of reciprocal determinism is the **bidirectionality** of causation — each arrow can point in either direction:

| 因果方向 / Direction | 示例 / Example |
|:---:|------|
| P → B | 高自我效能感的个体更可能选择挑战性任务 / Individuals with high self-efficacy are more likely to choose challenging tasks |
| B → P | 成功完成困难任务提升了自我效能感 / Successfully completing a difficult task raises self-efficacy |
| E → B | 支持性的学习环境促进积极参与 / Supportive learning environments promote active engagement |
| B → E | 积极参与改变了教师的教学方式 / Active engagement changes the teacher's instructional approach |
| P → E | 外向的个体主动选择社交丰富的环境 / Extroverted individuals actively select socially rich environments |
| E → P | 贫困环境降低了个体的自我效能感和期望 / Impoverished environments lower individuals' self-efficacy and aspirations |

### 非对称性 / Asymmetry

虽然三因素之间的影响在原则上是双向的，但班杜拉强调这种影响在**具体情境中并非对称**的——在特定时刻和特定情境中，某一因素的影响可能远大于其他因素。例如：

Although the influences among the three factors are bidirectional in principle, Bandura emphasized that this influence is **not symmetrical in specific situations** — at a given moment and in a specific context, one factor's influence may be far greater than the others. For example:

- 在高度结构化的环境中（如军队），环境对行为的约束力可能远大于个体因素。In highly structured environments (e.g., military), environmental constraints on behavior may far outweigh personal factors.
- 在自由选择的情境中（如职业选择），个体因素（价值观、自我效能感）的影响可能更大。In free-choice contexts (e.g., career choice), personal factors (values, self-efficacy) may have greater influence.
- 在紧急情境中（如自然灾害），环境几乎完全决定了行为。In emergency situations (e.g., natural disasters), environment almost entirely determines behavior.

### 代理性的四个特征 / Four Properties of Human Agency

交互决定论中个体因素的核心是**代理性**（human agency）——班杜拉将其定义为四个特征：

The core of personal factors in reciprocal determinism is **human agency** — Bandura defined it through four properties:

1. **意向性 / Intentionality**：设定目标并制定实现计划的主动能力。The proactive capacity to set goals and formulate plans to achieve them.
2. **预谋性 / Forethought**：预见行为后果并据此规划行为的能力——不仅包括对近期后果的预见，也包括对远期后果的预见。The capacity to anticipate behavioral consequences and plan accordingly — including both near-term and long-term consequences.
3. **自我反应性 / Self-Reactiveness**：通过自我调节机制（自我监控、自我评价、自我强化）来管理自身行为的能力。The capacity to manage one's own behavior through self-regulatory mechanisms (self-monitoring, self-evaluation, self-reinforcement).
4. **自我反思性 / Self-Reflectiveness**：反思自身思维和行为的能力——包括对自身信念、价值观和效能的元认知审视。The capacity to reflect on one's own thinking and behavior — including metacognitive examination of one's own beliefs, values, and efficacy.

## 发展脉络 / Development

### 对行为主义的反叛 / Rebellion Against Behaviorism

交互决定论的提出是对两种主流范式的批判性回应：

Reciprocal determinism was proposed as a critical response to two mainstream paradigms:

- **激进行为主义 / Radical Behaviorism**（Skinner）：认为行为完全由环境的强化历史决定——"给我一打健康的婴儿，我可以把他们培养成任何类型的人"。班杜拉反驳：人不是环境的被动产物。Radical behaviorism held that behavior is entirely determined by environmental reinforcement history. Bandura countered: humans are not passive products of environment.
- **特质论 / Trait Theory**：认为行为由稳定的内在人格特质决定——"他之所以攻击是因为他有攻击性人格"。班杜拉反驳：行为是情境与个体交互的产物，不是固定特质的表现。Trait theory held that behavior is determined by stable internal personality traits. Bandura countered: behavior is a product of person-situation interaction, not an expression of fixed traits.

### 1978年的正式提出 / Formal Proposal in 1978

虽然交互决定论的思想在班杜拉1977年的《社会学习理论》中已有体现，但直到1978年的论文"The Self System in Reciprocal Determinism"中才被正式系统阐述。1986年的《思想与行动的社会基础》进一步将其发展为社会认知理论的完整架构原则。

Although the ideas of reciprocal determinism were present in Bandura's 1977 *Social Learning Theory*, it was not formally systematized until the 1978 paper "The Self System in Reciprocal Determinism." The 1986 *Social Foundations of Thought and Action* further developed it into the complete architectural principle of social cognitive theory.

### 与Mischel的情境-人格争论 / The Mischel Person-Situation Debate

交互决定论与Walter Mischel在1968年引发的人格-情境争论高度相关。Mischel论证了人格特质对行为的预测力远低于人们通常假设的水平（"一致性悖论"），而情境主义者则主张行为主要由情境决定。班杜拉的交互决定论提供了一个超越二元对立的框架——行为和人格都是动态交互过程的产物。

Reciprocal determinism is highly relevant to Walter Mischel's 1968 person-situation debate. Mischel argued that personality traits predict behavior far less than commonly assumed (the "consistency paradox"), while situationists argued behavior is primarily determined by situations. Bandura's reciprocal determinism offered a framework transcending this dichotomy — both behavior and personality are products of dynamic interactive processes.

## 临床应用 / Clinical Applications

### 系统评估 / Systemic Assessment

交互决定论要求临床心理学家在评估患者问题时同时考虑三个层面：个体因素（认知、情感、生理）、行为模式和环境条件。仅关注其中一个或两个层面可能导致不完整的评估和无效的治疗。

Reciprocal determinism requires clinical psychologists to consider three levels when assessing patient problems: personal factors (cognition, affect, physiology), behavioral patterns, and environmental conditions. Focusing on only one or two levels may lead to incomplete assessment and ineffective treatment.

### 多层面干预 / Multi-Level Intervention

最有效的心理干预通常在多个层面同时作用：（1）改变个体因素（如通过认知重构改变自动思维和信念）；（2）改变行为模式（如通过行为激活和暴露疗法）；（3）改变环境条件（如家庭治疗、学校环境调整）。

The most effective psychological interventions typically operate simultaneously on multiple levels: (1) changing personal factors (e.g., cognitive restructuring to modify automatic thoughts and beliefs); (2) changing behavioral patterns (e.g., behavioral activation and exposure therapy); (3) changing environmental conditions (e.g., family therapy, school environment modification).

### 生态取向干预 / Ecological Interventions

交互决定论推动了心理学从个体取向干预向生态取向干预的转变——认识到改变环境条件（如减少贫困、改善学校氛围、修改有害的社会规范）可以比单纯改变个体更有效地促进心理健康。

Reciprocal determinism propelled psychology's shift from individual-oriented to ecological interventions — recognizing that changing environmental conditions (e.g., reducing poverty, improving school climate, modifying harmful social norms) can promote mental health more effectively than changing individuals alone.

## 关联概念 / Related Concepts

- **[自我效能感 / Self-Efficacy](self-efficacy.md)**：自我效能感是个体因素（P）的核心组成，通过交互决定论影响行为和环境选择。Self-efficacy is a core component of personal factors, influencing behavior and environment selection through reciprocal determinism.
- **[社会学习 / Social Learning](social-learning.md)**：社会学习在个体-行为-环境的三元框架中发生。Social learning occurs within the triadic person-behavior-environment framework.
- **[观察学习 / Observational Learning](observational-learning.md)**：观察学习是交互决定论中环境（E）影响个体（P）的重要路径。Observational learning is a key pathway through which environment (E) influences person (P) in reciprocal determinism.
- **[榜样示范 / Modeling](modeling.md)**：示范是环境中可供个体观察和学习的刺激——是E→P方向因果的具体体现。Modeling provides observable stimuli in the environment — a concrete manifestation of E→P directional causation.

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

**与佛教缘起论 / Buddhist Dependent Origination (pratītya-samutpāda)**：缘起论主张一切现象都是条件性的共起——「此有故彼有，此生故彼生」。这与交互决定论拒绝线性因果、强调多因素双向决定的立场深度一致。两者的差异在于：缘起论彻底否定任何独立实体（包括「自我」），而交互决定论仍然保留了「代理性」作为个体因素的核心。

Dependent origination holds that all phenomena arise conditionally — "when this exists, that exists; when this arises, that arises." This aligns deeply with reciprocal determinism's rejection of linear causation and emphasis on multi-factor bidirectional determination. The difference: dependent origination thoroughly denies any independent entity (including "self"), while reciprocal determinism retains "agency" as the core of personal factors.

**与道家「天人合一」/ Daoist "Unity of Heaven and Humanity"**：道家的「天人合一」思想强调人与自然/环境不是主客对立的关系，而是相互生成、相互转化的统一体。这与交互决定论反对环境决定论和个体决定论、主张人与环境共舞的立场高度共鸣。但道家更强调「无为」——在顺应自然中实现和谐，而班杜拉更强调「代理性」——在主动塑造中实现发展。

The Daoist "unity of heaven and humanity" emphasizes that humans and nature/environment are not in a subject-object opposition but are a mutually generative and transformative unity. This resonates with reciprocal determinism's opposition to both environmental and personal determinism, advocating a "dance" between person and environment. However, Daoism emphasizes "non-action" (wúwéi) — achieving harmony through natural attunement, while Bandura emphasizes "agency" — achieving development through proactive shaping.

**与庄子「逍遥」/ Zhuangzi's "Free and Easy Wandering"**：庄子的「逍遥」既非被环境所困（拒绝环境决定论），也非执着于自我意志（拒绝个体决定论），而是在物我两忘中实现真正的自由——超越了P-B-E三元框架本身的束缚。这是对交互决定论的一种深层超越：不是在三元交互中寻求平衡，而是超越三元框架本身。

Zhuangzi's "free and easy wandering" (xiāoyáo) is neither trapped by environment (rejecting environmental determinism) nor attached to self-will (rejecting personal determinism), but achieves true freedom in the forgetting of both self and other — transcending the very constraints of the P-B-E triadic framework. This represents a deep transcendence of reciprocal determinism: not seeking balance within the triadic interaction, but transcending the triadic framework itself.
