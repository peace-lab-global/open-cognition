# 皮亚杰 · Jean Piaget - 生平年表 / Life Timeline

## 早年与生物学时期 / Early Life and Biology Period (1896–1921)

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|---|---|
| 1896 | 8 月 9 日出生于瑞士纳沙泰尔（Neuchâtel）。父亲 Arthur Piaget 是纳沙泰尔大学中世纪史教授，母亲 Rebecca Jackson 是法国人 / Born August 9 in Neuchâtel, Switzerland. Father Arthur Piaget was a medieval history professor; mother Rebecca Jackson was French |
| 1907 | 11 岁发表第一篇科学论文——关于一只白化麻雀的观察 / Published first scientific paper at age 11 — observation of an albino sparrow |
| 1910–1915 | 在纳沙泰尔博物馆担任软体动物学助理，发表多篇分类学论文 / Served as malacology assistant at the Neuchâtel Museum; published several taxonomy papers |
| 1916 | 进入纳沙泰尔大学学习自然科学 / Entered the University of Neuchâtel to study natural sciences |
| 1918 | 获得自然科学学士学位；出版小说 *Recherche*（《探索》），以文学形式表达哲学思考 / Obtained Bachelor of Science degree; published novel *Recherche* (*Quest*), expressing philosophical ideas in literary form |
| 1918 | 获得自然科学博士学位，博士论文研究瓦莱州淡水蜗牛的分类学 / Earned PhD in natural sciences; dissertation on taxonomy of freshwater snails in the Valais region |
| 1919 | 前往苏黎世大学学习精神分析（聆听荣格和 Pfister 的讲座）/ Went to University of Zurich to study psychoanalysis (attended lectures by Jung and Pfister) |
| 1919–1921 | 在巴黎大学学习哲学、逻辑学和科学方法论；聆听 Pierre Janet 的讲座 / Studied philosophy, logic, and scientific methodology at the University of Paris; attended Pierre Janet's lectures |

## 转向心理学 / Turn to Psychology (1921–1929)

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|---|---|
| 1920 | 受 Théodore Simon 邀请进入巴黎阿尔弗雷德·比奈学校（Binet laboratory），负责标准化儿童智力测验。发现儿童的错误答案呈现出系统性的模式 / Invited by Théodore Simon to work at the Alfred Binet School in Paris; standardized children's intelligence tests. Discovered systematic patterns in children's wrong answers |
| 1921 | 被任命为日内瓦卢梭研究所（Institut Jean-Jacques Rousseau）研究主任 / Appointed Director of Studies at the Rousseau Institute in Geneva |
| 1923 | 出版《儿童的语言与思维》(*Le langage et la pensée chez l'enfant*) / Published *The Language and Thought of the Child* |
| 1923 | 与 Valentine Châtenay 结婚，婚后育有三个孩子：Jacqueline (1925), Lucienne (1927), Laurent (1931) / Married Valentine Châtenay; they had three children: Jacqueline (1925), Lucienne (1927), Laurent (1931) |
| 1924 | 出版《儿童的判断与推理》(*Le jugement et le raisonnement chez l'enfant*) / Published *Judgment and Reasoning in the Child* |
| 1926 | 出版《儿童的世界观》(*La représentation du monde chez l'enfant*) / Published *The Child's Conception of the World* |
| 1928 | 出版《儿童的因果推理》(*La causalité physique chez l'enfant*) / Published *The Child's Conception of Physical Causality* |

## 日内瓦学派与核心理论建构 / Geneva School and Core Theory Construction (1929–1955)

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|---|---|
| 1929 | 正式担任日内瓦卢梭研究所所长 / Formally became Director of the Rousseau Institute in Geneva |
| 1929 | 开始在日内瓦大学担任心理学教授 / Began serving as Professor of Psychology at the University of Geneva |
| 1932 | 出版《儿童的道德判断》(*Le jugement moral chez l'enfant*)——研究从他律道德到自律道德的发展 / Published *The Moral Judgment of the Child* — studying development from heteronomous to autonomous morality |
| 1936 | 出版《婴儿智慧的诞生》(*La naissance de l'intelligence chez l'enfant*)——追踪感知运动智慧的六个亚阶段 / Published *The Origins of Intelligence in Children* — tracing six substages of sensorimotor intelligence |
| 1937 | 出版《儿童现实的建构》(*La construction du réel chez l'enfant*)——客体永久性、空间、时间、因果性概念的建构 / Published *The Construction of Reality in the Child* — construction of object permanence, space, time, and causality concepts |
| 1940 | 纳粹占领法国期间，拒绝与维希政权合作，保护卢梭研究所的独立性 / During Nazi occupation of France, refused to collaborate with the Vichy regime, protecting the independence of the Rousseau Institute |
| 1945 | 出版《儿童符号功能的形成》(*La formation du symbole chez l'enfant*)——模仿、游戏、梦、绘画中的符号功能 / Published *Play, Dreams and Imitation in Childhood* — symbolic function in imitation, play, dreams, drawing |
| 1947 | 出版《智慧心理学》(*La psychologie de l'intelligence*)——认知发展理论的系统阐述 / Published *The Psychology of Intelligence* — systematic exposition of cognitive development theory |
| 1950 | 出版三卷本《发生认识论导论》(*Introduction à l'épistémologie génétique*)——发生认识论的系统宣言 / Published three-volume *Introduction to Genetic Epistemology* — systematic manifesto of genetic epistemology |
| 1952 | 出版《儿童智慧的起源》(*Les origines de l'intelligence chez l'enfant*, 修订版) / Published revised *The Origins of Intelligence in Children* |
| 1954 | 开始与 Bärbel Inhelder 长期合作研究具体运算 / Began long-term collaboration with Bärbel Inhelder on concrete operations research |

## 国际发生认识论中心与学术巅峰 / CIEG and Academic Peak (1955–1970)

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|---|---|
| 1955 | 在日内瓦大学创立国际发生认识论中心（CIEG），汇聚多学科研究者 / Founded the International Center for Genetic Epistemology (CIEG) at the University of Geneva, assembling multidisciplinary researchers |
| 1955 | 开始在法兰西学院（Collège de France）担任社会学教授 / Began serving as Professor of Sociology at the Collège de France |
| 1958 | 与 Inhelder 合著《儿童逻辑的成长》(*De la logique de l'enfant à la logique de l'adolescent*)——正式提出形式运算阶段 / Co-authored *The Growth of Logical Thinking from Childhood to Adolescence* with Inhelder — formally proposed the formal operational stage |
| 1958 | 与 Inhelder 合著《儿童逻辑的早期发展》(*La genèse des structures logiques élémentaires*) / Co-authored *The Early Growth of Logic in the Child* with Inhelder |
| 1961 | 出版《儿童数概念的发生》(*La genèse du nombre chez l'enfant*, with Szeminska) / Published *The Child's Conception of Number* (with Szeminska) |
| 1962 | 发表对维果茨基《思维与语言》的评论 / Published commentary on Vygotsky's *Thought and Language* |
| 1964 | 当选美国国家科学院外籍院士 / Elected Foreign Associate of the US National Academy of Sciences |
| 1966 | 与 Inhelder 合著《儿童心理学》(*La psychologie de l'enfant*)——四阶段理论的经典综合 / Co-authored *The Psychology of the Child* with Inhelder — classic synthesis of four-stage theory |
| 1967 | 出版《生物学与知识》(*Biologie et connaissance*)——将认知理论根植于生物学框架 / Published *Biology and Knowledge* — rooted cognitive theory in a biological framework |
| 1968 | 获得荷兰 Erasmus 奖 / Received the Erasmus Prize (Netherlands) |
| 1969 | 获得美国心理学会杰出科学贡献奖 / Received the APA Award for Distinguished Scientific Contributions |
| 1970 | 出版《发生认识论原理》(*Épistémologie génétique*)——在哥伦比亚大学的讲座 / Published *Genetic Epistemology* — lectures at Columbia University |

## 晚期：深化、反思与论争 / Late Period: Deepening, Reflection, and Debate (1970–1980)

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|---|---|
| 1971 | 妻子 Valentine 去世 / Wife Valentine passed away |
| 1972 | 获得 Balzan 奖（国际心理学奖） / Received the Balzan Prize (International Prize for Psychology) |
| 1974 | 出版《成功与理解》(*Réussir et comprendre*)——探讨行动成功与认知理解的关系 / Published *Success and Understanding* — exploring relationship between successful action and cognitive understanding |
| 1975 | 出版《认知结构的平衡化》(*L'équilibration des structures cognitives*)——完善平衡化机制理论 / Published *The Equilibration of Cognitive Structures* — refined the equilibration mechanism theory |
| 1975 | 参加 Royaumont 会议，与 Chomsky 就语言与认知的关系展开著名论战 / Attended the Royaumont conference; engaged in famous debate with Chomsky on the relationship between language and cognition |
| 1976 | 正式从日内瓦大学退休，但继续指导 CIEG 的研究 / Formally retired from the University of Geneva but continued directing CIEG research |
| 1977 | Howard Gardner 出版《心智的探险者》(*The Quest for Mind*)——皮亚杰思想的重要传记性研究 / Howard Gardner published *The Quest for Mind* — important biographical study of Piaget's thought |
| 1980 | 出版《辩证法的基本形式》(*Les formes élémentaires de la dialectique*) / Published *The Elementary Forms of Dialectic* |
| 1980 | 9 月 16 日在日内瓦去世，享年 84 岁 / Died September 16 in Geneva, aged 84 |

## 身后 / Posthumous (1980–)

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|---|---|
| 1981 | 遗作《可能性与必然性》(*Possibility and Necessity*, 2 vols) 出版 / Posthumous *Possibility and Necessity* (2 vols) published |
| 1983 | 遗作 *Piaget on Piaget*（访谈录）出版 / Posthumous *Piaget on Piaget* (filmed interviews) published |
| 1983 | Howard Gardner 出版《心灵的框架》(*Frames of Mind*)——多元智能理论，部分继承皮亚杰传统 / Gardner published *Frames of Mind* — multiple intelligences theory, partly inheriting Piaget's tradition |
| 1990s | 日内瓦皮亚杰档案馆（Archives Jean Piaget）成立 / Archives Jean Piaget established in Geneva |
| 1996 | 百年诞辰纪念：全球举办多场学术会议，重新评估皮亚杰遗产 / Centennial celebrations: worldwide academic conferences reassessing Piaget's legacy |
| 2000s– | 新皮亚杰学派持续发展；发展机器人学等领域继承皮亚杰思想 / Neo-Piagetian schools continue developing; developmental robotics and other fields inherit Piaget's thought |
