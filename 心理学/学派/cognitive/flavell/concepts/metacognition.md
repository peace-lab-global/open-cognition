# 元认知 · Metacognition

> "Metacognition refers to one's knowledge concerning one's own cognitive processes and products or anything related to them."
>
> "元认知指的是关于自身认知过程及其产物的知识，或任何与之相关的事物。"
> — Flavell, 1976

## 定义 · Definition

**中文**：**元认知**（metacognition）是"关于认知的认知"——个体对自身认知过程的知识、觉察、监控与调节。这一概念由弗拉维尔（John Flavell）在1976年的论文《问题解决中的元认知层面》中首次系统提出。弗拉维尔将元认知视为一种高阶认知过程：如果说认知是"处理信息"，那么元认知就是"对信息处理过程本身的了解和管理"。

**EN**: **Metacognition** is "cognition about cognition" — an individual's knowledge, awareness, monitoring, and regulation of their own cognitive processes. This concept was first systematically introduced by John Flavell in his 1976 paper *Metacognitive Aspects of Problem Solving*. Flavell conceptualized metacognition as a higher-order cognitive process: if cognition is "processing information," then metacognition is "understanding and managing the information processing itself."

## 核心机制 · Core Mechanisms

### 元认知的四要素模型 · Four-Component Model

**中文**：弗拉维尔在1979年的奠基论文中提出了元认知的四要素交互模型：

1. **元认知知识**（Metacognitive Knowledge）：存储在长时记忆中的、关于认知的稳定知识。包括个人知识（"我数学不好"）、任务知识（"阅读理解需要反复确认"）、策略知识（"列提纲有助于写作"）。

2. **元认知体验**（Metacognitive Experiences）：在认知活动中产生的即时意识体验。如阅读时的困惑感、问题解决中的"啊哈"时刻、记忆搜索中的知道感（FOK）。

3. **认知目标**（Cognitive Goals）：个体在认知任务中设定的目标（如"我要彻底理解这个概念"或"记住这十个单词"）。目标决定了元认知监控的标准。

4. **认知策略**（Cognitive Strategies）：用于执行认知任务的具体方法和技巧（如复述、精加工、组织化）。策略本身不是元认知，但策略的选择和使用受元认知控制。

**EN**: In his 1979 landmark paper, Flavell proposed a four-component interactive model:

1. **Metacognitive Knowledge**: Stable knowledge about cognition stored in long-term memory, including person knowledge ("I'm bad at math"), task knowledge ("reading comprehension requires repeated verification"), and strategy knowledge ("outlining helps writing").

2. **Metacognitive Experiences**: Momentary conscious experiences during cognitive activity, such as confusion while reading, "aha" moments in problem solving, and feelings of knowing (FOK) during memory search.

3. **Cognitive Goals**: Goals set for cognitive tasks (e.g., "I want to thoroughly understand this concept" or "memorize these ten words"). Goals determine the standards for metacognitive monitoring.

4. **Cognitive Strategies**: Specific methods and techniques for executing cognitive tasks (e.g., rehearsal, elaboration, organization). Strategies themselves are not metacognitive, but their selection and use are metacognitively controlled.

### 元认知知识与元认知体验的区分 · Knowledge vs. Experience

**中文**：弗拉维尔特别强调元认知知识（metacognitive knowledge）与元认知体验（metacognitive experience）的区分。前者是相对稳定的、可陈述的"我知道什么"——存储在长时记忆中，可以被反思和报告。后者是情境化的、即时涌现的"此刻我感觉到什么"——它是有意识的，但往往是短暂的、前语言性的。

例如：一个学生"知道"自己不擅长记忆人名（元认知知识），但在聚会上遇到一个新认识的人时，感到"我可能记不住这个名字"的担忧（元认知体验）。二者虽相关，却是不同的认知现象。

**EN**: Flavell particularly emphasized the distinction between metacognitive knowledge (relatively stable, declarable "what I know" — stored in long-term memory, can be reflected upon and reported) and metacognitive experience (situated, momentary "what I feel right now" — conscious but often brief and pre-linguistic).

For example: a student "knows" they are bad at remembering names (metacognitive knowledge), but when meeting someone new at a party, feels the worry "I probably won't remember this name" (metacognitive experience). Though related, they are distinct cognitive phenomena.

## 发展脉络 · Developmental Trajectory

### 概念的起源 · Origins of the Concept

**中文**：元认知概念的提出并非凭空创造，而是源于弗拉维尔对儿童记忆策略研究的长期积累。1960年代，他在研究儿童的记忆策略使用时发现了"产出不足"（production deficiency）现象——儿童具备策略知识却不主动使用。这一发现促使他思考：儿童不仅缺少认知策略，更缺少对"何时使用策略"的觉察——后者正是元认知的核心。

**EN**: The concept of metacognition was not created from nothing but emerged from Flavell's long-term research on children's memory strategy use. In the 1960s, he discovered "production deficiency" — children possessed strategic knowledge but did not spontaneously use it. This prompted him to think: children lack not only cognitive strategies but also awareness of "when to use strategies" — the latter being the core of metacognition.

### 从元记忆到元认知 · From Metamemory to Metacognition

**中文**：在弗拉维尔之前，记忆研究者已经使用"元记忆"（metamemory）一词来指称关于记忆系统的知识。弗拉维尔的贡献在于将这一概念从记忆领域推广到所有认知领域——不仅是"关于记忆的认知"，还有"关于思维、理解、判断、学习的认知"。这一推广使元认知成为一个统一的理论框架，能够解释多种认知现象。

**EN**: Before Flavell, memory researchers already used the term "metamemory" to refer to knowledge about the memory system. Flavell's contribution was extending this concept from memory to all cognitive domains — not just "cognition about memory" but "cognition about thinking, understanding, judging, and learning." This generalization made metacognition a unified theoretical framework capable of explaining multiple cognitive phenomena.

### 后续发展 · Subsequent Development

**中文**：弗拉维尔之后，元认知研究在多个方向蓬勃发展：Nelson & Narens（1990）提出了元记忆的双过程模型（监控与控制）；Brown（1987）强调元认知在教育中的应用；Paris & Jacobs（1984）开发了测量儿童元认知的工具；Wells（2009）将其系统化为临床疗法。

**EN**: After Flavell, metacognitive research flourished in multiple directions: Nelson & Narens (1990) proposed the dual-process model (monitoring and control); Brown (1987) emphasized metacognition in education; Paris & Jacobs (1984) developed tools for measuring children's metacognition; Wells (2009) systematized it into clinical therapy.

## 临床应用 · Clinical Applications

### 元认知与心理障碍 · Metacognition & Psychopathology

**中文**：元认知理论为理解心理障碍提供了新视角。在焦虑障碍中，患者对自身想法的元认知评估出现扭曲——将侵入性想法评估为危险的、需要控制的（如"如果我不想这些，可能会出事"）。在抑郁症中，反复的消极元认知信念（"我无法停止思考这些"）导致难以从消极思维中脱离。

**EN**: Metacognitive theory provides new perspectives on psychopathology. In anxiety disorders, patients' metacognitive evaluations of their own thoughts become distorted — appraising intrusive thoughts as dangerous and needing control (e.g., "if I don't think about these, something bad might happen"). In depression, persistent negative metacognitive beliefs ("I can't stop thinking about these") make it difficult to disengage from negative thinking.

### 元认知疗法 · Metacognitive Therapy

**中文**：Adrian Wells 发展的**元认知疗法**（MCT）直接基于弗拉维尔的理论框架，但聚焦于临床情境。MCT 的核心假设是：心理障碍的维持不是由事件本身引起的，而是由个体对事件的元认知反应（如过度担忧、反复思考、威胁监控）维持的。治疗目标是修正这些元认知信念和应对策略。

**EN**: Adrian Wells' **Metacognitive Therapy (MCT)** is directly based on Flavell's theoretical framework but focuses on clinical contexts. MCT's core hypothesis is that psychological disorders are maintained not by events themselves but by metacognitive responses to events (such as excessive worry, rumination, and threat monitoring). Treatment aims to modify these metacognitive beliefs and coping strategies.

## 东西方对话 · East-West Dialogue

**中文**：在东方思想传统中，类似元认知的概念早有出现。禅宗的"参禅"——对思维过程本身的观照——可视为一种极端的元认知训练。《六祖坛经》中"不思善、不思恶，正与么时，那个是明上座本来面目"指向一种超越认知内容的纯粹觉察——这接近弗拉维尔所描述的元认知体验的最纯粹形式：觉察认知过程本身，而不被内容牵走。

然而，二者在目的论上有根本差异：弗拉维尔的元认知是为了"更好的认知效能"（instrumental），佛教的觉观是为了"从认知的执取中解脱"（soteriological）。这一差异映射了西方认知科学的工具理性与东方智慧的解脱理性之间的张力。

**EN**: In Eastern thought traditions, concepts analogous to metacognition appeared long ago. Chan Buddhism's "investigating Chan" — observing the thinking process itself — can be viewed as an extreme form of metacognitive training. The Platform Sutra's "Without thinking good or evil, at this very moment, what is your original face?" points to pure awareness transcending cognitive content — approaching the purest form of metacognitive experience Flavell described: awareness of the cognitive process itself without being carried away by content.

However, a fundamental teleological difference exists: Flavell's metacognition aims at "better cognitive performance" (instrumental), while Buddhist awareness aims at "liberation from cognitive attachment" (soteriological). This difference reflects the tension between Western cognitive science's instrumental rationality and Eastern wisdom's soteriological rationality.
