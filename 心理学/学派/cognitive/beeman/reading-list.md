# 比曼进阶阅读 · Mark Beeman Reading List

> 分类阅读指南，适合不同背景的读者。
>
> A categorized reading guide for readers of different backgrounds.

---

## 一、原典 / Primary Sources

Beeman 本人最重要的学术著作和论文。

Beeman's own most important academic works and publications.

### 必读 / Must-Read

1. **Kounios, J. & Beeman, M. (2009).** "The Aha! Moment: The Cognitive Neuroscience of Insight." *Current Directions in Psychological Science*, 18(4), 210–216.
   - 顿悟神经科学的最佳入门综述 / The best introductory review of insight neuroscience
   - 系统总结了 EEG 和 fMRI 研究成果 / Systematically summarizes EEG and fMRI research findings

2. **Jung-Beeman, M., Bowden, E.M., Haberman, J., Frymiare, J.L., Arambel-Liu, S., Greenblatt, R., Reber, P.J., & Kounios, J. (2004).** "Neural Activity When People Solve Verbal Problems with Insight." *PLoS Biology*, 2(4), e97.
   - 里程碑论文：首次报告顿悟 γ 波信号 / Milestone paper: first report of the insight gamma signal
   - 开放获取 / Open access

3. **Beeman, M. (1998).** "Coarse semantic coding." In M. Beeman & C. Chiarello (Eds.), *Right Hemisphere Language Comprehension*. Lawrence Erlbaum Associates.
   - 粗编码理论的完整阐述 / Complete exposition of coarse coding theory
   - 理解右脑语义处理的基础文献 / Foundational literature for understanding right hemisphere semantic processing

### 推荐阅读 / Recommended

4. **Bowden, E.M. & Beeman, M. (1998).** "Getting the right idea: Semantic activation in the right hemisphere may help solve insight problems." *Psychological Science*, 9(6), 435–440.
   - 右脑语义激活与顿悟问题解决的首次实验联系 / First experimental link between right hemisphere semantic activation and insight problem-solving

5. **Kounios, J., Frymiare, J.L., Bowden, E.M., Fleck, J.I., Miller, K., Bryan, W.L., & Beeman, M. (2006).** "The prepared mind: Neural activity prior to problem insight." *Psychological Science*, 17(10), 882–890.
   - 顿悟前的"准备性"神经活动 / Preparatory neural activity before insight
   - 揭示了顿悟可以被预测 / Revealed that insight can be predicted

6. **Subramaniam, K., Kounios, J., Bowden, E.M., & Beeman, M. (2009).** "A brain mechanism for facilitation of insight by positive affect." *Journal of Cognitive Neuroscience*, 21(3), 415–432.
   - 积极情绪促进顿悟的神经机制 / Neural mechanisms by which positive mood facilitates insight

---

## 二、专著 / Monographs

1. **Kounios, J. & Beeman, M. (2015).** *The Eureka Factor: Aha! Moments, Creative Insight, and the Brain*. New York: Random House.
   - 面向公众的顿悟科学全面阐述 / Comprehensive popular account of insight science
   - 包含实践建议和日常应用 / Includes practical advice and everyday applications
   - **推荐所有读者首先阅读此书 / Recommended as first reading for all readers**

2. **Beeman, M. & Chiarello, C. (Eds.). (1998).** *Right Hemisphere Language Comprehension: Perspectives from Cognitive Neuroscience*. Mahwah, NJ: Lawrence Erlbaum Associates.
   - 右脑语言理解的研究文集 / Research anthology on right hemisphere language comprehension
   - 适合有学术背景的读者 / Suited for readers with academic backgrounds

---

## 三、二手研究 / Secondary Sources

### 英语 / English

1. **Dietrich, A. (2015).** *How Creativity Happens in the Brain*. London: Palgrave Macmillan.
   - 创造力的神经科学全景，含对 Beeman 工作的评价 / Panoramic neuroscience of creativity, with commentary on Beeman's work

2. **Sawyer, R.K. (2011).** *Explaining Creativity: The Science of Human Innovation* (2nd ed.). Oxford University Press.
   - 创造力科学的系统教材，将 Beeman 的发现置于更广泛的理论框架中 / Systematic textbook on creativity science, placing Beeman's findings in a broader theoretical framework

3. **Sternberg, R.J. & Davidson, J.E. (Eds.). (1995).** *The Nature of Insight*. Cambridge, MA: MIT Press.
   - 顿悟研究的经典文集，Beeman 工作的前身 / Classic anthology on insight research, predecessor to Beeman's work

4. **Finke, R.A., Ward, T.B., & Smith, S.M. (1992).** *Creative Cognition: Theory, Research, and Applications*. Cambridge, MA: MIT Press.
   - 创造性认知的 Geneplore 模型 / The Geneplore model of creative cognition

5. **McGilchrist, I. (2009).** *The Master and His Emissary: The Divided Brain and the Making of the Western World*. Yale University Press.
   - 关于左右脑差异的宏大论述，与 Beeman 的粗编码理论形成互补 / Grand narrative on hemispheric differences, complementing Beeman's coarse coding theory

6. **Runco, M.A. (2014).** *Creativity: Theories and Themes: Research, Development, and Practice* (2nd ed.). Academic Press.
   - 创造力理论的全面教材 / Comprehensive textbook on creativity theories

7. **Goleman, D. (1995).** *Emotional Intelligence*. Bantam Books.
   - 情绪与认知的关系，与 Beeman 关于积极情绪促进顿悟的发现相关 / The emotion-cognition relationship, relevant to Beeman's finding that positive mood facilitates insight

### 中文 / Chinese

1. **库尼奥斯 & 比曼 (Kounios & Beeman).** 《尤里卡因子：顿悟、创造性洞见与大脑》(*The Eureka Factor* 中译本，如已出版).
   - 科普著作的中文译本 / Chinese translation of the popular science book (if available)

2. **创造性思维的神经科学**相关综述散见于《心理学报》和《心理科学进展》。
   - Reviews on the neuroscience of creative thinking are scattered across *Acta Psychologica Sinica* and *Advances in Psychological Science*.

3. **沈汪兵, 罗劲, et al.** 顿悟的认知神经科学研究综述。《心理科学进展》。
   - 国内学者对顿悟神经科学的综述 / Chinese scholars' reviews of insight neuroscience

4. **邱江, 张庆林.** 顿悟问题认知机制的研究进展。《心理科学》。
   - 顿悟问题认知机制的中文综述 / Chinese review of cognitive mechanisms of insight problems

## 四、相关领域 / Related Fields

### 创造性心理学 / Psychology of Creativity

- **Guilford, J.P. (1967).** *The Nature of Human Intelligence*. McGraw-Hill. — 发散思维的经典理论 / Classic theory of divergent thinking
- **Amabile, T.M. (1996).** *Creativity in Context*. Westview Press. — 创造力的社会心理学 / Social psychology of creativity
- **Csikszentmihalyi, M. (1996).** *Creativity: Flow and the Psychology of Discovery and Invention*. Harper Collins. — 心流与创造力 / Flow and creativity

### 认知神经科学 / Cognitive Neuroscience

- **Gazzaniga, M.S. (Ed.). (2004).** *The Cognitive Neurosciences* (3rd ed.). MIT Press. — 认知神经科学百科全书 / Encyclopedia of cognitive neuroscience
- **Kandel, E.R., Schwartz, J.H., & Jessell, T.M. (2013).** *Principles of Neural Science* (5th ed.). McGraw-Hill. — 神经科学基础教材 / Foundational neuroscience textbook

### 东方哲学与顿悟 / Eastern Philosophy and Insight

- **铃木大拙 (D.T. Suzuki). (1956).** *Zen Buddhism: Selected Writings*. Doubleday. — 禅宗顿悟的经典阐述 / Classic exposition of Zen insight
- **Heine, S. & Wright, D.S. (Eds.). (2000).** *The Koan: Texts and Contexts in Zen Buddhism*. Oxford University Press. — 公案研究 / Koan studies

## 五、阅读路径建议 / Suggested Reading Paths

### 路径 A：科普入门 / Path A: Popular Introduction

1. *The Eureka Factor* (2015) — 全景概览 / Panoramic overview
2. "The Aha! Moment" (2009) — 学术综述 / Academic review
3. Dietrich, *How Creativity Happens in the Brain* (2015) — 更广视野 / Broader perspective

### 路径 B：学术深入 / Path B: Academic Deep Dive

1. Beeman (1998) "Coarse semantic coding" — 理论基础 / Theoretical foundation
2. Jung-Beeman et al. (2004) *PLoS Biology* — 核心实验 / Core experiment
3. Kounios et al. (2006) "The prepared mind" — 前兆研究 / Precursor research
4. Kounios & Beeman (2009) *Current Directions* — 系统综述 / Systematic review

### 路径 C：跨文化比较 / Path C: Cross-Cultural Comparison

1. *The Eureka Factor* (2015) — 科学框架 / Scientific framework
2. D.T. Suzuki, *Zen Buddhism* (1956) — 禅宗顿悟 / Zen insight
3. 《庄子》/ *Zhuangzi* — 道家"无为"与创造性 / Daoist wu wei and creativity
4. McGilchrist, *The Master and His Emissary* (2009) — 半球差异的文明维度 / Hemispheric differences and civilization
