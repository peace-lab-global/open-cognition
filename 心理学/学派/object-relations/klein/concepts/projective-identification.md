# 投射性认同 · Projective Identification

## 定义 / Definition

投射性认同（projective identification）是克莱因在1946年的论文"关于分裂样机制的若干说明"（"Notes on Some Schizoid Mechanisms"）中首次提出的概念——它是精神分析理论中最深刻、也最复杂的人际动力学概念之一。与简单的投射（projection）不同，投射性认同不仅将自身不接受的部分"放到"他人身上，还**在人际关系中制造一种压力，迫使接收者认同并体验这些被投射的内容**。

Projective identification is a concept first introduced by Klein in her 1946 paper "Notes on Some Schizoid Mechanisms"—it is one of the most profound and complex interpersonal dynamics concepts in psychoanalytic theory. Unlike simple projection, projective identification not only "places" unacceptable parts of the self onto others but also **creates a pressure in the interpersonal relationship that forces the recipient to identify with and experience the projected content**.

投射性认同包含三个阶段：（1）**投射**——个体将自身不接受的情感、冲动或自我部分分裂并投射出去；（2）**人际压力**——通过微妙的互动方式，投射者对接收者施加压力，使接收者体验被投射的内容；（3）**认同**——接收者开始认同并体验这些被投射的内容，仿佛它们是自己的。

Projective identification involves three phases: (1) **Projection**—the individual splits off and projects unacceptable feelings, impulses, or parts of the self; (2) **Interpersonal pressure**—through subtle interactional means, the projector exerts pressure on the recipient, causing the recipient to experience the projected content; (3) **Identification**—the recipient begins to identify with and experience the projected content as if it were their own.

## 核心机制 / Core Mechanisms

### 投射与认同的结合 / The Combination of Projection and Identification

经典弗洛伊德理论中的**投射**是一个单向过程——个体将自身不接受的冲动归因于他人（例如，一个压抑愤怒的人觉得别人在对他生气）。投射性认同增加了一个**人际维度**：投射不仅发生在心理内部，还在现实关系中产生了实际效果。

**Projection** in classical Freudian theory is a unidirectional process—the individual attributes unacceptable impulses to others (e.g., a person who suppresses anger feels that others are angry with them). Projective identification adds an **interpersonal dimension**: projection occurs not only intrapsychically but also produces real effects in the actual relationship.

例如，一个无法承受自身依赖感的来访者可能通过投射性认同使治疗师体验到强烈的想要照顾来访者的冲动——治疗师"被迫"承载了来访者无法承受的依赖性。在这个过程中，来访者不仅"看到"了他人身上的依赖性（投射），还使他人实际地**体验到**了依赖性（投射性认同）。

For example, a patient who cannot bear their own dependency may, through projective identification, cause the therapist to experience a strong impulse to care for the patient—the therapist is "forced" to carry the dependency the patient cannot bear. In this process, the patient not only "sees" dependency in others (projection) but also causes others to actually **experience** dependency (projective identification).

### 投射性认同的功能 / Functions of Projective Identification

投射性认同具有多种心理功能：

Projective identification serves multiple psychological functions:

1. **防御功能 / Defensive function**：将无法承受的心理内容排出自我，放到他人身上（排出坏的、保留好的）。Expelling unbearable psychological content from the self, placing it in others (expelling the bad, retaining the good).

2. **沟通功能 / Communicative function**：通过让他人体验自己的内在状态来进行非语言沟通——"让你知道我的感受"。Communicating non-verbally by causing others to experience one's internal state—"letting you know how I feel."

3. **控制功能 / Controlling function**：通过将自己的部分放到他人身上并控制他人来间接控制这些部分——"我控制了你，就控制了我在你身上放置的部分"。Indirectly controlling these parts by placing parts of oneself in others and controlling them—"by controlling you, I control the parts I placed in you."

4. **成长功能 / Growth function**：在健康的母婴关系中，婴儿通过投射性认同将焦虑"放入"母亲，母亲（作为容器）接受并转化这些焦虑，然后婴儿再内射转化后的体验——这是心理成长的基本机制。In healthy mother-infant relationships, the infant uses projective identification to "place" anxiety in the mother, the mother (as container) accepts and transforms this anxiety, and the infant then introjects the transformed experience—this is a fundamental mechanism of psychological growth.

### 投射性认同与反移情 / Projective Identification and Countertransference

投射性认同理论对理解**反移情**（countertransference）产生了革命性影响。在克莱因之前，反移情被视为治疗师的"盲点"或"干扰"——需要通过自我分析来消除。克莱因学派认为，反移情是来访者通过投射性认同在治疗师心中激发的体验——因此，反移情是理解来访者内在世界的**重要工具**。

Projective identification theory has had a revolutionary impact on understanding **countertransference**. Before Klein, countertransference was viewed as the therapist's "blind spot" or "interference"—to be eliminated through self-analysis. The Kleinian school holds that countertransference is the experience the patient evokes in the therapist through projective identification—therefore, countertransference is an **important tool** for understanding the patient's internal world.

治疗师的任务是：觉察自己在反移情中的体验；判断这些体验在多大程度上是来访者通过投射性认同激发的；将这些体验作为理解来访者内在世界的线索；并在适当的时机通过解释将来访者投射的内容归还给来访者。

The therapist's task is to: attend to their own countertransference experience; judge to what extent these experiences are evoked by the patient through projective identification; use these experiences as clues to understanding the patient's internal world; and at appropriate moments, through interpretation, return the patient's projected content to the patient.

## 发展脉络 / Developmental Trajectory

### 克莱因的原始阐述 / Klein's Original Formulation

克莱因最初在1946年的论文中将投射性认同描述为偏执-分裂位态中的一种防御机制——婴儿通过投射性认同将无法承受的焦虑排出自我。在这一阶段，她的关注点主要在投射性认同的防御功能上。

Klein initially described projective identification in her 1946 paper as a defense mechanism in the paranoid-schizoid position—the infant uses projective identification to expel unbearable anxiety from the ego. At this stage, her focus was primarily on the defensive function of projective identification.

### 比昂的容器理论 / Bion's Container Theory

比昂（W.R. Bion）在1950-1960年代极大地发展了投射性认同理论。他提出了**容器-被容纳**（container-contained, ♀-♂）模型：母亲作为"容器"（container），接受婴儿通过投射性认同放入的焦虑体验，通过"梦的工作"（α功能）将这些原始体验转化为可承受的心理内容，然后婴儿通过内射重新获得转化后的体验。

Bion greatly developed projective identification theory in the 1950s-1960s. He proposed the **container-contained** (♀-♂) model: the mother as "container" accepts the anxiety experiences the infant places through projective identification, transforms these raw experiences into bearable psychological content through "dream work" (α-function), and the infant then re-acquires the transformed experience through introjection.

比昂的理论将投射性认同从单纯的防御机制提升为**心理成长的基本机制**——正是通过投射性认同和容器的互动，原始的感觉印象（β元素）被转化为可思考的心理内容（α元素）。

Bion's theory elevated projective identification from a mere defense mechanism to a **fundamental mechanism of psychological growth**—it is through the interaction of projective identification and the container that raw sense impressions (β-elements) are transformed into thinkable psychological content (α-elements).

### 当代关系精神分析的发展 / Contemporary Relational Psychoanalysis Development

当代关系精神分析学家如托马斯·奥格登（Thomas Ogden）进一步发展了投射性认同理论。奥格登强调投射性认同的**主体间性**（intersubjectivity）维度——投射性认同不仅是单向的投射，还创造了一个**主体间场域**（intersubjective field），在这个场域中，投射者和接收者共同参与了一种新的心理现实。

Contemporary relational psychoanalysts such as Thomas Ogden have further developed projective identification theory. Ogden emphasized the **intersubjectivity** dimension of projective identification—projective identification is not merely a unidirectional projection but creates an **intersubjective field** in which projector and recipient jointly participate in a new psychological reality.

## 临床应用 / Clinical Applications

投射性认同的临床应用极为广泛：

The clinical applications of projective identification are extremely broad:

1. **分析反移情 / Analyzing countertransference**：治疗师将自己的反移情体验作为理解来访者内在客体关系的线索。The therapist uses their countertransference experience as a clue to understanding the patient's internal object relations.

2. **识别活现 / Identifying enactments**：治疗师识别治疗关系中的"活现"（enactment）——即来访者的内在客体关系模式在治疗关系中的重演。The therapist identifies "enactments" in the therapeutic relationship—the replaying of the patient's internal object relation patterns in the therapeutic relationship.

3. **容器功能 / Container function**：治疗师充当容器，接受并转化来访者无法承受的心理内容。The therapist serves as container, accepting and transforming psychological content the patient cannot bear.

4. **解释投射性认同 / Interpreting projective identification**：在适当的时机，治疗师解释来访者的投射性认同模式，帮助来访者意识到并重新拥有被投射的部分。At appropriate moments, the therapist interprets the patient's projective identification patterns, helping the patient become aware of and re-own projected parts.

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

投射性认同可与佛教"业力"（karma）理论进行对话。业力是行为的"印记"——它们被"投射"到意识流中，然后在适当的条件下"认同"——即被体验为现实。类似地，投射性认同将心理内容"放置"到他人身上，然后在人际关系中被"体验"为现实。两者都描述了一种"种下种子，然后收获果实"的心理动力学。

Projective identification can dialogue with the Buddhist theory of "karma." Karma is the "imprint" of actions—they are "projected" into the stream of consciousness and then "identified" under appropriate conditions—i.e., experienced as reality. Similarly, projective identification "places" psychological content onto others, which is then "experienced" as reality in interpersonal relationships. Both describe a psychological dynamic of "planting seeds, then harvesting fruit."

印度哲学中的"投射"（adhyāsa / superimposition）概念也与投射性认同相通。商羯罗（Śaṅkara）认为，无明就是将自我的特征投射到非自我上——这与投射性认同的核心机制（将自我的部分放到他人身上）有着深刻的结构性相似。

The concept of "superimposition" (adhyāsa) in Indian philosophy also connects with projective identification. Śaṅkara held that ignorance is the projection of self's characteristics onto the non-self—this shares a deep structural similarity with the core mechanism of projective identification (placing parts of the self onto others).
