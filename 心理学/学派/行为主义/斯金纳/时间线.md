# B.F.斯金纳 · B.F. Skinner - 生平年表 / Life Timeline

## 早年生活与教育 / Early Life and Education

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|---|---|
| 1904 | 3月20日出生于美国宾夕法尼亚州萨斯奎汉纳（Susquehanna, Pennsylvania）。父亲威廉·斯金纳（William Skinner）为当地律师，母亲格蕾丝·伯尔赫斯（Grace Burrhus）为家庭主妇。/ Born March 20 in Susquehanna, Pennsylvania. Father William Skinner was a local lawyer; mother Grace Burrhus was a homemaker. |
| 1910s | 在萨斯奎汉纳长大，童年时期对机械装置和发明表现出浓厚兴趣，曾制作过各种小装置和模型。/ Grew up in Susquehanna; showed early interest in mechanical devices and invention, building various gadgets and models. |
| 1922 | 进入纽约州克林顿的汉密尔顿学院（Hamilton College），主修英语文学。/ Entered Hamilton College in Clinton, New York, majoring in English 文学. |
| 1926 | 获得汉密尔顿学院文学学士学位（B.A. in English）。在《汉密尔顿文学月刊》发表短篇小说。/ Received B.A. in English from Hamilton College. Published short fiction in the *Hamilton Literary Monthly*. |
| 1926–1928 | 毕业后在纽约市格林威治村居住，尝试从事文学创作。期间撰写了一部未出版的小说。这段经历让他认识到文学无法提供对人类行为的"科学理解"。/ Lived in Greenwich Village, New York City, attempting a literary career. Wrote an unpublished novel. This experience led him to realize that 文学 could not provide "scientific understanding" of human behavior. |
| 1927 | 阅读了约翰·B·华生（John B. Watson）的《行为主义》（*Behaviorism*, 1924）和伯特兰·罗素（Bertrand Russell）关于行为主义的哲学论述，决定转向心理学。/ Read Watson's *Behaviorism* (1924) and Bertrand Russell's philosophical treatment of behaviorism, deciding to pursue 心理学. |
| 1928 | 进入哈佛大学心理学系攻读研究生，在威廉·克罗齐尔（William J. Crozier）指导下研究大鼠的学习行为。/ Entered Harvard University's 心理学 department as a graduate student, studying rat learning behavior under William J. Crozier. |
| 1930 | 获得哈佛大学文学硕士学位（A.M.）。/ Received A.M. degree from Harvard University. |
| 1931 | 获得哈佛大学哲学博士学位（Ph.D.），博士论文研究大鼠在不同驱动状态下的学习行为。/ Received Ph.D. from Harvard University; dissertation studied rat learning behavior under different drive states. |

## 学术生涯早期 / Early Academic Career

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|---|---|
| 1931–1936 | 在哈佛大学担任研究员（Junior Fellow, Society of Fellows），继续在大鼠和鸽子身上进行操作性条件反射实验。发明了累积记录器（cumulative recorder），为行为频率的精确记录提供了革命性工具。/ Served as Junior Fellow in Harvard's Society of Fellows, continuing operant conditioning experiments with rats and pigeons. Invented the cumulative recorder, a revolutionary tool for precise recording of behavior frequency. |
| 1936 | 接受明尼苏达大学（University of Minnesota）心理学助理教授职位，离开哈佛大学。/ Accepted assistant professor position in 心理学 at the University of Minnesota, leaving Harvard. |
| 1937 | 与伊冯·布鲁（Yvonne Blue）结婚。/ Married Yvonne Blue. |
| 1938 | 出版《有机体的行为》（*The Behavior of Organisms*），系统阐述操作性条件反射的基本原理。这是斯金纳最重要的学术著作之一。/ Published *The Behavior of Organisms*, systematically presenting the basic principles of operant conditioning. One of Skinner's most important academic works. |
| 1938 | 女儿朱莉（Julie）出生。/ Daughter Julie born. |
| 1940 | 晋升为明尼苏达大学副教授。女儿黛博拉（Deborah）出生。/ Promoted to associate professor at Minnesota. Daughter Deborah born. |
| 1943–1945 | 在第二次世界大战期间参与"鸽子计划"（Project Pigeon），试图训练鸽子引导导弹。虽然项目最终被取消，但这一研究深化了他对操作性条件反射的理解。还发明了"空气婴儿床"（Air Crib）——一种温度控制、隔音的婴儿箱。/ Participated in "Project Pigeon" during WWII, attempting to train pigeons to guide missiles. Although cancelled, the 研究 deepened his understanding of operant conditioning. Also invented the "Air Crib" — a temperature-controlled, soundproof baby enclosure. |

## 学术生涯中期 / Middle Academic Career

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|---|---|
| 1945 | 离开明尼苏达大学，接受印第安纳大学（Indiana University）心理学系主任职位。/ Left Minnesota to accept position as chair of the 心理学 department at Indiana University. |
| 1947 | 开始在印第安纳大学建立鸽子实验室（Pigeon Lab），进行更复杂的操作性条件反射研究。/ Began establishing the Pigeon Lab at Indiana University for more complex operant conditioning 研究. |
| 1948 | 出版乌托邦小说《瓦尔登湖第二》（*Walden Two*），描绘了一个基于行为科学原理设计的理想社区。该书在当时未引起广泛关注，但在1960–1970年代成为畅销书。/ Published utopian novel *Walden Two*, depicting an ideal community designed on behavioral science principles. The book received little attention initially but became a bestseller in the 1960s–1970s. |
| 1948 | 离开印第安纳大学，重返哈佛大学，担任心理学教授。/ Left Indiana University to return to Harvard as professor of 心理学. |
| 1949 | 被任命为哈佛大学埃德加·皮尔斯心理学教授（Edgar Pierce Professor of Psychology），这一荣誉讲席他一直保持到荣休。/ Appointed Edgar Pierce Professor of Psychology at Harvard, a chair he held until retirement. |
| 1953 | 出版《科学与人类行为》（*Science and Human Behavior*），这是他最系统的教科书式著作。/ Published *Science and Human Behavior*, his most systematic textbook. |
| 1957 | 出版《言语行为》（*Verbal Behavior*），尝试用操作性条件反射原理解释语言的获得和使用。同年与查尔斯·费斯特（C.B. Ferster）合著出版《强化时程表》（*Schedules of Reinforcement*）。/ Published *Verbal Behavior*, attempting to explain language via operant conditioning. Also published *Schedules of Reinforcement* with C.B. Ferster. |
| 1958 | 发表重要论文"Teaching Machines"（《教学机器》）于*Science*期刊，开始推广程序教学和教学机器。获美国心理学会杰出科学贡献奖（Distinguished Scientific Contribution Award）。/ Published "Teaching Machines" in *Science*, beginning promotion of programmed instruction and teaching machines. Received APA Distinguished Scientific Contribution Award. |
| 1959 | 诺姆·乔姆斯基（Noam Chomsky）发表对《言语行为》的著名批评文章，刊载于*Language*期刊。这篇批评被认为是认知革命的重要催化剂。/ Noam Chomsky published his famous critique of *Verbal Behavior* in *Language*. This critique is considered an important catalyst for the cognitive revolution. |

## 学术生涯晚期 / Late Academic Career

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|---|---|
| 1961 | 出版《累积记录》（*Cumulative Record*），收录1938–1961年间的重要论文。/ Published *Cumulative Record*, collecting important papers from 1938–1961. |
| 1968 | 出版《教学技术》（*The Technology of Teaching*），系统论述程序教学的原理和方法。获美国国家科学奖章（National Medal of Science）。/ Published *The Technology of Teaching*, systematically discussing programmed instruction. Received the National Medal of Science. |
| 1969 | 出版《强化的偶联》（*Contingencies of Reinforcement*），深入分析强化偶联的理论问题。/ Published *Contingencies of Reinforcement*, analyzing theoretical issues of reinforcement contingencies. |
| 1971 | 出版《超越自由与尊严》（*Beyond Freedom and Dignity*），对自由意志和尊严观念进行系统性批判。该书成为畅销书并引发广泛社会争论，使斯金纳成为当时最具争议的公共知识分子之一。/ Published *Beyond Freedom and Dignity*, systematic critique of free will and dignity. Became a bestseller and provoked widespread public debate, making Skinner one of the most controversial public intellectuals. |
| 1974 | 出版《关于行为主义》（*About Behaviorism*），以对话体形式系统阐述激进行为主义的哲学立场。从哈佛大学荣休。/ Published *About Behaviorism*, systematically presenting radical behaviorism in dialogue form. Retired from Harvard University. |
| 1976 | 与妻子合著出版《享受老年》（*Enjoy Old Age*）。/ Co-authored *Enjoy Old Age* with his wife. |
| 1979 | 出版自传第一卷《我生活的细节》（*Particulars of My Life*）。/ Published first volume of autobiography, *Particulars of My Life*. |
| 1981 | 在*Science*期刊发表"Selection by Consequences"，提出选择主义的三层框架。/ Published "Selection by Consequences" in *Science*, proposing three-level selectionist framework. |
| 1983 | 出版自传第二卷《行为主义者的塑造》（*The Shaping of a Behaviorist*）。出版论文集《进一步反思》（*Upon Further Reflection*）。/ Published second volume of autobiography, *The Shaping of a Behaviorist*, and essay collection *Upon Further Reflection*. |
| 1984 | 发表"The Operant Behaviorism of B.F. Skinner"于*American Psychologist*，对自己理论体系进行总结。/ Published summary of his theoretical system in *American Psychologist*. |
| 1987 | 发表"Why I Am Not a Cognitivist"于*American Psychologist*，对认知革命进行最后的系统回应。/ Published "Why I Am Not a Cognitivist" in *American Psychologist*, final systematic response to the cognitive revolution. |
| 1989 | 出版自传第三卷《后果问题》（*A Matter of Consequences*）。/ Published third volume of autobiography, *A Matter of Consequences*. |
| 1990 | 发表最后一篇论文"Can Psychology Be a Science of Mind?"于*American Psychologist*。获美国心理学会终身成就奖（Lifetime Achievement Award）。8月18日因白血病在波士顿去世，享年86岁。/ Published final paper "Can Psychology Be a Science of Mind?" in *American Psychologist*. Received APA Lifetime Achievement Award. Died August 18 of leukemia in Boston, aged 86. |
