# 个体心理学 · Individual Psychology

---

## 定义 / Definition

个体心理学（德语：Individualpsychologie）是阿德勒于1912年创立的心理学学派和治疗方法。名称中的"个体"（individual）一词来自拉丁语"individuum"（不可分割的），强调的是人的整体性和不可分割性——这与弗洛伊德将人格分割为本我、自我、超我的做法形成鲜明对比。个体心理学是深度心理学的三大创始学派之一（另两个是弗洛伊德的精神分析和荣格的分析心理学），但其影响范围远超心理治疗领域，延伸至教育、社会工作、组织管理和文化研究。

Individual Psychology (*Individualpsychologie*) is the psychological school and therapeutic approach founded by Adler in 1912. The word "individual" derives from the Latin *individuum* (indivisible), emphasizing the wholeness and indivisibility of the human person — in stark contrast to Freud's division of personality into id, ego, and superego. Individual Psychology is one of the three founding schools of depth psychology (alongside Freud's psychoanalysis and Jung's analytical psychology), but its influence extends far beyond psychotherapy into education, social work, organizational management, and cultural studies.

## 核心机制 / Core Mechanisms

### 整体论 / Holism

个体心理学的首要原则是整体论：人是一个不可分割的整体，思想与情感、意识与无意识、身体与心灵并非对立的实体，而是同一个体在不同维度上的表现。阿德勒反对一切形式的二元论——身心二元论、意识-无意识二元论、遗传-环境二元论。在个体心理学看来，个体的每一个行为、每一种情感、每一个症状都服务于同一个生活目标，都可以被理解为其生活风格的表达。

The first principle of Individual Psychology is holism: the human being is an indivisible whole. Thought and emotion, consciousness and unconsciousness, body and mind are not opposing entities but expressions of the same individual across different dimensions. Adler rejected all forms of dualism — mind-body dualism, conscious-unconscious dualism, heredity-environment dualism. In Individual Psychology, every behavior, every emotion, every symptom serves the same life goal and can be understood as an expression of the individual's lifestyle.

### 目的论 / Teleology

个体心理学坚持目的论取向：人的行为是由未来的目标所拉动的（pull），而非被过去的原因所推动（push）。阿德勒用"虚构目的论"（fictional finalism）这一概念来描述个体受主观建构的最终目标引导的现象。这个"虚构的最终目标"可能是无意识的，但它像指南针一样指导着个体的所有行为。治疗师的任务之一就是帮助来访者识别和理解其潜在的虚构目标。

Individual Psychology maintains a teleological orientation: human behavior is pulled by future goals, not pushed by past causes. Adler used the concept of "fictional finalism" to describe the phenomenon of individuals being guided by subjectively constructed ultimate goals. This "fictional final goal" may be unconscious, but it guides all of the individual's behavior like a compass. One of the therapist's tasks is to help clients identify and understand their latent fictional goals.

### 现象学立场 / Phenomenological Stance

个体心理学采取现象学立场：重要的不是客观现实，而是个体的主观感知和诠释。阿德勒称之为"统觉图式"（schema of apperception）——个体通过自己独特的"眼镜"来看世界。一个人的行为不是对客观环境的反应，而是对其主观感知到的环境的反应。这一立场使个体心理学成为认知心理学的先驱之一。

Individual Psychology adopts a phenomenological stance: what matters is not objective reality but the individual's subjective perception and interpretation. Adler called this the "schema of apperception" — the individual sees the world through their own unique "lens." A person's behavior is not a reaction to the objective environment but to their subjectively perceived environment. This stance made Individual Psychology a precursor to cognitive psychology.

### 社会嵌入性 / Social Embeddedness

个体心理学强调人本质上是社会存在。个体的心理发展不是发生在真空中，而是嵌入在特定的社会关系和文化背景中。家庭、学校、社区和文化构成了个体发展的社会场域。阿德勒的"社会兴趣"概念正是对人类作为社会存在的这一事实的理论回应。

Individual Psychology emphasizes that humans are fundamentally social beings. Individual psychological development does not occur in a vacuum but is embedded in specific social relationships and cultural contexts. Family, school, community, and culture constitute the social field of individual development. Adler's concept of "social interest" is precisely the theoretical response to the fact of humans as social beings.

## 发展脉络 / Historical Development

### 创立期（1912–1914）/ Founding Period

1911年与弗洛伊德决裂后，阿德勒于1912年正式创立个体心理学，出版《神经性格》作为其理论宣言。1914年创办《个体心理学国际期刊》。

After breaking with Freud in 1911, Adler formally founded Individual Psychology in 1912, publishing *The Neurotic Character* as its theoretical manifesto. In 1914, he launched the *International Journal of Individual Psychology*.

### 维也纳时期（1919–1932）/ Vienna Period

一战后，阿德勒在维也纳社会主义市政府的支持下，建立了30多所儿童指导诊所，并在维也纳师范学校任教。这一时期，个体心理学获得了制度化的基础，吸引了大量学生和合作者。阿德勒与维也纳教育改革运动密切合作，将其理论应用于课堂和家庭。

After World War I, with the support of Vienna's socialist municipal government, Adler established over 30 child guidance clinics and taught at the Vienna Pedagogical Institute. During this period, Individual Psychology gained institutional foundations and attracted numerous students and collaborators. Adler worked closely with the Viennese educational reform movement, applying his theories in classrooms and homes.

### 国际传播期（1930年代至今）/ International Dissemination

1930年代阿德勒移居美国后，个体心理学开始在北美和全球传播。鲁道夫·德莱库尔斯将个体心理学带到了芝加哥，建立了阿尔弗雷德·阿德勒研究所。海因茨和罗威娜·安斯巴赫尔夫妇编辑出版了阿德勒的系统选集。今天，全球有数十个阿德勒心理学组织，包括北美阿德勒心理学会（NASAP）、国际阿德勒心理学会（IAIP）、北美阿德勒研究生协会（NAAAPSA）等。

After Adler emigrated to the United States in the 1930s, Individual Psychology began spreading across North America and globally. Rudolf Dreikurs brought Individual Psychology to Chicago, establishing the Alfred Adler Institute. Heinz and Rowena Ansbacher edited and published a systematic anthology of Adler's works. Today, dozens of Adlerian psychology organizations exist worldwide, including NASAP, IAIP, and the North American Association of Adlerian Graduate Students (NAAAPSA).

### 当代发展 / Contemporary Developments

21世纪以来，个体心理学在以下几个方面获得了新的发展：

Since the 21st century, Individual Psychology has developed in several directions:

- **循证研究 / Evidence-based Research** — 阿德勒学派治疗的效果研究不断增加 / Growing effectiveness research for Adlerian therapy
- **整合取向 / Integrative Approaches** — 与认知行为疗法、积极心理学等的整合 / Integration with CBT, positive psychology, and other approaches
- **跨文化应用 / Cross-cultural Applications** — 在全球不同文化背景中的适应性发展 / Adaptive development across diverse global cultural contexts
- **神经科学验证 / Neuroscience Validation** — 当代神经科学研究为阿德勒的整体论和目的论提供了新的支持 / Contemporary neuroscience providing new support for Adler's holism and teleology

## 临床应用 / Clinical Applications

### 阿德勒治疗的四个阶段 / Four Stages of Adlerian Therapy

1. **建立关系 / Establishing the Relationship** — 基于平等、尊重和合作的治疗联盟 / A therapeutic alliance based on equality, respect, and collaboration
2. **评估与分析 / Assessment and Analysis** — 通过生活风格问卷和早期记忆分析了解个体的心理动力 / Understanding the individual's psychological dynamics through lifestyle questionnaires and early recollection analysis
3. **洞见 / Insight** — 帮助个体理解其行为模式和潜在目标 / Helping the individual understand their behavioral patterns and underlying goals
4. **重新定向 / Reorientation** — 鼓励个体尝试新的行为方式和思维模式 / Encouraging the individual to try new behavioral and cognitive patterns

### 核心技术 / Core Techniques

- **生活风格分析 / Lifestyle Analysis** — 系统探索个体的核心信念和行为模式
- **早期记忆分析 / Early Recollection Analysis** — 分析个体的最早记忆以揭示生活风格主题
- **"仿佛"技术 / "As If" Technique** — 让个体表现得"仿佛"他们已经改变了，以打破旧的行为模式
- **矛盾意向 / Paradoxical Intention** — 要求个体故意表现其症状，以揭示症状的目的性
- **推动按钮 / Push Button Technique** — 帮助个体认识到自己对情绪的控制能力

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

个体心理学的整体论与中国哲学中"天人合一"的传统有着深层共鸣。儒家强调人是关系性的存在（"仁者，人也"），这与阿德勒对人的社会嵌入性的强调相通。道家"道法自然"的整体观——将万物视为不可分割的统一体——与阿德勒反对一切形式二元论的整体论立场相呼应。

The holism of Individual Psychology resonates deeply with the Chinese philosophical tradition of "heaven and humanity as one" (*tiānrén héyī*). Confucianism emphasizes that humans are relational beings ("仁者，人也" — to be human is to be in relation), which aligns with Adler's emphasis on social embeddedness. The Daoist holistic vision of "the Dao follows nature" — viewing all things as an indivisible unity — echoes Adler's anti-dualistic holistic stance.

在印度哲学传统中，吠檀多不二论（Advaita Vedānta）关于梵我合一（Brahman-Ātman identity）的教义与阿德勒的整体论也有有趣的平行：两者都拒绝将人的不同面向割裂开来，而坚持一种根本的统一性。

In the Indian philosophical tradition, Advaita Vedānta's teaching of Brahman-Ātman identity also parallels Adler's holism: both refuse to fragment the different aspects of the person and insist on a fundamental unity.
