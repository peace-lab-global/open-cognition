# 社会兴趣 · Social Interest (Gemeinschaftsgefühl)

---

## 定义 / Definition

社会兴趣（德语：Gemeinschaftsgefühl，直译为"共同体感觉"；英语：Social Interest / Social Feeling）是阿德勒个体心理学中衡量心理健康的核心指标，也是其整个理论体系中最具原创性的概念之一。它指的是个体对人类社会共同体的归属感、认同感和贡献意愿——一种超越狭隘的自我中心、与他人和社会建立合作性联结的内在能力和倾向。

Social Interest (*Gemeinschaftsgefühl*, literally "community feeling"; also translated as "social feeling") is the core indicator of psychological health in Adler's Individual Psychology and one of the most original concepts in his entire theoretical system. It refers to the individual's sense of belonging, identification with, and willingness to contribute to the human community — an innate capacity and tendency to transcend narrow egocentrism and establish cooperative connections with others and society.

Gemeinschaftsgefühl 是一个难以精确翻译的德语词汇。它同时包含了认知（对社会相互依存性的理解）、情感（对共同体的归属感和同理心）和行为（合作与贡献的实际行动）三个维度。安斯巴赫尔（Ansbacher）将其翻译为"社会兴趣"，但许多学者认为"社会情感"或"共同体感觉"更能捕捉其完整含义。

*Gemeinschaftsgefühl* is a German term difficult to translate precisely. It simultaneously encompasses cognitive (understanding of social interdependence), emotional (sense of belonging and empathy for community), and behavioral (cooperative and contributive action) dimensions. Ansbacher translated it as "social interest," but many scholars believe "social feeling" or "community feeling" better captures its full meaning.

## 核心机制 / Core Mechanisms

### 先天潜能与后天培养 / Innate Potential and Cultivation

阿德勒认为社会兴趣是一种先天的潜能——人类作为社会性物种，天然具有与他人合作的倾向。然而，这种潜能不是自动发展的，它需要在适宜的社会环境中被唤醒和培养。母亲（或主要照顾者）是培养社会兴趣的第一个也是最重要的代理人：她通过温柔而坚定的互动，帮助儿童从最初的自我中心中走出来，学会信任他人和与外界建立联系。父亲则扮演着将社会兴趣从家庭扩展到更广泛社会的关键角色。

Adler considered social interest an innate potential — humans as a social species naturally possess the tendency to cooperate with others. However, this potential does not develop automatically; it must be awakened and cultivated in a favorable social environment. The mother (or primary caregiver) is the first and most important agent for cultivating social interest: through tender yet firm interaction, she helps the child emerge from initial egocentrism, learning to trust others and connect with the external world. The father plays the key role of extending social interest from the family to the wider society.

### 社会兴趣的维度 / Dimensions of Social Interest

社会兴趣表现为多个相互关联的维度：

Social interest manifests across multiple interrelated dimensions:

1. **合作 / Cooperation** — 与他人共同工作的能力和意愿 / The ability and willingness to work with others
2. **同理心 / Empathy** — 理解和感受他人处境的能力 / The ability to understand and feel others' situations
3. **归属感 / Belonging** — 感觉自己是人类共同体一部分的体验 / The experience of feeling oneself part of the human community
4. **贡献 / Contribution** — 为社会福祉做出积极贡献的意愿 / The willingness to make positive contributions to social welfare
5. **信任 / Trust** — 对他人和世界的基本信任 / Basic trust in others and the world
6. **关怀 / Care** — 对他人福祉的真正关心 / Genuine concern for others' well-being

### 社会兴趣与生活任务 / Social Interest and Life Tasks

阿德勒提出人类面临三大生活任务：工作（职业）、社交（友谊）和爱（亲密关系）。社会兴趣的水平直接决定了个体应对这三大任务的能力：

Adler proposed three life tasks: work (occupation), social relations (friendship), and love (intimacy). The level of social interest directly determines the individual's ability to address these tasks:

- **工作任务 / Work Task** — 高度社会兴趣的个体将工作视为对社会贡献的方式 / Individuals with high social interest view work as a way to contribute to society
- **社交任务 / Social Task** — 高度社会兴趣的个体能够建立真诚的友谊 / Individuals with high social interest can establish genuine friendships
- **爱的任务 / Love Task** — 高度社会兴趣的个体能够在亲密关系中平等地给予和接受 / Individuals with high social interest can give and receive equally in intimate relationships

### 社会兴趣的缺乏 / Lack of Social Interest

阿德勒认为，一切心理障碍——包括神经症、犯罪、自杀、成瘾——的根本原因都在于社会兴趣的缺乏。当个体的追求优越偏离了社会兴趣的方向，变成纯粹的个人竞争或支配他人时，就会产生各种心理问题。社会兴趣的缺乏通常源于早年的溺爱（过度保护导致自我中心）或忽视（导致对他人和社会的不信任）。

Adler believed that the root cause of all psychological disturbances — including neurosis, crime, suicide, and addiction — lies in the lack of social interest. When the individual's striving for superiority deviates from the direction of social interest, becoming purely personal competition or domination of others, various psychological problems arise. Lack of social interest typically stems from childhood pampering (overprotection leading to egocentrism) or neglect (leading to distrust of others and society).

## 发展脉络 / Historical Development

社会兴趣概念在阿德勒思想中经历了显著的发展：

The concept of social interest underwent significant development in Adler's thought:

**1912–1920年代 / 1912–1920s** — 社会兴趣最初被理解为一种次要概念，主要作为"追求优越"的修正因素。阿德勒在这一时期更多关注个体的权力追求和补偿机制 / Social interest was initially a secondary concept, mainly serving as a corrective to the "striving for superiority." During this period, Adler focused more on individual power striving and compensation mechanisms

**1927–1930年代 / 1927–1930s** — 社会兴趣逐渐成为阿德勒理论的核心概念。在《理解人性》（1927）和后续著作中，阿德勒将社会兴趣提升为心理健康的根本标准，并认为追求优越只有在社会兴趣的框架内才是健康的 / Social interest gradually became the central concept of Adler's theory. In *Understanding Human Nature* (1927) and subsequent works, Adler elevated social interest to the fundamental criterion of psychological health, arguing that striving for superiority is healthy only within the framework of social interest

**晚期与遗产 / Late Period and Legacy** — 在阿德勒生命的最后几年，社会兴趣的概念变得更加哲学化和宇宙化。他开始谈论"宇宙感觉"（cosmic feeling），将社会兴趣从人类社会扩展到整个宇宙的生命。这一发展被一些学者批评为过于理想化，但也被另一些学者视为阿德勒思想深度的体现 / In his final years, the concept of social interest became more philosophical and cosmic. He began speaking of a "cosmic feeling," extending social interest from human society to the life of the entire cosmos. This development has been criticized by some as overly idealistic but seen by others as reflecting the depth of Adler's thought

## 临床应用 / Clinical Applications

### 评估社会兴趣 / Assessing Social Interest

阿德勒学派治疗师通过以下方式评估来访者的社会兴趣水平：

Adlerian therapists assess clients' social interest levels through:

- **对话和观察 / Dialogue and Observation** — 注意来访者谈论他人和社会的方式 / Noting how clients speak about others and society
- **生活风格评估 / Lifestyle Assessment** — 分析来访者在三大生活任务中的功能水平 / Analyzing clients' functioning across the three life tasks
- **早期记忆分析 / Early Recollection Analysis** — 分析记忆中社会互动的质量和频率 / Analyzing the quality and frequency of social interactions in recollections
- **Kerr的社会兴趣量表 / Kerr's Social Interest Scale** — 量化评估工具 / Quantitative assessment tool
- **Crandall的社会兴趣量表 / Crandall's Social Interest Scale** — 另一种量化评估方法 / Another quantitative assessment method

### 培养社会兴趣 / Cultivating Social Interest

治疗的核心目标之一是帮助来访者发展社会兴趣。这包括：

A core therapeutic goal is helping clients develop social interest, which includes:

1. **鼓励 / Encouragement** — 帮助来访者体验自己的力量和归属感 / Helping clients experience their own strength and sense of belonging
2. **社会兴趣行为实验 / Social Interest Behavioral Experiments** — 鼓励来访者参与志愿活动、合作项目和社区服务 / Encouraging participation in volunteer activities, cooperative projects, and community service
3. **视角转换 / Perspective Shifting** — 帮助来访者从"这对我的意义是什么"转向"这对他人的意义是什么" / Helping clients shift from "what does this mean for me" to "what does this mean for others"
4. **团体治疗 / Group Therapy** — 阿德勒学派团体治疗是培养社会兴趣的理想环境 / Adlerian group therapy provides an ideal environment for cultivating social interest

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

社会兴趣与儒家"仁爱"概念之间有着深刻的共鸣。儒家的"仁"（rén）不仅是个人德性，更是人与人之间关系的理想状态——"仁者爱人"。阿德勒的社会兴趣同样不是单纯的道德要求，而是心理健康的自然表现。两者都认为，人的完善不能脱离与他人的关系来实现。

Social interest resonates profoundly with the Confucian concept of *rén* (benevolence/humaneness). Confucian *rén* is not merely personal virtue but the ideal state of human relationships — "the benevolent person loves others" (*rén zhě ài rén*). Adler's social interest is likewise not a mere moral requirement but a natural expression of psychological health. Both hold that human fulfillment cannot be realized apart from relationships with others.

在大乘佛教中，菩萨的"利他行"（parahita-caryā）——为一切众生的福祉而行动——与社会兴趣的精神高度契合。阿德勒的"共同体感觉"在菩萨的"同体大悲"（视众生之苦为己苦）中找到了东方的对应。区别在于，菩萨的慈悲基于"空性"的智慧——认识到自他不二；而阿德勒的社会兴趣基于对人类作为社会性存在的认识论洞察。

In Mahayana Buddhism, the bodhisattva's *parahita-caryā* (practice for others' benefit) — acting for the welfare of all beings — aligns closely with the spirit of social interest. Adler's "community feeling" finds its Eastern counterpart in the bodhisattva's "great compassion of same-body" (*tóngtǐ dàbēi*) — experiencing others' suffering as one's own. The difference lies in the fact that the bodhisattva's compassion is based on the wisdom of emptiness — recognizing the non-duality of self and other; while Adler's social interest is based on an epistemological insight into humans as social beings.
