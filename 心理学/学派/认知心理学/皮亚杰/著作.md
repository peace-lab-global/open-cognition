# 皮亚杰 · Jean Piaget - 著作年表 / Works Chronology

## 早期著作（生物学与哲学转向） / Early Works (Biology and Philosophical Turn)

| 年份 / Year | 著作 / Title | 核心贡献 / Core Contribution |
|---|---|---|
| 1918 | *Recherche* (小说) / *Recherche* (novel) | 以小说形式表达哲学和科学思想 / Expressed philosophical and scientific ideas in novel form |
| 1923 | *Le langage et la pensée chez l'enfant* / *The Language and Thought of the Child* | 揭示儿童语言的自我中心特征，提出"集体独白"概念 / Revealed egocentric features of children's language; coined "collective monologue" |
| 1924 | *Le jugement et le raisonnement chez l'enfant* / *Judgment and Reasoning in the Child* | 发现儿童推理的转导推理（transduction）模式 / Discovered transductive reasoning patterns in children |
| 1926 | *La représentation du monde chez l'enfant* / *The Child's Conception of the World* | 揭示泛灵论、人造论、目的论等儿童宇宙观特征 / Revealed animism, artificialism, finalism, and other features of children's worldview |
| 1928 | *Le jugement moral chez l'enfant* (partial) | 早期道德判断研究 / Early moral judgment research |

## 中期著作（感知运动期与道德发展） / Middle Works (Sensorimotor Period and Moral Development)

| 年份 / Year | 著作 / Title | 核心贡献 / Core Contribution |
|---|---|---|
| 1932 | *Le jugement moral chez l'enfant* / *The Moral Judgment of the Child* | 从"他律道德"到"自律道德"的发展研究 / Studied development from heteronomous to autonomous morality |
| 1936 | *La naissance de l'intelligence chez l'enfant* / *The Origins of Intelligence in Children* | 追踪感知运动智慧的六个亚阶段 / Traced six substages of sensorimotor intelligence |
| 1937 | *La construction du réel chez l'enfant* / *The Construction of Reality in the Child* | 客体永久性、空间、时间、因果性等概念的建构过程 / Construction processes of object permanence, space, time, causality, and other concepts |
| 1941 | *Le mécanisme du développement mental et les problèmes du psychisme* | 发展心理学的方法论反思 / Methodological reflection on developmental psychology |
| 1945 | *La formation du symbole chez l'enfant* / *Play, Dreams and Imitation in Childhood* | 符号功能的出现：模仿、游戏、梦、绘画 / Emergence of symbolic function: imitation, play, dreams, drawing |
| 1946 | *Le développement de la notion de temps chez l'enfant* / *The Child's Conception of Time* | 时间概念的建构过程 / Construction process of the concept of time |
| 1946 | *Les notions de mouvement et de vitesse chez l'enfant* / *The Child's Conception of Movement and Speed* | 运动与速度概念的建构 / Construction of movement and speed concepts |
| 1947 | *La psychologie de l'intelligence* / *The Psychology of Intelligence* | 系统阐述认知发展理论的入门著作 / Introductory systematic exposition of cognitive development theory |
| 1948 | *Où va l'éducation?* / *The Direction of Education* | 教育哲学的建构主义立场 / Constructivist position in educational philosophy |

## 成熟期著作（具体运算与发生认识论） / Mature Works (Concrete Operations and Genetic Epistemology)

| 年份 / Year | 著作 / Title | 核心贡献 / Core Contribution |
|---|---|---|
| 1950 | *Introduction à l'épistémologie génétique* (3 vols) / *Introduction to Genetic Epistemology* | 发生认识论的系统宣言 / Systematic manifesto of genetic epistemology |
| 1952 | *Les origines de l'intelligence chez l'enfant* (revised) | 感知运动期理论的最终版本 / Final version of sensorimotor period theory |
| 1954 | *La construction des structures opératoires élémentaires* (with Inhelder & Gréco) / *The Construction of Elementary Operational Structures* | 分类、序列化、数概念的发生学研究 / Genetic study of classification, seriation, and number concepts |
| 1956 | *Les épreuves de conservation* (with Inhelder) | 守恒实验的系统研究 / Systematic study of conservation experiments |
| 1958 | *La genèse des structures logiques élémentaires* (with Inhelder) / *The Early Growth of Logic in the Child* | 具体运算阶段分类与序列化的详细分析 / Detailed analysis of classification and seriation in the concrete operational stage |
| 1958 | *De la logique de l'enfant à la logique de l'adolescent* (with Inhelder) / *The Growth of Logical Thinking from Childhood to Adolescence* | 形式运算阶段的系统阐述 / Systematic exposition of the formal operational stage |
| 1961 | *La genèse du nombre chez l'enfant* (with Szeminska) / *The Child's Conception of Number* | 数概念的发生学研究 / Genetic study of the concept of number |
| 1962 | *Les mécanismes perceptifs* / *The Mechanisms of Perception* | 感知觉的认知建构研究 / Study of cognitive construction of perception |
| 1963 | *Six études de psychologie* / *Six Psychological Studies* | 认知发展理论的精要概述 / Concise summary of cognitive development theory |
| 1964 | *Le développement intellectuel chez l'enfant* (selected works) | 认知发展研究论文集 / Collected papers on cognitive development |
| 1966 | *La psychologie de l'enfant* (with Inhelder) / *The Psychology of the Child* | 四阶段理论的综合阐述（经典入门著作） / Comprehensive exposition of four-stage theory (classic introductory work) |
| 1967 | *Biologie et connaissance* / *Biology and Knowledge* | 将认知理论根植于生物学框架 / Rooted cognitive theory in a biological framework |
| 1967 | *Logique et connaissance scientifique* (ed.) / *Logic and Scientific Knowledge* | 逻辑学与认识论的交叉研究 / Cross-disciplinary study of logic and epistemology |
| 1970 | *Épistémologie génétique* / *Genetic Epistemology* | 认识论的总体框架（哥伦比亚大学讲座） / Overall framework of genetic epistemology (Columbia University lectures) |
| 1970 | *Piaget: Theory and Practice* (with Hall) | 理论到实践的桥接 / Bridging theory to practice |
