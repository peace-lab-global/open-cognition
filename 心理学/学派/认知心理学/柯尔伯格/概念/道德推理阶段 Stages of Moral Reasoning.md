# 道德推理阶段 Stages of Moral Reasoning

## 定义 / Definition

**道德推理阶段**（Stages of Moral Reasoning）是柯尔伯格道德发展理论的核心架构，描述了个体道德推理能力从自我中心到社会中心再到原则中心的三个发展水平、六个质变阶段。每个阶段代表一种"结构整体"（structured whole）——一套内在一致的、组织化的理解道德问题和做出道德判断的思维方式。阶段之间的差异是质的差异（思维方式的根本转变），而非量的差异（知道更多或更少的道德规则）。

**Stages of Moral Reasoning** is the central architecture of Kohlberg's moral development theory, describing the three developmental levels and six qualitative transformation stages of individual moral reasoning capacity from egocentric to society-centered to principle-centered orientations. Each stage represents a "structured whole" — an internally consistent, organized mode of understanding moral problems and making moral judgments. Differences between stages are qualitative (fundamental transformation in thinking mode) rather than quantitative (knowing more or fewer moral rules).

## 核心机制 / Core Mechanisms

### 水平一：前习俗道德（Pre-Conventional Level）

前习俗水平的个体主要根据行为的直接后果（惩罚或奖赏）和自身的利益来判断行为的对错。社会规则和权威被视为外在的、强制性的力量。这一水平在儿童期最为常见，但部分成年人也可能停留在这一水平。

Individuals at the pre-conventional level judge right and wrong primarily based on the direct consequences of behavior (punishment or reward) and their own self-interest. Social rules and authority are viewed as external, coercive forces. This level is most common in childhood, but some adults may also remain at this level.

**阶段1：惩罚与服从定向（Punishment and Obedience Orientation）**

"对"就是不被惩罚的行为。行为的道德价值完全由其物理后果决定——受到惩罚的行为就是"错的"，没有受到惩罚的行为就是"对的"。个体服从权威纯粹是为了避免惩罚。在海因茨两难中，阶段1的推理可能是："他不能偷药，因为偷东西会被警察抓"或者"他可以偷药，因为偷一点药不会被抓"。

"Right" is behavior that avoids punishment. The moral value of behavior is determined entirely by its physical consequences — punished behavior is "wrong," unpunished behavior is "right." The individual obeys authority purely to avoid punishment. In the Heinz dilemma, Stage 1 reasoning might be: "He cannot steal the drug because stealing will get him arrested by police" or "He can steal it because stealing a little won't get him caught."

**阶段2：工具-相对主义定向（Instrumental-Relativist Orientation）**

"对"就是能满足自己需要（有时也包括他人需要）的行为。道德被视为一种利益交换——"你帮我，我也帮你"。个体开始认识到不同人有不同的利益和观点，但认为每个人都应该追求自己的利益。公平被理解为等价交换（"以眼还眼"）。阶段2推理："他应该偷药，因为他的妻子需要药，而且以后他可以还钱给药剂师"或"他不应该偷药，因为坐牢对他来说太不划算了"。

"Right" is behavior that satisfies one's own needs (sometimes also others' needs). Morality is viewed as interest exchange — "you help me, I help you." The individual begins to recognize that different people have different interests and perspectives, but believes everyone should pursue their own interests. Fairness is understood as equivalent exchange ("an eye for an eye"). Stage 2 reasoning: "He should steal the drug because his wife needs it, and he can pay the pharmacist back later" or "He shouldn't steal because going to jail isn't worth it for him."

### 水平二：习俗道德（Conventional Level）

习俗水平的个体将道德等同于遵守社会规范、法律和他人的期望。维持社会秩序和人际关系成为道德判断的核心标准。大多数青少年和成年人处于这一水平。

Individuals at the conventional level equate morality with conforming to social norms, laws, and others' expectations. Maintaining social order and interpersonal relationships becomes the central criterion for moral judgment. Most adolescents and adults are at this level.

**阶段3：人际和谐定向（Interpersonal Concordance Orientation）**

"对"就是符合亲近之人的期望、维持良好人际关系的行为。个体重视信任、忠诚、尊重和感恩等关系性德性。道德判断开始考虑行为者的意图——"他是好意的"成为重要的道德评价标准。个体希望被视为"好人"。阶段3推理："他应该偷药，因为他是好丈夫，爱他的妻子"或"他不应该偷药，因为这会让他的家人蒙羞"。

"Right" is behavior conforming to the expectations of close ones and maintaining good interpersonal relationships. The individual values relational virtues such as trust, loyalty, respect, and gratitude. Moral judgment begins to consider the actor's intentions — "he meant well" becomes an important moral evaluation criterion. The individual wants to be seen as a "good person." Stage 3 reasoning: "He should steal the drug because he's a good husband who loves his wife" or "He shouldn't steal because it would shame his family."

**阶段4：法律与秩序定向（Law-and-Order Orientation）**

"对"就是遵守法律、履行义务和维护社会秩序的行为。个体从社会整体的视角看待道德问题——每个人都有自己的社会角色和义务，遵守规则对社会运转至关重要。法律被视为不可例外的普遍规范。阶段4推理："他不能偷药，因为偷窃违法，如果人人都偷东西社会就乱套了"或"他应该偷药，因为作为丈夫他有义务保护妻子的生命"。

"Right" is behavior that obeys laws, fulfills obligations, and maintains social order. The individual views moral problems from the perspective of society as a whole — everyone has their social roles and obligations, and obeying rules is essential for social functioning. Laws are viewed as universal norms admitting no exceptions. Stage 4 reasoning: "He cannot steal the drug because theft is illegal, and if everyone stole things society would collapse" or "He should steal the drug because as a husband he has a duty to protect his wife's life."

### 水平三：后习俗道德（Post-Conventional Level）

后习俗水平的个体超越具体的社会规范和法律，基于自主选择的、可普遍化的道德原则进行道德推理。法律和社会规范只有在服务于更高层次的正义原则时才具有正当性。只有少数成年人（通常是受过高等教育或具有深刻反思经历的人）能达到这一水平。详见 → [post-conventional.md](post-conventional.md)

Individuals at the post-conventional level transcend specific social norms and laws, engaging in moral reasoning based on autonomously chosen, universalizable moral principles. Laws and social norms are legitimate only insofar as they serve higher-level justice principles. Only a minority of adults (typically highly educated or deeply reflective individuals) reach this level. See → [post-conventional.md](post-conventional.md)

**阶段5：社会契约定向（Social Contract Orientation）**

个体认识到法律和社会制度是人类协商的产物，可以通过民主程序加以修改。法律的价值在于保护基本权利和促进公共福祉。当法律与基本权利冲突时，个体认为法律应该被修改。阶段5推理："虽然偷窃违法，但生命权高于财产权，法律应该保护生命——因此法律本身需要修改"。

The individual recognizes that laws and social institutions are products of human negotiation and can be modified through democratic procedures. The value of law lies in protecting fundamental rights and promoting public welfare. When laws conflict with fundamental rights, the individual believes laws should be modified. Stage 5 reasoning: "Although theft is illegal, the right to life outweighs property rights, and laws should protect life — therefore the law itself needs to be amended."

**阶段6：普遍伦理原则定向（Universal Ethical Principle Orientation）**

个体基于自我选择的、逻辑上全面的、可普遍化的伦理原则（如正义、平等、人的尊严）进行道德推理。这些原则不是具体的道德规则，而是抽象的伦理原则——康德式的"绝对命令"和罗尔斯式的"正义即公平"。当法律与这些原则冲突时，个体依据原则行动。阶段6推理："每个人都具有不可侵犯的尊严和价值，生命的价值超越了任何财产权或法律条款。作为一个理性的道德主体，我有义务按照所有人都应该遵循的原则行动——保护人的尊严和生命"。

The individual engages in moral reasoning based on self-chosen, logically comprehensive, universalizable ethical principles (such as justice, equality, human dignity). These principles are not specific moral rules but abstract ethical principles — Kantian "categorical imperatives" and Rawlsian "justice as fairness." When laws conflict with these principles, the individual acts according to principles. Stage 6 reasoning: "Every person possesses inviolable dignity and worth, and the value of life transcends any property right or legal provision. As a rational moral agent, I have an obligation to act according to principles that everyone should follow — protecting human dignity and life."

## 发展脉络 / Historical Development

### 皮亚杰的两阶段模型 / Piaget's Two-Stage Model

皮亚杰1932年的开创性研究将儿童的道德判断发展分为两个阶段：他律道德（heteronomous morality，约5-10岁）和自律道德（autonomous morality，约10岁以上）。他律阶段的儿童将规则视为不可更改的外部权威产物，自律阶段的儿童开始理解规则是社会协商的结果。

Piaget's pioneering 1932 research divided children's moral judgment development into two stages: heteronomous morality (approximately 5–10 years) and autonomous morality (approximately 10+ years). Heteronomous-stage children view rules as unchangeable products of external authority; autonomous-stage children begin to understand rules as products of social negotiation.

### 柯尔伯格的六阶段扩展 / Kohlberg's Six-Stage Extension

柯尔伯格将皮亚杰的两阶段模型扩展为三水平六阶段模型，关键创新包括：(1) 将发展范围从儿童期延伸到成年期；(2) 增加了"后习俗"水平，超越了皮亚杰的"自律"概念；(3) 区分了道德推理的"形式"（结构特征）与"内容"（具体结论）；(4) 开发了标准化的阶段评估工具（MJI）。

Kohlberg extended Piaget's two-stage model to three levels and six stages, with key innovations including: (1) extending the developmental range from childhood to adulthood; (2) adding the "post-conventional" level, surpassing Piaget's "autonomy" concept; (3) distinguishing the "form" (structural characteristics) from the "content" (specific conclusions) of moral reasoning; (4) developing a standardized stage assessment instrument (MJI).

### 后期修订与第六阶段的移除 / Later Revisions and Removal of Stage 6

柯尔伯格在后期研究中面临一个实证困难：第六阶段在经验研究中极为罕见，评分者难以可靠地区分第五阶段和第六阶段的推理。1980年代，柯尔伯格和Colby修订了评分手册，将第六阶段从正式的评分系统中移除，保留为理论理想类型。修订后的评分系统只评估阶段1至阶段5。

Kohlberg faced an empirical difficulty in later research: Stage 6 was extremely rare in empirical studies, and scorers could not reliably distinguish Stage 5 from Stage 6 reasoning. In the 1980s, Kohlberg and Colby revised the scoring manual, removing Stage 6 from the formal scoring system while retaining it as a theoretical ideal type. The revised scoring system assesses only Stages 1 through 5.

## 临床应用 / Clinical Applications

### 阶段评定工具 / Stage Assessment Instruments

- **道德判断访谈（MJI）**：半结构化访谈，包含3-5个道德两难故事，需约45分钟 / Semi-structured interview with 3–5 moral dilemma stories, requiring approximately 45 minutes
- **确定问题测验（DIT）**：Rest开发的标准化问卷，适用于大样本研究 / Standardized questionnaire developed by Rest, suitable for large-sample research
- **道德判断测验（MJT）**：Lind开发的在线测验工具 / Online assessment tool developed by Lind

### 教育实践 / Educational Practice

教师可以通过识别学生的道德推理阶段，设计"加一阶段"的讨论策略。例如，对处于阶段3的学生，呈现阶段4的论证（"如果每个人都这样做，社会会怎样？"）；对处于阶段4的学生，呈现阶段5的论证（"这条法律是否保护了所有人的基本权利？"）。

Teachers can design "+1 stage" discussion strategies by identifying students' moral reasoning stages. For example, for students at Stage 3, present Stage 4 arguments ("If everyone did this, what would happen to society?"); for students at Stage 4, present Stage 5 arguments ("Does this law protect everyone's fundamental rights?").

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

### 与孔子"从心所欲不逾矩"的对话 / Dialogue with Confucius's "Following Heart's Desires without Transgressing Boundaries"

孔子自述"七十而从心所欲，不逾矩"，描述了一种高度内化的道德境界——道德不再是需要刻意遵守的外部规则，而是成为内在自然的表达。这与柯尔伯格第六阶段的理想高度呼应：在最高阶段，个体基于自主选择的原则行动，但这些原则已经完全内化，成为个体道德人格的一部分。两者的区别在于：孔子的境界是通过终身修养而达到的"德性"（virtue），而柯尔伯格的阶段是通过认知发展而达到的"原则"（principle）。

Confucius described himself as "at seventy, following heart's desires without transgressing boundaries" — a highly internalized moral state where morality is no longer an external rule requiring deliberate compliance but becomes natural inner expression. This resonates closely with Kohlberg's Stage 6 ideal: at the highest stage, the individual acts based on autonomously chosen principles, but these principles have been fully internalized, becoming part of the individual's moral personality. The distinction lies in: Confucius's attainment is a "virtue" (dexing) achieved through lifelong cultivation, while Kohlberg's stage is a "principle" (yuanze) achieved through cognitive development.

### 与佛教"戒定慧"三学的对话 / Dialogue with Buddhism's Threefold Training

佛教的"戒定慧"三学（sila-samadhi-prajna）可以被解读为一种道德发展阶段模型："戒"（sila，道德戒律）类似于习俗水平——遵循既定的道德规范；"定"（samadhi，禅定）代表了从外在规范到内在修养的过渡；"慧"（prajna，智慧）则对应后习俗水平——基于对事物本质的深刻洞察而自然产生的道德行为，超越了对规则的机械遵守。

Buddhism's threefold training of "sila-samadhi-prajna" (morality-concentration-wisdom) can be read as a moral development stage model: "sila" (moral precepts) parallels the conventional level — following established moral norms; "samadhi" (meditation) represents the transition from external norms to inner cultivation; "prajna" (wisdom) corresponds to the post-conventional level — moral behavior arising naturally from profound insight into the nature of things, transcending mechanical rule-following.
