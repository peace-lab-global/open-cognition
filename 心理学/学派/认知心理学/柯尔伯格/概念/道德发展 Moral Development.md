# 道德发展 Moral Development

## 定义 / Definition

**道德发展**（Moral Development）在柯尔伯格的理论框架中，指个体道德推理能力从自我中心的前习俗定向，经由社会中心的习俗定向，最终发展为基于普遍正义原则的后习俗定向的认知结构发展过程。道德发展不是道德知识的线性积累，而是道德认知结构的质变——每一次阶段跃迁都意味着个体理解、组织和评判道德经验的思维方式的根本重构。

**Moral Development** in Kohlberg's theoretical framework refers to the cognitive-structural developmental process by which an individual's moral reasoning capacity evolves from egocentric pre-conventional orientations, through society-centered conventional orientations, to post-conventional orientations based on universal justice principles. Moral development is not the linear accumulation of moral knowledge but the qualitative transformation of moral cognitive structures — each stage transition represents a fundamental reconstruction of the way the individual understands, organizes, and evaluates moral experience.

## 核心机制 / Core Mechanisms

### 1. 认知冲突与失衡 / Cognitive Conflict and Disequilibrium

道德发展的根本动力是认知冲突（cognitive conflict）。当个体面临现有道德推理框架无法充分解决的道德问题时，产生认知失衡（disequilibrium），促使个体重建更高层次的认知结构。这与皮亚杰的"平衡化"（equilibration）概念一脉相承——同化（assimilation）与顺应（accommodation）的辩证运动推动认知发展。

The fundamental driving force of moral development is cognitive conflict. When individuals face moral problems their existing moral reasoning framework cannot adequately resolve, cognitive disequilibrium is produced, prompting the individual to reconstruct higher-level cognitive structures. This is continuous with Piaget's concept of "equilibration" — the dialectical movement of assimilation and accommodation drives cognitive development.

### 2. 结构整体性 / Structural Wholeness

每个道德阶段都是一个"结构整体"（structured whole）——一套内在一致的、组织化的道德思维方式。阶段之间的差异不是量的差异（知道更多或更少的道德规则），而是质的差异（以根本不同的方式理解道德）。处于特定阶段的个体倾向于在不同类型的道德情境中使用一致的推理模式。

Each moral stage is a "structured whole" — an internally consistent, organized mode of moral thinking. Differences between stages are not quantitative (knowing more or fewer moral rules) but qualitative (understanding morality in fundamentally different ways). Individuals at a particular stage tend to use consistent reasoning patterns across different types of moral situations.

### 3. 不变的序列 / Invariant Sequence

道德发展阶段遵循不可逆转、不可跳过的固定序列。没有人可以跳过某个阶段而直接达到更高的阶段；一旦达到更高的阶段，通常不会退回到更低的阶段（除非面临极端压力或认知崩溃）。这一序列在不同文化、不同历史时期具有基本的普遍性。

Moral development stages follow an irreversible, non-skippable fixed sequence. No one can skip a stage to directly reach a higher stage; once a higher stage is attained, regression to lower stages is uncommon (except under extreme pressure or cognitive breakdown). This sequence has basic universality across cultures and historical periods.

### 4. 层级整合 / Hierarchical Integration

更高的阶段不是简单地取代更低的阶段，而是将其整合为更复杂认知结构的一个组成部分。例如，后习俗水平的个体能够理解习俗水平的论证（如法律秩序的重要性），但将其置于更广阔的道德框架之中——法律之所以重要不是因为法律本身，而是因为法律服务于正义和人类尊严。

Higher stages do not simply replace lower stages but integrate them as components within a more complex cognitive structure. For example, post-conventional individuals can understand conventional-level arguments (such as the importance of law and order) but situate them within a broader moral framework — laws matter not because of laws themselves but because laws serve justice and human dignity.

## 发展脉络 / Historical Development

### 皮亚杰的先驱工作 / Piaget's Pioneering Work

柯尔伯格的道德发展理论直接建立在皮亚杰的道德判断研究之上。皮亚杰在1932年的《儿童的道德判断》（*The Moral Judgment of the Child*）中首次提出，儿童的道德判断经历从"他律"（heteronomy，道德是外部权威的规则）到"自律"（autonomy，道德是主体间协商的产物）的发展过程。皮亚杰发现，年幼儿童倾向于根据行为的客观后果判断对错（客观责任），而年长儿童则根据行为者的意图判断（主观责任）。

Kohlberg's moral development theory is directly built upon Piaget's moral judgment 研究. In *The Moral Judgment of the Child* (1932), Piaget first proposed that children's moral judgments develop from "heteronomy" (morality as external authority's rules) to "autonomy" (morality as product of intersubjective negotiation). Piaget discovered that younger children tend to judge right and wrong based on objective consequences of behavior (objective responsibility), while older children judge based on the actor's intentions (subjective responsibility).

### 柯尔伯格的系统扩展 / Kohlberg's Systematic Extension

柯尔伯格将皮亚杰的两阶段模型扩展为三水平六阶段模型，并将其适用范围从儿童期延伸到成年期。他的关键贡献包括：

Kohlberg extended Piaget's two-stage model to a three-level, six-stage model, extending its applicability from childhood to adulthood. His key contributions include:

- **1958年**：博士论文首次提出道德发展阶段论，以72名男孩的纵向研究为基础 / Doctoral dissertation first proposed moral development stage theory, based on longitudinal study of 72 boys
- **1969年**："Stage and Sequence"系统阐述了理论的认识论基础和阶段的结构特征 / "Stage and Sequence" systematically articulated the theory's epistemological foundations and structural characteristics of stages
- **1971年**："From Is to Ought"论证了道德发展理论的哲学正当性——"是"（is）与"应该"（ought）在发展理论中的统一 / "From Is to Ought" argued for the philosophical legitimacy of moral development theory — the unity of "is" and "ought" in developmental theory
- **1981–1984年**：两卷本《道德发展文集》全面总结了理论的哲学和心理学维度 / Two-volume *Essays on Moral Development* comprehensively summarized the theory's philosophical and psychological dimensions

### 后柯尔伯格时代的发展 / Post-Kohlberg Developments

柯尔伯格去世后，道德发展研究经历了重要发展：

After Kohlberg's death, moral development 研究 underwent significant developments:

- **雷斯特（James Rest）的四成分模型**（Four Component Model）：将道德行为分解为道德敏感性、道德判断、道德动机和道德品格四个相对独立的成分 / Decomposing moral behavior into four relatively independent components: moral sensitivity, moral judgment, moral motivation, and moral character
- **DIT测验**（Defining Issues Test）：Rest开发的标准化问卷工具，使大样本道德判断评估成为可能 / Standardized questionnaire tool developed by Rest, enabling large-sample moral judgment assessment
- **社会直觉主义模型**（Social Intuitionist Model）：海特（Jonathan Haidt）提出道德判断主要由直觉驱动，理性推理起"事后辩护"作用 / Haidt proposed that moral judgments are primarily driven by intuition, with rational reasoning serving "post-hoc justification"
- **道德基础理论**（Moral Foundations Theory）：海特等人提出五种（后扩展为六种）跨文化的道德基础 / Haidt and others proposed five (later expanded to six) cross-cultural moral foundations

## 临床应用 / Clinical Applications

### 道德判断评估 / Moral Judgment Assessment

道德判断访谈（MJI）和确定问题测验（DIT）被广泛应用于评估个体的道德发展水平。在临床心理学中，道德发展评估可用于理解个体的道德决策困境、评估反社会行为的认知基础、以及指导道德教育介入策略。

The Moral Judgment Interview (MJI) and Defining Issues Test (DIT) are widely used to assess individuals' moral development levels. In clinical psychology, moral development assessment can be used to understand individuals' moral decision-making dilemmas, evaluate the cognitive basis of antisocial behavior, and guide moral education intervention strategies.

### 青少年犯罪预防 / Juvenile Delinquency Prevention

研究表明，青少年犯罪者的道德推理水平通常显著低于同龄对照组。基于柯尔伯格理论的道德教育项目（如"道德两难讨论法"）被应用于少年管教机构，通过促进道德推理发展来降低再犯率。研究表明，结合"加一阶段"策略的团体讨论法效果最为显著。

Research shows that juvenile delinquents' moral reasoning levels are typically significantly lower than age-matched control groups. Moral education programs based on Kohlberg's theory (such as "moral dilemma discussion methods") have been applied in juvenile correctional facilities, reducing recidivism rates by promoting moral reasoning development. Research indicates that group discussion methods incorporating "+1 stage" strategies are most effective.

### 心理咨询中的道德发展视角 / Moral Development Perspective in Counseling

道德发展理论为心理咨询提供了理解来访者道德困惑的框架。来访者的道德困境常常反映了其道德认知结构与现实情境之间的不匹配。咨询师可以通过识别来访者的道德推理阶段，帮助其探索更高阶段的道德视角，促进道德认知的发展。

Moral development theory provides counseling with a framework for understanding clients' moral confusion. Clients' moral dilemmas often reflect a mismatch between their moral cognitive structures and real-world situations. Counselors can help clients explore higher-stage moral perspectives by identifying their moral reasoning stage, promoting moral cognitive development.

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

### 与荀子"化性起伪"的对话 / Dialogue with Xunzi's "Transforming Nature through Artifice"

荀子认为人性本恶，道德需要通过"礼义"的外在教化来"化性起伪"——将自然的生物性冲动转化为文化性的道德行为。柯尔伯格的道德发展理论提供了一条不同的路径：道德发展不是简单的外部教化，而是认知结构在教育性经验中的主动建构。两者的对话揭示了一个深层问题——道德发展究竟是从外部"塑造"还是从内部"生长"？当代研究倾向于认为两者并非对立：外部社会经验是道德发展的必要条件，但道德认知结构的质变需要个体的主动建构。

Xunzi held that human nature is inherently evil, and morality requires external civilizing education through "ritual and righteousness" (liyi) to "transform nature through artifice" — converting natural biological impulses into cultural moral behavior. Kohlberg's moral development theory offers a different path: moral development is not simple external civilizing but the active construction of cognitive structures through educational experiences. The dialogue between the two reveals a deep question — is moral development "shaped" from outside or "grown" from inside? Contemporary 研究 tends to view the two as non-oppositional: external social experience is a necessary condition for moral development, but qualitative transformation of moral cognitive structures requires individual active construction.

### 与王阳明"致良知"的对话 / Dialogue with Wang Yangming's "Extending Innate Moral Knowing"

王阳明的心学传统主张"良知"是人人本有的道德直觉，道德修养的关键是"致良知"——去除遮蔽良知的私欲。这与柯尔伯格的观点形成了有趣的对比：王阳明似乎认为道德能力是先天的（需要"恢复"而非"建构"），而柯尔伯格认为道德能力是在认知发展中逐步"建构"的。然而，两者也有深层共识：都强调道德不仅仅是遵循外部规则，都追求道德主体性的觉醒——王阳明通过"致良知"，柯尔伯格通过后习俗阶段的自主道德推理。

Wang Yangming's School of Mind tradition holds that "innate moral knowing" (liangzhi) is possessed by all, and the key to moral cultivation is "extending innate moral knowing" — removing self-interested desires that obscure liangzhi. This forms an interesting contrast with Kohlberg: Wang Yangming seems to view moral capacity as innate (requiring "restoration" rather than "construction"), while Kohlberg views moral capacity as gradually "constructed" through cognitive development. However, both share deep agreement: both emphasize that morality is not merely following external rules; both pursue the awakening of moral subjectivity — Wang Yangming through "extending liangzhi," Kohlberg through post-conventional autonomous moral reasoning.
