# 认知疗法 \u00b7 Cognitive Therapy (CT)

## 定义 / Definition

**认知疗法 (Cognitive Therapy, CT)** 是由 Aaron T. Beck 于 1960-1970 年代发展的一种结构化、限时、目标导向的心理治疗方法。其核心假设是：**心理困扰在很大程度上由功能失调的思维（认知）所维持；通过系统地识别、检验和修正这些认知，可以显著改善情绪和行为。**

Cognitive Therapy (CT) is a structured, time-limited, goal-oriented psychotherapy developed by Aaron T. Beck in the 1960s-1970s. Its core assumption: **psychological distress is largely maintained by dysfunctional thinking (cognition); by systematically identifying, testing, and modifying these cognitions, emotions and behavior can be significantly improved.**

认知疗法后来与行为技术深度融合，形成更广泛的**认知行为疗法 (Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, CBT)** 框架。在当代临床实践中，"CT"和"CBT"常交替使用，但严格说来，CT 侧重认知修正，CBT 包含更广泛的行为干预。

CT later merged with behavioral techniques to form the broader **Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)** framework. In contemporary practice, "CT" and "CBT" are often used interchangeably, though strictly speaking, CT emphasizes cognitive restructuring while CBT encompasses broader behavioral interventions.

## 核心机制 / Core Mechanisms

### 1. 认知模型 / The Cognitive Model

认知疗法建立在**认知模型**之上：情境 (Situation) \u2192 自动思维 (Automatic Thoughts) \u2192 情绪 (Emotion) / 行为 (Behavior) / 生理反应 (Physiological Response)。治疗的目标不是改变情境，而是改变个体对情境的认知加工。

CT is built on the **cognitive model**: Situation \u2192 Automatic Thoughts \u2192 Emotion / Behavior / Physiological Response. The therapeutic goal is not to change situations but to change how individuals cognitively process them.

### 2. 协作经验主义 / Collaborative Empiricism

这是认知疗法的**核心治疗立场**。治疗师不是"全知的诠释者"（精神分析），也不是"行为教练"（行为主义），而是与来访者并肩工作的**共同研究者 (co-investigator)**。双方共同：

This is the **core therapeutic stance** of CT. The therapist is not an "omniscient interpreter" (psychoanalysis) nor a "behavioral coach" (behaviorism) but a **co-investigator** working alongside the client. Together they:

- 识别功能失调的认知
- 将这些认知视为"有待检验的假设"而非"既定事实"
- 设计行为实验来在真实世界中检验这些假设
- 根据结果修正认知

### 3. 苏格拉底式提问 / Socratic Questioning

认知疗法不采用直接反驳（"你错了"），而是通过**引导式提问**帮助来访者自己发现认知中的矛盾：

CT avoids direct contradiction ("you're wrong") and instead uses **guided questioning** to help clients discover contradictions in their own thinking:

- "这个想法的证据是什么？" / "What is the evidence for this thought?"
- "有没有其他可能的解释？" / "Are there alternative explanations?"
- "最坏的结果是什么？你能应对吗？" / "What's the worst that could happen? Could you cope?"
- "如果你的朋友有这个想法，你会对他说什么？" / "What would you say to a friend who had this thought?"

### 4. 行为实验 / Behavioral Experiments

认知疗法不仅停留在"说"的层面，而是鼓励来访者在真实世界中**检验**自己的信念。例如，一个社交焦虑患者相信"如果我发言，所有人都会嘲笑我"，治疗师会帮助设计一个低风险的"实验"（在小组中说一句话），然后观察实际结果与预期是否一致。

CT goes beyond "talking" by encouraging clients to **test** their beliefs in the real world. For example, a socially anxious patient who believes "if I speak up, everyone will laugh at me" would work with the therapist to design a low-risk "experiment" (making a comment in a small group) and then observe whether actual outcomes match predictions.

### 5. 结构化会谈 / Structured Sessions

典型的 CBT 会谈遵循固定结构：

A typical CBT session follows a fixed structure:

1. **心境检查 (Mood check)** \u2014 用简表评估本周情绪
2. **议程设置 (Agenda setting)** \u2014 治疗师与来访者共同决定本次会谈的焦点
3. **回顾上次会谈 (Bridge from previous session)** \u2014 复习上周的要点和作业
4. **讨论议题 (Discussion of agenda items)** \u2014 识别自动思维、认知重构、行为计划
5. **家庭作业 (Homework assignment)** \u2014 布置下周的认知和行为练习
6. **总结与反馈 (Summary and feedback)** \u2014 概括本次会谈要点，收集来访者反馈

这种结构化使 CBT 具有高度的**可教授性和可复制性**，是它成为循证治疗首选的重要原因。

This structure gives CBT high **teachability and replicability**, a key reason it became the treatment of choice in evidence-based practice.

## 发展脉络 / Development History

- **1960s** \u2014 贝克在宾大研究抑郁症，发现精神分析假说无法被经验数据支持，转向关注自动思维
- **1967** \u2014 《抑郁症》出版，首次系统阐述认知模型
- **1970s** \u2014 认知疗法在门诊环境中逐步成型；1977 年发表里程碑性的 RCT，证明 CT 对抑郁症的疗效优于安慰剂，与药物等效
- **1979** \u2014 《抑郁症的认知疗法》出版，成为第一部操作手册化治疗指南
- **1980s-1990s** \u2014 认知模型扩展至焦虑、人格障碍、夫妻治疗；CBT 成为全球循证治疗的主流
- **2000s+** \u2014 第三浪潮 CBT（ACT、DBT、MBCT）整合正念与接纳；数字化 CBT 应用兴起
- **2010s-2020s** \u2014 精准 CBT（根据个体认知特征定制方案）；AI 驱动的 CBT 工具（Woebot 等）

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

### CT x 佛教因明学 / CT x Buddhist Pram\u0101\u1e47a (Epistemology)

认知疗法的苏格拉底式提问与佛教因明学 (pram\u0101\u1e47a, 量论) 共享一个基本立场：**信念应当被证据检验**。陈那 (Dign\u0101ga) 和法称 (Dharmak\u012brti) 发展了严密的逻辑-认识论体系，区分"现量"（直接感知）和"比量"（推理），强调只有可靠的认识来源 (pram\u0101\u1e47a) 才能支持有效的判断。贝克的"寻找证据"技术是这一认识论传统的世俗化临床版本。

CT's Socratic questioning shares with Buddhist epistemology (pram\u0101\u1e47a) a fundamental stance: **beliefs should be tested by evidence**. Dign\u0101ga and Dharmak\u012brti developed rigorous logic-epistemology systems distinguishing direct perception (pratyak\u1e63a) from inference (anum\u0101na), insisting only reliable epistemic sources can support valid judgments. Beck's "searching for evidence" technique is a secularized clinical version of this epistemological tradition.

### CT x 庄子"心斋" / CT x Zhuangzi's "Fasting of the Mind"

庄子的"心斋"要求"虚而待物"\u2014\u2014悬置已有成见以如实观照事物。这与 CBT 的苏格拉底式提问在方法论层面相通：两者都要求搁置预设判断。但"心斋"的目标是天人合一的超越体验，CBT 的目标是信念的理性修正。

Zhuangzi's "fasting of the mind" (x\u012bn zh\u0101i) requires suspending preconceptions to perceive things as they are. This aligns methodologically with CBT's Socratic questioning: both require suspending prejudgments. But "fasting of the mind" aims at transcendent union with the Dao, while CBT aims at rational belief modification.
