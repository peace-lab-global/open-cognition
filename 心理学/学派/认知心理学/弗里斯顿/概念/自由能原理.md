# 自由能原理 · Free Energy Principle

> "Everything that a self-organizing system does is aimed at minimizing its variational free energy."
> "一个自组织系统所做的一切，都旨在最小化其变分自由能。"

---

## 定义 / Definition

自由能原理（Free Energy Principle, FEP）是弗里斯顿于2006年正式提出的一条统一性理论命题：**任何能够自我维持的自组织系统，都趋向于最小化其状态的变分自由能（variational free energy）**。变分自由能衡量的是系统内部生成模型的预测与实际感官输入之间的偏差。最小化自由能意味着系统让自身的内部模型尽可能准确地反映外部世界的因果结构。

The Free Energy Principle (FEP), formally proposed by Friston in 2006, is a unifying theoretical proposition: **any self-organizing system capable of self-maintenance tends to minimize the variational free energy of its states**. Variational free energy quantifies the divergence between the predictions generated by the system's internal generative model and the actual sensory input it receives. Minimizing free energy means the system makes its internal model as accurate a reflection as possible of the causal structure of the external world.

## 核心机制 / Core Mechanism

### 数学定义 / Mathematical Definition

变分自由能 F 的标准分解为：

The standard decomposition of variational free energy F is:

```
F = D_KL[q(θ) || p(θ|s)] + log p(s)
  = E_q[log q(θ) - log p(s,θ)]
  = D_KL[q(θ) || p(θ)] + E_q[-log p(s|θ)]
  = Complexity + Accuracy
```

其中：
- `q(θ)` = 近似后验分布（系统对隐藏原因的当前信念）/ approximate posterior (system's current beliefs about hidden causes)
- `p(θ)` = 先验分布 / prior distribution
- `p(s|θ)` = 似然函数 / likelihood function
- `D_KL` = Kullback-Leibler散度 / Kullback-Leibler divergence
- **复杂度（Complexity）**：后验偏离先验的代价 / cost of posterior diverging from prior
- **精度（Accuracy）**：模型对数据的拟合程度 / how well the model fits the data

### 最小化自由能的三种途径 / Three Routes to Minimization

1. **感知（Perception）**：更新内部模型以更好地拟合感官数据——改变信念。
   更新 `q(θ)` 使其更接近真实后验 `p(θ|s)`。

   **Perception**: Update the internal model to better fit sensory data — change beliefs.
   Update `q(θ)` to be closer to the true posterior `p(θ|s)`.

2. **行动（Action）**：改变感官输入以符合模型的预测——改变世界。
   通过主动推理选择能验证自身预测的行动。

   **Action**: Change sensory input to conform to model predictions — change the world.
   Through active inference, select actions that confirm one's own predictions.

3. **学习（Learning）**：优化生成模型本身的结构和参数——改变模型。
   长期调整先验信念和似然函数的参数。

   **Learning**: Optimize the structure and parameters of the generative model itself — change the model.
   Long-term adjustment of prior beliefs and likelihood function parameters.

## 发展脉络 / Historical Development

### 前身 / Precursors

- **Helmholtz自由能（1882）**：热力学中衡量系统可用能量的概念，涉及能量与熵的权衡。
- **Boltzmann分布与能量模型（energy-based models）**：统计物理中系统趋向低能态的原理。
- **Helmholtz的"无意识推断"（1867）**：感知是对感官数据最佳解释的推断过程。
- **变分推断（Variational Inference）**：1990年代在机器学习中发展起来的近似贝叶斯推断方法。

### 关键节点 / Key Milestones

- **2003–2005**：弗里斯顿将预测编码与变分贝叶斯方法结合，提出大脑皮层是层级变分推断机器。
- **2006**：正式发表"A free energy principle for the brain"，将预测编码提升为普遍性原理。
- **2009–2010**：回应批评，扩展为"rough guide to the brain"系列论文，阐明原理的约束性（非预测性）特征。
- **2013–至今**：将自由能原理扩展到生物学（生命系统作为维持自身马尔可夫毯的过程）、社会学（集体自由能最小化）。

### Precursors

- **Helmholtz free energy (1882)**: Thermodynamic concept measuring usable energy, involving energy-entropy tradeoffs.
- **Boltzmann distribution and energy-based models**: Statistical physics principle that systems tend toward low-energy states.
- **Helmholtz's "unconscious inference" (1867)**: Perception as inference of the best explanation for sensory data.
- **Variational Inference**: Approximate Bayesian inference method developed in machine learning in the 1990s.

### Key Milestones

- **2003–2005**: Friston combined predictive coding with variational Bayesian methods, proposing the cortex as a hierarchical variational inference machine.
- **2006**: Formally published "A free energy principle for the brain," elevating predictive coding to a universal principle.
- **2009–2010**: Responded to criticism with the "rough guide to the brain" series, clarifying the principle's constraining (not predicting) character.
- **2013–present**: Extended the free energy principle to biology (living systems as processes maintaining their Markov blanket) and 社会学 (collective free energy minimization).

## 临床应用 / Clinical Applications

### 精神病学 / Psychiatry

- **精神分裂症**：先验信念的精确度过低→感官数据过度主导→幻觉；或先验信念僵化→无法被证据更新→妄想。
- **焦虑症**：对预测误差精确度的过度敏感→对不确定性的恐惧。
- **自闭症谱系**：预测误差精确度权重异常→对感官细节的过度关注（"弱中央统合"的预测编码解释）。
- **抑郁症**：对"行动无法改变结果"的先验过度确信→习得性无助的贝叶斯解释。

### Clinical Applications

- **Schizophrenia**: Overly low precision of priors → sensory data overdominates → hallucinations; or rigid priors → cannot be updated by evidence → delusions.
- **Anxiety disorders**: Hypersensitivity to prediction error precision → fear of uncertainty.
- **Autism spectrum**: Abnormal precision weighting of prediction errors → excessive attention to sensory details (predictive coding account of "weak central coherence").
- **Depression**: Overconfident prior that "action cannot change outcomes" → Bayesian account of learned helplessness.

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

### 佛学 / Buddhism

自由能最小化的理想状态是系统精确预测、无需修正——这与佛学中"如实知见"（yathābhūta-ñāṇadassana）的理想形成深层共鸣：觉悟者的心不再产生错误的概念投射（遍计所执性），而是直接、准确地回应现实（圆成实性）。预测误差的消除 ≈ 无明（avidyā）的消除。

The ideal state of free energy minimization — where the system predicts accurately without needing correction — resonates with the Buddhist ideal of "seeing things as they are" (yathābhūta-ñāṇadassana): the awakened mind no longer generates erroneous conceptual projections (parikalpita) but responds to reality directly and accurately (pariniṣpanna). Elimination of prediction error ≈ elimination of ignorance (avidyā).

### 道家 / Daoism

"无为"的理想是行动与世界的自然流动完美契合，不需要刻意造作——这与自由能最小化中系统精确预测感官输入、无需大幅修正模型的状态高度一致。"致虚极，守静笃"（《道德经》第16章）可以解读为将模型的复杂度（先验执念）降到最低，从而最大化对现实的敏感性。

The Daoist ideal of "wu wei" — action perfectly aligned with the natural flow of the world, without forcing — closely parallels the state in free energy minimization where the system accurately predicts sensory input without needing major model corrections. "Attain the utmost emptiness, hold firm to stillness" (Daodejing ch. 16) can be read as minimizing model complexity (prior attachments) to maximize sensitivity to reality.
