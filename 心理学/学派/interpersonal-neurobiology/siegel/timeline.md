# 西格尔生平年表 · Daniel J. Siegel Life Timeline

## 早年与教育 / Early Life and Education

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|:---:|:---|
| 1957 | 出生于美国纽约 / Born in New York, United States |
| 1970s | 成长于加州，对心理学与生物学产生早期兴趣 / Grew up in California, developing early interests in psychology and biology |
| ~1975–1979 | 进入哈佛大学学习，获得学士学位 / Entered Harvard University, earned bachelor's degree |
| ~1979–1983 | 哈佛大学医学院，获得医学博士（M.D.）学位 / Harvard Medical School, earned M.D. degree |
| 1983–1987 | 在加州大学洛杉矶分校（UCLA）完成精神病学住院医师训练 / Completed psychiatry residency training at UCLA |
| 1987 | 加入 UCLA 医学院任教 / Joined UCLA School of Medicine faculty |
| 1987–~1990 | 在UCLA进行发展心理学和依恋研究的博士后学习；系统研究母婴互动的视频材料 / Postdoctoral studies in developmental psychology and attachment research at UCLA; systematic study of mother-infant interaction video recordings |

## 学术生涯 / Academic Career

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|:---:|:---|
| 1990s | 与 Allan Schore 等学者共同推动依恋理论与神经科学的整合，被学术界称为"依恋理论三人组" / Collaborated with Allan Schore and others to advance the integration of attachment theory and neuroscience, known in academic circles as the "attachment theory troika" |
| 1990s | 注意到不同学科以不同术语描述相似的心智过程，萌生跨学科整合的想法 / Noticed that different disciplines described similar mental processes using different terminology, sparking the idea of cross-disciplinary integration |
| 1999 | 出版《发展中的心智》（*The Developing Mind*），标志着人际神经生物学（IPNB）作为独立学术框架的诞生 / Published *The Developing Mind*, marking the birth of Interpersonal Neurobiology (IPNB) as an independent academic framework |
| ~2000 | 联合创立 UCLA 意识研究中心（Mindsight Institute），致力于将IPNB的研究成果转化为临床实践和教育工具 / Co-founded the Mindsight Institute at UCLA, dedicated to translating IPNB research into clinical practice and educational tools |
| 2003 | 出版《Parenting from the Inside Out》，将依恋研究转化为面向父母的实用指南 / Published *Parenting from the Inside Out*, translating attachment research into a practical guide for parents |
| 2006 | 开始主编 Norton 出版社"人际神经生物学系列"（Series on IPNB），汇聚该领域顶尖学者的学术著作 / Began editing the Norton "Series on Interpersonal Neurobiology," bringing together top scholars' academic works |
| 2007 | 出版《The Mindful Brain》，推动正念冥想与神经科学的深度对话 / Published *The Mindful Brain*, advancing deep dialogue between mindfulness meditation and neuroscience |
| 2007 | 出版《发展中的心智》第二版，扩展整合框架 / Published 2nd edition of *The Developing Mind*, expanding the integrative framework |
| 2010 | 出版《心智之眼》（*Mindsight*），将"觉察-共情-整合"三维模型通俗化 / Published *Mindsight*, popularizing the three-dimensional "insight-empathy-integration" model |
| 2011 | 出版《全脑教养法》（*The Whole-Brain Child*），与 Bryson 合著；该书成为全球畅销书，被翻译为多种语言 / Published *The Whole-Brain Child* with Bryson; became an international bestseller translated into multiple languages |
| 2012 | 出版《人际神经生物学口袋指南》，为专业读者提供 IPNB 的系统入门 / Published *The Pocket Guide to Interpersonal Neurobiology*, providing a systematic IPNB introduction for professional readers |
| 2013–2015 | 出版《Brainstorm》，重新定义青少年大脑发育的理解 / Published *Brainstorm*, reframing understanding of adolescent brain development |
| 2014 | 出版《不吼不叫管教》（*No-Drama Discipline*），将脑科学纪律教育推广至全球 / Published *No-Drama Discipline*, promoting brain-based discipline education globally |
| 2018 | 出版《觉知》（*Aware*），系统阐述正念觉知的神经科学基础与"觉知之轮"练习 / Published *Aware*, systematically articulating the neuroscience foundations of mindful awareness and the "Wheel of Awareness" practice |
| 2020 | 出版《是的脑》（*The Yes Brain*）和《发展中的心智》第三版 / Published *The Yes Brain* and the 3rd edition of *The Developing Mind* |
| 2020 | 明确提出 MWe 概念——"Me + We"的整合性自我认同 / Formally proposed the MWe concept — "Me + We" integrative self-identity |
| 2023 | 出版《IntraConnected》，全面阐述 MWe 概念 / Published *IntraConnected*, comprehensively articulating the MWe concept |

## 影响力与认可 / Impact and Recognition

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|:---:|:---|
| 2000s– | 受邀在全球各大洲进行演讲和工作坊培训，推动IPNB的国际化传播 / Invited to speak and conduct workshops across all continents, advancing international dissemination of IPNB |
| 2010s | "耐受之窗"概念被创伤治疗领域广泛采纳，成为"创伤知情"运动的标志性术语 / "Window of tolerance" widely adopted in trauma treatment, becoming an iconic term of the "trauma-informed" movement |
| 2010s– | 《全脑教养法》系列在全球教育界产生深远影响，多国学校系统采纳其脑科学教育方法 / The *Whole-Brain Child* series profoundly influenced global education; school systems in multiple countries adopted its brain-based educational methods |
| 2018– | "觉知之轮"练习被翻译为多种语言，在全球学校、医院和社区中推广 / "Wheel of Awareness" practice translated into multiple languages, promoted in schools, hospitals, and communities worldwide |
| 2020s | MWe 概念被引入组织领导力和社会公正领域，推动"整合性领导力"模型的发展 / MWe concept introduced to organizational leadership and social justice, advancing the development of "integrative leadership" models |
| 2020s | 推动"正念学校"运动，将觉知训练纳入K-12教育体系 / Advanced the "mindful schools" movement, integrating awareness training into K-12 education |

- 持续主编 Norton 人际神经生物学系列 / Continuing to edit the Norton Series on Interpersonal Neurobiology
- 活跃于全球演讲、培训和公共倡导 / Active in global speaking, training, and public advocacy
