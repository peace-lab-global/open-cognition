# 米哈里推荐阅读 · Csikszentmihalyi Reading List

## 一、原典 / Primary Sources

### 核心必读 / Essential Reading

| 书名 / Title | 年份 / Year | 推荐理由 / Why Read |
|---|:---:|---|
| *Flow: The Psychology of Optimal Experience* | 1990 | **心流理论的奠基之作**，必读。中译本：《心流：最优体验心理学》（中信出版社）。涵盖心流的定义、特征、条件、以及在日常生活中的应用。/ **Foundational work of flow theory**, must-read. Covers the definition, characteristics, conditions, and applications of flow in everyday life. |
| *Creativity: Flow and the Psychology of Discovery and Invention* | 1996 | 基于91位杰出人物的创造力研究，提出系统模型。中译本：《创造力》（中信出版社）。/ Creativity research based on 91 eminent individuals, proposing the systems model. |
| *Finding Flow: The Psychology of Engagement with Everyday Life* | 1997 | 面向大众的心流入门读物，语言亲切，案例丰富。中译本：《发现心流》（中信出版社）。/ Popular introduction to flow, accessible language, rich examples. |

### 进阶原典 / Advanced Primary Sources

| 书名 / Title | 年份 / Year | 推荐理由 / Why Read |
|---|:---:|---|
| *Beyond Boredom and Anxiety* | 1975 | 心流概念的首次系统论述，学术性较强，适合深入了解理论起源。/ First systematic treatment of flow concept; more academic, ideal for understanding theoretical origins. |
| *The Evolving Self*（与 J.L. Singer 合著） | 1993 | 从进化心理学和复杂性理论视角探讨自我发展。中译本：《进化的自我》。/ Self-development from evolutionary psychology and complexity theory perspectives. |
| *Good Business: Leadership, Flow, and the Making of Meaning* | 2003 | 心流理论在组织管理中的应用，包含企业案例和领导实践。中译本：《好的工作》。/ Application of flow in organizational management, with business cases and leadership practices. |
| *Optimal Experience*（与 I.S. Csikszentmihalyi 编） | 1988 | 跨文化心流研究论文集，学术性强，适合研究者。/ Cross-cultural flow research essays, highly academic, suited for researchers. |
| *The Systems Model of Creativity*（论文集） | 2014 | 创造力系统模型的完整论述和回应。Springer出版。/ Complete exposition and responses on the creativity systems model. Published by Springer. |

## 二、英文二手研究 / English Secondary Research

### 学术综述与理论发展 / Academic Reviews and Theoretical Development

| 书名 / Title | 作者 / Author | 年份 / Year | 说明 / Notes |
|---|---|:---:|---|
| "The Concept of Flow" | Nakamura, J. & Csikszentmihalyi, M. | 2002 | 收入 *Handbook of Positive Psychology*（Oxford UP）。心流概念的权威学术综述。/ In *Handbook of Positive Psychology* (Oxford UP). Authoritative academic review. |
| "Positive Psychology: An Introduction" | Seligman, M.E.P. & Csikszentmihalyi, M. | 2000 | *American Psychologist* 55(1), 5–14。积极心理学宣言，理解米哈里在积极心理学中的定位。/ The positive psychology manifesto; understanding Csikszentmihalyi's position in positive psychology. |
| *Explaining Creativity: The Science of Human Innovation* | Sawyer, K. | 2012 | Oxford UP。当代创造力研究的权威综述，包含对系统模型的评价。/ Authoritative review of contemporary creativity research, including evaluation of the systems model. |
| *Flow in Sports* | Jackson, S.A. & Csikszentmihalyi, M. | 1999 | Human Kinetics。心流在运动心理学中的应用。/ Application of flow in sports psychology. |

### 心流的当代发展 / Contemporary Developments of Flow

| 书名 / Title | 作者 / Author | 年份 / Year | 说明 / Notes |
|---|---|:---:|---|
| *The Rise of Superman* | Kotler, S. | 2014 | 极限运动中的心流科学研究，通俗易懂。/ Flow science in extreme sports, accessible. |
| *Stealing Fire* | Kotler, S. & Wheal, J. | 2017 | 心流状态的技术增强（神经科学、冥想、药物）。/ Technological enhancement of flow states (neuroscience, meditation, pharmacology). |
| *The Art of Impossible* | Kotler, S. | 2021 | 心流与巅峰表现的系统性整合。/ Systematic integration of flow and peak performance. |
| "Flow and Regulatory Compatibility" | Keller, J. & Bless, H. | 2008 | *Personality and Social Psychology Bulletin*。调节聚焦理论与心流的关系。/ Regulatory focus theory and flow relationship. |
| "Development and Validation of a Scale to Measure Optimal Experience: The Flow State Scale" | Jackson, S.A. & Marsh, H.W. | 1996 | *Journal of Sport & Exercise Psychology*。心流量表的开发与验证。/ Development and validation of the Flow State Scale. |

## 三、中文延伸阅读 / Chinese Extended Reading

### 实用类 / Practical

| 书名 / Title | 作者 / Author | 说明 / Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 《深度工作》（*Deep Work*） | Cal Newport | 心流概念的实用主义版本，聚焦知识工作者的专注力训练。/ Pragmatic version of flow concept, focusing on knowledge workers' attention training. |
| 《刻意练习》（*Peak*） | Anders Ericsson | 心流通道中"技能提升"维度的深入探讨。/ In-depth exploration of the "skill improvement" dimension of the flow channel. |
| 《驱动力》（*Drive*） | Daniel Pink | 内在动机的大众读物，与米哈里的内在动机研究深度呼应。/ Popular book on intrinsic motivation, deeply resonating with Csikszentmihalyi's intrinsic motivation research. |
| 《心流：最优体验心理学》导读 | 赵昱鲲 | 中信版中译本的导读，帮助中文读者理解核心概念。/ Guide to the Chinese translation, helping Chinese readers understand core concepts. |

### 学术类 / Academic

| 书名 / Title | 作者 / Author | 说明 / Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 《积极心理学》 | 任俊 | 中文积极心理学教材，包含心流理论的系统介绍。/ Chinese positive psychology textbook, including systematic introduction to flow theory. |
| 《心流体验与积极心理》 | 多位中国学者 | 中国心理学界对心流理论的本土化研究。/ Domestic Chinese research on flow theory by Chinese psychologists. |
| 契克森米哈赖著作中译本系列 | 中信出版社 | 包括《心流》《发现心流》《创造力》《好的工作》《进化的自我》等。/ Includes Chinese translations of *Flow*, *Finding Flow*, *Creativity*, *Good Business*, *The Evolving Self*, etc. |

## 四、关联领域阅读 / Related Domain Reading

### 自我决定理论 / Self-Determination Theory

| 书名 / Title | 作者 / Author | 年份 / Year | 说明 / Notes |
|---|---|:---:|---|
| *Intrinsic Motivation and Self-Determination in Human Behavior* | Deci, E.L. & Ryan, R.M. | 1985 | SDT经典，理解内在动机的理论基础。/ SDT classic, understanding the theoretical basis of intrinsic motivation. |
| *Why We Do What We Do* | Deci, E.L. & Flaste, R. | 1995 | 面向大众的动机心理学读物。/ Popular introduction to motivational psychology. |

### 高峰体验与存在心理学 / Peak Experience and Existential Psychology

| 书名 / Title | 作者 / Author | 年份 / Year | 说明 / Notes |
|---|---|:---:|---|
| *Religions, Values, and Peak-Experiences* | Maslow, A.H. | 1964 | 高峰体验的原始论述，心流概念的重要前驱。/ Original treatment of peak experiences, important precursor to flow. |
| *Man's Search for Meaning* | Frankl, V.E. | 1946 | 意义感与心理韧性，与最优体验理论互补。/ Meaning and resilience, complementary to optimal experience theory. |

### 东方哲学参照 / Eastern Philosophy References

| 书名 / Title | 作者 / Author | 说明 / Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 《庄子》（特别是"养生主""达生""知北游"等篇） | 庄子 | "庖丁解牛""梓庆削木为鐻"等寓言与心流高度对应。/ Parables like "Cook Ding" and "Woodcarver Qing" correspond closely to flow. |
| 《薄伽梵歌》（*Bhagavad Gita*） | 传统经典 | "无执行动"（Nishkama Karma）与内在动机对话。/ "Action without attachment" (Nishkama Karma) dialogues with intrinsic motivation. |
| 《正念的奇迹》（*The Miracle of Mindfulness*） | 一行禅师（Thich Nhat Hanh） | 正念与心流在"全然投入当下"层面的汇通。/ Convergence of mindfulness and flow at the level of "complete presence." |
| 《禅与摩托车维修艺术》（*Zen and the Art of Motorcycle Repairing*） | Pirsig, R.M. | "良质"（Quality）概念与心流的哲学对话。/ The concept of "Quality" in philosophical dialogue with flow. |

## 推荐阅读路径 / Suggested Reading Paths

**入门路径 / Beginner Path**:
1. *Finding Flow*（《发现心流》）→ 2. *Flow*（《心流》）→ 3. Cal Newport *Deep Work*（《深度工作》）

**学术路径 / Academic Path**:
1. *Beyond Boredom and Anxiety* → 2. *Flow* → 3. Nakamura & Csikszentmihalyi (2002) "The Concept of Flow" → 4. *Optimal Experience*

**创造力路径 / Creativity Path**:
1. *Creativity* → 2. Sawyer *Explaining Creativity* → 3. *The Systems Model of Creativity*

**东西方对话路径 / East-West Dialogue Path**:
1. *Flow* → 2. 《庄子·养生主》→ 3. 《薄伽梵歌》→ 4. Thich Nhat Hanh *The Miracle of Mindfulness* → 5. Pirsig *Zen and the Art of Motorcycle Repairing*
