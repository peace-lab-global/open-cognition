# 怀特生平年表 · Michael White Life Timeline

## 早年与教育 / Early Life and Education

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|:---:|:---|
| 1948 | 出生于澳大利亚阿德莱德 / Born in Adelaide, South Australia |
| 1960s–1970s | 在南澳大利亚理工学院接受社会工作训练 / Trained as a social worker at the South Australian Institute of Technology |
| 1970s | 在阿德莱德从事家庭治疗实践，深受系统家庭治疗和米兰学派的影响 / Practiced family therapy in Adelaide, deeply influenced by systemic family therapy and the Milan School |

## 早期职业生涯 / Early Career

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|:---:|:---|
| 1970s | 对传统家庭治疗中将家庭"病理化"的倾向感到不满，开始寻找更尊重来访者主体性的治疗方式 / Grew dissatisfied with traditional family therapy's tendency to "pathologize" families; began searching for a more client-respecting approach |
| 1970s–1980s | 与新西兰奥克兰的大卫·艾普斯顿（David Epston）建立跨塔斯曼海的学术合作关系 / Established trans-Tasman academic partnership with David Epston in Auckland, New Zealand |
| 1980s | 开始关注治疗中的权力关系问题 / Began attending to power relations in therapy |
| 1983 | 在阿德莱德创立杜尔维奇中心（Dulwich Centre） / Founded the Dulwich Centre in Adelaide |

## 中期：叙事疗法的创立与发展 / Middle Period: Founding and Development of Narrative Therapy

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|:---:|:---|
| 1980s中期 | 接触福柯的权力/知识理论和布鲁纳的叙事心理学，从根本上重新思考治疗实践的本质 / Encountered Foucault's power/knowledge theory and Bruner's narrative psychology; fundamentally rethought the nature of therapeutic practice |
| 1988 | 发表"The Externalizing Conversation"，首次系统阐述外化对话技术 / Published "The Externalizing Conversation," first systematic exposition of externalizing conversation techniques |
| 1989 | 与艾普斯顿合著 *Literate Means to Therapeutic Ends*，探讨书写在治疗中的运用 / Co-authored *Literate Means to Therapeutic Ends* with Epston |
| 1990 | 与艾普斯顿出版里程碑式著作 *Narrative Means to Therapeutic Ends*，系统提出叙事疗法核心概念 / Published landmark *Narrative Means to Therapeutic Ends* with Epston, systematically proposing narrative therapy core concepts |
| 1991 | 出版 *Experience, Contradiction, Narrative and Imagination* / Published *Experience, Contradiction, Narrative and Imagination* |
| 1990s | 叙事疗法迅速在家庭治疗、社区工作和跨文化治疗领域传播 / Narrative therapy spread rapidly in family therapy, community work, and cross-cultural therapeutic fields |
| 1995 | 出版 *Narrative Therapy and Community Work*，将叙事实践拓展至社区工作领域 / Published *Narrative Therapy and Community Work* |
| 1997 | 出版 *Narratives of Therapists' Lives* / Published *Narratives of Therapists' Lives* |

## 晚期：叙事实践地图的精细化与全球传播 / Late Period: Refinement and Global Dissemination

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|:---:|:---|
| 2000 | 出版 *Reflections on Narrative Practice*，对叙事实践进行反思与总结 / Published *Reflections on Narrative Practice* |
| 2000s | 越来越多地在国际场合——特别是非西方文化语境中——开展工作 / Increasingly worked in international contexts, particularly non-Western cultural settings |
| 2004 | 出版 *Narrative Practice and Exotic Sounds*，关注创造性与想象力维度 / Published *Narrative Practice and Exotic Sounds* |
| 2000s | 探索叙事实践在原住民社区、受创伤影响的社群和跨文化对话中的应用 / Explored narrative practice in Indigenous communities, trauma-affected populations, and cross-cultural dialogue |
| 2007 | 出版 *Maps of Narrative Practice*——最系统化的著作，提出六大治疗地图 / Published *Maps of Narrative Practice* — most systematic work, proposing six therapeutic maps |

## 逝世与遗产 / Death and Legacy

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|:---:|:---|
| 2008 | 4月4日在美国圣迭戈进行教学工作坊时因心脏病突发去世，享年60岁 / Died April 4 in San Diego of a heart attack while conducting a teaching workshop, aged 60 |

## 影响力与认可 / Impact and Recognition

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|:---:|:---|
| 1990s– | 杜尔维奇中心培训的治疗师和社区工作者遍布全球，叙事实践成为当代心理治疗的重要流派 / Dulwich Centre trained therapists and community workers worldwide; narrative practice became a major psychotherapy school |
| 2000s– | 恩库比（Ncazelo Ncube）、德纳姆（David Denborough）等人在非洲、澳洲原住民社区推进集体叙事实践 / Ncazelo Ncube, David Denborough advanced collective narrative practice in African and Australian Indigenous communities |
| 2008– | 叙事实践与焦点解决短期治疗、合作对话实践等共同构成后现代治疗运动的核心 / Narrative practice, along with solution-focused brief therapy and collaborative dialogic practice, forms the core of postmodern therapy movement |
| 2010s– | 叙事实践在社会工作、教育、组织咨询和叙事医学等领域得到广泛应用 / Narrative practice widely applied in social work, education, organizational consulting, and narrative medicine |
