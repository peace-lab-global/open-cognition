# 班杜拉进阶阅读书目 · Bandura Reading List

## 一、原著精选 / Primary Works (Selected)

### 必读原著 / Essential Primary Readings

| 优先级 / Priority | 著作 / Work | 说明 / Notes |
|:---:|------|------|
| ★★★ | Bandura, A. (1977). *Social Learning Theory*. Englewood Cliffs, NJ: Prentice Hall. | 社会学习理论的经典入门——篇幅精炼，结构清晰。Classic introduction to social learning theory — concise and well-structured. |
| ★★★ | Bandura, A. (1977). "Self-Efficacy: Toward a Unifying Theory of Behavioral Change." *Psychological Review*, 84(2), 191–215. | 自我效能感理论的奠基论文——心理学史上最有影响力的论文之一。Foundational paper of self-efficacy theory — among the most influential papers in psychology history. |
| ★★★ | Bandura, A. (1986). *Social Foundations of Thought and Action: A Social Cognitive Theory*. Englewood Cliffs, NJ: Prentice Hall. | 社会认知理论的完整体系——理论最成熟、最全面的版本。Complete system of social cognitive theory — the most mature and comprehensive version. |
| ★★★ | Bandura, A. (1997). *Self-Efficacy: The Exercise of Control*. New York: W.H. Freeman. | 自我效能感理论的集大成之作——横跨多个应用领域的整合。Crowning achievement of self-efficacy theory — integration across multiple application domains. |
| ★★☆ | Bandura, A. (1973). *Aggression: A Social Learning Analysis*. Englewood Cliffs, NJ: Prentice Hall. | 攻击行为的社会学习分析——波波玩偶实验的理论化。Social learning analysis of aggression — theoretical framework for Bobo Doll experiments. |
| ★★☆ | Bandura, A. (2016). *Moral Disengagement*. New York: Worth Publishers. | 道德脱离理论——晚年对人类残暴行为的深入分析。Moral disengagement theory — late-career deep analysis of human cruelty. |
| ★★☆ | Bandura, A., Ross, D., & Ross, S.A. (1961). "Transmission of Aggression Through Imitation of Aggressive Models." *Journal of Abnormal and Social Psychology*, 63, 575–582. | 波波玩偶实验原始报告——心理学史上最著名的实验之一。Original Bobo Doll experiment report — one of the most famous experiments in psychology history. |
| ★☆☆ | Bandura, A. (1978). "The Self System in Reciprocal Determinism." *American Psychologist*, 33(4), 344–358. | 交互决定论的正式阐述。Formal articulation of reciprocal determinism. |
| ★☆☆ | Bandura, A. (1989). "Human Agency in Social Cognitive Theory." *American Psychologist*, 44(9), 1175–1184. | 代理性理论的系统阐述。Systematic articulation of agency theory. |
| ★☆☆ | Bandura, A. (2001). "Social Cognitive Theory: An Agentic Perspective." *Annual Review of Psychology*, 52, 1–26. | 社会认知理论的综合回顾。Comprehensive review of social cognitive theory. |

### 推荐原著 / Recommended Primary Readings

- Bandura, A. & Walters, R.H. (1963). *Social Learning and Personality Development*. New York: Holt, Rinehart & Winston.
- Bandura, A. (1965). "Influence of Models' Reinforcement Contingencies on the Acquisition of Imitative Responses." *Journal of Personality and Social Psychology*, 1, 589–595.
- Bandura, A. (1991). "Social Cognitive Theory of Self-Regulation." *Organizational Behavior and Human Decision Processes*, 50(2), 248–287.
- Bandura, A. (1999). "Moral Disengagement in the Perpetration of Inhumanities." *Personality and Social Psychology Review*, 3(3), 193–209.

## 二、二手研究 / Secondary Sources

### 传记与学术评价 / Biography and Scholarly Evaluation

- Pajares, F. (2004). "Albert Bandura: Biographical Sketch." In *Encyclopedia of Education*, 2nd ed. New York: Macmillan. — 最详尽的班杜拉传记概述。Most detailed biographical overview of Bandura.
- Pajares, F. (2002). "Albert Bandura." In *The History of Psychology: Critical Concepts in Psychology*. London: Routledge. — 历史定位和学术评价。Historical positioning and scholarly evaluation.
- Zimmerman, B.J. & Schunk, D.H. (Eds.) (2003). *Educational Psychology: A Century of Contributions*. Greenwich, CT: Information Age Publishing. — 包含对班杜拉教育贡献的评价。Contains evaluation of Bandura's educational contributions.
- Cervone, D. & Shoda, Y. (Eds.) (1999). *The Coherence of Personality: Social-Cognitive Explanations*. New York: Guilford Press. — 班杜拉学生的学术传承。Academic legacy from Bandura's students.

### 理论批评与发展 / Theoretical Criticism and Development

- Cervone, D. (2004). "The Architecture of Personality." *Psychological Review*, 111(1), 183–204. — 对社会认知理论人格模型的进一步发展。Further development of the social-cognitive personality model.
- Mischel, W. & Shoda, Y. (1995). "A Cognitive-Affective System Theory of Personality." *Psychological Review*, 102(2), 246–268. — 受班杜拉影响的人格认知-情感系统理论。Cognitive-affective system theory of personality, influenced by Bandura.
- Luszczynska, A. & Schwarzer, R. (2005). "Self-Efficacy." In M. Boekaerts et al. (Eds.), *Handbook of Competence and Motivation*. New York: Guilford. — 自我效能感研究的当代综述。Contemporary review of self-efficacy research.

## 三、英文进阶阅读 / English Further Reading

### 自我效能感专题 / Self-Efficacy Focus

- Maddux, J.E. (Ed.) (1995). *Self-Efficacy, Adaptation, and Adjustment: Theory, Research, and Application*. New York: Plenum. — 自我效能感的多领域应用论文集。Multi-domain application essays on self-efficacy.
- Schunk, D.H. & Pajares, F. (2009). "Self-Efficacy Theory." In K.R. Wentzel & A. Wigfield (Eds.), *Handbook of Motivation at School*. New York: Routledge. — 教育领域的自我效能感研究。Self-efficacy research in education.
- Schwarzer, R. (Ed.) (1992). *Self-Efficacy: Thought Control of Action*. Washington, DC: Hemisphere. — 自我效能感与行为控制的欧洲视角。European perspectives on self-efficacy and behavioral control.
- Pajares, F. (1997). "Current Directions in Self-Efficacy Research." In M. Maehr & P.R. Pintrich (Eds.), *Advances in Motivation and Achievement*, Vol. 10. Greenwich, CT: JAI Press.

### 社会学习与社会认知理论专题 / Social Learning and Social Cognitive Theory Focus

- Grusec, J.E. (1992). "Social Learning Theory and Developmental Psychology: The Legacies of Robert Sears and Albert Bandura." *Developmental Psychology*, 28(5), 776–786. — 班杜拉在发展心理学中的历史定位。Bandura's historical positioning in developmental psychology.
- Schunk, D.H. & DiBenedetto, M.K. (2020). "Motivation and Social Cognitive Theory." *Contemporary Educational Psychology*, 60, 101832. — 社会认知理论在教育动机研究中的最新进展。Latest developments in social cognitive theory in educational motivation research.
- Bandura, A. (Ed.) (1995). *Self-Efficacy in Changing Societies*. New York: Cambridge University Press. — 社会变迁中的自我效能感——跨文化视角。Self-efficacy in changing societies — cross-cultural perspectives.

### 道德脱离专题 / Moral Disengagement Focus

- Moore, C. (2015). "Moral Disengagement." *Current Opinion in Psychology*, 6, 199–204. — 道德脱离研究的当代综述。Contemporary review of moral disengagement research.
- Moore, C. & Gino, F. (2015). "Approach, Ability, Aftermath: A Psychological Process Framework of Unethical Behavior." *Academy of Management Annals*, 9(1), 235–289. — 组织不道德行为的心理过程框架。Psychological process framework of organizational unethical behavior.

## 四、关联领域阅读 / Related Fields Reading

### 行为主义 / Behaviorism

- Skinner, B.F. (1953). *Science and Human Behavior*. New York: Macmillan. — 班杜拉批判和超越的主要对象。The primary target of Bandura's critique and transcendence.
- Miller, N.E. & Dollard, J. (1941). *Social Learning and Imitation*. New Haven: Yale University Press. — 社会学习思想的先驱。Precursor of social learning thought.

### 认知心理学 / Cognitive Psychology

- Piaget, J. (1970). *Genetic Epistemology*. New York: Columbia University Press. — 认知发展理论——与班杜拉关注社会因素的互补。Cognitive development theory — complementary to Bandura's social factor emphasis.
- Beck, A.T. (1976). *Cognitive Therapy and the Emotional Disorders*. New York: International Universities Press. — 认知治疗——与班杜拉的认知中介理论平行。Cognitive therapy — parallel to Bandura's cognitive mediation theory.

### 镜像神经元 / Mirror Neurons

- Rizzolatti, G. & Craighero, L. (2004). "The Mirror-Neuron System." *Annual Review of Neuroscience*, 27, 169–192. — 观察学习的神经生物学基础。Neurobiological basis of observational learning.
- Iacoboni, M. (2009). "Imitation, Empathy, and Mirror Neurons." *Annual Review of Psychology*, 60, 653–670. — 镜像神经元与模仿、共情的关系。Mirror neurons' relationship to imitation and empathy.

### 人格心理学 / Personality Psychology

- Mischel, W. (1968). *Personality and Assessment*. New York: Wiley. — 人格-情境争论——班杜拉的交互决定论提供了一个超越二元对立的框架。Person-situation debate — Bandura's reciprocal determinism offers a framework transcending the dichotomy.
- Cervone, D. & Mischel, W. (Eds.) (2002). *Personality Theory and Research*. San Francisco: Jossey-Bass.

## 五、阅读建议 / Reading Suggestions

### 入门路径 / Entry Path

1. 先读 Bandura (1977) *Social Learning Theory* — 最精炼的理论概述。Start with Bandura (1977) — most concise theoretical overview.
2. 再读 Bandura (1977) "Self-Efficacy" 论文 — 自我效能感的奠基之作。Then read Bandura (1977) "Self-Efficacy" paper — foundational self-efficacy work.
3. 然后读 Pajares (2004) 传记概述 — 了解学术背景。Then read Pajares (2004) biographical sketch — understand academic context.

### 深入路径 / Advanced Path

4. Bandura (1986) *Social Foundations* — 完整理论体系。Complete theoretical system.
5. Bandura (1997) *Self-Efficacy* — 应用领域整合。Application domain integration.
6. Bandura (2016) *Moral Disengagement* — 晚年道德心理学。Late-career moral psychology.

### 批判路径 / Critical Path

7. Skinner (1953) — 理解班杜拉批判的对象。Understand what Bandura was critiquing.
8. Mischel (1968) — 理解人格-情境争论的背景。Understand the person-situation debate context.
9. Cervone & Shoda (1999) — 了解理论的当代发展。Understand contemporary developments of the theory.
