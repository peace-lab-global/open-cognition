# 防御机制 · Defense Mechanisms

## 定义 / Definition

防御机制（德语：Abwehrmechanismen；英语：defense mechanisms）是弗洛伊德精神分析理论中描述自我（ego）用以保护自身免受焦虑侵扰的无意识心理策略的总称。当本我的冲动、超我的要求或外部现实的威胁之间的冲突产生无法承受的焦虑时，自我通过一系列自动的、无意识的心理操作来减轻或避免这种焦虑。这些操作虽然在短期内保护了心理的平衡，但长期使用可能导致症状形成和人格僵化。

Defense mechanisms (German: Abwehrmechanismen) is the general term in Freud's psychoanalytic theory for the unconscious psychological strategies used by the ego to protect itself from anxiety. When conflicts between the id's impulses, the superego's demands, or threats from external reality produce unbearable anxiety, the ego employs a series of automatic, unconscious psychological operations to reduce or avoid this anxiety. While these operations protect psychological equilibrium in the short term, their prolonged use can lead to symptom formation and personality rigidity.

虽然弗洛伊德在1894年就使用了"防御"（Abwehr）这一概念，但防御机制的系统化分类主要由他的女儿安娜·弗洛伊德在《自我与防御机制》（1936）中完成。当代心理学（如George Vaillant的层级模型）在此基础上进一步发展了这一概念。

Although Freud used the concept of "defense" (Abwehr) as early as 1894, the systematized classification of defense mechanisms was primarily accomplished by his daughter Anna Freud in *The Ego and the Mechanisms of Defence* (1936). Contemporary psychology (such as George Vaillant's hierarchical model) has further developed this concept.

## 核心机制 / Core Mechanism

### 焦虑的信号功能 / The Signal Function of Anxiety

弗洛伊德在《抑制、症状与焦虑》（1926）中提出了焦虑的"信号理论"：焦虑不是压抑的结果（如早期理论所认为），而是压抑的原因——焦虑作为一种"信号"（Signal）警告自我即将面临的危险，促使自我启动防御机制。弗洛伊德区分了三种焦虑来源：现实焦虑（对外部危险的反应）、神经症焦虑（对本我冲动的恐惧）和道德焦虑（对超我惩罚的恐惧）。

In *Inhibitions, Symptoms and Anxiety* (1926), Freud proposed the "signal theory" of anxiety: anxiety is not the result of repression (as the early theory held) but its cause — anxiety serves as a "signal" warning the ego of impending danger, prompting the ego to activate defense mechanisms. Freud distinguished three sources of anxiety: realistic anxiety (response to external danger), neurotic anxiety (fear of id impulses), and moral anxiety (fear of superego punishment).

### 主要防御机制 / Major Defense Mechanisms

弗洛伊德及其后继者识别了多种防御机制，按其成熟程度可分为不同层级：

Freud and his successors identified numerous defense mechanisms, classifiable into different levels according to their maturity:

**原始防御 / Primitive Defenses**：

1. **否认 / Denial (Verleugnung)**：拒绝承认痛苦的外部现实。例如，丧亲者坚持认为逝者"只是出远门了" / Refusing to acknowledge painful external reality. For example, a bereaved person insists the deceased "just went away on a trip"
2. **分裂 / Splitting (Spaltung)**：将人和事物分为"全好"或"全坏"，无法整合矛盾的体验。常见于边缘人格组织 / Categorizing people and things as "all good" or "all bad," unable to integrate contradictory experiences. Common in borderline personality organization
3. **投射 / Projection (Projektion)**：将自身不可接受的冲动或品质归因于他人。例如，一个有敌意冲动的人认为"别人都想伤害我" / Attributing one's own unacceptable impulses or qualities to others. For example, a person with hostile impulses believes "everyone wants to hurt me"

**神经症防御 / Neurotic Defenses**：

4. **压抑 / Repression (Verdrängung)**：将不可接受的冲动或记忆推入潜意识（所有防御机制的基础）/ Pushing unacceptable impulses or memories into the unconscious (the foundation of all defense mechanisms)
5. **反向形成 / Reaction Formation (Reaktionsbildung)**：表现出与被压抑冲动截然相反的态度或行为。例如，被压抑的敌意表现为过度友好 / Displaying attitudes or behaviors diametrically opposed to repressed impulses. For example, repressed hostility manifests as excessive friendliness
6. **移置 / Displacement (Verschiebung)**：将情感从原始对象转移到更安全的替代对象。例如，对老板的愤怒在家中对孩子发泄 / Transferring emotions from the original object to a safer substitute. For example, anger at one's boss is displaced onto one's child at home
7. **合理化 / Rationalization (Rationalisierung)**：为不可接受的行为或情感编造看似合理的理由。例如，"我不是因为嫉妒才批评他，而是因为他确实做得不好" / Fabricating plausible-sounding reasons for unacceptable behavior or feelings
8. **退行 / Regression (Regression)**：在面对压力时退回更早期的心理发展阶段。例如，成年人在压力下表现出孩子般的依赖行为 / Retreating to an earlier stage of psychological development under stress. For example, an adult displaying childlike dependency under pressure

**成熟防御 / Mature Defenses**：

9. **升华 / Sublimation (Sublimierung)**：将不可接受的本能冲动转化为社会可接受的活动。例如，攻击驱力转化为竞技运动，性欲升华为艺术创作 / Transforming unacceptable instinctual impulses into socially acceptable activities. For example, aggressive drives channeled into competitive sports, sexual desires sublimated into artistic creation
10. **幽默 / Humor (Humor)**：以幽默的方式面对困境，在承认痛苦的同时保持心理距离 / Confronting difficulties with humor, maintaining psychological distance while acknowledging pain
11. **利他主义 / Altruism**：通过帮助他人来满足自身的内在需求 / Meeting one's own inner needs through helping others

### 防御机制的层级模型 / Hierarchical Model of Defense Mechanisms

George Vaillant在1977年提出了防御机制的四层级模型，将防御机制按成熟程度从低到高排列：病理性防御（如否认、妄想性投射）→ 不成熟防御（如投射、被动攻击、行动化）→ 神经症防御（如压抑、反向形成、移置）→ 成熟防御（如升华、幽默、利他主义、预期）。研究表明，使用更成熟防御机制的个体在心理健康、人际关系和职业成就方面表现更好。

George Vaillant proposed a four-level hierarchical model of defense mechanisms in 1977, arranging them by maturity from lowest to highest: pathological defenses (e.g., denial, delusional projection) → immature defenses (e.g., projection, passive aggression, acting out) → neurotic defenses (e.g., repression, reaction formation, displacement) → mature defenses (e.g., sublimation, humor, altruism, anticipation). Research shows that individuals using more mature defense mechanisms perform better in mental health, interpersonal relationships, and professional achievement.

## 发展脉络 / Development

### 弗洛伊德的早期论述 / Freud's Early Discussion

弗洛伊德在1894年的《防御性神经精神病》中首次使用了"防御"概念。在地形学模型时期，防御主要等同于压抑——将不可接受的表象驱逐出意识。1920年代结构模型的引入使得防御机制的概念大幅扩展——防御不再仅仅是"压抑"，而是自我的多种功能之一。

Freud first used the concept of "defense" in his 1894 paper "The Neuro-Psychoses of Defence." During the topographic model period, defense was primarily equated with repression — expelling unacceptable representations from consciousness. The introduction of the structural model in the 1920s greatly expanded the concept — defense was no longer merely "repression" but one of the ego's multiple functions.

### 安娜·弗洛伊德与自我心理学 / Anna Freud and Ego Psychology

安娜·弗洛伊德在《自我与防御机制》（1936）中系统化了防御机制的分类，识别了十种主要防御机制，并特别强调了"对内的认同"（identification with the aggressor）这一机制——儿童通过认同施虐者来应对恐惧。这本书奠定了自我心理学的基础，使防御机制成为独立的研究领域。

Anna Freud systematized the classification of defense mechanisms in *The Ego and the Mechanisms of Defence* (1936), identifying ten major defense mechanisms and particularly emphasizing "identification with the aggressor" — children cope with fear by identifying with the abuser. This book laid the foundation for ego psychology, establishing defense mechanisms as an independent field of study.

### 当代发展 / Contemporary Developments

1970年代以来，防御机制研究经历了重要的实证转向。Vaillant的纵向研究证明了防御机制的层级性和发展性；Christopher Perry开发了防御机制评定量表（DMRS）；当代关系精神分析则强调防御不是孤立的个体内过程，而是在人际场域中动态运作的。

Since the 1970s, defense mechanism research has undergone an important empirical turn. Vaillant's longitudinal studies demonstrated the hierarchical and developmental nature of defense mechanisms; Christopher Perry developed the Defense Mechanism Rating Scales (DMRS); contemporary relational psychoanalysis emphasizes that defenses are not isolated intrapsychic processes but operate dynamically within interpersonal fields.

## 临床应用 / Clinical Applications

### 防御机制评估 / Defense Mechanism Assessment

在临床评估中，识别患者主导的防御机制风格对诊断和治疗规划至关重要：

In clinical assessment, identifying the patient's predominant defense mechanism style is crucial for diagnosis and treatment planning:

- **主导防御与人格障碍**：边缘人格组织以分裂和投射性认同为主导；自恋人格以理想化和贬低为主导；强迫人格以隔离和合理化为主导 / Predominant defenses and personality disorders: borderline personality organization is characterized by splitting and projective identification; narcissistic personality by idealization and devaluation; obsessive personality by isolation and rationalization
- **防御成熟度与预后**：使用成熟防御（升华、幽默）的患者预后更好 / Defense maturity and prognosis: patients using mature defenses (sublimation, humor) have better prognoses
- **防御风格的变化作为治疗进展指标**：随着治疗推进，患者应从原始防御逐渐过渡到更成熟的防御 / Defense style change as indicator of treatment progress: as treatment progresses, patients should gradually transition from primitive to more mature defenses

### 治疗中的防御分析 / Defense Analysis in Treatment

精神分析治疗采用"先分析防御，再分析被防御的内容"的策略（ego-psychological approach）。治疗师首先帮助患者识别其惯用的防御模式——例如，指出"每当话题接近你的愤怒时，你就会开玩笑"——然后逐步探索防御背后的冲突。直接"拆除"防御是危险的，因为防御虽然限制了心理自由，但也在保护患者免于被淹没。

Psychoanalytic treatment adopts the strategy of "analyzing defenses before analyzing what is being defended against" (ego-psychological approach). The therapist first helps the patient identify habitual defense patterns — for example, noting "every time the topic approaches your anger, you start joking" — then gradually explores the conflicts behind the defenses. Directly "dismantling" defenses is dangerous because, although defenses limit psychological freedom, they also protect the patient from being overwhelmed.

## 与其他概念的关联 / Related Concepts

- **压抑 / Repression**：压抑是所有防御机制的原型和基础 / Repression is the prototype and foundation of all defense mechanisms
- **潜意识 / The Unconscious**：防御机制的运作本身是无意识的——自我并不知道自己在进行防御 / The operation of defense mechanisms is itself unconscious — the ego does not know it is defending
- **焦虑 / Anxiety**：焦虑是触发防御机制的信号 / Anxiety is the signal that triggers defense mechanisms
- **人格结构 / Personality Structure**：不同的防御机制风格与不同的人格组织水平相关 / Different defense mechanism styles are associated with different levels of personality organization
- **梦的工作 / Dream-Work**：梦的凝缩和移置机制与防御机制中的移置和象征化有密切关系 / The condensation and displacement mechanisms of dream-work are closely related to displacement and symbolization in defense mechanisms

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

### 与佛教"烦恼"概念的对话 / Dialogue with the Buddhist Concept of Kleśa (Afflictions)

佛教心理学中的"烦恼"（kleśa）——贪、嗔、痴、慢、疑、恶见——描述了一系列扭曲认知的心理模式，与弗洛伊德的防御机制概念有着深层对话空间。两者都认识到人类心理具有自动化的自我保护/自我欺骗倾向。佛教将烦恼视为需要通过正念和智慧来认知和转化的障碍，类似于精神分析将防御视为需要通过洞察来识别和修通的僵化模式。但佛教的目标是彻底根除烦恼（断惑证真），而精神分析的目标是将僵化的防御转化为更灵活、更成熟的应对方式。

The Buddhist psychological concept of "kleśa" (afflictions) — greed, hatred, delusion, pride, doubt, and wrong views — describes a series of cognitive-distorting psychological patterns that enter deep dialogue with Freud's defense mechanism concept. Both recognize that human psychology has automatic self-protective/self-deceptive tendencies. Buddhism views kleśas as obstacles to be recognized and transformed through mindfulness and wisdom, similar to psychoanalysis viewing defenses as rigid patterns to be identified and worked through via insight. But Buddhism's goal is to completely eradicate kleśas (cutting through delusion to realize truth), while psychoanalysis aims to transform rigid defenses into more flexible, mature coping strategies.

### 与禅宗"机锋"的对话 / Dialogue with Zen Buddhism's "Encounter Dialogue"

禅宗的"机锋"——禅师用出其不意的方式（如棒喝、反问、沉默）打破弟子的概念化思维——可以被理解为一种"反防御"技术。概念化思维和理性化解释正是弗洛伊德所描述的"合理化"防御，而禅宗机锋的目的恰恰是在弟子最不经意的时刻瓦解这些防御，使其直接体验"本来面目"（真实自性）。这与精神分析治疗师通过诠释来松动患者防御的功能有异曲同工之妙。

Zen Buddhism's "encounter dialogue" (jifeng / 机锋) — the master using unexpected methods (strikes, shouts, counter-questions, silence) to break the disciple's conceptual thinking — can be understood as an "anti-defense" technique. Conceptual thinking and rationalizing explanation are precisely the "rationalization" defense Freud described, and the purpose of Zen encounter dialogue is precisely to dissolve these defenses at the disciple's most unguarded moment, enabling direct experience of the "original face" (true self-nature). This resonates with how psychoanalytic therapists use interpretation to loosen patients' defenses.
