# 后习俗道德 Post-Conventional Morality

## 定义 / Definition

**后习俗道德**（Post-Conventional Morality），又称"原则水平"（Principled Level），是柯尔伯格道德发展阶段理论中的第三个也是最高的发展水平，包含第五阶段（社会契约定向）和第六阶段（普遍伦理原则定向）。在这一水平，个体的道德判断不再基于对既有社会规范和法律的遵从，而是基于自主选择的、可普遍化的道德原则。社会规范和法律只有在服务于这些更高层次的原则时才具有道德正当性。后习俗道德代表了道德认知发展的"成熟"状态——个体成为能够进行自主道德推理的道德主体。

**Post-Conventional Morality**, also called the "Principled Level," is the third and highest developmental level in Kohlberg's moral development stage theory, encompassing Stage 5 (social contract orientation) and Stage 6 (universal ethical principle orientation). At this level, individuals' moral judgments are no longer based on conformity to existing social norms and laws but on autonomously chosen, universalizable moral principles. Social norms and laws have moral legitimacy only insofar as they serve these higher-level principles. Post-conventional morality represents the "mature" state of moral cognitive development — the individual becomes a moral agent capable of autonomous moral reasoning.

## 核心机制 / Core Mechanisms

### 1. 超越社会约定的道德 / Morality Beyond Social Convention

后习俗道德的根本特征是将"道德"（morality）与"社会约定"（social convention）区分开来。习俗水平的个体将两者等同——"合法的"就是"道德的"，"社会接受的"就是"对的"。后习俗水平的个体则认识到：(1) 社会规范是人类协商的产物，可以被质疑和修改；(2) 某些道德原则超越任何特定的社会规范；(3) 当法律与正义原则冲突时，道德义务高于法律义务。

The fundamental characteristic of post-conventional morality is distinguishing "morality" from "social convention." Conventional-level individuals equate the two — "legal" means "moral," "socially accepted" means "right." Post-conventional individuals recognize that: (1) social norms are products of human negotiation and can be questioned and modified; (2) certain moral principles transcend any particular social norm; (3) when laws conflict with justice principles, moral obligations override legal obligations.

### 2. 第五阶段：社会契约定向 / Stage 5: Social Contract Orientation

第五阶段的个体将社会视为自由个体之间基于互利原则的契约性联合。法律和社会制度的价值在于保护个体的基本权利（如生命、自由、财产）和促进公共福祉。关键特征包括：

Stage 5 individuals view society as a contractual association among free individuals based on mutual benefit principles. The value of laws and social institutions lies in protecting individuals' fundamental rights (such as life, liberty, property) and promoting public welfare. Key characteristics include:

- **功利主义倾向**：法律应该"为最多数人谋求最大幸福" / Utilitarian tendency: laws should "seek the greatest happiness for the greatest number"
- **权利意识**：某些基本权利（生命权、自由权）不可被多数票剥夺 / Rights consciousness: certain fundamental rights (right to life, liberty) cannot be overridden by majority vote
- **法律的可修改性**：不公正的法律应该通过民主程序加以修改 / Modifiability of law: unjust laws should be amended through democratic procedures
- **价值多元主义**：认识到不同群体可能持有不同的价值观，但基本权利具有普遍性 / Value pluralism: recognizing different groups may hold different values, but fundamental rights have universality

在海因茨两难中，阶段5的推理可能是："虽然偷窃通常违法，但生命权高于财产权。现有法律没有考虑到这种极端情况，因此法律应该被修改以保护生命权。海因茨有道德义务保护妻子的生命，即使这意味着违反现行法律。"

In the Heinz dilemma, Stage 5 reasoning might be: "Although theft is generally illegal, the right to life outweighs property rights. Existing law hasn't considered this extreme situation, so the law should be amended to protect the right to life. Heinz has a moral obligation to protect his wife's life, even if this means violating current law."

### 3. 第六阶段：普遍伦理原则定向 / Stage 6: Universal Ethical Principle Orientation

第六阶段（柯尔伯格后期将其从评分系统中移除，保留为理论理想）的个体基于自我选择的、逻辑上一致的、可普遍化的伦理原则进行道德推理。这些原则不是具体的道德规则，而是抽象的伦理原则——类似于康德的"绝对命令"（categorical imperative）和罗尔斯的"正义即公平"（justice as fairness）。关键特征包括：

Stage 6 individuals (which Kohlberg later removed from the scoring system, retaining as a theoretical ideal) engage in moral reasoning based on self-chosen, logically consistent, universalizable ethical principles. These principles are not specific moral rules but abstract ethical principles — similar to Kant's "categorical imperative" and Rawls's "justice as fairness." Key characteristics include:

- **可普遍化**：道德原则必须适用于所有理性存在者 / Universalizability: moral principles must apply to all rational beings
- **可逆性**（reversibility）：道德判断必须在所有当事人的立场上都能被接受 / Reversibility: moral judgments must be acceptable from all parties' positions
- **人的尊严**：每个人具有不可侵犯的内在价值，不能被视为手段 / Human dignity: each person possesses inviolable intrinsic value and cannot be treated as a means
- **正义优先**：公正原则优先于效用考量 / Justice priority: justice principles take precedence over utility considerations

在海因茨两难中，阶段6的推理可能是："人的尊严和生命价值是不可还原的道德基础。无论法律如何规定，作为一个理性的道德主体，我必须按照所有人都应该遵循的原则行动。保护人的尊严和生命——即使面对不公正的法律——是每个道德主体的义务。"

In the Heinz dilemma, Stage 6 reasoning might be: "Human dignity and the value of life are irreducible moral foundations. Regardless of what the law prescribes, as a rational moral agent, I must act according to principles everyone should follow. Protecting human dignity and life — even in the face of unjust laws — is every moral agent's obligation."

### 4. 后习俗道德的稀少性 / The Rarity of Post-Conventional Morality

大量纵向研究表明，后习俗道德在经验上相当稀少。Rest的DIT测验研究估计：

Extensive longitudinal research indicates that post-conventional morality is empirically quite rare. Rest's DIT research estimates:

- 大多数成年人（约70%）停留在习俗水平（阶段3-4）/ Most adults (approximately 70%) remain at the conventional level (Stages 3–4)
- 达到阶段5的人口比例约为15-20%，主要集中于受过高等教育的人群 / The proportion reaching Stage 5 is approximately 15–20%, concentrated mainly among highly educated populations
- 阶段6在经验研究中极为罕见，以至于柯尔伯格在后期将其从评分系统中移除 / Stage 6 is so rare in empirical research that Kohlberg removed it from the scoring system in later years
- 后习俗推理在哲学、法学、政治学等专业领域的高学历人群中比例最高 / Post-conventional reasoning is most prevalent among highly educated individuals in professional fields such as philosophy, law, and political science

## 发展脉络 / Historical Development

### 理论演变 / Theoretical Evolution

柯尔伯格对后习俗水平的理解经历了重要演变：

Kohlberg's understanding of the post-conventional level underwent significant evolution:

- **1958年（博士论文）**：第六阶段被定义为"以良心为导向"的道德，强调个体良知超越社会规范 / Defined as "conscience-oriented" morality, emphasizing individual conscience transcending social norms
- **1969年（"Stage and Sequence"）**：第六阶段被重新定义为"基于可普遍化的公正原则"的道德推理 / Redefined as moral reasoning "based on universalizable justice principles"
- **1971年（"From Is to Ought"）**：将罗尔斯的正义论整合为第六阶段的哲学基础 / Integrated Rawls's theory of justice as the philosophical foundation for Stage 6
- **1983年（修订评分手册）**：由于第六阶段在经验上难以可靠评定，从正式评分系统中移除 / Removed from formal scoring system due to empirical difficulty in reliable Stage 6 assessment

### 新柯尔伯格学派的发展 / Neo-Kohlbergian Developments

柯尔伯格去世后，Rest等人发展的"新柯尔伯格学派"（Neo-Kohlbergian approach）对后习俗概念进行了重要修订：

After Kohlberg's death, the "Neo-Kohlbergian approach" developed by Rest and others made important revisions to the post-conventional concept:

- **DIT测验**：不评估第六阶段，聚焦于"后习俗思维"的量化测量（P分数）/ Does not assess Stage 6, focuses on quantitative measurement of "post-conventional thinking" (P-score)
- **四成分模型**：将后习俗道德视为道德行为的一个成分（道德判断），而非全部 / Views post-conventional morality as one component (moral judgment) of moral behavior, not the whole
- **社会直觉主义批评**：海特（Haidt）等人质疑后习俗道德是否真正反映了更"好"的道德思维，还是仅仅反映了西方自由主义知识分子的价值偏好 / Haidt and others question whether post-conventional morality truly reflects "better" moral thinking, or merely reflects Western liberal intellectual value preferences

## 临床应用 / Clinical Applications

### 高等教育中的道德发展 / Moral Development in Higher Education

研究表明，大学教育——特别是哲学、法学和医学等专业的研究生教育——能有效促进学生的后习俗道德推理发展。这种发展主要通过以下方式实现：

Research shows that university education — especially graduate education in philosophy, law, and medicine — effectively promotes students' post-conventional moral reasoning development. This development occurs primarily through:

- **接触多元价值观**：大学环境暴露学生于不同的文化和道德视角 / Exposure to diverse values: university environment exposes students to different cultural and moral perspectives
- **批判性思维训练**：哲学和法学教育培养分析道德论证的能力 / Critical thinking training: philosophy and legal education cultivate the ability to analyze moral arguments
- **专业伦理教育**：医学伦理、商业伦理等专业课程提供结构化的道德反思机会 / Professional ethics education: medical ethics, business ethics, and other professional courses provide structured moral reflection opportunities
- **同伴讨论**：与不同背景的同学讨论道德问题促进认知冲突 / Peer discussion: discussing moral issues with peers from different backgrounds promotes cognitive conflict

### 领导力发展与组织伦理 / Leadership Development and Organizational Ethics

后习俗道德推理被视为高级领导力的关键能力。研究表明，具有后习俗道德推理能力的领导者更能够：(1) 在复杂利益冲突中做出公正的决策；(2) 超越短期利益考量长期后果；(3) 建立基于原则而非个人关系的组织文化。

Post-conventional moral reasoning is viewed as a key capacity for advanced leadership. Research shows that leaders with post-conventional moral reasoning ability are better able to: (1) make just decisions in complex conflicts of interest; (2) transcend short-term interests to consider long-term consequences; (3) build organizational cultures based on principles rather than personal relationships.

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

### 与道家"上德不德"的对话 / Dialogue with Daoism's "Highest Virtue Is Not Virtue"

《道德经》第38章云："上德不德，是以有德；下德不失德，是以无德。"这揭示了道家对道德的不同理解：最高的德性不是对规则的遵守，而是自然的、无为的表达。这与柯尔伯格的后习俗道德有结构性平行——两者都认为最高层次的道德超越了对外部规则的机械遵守。然而，道家认为这种超越是通过"无为"（wu wei）——放弃刻意的道德努力而回归自然——实现的；而柯尔伯格认为这种超越是通过更高层次的理性推理实现的。两者的对话揭示了"道德的最高境界是超越道德"这一共同洞见，但也暴露了理性主义与自然主义在实现路径上的根本分歧。

Chapter 38 of the *Daodejing* states: "Highest virtue is not virtuous, therefore it has virtue; lower virtue does not lose virtue, therefore it has no virtue." This reveals Daoism's different understanding of morality: the highest virtue is not conformity to rules but natural, effortless expression. This structurally parallels Kohlberg's post-conventional morality — both hold that the highest level of morality transcends mechanical conformity to external rules. However, Daoism holds this transcendence is achieved through "wu wei" (non-action) — abandoning deliberate moral effort and returning to nature; while Kohlberg holds it is achieved through higher-level rational reasoning. The dialogue between the two reveals the shared insight that "the highest state of morality transcends morality," but also exposes the fundamental divergence between rationalism and naturalism in the path of realization.

### 与甘地"Satyagraha"的对话 / Dialogue with Gandhi's "Satyagraha"

甘地的"真理坚持"（Satyagraha）——基于道德原则的非暴力不服从——是后习俗道德在政治实践中的典范。甘地认为，当法律与更高的道德真理冲突时，公民有道德义务以非暴力方式违反不公正的法律，同时自愿接受法律制裁。这与柯尔伯格第六阶段"正义优先于法律"的理想完全吻合——马丁·路德·金（Martin Luther King Jr.）在《从伯明翰监狱的信》中也表达了同样的后习俗推理："不公正的法律根本不是法律"（An unjust law is no law at all）。

Gandhi's "Satyagraha" (truth-force) — principled nonviolent civil disobedience — is a paradigm of post-conventional morality in political practice. Gandhi held that when laws conflict with higher moral truth, citizens have a moral obligation to nonviolently disobey unjust laws while voluntarily accepting legal sanctions. This perfectly aligns with Kohlberg's Stage 6 ideal of "justice over law" — Martin Luther King Jr. expressed the same post-conventional reasoning in his "Letter from Birmingham Jail": "An unjust law is no law at all."
