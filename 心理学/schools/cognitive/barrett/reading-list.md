# 巴雷特进阶阅读 · Barrett Reading List

## 一、阅读路径 / Reading Paths

### 入门 (3 本, 6 小时)

1. **Barrett, *Seven and a Half Lessons About the Brain* (2020)** — 150 页, 最简洁的脑科学入门; 理解预测加工的基础
2. **Barrett, *How Emotions Are Made* (2017), Introduction + Ch. 1-5** — 构造论的核心论证; 约 3 小时
3. **"Are emotions natural kinds?" (2006)** — 20 页, 对基本情绪论的系统批判

### 进阶 (7 篇, 15 小时)

4. *How Emotions Are Made* (2017), Chapters 6-12 — 构造论的应用: 法律、医学、AI
5. "The theory of constructed emotion: an active inference account" (2017) — 学术版构造论
6. "The conceptual act theory: A précis" (2014) — 构造论的前身
7. Lindquist et al., "The brain basis of emotion: A meta-analytic review" (2012) — 神经影像元分析
8. "Emotions are real" (2019) — 回应批评, 阐明社会现实立场
9. Hoemann et al., "Emotion words as emotion schemas" (2019) — 情绪词汇的作用
10. "Context reconsidered" (2022) — 情境角色的深化

### 深潜 (全部论文 + 专著 + 跨学科)

11. *Handbook of Emotions* (4th ed., 2016) — 情绪科学全景 (选读相关章节)
12. 早期情绪粒度论文 (2001, 2004)
13. 跨文化研究 (Himba 人, 2012)
14. Friston, "The free-energy principle" (2009) — 预测加工的计算基础
15. Clark, *Surfing Uncertainty* (2016) — 预测加工的哲学框架
16. Seth, *Being You* (2021) — 意识与预测加工

## 二、原典 (必读) / Primary Sources (Must-Read)

## 四、二手研究 / Secondary Sources

### 预测加工 / Predictive Processing

- **Clark, A.** (2016). *Surfing Uncertainty: Prediction, Action, and the Embodied Mind*. Oxford University Press. — 预测加工的最佳哲学阐释
- **Friston, K.** (2009). "The free-energy principle: A rough guide to the brain?" *Trends in Cognitive Sciences*, 13(7), 293-301. — 自由能原理的核心论文
- **Hohwy, J.** (2013). *The Predictive Mind*. Oxford University Press. — 预测加工的哲学分析
- **Seth, A.** (2021). *Being You: A New Science of Consciousness*. Faber & Faber. — 意识的预测理论; 大量引用 Barrett

### 情绪科学 (支持与批评) / Emotion Science (Support and Critique)

- **Russell, J. A.** (2003). "Core affect and prototypical emotional episodes: What they are and what they are not." *Psychological Review*, 110(1), 145-172. — Barrett 的早期合作者, 环状模型
- **Gross, J. J.** (2015). "Emotion regulation: Current status and future prospects." *Psychological Inquiry*, 26(1), 1-26. — 情绪调节研究的先驱
- **LeDoux, J.** (2015). *Anxious: Understanding the Brain to Understand and Treat Fear and Anxiety*. Viking. — LeDoux 的自我修正
- **Adolphs, R.** (2017). "How emotions are not made." *Science* (书评). — 对构造论的批评性评论
- **Celeghin, G., et al.** (2017). "Basic emotions are universal." — 对构造论的直接批评

### 情绪粒度 / Emotional Granularity

- **Kashdan, T. B., Barrett, L. F., & McKnight, P. E.** (2015). "Unpacking emotion differentiation: Transforming unpleasant experience by perceiving distinctions in negativity." *Current Directions in Psychological Science*, 24(1), 10-16.
- **Smidt, K. E., & Suvak, M. K.** (2018). "A brief, but comprehensive, review of emotion differentiation." *Clinical Psychology Review*, 64, 48-59.
- **Erbas, Y., et al.** (2014). "Emotion differentiation and emotion regulation." *Emotion*, 14(5), 885-891.

### 跨文化情绪研究 / Cross-Cultural Emotion Research

- **Gendron, M., Roberson, D., van der Vyver, J. M., & Barrett, L. F.** (2014). "Cultural relativity in perceiving emotion from vocalizations." *Psychological Science*, 25(4), 911-920.
- **Mesquita, B.** (2022). *Between Us: How Cultures Create Emotions*. W. W. Norton. — 文化如何构造情绪

### 内感受与身体 / Interoception and the Body

- **Craig, A. D.** (2002). "How do you feel? Interoception: The sense of the physiological condition of the body." *Nature Reviews Neuroscience*, 3(8), 655-666.
- **Sterling, P.** (2012). "Allostasis: A theory of predictive regulation." *Frontiers in Neuroendocrinology*, 33(1), 47-54.
- **Damasio, A.** (1999). *The Feeling of What Happens*. Harcourt. — 对照阅读: 躯体标记假说

### AI 与情感计算 / AI and Affective Computing

- **Crawford, K.** (2021). *Atlas of AI*. Yale University Press. — 含对面部情感识别 AI 的批判
- **Picard, R.** (1997). *Affective Computing*. MIT Press. — 情感计算的开创著作 (对照阅读)
- **Barrett, L. F., Adolphs, R., Marsella, S., Martinez, A. M., & Pollak, S. D.** (2019). "Emotional expressions reconsidered: Challenges to inferring emotion from human facial movements." *Psychological Science in the Public Interest*, 20(1), 1-68. — 对情感识别 AI 的根本质疑

## 五、巴雷特的思想源头 / Barrett's Intellectual Sources

巴雷特自述其思想受以下人物和理论影响最深:

- **William James** — "情绪是对身体变化的感知" (1884); 身体优先于情绪体验
- **James Russell** — 环状模型 (Circumplex Model); Barrett 的早期合作者
- **Stanley Schachter & Jerome Singer** — 情绪二因素理论 (1962): 生理唤醒 + 认知标注 = 情绪
- **Karl Friston** — 自由能原理与主动推断; 构造论的计算基础
- **Andy Clark** — 预测加工哲学; *Surfing Uncertainty* (2016)
- **Richard Lazarus** — 情绪的评估理论 (appraisal theory)
- **Lev Vygotsky** — 概念发展与社会建构
- **George Lakoff** — 概念隐喻与范畴化理论

## 六、下游 (受她影响) / Downstream (Influenced by Barrett)

### 心理学与神经科学

- **Kristen Lindquist** — Barrett 的核心合作者, 情绪构造论的实证研究
- **Anil Seth** — 意识的预测理论, 在 *Being You* (2021) 中大量引用 Barrett
- **Kevin Ochsner** — 情绪调节的神经科学
- **Katie Hoemann** — 情绪概念与情绪词汇的研究
- **Jennifer MacCormack** — 身体预算与压力研究

### 其他领域

- **情感计算 (Affective Computing)** — Barrett 的批判推动了面部情感识别 AI 的方法论反思
- **正念与冥想 (Mindfulness)** — 构造论为正念的"精细觉察"提供认知科学解释
- **心理治疗** — 情绪粒度成为多种疗法的治疗目标
- **教育** — 情绪素养 (emotional literacy) 课程从"认识基本情绪"转向"拓展情绪词汇"

## 九、阅读顺序建议 / Suggested Reading Order

| 目标 | 推荐顺序 |
|---|---|
| 想快速了解构造论 | *Seven and a Half Lessons* → *How Emotions Are Made* Ch. 1-5 → "Are emotions natural kinds?" |
| 想深入学术理论 | "The theory of constructed emotion" (2017) → "The conceptual act theory" (2014) → Lindquist et al. (2012) |
| 想了解预测加工 | Clark (2016) → Seth (2021) → Friston (2009) → Barrett (2017) |
| 想做临床应用 | 情绪粒度论文 (2001, 2015) → *How Emotions Are Made* Ch. 11-12 → Smidt & Suvak (2018) |

| 想做 AI 批判 | Barrett et al. (2019) "Emotional expressions reconsidered" → Crawford (2021) |
| 想做东西方比较 | Varela et al. (1991) → Abhidharma 心所分析 → 庄子 "无情" |
