# 塔特进阶阅读 · Charles Tart Reading List

## 原典 / Primary Works

### 核心著作（必读） / Core Works (Essential)

| 著作 / Title | 年份 / Year | 说明 / Notes |
|---|---|---|
| *Altered States of Consciousness* (ed.) | 1969/1990 | ASC 研究的奠基文集，首次系统整理多学科研究。第三版（1990）有重要增补。 / Foundational anthology of ASC research, first systematic multidisciplinary organization. Third edition (1990) has important additions. |
| *States of Consciousness* | 1975 | 意识状态理论的系统巨著——d-SoC 框架、SSS 方法论、状态转换机制的完整阐述。 / Systematic magnum opus of consciousness states theory — complete exposition of d-SoC framework, SSS methodology, and state transition mechanisms. |
| *Waking Up: Overcoming the Obstacles to Human Potential* | 1986 | 整合东方冥想与西方心理学的实践指南。"共识恍惚"概念在此得到最充分的展开。 / Practical guide integrating Eastern meditation with Western psychology. "Consensus trance" concept most fully developed here. |

### 重要著作（推荐） / Important Works (Recommended)

| 著作 / Title | 年份 / Year | 说明 / Notes |
|---|---|---|
| *On Being Stoned* | 1972 | 大麻意识状态的精细心理学分析，展示了塔特的实验方法论。 / Fine-grained psychological analysis of marijuana consciousness states, showcasing Tart's experimental methodology. |
| *Open Mind, Discriminating Mind* | 1989 | 对意识研究方法论的反思性文集，包含对批评者的回应。 / Reflective essay collection on consciousness research methodology, including responses to critics. |
| *Living the Mindful Life* | 1994 | 正念日常实践指南，比 *Waking Up* 更具操作性。 / Everyday mindfulness practice guide, more operational than *Waking Up*. |
| *The End of Materialism* | 2009 | 最具争议性——主张 psi 证据已足以挑战唯物主义范式。阅读时需保持批判性思维。 / Most controversial — argues psi evidence is sufficient to challenge materialist paradigm. Read with critical thinking. |

## 建议阅读路径 / Suggested Reading Path

### 入门路径 / Beginner Path

1. *Waking Up* (1986) → 理解"共识恍惚"和觉醒路径 / Understand "consensus trance" and paths to awakening
2. *Living the Mindful Life* (1994) → 正念实践入门 / Introduction to mindfulness practice
3. 本维基 [README.md](README.md) → 获取整体框架 / Get overall framework

### 学术路径 / Academic Path

1. *Altered States of Consciousness* (1969/1990) → ASC 研究基础 / ASC research foundation
2. *States of Consciousness* (1975) → 理论框架深入 / Deep dive into theoretical framework
3. "The State-Specific Study of Consciousness" (1979, *American Psychologist*) → SSS 方法论 / SSS methodology
4. Wilber, Engler & Brown, *Transformations of Consciousness* (1986) → 超个人发展的理论 / Theory of transpersonal development

### 批判性路径 / Critical Path

1. *The End of Materialism* (2009) → 塔特最具争议性的主张 / Tart's most controversial claims
2. Friedman & Hartelius, *Wiley-Blackwell Handbook of Transpersonal Psychology* (2013) → 对超个人心理学的批判性审视 / Critical examination of transpersonal psychology
3. 对照阅读 Goleman & Davidson, *Altered Traits* (2017) → 冥想研究的当代神经科学视角 / Contemporary neuroscience perspective on meditation research
