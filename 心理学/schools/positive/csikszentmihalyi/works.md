# 米哈里著作年表 · Csikszentmihalyi Works

## 专著 / Monographs

| 年份 / Year | 书名 / Title | 出版社 / Publisher | 说明 / Notes |
|:---:|---|---|---|
| 1966 | *The Artist and His Audience*（与 Getzels 合著） | University of Chicago Press | 艺术创造力的早期研究 / Early research on artistic creativity |
| 1975 | *Beyond Boredom and Anxiety: The Experience of Play in Work and Games* | Jossey-Bass | 首次系统提出心流概念 / First systematic presentation of flow concept |
| 1988 | *Optimal Experience: Psychological Studies of Flow in Consciousness*（与 I.S. Csikszentmihalyi 编） | Cambridge University Press | 最优体验的跨文化研究文集 / Cross-cultural research collection on optimal experience |
| 1990 | *Flow: The Psychology of Optimal Experience* | Harper & Row | **核心代表作**，译三十余种语言 / **Core magnum opus**, translated into 30+ languages |
| 1993 | *The Evolving Self: A Psychology for the Third Millennium*（与 J.L. Singer 合著） | Kluwer Academic | 进化心理学视角下的自我发展 / Self-development from evolutionary psychology perspective |
| 1996 | *Creativity: Flow and the Psychology of Discovery and Invention* | HarperCollins | 基于91位杰出人物的创造力系统模型 / Systems model of creativity based on 91 eminent individuals |
| 1996 | *Social Evolution: A Paradigm for the Third Millennium*（编著） | 未广泛发行 | 社会演化与道德发展 / Social evolution and moral development |
| 1997 | *Finding Flow: The Psychology of Engagement with Everyday Life* | Basic Books | 大众入门，日常生活中的心流 / Popular introduction, flow in everyday life |
| 1998 | *Being Human*（编著） | 未广泛发行 | 人本主义与积极心理学交汇 / Intersection of humanistic and positive psychology |
| 2003 | *Good Business: Leadership, Flow, and the Making of Meaning* | Viking | 心流在组织管理中的应用 / Application of flow in organizational management |
| 2003 | *Fostering Enterprise: The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Nexus*（编著） | Edward Elgar | 创新与创业 / Innovation and entrepreneurship |
| 2006 | *A Life Worth Living: Contributions to Positive Psychology*（编著） | Oxford University Press | 积极心理学论文集 / Positive psychology essay collection |
| 2014 | *The Systems Model of Creativity: The Collected Works of Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi* | Springer | 创造力系统模型完整论文集 / Complete collected works on creativity systems model |
| 2014 | *Applications of Flow in Human Development and Education* | Springer | 心流在教育中的应用 / Applications of flow in education |
| 2014 | *Society and the Adolescent Self-Image*（重印） | Springer | 青少年自我形象研究 / Adolescent self-image research |
