# 关键著作年表 · Key Works Chronology

## 主要著作 / Major Books

| 年份 / Year | 著作 / Work | 出版社 / Publisher | 核心内容 / Core Content |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1975 | *Helplessness: On Depression, Development, and Death* | W.H. Freeman | 习得性无助理论的系统建构——从动物实验到人类抑郁 / Systematic construction of learned helplessness theory |
| 1990 | *Learned Optimism: How to Change Your Mind and Your Life* | Knopf | 解释风格理论与习得性乐观的ABCDE训练 / Explanatory style theory and ABCDE training |
| 1991 | *The Optimistic Child: A Proven Program to Safeguard Children Against Depression* | Houghton Mifflin | 儿童乐观教育——预防青少年抑郁的实践方案 / Children's optimism education |
| 1995 | *What You Can Change and What You Can't: The New Science of Successful Change* | Knopf | 心理改变的边界——什么可以改变，什么必须接受 / Boundaries of psychological change |
| 2002 | *Authentic Happiness: Using the New Positive Psychology to Realize Your Potential for Lasting Fulfillment* | Free Press | 积极心理学的全面纲领——幸福三维度模型 / Comprehensive positive psychology—three-dimensional happiness |
| 2004 | *Character Strengths and Virtues: A Handbook and Classification*（与C. Peterson合著） | Oxford University Press | 24种性格优势与六大美德的跨文化分类 / 24 character strengths and six virtues cross-cultural classification |
| 2011 | *Flourish: A Visionary New Understanding of Happiness and Well-being* | Free Press | PERMA模型——从幸福到蓬勃发展的范式转换 / PERMA model—paradigm shift from happiness to flourishing |
| 2018 | *The Hope Circuit: A Psychologist's Journey from Helplessness to Optimism* | PublicAffairs | 自传——从无助到希望的智识旅程 / Autobiography—from helplessness to hope |

| 1988 | "Explanatory Style as a Risk Factor for Depressive Symptoms"（与G.I. Metalsky等合著） | *Journal of Abnormal Psychology* | 解释风格作为抑郁风险因素 / Explanatory style as depression risk factor |
| 2000 | "Positive Psychology: An Introduction"（与M. Csikszentmihalyi合著） | *American Psychologist* 55(1), 5–14 | 积极心理学运动宣言 / Positive psychology movement manifesto |
| 2005 | "Positive Psychology Progress: Empirical Validation of Interventions"（与T. Steen, N. Park, C. Peterson合著） | *American Psychologist* 60(5), 410–421 | 积极心理干预的实证验证 / Empirical validation of positive psychology interventions |
| 2009 | "Positive Education: Positive Psychology and Classroom Interventions"（与R.M.L. Ernst等合著） | *Oxford Review of Education* 35(3), 293–311 | 积极教育的理论与实证 / Positive education theory and evidence |
| 2011 | "Comprehensive Soldier Fitness: A Vision for the Army"（与P.B. Lester, E.C. McBride等合著） | *American Psychologist* 66(1), 14–21 | 军队韧性训练项目 / Military resilience training program |
| 2016 | "Learned Helplessness at 50: Insights from Neuroscience"（与S.F. Maier合著） | *Psychological Review* 123(3), 251–272 | 基于神经科学的理论修正 / Theory revision based on neuroscience |
