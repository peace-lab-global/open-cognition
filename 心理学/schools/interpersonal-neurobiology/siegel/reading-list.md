# 西格尔推荐阅读 · Daniel J. Siegel Reading List

## 一、原典精读 / Primary Works (Essential Reading)

### 必读 / Must-Read

1. **Siegel, D.J.** *The Developing Mind: How Relationships and the Brain Interact to Shape Who We Are*. 3rd ed. New York: Guilford Press, 2020.
   - IPNB 的奠基之作，系统阐述了心智的整合模型。第三版纳入了多迷走神经理论和表观遗传学的最新成果。适合专业读者深入学习。
   - The foundational work of IPNB, systematically articulating the integrative model of mind. The 3rd edition incorporates polyvagal theory and epigenetics. Essential for professional readers.

2. **Siegel, D.J.** *Mindsight: The New Science of Personal Transformation*. New York: Bantam Books, 2010.
   - "心智之眼"概念的通俗阐述，通过丰富的临床案例展示觉察如何改变大脑。适合一般读者和临床工作者。
   - Popular exposition of the "mindsight" concept, demonstrating through rich clinical cases how awareness changes the brain. Suitable for general readers and clinicians.

3. **Siegel, D.J.** *The Pocket Guide to Interpersonal Neurobiology*. New York: W.W. Norton, 2012.
   - IPNB 的系统性专业入门。以精炼的方式汇集了20余年的研究成果，涵盖9个学科视角。适合作为专业参考手册。
   - Systematic professional introduction to IPNB. Concisely synthesizes 20+ years of research across 9 disciplinary perspectives. Ideal as a professional reference.

4. **Siegel, D.J.** *Aware: The Science and Practice of Presence*. New York: TarcherPerigee, 2018.
   - 正念觉知的科学基础与"觉知之轮"练习的系统阐述。兼具理论深度和实践可操作性。
   - Systematic articulation of the scientific foundations of mindful awareness and the "Wheel of Awareness" practice. Combines theoretical depth with practical applicability.

### 推荐阅读 / Recommended

5. **Siegel, D.J. & Hartzell, M.** *Parenting from the Inside Out*. New York: TarcherPerigee, 2003.
   - 将依恋研究转化为父母自我反思指南，帮助父母理解自身的依恋历史如何影响亲子关系。
   - Translates attachment research into a parental self-reflection guide, helping parents understand how their own attachment history affects parenting.

6. **Siegel, D.J. & Bryson, T.P.** *The Whole-Brain Child*. New York: Bantam Books, 2011.
   - 基于脑科学的12条育儿策略，全球畅销书。以通俗生动的方式解释了大脑发育与情绪调节的关系。
   - 12 brain-based parenting strategies, international bestseller. Explains brain development and emotion regulation in accessible, engaging ways.

7. **Siegel, D.J. & Bryson, T.P.** *No-Drama Discipline*. New York: Bantam Books, 2014.
   - 基于脑科学的纪律教育方法，将IPNB理念应用于日常育儿情境。
   - Brain-based disciplinary methods, applying IPNB concepts to everyday parenting situations.

8. **Siegel, D.J.** *Brainstorm: The Power and Purpose of the Teenage Brain*. New York: TarcherPerigee, 2013.
   - 重新定义青少年大脑发育——从"问题期"到"机遇期"的范式转换。
   - Reframes adolescent brain development — a paradigm shift from "problem period" to "opportunity period."

9. **Siegel, D.J. & Bryson, T.P.** *The Yes Brain*. New York: Ballantine Books, 2020.
   - 培养儿童心理弹性与整合能力的实践方法。
   - Practical methods for cultivating children's resilience and integrative capacity.

10. **Siegel, D.J.** *IntraConnected: MWe (Me + We) as the Foundation of Well-Being*. New York: TarcherPerigee, 2023.
    - MWe 概念的全面阐述，探讨关系性自我如何成为幸福的基础。
    - Comprehensive exposition of the MWe concept, exploring how the relational self serves as the foundation of well-being.

## 二、二手研究 / Secondary Research

### IPNB 核心学者著作 / Core IPNB Scholars

1. **Cozolino, L.** *The Neuroscience of Psychotherapy: Healing the Social Brain*. 3rd ed. New York: W.W. Norton, 2017.
   - 从神经科学角度阐述心理治疗的变化机制，与IPNB框架高度互补。
   - Articulates psychotherapeutic change mechanisms from a neuroscience perspective, highly complementary to the IPNB framework.

2. **Schore, A.N.** *The Science of the Art of Psychotherapy*. New York: W.W. Norton, 2012.
   - 右脑发育与情绪调节的神经科学研究，是IPNB的重要理论支柱。
   - Neuroscience research on right-brain development and emotion regulation, a major theoretical pillar of IPNB.

3. **Porges, S.W.** *The Polyvagal Theory: Neurophysiological Foundations of Emotions, Attachment, Communication, and Self-Regulation*. New York: W.W. Norton, 2011.
   - 多迷走神经理论为耐受之窗和自主神经调节提供了更精细的神经生理学解释。
   - Polyvagal theory provides more refined neurophysiological explanations for the window of tolerance and autonomic regulation.

4. **Levine, P.A.** *Waking the Tiger: Healing Trauma*. Berkeley: North Atlantic Books, 1997.
   - 身体体验疗法（Somatic Experiencing）的奠基之作，与IPNB的耐受之窗概念深度互补。
   - Foundational work of Somatic Experiencing, deeply complementary to IPNB's window of tolerance concept.

5. **Ogden, P., Minton, C. & Pain, C.** *Trauma and the Body: A Sensorimotor Approach to Psychotherapy*. New York: W.W. Norton, 2006.
   - 感觉运动心理治疗的方法论，整合了身体觉察与创伤治疗。
   - Sensorimotor Psychotherapy methodology, integrating body awareness with trauma treatment.

### 依恋理论背景 / Attachment Theory Background

6. **Bowlby, J.** *Attachment and Loss* (3 vols.). London: Hogarth Press, 1969–1980.
   - 依恋理论的原始文献，是理解IPNB关系性心智观的理论基础。
   - Original texts of attachment theory, the theoretical foundation for understanding IPNB's relational mind concept.

7. **Ainsworth, M.D.S. et al.** *Patterns of Attachment: A Psychological Study of the Strange Situation*. Hillsdale, NJ: Lawrence Erlbaum, 1978.
   - 依恋类型的经典分类研究，是理解IPNB依恋-脑发育关联的实证基础。
   - Classic classification research on attachment patterns, the empirical foundation for understanding IPNB's attachment-brain development connection.

8. **Main, M.** 相关研究（成人依恋访谈 AAI）/ Related research (Adult Attachment Interview, AAI)
   - 成人依恋研究是理解"获得性安全依恋"和"内在反思"概念的关键。
   - Adult attachment research is key to understanding "earned secure attachment" and "inside-out reflection."

### 正念与冥想 / Mindfulness and Meditation

9. **Kabat-Zinn, J.** *Full Catastrophe Living: Using the Wisdom of Your Body and Mind to Face Stress, Pain, and Illness*. Rev. ed. New York: Bantam Books, 2013.
   - 正念减压（MBSR）的奠基之作，为IPNB的觉知训练提供了循证基础。
   - Foundational work of MBSR, providing evidence-based foundations for IPNB's awareness training.

10. **Davidson, R.J. & Goleman, D.** *Altered Traits: Science Reveals How Meditation Changes Your Mind, Brain, and Body*. New York: Avery, 2017.
    - 冥想改变大脑的科学研究综述，支持IPNB关于"注意力改变大脑"的核心命题。
    - Scientific review of meditation's brain-changing effects, supporting IPNB's core proposition that "attention changes the brain."

### 创伤心理学 / Trauma Psychology

11. **Van der Kolk, B.** *The Body Keeps the Score: Brain, Mind, and Body in the Healing of Trauma*. New York: Viking, 2014.
    - 创伤的身体记忆与神经科学，与IPNB的耐受之窗概念形成深度互补。
    - The body's memory of trauma and neuroscience, deeply complementary to IPNB's window of tolerance concept.

12. **Herman, J.L.** *Trauma and Recovery*. New York: Basic Books, 1992.
    - 创伤恢复的经典论述，为理解整合与创伤的关系提供了重要背景。
    - Classic exposition of trauma recovery, providing important context for understanding the relationship between integration and trauma.
