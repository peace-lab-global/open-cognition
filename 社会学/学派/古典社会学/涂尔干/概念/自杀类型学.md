# 自杀类型学 · Typology of Suicide (Typologie du Suicide)

## 概念界定 / Concept Definition

《自杀论》（Le Suicide, 1897）是涂尔干最杰出的经验研究，也是社会学方法论的典范之作。涂尔干通过对欧洲各国自杀统计数据的系统分析，证明了看似最个人化的行为——自杀——实际上是一种社会事实，受社会整合程度（integration）和规范调节程度（regulation）两个维度的影响。

Suicide (Le Suicide, 1897) is Durkheim's most outstanding empirical research and a model of sociological methodology. Through systematic analysis of European suicide statistics, Durkheim demonstrated that even the most apparently individual act—suicide—is in fact a social fact, influenced by two dimensions: the degree of social integration and the degree of normative regulation.

## 方法论创新 / Methodological Innovation

涂尔干的论证策略是先排除非社会因素（心理疾病、种族、气候、模仿等），然后证明自杀率的变化只能用社会因素来解释。他使用了当时最先进的统计方法——比较不同国家、不同宗教群体、不同婚姻状态、不同时期的自杀率，揭示了隐藏在个体行为背后的"自杀倾向"（courant suicidogene）——一种集体性的社会力量。

Durkheim's argumentative strategy was first to exclude non-social factors (mental illness, race, climate, imitation, etc.), then to demonstrate that variations in suicide rates can only be explained by social factors. He used the most advanced statistical methods of his time—comparing suicide rates across countries, religious groups, marital statuses, and periods—revealing the hidden "suicidogenic currents" (courant suicidogene)—collective social forces—behind individual behaviour.

## 四种自杀类型 / Four Types of Suicide

### 1. 利己型自杀 / Egoistic Suicide

**社会条件 / Social Condition:** 社会整合程度过低 / Excessively low social integration

**机制 / Mechanism:** 个人与社会群体的联系过于薄弱，缺乏生活的意义和目标。个人陷入孤立和自我中心的状态。
Individuals' ties to social groups are too weak, lacking meaning and purpose in life. Individuals fall into isolation and self-centredness.

**典型群体 / Typical Groups:** 新教徒（vs. 天主教徒）、单身者（vs. 已婚者）、无子女者 / Protestants (vs. Catholics), unmarried (vs. married), childless individuals

**涂尔干的解释 / Durkheim's Explanation:** 新教强调个人信仰自主，集体意识较弱；天主教和犹太教的集体仪式和共同信仰提供了更强的社会整合。
Protestantism emphasizes individual faith autonomy with weaker collective consciousness; Catholic and Jewish collective rituals and shared faith provide stronger social integration.

### 2. 利他型自杀 / Altruistic Suicide

**社会条件 / Social Condition:** 社会整合程度过高 / Excessively high social integration

**机制 / Mechanism:** 个人完全融入集体，个人生命的价值低于集体的价值。自杀被视为一种义务或荣誉。
Individuals are completely absorbed into the collective; individual life is valued less than the collective. Suicide is seen as a duty or honour.

**典型形态 / Typical Forms:** 军人自杀（军队的高度整合文化）、殉道者、日本武士的切腹 / Military suicide (military's highly integrated culture), martyrs, Japanese samurai's seppuku

### 3. 失范型自杀 / Anomic Suicide

**社会条件 / Social Condition:** 社会规范调节程度过低（失范）/ Excessively low normative regulation (anomie)

**机制 / Mechanism:** 社会规范系统瓦解，个人欲望失去社会设定的界限。人的欲望本质上是无限的，只有社会规范才能为其设定合理的界限。
Social normative systems disintegrate; individual desires lose socially imposed limits. Human desires are inherently unlimited; only social norms can set reasonable boundaries.

**典型情境 / Typical Situations:** 经济危机、经济繁荣（暴发户）、离婚 / Economic crises, economic booms (sudden wealth), divorce

### 4. 宿命型自杀 / Fatalistic Suicide

**社会条件 / Social Condition:** 社会规范调节程度过高 / Excessively high normative regulation

**机制 / Mechanism:** 个人受到过度压制和束缚，看不到任何改变的可能。涂尔干在正文中对此类型着墨甚少，仅在脚注中提及。
Individuals are excessively suppressed and constrained, seeing no possibility of change. Durkheim devoted little space to this type in the main text, mentioning it only in a footnote.

**典型群体 / Typical Groups:** 奴隶、极度专制社会中的居民 / Slaves, inhabitants of extremely oppressive societies

## 学术争论 / Scholarly Debates

### 统计方法的批评 / Statistical Method Criticism
后来的学者（如J.D. Douglas, 1967）批评涂尔干对官方自杀统计的天真信任。自杀的认定本身就是一个社会过程——验尸官、家属和社区的判断影响统计数据。这意味着涂尔干所分析的"社会事实"可能部分是由标签过程建构的。

Later scholars (e.g., J.D. Douglas, 1967) criticized Durkheim's naive trust in official suicide statistics. The classification of suicide is itself a social process—coroners', families', and communities' judgments affect the statistics. This means the "social facts" Durkheim analysed may be partly constructed by labelling processes.

### 默顿的发展 / Merton's Development
默顿将失范概念发展为"紧张理论"（strain theory），分析了美国社会中文化目标（物质成功）与制度化手段（合法途径）之间的断裂如何导致越轨行为。这超越了涂尔干的框架，将失范从一种社会状态转化为一种结构性的分析工具。

Merton developed the anomie concept into "strain theory," analysing how the disjunction between cultural goals (material success) and institutionalized means (legitimate pathways) in American society leads to deviant behaviour. This moved beyond Durkheim's framework, transforming anomie from a social condition into a structural analytical tool.

## 当代意义 / Contemporary Significance

涂尔干的自杀类型学仍然是当代自杀研究的基本框架。WHO的全球自杀预防策略直接运用了社会整合指标。当代研究将涂尔干的分析扩展到新的情境：经济紧缩政策与自杀率的关联、网络欺凌与青少年自杀、孤独流行病（loneliness epidemic）与利己型自杀。

Durkheim's suicide typology remains the basic framework for contemporary suicide research. WHO's global suicide prevention strategy directly employs social integration indicators. Contemporary research extends Durkheim's analysis to new contexts: the link between austerity policies and suicide rates, cyberbullying and youth suicide, and the "loneliness epidemic" and egoistic suicide.
