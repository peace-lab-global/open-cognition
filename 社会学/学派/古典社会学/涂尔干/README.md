# 涂尔干 · Emile Durkheim

## 基本信息 / Basic Information

**全名 / Full Name:** David Emile Durkheim / 大卫·埃米尔·涂尔干
**生卒 / Life:** 1858年4月15日—1917年11月15日（享年59岁）
**出生地 / Birthplace:** 法国洛林大区埃皮纳勒（Epinal, Lorraine, France），犹太拉比家庭 / Jewish rabbinical family
**国籍 / Nationality:** 法国 / French
**学术背景 / Academic Background:** 巴黎高等师范学院哲学学位 / Philosophy degree, Ecole Normale Superieure, Paris
**任教经历 / Academic Positions:** 波尔多大学（1887，法国第一个社会学教席）/ Bordeaux (1887, first sociology chair in France); 巴黎大学/索邦大学（1902）/ Paris/Sorbonne (1902)
**学术传承 / Academic Legacy:** 创办《社会学年鉴》（L'Annee Sociologique, 1898），建立法国社会学学派 / Founded L'Annee Sociologique (1898), established the French school of sociology

## 核心命题 / Core Propositions

### 1. 社会事实 / Social Facts (Faits Sociaux)
> **"社会事实是任何在社会中产生的、对个人的行为方式具有或不具有强制力的、不依赖于个人而独立存在的行动、思考和感觉的方式。"**
> "A social fact is any way of acting, whether fixed or not, capable of exerting over the individual a constraint, or which is general over the whole of a given society whilst having an existence of its own, independent of its individual manifestations."

涂尔干为社会学确立了独特的研究对象——"社会事实"。社会事实具有外在性（存在于个人意识之外）和强制性（对个人施加约束力）两个基本特征。"将社会事实当作事物（choses）来研究"是社会学方法论的第一准则。社会事实必须用其他社会事实来解释，而非还原到心理学或生物学层面。

Durkheim established a distinctive subject matter for sociology—"social facts." Social facts possess two basic characteristics: externality (existing outside individual consciousness) and coerciveness (exerting constraint on the individual). "Treat social facts as things (choses)" is the first rule of sociological methodology. Social facts must be explained by other social facts, not reduced to psychological or biological levels.

### 2. 社会团结 / Social Solidarity
> **"社会的进步不在于个人活动范围的扩大，而在于这些活动更加集中和协作。"**
> "Social progress does not consist in the extension of the sphere of individual activity, but in the greater concentration and cooperation of these activities."

涂尔干区分了两种社会团结：机械团结（mechanical solidarity，基于相似性的团结，对应"集体意识"强大的传统社会）和有机团结（organic solidarity，基于功能互补的团结，对应分工复杂的现代社会）。现代社会的危机在于有机团结未能充分发展，导致"失范"（anomie）。

Durkheim distinguished two types of social solidarity: mechanical solidarity (based on similarity, corresponding to traditional societies with strong "collective consciousness") and organic solidarity (based on functional complementarity, corresponding to modern societies with complex division of labour). The crisis of modern society lies in the inadequate development of organic solidarity, leading to "anomie."

### 3. 失范 / Anomie
> **"当社会受到严重危机或繁荣的扰乱时，无论是哪一种，社会的规范都暂时失去效力。"**
> "When society is disturbed, whether by painful crises or fortunate but abrupt transitions, the scale is upset and a state of anomie results."

"失范"指社会规范系统的瓦解或弱化状态——个人行为缺乏明确的社会规范指引。在失范状态下，个人的欲望失去了社会设定的界限，导致不满、焦虑和越轨行为的增加。涂尔干在《自杀论》中将失范型自杀视为现代社会病理的集中表现。默顿后来将失范概念发展为"紧张理论"（strain theory），成为犯罪社会学的核心理论之一。

"Anomie" refers to the disintegration or weakening of the social normative system—a state in which individual behaviour lacks clear social normative guidance. In an anomic state, individual desires lose socially imposed limits, leading to increased dissatisfaction, anxiety, and deviant behaviour. In Suicide, Durkheim treated anomic suicide as a concentrated expression of modern social pathology. Merton later developed the anomie concept into "strain theory," one of the core theories in criminological sociology.

### 4. 集体表象 / Collective Representations (Representations Collectives)
> **"集体表象是集体思维的结果……它们不是个体的，而是社会的。"**
> "Collective representations are the result of collective thinking... they are not individual but social."

"集体表象"是涂尔干知识社会学的核心概念，指社会群体共享的观念、信仰、象征和分类体系。在《"原始"分类》（与莫斯合著，1903）中，涂尔干论证了时间、空间、因果关系等基本认知范畴都具有社会起源。这一概念为后来的知识社会学（曼海姆）、结构人类学（列维-斯特劳斯）和符号互动论奠定了理论基础。

"Collective representations" is the core concept of Durkheim's sociology of knowledge, referring to ideas, beliefs, symbols, and classification systems shared by social groups. In Primitive Classifications (with Mauss, 1903), Durkheim argued that basic cognitive categories—time, space, causality—all have social origins. This concept laid the theoretical foundations for the later sociology of knowledge (Mannheim), structural anthropology (Levi-Strauss), and symbolic interactionism.

### 5. 宗教的社会功能 / Social Function of Religion
> **"宗教是一种与神圣事物相关的信仰和实践的统一体系……这些信仰和实践将所有信奉者联合在一个称为'教会'的道德共同体中。"**
> "A religion is a unified system of beliefs and practices relative to sacred things... beliefs and practices which unite into one single moral community called a Church, all those who adhere to them."

在《宗教生活的基本形式》中，涂尔干通过对澳大利亚图腾制度的分析，提出了宗教的社会学定义：宗教的本质不在于超自然信仰，而在于"神圣/世俗"的二元分类和社会整合功能。宗教仪式通过"集体亢奋"（effervescence collective）强化社会纽带和集体认同。最终，涂尔干得出了震撼性的结论："神即社会"（Dieu, c'est la societe）。

In The Elementary Forms of Religious Life, through his analysis of Australian totemism, Durkheim proposed a sociological definition of religion: the essence of religion lies not in supernatural belief but in the "sacred/profane" binary classification and its social integration function. Religious rituals strengthen social bonds and collective identity through "collective effervescence" (effervescence collective). Ultimately, Durkheim reached the startling conclusion: "God is society" (Dieu, c'est la societe).

### 6. 社会学方法论 / Methodological Rules for Sociology
> **"必须将社会事实当作事物（choses）来研究。"**
> "Social facts must be treated as things (choses)."

涂尔干在《社会学方法的准则》中为社会学确立了方法论独立性。他提出四条核心方法论规则：（1）将社会事实当作"事物"来观察，摆脱"预断"（pre-notions）；（2）通过外在指标客观地定义社会现象；（3）区分"正常"（normal）和"病态"（pathological）现象；（4）用社会事实解释社会事实，禁止还原主义。

In The Rules of Sociological Method, Durkheim established sociology's methodological independence. He proposed four core methodological rules: (1) observe social facts as "things," free from "pre-notions"; (2) define social phenomena objectively through external indicators; (3) distinguish "normal" from "pathological" phenomena; (4) explain social facts by social facts, prohibiting reductionism.

## 思想脉络 / Intellectual Development

### 第一阶段：学科奠基与方法论建立（1885—1895）
**Phase I: Disciplinary Foundation and Methodological Establishment (1885-1895)**

涂尔干1887年在波尔多大学获得法国第一个社会学教席。早期工作集中于为社会学确立学科地位和方法论基础。《社会分工论》（1893）是他的博士论文，探讨了社会团结如何从机械团结向有机团结转变。《社会学方法的准则》（1895）系统阐述了社会学的研究对象和方法。

Durkheim obtained France's first sociology chair at the University of Bordeaux in 1887. His early work concentrated on establishing sociology's disciplinary status and methodological foundations. The Division of Labour in Society (1893), his doctoral dissertation, explored how social solidarity transforms from mechanical to organic. The Rules of Sociological Method (1895) systematically articulated sociology's subject matter and methods.

### 第二阶段：经验研究与理论成熟（1895—1902）
**Phase II: Empirical Research and Theoretical Maturation (1895-1902)**

《自杀论》（1897）是涂尔干方法论原则的典范性应用——通过统计方法证明自杀率是社会事实，受社会整合程度和规范强度的影响。1898年创办《社会学年鉴》，建立了以涂尔干为核心的法国社会学学派（"涂尔干学派"）。

Suicide (1897) was a model application of Durkheim's methodological principles—using statistical methods to demonstrate that suicide rates are social facts, influenced by the degree of social integration and normative strength. In 1898 he founded L'Annee Sociologique, establishing the French school of sociology centred on Durkheim.

### 第三阶段：宗教社会学与晚年（1902—1917）
**Phase III: Sociology of Religion and Late Years (1902-1917)**

1902年转任巴黎大学/索邦大学教育学教授。《宗教生活的基本形式》（1912）是涂尔干最成熟的理论著作。一战中独子安德烈阵亡（1915），涂尔干深受打击，1917年因中风去世。遗著《道德教育》和《社会主义》由学生整理出版。

In 1902 he transferred to the University of Paris/Sorbonne as professor of education. The Elementary Forms of Religious Life (1912) is Durkheim's most mature theoretical work. His only son Andre was killed in WWI (1915); Durkheim was deeply affected and died of a stroke in 1917. Posthumous works Moral Education and Socialism were published by students.

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

### 1. 社交媒体时代的失范 / Anomie in the Age of Social Media
涂尔干的失范概念为理解当代社会现象提供了有力工具：社交媒体加速了传统规范的瓦解、信息过载导致的价值混乱、以及"后真相"时代的认知失范。

### 2. 自杀研究 / Suicide Research
涂尔干的自杀类型学至今仍是自杀社会学研究的基本框架。当代研究将其扩展到网络欺凌、经济危机和社会隔离等新情境。

### 3. 仪式与集体亢奋 / Rituals and Collective Effervescence
涂尔干的仪式理论被广泛运用于分析当代集体事件：体育赛事、音乐节、政治运动、乃至网络"刷屏"现象中的集体情感体验。

### 4. 知识社会学与认知科学 / Sociology of Knowledge and Cognitive Science
涂尔干关于分类体系社会起源的理论与当代认知科学和社会认识论形成了有趣的对话。

### 5. 教育社会学与道德教育 / Sociology of Education and Moral Education
涂尔干的道德教育理论影响了当代公民教育和品格教育运动，强调学校在道德社会化中的核心功能。

## 常见误读 / Common Misreadings

### ❌ 误读一：涂尔干是保守的功能主义者
涂尔干常被简化为只关心"社会秩序"的保守思想家。然而他对失范、强制分工和社会不公正有深刻批判，主张通过职业团体修复有机团结。

### ✅ 正确理解：涂尔干既关心社会团结的条件，也批判现代社会的病理。他的立场更接近"道德个人主义"。

### ❌ 误读二：涂尔干将社会视为超越个人的"实体"
涂尔干主张社会不能还原为个人总和，但并非将社会神秘化为独立实体。社会是人与人之间形成的关系模式，具有"涌现性"特征。

### ✅ 正确理解："社会"是一个关系性概念，指特定的关系模式而非独立实体。

### ❌ 误读三：涂尔干忽视了个人能动性
涂尔干晚年关注"个人崇拜"（cult of the individual）——对个人尊严和权利的普遍尊重。这不是对社会的否定，而是社会本身的道德成就。

### ✅ 正确理解：真正的个人主义本身就是社会发展的产物。

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

涂尔干的"集体意识"概念与中国传统文化中"礼"的概念形成了有趣的对话空间。儒家的"礼"可以被理解为一种维持机械团结的规范体系。费孝通的"差序格局"概念也可以从涂尔干的社会团结理论获得新的理解。

Durkheim's "collective consciousness" concept dialogues with the Chinese concept of "Li" (ritual propriety). Confucian "Li" can be understood as a normative system maintaining mechanical solidarity. Fei Xiaotong's "differential mode of association" can also receive new understanding from Durkheim's solidarity theory.
