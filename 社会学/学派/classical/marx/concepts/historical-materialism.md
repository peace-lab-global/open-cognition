# 历史唯物主义 · Historical Materialism

## 概念界定 / Concept Definition

历史唯物主义（Historical Materialism / Historischer Materialismus）是马克思社会理论的方法论基石。其核心主张是：人类社会的物质生产方式——即人们为满足生存需要而进行的实际生产活动及其组织形式——构成了社会存在和历史发展的基础。社会的经济结构（生产关系的总和）构成"现实基础"（reale Basis），法律和政治的上层建筑（Uberbau）竖立其上，社会意识形式（意识形态、宗教、哲学、艺术）与之相适应。

Historical Materialism is the methodological cornerstone of Marx's social theory. Its core claim is that the material mode of production—the actual productive activities and their organizational forms through which humans satisfy their survival needs—constitutes the foundation of social existence and historical development. The economic structure of society (the sum total of relations of production) constitutes the "real foundation" (reale Basis), on which rises a legal and political superstructure (Uberbau), and to which correspond definite forms of social consciousness (ideology, religion, philosophy, art).

## 理论构成 / Theoretical Components

### 生产力与生产关系 / Productive Forces and Relations of Production

生产力（Produktivkrafte）包括劳动力、生产工具、技术和自然条件等要素。生产关系（Produktionsverhaltnisse）是人们在生产过程中结成的社会关系，核心是生产资料的所有制形式。生产力决定生产关系，但当生产力发展到一定阶段，现存生产关系就从发展的形式变成桎梏（Fesseln），社会革命的时代就到来了。

Productive forces (Produktivkrafte) include labour-power, instruments of production, technology, and natural conditions. Relations of production (Produktionsverhaltnisse) are the social relations people enter into during production, with ownership of the means of production at their core. Productive forces determine relations of production, but when productive forces reach a certain stage of development, existing relations of production transform from forms of development into fetters (Fesseln), and an epoch of social revolution begins.

### 社会形态的序列 / Sequence of Social Formations

马克思在《政治经济学批判》序言中大致勾画了社会形态的演进序列：亚细亚的、古代的、封建的和现代资产阶级的生产方式。但这一序列并非普适的历史哲学公式——马克思晚年明确反对将其理论机械地套用于所有民族。

In the Preface to A Contribution to the Critique of Political Economy, Marx sketched a rough sequence of social formations: Asiatic, ancient, feudal, and modern bourgeois modes of production. However, this sequence is not a universally applicable formula of historical philosophy—Marx explicitly opposed the mechanical application of his theory to all nations in his later years.

### "归根到底"的决定 / Determination "in the Last Instance"

恩格斯晚年书信中特别强调，经济因素"归根到底"（in letzter Instanz）起决定作用，但这并不意味着它是唯一决定性的因素。上层建筑——政治形式、法律制度、哲学理论、宗教观念——对经济基础具有反作用，并在许多情况下"主要是决定着这一斗争的形式"。

Engels' later letters emphasized that the economic factor is determinant "in the last instance" (in letzter Instanz), but this does not mean it is the sole determining factor. The superstructure—political forms, legal systems, philosophical theories, religious ideas—reacts upon the economic base and in many cases "mainly determines the form" of the struggle.

## 学术争论 / Scholarly Debates

### 决定论 vs. 辩证论 / Determinism vs. Dialectics
关于历史唯物主义是严格的经济决定论还是辩证的分析方法，学术界长期争论。第二国际的理论家（如考茨基）倾向于决定论解读；西方马克思主义传统（卢卡奇、葛兰西、法兰克福学派）强调辩证维度；分析马克思主义（G.A. Cohen）试图以分析哲学方法重建历史唯物主义。

The question of whether historical materialism is a strict economic determinism or a dialectical analytical method has been the subject of long-standing scholarly debate. Second International theorists (e.g., Kautsky) leaned toward determinist readings; the Western Marxist tradition (Lukacs, Gramsci, Frankfurt School) emphasized the dialectical dimension; analytical Marxism (G.A. Cohen) attempted to reconstruct historical materialism using analytical philosophy.

### 与韦伯的对话 / Dialogue with Weber
韦伯的《新教伦理与资本主义精神》常被解读为对历史唯物主义的"文化主义"反驳。但韦伯本人否认其意图是"用一种同样片面但同样正确的因果解释来代替对历史的唯物论解释"。两者的关系更多是互补而非对立——韦伯关注意义和文化如何赋予经济行为以方向，马克思关注经济结构如何设定社会变迁的基本参数。

Weber's Protestant Ethic is often read as a "culturalist" rebuttal to historical materialism. Yet Weber himself denied that his intent was to "substitute for a materialistic interpretation of history a similarly one-sided but equally correct causal explanation." Their relationship is more complementary than oppositional—Weber focused on how meaning and culture give direction to economic action; Marx on how economic structures set the basic parameters of social change.

## 当代意义 / Contemporary Significance

历史唯物主义为理解当代全球化、技术革命和社会不平等提供了不可替代的宏观分析框架。在数字资本主义时代，生产力概念需要涵盖数据、算法和平台；生产关系概念需要涵盖数字劳动、知识产权和平台垄断。历史唯物主义的真正价值不在于提供历史发展的固定公式，而在于其方法论启示：要理解任何社会现象，必须首先追问其物质生产条件。

Historical materialism provides an irreplaceable macro-analytical framework for understanding contemporary globalization, technological revolution, and social inequality. In the era of digital capitalism, the concept of productive forces must encompass data, algorithms, and platforms; the concept of relations of production must encompass digital labour, intellectual property, and platform monopoly. The true value of historical materialism lies not in providing fixed formulas for historical development, but in its methodological insight: to understand any social phenomenon, one must first interrogate its material conditions of production.
