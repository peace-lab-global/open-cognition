# 支配类型 · Types of Legitimate Domination (Herrschaftstypen)

## 概念界定 / Concept Definition

韦伯区分了"权力"（Macht）与"支配"（Herrschaft）。权力是"一个人或若干人在社会行动中，即使遇到其他参与者的抵制也要实现自己意志的机会"。支配则是"具有特定内容的命令被特定群体服从的概率"。支配的关键特征是正当性（Legitimitat）——服从者承认支配者的命令具有正当性。

Weber distinguished between "power" (Macht) and "domination" (Herrschaft). Power is "the probability that one actor within a social relationship will be in a position to carry out his own will despite resistance." Domination is "the probability that a command with a definite specific content will be obeyed by a given group of persons." The key characteristic of domination is legitimacy (Legitimitat)—the governed acknowledge the legitimacy of the ruler's commands.

## 三种纯粹类型 / Three Pure Types

### 1. 传统型支配 / Traditional Domination

**正当性基础 / Basis of Legitimacy:** "历来如此"（Immer-so-Gewesenheit）——对古老传统和习惯的神圣性的信仰。
"It has always been so" (Immer-so-Gewesenheit)—belief in the sanctity of age-old traditions and customs.

**组织形式 / Organizational Form:** 家长制（Patrimonialismus）、封建制（Feudalismus）。统治者与被统治者的关系类似于主人与家仆的关系。
Patriarchalism (Patrimonialismus), feudalism (Feudalismus). The relationship between ruler and ruled resembles that of master and household servants.

**行政人员 / Administrative Staff:** 家臣、仆从、亲信——基于个人忠诚而非专业能力选拔。
Retainers, servants, favourites—selected on the basis of personal loyalty rather than professional competence.

**法律特征 / Legal Characteristic:** 没有形式化的法律体系；统治者的意志就是法律，但受传统约束。
No formalized legal system; the ruler's will is law, but constrained by tradition.

### 2. 魅力型支配 / Charismatic Domination

**正当性基础 / Basis of Legitimacy:** 对领袖超凡人格特质——"魅力"（Charisma）——的信仰。魅力是一种被认为超自然或至少非凡的品质。
Belief in the extraordinary personal qualities—"charisma"—of the leader. Charisma is a quality regarded as supernatural or at least exceptional.

**组织形式 / Organizational Form:** 围绕魅力领袖形成的追随者共同体。没有正式的等级制度和规章制度。
A community of followers formed around the charismatic leader. No formal hierarchy or regulations.

**行政人员 / Administrative Staff:** 门徒、使徒、战友——基于对领袖的个人忠诚和共享的魅力体验。
Disciples, apostles, comrades-in-arms—selected on the basis of personal devotion to the leader and shared charismatic experience.

**不稳定性 / Instability:** 魅力型支配是最不稳定的支配类型。领袖去世或"魅力消退"后，面临"常规化"（Veralltaglichung / routinization）的必然命运——向传统型或法理型支配转变。
Charismatic domination is the most unstable type. After the leader's death or "fading of charisma," it faces the inevitable fate of "routinization" (Veralltaglichung)—transformation into traditional or legal-rational domination.

### 3. 法理型支配 / Legal-Rational Domination

**正当性基础 / Basis of Legitimacy:** 对合法章程和规章制度的信仰。服从的对象不是个人，而是非人格化的法律秩序。
Belief in the legality of enacted rules and regulations. The object of obedience is not the person but the impersonal legal order.

**组织形式 / Organizational Form:** 官僚制（Burokratie）。等级制、专业化、规则化、非人格化。
Bureaucracy (Burokratie). Hierarchical, specialized, rule-governed, impersonal.

**行政人员 / Administrative Staff:** 经过专业训练的公务员，按能力和资格选拔，享有固定薪酬和职业保障。
Professionally trained civil servants, selected by competence and qualifications, enjoying fixed salaries and career security.

**法律特征 / Legal Characteristic:** 形式化的法律体系，由专业法律人员按照逻辑程序运作。
Formalized legal system operated by professional legal personnel according to logical procedures.

## 支配类型的动态 / Dynamics of Domination Types

### 魅力的常规化 / Routinization of Charisma
韦伯分析了魅力型支配向稳定支配形式转化的多种路径：
Weber analysed multiple pathways for the transformation of charismatic domination into stable forms:
- **世袭魅力**（Erbcharisma）— 魅力传给领袖的后代 / Charisma transmitted to the leader's descendants
- **指定继承**（Designation）— 领袖指定继承人 / Leader designates a successor
- **教团选举**（Selection by the community）— 追随者团体选举新领袖 / Followers' community elects a new leader
- **制度化**（Institutionalization）— 魅力被转化为制度和规章 / Charisma transformed into institutions and regulations

### 混合形态 / Mixed Forms
现实中的支配形态通常是混合类型。例如：
Actual forms of domination are typically mixed types. For example:
- **恺撒式民主**（Caesarist democracy）— 魅力型领袖通过民主选举获得正当性 / Charismatic leader gains legitimacy through democratic elections
- **新传统主义**（Neo-traditionalism）— 现代政权中传统象征的复兴 / Revival of traditional symbols in modern regimes

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

韦伯的支配类型理论在当代政治分析中仍然极为活跃：

Weber's types of domination remain highly active in contemporary political analysis:

- **民粹主义研究：** 特朗普、博索纳罗等民粹领袖的魅力型支配特征 / Populist studies: charismatic domination features of populist leaders like Trump and Bolsonaro
- **技术治理：** 算法和数据驱动的"新法理型"支配 / Techno-governance: "neo-legal-rational" domination driven by algorithms and data
- **组织研究：** 科技公司中创始人魅力与职业经理人法理权威的张力 / Organizational studies: tension between founders' charisma and professional managers' legal-rational authority in tech companies
- **宗教运动研究：** 新兴宗教中魅力型领袖的常规化过程 / Religious movement studies: routinization of charismatic leaders in new religious movements
