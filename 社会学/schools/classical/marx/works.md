# 马克思主要著作详述 / Marx Major Works: Detailed Survey

## 一、早期著作（1837—1844）/ Early Works (1837-1844)

### 1. 《博士论文》（1841）/ Doctoral Dissertation
**全称**: 《德谟克利特的自然哲学和伊壁鸠鲁的自然哲学的差别》
*The Difference Between the Democritean and Epicurean Philosophy of Nature*

马克思在柏林大学的博士论文，比较了古希腊两位原子论者的自然哲学差异。马克思站在伊壁鸠鲁一边，强调偶然性和自我意识的自由，反对机械决定论。这篇论文已显露出马克思对黑格尔辩证法的创造性运用，以及对自由与必然关系的终身关切。

Marx's doctoral dissertation at the University of Berlin compared the natural philosophies of two ancient Greek atomists. Siding with Epicurus, Marx emphasized contingency and the freedom of self-consciousness against mechanical determinism. The dissertation already reveals Marx's creative appropriation of Hegelian dialectics and his lifelong concern with the relationship between freedom and necessity.

### 2. 《黑格尔法哲学批判》（1843）/ Critique of Hegel's Philosophy of Right
马克思系统批判黑格尔的国家哲学，颠倒了黑格尔关于国家决定市民社会的命题，主张市民社会决定国家。这一"颠倒"标志着马克思从唯心主义向唯物主义的转变。手稿中首次出现了对无产阶级历史使命的论述。

Marx systematically critiqued Hegel's philosophy of the state, inverting Hegel's thesis that the state determines civil society, arguing instead that civil society determines the state. This "inversion" marks Marx's transition from idealism to materialism. The manuscript contains the first articulation of the proletariat's historical mission.

### 3. 《论犹太人问题》（1843）/ On the Jewish Question
回应布鲁诺·鲍威尔关于犹太人解放的论文。马克思区分了"政治解放"与"人的解放"，指出资产阶级国家中的政治自由只是形式上的解放，真正的解放需要消灭私有财产和市民社会的利己主义。

A response to Bruno Bauer's essay on Jewish emancipation. Marx distinguished "political emancipation" from "human emancipation," arguing that political freedom in the bourgeois state is merely formal emancipation; genuine emancipation requires the abolition of private property and the egoism of civil society.

### 4. 《1844年经济学哲学手稿》（1844）/ Economic and Philosophic Manuscripts of 1844
又称"巴黎手稿"，马克思生前未发表，1932年才首次完整出版。这是马克思异化理论的成熟之作，系统阐述了异化劳动的四个维度：劳动者与产品、劳动过程、类本质以及他人之间的异化。手稿还包含了对私有财产的批判性分析和共产主义的哲学论证。尽管用语尚带有浓厚的人本主义色彩，但已包含历史唯物主义的萌芽。

Also known as the "Paris Manuscripts," unpublished during Marx's lifetime and first published in full in 1932. This is Marx's mature work on alienation theory, systematically elaborating four dimensions of alienated labour: the worker's alienation from the product, from the process, from species-being, and from others. The manuscripts also contain critical analysis of private property and a philosophical argument for communism. Although the language retains strong humanist overtones, the seeds of historical materialism are already present.

## 二、过渡期著作（1845—1848）/ Transitional Works (1845-1848)

### 5. 《关于费尔巴哈的提纲》（1845）/ Theses on Feuerbach
十一条简短的哲学提纲，恩格斯称之为"包含着新世界观的天才萌芽的第一个文件"。核心命题：哲学家们只是用不同方式解释世界，而问题在于改变世界（第十一条）。马克思在此超越了费尔巴哈的直观唯物主义，确立了"实践"（Praxis）作为新哲学的核心范畴。

Eleven brief philosophical theses, described by Engels as "the first document in which is deposited the brilliant germ of the new world outlook." Core proposition: philosophers have only interpreted the world in various ways; the point is to change it (Thesis XI).

### 6. 《德意志意识形态》（1846，与恩格斯合著）/ The German Ideology (w/ Engels)
历史唯物主义的首次系统阐述。马克思和恩格斯在此清算了他们"从前的哲学信仰"，批判了费尔巴哈的唯物主义的不彻底性和青年黑格尔派的唯心主义。核心贡献：确立了"现实的个人"作为历史的前提，阐述了生产力决定交往形式的基本原理，勾画了意识形态作为"虚假意识"的理论。

The first systematic articulation of historical materialism. Marx and Engels here "settled accounts with their former philosophical conscience," critiquing the incompleteness of Feuerbach's materialism and the idealism of the Young Hegelians. Core contributions: establishing "real individuals" as the premise of history; articulating the principle that productive forces determine forms of intercourse; sketching the theory of ideology as "false consciousness."

### 7. 《哲学的贫困》（1847）/ The Poverty of Philosophy
对蒲鲁东《贫困的哲学》的批判。马克思在此将政治经济学批判与历史唯物主义相结合，论证了经济范畴不过是现实生产关系的理论抽象，而非先验的存在。

A critique of Proudhon's *The Philosophy of Poverty*. Marx here combined the critique of political economy with historical materialism, arguing that economic categories are merely theoretical abstractions of real relations of production.

### 8. 《共产党宣言》（1848，与恩格斯合著）/ The Communist Manifesto (w/ Engels)
受共产主义者同盟委托撰写的纲领性文件，可能是现代历史上最有影响力的政治文献。核心命题："至今一切社会的历史都是阶级斗争的历史"。《宣言》以雄辩的文体阐述了资产阶级的历史进步性和内在矛盾、无产阶级的历史使命、共产党的性质和目标。

A programmatic document commissioned by the Communist League, possibly the most influential political document in modern history. Core proposition: "The history of all hitherto existing society is the history of class struggles."

## 三、政治分析与历史著作 / Political Analysis and Historical Works

### 9. 《路易·波拿巴的雾月十八日》（1852）/ The Eighteenth Brumaire of Louis Bonaparte
政治社会学的经典之作。马克思分析了1851年路易·波拿巴政变，展示了阶级斗争如何在特定历史条件下导致"行政权力"凌驾于一切社会阶级之上。

A classic of political sociology. Marx analysed the 1851 coup of Louis Bonaparte, showing how class struggle under specific conditions can lead to "executive power" rising above all social classes.

### 10. 《法兰西内战》（1871）/ The Civil War in France
对巴黎公社的分析，马克思从中总结出无产阶级不能简单地掌握现成的国家机器，而必须打碎旧的国家机器的结论。

Analysis of the Paris Commune, from which Marx concluded that the working class cannot simply lay hold of the ready-made state machinery but must shatter the old state apparatus.

## 四、政治经济学批判 / Critique of Political Economy

### 11. 《政治经济学批判大纲》（Grundrisse, 1857-58）/ Grundrisse
《资本论》的预备性手稿，直到1939-41年才首次出版。包含对前资本主义社会形态的分析、"一般智力"（General Intellect）概念、以及对机器和自动化的前瞻性讨论。

The preparatory manuscript for Capital, not published until 1939-41. Contains analysis of pre-capitalist social formations, the concept of "General Intellect," and forward-looking discussions of machinery and automation.

### 12. 《资本论》第一卷（1867）/ Capital, Volume I
马克思生前唯一出版的《资本论》卷次。系统阐述了商品与价值理论、剩余价值理论、资本积累的一般规律和"原始积累"的历史过程。

The only volume of Capital published during Marx's lifetime. Systematically articulates the theory of commodities and value, the theory of surplus value, the general law of capital accumulation, and the historical process of "primitive accumulation."

### 13. 《资本论》第二卷（1885，恩格斯编辑）/ Capital, Volume II
分析资本的流通过程，包括资本循环、资本周转和社会总资本的再生产。

Analyses the process of circulation of capital, including circuits of capital, turnover, and reproduction of total social capital.

### 14. 《资本论》第三卷（1894，恩格斯编辑）/ Capital, Volume III
分析资本主义生产总过程，包括利润率下降趋势、商业资本与生息资本、地租理论。关于"虚拟资本"的分析对理解当代金融化具有重要意义。

Analyses the capitalist process of production as a whole, including the tendency of the rate of profit to fall, commercial and interest-bearing capital, and the theory of rent. Its analysis of "fictitious capital" is significant for understanding contemporary financialization.

## 五、晚期著作 / Later Works

### 15. 《哥达纲领批判》（1875）/ Critique of the Gotha Programme
对德国工人党纲领草案的批判。阐述了共产主义社会的两个阶段和"各尽所能，按需分配"的原则。

A critique of the draft programme of the German Workers' Party. Elaborated the two stages of communist society and the principle "from each according to his ability, to each according to his needs."

### 16. 《人类学笔记》（1879-82）/ Ethnological Notebooks
马克思晚年对摩尔根《古代社会》等人类学著作的研究笔记，关注前资本主义社会形态和非西方社会的演进道路。

Research notes on anthropological works including Morgan's *Ancient Society*, concerning pre-capitalist social formations and developmental paths of non-Western societies.
