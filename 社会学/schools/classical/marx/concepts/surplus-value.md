# 剩余价值 · Surplus Value (Mehrwert)

## 概念界定 / Concept Definition

剩余价值（Surplus Value / Mehrwert）是马克思政治经济学批判的核心范畴，指工人在生产过程中创造的、超过其劳动力价值的那部分价值，它被资本家无偿占有，构成利润、地租和利息的来源。剩余价值理论是马克思对古典政治经济学（特别是李嘉图）的根本超越，它揭示了资本主义剥削的秘密机制。

Surplus Value (Mehrwert) is the core category of Marx's critique of political economy, referring to the value created by workers in the production process that exceeds the value of their labour-power, appropriated without compensation by the capitalist, constituting the source of profit, rent, and interest. The theory of surplus value represents Marx's fundamental advance beyond classical political economy (especially Ricardo), revealing the secret mechanism of capitalist exploitation.

## 劳动力商品 / Labour-Power as Commodity

马克思区分了"劳动"（Arbeit）和"劳动力"（Arbeitskraft / labour-power）。工人出卖的不是"劳动"本身，而是"劳动力"——即在一定时间内进行劳动的能力。劳动力的价值由维持工人生存和再生产劳动力所需的社会必要劳动时间决定（即工资所代表的生存资料的价值）。

Marx distinguished between "labour" (Arbeit) and "labour-power" (Arbeitskraft). What the worker sells is not "labour" itself but "labour-power"—the capacity to labour for a given period. The value of labour-power is determined by the socially necessary labour time required to maintain the worker and reproduce labour-power (i.e., the value of subsistence goods represented by wages).

关键在于：劳动力在使用过程中能够创造出超过自身价值的价值。例如，工人可能只需要4小时的劳动就能再生产出自己的工资价值，但他被要求工作8小时。多出的4小时就是"剩余劳动时间"，所创造的价值就是剩余价值。

The key insight: labour-power in use can create value exceeding its own value. For example, a worker may need only 4 hours of labour to reproduce the value of his wages, but is required to work 8 hours. The additional 4 hours constitute "surplus labour time," and the value created in that time is surplus value.

## 两种剩余价值 / Two Forms of Surplus Value

### 绝对剩余价值 / Absolute Surplus Value

通过延长工作日或增加劳动强度来获取的剩余价值。这是资本主义早期的主要剥削方式。工作日长度的斗争——资本家力图延长工作日，工人阶级争取缩短工作日——构成了19世纪工人运动的重要内容。

Surplus value obtained by extending the working day or intensifying labour. This was the primary mode of exploitation in early capitalism. The struggle over the length of the working day—capitalists seeking to extend it, the working class fighting to reduce it—was a central theme of 19th-century labour movements.

### 相对剩余价值 / Relative Surplus Value

通过提高劳动生产率、缩短必要劳动时间来获取的剩余价值。技术进步、机器应用、分工协作和组织革新都是提高相对剩余价值的手段。相对剩余价值的生产推动了资本主义生产力的巨大发展，同时也加剧了劳动的异化。

Surplus value obtained by raising labour productivity and reducing necessary labour time. Technological progress, machine application, division of labour and cooperation, and organizational innovation are all means of increasing relative surplus value. The production of relative surplus value drives the enormous development of capitalist productive forces while simultaneously intensifying the alienation of labour.

## 资本积累的内在逻辑 / Inner Logic of Capital Accumulation

剩余价值转化为资本（即资本积累）是资本主义再生产的核心过程。资本家将部分剩余价值再投入生产，购买更多的劳动力和生产资料，以获取更多的剩余价值。这一过程导致：

The conversion of surplus value into capital (i.e., capital accumulation) is the core process of capitalist reproduction. Capitalists reinvest part of the surplus value in production, purchasing more labour-power and means of production to obtain more surplus value. This process leads to:

1. **资本集中与积聚** / Concentration and centralization of capital — 大资本吞并小资本 / Large capital absorbs small capital
2. **资本有机构成提高** / Rising organic composition of capital — 不变资本（机器、原料）相对于可变资本（劳动力）的比重增大 / The ratio of constant capital (machines, raw materials) to variable capital (labour-power) increases
3. **相对过剩人口** / Relative surplus population — 产业后备军（失业人口）的形成 / Formation of an industrial reserve army (unemployed population)
4. **贫富两极化** / Polarization of wealth and poverty — 财富在一极积累，贫困在另一极积累 / Wealth accumulates at one pole, poverty at the other

## 利润率下降趋势 / Tendency of the Rate of Profit to Fall

随着资本有机构成的提高，以可变资本（唯一能创造剩余价值的要素）为基数的利润率呈现下降趋势。马克思称之为"资本主义生产方式的规律"。资本家通过提高剥削率、扩大市场、发展金融等手段来"对抗"这一趋势，但这些对策本身又会加剧系统的矛盾。

As the organic composition of capital rises, the rate of profit—calculated on the basis of variable capital (the only element that creates surplus value)—shows a tendency to fall. Marx called this a "law of the capitalist mode of production." Capitalists "counteract" this tendency through raising the rate of exploitation, expanding markets, and developing finance, but these countermeasures themselves exacerbate systemic contradictions.

## 当代争论 / Contemporary Debates

### 转形问题 / The Transformation Problem
马克思在《资本论》第三卷中试图将价值转化为生产价格，但其数学方法受到质疑（Bortkiewicz, 1907）。这一问题至今仍是马克思主义经济学的核心争论之一。当代学者如斯蒂德曼（Steedman）从斯拉法主义立场出发质疑劳动价值论的必要性，而马克思主义者则为其辩护。

Marx attempted in Capital Volume III to transform values into prices of production, but his mathematical procedure has been challenged (Bortkiewicz, 1907). This "transformation problem" remains a central debate in Marxist economics. Contemporary scholars like Steedman have questioned the necessity of the labour theory of value from a Sraffian perspective, while Marxists have defended it.

### 数字时代的剩余价值 / Surplus Value in the Digital Age
数字平台经济提出了新的理论问题：用户在社交媒体上的无偿数据劳动是否构成新型的剩余价值生产？"产消者"（prosumer）概念挑战了传统的生产/消费二分。学者如Christian Fuchs和Nick Srnicek试图在新的技术条件下重建剩余价值理论。

The digital platform economy poses new theoretical questions: does users' unpaid data labour on social media constitute a new form of surplus value production? The "prosumer" concept challenges the traditional production/consumption dichotomy. Scholars such as Christian Fuchs and Nick Srnicek attempt to reconstruct surplus value theory under new technological conditions.
