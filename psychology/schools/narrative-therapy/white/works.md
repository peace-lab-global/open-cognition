# 怀特著作年表 · Michael White Works Chronology

## 主要著作 / Major Works

| 年份 / Year | 著作 / Work | 出版社 / Publisher | 核心贡献 / Core Contribution |
|:---:|:---|:---|:---|
| 1988 | "The Externalizing Conversation" | *Dulwich Centre Newsletter* | 首次系统阐述外化对话技术，标志叙事实践核心技术的确立 / First systematic exposition of externalizing conversation techniques |
| 1989 | *Literate Means to Therapeutic Ends*（与艾普斯顿合著 / with Epston） | Dulwich Centre Publications | 探讨书写、信件和文件在治疗中的运用 / Explored the use of writing, letters, and documents in therapy |
| 1990 | *Narrative Means to Therapeutic Ends*（与艾普斯顿合著 / with Epston） | W.W. Norton | 叙事疗法的奠基之作；系统提出外化、重写和独特结果等核心概念 / Foundational text of narrative therapy; systematically proposing core concepts of externalization, re-authoring, and unique outcomes |
| 1991 | *Experience, Contradiction, Narrative and Imagination: Selected Papers of David Epston and Michael White* | Dulwich Centre Publications | 进一步拓展叙事实践的理论和临床范围 / Further expanded the theoretical and clinical scope of narrative practice |
| 1995 | *Narrative Therapy and Community Work* | Dulwich Centre Publications | 将叙事实践从个体治疗拓展至社区工作领域 / Extended narrative practice from individual therapy to community work |
| 1997 | *Narratives of Therapists' Lives* | Dulwich Centre Publications | 探讨治疗师自身身份认同的叙事建构 / Explored the narrative construction of therapists' own identities |
| 2000 | *Reflections on Narrative Practice* | Dulwich Centre Publications | 对叙事实践的反思与总结，回应批评和质疑 / Reflections and summaries of narrative practice, addressing criticisms |
| 2004 | *Narrative Practice and Exotic Sounds: Personalities of the Inner World* | Dulwich Centre Publications | 关注叙事实践中的创造性和想象力维度 / Attended to the creative and imaginative dimensions of narrative practice |
| 2007 | *Maps of Narrative Practice* | W.W. Norton | 最系统化的著作；提出六大治疗地图，整合毕生实践智慧 / Most systematic work; proposing six therapeutic maps, integrating lifetime of practice wisdom |

## 杜尔维奇中心出版物 / Dulwich Centre Publications

怀特创立的杜尔维奇中心（Dulwich Centre, Adelaide）是叙事实践的国际出版与培训枢纽，出版了大量叙事实践的专著、论文集和培训材料。 / The Dulwich Centre in Adelaide, founded by White, is the international publishing and training hub for narrative practice, publishing numerous monographs, collected papers, and training materials.

- *Dulwich Centre Journal* — 叙事实践研究期刊
- *International Journal of Narrative Therapy and Community Work* — 国际叙事疗法与社区工作期刊
- 集体叙事实践系列文集 / Collective narrative practice collected volumes
