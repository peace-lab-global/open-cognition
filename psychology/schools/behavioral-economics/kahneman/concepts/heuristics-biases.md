# 启发式与偏差 · Heuristics and Biases

## 定义 · Definition

**启发式**（heuristics）是人类在面对复杂判断任务时使用的认知捷径——简单、高效的心理规则，能够在信息不完全、时间有限的条件下快速产生"足够好"的判断。**偏差**（biases）是启发式在某些条件下产生的系统性、方向性的判断错误——不是随机误差，而是可预测的、结构性的偏离。

**Heuristics** are cognitive shortcuts humans use when facing complex judgment tasks — simple, efficient mental rules that rapidly produce "good enough" judgments under conditions of incomplete information and limited time. **Biases** are the systematic, directional judgment errors that heuristics produce under certain conditions — not random errors, but predictable, structural deviations.

卡尼曼与特沃斯基的核心洞见是：**偏差不是偶发的例外，而是人类认知架构的内在特征**。理解启发式的运作方式，就能预测人类判断会在哪些方向上偏离理性标准。

Kahneman and Tversky's core insight is that **biases are not occasional exceptions but intrinsic features of human cognitive architecture**. By understanding how heuristics operate, one can predict in which directions human judgment will deviate from rational standards.

## 核心机制 · Core Mechanisms

### 三大经典启发式 · Three Classic Heuristics

#### 1. 代表性启发（Representativeness Heuristic）

人们根据一个事物与某个类别的**典型原型**的相似程度来判断其属于该类别的概率，而忽视基础率（先验概率）和样本大小等统计信息。

People judge the probability that something belongs to a category based on its **similarity to the typical prototype** of that category, neglecting base rates (prior probabilities) and sample size.

**经典实验**："琳达问题"（Linda Problem）——被试被告知琳达是一位关心社会正义的女性，然后被要求判断"琳达是银行出纳员"和"琳达是银行出纳员且是女权主义者"哪个更可能。大多数人选择后者，违反了概率论的合取规则（P(A∧B) ≤ P(A)）。

**Classic experiment**: The "Linda Problem" — subjects are told Linda is a woman concerned with social justice, then asked to judge whether "Linda is a bank teller" or "Linda is a bank teller and a feminist" is more probable. Most choose the latter, violating the conjunction rule of probability (P(A∧B) ≤ P(A)).

#### 2. 可得性启发（Availability Heuristic）

人们根据某类事件在记忆中的**容易提取程度**来估计其频率或概率。→ 详见 [可得性启发](availability.md)

People estimate the frequency or probability of events based on how **easily instances come to mind**. → See [Availability Heuristic](availability.md)

#### 3. 锚定与调整（Anchoring and Adjustment）

人们在进行数值估计时，从一个初始值（锚）出发进行调整，但调整通常不充分。→ 详见 [锚定效应](anchoring.md)

When making numerical estimates, people start from an initial value (anchor) and adjust, but the adjustment is typically insufficient. → See [Anchoring Effect](anchoring.md)

### 其他重要启发式与偏差 · Other Important Heuristics and Biases

| 启发式/偏差 | 描述 |
|---|---|
| 确认偏误（Confirmation Bias）| 倾向于搜寻、解读支持既有信念的信息 |
| 过度自信（Overconfidence）| 对自己的判断准确性估计过高 |
| 后见之明（Hindsight Bias）| 事件发生后认为自己"早就知道" |
| 框架效应（Framing Effect）| 同一信息以不同方式呈现导致不同决策 |
| 现状偏见（Status Quo Bias）| 倾向于维持当前状态 |
| 沉没成本谬误（Sunk Cost Fallacy）| 因已投入的成本而继续失败的项目 |

## 发展脉络 · Historical Development

### 1970年代：纲领确立 · 1970s: Program Establishment

1971年，特沃斯基与卡尼曼发表"Belief in the Law of Small Numbers"，揭示人们对小样本的统计直觉是错误的。1972年，他们提出代表性启发概念。1973–1974年，在《科学》杂志发表系列论文，系统阐述三大启发式及其偏差。1982年，两人合编的《Judgment under Uncertainty》论文集正式确立了"启发式与偏差"作为心理学研究纲领的地位。

In 1971, Tversky and Kahneman published "Belief in the Law of Small Numbers," revealing that people's statistical intuitions about small samples are erroneous. In 1972, they introduced the representativeness heuristic. In 1973–1974, a series of papers in *Science* systematically articulated the three major heuristics and their biases. In 1982, their co-edited volume *Judgment under Uncertainty* formally established "heuristics and biases" as a psychology research program.

### 1980–1990年代：扩展与争论 · 1980s–1990s: Extension and Debate

启发式与偏差研究迅速扩展到社会心理学、医学决策、法律等领域。同时，加里·吉仁泽（Gigerenzer）等人提出"快速节俭启发式"（fast and frugal heuristics），认为许多启发式在真实环境中比复杂的统计模型更准确——这场争论被称为"理性之战"（rationality wars）。

Heuristics and biases research rapidly expanded into social psychology, medical decision-making, law, and other fields. Meanwhile, Gerd Gigerenzer and others proposed "fast and frugal heuristics," arguing that many heuristics outperform complex statistical models in real environments — a debate dubbed the "rationality wars."

### 2000年代至今：整合与深化 · 2000s–Present: Integration and Deepening

双系统理论为启发式与偏差提供了统一的理论框架：启发式是系统1的核心运作方式，偏差是系统2未能有效监控的结果。近年来，"助推"运动将启发式与偏差研究转化为实用的政策工具。

Dual-process theory provided a unified framework: heuristics are System 1's core operating mode, and biases result from System 2's failure to effectively monitor. In recent years, the "nudge" movement has transformed heuristics and biases research into practical policy tools.

## 临床应用 · Applications

### 医疗决策 · Medical Decision-Making
医生在诊断过程中大量依赖启发式。例如，"代表性"可能导致医生在症状与某种疾病的典型表现不完全匹配时忽视该诊断。"过度自信"可能导致医生在信息不足时过于确信自己的初步判断。医学教育正在引入"认知去偏差"训练。

Doctors rely heavily on heuristics during diagnosis. For example, "representativeness" may cause doctors to overlook a diagnosis when symptoms don't fully match a disease's typical presentation. "Overconfidence" may lead doctors to be overly certain of initial judgments with insufficient information. Medical education is increasingly incorporating "cognitive debiasing" training.

### 金融投资 · Financial Investment
投资者的许多系统性错误可追溯到启发式：用过去的走势预测未来（代表性）；因为最近看到某类资产上涨而高估其未来收益（可得性）；以买入价为"锚"决定何时卖出（锚定）。

Many systematic investor errors trace back to heuristics: predicting future trends from past patterns (representativeness); overestimating future returns after recently observing an asset class rise (availability); using purchase price as an "anchor" for sell decisions (anchoring).

### 公共政策 · Public Policy
理解公民的启发式思维模式后，政策设计者可以调整信息的呈现方式（选择架构），帮助人们在不完全理性的条件下做出更好的选择。例如：默认选项设计、简化复杂信息、利用社会规范。

Understanding citizens' heuristic thinking allows policy designers to adjust information presentation (choice architecture), helping people make better choices under conditions of imperfect rationality. Examples: default-option design, simplifying complex information, leveraging social norms.

## 东西方对话 · East-West Dialogue

### 因明学 · Buddhist Logic (Hetuvidya)
印度佛教因明学对"量"（pramana，有效认知来源）的精细分析——区分现量（直接感知）与比量（推论认知）——可被解读为一种前现代的启发式分析。因明学特别关注"似现量"和"似比量"（看似有效但实际无效的认知），这与卡尼曼对"系统性偏差"的揭示在结构上高度一致：两者都关注认知系统在何种条件下会产生看似正确但实际错误的判断。

Indian Buddhist Hetuvidya's fine-grained analysis of *pramana* (valid cognitive sources) — distinguishing *pratyaksha* (direct perception) from *anumana* (inferential cognition) — can be read as a pre-modern heuristics analysis. Hetuvidya pays special attention to *pratyakshabhasa* and *anumanabhasa* (cognitions that appear valid but are actually invalid), structurally paralleling Kahneman's revelation of systematic biases: both focus on the conditions under which cognitive systems produce judgments that appear correct but are actually erroneous.

### 荀子 · Xunzi
荀子"解蔽"篇的核心论点是：人的认知容易被各种"蔽"（片面性）所蒙蔽——"欲为蔽，恶为蔽；始为蔽，终为蔽"。这与启发式与偏差研究的核心洞察一致：人类认知不是中立的，而是被结构性因素系统性地"遮蔽"。

Xunzi's "Dispelling Obsessions" (*Jie Bi*) chapter argues that human cognition is easily obscured by various forms of one-sidedness — "desire obscures, hatred obscures; beginnings obscure, endings obscure." This aligns with the core insight of heuristics and biases research: human cognition is not neutral but systematically "obscured" by structural factors.
