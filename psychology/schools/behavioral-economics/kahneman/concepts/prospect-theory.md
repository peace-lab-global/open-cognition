# 前景理论 · Prospect Theory

## 定义 · Definition

前景理论（Prospect Theory）是卡尼曼与特沃斯基在1979年提出的描述性决策理论，用以替代传统经济学的期望效用理论（Expected Utility Theory）。它描述了人们在面对风险和不确定性时**实际如何**做出决策，而非他们**应当如何**决策。前景理论的核心发现是：人们的决策不是基于绝对财富水平，而是基于相对于某个参考点的**收益与损失**；人们对损失的敏感度约为收益的两倍；概率不是线性加权的。

Prospect Theory is a descriptive decision theory proposed by Kahneman and Tversky in 1979 as an alternative to traditional economics' Expected Utility Theory. It describes how people **actually** make decisions under risk and uncertainty, not how they **should** decide. The theory's core findings are: decisions are not based on absolute wealth levels but on **gains and losses relative to a reference point**; people are roughly twice as sensitive to losses as to equivalent gains; and probabilities are not linearly weighted.

## 核心机制 · Core Mechanisms

### 1. 参考点依赖（Reference Dependence）

人们评价结果时，不是看绝对的最终状态，而是看相对于**参考点**（通常是当前状态/现状）的变化——是收益还是损失。参考点的选择极大地影响决策。

People evaluate outcomes not by their absolute final state but by changes relative to a **reference point** (typically the current status quo) — whether the outcome is a gain or a loss. The choice of reference point profoundly influences decisions.

**例子**：年薪从30万涨到50万的人，比从80万降到60万的人感到更满意——尽管后者的绝对收入更高。参考点分别是30万和80万。

**Example**: A person whose salary rises from $40K to $65K feels more satisfied than one whose salary drops from $100K to $80K — despite the latter's higher absolute income. The reference points are $40K and $100K respectively.

### 2. 损失厌恶（Loss Aversion）

**损失带来的痛苦约为同等收益快乐的两倍**。在大多数研究中，损失厌恶系数（λ）约为2.0–2.5。这意味着人们需要至少两倍于潜在损失的收益才会接受一个50/50的风险赌注。

**The pain of losses is roughly twice the pleasure of equivalent gains.** In most studies, the loss aversion coefficient (λ) is approximately 2.0–2.5. This means people require at least twice the potential loss in gains to accept a 50/50 gamble.

**例子**：如果抛硬币输了要付100元，大多数人需要至少赢200元才愿意参与这个赌局。

**Example**: If losing a coin flip costs $100, most people need to win at least $200 to be willing to take the gamble.

### 3. 敏感度递减（Diminishing Sensitivity）

无论是收益还是损失，边际价值都随金额增大而递减。也就是说，从0到100的差别在心理上的冲击大于从900到1000的差别。这导致：
- 面对收益时，人们倾向于**风险规避**（确定拿走较小收益，而非赌取更大收益）
- 面对损失时，人们倾向于**风险寻求**（赌一把有机会避免损失，而非接受确定的较小损失）

Whether for gains or losses, marginal value diminishes as amounts increase. The psychological impact of going from 0 to 100 is greater than going from 900 to 1000. This leads to:
- **Risk aversion** when facing gains (preferring a certain smaller gain over a gamble for a larger one)
- **Risk seeking** when facing losses (preferring a gamble that might avoid the loss over accepting a certain smaller loss)

### 4. 概率加权（Probability Weighting）

人们不是按照概率的客观值来评估风险，而是通过一个非线性的**决策权重函数**进行转换：
- **高估小概率事件**（解释彩票购买和保险购买）
- **低估大概率事件**（解释为什么人们在接近确定的收益面前仍然选择规避风险）

People do not evaluate risk according to objective probability values but transform them through a nonlinear **decision weighting function**:
- **Overweighting of small probabilities** (explains lottery and insurance purchases)
- **Underweighting of large probabilities** (explains why people remain risk-averse even in the face of near-certain gains)

## 发展脉络 · Historical Development

### 前史：期望效用理论的困境 · Prelude: The Crisis of Expected Utility Theory

冯·诺依曼和摩根斯坦（1944）的期望效用理论假设理性行动者总是选择最大化期望效用的选项。阿莱悖论（Allais Paradox, 1953）和埃尔斯伯格悖论（Ellsberg Paradox, 1961）揭示了人们系统性地违反期望效用公理，但缺乏替代性的理论框架。

Von Neumann and Morgenstern's (1944) expected utility theory assumed that rational agents always choose options maximizing expected utility. The Allais Paradox (1953) and Ellsberg Paradox (1961) revealed systematic violations of expected utility axioms, but lacked an alternative theoretical framework.

### 1979年：前景理论的提出 · 1979: Birth of Prospect Theory

卡尼曼与特沃斯基在《Econometrica》发表"Prospect Theory: An Analysis of Decision under Risk"。这篇论文以严格的实验证据和简洁的数学模型，直接挑战了期望效用理论的描述性有效性。论文成为《Econometrica》历史上被引用最多的文章之一。

Kahneman and Tversky published "Prospect Theory: An Analysis of Decision under Risk" in *Econometrica*. With rigorous experimental evidence and elegant mathematical models, this paper directly challenged the descriptive validity of expected utility theory. It became one of the most-cited articles in *Econometrica*'s history.

### 1992年：累积前景理论 · 1992: Cumulative Prospect Theory

特沃斯基和卡尼曼提出累积前景理论（Cumulative Prospect Theory），修正了原始版本中概率加权函数的技术问题（可能违反随机支配原则），使用秩依赖的权重函数，使理论在数学上更加严谨。

Tversky and Kahneman proposed Cumulative Prospect Theory, correcting technical problems with the original probability weighting function (which could violate stochastic dominance), using rank-dependent weighting functions that made the theory mathematically more rigorous.

### 2002年至今：广泛应用 · 2002–Present: Widespread Application

前景理论成为行为经济学的核心理论框架，被广泛应用于金融学（行为金融学的基础）、保险学、健康经济学、法律经济学等领域。2002年卡尼曼获诺贝尔经济学奖，前景理论是核心贡献。

Prospect Theory became the core theoretical framework of behavioral economics, widely applied in finance (the foundation of behavioral finance), insurance, health economics, law and economics, and other fields. Kahneman's 2002 Nobel Prize in Economics centered on this contribution.

## 临床应用 · Applications

### 金融投资 · Financial Investment
- **处置效应（Disposition Effect）**：投资者倾向于过早卖出盈利股票（锁定收益/风险规避）、过久持有亏损股票（拒绝实现损失/损失厌恶）。
- **框架效应**：投资顾问将同一策略呈现为"避免损失"而非"获得收益"时，客户更愿意接受风险。

- **Disposition Effect**: Investors tend to sell winning stocks too early (locking in gains / risk aversion) and hold losing stocks too long (refusing to realize losses / loss aversion).
- **Framing**: Investment advisors who present the same strategy as "avoiding losses" rather than "gaining returns" find clients more willing to accept risk.

### 消费行为 · Consumer Behavior
- **免费试用**：用户获得产品后，禀赋效应（损失厌恶的表现）使他们不愿归还。
- **限时优惠**：框架为"即将失去的折扣"而非"可获得的节省"，激活损失厌恶。

- **Free trials**: After receiving a product, the endowment effect (a manifestation of loss aversion) makes users reluctant to return it.
- **Limited-time offers**: Framed as "a discount about to be lost" rather than "savings to be gained," activating loss aversion.

### 公共政策 · Public Policy
- **器官捐献**：将默认选项设为"同意捐献"（opt-out）而非"需要主动登记"（opt-in），利用现状偏见（参考点依赖的表现）大幅提高捐献率。
- **税收合规**：告知纳税人"95%的人已按时纳税"（社会规范框架）比强调罚款更有效。

- **Organ donation**: Setting the default to "consent to donate" (opt-out) rather than "requires active registration" (opt-in), leveraging status quo bias (a manifestation of reference dependence) to dramatically increase donation rates.
- **Tax compliance**: Informing taxpayers that "95% of people pay on time" (social norm framing) is more effective than emphasizing penalties.

## 东西方对话 · East-West Dialogue

### 佛教"苦"的概念 · Buddhist Concept of Dukkha
佛教第一圣谛——苦（dukkha）——可被解读为一种深刻的"损失厌恶心理学"：佛陀观察到，有情的根本困境是对失去的恐惧（对"无常"的抗拒）远比对获得的满足更为强烈。这与前景理论的核心发现——损失厌恶——形成跨时空的呼应。佛教的解脱之道——放下执著——可被理解为一种根本性的"去损失厌恶"训练。

The First Noble Truth — *dukkha* (suffering/unsatisfactoriness) — can be read as a profound "psychology of loss aversion": the Buddha observed that the fundamental predicament of sentient beings is that fear of loss (resistance to impermanence) is far more intense than satisfaction from gain. This echoes prospect theory's core finding — loss aversion — across time and culture. The Buddhist path of liberation — releasing attachment — can be understood as a fundamental "de-loss-aversion" training.

### 老子 · Laozi
《道德经》"祸兮福之所倚，福兮祸之所伏"直接揭示了参考点依赖的相对性：祸与福不是绝对的，而是相对于观察者的参考框架。老子"知足不辱，知止不殆"则是一种主动管理参考点的智慧——通过降低参考点来减少"损失"的可能性。

The *Daodejing*'s "Misfortune is where fortune leans; fortune is where misfortune lurks" directly reveals the relativity of reference points: misfortune and fortune are not absolute but relative to the observer's reference frame. Laozi's "knowing contentment avoids disgrace; knowing when to stop avoids danger" represents a wisdom of actively managing reference points — lowering the reference point to reduce the possibility of "loss."
