# 范德科尔克生平年表 · Bessel van der Kolk Life Timeline

## 早年与教育 / Early Life and Education

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|:---:|:---|
| 1943 | 出生于荷兰海牙 / Born in The Hague, Netherlands |
| 1950s–1960s | 少年时期随家人移居美国 / Emigrated to the United States as a young man |
| 1960s | 在夏威夷大学求学 / Studied at the University of Hawaii |
| ~1970 | 获得波士顿大学医学院医学博士（M.D.）学位 / Earned M.D. from Boston University School of Medicine |
| 1970s | 完成精神科住院医师培训；越南战争结束，大量退伍军人涌入精神病院 / Completed psychiatric residency training; Vietnam War ended, veterans flooded psychiatric hospitals |

## 早期职业生涯 / Early Career

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|:---:|:---|
| 1970s | 在临床实践中发现传统"谈话疗法"对PTSD患者帮助有限，开始转向神经科学研究 / Discovered traditional "talk therapy" offered limited help for PTSD patients, began turning toward neuroscience |
| 1980s | 在波士顿创伤中心的研究团队率先利用PET扫描研究创伤记忆激活时的大脑功能变化 / Research team at Boston Trauma Center was among the first to use PET scans to study brain function during traumatic memory activation |
| 1982 | 在波士顿创建创伤中心（Trauma Center，原HRI Hospital），担任医学主任 / Founded the Trauma Center in Boston (originally at HRI Hospital), serving as Medical Director |
| 1987 | 主编《Psychological Trauma》，首次系统论述创伤的心理生物学框架 / Edited *Psychological Trauma*, first systematic account of psychobiology of trauma |

## 中期：发展性创伤与身体导向治疗 / Middle Period: Developmental Trauma and Body-Oriented Therapies

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|:---:|:---|
| 1990s | 研究重心从成人PTSD转向儿童发展性创伤 / Shifted research focus from adult PTSD to childhood developmental trauma |
| 1993 | 主编 *Posttraumatic Therapy and Victims of Violence* / Edited *Posttraumatic Therapy and Victims of Violence* |
| 1996 | 主编里程碑式教科书 *Traumatic Stress*，确立创伤研究的独立学科地位 / Edited landmark textbook *Traumatic Stress*, establishing trauma research as independent discipline |
| 1990s–2000s | 系统探索EMDR、瑜伽、神经反馈和戏剧治疗等身体导向方法的临床效果 / Systematically explored clinical effects of EMDR, yoga, neurofeedback, and theater-based therapy |
| 2000 | 在《新英格兰医学杂志》发表"The Black Hole of Trauma" / Published "The Black Hole of Trauma" in *New England Journal of Medicine* |
| 2005 | 向DSM-V工作组提交发展性创伤障碍（DTD）提案 / Submitted DTD proposal to DSM-V task force |
| 2005–2010 | 持续推动EMDR和瑜伽治疗PTSD的实证研究 / Continued advancing empirical research on EMDR and yoga for PTSD treatment |

## 晚期：大众传播与系统性变革 / Late Period: Public Communication and Systemic Change

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|:---:|:---|
| 2014 | 出版《身体从未忘记》（*The Body Keeps the Score*），迅速成为全球畅销书 / Published *The Body Keeps the Score*, quickly becoming a global bestseller |
| 2014–2020 | 该书在 *纽约时报* 畅销榜上停留超过五年 / Book remained on *New York Times* bestseller list for over five years |
| 2015 | 为赫曼《创伤与恢复》新版撰写序言 / Wrote foreword for new edition of Herman's *Trauma and Recovery* |
| 2015– | 推动"创伤知情"（trauma-informed）理念在教育、医疗、司法和社会服务系统中的普及 / Promoted "trauma-informed" principles in education, healthcare, justice, and social services |
| 2018– | 持续发表关于神经反馈、瑜伽和EMDR的实证研究 / Continued publishing empirical research on neurofeedback, yoga, and EMDR |
| 2020s | 将目光投向大规模社会创伤——战争难民危机、新冠疫情创伤、种族暴力和气候灾难 / Turned attention to large-scale social trauma — war refugee crises, pandemic trauma, racial violence, and climate disasters |

## 影响力与认可 / Impact and Recognition

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|:---:|:---|
| 2000s– | 国际创伤应激研究学会（ISTSS）早期核心成员 / Early core member of ISTSS |
| 2010s | 推动复杂性PTSD在ICD-11中的正式收录 / Contributed to formal inclusion of Complex PTSD in ICD-11 |
| 2010s– | 创伤中心发展为创伤中心附属网络（Trauma Center Affiliates），在全球培训数千名治疗师 / Trauma Center grew into Trauma Center Affiliates network, training thousands worldwide |
| 2020s | 《身体从未忘记》全球售出逾三百万册，翻译为三十余种语言 / *The Body Keeps the Score* sold over 3 million copies in 30+ languages |
