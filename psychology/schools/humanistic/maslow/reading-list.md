# 马斯洛 · Abraham Maslow - 推荐阅读 / Reading List

## 一、原典 / Primary Works

### 核心著作 / Core Works

| 优先级 / Priority | 著作 / Work | 说明 / Description |
|:---|:---|:---|
| ★★★ | Maslow, A. H. (1954). *Motivation and Personality*. Harper & Row. | 需求层次理论的系统阐述；自我实现者的首次实证研究。中译：《动机与人格》，许金声等译，中国人民大学出版社。/ Systematic formulation of hierarchy of needs; first empirical study of self-actualizers. |
| ★★★ | Maslow, A. H. (1962). *Toward a Psychology of Being*. Van Nostrand. | 存在性价值、高峰体验、B-认知、元动机——马斯洛理论最成熟的表达。中译：《存在心理学探索》，李文湉译，云南人民出版社。/ Being-values, peak experiences, B-cognition, metamotivation — Maslow's most mature theoretical expression. |
| ★★☆ | Maslow, A. H. (1971). *The Farther Reaches of Human Nature*. Viking Press. | 遗著，涵盖自我超越、元动机、Z理论和高原体验。中译：《人性能达的境界》，林方译，云南人民出版社。/ Posthumous; covers self-transcendence, metamotivation, Theory Z, plateau experience. |
| ★★☆ | Maslow, A. H. (1964). *Religions, Values, and Peak-Experiences*. Ohio State University Press. | 高峰体验与宗教体验的系统比较。中译：《宗教、价值与高峰体验》。/ Systematic comparison of peak and religious experiences. |
| ★☆☆ | Maslow, A. H. (1966). *The Psychology of Science*. Harper & Row. | 科学认识论的人本批判；方法论多元主义。中译：《科学心理学》。/ Humanistic critique of scientific epistemology; methodological pluralism. |
| ★☆☆ | Maslow, A. H. (1965). *Eupsychian Management*. Richard D. Irwin. | 人本主义管理学；Z理论的前驱。/ Humanistic management; precursor to Theory Z. |

### 关键论文 / Key Articles

- Maslow, A. H. (1943). "A Theory of Human Motivation." *Psychological Review*, 50(4), 370–396. — 需求层次理论的原始论文 / Original paper on hierarchy of needs
- Maslow, A. H. (1950). "Self-Actualizing People: A Study of Psychological Health." — 自我实现者的首次系统描述 / First systematic description of self-actualizers
- Maslow, A. H. (1959). "Cognition of Being in the Peak-Experiences." *Proceedings of the American Philosophical Society*. — B-认知理论 / B-cognition theory
- Maslow, A. H. (1969). "Theory Z." *Journal of Transpersonal Psychology*, 1(2), 31–47. — 超越X/Y的管理哲学 / Management philosophy beyond X/Y

## 三、英语世界重要研究 / Important English-Language Scholarship

### 当代重述与修订 / Contemporary Restatements and Revisions

| 优先级 / Priority | 著作 / Work | 说明 / Description |
|:---|:---|:---|
| ★★★ | Kaufman, S. B. (2020). *Transcend: The New Science of Self-Actualization*. TarcherPerigee. | 以当代实证研究重新验证马斯洛理论；修订需求层次为"帆船模型"。/ Re-validates Maslow with contemporary empirical research; revises hierarchy into "sailboat model." |

| ★★☆ | Rowan, J. (2001). *Ordinary Ecstasy: The Dialectics of Humanistic Psychology* (3rd ed.). Brunner-Routledge. | 人本主义心理学的辩证法分析。/ Dialectical analysis of humanistic psychology. |
| ★☆☆ | Koltko-Rivera, M. E. (2006). "Rediscovering the Later Version of Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs: Self-Transcendence." *Review of General Psychology*, 10(4), 302–317. | 重新发现马斯洛晚期的自我超越层。/ Rediscovery of Maslow's late self-transcendence level. |

- Tay, L., & Diener, E. (2011). "Needs and Subjective Well-Being Around the World." *Journal of Personality and Social Psychology*, 101(2), 354–365. — 跨文化实证检验 / Cross-cultural empirical test
- Fallatah, H. M., & Syed, E. H. (2010). "A Critical Review of Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs." *European Journal of Social Sciences*, 16(3), 409–419.

## 阅读建议 / Reading Suggestions

### 入门路径 / Beginner's Path

1. 先读 Maslow (1954) 第3-5章（需求层次）和第11-14章（自我实现者）
2. 再读 Hoffman (1988) 传记的关键章节
3. 然后读 Kaufman (2020) 的当代重述

Start with Maslow (1954) Chs. 3–5 (hierarchy) and Chs. 11–14 (self-actualizers), then Hoffman (1988) key biographical chapters, then Kaufman (2020) for contemporary restatement.

### 进阶路径 / Advanced Path

1. 系统阅读 Maslow (1962)《存在心理学探索》
2. 对照阅读 Rogers (1961) 和 Frankl (1959)
3. 深入 Kenrick et al. (2010) 和 Koltko-Rivera (2006) 的学术争论
4. 拓展到 Wilber (2000) 的整合心理学框架

Systematically read Maslow (1962), compare with Rogers (1961) and Frankl (1959), engage the Kenrick et al. (2010) and Koltko-Rivera (2006) debates, then extend to Wilber (2000)'s integral framework.
