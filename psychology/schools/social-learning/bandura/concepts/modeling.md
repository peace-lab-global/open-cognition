# 榜样示范 · Modeling

## 定义 / Definition

**榜样示范**（Modeling）是指通过展示行为（及其后果）来传递知识、技能、价值观和行为模式的过程。在社会认知理论中，示范不仅是观察学习的触发条件，更是社会化的核心机制——人类的文化知识、社会规范和行为模式在很大程度上是通过代际间的示范传递的。

**Modeling** refers to the process of transmitting knowledge, skills, values, and behavioral patterns by demonstrating behavior (and its consequences). In social cognitive theory, modeling is not merely the triggering condition for observational learning but the core mechanism of socialization — human cultural knowledge, social norms, and behavioral patterns are largely transmitted through intergenerational modeling.

班杜拉区分了三种示范类型：

Bandura distinguished three types of modeling:

- **真实示范** / Live modeling：一个真实的人在观察者面前展示行为。A real person demonstrates behavior in front of the observer.
- **符号示范** / Symbolic modeling：通过媒体（书籍、电影、电视、互联网）展示的行为。Behavior demonstrated through media (books, film, television, internet).
- **参与式示范** / Participant modeling：示范者展示行为后，引导观察者逐步模仿并提供反馈。The model demonstrates behavior, then guides the observer through progressive imitation with feedback.

## 核心机制 / Core Mechanisms

### 示范的三种效果 / Three Effects of Modeling

班杜拉指出示范可以产生三种不同的效果：

Bandura identified three distinct effects that modeling can produce:

**1. 观察学习效应 / Observational Learning Effect**

观察者从模型那里学到新的行为模式——这是示范最基本、最直接的效果。学到的行为可以是具体的动作序列（如烹饪步骤），也可以是抽象的行为规则（如「在这个社交场合中人们如何互动」）。

The observer acquires new behavioral patterns from the model — this is the most basic and direct effect of modeling. Learned behaviors can be specific action sequences (e.g., cooking steps) or abstract behavioral rules (e.g., "how people interact in this social setting").

**2. 抑制/去抑制效应 / Inhibitory/Disinhibitory Effect**

当模型因某行为受到惩罚时，观察者执行该行为的倾向降低（抑制效应）；当模型因某行为受到奖赏或未受惩罚时，观察者执行该行为的倾向增加（去抑制效应）。这种效应不涉及新行为的学习，而是影响已有行为的表现概率。

When a model is punished for a behavior, observers' tendency to perform that behavior decreases (inhibitory effect); when a model is rewarded or not punished, observers' tendency increases (disinhibitory effect). This effect does not involve learning new behaviors but affects the performance probability of existing behaviors.

**3. 反应促进效应 / Response Facilitation Effect**

模型的行为作为一种社会线索，提示观察者在该情境中什么是适当的行为——从而促进观察者表现出已有的（而非新学的）行为。例如，看到他人捐款会促进自己也捐款的倾向——不是因为学到了「如何捐款」，而是因为被提示了「在这个情境中捐款是适当的」。

The model's behavior serves as a social cue, indicating to the observer what is appropriate behavior in that situation — thereby facilitating the observer's performance of existing (not newly learned) behaviors. For example, seeing others donate promotes one's own tendency to donate — not because one learns "how to donate," but because one is cued that "donating is appropriate in this situation."

### 影响示范效果的因素 / Factors Influencing Modeling Effectiveness

**模型特征 / Model Characteristics**：
- **能力感知** / Perceived competence：观察者更可能模仿被认为有能力、有地位的模型。Observers are more likely to imitate models perceived as competent and high-status.
- **相似性** / Similarity：与观察者越相似的模型（年龄、性别、背景），示范效果越强。Models more similar to observers (age, gender, background) produce stronger modeling effects.
- **吸引力** / Attractiveness：有吸引力的模型更容易被模仿。Attractive models are more readily imitated.

**示范方式 / Modeling Approach**：
- **掌握示范** / Mastery modeling：模型从一开始就完美执行目标行为。The model performs the target behavior flawlessly from the start.
- **应对示范** / Coping modeling：模型起初也有困难和错误，但通过努力逐步克服。The model initially struggles and makes errors but gradually overcomes them through effort.

研究表明，**应对示范通常比掌握示范更有效**——因为观察者不仅学到了目标行为，还学到了面对困难时如何坚持和应对。当观察者自己遇到困难时，他们会回忆起模型是如何克服困难的，从而增强自我效能感。

Research shows that **coping modeling is generally more effective than mastery modeling** — because observers learn not only the target behavior but also how to persist and cope when facing difficulties. When observers encounter their own difficulties, they recall how the model overcame challenges, thereby enhancing self-efficacy.

### 抽象建模 / Abstract Modeling

班杜拉强调观察学习不等于简单模仿——观察者可以从多个模型的不同行为中提取出**抽象规则**，然后将这些规则应用到新情境中。例如，儿童通过观察多个成人的道德判断行为，提取出一般的道德原则（如「公平」），然后将这些原则应用到从未见过的道德两难情境中。

Bandura emphasized that observational learning is not simple imitation — observers can extract **abstract rules** from the different behaviors of multiple models, then apply these rules to novel situations. For example, children observe multiple adults' moral judgments, extract general moral principles (e.g., "fairness"), and apply them to moral dilemmas they have never encountered before.

这种抽象建模能力是人类文化传递的核心机制——每一代人不需要从零开始学习所有知识，而是通过观察上一代人的行为模式，提取其中的规则和原则，然后在此基础上创新。

This abstract modeling capacity is the core mechanism of human cultural transmission — each generation does not need to learn everything from scratch but extracts rules and principles from observing the preceding generation's behavioral patterns, then innovates on that foundation.

## 发展脉络 / Development

### 从模仿到建模 / From Imitation to Modeling

早期的模仿研究（如Miller & Dollard, 1941）将模仿视为一种简单的复制行为。班杜拉的贡献在于将模仿概念扩展为「建模」——一个涉及认知加工、规则提取和创造性应用的复杂过程。

Early imitation research (e.g., Miller & Dollard, 1941) treated imitation as a simple copying behavior. Bandura's contribution was expanding the concept of imitation into "modeling" — a complex process involving cognitive processing, rule extraction, and creative application.

### 符号示范的扩展 / Expansion of Symbolic Modeling

随着大众媒体（电视、电影）和数字媒体（社交媒体、视频平台）的发展，符号示范的影响力急剧增长。班杜拉从1960年代就开始关注媒体暴力的示范效应——波波玩偶实验的直接延伸就是关于电视暴力对儿童行为影响的研究。

With the development of mass media (television, film) and digital media (social media, video platforms), the influence of symbolic modeling has grown dramatically. Bandura began关注 media violence modeling effects from the 1960s — the Bobo Doll experiment's direct extension was research on television violence's effects on children's behavior.

### 当代发展 / Contemporary Developments

21世纪以来，建模研究在几个方向上取得了重要进展：（1）视频示范（video modeling）在自闭症干预中的广泛应用；（2）在线学习中的示范设计（如思维可视化、问题解决过程展示）；（3）社交媒体影响者作为符号模型对青少年行为的影响研究。

Since the 21st century, modeling research has made important advances in several directions: (1) widespread application of video modeling in autism intervention; (2) modeling design in online learning (e.g., thinking visualization, problem-solving process demonstration); (3) research on social media influencers as symbolic models affecting adolescent behavior.

## 临床应用 / Clinical Applications

### 参与式建模治疗恐惧症 / Participant Modeling for Phobias

这是班杜拉发展的最有效的恐惧症治疗方法之一。步骤：（1）治疗师示范与恐惧刺激的安全互动；（2）引导患者在治疗师陪同下逐步接触恐惧刺激；（3）逐步撤除治疗师的支持，直到患者能够独立面对恐惧刺激。

This is one of the most effective phobia treatment methods developed by Bandura. Steps: (1) therapist models safe interaction with the feared stimulus; (2) guides patient through progressive contact with the feared stimulus with therapist accompaniment; (3) gradually withdraws therapist support until patient can face the feared stimulus independently.

### 社交技能示范 / Social Skills Modeling

广泛应用于社交焦虑障碍、自闭症谱系障碍和儿童社交技能训练。可以通过真实示范（治疗师或同伴）、视频示范或角色扮演来实现。

Widely applied in social anxiety disorder, autism spectrum disorder, and children's social skills training. Can be implemented through live modeling (therapist or peer), video modeling, or role-playing.

### 父母训练 / Parent Training

许多育儿项目（如Incredible Years、Triple P）的核心组件就是通过示范教父母如何使用积极的教养策略——包括如何设定规则、如何使用正面强化、如何应对问题行为。

Many parenting programs (e.g., Incredible Years, Triple P) have core components that teach parents positive parenting strategies through modeling — including how to set rules, use positive reinforcement, and respond to problem behaviors.

## 关联概念 / Related Concepts

- **[观察学习 / Observational Learning](observational-learning.md)**：示范是观察学习的刺激端——没有示范就没有观察学习。Modeling is the stimulus end of observational learning — without modeling there is no observational learning.
- **[社会学习 / Social Learning](social-learning.md)**：示范是社会学习的核心传递机制。Modeling is the core transmission mechanism of social learning.
- **[自我效能感 / Self-Efficacy](self-efficacy.md)**：替代经验（通过观察相似模型的成败）是自我效能感的重要来源。Vicarious experience (through observing similar models' successes and failures) is an important source of self-efficacy.
- **[交互决定论 / Reciprocal Determinism](reciprocal-determinism.md)**：示范效果受到环境因素（如文化背景）和个体因素（如自我效能感）的调节。Modeling effects are moderated by environmental factors (e.g., cultural context) and personal factors (e.g., self-efficacy).

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

**与儒家「师者，所以传道受业解惑也」/ Confucian "The Teacher Transmits the Way"**：儒家传统中，教师不仅是知识的传递者，更是人格的示范者——「学高为师，身正为范」。这与班杜拉的示范理论高度一致：最有效的学习来自观察有能力的模型的行为，并从中提取规则和原则。

In the Confucian tradition, teachers are not only knowledge transmitters but also character models — "great learning makes a teacher, upright conduct makes a role model." This aligns closely with Bandura's modeling theory: the most effective learning comes from observing competent models' behavior and extracting rules and principles.

**与禅宗「机锋」/ Zen "Encounter Dialogue"**：禅宗公案中，师父往往不通过言语教导，而是通过看似荒谬的行为（如赵州茶、南泉斩猫）来「示范」——弟子需要从这些行为中提取超越言语的悟性。这是一种极端的抽象建模——弟子需要超越表面行为，领悟深层原则。

In Zen koans, masters often teach not through words but through seemingly absurd actions (e.g., Zhaozhou's tea, Nanquan killing the cat) as "modeling" — disciples must extract insight beyond words from these behaviors. This is an extreme form of abstract modeling — disciples must transcend surface behavior to grasp deep principles.
