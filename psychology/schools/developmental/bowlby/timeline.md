# 约翰·鲍尔比 · John Bowlby - 生平年表 / Life Timeline

## 童年与教育 / Childhood and Education (1907–1933)

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|:---:|------|
| 1907 | 2月26日出生于伦敦，全名爱德华·约翰·莫斯特·鲍尔比（Edward John Mostyn Bowlby）。父亲安东尼·阿尔弗雷德·鲍尔比爵士（Sir Anthony Alfred Bowlby）是国王爱德华七世的外科医生。母亲玛丽安·布里奇特·霍奇森（Marian Bridget Hodgson）。家中六个孩子中的第四个。 / Born 26 February in London. Father Sir Anthony Alfred Bowlby, surgeon to King Edward VII. Mother Marian Bridget Hodgson. Fourth of six children. |
| 1907–1913 | 主要由保姆艾米丽·纽盖特（Emily Newgate）照料。母亲信奉上层社会的育儿理念，每天仅与孩子接触约一小时。 / Raised primarily by nanny Emily Newgate. Mother adhered to upper-class childrearing norms, spending approximately one hour daily with children. |
| ~1913 | 约六岁时，深爱的保姆离开家庭。鲍尔比后来将此描述为一生中最重要的丧失经验之一，这一经历被认为是他终生关注分离与丧失的个人根源。 / At approximately age six, beloved nanny departed the family. Bowlby later described this as one of life's most significant loss experiences, considered the personal root of his lifelong preoccupation with separation and loss. |
| 1920–1926 | 就读于马尔伯勒公学（Marlborough College），一所著名的英国公学。 / Attended Marlborough College, a prominent English public school. |
| 1926–1928 | 进入剑桥大学三一学院（Trinity College, Cambridge），最初学习自然科学，后转向医学预科。 / Entered Trinity College, Cambridge, initially studying natural sciences, later switching to pre-medical studies. |
| 1928 | 获得自然科学学士学位（BA in Natural Sciences）。 / Received BA in Natural Sciences. |
| 1928–1933 | 在伦敦大学学院医院（University College Hospital, London）学习医学。期间开始对精神病学和精神分析产生兴趣。 / Studied medicine at University College Hospital, London. During this period, developed interest in psychiatry and psychoanalysis. |
| 1933 | 获得医学学士学位（MB BChir）。开始在莫兹利医院（Maudsley Hospital）接受精神病学培训。 / Received medical degree (MB BChir). Began psychiatric training at the Maudsley Hospital. |

## 精神分析训练与早期职业生涯 / Psychoanalytic Training and Early Career (1933–1950)

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|:---:|------|
| 1933 | 开始在伦敦精神分析研究所（Institute of Psycho-Analysis, London）接受精神分析训练。 / Began psychoanalytic training at the Institute of Psycho-Analysis, London. |
| 1936 | 开始在伦敦东区 guidhall clinic（后来的塔维斯托克诊所）工作，担任精神科医师，专门诊治"问题儿童"。 / Began work at the London Child Guidance Clinic (later associated with the Tavistock Clinic) as psychiatrist, specializing in "problem children." |
| 1937 | 与厄休拉·朗克罗夫特-斯坦菲尔德（Ursula Longcroft-Stainton）结婚。两人后来育有四个孩子。 / Married Ursula Longcroft-Stainton. They later had four children. |
| 1937–1940 | 梅兰妮·克莱因（Melanie Klein）担任其精神分析训练督导师。鲍尔比深受克莱因的影响，但逐渐对其过度强调内部幻想（phantasy）而忽视真实环境因素的做法产生不满。 / Melanie Klein served as his psychoanalytic training supervisor. Bowlby was deeply influenced by Klein but grew increasingly dissatisfied with her overemphasis on internal phantasy at the expense of real environmental factors. |
| 1939–1945 | 第二次世界大战期间在英国皇家陆军医疗团（Royal Army Medical Corps）服役，军衔升至中校。战争期间他对群体动力学和组织心理学也产生了兴趣。 / Served in the Royal Army Medical Corps during WWII, rising to the rank of Lieutenant Colonel. During the war, also developed interests in group dynamics and organisational psychology. |
| 1944 | 发表《四十四名少年小偷》（*Forty-Four Juvenile Thieves*），这项对44名少年犯罪的开创性研究首次系统揭示了早期母子分离与"无情型人格"之间的统计关联。 / Published *Forty-Four Juvenile Thieves*, a landmark study of 44 juvenile delinquents, first systematically demonstrating the statistical association between early mother-child separation and the "affectionless character." |
| 1946 | 加入新成立的塔维斯托克诊所（Tavistock Clinic），负责儿童与家庭部门。 / Joined the newly reconstituted Tavistock Clinic, heading the Department for Children and Families. |
| 1948 | 詹姆斯·罗伯逊（James Robertson）加入鲍尔比的研究团队，负责观察和记录分离情境中儿童的行为反应。 / James Robertson joined Bowlby's research team, responsible for observing and recording children's behavioural responses in separation situations. |

## 依恋理论的建构 / Construction of Attachment Theory (1950–1970)

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|:---:|------|
| 1951 | 受世界卫生组织（WHO）委托撰写《母性照顾与心理健康》（*Maternal Care and Mental Health*），提出"母性剥夺"假说。该报告在欧洲和美国引发广泛的社会政策讨论，也招致了来自学术界和精神分析界的批评。 / Commissioned by WHO to write *Maternal Care and Mental Health*, proposing the "maternal deprivation" hypothesis. The report provoked widespread social policy discussion in Europe and the US, and drew criticism from academic and psychoanalytic circles. |
| 1952 | 罗伯逊拍摄纪录片《两岁的约翰去医院》（*A Two-Year-Old Goes to Hospital*），以影像方式记录了住院儿童的分离反应三阶段。 / Robertson filmed the documentary *A Two-Year-Old Goes to Hospital*, documenting the three-phase separation response of hospitalised children on film. |
| 1956 | 与玛丽·安斯沃思（Mary Ainsworth）开始在伦敦合作。安斯沃思后来移居美国巴尔的摩，在约翰·霍普金斯大学开展"陌生情境"（Strange Situation）实验。 / Began collaboration with Mary Ainsworth in London. Ainsworth later moved to Baltimore, USA, conducting the "Strange Situation" experiment at Johns Hopkins University. |
| 1957 | 发表重要论文《依恋行为的本能性质》（*An Ethological Approach to Research in Psychoanalysis*），首次将行为学（ethology）框架系统引入精神分析研究。 / Published important paper *An Ethological Approach to Research in Psychoanalysis*, first systematically introducing the ethological framework into psychoanalytic research. |
| 1958 | 发表奠基性论文《儿童对母亲的情感纽带本质》（*The Nature of the Child's Tie to His Mother*），标志着依恋概念的首次系统阐述——明确区分依恋与依赖，提出依恋作为独立行为系统的论点。 / Published foundational paper *The Nature of the Child's Tie to His Mother*, marking the first systematic exposition of the attachment concept — explicitly distinguishing attachment from dependence, advancing the thesis of attachment as an independent behavioural system. |
| 1959 | 发表《分离焦虑》（*Separation Anxiety*），系统阐述分离反应的三阶段模型。 / Published *Separation Anxiety*, systematically elaborating the three-phase model of separation response. |
| 1960 | 发表《婴儿期和幼儿期的哀伤与哀悼》（*Grief and Mourning in Infancy and Early Childhood*），描述婴幼儿的丧失反应。 / Published *Grief and Mourning in Infancy and Early Childhood*, describing loss responses in infants and young children. |
| 1969 | 出版《依恋与丧失》第一卷（*Attachment and Loss, Vol.1: Attachment*），系统建构依恋理论的完整框架——包括依恋行为系统的进化基础、内部工作模型、安全基地概念。这标志着依恋理论作为一个完整理论体系的正式确立。 / Published *Attachment and Loss, Vol.1: Attachment*, systematically constructing the complete framework of attachment theory — including the evolutionary basis of the attachment behavioural system, internal working models, and the secure base concept. This marked the formal establishment of attachment theory as a complete theoretical system. |
| 1969 | 安斯沃思出版《依恋模式》（*Patterns of Attachment*），报告"陌生情境"实验的结果，识别出安全型、回避型、焦虑-矛盾型三种依恋类型，为鲍尔比的理论提供了关键的实证支持。 / Ainsworth published *Patterns of Attachment*, reporting Strange Situation results, identifying secure, avoidant, and anxious-ambivalent attachment patterns, providing crucial empirical support for Bowlby's theory. |

## 理论深化与临床应用 / Theoretical Deepening and Clinical Applications (1970–1990)

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|:---:|------|
| 1973 | 出版《依恋与丧失》第二卷（*Attachment and Loss, Vol.2: Separation: Anxiety and Anger*），深入分析分离反应的心理机制。 / Published *Attachment and Loss, Vol.2: Separation: Anxiety and Anger*, analysing psychological mechanisms of separation response in depth. |
| 1975 | 被选为英国精神分析学会（British Psychoanalytical Society）理事会成员。 / Elected to the Council of the British Psychoanalytical Society. |
| 1979 | 出版论文集《依恋纽带的建立与断裂》（*The Making and Breaking of Affectional Bonds*）。 / Published collected papers *The Making and Breaking of Affectional Bonds*. |
| 1980 | 出版《依恋与丧失》第三卷（*Attachment and Loss, Vol.3: Loss: Sadness and Depression*），完成三部曲。探讨丧失与哀伤的心理动力学，区分正常哀伤与病理性抑郁。 / Published *Attachment and Loss, Vol.3: Loss: Sadness and Depression*, completing the trilogy. Explored psychodynamics of loss and grief, distinguishing normal grief from pathological depression. |
| 1985 | 玛丽·梅因（Mary Main）发表成人依恋访谈（AAI）的初步研究，将依恋理论扩展到成人领域，证实了依恋模式的代际传递。 / Mary Main published initial Adult Attachment Interview (AAI) research, extending attachment theory to adulthood and confirming intergenerational transmission of attachment patterns. |
| 1988 | 出版《安全基地》（*A Secure Base: Parent-Child Attachment and Healthy Human Development*），总结依恋理论在心理治疗中的临床应用，阐述治疗师作为"安全基地"的功能。这是鲍尔比生前出版的最后一部重要著作。 / Published *A Secure Base: Parent-Child Attachment and Healthy Human Development*, synthesising clinical applications of attachment theory in psychotherapy, articulating the therapist's "secure base" function. This was the last major work published during Bowlby's lifetime. |
| 1989 | 哈赞（Hazan）和谢弗（Shaver）发表成人浪漫依恋的开创性研究，将依恋类型概念应用于成人亲密关系。 / Hazan and Shaver published landmark research on adult romantic attachment, applying attachment pattern concepts to adult intimate relationships. |

## 晚年与遗产 / Later Years and Legacy (1990–)

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|:---:|------|
| 1990 | 9月2日在苏格兰斯凯岛（Isle of Skye）的夏季住所去世，享年83岁。遗作《查尔斯·达尔文传》（*Charles Darwin: A New Life*）于同年由约翰·默里出版社出版。 / Died 2 September at his summer home on the Isle of Skye, Scotland, aged 83. Posthumous work *Charles Darwin: A New Life* published by John Murray the same year. |
| 1993 | 约翰·霍姆斯（John Holmes）出版传记《约翰·鲍尔比与依恋理论》（*John Bowlby and Attachment Theory*），成为最权威的鲍尔比学术传记。 / John Holmes published biography *John Bowlby and Attachment Theory*, becoming the most authoritative Bowlby academic biography. |
| 2000s– | 依恋理论继续蓬勃发展：神经科学研究提供日益丰富的实证支持（催产素、杏仁核、前额叶皮层）；情绪聚焦治疗（EFT）、心智化治疗（MBT）等循证治疗模型广泛传播；成人依恋研究成为社会心理学的重要领域。 / Attachment theory continues to flourish: neuroscience provides increasingly rich empirical support (oxytocin, amygdala, prefrontal cortex); evidence-based treatment models (EFT, MBT) spread widely; adult attachment research becomes a major field in social psychology. |
