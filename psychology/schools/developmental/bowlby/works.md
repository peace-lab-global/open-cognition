# 约翰·鲍尔比 · John Bowlby - 著作年表 / Works Chronology

## 早期论文与报告 / Early Papers and Reports (1940–1958)

| 年份 / Year | 著作 / Work | 类型 / Type | 出版信息 / Publication | 核心内容 / Core Content |
|:---:|------|:---:|------|------|
| 1944 | *Forty-Four Juvenile Thieves: Their Characters and Home-Life* | 专著 / Monograph | London: Bailliere, Tindall & Cox | 对44名少年小偷的临床研究，首次系统揭示早期母子分离与"无情型人格"及反社会行为的统计关联 / Clinical study of 44 juvenile thieves, first systematic demonstration of statistical association between early mother-child separation, "affectionless character," and antisocial behaviour |
| 1951 | *Maternal Care and Mental Health* | WHO报告 / WHO Report | Geneva: World Health Organization | 受WHO委托撰写的关于无家可归儿童心理健康的综合报告，提出"母性剥夺"假说 / WHO-commissioned comprehensive report on mental health of homeless children, proposing the "maternal deprivation" hypothesis |
| 1953 | *Child Care and the Growth of Love* | 普及读物 / Popular | Harmondsworth: Penguin | *Maternal Care and Mental Health* 的大众版本，面向家长和社会工作者 / Popular version of *Maternal Care and Mental Health*, aimed at parents and social workers |
| 1956 | *The Effects of Separation Experiences in Early Childhood: Fact and Fiction* | 论文 / Paper | *Psychoanalytic Study of the Child*, 11, 353–385 | 对分离经验研究的系统回顾和理论反思 / Systematic review and theoretical reflection on separation experience research |
| 1958 | *The Nature of the Child's Tie to His Mother* | 论文 / Paper | *International Journal of Psycho-Analysis*, 39, 350–373 | 依恋概念的首次系统阐述，提出依恋独立于喂养驱力的核心论点 / First systematic exposition of the attachment concept, advancing the core thesis that attachment is independent of feeding drives |

## 依恋理论三部曲 / Attachment and Loss Trilogy (1959–1980)

| 年份 / Year | 著作 / Work | 类型 / Type | 出版信息 / Publication | 核心内容 / Core Content |
|:---:|------|:---:|------|------|
| 1959 | *Separation Anxiety* | 论文 / Paper | *International Journal of Psycho-Analysis*, 41, 1–14 | 分离焦虑的系统阐述，三阶段反应模型的初步提出 / Systematic exposition of separation anxiety, initial proposal of the three-phase response model |
| 1960 | *Grief and Mourning in Infancy and Early Childhood* | 论文 / Paper | *Psychoanalytic Study of the Child*, 15, 9–52 | 婴幼儿哀伤反应的描述，与成人哀伤过程的对比 / Description of infant and young child grief responses, comparison with adult grief processes |
| 1969 | *Attachment and Loss, Vol.1: Attachment* | 专著 / Book | New York: Basic Books | 依恋理论的完整建构——依恋行为系统的进化基础、内部工作模型、安全基地概念、依恋类型的形成 / Complete construction of attachment theory — evolutionary basis of attachment behavioural system, internal working models, secure base concept, formation of attachment patterns |
| 1973 | *Attachment and Loss, Vol.2: Separation: Anxiety and Anger* | 专著 / Book | New York: Basic Books | 分离反应的深入分析——抗议-绝望-脱离三阶段模型、分离焦虑的心理机制、愤怒作为依恋系统激活的信号 / In-depth analysis of separation responses — protest-despair-detachment three-phase model, psychological mechanisms of separation anxiety, anger as signal of attachment system activation |
| 1980 | *Attachment and Loss, Vol.3: Loss: Sadness and Depression* | 专著 / Book | New York: Basic Books | 丧失与哀伤的心理动力学——正常哀伤与病理性抑郁的区分、防御性排除机制、丧失对终身心理健康的影响 / Psychodynamics of loss and grief — distinction between normal grief and pathological depression, defensive exclusion mechanisms, loss's impact on lifelong mental health |

## 后期著作与临床总结 / Later Works and Clinical Synthesis (1980–1990)

| 年份 / Year | 著作 / Work | 类型 / Type | 出版信息 / Publication | 核心内容 / Core Content |
|:---:|------|:---:|------|------|
| 1981 | *Psychoanalysis as an Art and a Craft* | 论文 / Paper | *Bulletin of the Anna Freud Centre*, 4, 63–72 | 对精神分析作为临床实践的反思 / Reflection on psychoanalysis as clinical practice |
| 1986 | *C.G. Jung: A Maverick in Psychoanalysis* | 论文 / Paper | 收录于 *On Knowing in the Sciences of Human Nature* | 对荣格精神分析的评述 / Review of Jung's psychoanalysis |
| 1988 | *A Secure Base: Parent-Child Attachment and Healthy Human Development* | 专著 / Book | New York: Basic Books | 依恋理论的临床应用总结——治疗师作为安全基地、依恋理论在心理治疗中的具体应用、终身依恋视角 / Synthesis of clinical applications of attachment theory — therapist as secure base, specific applications in psychotherapy, lifespan attachment perspective |
| 1990 | *Charles Darwin: A New Life* (posthumous) | 传记 / Biography | London: John Murray | 达尔文传记，体现鲍尔比对进化论的终生兴趣 / Darwin biography, reflecting Bowlby's lifelong interest in evolutionary theory |
