# 母性剥夺 · Maternal Deprivation

## 定义 / Definition

**母性剥夺**（Maternal Deprivation）是鲍尔比在1951年为世界卫生组织（WHO）撰写的报告《母性照顾与心理健康》（*Maternal Care and Mental Health*）中提出的假说，主张婴幼儿期（尤其是生命的前三年）与母亲或母亲替代者的分离或持续缺乏温暖的母性照顾，会对儿童的心理健康和人格发展造成严重的、可能是不可逆的损害。

**Maternal Deprivation** is the hypothesis advanced by Bowlby in his 1951 WHO report *Maternal Care and Mental Health*, proposing that separation from the mother or mother-substitute, or the persistent absence of warm maternal care during infancy and early childhood (especially the first three years), can inflict serious and potentially irreversible damage on the child's mental health and personality development.

鲍尔比区分了两种不同形式的母性剥夺：

Bowlby distinguished two different forms of maternal deprivation:

1. **部分剥夺（Partial Deprivation）**：母亲在场但照顾质量不足——如情感冷淡、忽视、不一致的回应 / Mother is present but care quality is inadequate — e.g., emotionally cold, neglectful, inconsistent responding
2. **完全剥夺（Total Deprivation）**：完全缺乏母亲或母亲替代者的照顾——如机构养育（孤儿院）、长期住院 / Complete absence of mother or mother-substitute care — e.g., institutional care (orphanages), prolonged hospitalisation

## 核心机制 / Core Mechanism

### 剥夺的心理后果 / Psychological Consequences of Deprivation

鲍尔比识别出母性剥夺可能导致的多种心理后果：

Bowlby identified multiple psychological consequences that maternal deprivation may produce:

- **智力发展迟缓（Intellectual Retardation）**：机构养育的儿童在智力测试中的表现普遍低于家庭养育的同龄人，尤其在语言能力和抽象思维方面 / Institutionally reared children consistently underperform family-reared peers on intelligence tests, particularly in language ability and abstract thinking

- **情感冷淡（Emotional Blunting）**：长期缺乏温暖的人际接触可能导致儿童发展出情感上的冷淡和疏离——鲍尔比称之为"无情型人格"（affectionless character），其特征是对他人缺乏深层情感联系、无法形成持久的亲密关系 / Prolonged absence of warm interpersonal contact may lead children to develop emotional coldness and detachment — what Bowlby termed the "affectionless character," characterised by lack of deep emotional connection with others and inability to form lasting intimate relationships

- **反社会行为（Antisocial Behaviour）**：在《四十四名少年小偷》（1944）中，鲍尔比发现14名"无情型"少年中有12人有过早期长期分离的经历 / In *Forty-Four Juvenile Thieves* (1944), Bowlby found that 12 of 14 "affectionless" delinquents had experienced early prolonged separation

- **焦虑和抑郁（Anxiety and Depression）**：丧失依恋对象而未获得充分哀伤支持的儿童可能发展出慢性的焦虑和抑郁倾向 / Children who lose attachment figures without adequate grief support may develop chronic anxiety and depressive tendencies

- **关系困难（Relationship Difficulties）**：早期剥夺的个体在成年后可能在建立和维持亲密关系方面持续遇到困难 / Individuals with early deprivation may continue to face difficulties in forming and maintaining intimate relationships in adulthood

### 敏感期假说 / Sensitive Period Hypothesis

鲍尔比提出，生命的前三年（尤其是6个月至3岁）是依恋关系发展的**敏感期**（sensitive period）。在这一时期内发生的剥夺比在敏感期之后发生的剥夺更具破坏性。这一假说基于行为学的印记（imprinting）概念——洛伦茨发现幼鸟在出生后的特定时间窗口内会形成对第一个移动物体的依附。

Bowlby proposed that the first three years of life (especially 6 months to 3 years) constitute a **sensitive period** for attachment relationship development. Deprivation occurring within this period is more damaging than deprivation occurring later. This hypothesis drew on the ethological concept of imprinting — Lorenz's finding that young birds form attachment to the first moving object during a specific post-natal time window.

然而，鲍尔比后来修正了这一观点，承认敏感期的边界并不像最初设想的那样绝对——在敏感期之后的积极关系经验仍然可以部分补偿早期剥夺的影响。

However, Bowlby later revised this view, acknowledging that the sensitive period's boundaries are not as absolute as initially conceived — positive relational experiences after the sensitive period can still partially compensate for early deprivation.

## 发展脉络 / Development

### 假说的提出与社会反响 / Proposal and Social Impact

1951年的WHO报告产生了巨大的社会影响。鲍尔比的结论——"婴儿和幼儿应该体验与母亲（或母亲替代者）之间温暖的、亲密的、持续的关系"——被广泛解读为支持母亲全职在家的政策立场。这一报告影响了欧洲多国的战后社会政策，包括育儿假制度、托儿服务和收养法规。

The 1951 WHO report had enormous social impact. Bowlby's conclusion — "the infant and young child should experience a warm, intimate, and continuous relationship with the mother (or mother-substitute)" — was widely interpreted as supporting policies favouring mothers staying home full-time. This report influenced post-war social policies across multiple European countries, including parental leave systems, childcare services, and adoption regulations.

### 批评与回应 / Criticism and Response

母性剥夺假说招致了来自多个方向的严厉批评：

The maternal deprivation hypothesis attracted severe criticism from multiple directions:

- **方法论批评**：迈克尔·拉特（Michael Rutter）等学者指出，鲍尔比的许多证据来自机构养育研究，而机构养育中除了母子分离外还伴随着刺激贫乏、照顾者轮换等多种不利因素——因此无法确定损害是"分离"本身还是整体环境剥夺的结果 / Scholars such as Michael Rutter noted that much of Bowlby's evidence came from institutional care studies, where mother-child separation co-occurred with multiple other adverse factors (stimulus poverty, caregiver rotation) — making it impossible to isolate whether damage resulted from "separation" per se or overall environmental deprivation

- **意识形态批评**：女性主义学者批评母性剥夺假说为传统的性别角色分工提供了"科学"背书，给职业母亲施加了不必要的内疚和压力 / Feminist scholars criticised the hypothesis for providing "scientific" endorsement of traditional gender role divisions, imposing unnecessary guilt and pressure on working mothers

- **文化偏见**：跨文化研究表明，在许多文化中（如以色列基布兹集体养育、非洲多照顾者模式），儿童在多人照顾下同样可以健康发展 / Cross-cultural research showed that in many cultures (e.g., Israeli kibbutz collective rearing, African multiple-caregiver models), children can develop healthily under multi-caregiver arrangements

鲍尔比本人对这些批评做出了重要回应。他在后续工作中更加精确地区分了"母性剥夺"与"缺乏敏感照顾"——承认核心问题不在于"母亲是否在场"，而在于"是否有至少一位敏感的、可及的照顾者持续提供安全基地功能"。

Bowlby himself made important responses to these criticisms. In subsequent work he more precisely distinguished "maternal deprivation" from "lack of sensitive care" — acknowledging that the core issue is not "whether the mother is present" but "whether at least one sensitive, available caregiver continuously provides secure base function."

### 当代修正 / Contemporary Revision

拉特在1970-1980年代的研究对母性剥夺假说进行了系统修正：

Rutter's research in the 1970s-1980s systematically revised the maternal deprivation hypothesis:

- 区分了**剥夺**（privation, 从未形成依恋关系）与**分离**（separation, 已形成依恋关系后的断裂）——前者的损害远大于后者 / Distinguished **privation** (never having formed an attachment relationship) from **separation** (disruption of an already-formed attachment relationship) — the former being far more damaging than the latter
- 确认了**恢复的可能性**——罗马尼亚孤儿研究（Romanian Orphan Studies）表明，即使在严重的机构剥夺之后，被收养到温暖家庭中的儿童仍能取得显著的恢复和发展 / Confirmed **the possibility of recovery** — Romanian Orphan Studies showed that even after severe institutional deprivation, children adopted into warm families could achieve significant recovery and development
- 强调了**累积风险**——单一的不利因素（如短暂分离）通常不会造成严重损害，但多个风险因素的累积效应可能是破坏性的 / Emphasised **cumulative risk** — single adverse factors (e.g., brief separation) typically do not cause serious damage, but the cumulative effect of multiple risk factors can be devastating

## 临床应用 / Clinical Applications

### 机构养育质量评估 / Institutional Care Quality Assessment

母性剥夺的概念直接影响了当代机构养育质量评估标准：

The maternal deprivation concept directly influenced contemporary institutional care quality assessment standards:

- **照顾者一致性**：尽量确保每位儿童有固定的主要照顾者（key worker） / Ensure each child has a consistent primary caregiver (key worker) wherever possible
- **小组规模**：限制每个照顾小组的儿童数量，以促进个体化的关注和依恋关系的形成 / Limit the number of children per care group to facilitate individualised attention and attachment relationship formation
- **环境丰富性**：提供充足的认知刺激、社交机会和情感回应 / Provide ample cognitive stimulation, social opportunities, and emotional responsiveness

### 寄养和收养实践 / Foster Care and Adoption Practice

母性剥夺研究对寄养和收养实践的指导包括：

Maternal deprivation research's guidance for foster care and adoption practice includes:

- **尽早安置**：减少儿童在机构中等待安置的时间 / Minimise children's time in institutional care awaiting placement
- **减少安置中断**：每次安置中断都意味着依恋关系的断裂，应尽量避免 / Every placement disruption means an attachment relationship rupture, which should be minimised
- **过渡支持**：在安置变化期间提供充分的情感支持和过渡安排 / Provide ample emotional support and transitional arrangements during placement changes
- **收养后支持**：为收养家庭提供持续的支持，帮助他们理解被收养儿童可能面临的依恋困难 / Provide ongoing support for adoptive families, helping them understand attachment difficulties the adopted child may face

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

母性剥夺假说与中国文化中"孟母三迁"的传统形成了有趣的对照。"孟母三迁"的故事强调母亲主动选择有利于儿童发展的环境——这一传统暗含了对"养育环境质量"（而非仅仅是"母亲在场"）的重视，与鲍尔比后来修正的"敏感照顾质量比母亲身份更重要"的观点一致。

The maternal deprivation hypothesis creates an interesting contrast with the Chinese tradition of "Mencius' mother moving three times" (Meng mu san qian). The story of Mencius' mother emphasizes a mother's active choice of environments conducive to child development — a tradition implicitly valuing "caregiving environment quality" (rather than merely "mother's presence"), consistent with Bowlby's later revised view that "quality of sensitive care matters more than the mother's identity."

同时，中国传统中的"隔代养育"（祖父母照顾孙辈）现象为母性剥夺假说提供了一个自然的文化检验——在中国文化中，祖父母作为"母亲替代者"通常被视为正常且有益的，这与鲍尔比最初的"母亲不可替代"的立场形成了修正性的对话。

Meanwhile, the Chinese tradition of "intergenerational caregiving" (grandparents caring for grandchildren) provides a natural cultural test of the maternal deprivation hypothesis — in Chinese culture, grandparents as "mother-substitutes" are typically regarded as normal and beneficial, creating a revisionary dialogue with Bowlby's initial "mother is irreplaceable" position.

佛教寺院教育传统也可以从这个角度审视——年幼的沙弥（novice monk）离开家庭进入寺院生活，在师父的照顾下成长。这一模式是否构成"母性剥夺"取决于师父是否能够提供足够的敏感照顾和安全基地功能。当代佛教教育中对这一问题的反思——如强调师父的慈悲（karuna）和耐心——与依恋理论的"敏感照顾"概念形成了跨传统的对话。

The Buddhist monastic education tradition can also be examined from this perspective — young novice monks leaving family to enter monastic life, growing up under the master's care. Whether this constitutes "maternal deprivation" depends on whether the master can provide sufficient sensitive care and secure base function. Contemporary Buddhist reflections on this issue — emphasising the master's compassion (karuna) and patience — create a cross-traditional dialogue with attachment theory's "sensitive care" concept.
