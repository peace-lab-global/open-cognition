# 内部工作模型 · Internal Working Models

## 定义 / Definition

**内部工作模型**（Internal Working Models, IWMs）是鲍尔比依恋理论中的核心概念，指个体基于早期与主要照顾者的互动经验，在无意识层面建构的关于**自我**、**他人**和**关系**的心理表征系统。这些模型充当一个"滤镜"或"模板"，在无意识层面组织个体对关系世界的感知、预期和行为反应。

**Internal Working Models (IWMs)** are a central concept in Bowlby's attachment theory, referring to the mental representational system that individuals construct at unconscious levels, based on early interactions with primary caregivers, concerning **self**, **others**, and **relationships**. These models function as a "filter" or "template," unconsciously organising the individual's perception, expectations, and behavioural responses to the relational world.

鲍尔比借用了认知心理学家肯尼斯·克雷克（Kenneth Craik）的"工作模型"概念——克雷克认为人类大脑会建构外部世界的小型化心理模型，用于预测事件和规划行动。鲍尔比将这一概念应用于关系领域，提出了关于依恋对象和自我的内部工作模型。

Bowlby borrowed the "working model" concept from cognitive psychologist Kenneth Craik — who proposed that the human brain constructs small-scale mental models of the external world for predicting events and planning actions. Bowlby applied this concept to the relational domain, proposing internal working models of attachment figures and the self.

## 核心机制 / Core Mechanism

### 两个维度的工作模型 / Two Dimensions of Working Models

内部工作模型包含两个相互关联的维度：

Internal working models comprise two interrelated dimensions:

1. **自我模型（Self Model）**：关于"我是否值得被爱和被关注"的无意识信念。 / Unconscious beliefs about "whether I am worthy of love and attention."
   - 如果照顾者敏感、可及、回应及时 → "我是有价值的、值得被爱的" / If the caregiver is sensitive, available, responsive → "I am valuable, worthy of love"
   - 如果照顾者拒绝、冷淡、忽视 → "我是不值得被爱的、我的需求不重要" / If the caregiver is rejecting, cold, neglectful → "I am unworthy of love, my needs don't matter"

2. **他人模型（Other Model）**：关于"他人是否可信赖和可及"的无意识信念。 / Unconscious beliefs about "whether others are trustworthy and available."
   - 如果照顾者一致地满足儿童的需要 → "他人是可靠的、会在需要时提供帮助" / If the caregiver consistently meets the child's needs → "Others are reliable, will help when needed"
   - 如果照顾者不可预测或不可靠 → "他人是不可信赖的、会在关键时刻缺席" / If the caregiver is unpredictable or unreliable → "Others are untrustworthy, will be absent at critical moments"

### 运作机制 / Operational Mechanisms

内部工作模型一旦形成，通过以下机制自我维持：

Once formed, internal working models self-perpetuate through the following mechanisms:

- **选择性注意（Selective Attention）**：个体倾向于注意与现有工作模型一致的信息，忽视不一致的信息。例如，拥有"他人不可信赖"模型的个体更容易注意他人失信的行为，而忽视他人守信的行为。 / Individuals tend to attend to information consistent with existing working models and ignore inconsistent information. For example, someone with an "others are untrustworthy" model more readily notices others' betrayals while overlooking their faithfulness.

- **确认性偏差（Confirmation Bias）**：个体的行为模式往往引发与工作模型一致的他人反应，从而"证实"原有模型。例如，预期"他人会拒绝我"的个体可能表现出防御性的冷淡，而这种冷淡恰恰引发他人的疏远——形成自我实现的预言。 / Individuals' behavioural patterns often elicit responses from others that confirm existing models. For example, someone who expects "others will reject me" may display defensive coldness, which actually provokes others' distancing — creating a self-fulfilling prophecy.

- **防御性排除（Defensive Exclusion）**：与工作模型相矛盾的经验可能被排除在意识之外——这是一种保护性机制，防止心理结构被突然瓦解，但也限制了模型的更新机会。 / Experiences contradicting the working model may be excluded from awareness — a protective mechanism preventing sudden structural collapse, but also limiting opportunities for model updating.

### 代际传递 / Intergenerational Transmission

内部工作模型具有显著的代际传递性——父母的依恋工作模型影响其养育行为，进而塑造儿童的依恋模式。玛丽·梅因（Mary Main）通过成人依恋访谈（AAI）的研究令人信服地证明了这一传递机制：父母的AAI依恋状态可以高准确率地预测其子女在陌生情境实验中的依恋类型。

Internal working models show significant intergenerational transmission — parents' attachment working models influence their caregiving behaviour, which in turn shapes the child's attachment pattern. Mary Main's research using the Adult Attachment Interview (AAI) compellingly demonstrated this transmission mechanism: parents' AAI attachment states can predict with high accuracy their children's attachment patterns in the Strange Situation.

## 发展脉络 / Development

### 形成过程 / Formation Process

内部工作模型的形成是一个渐进的过程：

The formation of internal working models is a gradual process:

- **0-6个月**：婴儿通过反复的互动经验开始形成对照顾者的初步预期（如"哭泣后是否会被安抚"）/ Infants begin forming preliminary expectations of the caregiver through repeated interaction experiences (e.g., "will crying lead to soothing?")
- **6-12个月**：随着认知能力的发展，这些预期逐渐内化为更稳定的心理表征——内部工作模型的雏形 / As cognitive capacities develop, these expectations gradually internalise into more stable mental representations — the proto-form of internal working models
- **12-36个月**：工作模型变得更加复杂和系统化，开始包含关于自我价值、他人可靠性和关系可预测性的综合性信念 / Working models become more complex and systematic, incorporating integrated beliefs about self-worth, other-reliability, and relationship predictability
- **儿童期至成人期**：工作模型通过新的关系经验持续被修正或强化，但核心结构具有相当的稳定性——因为它们倾向于通过上述自我维持机制抵御矛盾信息 / Working models are continually revised or reinforced through new relational experiences, but core structures show considerable stability — as they tend to resist contradictory information through the self-maintenance mechanisms described above

### 修正与更新 / Revision and Updating

尽管内部工作模型具有稳定性，但它们并非不可改变。鲍尔比指出，工作模型可以通过以下方式被修正：

Although internal working models show stability, they are not immutable. Bowlby noted that working models can be revised through:

- **新的关系经验**：一段持续的、安全的关系（如稳定的伴侣关系、深度的友谊）可以提供与旧模型矛盾的新证据 / New relational experiences: a sustained, secure relationship (e.g., stable partnership, deep friendship) can provide new evidence contradicting the old model
- **心理治疗**：治疗关系本身作为一种"矫正性情感体验"，帮助来访者觉察和修正不适应的工作模型 / Psychotherapy: the therapeutic relationship as a "corrective emotional experience," helping clients become aware of and revise maladaptive working models
- **反思性功能（Reflective Functioning）的提升**：当个体发展出更强的反思自己和他人心智状态的能力时，工作模型可以从僵化变得灵活 / Enhancement of reflective functioning: when individuals develop stronger capacity to reflect on their own and others' mental states, working models can shift from rigid to flexible

玛丽·梅因提出的"习得的安全感"（earned security）概念正是对这一修正过程的最好说明——一些童年经历不安全依恋的个体，通过后续的积极关系经验和深度反思，发展出了安全的依恋状态。

Mary Main's concept of "earned security" perfectly illustrates this revision process — some individuals who experienced insecure attachment in childhood develop secure attachment states through subsequent positive relational experiences and deep reflection.

## 临床应用 / Clinical Applications

### 成人依恋访谈（AAI）/ Adult Attachment Interview

玛丽·梅因开发的成人依恋访谈是目前评估成人内部工作模型最权威的工具。AAI通过20个半结构化问题评估个体对童年依恋经验的**叙述方式**（而非具体内容），识别四种成人依恋状态：

The Adult Attachment Interview developed by Mary Main is the most authoritative instrument for assessing adult internal working models. The AAI evaluates the **narrative manner** (rather than specific content) of individuals' accounts of childhood attachment experiences through 20 semi-structured questions, identifying four adult attachment states:

1. **自主型/安全型（Autonomous/Secure）**：叙述连贯、平衡，能够客观评价父母 / Coherent, balanced narrative, able to evaluate parents objectively
2. **-dismissing型（Dismissing）**：叙述简短、理想化或贬低依恋关系的重要性 / Brief narrative, idealising or devaluing the importance of attachment relationships
3. **沉浸型（Preoccupied）**：叙述混乱、冗长，仍被过去的依恋经验所困扰 / Confused, lengthy narrative, still preoccupied with past attachment experiences
4. **未解决型（Unresolved）**：在涉及丧失或创伤的话题时表现出推理或叙述的瓦解 / Display of reasoning or narrative collapse when discussing loss or trauma topics

### 心理治疗中的应用 / Application in Psychotherapy

在心理治疗中，识别来访者的内部工作模型是治疗工作的关键起点：

In psychotherapy, identifying the client's internal working models is a crucial starting point for therapeutic work:

- 治疗师首先帮助来访者**觉察**自己的工作模型——"我在关系中习惯性地预期什么？" / The therapist first helps the client **become aware** of their working models — "What do I habitually expect in relationships?"
- 然后**探索**这些模型的起源——"这些预期从何而来？它们在我童年时有何功能？" / Then **explore** the origins of these models — "Where do these expectations come from? What function did they serve in my childhood?"
- 最后通过治疗关系中的**新体验**来**修正**不适应的模型——"在治疗中体验到被接纳和被理解，这种感受如何改变我对关系的预期？" / Finally, **revise** maladaptive models through **new experiences** in the therapeutic relationship — "How does experiencing being accepted and understood in therapy change my expectations of relationships?"

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

内部工作模型与唯识学（Yogacara）的"阿赖耶识"（alaya-vijnana）和"种子"（bija）概念之间存在显著的结构相似性。唯识学主张，过去的身口意行为（业/karma）在阿赖耶识中留下"种子"，这些种子在因缘和合时"现行"，影响个体当下的感知和行为。这与内部工作模型中"早期经验形成心理表征，在无意识层面组织后续经验"的机制高度吻合。

Internal working models share significant structural similarities with Yogacara Buddhism's "alaya-vijnana" (storehouse consciousness) and "bija" (seeds) concepts. Yogacara holds that past body-speech-mind actions (karma) leave "seeds" in the alaya-vijnana, which "manifest" when conditions converge, shaping the individual's present perception and behaviour. This closely parallels the mechanism whereby "early experiences form mental representations that unconsciously organise subsequent experience" in internal working models.

两者的差异同样富有启发性：唯识学的种子概念暗示了跨越多世的时间维度，而鲍尔比的工作模型仅限于此生的关系经验。但从实用角度看，两者都为心理转化提供了路径——唯识学通过"转识成智"（transforming consciousness into wisdom）来净化种子，鲍尔比通过新的关系经验来修正工作模型。两者的共同核心洞见是：**改变的关键在于觉察和理解那些无意识运作的心理结构**。

The differences are equally illuminating: Yogacara's seed concept implies a temporal dimension spanning multiple lifetimes, while Bowlby's working models are limited to this life's relational experiences. Yet from a practical perspective, both offer pathways for psychological transformation — Yogacara through "transforming consciousness into wisdom" to purify seeds, Bowlby through new relational experiences to revise working models. The shared core insight is: **the key to change lies in becoming aware of and understanding those mental structures that operate unconsciously**.

在儒家传统中，"性善论"（孟子）与"性恶论"（荀子）的千年辩论也可以从内部工作模型的视角重新审视——个体的"善"或"恶"倾向并非先天固定，而是早期关系经验塑造的工作模型在成年后的表达。这为儒家的"教化"（education/transformation）理想提供了心理学支持：通过良好的教育和关系环境，不安全的工作模型可以被修正。

In the Confucian tradition, the millennium-long debate between "human nature is good" (Mencius) and "human nature is evil" (Xunzi) can be revisited from the internal working models perspective — individuals' "good" or "evil" tendencies are not innately fixed but are expressions of working models shaped by early relational experience. This provides psychological support for the Confucian ideal of "jiaohua" (education/transformation): through good education and relational environments, insecure working models can be revised.
