# 埃里克·埃里克森 · Erik Erikson - 著作年表 / Works Chronology

## 主要著作 / Major Works

| 年份 / Year | 著作 / Title | 出版信息 / Publisher | 核心贡献 / Core Contribution |
|:---:|------|------|------|
| 1950 | *Childhood and Society*《童年与社会》 | New York: W.W. Norton | 心理社会发展八阶段理论的首次系统阐述；融合精神分析、人类学和发展心理学 / First systematic articulation of the eight psychosocial stages; integrating psychoanalysis, anthropology, and developmental psychology |
| 1958 | *Young Man Luther: A Study in Psychoanalysis and History*《青年路德》 | New York: W.W. Norton | 心理历史学方法的开创性应用；分析马丁·路德的身份危机与宗教改革 / Pioneering application of psychohistorical method; analyzing Martin Luther's identity crisis and the Reformation |
| 1959 | *Identity and the Life Cycle*《同一性与生命周期》 | New York: International Universities Press (重印 Norton, 1980) | 同一性概念的系统提出；将同一性放在生命周期框架中讨论 / Systematic formulation of the identity concept within the life cycle framework |
| 1964 | *Insight and Responsibility*《洞见与责任》 | New York: W.W. Norton | 论文集，探讨精神分析的伦理维度和治疗师的"责任"概念 / Essay collection exploring ethical dimensions of psychoanalysis and the therapist's "responsibility" |
| 1968 | *Identity: Youth and Crisis*《同一性：青年与危机》 | New York: W.W. Norton | 同一性理论的系统发展与深化；分析1960年代青年运动和身份认同问题 / Systematic development and deepening of identity theory; analyzing 1960s youth movements and identity issues |
| 1969 | *Gandhi's Truth: On the Origins of Militant Nonviolence*《甘地的真理》 | New York: W.W. Norton | 心理传记研究的典范；分析甘地的道德发展与非暴力抵抗的心理根源 / Exemplary psychobiographical study; analyzing Gandhi's moral development and psychological roots of nonviolent resistance |
| 1975 | *Life History and the Historical Moment*《生命历史与历史时刻》 | New York: W.W. Norton | 心理历史学方法论的系统反思；探讨个人生命史与历史时刻的交互 / Systematic methodological reflection on psychohistory; exploring the interaction between personal life history and historical moments |
| 1981 | "Reflections on the Last Stage—and the First" / "对最后一个阶段——也是第一个阶段——的反思" | *Psychoanalytic Study of the Child* 36 | 对老年期和婴儿期的深入反思 / Deep reflections on old age and infancy |
| 1982 | *The Life Cycle Completed*《生命周期的完成》 | New York: W.W. Norton | 八阶段理论的完整成熟表述；与琼·埃里克森合作 / Complete, mature statement of the eight-stage theory; collaboration with Joan Erikson |
| 1987 | *A Way of Looking at Things: Selected Papers from 30 Years of Work* / 《一种看待事物的方式》 | New York: W.W. Norton (S. Schlein 编) | 30年论文选集；涵盖从早期到成熟期的所有核心主题 / Selected papers spanning 30 years; covering all core themes from early to mature periods |
| 1997 | *The Life Cycle Completed: Extended Version*《生命周期的完成：扩展版》 | New York: W.W. Norton (琼·埃里克森 Joan Erikson 编) | 增加第九阶段；讨论极高龄老人的发展挑战 / Addition of ninth stage; addressing developmental challenges of very old age |

|:---:|------|------|------|

| 1954 | "The Problem of Ego Identity" | *Journal of the American Psychoanalytic Association* | 自我同一性概念的早期系统论述 / Early systematic formulation of ego identity |

| 1986 | "The Galilean Sayings and the Sense of 'I'" | *Psychoanalysis and Contemporary Thought* | 宗教心理学视角下的自我意识 / Self-consciousness from a psychology of religion perspective |
