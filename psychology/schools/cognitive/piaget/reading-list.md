# 皮亚杰 · Jean Piaget - 推荐阅读 / Reading List

## 一、原典（核心著作） / Primary Works (Core Texts)

### 必读入门 / Essential Starting Points

| 著作 / Work | 说明 / Notes |
|---|---|
| 《儿童心理学》 *The Psychology of the Child* (1966, with Inhelder) | 四阶段理论的最精炼综合，约 180 页，是了解皮亚杰思想的最佳起点 / Most concise synthesis of four-stage theory, ~180 pages; best starting point for understanding Piaget |
| 《发生认识论原理》 *Genetic Epistemology* (1970) | 哥伦比亚大学讲座，认识论视角的总纲，约 80 页 / Columbia University lectures; epistemological overview, ~80 pages |
| 《六篇心理学研究》 *Six Psychological Studies* (1963) | 五篇短论涵盖认知发展各核心主题 / Five short essays covering core themes of cognitive development |

### 经典专著 / Classic Monographs

| 著作 / Work | 说明 / Notes |
|---|---|
| 《儿童的语言与思维》 *The Language and Thought of the Child* (1923) | 揭示儿童语言自我中心特征，提出"集体独白" / Revealed egocentric features of children's language; coined "collective monologue" |
| 《儿童的判断与推理》 *Judgment and Reasoning in the Child* (1924) | 转导推理模式的发现 / Discovery of transductive reasoning patterns |
| 《儿童的世界观》 *The Child's Conception of the World* (1926) | 泛灵论、人造论等儿童宇宙观特征 / Animism, artificialism, and features of children's worldview |
| 《儿童的道德判断》 *The Moral Judgment of the Child* (1932) | 从他律到自律的道德发展研究 / Moral development from heteronomy to autonomy |
| 《儿童智慧的起源》 *The Origins of Intelligence in Children* (1952) | 感知运动期六个亚阶段的完整追踪 / Complete tracing of six sensorimotor substages |
| 《儿童现实的建构》 *The Construction of Reality in the Child* (1937) | 客体永久性概念的详细研究 / Detailed study of the object permanence concept |
| 《儿童符号功能的形成》 *Play, Dreams and Imitation in Childhood* (1945) | 符号功能的出现：模仿、游戏、梦 / Emergence of symbolic function: imitation, play, dreams |

### 进阶专著 / Advanced Monographs

| 著作 / Work | 说明 / Notes |
|---|---|
| 《智慧心理学》 *The Psychology of Intelligence* (1947) | 系统阐述，适合有一定基础的读者 / Systematic exposition for readers with some background |
| 《发生认识论导论》 *Introduction to Genetic Epistemology* (1950, 3 vols) | 三卷本巨著，涵盖逻辑、数学、物理、生物、心理学和社会学 / Three-volume magnum opus covering logic, mathematics, physics, biology, psychology, and sociology |
| 《生物学与知识》 *Biology and Knowledge* (1967) | 认知理论的生物学根基 / Biological roots of cognitive theory |
| 《认知结构的平衡化》 *The Equilibration of Cognitive Structures* (1975) | 平衡化机制的深度理论 / In-depth theory of the equilibration mechanism |
| 《辩证法的基本形式》 *The Elementary Forms of Dialectic* (1980) | 晚期关于认知辩证法的探索 / Late-period exploration of cognitive dialectics |

### 与 Inhelder 合著 / Co-authored with Inhelder

| 著作 / Work | 说明 / Notes |
|---|---|
| 《儿童逻辑的早期发展》 *The Early Growth of Logic in the Child* (1958) | 具体运算期的分类与序列化研究 / Classification and seriation in the concrete operational stage |
| 《儿童逻辑的成长》 *The Growth of Logical Thinking from Childhood to Adolescence* (1958) | 形式运算阶段的系统阐述 / Systematic exposition of the formal operational stage |
| 《儿童的记忆》 *Memory and Intelligence* (1973) | 记忆与认知发展的关系 / Relationship between memory and cognitive development |

## 二、研究著作（二手文献） / Secondary Research

### 传记与思想史 / Biography and Intellectual History

| 著作 / Work | 作者 / Author | 说明 / Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 《心智的探险者》 *The Quest for Mind* (1973) | Howard Gardner | 将皮亚杰置于结构主义运动中的思想传记 / Intellectual biography placing Piaget in the structuralist movement |
| *Jean Piaget* (1979) | Margaret Boden | Fontana Modern Masters 系列，精炼的思想评传 / Fontana Modern Masters series; concise intellectual assessment |

### 理论评述 / Theoretical Commentary

| 著作 / Work | 作者 / Author | 说明 / Notes |
|---|---|---|
| *The Developmental Psychology of Jean Piaget* (1963) | John Flavell | 早期最全面的理论评述 / Earliest comprehensive theoretical commentary |
| *Piaget's Theory of Cognitive and Affective Development* (2004, 5th ed.) | Barry Wadsworth | 最广泛使用的教学参考书 / Most widely used teaching reference |

| *Piaget: A Practical Consideration* (1971) | G. Helmore | 皮亚杰理论在课堂中的实际应用 / Practical application of Piaget's theory in the classroom |

### 批判与修正 / Critique and Revision

| 著作 / Work | 作者 / Author | 说明 / Notes |
|---|---|---|
| *Children's Thinking* (1998, 2nd ed.) | Robert Siegler | 从信息加工视角审视阶段理论 / Examines stage theory from information processing perspective |
| *Intellectual Development: Birth to Adulthood* (1985) | Robbie Case | 新皮亚杰学派的代表性修正 / Representative neo-Piagetian revision |
| *The Development of Memory in Infancy and Childhood* (2009) | Mary Courage & Nelson Cowan (eds.) | 现代记忆研究对皮亚杰理论的挑战 / Challenges to Piaget's theory from modern memory research |
| "Piagetian Research in a Cross-Cultural Perspective" (1972) | Pierre Dasen | 跨文化检验的经典论文 / Classic paper on cross-cultural validation |

## 五、中文阅读路径 / Chinese Reading Path

### 入门推荐 / Introductory Path

1. 任何发展心理学教材中关于认知发展的章节 / Any developmental psychology textbook chapter on cognitive development
2. 《儿童心理学》中译本（皮亚杰 & 英海尔德）/ *The Psychology of the Child* Chinese translation
3. 《发生认识论原理》中译本（商务印书馆）/ *Genetic Epistemology* Chinese translation (Commercial Press)

### 进阶推荐 / Advanced Path

4. 《儿童智慧的起源》中译本（教育科学出版社）/ *The Origins of Intelligence in Children* Chinese translation
5. 《儿童的道德判断》中译本 / *The Moral Judgment of the Child* Chinese translation
6. Gardner《心智的探险者》中译本 / Gardner's *The Quest for Mind* Chinese translation
7. 阅读皮亚杰与 Inhelder 合著的具体运算和形式运算研究 / Read Piaget and Inhelder's co-authored works on concrete and formal operations

### 跨学科阅读 / Interdisciplinary Reading

8. 康德《纯粹理性批判》"先验分析论"部分 / Kant's *Critique of Pure Reason* — "Transcendental Analytic"
9. 维果茨基《思维与语言》/ Vygotsky's *Thought and Language*
10. 布鲁纳《教育过程》/ Bruner's *The Process of Education*
