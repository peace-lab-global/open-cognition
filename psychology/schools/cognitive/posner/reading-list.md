# 波斯纳进阶阅读 / Posner Reading List

Michael I. Posner 的核心阅读资源，分为原典、二手研究、英文资源和相关领域。

Core reading resources for Michael I. Posner, organized into primary works, secondary research, English resources, and related fields.

## 一、原典 / Primary Works

### 必读 / Essential

1. **Posner, M.I. (1978). *Chronometric Explorations of Mind*. Hillsdale, NJ: Erlbaum.**
   - 心理计时学方法论的经典著作。系统阐述了用反应时间分析推断认知处理结构的方法，包括字母匹配实验、记忆搜索范式等。是理解波斯纳早期工作的核心文本。
   - Classic work on chronometric methodology. Systematically articulates the method of inferring cognitive processing structure through reaction time analysis, including letter-matching experiments and memory search paradigms. Essential for understanding Posner's early work.

2. **Posner, M.I. (1980). "Orienting of attention." *Quarterly Journal of Experimental Psychology*, 32(1), 3-25.**
   - 空间注意力的聚光灯隐喻和线索提示范式的原始论文。被引用超过10,000次，是认知心理学史上被引用最多的论文之一。
   - Original paper on the spotlight metaphor of spatial attention and the cueing paradigm. Cited over 10,000 times, one of the most cited papers in the history of cognitive psychology.

3. **Posner, M.I. & Petersen, S.E. (1990). "The attention system of the human brain." *Annual Review of Neuroscience*, 13, 25-42.**
   - **三网络模型的奠基论文。** 整合了行为实验、脑损伤研究和 PET 成像证据，正式提出注意力由警觉、定向和执行控制三个网络组成的理论框架。任何研究注意力的学者都必须阅读此文。
   - **The foundational paper of the three-network model.** Integrates behavioral experiments, lesion studies, and PET imaging evidence, formally proposing the theoretical framework that attention consists of alerting, orienting, and executive control networks. Essential reading for any attention researcher.

4. **Fan, J., McCandliss, B.D., Sommer, T., Raz, A., & Posner, M.I. (2002). "Testing the efficiency and independence of attentional networks." *Journal of Cognitive Neuroscience*, 14(3), 340-347.**
   - ANT 测试的开发论文。描述了如何在单一任务中同时测量三个注意力网络的效率，并验证了三网络的独立性假设。
   - Development paper for the ANT. Describes how to simultaneously measure all three attention networks' efficiency in a single task and validates the independence hypothesis.

5. **Posner, M.I. (2012). *Attention in a Social World*. New York: Oxford University Press.**
   - 注意力社会维度的系统论述。讨论了联合注意、共情、社会认知等高级社会功能如何依赖注意力网络。
   - Systematic treatment of the social dimension of attention. Discusses how joint attention, empathy, social cognition, and other higher social functions depend on attention networks.

### 重要 / Important

6. **Posner, M.I. & Cohen, Y. (1984). "Components of visual orienting." In H. Bouma & D. Bouwhuis (Eds.), *Attention and Performance X*. Hillsdale, NJ: Erlbaum.**
   - 定向的三个子过程和回返抑制的首次报告。
   - First report of three orienting sub-processes and inhibition of return.

7. **Posner, M.I. & Raichle, M.E. (1994). *Images of Mind*. New York: Scientific American Library.**
   - 脑成像与认知神经科学的普及读物。中译本：《脑的影像》。
   - Popular work on brain imaging and cognitive neuroscience.

8. **Posner, M.I. & Rothbart, M.K. (2007). *Educating the Human Brain*. Washington, DC: American Psychological Association.**
   - 注意力网络理论的教育应用。
   - Educational applications of attention network theory.

9. **Petersen, S.E. & Posner, M.I. (2012). "The attention system of the human brain: 20 years after." *Annual Review of Neuroscience*, 35, 73-89.**
   - 三网络理论20周年的回顾与更新，总结了20年间的重要进展。
   - 20-year retrospective and update of the three-network theory, summarizing major advances over two decades.

10. **Tang, Y.Y., Holzel, B.K., & Posner, M.I. (2015). "The neuroscience of mindfulness meditation." *Nature Reviews Neuroscience*, 16(4), 213-225.**
    - 正念冥想的神经科学综合评述，包括冥想对注意力网络的影响。
    - Comprehensive neuroscience review of mindfulness meditation, including effects on attention networks.

## 二、二手研究 / Secondary Research

### 注意力网络理论 / Attention Network Theory

1. **Raz, A. & Buhle, J. (2006). "Typologies of attentional networks." *Nature Reviews Neuroscience*, 7(5), 367-379.**
   - 注意力网络的类型学综述，比较了三网络模型与其他理论框架。
   - Typological review of attention networks, comparing the three-network model with other theoretical frameworks.

2. **Corbetta, M. & Shulman, G.L. (2002). "Control of goal-directed and stimulus-driven attention in the brain." *Nature Reviews Neuroscience*, 3(3), 201-215.**
   - 背侧和腹侧注意系统的经典论文，与波斯纳的定向网络理论形成互补。
   - Classic paper on dorsal and ventral attention systems, complementary to Posner's orienting network theory.

3. **Rueda, M.R., Fan, J., McCandliss, B.D., et al. (2004). "Development of attentional networks in childhood." *Neuropsychologia*, 42(8), 1029-1040.**
   - 儿童注意力网络发展的关键研究。
   - Key study on attention network development in childhood.

### 冲突监测与执行控制 / Conflict Monitoring and Executive Control

4. **Botvinick, M.M., Braver, T.S., Barch, D.M., Carter, C.S., & Cohen, J.D. (2001). "Conflict monitoring and cognitive control." *Psychological Review*, 108(3), 624-652.**
   - 冲突监测理论的奠基论文。
   - Foundational paper of conflict monitoring theory.

5. **Diamond, A. (2013). "Executive functions." *Annual Review of Psychology*, 64, 135-168.**
   - 执行功能的综合评述。
   - Comprehensive review of executive functions.

### 冥想与注意力 / Meditation and Attention

6. **Tang, Y.Y., Ma, Y., Wang, J., et al. (2007). "Short-term meditation training improves attention and self-regulation." *PNAS*, 104(43), 17152-17156.**
   - 短期冥想训练改善注意力的实证研究。
   - Empirical study on short-term meditation training improving attention.

7. **Lutz, A., Slagter, H.A., Dunne, J.D., & Davidson, R.J. (2008). "Attention regulation and monitoring in meditation." *Trends in Cognitive Sciences*, 12(4), 163-169.**
   - 冥想中注意力调节与监测的研究。
   - Research on attention regulation and monitoring in meditation.

### 中文资源 / Chinese Resources

8. **刘昌 (2007). 《认知神经科学中注意网络的研究》. 心理科学进展.**
   - Liu Chang (2007). "Research on attention networks in cognitive neuroscience." *Advances in Psychological Science*.

9. **彭聃龄主编 (2001). 《普通心理学》（注意章节）. 北京师范大学出版社.**
   - Peng Danling (Ed.) (2001). *General Psychology* (attention chapters). Beijing Normal University Press.

## 三、英文资源 / English Resources

### 核心期刊 / Core Journals

- *Journal of Cognitive Neuroscience* — 认知神经科学核心期刊，ANT 测试的首次发表刊物 / Core journal in cognitive neuroscience, where ANT was first published
- *Annual Review of Neuroscience* — 年度评论系列，三网络理论的首次发表刊物 / Annual review series, where the three-network theory was first published
- *Nature Reviews Neuroscience* — 神经科学评论，发表多篇注意力网络的重要综述 / Neuroscience reviews, published multiple important attention network reviews
- *Neuropsychologia* — 神经心理学，发表注意力网络发展和临床研究的论文 / Neuropsychology, publishes papers on attention network development and clinical research
- *Attention, Perception, & Psychophysics* — 注意、感知与心理物理学，注意力基础研究的重要平台 / Attention, perception, and psychophysics, an important venue for basic attention research

### 在线资源 / Online Resources

- **Posner Lab, University of Oregon** — 波斯纳实验室主页，包含最新研究动态 / Posner Lab homepage, containing latest research updates
- **ANT 测试资源** — Fan 实验室提供的 ANT 测试材料和数据 / ANT test materials and data provided by Fan's laboratory
- **Cognitive Neuroscience Society** — 认知神经科学学会，波斯纳为重要成员 / Cognitive Neuroscience Society, Posner is a prominent member
