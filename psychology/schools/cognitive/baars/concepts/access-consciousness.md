# 通达意识 · Access Consciousness

---

## 定义 · Definition

通达意识（access consciousness）是指信息经过全局广播后变得对多种认知系统"可及"（accessible）的功能性意识状态。在巴尔斯的GWT框架中，当信息被注意力选中、进入全局工作空间并被成功广播到全脑各处理器时，该信息就获得了"通达"地位——它可以被语言系统报告、被记忆系统编码、被推理系统使用、被运动系统响应。通达意识与"现象意识"（phenomenal consciousness）——即主观体验的质性"感受"——形成对比，GWT主要解释的是通达意识的功能机制。

Access consciousness refers to the functional state of consciousness in which information, after being globally broadcast, becomes "accessible" to multiple cognitive systems. In Baars's GWT framework, when information is selected by attention, enters the global workspace, and is successfully broadcast to processors brain-wide, that information achieves "access" status — it can be reported by the language system, encoded by the memory system, used by the reasoning system, and responded to by the motor system. Access consciousness is contrasted with "phenomenal consciousness" — the qualitative "feeling" of subjective experience — and GWT primarily explains the functional mechanisms of access consciousness.

这一区分最早由哲学家 Ned Block（1995）明确提出，但Baars的GWT在Block之前就隐含了这一功能性意识概念。GWT的贡献在于提供了通达意识的详细功能架构和可检验的神经机制假说。

This distinction was first explicitly proposed by philosopher Ned Block (1995), but Baars's GWT had implicitly employed this functional consciousness concept before Block. GWT's contribution lies in providing a detailed functional architecture and testable neural mechanism hypotheses for access consciousness.

## 核心机制 · Core Mechanisms

### 1. 通达的功能标准 / Functional Criteria for Access

一个信息获得通达意识地位需要满足以下功能标准：

For information to achieve access consciousness status, it must meet the following functional criteria:

- **可报告性（Reportability）**：主体能够用语言描述该信息。这是通达意识最直接的行为指标。
- **可使用性（Usability）**：该信息可以被多种下游认知系统同时使用——不仅仅是语言，还包括记忆、推理、行动计划等。
- **全局可及性（Global Accessibility）**：该信息不是只对某一个系统可见，而是对广泛的认知系统可见。
- **持续时间（Duration）**：信息在全局工作空间中保持足够长的时间（约200-500毫秒），以允许多个系统对其进行处理。

- **Reportability**: The subject can verbally describe the information. This is the most direct behavioral indicator of access consciousness.
- **Usability**: The information can be simultaneously used by multiple downstream cognitive systems — not just language, but also memory, reasoning, action planning, etc.
- **Global Accessibility**: The information is visible not to just one system but to a broad range of cognitive systems.
- **Duration**: The information persists in the global workspace long enough (approximately 200–500ms) to allow multiple systems to process it.

### 2. 通达意识 vs 现象意识 / Access vs Phenomenal Consciousness

这是当代意识哲学和科学中最核心的区分之一：

This is one of the most central distinctions in contemporary philosophy and science of consciousness:

| 维度 | 通达意识 / Access Consciousness | 现象意识 / Phenomenal Consciousness |
|------|------|------|
| **定义** | 信息的全局可及性 / Global accessibility of information | 主观体验的质性感受 / Qualitative feel of subjective experience |
| **可测量性** | 可通过行为报告测量 / Measurable via behavioral report | 难以从第三人称测量 / Difficult to measure third-person |
| **GWT解释** | GWT的核心解释对象 / GWT's core explanatory target | GWT不直接解释 / GWT does not directly explain |
| **哲学家** | Block, Baars, Dehaene | Chalmers, Nagel, Levine |
| **神经关联** | P3b波，前额叶-顶叶点火 / P3b, prefrontal-parietal ignition | 争议中 / Contested |

GWT的立场是：通达意识是可以用功能架构解释的，而现象意识（qualia）虽然不被否认，但可能需要在通达意识的功能解释完成之后才能得到充分理解。Baars认为，当一个信息被充分广播到全脑时，"感受到"该信息可能就是这个广播过程的主观伴随物。

GWT's position is that access consciousness can be explained in terms of functional architecture, while phenomenal consciousness (qualia), though not denied, may need to be fully understood only after the functional explanation of access consciousness is complete. Baars suggests that when information is sufficiently broadcast brain-wide, the "feeling" of that information may be the subjective concomitant of this broadcasting process.

### 3. 通达的层级 / Levels of Access

通达意识不是全有或全无的二元状态，而是存在层级：

Access consciousness is not an all-or-nothing binary state but exists in levels:

- **前意识（Preconscious）**：信息已被充分加工，具有进入全局工作空间的潜力，但当前未被注意力选中。如果被问到，主体可以报告它（如"你现在感觉到椅子对你背部的压力"）。
- **完全通达意识（Full Access Consciousness）**：信息当前正占据全局工作空间并被全局广播。主体可以立即报告它，且多个认知系统正在使用它。
- **阈下处理（Subliminal Processing）**：信息的加工强度不足以进入全局工作空间。主体无法报告它，但它仍然可以影响后续处理（如阈下启动效应）。

- **Preconscious**: Information has been sufficiently processed and has the potential to enter the global workspace, but is not currently selected by attention. If asked, the subject could report it (e.g., "you can now feel the chair pressing against your back").
- **Full Access Consciousness**: Information currently occupies the global workspace and is being globally broadcast. The subject can immediately report it, and multiple cognitive systems are using it.
- **Subliminal Processing**: Information processing intensity is insufficient for entry into the global workspace. The subject cannot report it, but it can still influence subsequent processing (e.g., subliminal priming effects).

## 发展脉络 · Historical Development

### 1988–1995：隐含的功能概念 / Implicit Functional Concept

Baars在1988年的GWT中已经隐含了通达意识的概念——全局广播的信息就是"被意识到的"信息。1995年，Ned Block明确区分了"通达意识"和"现象意识"，为这一隐含概念提供了哲学术语。

Baars had already implicitly employed the concept of access consciousness in his 1988 GWT — globally broadcast information is "consciously perceived" information. In 1995, Ned Block explicitly distinguished "access consciousness" from "phenomenal consciousness," providing philosophical terminology for this implicit concept.

### 2001–2011：神经科学操作化 / Neuroscience Operationalization

Dehaene 和 Changeux 将通达意识操作化为可测量的神经事件：前额叶-顶叶网络中的"点火"（ignition）标志着信息获得了通达地位。P3b脑电波被确定为通达意识的神经标记物。这一操作化使得通达意识可以在实验室中被精确测量和操控。

Dehaene and Changeux operationalized access consciousness as measurable neural events: "ignition" in the prefrontal-parietal network marks information achieving access status. The P3b EEG wave was identified as a neural marker of access consciousness. This operationalization enabled precise laboratory measurement and manipulation of access consciousness.

### 2020s：对抗性检验 / Adversarial Testing

2023年的对抗性合作实验对通达意识的神经标记进行了严格检验。结果表明，通达意识的某些神经相关物（如P3b）与GWT的预测不完全一致，推动了理论的精细化。一些研究者开始质疑通达意识是否总是需要前额叶的参与，或者后皮层区域（posterior cortex）的全局广播是否就足以产生通达意识。

The 2023 adversarial collaboration experiment rigorously tested neural markers of access consciousness. Results showed that some neural correlates (such as P3b) were not entirely consistent with GWT predictions, driving theoretical refinement. Some researchers have begun questioning whether access consciousness always requires prefrontal involvement, or whether global broadcasting in posterior cortical regions might suffice.

## 临床应用 · Clinical Applications

1. **植物状态评估 / Vegetative State Assessment**：通达意识的检测是区分植物状态（无通达意识）和最小意识状态（间歇性通达意识）的关键。通过检测大脑对标准刺激（如自己的名字）是否产生P3b等通达标记来进行评估。
2. **麻醉深度监测 / Anesthesia Depth Monitoring**：全身麻醉的目标是消除通达意识。通过监测全局广播指标（如P3b的消失和恢复）来精确控制麻醉深度。
3. **认知康复 / Cognitive Rehabilitation**：在脑损伤后的认知康复中，训练的目标之一是恢复患者对关键信息的通达意识——即帮助信息重新获得全局广播的能力。

1. **Vegetative State Assessment**: Detection of access consciousness is key to distinguishing vegetative state (no access consciousness) from minimally conscious state (intermittent access consciousness). Assessment is conducted by detecting whether the brain produces access markers such as P3b in response to standard stimuli (e.g., one's own name).
2. **Anesthesia Depth Monitoring**: The goal of general anesthesia is to eliminate access consciousness. Monitoring global broadcasting indicators (such as disappearance and recovery of P3b) enables precise control of anesthesia depth.
3. **Cognitive Rehabilitation**: In post-brain-injury cognitive rehabilitation, one training goal is to restore patients' access consciousness to key information — helping information regain the ability to be globally broadcast.

## 东西方对话 · East-West Dialogue

通达意识与现象意识的区分在佛教心识论中有着深刻的对应。唯识学（Yogācāra）区分了"分别"（vikalpa, 概念分别）和"现量"（pratyakṣa, 直接知觉）。通达意识更接近"分别"——信息被概念系统所加工和使用；而现象意识更接近"现量"——纯粹的、未被概念染色的直接感受。佛教修行中"离分别"的目标可以被理解为暂时中止通达意识的功能（停止全局广播的概念化加工），以便更直接地体验现象意识的纯粹质性。

The access-phenomenal consciousness distinction has deep correspondences in Buddhist Yogācāra philosophy. Yogācāra distinguishes between vikalpa (conceptual discrimination) and pratyakṣa (direct perception). Access consciousness is closer to vikalpa — information processed and used by conceptual systems; phenomenal consciousness is closer to pratyakṣa — pure, uncolored direct experience. The Buddhist cultivation goal of "transcending discrimination" (lí fēnbié) can be understood as temporarily suspending the function of access consciousness (stopping the conceptual processing of global broadcasting) to more directly experience the pure quality of phenomenal consciousness.

在禅宗传统中，"不立文字"的教导可以被理解为对通达意识局限性的认识——全局广播使信息可被语言系统报告，但语言报告永远是对直接经验的简化和抽象。"直指人心"则是绕过通达意识的概念化加工，直接指向现象意识的原初体验。

In the Chan/Zen tradition, the teaching of "not establishing words and letters" (bù lì wénzì) can be understood as recognition of the limitations of access consciousness — global broadcasting makes information reportable by the language system, but verbal report is always a simplification and abstraction of direct experience. "Directly pointing to the human mind" bypasses the conceptual processing of access consciousness, pointing directly to the primordial experience of phenomenal consciousness.
