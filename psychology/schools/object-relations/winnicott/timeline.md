# 温尼科特年表 · Timeline of Donald Winnicott

## 早年与教育 / Early Life and Education

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|:---:|------|
| 1897 | 4月7日出生于英国普利茅斯（Plymouth），父亲约翰·弗雷德里克·温尼科特（John Frederick Winnicott）是商人，母亲伊丽莎白·伍兹（Elizabeth Woods）是卫理公会教徒 / Born April 7 in Plymouth, England; father John Frederick Winnicott was a merchant; mother Elizabeth Woods was a Methodist |
| 1910 | 进入剑桥的莱伊斯学校（The Leys School, Cambridge）就读 / Entered The Leys School, Cambridge |
| 1914–1918 | 一战期间在英国皇家海军服役（1917–1918）；战争经历深刻影响了他对人类脆弱性和照护的理解 / Served in the Royal Navy during WWI (1917–1918); war experience deeply influenced his understanding of human vulnerability and care |
| 1919 | 进入剑桥大学耶稣学院（Jesus College, Cambridge）学习医学 / Entered Jesus College, Cambridge to study medicine |
| 1920 | 转入伦敦巴特医院（St Bartholomew's Hospital, London）完成医学训练 / Transferred to St Bartholomew's Hospital, London to complete medical training |
| 1923 | 获得医学资格（MRCS, LRCP）；开始在帕丁顿·格林儿童医院（Paddington Green Children's Hospital）担任儿科医生——这一职位他保持了40年 / Qualified in medicine (MRCS, LRCP); began as physician at Paddington Green Children's Hospital — a position he held for 40 years |

## 精神分析训练与早期生涯 / Psychoanalytic Training and Early Career

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|:---:|------|
| 1923 | 开始接受詹姆斯·斯特雷奇（James Strachey）的个人分析 / Began personal analysis with James Strachey |
| 1927 | 成为英国精神分析学会（British Psychoanalytical Society）的候选成员 / Became an associate member of the British Psychoanalytical Society |
| 1935 | 成为英国精神分析学会正式成员 / Became a full member of the British Psychoanalytical Society |
| 1936 | 开始在梅兰妮·克莱因（Melanie Klein）指导下接受督导——这一经历对他理解"内在客体"至关重要 / Began supervision with Melanie Klein — crucial for his understanding of internal objects |
| 1937 | 与第一任妻子爱丽丝·泰勒（Alice Buxton Taylor）结婚（后于1951年离婚）/ Married first wife Alice Buxton Taylor (divorced 1951) |
| 1939 | 二战爆发；温尼科特作为儿科顾问参与儿童疏散计划（evacuation scheme）——在战争期间观察了大量因与母亲分离而出现心理问题的儿童 / WWII broke out; Winnicott served as paediatric consultant to the government evacuation scheme — observing many children with psychological difficulties from maternal separation |

## 思想发展关键节点 / Key Intellectual Turning Points

1. **1923：儿科起点** / **1923: Paediatric Starting Point** — 帕丁顿·格林儿童医院的职位奠定了40年临床观察的基础 / Paddington Green Children's Hospital position laid the foundation for 40 years of clinical observation
2. **1939–1945：战争疏散** / **1939–1945: War Evacuation** — 疏散儿童的观察催生了对分离和环境失败的深刻思考 / Observation of evacuated children catalysed deep thinking about separation and environmental failure
3. **1953：过渡客体** / **1953: Transitional Object** — 开创性论文的发表标志着温尼科特独特理论声音的成熟 / Publication of the pioneering paper marked the maturation of Winnicott's distinctive theoretical voice
4. **1960：真/假自体** / **1960: True/False Self** — 最系统的人格结构理论的提出 / Presentation of the most systematic personality structure theory
5. **1968：客体使用** / **1968: Use of an Object** — 晚期理论转向：从"客体关系"到"客体使用"——强调客体的"存活" / Late theoretical turn: from "object relations" to "object use" — emphasising the object's "survival"
6. **1971：游戏与现实** / **1971: Playing and Reality** — 从临床到文化的最终扩展 / Final expansion from the clinical to the cultural
