# 温尼科特阅读清单 · Reading List for Donald Winnicott

## 一、原始著作 / Primary Works

### 核心三书 / Core Three Books

| 优先级 / Priority | 著作 / Work | 说明 / Notes |
|:---:|------|------|
| ★★★ | Winnicott, D.W. (1971). *Playing and Reality*. London: Tavistock. | 代表作；过渡客体、潜在空间、游戏、创造性 / Magnum opus; transitional objects, potential space, play, creativity |
| ★★★ | Winnicott, D.W. (1965). *The Maturational Processes and the Facilitating Environment*. London: Hogarth Press. | 成熟期论文集；真/假自体、抱持环境、独处能力 / Mature collected papers; true/false self, holding environment, capacity to be alone |
| ★★☆ | Winnicott, D.W. (1958). *Through Paediatrics to Psycho-Analysis*. London: Tavistock. | 早期论文集；从儿科到精神分析的思想轨迹 / Early collected papers; intellectual trajectory from paediatrics to psychoanalysis |

### 推荐阅读顺序 / Suggested Reading Order

**入门级 / Beginner**:
1. Phillips, A. (1988). *Winnicott*. — 传记与思想概览 / Biography and intellectual overview
2. Winnicott, D.W. (1964). *The Child, the Family, and the Outside World*. — 通俗化的温尼科特 / Popularised Winnicott

**中级 / Intermediate**:
3. Winnicott, D.W. (1971). *Playing and Reality*. — 代表作 / Magnum opus
4. Abram, J. (1996). *The Language of Winnicott*. — 概念工具书 / Conceptual reference tool
5. Winnicott, D.W. (1965). *The Maturational Processes*. — 成熟期理论 / Mature theory

**高级 / Advanced**:
6. Ogden, T.H. (2001). "Reading Winnicott." — 深度阅读方法 / Deep reading method
7. Bollas, C. (1987). *The Shadow of the Object*. — 后温尼科特理论 / Post-Winnicott theory
8. Slochower, J. (1996). *Holding and Psychoanalysis*. — 抱持的当代发展 / Contemporary development of holding
9. Eigen, M. (1985). "The Area of Faith in Winnicott, Lacan and Bion." — 比较研究 / Comparative study
