# 抑郁位态 · Depressive Position

## 定义 / Definition

抑郁位态（depressive position）是克莱因提出的第二个心理发展阶段——约在4-6个月开始，并贯穿整个心理发展过程。在这个阶段，婴儿开始认识到"好乳房"和"坏乳房"不是两个不同的对象，而是**同一个对象（母亲）的不同方面**。这一认识带来了深刻的心理转变：婴儿意识到，自己对"坏母亲"的攻击性幻想实际上也伤害了"好母亲"——这引发了内疚感（guilt）、哀伤（mourning）和修复的愿望（desire for reparation）。

The depressive position is the second developmental stage proposed by Klein—beginning around 4-6 months and continuing throughout psychological development. In this stage, the infant begins to recognize that the "good breast" and "bad breast" are not two different objects but **different aspects of the same object (the mother)**. This recognition brings a profound psychological shift: the infant realizes that its aggressive phantasies toward the "bad mother" have actually harmed the "good mother"—this evokes guilt, mourning, and the desire for reparation.

"抑郁"（depressive）一词容易引起误解——它不是指临床抑郁症（clinical depression），而是指一种**心理上的沉重感**：当个体意识到自己的攻击性可能伤害了所爱的对象时，体验到的一种深层的哀伤和责任感。克莱因认为，能够体验抑郁位态是**心理成熟的标志**——它意味着个体能够承受矛盾情感、承担责任并寻求修复。

The term "depressive" can be misleading—it does not refer to clinical depression but to a **psychological heaviness**: a deep sadness and sense of responsibility experienced when the individual realizes that its own aggression may have harmed the loved object. Klein held that the capacity to experience the depressive position is a **sign of psychological maturity**—it means the individual can tolerate ambivalence, take responsibility, and seek reparation.

## 核心机制 / Core Mechanisms

### 整合 / Integration

抑郁位态的核心操作是**整合**（integration）——将之前分裂的好与坏重新统一为完整的对象。婴儿开始认识到：满足自己的母亲和挫折自己的母亲是同一个人；自己既爱又恨的是同一个对象。这种认识需要更成熟的自我功能——能够同时持有矛盾的情感（ambivalence）。

The core operation of the depressive position is **integration**—reunifying the previously split good and bad into a whole object. The infant begins to recognize: the mother who satisfies and the mother who frustrates are the same person; the object it both loves and hates is the same object. This recognition requires more mature ego functioning—the capacity to hold ambivalent feelings simultaneously.

整合带来了心理上的复杂性和丰富性。个体不再生活在一个非黑即白的简单世界中，而是进入了一个矛盾但真实的复杂世界——在这个世界中，爱和恨可以共存，好对象可以有缺点，坏对象可以有优点。

Integration brings psychological complexity and richness. The individual no longer lives in a simple black-and-white world but enters an ambivalent yet real complex world—in this world, love and hate can coexist, good objects can have flaws, bad objects can have virtues.

### 内疚感与修复 / Guilt and Reparation

整合的直接后果是**内疚感**——婴儿意识到，在偏执-分裂位态中，它对"坏乳房"的攻击性幻想实际上指向了"好乳房"。这种内疚感不同于偏执-分裂位态中的"迫害焦虑"——迫害焦虑是关于自身安全的恐惧（"坏对象会伤害我"），而内疚感是关于对象安全的关切（"我可能伤害了好的对象"）。

The direct consequence of integration is **guilt**—the infant realizes that in the paranoid-schizoid position, its aggressive phantasies toward the "bad breast" were actually directed at the "good breast." This guilt differs from the "persecutory anxiety" of the paranoid-schizoid position—persecutory anxiety is about fear for one's own safety ("the bad object will harm me"), while guilt is about concern for the object's safety ("I may have harmed the good object").

内疚感激发了**修复的愿望**——个体想要修复自己对所爱对象造成的伤害。克莱因认为，修复愿望是人类创造性活动的重要动力之一。艺术家、作家、科学家在创造的过程中，潜意识中都在进行一种修复——修复内在世界中受损的好对象。

Guilt motivates the **desire for reparation**—the individual wants to repair the harm it has caused to the loved object. Klein held that the desire for reparation is one of the important motivations of human creative activity. Artists, writers, and scientists are all unconsciously engaged in reparation during the creative process—repairing the damaged good objects in the internal world.

### 哀悼能力 / Capacity for Mourning

抑郁位态使个体获得了**哀悼的能力**——能够承受失去好对象的哀伤，而不至于崩溃或退行到偏执-分裂位态。克莱因在1940年的论文"哀悼及其与躁狂-抑郁状态的关系"（"Mourning and Its Relation to Manic-Depressive States"）中指出，正常的哀悼过程涉及重新激活抑郁位态——哀悼者在失去外部对象后，必须在内在世界中重建与对象的关系。

The depressive position enables the individual's **capacity for mourning**—the ability to bear the grief of losing the good object without collapsing or regressing to the paranoid-schizoid position. In her 1940 paper "Mourning and Its Relation to Manic-Depressive States," Klein pointed out that the normal mourning process involves reactivating the depressive position—the mourner, after losing the external object, must rebuild the relationship with the object in the internal world.

这一洞见深刻影响了后来的丧失与哀悼研究。弗洛伊德在"哀悼与忧郁"（"Mourning and Melancholia", 1917）中区分了正常哀悼和病理性忧郁，克莱因进一步解释了为什么有些人能够完成哀悼（已经建立了稳固的抑郁位态），而有些人不能（抑郁位态不够稳固，退行到偏执-分裂位态）。

This insight profoundly influenced later research on loss and mourning. Freud in "Mourning and Melancholia" (1917) distinguished normal mourning from pathological melancholia; Klein further explained why some people can complete mourning (having established a stable depressive position) while others cannot (depressive position not stable enough, regressing to paranoid-schizoid position).

## 发展脉络 / Developmental Trajectory

### 从偏执-分裂到抑郁 / From Paranoid-Schizoid to Depressive

抑郁位态不是取代偏执-分裂位态，而是在其基础上增加了一个更复杂的心理层次。即使在心理成熟的个体中，偏执-分裂位态的机制仍然存在——它们只是在正常情况下被抑郁位态的整合能力所调节。面对极端压力或威胁时，个体可能暂时退行到偏执-分裂位态。

The depressive position does not replace the paranoid-schizoid position but adds a more complex psychological layer on its basis. Even in psychologically mature individuals, paranoid-schizoid mechanisms still exist—they are simply modulated by the depressive position's integrative capacity under normal circumstances. Under extreme stress or threat, the individual may temporarily regress to the paranoid-schizoid position.

### 躁狂防御 / Manic Defense

当抑郁位态的痛苦过于强烈时，个体可能使用**躁狂防御**（manic defense）来逃避内疚和哀伤。躁狂防御表现为否认依赖、全能感和对客体的轻蔑——这些都是为了否认对象的丧失和自身对伤害的责任。

When the pain of the depressive position is too intense, the individual may use **manic defense** to escape guilt and grief. Manic defense manifests as denial of dependency, omnipotence, and contempt for the object—all to deny the loss of the object and one's own responsibility for the harm.

克莱因对躁狂防御的分析预示了后来温尼科特关于"虚假自体"（false self）的理论——两者都描述了个体如何通过防御性地否认真实的脆弱性来维持一种表面的心理平衡。

Klein's analysis of manic defense anticipated Winnicott's later theory of the "false self"—both describe how individuals maintain a superficial psychological equilibrium by defensively denying authentic vulnerability.

### 当代发展 / Contemporary Developments

当代克莱因学派学者如伊丽莎白·斯皮利厄斯（Elizabeth Spillius）和迈克尔·费尔德曼（Michael Feldman）进一步发展了抑郁位态理论，强调其在分析情境中的临床表现。当代关系精神分析学家如杰西卡·本杰明（Jessica Benjamin）则将抑郁位态与主体间性理论整合。

Contemporary Kleinian scholars such as Elizabeth Spillius and Michael Feldman have further developed depressive position theory, emphasizing its clinical manifestations in the analytic situation. Contemporary relational psychoanalysts such as Jessica Benjamin have integrated the depressive position with intersubjectivity theory.

## 临床应用 / Clinical Applications

在治疗中，帮助来访者进入和维持在抑郁位态是核心治疗目标之一。治疗师需要：识别来访者何时退行到偏执-分裂位态（表现为分裂思维、迫害焦虑、理想化与贬低）；提供一个安全的容器来承受来访者的攻击性和焦虑；帮助来访者容忍矛盾情感并承担责任；促进修复的体验——使来访者能够在关系中修复而非逃避伤害。

In treatment, helping the patient enter and maintain the depressive position is one of the core therapeutic goals. The therapist needs to: identify when the patient regresses to the paranoid-schizoid position (manifesting as splitting thinking, persecutory anxiety, idealization and devaluation); provide a safe container to hold the patient's aggression and anxiety; help the patient tolerate ambivalence and take responsibility; facilitate experiences of reparation—enabling the patient to repair rather than escape from harm in relationships.

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

抑郁位态与佛教"慈悲"（karuṇā）的修持有深刻共鸣。慈悲修持的核心是承受世间苦难而不逃避——这与抑郁位态的核心能力（承受内疚和哀伤而不退行）形成了平行。慈悲修持中的"自他交换"（tonglen）——吸入他人的痛苦，呼出安乐——也可与修复的动力学相联系：通过承受痛苦来修复关系。

The depressive position resonates deeply with Buddhist compassion (karuṇā) practice. The core of compassion practice is bearing the world's suffering without escape—this parallels the core capacity of the depressive position (bearing guilt and grief without regression). The "exchanging self and other" (tonglen) practice in compassion meditation—breathing in others' suffering, breathing out happiness—can also be linked to the dynamics of reparation: repairing relationships through bearing suffering.

在儒家思想中，"恕"（shù）的概念——"己所不欲，勿施于人"——与抑郁位态的道德维度相呼应。恕要求个体超越自我中心的立场，考虑自己的行为对他人的影响——这正是抑郁位态中从迫害焦虑到内疚感的转变所体现的。

In Confucian thought, the concept of "shù" (reciprocity)—"do not impose on others what you yourself do not desire"—echoes the moral dimension of the depressive position. Shù requires the individual to transcend the self-centered standpoint and consider the impact of one's actions on others—this is precisely what the shift from persecutory anxiety to guilt in the depressive position embodies.
