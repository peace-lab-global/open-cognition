# 好乳房与坏乳房 · Good Breast and Bad Breast

## 定义 / Definition

"好乳房"（good breast）与"坏乳房"（bad breast）是克莱因理论中最具标志性的概念——它们描述了婴儿在偏执-分裂位态中对母亲的**部分客体**（part object）体验。当乳房满足婴儿的饥饿需求时，它被体验为"好乳房"——慷慨的、滋养的、令人满足的。当乳房缺席或不能满足需求时，它被体验为"坏乳房"——剥夺的、攻击的、令人挫折的。

"Good breast" and "bad breast" are the most iconic concepts in Kleinian theory—they describe the infant's experience of the mother as a **part object** in the paranoid-schizoid position. When the breast satisfies the infant's hunger, it is experienced as the "good breast"—generous, nourishing, gratifying. When the breast is absent or fails to satisfy, it is experienced as the "bad breast"—depriving, attacking, frustrating.

关键在于：好乳房和坏乳房不是客观现实的描述，而是**婴儿主观体验的投射**。同一个物理乳房在不同的时刻被婴儿体验为完全不同的对象——这反映了婴儿心理中幻想（phantasy）的核心作用。婴儿的幻想生活赋予了乳房不同的"人格"——好乳房是爱的对象，坏乳房是恨的对象。

Crucially, the good breast and bad breast are not descriptions of objective reality but **projections of the infant's subjective experience**. The same physical breast is experienced by the infant as completely different objects at different moments—this reflects the central role of phantasy in infant psychology. The infant's phantasy life endows the breast with different "personalities"—the good breast is the object of love, the bad breast is the object of hatred.

## 核心机制 / Core Mechanisms

### 部分客体关系 / Part Object Relations

在偏执-分裂位态中，婴儿还没有将母亲体验为一个**完整的人**（whole object）——而是将母亲体验为提供满足或挫折的**部分**（part）。乳房是婴儿最早的客体关系对象——它不是一个"人"，而是一个功能性的体验单位。

In the paranoid-schizoid position, the infant does not yet experience the mother as a **whole object**—but rather experiences the mother as a **part** that provides satisfaction or frustration. The breast is the infant's earliest object relation—it is not a "person" but a functional unit of experience.

克莱因使用"部分客体"（part object）来指代这种不完整的客体体验——乳房、面孔、声音、手等都可以成为部分客体。只有当婴儿发展到抑郁位态时，才能将这些部分整合为一个完整的人。

Klein used "part object" to refer to this incomplete object experience—the breast, face, voice, hands, etc. can all become part objects. Only when the infant develops to the depressive position can these parts be integrated into a whole person.

### 幻想赋予意义 / Phantasy Endows Meaning

好乳房和坏乳房的区分揭示了克莱因理论的一个核心原则：**客体不是被"发现"的，而是被"创造"的**。婴儿不是被动地接受母亲的照顾，而是通过幻想主动地赋予照顾以意义——同样的喂养行为可能被幻想为"爱的馈赠"或"恶意的控制"。

The distinction between good breast and bad breast reveals a core principle of Kleinian theory: **objects are not "discovered" but "created."** The infant does not passively receive the mother's care but actively endows care with meaning through phantasy—the same feeding behavior may be phantasied as a "gift of love" or "malicious control."

这一原则对精神分析治疗有深远影响：治疗师关注的不是客观发生了什么，而是来访者如何**通过幻想建构**了他们的体验。两个经历了同样创伤的人可能有完全不同的内在体验——因为他们的幻想赋予了创伤不同的意义。

This principle has profound implications for psychoanalytic treatment: the therapist focuses not on what objectively happened but on how the patient **constructed their experience through phantasy**. Two people who experienced the same trauma may have completely different internal experiences—because their phantasies endowed the trauma with different meanings.

### 嫉羡对好乳房的攻击 / Envy's Attack on the Good Breast

在《嫉羡与感恩》（1957）中，克莱因提出了一个重要洞见：**嫉羡**（envy）是对好乳房的攻击性反应。嫉羡不同于嫉妒（jealousy）——嫉妒是害怕失去已拥有的好对象，而嫉羡是对好对象本身的恨意，因为它的"好"使个体意识到自己的匮乏和依赖。

In *Envy and Gratitude* (1957), Klein proposed an important insight: **envy** is an aggressive reaction toward the good breast. Envy differs from jealousy—jealousy is the fear of losing a good object one possesses, while envy is hatred toward the good object itself, because its "goodness" makes the individual aware of their own lack and dependency.

嫉羡的独特之处在于它攻击的是**好的**对象而非坏的对象——婴儿嫉羡好乳房，因为它拥有婴儿想要的东西（乳汁、温暖、满足）。嫉羡的破坏性在于它损害了内在好对象的建立——如果好乳房被嫉羡破坏，婴儿就无法内射一个稳固的好对象，心理发展因此受阻。

The uniqueness of envy lies in its attacking the **good** object rather than the bad object—the infant envies the good breast because it possesses what the infant wants (milk, warmth, satisfaction). The destructiveness of envy lies in its damaging the establishment of internal good objects—if the good breast is damaged by envy, the infant cannot introject a stable good object, and psychological development is impeded.

## 发展脉络 / Developmental Trajectory

### 从乳房到完整客体 / From Breast to Whole Object

好乳房与坏乳房的分化是心理发展的起点——它标志着婴儿开始组织客体关系体验。随着心理发展，部分客体关系逐渐被完整客体关系取代：乳房不再只是一个功能性的部分，而是属于一个完整的、有优点也有缺点的人（母亲）。

The differentiation of good breast and bad breast is the starting point of psychological development—it marks the infant beginning to organize object relation experiences. With psychological development, part object relations are gradually replaced by whole object relations: the breast is no longer merely a functional part but belongs to a whole person with both virtues and flaws (the mother).

这一从部分到整体的过渡是抑郁位态的核心成就。在抑郁位态中，好乳房和坏乳房被整合为同一个母亲——婴儿认识到，让它满足的同一个母亲也会让它挫折。这一认识带来内疚感（对坏乳房的攻击实际上也伤害了好乳房）和修复的愿望。

This transition from part to whole is the core achievement of the depressive position. In the depressive position, the good breast and bad breast are integrated into the same mother—the infant recognizes that the same mother who satisfies also frustrates. This recognition brings guilt (attacks on the bad breast actually also harmed the good breast) and the desire for reparation.

### 成人心理中的部分客体 / Part Objects in Adult Psychology

虽然好乳房和坏乳房是婴儿期的体验，但**部分客体关系**在成人心理中仍然存在。当我们在极度愤怒时将一个人视为"全坏的"，或在极度理想化时将一个人视为"全好的"，我们就在使用部分客体关系——我们不是在与一个完整的人建立关系，而是在与这个人的某个部分建立关系。

Although the good breast and bad breast are infantile experiences, **part object relations** persist in adult psychology. When we see a person as "all bad" in extreme anger, or "all good" in extreme idealization, we are using part object relations—we are not relating to a whole person but to a part of that person.

在恋爱关系中，这种现象尤为常见：恋人可能被理想化为"完美的"（好乳房的成人版本），然后在发现对方有缺点时感到极度失望。这种"全好-全坏"的摆动就是部分客体关系的标志。

In romantic relationships, this phenomenon is particularly common: the lover may be idealized as "perfect" (the adult version of the good breast), then extreme disappointment follows upon discovering the other's flaws. This "all good—all bad" oscillation is the hallmark of part object relations.

### 文化中的好乳房与坏乳房 / Good Breast and Bad Breast in Culture

好乳房和坏乳房的原型在人类文化中广泛存在。童话中的"好仙女"和"坏仙女"、宗教中的"善神"和"恶神"、神话中的"大地母亲"和"毁灭女神"——这些都是好乳房和坏乳房的文化投射。

The archetypes of good breast and bad breast are widely present in human culture. The "good fairy" and "bad fairy" in fairy tales, the "benevolent deity" and "malevolent deity" in religion, the "earth mother" and "destroying goddess" in mythology—these are all cultural projections of the good breast and bad breast.

玛丽亚·托罗克（Maria Torok）和尼古拉斯·亚伯拉罕（Nicolas Abraham）将好乳房与坏乳房的动力学扩展到代际传递的研究——他们认为，未处理的创伤（坏乳房体验）可以通过"加密"（encryptment）的方式在家族中传递。

Maria Torok and Nicolas Abraham extended the dynamics of the good breast and bad breast to the study of transgenerational transmission—they argued that unprocessed trauma (bad breast experiences) can be transmitted in families through "encryptment."

## 临床应用 / Clinical Applications

在临床实践中，治疗师需要关注来访者如何体验治疗师——是作为"好乳房"（理想化的、全能的拯救者）还是"坏乳房"（剥夺的、令人失望的挫折者）。这两种体验的极端摆动表明来访者仍处于偏执-分裂位态的部分客体关系模式中。

In clinical practice, the therapist needs to attend to how the patient experiences the therapist—as the "good breast" (idealized, omnipotent rescuer) or the "bad breast" (depriving, disappointing frustrator). Extreme oscillation between these two experiences indicates that the patient is still in a part object relations mode of the paranoid-schizoid position.

治疗的目标是帮助来访者从部分客体关系过渡到完整客体关系——能够将治疗师（以及生活中的其他人）体验为有好有坏的完整的人，而不是分裂的部分客体。

The therapeutic goal is to help the patient move from part object relations to whole object relations—to be able to experience the therapist (and others in life) as whole people with good and bad, rather than as split part objects.

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

好乳房与坏乳房的二元对立与道教"阴阳"思想形成了有趣的对话。阴阳不是绝对对立的两极，而是相互包含、相互转化的——阴中有阳，阳中有阴。克莱因的抑郁位态正是要实现这种整合：认识到好乳房中有坏的成分（母亲不可能完美），坏乳房中有好的成分（挫折也是成长的一部分）。这与阴阳智慧的"对立统一"异曲同工。

The binary opposition of good breast and bad breast forms an interesting dialogue with the Daoist concept of "yin-yang." Yin and yang are not absolutely opposing poles but mutually containing and transforming—there is yang within yin, and yin within yang. Klein's depressive position aims precisely to achieve this integration: recognizing that the good breast contains bad elements (the mother cannot be perfect) and the bad breast contains good elements (frustration is also part of growth). This resonates with the yin-yang wisdom of "unity of opposites."

在唯识学中，"遍计所执性"（parikalpita-svabhāva）——即对事物的虚妄分别——与好乳房/坏乳房的分裂机制相似。婴儿将乳房"遍计"为好或坏，这是对实相的扭曲。抑郁位态的到来类似"依他起性"（paratantra-svabhāva）——认识到乳房不是独立的好或坏，而是因缘和合的产物。

In Yogacara, "imagined nature" (parikalpita-svabhāva)—the false discrimination of things—is similar to the splitting mechanism of good breast/bad breast. The infant "imputes" the breast as good or bad, which is a distortion of reality. The arrival of the depressive position is similar to "dependent nature" (paratantra-svabhāva)—recognizing that the breast is not independently good or bad but a product of dependent arising.
