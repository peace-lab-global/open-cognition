# 偏执-分裂位态 · Paranoid-Schizoid Position

## 定义 / Definition

偏执-分裂位态（paranoid-schizoid position）是克莱因提出的最早的心理发展阶段——出现在生命的最初几个月（大约0-4个月）。在这个阶段，婴儿通过**分裂**（splitting）机制将世界分为"好"与"坏"两个完全独立的部分：满足需求的乳房被体验为"好乳房"，挫折的乳房被体验为"坏乳房"。婴儿还没有能力将这两个方面整合为同一个对象。

The paranoid-schizoid position is the earliest developmental stage proposed by Klein—appearing in the first few months of life (approximately 0-4 months). In this stage, the infant uses the **splitting** mechanism to divide the world into "good" and "bad" as two completely separate parts: the satisfying breast is experienced as "good breast," the frustrating breast as "bad breast." The infant does not yet have the capacity to integrate these two aspects into the same object.

"偏执"（paranoid）指的是婴儿对"坏对象"的恐惧——由于投射了自身的攻击性，婴儿体验到外部世界充满了迫害性的威胁。"分裂"（schizoid）指的是将好与坏完全分开的心理操作——这是一种原始的防御机制，目的是保护内在好对象免受坏对象的污染。

"Paranoid" refers to the infant's fear of the "bad object"—having projected its own aggression, the infant experiences the external world as full of persecutory threats. "Schizoid" refers to the psychological operation of completely separating good from bad—this is a primitive defense mechanism aimed at protecting the internal good object from contamination by the bad object.

## 核心机制 / Core Mechanisms

### 分裂 / Splitting

分裂是偏执-分裂位态的核心操作。婴儿的自我还不够整合，无法同时持有矛盾的情感——因此，它将"好的"体验与"坏的"体验完全分开。这不是有意识的选择，而是心理结构的原始组织方式。

Splitting is the core operation of the paranoid-schizoid position. The infant's ego is not yet integrated enough to hold ambivalent feelings simultaneously—so it completely separates "good" experiences from "bad" experiences. This is not a conscious choice but a primitive way of organizing psychological structure.

分裂有两个维度：**客体分裂**（将母亲分为好乳房和坏乳房）和**自我分裂**（将自我分为好的部分和坏的部分）。好的部分被保留和滋养，坏的部分被投射出去。

Splitting has two dimensions: **object splitting** (dividing the mother into good breast and bad breast) and **ego splitting** (dividing the self into good parts and bad parts). The good parts are retained and nurtured, the bad parts projected out.

### 投射与内射的原始循环 / The Primitive Cycle of Projection and Introjection

在偏执-分裂位态中，投射和内射以最原始的形式运作。婴儿将自身的攻击性和破坏性**投射**到外部客体上，使外部客体变得"可怕"和"迫害性"。同时，婴儿**内射**好的体验，建立内在的安全基地。

In the paranoid-schizoid position, projection and introjection operate in their most primitive form. The infant **projects** its own aggression and destructiveness onto external objects, making them "frightening" and "persecutory." Simultaneously, the infant **introjects** good experiences, building an internal secure base.

这一循环的失衡会导致病理后果：如果投射过度，外部世界变得过于可怕，婴儿被迫害焦虑淹没；如果内射不足，内在好对象无法建立，婴儿缺乏内在安全感。

Imbalance in this cycle leads to pathological consequences: if projection is excessive, the external world becomes too frightening and the infant is overwhelmed by persecutory anxiety; if introjection is insufficient, internal good objects cannot be established and the infant lacks internal security.

### 理想化 / Idealization

为了对抗迫害焦虑，婴儿对好对象进行**理想化**——将好对象夸大为完美的、全能的。理想化是分裂的补充机制：它将好对象推到极端，以确保它不会被坏对象污染。

To counteract persecutory anxiety, the infant **idealizes** the good object—exaggerating it as perfect and omnipotent. Idealization is a complementary mechanism to splitting: it pushes the good object to the extreme to ensure it is not contaminated by the bad object.

理想化在正常发展中会逐渐减弱——随着抑郁位态的到来，婴儿开始认识到好对象不是完美的，但仍然可以是"足够好"的。过度理想化在成人心理中表现为不切实际的期望和对失望的极端反应。

Idealization gradually diminishes in normal development—with the arrival of the depressive position, the infant begins to recognize that the good object is not perfect but can still be "good enough." Excessive idealization in adult psychology manifests as unrealistic expectations and extreme reactions to disappointment.

## 发展脉络 / Developmental Trajectory

### 正常发展与病理固着 / Normal Development and Pathological Fixation

偏执-分裂位态是心理发展的**必要阶段**——没有分裂，婴儿的自我会被矛盾的情感淹没。健康的心理发展不是消除分裂，而是在分裂的基础上逐步整合。

The paranoid-schizoid position is a **necessary stage** of psychological development—without splitting, the infant's ego would be overwhelmed by ambivalent feelings. Healthy psychological development does not eliminate splitting but gradually integrates on its basis.

如果偏执-分裂位态过于强烈或持续时间过长，个体可能固着在这个阶段。偏执-分裂位态的固着与多种精神病理状态相关：边缘型人格障碍（borderline personality disorder）的核心特征就是无法将好与坏整合；精神分裂症（schizophrenia）的某些症状可以理解为偏执-分裂机制的极端表现。

If the paranoid-schizoid position is too intense or persists too long, the individual may become fixated at this stage. Fixation in the paranoid-schizoid position is associated with various psychopathological states: the core feature of borderline personality disorder is the inability to integrate good and bad; certain symptoms of schizophrenia can be understood as extreme manifestations of paranoid-schizoid mechanisms.

### 向抑郁位态的过渡 / Transition to the Depressive Position

约在4-6个月，婴儿的认知和情感能力发展到足以整合矛盾体验的程度——好乳房和坏乳房开始被感知为同一个对象。这标志着向抑郁位态的过渡。这一过渡不是一次性完成的，而是一个持续的过程——即使在成人中，面对强烈的焦虑或威胁时，个体可能暂时退行到偏执-分裂位态。

Around 4-6 months, the infant's cognitive and emotional capacities develop to the point of integrating ambivalent experiences—the good breast and bad breast begin to be perceived as the same object. This marks the transition to the depressive position. This transition is not a one-time event but a continuous process—even in adults, facing intense anxiety or threat, the individual may temporarily regress to the paranoid-schizoid position.

### 克莱因与比昂的扩展 / Klein and Bion's Extension

比昂（W.R. Bion）扩展了偏执-分裂位态理论，将其发展为认识论模型。比昂区分了**偏执-分裂位态**（PS）和**抑郁位态**（D）之间的振荡——他认为心理成长就是在这两个位态之间不断移动的过程。比昂还提出，即使在高度发展的个体中，PS↔D的振荡仍在继续——这是思维本身的动态结构。

Bion extended the paranoid-schizoid position theory, developing it into an epistemological model. Bion distinguished the oscillation between **paranoid-schizoid position** (Ps) and **depressive position** (D)—he held that psychological growth is the continuous movement between these two positions. Bion also proposed that even in highly developed individuals, the Ps↔D oscillation continues—this is the dynamic structure of thinking itself.

## 临床应用 / Clinical Applications

在临床实践中，识别来访者是否处于偏执-分裂位态至关重要。处于偏执-分裂位态的来访者通常表现出以下特征：**非黑即白的思维**（将人和事分为全好或全坏）；**迫害性焦虑**（感到被外部力量威胁）；**理想化与贬低的交替**（对治疗师的态度在极度崇拜和极度贬低之间摆动）。

In clinical practice, identifying whether a patient is in the paranoid-schizoid position is crucial. Patients in the paranoid-schizoid position typically display: **black-and-white thinking** (dividing people and things into all-good or all-bad); **persecutory anxiety** (feeling threatened by external forces); **alternation of idealization and devaluation** (attitude toward the therapist swings between extreme adoration and extreme devaluation).

治疗的目标不是消除分裂，而是帮助来访者逐步建立整合的能力——这通常需要在治疗关系中提供长期的安全体验，使来访者的内在好对象逐渐强大到足以承受坏对象的污染。

The therapeutic goal is not to eliminate splitting but to help the patient gradually develop the capacity for integration—this usually requires providing long-term secure experiences in the therapeutic relationship, enabling the patient's internal good objects to become strong enough to withstand contamination by bad objects.

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

偏执-分裂位态与佛教"无明"（avidyā）概念有深层呼应。无明是对现实的错误认知——将世界分为永恒的好与绝对的坏，而看不到事物的无常和相互依存。偏执-分裂位态正是这种二元分裂的心理学描述——将矛盾的体验分裂为绝对的好与绝对的坏。

The paranoid-schizoid position resonates deeply with the Buddhist concept of "ignorance" (avidyā). Ignorance is a misperception of reality—dividing the world into eternal good and absolute bad, without seeing the impermanence and interdependence of things. The paranoid-schizoid position is precisely a psychological description of this binary splitting—splitting ambivalent experiences into absolute good and absolute bad.

庄子"齐物论"的智慧也可与偏执-分裂位态对话。庄子指出，人们习惯将世界分为对立的两极——是与非、美与丑、善与恶。这种分裂不是世界的本来面目，而是人心的投射。这与克莱因发现婴儿通过分裂机制组织心理世界的方式惊人地相似。

Zhuangzi's wisdom in "Making All Things Equal" can also dialogue with the paranoid-schizoid position. Zhuangzi points out that people habitually divide the world into opposing poles—right and wrong, beautiful and ugly, good and evil. This splitting is not the true nature of the world but a projection of the human mind. This is remarkably similar to Klein's discovery that infants organize the psychological world through splitting mechanisms.
