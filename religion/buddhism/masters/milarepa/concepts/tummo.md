# 拙火 Tummo (Inner Heat / Psychic Heat)

> "我身如炉火，外寒何所惧？单衣坐雪山，拙火暖三界。"
>
> "My body is like a blazing furnace — what fear have I of outer cold? In thin cloth I sit on snowy peaks; tummo's warmth pervades the three realms."

## 概述 Overview

拙火（藏文：གཏུམ་མོ་，gtum mo；梵文：Caṇḍālī），字面义为"猛厉"或"炽热"，是那洛六法之首，也是密勒日巴修行体系中最引人注目的密法。它通过观修体内的气脉明点系统来生起强大的内在热能，使修行者能够在极端寒冷的环境中仅以单薄衣物维生。

Tummo (Tibetan: གཏུམ་མོ་, gtum mo; Sanskrit: Caṇḍālī), literally "fierce" or "blazing," is the first of the Six Yogas of Naropa and the most striking practice in Milarepa's system. It generates powerful inner heat through meditation on the body's channel-wind-drop system, enabling practitioners to survive extreme cold wearing only thin garments.

但拙火的意义远不止于身体层面的产热。在密续修行的深层框架中，拙火的终极目的是引生大乐智慧——通过内在热能的升腾，融化顶轮的白明点，使其沿中脉下降，从而引生四喜四空的大乐体验，最终证悟心性的明空不二。

But tummo's significance extends far beyond physical heat production. In the deeper framework of tantric practice, tummo's ultimate purpose is to give rise to the wisdom of great bliss — through the rising of inner heat, melting the white bodhichitta at the crown, causing it to descend through the central channel, thereby generating the experience of four joys and four emptinesses, culminating in the realization of the unity of bliss and emptiness.

## 理论基础 Theoretical Foundation

### 气脉明点系统 Channels, Winds, and Drops

拙火修行基于密续生理学（subtle body）的三个核心要素：

Tummo practice is based on three core elements of tantric subtle-body physiology:

**脉（Nāḍī / rtsa）**：体内有七万二千条微细脉道，其中最重要的是三主脉——中脉（avadhūti）、左脉（lalana）和右脉（rasana）。中脉从会阴直通顶轮，是觉悟能量运行的通道。

**Channels (Nāḍī / rtsa)**: The body contains 72,000 subtle channels, the most important being the three principal channels — central (avadhūti), left (lalana), and right (rasana). The central channel runs from the perineum to the crown and is the pathway for enlightened energy.

**气（Prāṇa / rlung）**：生命能量在脉道中运行。未修行时，气主要在左右脉中运行，产生二元对立的思维模式。修行的目的是将气引入中脉。

**Winds (Prāṇa / rlung)**: Vital energy flows through the channels. When untrained, winds primarily flow in the left and right channels, producing dualistic thought patterns. The goal of practice is to draw the winds into the central channel.

**明点（Bindu / thig le）**：精微能量凝聚为明点。顶轮有白明点（来自父亲的精粹），脐轮有红明点（来自母亲的精粹）。拙火的目的之一就是融化白明点使其下降。

**Drops (Bindu / thig le)**: Subtle energy condenses as drops. The crown holds white drops (paternal essence), the navel holds red drops (maternal essence). One purpose of tummo is to melt the white drops and cause them to descend.

### 四轮系统 The Four Chakras

密续传统描述了沿中脉分布的四个主要脉轮：

The tantric tradition describes four main chakras along the central channel:

| 脉轮 Chakra | 位置 Location | 特征 Characteristic |
|---|---|---|
| 顶轮 Crown | 头顶 | 大乐轮，白明点所在 / Great bliss wheel, white drops |
| 喉轮 Throat | 喉部 | 受用轮 / Enjoyment wheel |
| 心轮 Heart | 胸口 | 法轮 / Dharma wheel |
| 脐轮 Navel | 脐下 | 化身轮，拙火生起之处 / Emanation wheel, where tummo ignites |

## 密勒日巴的拙火修行 Milarepa's Tummo Practice

密勒日巴的拙火修行是藏传佛教史上最著名的修行事迹之一。根据传记记载，他在海拔五千多米的喜马拉雅山洞穴中，仅穿一件白色棉布衣（因此被称为"日巴"——穿棉布者），在零下数十度的严寒中修行。

Milarepa's tummo practice is one of the most celebrated feats in Tibetan Buddhist history. According to biographical accounts, he meditated in Himalayan caves at altitudes exceeding 5,000 meters, wearing only a white cotton cloth (hence the name "Repa" — "cotton-clad"), in temperatures far below freezing.

他的道歌中多次描述拙火体验：

His songs repeatedly describe the tummo experience:

> "脐下拙火燃，烈焰冲天冠。
> 周身暖融融，何惧冰雪寒？
> 气入中脉行，妄念自消散。
> 明点融降时，大乐满三间。"

> "Tummo blazes below the navel, flames shoot to the crown.
> My whole body warm and blissful — what fear of ice and snow?
> As winds enter the central channel, discursive thoughts dissolve of themselves.
> When drops melt and descend, great bliss fills the three realms."

### 修行方法 Practice Method

虽然拙火的具体修法属于密法，需要灌顶和口传，但其基本框架可以概述：

While the specific tummo practices are esoteric and require empowerment and oral transmission, the basic framework can be outlined:

1. **观想中脉 Visualizing the central channel**：行者观想中脉如透明管道，从会阴到头顶，粗如芦管，明亮如水晶。
   The practitioner visualizes the central channel as a transparent tube from perineum to crown, thick as a reed, bright as crystal.

2. **观想种子字 Visualizing the seed syllable**：在脐轮处观想短"阿"（ཨ）字或火焰，形如倒置的梵文字母，炽热燃烧。
   At the navel, visualize the short "A" (ཨ) syllable or flame, shaped like an inverted Sanskrit letter, blazing with heat.

3. **宝瓶气 Vase breathing (Kumbhaka)**：通过特殊的呼吸技巧，将上下气合于脐下，如同宝瓶封口，增强拙火。
   Through special breathing techniques, upper and lower winds are united below the navel, as if sealed in a vase, intensifying tummo.

4. **拙火升腾 Tummo ascending**：内在热能从脐轮升起，沿中脉上升，依次融化各脉轮的明点。
   Inner heat rises from the navel chakra up through the central channel, melting drops at each chakra in sequence.

5. **四喜体验 Four joys**：明点下降时产生四喜——喜（joy）、胜喜（supreme joy）、极喜（special joy）、俱生喜（innate joy）。
   As drops descend, four joys arise — joy, supreme joy, special joy, and innate joy.

## 拙火与大乐智慧 Tummo and Bliss-Wisdom

拙火的终极目的不是产热本身，而是通过大乐体验来证悟空性。这是无上瑜伽密续的核心见地：大乐与空性不是两个分离的东西，而是同一实相的两个面向。

Tummo's ultimate purpose is not heat production itself but realizing emptiness through the experience of great bliss. This is the core insight of unsurpassed yoga tantra: great bliss and emptiness are not two separate things but two facets of the same reality.

密勒日巴在道歌中如此描述：

Milarepa describes this in his songs:

> "大乐不离空，空不离大乐。
> 乐空无二时，即是佛心要。"

> "Great bliss is inseparable from emptiness; emptiness is inseparable from great bliss.
> When bliss and emptiness are non-dual — this is the heart-essence of Buddhahood."

这种大乐不是世俗的感官快乐，而是当一切概念活动暂时止息时，心性的本来光明自然显现的状态。拙火通过身体层面的操作（气脉修持）来创造这种心灵状态出现的条件。

This great bliss is not worldly sensory pleasure but the state in which mind's innate luminosity naturally manifests when all conceptual activity temporarily ceases. Tummo creates the conditions for this mental state to arise through somatic operations (channel-wind practices).

## 科学视角 Scientific Perspectives

现代科学对拙火进行了有限的研究：

Modern science has conducted limited research on tummo:

**Herbert Benson 的研究**（1980年代）：哈佛大学医学院的 Benson 教授对印度北部修行拙火的僧侣进行了生理测量。研究发现，这些僧侣能够将手指和脚趾的温度提升多达8.3°C，并能在寒冷环境中用身体热量烘干浸泡过冰水的湿布单。

**Herbert Benson's research** (1980s): Professor Benson of Harvard Medical School measured monks practicing tummo in northern India. The study found that practitioners could raise finger and toe temperatures by up to 8.3°C and could dry ice-water-soaked sheets with body heat in cold environments.

**Maria Kozhevnikov 的研究**（2013年）：新加坡国立大学的研究团队在《PLOS ONE》发表研究，发现拙火修行者能够通过特殊的呼吸和观想技术显著提升核心体温，甚至进入轻度发热状态（38.3°C）。

**Maria Kozhevnikov's research** (2013): A team from the National University of Singapore published in *PLOS ONE*, finding that tummo practitioners could significantly raise core body temperature through specialized breathing and visualization techniques, even entering a mild fever state (38.3°C).

值得注意的是，科学研究只能测量拙火的身体效应，而无法触及拙火修行的精神维度——大乐智慧的体验超出了当前科学仪器的测量范围。

Notably, scientific research can only measure tummo's physical effects and cannot access its spiritual dimension — the experience of bliss-wisdom lies beyond current scientific instrumentation.

## 修行警示 Practice Cautions

传统密续文献对拙火修行提出了多重警示：

Traditional tantric literature offers multiple cautions regarding tummo practice:

- **必须依止上师 Rely on a qualified teacher**：拙火修法不可自学，必须在具德上师的指导下修行，否则可能导致"气脉错乱"（lung disorder）。
  Tummo must never be self-taught; practicing without a qualified guru can lead to wind disorders (rlung disorder).

- **前行基础 Preliminary foundation**：需要先完成共同前行（皈依、发心、金刚萨埵等）和不共前行（上师瑜伽）的修持。
  Common preliminaries (refuge, bodhichitta, Vajrasattva, etc.) and uncommon preliminaries (guru yoga) must be completed first.

- **身体准备 Physical preparation**：需要进行特定的身体练习（如幻轮瑜伽/trul khor）来疏通脉道。
  Specific physical exercises (such as trul khor / yantra yoga) are needed to open the channels.

- **动机纯正 Pure motivation**：如果修行拙火是为了追求神通或身体舒适，则偏离了正道。
  Practicing tummo for supernatural powers or physical comfort deviates from the true path.

## 与密勒日巴其他概念的关系 Relation to Other Milarepa Concepts

- **那洛六法 Six Yogas of Naropa**：拙火是六法之首，为其他五法提供能量基础。
  Tummo is the first of the six, providing the energetic foundation for the other five.

- **大手印 Mahamudra**：拙火生起的大乐体验可以成为证悟大手印（心性明空不二）的助缘。
  The bliss experience generated by tummo can serve as a catalyst for realizing Mahamudra.

- **苦行转化 Ascetic Transformation**：拙火使密勒日巴能够在极端恶劣的自然环境中持续苦修。
  Tummo enabled Milarepa to sustain his austere practice in extremely harsh natural environments.
