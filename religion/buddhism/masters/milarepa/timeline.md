# 密勒日巴年表 Milarepa Timeline

> 密勒日巴的生平年代基于桑杰坚赞编纂的传记。部分日期为学术界推算的近似值。传统记载密勒日巴生卒为 1040-1123 或 1052-1135，学界多采后者。
>
> Dates are based on Tsangnyön Heruka's biography. Some dates are scholarly approximations. Traditional records give 1040-1123 or 1052-1135; scholars favor the latter.

## 学术争议 Scholarly Debates

### 年代问题 Dating Issues

- 传统记载密勒日巴生卒为 1040-1123 或 1052-1135，学界多采后者
- Traditional records give 1040-1123 or 1052-1135; scholars favor the latter
- 部分事件的具体年代为推算 / Some specific dates are estimated

### 传记可靠性 Biographical Reliability

- 桑杰坚赞的传记写作于密勒日巴圆寂后约 350 年
- Tsangnyön Heruka's biography was written ~350 years after Milarepa's death
- 传记融合了口传传统和文学创作，但核心事件被认为有历史基础
- Blends oral tradition and literary creation; core events considered historically grounded
- 道歌的早期版本可能更接近密勒日巴本人的教学
- Earlier versions of the songs may be closer to Milarepa's own teaching
