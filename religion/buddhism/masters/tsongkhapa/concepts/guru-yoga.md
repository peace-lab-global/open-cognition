# 上师瑜伽 / Guru Yoga: The Practice of Guru Devotion

## 概念概述 / Overview

上师瑜伽（藏文 bla-ma'i rnal-'byor）是藏传佛教中最核心的修行之一——通过观想、祈请和与上师的精神融合，获得加持（byin-rlabs）与证悟。在宗喀巴的体系中，上师瑜伽占据着独特而关键的位置：它既是菩提道次第的起点（依止善知识），也是密法修持的核心枢纽。

Guru Yoga (Tib. bla-ma'i rnal-'byor) is one of the most central practices in Tibetan Buddhism—achieving blessings (byin-rlabs) and realization through visualization, supplication, and spiritual merging with the guru. In Tsongkhapa's system, guru yoga occupies a unique and pivotal position: it is both the starting point of the lamrim (relying on a spiritual teacher) and the central pivot of tantric practice.

## 依止善知识 / Relying on a Spiritual Teacher

### 在菩提道次第中的位置 / Position in the Lamrim

宗喀巴在《菩提道次第广论》中将"依止善知识"置于整个修行的**最前面**——甚至在讨论暇满难得、念死无常之前。他的理由是：一切修行功德都来源于善知识的教导，如果不首先建立正确的师徒关系，后续的修行都失去了根基。

Tsongkhapa placed "relying on a spiritual teacher" at the very **beginning** of the lamrim—even before discussing precious human rebirth or mindfulness of death. His reasoning: all qualities of practice originate from the teacher's guidance; without first establishing a proper teacher-student relationship, subsequent practice loses its foundation.

### 合格上师的标准 / Qualifications of a Qualified Guru

宗喀巴依据《庄严经论》等经典，列出了合格上师应具备的十项条件：

Drawing on sūtras such as the *Mahāyānasūtrālaṃkāra*, Tsongkhapa listed ten qualifications a qualified guru should possess:

1. **戒律清净** Disciplined (having tamed the mind through ethical discipline)
2. **定力具足** Calm (having pacified afflictions through concentration)
3. **智慧通达** Thoroughly pacified (having attained insight into emptiness)
4. **功德高于弟子** Having qualities superior to the student
5. **精进不懈** Energetic and diligent
6. **博学多闻** Rich in scriptural knowledge
7. **证悟真实** Having realized the nature of reality
8. **善巧说法** Skilled in expounding the Dharma
9. **慈悲为怀** Motivated by loving compassion
10. **不惧疲劳** Having abandoned discouragement

### 依止的方式 / Modes of Reliance

宗喀巴将依止善知识分为两个层面：

Tsongkhapa divided reliance on a guru into two dimensions:

**意乐依止（心理层面的依止）** / Mental Reliance:
- 净信（seeing the guru's qualities, not faults）——视师如佛
- 念恩（remembering the guru's kindness）——思维上师给予的教导之恩
- 净观（pure perception）——以上师的言行为修行的指南

**加行依止（行为层面的依止）** / Behavioral Reliance:
- 以身承事（serving with body）——为上师做事务
- 以语承事（serving with speech）——赞颂上师功德
- 以意承事（serving with mind）——如教修行，这是最高的承事

## 上师瑜伽的修持 / The Practice of Guru Yoga

### 基本修法 / Basic Practice Method

在密乘的传统中，上师瑜伽的修持通常包括以下步骤：

In the tantric tradition, guru yoga practice typically includes these steps:

1. **观想** Visualization：在头顶或心间观想上师——上师的本质与一切诸佛无二无别。在格鲁派传统中，常观想宗喀巴大师的形象。
2. **祈请** Supplication：念诵祈请文（如宗喀巴的"密则玛" mTshams-brDun-ma），请求上师的加持融入自心。
3. **灌顶/加持** Empowerment/Blessing：观想上师身语意的加持以光的形式从三处（顶、喉、心）融入自身。
4. **融合** Merging：观想上师融入自身，自心与上师的智慧心无二无别。安住于此无分别的状态中。

1. **Visualization**: Visualizing the guru above the crown or at the heart—the guru's essence being inseparable from all Buddhas. In the Gelug tradition, one often visualizes Tsongkhapa.
2. **Supplication**: Reciting prayers (such as Tsongkhapa's *Migtsema* / mTshams-brDun-ma), requesting the guru's blessings to enter one's mindstream.
3. **Empowerment/Blessing**: Visualizing the guru's body-speech-mind blessings entering oneself in the form of light from three points (crown, throat, heart).
4. **Merging**: Visualizing the guru dissolving into oneself, one's mind becoming inseparable from the guru's wisdom mind. Resting in this non-dual state.

### 宗喀巴与文殊菩萨的因缘 / Tsongkhapa and Mañjuśrī

宗喀巴的上师瑜伽有着独特的背景——他与文殊菩萨（Mañjuśrī，智慧的化身）有着深厚的精神联系。据传记记载，宗喀巴在修行中多次获得文殊菩萨的直接启示，文殊菩萨不仅指导他的中观见地，还纠正了他对空性的理解偏差。这种"本尊-上师无二"的体验深刻影响了宗喀巴对上师瑜伽的理解——上师不仅是人间的老师，更是智慧本尊的示现。

Tsongkhapa's guru yoga has a unique background—his deep spiritual connection with Mañjuśrī (the embodiment of wisdom). According to biographical accounts, Tsongkhapa received direct guidance from Mañjuśrī multiple times during his practice, with Mañjuśrī not only guiding his Madhyamaka view but also correcting his misunderstandings of emptiness. This "deity-guru non-duality" experience profoundly shaped Tsongkhapa's understanding of guru yoga—the guru is not merely an earthly teacher but a manifestation of the wisdom deity.

### "密则玛"祈请文 / The Migtsema Prayer

宗喀巴最著名的祈请文——"密则玛"（mTshams-brDun-ma），至今仍是格鲁派日常修持的核心内容：

The most famous prayer associated with Tsongkhapa—the *Migtsema* (mTshams-brDun-ma)—remains a core element of daily Gelug practice:

> 无缘大悲藏观自在，/ 无垢智慧主妙音尊，/ 尽除魔军秘密主，/ 贤慧名称正法源，/ 善慧名称我祈请。
>
> *Lord of unpolluted wisdom, gentle Mañjuśrī; / Objectless compassion treasury, noble Avalokiteśvara; / Lord of secrets, destroyer of all demonic forces, Vajrapāṇi; / Crown ornament of all the wise ones of the land of snow, / Losang Drakpa—at your feet I pray.*

此祈请文将宗喀巴视为观音（慈悲）、文殊（智慧）、金刚手（力量）三者的合一——体现了格鲁派上师瑜伽中"上师-本尊-护法无二"的核心理念。

This prayer presents Tsongkhapa as the embodiment of Avalokiteśvara (compassion), Mañjuśrī (wisdom), and Vajrapāṇi (power)—reflecting the Gelug guru yoga principle of "guru-deity-protector non-duality."

## 上师瑜伽的认知意义 / Cognitive Significance

从认知科学的角度看，上师瑜伽可以被理解为一种**模型学习**（model-based learning）的高级形式：

From a cognitive science perspective, guru yoga can be understood as an advanced form of **model-based learning**:

- **镜像与模仿** Mirror and Imitation：通过与上师的"精神融合"，修行者试图内化上师的认知模式、情感模式和行为模式。
- **预期效应** Expectancy Effect：对上师的信心（虔敬）本身产生强烈的积极预期，这在心理学中与安慰剂效应和期望理论相关。
- **权威与信任** Authority and Trust：在信息过载的修行环境中，"依止善知识"提供了一种认知捷径——信任一个经过验证的权威，避免无尽的自我怀疑。

## 争议与反思 / Controversies and Reflections

上师瑜伽在当代面临着重要的挑战：

Guru yoga faces significant challenges in the contemporary era:

- **权力滥用** Abuse of Power：历史上和当代都有上师利用弟子的信心进行剥削和虐待的案例。
- **理性与信仰的张力** Tension between Reason and Faith：宗喀巴本人强调弟子应以理性审视上师的教导（"依法不依人"），但实践中，"视师如佛"的要求可能与批判性思维产生冲突。
- **现代性的挑战** Challenges of Modernity：在信息透明、权力平等的现代社会，传统师徒关系的权威模式面临重新定义。

宗喀巴自己的教导提供了平衡：他强调在依止之前应**长期、仔细地考察**上师的品格与学识，但一旦建立了师徒关系，就应以恭敬心依止。

Tsongkhapa's own teaching offers a balance: he emphasized that students should **carefully and extensively examine** the guru's character and learning before committing, but once the relationship is established, one should rely on the teacher with respect.
