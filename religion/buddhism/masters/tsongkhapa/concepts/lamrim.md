# 菩提道次第 / Lamrim: The Stages of the Path to Enlightenment

## 概念概述 / Overview

菩提道次第（Lam-rim，梵文 *mārga-krama*）是宗喀巴最具影响力的教学贡献之一。它将佛陀的全部教法组织为一条从凡夫到成佛的渐进路径，使修行者能够清晰地看到自己在修行道上的位置、下一步的方向以及各阶段之间的关系。

The Stages of the Path (Lam-rim; Skt. *mārga-krama*) is one of Tsongkhapa's most influential pedagogical contributions. It organizes the entirety of the Buddha's teachings into a gradual path from ordinary being to Buddhahood, enabling practitioners to clearly see their position on the path, the next direction to take, and the relationships between stages.

这一体系并非宗喀巴首创——其原型可追溯至阿底峡尊者（Atiśa, 982–1054）的《菩提道灯论》（*Bodhipathapradīpa*）。但宗喀巴的《菩提道次第广论》（Lam-rim chen-mo）将阿底峡的纲要扩展为一部百科全书式的巨著，涵盖了从基本的因果信仰到最精微的空性禅修的全部内容。

This system was not Tsongkhapa's original invention—its prototype traces back to Atiśa's *Bodhipathapradīpa* (*Lamp for the Path to Enlightenment*, 982–1054). But Tsongkhapa's *Great Treatise on the Stages of the Path* expanded Atiśa's outline into an encyclopedic magnum opus, covering everything from basic karmic faith to the most subtle emptiness meditation.

## 三士道结构 / The Tri-scope Structure

### 下士道 / Initial Scope (skyes-bu chung-ngu)

**核心动机**：追求来世善趣（天、人善道），避免堕入恶趣（地狱、饿鬼、畜生）。
**Core motivation**: Seeking better future lives (heavenly or human realms), avoiding rebirth in lower realms (hell, hungry ghosts, animals).

下士道的修行内容包括：
Practice content of the initial scope includes:

- **依止善知识**（bsTen-pa bsten-pa）：寻找并依止合格的修行导师。宗喀巴认为这是"一切功德之根本"。
- **Relying on a Spiritual Teacher**: Finding and relying on a qualified guide. Tsongkhapa considers this "the root of all qualities."

- **暇满难得**（dal-'byor rnyed-dka'）：认识到具足修行条件的人身极为珍贵且难得，从而生起修行的紧迫感。
- **Precious Human Rebirth**: Recognizing that a human life with the conditions for practice is extremely precious and rare, generating urgency for practice.

- **念死无常**（'chi-ba mi-rtag-pa）：深入思维死亡的必然性与时间的不确定性，打破对今生的贪执。
- **Mindfulness of Death and Impermanence**: Deeply contemplating the certainty of death and the uncertainty of its timing, breaking attachment to this life.

- **三恶趣苦**（ngan-'gro'i sdug-bsngal）：思维堕入恶趣的痛苦，生起对轮回的警觉。
- **Suffering of Lower Realms**: Contemplating the suffering of lower rebirths, cultivating vigilance regarding samsara.

- **皈依三宝**（skyabs-'gro）：基于对因果与轮回的理解，至诚皈依佛、法、僧。
- **Taking Refuge**: Based on understanding karma and samsara, sincerely taking refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and Sangha.

- **因果业报**（las-'bras）：深信业力法则，断恶修善。
- **Karma**: Deep faith in the law of cause and effect, abandoning non-virtue and cultivating virtue.

### 中士道 / Middle Scope (skyes-bu 'bring)

**核心动机**：追求从轮回中彻底解脱（涅槃）。
**Core motivation**: Seeking complete liberation from samsara (nirvāṇa).

中士道的关键修行：
Key practices of the middle scope:

- **四圣谛**（'phags-pa'i bden-pa bzhi）：苦谛（认识苦的本质）、集谛（认识苦的根源——烦恼与业）、灭谛（解脱的可能性）、道谛（通往解脱的道路）。
- **The Four Noble Truths**: Suffering (recognizing its nature), Origin (recognizing its root—afflictions and karma), Cessation (the possibility of liberation), Path (the road to liberation).

- **十二缘起**（rten-'brel yan-lag bcu-gnyis）：从无明到老死的因果链条，理解轮回的运作机制。
- **Twelve Links of Dependent Origination**: The causal chain from ignorance to aging and death, understanding the mechanics of samsara.

- **出离心**（nges-'byung）：对轮回产生真实的厌离，不再被世间八法（利衰毁誉称讥苦乐）所左右。这是进入上士道的前提。
- **Renunciation**: Genuine disillusionment with samsara, no longer swayed by the eight worldly concerns (gain/loss, praise/blame, fame/disgrace, pleasure/pain). This is the prerequisite for entering the great scope.

### 上士道 / Great Scope (skyes-bu rgan-po)

**核心动机**：为利益一切众生而追求无上正等正觉。
**Core motivation**: Seeking unsurpassed perfect enlightenment for the benefit of all sentient beings.

上士道的两大支柱：
Two pillars of the great scope:

**一、菩提心修持 / Cultivating Bodhicitta**：

- **七因果教授**（rgyu-'bras man-ngag bdun）：知母→念恩→报恩→悦意慈→大悲→增上意乐→菩提心。
- **Sevenfold Cause-and-Effect**: Recognizing all beings as mothers → remembering kindness → wishing to repay → affectionate love → great compassion → exceptional resolve → bodhicitta.

- **自他交换**（bdag-gzhan brje-ba / tonglen）：将自我中心转化为他者中心的认知重构练习。
- **Exchanging Self and Others** (tonglen): A cognitive restructuring exercise transforming self-centeredness into other-centeredness.

- **菩萨戒**（byang-sems sdom-pa）：受持十八根本戒与四十六恶作戒。
- **Bodhisattva Vows**: Taking the eighteen root vows and forty-six secondary precepts.

**二、六度四摄 / Six Pāramitās and Four Ways of Gathering**：

六度（pāramitā）：布施、持戒、忍辱、精进、禅定、般若。四摄：布施、爱语、利行、同事。
Six Perfections: generosity, ethical discipline, patience, joyful effort, concentration, wisdom. Four Ways of Gathering: giving, kind speech, beneficial conduct, companionship.

## 认知意义 / Cognitive Significance

从认知科学的角度看，菩提道次第是一个精密的**认知发展课程**：

From a cognitive science perspective, lamrim is a sophisticated **cognitive development curriculum**:

- **下士道 = 动机管理**：通过思维因果与死亡，重新校准短期动机与长期目标。
- **Initial Scope = Motivation Management**: Recalibrating short-term motivations against long-term goals through contemplation of karma and death.

- **中士道 = 认知出离**：通过分析苦的根源，学会从自动化的执取模式中退出。
- **Middle Scope = Cognitive Disengagement**: Learning to exit automated clinging patterns by analyzing the roots of suffering.

- **上士道 = 认知重定向与认知解构**：通过菩提心将认知从自我中心转向他者中心（重定向），通过空性见彻底瓦解一切自性见（解构）。
- **Great Scope = Cognitive Redirection and Deconstruction**: Redirecting cognition from self-centered to other-centered through bodhicitta, and thoroughly deconstructing all essentialist views through emptiness insight.
