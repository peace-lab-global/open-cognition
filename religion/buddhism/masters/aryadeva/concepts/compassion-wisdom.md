# 悲智双运 Karuna-Prajna / The Union of Compassion and Wisdom

## 定义 / Definition

**"悲智双运"(karuna-prajna-yuganaddha)** 是大乘佛教菩萨道的核心理想——大悲 (karuna) 与般若智慧 (prajna) 不可分离、相互支撑。提婆在《四百论》第 8 品第 15 颂中提出了中观学派关于此命题最具影响力的表述：

**"The union of compassion and wisdom"** (karuna-prajna-yuganaddha) is the core ideal of the Mahayana bodhisattva path — great compassion (karuna) and wisdom (prajna) are inseparable and mutually sustaining. In verse 8.15 of the *Catuhsataka*, Aryadeva offers the most influential Madhyamaka formulation of this principle:

> "诸佛世尊以大悲为体，不以空性为体。"
> "The Buddhas take great compassion as their essence, not emptiness."

这一命题的意义在于：空性智慧是菩萨修行的手段，大悲利他才是菩萨修行的目的。觉悟不是认知的完成，而是伦理行动的无限展开。

The significance of this proposition: emptiness-wisdom is the *means* of bodhisattva practice; great compassion for others is its *purpose*. Awakening is not the completion of cognition but the infinite unfolding of ethical action.

---

## 命题的哲学分析 / Philosophical Analysis of the Thesis

### "以……为体"的含义 / The Meaning of "Taking as Essence"

在梵文和藏文语境中，"以……为体"(svabhava/bdagnid) 意味着"以……为根本特征"或"以……为定义性属性"。提婆的意思是：如果我们要用一个词来定义佛的本质特征，那个词不是"空性"(sunyata)，而是"大悲"(mahakaruna)。

In the Sanskrit and Tibetan context, "taking as essence" (svabhava) means "having as fundamental characteristic" or "having as defining attribute." Aryadeva's point: if we must identify the defining characteristic of a Buddha in one word, that word is not "emptiness" (sunyata) but "great compassion" (mahakaruna).

这不意味着空性不重要——空性是通向大悲的必经之路。没有空性智慧，大悲就会退化为有情执（有能悲者、所悲者、悲的行为三者实有的执著）。但没有大悲，空性就可能退化为"自了汉"的个人解脱——这正是大乘佛教批判小乘的核心论点。

This does not mean emptiness is unimportant — emptiness is the necessary path to compassion. Without emptiness-wisdom, compassion degenerates into sentimental attachment (clinging to the reality of the compassionate agent, the object of compassion, and the compassionate act). But without compassion, emptiness risks degenerating into personal liberation — precisely the Mahayana critique of the Hinayana.

### 空性与伦理的优先性问题 / The Priority Question: Emptiness or Ethics?

提婆的命题引发了一个深刻的哲学问题：在菩萨道中，空性和大悲哪个更为根本？

Aryadeva's thesis raises a profound philosophical question: in the bodhisattva path, which is more fundamental — emptiness or compassion?

| 立场 Position | 主张 Claim | 代表 Representative |
|-------------|-----------|-------------------|
| 智慧优先 | 空性是基础，大悲是其自然流露 | 部分中观学者 |
| 大悲优先 | 大悲是目的，空性是手段 | 提婆、月称 |
| 不二 | 二者不可分割，无所谓先后 | 般若经传统 |

提婆明确站在"大悲优先"的立场——但这不是一种线性优先关系，而是一种目的论优先：空性是认知条件，大悲是终极目的。

Aryadeva clearly stands on the "compassion-first" position — but this is not a linear priority but a teleological one: emptiness is the cognitive condition, compassion is the ultimate purpose.

## "无缘大悲"的概念 / The Concept of "Objectless Great Compassion"

提婆的"大悲为体"命题指向一种特殊的悲心形态——**无缘大悲 (niralambana-karuna)**，即不依托任何实有对象的大悲。

Aryadeva's "compassion as essence" thesis points toward a special form of compassion — **objectless great compassion (niralambana-karuna)**, compassion that does not rely on any substantially real object.

### 三种悲心 / Three Types of Compassion

月称在《入中论》(Madhyamakavatara) 中区分三种悲心，提婆的思想为这一区分提供了基础：

Candrakirti in the *Madhyamakavatara* distinguishes three types of compassion, building on Aryadeva's foundation:

1. **众生缘悲 (sattvalambana-karuna)**: 以实有众生为对象的悲心——凡夫的悲心
2. **法缘悲 (dharmalambana-karuna)**: 了知众生如幻但仍以"法"为对象的悲心——初地以上菩萨的悲心
3. **无缘悲 (niralambana-karuna)**: 不依托任何对象、自然流露的悲心——佛的悲心

提婆的"大悲为体"指的是第三种——无缘大悲。这是空性智慧彻底完成后的悲心形态：不再有能悲者、所悲者的二元对立，悲心如阳光般自然照耀。

Aryadeva's "compassion as essence" refers to the third type — objectless compassion. This is the form of compassion that arises when emptiness-wisdom is fully realized: no more subject-object duality between the compassionate one and the object of compassion; compassion radiates naturally, like sunlight.

## 与龙树思想的关联 / Connection with Nagarjuna's Thought

龙树在《大智度论》（若归于其学派传统）中有类似表述："般若为母，方便为父"——般若智慧是生出觉悟的"母亲"，但大悲和善巧方便是将觉悟转化为利他行动的"父亲"。

Nagarjuna (or his school tradition in the *Mahaprajnaparamita-sastra*) offers a similar expression: "Wisdom is the mother, skillful means the father" — wisdom gives birth to awakening, but compassion and skillful means transform awakening into altruistic action.

但提婆将这一关系更加明确化和极端化——"不以空性为体"的表述是对"空性崇拜"的直接警告：不要把空性本身当作目的。

But Aryadeva makes this relationship more explicit and more radical — the expression "not emptiness as essence" is a direct warning against "emptiness-worship": do not make emptiness itself the goal.

## 菩萨道的伦理结构 / Ethical Structure of the Bodhisattva Path

提婆的"悲智双运"为菩萨道提供了一个清晰的伦理结构：

Aryadeva's "union of compassion and wisdom" provides a clear ethical structure for the bodhisattva path:

```
空性智慧 (Prajna)
    ↓ 瓦解自我中心的认知
破除四倒 (Viparyasa-prahana)
    ↓ 消解能所二元对立
无我大悲 (Niratman-karuna)
    ↓ 自然流向一切众生
利他行动 (Parartha-kriya)
    ↓ 无穷尽的菩萨行
成佛 (Buddhatva)
    = 大悲的完全实现 (而非空性的"拥有")
```

**Emptiness-wisdom (Prajna)** → dismantles egocentric cognition → **Refuting four inversions** → dissolves subject-object duality → **Selfless great compassion** → flows naturally to all beings → **Altruistic action** → endless bodhisattva activity → **Buddhahood** = the complete realization of compassion (not the "possession" of emptiness).

## 与西方伦理学的对话 / Dialogue with Western Ethics

### 列维纳斯 Levinas: 伦理优先于本体论

列维纳斯主张"伦理学是第一哲学"——对"他者面容"(visage) 的伦理回应先于一切本体论追问。提婆的"大悲为体"与列维纳斯形成跨文化共鸣：伦理行动（大悲）不是本体论认知（空性）的附属品，而是其目的和完成。

Levinas argues "ethics is first philosophy" — the ethical response to the "face of the Other" (visage) precedes all ontological inquiry. Aryadeva's "compassion as essence" resonates across cultures: ethical action (compassion) is not an appendage to ontological cognition (emptiness) but its purpose and fulfillment.

### 叔本华 Schopenhauer: 悲悯作为伦理基础

叔本华认为悲悯 (Mitleid) 是唯一的伦理动机——通过认识到"他者即自我"（梵我一如的影响），自然生起悲悯。与提婆的区别在于：叔本华的悲悯基于形而上学的一元论，而提婆的无缘大悲恰恰基于对一切形而上学（包括一元论）的否定。

Schopenhauer holds that compassion (Mitleid) is the sole ethical motivation — arising naturally from recognizing "the other is myself." The difference: Schopenhauer's compassion rests on metaphysical monism, while Aryadeva's objectless compassion arises precisely from negating all metaphysics, including monism.

### 功利主义 Utilitarianism: 利他的计算 vs 利他的自然流露

功利主义以"最大多数人的最大幸福"为伦理原则，需要理性计算。提婆的大悲则超越了计算——无缘大悲不是基于效用最大化的考量，而是空性智慧的自然流露，如阳光普照不计对象。

Utilitarianism takes "the greatest happiness of the greatest number" as its ethical principle, requiring rational calculation. Aryadeva's great compassion transcends calculation — objectless compassion is not based on utility maximization but is the natural expression of emptiness-wisdom, like sunlight shining without discriminating its objects.

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

### 社会参与佛教 / Engaged Buddhism

提婆的"大悲为体"为当代"入世佛教"(Engaged Buddhism) 提供了古典理论基础——一行禅师 (Thich Nhat Hanh) 和 Sulak Sivaraksa 等人的社会实践可以被解读为"空性智慧转化为利他行动"的现代案例。

Aryadeva's "compassion as essence" provides classical theoretical grounding for contemporary "Engaged Buddhism" — the social practice of figures like Thich Nhat Hanh and Sulak Sivaraksa can be read as modern instances of "emptiness-wisdom transforming into altruistic action."

### 护理伦理 / Care Ethics

现代护理伦理学 (care ethics, 如 Carol Gilligan, Nel Noddings) 强调关系性和回应性的伦理——而非抽象的正义原则。提婆的无缘大悲与护理伦理的"回应他者需要"的核心精神形成共鸣。

Modern care ethics (Carol Gilligan, Nel Noddings) emphasizes relational and responsive ethics rather than abstract principles of justice. Aryadeva's objectless compassion resonates with care ethics' core spirit of "responding to the needs of the other."

### AI 伦理 / AI Ethics

"大悲为体"的框架为 AI 伦理提出独特视角：AI 系统的"智慧"（计算能力、认知能力）必须服务于"悲"（利益众生）。能力本身不是目的——如果 AI 拥有超人智慧但不具有利他导向，它就不是"觉悟"的系统。

The "compassion as essence" framework offers a unique perspective for AI ethics: an AI system's "wisdom" (computational power, cognitive ability) must serve "compassion" (benefit of all beings). Capability itself is not the goal — if AI possesses superhuman intelligence without an altruistic orientation, it is not an "awakened" system.
