# 戒相 · Precept-Manifestation (Jiè Xiàng)

> "戒法有体，戒体有相。相以显体，体以起用。"
> "The precept-law has its substance; the precept-substance has its manifestation. Manifestation reveals substance; substance gives rise to function."

---

## 一、概念定义 · Definition

**戒相**（jiè xiàng），是道宣律学中关于戒条**具体表现形式和适用条件**的概念。每一戒条都不是抽象的原则，而是有其特定的**犯缘**（构成犯戒的条件）、**开遮**（允许与禁止的情况）和**轻重等级**。戒相就是对每一条戒的完整"操作说明"。

**Precept-manifestation** (戒相, *jiè xiàng*) is the concept in Daoxuan's Vinaya system that addresses the **concrete expression and operational conditions** of each precept. Every precept is not an abstract principle but possesses specific **conditions of transgression** (犯缘), **allowances and restrictions** (开遮), and **gradations of severity**. Precept-manifestation is, in effect, the complete "operational specification" of each rule.

在道宣的律学体系中，戒相与戒体、戒法构成了一个三位一体的结构：

In Daoxuan's Vinaya system, precept-manifestation forms a triadic structure with precept-substance (戒体) and precept-law (戒法):

| 概念 | 含义 | 性质 |
|------|------|------|
| **戒法** | 佛陀制定的戒律条文 | 客观标准 |
| **戒体** | 受戒时在心中获得的防非止恶之力 | 主观获得 |
| **戒行** | 依戒体而发的具体持戒行为 | 实践行为 |
| **戒相** | 戒条的具体表现和适用条件 | 分析框架 |

| Concept | Meaning | Nature |
|---------|---------|--------|
| **Precept-law** (戒法) | The rules established by the Buddha | Objective standard |
| **Precept-substance** (戒体) | The anti-transgression force received during ordination | Subjective acquisition |
| **Precept-conduct** (戒行) | Concrete acts of precept-keeping arising from the substance | Practical behavior |
| **Precept-manifestation** (戒相) | Concrete expression and application conditions of each rule | Analytical framework |

## 二、犯缘分析 · Analysis of Transgression Conditions

### 2.1 何谓"犯缘" · What Are "Conditions of Transgression"?

**犯缘**（fàn yuán）是指构成犯戒所必须具备的条件。在道宣的分析中，一个行为要被判定为"犯戒"，必须同时满足若干条件。如果其中任何一个条件不具备，则不构成犯戒，或犯戒的等级降低。

**Conditions of transgression** (犯缘, *fàn yuán*) are the conditions that must all be met for an action to constitute a precept violation. In Daoxuan's analysis, for an act to be judged as "transgressing a precept," several conditions must be simultaneously satisfied. If any single condition is absent, the act either does not constitute a transgression or the severity is reduced.

### 2.2 以"杀戒"为例 · Example: The Precept Against Killing

以比丘戒第一条波罗夷——杀戒为例，道宣分析了构成犯戒的条件：

Taking the first pārājika for bhikṣus — the precept against killing — Daoxuan analyzed the conditions required for a full transgression:

1. **对象条件**：所杀的对象必须是"人"（非畜生、非非人）
   **Object condition**: The target must be a "human being" (not an animal, not a non-human being)

2. **认知条件**：行者必须知道对方是"人"
   **Cognitive condition**: The agent must know the target is a "human being"

3. **动机条件**：行者必须有"杀心"（故意杀人）
   **Motivational condition**: The agent must have the "intent to kill" (deliberate homicide)

4. **行为条件**：行者必须实施了杀的行为（亲手杀、使人杀等）
   **Action condition**: The agent must have performed the act of killing (killing by one's own hand, causing another to kill, etc.)

5. **结果条件**：被杀者确实因此死亡
   **Result condition**: The victim actually dies as a result

如果五个条件全部满足，构成**波罗夷**（pārājika），即最严重的犯戒，行者被逐出僧团。如果某些条件不完全满足，可能构成较轻的犯戒（如僧残、波逸提等）。

If all five conditions are met, a full **pārājika** (the gravest offense) is constituted, and the agent is expelled from the saṅgha. If some conditions are not fully met, a lesser offense (such as saṅghāvaśeṣa or pācittika) may be constituted.

### 2.3 犯缘分析的方法论意义 · Methodological Significance

这种精细的犯缘分析，展示了道宣律学的**分析精确性**。每一条戒都有其特定的犯缘组合，不能一概而论。这种方法的精密程度，可以与现代刑法学中的犯罪构成要件分析相类比。

This meticulous analysis of transgression conditions demonstrates the **analytical precision** of Daoxuan's Vinaya scholarship. Each precept has its specific combination of transgression conditions and cannot be treated uniformly. The sophistication of this method invites comparison with the analysis of elements of a crime in modern criminal law.

## 三、开遮原则 · Principles of Allowance and Restriction

### 3.1 何谓"开遮" · What Is "Allowance and Restriction"?

**开遮**（kāi zhē）是戒相分析中另一个核心概念：

**Allowance and restriction** (开遮, *kāi zhē*) is another core concept in precept-manifestation analysis:

- **遮**（zhē, Restriction）：在通常情况下禁止某种行为——这是戒条的常态。
  **Restriction** (遮): Under normal circumstances, a certain behavior is prohibited — this is the default state of the rule.

- **开**（kāi, Allowance）：在特殊情况下允许通常被禁止的行为——这是戒条的例外。
  **Allowance** (开): Under special circumstances, normally prohibited behavior is permitted — this is the exception to the rule.

### 3.2 "开"的条件 · Conditions for Allowance

道宣强调，"开"不是随意的，而是有严格的条件限制。一般来说，"开"的条件包括：

Daoxuan emphasized that "allowance" is not arbitrary but subject to strict conditions. Generally, the conditions for allowance include:

1. **生命危急**：涉及生命危险的情况（如病人需要食用通常被禁止的食物）
   **Life-threatening emergency**: Situations involving danger to life (e.g., a patient needing food normally prohibited)

2. **无心之失**：行者没有犯戒的主观意图
   **Unintentional action**: The agent had no subjective intent to transgress

3. **精神异常**：行者在精神不正常状态下的行为
   **Mental incapacity**: Actions performed while mentally incapacitated

4. **被迫行为**：行者在被强迫情况下的行为
   **Coerced action**: Actions performed under duress

### 3.3 "开遮"的伦理意义 · Ethical Significance of Allowance and Restriction

"开遮"原则体现了道宣律学的**灵活性和人本关怀**。戒律不是僵硬的教条，而是有弹性的规范体系。在坚持原则（遮）的同时，允许在特殊情况下灵活变通（开），这显示了戒律的人文精神和实践智慧。

The allowance-restriction principle embodies the **flexibility and humanistic concern** of Daoxuan's Vinaya scholarship. The precepts are not rigid dogma but an elastic normative system. Upholding principles (restriction) while allowing flexibility in special circumstances (allowance) demonstrates the humanistic spirit and practical wisdom of the Vinaya.

## 四、轻重等级 · Gradations of Severity

### 4.1 戒罪的分类 · Classification of Precept Offenses

道宣对犯戒行为进行了系统的等级分类：

Daoxuan systematically classified transgressions into gradations:

| 等级 | 梵文 | 中文 | 严重程度 | 处分方式 |
|------|------|------|----------|----------|
| 1 | Pārājika | 波罗夷 | 最严重 | 逐出僧团 |
| 2 | Saṅghāvaśeṣa | 僧残 | 严重 | 僧团处分、别住 |
| 3 | Aniyata | 不定 | 待定 | 根据情况判定 |
| 4 | Naiḥsargika-pācittika | 舍堕 | 中等 | 舍财+忏悔 |
| 5 | Pācittika | 波逸提 | 较轻 | 向他人忏悔 |
| 6 | Pratideśanīya | 提舍尼 | 轻微 | 自我忏悔 |
| 7 | Śaikṣa-dharma | 众学法 | 最轻微 | 学习改正 |

| Grade | Sanskrit | Chinese | Severity | Disposition |
|-------|----------|---------|----------|-------------|
| 1 | Pārājika | 波罗夷 | Gravest | Expulsion from saṅgha |
| 2 | Saṅghāvaśeṣa | 僧残 | Severe | Saṅgha disciplinary action, probation |
| 3 | Aniyata | 不定 | Undetermined | Determined case by case |
| 4 | Naiḥsargika-pācittika | 舍堕 | Moderate | Forfeiture + confession |
| 5 | Pācittika | 波逸提 | Less severe | Confession to another |
| 6 | Pratideśanīya | 提舍尼 | Minor | Self-confession |
| 7 | Śaikṣa-dharma | 众学法 | Least | Correction through learning |

### 4.2 量刑的考量因素 · Sentencing Considerations

在判定犯戒的轻重等级时，道宣考虑了多种因素：

In determining the severity grade of a transgression, Daoxuan considered multiple factors:

- **动机**（cetana）：故意犯戒重于无心犯戒
  **Motivation** (cetana): Deliberate transgression is heavier than unintentional

- **认知**：明知故犯重于不知而犯
  **Knowledge**: Knowing transgression is heavier than unknowing

- **结果**：造成严重后果重于未造成后果
  **Consequence**: Causing serious harm is heavier than no harm caused

- **频率**：屡犯不改重于初犯
  **Frequency**: Repeated offense is heavier than first offense

- **忏悔态度**：隐瞒不忏悔重于主动忏悔
  **Confession attitude**: Concealment is heavier than voluntary confession

## 五、持犯分析 · Analysis of Observance and Transgression

### 5.1 持戒与犯戒 · Observance and Transgression

道宣将戒相的分析分为**持**（observance）和**犯**（transgression）两个方面：

Daoxuan divided precept-manifestation analysis into two aspects — **observance** (持) and **transgression** (犯):

- **持戒**：在面临可能犯戒的情境时，戒体发挥作用，行者自觉远离犯戒行为。
  **Observance**: When facing circumstances that might lead to transgression, the precept-substance functions and the practitioner consciously avoids the transgressive act.

- **犯戒**：戒体未能发挥作用，行者实施了犯戒行为。
  **Transgression**: The precept-substance fails to function and the practitioner commits the transgressive act.

### 5.2 止持与作持 · Cessation-Observance and Action-Observance

道宣进一步将"持"分为两类：

Daoxuan further subdivided "observance" into two types:

- **止持**（zhǐ chí）：通过**不做**某事来持戒——如不杀、不盗、不淫、不妄语。这是针对"性戒"（本质上即为恶的行为）的持守方式。
  **Cessation-observance** (止持): Observing precepts by **refraining from** certain actions — not killing, not stealing, not engaging in sexual misconduct, not lying. This applies to "natural offenses" (性戒, actions inherently wrong).

- **作持**（zuò chí）：通过**主动做**某事来持戒——如参加布萨、如法行羯磨、按时忏悔。这是针对"遮戒"（佛陀因特定因缘而制定的戒条）的持守方式。
  **Action-observance** (作持): Observing precepts by **actively performing** certain actions — participating in the uposatha ceremony, properly conducting karma rituals, confessing on time. This applies to "regulatory precepts" (遮戒, rules established by the Buddha for specific circumstances).

这一区分显示了道宣对戒律实践的深刻理解：持戒不仅是"不做坏事"（止持），还包括"积极行善"（作持）。

This distinction reveals Daoxuan's deep understanding of precept practice: observing precepts means not only "refraining from wrong" (cessation-observance) but also "actively doing good" (action-observance).

## 六、与现代法学的对话 · Dialogue with Modern Legal Theory

### 6.1 犯罪构成要件 · Elements of a Crime

道宣的犯缘分析可以与现代刑法学中的**犯罪构成要件**理论进行有意义的比较：

Daoxuan's transgression-condition analysis invites meaningful comparison with the **elements of a crime** in modern criminal law:

| 现代刑法 | 道宣律学 |
|----------|----------|
| 犯罪客体（受保护的法益） | 犯缘中的"对象条件" |
| 犯罪客观方面（行为、结果、因果关系） | 犯缘中的"行为条件"和"结果条件" |
| 犯罪主体（责任能力） | 犯缘中的"认知条件" |
| 犯罪主观方面（故意、过失） | 犯缘中的"动机条件" |

| Modern Criminal Law | Daoxuan's Vinaya |
|---------------------|------------------|
| Object of the crime (protected legal interest) | "Object condition" in transgression conditions |
| Objective aspect (act, result, causation) | "Action condition" and "result condition" |
| Subject (capacity for responsibility) | "Cognitive condition" |
| Subjective aspect (intent, negligence) | "Motivational condition" |

### 6.2 比例原则 · Principle of Proportionality

道宣的轻重等级分类体现了类似现代法学中的**比例原则**：犯戒的处罚应与犯戒行为的严重程度成比例。这种"量刑"的精细程度，显示了佛教戒律体系的成熟和理性。

Daoxuan's severity grading embodies a principle analogous to the **proportionality principle** in modern law: the penalty for transgression should be proportional to the severity of the act. This sophisticated "sentencing" system demonstrates the maturity and rationality of the Buddhist Vinaya system.

## 七、弘一法师的戒相研究 · Master Hongyi's Precept-Manifestation Studies

近代弘一法师对戒相的研究做出了重要贡献。他的**《四分律比丘戒相表记》**以表格形式清晰呈现了比丘二百五十戒的犯缘、开遮和轻重等级，是现代学习南山律最重要的参考书之一。

Master Hongyi made significant contributions to the study of precept-manifestation in modern times. His **Tabular Presentation of the Bhikṣu Precepts of the Dharmaguptaka Vinaya** (四分律比丘戒相表记) presents the transgression conditions, allowances/restrictions, and severity gradations of the 250 bhikṣu precepts in clear tabular format, making it one of the most important modern references for studying Nanshan Vinaya.

弘一法师的工作使得道宣的戒相分析从浩繁的注疏中**提炼出来**，变得更加清晰和易于学习，这对南山律的现代传承具有重要意义。

Master Hongyi's work **extracted** Daoxuan's precept-manifestation analysis from voluminous commentaries, making it clearer and more accessible for study — a contribution of great significance for the modern transmission of Nanshan Vinaya.
