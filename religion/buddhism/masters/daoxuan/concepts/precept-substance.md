# 戒体论 · Theory of Precept-Substance (Jiè Tǐ)

> "戒体者，所谓纳圣法于心胸，防非止恶之功能也。"
> "The precept-substance is the function of receiving the noble Dharma into one's mind-stream — a power that prevents wrongdoing and halts evil."

---

## 一、概念定义 · Definition

**戒体**（jiè tǐ），是道宣律学的核心概念，指在受戒者通过如法的羯磨仪式（karma ritual）接受戒律时，在其身心相续中所产生的一种**"防非止恶"的功能体**。换言之，戒体不是外在的条文，而是受戒仪式在受戒者内在建立的一种持续性的道德力量。

The **precept-substance** (*jiè tǐ*) is the foundational concept in Daoxuan's Vinaya theory. It refers to the **anti-transgression force** (防非止恶) that arises in the mind-body continuum of the ordinand upon receiving the precepts through a properly conducted karma ritual (羯磨). In other words, the precept-substance is not an external code but an enduring moral power established within the ordinand by the ordination ritual.

---

## 二、部派佛教的争论 · Sectarian Debates on Precept-Substance

关于戒体的性质，印度部派佛教各派存在重大分歧：

The nature of the precept-substance was a major point of contention among Indian Buddhist sects:

### 2.1 说一切有部（Sarvāstivāda）· The Realist Position

有部认为戒体是一种**"无表色"**（avijñapti-rūpa）——即一种不可见、不可对的物质性存在。受戒时的身口意行为（表业）产生了一种持续的物质性力量（无表业），这种力量就是戒体。有部主张"色法实有"，因此将戒体归入色法范畴。

The Sarvāstivādins held that the precept-substance is a form of **"non-manifest matter"** (无表色, *avijñapti-rūpa*) — an invisible, non-resistant material entity. The bodily and verbal actions during ordination (manifest karma) produce a persistent material force (non-manifest karma), which constitutes the precept-substance. Since they maintained that dharmas are substantially real, they classified the precept-substance as a material dharma.

### 2.2 经量部（Sautrāntika）· The Nominalist Position

经量部不承认"无表色"的实有性，认为戒体不过是**身口意行为的相续状态**——即"思心所"（cetanā，意志力）的延续。受戒时的善"思"在心中形成了一种习惯性力量，这就是所谓的戒体。

The Sautrāntikas rejected the substantial reality of non-manifest matter, holding that the precept-substance is merely the **continuing state of mental volition** (思心所, *cetanā*). The wholesome volition present during ordination establishes a habitual force in the mind-stream — this constitutes the precept-substance.

### 2.3 大众部（Mahāsāṅghika）· The Mentalist Position

大众部倾向于将戒体理解为一种**心理状态**或**心所法**，强调其与心识的关联性。

The Mahāsāṅghikas tended to understand the precept-substance as a **mental state** or **mental factor** (心所法), emphasizing its association with consciousness.

## 三、道宣的独创性解答 · Daoxuan's Original Resolution

### 3.1 "非色非心"说 · "Neither Form Nor Mind"

道宣对上述争论进行了创造性的综合。他依据唯识学的理论框架，提出了**"非色非心"**的戒体观：

Daoxuan offered a creative synthesis of these sectarian positions. Drawing on the theoretical framework of Yogācāra (唯识), he proposed the **"neither form nor mind"** (非色非心) account of precept-substance:

- **非色**（非物质性）：戒体不是有部所说的"无表色"，因为它不具有空间延展性或物理属性。
  **Not form** (非物质性): The precept-substance is not the Sarvāstivādin "non-manifest matter," because it lacks spatial extension or physical properties.

- **非心**（非心理活动）：戒体也不是经量部所说的纯粹心理现象，因为它具有超越单一心理刹那的持续性——它不需要时刻被意识到就能发挥防非止恶的作用。
  **Not mind** (非心理活动): Nor is it a purely mental phenomenon as the Sautrāntikas held, because it possesses a persistence that transcends individual mental moments — it prevents wrongdoing without requiring constant conscious attention.

### 3.2 唯识种子说 · Yogācāra Seed Theory

道宣以唯识学的**"种子"（bīja）理论**来解释戒体的本质和运作机制：

Daoxuan used the Yogācāra **seed theory** (种子说) to explain the nature and mechanism of the precept-substance:

1. **受戒时**：在如法的羯磨仪式中，受戒者的身业（礼拜、胡跪等）、口业（表白、答问等）和意业（至诚恳切的发心）三业清净活动，在**阿赖耶识**（ālaya-vijñāna，第八识）中熏习了善种子。
   **During ordination**: The pure activities of body (prostrations, kneeling), speech (declarations, responses), and mind (sincere aspiration) during the properly conducted karma ritual plant wholesome seeds in the **storehouse consciousness** (阿赖耶识, *ālaya-vijñāna*).

2. **戒体即种子**：这些被熏习的善种子就是戒体。它们不是外来的附加物，而是受戒者自身心识中的潜在力量。
   **The precept-substance IS the seeds**: These implanted wholesome seeds constitute the precept-substance. They are not external additions but latent powers within the ordinand's own mind-stream.

3. **现行防非**：这些种子在日后持续发挥功能，遇境逢缘时自发地产生防非止恶的作用——当面临可能导致犯戒的情境时，戒体种子会自动生起善心所，制止恶行。
   **Manifest prevention**: These seeds continuously function thereafter, spontaneously preventing transgression when encountering relevant circumstances — when facing situations that might lead to precept violation, the precept-substance seeds automatically arouse wholesome mental factors that restrain wrongdoing.

### 3.3 戒体的三要素 · Three Elements of Precept-Substance

道宣进一步分析了戒体概念的三个层次：

Daoxuan further analyzed the precept-substance concept into three layers:

| 层次 | 中文 | 含义 |
|------|------|------|
| **戒法** | 佛陀制定的戒律规范 | The precept-law: the rules established by the Buddha |
| **戒体** | 受戒时在心中获得的防非止恶之体 | The precept-substance: the anti-transgression force received during ordination |
| **戒行** | 日常生活中依戒体而发的具体持戒行为 | The precept-conduct: specific acts of precept-keeping arising from the substance in daily life |

戒法是客观标准，戒体是主观获得，戒行是具体实践。三者构成了道宣戒律学的完整结构。

The precept-law is the objective standard, the precept-substance is the subjective acquisition, and the precept-conduct is the concrete practice. Together they constitute the complete structure of Daoxuan's Vinaya theory.

## 四、哲学意涵 · Philosophical Implications

### 4.1 道德内在化 · Moral Internalization

戒体论的核心哲学意义在于：**它将道德规范从外在约束转化为内在力量**。戒律不再仅仅是佛陀的"命令"，而是受戒者自身心识中的功能——这是道德的主体化和内在化。

The core philosophical significance of the precept-substance theory is that **it transforms moral norms from external constraints into internal powers**. The precepts cease to be merely the Buddha's "commands" and become functions of the ordinand's own mind-stream — this is the subjectivization and internalization of morality.

### 4.2 与康德的对话 · Dialogue with Kant

戒体论与康德的**道德律令**理论存在有意义的平行关系。康德认为道德行为源于理性主体的自律（autonomy），而非外在的他律（heteronomy）。类似地，道宣的戒体论将持戒行为建立在受戒者内在的道德功能（戒体）之上，而非仅仅依赖外在的戒条。

The precept-substance theory bears meaningful parallels with Kant's theory of the **moral imperative**. Kant argued that moral action arises from the rational subject's autonomy rather than external heteronomy. Similarly, Daoxuan grounded precept-keeping in the ordinand's internal moral function (precept-substance) rather than merely in external rules.

然而，两者的差异也很重要：康德的道德律令源于纯粹理性，而戒体源于特定的宗教仪式（羯磨）和修行实践。这提示我们，戒体论提供了一种介于"自律"与"他律"之间的独特道德模式。

Yet the differences are also significant: Kant's moral imperative derives from pure reason, while the precept-substance originates from a specific religious ritual (karma) and contemplative practice. This suggests that the precept-substance theory offers a distinctive moral model that lies between "autonomy" and "heteronomy."

### 4.3 与福柯"自我技术"的对话 · Dialogue with Foucault's "Technologies of the Self"

福柯晚年提出的"自我技术"（technologies of the self）概念——个体通过特定实践对自身进行转化——与戒体论存在有趣的对照。受戒仪式可以理解为一种"自我技术"：通过仪式化的行为，个体在心识层面实现了一种结构性的转化。

Foucault's late concept of **"technologies of the self"** — practices through which individuals transform themselves — offers an interesting counterpoint to the precept-substance theory. The ordination ritual can be understood as a "technology of the self": through ritualized action, the individual achieves a structural transformation at the level of consciousness.

## 五、历史争论 · Historical Controversies

### 5.1 与法砺（相部宗）的分歧 · Disagreement with Fali (Xiangbu School)

法砺（569–635）主张戒体为"非色非心"，但其理论依据不同于道宣。法砺依《成实论》，而道宣依唯识学。这导致两者对"非色非心"的具体理解存在差异。

Fali (569–635) also held that the precept-substance is "neither form nor mind," but his theoretical basis differed from Daoxuan's. Fali relied on the *Satyasiddhi Śāstra* (成实论), while Daoxuan relied on Yogācāra. This led to divergent understandings of what "neither form nor mind" actually means.

### 5.2 与怀素（东塔宗）的分歧 · Disagreement with Huaisu (Dongta School)

怀素（625–698）主张戒体为"色法"，回归了有部的立场。他认为道宣的"非色非心"说过于抽象，无法解释戒体的实际运作。

Huaisu (625–698) held that the precept-substance is a "material dharma," reverting to the Sarvāstivādin position. He argued that Daoxuan's "neither form nor mind" thesis was too abstract to explain the actual operation of the precept-substance.

### 5.3 南山宗的最终胜出 · The Ultimate Triumph of Nanshan

经过数百年的争论，南山宗的戒体论最终成为中国律学的正统。其胜出原因在于：唯识学的种子说提供了一个既能解释戒体的持续性、又能避免物质化和心理化两种极端的理论框架。

After centuries of debate, the Nanshan school's account of precept-substance ultimately became the orthodox position in Chinese Vinaya studies. Its victory rested on the fact that Yogācāra seed theory provided a framework that could explain the persistence of the precept-substance while avoiding both the materialist and mentalist extremes.

## 六、当代讨论 · Contemporary Discussion

在现代佛教学术研究中，戒体论仍然是重要的讨论主题：

In modern Buddhist scholarship, the precept-substance theory remains an active area of discussion:

- **印顺法师**从"缘起性空"的中观立场重新审视戒体，倾向于将其理解为缘起的假名施设。
  **Yin Shun** re-examined the precept-substance from a Madhyamaka perspective of "dependent origination and emptiness," tending to understand it as a conventional designation within dependent origination.

- **劳政武**从法学角度分析戒体论，将其与法律中的"法律主体"概念进行比较。
  **Lao Zhengwu** analyzed the precept-substance from a legal perspective, comparing it with the concept of "legal subject" in jurisprudence.

- **日本学者**（如佐藤达玄、平川彰等）从文献学和比较宗教学的角度研究了印度各部派对戒体的不同理解。
  **Japanese scholars** (such as Sato Tatugen and Hirakawa Akira) studied the divergent sectarian understandings of precept-substance from philological and comparative-religious perspectives.
