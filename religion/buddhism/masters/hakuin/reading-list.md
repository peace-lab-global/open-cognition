# 白隐慧鹤进阶阅读 / Reading List for Hakuin Ekaku

## 原典与译注 / Primary Sources & Translations

### 必读 Essential

- **Norman Waddell 译**, *The Essential Teachings of Zen Master Hakuin* (Shambhala, 1994)
  - 白隐核心著作的英译
- **Norman Waddell 译**, *Wild Ivy: The Spiritual Autobiography of Zen Master Hakuin* (Shambhala, 1999)
  - 白隐自传

### 推荐 Recommended

- **Katsuhiro Yoshizawa**, *The Religious Thought and Life of Hakuin* (SUNY, 2018) — 最新学术研究
- **《远罗天釜》** — 禅修指导核心

## 研究专著 / Scholarly Monographs

- **Norman Waddell**, *The Zen Master Hakuin: Selected Writings* (Columbia, 1971)
- **Stephen Addiss**, *The Art of Hakuin Ekaku* (1995) — 白隐禅画研究
- **胜牧泰哲《白隐禅师》** — 日文研究经典

## 入门读物 / Introductory

- **铃木大拙**, *《禅的研究》* — 包含白隐禅的讨论
- **Philip Kapleau**, *The Three Pillars of Zen* — 现代禅修入门
- **Winston King**, *Zen and the Way of the Sword* — 禅与武道

## 跨学科阅读 / Interdisciplinary

- **大慧宗杲《正法眼藏》** — 中国公案禅传统
- **Alan Watts**, *The Way of Zen* — 禅的西方导论
