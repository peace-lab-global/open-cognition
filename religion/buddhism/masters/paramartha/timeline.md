# 真谛年表 / Timeline of Paramārtha (真谛年表)

> 真谛（499–569）的一生跨越了印度笈多王朝晚期和中国南北朝动荡时期。
> Paramārtha (499–569) lived through the late Gupta period in India and the turbulent Southern and Northern Dynasties of China.

---

## 印度时期 / India Period

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event | 历史背景 / Historical Context |
|---|---|---|
| **499** | 生于西印度优禅尼国（Ujjain），婆罗门种姓 / Born in Ujjain, western India, Brahmin caste | 笈多王朝（Gupta Empire）晚期，佛教在印度仍为重要的学术力量 / Late Gupta period; Buddhism remains a significant intellectual force in India |
| c.510–520s | 早年婆罗门教育，后皈依佛教 / Early Brahmanical education, later converts to Buddhism | 那烂陀寺（Nālandā）鼎盛时期，唯识学蓬勃发展 / Nālandā at its height; Yogācāra flourishes |
| c.520–540s | 深入学习唯识学，继承无著—世亲传统 / Intensive Yogācāra study, inheriting the Asaṅga–Vasubandhu tradition | 安慧（Sthiramati, c.475–555）活跃时期，唯识学内部多元诠释传统并存 / Sthiramati active; pluralistic Yogācāra interpretations coexist |
| c.540–545 | 决定赴中国传法 / Decides to travel to China to propagate the Dharma | 梁武帝（r.502–549）积极延请外国僧侣 / Emperor Wǔ of Liáng actively invites foreign monks |

---

## 来华初期 / Early Years in China

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event | 历史背景 / Historical Context |
|---|---|---|
| **546** | 经海路抵达南海郡（今广州）/ Arrives by sea at Nánhǎi Commandery (modern Guǎngzhōu) | 梁武帝中大同元年 / First year of Zhōngdàtóng era of Liáng Wǔdì |
| **547** | 应梁武帝之邀北上建康（今南京）/ Proceeds north to Jiànkāng (modern Nánjīng) at Emperor Wǔ's invitation | 梁朝政治稳定，佛教义学繁荣 / Liáng politically stable; Buddhist scholasticism thriving |
| **548** | 抵达建康，开始译经 / Arrives in Jiànkāng, begins translation work | 侯景之乱爆发（548–552）/ Outbreak of the Hóu Jǐng Rebellion (548–552) |

---

## 流离时期 / Period of Displacement

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event | 历史背景 / Historical Context |
|---|---|---|
| **549** | 建康陷落，梁武帝饿死台城。真谛被迫南逃 / Jiànkāng falls; Emperor Wǔ starves to death. Paramārtha flees south | 梁朝崩溃，南方陷入长期战乱 / Liáng dynasty collapses; southern China descends into prolonged warfare |
| **549–552** | 辗转于富春、豫章（今南昌）、始兴等地 / Wanders through Fùchūn, Yùzhāng (modern Nánchāng), Shǐxīng, etc. | 侯景之乱持续，政局极度动荡 / Hóu Jǐng Rebellion continues; extreme political instability |
| **552** | 侯景之乱平定，梁元帝即位江陵 / Rebellion suppressed; Liáng Yuándì enthroned at Jiānglíng | 南方开始恢复，但局势仍不稳定 / Southern China begins recovery, but situation remains unstable |
| **553–554** | 在广州、南康等地继续译经 / Continues translating in Guǎngzhōu, Nánkāng, etc. | 西魏攻陷江陵（554），梁元帝被杀 / Western Wèi captures Jiānglíng (554); Liáng Yuándì killed |
| **555–556** | 辗转于豫章、新吴等地 / Moves between Yùzhāng, Xīnwú, etc. | 陈霸先起兵，陈朝建立前夕 / Chén Bàxiān rises militarily, prelude to Chén dynasty |

## 广州译经高峰期 / Peak Translation Period in Guǎngzhōu

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event | 历史背景 / Historical Context |
|---|---|---|
| **557** | 陈朝建立（557–589）。真谛回到广州 / Chén dynasty established (557–589). Paramārtha returns to Guǎngzhōu | 陈朝初期，南方政局相对稳定 / Early Chén period; southern politics relatively stable |
| **558** | 译出《中边分别论》二卷、《三无性论》一卷 / Translates *Madhyāntavibhāga* (2 fasc.) and *Triniḥsvabhāvatā* (1 fasc.) | — |
| **559** | 开始翻译《俱舍论》/ Begins translation of *Abhidharmakośa* | — |
| c.560 | 译出《佛性论》四卷 / Translates *Buddha-dhātu-śāstra* (4 fasc.) | — |
| **563** | 完成《摄大乘论》三卷和《摄大乘论释》十五卷的翻译。完成《俱舍论》二十二卷的翻译 / Completes *Mahāyānasaṃgraha* (3 fasc.), its commentary (15 fasc.), and *Abhidharmakośa* (22 fasc.) | 摄论宗的奠基之年 / Foundation year of the Shèlùn School |
| **563–569** | 在广州制旨寺等处讲授《摄大乘论》，培养弟子法泰、智恺等 / Lectures on *Mahāyānasaṃgraha* at Zhìzhǐ Temple, training disciples Fǎtài, Zhìkǎi, etc. | 摄论宗开始形成学术传承 / Shèlùn School begins forming as an academic lineage |

## 晚年与圆寂 / Final Years and Death

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event | 历史背景 / Historical Context |
|---|---|---|
| **567–568** | 继续讲学和翻译。弟子们开始撰写注疏 / Continues teaching and translation. Disciples begin composing commentaries | 陈朝中期，佛教义学繁荣 / Mid-Chén period; Buddhist scholasticism flourishes |
| **569** | 圆寂于广州，享年约七十岁 / Passes away in Guǎngzhōu, aged approximately seventy | 陈宣帝太建元年 / First year of Tàijiàn era of Chén Xuāndì |

## 身后影响 / Posthumous Influence

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|---|---|
| **569–589** | 弟子法泰、智恺等将摄论宗传播到全国 / Disciples spread the Shèlùn School nationally |
| **581–618** | 隋代，摄论宗达到鼎盛。道岳（568–636）等著《摄大乘论疏》/ Shèlùn School flourishes during the Suí dynasty. Dàoyuè (568–636) authors commentaries |
| **602** | 玄奘出生 / Xuánzàng is born |
| **627–645** | 玄奘西行求法 / Xuánzàng travels to India |
| **645** | 玄奘归国，开始翻译唯识经论 / Xuánzàng returns to China, begins translating Yogācāra texts |
| **659** | 窥基完成《成唯识论》，法相唯识宗正式成立 / Kuījī completes *Vijñaptimātratā-siddhi*; Fǎxiàng School formally established |
| **c.660–700** | 摄论宗逐渐被法相宗吸收 / Shèlùn School gradually absorbed into the Fǎxiàng School |
| **8世纪以后** | 法相宗自身也开始衰落，但真谛的思想遗产通过天台宗、华严宗、禅宗等延续 / After the 8th century, Fǎxiàng itself declines, but Paramārtha's intellectual legacy continues through Tiāntái, Huáyán, and Chán |
