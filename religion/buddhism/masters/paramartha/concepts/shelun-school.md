# 摄论宗 / The Shèlùn School (攝論宗)

> "摄大乘者，摄一切大乘要义，无不具足。"

---

## 定义 / Definition

**摄论宗**（Shèlùn Zōng）是中国佛教中以无著《摄大乘论》（*Mahāyānasaṃgraha*）为核心经典的宗派，由真谛（499–569）创立。它是法相唯识宗成立之前，中国唯识学的主要传承。摄论宗以真谛所译的《摄大乘论》及其世亲释为根本论典，以真谛独特的唯识诠释（九识说、解性黎耶、一性皆成）为理论特征。

The **Shèlùn School** (攝論宗, "School of the Summary of the Mahāyāna") is a Chinese Buddhist school centered on Asaṅga's *Mahāyānasaṃgraha*, founded by Paramārtha (499–569). It constituted the principal lineage of Chinese Yogācāra before the establishment of the Fǎxiàng School. Its foundational texts were Paramārtha's translations of the *Mahāyānasaṃgraha* and Vasubandhu's commentary, and its theoretical signature was Paramārtha's distinctive Yogācāra interpretation: the nine consciousnesses, awakening-nature ālaya, and universal Buddhahood.

## 创立 / Foundation

### 历史背景 / Historical Context

摄论宗创立于中国佛教史上一个特殊的时期——南北朝末期至隋代初期（约560–600年）。这一时期的特点是：

The Shèlùn School was founded during a distinctive period in Chinese Buddhist history—the late Southern and Northern Dynasties through early Suí period (c.560–600 CE). This era was characterized by:

1. **政治动荡**：梁朝灭亡、陈朝建立、北周灭佛、隋朝统一——政局剧烈变动。
   **Political upheaval**: The fall of Liáng, rise of Chén, Northern Zhōu persecution of Buddhism, and Suí reunification.

2. **佛教繁荣**：尽管政治动荡，佛教义学研究仍在南方蓬勃发展。
   **Buddhist flourishing**: Despite political turmoil, Buddhist scholastic studies thrived in the south.

3. **译经事业**：真谛在极其困难的条件下完成了大量重要经论的翻译。
   **Translation enterprise**: Paramārtha completed numerous important translations under extremely difficult conditions.

### 创立过程 / Process of Foundation

摄论宗的创立可以分为几个阶段：

The school's establishment proceeded in stages:

**第一阶段：翻译（563年）。** 真谛在广州完成了《摄大乘论》三卷及《摄大乘论释》十五卷的翻译。这是无著唯识学纲要性著作的首个完整汉译本。

**Phase One: Translation (563 CE).** Paramārtha completed the translation of the *Mahāyānasaṃgraha* (3 fascicles) and Vasubandhu's commentary (15 fascicles) in Guǎngzhōu—the first complete Chinese rendering of Asaṅga's Yogācāra compendium.

**第二阶段：讲学（563–569年）。** 真谛在广州制旨寺等处讲授《摄大乘论》，培养了法泰、智恺等一批弟子。

**Phase Two: Teaching (563–569).** Paramārtha lectured on the *Mahāyānasaṃgraha* at Zhìzhǐ Temple and other venues, training disciples including Fǎtài and Zhìkǎi.

**第三阶段：传播（569年之后）。** 真谛圆寂后，其弟子将摄论宗传播到全国各地，特别是在隋代的长安和洛阳形成重要的学术中心。

**Phase Three: Dissemination (post-569).** After Paramārtha's death, disciples spread the school nationally, establishing important academic centers in Suí-era Cháng'ān and Luòyáng.

## 核心教义 / Core Doctrines

摄论宗的教义体系以《摄大乘论》为基础，结合真谛的独特诠释，形成了以下几个核心教义：

The Shèlùn School's doctrinal system, based on the *Mahāyānasaṃgraha* and enriched by Paramārtha's interpretation, developed several core doctrines:

### 一、唯识无境 / Consciousness-Only Without External Objects

继承唯识学的基本立场：一切现象都是识的变现（vijñapti-mātra），不存在独立于识之外的外部对象。《摄大乘论》通过"十种道理"论证唯识无境，摄论宗完整继承了这一论证。

Inheriting the fundamental Yogācāra position: all phenomena are mere representations of consciousness (*vijñapti-mātra*), with no external objects independent of consciousness. The *Mahāyānasaṃgraha* argues this through "ten reasonings," which the Shèlùn School adopted in full.

### 二、三性说 / The Three Natures

继承唯识学的三性说：

Inheriting the Yogācāra doctrine of three natures:

1. **遍计所执性**（parikalpita-svabhāva）——妄分别所执的虚假存在 / Imagined nature: false existence clung to through conceptual discrimination
2. **依他起性**（paratantra-svabhāva）——因缘和合而生的现象存在 / Dependent nature: phenomenal existence arising from conditions
3. **圆成实性**（pariniṣpanna-svabhāva）——诸法的真实本性，即真如 / Perfected nature: the true nature of phenomena, i.e., suchness

摄论宗对三性的诠释与后来的法相宗有微妙差异，特别是在"依他起性"与"圆成实性"的关系上更倾向于融合而非分离。

The Shèlùn School's interpretation differs subtly from the later Fǎxiàng School, particularly in tending toward integration rather than separation of the dependent and perfected natures.

### 三、九识说 / The Nine Consciousnesses

摄论宗最具标志性的教义。在八识之上增设第九阿摩罗识（无垢识），为转识成智提供积极的存在论基础。

The school's most distinctive doctrine. The ninth amala-vijñāna (immaculate consciousness) provides a positive ontological ground for consciousness-transformation.

→ 详见 [九识说](nine-consciousnesses.md) / See [Nine Consciousnesses](nine-consciousnesses.md)

### 四、一性皆成 / Universal Buddhahood

摄论宗主张一切有情皆有佛性，皆能成佛。这与法相宗的"五种姓"说形成鲜明对比。

The school holds that all sentient beings possess Buddha-nature and can attain Buddhahood, in sharp contrast to the Fǎxiàng School's "five-lineage" theory.

→ 详见 [解性黎耶](awakening-nature.md) / See [Awakening-Nature](awakening-nature.md)

## 传承谱系 / Lineage

### 第一代：真谛（499–569） / First Generation: Paramārtha

宗派创立者，翻译并讲授《摄大乘论》。

Founder; translated and lectured on the *Mahāyānasaṃgraha*.

### 第二代：直传弟子 / Second Generation: Direct Disciples

- **法泰**（Fǎtài）：真谛最重要的弟子之一，在真谛圆寂后继续弘扬摄论宗。
  **Fǎtài**: One of Paramārtha's most important disciples; continued propagating the school after his teacher's death.

- **智恺**（Zhìkǎi）：协助真谛翻译《摄大乘论》，并撰写注释。
  **Zhìkǎi**: Assisted in the translation and composed commentaries.

- **曹毗**（Cáo Pí）：居士学者，对摄论宗在士大夫阶层的传播起了重要作用。
  **Cáo Pí**: A lay scholar instrumental in spreading the school among the literati.

### 第三代及以后：隋代传播 / Third Generation and Beyond: Suí Dynasty Dissemination

- **道岳**（Dàoyuè, 568–636）：隋代摄论宗最重要的学者，著有《摄大乘论疏》。
  **Dàoyuè** (568–636): The most important Shèlùn scholar of the Suí period; authored a commentary on the *Mahāyānasaṃgraha*.

- **慧旷**（Huìkuàng, 554–613）：精通摄论，在长安讲授。
  **Huìkuàng** (554–613): Master of the *Mahāyānasaṃgraha* who lectured in Cháng'ān.

- **智凝**（Zhìníng）：隋代摄论宗学者，著有注疏。
  **Zhìníng**: Suí-era Shèlùn scholar who authored commentaries.

### 唐代衰落 / Táng Dynasty Decline

随着玄奘（602–664）从印度归来并创立法相唯识宗，摄论宗逐渐被边缘化。玄奘的弟子窥基（632–682）在《成唯识论述记》中系统批评了摄论宗的"九识说"等教义。到唐代中期，摄论宗作为一个独立宗派已经消亡，其学者和传承被法相宗所吸收。

With Xuánzàng's (602–664) return from India and the founding of the Fǎxiàng School, the Shèlùn School was gradually marginalized. Xuánzàng's disciple Kuījī (632–682) systematically criticized Shèlùn doctrines including the "nine consciousnesses" in his *Commentary on the Vijñaptimātratā-siddhi*. By the mid-Táng period, the Shèlùn School had ceased to exist as an independent institution, its scholars and lineage absorbed into the Fǎxiàng School.

## 与其他宗派的关系 / Relations with Other Schools

### 与法相宗 / With the Fǎxiàng School

两者都是唯识学的传承，但代表了印度唯识学的不同路线。法相宗以玄奘所传的护法系唯识为正统，将摄论宗视为"旧译"或"不完整的"唯识学。摄论宗则代表了另一条可能更接近安慧系诠释的传承。

Both are Yogācāra lineages but represent different Indian lineages. Fǎxiàng regarded the Dharmapāla system transmitted by Xuánzàng as orthodox, viewing Shèlùn as "old translation" or "incomplete" Yogācāra. Shèlùn represented an alternative lineage possibly closer to Sthiramati's interpretation.

### 与天台宗 / With the Tiāntái School

摄论宗的"一性皆成"思想与天台宗的"一切众生皆有佛性"相契合。天台宗的智者大师（538–597）对摄论宗的学说有所借鉴。

Shèlùn's universal Buddhahood resonated with Tiāntái's "all beings possess Buddha-nature." The Tiāntái master Zhìzhě (538–597) drew on Shèlùn doctrines.

### 与华严宗 / With the Huáyán School

摄论宗的如来藏思想与华严宗的"法界缘起"有亲和力。华严宗的"性起"学说（一切法从如来藏性起）与摄论宗的"解性黎耶"概念有思想上的联系。

Shèlùn's tathāgata-garbha thought showed affinity with Huáyán's "dharmadhātu-dependent-origination." Huáyán's "nature-arising" (*xìngqǐ*) doctrine connects intellectually with Shèlùn's "awakening-nature ālaya."

## 历史评价 / Historical Assessment

### 传统评价 / Traditional Assessment

在法相宗的传统叙事中，摄论宗被视为唯识学的"旧译"传统，不如玄奘的"新译"精确和完整。这种评价在中国佛教史上长期占据主流。

In Fǎxiàng's traditional narrative, Shèlùn was regarded as the "old translation" tradition, less precise and complete than Xuánzàng's "new translation." This assessment dominated Chinese Buddhist historiography for centuries.

### 现代评价 / Modern Assessment

近现代佛教学者重新评价了摄论宗的地位：

Modern Buddhist scholars have reassessed the school's standing:

- **印顺法师**：认为摄论宗代表了印度唯识学的一条独立传承，不应简单地以法相宗的标准来衡量。
  **Venerable Yìnshùn**: Shèlùn represents an independent Indian Yogācāra lineage that should not be measured simply by Fǎxiàng standards.

- **日本学者**（如宇井伯寿、横山纮一）：通过比较梵文、藏文和汉文文献，发现真谛的翻译在某些方面可能比玄奘更接近印度原文。
  **Japanese scholars** (e.g., Ui Hakuju, Yokoyama Kōitsu): Through comparative study of Sanskrit, Tibetan, and Chinese texts, found that Paramārtha's translations may in some respects be closer to Indian originals than Xuánzàng's.

- **牟宗三**：在《佛性与般若》中高度评价了摄论宗融合唯识与如来藏的思想尝试。
  **Móu Zōngsān**: Highly valued the Shèlùn School's attempt to synthesize Yogācāra and tathāgata-garbha in *Buddha-nature and Prajñā*.
