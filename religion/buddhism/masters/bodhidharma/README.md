# 菩提达摩 Bodhidharma

> "不立文字，教外别传；直指人心，见性成佛。"
> "A special transmission outside the scriptures; no dependence on words and letters; direct pointing to the human mind; seeing into one's nature and attaining Buddhahood."
> ——禅宗宗旨（虽非达摩亲说，实为其精神之总结 / Though not Bodhidharma's own words, this verse distills his spirit）

---

## 基本信息 / Basic Information

菩提达摩（Bodhidharma，约 470–543），南印度人，中国禅宗**初祖**，被尊为"东土初祖"。他是佛教史上最具传奇色彩与思想深度的人物之一。达摩将印度如来藏（Tathagatagarbha）思想与中国本土的禅修实践深度融合，开创了"直指人心、见性成佛"的独特禅风，奠定了此后一千五百年东亚精神传统的基础。

Bodhidharma (c. 470–543), a native of South India, is revered as the **First Patriarch** of Chinese Chan (Zen) Buddhism — "the First Patriarch of the Eastern Land." He stands as one of the most legendary and intellectually profound figures in Buddhist history. By deeply integrating the Indian Tathagatagarbha (Buddha-nature) doctrine with Chinese meditative praxis, Bodhidharma pioneered the distinctive Chan approach of "directly pointing to the human mind, seeing into one's nature, and attaining Buddhahood," laying the foundation for East Asian spiritual traditions over the next fifteen centuries.

其"**二人四行**"论（Two Entries and Four Practices）是中国禅宗最早的系统修行纲领；"**壁观**"（wall-gazing meditation）成为禅宗标志性的修行方式。达摩的思想影响了后世几乎所有禅宗流派，从慧能的南宗顿教到道元的曹洞宗，从临济的棒喝到默照禅的静坐，均可追溯至达摩的原创洞见。

His **"Two Entries and Four Practices"** constitutes the earliest systematic practice framework in Chinese Chan; **"wall-gazing"** (壁观) became the hallmark meditation method. Bodhidharma's thought influenced virtually every subsequent Chan lineage — from Huineng's Southern sudden-teaching to Dogen's Soto school, from Linji's shouts and blows to the silent-illumination practice of Hongzhi — all traceable to Bodhidharma's original insights.

## 核心命题 / Core Propositions

### 1. 藉教悟宗 / Using Teachings to Awaken to the Source

经典是入门的工具，但真正的觉悟超越文字。达摩提出"理入"须"藉教悟宗"，即借助经典（尤其是《楞伽经》）的指引来契入真实。然而他同时强调，文字只是指月之指，不可执著。这一立场为后来禅宗"不立文字"的宗旨埋下了种子。

Scriptures serve as a gateway, but true awakening transcends language. Bodhidharma proposed that the "entry through principle" requires "using teachings to awaken to the source" — relying on scriptures (especially the *Lankavatara Sutra*) as guides to penetrate reality. Yet he simultaneously stressed that words are merely fingers pointing at the moon, never to be grasped. This stance planted the seed for Chan's later motto of "not establishing words and letters."

### 2. 二人四行 / Two Entries and Four Practices

"理入"（壁观安心）与"行入"（报怨行、随缘行、无所求行、称法行）构成了达摩禅的完整修行体系。"理入"侧重内在的心性体认，"行入"则落实于日常生活的四种具体实践。这是中国佛教史上首次将理论认知与行为实践如此系统地整合。

"Entry through principle" (wall-gazing to pacify the mind) and "entry through practice" (accepting karmic retribution, adapting to conditions, non-striving, and practicing in accord with Dharma) form Bodhidharma's complete cultivation system. The "principle entry" emphasizes inner realization of mind-nature, while the "practice entry" grounds itself in four concrete daily practices. This represents the first systematic integration of theoretical insight and behavioral practice in Chinese Buddhist history.

### 3. 即心是佛 / This Mind Is Buddha

众生本具佛性，不假外求。达摩的核心洞见是：佛性不在身外，不在经典，不在偶像，而就在当下念念不断的本心之中。这一"即心是佛"的立场彻底改变了东亚佛教的方向——从向外求法转向向内观照。

All beings inherently possess Buddha-nature; nothing need be sought externally. Bodhidharma's core insight is that Buddha-nature exists neither outside the body, nor in scriptures, nor in icons, but in the very mind that unfolds moment by moment. This "this mind is Buddha" stance radically redirected East Asian Buddhism — from seeking Dharma externally to turning inward in contemplation.

### 4. 壁观安心 / Wall-Gazing to Pacify the Mind

"外息诸缘，内心无喘，心如墙壁，可以入道。"壁观不是简单的面壁打坐，而是一种彻底截断外缘、使内心坚固不动的深度冥想状态。心如墙壁，既是比喻也是方法——以墙的不动摇来比喻心应有的安定，也以面对墙壁的物理姿态来创造这种安定。

"Externally, cease all conditions; internally, the mind does not waver. Mind like a wall — this is how one may enter the Way." Wall-gazing is not merely sitting facing a wall, but a deep meditative state that thoroughly cuts off external conditions and renders the inner mind immovable. Mind like a wall functions as both metaphor and method — using the wall's immovability to illustrate the stability the mind should achieve, and employing the physical posture of facing a wall to create that stability.

### 5. 无功德 / No Merit

达摩对梁武帝的回答——"无功德"——是禅宗史上最具震撼力的对话之一。梁武帝以造寺度僧为功德，达摩却直指这只是"人天小果，有漏之因"，真正的功德是"净智妙圆，体自空寂"。这一回答打破了宗教功利主义，确立了禅宗超越世俗价值的基本立场。

Bodhidharma's reply to Emperor Wu of Liang — "No merit" — is one of the most startling dialogues in Chan history. When Emperor Wu cited temple-building and monk-ordination as merit, Bodhidharma directly pointed out these were merely "small rewards in the human and heavenly realms, causes with outflows." True merit is "pure wisdom, wondrously complete, its substance naturally empty and still." This reply shattered religious utilitarianism and established Chan's fundamental stance of transcending worldly values.

### 6. 传法以心 / Mind-to-Mind Transmission

达摩传法给慧可时，传授了袈裟与《楞伽经》四卷，但更重要的是"以心传心"——一种超越语言的觉悟传承。这种"心印"（mind-seal）传统成为禅宗法脉延续的核心机制，每一代祖师不仅传递教义，更直接传递觉悟体验本身。

When transmitting Dharma to Huike, Bodhidharma handed down the robe and four volumes of the *Lankavatara Sutra*, but what mattered more was "mind-to-mind transmission" — an awakening-transmission that transcends language. This "mind-seal" (心印) tradition became the core mechanism of Chan lineage succession, where each patriarch transmitted not merely doctrine but the awakening experience itself.

## 思想脉络 / Intellectual Genealogy

达摩的思想根植于印度大乘佛教两大传统：**如来藏思想**（以《楞伽经》为代表）与**般若空观**（以《金刚经》《心经》为代表）。他将这两大传统创造性地融合，形成了独特的中国禅风。

Bodhidharma's thought is rooted in two major Indian Mahayana traditions: **Tathagatagarbha thought** (represented by the *Lankavatara Sutra*) and **Prajnaparamita emptiness** (represented by the *Diamond Sutra* and *Heart Sutra*). He creatively synthesized these two traditions, forming a distinctly Chinese Chan style.

**师承谱系 / Lineage:**
- 西天传承：般若多罗（Prajñātāra，第二十七祖）→ 达摩（第二十八祖 / 东土初祖）
- 东土传承：达摩 → 慧可（二祖）→ 僧璨（三祖）→ 道信（四祖）→ 弘忍（五祖）→ 慧能（六祖）

**关键转折 / Key Turning Points:**
- 南印度出家与般若多罗的教导，奠定了其如来藏思想基础
- 渡海来华（约520年），将印度禅法带入中国文化语境
- 与梁武帝的对话失败，促使达摩转向隐修而非宫廷佛教
- 嵩山面壁九年，发展出独特的壁观禅法
- 传法慧可，确立了中国禅宗的传承模式

## 重要概念 / Key Concepts

1. **壁观 Biguan (Wall-Gazing)**：面壁而坐，外息诸缘，内心无喘——心如墙壁，可以入道。这是达摩禅的核心修行方法。详见 → [壁观](concepts/wall-gazing.md)
   Sitting facing a wall, externally ceasing all conditions, internally the mind does not waver — mind like a wall, one can enter the Way. The core practice method of Bodhidharma's Chan. See → [Wall-Gazing](concepts/wall-gazing.md)

2. **无心 Wuxin (No-Mind)**：不执著于任何念头，心无所住。超越有意识的分别心，达到自然无造作的心理状态。详见 → [无心](concepts/no-mind.md)
   Non-attachment to any thought, the mind abides nowhere. Transcending conscious discriminating mind to reach a natural, uncontrived mental state. See → [No-Mind](concepts/no-mind.md)

3. **顿悟 Dunwu (Sudden Enlightenment)**：直下承当，刹那见性。达摩虽未明确使用"顿悟"一词，但其"直指人心"的方法论隐含了顿悟的立场。详见 → [顿悟](concepts/sudden-enlightenment.md)
   Directly taking it upon oneself, seeing one's nature in a single instant. Although Bodhidharma did not explicitly use the term "sudden enlightenment," his methodology of "directly pointing to the human mind" implies a sudden-awakening stance. See → [Sudden Enlightenment](concepts/sudden-enlightenment.md)

4. **坐禅 Zuochan (Sitting Meditation)**：达摩在嵩山少林寺面壁九年，确立了坐禅作为禅宗修行的基本形式。详见 → [坐禅](concepts/zazen-practice.md)
   Bodhidharma faced the wall at Shaolin Temple on Mount Song for nine years, establishing seated meditation as the foundational form of Chan practice. See → [Sitting Meditation](concepts/zazen-practice.md)

5. **教外别传 Jiaowai Biechuan (Transmission Outside Scriptures)**：禅宗独特的法脉传承方式，强调超越文字经典的心心相传。详见 → [教外别传](concepts/transmission-outside-scriptures.md)
   Chan's unique mode of Dharma lineage transmission, emphasizing mind-to-mind transmission that transcends written scriptures. See → [Transmission Outside Scriptures](concepts/transmission-outside-scriptures.md)

6. **二人四行 Two Entries and Four Practices**：达摩禅的完整修行体系。"理入"通过壁观契入真实，"行入"通过四种日常实践落实修行。
   The complete cultivation system of Bodhidharma's Chan. "Entry through principle" penetrates reality via wall-gazing; "entry through practice" implements cultivation through four daily practices.

7. **安心 Anxin (Pacifying the Mind)**：慧可请达摩安心，达摩反问"将心来，与汝安"，慧可觅心不可得，达摩说"与汝安心竟"。这一公案揭示了安心的本质——心不可得，即是安心。
   When Huike asked Bodhidharma to pacify his mind, Bodhidharma countered: "Bring me your mind, and I will pacify it for you." Huike searched for his mind and could not find it. Bodhidharma said: "There — I have pacified your mind for you." This koan reveals the essence of pacifying the mind — that the mind cannot be grasped is itself the pacification.

8. **理入与行入 Entry through Principle and Entry through Practice**：达摩将修行路径分为两条。"理入"是顿悟层面，直接体认一切众生同一真性；"行入"是渐修层面，在日常生活四种情境中磨炼心性。
   Bodhidharma divided the cultivation path into two entries. "Entry through principle" is the sudden-awakening dimension — directly realizing that all beings share one true nature. "Entry through practice" is the gradual-cultivation dimension — tempering mind-nature across four types of daily life situations.

9. **佛性 Buddha-Nature (Foxing)**：达摩深信"含生同一真性"，一切众生本具如来智慧德相。这一信念是达摩禅的形而上学基础，也是后来中国禅宗一切思想的出发点。
   Bodhidharma deeply believed that "all sentient beings share one true nature," that every being inherently possesses the wisdom and virtue of the Tathagata. This conviction is the metaphysical foundation of Bodhidharma's Chan and the starting point of all subsequent Chinese Chan thought.

10. **藉教悟宗 Using Teachings to Awaken to the Source**：达摩不排斥经典，而是将经典定位为工具——借助教理的指引来契入超越教理的实相。《楞伽经》就是他选择的"藉教"之具。
    Bodhidharma did not reject scriptures but positioned them as tools — relying on doctrinal guidance to penetrate a reality that transcends doctrine. The *Lankavatara Sutra* was his chosen vehicle for "using teachings."

## 思想坐标 / Intellectual Coordinates

达摩处于印度佛教中国化的关键节点。他前承印度如来藏与般若两大传统，后启中国禅宗一千五百年的思想长河。

Bodhidharma stands at a pivotal juncture in the Sinicization of Indian Buddhism. He inherited the Indian Tathagatagarbha and Prajnaparamita traditions and initiated fifteen centuries of Chinese Chan intellectual currents.

- **前驱 / Precursors**：求那跋陀罗（Gunabhadra，译《楞伽经》）、佛陀禅师（Buddhabhadra）
- **同时代人 / Contemporaries**：僧稠（Sengchou，北方禅法大师）、真谛（Paramartha，译经师）
- **传承 / Successors**：慧可（二祖）→ 僧璨（三祖）→ 道信（四祖）→ 弘忍（五祖）→ 慧能（六祖）
- **哲学关联 / Philosophical Relations**：达摩禅融合了如来藏思想（《楞伽经》）与般若空观，为后来"真空妙有"的哲学综合奠定基础 / Bodhidharma's Chan fused Tathagatagarbha thought (*Lankavatara Sutra*) with Prajnaparamita emptiness, laying the groundwork for the later philosophical synthesis of "true emptiness, wondrous existence"
- **跨文化对话 / Cross-Cultural Dialogue**：达摩代表了印度佛教思想与中国本土修行传统的首次深度对话 / Bodhidharma represents the first deep dialogue between Indian Buddhist thought and Chinese indigenous practice traditions

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

达摩的思想在当代具有多维度的应用价值：

Bodhidharma's thought holds multidimensional contemporary relevance:

- **心理学与冥想研究 Psychology & Meditation Research**："壁观"可视为最早的"感官剥夺"冥想实验之一，与现代感知隔离研究（如 John Lilly 的隔离舱实验）形成有趣对照。当代正念冥想（MBSR）的核心理念——觉察而不执著——与达摩的"无所求行"高度契合。
  "Wall-gazing" can be viewed as one of the earliest "sensory deprivation" meditation experiments, offering interesting parallels with modern perceptual isolation research (such as John Lilly's isolation tank experiments). The core principle of contemporary mindfulness meditation (MBSR) — awareness without attachment — strongly aligns with Bodhidharma's "non-striving practice."

- **领导力与管理 Leadership & Management**："无所求行"对当代功利主义文化构成深刻批判。在结果导向的管理哲学之外，达摩提供了一种"无为而无不为"的替代视角。
  "Non-striving practice" offers a profound critique of contemporary utilitarian culture. Beyond results-oriented management philosophy, Bodhidharma provides an alternative perspective of "non-action yet nothing left undone."

- **心理治疗 Psychotherapy**："报怨行"与接纳承诺疗法（ACT）的接纳理念高度吻合。达摩对苦难的态度——不是抗拒，而是觉知并接纳——与第三代认知行为疗法的核心精神一致。
  "Accepting karmic retribution" closely mirrors the acceptance principle in Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT). Bodhidharma's attitude toward suffering — not resistance but awareness and acceptance — aligns with the core spirit of third-generation cognitive behavioral therapies.

- **教育哲学 Educational Philosophy**："不立文字"启发了一种超越知识灌输的教育观——真正的学习不是积累信息，而是转变认知方式。这与建构主义学习理论和批判性教育学形成共鸣。
  "Not establishing words" inspires an educational philosophy that transcends knowledge transmission — true learning is not accumulating information but transforming one's mode of cognition. This resonates with constructivist learning theory and critical pedagogy.

## 常见误读 / Common Misreadings

### 1. "达摩创立了少林武术" / "Bodhidharma founded Shaolin martial arts"

❌ **误读 Misreading**：达摩是少林武术的创始人，"易筋经"和"洗髓经"为达摩所著。
Bodhidharma created Shaolin martial arts; the *Yi Jin Jing* (Muscle-Tendon Change Classic) and *Xi Sui Jing* (Marrow Washing Classic) were authored by him.

✅ **澄清 Clarification**：武术传统是后世附会。达摩禅的核心是壁观安心，与武术无关。"易筋经"最早见于明代文献，距离达摩时代已逾千年。将达摩与武术联系起来是文化建构的产物，而非历史事实。
The martial arts tradition is a later attribution. The core of Bodhidharma's Chan is wall-gazing to pacify the mind, entirely unrelated to martial arts. The *Yi Jin Jing* first appears in Ming dynasty texts, over a millennium after Bodhidharma. Linking him to martial arts is a product of cultural construction, not historical fact.

### 2. "不立文字 = 不读经典" / "Not establishing words = Don't read scriptures"

❌ **误读 Misreading**：达摩主张不读经典、抛弃学问。
Bodhidharma advocated not reading scriptures and abandoning scholarship.

✅ **澄清 Clarification**：达摩本人精通《楞伽经》并以此传法。"不立文字"是指不以文字为究竟，非否定经典的价值。达摩的立场是"藉教悟宗"——借助经典来契入超越经典的实相。
Bodhidharma himself mastered the *Lankavatara Sutra* and transmitted Dharma through it. "Not establishing words" means not taking words as the ultimate, not denying the value of scriptures. Bodhidharma's stance is "using teachings to awaken to the source."

### 3. "达摩是神话人物" / "Bodhidharma is a mythical figure"

❌ **误读 Misreading**：达摩的大量传说（面壁九年、只履西归等）说明他是虚构人物。
The abundance of legends about Bodhidharma (nine years facing a wall, returning west with one shoe) suggests he is a fictional figure.

✅ **澄清 Clarification**：虽有大量传说成分，但达摩的历史存在被《续高僧传》（道宣撰，7世纪）等早期史料确认。《洛阳伽蓝记》（杨衒之撰，547年）也提到达摩。历史人物与传说人物并不矛盾——达摩确实存在，只是其生平被后世大量传说所覆盖。
Although legendary elements abound, Bodhidharma's historical existence is confirmed by early sources such as the *Continued Biographies of Eminent Monks* (compiled by Daoxuan, 7th century). The *Record of Buddhist Monasteries in Luoyang* (by Yang Xuanzhi, 547 CE) also mentions him. Historical figure and legendary figure are not contradictory — Bodhidharma certainly existed, but his life story was extensively overlaid with later legends.

### 4. "达摩禅 = 只是静坐" / "Bodhidharma's Chan = just sitting quietly"

❌ **误读 Misreading**：达摩禅不过是枯坐冥想，没有思想内容。
Bodhidharma's Chan is nothing more than sitting in meditation, lacking intellectual content.

✅ **澄清 Clarification**：达摩的"二人四行"体系既有深刻的理论认知（"理入"），也有完整的行为实践（"行入"）。壁观不是被动的发呆，而是一种高度自觉的心性修练。将达摩禅简化为"只是静坐"忽略了其完整的哲学体系和实践框架。
Bodhidharma's "Two Entries and Four Practices" system encompasses both profound theoretical insight ("entry through principle") and comprehensive behavioral practice ("entry through practice"). Wall-gazing is not passive blankness but a highly self-aware cultivation of mind-nature. Reducing Bodhidharma's Chan to "just sitting" ignores its complete philosophical system and practical framework.

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

达摩的思想与西方哲学传统形成了多处有意义的对话：

Bodhidharma's thought enters meaningful dialogue with Western philosophical traditions at multiple points:

- **与海德格尔 Heidegger**：达摩的"壁观"——截断日常世界的牵挂，直面存在本身——与海德格尔的"直面存在"（facing Being）形成精神共鸣。二者都主张从日常的工具性思维中抽离，回到存在的原初经验。
  Bodhidharma's "wall-gazing" — severing everyday worldly concerns to face existence itself — resonates spiritually with Heidegger's concept of "facing Being." Both advocate withdrawing from everyday instrumental thinking to return to the primordial experience of existence.

- **与维特根斯坦 Wittgenstein**：达摩对语言局限性的深刻认知（"不立文字"）与维特根斯坦的"对于不可说的，必须保持沉默"形成跨文化呼应。两者都认识到语言在表述终极实在时的根本局限。
  Bodhidharma's profound awareness of language's limitations ("not establishing words") cross-culturally echoes Wittgenstein's "Whereof one cannot speak, thereof one must be silent." Both recognize the fundamental limitation of language in expressing ultimate reality.

- **与现象学 Phenomenology**：达摩的壁观可被理解为一种现象学还原——悬置（epoche）对外部世界的判断，回到纯粹的意识体验。这与胡塞尔的先验还原和梅洛-庞蒂的身体现象学有可比之处。
  Bodhidharma's wall-gazing can be understood as a phenomenological reduction — suspending (epoche) judgments about the external world to return to pure conscious experience. This is comparable to Husserl's transcendental reduction and Merleau-Ponty's phenomenology of the body.

- **与实用主义 Pragmatism**：达摩的"四行"强调在具体生活情境中实践，与杜威等人强调的"在做中学"和反对脱离生活的抽象思辨有相通之处。
  Bodhidharma's "Four Practices" emphasize practice within concrete life situations, resonating with Dewey's emphasis on "learning by doing" and opposition to abstract speculation detached from life.
