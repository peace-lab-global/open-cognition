# 窥基 Kuiji (632-682)

> "三界唯心，万法唯识。"
> "The three realms are mind-only; the ten thousand dharmas are consciousness-only."

## 基本信息 / Basic Information

**窥基 Kuiji** (632-682 CE)，长安人，唐代将军尉迟敬德之侄，**法相唯识宗（慈恩宗）创立者**。玄奘最得意的弟子，协助翻译《成唯识论》并撰《成唯识论述记》，被誉为**"百部疏主"**（著述逾百部），是将印度瑜伽行派思想在中国系统化传播的关键人物。

Kuiji (632-682 CE), a native of Chang'an and nephew of the celebrated Tang general Yuchi Jingde, was the **founder of the Faxiang (Dharma-Characteristics) school** -- also known as the Cien school -- of Chinese Buddhism. As Xuanzang's most brilliant disciple, he co-translated the *Cheng Weishi Lun* (*Vijnaptimatratasiddhi-sastra*) and authored its definitive commentary, the *Cheng Weishi Lun Shuji*. Known as the **"Master of a Hundred Commentaries"** (百部疏主), Kuiji was the pivotal figure who systematized Indian Yogacara thought for the Chinese Buddhist tradition.

---

## 一句话定位 / One-Line Positioning

**窥基将玄奘从印度带回的瑜伽行派思想系统化、本土化，创立了法相唯识宗，使唯识学在七世纪的中国获得了最精密的理论表达——他是佛教史上最伟大的注释家之一。**

**Kuiji systematized and indigenized the Yogacara thought that Xuanzang brought back from India, founded the Faxiang school, and gave consciousness-only philosophy its most rigorous theoretical expression in seventh-century China -- making him one of the greatest commentarial scholars in Buddhist history.**

## 生平 / Biography

### 将门出身 / Military Family Origins

窥基俗姓尉迟，出身于显赫的武将家族。其叔父尉迟敬德是唐太宗麾下名将，封鄂国公。这一将门背景赋予窥基果敢坚毅的性格，后来在学术论争中表现出极强的系统性和战斗力。

Kuiji's lay surname was Yuchi, from a distinguished military family. His uncle Yuchi Jingde was one of Emperor Taizong's most celebrated generals, enfeoffed as Duke of E. This martial background endowed Kuiji with a resolute and determined character, which later manifested as formidable systematic rigor in scholarly debates.

### 少年出家 / Early Ordination

据《宋高僧传》记载，玄奘西行归来后，在长安寻觅英才以传承唯识学。他偶遇少年窥基，见其聪慧过人，遂说服其家族，令其出家。窥基年少时即以"三车和尚"的传说闻名——据说他出家时要求一车载书、一车载酒、一车载侍女，虽此故事很可能是后人附会，但反映了窥基出身贵族、性格不拘一格的特点。

According to the *Song Gaoseng Zhuan* (Song Dynasty Biographies of Eminent Monks), after Xuanzang returned from his pilgrimage to India, he searched Chang'an for talented disciples to transmit Yogacara teachings. He encountered the young Kuiji, recognized his extraordinary intelligence, and persuaded his family to allow his ordination. The legend of the "Three-Cart Monk" (三车和尚) -- that Kuiji requested one cart for books, one for wine, and one for attendants upon ordination -- is likely a later fabrication, but it reflects his aristocratic background and unconventional personality.

### 玄奘门下 / Under Xuanzang's Tutelage

窥基十七岁正式出家，入大慈恩寺，随玄奘学习瑜伽行派教义。在玄奘的数百弟子中，窥基以其超凡的理解力和写作才能脱颖而出。他不仅精通梵文，还深入理解了护法（Dharmapala）一系的唯识思想——这一系强调"有相唯识"（sakaravijnanavada），认为认识对象虽是识的变现，但认识活动中确实存在"相分"（表象）。

Kuiji formally took monastic vows at seventeen, entering the Great Cien Temple to study Yogacara doctrine under Xuanzang. Among Xuanzang's hundreds of disciples, Kuiji distinguished himself through his exceptional comprehension and literary talent. He mastered Sanskrit and deeply understood the Dharmapala lineage of Yogacara thought -- which emphasized "representational consciousness-only" (sakaravijnanavada), holding that while cognitive objects are projections of consciousness, cognitive acts genuinely involve "object-phases" (nimitta-bhaga / xiangfen).

### 《成唯识论》的编译 / Compiling the Cheng Weishi Lun

窥基最重要的学术贡献之一是参与《成唯识论》的编译工作。这部著作以世亲《唯识三十颂》为基础，汇集了护法、安慧等十位印度论师的注释。最初计划将十家注释分别翻译，但窥基建议玄奘"糅译"——将十家观点融为一体，以护法观点为主线，辅以其他论师意见。这一编辑策略使得《成唯识论》成为一部高度整合的系统性著作，而非松散的注释汇编。

One of Kuiji's most important scholarly contributions was his participation in compiling the *Cheng Weishi Lun*. This work, based on Vasubandhu's *Trimsika* (Thirty Verses on Consciousness-Only), integrates commentaries by ten Indian masters including Dharmapala and Sthiramati. Initially the plan was to translate each commentary separately, but Kuiji advised Xuanzang to adopt a "blended translation" (rouyi) approach -- synthesizing all ten perspectives into a unified work with Dharmapala's views as the main thread. This editorial strategy transformed the *Cheng Weishi Lun* into a highly integrated systematic treatise rather than a loose collection of glosses.

### 独立著述与宗派建设 / Independent Scholarship and School Building

玄奘圆寂（664 CE）后，窥基承担起传播唯识学的重任。他以大慈恩寺为基地，大量撰写注释和论著，系统整理了唯识学的教义体系。其著述之丰、涉及面之广，在汉传佛教史上罕有其匹。据载他一生著述逾百部，涵盖唯识学、因明学、法华学、般若学等多个领域。

After Xuanzang's passing (664 CE), Kuiji assumed the mantle of propagating Yogacara teachings. Based at the Great Cien Temple, he produced a prodigious body of commentaries and treatises, systematically organizing the Yogacara doctrinal system. The sheer volume and breadth of his writings is nearly unparalleled in Chinese Buddhist history -- he is credited with over one hundred works spanning Yogacara, Buddhist logic (hetuvidya), Lotus Sutra studies, and Prajnaparamita exegesis.

## 五个核心命题 / Five Core Doctrines

### 1. 唯识无境 / Consciousness-Only Without External Objects (Vijnapti-matrata)

一切所认知的对象，都是识的变现——不是"没有外境"，而是"外境不离识"。我们经验中的山河大地、自我他人，都是八识（尤其是阿赖耶识）种子现行的结果。这一命题是唯识学的认识论核心，而非本体论主张。

All objects of cognition are projections of consciousness -- not that "external objects do not exist," but that "external objects are inseparable from consciousness." The mountains, rivers, self, and others within our experience are all manifestations of the seeds (bija) within the eight consciousnesses, especially the storehouse consciousness (alaya-vijnana). This doctrine is the epistemological core of Yogacara, not an ontological claim.

See [唯识无境 / Consciousness-Only](concepts/consciousness-only.md)

### 2. 五种姓说 / Five Lineages (Panca-gotra)

声闻种姓（Sravaka-gotra）、缘觉种姓（Pratyekabuddha-gotra）、菩萨种姓（Bodhisattva-gotra）、不定种姓（Aniyata-gotra）、无种姓（Agotra/icchantika）——五种不同的修行潜能与解脱路径。这一分类在中国佛教中引发了巨大争议，因为它似乎与"一切众生皆有佛性"的主流观点相矛盾。

Five categories of spiritual potential: the Sravaka lineage (disciples), Pratyekabuddha lineage (solitary realizers), Bodhisattva lineage, indeterminate lineage, and the lineageless (icchantika). This classification provoked enormous controversy in Chinese Buddhism, as it appeared to contradict the dominant view that "all sentient beings possess Buddha-nature."

See [五种姓 / Five Lineages](concepts/five-lineages.md)

### 3. 百法明门 / Hundred Dharmas (Satadharma-vidyamukha)

将一切法归纳为一百种，分为五位：心法（8种）、心所法（51种）、色法（11种）、心不相应行法（24种）、无为法（6种）。这是佛教最精密的"心理元素周期表"，为修行者提供了系统的认知地图。

All phenomena (dharmas) are classified into one hundred types across five categories: mind (citta, 8 types), mental factors (caitta/cetasika, 51 types), form (rupa, 11 types), conditionings disassociated from mind (citta-viprayukta-samskara, 24 types), and unconditioned dharmas (asamskrta, 6 types). This is Buddhism's most precise "periodic table of mental elements," providing practitioners with a systematic cognitive map.

See [百法明门 / Hundred Dharmas](concepts/hundred-dharmas.md)

### 4. 三性三无性 / Three Natures and Three Non-Natures (Trisvabhava / Trinihsvarabhava)

遍计所执性（parikalpita-svabhava，虚妄分别的投射）、依他起性（paratantra-svabhava，因缘所生的存在方式）、圆成实性（parinispanna-svabhava，事物的真实本性）。配合三无性——相无性、生无性、胜义无性——构成唯识学的中道观。

The three natures: imagined nature (parikalpita, the projection of false discrimination), dependent nature (paratantra, the mode of existence arising from conditions), and perfected nature (parinispanna, the true nature of things). Combined with the three non-natures -- non-nature of characteristics, non-nature of arising, and non-nature of the ultimate -- these form the Yogacara middle way.

See [三性三无性 / Three Natures](concepts/three-natures.md)

### 5. 因明学 / Buddhist Logic (Hetuvidya)

窥基是汉传佛教因明学（佛教逻辑学）的重要传承者。他撰写的《因明入正理论疏》系统整理了陈那（Dignaga）和商羯罗主（Sankarasvamin）的因明体系，为三支论法（宗、因、喻）提供了清晰的汉文注释，使佛教逻辑学在中国得到传播。

Kuiji was a major transmitter of hetuvidya (Buddhist logic) in Chinese Buddhism. His commentary on the *Nyayapravesa-tarka-sastra* systematically organized the logical systems of Dignaga and Sankarasvamin, providing clear Chinese exegesis of the three-part syllogism (thesis, reason, example) and enabling Buddhist logic to take root in China.

See [因明 / Buddhist Logic](concepts/buddhist-logic.md)

## 学术特色 / Scholarly Characteristics

### 注释学的巨匠 / Master Commentator

窥基的注释风格以"详尽、系统、层次分明"著称。他不满足于简单的文句解释，而是将每一个概念置于整个唯识体系中加以定位，展示其与其他概念的关联。这种"全景式注释"使他的著作成为理解唯识学不可或缺的参考。

Kuiji's commentarial style is renowned for its thoroughness, systematicity, and clarity of structure. Rather than offering simple glosses on individual passages, he situated every concept within the entire Yogacara system, demonstrating its relationships to other concepts. This "panoramic commentary" approach made his works indispensable for understanding Yogacara philosophy.

### 融贯与独创 / Integration and Originality

虽然窥基以传承玄奘-护法一系的唯识学自居，但他在注释过程中展现了显著的独创性。他对"五种姓"说的坚持、对因明学的推广、对法华经的唯识解读，都超越了单纯的翻译和传承，构成了独立的思想贡献。

Although Kuiji presented himself as transmitting the Xuanzang-Dharmapala lineage of Yogacara, his commentarial work displayed considerable originality. His insistence on the five-lineage doctrine, his promotion of Buddhist logic, and his Yogacara reading of the Lotus Sutra all transcended mere translation and transmission, constituting independent intellectual contributions.

## 法相唯识宗的建立 / Founding the Faxiang School

窥基被公认为法相唯识宗（又称慈恩宗）的实际创立者。虽然玄奘是唯识学的主要传入者，但将唯识学组织为一个独立宗派——具有明确的教义体系、修行次第、判教理论——则是窥基的贡献。

Kuiji is universally recognized as the actual founder of the Faxiang (Dharma-Characteristics) school, also known as the Cien school. Although Xuanzang was the principal transmitter of Yogacara to China, it was Kuiji who organized Yogacara into an independent school -- with a defined doctrinal system, a graduated practice path, and a doctrinal classification scheme (panjiao).

### 判教 / Doctrinal Classification

窥基继承并发展了玄奘的"三时教判"：
- **第一时**：小乘教法（四谛、十二因缘）
- **第二时**：大乘般若（空性，但未能圆满）
- **第三时**：唯识中道（三性三无性，圆满究竟）

Kuiji inherited and developed Xuanzang's "Three-Turnings Doctrine Classification":
- **First Turning**: Hinayana teachings (Four Noble Truths, Twelve Links)
- **Second Turning**: Mahayana Prajnaparamita (emptiness, but not yet complete)
- **Third Turning**: Yogacara Middle Way (three natures and three non-natures, complete and ultimate)

### 传承 / Lineage

窥基传法于慧沼（650-714），慧沼传智周（668-723），形成法相宗三代祖师谱系。然而，法相宗在盛唐之后迅速衰落——其繁复的理论体系和对印度原典的严格依赖，使其难以适应中国佛教日益本土化的趋势。

Kuiji transmitted the teaching to Huizhao (650-714), who in turn transmitted to Zhizhou (668-723), forming the three-generation patriarchal lineage of the Faxiang school. However, the school declined rapidly after the High Tang period -- its elaborate theoretical system and strict reliance on Indian sources made it difficult to adapt to the increasingly sinicized trajectory of Chinese Buddhism.

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Relevance

### 认知科学 / Cognitive Science
唯识学的八识体系和心所分类，被越来越多认知科学家视为前现代最精密的认知模型。阿赖耶识的种子-现行机制与当代认知科学中的"图式"（schema）理论和"预测编码"（predictive coding）有显著的结构性对应。

The eight-consciousness system and mental-factor taxonomy of Yogacara are increasingly recognized by cognitive scientists as the most sophisticated pre-modern cognitive model. The seed-manifestation mechanism of the storehouse consciousness shows striking structural parallels with contemporary "schema" theory and "predictive coding" in cognitive science.

### 人工智能 / Artificial Intelligence
"识的变现"与当代生成模型（GAN, diffusion models）的认知论形成了有趣的对话：AI生成的图像并非"真实世界"的反映，而是训练数据中"种子"的"现行"——这与唯识学的核心命题形成了跨时代的呼应。

"Consciousness-only projection" enters into fascinating dialogue with the epistemology of contemporary generative models (GANs, diffusion models): AI-generated images are not reflections of the "real world" but "manifestations" of "seeds" within training data -- an echo of the Yogacara core thesis across centuries.

### 心理学 / Psychology
八识体系中的末那识（自我执着识）与弗洛伊德的自我（ego）概念、阿赖耶识与荣格的集体无意识之间，存在可比较的结构关系。窥基对这些概念的详细阐释为跨文化心理学对话提供了丰富的资源。

The seventh consciousness (manas, the self-grasping consciousness) and the storehouse consciousness in the eight-consciousness system bear comparable structural relationships with Freud's ego concept and Jung's collective unconscious respectively. Kuiji's detailed elaboration of these concepts provides rich resources for cross-cultural psychological dialogue.
