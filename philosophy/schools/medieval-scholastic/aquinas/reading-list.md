# 阿奎那进阶阅读 · Thomas Aquinas Reading List

## 一、原典 / Primary Sources

### 必读
- *Summa Theologiae* I, q. 2 — 五路论证
- *De Ente et Essentia* — 存在与本质
- *Summa Theologiae* I-II, q. 94 — 自然法

### 重要
- *Summa Contra Gentiles* — 理性神学
- *Summa Theologiae* I, qq. 3–26 — 上帝属性
- *Summa Theologiae* I, qq. 75–89 — 灵魂与认知
- *Summa Theologiae* I-II, qq. 1–21 — 幸福论
- *Quaestiones Disputatae de Veritate* — 真理与知识

## 二、经典研究 / Classic Studies

- Étienne Gilson, *The Christian Philosophy of St. Thomas Aquinas* (1956) — 20世纪最重要的阿奎那研究
- Étienne Gilson, *Being and Some Philosophers* (1949) — 存在概念的历史分析
- Jacques Maritain, *The Degrees of Knowledge* (1932) — 知识论
- Josef Pieper, *Guide to Thomas Aquinas* (1962) — 可读性强的导论
- Frederick Copleston, *Aquinas* (1955) — 英语世界的经典导论
- Cornelio Fabro, *Participation et Causalité selon S. Thomas d'Aquin* (1961) — 参与与因果性
- Réginald Garrigou-Lagrange, *God: His Existence and His Nature* (1934) — 上帝论

## 三、当代研究 / Contemporary Studies

- Brian Davies, *The Thought of Thomas Aquinas* (1992) — 当代最佳英语导论
- Eleonore Stump, *Aquinas* (2003) — 分析哲学视角下的阿奎那
- Brian Davies & Eleonore Stump (eds.), *The Oxford Handbook of Aquinas* (2012) — 最全面的英文论文集
- John Wippel, *The Metaphysical Thought of Thomas Aquinas* (2000) — 形而上学专著
- Robert Pasnau, *Thomas Aquinas on Human Nature* (2002) — 人性论
- Norman Kretzmann, *The Metaphysics of Theism: Aquinas's Natural Theology* (1997)
- Stephen Brock, *Action and Conduct: Thomas Aquinas and the Theory of Action* (1998)
- John Finnis, *Aquinas: Moral, Political, and Legal Theory* (1998) — 法律与政治哲学

## 四、入门推荐 / Introductory

- Josef Pieper, *Guide to Thomas Aquinas* (1962) — 最佳入门
- Brian Davies, *The Thought of Thomas Aquinas* (1992) — 系统且可读
- "Thomas Aquinas" in *Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy* (Stump)
- "Saint Thomas Aquinas" in *Internet Encyclopedia of Philosophy*
- Edward Feser, *Aquinas: A Beginner's Guide* (2009) — 当代分析哲学视角入门
- Herbert McCabe, *On Aquinas* (2008) — 神学导论

## 五、阅读路径建议

| 目标 | 推荐顺序 |
|------|----------|
| 快速了解 | Pieper *Guide* → Davies ch. 1-3 |
| 研究形而上学 | *De Ente et Essentia* → Wippel → Gilson |
| 研究上帝论 | ST I, qq. 2-26 → Kretzmann → Davies |
| 研究伦理学 | ST I-II → Finnis → MacIntyre *After Virtue* |
| 研究灵魂论 | ST I, qq. 75-89 → Pasnau → Stump |
| 研究自然法 | ST I-II, q. 94 → Finnis → George |
| 比较司各脱 | Cross *Duns Scotus* → Williams *Cambridge Companion* |
