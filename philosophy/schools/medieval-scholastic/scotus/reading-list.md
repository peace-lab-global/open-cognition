# 司各脱进阶阅读 · Duns Scotus Reading List

## 一、原典 / Primary Sources

### 必读
- *A Treatise on God as First Principle* (*De Primo Principio*), trans. Allan B. Wolter — 最紧凑的形而上学论文
- *Duns Scotus on the Will and Morality*, trans. Allan B. Wolter (1986) — 精选英译
- *Ordinatio* II, d. 3, q. 1 (haecceitas) — 此性理论

### 重要
- *Ordinatio* I, d. 3 (univocity) — 单义性学说
- *Ordinatio* I, d. 8 (infinity of God) — 上帝之无限
- *Reportatio Parisiensis* — 巴黎教学记录
- *Quodlibetal Questions* — 辩论性论文

## 二、经典研究 / Classic Studies

- Allan B. Wolter, *The Transcendentals and Their Function in the Metaphysics of Duns Scotus* (1946) — 超验属性的经典研究
- Allan B. Wolter, *Duns Scotus' Parisian Proof for the Existence of God* (1947)
- Effrem Bettoni, *Duns Scotus: The Basic Principles of His Philosophy* (1961)
- Thomas Williams (ed.), *The Cambridge Companion to Duns Scotus* (2003) — 最全面的英文论文集
- Stephen Dumont, "The Univocity of the Concept of Being in the Fourteenth Century" (1987) — 单义性学说的历史分析
- Richard Cross, *Duns Scotus* (1999) — 系统性的学术导论
- Richard Cross, *Duns Scotus on God* (2005) — 上帝论专题研究

## 三、当代研究 / Contemporary Studies

- Antonie Vos, *The Philosophy of John Duns Scotus* (2006) — 最系统的当代综合研究
- Mary Beth Ingham & Mechthild Dreyer, *The Philosophical Vision of John Duns Scotus* (2004) — 可读性强的导论
- Timothy Noone (ed.), *A Companion to Angels in Medieval Philosophy* (2010) — 含司各脱天使学论文
- John Haldane (ed.), "Scotus and the Making of Modernity" (*American Catholic Philosophical Quarterly*, 2006) — 司各脱与现代性
- Giorgio Pini, *Categories and Logic in Duns Scotus* (2002) — 范畴与逻辑
- Calvin Normore, "Scotus, Modality, Instants of Nature, and the Contingent" (1996) — 模态与偶然性

## 四、入门推荐 / Introductory

- Richard Cross, *Duns Scotus* (1999, Great Medieval Thinkers series) — 最佳英文入门
- Mary Beth Ingham, *Scotus for Dunces* (2003) — 面向初学者的通俗导论
- "John Duns Scotus" in *Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy* (Williams)
- "Duns Scotus" in *Internet Encyclopedia of Philosophy*
- Thomas Williams, *Introduction to Duns Scotus* (in Cambridge Companion)

## 五、阅读路径建议

| 目标 | 推荐顺序 |
|------|----------|
| 快速了解 | Ingham *Scotus for Dunces* → Cross *Duns Scotus* |
| 研究单义性 | *Ordinatio* I, d. 3 → Dumont 1987 → Pini |
| 研究此性 | *Ordinatio* II, d. 3 → Cross ch. 4 → King |
| 研究上帝论 | *De Primo Principio* → Cross *Duns Scotus on God* |
| 研究意志主义 | Wolter *Will and Morality* → Vos → Ingham |
| 比较阿奎那 | Cross *Duns Scotus* ch. 1-2 → Williams *Cambridge Companion* |
