# 容忍原则 · Principle of Tolerance (Toleranzprinzip)

## 概念定位 / Conceptual Position

"容忍原则"是卡尔纳普在《语言的逻辑句法》（1934）中提出的最具前瞻性的哲学主张："在逻辑中没有道德"（In der Logik gibt es keine Moral）。每个人有权选择自己的逻辑系统和语言形式，评判标准不是"真理"或"正确性"，而是实用性和便利性。这一思想预示了当代的多元主义和实用主义哲学观。

The "Principle of Tolerance" is Carnap's most forward-looking philosophical thesis, proposed in *The Logical Syntax of Language* (1934): "In logic, there is no morality." Everyone has the right to choose their own logical system and language form; the criterion is not "truth" or "correctness" but utility and convenience. This idea anticipated contemporary pluralist and pragmatist philosophical views.

## 核心论述 / Core Argument

### 语言框架的选择 / Choosing Language Frameworks

卡尔纳普论证：不存在唯一"正确"的语言或逻辑系统。选择经典逻辑还是直觉主义逻辑，选择集合论还是类型论，选择物理语言还是现象语言——这些都是实用性问题，不是真理问题。

Carnap argued: there is no uniquely "correct" language or logical system. Choosing between classical and intuitionistic logic, set theory and type theory, physical language and phenomenal language — these are pragmatic questions, not truth questions.

### 两种框架的比较 / Comparing Two Frameworks

以"数字框架"为例：
- 采纳"数字存在"的框架 → 可以更方便地讨论数学
- 不采纳的框架 → 必须用更迂回的方式表达同样的内容

选择不是关于"数字是否真的存在"，而是关于"哪种语言更方便"。

Taking the "number framework": adopting a framework where "numbers exist" allows more convenient mathematical discussion. The choice is not about "whether numbers really exist" but about "which language is more convenient."

### 容忍与约定 / Tolerance and Convention

容忍原则并不意味着"什么都行"——它要求：
1. 选择的框架必须内部一致
2. 选择的理由必须是明确的（实用性标准）
3. 不同框架之间的翻译必须是可能的

Tolerance does not mean "anything goes" — it requires internal consistency, explicit pragmatic reasons for choice, and translatability between frameworks.

## 哲学意义 / Philosophical Significance

1. **哲学多元主义 / Philosophical Pluralism**: 为方法论多元主义提供了最早的哲学论证
2. **本体论工程 / Ontology Engineering**: 当代本体论中"选择什么框架"的问题正是卡尔纳普框架选择的延续
3. **科学哲学 / Philosophy of Science**: 不同科学理论范式的选择问题
4. **AI知识表示 / AI Knowledge Representation**: 选择何种知识表示语言本质上也是框架选择

## 与东方思想的对话 / Dialogue with Eastern Thought

### 与庄子"因是因非" / With Zhuangzi's "This-That" Relativism
庄子《齐物论》中"彼亦一是非，此亦一是非"——不同立场有不同的是非标准。这与容忍原则有深层共鸣：两者都承认不存在绝对正确的框架，选择取决于实用目的。但庄子走向了超越是非的"道"，卡尔纳普停留在实用选择的层面。

Zhuangzi's "this side has its rights and wrongs, that side has its rights and wrongs" — different positions have different standards. This deeply resonates with the tolerance principle: both acknowledge no absolutely correct framework; choice depends on practical purpose. But Zhuangzi moved toward transcending right and wrong in the "Way," while Carnap stayed at the level of pragmatic choice.
