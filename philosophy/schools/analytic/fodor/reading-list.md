# 福多进阶阅读 · Jerry Fodor Reading List

## 一、原典 / Primary Sources

### 必读
- *The Language of Thought* (1975) — 思维语言假说
- *The Modularity of Mind* (1983) — 模块性理论
- *LOT 2* (2008) — LOT假说的修订

### 重要
- *Psychosemantics* (1987) — 内容理论
- *Concepts* (1998) — 概念理论
- "Connectionism and Cognitive Architecture" (1988) — 批评联结主义
- *The Mind Doesn't Work That Way* (2000) — 批评进化心理学

## 二、经典研究 / Classic Studies

- Noam Chomsky, *Aspects of the Theory of Syntax* (1965) — 普遍语法（Fodor的思想来源）
- Hilary Putnam, "The Meaning of 'Meaning'" (1975) — 语义外在论
- Jerry Fodor & Zenon Pylyshyn, "Connectionism and Cognitive Architecture" (1988) — 批评联结主义
- Paul Smolensky, "On the Proper Treatment of Connectionism" (1988) — 回应Fodor
- Stephen Pinker, *How the Mind Works* (1997) — 模块性的通俗阐述
- Dan Sperber, *Explaining Culture* (1996) — 模块性的扩展

## 三、当代研究 / Contemporary Studies

- Tim Crane, *The Mechanical Mind* (1995) — 计算主义导论
- Frances Egan, "Fodor's Theory of Content" (1995) — 内容理论分析
- Peter Carruthers, *The Architecture of the Mind* (2006) — 模块性的当代发展
- Peter Godfrey-Smith, "Fodor on Darwin" (2011) — 回应Fodor对适应主义的批评
- Robert Cummins, "Fodor's Representational Theory of Mind" — 表征理论批评
- Peter Carruthers, "HOP Over FOR and HOT" — 对Fodor意识理论的批评

## 四、入门推荐 / Introductory

- *The Modularity of Mind* (1983) — 最佳入门，紧凑清晰
- "The Mind-Body Problem" (*Scientific American*, 1981) — 面向大众
- "Jerry Fodor" in *Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy* (Various)
- *LOT 2* Introduction — 修订版概述

## 五、阅读路径建议

| 目标 | 推荐顺序 |
|------|----------|
| 快速了解 | "Mind-Body Problem" (1981) → *Modularity of Mind* |
| 研究LOT | *Language of Thought* (1975) → *LOT 2* (2008) |
| 研究模块性 | *Modularity of Mind* → Sperber → Carruthers |
| 研究内容理论 | *Psychosemantics* → *Elm and the Expert* → Egan |
| 批评联结主义 | "Connectionism and Cognitive Architecture" → Smolensky |
| 批评进化心理学 | *Mind Doesn't Work That Way* → Pinker → Godfrey-Smith |
