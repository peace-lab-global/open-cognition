# 霍维 · Jakob Hohwy

## 基本信息 / Basic Information

**中文名 / Chinese Name:** 雅各布·霍维 (Jakob Hohwy)
**出生 / Born:** 1968, 丹麦 / Denmark
**国籍 / Nationality:** 丹麦-澳大利亚 / Danish-Australian
**现职 / Current Position:** 蒙纳士大学哲学教授 / Professor of Philosophy, Monash University (Australia)
**研究领域 / Research Areas:** 预测加工哲学、意识哲学、认知科学哲学、心灵哲学 / Philosophy of predictive processing, philosophy of consciousness, philosophy of cognitive science, philosophy of mind
**学术背景 / Academic Background:** 奥胡斯大学哲学博士，曾在爱丁堡大学从事博士后研究 / PhD in Philosophy from Aarhus University; postdoctoral research at the University of Edinburgh

Jakob Hohwy 是当代心灵哲学与认知科学哲学领域最具影响力的思想家之一。他以对预测加工（predictive processing）框架的系统性哲学阐释闻名于世，特别是2013年出版的《预测心智》（*The Predictive Mind*）——这是第一部全面从哲学角度审视预测加工理论的专著。Hohwy 的核心贡献在于将一个源自计算神经科学的理论框架提升为一个统一的心灵哲学范式，并深入探讨其认识论、形而上学和现象学含义。

Jakob Hohwy is one of the most influential thinkers in contemporary philosophy of mind and philosophy of cognitive science. He is renowned for his systematic philosophical interpretation of the predictive processing framework, particularly through his 2013 monograph *The Predictive Mind* — the first comprehensive philosophical examination of predictive processing theory. Hohwy's central contribution lies in elevating a framework originating in computational neuroscience into a unified philosophy of mind paradigm, while deeply exploring its epistemological, metaphysical, and phenomenological implications.

## 核心命题 / Core Theses

### 1. 感知即预测 / Perception as Prediction
**中文：** 感知不是被动的信息接收，而是大脑主动进行的预测过程。大脑持续生成关于外部世界的内部模型，自上而下地预测感觉输入；感知的本质是"预测误差最小化"——大脑不断修正预测，使内部模型与实际输入趋于一致。
**English:** Perception is not passive information reception but an active predictive process. The brain continuously generates internal models of the external world, predicting sensory input top-down; the essence of perception is "prediction error minimization" — the brain constantly revises predictions to align internal models with actual input.

### 2. 精度即注意力 / Precision as Attention
**中文：** 大脑并非对所有预测误差一视同仁。它必须估计每个误差信号的可靠性（精度），并对高精度的误差赋予更大权重。注意力本质上就是精度加权的过程——大脑动态分配"信任度"给不同的信息来源。
**English:** The brain does not treat all prediction errors equally. It must estimate the reliability (precision) of each error signal and assign greater weight to high-precision errors. Attention is essentially the process of precision weighting — the brain dynamically allocates "trust" to different information sources.

### 3. 大脑是自我证明的系统 / The Brain as Self-Evidencing System
**中文：** Hohwy 最具独创性的论点：大脑不仅被动地预测和修正，还通过主动推理（行动）使感觉输入与预测一致。大脑积极寻找确认自身模型的证据——这是一个"自我证明"的循环过程。
**English:** Hohwy's most original thesis: the brain does not merely predict and correct passively but actively makes sensory input consistent with predictions through active inference (action). The brain actively seeks evidence confirming its own models — a "self-evidencing" circular process.

### 4. 心智封闭于颅骨之内 / The Mind is Skull-Bound
**中文：** 与 Andy Clark 的延展心智理论形成鲜明对比，Hohwy 坚持认为大脑只能通过感觉输入这一"马尔可夫毯"（Markov blanket）与世界接触。我们永远活在模型之中，永远无法直接接触世界本身——这是一种深刻的认识论悲观主义。
**English:** In sharp contrast to Andy Clark's extended mind theory, Hohwy maintains that the brain can only contact the world through the "Markov blanket" of sensory input. We always live within models, never directly accessing the world itself — a profound epistemological pessimism.

### 5. 意识与预测加工 / Consciousness and Predictive Processing
**中文：** 意识经验的内容由预测模型的高层级结构决定。主观感受的"现象特质"（phenomenal character）可以理解为大脑对特定预测及其精度的编码方式。这为理解意识的困难问题提供了一个新的框架。
**English:** The content of conscious experience is determined by the high-level structure of predictive models. The phenomenal character of subjective experience can be understood as the brain's encoding of specific predictions and their precisions. This provides a new framework for understanding the hard problem of consciousness.

### 6. 统一的认识论框架 / A Unified Epistemological Framework
**中文：** 预测加工不仅是一个认知科学理论，更是一个统一的认识论框架。从感知到行动，从注意力到意识，从学习到情感——所有心理过程都可以被理解为预测误差最小化的不同表现形式。
**English:** Predictive processing is not merely a cognitive science theory but a unified epistemological framework. From perception to action, from attention to consciousness, from learning to emotion — all mental processes can be understood as different manifestations of prediction error minimization.

## 思想脉络 / Intellectual Development

Hohwy 的哲学发展可追溯至他在丹麦奥胡斯大学的博士研究，期间他对康德的认识论传统和当代认知科学产生了浓厚兴趣。他早期的工作受到赫尔姆霍茨（Helmholtz）无意识推理思想的启发——赫尔姆霍茨在19世纪就已提出感知是大脑对外部世界的"假设"这一观点。

Hohwy's philosophical development traces back to his doctoral studies at Aarhus University in Denmark, where he developed strong interests in both the Kantian epistemological tradition and contemporary cognitive science. His early work was inspired by Helmholtz's notion of unconscious inference — Helmholtz had already proposed in the 19th century that perception is the brain's "hypothesis" about the external world.

**关键转折点 / Key Turning Point:**
2000年代中期，Karl Friston 提出的自由能原理（Free Energy Principle）和预测编码（predictive coding）为 Hohwy 提供了将认识论传统与计算神经科学统一的数学框架。Hohwy 敏锐地意识到，这一框架不仅是神经科学的工具，更是一个关于心智本质的哲学理论。

In the mid-2000s, Karl Friston's Free Energy Principle and predictive coding provided Hohwy with a mathematical framework unifying the epistemological tradition with computational neuroscience. Hohwy acutely recognized that this framework was not merely a neuroscience tool but a philosophical theory about the nature of mind.

**《预测心智》（2013）/ The Predictive Mind (2013):**
这本书标志着预测加工哲学的正式诞生。Hohwy 系统地论证了：(1) 预测编码是大脑的统一计算原理；(2) 这一原理具有深远的哲学含义；(3) 它重新定义了感知、行动和意识的关系。该书特别突出了 Hohwy 与 Clark 之间的关键分歧——Hohwy 强调预测加工的内部主义（internalist）解读，而 Clark 倾向于外部主义（externalist）解读。

This book marked the formal birth of predictive processing philosophy. Hohwy systematically argued that: (1) predictive coding is the brain's unified computational principle; (2) this principle has profound philosophical implications; (3) it redefines the relationship between perception, action, and consciousness. The book particularly highlighted the crucial divergence between Hohwy and Clark — Hohwy emphasized the internalist reading of predictive processing, while Clark favored the externalist reading.

**自我证明与主动推理 / Self-Evidencing and Active Inference:**
2016年至2020年间，Hohwy 进一步发展了"自我证明"（self-evidencing）的概念，将预测加工与贝叶斯模型选择理论联系起来。他论证道，大脑不仅最小化预测误差，还主动选择能确认其模型的行动——这使得预测加工框架具有了一种深刻的循环性和自指性。

Between 2016 and 2020, Hohwy further developed the concept of "self-evidencing," connecting predictive processing with Bayesian model selection theory. He argued that the brain not only minimizes prediction error but actively selects actions that confirm its models — giving the predictive processing framework a profound circularity and self-referentiality.

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

**精神病学 / Psychiatry:**
预测加工框架为理解精神分裂症提供了新视角——幻觉可被理解为先验预测过度加权（即大脑的"预测"压倒了感觉输入），而妄想则是高层信念模型的异常固化。精度加权异常可解释注意力缺陷和感觉门控障碍。

The predictive processing framework offers new perspectives on schizophrenia — hallucinations can be understood as excessive weighting of prior predictions (the brain's "predictions" overwhelming sensory input), while delusions reflect abnormal fixation of high-level belief models. Precision weighting abnormalities can explain attentional deficits and sensory gating disorders.

**人工智能 / Artificial Intelligence:**
预测编码启发了新一代神经网络架构，特别是预测编码网络（Predictive Coding Networks）和主动推理算法。这些架构试图模拟大脑的层级预测机制，在机器感知和机器人控制中展现出潜力。

Predictive coding has inspired new neural network architectures, particularly Predictive Coding Networks and active inference algorithms. These architectures attempt to simulate the brain's hierarchical prediction mechanisms, showing promise in machine perception and robotic control.

**教育科学 / Education Science:**
如果学习本质上是预测模型的更新，那么教学设计应当优化"可管理的预测误差"——既不太简单（无误差）也不太困难（误差过大），使学习者处于最佳学习区间。

If learning is essentially the updating of predictive models, instructional design should optimize "manageable prediction error" — neither too simple (no error) nor too difficult (excessive error) — placing learners in an optimal learning zone.

**美学与艺术 / Aesthetics and Art:**
审美体验可以被理解为预测误差的动态博弈——艺术通过创造和解决预测误差来产生愉悦。音乐的愉悦感来自对旋律预测的违反和解决；视觉艺术利用感知预期来制造惊奇。

Aesthetic experience can be understood as a dynamic play of prediction error — art produces pleasure by creating and resolving prediction errors. Musical pleasure comes from violating and resolving melodic predictions; visual art exploits perceptual expectations to create surprise.

## 常见误读 / Common Misreadings

### 误读 1 / Misreading 1
**误读：** 预测加工意味着大脑"忽视"外部世界，只关心自己的预测。
**Misreading:** Predictive processing means the brain "ignores" the external world and only cares about its own predictions.

**正解：** 预测加工恰恰是一种与世界深度互动的模型。预测误差信号来自感觉输入，大脑必须不断回应世界的"反馈"。大脑的预测不是任意的，而是被世界的因果结构所约束——不准确的预测会产生持续误差，迫使模型更新。
**Correction:** Predictive processing is precisely a model of deep interaction with the world. Prediction error signals come from sensory input; the brain must constantly respond to the world's "feedback." The brain's predictions are not arbitrary but constrained by the world's causal structure — inaccurate predictions generate persistent errors, forcing model updates.

### 误读 2 / Misreading 2
**误读：** "自我证明"意味着大脑是封闭的唯我论系统。
**Misreading:** "Self-evidencing" means the brain is a closed solipsistic system.

**正解：** 自我证明描述的是大脑的推理结构，而非其认识论地位。大脑确实通过行动寻求确认自身模型的证据，但这些模型必须最终追踪到世界的因果结构——否则预测误差将持续增大，系统将崩溃。Hohwy 称之为"认识论的悲观主义"而非"形而上学的唯我论"。
**Correction:** Self-evidencing describes the brain's inferential structure, not its epistemological status. The brain does seek evidence confirming its models through action, but these models must ultimately track the world's causal structure — otherwise prediction error would increase indefinitely and the system would collapse. Hohwy calls this "epistemological pessimism," not "metaphysical solipsism."

### 误读 3 / Misreading 3
**误读：** 精度加权意味着注意力是"自上而下"的完全控制。
**Misreading:** Precision weighting means attention is completely "top-down" controlled.

**正解：** 精度加权是一个双向过程。虽然高层模型设定了预期的精度分配，但突发的、高精度的感觉输入也能"自下而上"地重新分配精度。这正是为什么突然的巨响能不由自主地吸引注意力——它携带了无法被高层预测忽略的高精度误差信号。
**Correction:** Precision weighting is a bidirectional process. Although high-level models set expected precision allocation, sudden high-precision sensory input can also redistribute precision "bottom-up." This is why a sudden loud noise involuntarily captures attention — it carries a high-precision error signal that high-level predictions cannot ignore.

### 误读 4 / Misreading 4
**误读：** 预测加工只是贝叶斯推理的重述，没有新的理论贡献。
**Misreading:** Predictive processing is merely a restatement of Bayesian inference with no new theoretical contribution.

**正解：** 预测加工超越了标准的贝叶斯推理框架。它不仅涉及信念更新（epistemic inference），还涉及行动选择（active inference）和模型选择（self-evidencing）。特别是 Hohwy 强调的"自我证明"概念——大脑主动选择行动以确认自身模型——这是一个超越标准贝叶斯框架的独特贡献。
**Correction:** Predictive processing goes beyond standard Bayesian inference. It involves not only belief updating (epistemic inference) but also action selection (active inference) and model selection (self-evidencing). Particularly Hohwy's emphasis on "self-evidencing" — the brain actively selecting actions to confirm its own models — is a distinctive contribution beyond the standard Bayesian framework.

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

### 与唯识学的深层对话 / Deep Dialogue with Yogacara

Hohwy 的预测加工框架与佛教唯识学（Yogacara）的"三性"（tri-svabhava）理论形成了令人惊叹的结构性对应。这一对话不是简单的概念类比，而是触及了两个传统对认知本质的共同洞察。

Hohwy's predictive processing framework and the Buddhist Yogacara school's theory of "three natures" (tri-svabhava) form a stunning structural correspondence. This dialogue is not mere conceptual analogy but touches on both traditions' shared insight into the nature of cognition.

**遍计所执性 (Parikalpita-svabhava) ≈ 自上而下的先验预测 / Conceptual Projection ≈ Top-Down Prior Predictions**
唯识学认为，凡夫的认知被"遍计所执"所染——我们将概念投射到事物之上，把名称和范畴误认为事物的本质。这与预测加工中"先验预测"的功能惊人地类似：大脑将内部模型投射到感觉输入之上，我们所"看到"的往往是我们预期看到的东西。
Yogacara holds that ordinary cognition is stained by "conceptual projection" — we project concepts onto things, mistaking names and categories for the nature of things. This is remarkably analogous to "prior predictions" in predictive processing: the brain projects internal models onto sensory input; what we "see" is often what we expect to see.

**依他起性 (Paratantra-svabhava) ≈ 自下而上的感觉输入 / Dependent Origination ≈ Bottom-Up Sensory Input**
"依他起"指事物依因缘条件而生起的因果流转——这是事物的缘起层面，不受概念扭曲。在预测加工中，这对应于自下而上的感觉输入流——来自感觉器官的原始信号，携带着关于世界的真实因果信息。
"Dependent origination" refers to the causal flow of things arising from conditions — the dependent-arising aspect of things, undistorted by concepts. In predictive processing, this corresponds to the bottom-up sensory input stream — raw signals from sense organs carrying genuine causal information about the world.

**圆成实性 (Parinispanna-svabhava) ≈ 预测与输入的完美匹配 / Perfected Nature ≈ Perfect Prediction-Input Match**
"圆成实"是去除了遍计所执后的真实状态——当概念投射不再遮蔽事物的缘起本质时，智慧就显现了。在预测加工框架中，这可以类比为预测模型与感觉输入达到完美一致的状态——预测误差为零，模型完全追踪了世界的因果结构。
"Perfected nature" is the true state after removing conceptual projection — when conceptual projection no longer obscures the dependent nature of things, wisdom manifests. In the predictive processing framework, this can be analogized to the state where the predictive model and sensory input achieve perfect alignment — zero prediction error, the model perfectly tracking the world's causal structure.

### 与庄子认识论的对话 / Dialogue with Zhuangzi's Epistemology

庄子"齐物论"中对认知局限性的反思与 Hohwy 的"颅骨封闭"论点产生了深刻共鸣。庄子指出我们总是从特定的"立场"（视角）出发去认识世界，每一种认知都预设了一个未被检验的框架——这正是预测加工所说的"先验模型"。庄子的"心斋"（清空心灵的预设）可以被理解为一种减少先验预测权重的修行方法。

Zhuangzi's reflection on cognitive limitations in the "Equality of Things" chapter resonates deeply with Hohwy's "skull-bound" thesis. Zhuangzi observes that we always approach the world from a particular "stance" (perspective), every cognition presupposing an unexamined framework — precisely what predictive processing calls "prior models." Zhuangzi's "fasting of the mind" (emptying the mind's presuppositions) can be understood as a practice for reducing the weight of prior predictions.
