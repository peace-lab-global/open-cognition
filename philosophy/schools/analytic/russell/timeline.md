# 罗素年表 / Russell Timeline

## 早年 / Early Life (1872–1900)

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|---|---|
| 1872 | 5月18日生于威尔士特雷莱克。父母和姐姐在其幼年时相继去世，由祖父母抚养。/ Born May 18 at Trellech, Wales. Parents and sister died in his childhood; raised by grandparents. |
| 1890 | 进入剑桥三一学院学习数学。/ Entered Trinity College, Cambridge, to study mathematics. |
| 1893 | 获数学学位。/ Received mathematics degree. |
| 1894 | 获道德科学（哲学）学位。/ Received moral sciences (philosophy) degree. |
| 1894 | 与Alys Pearsall Smith结婚。/ Married Alys Pearsall Smith. |
| 1895 | 赴柏林研究经济学和政治学。发表《德国社会民主》。/ Went to Berlin to study economics and politics. Published *German Social Democracy*. |
| 1896 | 赴美讲学。出版《论几何学基础》。/ Lectured in the US. Published *An Essay on the Foundations of Geometry*. |

## 逻辑主义时期 / Logicist Period (1900–1920)

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|---|---|
| 1900 | 在巴黎国际哲学大会上接触皮亚诺的逻辑符号系统，深受启发。/ Encountered Peano's logical notation at the International Congress of Philosophy in Paris. |
| 1901 | 发现罗素悖论。/ Discovered Russell's Paradox. |
| 1903 | 出版《数学原理》(*The Principles of Mathematics*)。/ Published *The Principles of Mathematics*. |
| 1905 | 发表"On Denoting"——描述理论。/ Published "On Denoting" — theory of descriptions. |
| 1910 | 出版《数学原理》(*Principia Mathematica*) 第一卷。/ Published *Principia Mathematica* Vol. I. |
| 1911 | 维特根斯坦到剑桥求学，师从罗素。/ Wittgenstein arrived at Cambridge to study under Russell. |
| 1912 | 出版《哲学问题》(*The Problems of Philosophy*)。/ Published *The Problems of Philosophy*. |
| 1914 | 一战爆发。罗素成为和平主义者。/ WWI broke out. Russell became a pacifist. |
| 1916 | 因反战宣传被罚款，被剥夺剑桥教职。/ Fined and deprived of Cambridge lectureship for anti-war propaganda. |
| 1918 | 因反战活动入狱Brixton监狱6个月。在狱中写作《数理哲学导论》。/ Imprisoned for 6 months in Brixton Prison. Wrote *Introduction to Mathematical Philosophy* in prison. |
| 1918 | 发表《逻辑原子主义哲学》系列讲座。/ Delivered *The Philosophy of Logical Atomism* lectures. |

## 中期 / Middle Period (1920–1945)

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|---|---|
| 1920 | 访问苏俄，与列宁会面。对布尔什维克主义持批判态度。/ Visited Soviet Russia; met Lenin. Critical of Bolshevism. |
| 1920 | 访问中国，在北京大学讲学一年。/ Visited China; lectured at Peking University for a year. |
| 1921 | 与Dora Black结婚。出版《心的分析》。/ Married Dora Black. Published *The Analysis of Mind*. |
| 1927 | 与Dora创办实验学校（Beacon Hill School）。出版《物的分析》。/ Founded Beacon Hill School with Dora. Published *The Analysis of Matter*. |
| 1938 | 赴美国讲学（芝加哥大学、UCLA）。/ Lectured in the US (University of Chicago, UCLA). |
| 1940 | 纽约城市学院教职被撤销（因性伦理观点）。/ Appointment at City College of New York revoked (due to views on sexual ethics). |
| 1944 | 回到英国，重返剑桥三一学院。/ Returned to Britain and Trinity College, Cambridge. |
| 1945 | 出版《西方哲学史》(*History of Western Philosophy*)，成为畅销书。/ Published *History of Western Philosophy*, became a bestseller. |

## 晚年 / Late Years (1945–1970)

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|---|---|
| 1948 | 出版《人类的知识》(*Human Knowledge: Its Scope and Limits*)。/ Published *Human Knowledge*. |
| 1950 | 获诺贝尔文学奖。/ Received Nobel Prize in Literature. |
| 1955 | 与爱因斯坦联合发表《罗素-爱因斯坦宣言》，呼吁核裁军。/ Co-authored Russell-Einstein Manifesto calling for nuclear disarmament. |
| 1958 | 发起核裁军运动（CND）。/ Initiated Campaign for Nuclear Disarmament (CND). |
| 1961 | 因反核示威被逮捕，在91岁高龄入狱一周。/ Arrested for anti-nuclear demonstration; imprisoned for one week at age 91. |
| 1963 | 成立罗素和平基金会。/ Established the Russell Peace Foundation. |
| 1967–1969 | 出版三卷自传。/ Published three-volume *Autobiography*. |
| 1970 | 2月2日在威尔士Penrhyndeudraeth去世，享年97岁。/ Died February 2 at Penrhyndeudraeth, Wales, aged 97. |
