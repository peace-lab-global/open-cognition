# 麦金进阶阅读 · Colin McGinn Reading List

## 一、原典 / Primary Sources

### 必读
- "Can We Solve the Mind-Body Problem?" (*Mind*, 1989) — 认知闭合论题的首次系统阐述
- *The Problem of Consciousness* (1991) — 新神秘主义奠基之作
- *The Mysterious Flame* (1999) — 面向大众的通俗阐述

### 重要
- *Problems in Philosophy: The Limits of Inquiry* (1993) — 认知闭合的扩展应用
- *Consciousness and Its Objects* (2004) — 意识与意向性
- *Minds and Bodies* (1996) — 对当代心灵哲学家的评论
- *The Making of a Philosopher* (2006) — 学术自传

## 二、经典研究 / Classic Studies

- Owen Flanagan, *Consciousness Reconsidered* (1992) — 对新神秘主义最重要的回应
- David Chalmers, *The Conscious Mind* (1996) — 硬问题的经典阐述
- Thomas Nagel, "What Is It Like to Be a Bat?" (1974) — McGinn思想的直接先驱
- Thomas Nagel, *Mortal Questions* (1979) — 包含蝙蝠论文等经典
- Daniel Dennett, *Consciousness Explained* (1991) — 取消主义立场（McGinn的论敌）
- John Searle, *The Rediscovery of the Mind* (1992) — 生物自然主义
- Ned Block, Owen Flanagan & Güven Güzeldere (eds.), *The Nature of Consciousness* (1997) — 意识研究经典文集

## 三、当代研究 / Contemporary Studies

- Patricia Churchland, *Brain-Wise* (2002) — 取消主义对新神秘主义的批评
- Joseph Levine, *Purple Haze: The Puzzle of Consciousness* (2001) — 解释鸿沟
- Jaegwon Kim, *Physicalism, or Something Near Enough* (2005) — 对闭合问题的替代分析
- Uriah Kriegel (ed.), *The Oxford Handbook of Philosophy of Mind* (2009) — 含McGinn相关论文
- Katalin Balog, "Acquaintance and the Mind-Body Problem" (2012) — 亲知理论与意识
- Philip Goff, *Consciousness and Fundamental Reality* (2017) — 泛灵论对McGinn的回应
- Hedda Hassel Mørch, "Is Matter Conscious?" (2017) — 泛灵论与认知闭合

## 四、入门推荐 / Introductory

- *The Mysterious Flame* (1999) — 面向大众，最佳入门
- "Can We Solve the Mind-Body Problem?" (1989) — 核心论文，约30页
- "Colin McGinn" in *Internet Encyclopedia of Philosophy*
- "The Hard Problem of Consciousness" in *Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy*
- Flanagan, *Consciousness Reconsidered* ch. 1-3 — 最佳批评性导论

## 五、阅读路径建议

| 目标 | 推荐顺序 |
|------|----------|
| 快速了解 | *The Mysterious Flame* → "Can We Solve..." (1989) |
| 研究认知闭合 | "Can We Solve..." → *Problem of Consciousness* → *Problems in Philosophy* |
| 比较研究 | McGinn → Chalmers *Conscious Mind* → Dennett *Consciousness Explained* → Flanagan |
| 东方哲学对话 | McGinn → 龙树《中论》→ 庄子《齐物论》→ 《六祖坛经》 |
| AI意识问题 | McGinn → Chalmers "The Singularity" → Bostrom *Superintelligence* |
