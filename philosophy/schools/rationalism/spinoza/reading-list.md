# 斯宾诺莎进阶阅读 · Spinoza Reading List

## 原典 · Primary Sources

### 英文译本 / English Translations

- Spinoza, B. (1677). *The Collected Works of Spinoza*, 2 vols. Trans. Edwin Curley. Princeton University Press, 1985/2016. — 标准英文全集
- Spinoza, B. (1677). *Ethics*. Trans. Edwin Curley. Penguin Classics, 1996. — 最佳《伦理学》英译本
- Spinoza, B. (1677). *Complete Works*. Ed. Michael L. Morgan, trans. Samuel Shirley. Hackett, 2002. — 完整单卷本
- Spinoza, B. (1670). *Theological-Political Treatise*. Trans. Samuel Shirley. 2nd ed. Hackett, 2001.
- Spinoza, B. (1662). *Treatise on the Emendation of the Intellect*. In *Complete Works*.

### 中文译本 / Chinese Translations

- 斯宾诺莎著，贺麟译.《伦理学》. 商务印书馆, 1962. — 经典中译本
- 斯宾诺莎著，温锡增译.《神学政治论》. 商务印书馆, 1963.
- 斯宾诺莎著，王荫庭、洪汉鼎译.《知性改进论》. 商务印书馆, 1960.
- 斯宾诺莎著，洪汉鼎、孙祖寿译.《笛卡尔哲学原理》. 商务印书馆, 1980.
- 斯宾诺莎著，冯炳昆译.《简论上帝、人及其幸福》. 商务印书馆, 1987.

## 传记 · Biography

- Nadler, S. (2001). *Spinoza: A Life*. 2nd ed. Cambridge University Press, 2018. — 最佳英文传记
- Nadler, S. (2011). *A Book Forged in Hell: Spinoza's Scandalous Treatise and the Birth of the Secular Age*. Princeton University Press. — 关于《神学政治论》的历史叙事
- Goldstein, R. (2006). *Betraying Spinoza: The Renegade Jew Who Gave Us Modernity*. Nextbook/Schocken. — 面向普通读者
- Damasio, A. (2003). *Looking for Spinoza: Joy, Sorrow, and the Feeling Brain*. Harcourt. — 神经科学家的斯宾诺莎

## 二手研究 · Secondary Literature

### 综合研究 / Comprehensive Studies

- Garrett, D. (ed.) (1996). *The Cambridge Companion to Spinoza*. Cambridge University Press. — 最重要的论文集
- Della Rocca, M. (2008). *Spinoza*. Routledge. — 最佳入门级综合研究
- Nadler, S. (2006). *Spinoza's Ethics: An Introduction*. Cambridge University Press. — 《伦理学》导读
- Allison, H. (1987). *Benedict de Spinoza: An Introduction*. Yale University Press.

### 专题研究 / Topical Studies

- **形而上学 / Metaphysics**:
  - Melamed, Y. (2013). *Spinoza's Metaphysics: Substance and Thought*. Oxford University Press.
  - Curley, E. (1988). *Behind the Geometrical Method: A Reading of Spinoza's Ethics*. Princeton University Press.

- **心灵哲学 / Philosophy of Mind**:
  - Damasio, A. (2003). *Looking for Spinoza*. — 神经科学与斯宾诺莎
  - Lin, M. (2019). *Being and Reason: An Essay on Spinoza's Metaphysics*. Oxford University Press.

- **伦理学与情感 / Ethics and Affects**:
  - LeBuffe, M. (2010). *From Bondage to Freedom: Spinoza on Human Excellence*. Oxford University Press.
  - Kisner, M. (2011). *Spinoza on Human Freedom: Reason, Autonomy, and the Good Life*. Cambridge University Press.

- **政治哲学 / Political Philosophy**:
  - Balibar, É. (1998). *Spinoza and Politics*. Verso.
  - Gatens, M. & Lloyd, G. (1999). *Collective Imaginings: Spinoza, Past and Present*. Routledge.

- **德勒兹的斯宾诺莎 / Deleuze's Spinoza**:
  - Deleuze, G. (1968). *Spinoza and the Problem of Expression*. Trans. Martin Joughin. Zone Books, 1990.
  - Deleuze, G. (1981). *Spinoza: Practical Philosophy*. Trans. Robert Hurley. City Lights, 1988.

- [Spinoza at Internet Encyclopedia of Philosophy](https://iep.utm.edu/spinoza/) — 面向学生的入门条目

## 比较阅读 · Comparative Reading

- Descartes, R. *Meditations* — 斯宾诺莎的理性主义源头和批判对象
- Leibniz, G. W. *Monadology* — 斯宾诺莎一元论的多元论对照
- Maimonides. *Guide for the Perplexed* — 斯宾诺莎的犹太哲学背景
- Hegel, G. W. F. *Science of Logic* — 斯宾诺莎一元论的辩证法发展
- Nietzsche, F. *Thus Spoke Zarathustra* — 斯宾诺莎式的"命运之爱"
- Deleuze, G. *Difference and Repetition* — 德勒兹对斯宾诺莎的创造性发展

## 延伸阅读 · Extended Reading

- Smith, S. B. (2003). *Spinoza's Book of Life: Freedom and Redemption in the Ethics*. Yale University Press. — 文学性的斯宾诺莎解读
- Goethe, J. W. von. *Autobiography: Poetry and Truth* — 歌德谈斯宾诺莎的影响
- Einstein, A. (1954). *Ideas and Opinions* — 爱因斯坦谈斯宾诺莎的上帝
- Negri, A. (1991). *The Savage Anomaly: The Power of Spinoza's Metaphysics and Politics*. University of Minnesota Press.
- Massumi, B. (2002). *Parables for the Virtual: Movement, Affect, Sensation*. Duke University Press. — 斯宾诺莎式的情动理论
