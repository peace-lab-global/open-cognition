# 笛卡尔进阶阅读 · Descartes Reading List

## 原典 · Primary Sources

### 英文译本 / English Translations

- Descartes, R. (1641). *Meditations on First Philosophy*. Trans. John Cottingham. Cambridge University Press, 1996. — 最佳英文译本，附有反驳和答复
- Descartes, R. (1637). *Discourse on the Method*. Trans. John Cottingham, Robert Stoothoff, Dugald Murdoch. In *The Philosophical Writings of Descartes*, Vol. 1. Cambridge University Press, 1985.
- Descartes, R. (1644). *Principles of Philosophy*. Trans. V. R. Miller & R. P. Miller. Reidel, 1983.
- Descartes, R. (1649). *The Passions of the Soul*. Trans. Stephen Voss. Hackett, 1989.
- Descartes, R. *The Philosophical Writings of Descartes*, 3 vols. Trans. Cottingham, Stoothoff, Murdoch (and Kenny for Vol. 3). Cambridge University Press, 1984–1991. — 标准英文全集

### 中文译本 / Chinese Translations

- 笛卡尔著，庞景仁译.《第一哲学沉思集》. 商务印书馆, 1986. — 经典中译本
- 笛卡尔著，王太庆译.《谈谈方法》. 商务印书馆, 2000.
- 笛卡尔著，关文运译.《哲学原理》. 商务印书馆, 1958.
- 笛卡尔著，袁向东译.《探求真理的指导原则》. 商务印书馆, 1991. — 《指导心灵的规则》中译本

## 传记 · Biography

- Gaukroger, S. (1995). *Descartes: An Intellectual Biography*. Oxford University Press. — 最佳学术传记
- Rodis-Lewis, G. (1998). *Descartes: His Life and Thought*. Cornell University Press. — 法文传记英译本
- Clarke, D. (2006). *Descartes: A Biography*. Cambridge University Press. — 最新的学术传记
- Grayling, A. C. (2005). *Descartes: The Life and Times of a Genius*. Free Press. — 面向普通读者

## 二手研究 · Secondary Literature

### 综合研究 / Comprehensive Studies

- Cottingham, J. (ed.) (1992). *The Cambridge Companion to Descartes*. Cambridge University Press. — 最重要的论文集
- Williams, B. (1978). *Descartes: The Project of Pure Enquiry*. Routledge. — 经典研究
- Kenny, A. (1968). *Descartes: A Study of His Philosophy*. Random House. — 入门级研究
- Garber, D. (1992). *Descartes' Metaphysical Physics*. University of Chicago Press. — 物理学研究
- Hatfield, G. (2003). *Routledge Philosophy Guidebook to Descartes and the Meditations*. Routledge.

### 专题研究 / Topical Studies

- **认识论 / Epistemology**:
  - Van Cleve, J. (1979). "Foundationalism, Epistemic Principles, and the Cartesian Circle." *Philosophical Review*, 88(2).
  - Markie, P. (1992). "The Cogito and Its Importance." In *Cambridge Companion to Descartes*.

- **形而上学 / Metaphysics**:
  - Rozemond, M. (1998). *Descartes's Dualism*. Harvard University Press.
  - Baker, G. & Morris, K. (1996). *Descartes' Dualism*. Routledge.

- **心灵哲学 / Philosophy of Mind**:
  - Rorty, A. (ed.) (1986). *Essays on Descartes' Meditations*. University of California Press.
  - Wilson, M. (1978). *Descartes*. Routledge & Kegan Paul.

- **数学与科学 / Mathematics and Science**:
  - Bos, H. J. M. (1981). "The Invention of Seventeenth-Century Analytic Geometry." In *Mathematics and Rational Mechanics*.

## 在线资源 · Online Resources

- [Descartes at Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy](https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/descartes/) — 最权威的在线哲学百科条目
- [Descartes at Internet Encyclopedia of Philosophy](https://iep.utm.edu/descarte/) — 面向学生的入门条目
- [Descartes' Meditations — full text](https://www.earlymoderntexts.com/authors/descartes) — Jonathan Bennett 的英文早期现代文本
- [笛卡尔研究学会 · Descartes Studies Society](http://www.descartesstudies.org/) — 国际学术组织

## 比较阅读 · Comparative Reading

- Spinoza, B. *Ethics* — 对笛卡尔二元论的一元论回应
- Leibniz, G. W. *Monadology* — 对笛卡尔二元论的多元论回应
- Kant, I. *Critique of Pure Reason* — 对笛卡尔主体性哲学的先验转化
- Hume, D. *A Treatise of Human Nature* — 对笛卡尔自我概念的经验主义批判
- Merleau-Ponty, M. *Phenomenology of Perception* — 以身体现象学消解笛卡尔二元论

## 延伸阅读 · Extended Reading

- Chalmers, D. (1996). *The Conscious Mind: In Search of a Fundamental Theory*. Oxford University Press. — "困难问题"与笛卡尔遗产
- Nagel, T. (1986). *The View from Nowhere*. Oxford University Press. — 主观性与客观性
- Rorty, R. (1979). *Philosophy and the Mirror of Nature*. Princeton University Press. — 对笛卡尔认识论传统的批判
- Taylor, C. (1989). *Sources of the Self: The Making of the Modern Identity*. Cambridge University Press. — 近代自我观念的形成
