# 皮尔士进阶阅读 · Peirce Reading List

## 一、原典 / Primary Sources

### 必读
- "How to Make Our Ideas Clear" (1878) — 实用主义准则
- "The Fixation of Belief" (1877) — 信念确立方法
- Harvard Lectures on Pragmatism (1903) — 实用主义完整阐述
- *The Essential Peirce* (2 vols, Houser & Kloesel, 1992–1998) — 最佳选集

### 重要
- "On a New List of Categories" (1867) — 范畴表
- "The Logic of Abduction" (1903) — 溯因推理
- "Evolutionary Love" (1893) — 宇宙论
- "What Pragmatism Is" (1905) — pragmaticism

### 深潜
- *Collected Papers* (8 vols, 1931–1958) — 全集
- *Writings: A Chronological Edition* (1982–) — 编年全集
- *Peirce on Signs* (Hoopes, 1991) — 符号学选集

## 二、经典研究 / Classic Studies

- Max Fisch, *Peirce, Semeiotic, and Pragmatism* (1986) — 经典论文集
- Murray Murphey, *The Development of Peirce's Philosophy* (1961) — 思想发展史
- Christopher Hookway, *Peirce* (1985) — 哲学导论
- Susan Haack, "The Legacy of Peirce" (多篇) — 逻辑与认识论
- Cornelis de Waal, *Peirce: A Guide for the Perplexed* (2013) — 最佳现代导论
- Nathan Houser & Christian Kloesel (eds.), *Studies in the Logic of Charles Sanders Peirce* (1997)
- James Liszka, *A General Introduction to the Semeiotic of Charles Sanders Peirce* (1996)

## 三、当代研究 / Contemporary Studies

- Vincent Colapietro, *Peirce's Approach to the Self* (1989) — 自我理论
- Albert Atkin, *Peirce* (2016, Routledge) — 当代导论
- Robert Burch, *A Peircean Reduction Thesis* (1991) — 三元不可还原性
- Cheryl Misak, *Truth and the End of Inquiry* (1991) — 真理与探究
- Sami Pihlström, *Pragmatist Metaphysics* (2011) — 实用主义形而上学
- François Latraverse, "Peirce and the Logic of Abduction" — 溯因逻辑

## 四、入门推荐 / Introductory

- Cornelis de Waal, *Peirce: A Guide for the Perplexed* (2013) — 最佳导论
- Albert Atkin, *Peirce* (2016) — Routledge 哲学家系列
- Robert Burch, "Charles Sanders Peirce" in *Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy*
- 赵毅衡《符号学：原理与推演》（南京大学出版社）— 中文符号学导论

## 五、符号学专题 / Semiotics

- Umberto Eco, *A Theory of Semiotics* (1976) — 受皮尔士影响
- Thomas Sebeok (ed.), *Encyclopedic Dictionary of Semiotics* (1986)
- Marcel Danesi, *Of Cigarettes, High Heels, and Other Beautiful Things* (2008)
- 赵毅衡《符号学》— 中文世界的系统介绍

## 六、比较研究 / Comparative Studies

- Vincent Potter, *Peirce's Philosophical Perspectives* (1997)
- 佛教量论与皮尔士符号三元论的比较研究（散见学术论文）

## 七、阅读路径建议

| 目标 | 推荐顺序 |
|------|----------|
| 快速了解皮尔士 | de Waal *Guide* → "Fixation of Belief" → "How to Make Our Ideas Clear" |
| 研究实用主义 | Harvard Lectures → "What Pragmatism Is" → Murphey *Development* |
| 研究符号学 | *Peirce on Signs* → Liszka *Semeiotic* → Eco |
| 研究逻辑与科学方法 | "Logic of Abduction" → Hookway *Peirce* → Haack |
| 研究宇宙论 | "Evolutionary Love" → "Law of Mind" → Fisch |
