# 符号学 · Semiotics

> "This universe is perfused with signs, if it is not composed exclusively of signs."
>
> "这个宇宙充满了符号，如果不是完全由符号构成的话。"
>
> — C.S. Peirce, *Collected Papers* 5.448n

---

## 概述 / Overview

皮尔士的符号学（semiotics / semeiotic）是20世纪符号理论的两大独立源头之一（另一个是索绪尔的 semiology）。然而，与索绪尔的二元符号模型（能指-所指）不同，皮尔士建立了一个三元符号模型，其深刻性和适用范围远超语言学领域。皮尔士的符号学不仅涵盖语言符号，还涵盖图像、手势、自然现象、科学数据乃至思想本身——因为"一切思想都是通过符号进行的"。

Peirce's semiotics (or semeiotic) is one of the two independent sources of 20th-century sign theory (the other being Saussure's semiology). Unlike Saussure's dyadic sign model (signifier-signified), Peirce established a triadic model whose depth and scope far exceed linguistics. Peirce's semiotics encompasses not only linguistic signs but also images, gestures, natural phenomena, scientific data, and thought itself—because "all thought is conducted through signs."

---

## 符号三元论 / The Triadic Sign

皮尔士的符号由三个不可还原的要素构成：

Peirce's sign consists of three irreducible elements:

| 要素 / Element | 英文术语 / English Term | 说明 / Description |
|---|---|---|
| 符号本身 / The Sign Itself | Representamen / Sign Vehicle | 呈现给心灵的某物；符号的载体 / Something presented to the mind; the sign's vehicle |
| 对象 / Object | Object | 符号所指涉的东西 / That which the sign refers to |
| 解释项 / Interpretant | Interpretant | 符号在心灵中产生的效果或意义；本身又是一个新符号 / The effect or meaning produced in the mind; itself a new sign |

关键在于：这三项之间的关系是**不可还原的三元关系**。任何两项之间的关系都不能脱离第三项而独立存在。例如，符号与对象的关系必须通过解释项来中介；解释项与对象的关系必须通过符号来中介。这种不可还原性使皮尔士的符号学从根本上区别于索绪尔的二元论。

The crucial point: the relation among these three elements is an **irreducibly triadic relation**. The relation between any two elements cannot exist independently of the third. For example, the sign-object relation must be mediated by the interpretant; the interpretant-object relation must be mediated by the sign. This irreducibility fundamentally distinguishes Peirce's semiotics from Saussure's dyadic model.

### 解释项的层次 / Levels of Interpretant

皮尔士进一步区分了三种解释项：

Peirce further distinguished three types of interpretant:

1. **直接解释项**（Immediate Interpretant）：符号本身蕴含的潜在意义——如果理解条件充分，它应该产生的意义。/ The potential meaning inherent in the sign itself—the meaning it should produce under adequate conditions of understanding.
2. **动态解释项**（Dynamic Interpretant）：符号在实际接收者那里产生的实际效果——可能因接收者的背景、语境等因素而变化。/ The actual effect produced in an actual receiver—which may vary based on the receiver's background, context, etc.
3. **最终解释项**（Final Interpretant）：理想条件下、经过充分探究后，符号应当产生的最终意义——类似于皮尔士对"真理"的定义。/ The ultimate meaning the sign should produce under ideal conditions after thorough inquiry—parallel to Peirce's definition of "truth."

## 符号的三种基本类型 / Three Basic Types of Signs

皮尔士根据符号与对象之间的关系方式，将符号区分为三种基本类型：

Based on how the sign relates to its object, Peirce distinguished three basic sign types:

### 像似符 / Icon

像似符通过**相似性**（resemblance）指称其对象。像似符与对象共享某种品质或结构特征。

An icon refers to its object through **resemblance**. Icons share some quality or structural feature with their objects.

- 地图是地形的像似符（共享空间结构）/ A map is an icon of terrain (sharing spatial structure)
- 肖像是被画者的像似符（共享视觉特征）/ A portrait is an icon of the sitter (sharing visual features)
- 方程式是物理关系的像似符（共享数学结构）/ An equation is an icon of physical relations (sharing mathematical structure)

### 指示符 / Index

指示符通过**因果联系**或**时空邻接**（causal connection or spatiotemporal contiguity）指称其对象。指示符与对象之间存在实在的、物理的或存在的联系。

An index refers to its object through **causal connection** or **spatiotemporal contiguity**. There is a real, physical, or existential connection between the index and its object.

- 烟是火的指示符（因果关系）/ Smoke is an index of fire (causal relation)
- 温度计是水银柱高度的指示符（物理反应）/ A thermometer is an index of temperature (physical reaction)
- 路标指向目的地的指示符（空间指向）/ A signpost pointing to a destination (spatial indication)

### 象征符 / Symbol

象征符通过**约定**或**习惯**（convention or habit）指称其对象。象征符与对象之间没有自然的相似性或因果联系——意义完全由社会约定或规则决定。

A symbol refers to its object through **convention** or **habit**. There is no natural resemblance or causal connection between a symbol and its object—meaning is entirely determined by social convention or rule.

- 语言中的词汇是象征符（约定俗成）/ Words in language are symbols (conventional)
- 交通灯颜色是象征符（社会规则）/ Traffic light colors are symbols (social rules)
- 数学符号（如 "+" 号）是象征符（约定）/ Mathematical symbols (like "+") are symbols (conventional)

### 混合类型 / Mixed Types

在实践中，大多数符号是混合类型的。一张照片既是像似符（相似于被拍摄者），又是指示符（光线通过镜头在被摄时刻留下的物理痕迹）。一个人名既是象征符（约定），又在具体使用中成为指示符（指向特定个体）。

In practice, most signs are mixed types. A photograph is both an icon (resembling the subject) and an index (a physical trace left by light passing through the lens at the moment of capture). A personal name is both a symbol (conventional) and, in concrete use, an index (pointing to a specific individual).

## 更精细的符号分类 / Finer Sign Classifications

皮尔士的符号分类远比 icon/index/symbol 三分法更为复杂和精密。他的完整分类体系包含三个独立的三分维度，共产生 10 种基本符号类型（3 x 3 x 3 = 27 种理论可能，但由于逻辑约束，实际有效组合为 10 种）：

Peirce's sign classification is far more complex and refined than the icon/index/symbol trichotomy. His complete system comprises three independent trichotomous dimensions, yielding 10 basic sign types (3 x 3 x 3 = 27 theoretical possibilities, but logical constraints reduce actual valid combinations to 10):

| 维度 / Dimension | 第一项 / First | 第二项 / Second | 第三项 / Third |
|---|---|---|---|
| 符号本身 / Sign Itself | 质符 (Qualisign) | 单符 (Sinsign) | 型符 (Legisign) |
| 符号-对象关系 / Sign-Object | 像似符 (Icon) | 指示符 (Index) | 象征符 (Symbol) |
| 符号-解释项关系 / Sign-Interpretant | 呈符 (Rheme) | 申符 (Dicisign) | 论符 (Argument) |

- **质符/单符/型符**：符号本身分别是品质、个别存在物、一般法则。/ Qualisign/Sinsign/Legisign: the sign itself is a quality, an individual existent, or a general law.
- **呈符/申符/论符**：解释项将符号理解为可能性的呈现、事实的断言、或推理的论证。/ Rheme/Dicisign/Argument: the interpretant treats the sign as presentation of possibility, assertion of fact, or argument of reasoning.

## 无限衍义 / Infinite Semiosis

皮尔士符号学中最深刻的洞见之一是**无限衍义**（infinite semiosis / unlimited semiosis）：每一个解释项本身又成为一个新的符号，产生新的解释项，如此以至无穷。

One of the most profound insights in Peirce's semiotics is **infinite semiosis**: every interpretant itself becomes a new sign, generating further interpretants, *ad infinitum*.

这一洞见基于严密的逻辑论证：
1. 符号产生解释项。
2. 解释项本身是一个符号（因为它是"在某人心灵中代表某物的某物"）。
3. 因此，解释项也必然产生自己的解释项。
4. 这个过程没有逻辑终点。

This insight rests on rigorous logical reasoning:
1. A sign produces an interpretant.
2. The interpretant is itself a sign (it is "something that represents something to someone").
3. Therefore, the interpretant must produce its own interpretant.
4. This process has no logical terminus.

无限衍义的哲学意义深远：

The philosophical implications of infinite semiosis are profound:

- **意义永远不在场**：意义不是一个固定的实体，而是符号链中不断流动的效应。/ **Meaning is never fully present**: meaning is not a fixed entity but an effect flowing continuously through the chain of signs.
- **诠释的开放性**：任何文本、任何符号都可以被进一步诠释——不存在"最终的"解读。/ **Openness of interpretation**: any text, any sign can be further interpreted—there is no "final" reading.
- **与解构主义的共鸣**：德里达（Derrida）的"延异"（différance）概念与无限衍义有深层联系，尽管德里达的路径有所不同。/ **Resonance with deconstruction**: Derrida's concept of *différance* is deeply connected to infinite semiosis, though their paths diverge.

然而，皮尔士并不认为无限衍义意味着意义的彻底不确定。他引入了**最终解释项**（final interpretant）的概念——在理想的探究条件下，符号过程会趋近一个稳定的意义极限。这个极限类似于皮尔士对"真理"的定义——它是探究的理想终点。

Yet Peirce did not hold that infinite semiosis implies total indeterminacy of meaning. He introduced the concept of the **final interpretant**—under ideal conditions of inquiry, semiosis would converge on a stable limit of meaning. This limit parallels Peirce's definition of "truth"—the ideal endpoint of inquiry.

## 皮尔士 vs 索绪尔 / Peirce vs Saussure

| 维度 / Dimension | 皮尔士 / Peirce | 索绪尔 / Saussure |
|---|---|---|
| 符号结构 / Sign Structure | 三元（符号-对象-解释项）/ Triadic (sign-object-interpretant) | 二元（能指/所指）/ Dyadic (signifier/signified) |
| 对象的地位 / Status of Object | 独立的实在项 / Independent real element | 被排除在符号学之外 / Excluded from semiology |
| 符号类型 / Sign Types | Icon/Index/Symbol 及更精细分类 / Icon/Index/Symbol with finer classifications | 以语言符号为中心 / Centered on linguistic signs |
| 无限衍义 / Infinite Semiosis | 核心论题 / Core thesis | 无明确对应概念 / No explicit counterpart |
| 应用范围 / Scope | 一切符号过程（自然+文化）/ All semiosis (natural + cultural) | 主要面向语言系统 / Primarily linguistic systems |
| 理论传统 / Theoretical Tradition | 逻辑学传统 / Logic tradition | 语言学传统 / Linguistics tradition |

## 当代影响 / Contemporary Influence

- **生物符号学**（Biosemiotics）：托马斯·西比奥克（Thomas Sebeok）将皮尔士符号学扩展到生物界——细胞信号、动物交流都是符号过程。/ Thomas Sebeok extended Peircean semiotics to the biological realm—cell signaling and animal communication are semiosic processes.
- **认知科学**：皮尔士的三元模型为理解感知、表征和推理的关系提供了比二元模型更丰富的框架。/ Peirce's triadic model provides a richer framework than dyadic models for understanding the relations among perception, representation, and reasoning.
- **数字媒体研究**：UI 设计、信息架构、人机交互都可以用 icon/index/symbol 的框架来分析。/ UI design, information architecture, and human-computer interaction can all be analyzed through the icon/index/symbol framework.
- **知识图谱**：RDF 三元组（主体-谓词-客体）与皮尔士的符号三元论在结构上同构。/ RDF triples (subject-predicate-object) are structurally isomorphic with Peirce's sign triad.
