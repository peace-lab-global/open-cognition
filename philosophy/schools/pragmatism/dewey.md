# 约翰·杜威 · John Dewey

## 基本信息 / Biographical Note

约翰·杜威（1859–1952），美国哲学家、心理学家与教育改革家，实用主义三大代表之一（与皮尔士、詹姆斯并称）。佛蒙特大学毕业后在约翰·霍普金斯大学获哲学博士，历任密歇根大学、芝加哥大学与哥伦比亚大学。他创办了芝加哥大学实验学校，对20世纪美国教育改革产生了深远影响。1919–1921年曾访问中国，对中国现代教育思想影响显著。

John Dewey (1859–1952) was an American philosopher, psychologist, and educational reformer, one of the three pillars of classical pragmatism alongside Peirce and James. After earning his PhD from Johns Hopkins, he held positions at Michigan, Chicago, and Columbia. He founded the Laboratory School at the University of Chicago and profoundly shaped 20th-century American education. He lectured in China (1919–1921), significantly influencing modern Chinese educational thought.

## 核心命题 / Core Theses

1. **经验自然主义**：经验不是主观的，而是有机体与环境交互作用的自然过程。
2. **工具主义**：思想和理论是解决问题的工具，其价值在于能否有效指导行动。
3. **探究理论**：逻辑就是探究的方法论；探究始于不确定情境，终于确定情境的重建。
4. **民主作为生活方式**：民主不仅是政治制度，更是一种共同经验的交流方式。
5. **艺术即经验**：审美经验是完整、统一、强化了的经验的典范形态。
6. **教育即生长**：教育不是为未来生活做准备，而是生活本身；生长即目的。

1. **Experiential Naturalism**: Experience is not subjective but the natural process of organism-environment interaction.
2. **Instrumentalism**: Ideas and theories are tools for problem-solving, valued by their effectiveness in guiding action.
3. **Theory of Inquiry**: Logic is the methodology of inquiry; inquiry begins in an indeterminate situation and ends in its resolution.
4. **Democracy as Way of Life**: Democracy is not merely a political system but a mode of shared experiential communication.
5. **Art as Experience**: Aesthetic experience is the paradigmatic form of complete, unified, intensified experience.
6. **Education as Growth**: Education is not preparation for life but life itself; growth is its own end.

## 思想脉络 / Intellectual Context

杜威的思想在黑格尔绝对唯心主义的早期影响下萌芽，后转向达尔文进化论和詹姆斯的实用主义。他试图在传统经验主义与理性主义之间开辟第三条道路。杜威深受美国民主传统和现代科学方法的影响，将"经验"概念从洛克式的被动感知改造为主动的探究过程。

Dewey's thought germinated under early Hegelian idealism, then pivoted toward Darwinian evolution and James's pragmatism. He sought a third path between classical empiricism and rationalism. Deeply influenced by American democratic traditions and modern scientific method, he transformed the concept of "experience" from Lockean passive reception into active inquiry.

## 重要概念 / Key Concepts

- **经验（Experience）**：有机体与环境的主动交互
- **探究（Inquiry）**：将不确定情境转化为确定情境的有控制的转化
- **习惯（Habit）**：主动的获得能力，非被动的重复
- **情境（Situation）**：经验的整体环境背景
- **连续性（Continuity）**：经验的生长原则
- **交流（Communication）**：共享经验的社会基础

## 思想坐标 / Position in Intellectual History

杜威是经典实用主义的集大成者，也是20世纪最重要的公共知识分子之一。他连接了皮尔士的科学方法论与詹姆斯的心理学洞见，将其发展为一套完整的社会哲学。他对分析哲学的兴起持保留态度，但其探究理论预示了库恩的科学革命理论。在中国，杜威通过胡适等人深刻影响了五四运动时期的思想启蒙。

Dewey is the culminating figure of classical pragmatism and one of the 20th century's most important public intellectuals. He connected Peirce's scientific methodology with James's psychological insights, developing them into a comprehensive social philosophy. Though cautious about analytic philosophy's rise, his theory of inquiry anticipated Kuhn's paradigm shifts. In China, Dewey profoundly influenced May Fourth intellectual awakening through Hu Shih and others.

## 常见误读 / Common Misunderstandings

1. ❌ "杜威的实用主义就是'有用即真理'" → ✅ 真理是探究过程的成果，非简单的功利计算
2. ❌ "杜威反对学科知识" → ✅ 他反对的是知识与经验的脱节，非知识本身
3. ❌ "民主教育就是放任自流" → ✅ 教师是学习环境的设计者和共同体的引导者
4. ❌ "杜威只关心教育" → ✅ 他构建了涵盖逻辑、美学、政治、伦理的完整哲学体系
