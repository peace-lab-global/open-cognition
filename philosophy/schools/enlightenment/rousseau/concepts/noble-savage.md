> "我认为在自然状态中，人类既不是好的也不是坏的……但如果我们仔细审视自然人的状态，我们就会发现他天生是善良的。"
> "I do not think that man in the state of nature is either good or bad... but if we examine carefully the state of natural man, we will find him naturally good."
> — 卢梭对波兰国王的答复 / Reply to the King of Poland

# 高贵的野蛮人 · Noble Savage (Le Bon Sauvage)

## 术语辨析 / Terminological Clarification

首先需要澄清一个广泛流传的误解：**卢梭从未使用过"高贵的野蛮人"（bon sauvage / noble savage）这一术语。** 这一表达最早出现在约翰·德莱顿（John Dryden）1672年的戏剧《格拉纳达的征服》中。然而，这一标签之所以被普遍关联于卢梭，是因为它确实捕捉了卢梭关于自然状态中人类的核心洞见——尽管以简化和扭曲的方式。

A widely circulated misunderstanding requires clarification first: **Rousseau never used the term "noble savage" (bon sauvage / noble savage).** The expression first appeared in John Dryden's 1672 play *The Conquest of Granada*. Yet the label became universally associated with Rousseau because it does capture his core insight about humans in the state of nature — albeit in simplified and distorting ways.

卢梭更准确的表述是"自然人"（l'homme naturel）或"野蛮人"（sauvage，在他那里并非贬义词，而是"生活在自然状态中的人"）。他的核心论点不是原始人是"高贵的"，而是**自然状态中的人既不是好的也不是坏的——他们是前道德的（pré-moral），因为道德判断需要社会关系才能存在**。

Rousseau's more precise terms were "natural man" (l'homme naturel) or "savage" (sauvage, for him a non-pejorative term meaning "one living in the state of nature"). His core argument is not that primitive man is "noble" but that **humans in the state of nature are neither good nor bad — they are pre-moral, because moral judgment requires social relations to exist**.

## 自然状态的描绘 / Depiction of the State of Nature

### 方法论：假说的思想实验 / Methodology: A Hypothetical Thought Experiment

卢梭在《论人类不平等的起源》的序言中明确声明：他的自然状态描述不是历史事实，而是一个**假说性的思想实验**（des raisonnements hypothétiques et conditionnels），类似于物理学家关于世界起源的推理。他"剥去了"社会在人身上添加的一切人为属性，以发现人的原始面貌。

Rousseau explicitly stated in the *Second Discourse*'s preface that his state of nature description is not historical fact but a **hypothetical thought experiment** (des raisonnements hypothétiques et conditionnels), analogous to physicists' reasoning about the origin of the world. He "stripped away" everything social added to humans to discover their original appearance.

### 自然人的特征 / Characteristics of Natural Humans

卢梭描绘的自然人有以下特征：

Rousseau's natural humans have the following characteristics:

1. **孤独的（Solitaire）**：自然人是独居的，没有家庭、社会关系或语言。他们偶然相遇，交配后分离，母亲独自抚养幼崽直到其能独立生存。
2. **自足的（Autosuffisant）**：自然的欲望简单且容易满足——食物、休息、异性。"他的欲望不超出他的体力。"
3. **和平的（Pacifique）**：不同于霍布斯的"一切人对一切人的战争"，自然人回避冲突。他们强壮、敏捷，但面对危险的第一反应是逃跑而非战斗。
4. **强壮且健康的（Robuste et Sain）**：自然选择淘汰了体弱者，自然人在严酷的自然环境中变得强壮。他们没有复杂疾病，因为没有复杂的生活方式。
5. **前理性的（Pré-rationnel）**：自然人没有理性、反思、语言或抽象思维。他们生活在当下的感知中，没有对未来的忧虑或对过去的回忆。
6. **自由的（Libre）**：自然人拥有"自由能动性"（libre arbitre），不受本能程序的完全决定——这是人与动物的根本区别。

1. **Solitary**: Natural humans are loners, without family, social bonds, or language. They meet by chance, mate and separate; mothers raise offspring alone until independence.
2. **Self-sufficient**: Natural desires are simple and easily satisfied — food, rest, a mate. "His desires do not exceed his physical strength."
3. **Peaceful**: Unlike Hobbes's "war of all against all," natural humans avoid conflict. They are strong and agile, but their first response to danger is flight, not fight.
4. **Robust and healthy**: Natural selection eliminates the weak; natural humans become robust in harsh environments. They lack complex diseases because they lack complex lifestyles.
5. **Pre-rational**: Natural humans lack reason, reflection, language, or abstract thought. They live in present sensation, without anxiety about the future or memory of the past.
6. **Free**: Natural humans possess "free agency" (libre arbitre), not fully determined by instinctual programming — the fundamental difference between humans and animals.

## 两种自然原则 / Two Natural Principles

卢梭认为自然人受两个先于理性的原则驱动：

Rousseau held that natural humans are driven by two pre-rational principles:

### 自爱（Amour de Soi）

自爱是所有生物共有的自我保存本能。在自然人身上，它是温和的、有限的，只关注基本需求的满足。一旦需求满足，自然人就处于宁静的自足状态。自爱不涉及与他人的比较——它完全是自我指涉的。

Self-love is the self-preservation instinct shared by all living beings. In natural humans, it is gentle, limited, focused only on satisfying basic needs. Once needs are met, natural humans exist in tranquil self-sufficiency. Self-love involves no comparison with others — it is entirely self-referential.

### 怜悯（Pitié）

怜悯是人类与某些动物共有的自然情感——看到同类受苦时的本能厌恶。卢梭认为怜悯先于理性和反思，是一切社会美德的自然基础。"是怜悯把自然人引导到对弱者、病者和受苦者的帮助中去。"怜悯缓和了自爱，使自然人在不损害自身的前提下避免伤害他人。

Pity is a natural sentiment shared with some animals — instinctive revulsion at seeing one's kind suffer. Rousseau held that pity precedes reason and reflection, the natural foundation of all social virtues. "It is pity that carries natural man to the aid of the weak, the sick, and the suffering." Pity tempers self-love, leading natural humans to avoid harming others when doing so costs them nothing.

## 从自然到社会的堕落 / The Fall from Nature to Society

### 完善能力（Perfectibilité）

自然人拥有动物没有的独特能力：**完善能力**（perfectibilité）——在社会环境刺激下发展新的能力和需求的可能性。这是人类历史的引擎，也是堕落的根源。"这种独特的、几乎无限的能力，正是人类一切不幸的根源。"

Natural humans possess a unique capacity absent in animals: **perfectibility** (perfectibilité) — the capacity to develop new abilities and needs under social stimuli. This is the engine of human history and the source of the fall. "This distinctive and almost unlimited faculty is the source of all human misfortunes."

### 社会形成的阶段 / Stages of Social Formation

卢梭描述了人类从自然状态到公民社会的逐步堕落：

Rousseau described humanity's gradual fall from nature to civil society:

1. **最初的社会联系**：人口增长、气候变化迫使人们聚居，形成最初的家庭和部落。语言在这一阶段产生。
2. **冶金术与农业**：这两种技术催生了劳动分工——"一个人需要另一个人的帮助的时刻到来了。"
3. **私有财产**："第一个圈地并声称'这是我的'的人……就是公民社会的真正奠基者。"
4. **不平等的三个阶段**：
   - 富人与穷人（财产差异）
   - 强者与弱者（权力差异）
   - 主人与奴隶（人身支配）
5. **专制政府**：不平等达到极端，所有人都"等于零"——在专制者面前，所有人都同样无权。

1. **First social bonds**: Population growth and climate change forced people together, forming initial families and tribes. Language emerged at this stage.
2. **Metallurgy and agriculture**: These technologies spawned division of labor — "the moment one person needed the help of another arrived."
3. **Private property**: "The first person who, having enclosed a plot of land, said 'this is mine'... was the true founder of civil society."
4. **Three stages of inequality**:
   - Rich and poor (wealth difference)
   - Strong and weak (power difference)
   - Master and slave (personal domination)
5. **Despotism**: Inequality reaches its extreme; all become "equal to nothing" — before the despot, all are equally powerless.

## 与霍布斯的对比 / Contrast with Hobbes

| 维度 | 霍布斯的自然状态 | 卢梭的自然状态 |
|------|------------------|----------------|
| 人性 | 竞争、猜疑、荣耀 | 自足、和平、孤独 |
| 社会性 | 天然社会性（渴望支配他人） | 天然非社会性（偶然相遇） |
| 冲突 | 一切人对一切人的战争 | 避免冲突，恐惧而非攻击 |
| 战争根源 | 人性固有的攻击性 | 社会制度的产物 |
| 自然权利 | 对一切事物的权利 | 自我保存和怜悯 |
| 自然法 | 理性的诫命 | 前理性的自然情感 |

| Dimension | Hobbes's State of Nature | Rousseau's State of Nature |
|-----------|--------------------------|----------------------------|
| Human nature | Competitive, suspicious, glory-seeking | Self-sufficient, peaceful, solitary |
| Sociability | Naturally social (desires domination) | Naturally asocial (chance encounters) |
| Conflict | War of all against all | Conflict-avoidant; fear, not aggression |
| Source of war | Innate human aggressiveness | Product of social institutions |
| Natural right | Right to everything | Self-preservation and pity |
| Natural law | Rational precepts | Pre-rational natural sentiments |

## 对"高贵的野蛮人"标签的批评 / Critique of the "Noble Savage" Label

将卢梭的自然状态理论简化为"高贵的野蛮人"有几个问题：

Reducing Rousseau's state of nature theory to the "noble savage" is problematic for several reasons:

1. **忽视了自然人的前道德性质**：卢梭的自然人不是"好的"（那需要道德选择），而是前道德的——他们不具备善恶的概念。
2. **忽视了卢梭自己的方法论声明**：他明确表示自然状态是思想实验，不是历史描述。
3. **掩盖了完善能力的双重性**：完善能力既是堕落的根源，也是人类尊严和道德能力的基础。
4. **忽视了卢梭的建设性方案**：卢梭不是要回归自然状态，而是要在社会条件下重建自由。

1. **Ignores pre-moral nature**: Rousseau's natural man is not "good" (which requires moral choice) but pre-moral — incapable of concepts of good and evil.
2. **Ignores methodological disclaimers**: He explicitly stated the state of nature is a thought experiment, not historical description.
3. **Obscures perfectibility's duality**: Perfectibility is both the source of the fall and the foundation of human dignity and moral capacity.
4. **Ignores the constructive program**: Rousseau aimed not to return to the state of nature but to rebuild freedom within social conditions.

## 当代意义 / Contemporary Significance

卢梭的自然状态思想实验对当代仍有深刻影响：

Rousseau's state of nature thought experiment remains deeply relevant:

- **进化心理学**：关于人类攻击性是天生的还是文化的争论直接回应了霍布斯与卢梭的对立。
- **环境伦理**：自然人与自然的和谐共处成为生态批判文明掠夺的哲学资源。
- **发展心理学**：儿童是否"天然善良"的问题呼应了卢梭对自然善良的论证。
- **政治哲学**：罗尔斯的"原初状态"是卢梭自然状态思想实验的当代版本。

- **Evolutionary psychology**: Debates about whether human aggression is innate or cultural directly echo the Hobbes-Rousseau opposition.
- **Environmental ethics**: Natural humans' harmony with nature provides philosophical resources for ecological critiques of civilization's depredations.
- **Developmental psychology**: Whether children are "naturally good" echoes Rousseau's argument for natural goodness.
- **Political philosophy**: Rawls's "original position" is a contemporary version of Rousseau's state of nature thought experiment.
