# 实体论 · Substance (Ousia)

> "存在以多种方式被言说……但所有存在都指向一个本原和某种单一的实体。" — 《形而上学》IV.2
>
> "Being is said in many ways... but all being is related to one principle and some one substance." — *Metaphysics* IV.2

---

## 定义 Definition

实体（ousia, substance）是亚里士多德形而上学的核心概念，指最基本、最独立的存在。在亚里士多德的十范畴（Categories）体系中，实体是第一范畴——所有其他范畴（性质、数量、关系、位置等）都是"实体的属性"，依附于实体而存在。没有实体，就没有属性；但没有属性，实体仍然可以存在。

Substance (*ousia*) is the central concept of Aristotle's metaphysics, referring to the most basic, independent mode of being. In Aristotle's system of ten Categories, substance is the primary category—all other categories (quality, quantity, relation, place, etc.) are "attributes of substance," depending on substance for their existence. Without substance, there are no attributes; but without attributes, substance can still exist.

---

## 十范畴 The Ten Categories

亚里士多德在《范畴篇》中区分了存在的十种方式：

Aristotle distinguishes ten modes of being in the *Categories*:

| 范畴 Category | 希腊文 Greek | 例子 Example |
|-------------|-------------|-------------|
| **实体 Substance** | **ousia** | **这匹马 this horse** |
| 性质 Quality | poion | 白色的 white |
| 数量 Quantity | poson | 六尺高 six feet tall |
| 关系 Relation | pros ti | 是父亲 being a father |
| 位置 Place | pou | 在市集 in the agora |
| 时间 Time | pote | 昨天 yesterday |
| 姿态 Posture | keisthai | 坐着 sitting |
| 状态 State | echein | 穿着鞋 wearing shoes |
| 动作 Action | poiein | 切割 cutting |
| 被动 Affection | paschein | 被切割 being cut |

实体是"基底"（hypokeimenon）——所有属性的承载者，自身不是任何其他事物的属性。

Substance is the "underlying subject" (*hypokeimenon*)—the bearer of all attributes, itself not an attribute of anything else.

## 第一实体与第二实体 Primary and Secondary Substance

### 第一实体（Primary Substance, prōtē ousia）
具体的个别事物：这匹马（Bucephalus）、这个人（苏格拉底）、这棵树。第一实体是最基本的存在单位。

Concrete individual things: this horse (Bucephalus), this person (Socrates), this tree. Primary substance is the most basic unit of existence.

### 第二实体（Secondary Substance, deuterā ousia）
种（eidos）和属（genos）：如"人"（种）和"动物"（属）。第二实体是对第一实体的分类，不具有独立的存在。

Species (*eidos*) and genus (*genos*): e.g., "human" (species) and "animal" (genus). Secondary substances classify primary substances and have no independent existence.

## 形式与质料：实体分析的深化 Form and Matter: Deepening Substance Analysis

在《形而上学》第七卷中，亚里士多德进一步深化了实体分析，提出实体由两个原则构成：

In *Metaphysics* Book VII, Aristotle deepens the substance analysis, proposing that substance is constituted by two principles:

### 质料 (Hylē, Matter)
事物所由构成的材料。如雕像的大理石、马的血肉。质料本身不是实体——它是"潜在地"（dynamis）成为某物的材料。

The material from which a thing is constituted. E.g., marble of a statue, flesh of a horse. Matter is not itself substance—it is "potentially" (*dynamis*) something.

### 形式 (Eidos/Morphē, Form)
使质料成为"这一个"（tode ti）的本质结构。如雕像的形状、马的灵魂（psyche）。形式是"现实地"（energeia）使某物成为其所是的本质。

The essential structure that makes matter into "this particular thing" (*tode ti*). E.g., the statue's shape, the horse's soul (*psychē*). Form is what "actually" (*energeia*) makes something what it is.

### 质料与形式的结合：复合实体 (Synholon)
具体的个别事物是形式与质料的复合体。亚里士多德对"哪一个更根本——形式还是复合实体"的问题存在摇摆：有时他认为形式是"第一实体"（因为形式决定了事物是什么），有时他认为复合实体才是。

The concrete individual is a composite of form and matter. Aristotle oscillates on which is more fundamental—form or composite: sometimes form is "primary substance" (because form determines what a thing is), sometimes the composite is.

## 对柏拉图理念论的批评 Criticism of Plato's Theory of Forms

亚里士多德的实体论直接回应了柏拉图的理念论。核心批评分为几点：

Aristotle's substance theory directly responds to Plato's Theory of Forms:

1. **分离问题 The Separation Problem**：如果理念独立于具体事物而存在，那么"分有"（methexis）是什么意思？具体事物如何与分离的理念发生关系？亚里士多德认为，形式应当内在于事物，而非分离存在。

2. **第三人论证 The Third Man Argument**：如果具体事物因分有理念而具有某种性质，那么具体事物和理念一起构成的新群体需要另一个更高的理念来解释——导致无限回溯。

3. **无用性 Uselessness**：分离的理念对于解释可感世界的运动和变化是"无用的"——一个分离的"善的理念"如何帮助我们成为善的人？

1. **Separation Problem**: If Forms exist independently of concrete things, what does "participation" mean? Aristotle argues form should be immanent in things, not separate.
2. **Third Man Argument**: If concrete things share properties through participation in Forms, the group of concrete things + Form requires a higher Form—leading to infinite regress.
3. **Uselessness**: Separate Forms are "useless" for explaining motion and change in the sensible world—how does a separate "Form of Good" help us become good people?

## 东西方对话 East-West Dialogue

### 与唯识学"依他起性"与"圆成实性" Dialogue with Yogācāra

唯识学的"三性"理论可以为亚里士多德的实体论提供一个有趣的重读视角。亚里士多德的"第一实体"（具体的个别事物）可以被理解为"依他起性"（paratantra-svabhāva，因缘条件所生的存在），而"形式"（事物的本质）可以被理解为"圆成实性"（pariniṣpanna-svabhāva，真实的本质）。唯识学会追问：亚里士多德的"实体"是否仍然是一种"遍计所执"（parikalpita）——即我们的心识将连续的经验之流错误地切割为离散的"实体"？

Yogācāra's "three natures" theory offers an interesting rereading of Aristotle's substance. Aristotle's "primary substance" (concrete individuals) can be understood as "dependent nature" (*paratantra*), while "form" (essence) as "perfected nature" (*pariniṣpanna*). Yogācāra would ask: is Aristotle's "substance" still a form of "imagined nature" (*parikalpita*)—our mind erroneously cutting the continuous stream of experience into discrete "substances"?

### 与华严宗"事法界"与"理法界" Dialogue with Huayan

华严宗的"四法界"理论——事法界（现象世界）、理法界（原理世界）、理事无碍法界（事理交融）、事事无碍法界（现象间完全交融——为亚里士多德的实体论提供了一个东方补充。在亚里士多德看来，实体之间是分离的、有边界的（这匹马不是那匹马）；在华严宗看来，一切事物互相含摄，每一事相都包含一切事相（"一即一切"）。

Huayan's "four dharma-realms"—phenomenal realm, noumenal realm, non-obstruction of phenomena and noumena, non-obstruction among phenomena—offers an Eastern supplement. For Aristotle, substances are separate and bounded (this horse is not that horse); for Huayan, all things interpenetrate—each phenomenon contains all phenomena ("one is all").

## 当代应用 Contemporary Applications

- **本体论工程 Ontology Engineering**：计算机科学中的"本体论"（ontology）概念直接借用了亚里士多德的范畴体系——将现实世界建模为"实体"和"属性"的结构。
- **生物学哲学 Philosophy of Biology**：亚里士多德的实体-形式论为理解生物个体性（individuality）提供了替代还原论的方案。
- **人工智能 AI**：知识图谱（knowledge graph）中的"实体-关系"建模是亚里士多德范畴论的技术实现。

- **Ontology engineering**: CS "ontology" directly borrows Aristotle's categorical system—modeling reality as "entities" and "attributes."
- **Philosophy of biology**: Aristotelian substance-form theory offers an alternative to reductionism for understanding biological individuality.
- **AI**: "Entity-relation" modeling in knowledge graphs is a technical realization of Aristotelian categorization.
