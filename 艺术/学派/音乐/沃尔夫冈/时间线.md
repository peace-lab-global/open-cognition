# 莫扎特生平大事记 / Mozart Biographical Timeline

本大事记按时间顺序呈现莫扎特生涯的关键事件、创作里程碑与历史背景。This timeline presents key events, creative milestones, and historical context in Mozart's career in chronological order.

## 童年与巡演 / Childhood and Tours (1756–1773)

- **1756年1月27日**：生于萨尔茨堡，父亲列奥波德·莫扎特为萨尔茨堡大主教宫廷副乐长和作曲家，母亲安娜·玛丽亚。出生当日受洗，全名 Johannes Chrysostomus Wolfgangus Theophilus Mozart。/ **January 27, 1756**: Born in Salzburg to Leopold Mozart (Vice-Kapellmeister at the Archbishop's court) and Anna Maria. Baptized the same day as Johannes Chrysostomus Wolfgangus Theophilus Mozart.

- **1759–1761**：三岁开始接触键盘乐器，展示非凡的音乐天赋。五岁创作最早的小品（由其父记录）。/ **1759–1761**: Begins keyboard study at age three, showing extraordinary musical talent. Composes earliest pieces at age five (recorded by father).

- **1762**：六岁开始在慕尼黑和维也纳的宫廷巡演，展示钢琴与小提琴天赋。在维也纳美泉宫为玛丽亚·特蕾西亚女皇演奏。/ **1762**: Court tours in Munich and Vienna at age six, displaying piano and violin talent. Performs for Empress Maria Theresa at Schönbrunn Palace.

- **1763–1766**：家族欧洲大巡演——途经慕尼黑、奥格斯堡、巴黎（1763–64）、伦敦（1764–65）、荷兰（1765–66）。这是莫扎特音乐教育的关键阶段。/ **1763–1766**: Grand European family tour—Munich, Augsburg, Paris (1763–64), London (1764–65), Netherlands (1765–66). A crucial phase of Mozart's musical education.

- **1764**：在伦敦结识约翰·克里斯蒂安·巴赫（J.C. Bach），受其优雅键盘风格深刻影响。创作最早的交响曲（K. 16, K. 19）。/ **1764**: Meets Johann Christian Bach in London, deeply influenced by his elegant keyboard style. Composes earliest symphonies (K. 16, K. 19).

- **1768**：在维也纳创作第一部歌剧《装傻姑娘》（K. 51），但因歌手抵制未能上演。/ **1768**: Composes first opera *La finta semplice* (K. 51) in Vienna, but it is not performed due to singer resistance.

- **1769–1773**：三次意大利之旅（1769–71, 1771, 1772–73）。在博洛尼亚师从马蒂尼神父学习对位法。创作《米特里达特》（K. 87, 1770）和《卢乔·西拉》（K. 135, 1772）。被教宗授予金马刺骑士勋章。/ **1769–1773**: Three Italian journeys (1769–71, 1771, 1772–73). Studies counterpoint with Padre Martini in Bologna. Composes *Mitridate* (K. 87, 1770) and *Lucio Silla* (K. 135, 1772). Receives the Order of the Golden Spur from the Pope.

## 萨尔茨堡时期 / Salzburg Period (1773–1781)

- **1773**：定居萨尔茨堡，任大主教科洛雷多的宫廷乐师。创作多部交响曲和室内乐。/ **1773**: Settles in Salzburg as court musician under Archbishop Colloredo. Composes symphonies and chamber music.

- **1775**：创作五首小提琴协奏曲（K. 207–219）和早期钢琴协奏曲。/ **1775**: Composes five violin concertos (K. 207–219) and early piano concertos.

- **1777**：创作《"Jeunehomme"钢琴协奏曲》（K. 271），展示成熟的个人声音。辞去萨尔茨堡宫廷职位，与母亲赴巴黎求职。/ **1777**: Composes "Jeunehomme" Piano Concerto (K. 271), revealing mature personal voice. Resigns Salzburg court position, travels to Paris with mother seeking employment.

- **1778**：母亲安娜·玛丽亚病逝于巴黎（7月3日）。创作《"巴黎"交响曲》（K. 297）。返回萨尔茨堡，重新担任宫廷乐师。/ **1778**: Mother Anna Maria dies in Paris (July 3). Composes "Paris" Symphony (K. 297). Returns to Salzburg, resumes court position.

- **1779**：创作《加冕弥撒》（K. 317）。重新适应萨尔茨堡的宫廷生活。/ **1779**: Composes *Coronation Mass* (K. 317). Readjusts to Salzburg court life.

- **1781年1月**：《伊多梅纽斯》（K. 366）在慕尼黑首演，标志着成熟期歌剧风格的到来。/ **January 1781**: *Idomeneo* (K. 366) premiered in Munich, marking the arrival of mature operatic style.

- **1781年5月**：与大主教科洛雷多发生不可调和的冲突。被大主教的管家阿尔科伯爵"用脚踢了出去"。离开萨尔茨堡，定居维也纳。这是莫扎特生涯的转折点——他成为最早的自由作曲家之一。/ **May 1781**: Irreconcilable conflict with Archbishop Colloredo. Kicked out by the Archbishop's steward Count Arco. Leaves Salzburg, settles in Vienna. This is the turning point of Mozart's career—he becomes one of the earliest freelance composers.

## 维也纳高峰 / Vienna Zenith (1781–1787)

- **1782**：与康斯坦策·韦伯结婚（8月4日），遭父亲强烈反对。《后宫诱逃》（K. 384）首演成功。研究巴赫与亨德尔的作品（在范·斯维滕男爵的私人图书馆），开始吸收巴洛克对位法。/ **1782**: Marries Constanze Weber (August 4) against father's strong opposition. *Die Entführung aus dem Serail* (K. 384) successfully premiered. Studies Bach and Handel works (in Baron van Swieten's private library), begins absorbing Baroque counterpoint.

- **1783**：长子里安纳出生（仅存活两个月）。创作《大弥撒》（K. 427，未完成）。/ **1783**: First son Raimund Leopold born (survives only two months). Composes *Great Mass* (K. 427, unfinished).

- **1784**：加入共济会（"慈善"分会）。开始密集创作成熟期钢琴协奏曲——1784至1786年间写下十二首。次子卡尔·托马斯出生。/ **1784**: Joins the Freemasons ("Zur Wohltätigkeit" lodge). Begins intensive creation of mature piano concertos—twelve written between 1784 and 1786. Second son Karl Thomas born.

- **1785**：d小调钢琴协奏曲（K. 466）首演。父亲列奥波德来维也纳访问。完成"海顿"弦乐四重奏。/ **1785**: D-minor Piano Concerto (K. 466) premiered. Father Leopold visits Vienna. Completes "Haydn" String Quartets.

- **1786**：《费加罗的婚礼》（K. 492）在维也纳首演（5月1日）。布拉格演出获得巨大成功。创作c小调钢琴协奏曲（K. 491）。/ **1786**: *The Marriage of Figaro* (K. 492) premiered in Vienna (May 1). Enormous success in Prague. Composes C-minor Piano Concerto (K. 491).

- **1787**：访问布拉格。《唐·乔瓦尼》（K. 527）在布拉格首演（10月29日），获得极大成功。父亲列奥波德去世（5月28日）。被任命为约瑟夫二世的宫廷作曲家（年薪800弗罗林，远少于前任格鲁克的2000弗罗林）。/ **1787**: Visits Prague. *Don Giovanni* (K. 527) premiered in Prague (October 29) to great success. Father Leopold dies (May 28). Appointed court composer to Joseph II (annual salary 800 florins, far less than predecessor Gluck's 2000).

## 晚期 / Late Period (1788–1791)

- **1788**：经济状况开始恶化（奥土战争导致维也纳文化消费萎缩）。夏天在极短时间内完成最后三首交响曲——降E大调 K. 543（6月26日）、g小调 K. 550（7月25日）、C大调"朱庇特" K. 551（8月10日）。女儿特蕾西亚出生（仅存活六个月）。/ **1788**: Economic situation deteriorates (Austro-Turkish War shrinks Vienna's cultural economy). Completes final three symphonies in rapid succession—E-flat K. 543 (June 26), G-minor K. 550 (July 25), C-major "Jupiter" K. 551 (August 10). Daughter Theresia born (survives only six months).

- **1789**：赴柏林、莱比锡、德累斯顿旅行，试图寻找新的赞助。经济持续困难，频繁向朋友借贷。/ **1789**: Travels to Berlin, Leipzig, Dresden seeking new patronage. Continued economic difficulties, frequent borrowing from friends.

- **1790**：《女人心》（K. 588）在维也纳首演（1月26日）。约瑟夫二世去世，利奥波德二世继位。莫扎特赴法兰克福参加加冕典礼，但未获重要委托。/ **1790**: *Così fan tutte* (K. 588) premiered in Vienna (January 26). Joseph II dies, Leopold II succeeds. Mozart travels to Frankfurt for coronation but receives no major commissions.

- **1791**：最后的高产年。创作《狄托的仁慈》（K. 621）和《魔笛》（K. 620）。/ **1791**: Final year of extraordinary productivity. Composes *La clemenza di Tito* (K. 621) and *The Magic Flute* (K. 620).
  - **7月**：接受匿名委托创作《安魂曲》（后证实为瓦尔塞格伯爵）。/ **July**: Receives anonymous commission for *Requiem* (later identified as Count Walsegg).
  - **9月30日**：《魔笛》在维也纳维登剧院首演，莫扎特亲自指挥。/ **September 30**: *The Magic Flute* premiered at Theater auf der Wieden, Vienna, conducted by Mozart himself.
  - **10月**：创作《单簧管协奏曲》（K. 622）和《圣体颂》（K. 618）。/ **October**: Composes Clarinet Concerto (K. 622) and *Ave verum corpus* (K. 618).
  - **11月**：病倒，卧床不起。继续口授《安魂曲》给叙斯迈尔。/ **November**: Falls ill, confined to bed. Continues dictating *Requiem* to Süssmayr.
  - **12月5日**：逝于维也纳，年仅三十五岁。死因至今仍有争论（急性粟粒热、链球菌感染、肾衰竭等假说）。/ **December 5**: Dies in Vienna at age thirty-five. Cause of death still debated (acute miliary fever, streptococcal infection, renal failure among hypotheses).

## 身后 / Aftermath

- **1792**：叙斯迈尔完成《安魂曲》补写，交付瓦尔塞格伯爵。/ **1792**: Süssmayr completes the *Requiem*, delivered to Count Walsegg.

- **1793**：康斯坦策开始组织莫扎特作品的出版和纪念音乐会。/ **1793**: Constanze begins organizing publication of Mozart's works and memorial concerts.

- **1798**：莫扎特的声望开始显著回升，其作品在全欧洲被广泛演奏和出版。/ **1798**: Mozart's reputation begins significant recovery; his works are widely performed and published across Europe.

- **1829**：莫扎特遗孀康斯坦策与丹麦外交官格奥尔格·尼古拉斯·冯·尼森结婚，后者撰写了第一部重要的莫扎特传记（1828年出版）。/ **1829**: Mozart's widow Constanze marries Danish diplomat Georg Nikolaus von Nissen, who wrote the first important Mozart biography (published 1828).

- **1862**：路德维希·冯·科赫尔出版第一部莫扎特作品编年主题目录（Köchel-Verzeichnis），确立了 K. 编号系统。/ **1862**: Ludwig von Köchel publishes the first chronological-thematic catalogue of Mozart's works, establishing the K. numbering system.
