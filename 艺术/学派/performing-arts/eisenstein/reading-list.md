# 爱森斯坦阅读指南 · Eisenstein Annotated Reading Guide

> 本指南按照入门、进阶、专业三个层次编排，为不同背景的读者提供阅读路径建议。中文资源单独标注。
> This guide is organized in three tiers — Introductory, Intermediate, and Advanced — providing reading paths for readers of different backgrounds. Chinese-language resources are annotated separately.

## 入门 / Introductory

适合初次接触爱森斯坦的读者。建议先观看《战舰波将金号》（约70分钟），配合以下入门读物建立基本框架。
For readers encountering Eisenstein for the first time. Recommended: watch *Battleship Potemkin* (~70 min) alongside the following introductory readings to establish a basic framework.

### 影片 / Films

1. **《战舰波将金号》** (*Battleship Potemkin*, 1925) — 70分钟。爱森斯坦最著名的作品，"敖德萨阶梯"段落是理解蒙太奇的起点。建议配合有声版（原版配Edmund Meisel音乐）观看。
   70 min. Eisenstein's most famous work; "Odessa Steps" is the starting point for understanding montage. Recommend viewing with sound (original Edmund Meisel score).

2. **《亚历山大·涅夫斯基》** (*Alexander Nevsky*, 1938) — 112分钟。理解声画对位的最佳入口。"冰湖大战"段落将音乐与影像的对位关系展现得淋漓尽致。
   112 min. Best entry point for understanding audio-visual counterpoint. "Battle on the Ice" vividly demonstrates the contrapuntal relationship between music and image.

### 入门读物 / Introductory Readings

3. **Eisenstein, "Methods of Montage"** — 收录于 *Film Form*。约15页，系统介绍五种蒙太奇类型，是爱森斯坦理论最精炼的入口。
   ~15 pages, systematic introduction to five montage types; the most concise entry into Eisenstein's theory.

4. **Bordwell, David. *The Cinema of Eisenstein*, Chapter 1.** — 波德维尔为爱森斯坦研究设定的框架性概述，语言清晰，适合入门。
   Bordwell's framework-setting overview of Eisenstein studies; clear prose, suitable for beginners.

5. **Cook, Pam. "Eisenstein and Montage" in *The Cinema Book*.** — 简明扼要的教科书式介绍，适合快速了解核心概念。
   Concise textbook-style introduction, suitable for quick understanding of core concepts.

## 进阶 / Intermediate

适合已具备基本了解的读者。建议按以下顺序阅读，同时观看《十月》和《伊凡雷帝》。
For readers with basic understanding. Recommended reading sequence below, alongside viewing *October* and *Ivan the Terrible*.

### 影片 / Films

6. **《十月》** (*October*, 1927) — 理解理性蒙太奇的必读影片。注意宗教偶像并置段落和克伦斯基-拿破仑交叉剪辑段落。
   Essential viewing for understanding intellectual montage. Note the religious-idol juxtaposition and Kerensky-Napoleon cross-cutting sequences.

7. **《伊凡雷帝》第一、二部** (*Ivan the Terrible*, Parts I & II, 1942/1946) — 理解爱森斯坦晚期风格转变的关键。第二部的彩色段落展现了他对色彩心理学的深入研究。
   Key to understanding Eisenstein's late stylistic shift. Part II's color sequences demonstrate his deep research into color psychology.

### 理论原典 / Primary Theoretical Texts

8. **Eisenstein, *Film Form* (selected essays)** — 重点阅读："A Dialectic Approach to Film Form"（辩证蒙太奇的哲学论述）、"The Filmic Fourth Dimension"（理性电影的早期论述）、"The Structure of the Film"（波将金号的结构分析）。
   Key readings: "A Dialectic Approach to Film Form," "The Filmic Fourth Dimension," "The Structure of the Film."

9. **Eisenstein, *The Film Sense*** — 重点阅读声画关系论述和色彩心理学章节。理解爱森斯坦从无声电影到有声电影的转型。
   Key readings: audio-visual relationship essays and color psychology chapters. Understanding Eisenstein's transition from silent to sound cinema.

10. **Eisenstein, "Montage of Attractions" (1923)** — 吸引力概念的原始文本。理解爱森斯坦思想的戏剧起源。
    Original text of the attractions concept. Understanding the theatrical origins of Eisenstein's thought.

### 二手研究 / Secondary Literature

11. **Bordwell, David. *The Cinema of Eisenstein*. Harvard UP, 1993.** — 最系统、最全面的学术分析。波德维尔从认知主义角度重新审视爱森斯坦的形式创新，是当代爱森斯坦研究的基准著作。约400页，可按章节选择性阅读。
    The most systematic and comprehensive scholarly analysis. Bordwell reexamines Eisenstein's formal innovations from a cognitivist perspective; the benchmark work for contemporary Eisenstein studies. ~400 pages, can be read selectively by chapter.

12. **Nesbet, Anne. *Savage Junctures: Sergei Eisenstein and the Shape of Thinking*. I.B. Tauris, 2003.** — 从认知角度重新解读爱森斯坦的思想，将其与同时代的心理学和科学思潮联系。提供了不同于波德维尔形式主义路径的独特视角。
    Reinterprets Eisenstein's thought from a cognitive perspective, linking him to contemporaneous psychological and scientific currents. Offers a unique perspective distinct from Bordwell's formalist approach.

13. **Salazkina, Masha. *In Excess: Sergei Eisenstein's Mexico*. Duke UP, 2009.** — 研究墨西哥项目与跨文化蒙太奇的重要著作。揭示了爱森斯坦思想中被低估的维度。
    Important study of the Mexico project and cross-cultural montage. Reveals an underestimated dimension of Eisenstein's thought.

## 专业 / Advanced

适合进行深入研究的专业读者。建议系统阅读BFI六卷本英文选集，并关注以下专题领域。
For professional readers conducting in-depth research. Recommended: systematic reading of the BFI six-volume English edition, with attention to the following specialized areas.

### 完整理论遗产 / Complete Theoretical Legacy

14. **Eisenstein, *Selected Works* (6 vols.), ed. Richard Taylor et al. BFI, 1986–95.** — 目前最完整的学术版本。六卷分别覆盖：(1) Writings 1922-34, (2) Towards a Theory of Montage, (3) Beyond the Shot, (4) On the Detective Story, (5) Nonindifferent Nature, (6) 索引与附录。
    Most complete scholarly edition to date. Six volumes covering: (1) Writings 1922-34, (2) Towards a Theory of Montage, (3) Beyond the Shot, (4) On the Detective Story, (5) Nonindifferent Nature, (6) Index and appendices.

15. **Эйзенштейн, С.М. *Избранные произведения в 6 томах*. Москва: Искусство, 1964–71.** — 俄文六卷本全集。对于阅读原文的读者不可或缺。
    Russian six-volume collected works. Indispensable for readers accessing original texts.

### 专题研究 / Specialized Studies

16. **Deleuze, Gilles. *Cinema 1: The Movement-Image* & *Cinema 2: The Time-Image*.** — 德勒兹从"运动-影像"和"时间-影像"的哲学框架重新审视爱森斯坦，将其定位于"辩证影像"的范畴。理解爱森斯坦在电影哲学史中的位置。
    Deleuze reexamines Eisenstein from the philosophical frameworks of "movement-image" and "time-image," locating him within the category of "dialectical image." Understanding Eisenstein's position in the history of film philosophy.

17. **Bergan, Ronald. *Eisenstein: A Life in Conflict*. Headline, 1997.** — 最为详尽的英文传记，覆盖爱森斯坦的全部生平。
    Most detailed English-language biography, covering Eisenstein's entire life.

18. **Seton, Marie. *Sergei M. Eisenstein: A Biography*. Dover, 1978 [1952].** — 最早的权威传记，作者与爱森斯坦有过直接接触。
    Earliest authoritative biography; author had direct contact with Eisenstein.

19. **Bullock, David, et al. (eds.). *Eisenstein at 100: A Reconsideration*. Rutgers UP, 1999.** — 百年诞辰纪念论文集，多角度重新评价爱森斯坦的遗产。
    Centenary commemorative essays; multi-perspective reevaluation of Eisenstein's legacy.

20. **Gunning, Tom. "The Cinema of Attraction: Early Film, Its Spectator and the Avant-Garde." *Wide Angle* 8.3-4 (1986).** — 将爱森斯坦的"吸引力"概念应用于早期电影研究，开创了"吸引力电影"这一重要研究领域。
    Applies Eisenstein's "attractions" concept to early cinema studies, pioneering the important research field of "cinema of attractions."

## 中文资源 / Chinese Resources

### 原典译本 / Translated Primary Sources

- **《蒙太奇论》**，爱森斯坦著，富澜译，中国电影出版社，1998. — 最完整的中译理论选集。翻译质量较高，注释详尽。
  Most complete Chinese translation of theoretical writings. High translation quality with detailed annotations.

- **《爱森斯坦论文选集》**，尤列涅夫编注，中国电影出版社，1962. — 早期经典译本。部分译文已过时，但在中文学术史上具有重要地位。
  Early classic translation. Some translations outdated, but holds important status in Chinese scholarly history.

- **《电影形式》**，多个中文版本. — 基于Jay Leyda英文编译本的再译。选择译文较新的版本。
  Multiple Chinese editions based on Jay Leyda's English compilation. Select newer translations.

### 中文研究 / Chinese Scholarship

- **巴赞：《电影是什么？》**，崔君衍译，中国电影出版社，1987. — 包含对蒙太奇美学的重要批评。理解爱森斯坦-巴赞之争的必读文献。
  Contains important critiques of montage aesthetics. Essential reading for understanding the Eisenstein-Bazin debate.

- **波德维尔：《电影艺术：形式与风格》**，曾伟祯译. — 包含对爱森斯坦的详细分析，适合作为入门教科书。
  Contains detailed analysis of Eisenstein; suitable as an introductory textbook.

- **《伊凡雷帝》与《战舰波将金号》中文字幕版** — 配合阅读使用，建议选择标准版或修复版。
  Chinese-subtitled versions for use alongside reading; select standard or restored editions.

## 在线资源 / Online Resources

- **Internet Archive** — 部分爱森斯坦著作的全文可免费获取（*Film Form*, *The Film Sense* 的早期版本）。
  Full texts of some Eisenstein works freely available (early editions of *Film Form*, *The Film Sense*).

- **Kanopy / Criterion Channel** — 提供爱森斯坦影片的高清流媒体版本，附有学者评论音轨。
  HD streaming versions of Eisenstein's films with scholar commentary tracks.

- **Senses of Cinema** — 收录关于爱森斯坦的学术文章和影片分析。
  Scholarly articles and film analyses on Eisenstein.

## 推荐阅读序列 / Suggested Reading Sequences

**两周快速入门 / Two-week quick start:**
第1周：观看《波将金号》→ 阅读 "Methods of Montage" → Bordwell Chapter 1
第2周：观看《涅夫斯基》→ 阅读 *The Film Sense* 选章 → Cook "Eisenstein and Montage"
Week 1: Watch *Potemkin* → Read "Methods of Montage" → Bordwell Ch. 1
Week 2: Watch *Nevsky* → Read *Film Sense* selections → Cook "Eisenstein and Montage"

**一学期深潜 / One-semester deep dive:**
月1：《波将金号》+《罢工》→ *Film Form* 核心论文 → Bordwell 全书
月2：《十月》+《旧与新》→ 理性蒙太奇专题 → Nesbet
月3：《涅夫斯基》+《伊凡雷帝》→ *The Film Sense* → 声画对位专题
月4：未完成项目 → Salazkina → 晚期理论手稿 → Deleuze 相关章节
Month 1: *Potemkin* + *Strike* → *Film Form* core essays → Bordwell full book
Month 2: *October* + *General Line* → Intellectual montage topic → Nesbet
Month 3: *Nevsky* + *Ivan* → *Film Sense* → Counterpoint topic
Month 4: Unfinished projects → Salazkina → Late manuscripts → Deleuze relevant chapters
