# 梵高生平与创作大事年表 / Van Gogh Life and Creative Timeline

## 历史背景 / Historical Context

梵高生活的时代（1853-1890）正值欧洲经历深刻的社会和文化变革：第二次工业革命加速了城市化和工业化进程；印象派运动（1870年代起）彻底革新了西方绘画的技法和美学；日本在明治维新后向西方开放，浮世绘版画大量流入欧洲，引发了"日本主义"（Japonisme）潮流；尼采和叔本华的哲学正在重塑欧洲的思想版图。梵高的艺术——融合了荷兰传统、印象派创新、日本美学和个人精神体验——是这一多元时代的独特产物。

The era of Van Gogh's life (1853-1890) coincided with profound European social and cultural transformation: the Second Industrial Revolution accelerated urbanization and industrialization; the Impressionist movement (from the 1870s) revolutionized Western painting technique and aesthetics; Japan's opening to the West after the Meiji Restoration brought ukiyo-e prints flooding into Europe, sparking the "Japonisme" craze; Nietzsche's and Schopenhauer's philosophy was reshaping Europe's intellectual landscape. Van Gogh's art — fusing Dutch tradition, Impressionist innovation, Japanese aesthetics, and personal spiritual experience — was a unique product of this pluralistic era.

## 荷兰时期（1880—1886）/ Dutch Period (1880-1886)

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event | 意义 / Significance |
|------|------|------|
| 1880-82 | 在布鲁塞尔自学绘画 / Self-taught painting in Brussels | 临摹米勒作品；学习透视和解剖 / Copying Millet; learning perspective and anatomy |
| 1881-83 | 在海牙跟随安东·莫夫（Anton Mauve）学习 / Studied with Anton Mauve in The Hague | 学院训练的补充 / Supplement to academic training |
| 1882 | 首次创作石版画 / First lithographs | 版画实验的开始 / Beginning of printmaking experiments |
| 1883 | 在荷兰德伦特（Drenthe）地区写生 / Plein air painting in Drenthe region | 荒原和农民主题 / Heathland and peasant themes |
| 1883-85 | 在纽南（Nuenen）与父母同住 / Lived with parents in Nuenen | 大量农民和纺织工主题 / Extensive peasant and weaver themes |
| 1885 | 创作《吃土豆的人》/ Created *The Potato Eaters* | 荷兰时期的里程碑 / Dutch period milestone |
| 1885-86 | 在安特卫普美术学院短暂学习 / Brief study at Antwerp Academy | 接触鲁本斯的色彩 / Exposure to Rubens' color |

**历史语境 / Context**: 这一时期欧洲艺术的主流是印象派运动（莫奈、雷诺阿、德加等在1874年举办了第一次印象派展览）和自然主义文学（左拉的《卢贡-马卡尔家族》系列）。梵高对此几乎一无所知——他沉浸在荷兰黄金时代、米勒和现实主义版画中。 / The dominant European art of this period was the Impressionist movement (Monet, Renoir, Degas held their first exhibition in 1874) and Naturalist literature (Zola's *Rougon-Macquart* series). Van Gogh was largely unaware — immersed in the Dutch Golden Age, Millet, and realist prints.

## 巴黎时期（1886—1888）/ Paris Period (1886-1888)

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event | 意义 / Significance |
|------|------|------|
| 1886 | 赴巴黎与弟弟提奥同住 / Moved to Paris to live with brother Theo | 接触印象派和新印象派 / Exposure to Impressionism and Neo-Impressionism |
| 1886-87 | 大量创作自画像（约25幅）/ Prolific self-portraits (~25) | 风格实验——从暗到亮的转变 / Style experimentation — dark to bright transformation |
| 1887 | 收集和研究日本浮世绘版画 / Collected and studied Japanese ukiyo-e prints | 日本主义的深刻影响 / Profound Japonisme influence |
| 1887 | 结识贝尔纳、西涅克等前卫画家 / Met Émile Bernard, Paul Signac, and other avant-garde artists | 进入巴黎艺术圈 / Entered Paris art circles |
| 1887 | 在克利希大道的餐馆举办非正式画展 / Informal exhibition at Restaurant du Chalet, Boulevard de Clichy | 首次公开展示新风格 / First public showing of new style |
| 1888 | 2月离开巴黎前往阿尔勒 / Left Paris for Arles in February | 寻求更强烈的阳光和色彩 / Seeking stronger light and color |

**历史语境 / Context**: 1886年是巴黎艺术的关键年份——第八次也是最后一次印象派展览举办，修拉的《大碗岛星期天的下午》引发轰动，象征主义文学运动在巴黎兴起。梵高在这个充满实验精神的时刻来到了巴黎。 / 1886 was a pivotal year in Paris art — the eighth and final Impressionist exhibition, Seurat's *A Sunday Afternoon on the Island of La Grande Jatte* caused a sensation, the Symbolist literary movement emerged. Van Gogh arrived in Paris at this moment of experimental spirit.

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event | 意义 / Significance |
|------|------|------|

## 圣雷米与奥维尔（1889—1890）/ Saint-Rémy and Auvers (1889-1890)

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event | 意义 / Significance |
|------|------|------|
| 1889.6 | 创作《星夜》/ Created *The Starry Night* | 最著名的作品——旋涡状星空 / Most famous work — swirling starry sky |
| 1889 | 创作《鸢尾花》《丝柏》系列 / Created *Irises* and *Cypresses* series | 疗养院花园的色彩研究 / Asylum garden color studies |
| 1889 | 创作《橄榄树》系列（约18幅）/ Created *Olive Trees* series (~18 works) | 精神性风景画的高峰 / Peak of spiritual landscape painting |
| 1889-90 | 多次精神发作 / Multiple mental episodes | 创作与疾病的交替 / Alternation of creation and illness |
| 1890.1 | 评论家奥里耶在《法兰西信使》发表赞扬文章 / Critic Aurier published praise in *Mercure de France* | 生前罕见的重要认可 / Rare significant recognition during lifetime |
| 1890.2 | 布鲁塞尔"二十人展"展出梵高作品（6幅）/ Van Gogh works (6) exhibited at Les XX in Brussels | 前卫艺术圈中的认可 / Recognition in avant-garde circles |
| 1890.3 | 莫奈写信表达对梵高作品的赞赏 / Monet wrote expressing admiration for Van Gogh's work | 同行大师的认可 / Recognition from master peers |
| 1890.5 | 离开圣雷米前往奥维尔 / Left Saint-Rémy for Auvers-sur-Oise | 在加歇医生照料下继续创作 / Continued painting under Dr. Gachet's care |
| 1890.5-7 | 在奥维尔完成约70幅油画 / Completed ~70 oil paintings in Auvers | 惊人的晚期产量 / Astonishing late-period output |
| 1890.7 | 创作《加歇医生肖像》《奥维尔教堂》《麦田群鸦》/ Created *Dr. Gachet*, *Church at Auvers*, *Wheatfield with Crows* | 最后的杰作 / Final masterpieces |
| 1890.7.27 | 在麦田中开枪自杀 / Shot himself in a wheat field | 致命枪伤 / Fatal gunshot wound |
| 1890.7.29 | 在提奥怀中去世，享年37岁 / Died in Theo's arms, age 37 | "悲伤将永远存在" / "La tristesse durera toujours" |

| 1914 | 乔安娜出版《梵高书信集》第一版 / Johanna published first edition of *Letters* | 最重要的第一手文献面世 / Most important first-person document |
