# 贝多芬生平大事记 · Beethoven Biographical Timeline

本条目按年份记录贝多芬生平中的重要事件、创作里程碑和历史背景，以呈现其思想与创作的发展脉络。

This entry records significant events, creative milestones, and historical context in Beethoven's life year by year, revealing the developmental trajectory of his thought and creative output.

## 波恩时期 / Bonn Period (1770–1792)

- **1770** 12月17日受洗于波恩（实际出生日期不详，可能为12月16日）。父亲约翰·范·贝多芬为波恩宫廷男高音歌手，母亲玛丽亚·玛格达莱娜·凯维利希。 / Baptized December 17 in Bonn (actual birth date unknown, likely December 16). Father Johann van Beethoven, Bonn court tenor; mother Maria Magdalena Keverich.
- **1778** 八岁首次公开钢琴演出。父亲试图将他培养为"新莫扎特"，施加严苛的音乐训练。 / First public piano performance at age eight. Father attempted to mold him into a "new Mozart" with rigorous training.
- **1780** 开始跟随克里斯蒂安·戈特洛布·内夫学习，接触巴赫《平均律钢琴曲集》和启蒙思想。 / Began studies with Christian Gottlob Neefe, encountering Bach's *Well-Tempered Clavier* and Enlightenment thought.
- **1784** 被任命为波恩宫廷助理管风琴师（领取薪水），正式开启职业音乐生涯。 / Appointed assistant court organist in Bonn (with salary), formally launching professional career.
- **1787** 首次赴维也纳，据传短暂会见莫扎特（莫扎特预言："注意这个年轻人，他将来会让世界谈论他"）。母亲去世，被迫返回波恩。 / First trip to Vienna; reportedly briefly met Mozart (who allegedly predicted: "Watch this young man, he will make the world talk about him"). Mother died, forcing return to Bonn.
- **1789** 法国大革命爆发。贝多芬在波恩大学旁听，接触康德哲学。 / French Revolution erupts. Beethoven attends lectures at University of Bonn, encountering Kantian philosophy.
- **1792** 11月永久移居维也纳，跟随约瑟夫·海顿学习。波恩选帝侯马克斯·弗朗茨提供资助。 / November: permanently relocates to Vienna to study with Joseph Haydn. Elector Max Franz of Bonn provides funding.

## 维也纳早期 / Early Vienna Period (1793–1802)

- **1793** 跟随约翰·格奥尔格·阿尔布雷希茨贝格学习对位法；跟随安东尼奥·萨列里学习声乐作曲。 / Studies counterpoint with Johann Georg Albrechtsberger; vocal composition with Antonio Salieri.
- **1795** 首次在维也纳公开演出自己的作品（第二钢琴协奏曲，Op.19）。出版Op.1钢琴三重奏。 / First public Vienna performance of own works (Piano Concerto No.2, Op.19). Publishes Op.1 Piano Trios.
- **1798** 创作"悲怆"奏鸣曲（Op.13），展现突破性的情感深度。 / Composes "Pathétique" Sonata (Op.13), displaying breakthrough emotional depth.
- **1800** 第一交响曲（Op.21）在维也纳首演。4月2日举办首次个人音乐会。 / First Symphony (Op.21) premieres in Vienna. April 2: first benefit concert.
- **1801** 开始在信中向友人透露听力问题。创作"月光"奏鸣曲（Op.27 Nr.2）。芭蕾舞剧《普罗米修斯的创造物》（Op.43）。 / Begins revealing hearing problems in letters to friends. Composes "Moonlight" Sonata (Op.27 No.2). Ballet *The Creatures of Prometheus* (Op.43).
- **1802** 10月，在海利根施塔特写下著名的"遗嘱"——面对耳聋的绝望，但最终选择为艺术而活。这一危机成为创作转型的催化剂。 / October: writes the famous "Heiligenstadt Testament"—facing the despair of deafness, yet ultimately choosing to live for art. This crisis catalyzes a creative transformation.

## 英雄时期 / Heroic Period (1803–1814)

- **1803** 创作第三钢琴协奏曲（Op.37）。开始构思《第三交响曲》。 / Composes Piano Concerto No.3 (Op.37). Begins conceiving the Third Symphony.
- **1804** 完成《第三交响曲"英雄"》（Op.55）。原拟题献拿破仑，得知其称帝后愤怒撕去题献页。首演反响不一。 / Completes Third Symphony "Eroica" (Op.55). Originally intended dedication to Napoleon; angrily tears out dedication page upon learning of his imperial coronation. Mixed premiere reception.
- **1805** 歌剧《费德里奥》（原名《莱奥诺拉》）首演，因法军占领维也纳而票房惨淡。创作"热情"奏鸣曲（Op.57）。 / Opera *Fidelio* (originally *Leonore*) premieres; poor attendance due to French occupation of Vienna. Composes "Appassionata" Sonata (Op.57).
- **1806** 创作三首"拉祖莫夫斯基"弦乐四重奏（Op.59）——四重奏体裁的革命性突破。第四钢琴协奏曲（Op.58）。第四交响曲（Op.60）。小提琴协奏曲（Op.61）。 / Composes three "Razumovsky" String Quartets (Op.59)—revolutionary breakthrough in the quartet genre. Fourth Piano Concerto (Op.58). Fourth Symphony (Op.60). Violin Concerto (Op.61).
- **1808** 12月22日，在维也纳剧院举办马拉松式音乐会：同场首演第五交响曲（Op.67）和第六交响曲"田园"（Op.68），以及合唱幻想曲（Op.80）和第四钢琴协奏曲。 / December 22: marathon concert at Theater an der Wien—simultaneous premieres of Fifth Symphony (Op.67), Sixth Symphony "Pastoral" (Op.68), Choral Fantasy (Op.80), and Fourth Piano Concerto.
- **1810** 向特蕾泽·玛尔法蒂求婚，被拒。为歌德《埃格蒙特》创作配乐（Op.84）。创作"致爱丽丝"（WoO 59）。 / Proposes to Therese Malfatti; rejected. Composes incidental music for Goethe's *Egmont* (Op.84). Composes "Für Elise" (WoO 59).
- **1812** 7月，在特普利采与歌德会面（据传贝多芬拒绝向皇室行礼，而歌德恭敬让路）。写给"不朽的爱人"的神秘信件。完成第七（Op.92）和第八（Op.93）交响曲。 / July: meets Goethe at Teplitz (legend has it Beethoven refused to bow to royalty while Goethe respectfully stepped aside). Writes mysterious letters to the "Immortal Beloved." Completes Seventh (Op.92) and Eighth (Op.93) Symphonies.
- **1813–14** 在战争背景下创作"惠灵顿的胜利"等应景作品。歌剧《费德里奥》修订版大获成功。 / Composes occasional works like "Wellington's Victory" in wartime context. Revised *Fidelio* achieves great success.

## 过渡与危机 / Transition and Crisis (1815–1818)

- **1815** 弟弟卡斯帕·安东·卡尔去世。开始与弟媳约翰娜争夺侄子卡尔的监护权（诉讼持续至1820年）。 / Brother Kaspar Anton Karl dies. Begins custody battle with sister-in-law Johanna over nephew Karl (litigation continues until 1820).
- **1816** 创作第二十八钢琴奏鸣曲（Op.101），标志着晚期风格的开始。健康持续恶化。 / Composes Piano Sonata No.28 (Op.101), marking the beginning of the Late Style. Health continues deteriorating.
- **1818** 完全失聪。开始使用"对话本"与人交流。创作"锤子键琴"奏鸣曲（Op.106）——钢琴奏鸣曲的技术和思想极限。 / Complete deafness. Begins using "conversation books" for communication. Composes "Hammerklavier" Sonata (Op.106)—the technical and intellectual limit of piano sonata.

## 晚期 / Late Period (1819–1827)

- **1819** 开始创作《庄严弥撒》（Op.123），原为鲁道夫大公就任奥尔穆茨大主教典礼而作，但远超预定完成时间。获得侄子卡尔的完全监护权。 / Begins composing the *Missa Solemnis* (Op.123), originally for Archduke Rudolf's installation as Archbishop of Olomouc, but far exceeds planned completion date. Wins full custody of nephew Karl.
- **1820** 完成最后三首钢琴奏鸣曲的第一首（Op.109）。侄子卡尔尝试自杀（1826年），对贝多芬造成巨大打击。 / Completes first of final three piano sonatas (Op.109). Nephew Karl attempts suicide (1826), dealing a massive blow to Beethoven.
- **1822** 完成最后三首钢琴奏鸣曲（Op.110, 111）。完成《迪亚贝利变奏曲》（Op.120）。 / Completes final piano sonatas (Op.110, 111). Completes *Diabelli Variations* (Op.120).
- **1823** 完成《庄严弥撒》（Op.123）。开始全力投入《第九交响曲》。 / Completes *Missa Solemnis* (Op.123). Devotes full energy to the Ninth Symphony.
- **1824** 5月7日，《第九交响曲》和《庄严弥撒》选段在维也纳首演。贝多芬站在舞台上帮助打拍子（实际指挥为米夏埃尔·乌姆劳夫），终曲结束后背对观众，由女中音卡罗琳·恩格转向观众，让他"看到"掌声。 / May 7: premiere of Ninth Symphony and excerpts from *Missa Solemnis* in Vienna. Beethoven stands on stage beating time (actual conductor: Michael Umlauf); after the finale, he stands with back to audience until contralto Caroline Unger turns him to "see" the applause.
- **1825** 开始创作晚期弦乐四重奏：Op.127（3月首演）、Op.132（11月首演）、Op.130（1826年3月首演）。4月重病，病中创作Op.132第三乐章"感恩之歌"。 / Begins composing late string quartets: Op.127 (premiered March), Op.132 (premiered November), Op.130 (premiered March 1826). April: severe illness; composes Op.132 third movement "Thanksgiving Song" during convalescence.
- **1826** 完成Op.131（升c小调四重奏，贝多芬自认最完美的作品）、Op.133（大赋格）、Op.135（F大调四重奏，最后的四重奏）。12月与侄子卡尔在乡间旅行时患肺炎。 / Completes Op.131 (C-sharp minor quartet, Beethoven's own favorite), Op.133 (Grosse Fuge), Op.135 (F major quartet, the final quartet). December: contracts pneumonia while traveling with nephew Karl in the countryside.
- **1827** 3月26日，逝于维也纳。据说临终前暴风雨大作，他举起拳头向天空挥动。3月29日葬礼，约三万人参加送葬行列。遗体安葬于维也纳韦灵公墓（后迁至中央公墓）。 / March 26: dies in Vienna. Legend holds that a violent thunderstorm raged, and he raised his fist toward the sky. March 29: funeral, with approximately 30,000 people in the procession. Buried at Währing Cemetery, Vienna (later moved to the Zentralfriedhof).

## 历史背景 / Historical Context

贝多芬的一生横跨了欧洲历史上最动荡的时期之一：美国独立战争（1776）、法国大革命（1789）、拿破仑战争（1799–1815）、维也纳会议（1814–15）和神圣同盟时代的保守回潮。这些政治和社会剧变深刻影响了他的音乐和世界观——他对自由的信仰源于启蒙运动和法国大革命的理想，他对拿破仑的幻灭反映了对革命理想被背叛的愤怒，他晚期的内省则呼应了后拿破仑时代欧洲的精神危机。

Beethoven's life spanned one of the most turbulent periods in European history: the American Revolution (1776), the French Revolution (1789), the Napoleonic Wars (1799–1815), the Congress of Vienna (1814–15), and the conservative backlash of the Holy Alliance era. These political and social upheavals profoundly influenced his music and worldview—his belief in freedom stemmed from Enlightenment and French Revolutionary ideals; his disillusionment with Napoleon reflected anger at the betrayal of revolutionary ideals; his late inwardness echoed the spiritual crisis of post-Napoleonic Europe.
