# 莫扎特阅读指南 / Mozart Reading Guide

本阅读指南为不同层次的研究者提供系统性的阅读路径，从入门导引到专业研究。This reading guide provides systematic reading paths for researchers at different levels, from introductory orientation to specialized scholarship.

## 入门 / Introductory

适合初次系统了解莫扎特的读者。这些著作提供全面的概述，无需专业音乐分析背景。Suited for readers encountering Mozart systematically for the first time. These works provide comprehensive overviews without requiring specialized music analysis background.

### 传记与概述 / Biography and Overview

1. **Solomon, Maynard. *Mozart: A Life*. HarperCollins, 1995.**
   - 最具影响力的现代莫扎特传记，从心理分析角度解读莫扎特与父亲的关系。文笔优美，适合一般读者。/ The most influential modern Mozart biography, interpreting Mozart's relationship with his father from a psychoanalytic perspective. Beautifully written, suitable for general readers.
   - ⚠️ 部分心理分析的推断缺乏直接文献支持，需与更实证性的研究参照阅读。/ Some psychoanalytical inferences lack direct documentary support; read alongside more empirical studies.

2. **Einstein, Alfred. *Mozart: His Character, His Work*. Oxford UP, 1945.**
   - 经典的综合研究，将人格分析与作品分析融为一体。尽管年代较早，许多见解仍具启发性。/ Classic integrated study fusing personality analysis with work analysis. Though dated, many insights remain illuminating.

3. **克雷格·莱特（Craig Wright）.《聆听音乐》（*Listening to Music*）莫扎特章节.**
   - 耶鲁大学公开课教材，提供入门级音乐赏析框架。附有在线音频和视频资源。/ Yale open course textbook providing introductory music appreciation framework. Accompanied by online audio and video resources.
   - 中译本：北京大学出版社 / Chinese translation: Peking University Press.

4. **保罗·亨利·朗（Paul Henry Lang）.《西方文明中的音乐》（*Music in Western Civilization*）莫扎特章节.**
   - 将莫扎特置于西方文明史的宏大视野中，提供文化史语境。/ Places Mozart within the grand panorama of Western civilization history, providing cultural-historical context.

### 推荐聆听 / Recommended Listening

- 《费加罗的婚礼》（推荐：John Eliot Gardiner 指挥 / rec: Gardiner conducting）
- 第21钢琴协奏曲 K. 467（推荐：Murray Perahia 钢琴 / rec: Perahia, piano）
- 第40交响曲 K. 550（推荐：Karl Böhm 指挥维也纳爱乐 / rec: Böhm, Vienna Philharmonic）
- 《安魂曲》K. 626（推荐：Karl Böhm 指挥 / rec: Böhm conducting）

## 进阶 / Intermediate

适合已具备基本了解、希望深入分析莫扎特音乐语言和戏剧思维的读者。For readers with basic familiarity seeking deeper analysis of Mozart's musical language and dramatic thinking.

### 音乐分析 / Musical Analysis

5. **Rosen, Charles. *The Classical Style: Haydn, Mozart, Beethoven*. W.W. Norton, 1972/1997.**
   - 古典风格研究的范式性著作。莫扎特章节对奏鸣曲式、协奏曲和歌剧的分析至今仍是必读文献。/ Paradigmatic work of Classical style studies. The Mozart chapters on sonata form, concerto, and opera remain essential reading.
   - 中译本：杨燕迪译，《古典风格》，华东师范大学出版社 / Chinese translation: Yang Yandi trans., *The Classical Style*, ECNU Press.

6. **Rosen, Charles. *Sonata Forms*. W.W. Norton, 1980/1988.**
   - 对奏鸣曲式的深入专题研究，大量引用莫扎特作品作为例证。/ In-depth specialized study of sonata form, extensively citing Mozart's works as examples.

7. **Heartz, Daniel. *Mozart's Operas*. University of California Press, 1990.**
   - 歌剧分析的标杆著作。对达·蓬特三部曲的逐幕分析揭示了莫扎特音乐-戏剧思维的精密性。/ Benchmark work of opera analysis. Act-by-act analysis of the Da Ponte trilogy reveals the precision of Mozart's musical-dramatic thinking.

8. **Allanbrook, Wye Jamison. *Rhythmic Gesture in Mozart: Le nozze di Figaro and Don Giovanni*. University of Chicago Press, 1983.**
   - 从节奏姿态的角度分析莫扎特歌剧中的角色塑造，揭示了音乐节奏与社会身份之间的关联。/ Analyzes character portrayal in Mozart's operas from the perspective of rhythmic gesture, revealing connections between musical rhythm and social identity.

### 文化史与思想史 / Cultural and Intellectual History

9. **Till, Nicholas. *Mozart and the Enlightenment: Truth, Virtue and Beauty in Mozart's Operas*. Faber & Faber, 1992.**
   - 将莫扎特的歌剧创作置于启蒙运动思想背景中考察。/ Places Mozart's operatic creation within the intellectual context of the Enlightenment.

10. **Steptoe, Andrew. *The Mozart-Da Ponte Operas: The Cultural and Musical Background to Le nozze di Figaro, Don Giovanni, and Così fan tutte*. Oxford UP, 1988.**
    - 对达·蓬特合作的文化与音乐背景的全面考察。/ Comprehensive examination of the cultural and musical background of the Da Ponte collaboration.

### 推荐深入聆听 / Recommended Deep Listening

- "海顿"弦乐四重奏全集（推荐：Quartetto Italiano / rec: Quartetto Italiano）
- 晚期钢琴协奏曲 K. 466, 488, 491（推荐：Alfred Brendel / rec: Brendel）
- 弦乐五重奏 K. 515, 516（推荐：Grumiaux 等 / rec: Grumiaux et al.）
- 达·蓬特歌剧三部曲全剧（推荐：Harnoncourt 指挥 / rec: Harnoncourt conducting）

## 专业 / Advanced

适合音乐学专业研究者和深度分析者。需要乐谱阅读能力和专业分析工具。For musicology researchers and advanced analysts. Requires score-reading ability and professional analytical tools.

### 学术专著 / Scholarly Monographs

11. **Abert, Hermann. *W.A. Mozart*. Breitkopf & Härtel, 1919–23. 英译 Yale UP, 2007.**
    - 德语学界最全面的莫扎特研究，两卷本超过1500页。涵盖生平、作品与文化语境的所有方面。/ The most comprehensive Mozart study in German scholarship, two volumes exceeding 1500 pages. Covers all aspects of life, works, and cultural context.

12. **Konrad, Ulrich. *Mozart: Leben und Werk im Kontext*. 2005.**
    - 基于草稿和手稿研究的创作过程分析，揭示了莫扎特"自然性"背后的严格工作方式。/ Creative process analysis based on sketch and manuscript study, revealing the rigorous working methods behind Mozart's "naturalness."

13. **Sisman, Elaine. *Mozart: The "Jupiter" Symphony*. Cambridge UP, 1993.**
    - 对"朱庇特"交响曲的深入专题研究，包含历史语境、分析和接受史。/ In-depth specialized study of the "Jupiter" Symphony, including historical context, analysis, and reception history.

14. **Levin, Robert D. "Mozart's Concerto Forms." *The Cambridge Companion to the Concerto*. Cambridge UP, 2005.**
    - 对莫扎特协奏曲形式的前沿分析，包含即兴演奏实践的讨论。/ Cutting-edge analysis of Mozart's concerto forms, including discussion of improvisatory practice.

### 原典与文献 / Primary Sources and Documents

15. ***Neue Mozart-Ausgabe* (NMA). Bärenreiter, 1955–2007.**
    - 120卷权威学术版全集。研究莫扎特作品的第一手资料。数字化版本可通过 *Digital Mozart Edition* (DME) 在线获取。/ 120-volume authoritative scholarly complete edition. Primary source for Mozart research. Digital version available online via *Digital Mozart Edition* (DME).

16. **Mozart, W.A. *Briefe und Aufzeichnungen*. International Foundation Mozarteum, ed. 7 vols.**
    - 莫扎特书信集的权威版本。包含所有已存书信、日记条目和文件记录。/ Authoritative edition of Mozart's letters. Includes all surviving letters, diary entries, and documentary records.

17. **Köchel, Ludwig von. *Chronologisch-thematisches Verzeichnis sämtlicher Tonwerke W.A. Mozarts*. 6th ed. 1964.**
    - 莫扎特作品的标准编目系统（K. 编号）。第六版由 Giegling 等人修订，包含最新的年代考证。/ Standard catalogue system for Mozart's works (K. numbers). 6th edition revised by Giegling et al., incorporating latest chronological research.

### 分析方法论 / Analytical Methodology

18. **Schenker, Heinrich. *Free Composition* (*Der freie Satz*). Trans. Ernst Oster. Longman, 1979.**
    - 申克尔分析法的核心文献。虽然不是专门针对莫扎特，但其分析方法被广泛用于揭示莫扎特作品的深层结构。/ Core text of Schenkerian analysis. Though not Mozart-specific, its analytical methods are widely used to reveal deep structures in Mozart's works.

19. **Caplin, William. *Classical Form: A Theory of Formal Functions for the Instrumental Music of Haydn, Mozart, and Beethoven*. Oxford UP, 1998.**
    - 古典曲式理论的系统性著作，对莫扎特的乐句结构和段落类型进行了精细分类。/ Systematic work of Classical form theory, providing refined classification of Mozart's phrase structures and sectional types.

## 中文资源 / Chinese Resources

20. **所罗门著，韩斌等译.《莫扎特传》. 广西师范大学出版社.**
    - Solomon *Mozart: A Life* 的中译本。翻译质量较高，附有中译注释。/ Chinese translation of Solomon's *Mozart: A Life*. High translation quality with Chinese annotations.

21. **罗森著，杨燕迪译.《古典风格》. 华东师范大学出版社.**
    - Rosen *The Classical Style* 的中译本。杨燕迪教授的翻译附有大量导读和注释。/ Chinese translation of Rosen's *The Classical Style*. Professor Yang Yandi's translation includes extensive introduction and annotations.

22. **杨燕迪.《音乐人文通识译丛》相关卷册. 华东师范大学出版社.**
    - 包含多种西方音乐学经典的中文翻译，为中文读者提供了系统的音乐学导引。/ Includes Chinese translations of various Western musicology classics, providing systematic musicological guidance for Chinese readers.

23. **于润洋.《西方音乐通史》莫扎特章节. 上海音乐出版社.**
    - 中文语境中最系统的西方音乐史著作之一，莫扎特章节提供了清晰的历史定位。/ One of the most systematic Western music history works in Chinese context; Mozart chapter provides clear historical positioning.

## 在线资源 / Online Resources

- **Digital Mozart Edition (DME)**：https://dme.mozarteum.at — 莫扎特作品的数字化全集，包含乐谱、书信和文献。/ Digital complete edition of Mozart's works, including scores, letters, and documents.
- **Mozarteum Salzburg**：https://www.mozarteum.at — 国际莫扎特基金会，提供学术资源、音乐会信息和研究数据库。/ International Mozart Foundation providing academic resources, concert information, and research databases.
- **IMSLP Mozart 页面**：https://imslp.org — 提供莫扎特作品的免费公共领域乐谱下载。/ Free public domain score downloads of Mozart's works.
- **耶鲁公开课《聆听音乐》**：https://oyc.yale.edu/music — Craig Wright 教授的在线课程，含莫扎特专题讲解。/ Professor Craig Wright's online course with Mozart-specific lectures.

## 推荐阅读序列 / Suggested Reading Sequences

**路径一：传记→分析→文化史 / Path 1: Biography → Analysis → Cultural History**
1. Solomon, *Mozart: A Life* → 2. Rosen, *The Classical Style* → 3. Heartz, *Mozart's Operas* → 4. Till, *Mozart and the Enlightenment*

**路径二：作品→分析→文献 / Path 2: Works → Analysis → Documents**
1. 聆听晚期歌剧与协奏曲 / Listen to late operas and concertos → 2. Rosen, *Sonata Forms* → 3. Caplin, *Classical Form* → 4. NMA 选读 / Select readings from NMA

**路径三：快速入门 / Path 3: Quick Introduction**
1. Craig Wright《聆听音乐》莫扎特章 → 2. Einstein, *Mozart: His Character, His Work*（选读）→ 3. 聆听推荐录音 / Listen to recommended recordings
