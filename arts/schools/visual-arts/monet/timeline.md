# 莫奈生平大事记 · Monet Biographical Timeline

## 少年与启蒙 / Youth and Awakening (1840–1858)

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|---|---|
| **1840** | 11月14日生于巴黎。父亲为杂货商。Born November 14 in Paris. Father was a grocer. |
| **1845** | 随家迁往诺曼底的勒阿弗尔（Le Havre）。Moved with family to Le Havre, Normandy. |
| **1855** | 少年时代以漫画肖像在当地小有名气，每幅售价10-20法郎。Gained local fame as a caricaturist, earning 10-20 francs per portrait. |
| **1856** | 遇风景画家欧仁·布丹（Eugène Boudin），被带往户外写生。布丹的教导"当场画下的东西总有一种力量"成为莫奈终身信条。Met landscape painter Eugène Boudin, who introduced him to plein-air painting. Boudin's teaching became Monet's lifelong creed. |
| **1857** | 母亲去世。与布丹在勒阿弗尔附近的海岸写生。Mother died. Sketched coastal scenes near Le Havre with Boudin. |

## 巴黎学艺 / Paris Training (1859–1870)

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|---|---|
| **1859** | 赴巴黎，入瑞士学院（Académie Suisse），接触库尔贝与马奈的现实主义。Went to Paris, entered Académie Suisse, encountered realism of Courbet and Manet. |
| **1860** | 被征入伍，赴阿尔及利亚服役一年。因病提前返回。Conscripted into military, served one year in Algeria. Returned early due to illness. |
| **1862** | 入格莱尔（Charles Gleyre）画室学习，结识雷诺阿（Renoir）、西斯莱（Sisley）、巴齐耶（Bazille），形成日后印象派的核心圈。Entered Gleyre's studio, met Renoir, Sisley, and Bazille, forming the future Impressionist nucleus. |
| **1865** | 在枫丹白露森林户外写生，绘制《草地上的午餐》习作。Painted studies for *Le Déjeuner sur l'herbe* in the Forest of Fontainebleau. |
| **1866** | 创作《花园中的女人》——早期户外大作，尝试在大幅画面上呈现户外光效。Created *Women in the Garden* — early outdoor masterwork attempting to render outdoor light effects on large canvases. |
| **1867** | 长子让（Jean）出生。与卡米耶·东西欧（Camille Doncieux）同居。First son Jean born. Living with Camille Doncieux. |
| **1869** | 与雷诺阿在蛙塘（La Grenouillère）并肩写生，发展破碎笔触。Painted alongside Renoir at La Grenouillère, developing broken brushwork. |
| **1870** | 6月与卡米耶结婚。普法战争爆发后携家赴伦敦避难，研究透纳（Turner）与康斯太勃尔（Constable）的作品。Married Camille in June. Fled to London during Franco-Prussian War, studied Turner and Constable. |

## 印象派运动 / The Impressionist Movement (1871–1886)

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|---|---|
| **1871** | 定居阿让特伊（Argenteuil），与雷诺阿在塞纳河畔写生。Settled at Argenteuil, painted alongside Renoir on the banks of the Seine. |
| **1872** | 在勒阿弗尔港口绘制《日出·印象》。Painted *Impression, Sunrise* in the harbor of Le Havre. |
| **1873** | 结识经销商保罗·杜朗-鲁耶（Paul Durand-Ruel），此后成为其最重要的支持者。Met dealer Paul Durand-Ruel, who became his most important supporter. |
| **1874** | 首届印象派独立展——《日出·印象》遭评论家路易·勒鲁瓦讥讽为"印象"，印象派由此得名。First Impressionist exhibition — *Impression, Sunrise* mocked by critic Louis Leroy as an "impression," thus coining the term Impressionism. |
| **1875** | 创作《撑阳伞的女人》。经济状况不佳。Created *Woman with a Parasol*. Financial difficulties. |
| **1876** | 第二届印象派展。创作《穿日本和服的莫奈夫人》，体现日本主义影响。Second Impressionist exhibition. Created *La Japonaise*, reflecting Japonisme influence. |
| **1877** | 第三届印象派展。创作《圣拉扎尔车站》系列——现代工业主题。Third Impressionist exhibition. Created *Gare Saint-Lazare* series — modern industrial subject. |
| **1878** | 迁居韦特伊（Vétheuil）。次子米歇尔出生。经济极度困难。Moved to Vétheuil. Second son Michel born. Extreme financial hardship. |
| **1879** | 妻子卡米耶因肺结核去世，年仅32岁。莫奈绘制了她临终的肖像。Wife Camille died of tuberculosis, aged 32. Monet painted her deathbed portrait. |
| **1880** | 在韦特伊继续绘制冬季冰雪主题与塞纳河解冻主题。Continued painting winter ice and Seine thaw themes at Vétheuil. |
| **1881** | 与爱丽丝·欧希德（Alice Hoschedé）及其子女同住。Began living with Alice Hoschedé and her children. |
| **1883** | 迁居吉维尼（Giverny），开始营造花园。此后在此居住43年直至去世。Moved to Giverny, began creating the garden. Lived here for 43 years until death. |
| **1886** | 第八届（最后一届）印象派展。莫奈未参加，印象派运动正式终结。Eighth (final) Impressionist exhibition. Monet did not participate; Impressionist movement formally ended. |
| **1888–1889** | 赴地中海沿岸的昂蒂布（Antibes）写生，探索南方强烈光色。Traveled to Antibes on the Mediterranean coast, exploring intense southern light-color. |

## 系列绘画与巅峰 / Series Paintings and Zenith (1889–1914)

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|---|---|
| **1889** | 巴黎世博会期间与雕塑家罗丹联合举办回顾展。与爱丽丝正式结婚。Joint retrospective with sculptor Rodin during Paris World's Fair. Formally married Alice. |
| **1890** | 开始《干草堆》系列——首次系统性地在不同季节、不同时段对同一母题进行数十幅变奏。Begins *Haystacks* series — first systematic serial exploration of a single motif across seasons and times. |
| **1891** | 《干草堆》在杜朗-鲁耶画廊展出15幅，引起轰动。开始《白杨树》系列。15 *Haystacks* exhibited at Durand-Ruel gallery, causing a sensation. Begins *Poplars* series. |
| **1892** | 开始《鲁昂大教堂》系列——在鲁昂租住对面公寓，反复绘制教堂正面。Begins *Rouen Cathedral* series — rented an apartment opposite the cathedral, repeatedly painting its facade. |
| **1893** | 购买吉维尼房屋旁的土地，开始营造睡莲池。Purchased land adjacent to his Giverny house, began creating the water-lily pond. |
| **1894** | 《鲁昂大教堂》系列完成，共约30幅。*Rouen Cathedral* series completed, ~30 works total. |
| **1895** | 赴挪威写生，绘制冰雪主题。Traveled to Norway for painting, working on ice and snow themes. |
| **1897** | 开始《睡莲》系列——此后直至去世持续创作。Begins *Water Lilies* series — continues creating until death. |
| **1899** | 赴伦敦，开始《伦敦国会大厦》系列。Traveled to London, begins *London Parliament* series. |
| **1900** | 《伦敦国会大厦》系列持续创作。《鲁昂大教堂》系列在杜朗-鲁耶画廊展出。*London Parliament* series continues. *Rouen Cathedral* series exhibited at Durand-Ruel. |
| **1904** | 《伦敦国会大厦》系列在杜朗-鲁耶画廊展出，大获成功。*London Parliament* series exhibited at Durand-Ruel to great success. |
| **1908** | 赴威尼斯写生，创作《威尼斯》系列。Traveled to Venice, created *Venice* series. |
| **1909** | 《睡莲》48幅在杜朗-鲁耶画廊展出。48 *Water Lilies* exhibited at Durand-Ruel. |
| **1911** | 爱丽丝去世。莫奈陷入深切的悲痛。Alice died. Monet fell into deep grief. |
| **1912** | 被诊断出白内障，开始影响色觉。Diagnosed with cataracts, beginning to affect color perception. |
| **1914** | 开始为橘园美术馆创作《大睡莲》巨幅装饰面板——总长约90米的八幅巨制。长子让在第一次世界大战中服役。Begins creating the monumental *Grandes Décorations* for the Orangerie — eight panels totaling ~90 meters. Son Jean served in World War I. |

## 晚年与遗产 / Late Years and Legacy (1914–1926)

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|---|---|
| **1914–1918** | 第一次世界大战期间坚持在吉维尼创作《大睡莲》。好友克莱蒙梭（时任法国总理）常来探望并鼓励他继续创作。Continued creating *Grandes Décorations* at Giverny during World War I. Friend Clemenceau (then French Prime Minister) visited often and encouraged him to continue. |
| **1918** | 11月11日停战日，莫奈将两幅《睡莲》赠与法国政府作为"和平的象征"。On Armistice Day (November 11), Monet donated two *Water Lilies* to the French state as a "gesture of peace." |
| **1920** | 与法国政府达成协议，为橘园美术馆创作特制展览空间的《大睡莲》面板。Agreed with the French government to create *Grandes Décorations* panels for a specially designed exhibition space at the Orangerie. |
| **1921** | 克莱蒙梭出版《克劳德·莫奈：五十年友谊》，记录两人深厚的情谊。Clemenceau published *Claude Monet: Fifty Years of Friendship*, documenting their deep bond. |
| **1923** | 接受白内障手术。术后色觉发生变化（偏红黄），但继续创作。Underwent cataract surgery. Post-surgery color perception shifted (toward red-yellow), but continued painting. |
| **1925** | 完成《大睡莲》面板的最后调整。Completed final adjustments to the *Grandes Décorations* panels. |
| **1926** | 12月5日逝于吉维尼，享年86岁。葬于吉维尼教堂墓地。Died December 5 at Giverny, aged 86. Buried in the Giverny church cemetery. |
| **1927** | 5月17日，橘园美术馆《睡莲》椭圆厅正式对外开放——莫奈亲自参与了空间设计。May 17: Orangerie *Water Lilies* oval rooms officially opened — Monet had personally participated in the space design. |

## 身后影响 / Posthumous Influence

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|---|---|
| **1950s** | 纽约画派重新"发现"莫奈晚期作品，将其视为抽象表现主义的先驱。New York School "rediscovered" Monet's late works, regarding them as precursors of Abstract Expressionism. |
| **1960s** | 橘园美术馆翻新，莫奈的国际声誉再度攀升。Orangerie renovated; Monet's international reputation surged again. |
| **1980s** | 吉维尼花园经过大规模修复后重新开放，成为法国最受欢迎的旅游目的地之一。Giverny garden reopened after major restoration, becoming one of France's most popular tourist destinations. |
| **2010s–2020s** | "莫奈沉浸展"在全球主要城市巡回，成为艺术大众化的标志性事件。"Monet Immersive" exhibitions toured major global cities, becoming a landmark event in art democratization. |
