# 努力/欲力 · Conatus

## 概念概述 / Concept Overview

Conatus（拉丁语，意为"努力"、"倾向"、" striving"）是斯宾诺莎伦理学和心理学中最核心的概念。《伦理学》第三部分命题六断言："每个事物，就其在自身中而言，都努力保持其自身的存在"（Unaquaeque res, quantum in se est, in suo esse perseverare conatur）。这种"努力"不是一种有意识的意志行为，而是事物本质的内在倾向——它是事物存在的根本动力。

Conatus (Latin for "effort," "striving," "tendency") is the most central concept in Spinoza's ethics and psychology. *Ethics* Part III, Proposition 6 states: "Each thing, as far as it can by its own power, strives to persevere in its being" (Unaquaeque res, quantum in se est, in suo esse perseverare conatur). This "striving" is not a conscious act of will but the thing's essence's intrinsic tendency — the fundamental driving force of the thing's existence.

## Conatus 的三个层面 / Three Dimensions of Conatus

### 1. 形而上学层面 · Metaphysical Dimension

在本体论上，conatus 是一切事物保持自身存在的内在倾向。一块石头有保持其形态的倾向，一棵树有生长和繁殖的倾向，一个人有保持和增强其存在力量的倾向。Conatus 不是一个附加属性，而是事物的**本质**本身——一个事物的 conatus 就是它是什么。

Ontologically, conatus is the intrinsic tendency of all things to preserve their existence. A stone tends to maintain its form, a tree tends to grow and reproduce, a person tends to preserve and enhance their power of existence. Conatus is not an added attribute but the thing's **essence** itself — a thing's conatus is what it is.

### 2. 心理学层面 · Psychological Dimension

当 conatus 涉及到心灵和身体时，斯宾诺莎称之为：

When conatus involves both mind and body, Spinoza calls it:

- **欲望（Cupiditas）**：当 conatus 与心灵相关时——即心灵保持和增强其思考和理解能力的努力。欲望是"人的本质本身"（ipsa hominis essentia）。
- **冲动（Appetitus）**：当 conatus 与身体相关时——即身体保持和增强其行动能力的努力。

- **Desire (Cupiditas)**: When conatus relates to the mind — the mind's striving to preserve and enhance its power of thinking and understanding. Desire is "the very essence of man" (ipsa hominis essentia).
- **Appetite (Appetitus)**: When conatus relates to the body — the body's striving to preserve and enhance its power of acting.

斯宾诺莎有一句极为重要的命题："我们不是因为判断某物是善的才去追求它，而是因为我们追求某物，所以称它为善的"（EIP39S）。这颠覆了传统的"善"概念——善不是客观的价值，而是与我们 conatus 的一致。

Spinoza has a crucial proposition: "We do not desire something because we judge it to be good; rather, we call it good because we desire it" (EIP39S). This overturns the traditional concept of "good" — goodness is not an objective value but a correspondence with our conatus.

### 3. 伦理学层面 · Ethical Dimension

Conatus 是斯宾诺莎伦理学的基础。善恶的标准不是来自上帝的命令或理性的法则，而是来自 conatus 的增强或减弱：

Conatus is the foundation of Spinoza's ethics. The standard of good and evil comes not from divine commandment or rational law but from conatus's enhancement or diminishment:

- **善（Bonum）**：增强我们行动能力和理解能力的东西
- **恶（Malum）**：减弱我们行动能力和理解能力的东西

- **Good (Bonum)**: What increases our power of acting and understanding
- **Evil (Malum)**: What decreases our power of acting and understanding

伦理学的目标不是压抑 conatus（如禁欲主义），而是通过理性引导 conatus，使其从被动的外部驱动转化为主动的内在驱动——从被"不充分观念"决定的被动情感，走向被"充分观念"决定的主动情感。

The goal of ethics is not to suppress conatus (as asceticism does) but to guide conatus through reason, transforming it from passive external determination to active internal determination — from passive affects determined by "inadequate ideas" to active affects determined by "adequate ideas."

## 与尼采"权力意志"的比较 / Comparison with Nietzsche's Will to Power

斯宾诺莎的 conatus 和尼采的"权力意志"（Wille zur Macht）之间存在显著的平行和差异：

Spinoza's conatus and Nietzsche's "will to power" (Wille zur Macht) share notable parallels and differences:

| 维度 | Conatus | 权力意志 |
|------|---------|----------|
| 核心主张 | 保持存在的努力 | 增强力量的意志 |
| 善恶标准 | 增强力量即善 | 增强生命即善 |
| 对待传统道德 | 批判传统善恶观念 | "重估一切价值" |
| 宇宙论 | 一元论的（实体内部的力量） | 多元论的（力量的竞争） |
| 理性角色 | 理性是获得自由的途径 | 理性可能是生命的否定 |

尼采本人对斯宾诺莎有很高的评价。1881年，他在给朋友的信中写道："我有一位先驱——斯宾诺莎……他在五个主要观点上与我一致。这个最孤独、最独立的思想家，在这些方面与我最接近。"

Nietzsche himself held Spinoza in high regard. In 1881, he wrote to a friend: "I have a precursor — Spinoza … he agrees with me on five main points. This most lonesome and most independent thinker is closest to me in these respects."

## 当代回响 / Contemporary Echoes

Conatus 概念在当代哲学和科学中以多种形式延续。在进化生物学中，"适者生存"的自然选择机制可以被视为 conatus 的生物学版本——有机体保持和复制自身的倾向。在心理学中，弗洛伊德的"生命驱力"（Eros/libido）和马斯洛的"自我实现"需求都可以追溯到斯宾诺莎的 conatus。在政治哲学中，斯宾诺莎将"权利"等同于"力量"（jus sive potestas）——一个事物的权利就是它实际拥有的力量——这一观点影响了从福柯的权力分析到当代现实主义政治哲学。

The conatus concept continues in contemporary philosophy and science in various forms. In evolutionary biology, natural selection's "survival of the fittest" is a biological version of conatus — organisms' tendency to preserve and replicate themselves. In psychology, Freud's "life drive" (Eros/libido) and Maslow's "self-actualization" trace back to Spinozist conatus. In political philosophy, Spinoza's equation of "right" with "power" (jus sive potestas) influenced power analysis from Foucault to contemporary realist political philosophy.
