# 莱布尼茨著作详述 · Leibniz's Major Works

莱布尼茨是哲学史上最著名的"未出版者"之一——他的大量著作以手稿和通信的形式存在，生前出版的只是冰山一角。以下按时间顺序介绍其最重要的著作。

Leibniz is one of philosophy's most famous "non-publishers" — the bulk of his writings exist as manuscripts and correspondence; what was published in his lifetime is merely the tip of the iceberg. The following presents his most important works chronologically.

## 《论组合术》 · De Arte Combinatoria (1666)

莱布尼茨的博士论文，也是他最早的系统性哲学著作。全书试图创建一种"组合术"——一种通过基本概念的排列组合来生成一切复杂观念的方法。这一构想是莱布尼茨"普遍特征术"（characteristica universalis）的雏形——一种能够表达一切思想的标准化符号系统，使得哲学争论可以像数学计算一样被"计算"解决。

Leibniz's doctoral dissertation and earliest systematic philosophical work. It attempts to create an "art of combinations" — a method for generating all complex ideas through arrangement of basic concepts. This is the prototype of Leibniz's "universal characteristic" (characteristica universalis) — a standardized symbolic system capable of expressing all thought, enabling philosophical disputes to be "calculated" like mathematics.

## 《形而上学谈》 · Discours de métaphysique (1686)

莱布尼茨最系统的形而上学著作，但在他生前未出版（1846年才被发现并出版）。全书分为55节，涵盖了莱布尼茨成熟时期形而上学的核心主题：

Leibniz's most systematic metaphysical work, unpublished in his lifetime (discovered and published only in 1846). Divided into 55 sections, covering the core themes of his mature metaphysics:

- **§1–7**：上帝作为万物的创造者和前定和谐的安排者
- **§8–16**：个体概念（notion individuelle）——每个实体的完整概念包含其所有事件
- **§17–22**：身心关系和前定和谐
- **§23–37**：自由、偶然性和充足理由律
- **§38–55**：上帝与受造物的关系，最好世界的选择

- **§§1–7**: God as creator and arranger of pre-established harmony
- **§§8–16**: Individual concept — each substance's complete concept contains all its events
- **§§17–22**: Mind-body relations and pre-established harmony
- **§§23–37**: Freedom, contingency, and sufficient reason
- **§§38–55**: God-creature relations, choosing the best world

## 与阿尔诺的通信 · Correspondence with Arnauld (1686–1690)

莱布尼茨与法国詹森派神学家安托万·阿尔诺（Antoine Arnauld）之间的一系列深入哲学通信。阿尔诺对莱布尼茨的"完整概念"理论提出了尖锐的质疑：如果凯撒的个体概念包含了"渡过卢比孔河"这一谓词，那么凯撒是否自由地渡过了卢比孔河？莱布尼茨在答复中发展了他关于自由和必然性的精细区分——"inclined but not necessitated"（倾向于而非必然地决定）。

A series of deep philosophical exchanges between Leibniz and the French Jansenist theologian Antoine Arnauld. Arnauld sharply questioned Leibniz's "complete concept" theory: if Caesar's individual concept contains "crossing the Rubicon," did Caesar cross freely? Leibniz developed in reply his nuanced distinction between freedom and necessity — "inclined but not necessitated."

## 《新体系》 · Système nouveau de la nature (1695)

莱布尼茨首次在公开出版物中介绍其前定和谐体系的著作。以法文发表在《学者杂志》（Journal des sçavants）上。全书以简洁的形式阐述了单子论和前定和谐学说，使用了著名的"时钟比喻"来比较三种身心关系理论。

Leibniz's first public presentation of his pre-established harmony system. Published in French in the *Journal des sçavants*. It concisely presents monadology and pre-established harmony, using the famous "clock analogy" to compare three mind-body theories.

## 《神义论》 · Essais de Théodicée (1710)

莱布尼茨生前唯一出版的哲学专著，也是他最广为阅读的著作。"神义论"（theodicy）这一术语由莱布尼茨创造，源自希腊语 theos（上帝）和 dikē（正义），意为"为上帝的正义辩护"。全书分为三大部分：

Leibniz's only philosophy book published in his lifetime and his most widely read work. The term "theodicy" was coined by Leibniz from Greek theos (God) and dikē (justice), meaning "justifying God's justice." Divided into three main parts:

1. **信仰与理性的和谐**：论证信仰不会违背理性
2. **上帝的善与人的自由**：解决自由意志与神圣预知的矛盾
3. **恶的起源与最好世界**：论证恶在最好世界中的必要性

1. **Harmony of faith and reason**: Faith does not contradict reason
2. **God's goodness and human freedom**: Resolving free will vs. divine foreknowledge
3. **Origin of evil and the best world**: Evil's necessity in the best world

## 《单子论》 · Monadologie (1714)

莱布尼茨哲学的最终总结，也是西方哲学史上最精炼的形而上学文本之一。全书仅90条简短的论述，却涵盖了莱布尼茨整个哲学体系的核心——从单子的定义和特征，到前定和谐，到充足理由律，到上帝的存在和属性，到最好世界的选择。它最初是为友人雷蒙（Nicholas Remond）撰写的，以法文写成。

The final summation of Leibniz's philosophy and one of the most condensed metaphysical texts in Western philosophy. Only 90 brief sections, yet covering the entire core of Leibniz's system — from monad definitions, to pre-established harmony, sufficient reason, God's existence and attributes, to the best world's selection. Originally written in French for his friend Nicholas Remond.

## 《人类理智新论》 · Nouveaux essais sur l'entendement humain (1704, published 1765)

莱布尼茨对洛克《人类理解论》的系统回应，是他最长的哲学著作。以对话体写成，逐章逐节地回应洛克的观点。洛克否认天赋观念的存在，主张心灵是一块"白板"（tabula rasa）；莱布尼茨则论证心灵先天具有某些倾向和结构，经验只是"唤醒"了这些先天结构。

Leibniz's systematic response to Locke's *Essay Concerning Human Understanding*, his longest philosophical work. Written as a dialogue, responding chapter by chapter to Locke. Locke denied innate ideas, claiming the mind is a "tabula rasa"; Leibniz argues the mind innately has certain dispositions and structures that experience merely "awakens."

## 与克拉克的通信 · Correspondence with Clarke (1715–1716)

莱布尼茨去世前最后一年的重要哲学通信，对手是牛顿的代言人塞缪尔·克拉克（Samuel Clarke）。争论的焦点是空间和时间的本质：

Leibniz's last significant philosophical correspondence, against Newton's representative Samuel Clarke. The central dispute concerns the nature of space and time:

- **牛顿/克拉克**：空间和时间是绝对的、独立于事物存在的实体
- **莱布尼茨**：空间是事物之间共存的秩序（关系论），时间是事件之间接续的秩序

- **Newton/Clarke**: Space and time are absolute entities existing independently of things
- **Leibniz**: Space is the order of coexisting things (relationalism); time is the order of successive events
