# 柏拉图推荐阅读 · Plato Reading List

---

## 入门级 Beginner

| 书名 Title | 作者 Author | 说明 Notes |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|
| 《理想国》*Republic* | 柏拉图 Plato (Grube/Reeve trans., Hackett) | 柏拉图哲学的核心文本；重点读第六、七卷 / Core text; focus on Books VI-VII |
| *Plato: Complete Works* | Cooper ed., Hackett, 1997 | 英文标准全集 / Standard English complete works |
| 《柏拉图导论》*Plato: A Very Short Introduction* | Julia Annas, 2003 | 牛津通识读本 / Oxford VSI |
| 《柏拉图〈理想国〉导读》| 陈玮 | 适合中文读者的入门 / Chinese-language introduction |

## 进阶级 Intermediate

| 书名 Title | 作者 Author | 说明 Notes |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|
| 《会饮篇》*Symposium* | 柏拉图 Plato | 爱的阶梯理论 / Ladder of love theory |
| 《斐多篇》*Phaedo* | 柏拉图 Plato | 灵魂不朽论证 / Arguments for immortality |
| 《巴门尼德篇》*Parmenides* | 柏拉图 Plato | 理念论的自我批评 / Self-critique of Forms |
| *An Introduction to Plato's Republic* | Julia Annas, 1981 | 最经典的《理想国》导读 / Classic guide to the Republic |
| *Plato's Ethics* | Terence Irwin, 1995 | 柏拉图伦理学的系统分析 / Systematic analysis of Platonic ethics |
| *Plato's Cosmology* | F.M. Cornford, 1937 | 《蒂迈欧篇》经典注释 / Classic commentary on Timaeus |

## 研究级 Advanced

| 书名 Title | 作者 Author | 说明 Notes |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|
| *On Ideas: Aristotle's Criticism of Plato's Theory of Forms* | Gail Fine, 1993 | 亚里士多德对理念论的批评 / Aristotle's criticism of Forms |
| *Plato's Late Ontology* | Kenneth Sayre, 1983 | 晚期对话中的形而上学 / Metaphysics in late dialogues |
| *The Philosophy of Plato* | R.C. Cross & A.D. Woozley, 1964 | 经典系统研究 / Classic systematic study |
| *Plato on Knowledge and Reality* | Nicholas White, 1976 | 柏拉图认识论 / Platonic epistemology |
| *Studies in Plato's Metaphysics* | R.E. Allen ed., 1965 | 论文集 / Collected essays |
| "Plato on Why Mathematics is Good for the Soul" | Myles Burnyeat, 2000 | 数学与灵魂教育 / Mathematics and soul-education |
