## 二、重要论文 / Key Essays

- "What Is Existenz Philosophy?" (1946) — 存在哲学概述
- "The Concept of History" (1961) — 历史概念
- "Truth and Politics" (1967) — 真理与政治
- "Thinking and Moral Considerations" (1971) — 思维与道德
- "Some Questions of Moral Philosophy" (1965–1966 讲演)

## 三、书信集 / Correspondence

- *Hannah Arendt / Karl Jaspers Correspondence* (1992) — 与雅斯贝尔斯通信
- *Hannah Arendt / Martin Heidegger Correspondence* (2004) — 与海德格尔通信
- *Hannah Arendt / Mary McCarthy Correspondence* (1995) — 与麦卡锡通信
- *Within Four Walls: The Correspondence Between Hannah Arendt and Heinrich Blücher* (2000)
