# 女性主义批判 / Feminist Critique

> "除了少数例外，女性天生不适合伟大的成就……她们是'sexus sequior'，第二性。"
> "With few exceptions, women are by nature unsuited to great achievements… they are the 'sexus sequior,' the second sex."
> — Schopenhauer, *Über die Weiber* (1851)

---

## 概述 / Overview

叔本华是西方哲学传统中**最具争议性的性别观点**持有者之一。他的短文《论女人》（Über die Weiber, 1851）包含了一系列对女性的贬低性论断，使他成为女性主义哲学批评的重要对象。女性主义批评不仅涉及叔本华的性别偏见本身，还涉及这些偏见如何渗透到他的核心哲学体系中。

Schopenhauer is one of the holders of **the most controversial gender views** in the Western philosophical tradition. His essay *On Women* (Über die Weiber, 1851) contains a series of derogatory assertions about women, making him an important target of feminist philosophical criticism. Feminist criticism addresses not only Schopenhauer's gender prejudices themselves but also how these prejudices permeate his core philosophical system.

---

## 叔本华的性别观点 / Schopenhauer's Gender Views

### 《论女人》的核心论断 / Core Assertions of "On Women"

叔本华在《论女人》中提出了一系列对女性的贬低性论断：

Schopenhauer advances a series of derogatory assertions about women in *On Women*:

1. **理智低劣**：女性天生理智能力较低，不适合抽象思维和伟大的智识成就。
2. **审美低劣**：女性对美没有真正的感受力——她们对音乐、诗歌和艺术的"兴趣"只是为了吸引男性。
3. **道德低劣**：女性天生缺乏正义感和荣誉感——她们更倾向于欺骗和不公正。
4. **实用性**：女性的唯一自然功能是生育和养育——她们是"种族延续的工具"。
5. **"第二性"**：女性在一切方面都低于男性——她们是 *sexus sequior*（拉丁语：较弱/次等的性别）。

1. **Intellectual inferiority**: Women are innately lower in intellectual capacity, unsuited to abstract thinking and great intellectual achievements.
2. **Aesthetic inferiority**: Women have no genuine sensitivity to beauty — their "interest" in music, poetry, and art exists only to attract men.
3. **Moral inferiority**: Women are innately lacking in sense of justice and honour — they are more inclined to deception and injustice.
4. **Instrumentality**: Women's sole natural function is reproduction and nurturing — they are "instruments of species propagation."
5. **"Second sex"**: Women are inferior to men in all respects — they are the *sexus sequior* (Latin: the weaker/inferior sex).

### 与性欲形而上学的关系 / Relation to the Metaphysics of Sexual Love

叔本华的性别偏见与其性欲形而上学密切相关：他将女性主要定义为意志在生殖层面的客体化工具。在他的框架中，女性的存在意义被还原为生命意志的延续——通过诱惑男性来实现种族繁衍。

Schopenhauer's gender prejudices are closely linked to his metaphysics of sexual love: he defines women primarily as instruments of the Will's objectification at the reproductive level. In his framework, women's existential significance is reduced to the continuation of the will-to-live — achieving species propagation by seducing men.

## 女性主义哲学的批判 / Feminist Philosophical Critique

### 一、经验论断的错误 / Errors in Empirical Claims

女性主义哲学家首先指出，叔本华关于女性的许多"经验"论断在科学上站不住脚：

Feminist philosophers first note that many of Schopenhauer's "empirical" claims about women are scientifically untenable:

- **理智能力的性别差异**：当代认知科学未能发现支持"女性天生理智低劣"的证据。性别差异（如果存在）远小于个体差异。
- **审美能力的性别差异**：艺术史和心理学研究表明，审美能力的性别差异主要是社会化的结果，而非生物学给定的。
- **道德能力的性别差异**：卡罗尔·吉利根（Carol Gilligan）的工作表明，女性的道德推理不是"低劣的"，而是**不同类型的**——强调关系和关怀而非抽象规则。

- **Gender differences in intellectual capacity**: Contemporary cognitive science has found no evidence supporting "women's innate intellectual inferiority." Gender differences (if any) are far smaller than individual differences.
- **Gender differences in aesthetic capacity**: Art history and psychology research shows that gender differences in aesthetic capacity are primarily products of socialisation, not biologically given.
- **Gender differences in moral capacity**: Carol Gilligan's work shows that women's moral reasoning is not "inferior" but of a **different kind** — emphasising relationships and care rather than abstract rules.

### 二、体系性偏见 / Systemic Bias

更深层的批评指向叔本华哲学体系中的**结构性性别偏见**：

Deeper criticism targets the **structural gender bias** in Schopenhauer's philosophical system:

1. **意志-表象二元论的性别编码**：在叔本华体系中，"意志"（盲目的、非理性的力量）常被隐性地与女性气质关联，而"认识"（客观的、超越的能力）被隐性地与男性气质关联。天才——能够超越意志的人——被定义为男性。
2. **同情伦理的矛盾**：叔本华的同情伦理理论上应该扩展到所有人——包括女性。但他在实践中将女性排除在道德平等的范围之外。
3. **禁欲主义的性别排斥**：叔本华将禁欲主义的最高成就（贞洁、完全的内在弃绝）描述为主要是男性圣人的成就。

1. **Gender coding of Will-Representation dualism**: In Schopenhauer's system, "Will" (blind, irrational force) is often implicitly associated with femininity, while "cognition" (objective, transcendent capacity) is implicitly associated with masculinity. Genius — one who can transcend Will — is defined as male.
2. **Contradiction in compassion ethics**: Schopenhauer's compassion ethics should theoretically extend to all — including women. But he practically excludes women from the scope of moral equality.
3. **Gender exclusion in asceticism**: Schopenhauer describes asceticism's highest achievements (chastity, complete inner renunciation) primarily as male saints' achievements.

### 三、哲学中的厌女传统 / Misogyny in the Philosophical Tradition

女性主义哲学家将叔本华置于更广泛的哲学厌女传统中——从亚里士多德（女性是"有缺陷的男性"）到康德（女性的理性能力有限）到尼采（对女性的矛盾态度）。叔本华在这一传统中尤为突出，因为他的厌女不仅是个人的偏见，而是被系统性地整合进了哲学体系。

Feminist philosophers situate Schopenhauer within a broader tradition of philosophical misogyny — from Aristotle (woman as "defective male") to Kant (women's limited rational capacity) to Nietzsche (ambivalent attitudes toward women). Schopenhauer stands out in this tradition because his misogyny is not merely personal prejudice but systematically integrated into his philosophical system.

## 辩护与回应 / Defences and Responses

### 历史语境论 / Historical Contextualism

一些学者试图通过将叔本华的性别观点置于19世纪的历史语境中来减轻其严重性——论证他的观点在当时并不特别极端，许多同时代的思想家持有类似观点。但这种辩护的局限在于：叔本华的哲学声称是普遍的、超历史的真理——如果他的性别观点是历史性的偏见，那么他的其他哲学论断的普遍性声称也值得质疑。

Some scholars attempt to mitigate the severity of Schopenhauer's gender views by situating them in their 19th-century historical context — arguing his views were not particularly extreme for the time and many contemporary thinkers held similar views. But the limitation of this defence is: Schopenhauer's philosophy claims to be universal, transhistorical truth — if his gender views are historically contingent prejudices, then the universality claims of his other philosophical theses are also questionable.

### 体系与偏见的分离 / Separating System from Prejudice

另一种辩护策略是论证叔本华的性别偏见是其哲学体系的**附属物**而非**核心**——可以从体系中移除而不损害核心论证。但这种策略面临挑战：叔本华的性别观点与他的性欲形而上学和意志客体化理论深度交织——移除性别偏见可能动摇体系的其他部分。

Another defensive strategy argues that Schopenhauer's gender prejudices are **incidental** rather than **central** to his philosophical system — removable without damaging the core arguments. But this strategy faces a challenge: Schopenhauer's gender views are deeply entangled with his metaphysics of sexual love and theory of Will's objectification — removing gender prejudices may destabilise other parts of the system.

## 女性主义哲学家对叔本华的重新解读 / Feminist Reinterpretations

一些女性主义哲学家试图从叔本华哲学中提取有价值的资源：

Some feminist philosophers attempt to extract valuable resources from Schopenhauer's philosophy:

1. **同情伦理的女性主义潜力**：叔本华的同情伦理——强调关系性、情感性和对他者痛苦的关注——与女性主义关怀伦理学（care ethics）有结构性的亲和力。
2. **对理性主义的批判**：叔本华对理性至上主义的批判可以被女性主义利用——挑战将理性等同于男性、将情感等同于女性的传统二元对立。
3. **身体哲学的先驱**：叔本华的身体双重性理论为身体哲学提供了重要资源——可以绕过传统的身心二元论中的性别编码。

1. **Feminist potential of compassion ethics**: Schopenhauer's compassion ethics — emphasising relationality, emotionality, and attention to others' suffering — has structural affinities with feminist care ethics.
2. **Critique of rationalism**: Schopenhauer's critique of rationalism can be appropriated by feminism — challenging the traditional binary that equates reason with masculinity and emotion with femininity.
3. **Precursor of body philosophy**: Schopenhauer's body duality theory provides important resources for body philosophy — bypassing the gender coding in traditional mind-body dualism.

## 对MOCICI冥想的启示 / Implications for MOCICI Meditation

1. **性别中立的冥想实践**：叔本华的性别偏见提醒MOCICI，冥想实践必须是性别中立的——直接认知的能力不是某一性别的特权。
2. **批判性审视传统**：在与佛教传统的对话中，MOCICI也需要批判性地审视佛教传统中可能存在的性别偏见（如某些传统中将女性身体视为修行障碍）。
3. **同情伦理的普遍化**：叔本华的同情伦理在原则上是性别中立的——MOCICI可以将同情作为一种普遍的、超越性别的直接认知能力来培养。

1. **Gender-neutral meditation practice**: Schopenhauer's gender prejudices remind MOCICI that meditation practice must be gender-neutral — the capacity for direct cognition is not the privilege of any gender.
2. **Critical examination of traditions**: In dialogue with Buddhist traditions, MOCICI also needs to critically examine potential gender biases within those traditions (such as traditions viewing the female body as an obstacle to practice).
3. **Universalising compassion ethics**: Schopenhauer's compassion ethics is gender-neutral in principle — MOCICI can cultivate compassion as a universal, gender-transcending direct cognition capacity.
