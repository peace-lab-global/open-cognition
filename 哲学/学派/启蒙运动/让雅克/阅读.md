# 卢梭阅读指南 · Rousseau Reading List

## 阅读路径建议 / Suggested Reading Paths

### 路径一：快速入门（1-2周）/ Path 1: Quick Introduction (1-2 weeks)

适合对卢梭有初步兴趣但时间有限的读者。

For readers with initial interest but limited time.

1. **《论人类不平等的起源》第一部分**（约30页）—— 体验卢梭的自然状态描绘和文风
2. **《社会契约论》第一卷**（约20页）—— 把握政治理论的核心框架
3. **《一个孤独漫步者的遐想》第五次漫步**（约10页）—— 感受晚年卢梭的存在主义宁静

1. ***Discourse on Inequality*, Part I** (~30 pages) — Experience Rousseau's state of nature and literary style
2. ***The Social Contract*, Book I** (~20 pages) — Grasp the core framework of political theory
3. ***Reveries of a Solitary Walker*, Fifth Walk** (~10 pages) — Feel the late Rousseau's existentialist tranquility

---

### 路径二：政治哲学重点（3-4周）/ Path 2: Political Philosophy Focus (3-4 weeks)

适合对政治理论、民主理论感兴趣的读者。

For readers interested in political theory and democracy.

1. **《论人类不平等的起源》** 全文 —— 自然状态、财产制度、不平等的三个阶段
2. **《社会契约论》** 全文 —— 公意、主权、立法者、公民宗教
3. **《论政治经济学》** —— 公意在经济领域的应用
4. **进阶阅读**：
   - Rawls, J. (1971). *A Theory of Justice* —— 卢梭遗产的现代重构
   - Berlin, I. (1958). "Two Concepts of Liberty" —— 自由主义对卢梭的批评
   - Talmon, J. (1952). *The Origins of Totalitarian Democracy* —— 极权主义民主的谱系

1. ***Discourse on Inequality***, complete — State of nature, property, three stages of inequality
2. ***The Social Contract***, complete — General will, sovereignty, legislator, civil religion
3. ***Discourse on Political Economy*** — General will applied to economics
4. **Further reading**:
   - Rawls, J. (1971). *A Theory of Justice* — Modern reconstruction of Rousseau's legacy
   - Berlin, I. (1958). "Two Concepts of Liberty" — Liberal criticism of Rousseau
   - Talmon, J. (1952). *The Origins of Totalitarian Democracy* — Genealogy of totalitarian democracy

---

### 路径三：教育与人性重点（3-4周）/ Path 3: Education and Human Nature Focus (3-4 weeks)

适合对教育学、发展心理学、人性论感兴趣的读者。

For readers interested in education, developmental psychology, and human nature.

1. **《爱弥儿》** 第一至四卷 —— 消极教育的完整展开
2. **《论人类不平等的起源》** —— 自然善良与文明堕落的人性论基础
3. **进阶阅读**：
   - Montessori, M. (1909). *The Montessori Method* —— 卢梭教育遗产的实践转化
   - Piaget, J. (1932). *The Moral Judgment of the Child* —— 认知发展的日内瓦传统
   - Wollstonecraft, M. (1792). *A Vindication of the Rights of Woman* —— 对卢梭性别观的经典批评

1. ***Emile***, Books I-IV — Complete unfolding of negative education
2. ***Discourse on Inequality*** — Foundation in natural goodness and civilizational corruption
3. **Further reading**:
   - Montessori, M. (1909). *The Montessori Method* — Practical transformation of Rousseau's educational legacy
   - Piaget, J. (1932). *The Moral Judgment of the Child* — Geneva tradition of cognitive development
   - Wollstonecraft, M. (1792). *A Vindication of the Rights of Woman* — Classic feminist critique of Rousseau's gender views

---

### 路径四：文学与自传（4-6周）/ Path 4: Literature and Autobiography (4-6 weeks)

适合对文学、自传写作、存在主义感兴趣的读者。

For readers interested in literature, autobiographical writing, and existentialism.

1. **《新爱洛伊斯》**（选读）—— 18世纪最畅销的情感小说
2. **《忏悔录》** 第一至六卷 —— 现代自传文学的开创
3. **《一个孤独漫步者的遐想》** 全文 —— 晚年沉思
4. **进阶阅读**：
   - Starobinski, J. (1957). *Jean-Jacques Rousseau: La transparence et l'obstacle* —— 文学批评视角的经典研究
   - Wordsworth, W. (1805). *The Prelude* —— 卢梭浪漫主义遗产的英国表达

1. ***Julie, or the New Heloise*** (selections) — The 18th century's bestselling sentimental novel
2. ***Confessions***, Books I-VI — Pioneering work of modern autobiography
3. ***Reveries of a Solitary Walker***, complete — Late meditations
4. **Further reading**:
   - Starobinski, J. (1957). *Jean-Jacques Rousseau: Transparency and Obstruction* — Classic study from a literary-critical perspective
   - Wordsworth, W. (1805). *The Prelude* — British expression of Rousseau's Romantic legacy

## 二手研究分级 / Secondary Literature by Level

### 入门级 / Introductory

- **Wokler, R.** (1995). *Rousseau*. Oxford University Press (Very Short Introduction series). —— 最佳入门概览
- **Bertram, C.** (2004). *Rousseau and the Social Contract*. Routledge. —— 聚焦《社会契约论》的系统解读
- **O'Hagan, T.** (1999). *Rousseau*. Routledge. —— 全面的导论

- **Wokler, R.** (1995). *Rousseau*. OUP. — Best introductory overview
- **Bertram, C.** (2004). *Rousseau and the Social Contract*. Routledge. — Systematic reading focused on *The Social Contract*
- **O'Hagan, T.** (1999). *Rousseau*. Routledge. — Comprehensive introduction

### 进阶级 / Intermediate

- **Neuhouser, F.** (2008). *Rousseau's Theodicy of Self-Love*. Oxford University Press. —— 关于amour-propre的最重要当代研究
- **Riley, P.** (1982). *Will and Political Legitimacy*. Harvard University Press. —— 从霍布斯到黑格尔的社会契约传统
- **Cohen, J.** (2010). *Rousseau: A Free Community of Equals*. Princeton University Press. —— 平等主义解读
- **Cranston, M.** (1982–97). *Jean-Jacques: The Early Life and Work* / *The Noble Savage* / *The Solitary Self*. 三卷本传记。 —— 最详尽的英文传记

- **Neuhouser, F.** (2008). *Rousseau's Theodicy of Self-Love*. OUP. — Most important contemporary study of amour-propre
- **Riley, P.** (1982). *Will and Political Legitimacy*. Harvard UP. — Social contract tradition from Hobbes to Hegel
- **Cohen, J.** (2010). *Rousseau: A Free Community of Equals*. Princeton UP. — Egalitarian reading
- **Cranston, M.** (1982–97). Three-volume biography. — Most detailed English-language biography

### 专家级 / Advanced

- **Starobinski, J.** (1957/1988). *Jean-Jacques Rousseau: Transparency and Obstruction*. University of Chicago Press. —— 文学批评视角的经典
- **Derrida, J.** (1967). "Of Grammatology" (Part II: Nature, Culture, Writing). —— 解构主义对卢梭的阅读
- **de Man, P.** (1979). *Allegories of Reading*. —— 修辞学解读
- **Cassirer, E.** (1963). *The Question of Jean-Jacques Rousseau*. —— 新康德主义的经典诠释
- **Melzer, A.** (1988). *The Natural Goodness of Man*. University of Chicago Press. —— 论证卢梭思想的内在一致性

- **Starobinski, J.** (1957/1988). *Jean-Jacques Rousseau: Transparency and Obstruction*. UChicago Press. — Classic from a literary-critical perspective
- **Derrida, J.** (1967). *Of Grammatology*, Part II. — Deconstructive reading of Rousseau
