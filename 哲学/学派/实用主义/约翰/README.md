# 约翰·杜威 · John Dewey

> "Education is not preparation for life; education is life itself."
>
> "教育不是为生活做准备；教育就是生活本身。"

---

## 基本信息 / Biographical Note

约翰·杜威（1859年10月20日–1952年6月1日），美国哲学家、心理学家与教育改革家，实用主义三大代表之一（与皮尔士、詹姆斯并称）。佛蒙特大学（1879）毕业后在约翰·霍普金斯大学获哲学博士（1884），历任密歇根大学（1884–1894）、芝加哥大学（1894–1904）与哥伦比亚大学（1904–1930）。他于1896年在芝加哥创办了实验学校（Laboratory School），对20世纪美国乃至全球的教育改革产生了深远影响。1919–1921年访问中国，在北京大学等机构讲学，对中国现代教育思想影响显著。他是美国教师联盟（AFT）的创始成员之一，也是20世纪最重要的公共知识分子之一。

John Dewey (October 20, 1859 – June 1, 1952) was an American philosopher, psychologist, and educational reformer, one of the three pillars of classical pragmatism alongside Peirce and James. After graduating from the University of Vermont (1879) and earning his PhD from Johns Hopkins (1884), he held positions at the University of Michigan (1884–1894), the University of Chicago (1894–1904), and Columbia University (1904–1930). In 1896 he founded the Laboratory School at Chicago, profoundly shaping 20th-century education worldwide. He lectured in China (1919–1921), significantly influencing modern Chinese educational thought. A founding member of the American Federation of Teachers, he remains one of the most important public intellectuals of the 20th century.

**实用主义的体系建构者、教育改革的哲学奠基人、民主作为生活方式的理论家。**

**Pragmatism's systematizer, the philosophical architect of education reform, and the theorist of democracy as a way of life.**

## 六大核心命题 / Six Core Theses

### 1. 经验自然主义 / Experiential Naturalism

经验不是主观意识的封闭剧场，而是有机体与环境交互作用的自然过程。杜威将"经验"从洛克式的被动感知改造为包含主动行动与被动承受的完整循环。经验不是认识论的起点或终点，而是生命过程本身。

Experience is not a closed theater of subjective consciousness but the natural process of organism-environment interaction. Dewey transformed "experience" from Lockean passive reception into a complete cycle encompassing both active doing and passive undergoing. Experience is neither the starting point nor the endpoint of epistemology—it is the life process itself.

### 2. 工具主义 / Instrumentalism

思想和理论是解决问题的工具，其价值在于能否有效指导行动、将不确定情境转化为确定情境。杜威以"有理由的断定性"（warranted assertibility）取代传统"真理"概念，强调知识的实践功能而非静态对应。

Ideas and theories are tools for problem-solving, valued by their effectiveness in guiding action and transforming indeterminate situations into determinate ones. Dewey replaced the traditional concept of "truth" with "warranted assertibility," emphasizing knowledge's practical function over static correspondence.

### 3. 探究理论 / Theory of Inquiry

逻辑就是探究的方法论。探究始于不确定情境（problematic situation），经过观察、假设、推理与实验检验，终于确定情境的重建。这不是线性步骤，而是一个动态的、自我修正的过程。

Logic is the methodology of inquiry. Inquiry begins in an indeterminate (problematic) situation, proceeds through observation, hypothesis, reasoning, and experimental testing, and culminates in the reconstruction of a determinate situation. This is not a linear procedure but a dynamic, self-correcting process.

### 4. 民主作为生活方式 / Democracy as Way of Life

民主不仅是投票和政治制度，更是"联合生活的方式"（a mode of associated living）。民主的本质在于经验的共享与交流——通过沟通，个体经验得以扩展、丰富和改造。民主社会是那些为共同利益提供最多参与机会的社会。

Democracy is not merely voting and political institutions but "a mode of associated living." Its essence lies in the sharing and communication of experience—through communication, individual experience is expanded, enriched, and transformed. A democratic society is one that provides the most opportunities for participation toward common interests.

### 5. 艺术即经验 / Art as Experience

审美经验是完整、统一、强化了的经验的典范形态。杜威反对将艺术隔离于博物馆的"博物馆概念"（museum conception），主张审美经验植根于日常经验的完满实现。当一个经验具有统一的质性整体并达到 consummation 时，它就是审美的。

Aesthetic experience is the paradigmatic form of complete, unified, intensified experience. Dewey opposed the "museum conception" that isolates art from life, arguing that aesthetic experience is rooted in the consummation of ordinary experience. When an experience possesses a unified qualitative wholeness and reaches consummation, it is aesthetic.

### 6. 教育即生长 / Education as Growth

教育不是为未来生活做准备，而是生活本身。生长是持续重建经验的过程，没有外在的目的——生长自身即是目的。教育者的任务是设计促进生长的环境和条件。

Education is not preparation for future life but life itself. Growth is the continuous reconstruction of experience, having no external end—growth itself is the end. The educator's task is to design environments and conditions that promote growth.

## 思想脉络 / Intellectual Development

### 早期：黑格尔影响与约翰·霍普金斯时期（1880s–1894）/ Early Period: Hegelian Influence & Johns Hopkins (1880s–1894)

杜威在佛蒙特大学受业于 Torrey 和 Perkins，对哲学产生兴趣。在约翰·霍普金斯大学，他师从 George Sylvester Morris（黑格尔主义者），完成了关于康德的博士论文。这一时期他接受了一种有机统一的世界观，认为个体与宇宙通过精神活动而统一。他的早期著作《心理学》（1887）试图调和黑格尔主义与新兴实验心理学。

Dewey developed an interest in 哲学 at the University of Vermont under Torrey and Perkins. At Johns Hopkins, he studied with George Sylvester Morris (a Hegelian) and completed a dissertation on Kant. During this period he embraced an organic, unified worldview, holding that individuals and the cosmos are united through spiritual activity. His early work *Psychology* (1887) attempted to reconcile Hegelianism with emerging experimental 心理学.

### 中期：芝加哥实验学校与《民主与教育》（1894–1916）/ Middle Period: Chicago Laboratory School & Democracy and Education (1894–1916)

1894年杜威转至芝加哥大学，开始将哲学与教育实践相结合。1896年创办的实验学校是其教育理论的实验室。在这一时期，他逐渐摆脱黑格尔的唯心主义框架，转向以达尔文进化论和詹姆斯实用主义为基础的经验自然主义。1916年出版的《民主与教育》标志着其教育哲学的成熟——将教育定义为"社会生活的延续"，主张民主社会的教育应培养批判性思维和协作能力。

In 1894 Dewey moved to the University of Chicago, beginning to integrate 哲学 with educational practice. The Laboratory School (1896) served as a testing ground for his educational theories. During this period he gradually shed the Hegelian idealist framework, turning toward an experiential naturalism grounded in Darwinian evolution and James's pragmatism. *Democracy and Education* (1916) marked the maturity of his educational philosophy—defining education as "the continuation of social life" and arguing that democratic education should cultivate critical thinking and cooperative capacity.

### 晚期：哥伦比亚时期、《艺术即经验》与中国之行（1916–1952）/ Late Period: Columbia, Art as Experience & China Visit (1916–1952)

1904年杜威转入哥伦比亚大学，此后四十年间持续拓展其哲学体系。1925年《经验与自然》系统阐述了经验自然主义的形而上学基础。1927年《公众及其问题》回应李普曼的精英民主论，主张民主需要积极的公民参与。1934年《艺术即经验》将审美经验纳入经验理论的框架，是其美学的巅峰之作。1938年《逻辑：探究理论》重新将逻辑学建立在探究的基础之上。1919–1921年间，杜威应北京大学之邀访问中国，在北京、南京、上海等地讲学，其教育思想通过胡适、陶行知等学生深刻影响了中国现代教育的走向。

Dewey joined Columbia University in 1904, and over the next four decades continuously expanded his philosophical system. *Experience and Nature* (1925) systematically articulated the metaphysical foundations of experiential naturalism. *The Public and Its Problems* (1927) responded to Lippmann's elite-democracy theory, arguing that democracy requires active citizen participation. *Art as Experience* (1934) integrated aesthetic experience into his experience theory, representing the pinnacle of his 美学. *Logic: The Theory of Inquiry* (1938) re-founded logic on the basis of inquiry. During 1919–1921, Dewey visited China at Peking University's invitation, lecturing in Beijing, Nanjing, and Shanghai; his educational thought profoundly influenced the direction of modern Chinese education through students such as Hu Shih and Tao Xingzhi.

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

### 建构主义教育 / Constructivist Education

杜威是建构主义学习理论的哲学先驱。他主张学习者在主动经验中建构知识，而非被动接受信息。项目式学习（PBL）、体验式教育、服务学习等当代教育模式都根植于杜威"做中学"（learning by doing）的理念。

Dewey is the philosophical forerunner of constructivist learning theory. He argued that learners construct knowledge through active experience rather than passively receiving information. Contemporary educational models such as project-based learning (PBL), experiential education, and service learning are all rooted in Dewey's "learning by doing."

### 审议民主 / Deliberative Democracy

杜威的民主理论为哈贝马斯的交往行动理论和当代审议民主理论提供了重要资源。他强调民主不仅是投票，更是通过理性讨论和共享经验来形成公共判断的过程。

Dewey's democratic theory provides important resources for Habermas's communicative action theory and contemporary deliberative democracy. He emphasized that democracy is not merely voting but a process of forming public judgment through rational discussion and shared experience.

### 设计思维 / Design Thinking

杜威的探究理论——从不确定情境出发，经过观察、构思、原型和测试——与当代设计思维方法论高度契合。斯坦福 d.school 的设计思维流程可以看作杜威探究模式的实践版本。

Dewey's theory of inquiry—starting from an indeterminate situation, proceeding through observation, ideation, prototyping, and testing—closely aligns with contemporary design thinking methodology. Stanford d.school's design thinking process can be seen as a practical version of Dewey's inquiry model.

### 具身人工智能 / Embodied AI

杜威对身心二元论的批判、对有机体-环境交互的强调，与具身认知（embodied cognition）和具身AI的研究路径不谋而合。Rodney Brooks 等行为主义AI研究者的工作体现了杜威式的实用主义精神。

Dewey's critique of mind-body dualism and his emphasis on organism-environment interaction converge with embodied cognition and embodied AI 研究. The work of behaviorist AI researchers such as Rodney Brooks embodies the Deweyan pragmatist spirit.

### 积极心理学与心流 / Positive Psychology & Flow

Csikszentmihalyi 的心流理论（flow）——最优体验中行动与意识的融合——与杜威对审美经验的描述高度呼应：二者都描述了经验达到完满统一时的高峰状态。

Csikszentmihalyi's flow theory—the merging of action and consciousness in optimal experience—closely resonates with Dewey's description of aesthetic experience: both describe a peak state when experience reaches consummate unity.

### 环境哲学 / Environmental Philosophy

杜威的有机体-环境交互理论为生态哲学提供了非二元论的思想资源。经验不是人对自然的"旁观"，而是人作为自然一部分的参与过程。这一视角对深层生态学和生态现象学都有启发意义。

Dewey's organism-environment transaction theory provides non-dualist intellectual resources for ecological 哲学. Experience is not a human "spectating" of nature but a participatory process in which humans are part of nature. This perspective has implications for deep ecology and ecological phenomenology.

## 常见误读 / Common Misunderstandings

### 误读一："有用即真理" / Misreading 1: "Useful = True"

**误读**：杜威的实用主义就是"有用即真理"——任何能带来好处的信念都是真的。
**纠正**：杜威用"有理由的断定性"取代了简单的真理概念。一个信念被证成，不是因为它有用，而是因为它经过了严格探究过程的检验。探究的成功标准是问题解决，而非个人满足。

**Misreading**: Dewey's pragmatism means "whatever is useful is true"—any belief that brings benefits is true.
**Correction**: Dewey replaced the simple truth concept with "warranted assertibility." A belief is warranted not because it is useful but because it has been tested through rigorous inquiry. The success criterion of inquiry is problem resolution, not personal satisfaction.

### 误读二："反对学科知识" / Misreading 2: "Against Subject Knowledge"

**误读**：杜威反对传统的学科教学，认为知识不重要。
**纠正**：杜威反对的是知识与经验的脱节——将学科知识当作与儿童生活无关的现成结论来灌输。他主张学科知识应作为探究的资源被整合到学生的经验之中。

**Misreading**: Dewey opposed traditional subject teaching and thought knowledge was unimportant.
**Correction**: Dewey opposed the disconnect between knowledge and experience—treating subject knowledge as ready-made conclusions to be imposed on children regardless of their lives. He argued that subject knowledge should be integrated into students' experience as a resource for inquiry.

### 误读三："民主教育就是放任自流" / Misreading 3: "Democratic Education = Laissez-Faire"

**误读**：杜威的民主教育意味着让孩子为所欲为，教师不应干预。
**纠正**：杜威明确反对"放任"式教育。教师是学习环境的设计者和共同体的引导者，其职责是选择和组织能促进生长的经验。放任是对杜威最严重的误读之一。

**Misreading**: Dewey's democratic education means letting children do whatever they want; teachers should not intervene.
**Correction**: Dewey explicitly opposed "laissez-faire" education. The teacher is a designer of learning environments and a guide for the community, responsible for selecting and organizing experiences that promote growth. Laissez-faire is one of the most serious misreadings of Dewey.

### 误读四："杜威只关心教育" / Misreading 4: "Dewey Only Cared About Education"

**误读**：杜威只是一个教育理论家，其哲学贡献有限。
**纠正**：杜威构建了涵盖逻辑学、美学、政治哲学、伦理学、心理学和形而上学的完整哲学体系。教育哲学只是他整体哲学的一个应用领域。他在逻辑学（探究理论）、美学（艺术即经验）和政治哲学（公众理论）方面的贡献都具有独立的重要性。

**Misreading**: Dewey was merely an education theorist with limited philosophical contributions.
**Correction**: Dewey built a comprehensive philosophical system encompassing logic, aesthetics, political philosophy, ethics, psychology, and metaphysics. Educational 哲学 is only one application of his overall 哲学. His contributions to logic (inquiry theory), 美学 (art as experience), and political 哲学 (public theory) are all independently significant.

## 东西方对话：杜威 x 儒家/王阳明 / East-West Dialogue: Dewey x Confucianism / Wang Yangming

### 教育即生长 vs 儒家修身 / Education as Growth vs Confucian Self-Cultivation

杜威的"教育即生长"与儒家的"修身"传统形成有趣的对照。两者都将教育视为一种持续的、终身的过程，而非单纯的知识积累。杜威的生长强调经验的不断重建和环境的改造；儒家的修身则强调道德品格的培养和社会秩序的和谐。两者的共通之处在于：教育的目的不在外部，而在过程本身。王阳明的"致良知"与杜威的"生长"都指向内在潜能的实现。

Dewey's "education as growth" forms an illuminating contrast with the Confucian tradition of "self-cultivation" (xiushen). Both view education as a continuous, lifelong process rather than mere knowledge accumulation. Dewey's growth emphasizes the continuous reconstruction of experience and environmental transformation; Confucian self-cultivation emphasizes moral character development and social harmony. Their common ground lies in this: education's purpose is internal to the process, not external. Wang Yangming's "extending innate knowing" (zhiliangzhi) and Dewey's "growth" both point toward the realization of inner potential.

### 民主 vs 民本 / Democracy vs Minben (People-as-Root)

杜威的民主理念与儒家的"民本"思想既有交集也有张力。孟子"民为贵，社稷次之，君为轻"与杜威"民主即共同生活"的主张都强调人民的中心地位。但杜威的民主强调个体参与和自由交流，而儒家的民本更多是一种自上而下的仁政理想。两者的对话有助于思考：东亚社会如何在自身文化传统中发展出具有本土根基的民主理论。

Dewey's democratic ideal intersects with and diverges from the Confucian "people-as-root" (minben) thought. Mencius's "The people are most important; the state comes next; the ruler is least" and Dewey's "democracy as associated living" both emphasize the centrality of the people. Yet Dewey's democracy stresses individual participation and free communication, while Confucian minben is more a top-down ideal of benevolent governance. Dialogue between the two helps us think about how East Asian societies might develop democratic theories with indigenous cultural foundations.

### 探究 vs 格物致知 / Inquiry vs Gewu Zhizhi (Investigating Things to Extend Knowledge)

杜威的探究理论与《大学》"格物致知"的传统形成对话。朱熹的"格物"强调通过观察事物来获得知识，这与杜威的探究有表面相似性。但朱熹的格物最终指向天理的体认，而杜威的探究则指向问题的解决和情境的改造。王阳明对"格物"的重新诠释——将"格"解为"正"，将"物"解为"事"——与杜威的实践取向更为接近：知识在做事中产生，在实践中验证。

Dewey's inquiry theory enters into dialogue with the *Great Learning*'s tradition of "investigating things to extend knowledge" (gewu zhizhi). Zhu Xi's "gewu" emphasizes gaining knowledge through observing things, superficially resembling Dewey's inquiry. But Zhu Xi's gewu ultimately aims at comprehending heavenly principle (tianli), while Dewey's inquiry aims at problem resolution and situational reconstruction. Wang Yangming's reinterpretation of "gewu"—reading "ge" as "rectify" and "wu" as "affairs"—comes closer to Dewey's practical orientation: knowledge arises in doing and is verified in practice.

### 经验 vs 知行合一 / Experience vs Unity of Knowledge and Action

杜威的"经验"概念——做与受的统一——与王阳明的"知行合一"形成深层共鸣。王阳明主张"知而不行，只是未知"；杜威同样认为脱离行动的知识不是真正的知识。两者都打破了认知与实践的二元对立。杜威的经验概念更具科学方法论的色彩，而王阳明的知行合一则更多指向道德实践。将两者结合，可以为当代的实践哲学提供更丰富的资源。

Dewey's concept of "experience"—the unity of doing and undergoing—resonates deeply with Wang Yangming's "unity of knowledge and action" (zhixing heyi). Wang Yangming held that "to know and not act is not to know"; Dewey likewise maintained that knowledge divorced from action is not genuine knowledge. Both broke the binary opposition between cognition and practice. Dewey's experience concept carries more of a scientific-methodological character, while Wang Yangming's unity of knowledge and action points more toward moral practice. Combining both can provide richer resources for contemporary 哲学 of practice.

### 杜威的中国之行与胡适 / Dewey's China Visit and the Hu Shi Connection

1919年5月1日，杜威抵达上海，正值五四运动爆发前夕。他在中国停留两年多，足迹遍及十余省，发表演讲两百余次。其学生胡适成为新文化运动的领袖之一，将杜威的实验主义方法论（"大胆假设，小心求证"）引入中国的学术与思想实践。陶行知则将杜威的教育理论改造为适合中国农村的"生活教育"运动。杜威的中国之行不仅是一次学术访问，更是东西方思想交流的一个标志性事件。

On May 1, 1919, Dewey arrived in Shanghai just before the outbreak of the May Fourth Movement. He stayed in China for over two years, traveling to more than ten provinces and delivering over two hundred lectures. His student Hu Shi became a leader of the New Culture Movement, introducing Dewey's experimental method ("bold hypothesis, careful verification") into Chinese scholarship and intellectual practice. Tao Xingzhi adapted Dewey's educational theory into the "Life Education" movement suited to rural China. Dewey's China visit was not merely an academic trip but a landmark event in East-West intellectual exchange.

### 生长 vs 生生 / Growth vs Shengsheng (Ceaseless Generation)

杜威的"生长"概念与《易经》"生生之谓易"的宇宙观形成跨文化对话。"生生"指宇宙万物不断创生、变化的过程，具有本体论含义。杜威的"生长"同样具有本体论意味——经验的存在就是持续的生长过程。两者的差异在于："生生"具有宇宙论的维度，涵盖天地万物；而杜威的"生长"更聚焦于有机体-环境的交互。但两者都拒绝静态的存在观，都主张过程和变化优先于实体和固定性。

Dewey's concept of "growth" enters cross-cultural dialogue with the *Book of Changes* notion that "ceaseless generation (shengsheng) is what is meant by Change." "Shengsheng" refers to the continuous creative transformation of all things in the cosmos, carrying ontological significance. Dewey's "growth" likewise has ontological import—the being of experience is the continuous process of growth. The difference is that "shengsheng" has a cosmological dimension encompassing all of heaven and earth, while Dewey's "growth" focuses more specifically on organism-environment interaction. Yet both reject a static ontology, both privileging process and change over substance and fixity.
