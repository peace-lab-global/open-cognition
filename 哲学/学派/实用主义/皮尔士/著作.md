# 皮尔士作品总览 · Peirce Works

## 一、生前发表的重要论文 / Published Papers

| 年份 | 论文 | 发表刊物 | 核心贡献 |
|------|------|----------|----------|

| 1868 | "On Some Consequences of Four Incapacities" | *Journal of Speculative Philosophy* | 反笛卡尔主义宣言 |
| 1877 | "The Fixation of Belief" | *Popular Science Monthly* | 信念确立的四种方法 |
| 1878 | "How to Make Our Ideas Clear" | *Popular Science Monthly* | 实用主义准则 |
| 1882 | "The Law of Mind" | *The Monist* | 连续论 |

| 1893 | "Evolutionary Love" | *The Monist* | 宇宙论与爱 |

## 三、重要论文分类 / Key Papers by Topic

### 实用主义
- "How to Make Our Ideas Clear" (1878)
- "What Pragmatism Is" (1905)
- Harvard Lectures on Pragmatism (1903)
- "Issues of Pragmaticism" (1905)

### 符号学
- "On a New List of Categories" (1867)
- "Logic as Semiotic: The Theory of Signs" (收录于 *Philosophical Writings*)
- "A Letter to Lady Welby" (1904)

### 逻辑与科学方法
- "The Fixation of Belief" (1877)
- "The Logic of Abduction" (1903)
- "Notes on Scientific Philosophy" (1902)

### 形而上学与宇宙论
- "The Law of Mind" (1892)
- "Evolutionary Love" (1893)
- "A Guess at the Riddle" (1887–1888)

## 五、阅读路径

### 入门（3 文本，3 小时）
1. "The Fixation of Belief" (1877)
2. "How to Make Our Ideas Clear" (1878)
3. *The Essential Peirce* 导论

### 进阶（5 文本，20 小时）
4. Harvard Lectures on Pragmatism (1903)
5. "On a New List of Categories" (1867)
6. "The Logic of Abduction" (1903)
7. *Peirce on Signs* 选读
8. "Evolutionary Love" (1893)

### 深潜
9. *Collected Papers* 系统阅读
10. *Writings* 编年版系统阅读
11. 与弗雷格、索绪尔的比较研究
