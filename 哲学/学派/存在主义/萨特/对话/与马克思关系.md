# 萨特与马克思的关系 / Sartre and Marx: Existentialism Meets Historical Materialism

> "马克思主义是我们时代不可超越的哲学。"
> "Le marxisme est la philosophie indépassable de notre époque."
> — Sartre, *Critique de la raison dialectique* (1960)

---

## 概述 / Overview

卡尔·马克思（Karl Marx, 1818–1883）是萨特晚期思想的最重要对话者之一。萨特在1950年代逐渐从"个体存在主义"转向"历史存在主义"——这一转向的核心事件就是他与马克思主义的相遇。1957年的《方法问题》（*Questions de méthode*）和1960年的《辩证理性批判》（*Critique de la raison dialectique*）是萨特试图将存在主义与马克思主义综合的主要成果。这一综合既被赞赏为"二十世纪最雄心勃勃的哲学计划之一"，也被批评为"在两种不可调和的传统之间的不可能综合"。

Karl Marx (1818–1883) is one of the most important interlocutors of Sartre's later thought. In the 1950s Sartre gradually shifted from "individual existentialism" to "historical existentialism" — the core event of this shift being his encounter with Marxism. *Search for a Method* (*Questions de méthode*, 1957) and *Critique of Dialectical Reason* (*Critique de la raison dialectique*, 1960) are Sartre's major attempts to synthesize existentialism and Marxism. This synthesis has been praised as "one of the twentieth century's most ambitious philosophical projects" and criticized as "an impossible synthesis between two irreconcilable traditions."

---

## 综合的尝试 / The Attempt at Synthesis

### 1. "前进-逆溯方法" / The Progressive-Regressive Method

萨特在《方法问题》中提出了"前进-逆溯方法"（méthode progressive-régressive）作为存在主义对马克思主义的贡献。他论证：正统马克思主义的"经济决定论"过于抽象——它将一切历史事件还原为阶级和经济关系，忽视了**个体的具体生命史**。

Sartre proposes the "progressive-regressive method" in *Search for a Method* as existentialism's contribution to Marxism. He argues: orthodox Marxism's "economic determinism" is too abstract — it reduces all historical events to class and economic relations, overlooking **the concrete life-history of individuals**.

前进-逆溯方法包含两个运动：**逆溯**（régressive）——回到个体的童年、家庭和心理结构，理解其"处境"（situation）的形成；**前进**（progressive）——从个体的处境出发，理解个体如何通过"计划"（projet）超越处境并介入历史。萨特以福楼拜（Flaubert）为例：要理解福楼拜，不能仅将他还原为"小资产阶级作家"（马克思主义的做法），还必须理解他的家庭动态、个人心理和文学选择。

The progressive-regressive method comprises two movements: **regressive** — returning to the individual's childhood, family, and psychological structure to understand the formation of their "situation"; **progressive** — starting from the individual's situation, understanding how the individual transcends situation through "project" (projet) and engages history. Sartre takes Flaubert as an example: to understand Flaubert, one cannot merely reduce him to "petty-bourgeois writer" (the Marxist approach) but must also understand his family dynamics, personal psychology, and literary choices.

### 2. "实践-惰性" / The Practico-Inert

萨特在《辩证理性批判》中提出了"实践-惰性"（pratico-inerte）概念，作为对马克思"异化"（Entfremdung）概念的存在主义重述。实践-惰性指的是：人的自由行动产生的物质成果反过来**束缚人的自由**。工人建造了工厂，工厂却成为压迫工人的工具；农民耕种了土地，土地却导致了水土流失和饥荒。

Sartre proposes the "practico-inert" (pratico-inerte) concept in *Critique of Dialectical Reason* as an existentialist restatement of Marx's "alienation" (Entfremdung). The practico-inert refers to: the material results of human free action that **in turn constrain human freedom**. Workers build factories, and factories become tools of worker oppression; farmers cultivate land, and the land leads to erosion and famine.

实践-惰性与马克思的异化概念的差异在于：萨特的分析始终保持**个体自由的不可还原性**——即使在最极端的异化中，个体仍然保有选择态度的自由。马克思则倾向于认为异化可以被历史性地克服——在共产主义社会中，异化将被消灭。

The difference from Marx's alienation concept: Sartre's analysis always maintains **the irreducibility of individual freedom** — even in the most extreme alienation, the individual retains the freedom to choose their attitude. Marx tends to hold that alienation can be historically overcome — in communist society, alienation will be abolished.

### 3. "融合群体"与集体行动 / The Group-in-Fusion and Collective Action

萨特面临的一个核心问题是：如果意识（自为存在）本质上是个体性的，那么**集体行动如何在存在论上被解释？** 马克思的"阶级意识"概念假设了阶级可以成为集体主体，但萨特的早期存在论无法解释这一点。

A core problem Sartre faces: if consciousness (being-for-itself) is essentially individual, how can **collective action be explained ontologically?** Marx's concept of "class consciousness" assumes classes can become collective subjects, but Sartre's early existential ontology cannot explain this.

萨特的回答是"融合群体"（groupe en fusion）概念：在革命性的历史时刻——如1789年攻占巴士底狱——个体自由在共同处境和共同威胁下**临时汇聚**为一个集体主体。这种汇聚不是黑格尔式的"精神"显现，而是个体自由在紧急处境中的同步投射。但它是不稳定的——一旦威胁消退，融合群体就会退化为"制度"（institution）和"实践-惰性"。

Sartre's answer is the "group-in-fusion" (groupe en fusion) concept: in revolutionary historical moments — like the storming of the Bastille in 1789 — individual freedoms **temporarily converge** into a collective subject under shared situation and shared threat. This convergence is not a Hegelian "Spirit" manifestation but the synchronous projection of individual freedoms in an emergency situation. But it is unstable — once the threat dissipates, the group-in-fusion degenerates into "institution" and "practico-inert."

## 张力与批判 / Tensions and Critiques

### 自由 vs 决定论 / Freedom vs Determinism

萨特与马克思的根本张力在于：**个体自由是否可以被还原为社会-经济条件的效果？** 萨特坚持个体自由的不可还原性——即使在资本主义异化中，工人仍然"选择"如何面对自己的处境。正统马克思主义者（如[阿尔都塞](../批评/马克思主义批评.md)）则认为这种"自由"是一种资产阶级意识形态的幻象——工人的"选择"在根本上被生产关系所决定。

The fundamental tension between Sartre and Marx: **can individual freedom be reduced to the effects of socio-economic conditions?** Sartre insists on the irreducibility of individual freedom — even under capitalist alienation, the worker still "chooses" how to face their situation. Orthodox Marxists (such as [Althusser](../批评/马克思主义批评.md)) hold that this "freedom" is an illusion of bourgeois ideology — the worker's "choices" are fundamentally determined by relations of production.

### 人道主义的争论 / The Humanism Debate

萨特在《辩证理性批判》中明确站在"青年马克思"（1844年手稿）一边——将马克思主义理解为一种关于人的解放的人道主义哲学。这与阿尔都塞的"理论反人道主义"形成直接对立。萨特认为马克思主义的核心价值在于**人的自由和解放**；阿尔都塞认为马克思主义的核心价值在于**对资本主义生产方式的科学分析**。

Sartre explicitly sides with the "Young Marx" (1844 Manuscripts) in *Critique of Dialectical Reason* — understanding Marxism as a humanist philosophy of human liberation. This directly opposes Althusser's "theoretical anti-humanism." Sartre holds that Marxism's core value lies in **human freedom and liberation**; Althusser holds that Marxism's core value lies in **the scientific analysis of the capitalist mode of production**.
