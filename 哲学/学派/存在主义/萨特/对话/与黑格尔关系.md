# 萨特与黑格尔的关系 / Sartre and Hegel: Dialectics Refused and Recovered

> "黑格尔是最后一个试图将一切包含在体系中的哲学家——而我拒绝体系。"
> — 萨特对黑格尔的一般立场（概述）

---

## 概述 / Overview

格奥尔格·威廉·弗里德里希·黑格尔（G.W.F. Hegel, 1770–1831）对萨特思想的影响是复杂而曲折的。萨特在1930年代通过亚历山大·科耶夫（Alexandre Kojève）在巴黎高研院的著名讲座——特别是科耶夫对《精神现象学》（*Phänomenologie des Geistes*）中"主奴辩证法"的创造性解读——接触了黑格尔。科耶夫的存在主义化黑格尔深刻影响了萨特的主体间性理论。但在后期，萨特在《辩证理性批判》（1960）中试图将黑格尔的辩证法与马克思主义综合时，与黑格尔的关系变得更加复杂。

G.W.F. Hegel (1770–1831) influenced Sartre in complex and circuitous ways. Sartre encountered Hegel in the 1930s through Alexandre Kojève's famous lectures at the École Pratique des Hautes Études — particularly Kojève's creative reading of the "master-slave dialectic" in *Phenomenology of Spirit*. Kojève's existentialized Hegel profoundly shaped Sartre's theory of intersubjectivity. But later, when Sartre attempted to synthesize Hegelian dialectics with Marxism in *Critique of Dialectical Reason* (1960), the relation to Hegel became even more complex.

## 主奴辩证法与注视 / The Master-Slave Dialectic and the Look

### 科耶夫的中介 / Kojève's Mediation

科耶夫在1933–1939年的讲座中将黑格尔的"主奴辩证法"（Herrschaft und Knechtschaft）解读为一个关于**欲望、承认和斗争**的存在主义寓言。在科耶夫的解读中，人的根本欲望不是对物的欲望，而是对**他者承认**（Anerkennung）的欲望——"人欲求的是被欲望"。这一解读直接影响了萨特对[注视](../概念/注视.md)和[为他存在](../概念/为他存在.md)的分析。

Kojève's 1933–1939 lectures interpreted Hegel's "master-slave dialectic" as an existentialist allegory of **desire, recognition, and struggle**. In Kojève's reading, man's fundamental desire is not desire for things but desire for **the other's recognition** (Anerkennung) — "man desires to be desired." This reading directly influenced Sartre's analysis of the [look](../概念/注视.md) and [being-for-others](../概念/为他存在.md).

### 萨特的关键偏离 / Sartre's Key Departure

萨特接受了科耶夫-黑格尔"承认欲望"的基本框架，但在一个关键点上偏离了黑格尔：**黑格尔认为主奴辩证法最终可以通过"相互承认"得到解决——奴隶通过劳动获得自我意识，最终与主人达成和解。萨特否认了这种和解的可能性**。

Sartre accepts the Kojève-Hegelian framework of "desire for recognition" but departs from Hegel at one crucial point: **Hegel holds that the master-slave dialectic can ultimately be resolved through "mutual recognition" — the slave achieves self-consciousness through labor and ultimately reconciles with the master. Sartre denies the possibility of such reconciliation**.

对萨特而言，主体间的冲突不是历史辩证法的一个"阶段"（可以被"扬弃" Aufhebung），而是**存在论的永恒结构**。注视-客体化的辩证法没有"综合"——它永远在"我注视你-你注视我"之间循环，无法被超越。

For Sartre, intersubjective conflict is not a "stage" of historical dialectics (to be "sublated," aufgehoben) but an **eternal ontological structure**. The look-objectification dialectic has no "synthesis" — it perpetually cycles between "I look at you" and "you look at me," incapable of transcendence.

## 辩证法的接受与拒绝 / Acceptance and Rejection of Dialectics

### 早期：拒绝体系辩证法 / Early: Rejection of Systematic Dialectics

在《存在与虚无》（1943）中，萨特明确拒绝了黑格尔的体系辩证法。黑格尔的"绝对精神"（absoluter Geist）——一切矛盾最终在绝对知识中被扬弃和和解——在萨特看来是一种哲学上的[自欺](../概念/自欺.md)：**它用体系的完美取消了个体存在的真实焦虑**。

In *Being and Nothingness* (1943), Sartre explicitly rejects Hegel's systematic dialectics. Hegel's "absolute Spirit" (absoluter Geist) — all contradictions ultimately sublated and reconciled in absolute knowledge — is for Sartre a form of philosophical [bad faith](../概念/自欺.md): **it cancels the real anxiety of individual existence through the perfection of the system**.

萨特的"虚无"（néant）与黑格尔的"否定"（Negation）有根本区别：黑格尔的否定总是辩证的——它产生新的综合（Aufhebung）；萨特的虚无不是辩证的——它不产生综合，只产生焦虑和自由。

Sartre's "nothingness" (néant) differs fundamentally from Hegel's "negation" (Negation): Hegel's negation is always dialectical — it produces new synthesis (Aufhebung); Sartre's nothingness is not dialectical — it produces no synthesis, only anxiety and freedom.

### 晚期：恢复辩证理性 / Late: Recovering Dialectical Reason

在《辩证理性批判》（1960）中，萨特试图将存在主义与马克思主义综合——而黑格尔的辩证法成为这种综合的关键中介。萨特接受了辩证法作为**历史的方法**，但拒绝将其作为**本体论的原理**：辩证法不是"存在的法则"（如黑格尔所主张），而是"历史中的人理解自身处境的方法"。

In *Critique of Dialectical Reason* (1960), Sartre attempts to synthesize existentialism and Marxism — and Hegelian dialectics becomes the crucial mediating term. Sartre accepts dialectics as **a method for history** but rejects it as **an ontological principle**: dialectics is not "the law of being" (as Hegel claims) but "the method by which historical man understands his own situation."

萨特提出的"融合群体"（groupe en fusion）——如攻占巴士底狱的巴黎群众——是他试图在保持个体自由的前提下解释集体行动的努力。这是对黑格尔"精神"（Geist）概念的个体化替代：集体不是"精神"的显现，而是**个体自由在共同处境中的临时汇聚**。

Sartre's "group-in-fusion" (groupe en fusion) — like the Parisian crowd storming the Bastille — represents his effort to explain collective action while preserving individual freedom. This is an individualized substitute for Hegel's "Spirit" (Geist): the collective is not the manifestation of "Spirit" but **the temporary convergence of individual freedoms in a shared situation**.
