# 萨特与海德格尔的关系 / Sartre and Heidegger: Inheritance and Divergence

> "萨特误解了我的存在论……他把此在当成了主体。"
> — Heidegger, *Brief über den Humanismus* (1947)

---

## 概述 / Overview

马丁·海德格尔（Martin Heidegger, 1889–1976）是萨特哲学的最重要思想来源——也是与萨特产生最深刻分歧的思想家。萨特在1933–1934年留学柏林期间研读了《存在与时间》（*Sein und Zeit*, 1927），深受震撼，并将其核心概念——此在（Dasein）、被抛状态（Geworfenheit）、向死而在（Sein-zum-Tode）——引入法国哲学语境。然而，萨特对海德格尔的"挪用"是如此激进，以至于海德格尔本人在1947年的《关于人道主义的信》（*Brief über den Humanismus*）中明确与萨特划清界限："萨特把'存在先于本质'当作形而上学的命题——但这恰恰是对存在论差异的误解。"

Martin Heidegger (1889–1976) is the most important intellectual source for Sartre's philosophy — and also the thinker with whom Sartre most profoundly diverges. Sartre studied *Being and Time* (1927) during his Berlin sojourn (1933–1934), was deeply shaken by it, and introduced its core concepts — Dasein, thrownness (Geworfenheit), being-toward-death (Sein-zum-Tode) — into the French philosophical context. However, Sartre's "appropriation" of Heidegger was so radical that Heidegger himself explicitly distanced himself in the *Letter on Humanism* (1947): "Sartre takes 'existence precedes essence' as a metaphysical proposition — but this is precisely a misunderstanding of the ontological difference."

## 继承 / Inheritance

### 1. 此在与自为存在 / Dasein and Being-for-Itself

萨特将海德格尔的"此在"（Dasein）——人的存在方式，即"在世界中存在"（In-der-Welt-sein）——改造为"自为存在"（pour-soi）。两者共享的核心洞见是：**人的存在不能被还原为物的存在**。人不是一个"在世界中的物"，而是一种始终"超越"自身的存在——人总是"比自己所是的多"（plus qu'il n'est）。

Sartre transforms Heidegger's "Dasein" — the mode of being proper to humans, i.e., "being-in-the-world" (In-der-Welt-sein) — into "being-for-itself" (pour-soi). The shared core insight: **human being cannot be reduced to the being of things**. Man is not a "thing in the world" but a being that always "transcends" itself — one is always "more than one is."

### 2. 被抛状态与事实性 / Thrownness and Facticity

海德格尔的"被抛状态"（Geworfenheit）——此在发现自己已经被抛入一个它不曾选择的世界——被萨特转化为"事实性"（facticité）概念。两者都拒绝将人的存在起点视为"选择"的结果：人不是自己选择出生的。但萨特比海德格尔更强调：**事实性不是命运的判决，而是自由运作的场域**。

Heidegger's "thrownness" — Dasein finding itself already thrown into a world it did not choose — is transformed by Sartre into the concept of "facticity." Both refuse to treat the starting point of human existence as the result of "choice": one does not choose to be born. But Sartre emphasizes more than Heidegger: **facticity is not fate's verdict but the field in which freedom operates**.

### 3. 向死而在与死亡 / Being-Toward-Death and Death

海德格尔的"向死而在"（Sein-zum-Tode）——此在通过面对自身死亡的不可替代性而获得本真的整体存在——被萨特激进化为一种更极端的立场：**死亡不是意义的赋予者，而是意义的剥夺者**。死亡从外部打断自为存在的一切投射，使一切意义变得不可能。萨特拒绝海德格尔"死亡使存在成为整体"的论证。

Heidegger's "being-toward-death" — Dasein achieving authentic wholeness by confronting the irreplaceability of its own death — is radicalized by Sartre into a more extreme position: **death is not the giver of meaning but the depriver of meaning**. Death interrupts all projections of being-for-itself from the outside, making all meaning impossible. Sartre refuses Heidegger's argument that "death makes being whole."

## 分歧 / Divergence

### 1. 存在论差异 vs 二元存在论 / Ontological Difference vs Binary Ontology

海德格尔坚持"存在论差异"（ontologische Differenz）——存在（Sein）与存在者（Seiendes）之间的区分。他认为萨特的"自在-自为"二元论仍然停留在"存在者"层面，没有触及"存在本身"（Sein selbst）的问题。萨特则认为海德格尔的"存在本身"是一种新的神秘主义——一种不可言说的、超越一切具体分析的"它"（Es gibt）。

Heidegger insists on the "ontological difference" — the distinction between Being (Sein) and beings (Seiendes). He holds that Sartre's "in-itself/for-itself" dualism remains at the "being" level without touching "Being itself" (Sein selbst). Sartre considers Heidegger's "Being itself" a new mysticism — an unsayable "It gives" (Es gibt) that transcends all concrete analysis.

### 2. 自由的位置 / The Place of Freedom

对海德格尔而言，此在的自由是"存在之真理的开放性"——自由不是人的属性，而是人被存在所"召唤"的方式。对萨特而言，自由是人的本体论条件——它是绝对的、不可逃避的、令人焦虑的。海德格尔的自由是"让存在"（Seinlassen），萨特的自由是"选择与行动"。

For Heidegger, Dasein's freedom is "the openness of the truth of Being" — freedom is not man's attribute but the way man is "called" by Being. For Sartre, freedom is man's ontological condition — absolute, inescapable, anxiety-inducing. Heidegger's freedom is "letting-be" (Seinlassen); Sartre's freedom is "choosing and acting."

### 3. 政治的歧路 / Political Divergence

海德格尔1933年短暂加入纳粹党的事实，成为萨特终生反思的问题。萨特认为海德格尔的哲学中缺乏一个伦理维度——"此在"的本真性可以服务于任何政治目的。萨特的介入哲学恰恰是对这一缺失的回应：**哲学必须包含政治承诺**。

Heidegger's brief 1933 enrollment in the Nazi Party became a lifelong preoccupation for Sartre. Sartre held that Heidegger's philosophy lacks an ethical dimension — Dasein's authenticity could serve any political purpose. Sartre's philosophy of engagement is precisely the response to this absence: **philosophy must include political commitment**.
