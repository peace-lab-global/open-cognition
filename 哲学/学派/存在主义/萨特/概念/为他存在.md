# 为他存在 · L'être-pour-autrui

## 一句话定义

**为他存在 (l'être-pour-autrui)** 是萨特存在论的第三个基本维度——在"自在存在" (en-soi) 与"自为存在" (pour-soi) 之外——它描述了**我与他者的存在论关系**，其核心结构是[注视](概念/注视.md)所揭示的冲突。

**Being-for-others** (l'être-pour-autrui) is the third fundamental dimension of Sartre's ontology — alongside "being-in-itself" (en-soi) and "being-for-itself" (pour-soi) — describing **my ontological relation to others**, whose core structure is the conflict revealed by the [look](概念/注视.md).

---

## 核心要义 / Core Arguments

### 1. 为他存在作为存在论的第三维度 / Being-for-Others as the Third Ontological Dimension

在《存在与虚无》(*L'Être et le Néant*, 1943) 中，萨特的存在论由三个维度构成：

In *Being and Nothingness* (1943), Sartre's ontology consists of three dimensions:

| 维度 | 法语 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| 自在存在 | L'être-en-soi | 物的存在——充实、不透明、与自身一致 |
| 自为存在 | L'être-pour-soi | 意识的存在——虚无、透明、与自身有裂隙 |
| **为他存在** | **L'être-pour-autrui** | **与他者的关系——通过注视被客体化** |

为他存在不是自为存在的偶然附加，而是**自为存在的必要构成要素**。萨特论证：如果没有他者的存在，我永远不会体验到羞耻 (honte)；而没有羞耻，我就永远不会意识到自己有一个"外部"——一个我看不见但他人看得见的面向。因此，他者的存在是我认识自身完整性的条件。

Being-for-others is not an accidental addition to being-for-itself but a **necessary constitutive element** of being-for-itself. Sartre argues: without the existence of others, I would never experience shame (honte); without shame, I would never become aware that I have an "outside" — a dimension invisible to me but visible to others. Therefore, the existence of others is the condition for my knowledge of my own completeness.

> "羞耻是在他人面前对自我的羞耻……通过羞耻，我发现了我存在的一个方面——我的'为他'维度。"
> "La honte est honte de soi devant autrui… Par la honte, je découvre un aspect de mon être — ma dimension 'pour-autrui'."
> — *L'Être et le Néant* (1943)

### 2. 主体间冲突的四种策略 / Four Strategies of Intersubjective Conflict

萨特认为主体间关系的根本结构是**冲突** (conflit)。每一个主体都试图在保持自身主体性的同时将他者客体化。萨特在《存在与虚无》中分析了四种基本策略：

Sartre holds that the fundamental structure of intersubjective relations is **conflict**. Every subject attempts to maintain their own subjectivity while objectifying the other. In *Being and Nothingness* he analyzes four basic strategies:

**爱 (L'amour)**——在爱中，我试图通过使他者爱我来"占有"他者的自由。我希望他者自愿地将我作为他世界的中心——不是通过强制，而是通过诱惑。但爱的悖论在于：如果他者真的完全臣服于我，他就失去了他的自由——而正是他的自由才是我所渴望的。因此，爱不可避免地滑向**受虐 (masochisme)**——我试图成为他者的客体，以换取他者对我的爱。

**Love** — in love, I attempt to "possess" the other's freedom by making the other love me. I wish the other to voluntarily make me the center of their world — not through coercion but through seduction. But love's paradox: if the other truly surrenders completely, they lose their freedom — and it is precisely their freedom that I desire. Thus love inevitably slides toward **masochism** — I attempt to become the other's object in exchange for their love.

**欲望 (Le désir)**——在性欲中，我试图通过他者的身体来"捕获"他者的意识。萨特的欲望分析极具原创性：他认为性欲的本质不是追求快感，而是追求**他者意识的化身**。我渴望的不是他者的身体本身，而是"通过他者的身体来占有他者的自由"。但欲望的悖论在于：当我成功"捕获"他者的身体时（他者在快感中失去自我意识），他者的意识恰恰逃走了。

**Desire** — in sexual desire, I attempt to "capture" the other's consciousness through the other's body. Sartre's analysis of desire is highly original: he holds that desire's essence is not the pursuit of pleasure but the pursuit of **the incarnation of the other's consciousness**. What I desire is not the other's body itself but "possession of the other's freedom through the other's body." But desire's paradox: when I succeed in "capturing" the other's body (the other losing self-consciousness in pleasure), the other's consciousness precisely escapes.

**施虐 (Le sadisme)**——施虐者试图通过暴力将他者完全还原为客体，使他者的自由在痛苦中崩溃。但施虐的悖论在于：如果受害者完全失去主体性，施虐者就失去了"被承认"的满足——因为只有自由的主体才能"承认"他者。

**Sadism** — the sadist attempts to reduce the other completely to object through violence, causing the other's freedom to collapse in pain. But sadism's paradox: if the victim completely loses subjectivity, the sadist loses the satisfaction of "being recognized" — for only a free subject can "recognize" the other.

**冷漠 (L'indifférence)**——试图通过否认他者的重要性来保护自己的主体性。"我根本不在乎你怎么看我"——但这种声称恰恰暴露了我其实非常在乎。冷漠是自欺 (mauvaise foi) 在主体间关系中的特殊形态。

**Indifference** — attempting to protect one's subjectivity by denying the other's importance. "I don't care at all what you think of me" — but this very claim exposes that I actually care deeply. Indifference is [bad faith](概念/自欺.md)'s special form in intersubjective relations.

### 3. "我们" (le nous) 的可能性 / The Possibility of "We"

萨特对"我们" (le nous) 持怀疑态度。他认为"我们-主体" (nous-sujet) 只是一种心理体验，不具有存在论的根基。当我与他人共同面对第三个注视者时——比如两个工人在工头面前——我体验到"我们被一起客体化了"。这种"客体-我们" (nous-objet) 是真实的，但它的基础不是团结而是**共同的被客体化**。

Sartre is skeptical of "we" (le nous). He holds that "we-subject" (nous-sujet) is merely a psychological experience without ontological foundation. When I face a third observer together with another — for example, two workers before a foreman — I experience "we are being objectified together." This "object-we" (nous-objet) is real, but its basis is not solidarity but **shared objectification**.

这一立场使萨特与[马克思](对话/与马克思关系.md)的"阶级意识" (conscience de classe) 概念产生张力：如果"我们"不具有存在论根基，那么无产阶级的阶级团结如何在存在论上被奠基？萨特在《辩证理性批判》中试图通过"融合群体" (groupe en fusion) 概念来解决这一问题。

This position creates tension with [Marx](对话/与马克思关系.md)'s concept of "class consciousness" (conscience de classe): if "we" lacks ontological foundation, how can proletarian class solidarity be ontologically grounded? Sartre attempts to resolve this in *Critique of Dialectical Reason* through the concept of the "group-in-fusion" (groupe en fusion).

### 4. 为他存在与自欺 / Being-for-Others and Bad Faith

为他存在的结构与[自欺](概念/自欺.md)密切相关。在为他存在中，我面临一种特殊的自欺诱惑：**通过他人的注视来定义自己**。当他人将我客体化为"一个懦夫""一个天才""一个失败者"时，我可能接受这种定义——这就是自欺的一种形态。我放弃了自己的超越性（我不是任何固定的东西），将自己认同于他人给予我的固化形象。

The structure of being-for-others is closely related to [bad faith](概念/自欺.md). In being-for-others, I face a special temptation of bad faith: **defining myself through the other's look**. When others objectify me as "a coward," "a genius," "a failure," I may accept this definition — this is a form of bad faith. I surrender my transcendence (I am not any fixed thing) and identify with the reified image others give me.

## 萨特 vs 其他思想家 / Sartre vs Other Thinkers

| 思想家 | 他者观 | 萨特的差异 |
|--------|--------|------------|
| **黑格尔** | 主奴辩证法：通过斗争达成相互承认 | 萨特否认相互承认的最终可能性——冲突是永恒的 |
| **海德格尔** | 共在 (Mitsein)：此在本质上与他人共在 | 海德格尔的共在更和平，萨特的为他存在更冲突 |
| **列维纳斯** | 面容 (le visage)：他者的面容呼唤无限责任 | 列维纳斯以伦理优先取代萨特的冲突优先 |
| **梅洛-庞蒂** | 身体间性 (intercorporeité) | 梅洛-庞蒂认为身体可以中介主体间关系，缓和冲突 |
| **波伏娃** | 他者的性别化：女性作为"绝对他者" | 波伏娃揭示了他者关系的性别维度，这是萨特忽视的 |
| **布伯** | 我-你 (I-Thou) 关系 | 布伯相信主体间可以有非客体化的"我-你"关系；萨特认为这在本体论上不可能 |

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

### 人际关系心理学 / Interpersonal Psychology

萨特的四种策略分析——爱、欲望、施虐、受虐——为当代人际关系心理学提供了深刻的概念框架。依恋理论 (attachment theory) 中"焦虑型依恋"与"回避型依恋"的动态，可以被理解为萨特所说的"主体间冲突的不同策略"。

Sartre's analysis of four strategies — love, desire, sadism, masochism — provides a profound conceptual framework for contemporary interpersonal psychology. The dynamics of "anxious attachment" and "avoidant attachment" in attachment theory can be understood as what Sartre called "different strategies of intersubjective conflict."

### 权力与微观政治 / Power and Micro-Politics

萨特的为他存在理论影响了福柯 (Foucault) 对权力微观物理学的分析。注视-客体化的机制不仅是个体间关系的结构，也是社会权力的微观基础。从校园霸凌到职场政治，从家庭权力动态到国际关系，为他存在的冲突结构无处不在。

Sartre's being-for-others theory influenced Foucault's analysis of the micro-physics of power. The look-objectification mechanism is not only the structure of individual relations but also the micro-foundation of social power. From school bullying to workplace politics, from family power dynamics to international relations, the conflict structure of being-for-others is ubiquitous.

### 数字时代的为他存在 / Being-for-Others in the Digital Age

社交媒体创造了为他存在的全新形态：自我被数字化为可量化、可比较、可传播的"数据-形象"。点赞数是注视的数字化度量，网络暴力是施虐的匿名化形态，"人设"是为他存在中的自欺的极端化。

Social media creates entirely new forms of being-for-others: the self is digitized into quantifiable, comparable, distributable "data-images." Like counts are the digitalized measure of the look; cyberbullying is the anonymized form of sadism; "persona management" is bad faith in being-for-others taken to its extreme.

## 常见误读 / Common Misunderstandings

- **误读一**："萨特认为人际关系注定是痛苦的"。纠正：萨特描述的是主体间关系的**存在论结构**——冲突是其根基。但这不意味着具体的人际关系总是痛苦的，而是意味着和谐需要持续的努力和清醒的意识。
- **误读二**："为他存在意味着萨特是反社会的"。纠正：恰恰相反，为他存在揭示了人类存在的根本社会性——我**不能不**与他者相关。萨特的贡献在于揭示了这种社会性的冲突结构，而非否定社会性本身。
- **误读三**："萨特的为他存在理论忽视了爱的美好"。纠正：萨特分析的不是"爱不好"，而是"爱为什么困难"。爱的困难——如何在保持双方自由的前提下相爱——是一个真实的哲学问题，不是一个可以被浪漫话语轻易消解的问题。
