# 萨特 · Jean-Paul Sartre

> "人无非是他自己造就的东西。这就是存在主义的第一原理。"
> "L'homme n'est rien d'autre que ce qu'il se fait. C'est le premier principe de l'existentialisme."
> — *L'existentialisme est un humanisme* (1946)

---

## 基本信息 / Biographical Note

让-保罗·萨特（Jean-Paul Sartre, 1905年6月21日–1980年4月15日），法国哲学家、小说家、剧作家、政论家，二十世纪最彻底地捍卫人类自由的思想家，也是存在主义哲学运动的建构者与核心人物。他出生于巴黎，父亲让-巴蒂斯特·萨特（Jean-Baptiste Sartre）是一名海军军官，在萨特十五个月大时死于热病。萨特由外祖父夏尔·施韦泽（Charles Schweitzer）——阿尔萨斯裔语言学者、阿尔贝·施韦泽（Albert Schweitzer）的堂兄——在充满书籍的环境中抚养长大。

Jean-Paul Sartre (21 June 1905 – 15 April 1980) was a French philosopher, novelist, playwright, and political essayist, the twentieth century's most thoroughgoing defender of human freedom and the architect of the existentialist movement. Born in Paris, his father Jean-Baptiste Sartre, a naval officer, died of fever when Sartre was fifteen months old. He was raised by his maternal grandfather Charles Schweitzer — an Alsatian linguist and cousin of Albert Schweitzer — in a book-filled household.

萨特先后在巴黎亨利四世中学和巴黎高等师范学校（École Normale Supérieure）求学，1929年以第一名通过哲学教师资格考试（agrégation de philosophie），同年的第二名是西蒙·德·波伏娃（Simone de Beauvoir）——此后两人共同度过了五十余年的思想与情感生活。1933–1934年留学柏林，研读胡塞尔（Edmund Husserl）和海德格尔（Martin Heidegger），将现象学方法引入法国。外祖父书房中的大量阅读塑造了萨特对文学的终生热爱——他在自传《词语》（*Les Mots*, 1964）中以深刻的自嘲回忆了这段"纸墨童年"："我把词语当作事物的替代品……我选择了文学作为宗教。"

Sartre studied at the Lycée Henri-IV and the École Normale Supérieure, ranking first in the agrégation de philosophie in 1929 — the second-place finisher that year was Simone de Beauvoir, with whom he would share over fifty years of intellectual and emotional partnership. In 1933–1934 he studied in Berlin under Husserl and Heidegger, introducing phenomenological method to France. Extensive reading in his grandfather's library shaped Sartre's lifelong love of literature — in the autobiographical *Words* (*Les Mots*, 1964) he recalls this "paper-and-ink childhood" with profound self-irony: "I took words for the substitutes of things… I chose literature as my religion."

第二次世界大战深刻改变了萨特的人生轨迹。1939年应征入伍，1940年在洛林被德军俘虏，关押在战俘营近九个月。战俘营的经历——特别是与狱友组织"社会主义与自由"（Socialisme et Liberté）地下抵抗网络——成为萨特从纯哲学走向政治介入的转折点。获释后，他回到巴黎，加入更广泛的抵抗运动。1945年创办《现代》（*Les Temps Modernes*）杂志，使之成为战后法国知识分子介入公共事务的核心阵地。1964年拒绝诺贝尔文学奖——"一个作家不应该让自己变成机构"（Un écrivain ne doit pas se laisser transformer en institution）。晚年双目近乎失明，但仍坚持口述工作。1980年在巴黎去世，送葬者超过五万人——这是自维克多·雨果以来巴黎最大规模的民间葬礼之一。

The Second World War profoundly changed Sartre's life trajectory. Drafted in 1939, he was captured by German forces in Lorraine in 1940 and held as a prisoner of war for nearly nine months. The POW camp experience — particularly organizing the underground resistance network "Socialisme et Liberté" with fellow prisoners — marked Sartre's turning point from pure philosophy to political engagement. After release he returned to Paris and joined the broader Resistance. In 1945 he founded *Les Temps Modernes*, making it the central platform for post-war French intellectual engagement. He declined the Nobel Prize in Literature in 1964 — "a writer should not let himself be transformed into an institution" (Un écrivain ne doit pas se laisser transformer en institution). Nearly blind in his final years, he continued dictating work. He died in Paris in 1980; over fifty thousand mourners attended his funeral — one of the largest civilian funerals in Paris since Victor Hugo.

## 一句话定位 / One-Line Positioning

**二十世纪最彻底地捍卫人类自由的哲学家——以现象学方法为工具，将"存在先于本质"推至逻辑极端，在哲学、文学与政治三个领域同时实践"介入"（engagement），迫使每一代读者重新面对"我是谁、我该如何活"的根本问题。**

**The twentieth century's most thoroughgoing philosopher of human freedom — deploying phenomenological method, pushing "existence precedes essence" to its logical extreme, practicing "engagement" simultaneously in philosophy, literature, and politics, compelling every generation to confront the fundamental question: "Who am I, how should I live?"**

## 六大核心命题 / Six Core Theses

### 1. 存在先于本质 / Existence Precedes Essence

→ 详见 [概念/存在先于本质.md](概念/存在先于本质.md)

人没有预先设定的本性、天命或目的。如果上帝不存在，就没有一个先于人的存在而设想的"人性"（nature humaine）。人首先作为虚无（néant）存在，然后通过自由选择创造自己。"存在先于本质"是存在主义的第一原理：没有"人的本性"可以作为行为的借口，没有命运可以推卸责任，每一个人都必须独自承担创造自己的任务。

Man has no pre-set nature, destiny, or purpose. If God does not exist, there is no "human nature" (nature humaine) conceived prior to man's existence. Man first exists as nothingness (néant), then creates himself through free choice. "Existence precedes essence" is existentialism's first principle: no "human nature" can serve as an excuse for behavior, no destiny can shift responsibility, each person must alone bear the task of creating themselves.

### 2. 绝对自由 / Absolute Freedom

→ 详见 [概念/自由.md](概念/自由.md)

人被判为自由（condamné à être libre）。自由不是"做想做的事"，而是"无法不选择"——即使在最极端的处境中——监禁、酷刑、死亡——人仍然可以选择自己的态度。自由伴随绝对责任：选择自己就是选择全人类的形象（en choisissant l'homme se choisit en tant qu'homme）。自由的体验不是愉悦，而是焦虑（angoisse）——当我意识到没有任何东西能决定我的选择时，我体验到根本的焦虑。

Man is condemned to freedom (condamné à être libre). Freedom is not "doing what one wants" but "being unable not to choose" — even in the most extreme situations — imprisonment, torture, death — one can still choose one's attitude. Freedom accompanies absolute responsibility: choosing oneself is choosing the image of humanity (en choisissant l'homme se choisit en tant qu'homme). The experience of freedom is not joy but anguish (angoisse) — when I realize nothing can determine my choice, I experience fundamental anxiety.

### 3. 虚无 / Nothingness

→ 详见 [概念/虚无.md](概念/虚无.md)

意识不是物，而是"虚无"——它通过否定（néantisation）将自身从自在存在（en-soi）的充实中挖空，由此产生自由、时间性与超越性。自在存在是物的存在方式——充实、不透明、与自身一致；自为存在是意识的存在方式——虚无、透明、与自身有裂隙。虚无不是"不存在"，而是"不-是-物"（non-thingness）——正因为意识不是任何固定的东西，它才是自由的。

Consciousness is not a thing but "nothingness" — through negation (néantisation) it hollows itself out from the plenitude of being-in-itself (en-soi), thereby producing freedom, temporality, and transcendence. Being-in-itself is the mode of being of things — full, opaque, self-coincident; being-for-itself is the mode of being of consciousness — nothingness, transparent, fissured. Nothingness is not "non-existence" but "non-thingness" — precisely because consciousness is not any fixed thing, it is free.

### 4. 注视 / The Look

→ 详见 [概念/注视.md](概念/注视.md)

他人的目光将我客体化，使我从"自为存在"跌落为"对象"，产生羞耻（honte）与冲突。"他人即地狱"（L'enfer, c'est les autres）不是厌世宣言，而是对主体间性（intersubjectivité）冲突结构的精确描述：在他人的注视下，我失去了对自己形象的控制权。主体间关系本质上是冲突——每一个主体都试图把对方客体化，同时抵抗被对方客体化。

The other's look objectifies me, causing me to fall from "being-for-itself" to "object," producing shame (honte) and conflict. "Hell is other people" (L'enfer, c'est les autres) is not a misanthropic declaration but a precise description of the conflictual structure of intersubjectivity: under the other's look, I lose control over my own image. Intersubjective relations are essentially conflictual — each subject attempts to objectify the other while resisting being objectified.

### 5. 自欺 / Bad Faith

→ 详见 [概念/自欺.md](概念/自欺.md)

人试图逃避自由的重负，假装自己"不得不如此"、"别无选择"——这就是自欺（mauvaise foi）。自欺不是对他人说谎，而是对自己否认自由的事实。咖啡馆侍者以夸张的精确性表演"侍者"角色，把自己当作一个有固定本质的物；约会中的女人让自己的手"在那里"如同无生命之物，逃避做出选择。自欺是人类意识的普遍倾向，本真（authenticité）则是对自身自由的承担。

Man attempts to evade the burden of freedom, pretending "I have no choice," "I must do this" — this is bad faith (mauvaise foi). Bad faith is not lying to others but denying to oneself the fact of freedom. The café waiter performs the "waiter" role with exaggerated precision, treating himself as a thing with fixed essence; the woman on a date lets her hand lie there "like a lifeless object," evading the choice. Bad faith is a universal tendency of human consciousness; authenticity (authenticité) is the assumption of one's own freedom.

### 6. 介入 / Engagement

→ 详见 [概念/介入.md](概念/介入.md)

知识分子、作家与艺术家必须在具体历史处境中承担政治与道德责任。写作就是行动（écrire, c'est agir）——词语是"上了子弹的手枪"（des pistolets chargés），每一次言说都在世界中投下力量。"不介入"本身就是一种介入——它选择了维护现状。萨特终生实践介入：创办《现代》杂志、为法侬《全世界受苦的人》写序、支持阿尔及利亚独立、参与1968年五月风暴。介入是自由的最高表达，而非对自由的限制。

Intellectuals, writers, and artists must assume political and moral responsibility within their concrete historical situation. Writing is action (écrire, c'est agir) — words are "loaded pistols" (des pistolets chargés), every utterance casts force into the world. "Not engaging" is itself engagement — it chooses to preserve the status quo. Sartre practiced engagement throughout his life: founding *Les Temps Modernes*, writing the preface to Fanon's *The Wretched of the Earth*, supporting Algerian independence, participating in May 1968. Engagement is freedom's highest expression, not its limitation.

## 思想脉络 / Intellectual Trajectory

### 早期（1905–1942）：现象学的习得与文学转化 / Early: Phenomenological Apprenticeship and Literary Transformation

留学柏林（1933–1934），研读胡塞尔与海德格尔，将现象学方法引入法国。发表《自我的超越》（*La Transcendance de l'Ego*, 1936）、《恶心》（*La Nausée*, 1938）、《墙》（*Le Mur*, 1939）。《恶心》中洛根丁（Roquentin）面对栗子树根的体验——物的偶然性、多余性（de trop）——是对"存在先于本质"的文学预演。这一时期的萨特在哲学与文学之间建立了独特的"现象学小说"风格：用第一人称叙事呈现意识对存在的直接体验，同时保持哲学分析的严格性。胡塞尔的意向性（intentionnalité）概念——意识总是"对某物的意识"——成为萨特这一时期的核心方法论工具。

Studied in Berlin (1933–1934) under Husserl and Heidegger, introducing phenomenological method to France. Published *The Transcendence of the Ego* (1936), *Nausea* (1938), *The Wall* (1939). Roquentin's experience in *Nausea* before the chestnut tree root — the contingency and superfluity (de trop) of things — is a literary preview of "existence precedes essence." In this period Sartre established a unique "phenomenological novel" style: first-person narrative presenting consciousness's direct experience of being while maintaining the rigour of philosophical analysis. Husserl's concept of intentionality (intentionnalité) — consciousness is always "consciousness of something" — became Sartre's central methodological tool.

### 成熟期（1943–1956）：存在论体系的建立与公共介入 / Mature: Ontological System and Public Engagement

《存在与虚无》（*L'Être et le Néant*, 1943）建立"自在存在/自为存在/为他存在"的存在论框架。战后《存在主义是一种人道主义》（1946）使存在主义成为公共思潮。创办《现代》杂志，介入冷战政治、反殖民运动。《什么是文学？》（1948）系统阐述介入文学理论。这一时期是萨特哲学影响力最大的阶段。

*Being and Nothingness* (1943) establishes the "being-in-itself / being-for-itself / being-for-others" ontological framework. The post-war *Existentialism Is a Humanism* (1946) makes existentialism a public intellectual current. Founded *Les Temps Modernes*, engaging Cold War politics and anti-colonial movements. *What Is Literature?* (1948) systematically elaborates committed literature theory. This is the period of Sartre's greatest philosophical influence.

### 晚期（1957–1980）：马克思主义综合与政治实践 / Late: Marxist Synthesis and Political Practice

《辩证理性批判》（*Critique de la raison dialectique*, 1960）试图调和存在主义与马克思主义，提出"前进-逆溯方法"和"融合群体"概念。1964年拒绝诺贝尔文学奖，出版自传体作品《词语》（*Les Mots*）。支持阿尔及利亚独立、反越战、1968年五月风暴。晚期萨特面对的核心挑战是：如何在不取消个体自由的前提下解释历史的结构性和集体性？《辩证理性批判》第二卷（未完成）和关于福楼拜的三卷本《家庭的白痴》（*L'Idiot de la famille*, 1971–1972）是他对这些问题的持续探索。晚年双目近乎失明，但仍坚持口述工作，直至1980年去世。

*Critique of Dialectical Reason* (1960) attempts to reconcile existentialism and Marxism, proposing the "progressive-regressive method" and "group-in-fusion" concept. In 1964 he declined the Nobel Prize and published the autobiographical *Words* (*Les Mots*). Supported Algerian independence, anti-Vietnam-War movements, May 1968. The core challenge for the late Sartre: how to explain historical structure and collectivity without cancelling individual freedom? The unfinished second volume of *Critique of Dialectical Reason* and the three-volume study of Flaubert, *The Family Idiot* (*L'Idiot de la famille*, 1971–1972), represent his sustained exploration of these questions. Nearly blind in his final years, he continued dictating work until his death in 1980.

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

### 政治哲学与介入理论 / Political Philosophy and Engagement Theory

萨特的介入理论直接影响了当代关于知识分子公共角色的讨论。从爱德华·萨义德（Edward Said）的《知识分子论》到诺姆·乔姆斯基（Noam Chomsky）的公共介入，萨特开创的"哲学家-公共知识分子"模式至今仍是学术介入的标杆。在"后真相"时代，萨特提出的问题——知识分子是否有权介入公共事务——比以往任何时候都更加紧迫。

Sartre's engagement theory directly influences contemporary discussions of the intellectual's public role. From Edward Said's *Representations of the Intellectual* to Noam Chomsky's public engagement, the "philosopher-public intellectual" model pioneered by Sartre remains a benchmark. In the "post-truth" era, the question Sartre raised — whether intellectuals have the right to engage in public affairs — is more urgent than ever.

### 文学批评与叙事伦理 / Literary Criticism and Narrative Ethics

萨特的"介入文学"理论和现象学小说实践，影响了从加缪的《局外人》到米兰·昆德拉（Milan Kundera）的"小说作为思想"的整个二十世纪文学-哲学传统。在当代，萨特的方法论为"文学哲学"（literary philosophy）和"叙事伦理"（narrative ethics）领域提供了经典范例——小说不仅是故事的载体，更是思想实验的场所。

Sartre's "committed literature" theory and phenomenological novel practice influenced the entire twentieth-century literature-philosophy tradition from Camus's *The Stranger* to Milan Kundera's "the novel as thought." In contemporary scholarship, Sartre's methodology provides a classical model for "literary philosophy" and "narrative ethics" — the novel as not merely a vehicle for story but a site of thought-experimentation.

### 心理学与存在主义疗法 / Psychology and Existentialist Therapy

欧文·亚隆（Irvin Yalom）将萨特的自由/责任/焦虑/死亡框架发展为存在主义心理治疗的核心范式。"你无法选择发生在身上的事，但你可以选择如何面对它"——这一在当代心理治疗中广泛引用的原则，是萨特"处境中的自由"的通俗版本。维克多·弗兰克尔（Viktor Frankl）的意义疗法（logotherapy）与萨特的[处境](概念/处境.md)理论共享同一逻辑。

Irvin Yalom developed Sartre's freedom/responsibility/anguish/death framework into the core paradigm of existential psychotherapy. "You cannot choose what happens to you, but you can choose how you face it" — this principle widely cited in contemporary psychotherapy is the popular version of Sartre's "freedom in situation." Viktor Frankl's logotherapy shares the same logic as Sartre's [situation](概念/处境.md) theory.

### 教育哲学与自主学习 / Educational Philosophy and Self-Directed Learning

"人是他自己造就的"——这一命题成为自主学习教育（self-directed learning）的哲学根基。保罗·弗莱雷（Paulo Freire）的"被压迫者教育学"直接受萨特处境理论启发：教育不是灌输抽象知识，而是帮助学习者认识自己的处境并在处境中发现自由行动的可能。卡罗尔·德韦克（Carol Dweck）的成长型思维（growth mindset）与"存在先于本质"共享同一逻辑：能力不是固定的"本质"。

"Man is nothing other than what he makes of himself" — this proposition becomes the philosophical foundation of self-directed learning. Paulo Freire's *Pedagogy of the Oppressed* is directly inspired by Sartre's situation theory: education is not the transmission of abstract knowledge but helping learners recognize their situation and discover possibilities for free action within it. Carol Dweck's growth mindset shares the same logic as "existence precedes essence": ability is not a fixed "essence."

### 社交媒体与数字注视 / Social Media and the Digital Gaze

萨特的注视理论在社交媒体时代获得了前所未有的应用场域。社交媒体的点赞、评论、浏览记录是[注视](概念/注视.md)的数字化形态——自我的形象被量化为"被多少人认可"。"人设"（persona）是数字时代的[自欺](概念/自欺.md)：把精心构建的表演性身份等同于自己，逃避"我是谁"的真实追问。网络暴力是施虐（sadisme）的匿名化形态。萨特的[为他存在](概念/为他存在.md)理论为分析数字时代的身份焦虑、"取消文化"和算法推荐机制提供了深刻的哲学框架。

Sartre's look theory finds unprecedented application in the social media era. Likes, comments, and view counts are the digitized forms of the [look](概念/注视.md) — the self's image is quantified as "how many approve of me." "Persona management" is digital-age [bad faith](概念/自欺.md): treating the carefully constructed performative identity as oneself, evading the real question "who am I?" Cyberbullying is the anonymized form of sadism. Sartre's [being-for-others](概念/为他存在.md) theory provides a profound philosophical framework for analyzing digital-age identity anxiety, "cancel culture," and algorithmic recommendation mechanisms.

### 后殖民研究与AI伦理 / Postcolonial Studies and AI Ethics

萨特为法侬（Frantz Fanon）《全世界受苦的人》写序，其反殖民立场直接影响后殖民理论。注视理论被法侬发展为"白人的注视"对黑人的客体化分析。在AI伦理领域，"存在先于本质"是否适用于AI？机器有"自为存在"吗？这些问题正进入AI哲学的核心讨论。

Sartre wrote the preface to Fanon's *The Wretched of the Earth*; his anti-colonial stance directly influenced postcolonial theory. The look theory was developed by Fanon into an analysis of "the white look's" objectification of Black people. In AI ethics: does "existence precedes essence" apply to AI? Can machines have "being-for-itself"? These questions are entering the core of AI philosophy discussions.

## 常见误读 / Common Misunderstandings

### 误读一："存在先于本质意味着人可以为所欲为" / "Existence precedes essence means man can do whatever he wants"

**纠正**：萨特明确说自由伴随绝对责任，选择自己就是选择全人类的形象。自由不是"什么都可以"（tout est permis），而是"每一个选择都由你全权负责"。存在主义是最彻底的责任伦理——你在选择自己的同时也在为全人类立法。

**Correction**: Sartre explicitly states that freedom accompanies absolute responsibility; choosing oneself is choosing the image of all humanity. Freedom is not "anything goes" but "every choice is your full responsibility." Existentialism is the most thoroughgoing responsibility ethic — in choosing yourself you simultaneously legislate for all humanity.

### 误读二："存在主义是虚无主义" / "Existentialism is nihilism"

**纠正**：萨特的"虚无"（néant）是意识的结构，不是"人生无意义"；存在主义恰恰强调人必须创造意义，是最彻底的人道主义。虚无是创造意义的条件（因为没有预先的本质），不是意义的否定。

**Correction**: Sartre's "nothingness" (néant) is the structure of consciousness, not "life is meaningless"; existentialism precisely emphasizes that man must create meaning — it is the most thoroughgoing humanism. Nothingness is the condition for creating meaning (because there is no pre-given essence), not the negation of meaning.

### 误读三："'他人即地狱'意味着萨特厌世" / "'Hell is other people' means Sartre is a misanthrope"

**纠正**：这句话出自戏剧《禁闭》，描述的是三个亡灵互相客体化、无法逃脱的主体间性结构——而非对人类关系的否定。萨特描述的是主体间关系的**结构性困境**：在他人的注视下，我失去了对自己形象的控制权。这不意味着他人是坏的，而意味着人际关系需要持续的努力和清醒的意识。

**Correction**: This line comes from the play *No Exit* (*Huis clos*), describing three dead souls mutually objectifying each other in an inescapable intersubjective structure — not a negation of human relations. Sartre describes the **structural predicament** of intersubjective relations: under the other's look, I lose control over my own image. This does not mean others are bad but that human relations require sustained effort and lucid consciousness.

### 误读四："萨特的自由观忽视了结构性压迫" / "Sartre's freedom concept ignores structural oppression"

**纠正**：波伏娃在《第二性》中正是用萨特的框架分析压迫——压迫不取消自由（这在存在论上不可能），但使自由的代价更加高昂。萨特的晚期著作（特别是《辩证理性批判》）深入分析了阶级、殖民和制度对处境的系统性扭曲。处境不是自由的对立面，而是自由运作的场域。

**Correction**: Beauvoir in *The Second Sex* uses precisely Sartre's framework to analyze oppression — oppression does not cancel freedom (which is ontologically impossible) but makes the cost of freedom far higher. Sartre's later works (especially *Critique of Dialectical Reason*) deeply analyze the systematic distortion of situation by class, colonialism, and institutions. Situation is not freedom's opposite but the field in which freedom operates.

### 误读五："萨特只是哲学家，文学只是副业" / "Sartre is only a philosopher; his literature is secondary"

**纠正**：萨特的文学作品——《恶心》《禁闭》《自由之路》三部曲、《词语》——不是哲学的"附属品"，而是哲学思想的**另一种表达形式**。《恶心》以第一人称叙事呈现了偶然性的体验，其哲学深度不亚于《存在与虚无》的任何章节。《禁闭》以三个亡灵的关系呈现了主体间性的冲突结构，其精确性不亚于任何学术论文。萨特认为文学与哲学是介入的两种互补形式——哲学提供概念的严格性，文学提供体验的直接性。

**Correction**: Sartre's literary works — *Nausea*, *No Exit*, the *Roads to Freedom* trilogy, *Words* — are not "appendices" to his philosophy but **another form of expressing philosophical thought**. *Nausea*'s first-person narrative presents the experience of contingency with a philosophical depth rivaling any chapter of *Being and Nothingness*. *No Exit* presents intersubjective conflict through three dead souls with a precision rivaling any academic paper. Sartre held that literature and philosophy are complementary forms of engagement — philosophy provides conceptual rigour, literature provides experiential immediacy.

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

### 萨特 × 佛教空性 / Sartre × Buddhist Śūnyatā

萨特的"虚无"（néant）与佛教的"空性"（śūnyatā）在结构上高度对应——两者都否定一个固定的、实体化的自我基础——但指向截然不同的方向。萨特说"意识是虚无"意味着"无根基"，令人焦虑，但正因如此人是自由的；佛教说"诸法皆空"意味着"无自性"（niḥsvabhāva），旨在解脱。萨特的无通向自由与行动，佛教的空通向智慧与涅槃。唯识学的"遍计所执"（parikalpita）与萨特的"自欺"（mauvaise foi）在结构上对应：两者都描述了人在依他起的现象上投射实体化自性的倾向。

Sartre's "nothingness" (néant) and Buddhist "emptiness" (śūnyatā) are structurally highly correspondent — both deny a fixed, substantialized basis for the self — but point in radically different directions. Sartre says "consciousness is nothingness" meaning "groundlessness," anxiety-inducing but therefore liberating; Buddhism says "all dharmas are empty" meaning "without self-nature" (niḥsvabhāva), aiming at liberation. Sartre's nothingness leads to freedom and action; Buddhist emptiness leads to wisdom and Nirvana. Yogācāra's "parikalpita" and Sartre's "bad faith" are structurally correspondent: both describe the tendency to project substantialized self-nature onto dependently arisen phenomena.

更深层的对照在于：萨特的焦虑（angoisse）与佛教的苦（duḥkha）都描述了人类存在的根本不安。但萨特认为焦虑是自由的条件——没有焦虑就没有对自由的觉知；佛教则认为苦是执着的产物——消除执着即可灭苦。萨特的"虚无化"（néantisation）——意识不断否定当下的过程——与佛教的"刹那生灭"（kṣaṇika）在现象描述上相似，但萨特赋予它存在论的积极意义（这是自由），佛教赋予它解脱论的意义（执着于连续性是苦的根源）。

A deeper parallel: Sartre's anguish (angoisse) and Buddhist suffering (duḥkha) both describe the fundamental unease of human existence. But Sartre holds anguish is the condition of freedom — without anguish there is no awareness of freedom; Buddhism holds suffering is the product of attachment — eliminating attachment eliminates suffering. Sartre's "néantisation" — consciousness's perpetual negation of the present — resembles Buddhist "momentariness" (kṣaṇika) at the descriptive level, but Sartre gives it positive ontological meaning (this is freedom) while Buddhism gives it soteriological meaning (attachment to continuity is suffering's root).

### 萨特 × 道家 / Sartre × Daoism

萨特的"自由"与道家的"无为"（wúwéi）形成有趣的对照。萨特主张自由要求积极的选择和行动——"人是他自己造就的"；道家则主张自由在于顺应自然之道（dào），不以人为的意志对抗天地的运行。萨特的自由是"不得不选择"的焦虑，道家的自由是"不选择而自然"的逍遥（xiāoyáo）。两者都追求自由，但对自由的实现路径截然相反：萨特走向介入与行动，道家走向退让与顺应。

Sartre's "freedom" and Daoism's "non-action" (wúwéi) form an illuminating contrast. Sartre holds that freedom demands active choice and action — "man is what he makes of himself"; Daoism holds that freedom lies in following the natural Way (dào), not opposing heaven-and-earth's operation with artificial will. Sartre's freedom is the anxiety of "having to choose"; Daoist freedom is the ease (xiāoyáo) of "not-choosing and being-natural." Both pursue freedom, but their paths to its realization are opposite: Sartre moves toward engagement and action, Daoism toward yielding and following.

更深一层：萨特的"自欺"（mauvaise foi）——人假装自己"不得不如此"以逃避自由——在道家看来恰恰是人类文明的通病。庄子（Zhuāngzǐ）批评儒家的礼仪制度和人为规范是一种集体性的"自欺"——人们用社会角色和道德标签掩盖了自己的自然本性。但萨特的解决方向与道家相反：萨特要求人更清醒地面对自由并做出更多的选择，道家则要求人放下人为的选择和判断，回归"无为"的自然状态。萨特视"介入"为自由的实现，道家视"无为"为自由的回归。

At a deeper level: Sartre's "bad faith" — man pretending "I must do this" to evade freedom — is precisely what Daoism sees as the endemic illness of human civilization. Zhuāngzǐ criticizes Confucian ritual systems and artificial norms as a collective form of "bad faith" — people use social roles and moral labels to mask their natural disposition. But Sartre's solution runs opposite to Daoism: Sartre demands that one confront freedom more lucidly and make more choices; Daoism demands that one release artificial choices and judgments, returning to "wu-wei's" natural state. Sartre sees "engagement" as freedom's realization; Daoism sees "non-action" as freedom's return.

### 萨特 × 吠檀多 / Sartre × Vedānta

萨特的"自为存在"（pour-soi）——意识作为虚无、作为非实体——与吠檀多（Vedānta）哲学中的"阿特曼"（Ātman）概念形成深刻对照。吠檀多认为个体意识的最深层是"梵"（Brahman）——一个无限的、不可分割的绝对存在；萨特认为意识的最深层是虚无——一个没有根基的、不可还原的"无"。吠檀多的"摩耶"（māyā）——现象世界的虚幻性——与萨特的"自欺"在功能上对应：两者都描述了人误认自身真实处境的倾向。但吠檀多的出路是"认识梵"（jñāna），萨特的出路是"承担自由"。

Sartre's "being-for-itself" (pour-soi) — consciousness as nothingness, as non-substance — forms a profound contrast with Vedānta's concept of "Ātman." Vedānta holds that the deepest layer of individual consciousness is "Brahman" — an infinite, indivisible absolute being; Sartre holds that the deepest layer of consciousness is nothingness — a groundless, irreducible "no-thing." Vedānta's "māyā" — the illusoriness of the phenomenal world — corresponds functionally to Sartre's "bad faith": both describe the tendency to misrecognize one's true condition. But Vedānta's way out is "knowing Brahman" (jñāna); Sartre's way out is "assuming freedom."

更深层的对照在于：吠檀多的不二论（Advaita Vedānta）——以商羯罗（Śaṅkara）为代表——主张"梵我合一"（tat tvam asi），即个体自我（jīva）与宇宙本体（Brahman）在本体论上同一。萨特则坚持意识的个体性和不可逾越的主体间裂隙——不存在任何"合一"的可能。对萨特而言，意识永远与自身有裂隙（pour-soi），永远与他者有冲突（pour-autrui）；对吠檀多而言，这一切裂隙和冲突都是摩耶的产物，在最终的解脱中被超越。

A deeper contrast: Vedānta's non-dualism (Advaita Vedānta) — represented by Śaṅkara — holds "tat tvam asi": the individual self (jīva) and the cosmic absolute (Brahman) are ontologically identical. Sartre insists on consciousness's individuality and the unbridgeable intersubjective rift — no "union" is possible. For Sartre, consciousness is always fissured within itself (pour-soi), always in conflict with others (pour-autrui); for Vedānta, all fissure and conflict are products of māyā, transcended in final liberation.
