# 结构主义对萨特的批评 / Structuralist Critique of Sartre

> "人是一种发明，其最新样本正在我们的眼前消亡。"
> "L'homme est une invention dont l'archéologie montre aisément la date récente. Et peut-être la fin prochaine."
> — Foucault, *Les Mots et les choses* (1966)

---

## 概述 / Overview

1960年代，法国思想界经历了一场从存在主义到结构主义的范式转移。米歇尔·福柯（Michel Foucault, 1926–1984）和克洛德·列维-斯特劳斯（Claude Lévi-Strauss, 1908–2009）从不同方向对萨特的主体哲学发起了根本性批判：**萨特将"主体"和"自由"视为哲学的出发点，而结构主义认为"主体"本身就是权力、语言和文化的建构产物**。这一批判不仅是对萨特个人的批评，更是对整个西方"主体哲学"传统的挑战。

In the 1960s, French thought underwent a paradigm shift from existentialism to structuralism. Michel Foucault (1926–1984) and Claude Lévi-Strauss (1908–2009) launched fundamental critiques of Sartre's philosophy of the subject from different directions: **Sartre takes "subject" and "freedom" as philosophy's starting point, while structuralism holds that the "subject" is itself a product of power, language, and cultural construction**. This critique challenges not only Sartre personally but the entire Western "philosophy of the subject" tradition.

---

## 批评的核心论点 / Core Arguments

### 批评一：福柯——"人之死"与主体的考古学 / Critique 1: Foucault — "Death of Man" and the Archaeology of the Subject

福柯在《词与物》（*Les Mots et les choses*, 1966）中提出了著名的"人之死"论题："人"作为一个认识论对象，不过是18世纪末知识型（épistémè）转换的产物——在古典知识型中，"人"根本不存在。萨特的存在主义将这个历史的、偶然的建构物——"人"——当作了哲学的先验基础，这在福柯看来是一种深刻的时代错误。

Foucault's *The Order of Things* (1966) advances the famous "death of man" thesis: "man" as an epistemological object is merely a product of the late-eighteenth-century epistemic shift — in the classical episteme, "man" simply did not exist. Sartre's existentialism takes this historical, contingent construct — "man" — as philosophy's transcendental foundation, which for Foucault is a profound anachronism.

福柯进一步论证：萨特所说的"自由主体"实际上被**权力-知识**（pouvoir-savoir）的网络所渗透和建构。疯癫、监狱、性——这些不是"自由主体"面对的"处境"，而是权力制造主体的机制。主体不是在权力"之前"存在的自由实体，主体恰恰是权力的**效果**。

Foucault further argues: Sartre's "free subject" is in fact permeated and constructed by **power-knowledge** (pouvoir-savoir) networks. Madness, the prison, sexuality — these are not "situations" that a "free subject" faces, but mechanisms by which power produces subjects. The subject does not exist "before" power as a free entity; the subject is precisely power's **effect**.

### 批评二：列维-斯特劳斯——结构先于主体 / Critique 2: Lévi-Strauss — Structure Precedes Subject

列维-斯特劳斯在《忧郁的热带》（*Tristes tropiques*, 1955）中对萨特的"前进-逆溯方法"（méthode progressive-régressive）提出了尖锐批评。萨特的方法试图通过个体生命史来理解历史——但列维-斯特劳斯认为，真正决定人类行为和文化形态的不是个体选择，而是**深层的、无意识的结构**。

Lévi-Strauss sharply criticized Sartre's "progressive-regressive method" in *Tristes tropiques* (1955). Sartre's method attempts to understand history through individual life-histories — but Lévi-Strauss holds that what truly determines human behavior and cultural forms is not individual choice but **deep, unconscious structures**.

亲属关系、神话叙事、分类系统——这些文化现象遵循的是结构性的逻辑（二元对立、转换规则），而非主体的"自由选择"。列维-斯特劳斯的名言"结构主义的目标不是人，而是人的消解"（Le but ultime des sciences humaines n'est pas de constituer l'homme, mais de le dissoudre），直接与萨特的"存在主义是一种人道主义"命题相对立。

Kinship systems, mythological narratives, classification systems — these cultural phenomena follow structural logic (binary oppositions, transformation rules), not the "free choice" of subjects. Lévi-Strauss's famous dictum that "the ultimate goal of the human sciences is not to constitute man but to dissolve him" stands in direct opposition to Sartre's proposition that "existentialism is a humanism."

### 批评三：知识分子模式的对立 / Critique 3: Opposing Models of the Intellectual

福柯在《真理与权力》（*Vérité et pouvoir*, 1977）中提出了"特殊知识分子"（l'intellectuel spécifique）概念，明确挑战萨特的"普遍知识分子"（l'intellectuel universel）模式。萨特以哲学家身份介入一切公共事务——从阿尔及利亚战争到越战——声称以"人类自由"的普遍价值为基础发言。福柯认为这种姿态在知识论上是可疑的：**没有任何人能够超越自身的权力位置而站在"普遍"的立场上**。

Foucault's *Truth and Power* (1977) proposes the "specific intellectual" (l'intellectuel spécifique) concept, explicitly challenging Sartre's "universal intellectual" model. Sartre engaged in all public affairs as a philosopher — from the Algerian War to Vietnam — claiming to speak from "human freedom's" universal values. Foucault holds this posture epistemologically suspect: **no one can transcend their own power-position to stand on "universal" ground**.

### 批评四：历史目的论的否定 / Critique 4: Rejection of Historical Teleology

结构主义者普遍反对萨特在《辩证理性批判》中保留的历史目的论色彩。萨特仍然相信历史有一个"方向"——即从异化走向自由的解放——而结构主义认为历史没有目的，只有结构的转换和断裂。福柯的"知识考古学"（archéologie du savoir）正是要取代萨特的"历史辩证法"。

Structuralists universally oppose the historical teleology retained in Sartre's *Critique of Dialectical Reason*. Sartre still believed history has a "direction" — from alienation toward liberation — while structuralism holds that history has no telos, only structural transformations and ruptures. Foucault's "archaeology of knowledge" is precisely intended to replace Sartre's "historical dialectics."

## 对批评的评价 / Evaluation

### 批评的力量 / Strengths

1. **主体批判的深度**：结构主义对主体的解构揭示了权力和话语对"自由"的深层渗透，这一洞察被后结构主义、后现代主义和后殖民理论广泛继承
2. **方法论的拓展**：考古学和谱系学方法为分析社会现象提供了萨特"前进-逆溯方法"之外的替代方案
3. **知识分子角色的反思**：福柯对"普遍知识分子"的批评促使后来的公共知识分子更加审慎地对待自身的权力位置

### 批评的局限 / Limitations

1. **结构主义的困境**：如果主体完全被结构建构，那么"批判结构"的主体从何而来？结构主义在解释"社会变革如何可能"时面临困难
2. **萨特的晚期回应**：萨特在晚期著作中对结构和制度的分析（"实践-惰性"、"制度"）表明他并非忽视结构因素
3. **后结构主义的自我修正**：福柯晚年从考古学转向"自我技术"（technologies de soi），某种程度上回到了萨特式的主体关切
