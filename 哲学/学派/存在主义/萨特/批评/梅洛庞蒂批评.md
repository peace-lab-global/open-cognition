# 梅洛-庞蒂对萨特的批评 / Merleau-Ponty's Critique of Sartre

> "萨特的意识是纯粹的空无——但一个没有任何厚度的意识，如何能真正'在世'？"
> — 梅洛-庞蒂对萨特意识哲学的一般批评（概述）

---

## 概述 / Overview

莫里斯·梅洛-庞蒂（Maurice Merleau-Ponty, 1908–1961）是萨特最亲密也最深刻的哲学对话者。两人同为法国现象学传统的核心人物，同在巴黎高师求学，同办《现代》杂志——但从1945年的《知觉现象学》（*Phénoménologie de la perception*）开始，梅洛-庞蒂就对萨特的存在论框架提出了一系列根本性的批评。这些批评不是外部的否定，而是"内部的修正"——一个同路人试图将现象学推向更彻底的方向。

Maurice Merleau-Ponty (1908–1961) was Sartre's closest and most profound philosophical interlocutor. Both were central figures in the French phenomenological tradition, both studied at the École Normale Supérieure, both co-founded *Les Temps Modernes* — but from *Phenomenology of Perception* (1945) onwards, Merleau-Ponty advanced a series of fundamental criticisms of Sartre's ontological framework. These critiques are not external negations but "internal corrections" — a fellow traveler attempting to push phenomenology to a more thoroughgoing conclusion.

---

## 批评的核心论点 / Core Arguments

### 批评一：身心二元论的残余 / Critique 1: Residual Mind-Body Dualism

梅洛-庞蒂认为萨特的存在论——自在存在（物）与自为存在（意识）的二元划分——实质上复制了笛卡尔的身心二元论。在萨特的框架中，意识（自为）是纯粹的虚无和否定性，身体则被归入自在存在的领域——身体只是意识的"事实性"之一。梅洛-庞蒂论证：**身体既不是纯粹的物（自在），也不是纯粹的意识（自为），而是一种"第三存在"——身体-主体（corps-sujet）**。

Merleau-Ponty argues that Sartre's ontology — the binary division of being-in-itself (things) and being-for-itself (consciousness) — effectively replicates Cartesian mind-body dualism. In Sartre's framework, consciousness (for-itself) is pure nothingness and negativity, while the body is relegated to the domain of being-in-itself — the body is merely one aspect of consciousness's "facticity." Merleau-Ponty argues: **the body is neither pure thing (in-itself) nor pure consciousness (for-itself), but a "third being" — the body-subject (corps-sujet)**.

知觉不是意识对世界的"投射"，而是身体与世界的原初交织（chiasme, entrelacs）。我在"看"世界之前就已经"被世界所看"——这种身体层面的相互性，在萨特的二元框架中无法被描述。

Perception is not consciousness's "projection" onto the world but the originary intertwining (chiasme, entrelacs) of body and world. Before I "see" the world I am already "seen by the world" — this bodily reciprocity cannot be described within Sartre's binary framework.

### 批评二：处境的"意识化" / Critique 2: The Over-Intellectualization of Situation

梅洛-庞蒂承认萨特的[处境](../概念/处境.md)概念的重要性，但认为萨特将处境"意识化"了——在萨特的分析中，处境总是已经被意识的"计划"（projet）所照亮和赋予意义。梅洛-庞蒂认为存在一种**前反思的、身体层面的处境**——它先于意识的明确投射，先于"计划"的清晰轮廓。

Merleau-Ponty acknowledges the importance of Sartre's [situation](../概念/处境.md) concept but argues that Sartre over-intellectualizes situation — in Sartre's analysis, situation is always already illuminated and given meaning by consciousness's "project" (projet). Merleau-Ponty argues for a **pre-reflective, bodily dimension of situation** — one that precedes consciousness's explicit projection, precedes the clear outline of the "project."

例如，我的身体姿势、肌肉紧张度、呼吸节奏——这些都构成我的"在世方式"，但它们不是意识的"选择"。萨特的自由概念忽视了身体层面的前意向性（fungierende Intentionalität），这种前意向性使一切明确的选择成为可能。

For example, my posture, muscle tension, breathing rhythm — all constitute my "way of being in the world," yet they are not consciousness's "choices." Sartre's freedom concept neglects the pre-intentionality (fungierende Intentionalität) at the bodily level that makes all explicit choice possible.

### 批评三：注视理论的过于悲观 / Critique 3: The Overly Pessimistic Account of the Look

梅洛-庞蒂同意[注视](../概念/注视.md)（le regard）是主体间关系的重要维度，但认为萨特将主体间关系完全还原为注视-冲突的模式是一种简化。梅洛-庞蒂提出**身体间性**（intercorporeité）概念：在我与他人的身体互动中——握手、共舞、对话——存在一种**前反思的主体间和谐**，它先于注视的客体化。

Merleau-Ponty agrees that the [look](../概念/注视.md) (le regard) is an important dimension of intersubjective relations, but argues that Sartre's reduction of intersubjectivity entirely to the look-conflict pattern is an oversimplification. Merleau-Ponty proposes the concept of **intercorporeity** (intercorporeité): in my bodily interaction with others — handshakes, dancing together, dialogue — there exists a **pre-reflective intersubjective harmony** that precedes the objectification of the look.

### 批评四：政治介入中的"超布尔什维克主义" / Critique 4: "Ultrabolshevism" in Political Engagement

1955年，梅洛-庞蒂在《辩证的历险》（*Les Aventures de la dialectique*）中对萨特的政治[介入](../概念/介入.md)提出了最尖锐的批评。他指责萨特在冷战期间对苏联的"同路人"（compagnon de route）立场是一种"超布尔什维克主义"（ultrabolshevisme）——萨特为了保持左翼的"纯粹性"而拒绝批判苏联的暴行，这在梅洛-庞蒂看来是一种政治上的[自欺](../概念/自欺.md)。

In 1955, *Adventures of the Dialectic* (*Les Aventures de la dialectique*) contained Merleau-Ponty's sharpest critique of Sartre's political [engagement](../概念/介入.md). He accused Sartre's Cold War "fellow traveler" (compagnon de route) stance of being an "ultrabolshevism" — Sartre's refusal to criticize Soviet atrocities in order to preserve the "purity" of the left was, for Merleau-Ponty, a form of political [bad faith](../概念/自欺.md).

这一批评直接导致了两人从1953年开始的政治分裂和私人疏远，直至梅洛-庞蒂1961年突然去世。

This critique directly led to their political divergence and personal estrangement from 1953 onwards, until Merleau-Ponty's sudden death in 1961.

## 对批评的评价 / Evaluation

### 批评的力量 / Strengths

1. **身体维度的补充**：梅洛-庞蒂的身体现象学确实弥补了萨特身心二元论的缺陷，后来的现象学传统（包括当代具身认知科学）普遍站在梅洛-庞蒂一边
2. **政治批评的合理性**：梅洛-庞蒂对萨特冷战立场的批评在 hindsight 中被证明是更有远见的——苏联的极权本质在赫鲁晓夫1956年秘密报告后已无法被合理辩护
3. **知觉优先性的论证**：知觉作为一切认识的根基，这一论证在当代认知科学中获得了大量支持

### 批评的局限 / Limitations

1. **萨特的回应**：萨特在《辩证理性批判》中回应了部分批评，特别是通过"实践-惰性"（pratico-inerte）概念来处理物质条件对自由的制约
2. **自由的不可还原性**：即使身体维度被充分承认，萨特的核心论点——人总是可以选择对自身处境的态度——仍然具有说服力
3. **梅洛-庞蒂的未完成**：梅洛-庞蒂1961年去世时，《可见与不可见》（*Le Visible et l'invisible*）尚未完成，他的"肉身本体论"（ontologie de la chair）未能充分展开
