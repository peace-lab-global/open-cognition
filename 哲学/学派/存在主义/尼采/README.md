# 尼采 · Friedrich Nietzsche

> "成为你自己。"
> *Werde, der du bist.*
> — Pindar, 尼采终身引用的箴言

---

## 基本信息 / Biographical Note

弗里德里希·威廉·尼采（Friedrich Wilhelm Nietzsche, 1844年10月15日–1900年8月25日），德国哲学家、古典语文学家、文化批评家，西方价值秩序最彻底的爆破手，也是存在主义、后结构主义和后现代哲学的共同先驱。他出生于普鲁士萨克森州勒肯（Röcken）一个路德宗牧师家庭，父亲卡尔·路德维希·尼采（Carl Ludwig Nietzsche）在他五岁时因脑疾去世——这一早年丧父的经历深刻塑造了尼采对基督教上帝和"天父"意象的复杂态度。

Friedrich Wilhelm Nietzsche (15 October 1844 – 25 August 1900) was a German philosopher, classical philologist, and cultural critic — the most thorough demolisher of Western value order and a shared precursor of existentialism, poststructuralism, and postmodern philosophy. Born in Röcken, Prussian Saxony, to a Lutheran pastor family, his father Carl Ludwig Nietzsche died of a brain ailment when Friedrich was five — an early loss that profoundly shaped Nietzsche's complex attitude toward the Christian God and the "father" image.

尼采先后在波恩大学和莱比锡大学学习古典语文学，师从弗里德里希·里奇尔（Friedrich Ritschl）。1865年在莱比锡一家旧书店偶然发现叔本华的《作为意志和表象的世界》，从此开启了与意志哲学的终生对话。1869年，年仅二十四岁的尼采被聘为巴塞尔大学古典语文学教授——该校史上最年轻的教授。在巴塞尔期间，他与理查德·瓦格纳（Richard Wagner）建立了深厚的友谊，又在1876年拜罗伊特音乐节后与其彻底决裂。

Nietzsche studied classical philology at Bonn and Leipzig under Friedrich Ritschl. In 1865, he stumbled upon Schopenhauer's *The World as Will and Representation* in a Leipzig bookshop, beginning a lifelong dialogue with will-philosophy. In 1869, at just twenty-four, he was appointed Professor of Classical Philology at Basel — the youngest ever. During his Basel years, he formed a deep friendship with Richard Wagner, rupturing definitively after the 1876 Bayreuth Festival.

1879年，尼采因严重的健康问题（头痛、眼疾、呕吐）辞去教职，此后十年间旅居瑞士、意大利和法国南部，以格言体和诗体写作完成了他最成熟的哲学著作。1889年1月3日，尼采在都灵街头目睹一匹马被鞭打，扑上去抱住马的脖子痛哭后精神崩溃——此后十一年在意想不到的精神失常中度过，由母亲和妹妹伊丽莎白照料，1900年在魏玛去世。妹妹伊丽莎白·福斯特-尼采（Elisabeth Förster-Nietzsche）篡改遗稿，将其编纂为支持德国民族主义与反犹主义的文本，为纳粹利用埋下伏笔——这一篡改直到考夫曼（Walter Kaufmann, 1950）和科利-蒙提纳里（Colli-Montinari, 1967–）校勘本的出版才被系统澄清。

In 1879, severe health problems forced Nietzsche to resign. For the next decade he travelled through Switzerland, Italy, and southern France, writing his most mature works in aphoristic and poetic style. On 3 January 1889 in Turin, witnessing a horse being whipped, Nietzsche collapsed in tears embracing its neck — spending the remaining eleven years in mental derangement, cared for by his mother and sister Elisabeth, dying in Weimar in 1900. Elisabeth Förster-Nietzsche falsified his manuscripts to support German nationalism and antisemitism — a falsification systematically corrected only by Kaufmann (1950) and the Colli-Montinari critical edition (1967–).

## 一句话定位 / One-Line Positioning

**西方价值秩序最彻底的爆破手——以"上帝已死"诊断现代性的根本危机，以"权力意志"揭示生命的内在驱力，以"永恒轮回"与"超人"提出肯定生命的极端方案，对整个西方道德、宗教、哲学传统发动了最彻底的"重估一切价值"（Umwertung aller Werte）。**

**The most thorough demolisher of the Western value order — diagnosing modernity's fundamental crisis through "God is dead," revealing life's inner drive through "will to power," proposing life-affirmation's extreme program through "eternal return" and "Übermensch," and launching the most radical "revaluation of all values" (Umwertung aller Werte) against the entire Western moral, religious, and philosophical tradition.**

## 六大核心命题 / Six Core Theses

### 1. 权力意志 / Wille zur Macht

→ 详见 [概念/权力意志.md](概念/权力意志.md)

生命的基本驱力不是叔本华的"求生意志"（Wille zum Leben），也不是功利主义的"趋乐避苦"，而是**扩张、克服阻力、自我超越的权力意志**。一切存在者都是权力意志的某种配置（Konfiguration）——从细胞到国家，从艺术到哲学。最高形式的权力意志是**自我克服**（Selbstüberwindung）——不是征服他人，而是征服自己的惯性、偏见和恐惧。

Life's fundamental drive is neither Schopenhauer's "will-to-live" (Wille zum Leben) nor utilitarian "pleasure-seeking and pain-avoidance," but the **will to power** — expansion, overcoming resistance, self-transcendence. All beings are configurations (Konfiguration) of will to power. The highest form of will to power is **self-overcoming** (Selbstüberwindung) — not conquering others but conquering one's own habits, prejudices, and fears.

### 2. 永恒轮回 / Ewige Wiederkunft

→ 详见 [概念/永恒轮回.md](概念/永恒轮回.md)

如果宇宙中的一切将无限次地以相同顺序重复，你是否仍愿意如此生活？永恒轮回是**肯定生命的极端考验**——不是宇宙论假说，而是存在论的伦理学试金石。能回答"我愿意"的人，达到了**命运之爱**（Amor Fati）：不但忍受必然之物，而且热爱它。

If everything in the universe will recur infinitely in the same sequence, would you still will to live thus? The eternal return is the **extreme test of life-affirmation** — not a cosmological hypothesis but an existential-ethical touchstone. Those who can answer "I will it" have attained **amor fati**: not merely enduring necessity but loving it.

### 3. 上帝已死 / Gott ist tot

→ 详见 [概念/上帝已死.md](概念/上帝已死.md)

"上帝已死"并非无神论口号，而是**对欧洲文明根本事件的诊断**：基督教信仰作为西方价值体系的基石已经崩塌，由此引发的虚无主义（Nihilismus）将笼罩欧洲两百年。问题不是"神是否存在"，而是"最高价值自行废黜了"——同时也打开了**价值重估**的可能性。

"God is dead" is not an atheistic slogan but a **diagnosis of the fundamental European event**: Christian faith as the cornerstone of the Western value system has collapsed, and the nihilism (Nihilismus) it triggers will engulf Europe for two centuries. The question is not "does God exist" but "the highest values have devalued themselves" — simultaneously opening the possibility of **revaluation of all values**.

### 4. 超人 / Übermensch

→ 详见 [概念/超人.md](概念/超人.md)

超人是**自我立法、自我超越的存在样态**——不是生物学上的"更高级物种"，而是能在大地上创造自身意义的人。人不是目的，而是通向超人的**桥梁**（"人是一根绳索，悬挂在动物与超人之间"）。超人的对立面是**末人**（der letzte Mensch）——追求安逸、安全、平等、无伟大渴望的现代消费社会的人。

The Übermensch is a **self-legislating, self-overcoming mode of existence** — not a biological "higher species" but one who creates meaning on this earth. Man is not an end but a **bridge** to the Übermensch. The Übermensch's opposite is the **last man** (der letzte Mensch) — the consumer-society individual pursuing comfort, security, equality, without great aspiration.

### 5. 主人道德与奴隶道德 / Herrenmoral und Sklavenmoral

→ 详见 [概念/主人道德与奴隶道德.md](概念/主人道德与奴隶道德.md)

道德不是普遍的，而是有两种起源：**主人道德**从自身的丰盈中说出"好"（gut），奴隶道德从对主人的**怨恨**（Ressentiment）中造出"善"与"恶"的对立。基督教道德是奴隶道德的最大胜利。这一分析通过**谱系学**（Genealogie）方法展开——不追问"善是什么"，而追问"善是如何被制造出来的"。

Morality is not universal but has two origins: **master morality** affirms "good" from its own abundance; slave morality fabricates the "good-evil" opposition from **ressentiment** against masters. Christian morality is slave morality's greatest triumph. This analysis unfolds through the **genealogical** (Genealogie) method — asking not "what is good?" but "how was good manufactured?"

### 6. 命运之爱与酒神精神 / Amor Fati und das Dionysische

→ 详见 [概念/命运之爱.md](概念/命运之爱.md) · [概念/酒神精神.md](概念/酒神精神.md) · [概念/日神精神.md](概念/日神精神.md)

**命运之爱**（Amor Fati）是尼采哲学的伦理理想——不被动忍受，而是**热爱**生命中发生的一切。**酒神精神**（das Dionysische）是生命原始力量的象征——醉（Rausch）、狂喜（Ekstase）、个体化的瓦解、痛苦与狂乐的不可分离。**日神精神**（das Apollinische）代表梦、形式、个体化、美的幻象。希腊悲剧诞生于二者的结合，苏格拉底理性主义以理性扼杀了酒神。在晚期，酒神精神被提升为**存在论概念**——对生命全体的肯定。

**Amor fati** is Nietzsche's ethical ideal — not passive endurance but **loving** everything that happens in life. The **Dionysian** symbolizes life's primordial force — intoxication (Rausch), ecstasy, dissolution of individuation, inseparability of pain and bliss. The **Apollonian** represents dream, form, individuation, beautiful illusion. Greek tragedy was born of their union; Socratic rationalism killed the Dionysian with reason. In the late period, the Dionysian is elevated to an **ontological concept** — affirmation of life as a whole.

## 思想脉络 / Intellectual Trajectory

→ 详见 [时间线.md](时间线.md)

### 早期（1872–1876）：悲剧诞生 / Early Period: Birth of Tragedy

《悲剧的诞生》（*Die Geburt der Tragödie aus dem Geiste der Musik*, 1872）以酒神精神（das Dionysische）与日神精神（das Apollinische）的张力解释希腊悲剧的诞生与衰亡。尼采认为希腊悲剧的伟大在于两种力量的完美结合：酒神的原始生命力通过日神的造型形式获得表达。苏格拉底-柏拉图理性主义的"罪过"在于以逻辑（Logos）扼杀了酒神精神，由此开启了西方文明两千年的理性专制。这一时期深受[叔本华](../../悲观主义/叔本华/README.md)意志哲学与[瓦格纳](对话/与瓦格纳关系.md)音乐戏剧的影响。《不合时宜的沉思》（*Unzeitgemäße Betrachtungen*, 1873–1876）四篇论文批判德国文化、历史意识的过剩、以及叔本华和瓦格纳作为"真正的教育家"的理想。

*The Birth of Tragedy* (1872) explains the birth and decline of Greek tragedy through the tension between the Dionysian and the Apollonian. Nietzsche holds that Greek tragedy's greatness lies in the perfect union of the two forces: the Dionysian's primordial life-force finding expression through Apollonian form. The "guilt" of Socratic-Platonic rationalism lies in killing the Dionysian with logic (Logos), inaugurating two millennia of rational tyranny in Western civilization. The *Untimely Meditations* (1873–1876) critique German culture, the excess of historical consciousness, and present Schopenhauer and Wagner as ideals of "true educators."

### 中期（1878–1882）：自由精神 / Middle Period: Free Spirit

1876年拜罗伊特音乐节的幻灭标志着与瓦格纳的决裂。《人性的，太人性的》（*Menschliches, Allzumenschliches*, 1878）采用格言体（Aphorismus），转向心理学-谱系学方法，以冷静的手术刀解剖一切"崇高的"道德和情感。《朝霞》（*Morgenröthe*, 1881）继续解构道德偏见。《快乐的科学》（*Die fröhliche Wissenschaft*, 1882）达到了中期思想的顶峰——"上帝已死"首次出现在第125节"疯子"寓言中，永恒轮回在第341节以"最大重负"的形式被提出。这一时期以"自由精神"（freier Geist）自居，用锤子敲击一切偶像。

The disillusionment at the 1876 Bayreuth Festival marked the Wagner rupture. *Human, All Too Human* (1878) adopted the aphoristic form (Aphorismus), turning to psychological-genealogical method, dissecting all "sublime" morals and sentiments with a cold scalpel. *Daybreak* (1881) continued deconstructing moral prejudices. *The Gay Science* (1882) reached the middle period's zenith — "God is dead" first appears in §125's "madman" fable, and eternal return is posed as the "greatest weight" in §341. Nietzsche styled himself a "free spirit" (freier Geist), hammering all idols.

### 晚期（1883–1888）：成熟体系 / Late Period: Mature System

《查拉图斯特拉如是说》（*Also sprach Zarathustra*, 1883–1885）以诗体呈现超人、永恒轮回、上帝已死三大主题——尼采自称此书为"给所有人的，也是给无人看的"。《善恶的彼岸》（*Jenseits von Gut und Böse*, 1886）和《道德的谱系》（*Zur Genealogie der Moral*, 1887）系统展开权力意志说与道德的谱系学批判。1888年——尼采生命最后一年——他以前所未有的强度完成了五部著作：《瓦格纳事件》《偶像的黄昏》《反基督》《瞧这个人》和《尼采反对瓦格纳》，进入最激烈的论战与自我总结。

*Thus Spoke Zarathustra* (1883–1885) presents the Übermensch, eternal return, and death of God in poetic form — Nietzsche called it "for everyone and no one." *Beyond Good and Evil* (1886) and *On the Genealogy of Morality* (1887) systematically develop will to power and genealogical critique of morality. In 1888 — his final productive year — Nietzsche completed five works with unprecedented intensity: *The Case of Wagner*, *Twilight of the Idols*, *The Antichrist*, *Ecce Homo*, and *Nietzsche contra Wagner*, entering his most fierce polemics and self-summary.

### 崩溃与死后（1889–1900） / Collapse and Aftermath

1889年1月3日在都灵街头精神崩溃——据传他抱住一匹被鞭打的马痛哭。此后11年在精神失常中度过，由母亲弗兰齐斯卡和妹妹伊丽莎白照料。伊丽莎白·福斯特-尼采篡改遗稿，将其编纂为支持德国民族主义与反犹主义的文本，为纳粹利用埋下伏笔。1900年8月25日在魏玛去世。考夫曼（Kaufmann, 1950）的开创性研究和科利-蒙提纳里（Colli-Montinari, 1967–）批判性全集版系统澄清了这一篡改，恢复了尼采思想的原貌。

Mental collapse on 3 January 1889 in Turin — reportedly embracing a whipped horse in tears. Eleven years of mental derangement followed, cared for by his mother Franziska and sister Elisabeth. Elisabeth Förster-Nietzsche falsified manuscripts to support German nationalism and antisemitism, planting the seeds for Nazi appropriation. Died 25 August 1900 in Weimar. Kaufmann's groundbreaking study (1950) and the Colli-Montinari critical complete edition (1967–) systematically corrected this falsification, restoring Nietzsche's thought to its original form.

## 思想坐标 / Intellectual Coordinates

- **承接**：从[叔本华](../../悲观主义/叔本华/README.md)的"意志作为物自体"出发，但以权力意志（Wille zur Macht）翻转生存意志（Wille zum Leben）；从[瓦格纳](对话/与瓦格纳关系.md)的酒神复兴幻想出发，但以颓废诊断取代理想化投射
- **批判**：反对基督教道德作为奴隶道德的最大胜利；反对柏拉图主义-黑格尔传统的超越性形而上学；反对民主平等主义作为怨恨（Ressentiment）的表达；反对实证主义的"客观真理"幻觉
- **影响**：直接催生了[海德格尔](对话/与海德格尔关系.md)的存在论追问、[福柯](../../../社会学/学派/结构主义/福柯.md)的权力分析、[德勒兹](对话/与德勒兹关系.md)的差异哲学、弗洛伊德的深度心理学、[加缪](../加缪/README.md)的荒诞哲学，以及整个后结构主义和后现代思想传统

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

### 后现代哲学 / Postmodern Philosophy

尼采的视角主义（Perspektivismus）和谱系学方法直接催生了后结构主义和后现代哲学。[福柯](对话/与海德格尔关系.md)的权力/知识分析、[德勒兹](对话/与德勒兹关系.md)的差异哲学、德里达（Derrida）的解构主义，都以尼采为思想起点。"没有事实，只有诠释"成为后现代认识论的基本命题。

Nietzsche's perspectivism (Perspektivismus) and genealogical method directly spawned poststructuralism and postmodern philosophy. Foucault's power/knowledge analysis, Deleuze's philosophy of difference, and Derrida's deconstruction all take Nietzsche as their starting point. "There are no facts, only interpretations" became postmodern epistemology's basic thesis.

### 心理学 / Psychology

弗洛伊德（Freud）承认尼采的内驱力理论与精神分析平行；阿德勒（Adler）的自卑-超越与权力意志结构相似；荣格（Jung）的阴影（shadow）概念和个体化过程（individuation）深受尼采影响。亚隆（Yalom）的存在心理治疗将尼采的生命意义追问作为核心资源。

Freud acknowledged Nietzsche's drive theory paralleled psychoanalysis; Adler's inferiority-transcendence is structurally similar to will to power; Jung's shadow concept and individuation process are deeply Nietzschean. Yalom's existential psychotherapy takes Nietzsche's life-meaning inquiry as a core resource.

### 文学批评 / Literary Criticism

尼采的格言体写作、面具（Maske）哲学和"生命作为文学"的理念深刻影响了二十世纪文学理论。从巴塔耶（Bataille）的越界写作到布鲁姆（Bloom）的"影响的焦虑"，尼采对创造力和传统之张力的分析是文学批评的基础资源。

Nietzsche's aphoristic writing, mask (Maske) philosophy, and "life as literature" idea profoundly influenced twentieth-century literary theory. From Bataille's transgressive writing to Bloom's "anxiety of influence," Nietzsche's analysis of the tension between creativity and tradition is a foundational resource for literary criticism.

### 管理学与组织理论 / Management and Organizational Theory

权力意志为理解组织动力学提供了超越理性选择模型的框架——组织不仅是利益计算的工具，更是权力意志的配置场域。超人的"自我超越"概念影响了变革型领导力和创新管理理论。

Will to power provides a framework for organizational dynamics beyond rational-choice models — organizations as configurations of power relations. The Übermensch's "self-overcoming" concept influences transformational leadership and innovation management theory.

### 文化批评 / Cultural Criticism

怨恨（Ressentiment）成为诊断网络暴力、民粹主义、身份政治的核心概念。末人（der letzte Mensch）是对消费主义和舒适文明的精准预言。尼采对"驯化"（Zähmung）教育的批判影响了进步主义教育学。

Ressentiment has become a central concept for diagnosing cyberbullying, populism, and identity politics. The last man (der letzte Mensch) is a prescient diagnosis of consumerism and comfort civilization. Nietzsche's critique of "taming" (Zähmung) education influences progressive pedagogy.

### AI伦理与技术哲学 / AI Ethics and Philosophy of Technology

尼采对"价值设定"（Wertesetzung）的追问——"谁在设定价值？为谁服务？"——直接启发了当代AI对齐（AI alignment）研究中对隐含价值的批判性反思。如果AI系统的目标函数（objective function）本质上是一种价值设定，那么谁有权设定这些价值？权力意志的概念也为理解技术系统的扩张动力提供了哲学框架——技术本身是否是权力意志的一种配置？海德格尔将尼采的虚无主义诊断推进到技术时代（Gestell），认为现代技术将一切存在者还原为可计算的"持存物"（Bestand），而尼采对工具理性的批判为这一诊断提供了先驱。

Nietzsche's questioning of "value-setting" (Wertesetzung) — "who sets values? for whom?" — directly inspires contemporary AI alignment research's critical reflection on implicit values. If AI systems' objective functions are essentially a form of value-setting, who has the authority to set these values? Will to power also provides a philosophical framework for understanding technological systems' expansionist dynamics — is technology itself a configuration of will to power? Heidegger pushed Nietzsche's nihilism diagnosis into the technological age (Gestell), arguing modern technology reduces all beings to calculable "standing-reserve" (Bestand), and Nietzsche's critique of instrumental rationality provides the precursor to this diagnosis.

## 常见误读 / Common Misunderstandings

### 误读一：尼采=纳粹先驱 / "Nietzsche = Nazi precursor"

**纠正**：这一关联主要源于妹妹伊丽莎白·福斯特-尼采对遗稿的篡改。尼采本人**公开反对反犹主义**（与瓦格纳决裂的原因之一）、**嘲笑德国民族主义**、**厌恶国家崇拜**。他在《善恶的彼岸》中写道："国家是最冷酷的怪物。"考夫曼（1950）和Colli-Montinari校勘本（1967–）已系统澄清此误读。

**Correction**: This association derives primarily from Elisabeth Förster-Nietzsche's falsification of his manuscripts. Nietzsche himself **openly opposed antisemitism** (one reason for the Wagner rupture), **mocked German nationalism**, and **loathed state worship**. He wrote in *Beyond Good and Evil*: "The state is the coldest of all cold monsters." Kaufmann (1950) and the Colli-Montinari critical edition (1967–) have systematically corrected this misreading.

### 误读二：超人=种族主义 / "Übermensch = racism"

**纠正**：超人（Übermensch）是**存在论**概念，不是生物学概念——它与种族、血统、民族无关。超人是**自我立法的可能性**，任何人都可能朝向超人，也可能堕落为末人。纳粹将其篡改为生物学上的"雅利安优等种族"（Herrenrasse），这一篡改由伊丽莎白主导。

**Correction**: The Übermensch is an **ontological** concept, not a biological one — it has nothing to do with race, bloodline, or nation. The Übermensch is a **possibility of self-legislation** accessible to anyone. The Nazi distortion into a biological "Aryan master race" (Herrenrasse) was led by Elisabeth.

### 误读三：上帝已死=无神论宣言 / "God is dead = atheistic manifesto"

**纠正**："上帝已死"不是庆祝无神论的胜利，而是**诊断一个文明事件**——基督教价值体系的基石已经崩塌，由此引发的虚无主义将笼罩欧洲两百年。这是一个**警告**而非**欢呼**：旧价值崩塌后，人类将面临最深刻的意义危机。

**Correction**: "God is dead" does not celebrate atheism's victory but **diagnoses a civilizational event** — the cornerstone of the Christian value system has collapsed, and the resulting nihilism will engulf Europe for two centuries. This is a **warning** not a **cheer**: after old values collapse, humanity faces its most profound meaning crisis.

### 误读四：权力意志=政治支配 / "Will to power = political domination"

**纠正**：权力意志首先是**对自身的克服与塑造**，是生命扩张和自我超越的内在驱力。对外部支配只是其最粗浅的表层形式。尼采鄙视单纯的暴力统治——真正的权力意志是**自我克服**：征服自己的恐惧、偏见和惯性。将权力意志等同于政治权力，恰恰是尼采所批判的"末人"视角。

**Correction**: Will to power is primarily **self-overcoming and self-formation** — life's inner drive toward expansion and self-transcendence. External domination is only its crudest surface form. Nietzsche despised mere violent rule — true will to power is **self-overcoming**: conquering one's own fears, prejudices, and inertia. Equating will to power with political power is precisely the "last man" perspective Nietzsche critiqued.

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

### 尼采 × 佛教 / Nietzsche × Buddhism

→ 详见 [对话/与佛教关系.md](对话/与佛教关系.md)

尼采将佛教归类为"消极虚无主义"（passiver Nihilismus），但这一评价基于十九世纪欧洲的有限了解。两者在结构上存在深层平行：谱系学（Genealogie）vs缘起论（pratītyasamutpāda）都拒绝实体主义，都强调过程和关系优先于实体；权力意志（Wille zur Macht）vs业力（karma）都强调动态过程而非静态存在。但方向根本不同：尼采**肯定**生命的扩张与创造，佛教旨在从无明（avidyā）中**觉醒**并超越轮回（saṃsāra）。永恒轮回（Ewige Wiederkunft）与轮回（saṃsāra）使用相同的"循环"意象，却给出相反评价——尼采热爱重复，佛教追求超越循环。值得注意的是，尼采对佛教的"消极虚无主义"标签忽视了大乘佛教中菩萨道（bodhisattva-mārga）积极入世的面向。

Nietzsche classified Buddhism as "passive nihilism" (passiver Nihilismus), but this assessment was based on limited nineteenth-century European knowledge. Deep structural parallels exist: genealogy (Genealogie) vs dependent origination (pratītyasamutpāda) both refuse substantialism and prioritize process over substance; will to power (Wille zur Macht) vs karma both emphasize dynamic process over static being. But the directions differ fundamentally: Nietzsche **affirms** life's expansion and creation, Buddhism aims at **awakening** from ignorance (avidyā) and transcending saṃsāra. Eternal return and saṃsāra use the same "cyclical" imagery yet reach opposite evaluations — Nietzsche loves recurrence, Buddhism seeks liberation from it. Notably, Nietzsche's "passive nihilism" label neglects the actively world-engaging dimension of Mahāyāna Buddhism's bodhisattva path (bodhisattva-mārga).

### 尼采 × 道家 / Nietzsche × Daoism

尼采的"生成的无辜"（Unschuld des Werdens）与道家的"自然"（zìrán）概念存在结构呼应：两者都拒绝超越性的道德立法，都肯定生成变化的自发性。尼采对"人为"道德的批判（主人-奴隶道德分析）与老子"绝圣弃智，民利百倍"（《道德经》第19章）的反规范立场形成对照。但尼采的权力意志强调**主动的自我超越**——征服自己的恐惧和惯性，道家则追求**无为**（wúwéi）——不干预、不造作的自然展开。庄子（Zhuāngzǐ）的"逍遥游"与尼采的"自由精神"（freier Geist）之间存在有趣的对话空间：两者都追求从既有规范中的解放，但尼采的解放是**战斗性的**，庄子的解放是**游戏性的**。两者的共同敌人是人为的、压抑的文明规范——尼采称之为"驯化"（Zähmung），道家称之为"人为"（rénwéi）。

Nietzsche's "innocence of becoming" (Unschuld des Werdens) resonates structurally with the Daoist concept of "naturalness" (zìrán): both refuse transcendent moral legislation and affirm the spontaneity of becoming. Nietzsche's critique of "artificial" morality resonates with Laozi's anti-normative stance — "abandon sagacity and discard cleverness, and the people will benefit a hundredfold" (*Daodejing* Ch.19). But Nietzsche's will to power emphasizes **active self-overcoming**, while Daoism pursues **non-action** (wúwéi) — natural unfolding without interference. Zhuāngzǐ's "free and easy wandering" (xiāoyáo yóu) and Nietzsche's "free spirit" (freier Geist) share an interesting dialogue space: both seek liberation from established norms, but Nietzsche's liberation is **combative** while Zhuāngzǐ's is **playful**.

### 尼采 × 印度教 / Nietzsche × Hinduism

尼采在《反基督》（*Der Antichrist*, 1888）中对《摩奴法典》（Manusmṛti）的引用——将其种姓制度（varṇa）赞颂为"健康的等级秩序"——是后殖民批评的焦点。这一引用存在严重的东方主义（Orientalismus）偏见：尼采将印度教的种姓制度理想化，忽视了其对被压迫种姓（达利特 Dalit）的系统性暴力。但尼采对印度教等级制的兴趣反映了他对现代平等主义的根本不满——他认为平等主义是奴隶道德（Sklavenmoral）的胜利，而等级差异（Rangordnung）是生命丰盈的条件。与叔本华将印度教吠檀多（Vedānta）的"梵"（Brahman）等同于自己的"意志"不同，尼采从未试图与印度教进行深层哲学对话——他使用印度教材料主要是作为**论战武器**来批判基督教。

Nietzsche's citation of the *Laws of Manu* (Manusmṛti) in *The Antichrist* (1888) — praising the caste system (varṇa) as a "healthy order of rank" — is a focal point of postcolonial critique. This citation contains serious orientalist (Orientalismus) bias: Nietzsche idealized the Hindu caste system, ignoring its systematic violence against oppressed castes (Dalits). Yet Nietzsche's interest in Hindu hierarchy reflects his fundamental dissatisfaction with modern egalitarianism — he regarded egalitarianism as slave morality's (Sklavenmoral) triumph, while rank-difference (Rangordnung) is a condition of life's abundance. Unlike Schopenhauer, who equated Vedānta's "Brahman" with his own "Will," Nietzsche never attempted deep philosophical dialogue with Hinduism — he used Hindu materials primarily as **polemical weapons** against Christianity.
