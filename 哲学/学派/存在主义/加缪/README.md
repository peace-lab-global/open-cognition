# 加缪 · Albert Camus

> "应该想象西西弗是幸福的。"
> "Il faut imaginer Sisyphe heureux."
> — *Le Mythe de Sisyphe* (1942)

---

## 基本信息 / Biographical Note

阿尔贝·加缪（Albert Camus, 1913年11月7日–1960年1月4日），法裔阿尔及利亚作家、哲学家、新闻工作者，1957年诺贝尔文学奖得主，也是二十世纪最具影响力的荒诞哲学与反抗哲学的建构者。他出生于法属阿尔及利亚东部小镇蒙多维（Mondovi），父亲吕西安·加缪（Lucien Camus）是一名阿尔萨斯裔的葡萄园工人，在第一次世界大战的马恩河战役中重伤，于1914年去世——加缪从未见过自己的父亲。母亲卡特琳娜·桑泰斯（Catherine Sintès）是西班牙裔的半文盲女佣，靠清洁工作将两个儿子抚养成人。贫困、沉默和地中海的阳光，构成加缪一生思想的底色。

Albert Camus (7 November 1913 – 4 January 1960) was a French-Algerian writer, philosopher, and journalist, the 1957 Nobel Laureate in Literature, and one of the twentieth century's most influential builders of absurd and revolt philosophy. Born in Mondovi, French Algeria, his father Lucien Camus — an Alsatian vineyard worker — was mortally wounded at the Battle of the Marne in 1914 and died before Albert's first birthday. His mother Catherine Sintès, a semi-literate Spanish-Minorcan cleaning woman, raised two sons in grinding poverty. Poverty, silence, and Mediterranean sunlight form the deep substratum of Camus's entire thought.

加缪在阿尔及尔大学（Université d'Alger）师从哲学家让·格勒尼耶（Jean Grenier），后者引导他阅读柏格森、叔本华、尼采和马尔罗，深刻塑造了他的哲学品味。1936年，他以一篇研究基督教形而上学与新柏拉图主义的学位论文获得哲学学位。然而肺结核迫使他放弃了成为大学教授的学术道路——这一疾病伴随了他终生，也使他始终与"身体的脆弱性"和"死亡的亲近感"保持直接的、非学院化的关系。

At the University of Algiers he studied under philosopher Jean Grenier, who introduced him to Bergson, Schopenhauer, Nietzsche, and Malraux, profoundly shaping his philosophical taste. In 1936 he received his diplôme in philosophy with a thesis on Christian metaphysics and Neoplatonism. Tuberculosis, however, forced him to abandon the academic path to a professorship — a disease that accompanied him throughout his life and kept his relationship to bodily fragility and mortality direct, unacademic, and immediate.

1940年加缪移居巴黎，加入抵抗运动地下报纸《战斗报》（*Combat*）编辑部，在第二次世界大战期间成为法国知识分子抵抗运动的重要声音。战后，他与[萨特](../萨特/README.md)的友谊从密切走向公开的、毁灭性的决裂。1960年1月4日，加缪在一次车祸中丧生，年仅四十六岁——口袋里装着未完成的自传体小说《第一人》（*Le Premier Homme*）的手稿。

In 1940 Camus moved to Paris and joined the editorial team of the Resistance newspaper *Combat*, becoming a leading voice of the French intellectual resistance during the Second World War. After the war, his friendship with Sartre moved from closeness to a public and devastating rupture. On 4 January 1960, Camus died in a car accident at the age of forty-six — carrying in his pocket the manuscript of his unfinished autobiographical novel *Le Premier Homme*.

## 一句话定位 / One-Line Positioning

**二十世纪最伟大的荒诞哲学家与反抗思想家，将"世界的无意义"不是当作绝望的理由，而是当作清醒生活与团结反抗的起点——同时也是一位以文学形式表达哲学思想的卓越小说家。**

**The twentieth century's greatest philosopher of the absurd and of revolt, who took "the world's meaninglessness" not as grounds for despair but as the starting point for lucid living and solidaristic revolt — and an outstanding novelist who expressed philosophy through literature.**

## 六大核心命题 / Six Core Theses

### 1. 荒诞作为人与世界的根本关系 / The Absurd as the Fundamental Human-World Relation

→ 详见 [概念/荒诞.md](概念/荒诞.md)

"荒诞"（L'Absurde）不是世界的属性，也不是人的属性，而是**人与世界之间的一种根本关系**——人对意义、统一性和理性的渴望，与世界对此渴望的绝对沉默之间的不可调和的冲突。加缪拒绝将这个冲突"解决"掉（无论是通过宗教信仰的跳跃还是哲学体系的缝合），而是要求人**直面**荒诞，在清醒中生活。

The "Absurd" is not a property of the world, nor of man, but a **fundamental relation** between the two — the irreconcilable conflict between man's hunger for meaning, unity, and reason and the world's absolute silence in the face of that hunger. Camus refuses to "resolve" this conflict (whether through religious leaps of faith or philosophical system-building) and demands that one **confront** the absurd, living in lucidity.

### 2. 哲学性自杀的拒绝 / The Refusal of Philosophical Suicide

→ 详见 [概念/哲学性自杀.md](概念/哲学性自杀.md)

面对荒诞，人的第一反应是"逃避"——加缪称之为**哲学性自杀**（Le Suicide philosophique）。克尔凯郭尔的信仰跳跃、胡塞尔的现象学还原、雅斯贝尔斯的超越性开放、甚至萨特的绝对自由——在加缪看来，这些都是在用某种"超越"来取消荒诞问题本身。加缪的立场是：保持荒诞，不取消它，不解决它，不逃避它。

Confronted with the absurd, man's first reaction is "evasion" — what Camus calls **philosophical suicide**. Kierkegaard's leap of faith, Husserl's phenomenological reduction, Jaspers' openness to transcendence, even Sartre's absolute freedom — for Camus, all of these use some form of "transcendence" to cancel the question of the absurd itself. Camus's stance: maintain the absurd, do not cancel it, do not solve it, do not flee it.

### 3. 反抗作为荒诞的回应 / Revolt as the Response to the Absurd

→ 详见 [概念/反抗.md](概念/反抗.md)

如果荒诞不能被解决或逃避，那么人应当如何生活？加缪的回答是：**反抗**（La Révolte）。反抗不是绝望，而是一种在清醒认识荒诞的前提下仍然说"不"的姿态——同时这个"不"内含一个"是"：对某种人类共同价值（尊严、团结、美）的肯定。从《西西弗神话》的个人反抗到《反抗者》的集体反抗，加缪建构了一套完整的反抗哲学。

If the absurd cannot be resolved or evaded, how should one live? Camus's answer: **revolt** (La Révolte). Revolt is not despair but a stance of saying "no" while lucidly aware of the absurd — and this "no" contains an implicit "yes": an affirmation of certain shared human values (dignity, solidarity, beauty). From the individual revolt of *The Myth of Sisyphus* to the collective revolt of *The Rebel*, Camus builds a complete philosophy of revolt.

### 4. 量与质的荒诞伦理 / Quantity and Quality: An Absurd Ethic

→ 详见 [概念/量与质.md](概念/量与质.md)

荒诞人（L'Homme absurde）的伦理不追求生活的**质量**——即某种"更好"的生活、更高的价值、更深的意义——而是追求生活的**数量**：尽可能多地经历、感受、意识。加缪以唐璜（Don Juan）、演员和征服者三种"荒诞英雄"为典范，论证了在无意义的世界中，最充实的生活恰恰是最清醒地意识到无意义的生活。

The absurd man's ethic does not pursue **quality** of life — some "better" life, higher value, deeper meaning — but **quantity**: to experience, feel, and be conscious as much as possible. Camus takes Don Juan, the actor, and the conqueror as three types of "absurd heroes," arguing that in a meaningless world, the most fulfilled life is precisely the one most lucidly aware of its meaninglessness.

### 5. 反抗的限度：正义与衡度 / The Limits of Revolt: Justice and Measure

→ 详见 [概念/正义与限度.md](概念/正义与限度.md)

加缪在《反抗者》（*L'Homme révolté*, 1951）中提出了他最核心也最具争议的政治哲学命题：**反抗必须有其限度**（La mesure）。没有限度的反抗——以绝对正义或历史必然性之名进行的革命——必然走向恐怖主义和极权主义。加缪以此批判了法国知识分子界对苏联共产主义的同情，也批判了一切以"历史目的论"为正当化暴力的意识形态。

In *The Rebel* (1951) Camus advances his most central and controversial political-philosophical thesis: **revolt must have its limits** (La mesure). Revolt without limits — revolution conducted in the name of absolute justice or historical necessity — inevitably descends into terrorism and totalitarianism. Camus thereby criticizes the French intellectual milieu's sympathy for Soviet communism and all ideologies that legitimate violence through "historical teleology."

### 6. 地中海思想：限度与阳光 / Mediterranean Thought: Limits and Sunlight

→ 详见 [概念/地中海思想.md](概念/地中海思想.md)

加缪自称其哲学属于"地中海思想"（La Pensée de Midi）传统——与德国哲学的"北方"体系建构（黑格尔、海德格尔）相对立，地中海思想植根于希腊遗产：**限度**（mesure）、**美**（beauté）、**身体**（le corps）和**此世**（ce monde）。这不是逃避形而上学，而是以身体经验、感官直接性和地中海阳光下的清醒，替代抽象概念的体系建构。

Camus described his philosophy as belonging to the "Mediterranean thought" (La Pensée de Midi) tradition — opposed to the "Northern" system-building of German philosophy (Hegel, Heidegger), Mediterranean thought is rooted in the Greek heritage: **limit** (mesure), **beauty** (beauté), **the body** (le corps), and **this world** (ce monde). This is not an evasion of metaphysics but a substitution of bodily experience, sensory immediacy, and the clarity of Mediterranean sunlight for the system-building of abstract concepts.

## 思想脉络 / Intellectual Trajectory

### 早期（1913–1942）：阿尔及利亚与荒诞的发现 / Early Period: Algeria and the Discovery of the Absurd

加缪的早期思想形成于阿尔及利亚的特殊语境：殖民地社会的种族暴力、贫困中的身体经验、地中海自然环境的感官丰富性。格勒尼耶老师引导他进入哲学世界，但他的肺结核和工人阶级背景使他从一开始就与学院哲学保持距离。1937年出版的《反与正》（*L'Envers et l'Endroit*）和1939年的《婚礼集》（*Noces*）已经显示出加缪思想的核心张力：世界的冷漠与美并存，死亡与阳光同在。1942年，《局外人》（*L'Étranger*）和《西西弗神话》（*Le Mythe de Sisyphe*）同时出版，标志着荒诞哲学的正式诞生——前者以文学呈现荒诞意识，后者以哲学论证荒诞的不可逃避性。

Camus's early thought was shaped by the particular context of Algeria: the racial violence of colonial society, bodily experience in poverty, the sensory richness of the Mediterranean natural environment. Grenier introduced him to philosophy, but his tuberculosis and working-class background kept him from the outset at a distance from academic philosophy. *The Wrong Side and the Right Side* (1937) and *Nuptials* (1939) already display the central tension of Camus's thought: the world's indifference coexists with beauty, death with sunlight. In 1942, *The Stranger* (*L'Étranger*) and *The Myth of Sisyphus* (*Le Mythe de Sisyphe*) appeared simultaneously, marking the formal birth of absurd philosophy — the former presenting absurd consciousness through literature, the latter arguing philosophically for its inescapability.

### 中期（1943–1951）：从荒诞到反抗 / Middle Period: From the Absurd to Revolt

战争经历使加缪从个人的荒诞意识转向集体的反抗伦理。作为《战斗报》的编辑，他直接参与了抵抗运动，体验了团结行动中的道德力量。1947年的小说《鼠疫》（*La Peste*）是这一转向的文学表达：里厄医生（Dr. Rieux）面对瘟疫的态度不再是默尔索式的冷漠旁观，而是清醒的、团结的、不求回报的抗争。1951年的《反抗者》（*L'Homme révolté*）则是这一转向的哲学表达——加缪系统论证了从"我反抗"（je me révolte）到"我们存在"（donc nous sommes）的逻辑。这本书也导致了与萨特的公开决裂。

The war experience shifted Camus from individual absurd consciousness to a collective ethic of revolt. As editor of *Combat*, he directly participated in the Resistance, experiencing the moral force of solidaristic action. The 1947 novel *The Plague* (*La Peste*) is the literary expression of this turn: Dr. Rieux's attitude toward the plague is no longer Meursault's indifferent observation but lucid, solidaristic, unselfseeking resistance. The 1951 *The Rebel* (*L'Homme révolté*) is the philosophical expression — Camus systematically argues the logic from "I revolt" (je me révolte) to "therefore we are" (donc nous sommes). This book also caused the public rupture with Sartre.

### 晚期（1952–1960）：限度、坠落与沉默 / Late Period: Measure, Fall, and Silence

与萨特决裂后，加缪陷入了越来越深的孤立。1956年的小说《坠落》（*La Chute*）以一个前律师克莱芒斯（Clamence）的独白形式，深刻反思了审判者自身的罪责——这是对加缪自己在《反抗者》中"法官-先知"姿态的自我批判。阿尔及利亚战争期间，加缪在殖民者与民族解放阵线之间的两难处境使他选择了公开沉默——"我相信正义，但在正义与母亲之间，我选择母亲"——这句话浓缩了他对抽象正义与具体忠诚之间张力的最终回答。未完成的《第一人》（*Le Premier Homme*）是一部回归阿尔及利亚、回归母亲、回归沉默与身体的自传性作品。

After the break with Sartre, Camus sank into increasing isolation. The 1956 novel *The Fall* (*La Chute*), in the form of a monologue by a former lawyer named Clamence, profoundly reflects on the judge's own guilt — a self-critique of Camus's own "judge-prophet" posture in *The Rebel*. During the Algerian War, Camus's dilemma between colonists and the FLN led him to choose public silence — "I believe in justice, but between justice and my mother, I choose my mother" — a sentence that condenses his final answer to the tension between abstract justice and concrete loyalty. The unfinished *The First Man* (*Le Premier Homme*) is an autobiographical work returning to Algeria, to his mother, to silence and the body.

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

### 存在心理治疗 / Existential Psychotherapy

加缪的荒诞概念——尤其是"在无意义面前如何继续生活"的问题——成为存在心理治疗（Irvin Yalom、Rollo May 等人发展）的核心议题之一。加缪对自杀问题的处理（《西西弗神话》开篇："真正严肃的哲学问题只有一个，那就是自杀"）为临床心理学中面对存在性危机和自杀意念的患者提供了一个非宗教的、非虚无主义的哲学框架。

Camus's concept of the absurd — especially the question "how to go on living in the face of meaninglessness" — has become a central theme in existential psychotherapy (as developed by Irvin Yalom, Rollo May, and others). Camus's treatment of suicide (the opening of *The Myth of Sisyphus*: "There is but one truly serious philosophical problem, and that is suicide") provides a non-religious, non-nihilistic philosophical framework for patients facing existential crises and suicidal ideation in clinical psychology.

### 人权与反抗理论 / Human Rights and Resistance Theory

《反抗者》中关于"限度"和"反抗vs革命"的区分，在冷战结束后获得了新的生命力。当代人权理论中对"绝对正义"的警惕、对"以正义之名行暴力之实"的批判、以及对"反抗必须有边界"的坚持，都可以在加缪那里找到先驱。汉娜·阿伦特（Hannah Arendt）对极权主义的分析、朱迪斯·施克拉（Judith Shklar）的"恐惧的自由主义"（liberalism of fear），与加缪的限度思想形成呼应。

The distinction between "limit" and "revolt vs revolution" in *The Rebel* gained new vitality after the Cold War. Contemporary human rights theory's vigilance against "absolute justice," its critique of "violence in the name of justice," and its insistence that "revolt must have boundaries" all find a precursor in Camus. Hannah Arendt's analysis of totalitarianism and Judith Shklar's "liberalism of fear" resonate with Camus's thought on limits.

### 后殖民研究与阿尔及利亚身份 / Postcolonial Studies and Algerian Identity

加缪的"黑脚"（pied-noir）身份——既是殖民者又是被殖民土地之子——使他成为后殖民研究中最复杂的思想人物之一。爱德华·萨义德（Edward Said）对加缪的批判性分析、当代阿尔及利亚知识分子对加缪遗产的重新评估，以及关于"殖民者的良知"的讨论，都以加缪为重要参照点。

Camus's "pied-noir" identity — both colonizer and son of the colonized land — makes him one of the most complex intellectual figures in postcolonial studies. Edward Said's critical analysis of Camus, contemporary Algerian intellectuals' reassessment of Camus's legacy, and discussions of "the colonizer's conscience" all take Camus as an important reference point.

### 文学与哲学的方法论融合 / Methodological Fusion of Literature and Philosophy

加缪以小说和戏剧表达哲学思想的做法，影响了从萨特的《恶心》到米兰·昆德拉（Milan Kundera）的"小说作为思想"（le roman comme pensée）的整个二十世纪文学-哲学传统。在当代，加缪的方法论为"文学哲学"（literary philosophy）和"叙事伦理"（narrative ethics）领域提供了经典范例。

Camus's practice of expressing philosophy through fiction and drama influenced the entire twentieth-century literature-philosophy tradition from Sartre's *Nausea* to Milan Kundera's "the novel as thought" (le roman comme pensée). In contemporary scholarship, Camus's methodology provides a classical model for "literary philosophy" and "narrative ethics."

### 生态哲学与地中海生态主义 / Ecological Philosophy and Mediterranean Environmentalism

加缪的地中海思想——尤其是他对自然之美、身体经验和此世的强调——在当代生态哲学中获得了新的解读。与深层生态学（deep ecology）的"万物平等"不同，加缪提供了一种基于感官直接性和身体经验的生态伦理：我们保护自然不是因为它有"内在价值"，而是因为它是我们的身体所属于的、我们的感官所体验的世界。

Camus's Mediterranean thought — especially his emphasis on natural beauty, bodily experience, and this world — has received new readings in contemporary ecological philosophy. Unlike deep ecology's "equality of all beings," Camus offers an ecological ethics based on sensory immediacy and bodily experience: we protect nature not because it has "intrinsic value" but because it is the world our bodies belong to and our senses experience.

### 新闻伦理与公共知识分子角色 / Journalism Ethics and the Public Intellectual Role

加缪作为《战斗报》编辑和终生新闻工作者的身份，为当代新闻伦理和公共知识分子的角色定位提供了重要参照。他对"清晰表达"（parler clair）的坚持、对"为无权者说话"的承诺、以及拒绝成为任何意识形态的代言人，在当代媒体伦理讨论中仍具参考价值。

Camus's identity as editor of *Combat* and lifelong journalist provides an important reference for contemporary journalism ethics and the role of the public intellectual. His insistence on "speaking clearly" (parler clair), his commitment to "speaking for the powerless," and his refusal to be a spokesman for any ideology remain relevant in contemporary media ethics discussions.

## 常见误读 / Common Misunderstandings

### 误读一：加缪是存在主义者 / "Camus is an existentialist"

**纠正**：加缪终生拒绝"存在主义"标签。他在1945年的演讲中明确说："我不是存在主义者……萨特和我总是惊讶于我们的名字被放在一起。"加缪认为萨特的存在主义仍然是一种体系哲学——它试图通过"存在先于本质"来解决意义问题，而加缪的荒诞哲学恰恰拒绝解决意义问题。萨特的自由本体论和加缪的荒诞意识在哲学根基上就有分歧。

**Correction**: Camus rejected the "existentialist" label throughout his life. In a 1945 lecture he stated explicitly: "I am not an existentialist… Sartre and I are always surprised to see our names linked together." Camus considered Sartre's existentialism still a system-philosophy — it attempts to solve the meaning problem through "existence precedes essence," while Camus's absurd philosophy precisely refuses to solve the meaning problem. Sartre's freedom-ontology and Camus's absurd consciousness diverge at their philosophical foundations.

### 误读二：荒诞哲学是虚无主义 / "Absurd philosophy is nihilism"

**纠正**：加缪的荒诞恰恰是虚无主义的**反面**。虚无主义说"没有意义，所以一切都不重要"；荒诞哲学说"没有意义，但人仍然反抗、仍然创造、仍然爱"。西西弗推石上山不是绝望的行为，而是在清醒认识无意义之后仍然选择行动的姿态。加缪将荒诞视为一种"张力"（tension）——保持这个张力本身就是对虚无主义的拒绝。

**Correction**: Camus's absurd is precisely the **opposite** of nihilism. Nihilism says "there is no meaning, so nothing matters"; absurd philosophy says "there is no meaning, but one still revolts, still creates, still loves." Sisyphus pushing his boulder uphill is not an act of despair but a stance of choosing to act after lucidly recognizing meaninglessness. Camus sees the absurd as a "tension" — maintaining this tension is itself a refusal of nihilism.

### 误读三：《反抗者》是右翼保守主义 / "The Rebel is right-wing conservatism"

**纠正**：萨特的追随者弗朗西斯·让松（Francis Jeanson）在1952年的书评中将《反抗者》斥为"右翼"和"警察灵魂"（âme de flic），这一标签影响了几代人对加缪的政治定位。但事实上，加缪的"限度"概念既批判了革命恐怖主义（左翼），也批判了保守的不正义（右翼）。加缪一生都是反殖民主义者——他在阿尔及利亚战争中保持沉默不是因为支持殖民主义，而是因为他无法在两种暴力之间做出选择。

**Correction**: Sartre's follower Francis Jeanson, in his 1952 review, dismissed *The Rebel* as "right-wing" and accused Camus of having a "policeman's soul" (âme de flic) — a label that influenced generations' political assessment of Camus. In fact, Camus's concept of "limit" critiques both revolutionary terrorism (left) and conservative injustice (right). Camus was a lifelong anti-colonialist — his silence during the Algerian War was not support for colonialism but inability to choose between two forms of violence.

### 误读四：加缪的哲学不够"哲学" / "Camus's philosophy is not philosophical enough"

**纠正**：学院哲学传统常常低估加缪的哲学深度，因为他用小说和随笔而非系统性论文来表达思想。但加缪的"非体系化"恰恰是他哲学立场的表达——地中海思想拒绝体系建构，因为体系总是以牺牲"限度"和"具体性"为代价。《西西弗神话》的论证结构之严密、《反抗者》对西方思想史的批判性解读之深入，都证明了加缪的哲学功力。2013年加缪百年诞辰以来，学术界对其哲学贡献的重新评估正在改变这一偏见。

**Correction**: Academic philosophy has often underestimated Camus's philosophical depth because he expressed his thought through novels and essays rather than systematic treatises. But Camus's "non-systematic" approach is precisely the expression of his philosophical stance — Mediterranean thought refuses system-building because systems always sacrifice "limit" and "concreteness." The rigour of *The Myth of Sisyphus*'s argumentative structure and the depth of *The Rebel*'s critical reading of Western intellectual history demonstrate Camus's philosophical mastery. Since the 2013 centenary of his birth, scholarly reassessment of his philosophical contribution is changing this bias.

## 东西方对话：加缪 × 佛教 / East-West Dialogue: Camus × Buddhism

虽然加缪本人并未系统性地研究东方哲学（与叔本华不同），但他的荒诞哲学与佛教传统之间存在若干值得深入探讨的结构性平行关系。

Although Camus himself did not systematically study Eastern philosophy (unlike Schopenhauer), structural parallels between his absurd philosophy and the Buddhist tradition merit sustained exploration.

### 苦与荒诞 / Duḥkha and the Absurd

佛教的四圣谛以"苦"（duḥkha）为出发点，加缪的荒诞哲学以"世界的无意义"为出发点。两者都拒绝粉饰太平——都要求直面存在的根本困境。但两者对"出路"的回答截然不同：佛教追求从苦中解脱（涅槃），加缪则拒绝一切形式的"解脱"——荒诞人必须在荒诞中生活，而不是超越荒诞。

The Four Noble Truths begin with suffering (duḥkha); Camus's absurd philosophy begins with the world's meaninglessness. Both refuse to whitewash reality — both demand confronting the fundamental predicament of existence. But their answers to the "way out" differ radically: Buddhism pursues liberation from suffering (Nirvana), while Camus rejects all forms of "liberation" — the absurd man must live within the absurd, not transcend it.

### 无我与局外人 / Anātman and the Stranger

佛教的"无我"（anātman）与加缪笔下默尔索的"无主体性"之间存在有趣的对照。默尔索在审判中拒绝为自己的行为提供动机或意义——这种拒绝在形式上类似于佛教对"自我"概念的解构。但加缪的目的不是消解主体，而是在无意义的世界上建立一种不依赖超越性的生活态度。

Buddhist anātman ("no-self") and Meursault's "subject-lessness" present an interesting contrast. Meursault's refusal in his trial to provide motives or meaning for his actions is formally similar to Buddhism's deconstruction of the concept of self. But Camus's aim is not to dissolve the subject but to establish an attitude to life that does not depend on transcendence in a meaningless world.

### 中道与限度 / The Middle Way and Measure

佛教的"中道"（madhyamā pratipad）——避免苦行与纵欲两个极端——与加缪的"限度"（mesure）概念在结构上相似。两者都拒绝极端，都要求在两个对立之间找到平衡。但佛教的中道以解脱为目标，加缪的限度以保持荒诞张力为目标。

The Buddhist "Middle Way" (madhyamā pratipad) — avoiding the extremes of asceticism and indulgence — is structurally similar to Camus's concept of "measure" (mesure). Both refuse extremes; both demand finding a balance between opposites. But the Buddhist Middle Way aims at liberation, while Camus's measure aims at maintaining the absurd tension.
