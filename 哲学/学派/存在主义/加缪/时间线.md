# 加缪生平与思想年表 / Camus's Life and Intellectual Timeline

---

## 阿尔及利亚岁月（1913–1940）/ Algerian Years (1913–1940)

| 年份 | 事件 | Event |
|------|------|-------|
| 1913 | 11月7日，出生于法属阿尔及利亚蒙多维（Mondovi），父亲吕西安·加缪是葡萄园工人，母亲卡特琳娜·桑泰斯是西班牙裔清洁女工 | Born 7 November in Mondovi, French Algeria; father Lucien Camus a vineyard worker, mother Catherine Sintès a Spanish-Minorcan cleaning woman |
| 1914 | 父亲在第一次世界大战马恩河战役中重伤，10月去世。加缪从未见过父亲。全家迁往阿尔及尔贝尔库尔（Belcourt）贫民区 | Father mortally wounded at the Battle of the Marne; dies in October. Albert never met his father. Family moves to the Belcourt slum in Algiers |
| 1923 | 小学教师路易·热尔曼（Louis Germain）发现加缪的天赋，说服其家庭送他进入中学。加缪终生感激这位老师——1957年诺贝尔奖致辞中特别感谢了他 | Primary school teacher Louis Germain recognises Camus's talent, persuades his family to send him to lycée. Camus remained grateful — his 1957 Nobel speech specifically thanked him |
| 1930 | 进入阿尔及尔大学，先学哲学后转文学。师从让·格勒尼耶（Jean Grenier），后者引导他阅读柏格森、尼采、叔本华 | Enters the University of Algiers; studies philosophy then literature. Studies under Jean Grenier, who guides him to read Bergson, Nietzsche, Schopenhauer |
| 1930 | 首次确诊肺结核（tuberculose），此后终生与这一疾病共存 | First diagnosed with tuberculosis; lives with this disease for the rest of his life |
| 1934 | 与西蒙娜·伊耶（Simone Hié）结婚，婚姻短暂且痛苦。同年加入法国共产党阿尔及利亚支部 | Marries Simone Hié; the marriage is brief and painful. Same year joins the Algerian branch of the French Communist Party |
| 1935 | 因党内分歧（尤其是关于阿拉伯人的政策）退出共产党 | Leaves the Communist Party over policy disagreements (especially regarding Arabs) |
| 1936 | 以论文《基督教形而上学与新柏拉图主义》（*Métaphysique chrétienne et Néoplatonisme*）获得哲学学位 | Receives his diplôme in philosophy with thesis *Christian Metaphysics and Neoplatonism* |
| 1937 | 出版第一部作品《反与正》（*L'Envers et l'Endroit*），收录五篇散文 | Publishes first work *The Wrong Side and the Right Side*, a collection of five essays |
| 1937 | 创办"队友剧团"（Théâtre du Travail），编导和演出面向工人阶级的戏剧 | Founds the Théâtre du Travail, directing and performing theatre for the working class |
| 1939 | 出版《婚礼集》（*Noces*）；在阿尔及尔《共和晚报》（*Alger républicain*）担任记者，发表大量报道 | Publishes *Nuptials*; works as journalist at *Alger républicain*, publishing extensive reportage |
| 1940 | 与钢琴家弗朗辛·富尔（Francine Faure）结婚。同年因政治文章被阿尔及利亚殖民当局驱逐，迁居巴黎 | Marries pianist Francine Faure. Same year expelled from Algeria by colonial authorities for political articles; moves to Paris |

---

## 巴黎与抵抗运动（1940–1947）/ Paris and the Resistance (1940–1947)

| 年份 | 事件 | Event |
|------|------|-------|
| 1940 | 在巴黎《巴黎晚报》（*Paris-Soir*）担任编辑助理 | Works as editorial assistant at *Paris-Soir* in Paris |
| 1942 | 《局外人》（*L'Étranger*）和《西西弗神话》（*Le Mythe de Sisyphe*）由伽利玛出版社（Gallimard）出版，荒诞哲学正式诞生 | *The Stranger* and *The Myth of Sisyphus* published by Gallimard; absurd philosophy formally born |
| 1943 | 加入抵抗运动地下报纸《战斗报》（*Combat*）编辑部。结识萨特、西蒙娜·德·波伏瓦等人 | Joins the editorial team of the Resistance newspaper *Combat*. Meets Sartre, Simone de Beauvoir, and others |
| 1944 | 巴黎解放。加缪以《战斗报》主编身份发表大量评论文章。戏剧《误会》（*Le Malentendu*）首演 | Liberation of Paris. Camus publishes extensive commentary as editor of *Combat*. The play *The Misunderstanding* premieres |
| 1945 | 双胞胎女儿卡特琳娜和儿子让出生。加缪在"存在主义讲座"上发表演讲，拒绝"存在主义者"标签 | Twins Catherine and Jean born. Camus delivers a lecture on existentialism, refusing the "existentialist" label |
| 1946 | 赴美和加拿大巡回演讲，受到热烈欢迎。开始写作《鼠疫》 | Tours the United States and Canada to great acclaim. Begins writing *The Plague* |
| 1947 | 《鼠疫》（*La Peste*）出版，获批评家奖（Prix des Critiques）。加缪离开《战斗报》 | *The Plague* published, awarded the Prix des Critiques. Camus leaves *Combat* |

## 反抗周期与决裂（1948–1956）/ The Revolt Cycle and the Rupture (1948–1956)

| 年份 | 事件 | Event |
|------|------|-------|
| 1948 | 开始研究反抗的历史，为《反抗者》做资料准备。戏剧《戒严》（*L'État de siège*）首演 | Begins researching the history of revolt in preparation for *The Rebel*. The play *State of Siege* premieres |
| 1949 | 戏剧《正义者》（*Les Justes*）首演，探讨革命暴力与道德的张力 | *The Just Assassins* premieres, exploring the tension between revolutionary violence and morality |
| 1951 | 《反抗者》（*L'Homme révolté*）出版，系统论证反抗的形而上学与限度。萨特的追随者弗朗西斯·让松（Francis Jeanson）在《现代》（*Les Temps Modernes*）发表激烈批评 | *The Rebel* published, systematically arguing the metaphysics and limits of revolt. Sartre's follower Francis Jeanson publishes a scathing critique in *Les Temps Modernes* |
| 1952 | 加缪在《现代》发表公开回应萨特和让松的长信，萨特随后发表反驳。两人正式决裂，不再往来 | Camus publishes an open letter responding to Sartre and Jeanson in *Les Temps Modernes*; Sartre replies. The two formally break, never speaking again |
| 1953 | 肺结核再次发作，在阿尔及利亚的提帕萨休养。编辑出版《时文集I》（*Actuelles I*） | Tuberculosis recurs; recuperates at Tipasa, Algeria. Edits and publishes *Actuelles I* |
| 1954 | 阿尔及利亚战争爆发。加缪陷入痛苦的沉默——作为"黑脚"（pied-noir），他既反对殖民主义的不正义，也无法支持民族解放阵线（FLN）的暴力 | Algerian War begins. Camus falls into anguished silence — as a "pied-noir," he opposes colonial injustice but cannot support the FLN's violence |
| 1956 | 《堕落》（*La Chute*）出版，以克莱芒斯的独白形式深刻反思审判与罪责 | *The Fall* published, profoundly reflecting on judgment and guilt through Clamence's monologue |
| 1956 | 在斯德哥尔摩发表呼吁阿尔及利亚停战的演讲，遭到两派共同抨击后宣布公开沉默 | Delivers a speech in Stockholm calling for a ceasefire in Algeria; attacked by both sides and announces public silence |

## 晚期与逝世（1957–1960）/ Late Period and Death (1957–1960)

| 年份 | 事件 | Event |
|------|------|-------|
| 1957 | 出版《流放与王国》（*L'Exil et le Royaume*），收录六篇短篇小说 | Publishes *Exile and the Kingdom*, a collection of six short stories |
| 1957 | 12月，获诺贝尔文学奖。在斯德哥尔摩的获奖致辞中感谢小学老师路易·热尔曼和母亲。名言："我相信正义，但在正义与母亲之间，我选择母亲" | December: awarded the Nobel Prize in Literature. In his Stockholm acceptance speech, thanks his primary school teacher Louis Germain and his mother. Famous remark: "I believe in justice, but between justice and my mother, I choose my mother" |
| 1958 | 重新出版《反与正》，写新序言。继续写作《第一人》。编辑出版《时文集III》（*Actuelles III: Chronique algérienne*） | Republishes *The Wrong Side and the Right Side* with a new preface. Continues writing *The First Man*. Publishes *Actuelles III: Algerian Chronicle* |
| 1959 | 在普罗旺斯卢尔马兰（Lourmarin）购置房产，计划在此安静写作。肺结核恶化 | Purchases a house in Lourmarin, Provence, planning to write in quiet. Tuberculosis worsens |
| 1960 | 1月4日，在从卢尔马兰返回巴黎途中，于维尔布勒万（Villeblevin）附近遭遇车祸身亡，年仅46岁。同车的出版商米歇尔·伽利玛（Michel Gallimard）也遇难。口袋里装着未完成的《第一人》手稿 | 4 January: dies in a car accident near Villeblevin while returning to Paris from Lourmarin, aged 46. Publisher Michel Gallimard, also in the car, dies too. Carries the unfinished manuscript of *The First Man* in his pocket |

## 身后影响 / Posthumous Influence

| 年份 | 事件 | Event |
|------|------|-------|
| 1962 | 《阿尔及利亚的夏》（*L'Été*）出版，收录地中海随笔 | *Summer* published, a collection of Mediterranean essays |
| 1965 | 戏剧《卡利古拉》（*Caligula*）在巴黎复演，获得新的关注 | *Caligula* revived in Paris to renewed attention |
| 1989 | 冷战结束。《反抗者》的"限度"思想开始获得重新评价 | Cold War ends. *The Rebel*'s thought on "limits" begins to be reassessed |
| 1994 | 《第一人》（*Le Premier Homme*）由女儿卡特琳娜整理出版，引发加缪研究新浪潮 | *The First Man* published by daughter Catherine, sparking a new wave of Camus scholarship |
| 2013 | 加缪百年诞辰。全球范围内举办大量纪念活动和学术会议，学术界对加缪哲学贡献进行系统性的重新评估 | Camus centenary. Extensive commemorative events and conferences worldwide; systematic scholarly reassessment of Camus's philosophical contribution |
| 2020s | 加缪的"限度"思想和地中海哲学在后殖民研究、生态哲学和人权理论中获得新的解读和应用 | Camus's thought on "limits" and Mediterranean philosophy receives new readings in postcolonial studies, ecological philosophy, and human rights theory |
