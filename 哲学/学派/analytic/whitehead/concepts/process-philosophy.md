# 过程哲学 · Process Philosophy

## 概念定位 / Conceptual Position
实在的基本单位不是"实体"（substance），而是"事件"（event）或"现实机缘"（actual occasion）。一切存在都是生成的过程。

The basic units of reality are not "substances" but "events" or "actual occasions." All existence is a process of becoming.

## 核心论述 / Core Argument

### 实体 vs 过程 / Substance vs Process
- **实体本体论 / Substance Ontology**: 基本实在是持久的、不变的实体（如原子、灵魂）
- **过程本体论 / Process Ontology**: 基本实在是瞬间的生成事件（actual occasions）

怀特海论证：实体本体论无法解释变化、关系和创造性；过程本体论可以。

Whitehead argues: substance ontology cannot explain change, relations, and creativity; process ontology can.

### "多成为一" / "The Many Become One"
过程的基本模式是：多个前身的现实机缘被整合为一个新的现实机缘——"多成为一，并增加一"（the many become one, and are increased by one）。

The basic pattern: multiple predecessor occasions are integrated into a new occasion — "the many become one, and are increased by one."

## 哲学意义 / Philosophical Significance
为生态哲学、过程神学和量子力学诠释提供了形而上学基础。

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue
### 与佛教缘起 / With Buddhist Dependent Origination
佛教"十二因缘"描述的正是事件流（而非实体）的动态生成过程——每一"支"摄受前一支，如同"现实机缘"摄受前身事件。过程哲学与缘起思想形成最深刻的对话。

Buddhist twelve links describe dynamic event-flow, not substances. Each link "prehends" its predecessor, like actual occasions prehend predecessor events. Process philosophy and dependent origination form the deepest dialogue.
