# 波普尔 Karl Popper

## 基本信息 / Basic Information

- **生卒年 / Lifespan**: 1902年7月28日 – 1994年9月17日
- **国籍 / Nationality**: 奥地利出生，后入英国籍 / Austrian-born, later British citizen
- **学术身份 / Academic Position**: 伦敦政治经济学院（LSE）教授（1946–1969）
- **学派 / School**: 批判理性主义（Critical Rationalism）
- **核心贡献 / Core Contribution**: 证伪主义科学划界标准；开放社会理论；三个世界本体论

## 核心命题 / Core Theses

1. **证伪原则 / Falsification Principle**: 一个理论是科学的，当且仅当它在原则上可以被经验反驳——"一切天鹅是白的"可被一只黑天鹅证伪。科学与非科学的划界标准不是可证实性，而是可证伪性。/ A theory is scientific if and only if it can in principle be refuted by experience. The demarcation criterion between science and non-science is falsifiability, not verifiability.

2. **猜想与反驳 / Conjectures and Refutations**: 科学方法 = 大胆猜想 + 严格反驳。好的理论是那些冒着最大证伪风险且暂时幸存的理论。科学通过排除错误而非积累证实来进步。/ Scientific method = bold conjectures + rigorous refutations. Good theories risk the most falsification and temporarily survive. Science progresses by eliminating error, not accumulating verification.

3. **开放社会 / Open Society**: 极权主义的共同特征是"历史决定论"——相信历史有必然规律。开放社会必须容忍批判和改革，接受"渐进社会工程"而非乌托邦工程。/ Totalitarianism's common feature is "historicism" — belief in inevitable laws of history. Open societies must tolerate criticism and reform, accepting "piecemeal social engineering" rather than utopian engineering.

4. **三个世界 / Three Worlds**: 世界1 = 物理实在；世界2 = 主观心智状态；世界3 = 客观知识（理论、问题、论证），具有相对自主性。世界3一旦被创造，就产生独立的逻辑后果。/ World 1 = physical reality; World 2 = subjective mental states; World 3 = objective knowledge (theories, problems, arguments) with relative autonomy. Once created, World 3 generates independent logical consequences.

5. **批判理性主义 / Critical Rationalism**: 理性不是证明，而是批判——理性的核心是对一切信念保持可错的、批判的态度。知识不是确定的信念，而是经过批判尚未被反驳的猜想。/ Rationality is not proof but criticism — rationality's core is maintaining a fallible, critical attitude toward all beliefs. Knowledge is not certain belief but conjectures that have survived criticism.

6. **进化认识论 / Evolutionary Epistemology**: 知识的增长类似生物进化——通过变异（猜想）和选择（反驳）来适应环境。科学知识是"适应度最高"的理论。/ Knowledge growth parallels biological evolution — through variation (conjectures) and selection (refutations) to adapt to environment. Scientific knowledge is the "fittest" theory.

## 思想脉络 / Intellectual Development

- **维也纳时期 / Vienna Period (1920s–1930s)**: 与维也纳学圈互动但保持批判距离。完成 *Logik der Forschung* (1934)，提出证伪主义。/ Interacted with Vienna Circle but maintained critical distance. Completed *Logik der Forschung* (1934), proposing falsificationism.
- **新西兰流亡 / New Zealand Exile (1937–1945)**: 写作 *The Open Society and Its Enemies* (1945)，回应极权主义威胁。/ Wrote *The Open Society and Its Enemies* (1945), responding to the totalitarian threat.
- **伦敦时期 / London Period (1946–1969)**: 任LSE教授，培养拉卡托斯（Lakatos）等后继者。/ LSE professor; mentored successors including Lakatos.
- **晚期 / Late Period (1970s–1994)**: 发展"三个世界"本体论，探索量子力学诠释。/ Developed "Three Worlds" ontology, explored quantum mechanics interpretation.

## 常见误读 / Common Misreadings

1. **误读**: "证伪主义意味着一次反例就抛弃理论。"
   **澄清**: Popper承认需要区分"反常"和"反驳"——只有严格的、可重复的反驳才算数。
   *Popper acknowledged the need to distinguish "anomalies" from "refutations."*

2. **误读**: "Popper是反马克思主义的保守派。"
   **澄清**: Popper自认是社会民主主义者，批评的是方法论（历史决定论），不是社会正义理想。
   *Popper identified as a social democrat, criticizing methodology (historicism), not social justice ideals.*

3. **误读**: "可证伪性已被库恩的范式论取代。"
   **澄清**: 两者处理不同问题：Popper关心规范性的划界标准，库恩关心描述性的科学实践。
   *They address different questions: Popper concerns normative demarcation criteria, Kuhn concerns descriptive scientific practice.*

4. **误读**: "三个世界理论是朴素的二元论变体。"
   **澄清**: 世界3不是"心灵"——它是客观的、主体间的知识内容，如数学定理在被证明之前就已蕴含逻辑后果。
   *World 3 is not "mind" — it is objective, intersubjective knowledge content.*

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

### 与量论"比量的可错性" / With Pramana's "Fallibility of Inference"
佛教量论承认"比量"（anumana, 推理认知）是可错的——推理可能出错，需要被更精确的现量（直接感知）所校正。这与证伪主义的认识论有深层共鸣：两者都承认真理不能通过积累证据来"证明"，只能通过不断排除错误来接近。

Buddhist Pramana acknowledges the fallibility of inference — reasoning can err and needs correction by more precise direct perception. This deeply resonates with falsificationist epistemology: both acknowledge that truth cannot be "proven" through accumulated evidence, only approached through continuously eliminating error.

### 与中观"破而不立" / With Madhyamaka's "Refutation without Establishment"
龙树的中观学派采用"破而不立"的方法——不建立自己的正面理论，只通过揭示对手理论的内在矛盾来展示其不成立。这与Popper的"通过反驳来进步"有方法论上的相似性。不同之处在于，中观的目标是解脱（超越一切理论立场），Popper的目标是更接近真理。

Nagarjuna's Madhyamaka school adopts "refutation without establishment" — not building positive theories, only revealing internal contradictions in opponents' theories. This resembles Popper's "progress through refutation" methodologically. The difference: Madhyamaka aims at liberation (transcending all theoretical positions), while Popper aims at approaching truth more closely.
