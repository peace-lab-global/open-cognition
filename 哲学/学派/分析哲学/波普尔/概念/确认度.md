# 确认度 · Corroboration (Bewährung)

## 概念定位 / Conceptual Position

"确认度"（Corroboration）是波普尔用来取代"证实"（verification）和"确认"（confirmation）的概念。一个理论具有高确认度，意味着它已经经受了许多严格的检验而未被反驳——但这不意味着它"更可能为真"。确认度是对理论过去表现的报告，不是对其未来成功的预测。

"Corroboration" is Popper's concept replacing "verification" and "confirmation." A theory has high corroboration when it has survived many rigorous tests without being refuted — but this does not mean it is "more likely true." Corroboration is a report on the theory's past performance, not a prediction of its future success.

## 核心论述 / Core Argument

### 与"概率"的区别 / Distinction from "Probability"

波普尔明确反对将确认度等同于概率：
- 高概率的理论通常是不精确的、不冒险的（如"明天会下雨或不下雨"概率为1但无信息量）
- 高确认度的理论是大胆的、精确的、经历了严格检验的

Popper explicitly opposed equating corroboration with probability: high-probability theories tend to be imprecise and unadventurous; highly corroborated theories are bold, precise, and have survived rigorous testing.

### 确认度的计算 / Calculating Corroboration

确认度 C(h, e) 衡量的是证据 e 对假说 h 的支持程度——它是检验严格性的函数，而非证据量的函数。一个理论的确认度取决于它冒了多大的风险。

Corroboration C(h, e) measures the degree to which evidence e supports hypothesis h — it is a function of test severity, not evidence quantity.

## 哲学意义 / Philosophical Significance

1. **避免归纳 / Avoiding Induction**: 确认度不依赖归纳推理
2. **方法论指导 / Methodological Guidance**: 鼓励科学家提出更大胆的理论
3. **被挑战 / Challenged**: 贝叶斯主义者认为概率确认比波普尔的确认度更合理

## 与东方思想的对话 / Dialogue with Eastern Thought

### 与量论"无误" / With Pramana's "Non-Error"
量论将有效认知（pramana）定义为"无误的认知"（avisaṃvādaka）。这与确认度有类似的动机：如何在不声称"绝对确定"的情况下区分好理论与坏理论。量论通过认知的"实效性"（arthakriyā）来判断，波普尔通过检验的严格性。

Pramana defines valid cognition as "non-erroneous cognition." This shares similar motivations with corroboration: how to distinguish good theories from bad without claiming "absolute certainty." Pramana judges by cognition's "pragmatic efficacyacy," Popper by test severity.
