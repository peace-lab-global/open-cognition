# 怀特海进阶阅读 · A.N. Whitehead Reading List

## 一、原典 / Primary Sources

### 必读
- *Process and Reality* (1929) — 过程形而上学核心巨著
- *Science and the Modern World* (1925) — 科学思想史与具体性误置谬误
- *The Aims of Education* (1929) — 教育哲学，最易读

### 重要
- *Adventures of Ideas* (1933) — 文明与观念
- *The Concept of Nature* (1920) — 自然哲学
- *Religion in the Making* (1926) — 宗教哲学
- *Modes of Thought* (1938) — 方法论反思

## 二、经典研究 / Classic Studies

- Victor Lowe, *Alfred North Whitehead: The Man and His Work* (1985, 1990) — 权威传记（2卷）
- William Ernest Hocking, "Whitehead the Man" (1956)
- Charles Hartshorne, *Whitehead's Philosophy* (1972) — 过程神学创立者的解读
- Ivor Leclerc, *Whitehead's Metaphysics* (1958) — 形而上学经典研究
- Donald W. Sherburne, *A Whiteheadian Aesthetic* (1961) — 美学维度
- Jorge Luis Nobo, *Whitehead's Metaphysics of Extension and Solidarity* (1986) — 外延与团结
- Isabel Stengers, *Thinking with Whitehead* (2002, English 2011) — 欧陆视角

## 三、当代研究 / Contemporary Studies

- Nicholas Rescher, *Process Philosophy: A Survey of Basic Issues* (2000) — 过程哲学概述
- Michel Weber, *Whitehead's Pancreativism* (2006) — 最系统的当代研究
- Steven Shaviro, *Without Criteria: Kant, Whitehead, Deleuze, and Aesthetics* (2009) — 与德勒兹的比较
- Roland Faber, *God as Poet of the World* (2008) — 过程神学当代发展
- Brian Henning, *The Ethics of Creativity* (2005) — 过程哲学与生态伦理
- Michael Weber & William Desmond (eds.), *Handbook of Whiteheadian Process Thought* (2008) — 最全面的手册

## 四、入门推荐 / Introductory

- Donald W. Sherburne (ed.), *A Key to Whitehead's Process and Reality* (1966) — 《过程与实在》导读
- Elizabeth Kraus, *The Metaphysics of Experience* (1979, 2nd ed. 1998) — 最佳系统导论
- "Alfred North Whitehead" in *Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy* (Ronald)
- "Process Philosophy" in *Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy* (Seibt)
- John B. Cobb Jr., *Living Options in Process Thought* (1978) — 过程思想的通俗阐释
- Helmut Mair, *Process and Reality: An Introduction* (2019) — 当代入门

## 五、阅读路径建议

| 目标 | 推荐顺序 |
|------|----------|
| 快速了解 | *Aims of Education* → *Science and the Modern World* ch. 1-3 |
| 进入过程哲学 | Sherburne *Key* → *Process and Reality* Part I → Kraus |
| 研究上帝论 | *Religion in the Making* → Hartshorne → Faber |
| 生态哲学 | Henning → Cobb → *Adventures of Ideas* |
| 比较德勒兹 | Shaviro → Deleuze *The Fold* → Stengers |
| 科学哲学 | *Concept of Nature* → *Science and the Modern World* → *Principle of Relativity* |
