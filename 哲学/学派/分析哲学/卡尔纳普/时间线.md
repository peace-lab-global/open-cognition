# 卡尔纳普年表 / Carnap Timeline

| 年份 / Year | 事件 / Event |
|---|---|
| 1891 | 5月18日生于德国Wuppertal附近Ronsdorf。/ Born May 18 in Ronsdorf, near Wuppertal, Germany. |
| 1910–1914 | 在耶拿大学学习数学、物理和哲学。师从弗雷格（Frege）。/ Studied mathematics, physics, and 哲学 at University of Jena. Studied under Frege. |
| 1914–1918 | 一战期间在德军服役。/ Served in German army during WWI. |
| 1921 | 获耶拿大学博士学位（论文论时空理论）。/ Received PhD from University of Jena (thesis on space-time theory). |
| 1926 | 加入维也纳大学哲学系，成为维也纳学圈核心成员。/ Joined University of Vienna 哲学 department; became core member of Vienna Circle. |
| 1928 | 出版《世界的逻辑构造》(*Aufbau*)和《哲学中的伪问题》。/ Published *Aufbau* and *Pseudo-Problems in Philosophy*. |
| 1929 | 起草《维也纳学圈宣言》（与汉恩、纽拉特合作）。/ Drafted Vienna Circle Manifesto (with Hahn and Neurath). |
| 1931 | 任布拉格大学哲学教授。/ Appointed professor at German University in Prague. |
| 1934 | 出版《语言的逻辑句法》。/ Published *The Logical Syntax of Language*. |
| 1935 | 因纳粹威胁移居美国。任芝加哥大学助理教授。/ Emigrated to US due to Nazi threat. Assistant professor at University of Chicago. |
| 1942 | 出版《语义学导论》。/ Published *Introduction to Semantics*. |
| 1947 | 出版《意义与必然》。/ Published *Meaning and Necessity*. |
| 1950 | 出版《概率的逻辑基础》和经典论文《经验主义、语义学与本体论》。/ Published *Logical Foundations of Probability* and classic paper *Empiricism, Semantics, and Ontology*. |
| 1952 | 转任UCLA哲学教授。/ Moved to UCLA as professor of 哲学. |
| 1954 | 从UCLA退休，继续研究和写作。/ Retired from UCLA; continued 研究 and writing. |
| 1963 | 出版《 intellectual Autobiography》（学术自传）。/ Published *Intellectual Autobiography*. |
| 1970 | 9月14日在加州Hollywood去世。/ Died September 14 in Hollywood, California. |
