# 亚里士多德生平年表 · Aristotle Timeline

| 年份 Year | 事件 Event | 背景 Context |
|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| 384 BCE | 出生于色雷斯半岛的斯塔吉拉（Stagira）。父尼各马可为马其顿王御医 | 柏拉图已创办雅典学园 3 年 / Born in Stagira. Father Nicomachus was court physician to Macedonian king. Plato's Academy already 3 years old |
| 367 | 17 岁到雅典，进入柏拉图学园 | 在学园学习和教学 20 年 / Age 17, arrives in Athens, enters Plato's Academy. Studies and teaches there for 20 years |
| 348/347 | 柏拉图去世。亚里士多德离开学园 | 可能因不认同新园长斯珀西波斯的数学化方向 / Plato dies. Aristotle leaves Academy—possibly disagreeing with new head Speusippus' mathematical direction |
| 347–345 | 在阿索斯（Assos, 小亚细亚）与赫米亚斯的宫廷交往 | 与皮提亚斯结婚 / At Assos (Asia Minor) with Hermias' court. Marries Pythias |
| 345–343 | 在莱斯博斯岛（Lesbos）进行海洋生物学研究 | 与泰奥弗拉斯托斯合作 / On Lesbos conducting marine biology research. Collaborates with Theophrastus |
| 343 | 受马其顿王腓力二世之邀，担任亚历山大（13 岁）的导师 | 在米埃扎（Mieza）教学 / Invited by Philip II of Macedon to tutor Alexander (age 13). Teaches at Mieza |
| 340 | 亚历山大开始摄政，亚里士多德结束直接教学 | 可能返回斯塔吉拉 / Alexander begins regency; Aristotle ends direct tutoring. May return to Stagira |
| 335 | 返回雅典，在吕克昂（Lyceum）创办自己的学校 | 建立"逍遥学派"（Peripatetic School）/ Returns to Athens, founds school at the Lyceum. Establishes the Peripatetic School |
| 335–323 | 吕克昂学园时期：建立涵盖所有学科的学术体系 | 收集标本、手稿、地图；建立可能是第一个图书馆和动物园 / Lyceum period: establishes comprehensive academic system spanning all disciplines. Collects specimens, manuscripts, maps; builds possibly the first library and zoo |
| 323 | 亚历山大大帝去世。雅典反马其顿情绪高涨。亚里士多德被控"不敬"（asebeia） | "不让雅典第二次对哲学犯罪"（指苏格拉底之死）。离开雅典前往卡尔基斯 / Alexander dies. Anti-Macedonian sentiment rises. Aristotle charged with "impiety." "Not letting Athens sin against philosophy twice." Leaves for Chalcis |
| 322 | 在卡尔基斯（Chalcis, 优卑亚岛）去世，享年约 62 岁 | 泰奥弗拉斯托斯继任吕克昂学园园长 / Dies in Chalcis (Euboea), aged ~62. Theophrastus succeeds as head of the Lyceum |

## 亚里士多德之后的直接影响 Aftermath and Direct Influence

| 年份 Year | 事件 Event |
|-----------|-----------|
| 322 | 泰奥弗拉斯托斯继任吕克昂学园 / Theophrastus succeeds at Lyceum |
| c. 300 | 逍遥学派继续发展；斯特拉托（Strato）强调自然主义 / Peripatetic school continues; Strato emphasizes naturalism |
| c. 50 BCE | 安德罗尼库斯（Andronicus of Rhodes）整理亚里士多德著作 / Andronicus of Rhodes edits Aristotle's works |
| c. 100 CE | 亚里士多德逻辑学通过亚历山大（Alexander of Aphrodisias）的注释得到发展 / Aristotelian logic developed through Alexander of Aphrodisias' commentaries |
| c. 900–1200 | 阿拉伯哲学家（法拉比、阿维森纳、阿威罗伊）注释和发展亚里士多德 / Islamic philosophers (Al-Farabi, Avicenna, Averroes) comment on and develop Aristotle |
| c. 1250 | 阿奎那将亚里士多德哲学与基督教神学融合 / Aquinas fuses Aristotelian philosophy with Christian theology |
| 1958 | Anscombe 发表 "Modern Moral Philosophy"，引发德性伦理学复兴 / Anscombe publishes "Modern Moral Philosophy," sparking virtue ethics revival |
