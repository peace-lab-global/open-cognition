# 莱布尼茨进阶阅读 · Leibniz Reading List

## 原典 · Primary Sources

### 英文译本 / English Translations

- Leibniz, G. W. *Philosophical Essays*. Trans. Roger Ariew & Daniel Garber. Hackett, 1989. — 最佳英文选集，涵盖核心文本
- Leibniz, G. W. *Monadology and Other Philosophical Essays*. Trans. George Montgomery. Bobbs-Merrill, 1965.
- Leibniz, G. W. *New Essays on Human Understanding*. Trans. Peter Remnant & Jonathan Bennett. Cambridge University Press, 1996.
- Leibniz, G. W. *Theodicy: Essays on the Goodness of God, the Freedom of Man, and the Origin of Evil*. Trans. E. M. Huggard. Open Court, 1985.
- Leibniz, G. W. *Philosophical Papers and Letters*. Ed. Leroy E. Loemker. 2nd ed. Reidel, 1969. — 标准英文选集
- Leibniz, G. W. *The Leibniz-Clarke Correspondence*. Ed. H. G. Alexander. Manchester University Press, 1956.
- Leibniz, G. W. *Discourse on Metaphysics and Correspondence with Arnauld*. Trans. R. T. W. George. Open Court, 1992.

### 中文译本 / Chinese Translations

- 莱布尼茨著，陈修斋译.《新科学》（即《人类理智新论》）. 商务印书馆.
- 莱布尼茨著，段德智译.《神义论》. 上海三联书店, 2007.
- 莱布尼茨著，陈乐焜等译.《单子论》. 收录于《莱布尼茨哲学著作选》.
- 莱布尼茨著，王荫庭等译.《形而上学谈》. 商务印书馆.

## 传记 · Biography

- Aiton, E. J. (1985). *Leibniz: A Biography*. Adam Hilger. — 最全面的英文传记
- Jolley, N. (2005). *Leibniz*. Routledge. — 入门级传记和哲学介绍
- Antognazza, M. R. (2009). *Leibniz: An Intellectual Biography*. Cambridge University Press. — 最新学术传记

## 二手研究 · Secondary Literature

### 综合研究 / Comprehensive Studies

- Jolley, N. (ed.) (1995). *The Cambridge Companion to Leibniz*. Cambridge University Press. — 最重要的论文集
- Adams, R. M. (1994). *Leibniz: Determinist, Theist, Idealist*. Oxford University Press. — 深入研究
- Rescher, N. (1979). *Leibniz: An Introduction to His Philosophy*. Rowman & Littlefield.
- Russell, B. (1937). *A Critical Exposition of the Philosophy of Leibniz*. 2nd ed. Allen & Unwin. — 经典分析

### 专题研究 / Topical Studies

- **单子论 / Monadology**:
  - Rescher, N. (1991). *G. W. Leibniz's Monadology*. University of Pittsburgh Press.
  - Lodge, P. (ed.) (2004). *Leibniz's Monadology: A New Translation and Guide*. Edinburgh University Press.

- **形而上学 / Metaphysics**:
  - Cover, J. & O'Leary-Hawthorne, J. (1999). *Substance and Individuation in Leibniz*. Cambridge University Press.
  - Sleigh, R. C. (1990). *Leibniz and Arnauld: A Commentary on Their Correspondence*. Yale University Press.

- **神义论 / Theodicy**:
  - Murray, M. J. (2013). "Leibniz on Divine Foreknowledge and Free Will." In *Leibniz: New Essays*.
  - Jorati, J. (2021). *Leibniz on Free Will*. Oxford University Press.

- **数学与逻辑 / Mathematics and Logic**:
  - Bos, H. J. M. (1974). "Differentials, Higher-Order Differentials and the Derivative in the Leibnizian Calculus." *Archive for History of Exact Sciences*.
  - Lenzen, W. (2004). "Leibniz's Logic." In *Handbook of the History of Logic*.

## 在线资源 · Online Resources

- [Leibniz at Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy](https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/leibniz/) — 最权威的在线哲学百科条目
- [Leibniz at Internet Encyclopedia of Philosophy](https://iep.utm.edu/leibniz/) — 面向学生的入门条目
- [Leibniz Translations — Lloyd Strickland](http://www.leibniz-translations.com/) — 大量英文翻译
- [Gottfried Wilhelm Leibniz Bibliothek](https://www.leibniz-bibliothek.de/) — 汉诺威莱布尼茨图书馆

## 比较阅读 · Comparative Reading

- Descartes, R. *Meditations* — 莱布尼茨的理性主义源头
- Spinoza, B. *Ethics* — 莱布尼茨的一元论对照
- Locke, J. *Essay Concerning Human Understanding* — 莱布尼茨在《人类理智新论》中逐节回应
- Newton, I. *Principia Mathematica* — 微积分优先权之争的背景
- Kant, I. *Critique of Pure Reason* — 莱布尼茨传统的先验转化
- Voltaire. *Candide* (1759) — 对莱布尼茨乐观主义的讽刺

## 延伸阅读 · Extended Reading

- Kripke, S. (1980). *Naming and Necessity*. Harvard University Press. — 莱布尼茨可能世界概念的当代发展
- Lewis, D. (1986). *On the Plurality of Worlds*. Blackwell. — 模态实在论
- Rescher, N. (1998). *Leibniz: A Practical Introduction*. — 面向普通读者的莱布尼茨入门
- Jorgensen, L. M. (2009). "The Principle of Sufficient Reason." *Philosophy Compass*, 4(6).
