# 阿拉斯戴尔·麦金泰尔 · Alasdair MacIntyre

> **"现代道德话语是一堆碎片——失去了使其有意义的语境。"**
> **"Modern moral discourse is a collection of fragments — bereft of the context that gave it meaning."**

## 基本信息 / Basic Information

阿拉斯戴尔·查尔默斯·麦金泰尔（Alasdair Chalmers MacIntyre），1929年1月12日生于苏格兰格拉斯哥，是当代最具影响力的道德哲学家之一。他在格拉斯哥大学获硕士学位，后在牛津大学深造。早年受马克思主义影响，曾参与英国共产党左翼知识分子圈子，并在曼彻斯特大学和利兹大学任教。1960年代起，他逐渐转向基督教神学和亚里士多德哲学传统，1980年代正式皈依天主教，这一思想转变深刻塑造了他后来的哲学方向。

Alasdair Chalmers MacIntyre, born January 12, 1929, in Glasgow, Scotland, is one of the most influential moral philosophers of the contemporary era. He earned his MA at the University of Glasgow and pursued postgraduate studies at Oxford. Influenced early on by Marxism, he participated in left-wing intellectual circles associated with the British Communist Party, and taught at the Universities of Manchester and Leeds. From the 1960s onward, he gradually turned toward Christian theology and the Aristotelian philosophical tradition; his formal conversion to Catholicism in the 1980s profoundly shaped his subsequent philosophical trajectory.

麦金泰尔先后执教于普林斯顿大学、波士顿大学、范德堡大学、杜克大学和圣母大学（University of Notre Dame），目前为圣母大学荣休高级研究教授。他被广泛认为是20世纪美德伦理学复兴运动的核心人物，其代表作《追寻美德》（*After Virtue*, 1981）被誉为"二战后最重要的伦理学著作之一"。他的思想横跨道德哲学、政治哲学、宗教哲学、教育哲学等多个领域，并对社群主义、叙事伦理学和实践哲学产生了深远影响。值得注意的是，尽管常被归为"社群主义者"，麦金泰尔本人一直拒绝这一标签。

MacIntyre has held positions at Princeton University, Boston University, Vanderbilt University, Duke University, and the University of Notre Dame, where he is currently Senior Research Professor Emeritus. He is widely regarded as the central figure in the late-twentieth-century revival of virtue ethics. His landmark work *After Virtue* (1981) has been called "one of the most important works of ethics written since World War II." His thought spans moral philosophy, political philosophy, 哲学 of religion, and 哲学 of education, and has had a profound impact on communitarianism, narrative ethics, and practical 哲学. Notably, although often classified as a "communitarian," MacIntyre himself has consistently rejected this label.

## 核心命题 / Core Theses

**1. 情感主义诊断 / The Emotivist Diagnosis**
现代道德争论——关于堕胎、战争、分配正义等议题——无法达成理性共识，因为参与者使用的是来自不同且互不相容传统的碎片化道德语言。当代道德话语已经退化为情感主义（emotivism）：道德判断表面上是客观断言，实质上不过是个人偏好和情感的表达。Modern moral disputes — on abortion, war, distributive justice — are incapable of rational resolution because the disputants employ fragmented moral vocabularies drawn from incompatible traditions. Contemporary moral discourse has degenerated into emotivism: moral judgments that are ostensibly objective claims are, in substance, nothing more than expressions of personal preference and feeling.

**2. 实践与内在善 / Practice and Internal Goods**
美德只能在"实践"（practice）中获得和展现。实践是人类活动的任何融贯而复杂的社会合作形式——如棋艺、建筑、科学探究、政治活动等。每种实践都有其"内在善"（internal goods），即只有通过参与该实践本身才能获得的卓越品质，区别于"外在善"（external goods）如金钱、名誉和权力。Virtues can only be acquired and exercised within "practices" — coherent and complex forms of socially established cooperative human activity, such as chess, architecture, scientific inquiry, or politics. Each practice has its "internal goods," excellences attainable only through participation in the practice itself, as distinct from "external goods" such as money, fame, and power.

**3. 叙事统一性 / Narrative Unity of a Human Life**
人的生活不是孤立事件的序列，而是一个叙事。人类行为的可理解性依赖于叙事的统一性——我们总是通过将行为嵌入更大的故事来理解它的意义。美德是使一个人的生活叙事趋向完善和统一的品质。A human life is not a mere sequence of isolated events but a narrative. The intelligibility of human action depends on narrative unity — we understand the meaning of actions only by situating them within a larger story. A virtue is a quality that enables the narrative of a human life to move toward completion and unity.

**4. 传统构成的合理性 / Tradition-Constituted Rationality**
理性不是在传统之外的抽象能力——所有推理都在某种传统之内进行。传统不是保守的、僵化的遗产，而是"关于争论的活着的论证"（a living argument about the goods of that tradition）。合理性本身就是传统构成的（tradition-constituted）：我们通过进入和延续某种传统来学习如何推理。Reason is not an abstract faculty standing outside all traditions — all reasoning occurs within some tradition. A tradition is not a conservative, fossilized inheritance but "a living argument about the goods" internal to that tradition. Rationality itself is tradition-constituted: we learn to reason by entering into and continuing a tradition.

**5. 对启蒙规划的批判 / Critique of the Enlightenment Project**
启蒙运动试图为道德找到独立于任何传统的普遍理性基础——从康德的绝对命令到功利主义的效用原则。麦金泰尔认为这一规划必然失败，因为道德推理总是嵌入在特定的历史与传统之中。启蒙的失败不仅是一个哲学问题，更是当代道德混乱的根源。The Enlightenment attempted to find a universal rational foundation for morality independent of any tradition — from Kant's categorical imperative to utilitarianism's principle of utility. MacIntyre argues this project was bound to fail, because moral reasoning is always embedded in a particular history and tradition. The Enlightenment's failure is not merely a philosophical problem but the root cause of contemporary moral confusion.

**6. 亚里士多德-托马斯主义的回归 / Aristotelian-Thomist Return**
在诊断了现代道德危机之后，麦金泰尔主张回归经过托马斯·阿奎那修正的亚里士多德美德伦理学传统。这一回归不是简单的复古，而是在承认现代条件的前提下，重新激活一个关于人类善和美德的活着的传统。Having diagnosed the modern moral crisis, MacIntyre advocates a return to the Aristotelian tradition of virtue ethics as modified by Thomas Aquinas. This return is not mere antiquarianism but a re-activation of a living tradition of inquiry into human goods and virtues, undertaken in full acknowledgment of modern conditions.

## 思想脉络 / Intellectual Development

### 早期 / Early Period (1950s-1960s)

麦金泰尔的思想生涯始于马克思主义和基督教社会主义的交汇处。1950年代，他是英国新左派（New Left）的活跃成员，撰写了大量关于马克思主义、阶级分析和革命政治的文章。1953年出版的《马克思主义：一种阐释》（*Marxism: An Interpretation*）展示了他试图将马克思主义与人道主义关怀相融合的努力。然而，他对马克思主义的不满逐渐增长——尤其是马克思主义在道德哲学方面的贫乏，以及它在实践中对斯大林主义的妥协。这一时期他还深入研究了存在主义（尤其是萨特）和基督教神学（巴特、蒂利希），试图为道德和政治行动找到更坚实的哲学基础。1966年的《伦理学简史》（*A Short History of Ethics*）标志着他从马克思主义向更广泛哲学传统探索的转变。

MacIntyre's intellectual career began at the intersection of Marxism and Christian socialism. In the 1950s, he was an active member of the British New Left, writing extensively on Marxism, class analysis, and revolutionary politics. His 1953 *Marxism: An Interpretation* displayed his attempt to fuse Marxism with humanistic concerns. However, his dissatisfaction with Marxism grew — particularly its philosophical poverty in moral theory and its practical accommodation with Stalinism. During this period he also engaged deeply with existentialism (especially Sartre) and Christian theology (Barth, Tillich), seeking firmer philosophical foundations for moral and political action. The 1966 *A Short History of Ethics* marked his transition from Marxism toward a broader exploration of philosophical traditions.

### 中期 / Middle Period (1970s-1990s)

1970年代，麦金泰尔移居美国，在波士顿大学和布兰迪斯大学任教。他越来越多地投入到分析哲学传统中的道德哲学争论——特别是通过对黑尔（R. M. Hare）和罗尔斯（John Rawls）的批判来发展自己的立场。1981年出版的《追寻美德》是他思想的转折点。该书提出了一个大胆的诊断：现代道德话语已经崩溃，而解决之道在于复兴亚里士多德的美德伦理学传统。此后，他在《谁之正义？何种合理性？》（1988）中进一步发展了"传统构成的合理性"概念，论证了不同传统之间的理性争论是可能的。1990年的《三种对立的道德探究》则区分了三种道德探究模式——百科全书派（启蒙理性主义）、谱系学（尼采式后现代主义）和传统（亚里士多德-托马斯主义），并将传统视为最具哲学说服力的方案。

In the 1970s, MacIntyre moved to the United States, teaching at Boston University and Brandeis University. He became increasingly engaged with moral 哲学 debates within the analytic tradition — particularly through critiques of R. M. Hare and John Rawls. The publication of *After Virtue* in 1981 was the watershed of his thought. The book offered a bold diagnosis: modern moral discourse had collapsed, and the solution lay in reviving the Aristotelian tradition of virtue ethics. Subsequently, in *Whose Justice? Which Rationality?* (1988), he further developed the concept of "tradition-constituted rationality," arguing that rational dispute between traditions is possible. *Three Rival Versions of Moral Enquiry* (1990) distinguished three modes of moral inquiry — encyclopaedic (Enlightenment rationalism), genealogical (Nietzschean postmodernism), and traditional (Aristotelian-Thomist) — and argued that the tradition-based approach is the most philosophically compelling.

### 晚期 / Late Period (1999-present)

1999年的《依赖性的理性动物》标志着麦金泰尔思想的一个重要转向：他将焦点从自主的理性行为者转向人类的脆弱性、依赖性和残疾。他指出，西方哲学传统——包括亚里士多德本人——长期忽视了人类作为动物性存在的现实，而美德伦理学必须正视人类生命中不可避免的依赖关系。他提出"承认依赖的美德"（virtues of acknowledged dependence）——包括感恩、慷慨和同情——作为对传统美德范畴的重要补充。2016年的《伦理学之后》（*Ethics in the Conflicts of Modernity*）进一步探讨了亚里士多德主义与当代社会实践之间的张力，审视了道德推理在具体社会冲突中的运作。近年来，麦金泰尔还越来越多地关注教育哲学和制度批判，探讨如何在现代大学中保存和传递传统。

The 1999 publication of *Dependent Rational Animals* marked an important turn in MacIntyre's thought: he shifted focus from the autonomous rational agent to human vulnerability, dependency, and disability. He argued that the Western philosophical tradition — including Aristotle himself — had long neglected the reality of humans as animal beings, and that virtue ethics must confront the inescapable dependencies of human life. He proposed "virtues of acknowledged dependence" — including gratitude, generosity, and compassion — as essential supplements to the traditional catalogue of virtues. *Ethics in the Conflicts of Modernity* (2016) further explored the tensions between Aristotelianism and contemporary social practices, examining how moral reasoning operates in concrete social conflicts. In recent years, MacIntyre has also turned increasingly to 哲学 of education and institutional critique, exploring how traditions can be preserved and transmitted within modern universities.

## 重要概念 / Key Concepts

1. **追寻美德 / After Virtue** — 对现代道德话语崩溃的诊断，以及通过回归亚里士多德传统来重建美德伦理学的方案。The diagnosis of the collapse of modern moral discourse and the project of rebuilding virtue ethics through a return to the Aristotelian tradition. [→ concepts/after-德性.md](concepts/after-德性.md)

2. **实践与叙事 / Practice and Narrative** — 实践是人类卓越品质得以展现的社会合作形式；叙事是人类行为获得可理解性的基本框架。Practice is the form of social cooperation within which human excellences are displayed; narrative is the fundamental framework through which human actions become intelligible. [→ concepts/practice-narrative.md](concepts/practice-narrative.md)

3. **传统构成的合理性 / Tradition-Constituted Rationality** — 所有理性推理都嵌入在特定传统之中；合理性不是超越传统的普遍能力，而是通过传统的学习和实践而获得的。All rational reasoning is embedded in particular traditions; rationality is not a universal faculty transcending traditions but is acquired through learning and practicing within a tradition. [→ concepts/tradition-constituted.md](concepts/tradition-constituted.md)

4. **内在善与外在善 / Internal Goods and External Goods** — 内在善是只有通过参与实践本身才能获得的卓越品质；外在善（金钱、权力、名誉）则可以通过多种途径获得。美德实践追求内在善而非外在善。Internal goods are excellences attainable only through participation in a practice itself; external goods (money, power, fame) can be achieved through many avenues. Virtuous practice pursues internal goods over external goods. [→ concepts/internal-goods.md](concepts/internal-goods.md)

5. **情感主义批判 / Critique of Emotivism** — 当代道德话语表面上是客观的道德断言，实质上是情感主义——道德判断不过是个人偏好和情感的表达，这是现代道德哲学的核心危机。Contemporary moral discourse appears to consist of objective moral assertions but is in substance emotivist — moral judgments are nothing more than expressions of personal preference and feeling, and this constitutes the core crisis of modern moral 哲学. [→ concepts/emotivism-critique.md](concepts/emotivism-critique.md)

6. **承认依赖的美德 / Virtues of Acknowledged Dependence** — 人类本质上是脆弱的、依赖性的动物；真正的美德伦理学必须承认这一现实，并将感恩、同情和慷慨等"承认依赖的美德"纳入核心范畴。Human beings are essentially vulnerable and dependent animals; genuine virtue ethics must acknowledge this reality and incorporate the "virtues of acknowledged dependence" — gratitude, compassion, and generosity — into its central catalogue.

7. **启蒙规划的失败 / Failure of the Enlightenment Project** — 启蒙思想家试图为道德建立独立于传统的普遍理性基础，这一努力在哲学上注定失败，因为道德推理本质上依赖于传统的语境。Enlightenment thinkers attempted to establish a universal rational foundation for morality independent of tradition; this effort was philosophically doomed to fail because moral reasoning is inherently dependent on the context of tradition.

8. **实践推理 / Practical Reasoning** — 不同于工具理性（instrumental rationality），实践推理涉及对人类善（human goods）的实质判断，需要在具体情境中权衡不同的善并做出明智的选择。Unlike instrumental rationality, practical reasoning involves substantive judgments about human goods and requires weighing competing goods and making wise choices in concrete situations.

9. **传统的内在争论 / Internal Debate of Traditions** — 传统不是封闭的教条体系，而是"关于争论的活着的论证"；传统内部始终存在着关于该传统的善和目的的持续争论。A tradition is not a closed dogmatic system but "a living argument about the goods" internal to it; within a tradition there is always ongoing debate about its goods and purposes.

10. **制度的腐蚀 / Institutional Corruption** — 当外在善（利润、效率、竞争成功）压制内在善（卓越、真理、合作），制度就会腐蚀其赖以存在的实践基础。When external goods (profit, efficiency, competitive success) override internal goods (excellence, truth, cooperation), institutions corrupt the practices on which they depend.

## 思想坐标 / Intellectual Coordinates

- **承接 / Inherits**: 亚里士多德（Aristotle）、托马斯·阿奎那（Thomas Aquinas）、黑格尔（Hegel，早期）、安斯康姆（G. E. M. Anscombe）
- **批判 / Critiques**: 康德主义（Kantianism）、功利主义（Utilitarianism）、罗尔斯（John Rawls）、情感主义（Emotivism）、尼采式后现代主义（Nietzschean postmodernism）、自由主义个人主义（Liberal individualism）
- **影响 / Influences**: 泰勒（Charles Taylor）、桑德尔（Michael Sandel）、麦金太尔学派的美德伦理学者（MacIntyrean virtue ethicists）、社群主义运动（Communitarian movement）、叙事伦理学（Narrative ethics）、教育哲学（Philosophy of education）、商业伦理学（Business ethics）

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

1. **商业伦理 / Business Ethics**: 麦金泰尔的"实践"概念被广泛应用于商业伦理学。穆尔（Geoff Moore）等学者论证了商业可以作为一种实践来理解，其中存在超越利润追求的内在善——如工匠精神、客户信任和产品卓越。MacIntyre's concept of "practice" has been widely applied in business ethics. Scholars like Geoff Moore have argued that business can be understood as a practice with internal goods beyond profit — such as craftsmanship, customer trust, and product excellence.

2. **医学伦理 / Medical Ethics**: 医学作为一种实践，其内在善是患者健康而非经济回报。麦金泰尔的框架为批判医疗商业化和过度技术化提供了有力的哲学工具。Medicine as a practice has internal goods — patient health rather than economic return. MacIntyre's framework provides powerful philosophical tools for critiquing the commercialization and over-technologization of healthcare.

3. **教育哲学 / Philosophy of Education**: 教育本身是否是一种实践？麦金泰尔认为教育是传递传统和培养实践推理能力的过程，这一观点对当代教育改革和大学治理具有深刻启示。Is education itself a practice? MacIntyre sees education as the process of transmitting traditions and cultivating practical reasoning — a view with profound implications for educational reform and university governance.

4. **环境伦理 / Environmental Ethics**: 《依赖性的理性动物》中对人类动物性和脆弱性的强调，为环境伦理学提供了新的理论基础——将人类重新嵌入自然世界而非凌驾于其上。The emphasis on human animality and vulnerability in *Dependent Rational Animals* provides new theoretical foundations for environmental ethics — re-embedding humans within the natural world rather than above it.

5. **政治哲学 / Political Philosophy**: 麦金泰尔对自由主义的批判和对地方性政治实践（local political practice）的倡导，影响了社群主义政治理论和当代民主理论的发展。MacIntyre's critique of liberalism and advocacy for local political practices has influenced communitarian political theory and contemporary democratic theory.

6. **科技伦理 / Technology Ethics**: 麦金泰尔关于制度腐蚀实践的分析可用于理解科技公司对新闻业、学术研究和社会关系的冲击——当算法优化的外在善压倒新闻质量和学术诚信的内在善。MacIntyre's analysis of institutions corrupting practices can be applied to understanding how tech companies impact journalism, academic research, and social relationships — when the external goods of algorithmic optimization overwhelm the internal goods of journalistic quality and academic integrity.

7. **跨文化伦理 / Cross-cultural Ethics**: 传统构成的合理性概念为跨文化伦理对话提供了框架——不同文化传统可以在承认各自前提的基础上进行理性争论，而非诉诸虚假的普遍主义或陷入相对主义。The concept of tradition-constituted rationality provides a framework for cross-cultural ethical dialogue — different cultural traditions can engage in rational dispute while acknowledging their respective premises, rather than resorting to false universalism or lapsing into relativism.

8. **社会制度分析 / Institutional Analysis**: 麦金泰尔的"实践-制度"（practice-institution）模型为分析当代各类制度——企业、医院、大学、媒体——中的结构性腐败提供了理论框架。MacIntyre's "practice-institution" model provides a theoretical framework for analyzing structural corruption in contemporary institutions of all kinds — corporations, hospitals, universities, and media.

## 常见误读 / Common Misreadings

**1.**
❌ "麦金泰尔是文化相对主义者" / "MacIntyre is a cultural relativist."
✅ 麦金泰尔明确反对相对主义。他认为传统之间可以进行理性的比较和批评——通过"认识论危机"（epistemological crisis）的概念，一个传统可以认识到自身的局限并向另一个传统学习。MacIntyre explicitly opposes relativism. He believes traditions can be rationally compared and criticized — through the concept of "epistemological crisis," one tradition can recognize its own limitations and learn from another.

**2.**
❌ "麦金泰尔反对一切现代制度和价值" / "MacIntyre opposes all modern institutions and values."
✅ 麦金泰尔批判的是现代道德哲学的前提和结构，而非现代性的一切面向。他承认现代性的某些成就，并试图在现代条件下重建美德传统。MacIntyre critiques the premises and structures of modern moral philosophy, not every aspect of modernity. He acknowledges certain achievements of modernity and seeks to rebuild the virtue tradition under modern conditions.

**3.**
❌ "麦金泰尔是社群主义者" / "MacIntyre is a communitarian."
✅ 麦金泰尔一直拒绝"社群主义"标签。他认为当代社群主义（如桑德尔、沃尔泽）仍然是自由主义框架内的争论，而他的方案是在更深层面上回到亚里士多德-托马斯传统。MacIntyre has consistently rejected the "communitarian" label. He views contemporary communitarians (Sandel, Walzer) as debating within a liberal framework, whereas his project involves a deeper return to the Aristotelian-Thomist tradition.

**4.**
❌ "麦金泰尔要我们回到中世纪" / "MacIntyre wants us to go back to the Middle Ages."
✅ 麦金泰尔主张的不是历史复古，而是哲学传统的复兴。他清楚地知道我们不可能也不应该简单地复制过去的社会形式，而是要在现代语境中重新激活亚里士多德-托马斯主义传统的核心洞见。MacIntyre advocates not historical revival but philosophical renaissance. He is fully aware that we cannot and should not simply replicate past social forms; rather, we must re-activate the core insights of the Aristotelian-Thomist tradition within modern contexts.

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

麦金泰尔的思想与东亚思想传统——尤其是儒家——存在着深刻的共鸣和富有成效的对话空间。首先，麦金泰尔对"实践"和"传统"的强调与儒家的"礼"（ritual propriety）概念高度契合：二者都认为美德不是抽象的原则应用，而是在具体的社会实践中培养的习惯和品质。其次，麦金泰尔对启蒙理性主义的批判——即道德不能建立在脱离传统的纯粹理性之上——与儒家对"道"（the Way）的理解相呼应：道不是一个可以被任何理性主体独立发现的普遍法则，而是在特定的历史文化传统中逐步展开的智慧。

MacIntyre's thought resonates deeply with East Asian intellectual traditions — especially Confucianism — and opens productive spaces for dialogue. First, his emphasis on "practice" and "tradition" aligns closely with the Confucian concept of *li* (ritual propriety): both hold that virtue is not the abstract application of principles but habits and dispositions cultivated through concrete social practices. Second, MacIntyre's critique of Enlightenment rationalism — that morality cannot be grounded in pure reason detached from tradition — echoes the Confucian understanding of *dao* (the Way): the Way is not a universal law discoverable independently by any rational subject, but wisdom that unfolds within a particular historical and cultural tradition.

此外，麦金泰尔晚年在《依赖性的理性动物》中对人类脆弱性和依赖性的关注，与儒家"仁"（benevolence/humaneness）的概念形成了有趣的对应——两者都将人与人之间的关怀和依赖关系视为道德生活的核心。然而，两者之间也存在重要的张力：麦金泰尔的托马斯主义传统强调自然法（natural law）和上帝秩序，而儒家传统则更侧重于"天人合一"的宇宙论框架。这种差异为跨文化哲学对话提供了丰富的素材。

Moreover, MacIntyre's late concern with human vulnerability and dependence in *Dependent Rational Animals* finds an intriguing counterpart in the Confucian concept of *ren* (benevolence/humaneness) — both place caring and dependent relationships at the heart of moral life. Yet important tensions remain: MacIntyre's Thomist tradition emphasizes natural law and divine order, while the Confucian tradition foregrounds the cosmological framework of "the unity of heaven and humanity" (tianren heyi). These differences provide rich material for cross-cultural philosophical dialogue.
