# 资格理论 · Entitlement Theory

## 概念概述 / Concept Overview

资格理论（Entitlement Theory）是罗伯特·诺齐克在《无政府、国家与乌托邦》（1974）中提出的分配正义理论。与罗尔斯的差异原则、功利主义或平等主义等"模式化"（patterned）或"目的状态"（end-state）正义理论不同，资格理论是一种"历史性"（historical）正义理论——它不关注分配的最终结果是否符合某种理想模式，而关注每个人所持有的财产是否通过正义的过程获得。

The Entitlement Theory is Robert Nozick's theory of distributive justice, presented in *Anarchy, State, and Utopia* (1974). Unlike "patterned" or "end-state" theories of justice — such as Rawls's difference principle, utilitarianism, or egalitarianism — the Entitlement Theory is a "historical" theory of justice. It is concerned not with whether the final distribution conforms to some ideal pattern, but with whether each person's holdings were acquired through a just process.

诺齐克的资格理论由三个核心原则构成：获取的正义（justice in acquisition）、转让的正义（justice in transfer）和矫正的正义（rectification of injustice）。这三个原则涵盖了财产权从最初获取到后续转让再到纠正不正义的完整链条。如果每个人的持有都符合这三个原则中的一个或另一个，那么该分配就是正义的——无论其结果多么不平等。

Nozick's Entitlement Theory consists of three core principles: justice in acquisition, justice in transfer, and rectification of injustice. These three principles cover the complete chain of property rights from initial acquisition through subsequent transfers to the correction of injustice. If everyone's holdings satisfy one or another of these principles, the distribution is just — however unequal the outcome.

## 理论内涵 / Theoretical Content

### 获取的正义 / Justice in Acquisition

获取的正义涉及最初如何将无主物（unowned things）变为私人财产。诺齐克继承了洛克的劳动混合理论：当一个人将自己的劳动与无主的自然资源相混合时，他就获得了对该资源的财产权。但诺齐克加入了"洛克式附带条件"（Lockean proviso）：获取行为不得使其他人的状况比在该资源未被占用时更差。

Justice in acquisition concerns how unowned things first become private property. Nozick inherits Locke's labor-mixing theory: when a person mixes their labor with unowned natural resources, they acquire property rights over those resources. But Nozick adds the "Lockean proviso": the act of acquisition must not make others worse off than they would have been had the resource remained unowned.

诺齐克对洛克式附带条件的解释相对宽松：只要私有占有的整体效果——通过提高生产效率和促进市场交易——使每个人的境况都优于无主状态，该附带条件就得到了满足。这一解释使得大量现实中的私有财产权在原则上得到了正当化。

Nozick's interpretation of the Lockean proviso is relatively permissive: as long as the overall effect of private appropriation — through improved efficiency and market exchange — leaves everyone better off than in the state of unowned resources, the proviso is satisfied. This interpretation renders a large portion of actual private property rights legitimate in principle.

### 转让的正义 / Justice in Transfer

转让的正义规定：财产只能通过自愿交易（包括交换、赠与和遗赠）从一个人转移到另一个人。任何非自愿的转移——包括盗窃、欺诈和强制性征税——都是不正义的。这一原则构成了诺齐克反对福利国家的核心论据：通过税收进行的强制性再分配违反了转让的正义，因为它未经财产所有者的同意而剥夺了其财产。

Justice in transfer stipulates that property may only pass from one person to another through voluntary transactions (including exchange, gift, and bequest). Any involuntary transfer — including theft, fraud, and coercive taxation — is unjust. This principle forms the core of Nozick's argument against the welfare state: coercive redistribution through taxation violates justice in transfer because it takes property without the owner's consent.

### 矫正的正义 / Rectification of Injustice

矫正的正义处理的是过去发生的不正义获取或转让。诺齐克承认，现实世界中的许多财产持有可以追溯到历史上的不正义行为——如征服、掠夺和奴役。在这种情况下，需要进行矫正以恢复正义。然而，诺齐克也坦承，矫正正义的具体实施是一个极其困难的问题，因为历史信息的缺失使得准确追溯不正义的链条几乎不可能。

Rectification of injustice addresses past unjust acquisitions or transfers. Nozick acknowledges that much property holding in the real world can be traced to historical injustices — conquest, plunder, and enslavement. In such cases, rectification is required to restore justice. However, Nozick candidly admits that the practical implementation of rectification is extremely difficult, since the loss of historical information makes it nearly impossible to trace chains of injustice accurately.

## 思想史脉络 / Intellectual History

资格理论的思想史根源可以追溯到洛克的《政府论》（1689），特别是洛克关于财产权的第五章。洛克论证了个人通过劳动获得财产权的自然权利，同时提出了"足够且同样好"的附带条件（"enough and as good" proviso）。诺齐克将这一传统重新阐释并严格化，使其服务于当代自由至上主义的政治哲学论证。

The intellectual roots of the Entitlement Theory trace back to Locke's *Second Treatise of Government* (1689), particularly Chapter V on property. Locke argued for the natural right of individuals to acquire property through labor, while proposing the "enough and as good" proviso. Nozick reinterpreted and rigorized this tradition, deploying it in the service of contemporary libertarian political philosophy.

在当代政治哲学中，资格理论最直接地回应了罗尔斯的差异原则。罗尔斯认为社会和经济不平等应当被安排为最不利者的最大利益服务，这要求一种持续的制度性再分配机制。诺齐克反驳说，任何要求维持特定分配模式的制度都必然持续干涉个人的自由选择，因此"自由会颠覆模式"（liberty upsets patterns）。

In contemporary political philosophy, the Entitlement Theory most directly responds to Rawls's difference principle. Rawls held that social and economic inequalities should be arranged to benefit the least advantaged, requiring a continuous institutional mechanism of redistribution. Nozick countered that any system requiring the maintenance of a specific distributional pattern must continuously interfere with individuals' free choices, hence "liberty upsets patterns."

G.A. 柯亨（G.A. Cohen）在《自我所有权、自由与平等》（1995）中对资格理论进行了最系统的马克思主义批判，指出诺齐克的自我所有权前提并不必然导致不平等的正当化。

G.A. Cohen's *Self-Ownership, Freedom, and Equality* (1995) offered the most systematic Marxist critique of the Entitlement Theory, arguing that Nozick's self-ownership premise does not necessarily justify inequality.

## 关键文本 / Key Passages

> "如果所有人对分配在其份下的持有都是有权利的，那么这个分配就是正义的。"
> "If all holdings are entitled, then the distribution is just."

这段引文概括了资格理论的核心：正义是一个关于过程的问题，而非关于结果的问题。只要每个人持有的东西都是通过正义的方式获取或转让的，那么无论分配的最终形态如何，它都是正义的。

This passage captures the core of the Entitlement Theory: justice is a matter of process, not outcome. As long as everyone's holdings were acquired or transferred justly, the distribution is just regardless of its final shape.

> "从一个正义的状态到一个不正义的状态的转变，并非必然通过不正义的步骤实现。"
> "The transition from a just situation to an unjust one need not occur through unjust steps."

这一洞见揭示了诺齐克论证的精妙之处：即使每一步都是自愿且正义的，最终的分配结果可能看起来"不平等"——但这不平等本身并不构成不正义。

This insight reveals the subtlety of Nozick's argument: even if every step is voluntary and just, the resulting distribution may appear "unequal" — but this inequality does not itself constitute injustice.

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

资格理论在当代关于财富税、遗产税和 reparations（历史赔偿）的辩论中仍然具有核心意义。支持者援引资格理论论证个人有权保留其劳动成果，反对高额的累进税率。批评者则指出，历史上的殖民主义、奴隶制和系统性歧视意味着许多当代财产持有的起源本身就是不正义的，因此矫正正义原则要求大规模的财富再分配。

The Entitlement Theory remains central to contemporary debates about wealth taxes, inheritance taxes, and reparations. Proponents invoke it to argue that individuals are entitled to the fruits of their labor, opposing high progressive tax rates. Critics point out that the history of colonialism, slavery, and systemic discrimination means many contemporary property holdings originated in injustice, so rectification principles require large-scale redistribution.

在数字经济的背景下，资格理论还被用来讨论数字劳动（如社交媒体上的用户生成内容）和数据产权问题——用户在平台上产生的数据是否构成他们的"劳动成果"？

In the digital economy, the Entitlement Theory is also applied to debates about digital labor (such as user-generated content on social media) and data property rights — does data generated by users on platforms constitute the fruits of their "labor"?

## 批评与回应 / Critiques and Responses

**批评一：洛克式附带条件的模糊性 / The Vagueness of the Lockean Proviso**
批评者指出，诺齐克对洛克式附带条件的"宽松"解释过于模糊——"不使他人更差"的基线如何确定？如果一个人占有了唯一的绿洲，即使他向其他人出售水并使他们总体上比在自然状态下更好，这是否就满足了附带条件？
Critics argue that Nozick's "weak" interpretation of the Lockean proviso is too vague — how is the baseline of "not making others worse off" determined? If someone appropriates the only oasis and sells water to others, making them overall better off than in the state of nature, does this satisfy the proviso?

**批评二：历史追溯的不可能性 / The Impossibility of Historical Tracing**
几乎所有现实中的财产权都可以追溯到某种历史上的暴力或欺诈。如果矫正正义无法实施，那么整个资格理论在现实中的适用性就大打折扣。
Nearly all actual property rights can be traced to some historical violence or fraud. If rectification cannot be implemented, the practical applicability of the entire Entitlement Theory is significantly diminished.

**批评三：忽视集体贡献 / Neglecting Collective Contribution**
个人的财富创造总是依赖于社会合作、公共基础设施和知识遗产。资格理论将财富视为纯粹个人劳动的产物，忽视了社会和集体的贡献。
Individual wealth creation always depends on social cooperation, public infrastructure, and intellectual heritage. The Entitlement Theory treats wealth as purely the product of individual labor, neglecting social and collective contributions.

## 跨文化回响 / Cross-cultural Echoes

在中国传统中，孟子"制民之产"的思想与资格理论形成了有趣的对比。孟子认为国家有责任确保每个家庭拥有足够的土地——这更接近一种"目的状态"的分配正义观。然而，孟子对"义利之辨"的强调——即道德原则优先于物质利益——与诺齐克将权利视为绝对约束的立场有某种共鸣。

In Chinese tradition, Mencius's idea of "securing the people's property" (*zhi min zhi chan*) offers an interesting contrast to the Entitlement Theory. Mencius believed the state has a responsibility to ensure each family possesses sufficient land — closer to an "end-state" view of distributive justice. However, Mencius's emphasis on the "distinction between righteousness and profit" — the priority of moral principle over material interest — resonates with Nozick's treatment of rights as absolute constraints.

印度哲学中的"达摩"（dharma，义务/正法）概念也提供了一个对比视角：与诺齐克以权利为中心的框架不同，达摩传统以义务为中心，强调每个人根据其社会角色应尽的义务而非其应享有的权利。

The concept of *dharma* (duty/righteousness) in Indian philosophy also offers a contrasting perspective: unlike Nozick's rights-centered framework, the dharma tradition is duty-centered, emphasizing what each person ought to do according to their social role rather than what rights they are entitled to.
