# 技术理性 · Technological Rationality

## 概念概述 / Concept Overview

"技术理性"（Technological Rationality）是马尔库塞批判理论的关键概念之一，指在发达工业社会中，技术不仅是一种生产工具或效率手段，更是一种全面的合理性（rationality）形式——它规定了什么是有意义的、什么是合理的、什么是可以思考和不可以思考的。技术理性将一切社会问题转化为技术问题，将一切价值判断转化为效率计算，从而取消了政治和伦理维度的讨论空间。

"Technological Rationality" is a key concept in Marcuse's critical theory, referring to the way that in advanced industrial society, technology is not merely a productive tool or means of efficiency, but a comprehensive form of rationality — it determines what is meaningful, what is reasonable, what is thinkable and unthinkable. Technological rationality transforms all social problems into technical problems, all value judgments into efficiency calculations, thereby canceling the discursive space for political and ethical dimensions.

马尔库塞的核心论点是："技术理性即政治理性"（technological rationality is political rationality）。技术看似中立——它只是解决问题的工具——但技术的组织方式、发展方向和应用范围都内含着特定的政治选择。当一个社会将所有问题都定义为"技术问题"时，这本身就是一个政治决定——它排除了对技术所服务的目标和价值的质疑。

Marcuse's core thesis is: "technological rationality is political rationality." Technology appears neutral — merely a tool for solving problems — but the organization, direction, and scope of technology all contain specific political choices. When a society defines all problems as "technical problems," this is itself a political decision — it excludes questioning the goals and values that technology serves.

## 理论内涵 / Theoretical Content

### 从工具理性到技术理性 / From Instrumental Reason to Technological Rationality

马尔库塞的技术理性概念建立在韦伯（Max Weber）的"合理化"（Rationalisierung）理论和霍克海默的"工具理性批判"之上。韦伯分析了现代社会如何通过"合理化"过程将传统、情感和信仰替换为计算、效率和科层管理。霍克海默在《工具理性批判》（1947）中进一步指出，启蒙理性已经退化为纯粹的工具理性——只关心手段的效率，不再追问目的的合理性。

Marcuse's concept of technological rationality builds upon Max Weber's theory of *Rationalisierung* (rationalization) and Horkheimer's "critique of instrumental reason." Weber analyzed how modern society replaces tradition, emotion, and belief with calculation, efficiency, and bureaucratic management through the "rationalization" process. Horkheimer further argued in *Eclipse of Reason* (1947) that Enlightenment reason has degenerated into pure instrumental reason — concerned only with the efficiency of means, no longer questioning the rationality of ends.

马尔库塞在此基础上提出了更激进的论点：技术理性不仅是工具理性的极端形式，它本身已经成为一种"政治理性"——一种社会统治的合法性基础。在发达工业社会中，统治不再依赖传统权威或暴力，而是依赖技术效率的"客观性"和"科学性"。"这不是我的决定，这是技术需要"——这种话语使统治看起来不是人为的选择，而是不可避免的技术必然性。

Marcuse advances a more radical thesis: technological rationality is not merely the extreme form of instrumental reason; it has itself become a "political rationality" — a legitimating basis for social domination. In advanced industrial society, domination no longer depends on traditional authority or violence, but on the "objectivity" and "scientificity" of technological efficiency. "This is not my decision, this is what technology requires" — such discourse makes domination appear not as human choice but as technological inevitability.

### 技术与统治 / Technology and Domination

马尔库塞强调，技术理性创造了一种"非人格化的统治"（impersonal domination）：没有人需要为压迫负责，因为压迫看起来是技术系统的内在逻辑。工厂的管理者不是压迫工人，而是"优化流程"；算法不是歧视少数群体，而是"基于数据的决策"；政府不是压制异见，而是"维护社会秩序的技术需要"。

Marcuse emphasizes that technological rationality creates an "impersonal domination": no one needs to take responsibility for oppression, because oppression appears to be the internal logic of the technical system. Factory managers are not oppressing workers but "optimizing processes"; algorithms are not discriminating against minorities but making "data-driven decisions"; governments are not suppressing dissent but meeting "technical requirements for social order maintenance."

这种非人格化统治的特殊之处在于：它不仅控制人的行为，还控制人的思维方式和欲望结构。技术理性塑造了人们对"合理"的理解——只有在技术理性框架内可表达的想法才被认为是"合理的"，超越这个框架的想法则被视为"不切实际的"、"乌托邦的"或"非理性的"。

What is special about this impersonal domination is that it controls not only human behavior but also modes of thinking and structures of desire. Technological rationality shapes people's understanding of what is "reasonable" — only ideas expressible within the technological-rational framework are considered "reasonable," while ideas transcending this framework are dismissed as "impractical," "utopian," or "irrational."

### 科学的一维性 / The One-Dimensionality of Science

马尔库塞还批判了现代科学本身的单向度性。他指出，现代自然科学的方法论——特别是操作主义（operationalism）——将概念等同于其操作定义，消除了超越性维度。例如，"能量"被定义为可以用仪器测量的东西，而不是某种更深层的本体论力量。这种方法论上的限制使科学无法思考超越现存框架的问题。

Marcuse also critiques the one-dimensionality of modern science itself. He notes that the methodology of modern natural science — particularly operationalism — equates concepts with their operational definitions, eliminating the transcendent dimension. For example, "energy" is defined as what can be measured by instruments, not as some deeper ontological force. This methodological limitation prevents science from thinking about problems that transcend the existing framework.

## 思想史脉络 / Intellectual History

技术理性概念的思想谱系可以追溯到海德格尔对"技术座架"（Gestell）的批判。海德格尔认为，现代技术的本质不是工具性的，而是一种"解蔽"（Entbergen）方式——它将一切事物（包括人）都揭示为"持存物"（Bestand），即等待被利用的资源。马尔库塞继承了海德格尔的基本洞见，但将其政治化：技术座架不仅是存在论的命运，更是特定社会关系的产物。

The intellectual genealogy of technological rationality traces back to Heidegger's critique of the *Gestell* (enframing). Heidegger argued that the essence of modern technology is not instrumental but a mode of "revealing" (*Entbergen*) — it reveals everything (including humans) as *Bestand* (standing-reserve), resources waiting to be exploited. Marcuse inherits Heidegger's basic insight but politicizes it: the *Gestell* is not merely an ontological destiny but the product of specific social relations.

雅克·埃吕尔（Jacques Ellul）的"技术系统"（La Technique）概念也为马尔库塞提供了重要参照。埃吕尔认为，技术已经成为一个自主的系统，按照自身的逻辑发展，不受人类意志的控制。马尔库塞与埃吕尔的分歧在于：马尔库塞认为技术理性是可以被改变的——在不同的社会关系下，同样的技术可以服务于不同的目的。

Jacques Ellul's concept of *La Technique* (The Technological System) also provided an important reference for Marcuse. Ellul argued that technology has become an autonomous system, developing according to its own logic, beyond human control. Where Marcuse diverges from Ellul is in believing that technological rationality can be transformed — under different social relations, the same technology can serve different purposes.

## 关键文本 / Key Passages

> "技术合理性已经成为政治合理性。技术的进步就是统治的进步。"
> "Technological rationality has become political rationality. The progress of technology is the progress of domination."

> "在一个技术的世界中，自然不再作为对立的维度而存在——它被技术所支配、被纳入生产和消费的循环。"
> "In a technological world, nature no longer exists as a contrasting dimension — it is dominated by technology, incorporated into the cycle of production and consumption."

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

在人工智能时代，技术理性概念具有前所未有的紧迫性。算法治理（algorithmic governance）——从信用评分到预测性警务，从自动招聘到内容推荐——将复杂的社会问题简化为技术优化问题。当一个算法决定某个社区需要更多警力时，这个"技术决策"实际上是一个深刻的政治决策：它强化了既有的种族不平等，但以"数据驱动"的面目出现。

In the age of artificial intelligence, the concept of technological rationality has unprecedented urgency. Algorithmic governance — from credit scoring to predictive policing, from automated hiring to content recommendation — simplifies complex social problems into technical optimization problems. When an algorithm decides a neighborhood needs more police, this "technical decision" is actually a profoundly political one: it reinforces existing racial inequalities but appears as "data-driven."

同样，"智慧城市"概念也体现了技术理性的政治维度：城市被重新定义为一个需要被技术优化的系统，而不是一个需要民主协商的公共空间。市民被降格为"用户"，公共参与被替换为"用户体验优化"。

Similarly, the "smart city" concept embodies the political dimension of technological rationality: the city is redefined as a system requiring technological optimization rather than a public space requiring democratic deliberation. Citizens are reduced to "users," and public participation is replaced by "user experience optimization."

## 批评与回应 / Critiques and Responses

**批评 / Critique**: 哈贝马斯认为马尔库塞的技术理性批判过于宽泛，将所有现代理性形式都等同于统治。哈贝马斯区分了"工具理性"和"交往理性"，认为后者可以成为民主沟通的基础。
Habermas argues Marcuse's critique of technological rationality is overly broad, equating all modern forms of rationality with domination. Habermas distinguishes "instrumental rationality" from "communicative rationality," arguing the latter can serve as a foundation for democratic communication.

**回应 / Response**: 马尔库塞可能回应说，"交往理性"在权力严重不对称的社会中只是一个理想化——在实际的公共讨论中，技术理性的逻辑无处不在，甚至塑造了"什么是合理的争论"的框架本身。
Marcuse might respond that "communicative rationality" is merely an idealization in a society of severe power asymmetry — in actual public discussion, the logic of technological rationality is ubiquitous, even shaping the framework of "what counts as reasonable debate" itself.

## 跨文化回响 / Cross-cultural Echoes

在中国语境中，技术理性概念与"科技治国"（technocracy）传统形成深刻对话。从古代的"术数"传统到当代的"技术官僚"治理模式，中国政治传统中一直存在着将政治问题转化为技术问题的倾向。当代中国的"健康码"系统、社会信用体系和大数据治理都可以从马尔库塞的技术理性视角分析——这些系统将复杂的社会管理问题转化为数据采集和算法优化问题，同时引发了关于隐私、自由和技术统治的深刻讨论。

In the Chinese context, the concept of technological rationality engages in deep dialogue with the technocratic tradition. From the ancient tradition of "techniques and numbers" (*shu shu*) to contemporary "technocratic" governance models, Chinese political tradition has always shown a tendency to transform political problems into technical problems. Contemporary China's "health code" system, social credit system, and big data governance can all be analyzed from Marcuse's perspective of technological rationality — these systems transform complex social management problems into data collection and algorithmic optimization problems, while raising profound discussions about privacy, freedom, and technological domination.
