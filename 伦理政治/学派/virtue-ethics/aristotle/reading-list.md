# 亚里士多德阅读指南 · Aristotle: Annotated Reading Guide

## 一、阅读路径总览 / Reading Pathways Overview

根据读者的背景和目的，我们推荐三条递进的阅读路径：

We recommend three progressive reading pathways based on reader background and purpose:

| 路径 / Path | 时长 / Time | 适合 / Suitable For | 核心阅读 / Core Reading |
|---|---|---|---|
| 入门 / Introductory | 4–6小时 | 哲学初学者、伦理学入门 / Beginners, ethics introduction | EN I–II + Pakaluk 导读 |
| 进阶 / Intermediate | 15–20小时 | 哲学专业学生、伦理学研究者 / Philosophy students, ethics researchers | EN I–II + VI + VIII–X + Politics I |
| 专业 / Advanced | 50+小时 | 博士生、专题研究者 / Doctoral students, specialized researchers | EN 全书 + Politics + Metaphysics + De Anima |

## 二、入门级 / Introductory Level

### 原典 / Primary Texts

1. **《尼各马可伦理学》第一卷**（幸福论与功能论证）/ *Nicomachean Ethics* Book I (Eudaimonia and the ergon argument)
   - 重点：1.7 功能论证 / Focus: 1.7 the ergon argument
   - 推荐阅读 Irwin 译本（Hackett, 1999），注释清晰 / Read Irwin translation (Hackett, 1999), clear notes
   - 中文读者推荐廖申白译注本（商务印书馆, 2003）/ Chinese readers: Liao Shenbai annotated edition

2. **《尼各马可伦理学》第二卷**（德性与中道）/ *Nicomachean Ethics* Book II (Virtue and the Mean)
   - 重点：II.1 习惯化、II.6 中道定义 / Focus: II.1 habituation, II.6 definition of the mean
   - 这是理解美德伦理学结构的核心章节 / Core chapter for understanding virtue ethics structure

### 导读 / Guides

3. **Pakaluk, Michael.** *Aristotle's Nicomachean Ethics: An Introduction* (Cambridge, 2005)
   - 最佳英文入门导读——逐章解读，清晰易懂 / Best English introductory guide — chapter-by-chapter, clear and accessible
   - 适合无哲学背景的读者 / Suitable for readers without philosophy background

4. **Barnes, Jonathan.** *Aristotle: A Very Short Introduction* (Oxford, 2000)
   - 简明扼要的亚里士多德思想概述 / Concise overview of Aristotle's thought
   - 100余页，适合快速了解全貌 / ~100 pages, suitable for quick overview

5. **赵敦华《西方哲学简史》中亚里士多德章节**（北京大学出版社）
   - 中文读者最佳入门——在西方哲学史的框架中定位亚里士多德 / Best Chinese entry point — situating Aristotle within Western philosophy history

## 三、进阶级 / Intermediate Level

### 原典 / Primary Texts

6. **《尼各马可伦理学》第六卷**（理智德性与实践智慧）/ Book VI (Intellectual Virtues and Practical Wisdom)
   - 伦理学中最具哲学深度的章节——理解 phronesis 必读 / Most philosophically deep section — essential for understanding phronesis
   - 搭配阅读 Sherman (1989) 的分析 / Read alongside Sherman (1989) analysis

7. **《尼各马可伦理学》第八–九卷**（友谊）/ Books VIII–IX (Friendship)
   - 亚里士多德最具人文关怀的章节——三种友谊、完美友谊 / Aristotle's most humanistic section — three types of friendship, perfect friendship
   - 廖申白《亚里士多德友爱论研究》是重要的中文参考 / Liao Shenbai's study on Aristotelian friendship is an important Chinese reference

8. **《尼各马可伦理学》第十卷**（快乐与沉思生活）/ Book X (Pleasure and Contemplative Life)
   - 理解 eudaimonia 的完整图景——theoria 作为最高幸福 / Complete picture of eudaimonia — theoria as highest happiness
   - 与第一卷形成首尾呼应 / Forms bookend with Book I

9. **《政治学》第一卷**（城邦与自然）/ *Politics* Book I (Polis and Nature)
   - "人天生是政治的动物"的原始语境 / Original context of "humans are by nature political animals"
   - 理解伦理学如何延伸到政治学 / Understanding how ethics extends to politics

### 二手研究 / Secondary Literature

10. **Annas, Julia.** *The Morality of Happiness* (Oxford, 1993)
    - 古代幸福论的权威研究——涵盖亚里士多德、斯多葛学派和伊壁鸠鲁学派 / Authoritative study of ancient eudaimonism — covering Aristotle, Stoics, and Epicureans

11. **Hursthouse, Rosalind.** *On Virtue Ethics* (Oxford, 1999)
    - 新亚里士多德美德伦理学的奠基之作——将亚里士多德的美德理论现代化 / Foundational work of neo-Aristotelian virtue ethics — modernizing Aristotle's virtue theory

12. **Sherman, Nancy.** *The Fabric of Character: Aristotle's Theory of Virtue* (Oxford, 1989)
    - 对亚里士多德品格理论的深入分析 / In-depth analysis of Aristotle's character theory

### 美德伦理学复兴 / Virtue Ethics Revival

13. **Anscombe, G. E. M.** "Modern Moral Philosophy" (*Philosophy*, 1958)
    - 美德伦理学复兴的"宣言"——尖锐批评现代道德哲学忽视品格 / The "manifesto" of virtue ethics revival — sharp critique of modern moral philosophy's neglect of character

14. **MacIntyre, Alasdair.** *After Virtue* (Notre Dame, 1981/1984)
    - 20世纪最重要的伦理学著作之一——诊断现代道德危机并呼吁回归亚里士多德传统 / One of the most important ethics works of the 20th century — diagnosing modern moral crisis and calling for return to Aristotelian tradition
    - 中文：麦金太尔《追寻美德》，宋继杰译（译林出版社）/ Chinese: *After Virtue*, trans. Song Jijie

15. **Foot, Philippa.** *Virtues and Vices* (Oxford, 1978) 及 *Natural Goodness* (Oxford, 2001)
    - 前者收集了重要的美德伦理学论文；后者提出"自然善"论证 / Former collects important virtue ethics essays; latter presents "natural goodness" argument

## 四、专业级 / Advanced Level

### 原典深潜 / Primary Texts Deep Dive

16. **《尼各马可伦理学》全书**（十卷）/ *Nicomachean Ethics* complete (ten books)
    - 推荐使用 Irwin 译本或 Ross/Urmson 修订本 / Recommend Irwin or Ross/Urmson revised
    - 搭配 Kraut (2007) 的专题研究 / Pair with Kraut (2007) topical study

17. **《政治学》全书**（八卷）/ *Politics* complete (eight books)
    - 推荐 Reeve 译本（Hackett, 1998）/ Recommend Reeve translation
    - 搭配 Kraut, *Aristotle: Political Philosophy* (Oxford, 2002)

18. **《形而上学》第一、七、十二卷** / *Metaphysics* Books I, VII, XII
    - 理解亚里士多德伦理学的形而上学基础 / Understanding the metaphysical foundations of Aristotle's ethics
    - 实体（ousia）、潜能（dynamis）与实现（energeia）/ Substance, potentiality, and actuality

19. **《灵魂论》（*De Anima*）** / *De Anima*
    - 灵魂的定义——形式/现实性（entelecheia）/ Soul's definition — form/actuality
    - 理解伦理学的心理学基础 / Understanding the psychological foundations of ethics

### 专业研究 / Specialized Scholarship

20. **Kraut, Richard.** *Aristotle on the Human Good* (Princeton, 2007)
    - 对亚里士多德幸福论的当代哲学分析 / Contemporary philosophical analysis of Aristotle's eudaimonism

21. **Burnyeat, M. F.** "Aristotle on Learning to Be Good" in *Aristotle's Ethics* (ed. Rorty, 1980)
    - 关于美德习得的经典论文 / Classic essay on virtue acquisition

22. **Broadie, Sarah.** *Ethics with Aristotle* (Oxford, 1991)
    - 对亚里士多德伦理学的细致哲学分析 / Careful philosophical analysis of Aristotle's ethics

23. **Curzer, Howard.** *Aristotle and the Virtues* (Oxford, 2012)
    - 逐一分析每种亚里士多德美德 / Analyzes each Aristotelian virtue individually

24. **Annas, Julia.** *Intelligent Virtue* (Oxford, 2011)
    - 将美德类比为技能（skill）——对品格概念的创新诠释 / Virtue as skill analogy — innovative interpretation of the character concept

## 七、建议阅读顺序 / Suggested Reading Sequences

| 目标 / Goal | 推荐顺序 / Recommended Sequence |
|---|---|
| 快速了解**伦理学核心** / Quick ethics overview | EN I + II → Pakaluk → Barnes |
| 深入**德性理论** / Deep virtue theory | EN II + VI → Hursthouse → Sherman |
| 了解**政治哲学** / Political philosophy | Politics I + III → Kraut (2002) |
| **美德伦理学复兴** / Virtue ethics revival | Anscombe (1958) → MacIntyre → Foot |
| **中文读者**入门 / Chinese reader intro | 廖申白译本 I–II → 赵敦华 → 余纪元 |
| **比较哲学** / Comparative philosophy | EN II.6 (中道) → 《中庸》→ 陈来 |
| **当代应用** / Contemporary applications | Hursthouse → Nussbaum → Annas (2011) |
