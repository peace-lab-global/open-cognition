# 亚里士多德生平大事记 · Aristotle: Biographical Timeline

## 早年 · 前384–前367 / Early Years · 384–367 BCE

| 年份 (约) | 事件 / Event | 历史语境 / Historical Context |
|---|---|---|
| 前384 | 生于色雷斯斯塔吉拉（Stagira），马其顿王国属地。父亲尼科马库斯（Nicomachus）为马其顿国王阿明塔斯三世（Amyntas III）的御医。母亲法埃斯蒂丝（Phaestis）来自哈尔基斯的富裕家庭。/ Born in Stagira, Thrace, a Macedonian dependency. Father Nicomachus was court physician to King Amyntas III. Mother Phaestis from a wealthy Chalcidian family. | 柏拉图约44岁，正在创建学园（约前387年创建）。苏格拉底已于前399年被处死。/ Plato ~44, founding the Academy (~387 BCE). Socrates executed 399 BCE. |
| 约前375 | 父亲去世，由监护人普罗克塞努斯（Proxenus）抚养。自幼接触医学和自然哲学的经验方法。/ Father dies; raised by guardian Proxenus. Exposed to empirical methods of medicine and natural philosophy from childhood. | 希腊城邦处于伯罗奔尼撒战争后的重建期 / Greek poleis in post-Peloponnesian War reconstruction |
| 约前367 | 约17–18岁时前往雅典，进入柏拉图学园（Academia）。/ At ~17–18, travels to Athens and enters Plato's Academy. | 柏拉图学园已运营约20年 / Plato's Academy operating ~20 years |

## 学园时期 · 前367–前347 / Academy Period · 367–347 BCE

| 年份 (约) | 事件 / Event | 学术里程碑 / Intellectual Milestones |
|---|---|---|
| 前367–347 | 在柏拉图学园学习和教学约20年，从学生成长为教师。与色诺克拉底（Xenocrates）和斯彪西波（Speusippus）等同学互动。/ Studies and teaches at the Academy for ~20 years, progressing from student to teacher. Interacts with Xenocrates and Speusippus. | 逐渐对理念论（Theory of Forms）产生批判性距离；开始探索逻辑学和自然哲学 / Gradually develops critical distance from the Theory of Forms; begins exploring logic and natural philosophy |
| 约前360–355 | 可能撰写了《论题篇》（*Topics*）等早期逻辑学著作 / Possibly composed early logical works like *Topics* | 建立区别于柏拉图"划分法"的推理系统 / Develops reasoning system distinct from Plato's "division" |
| 约前355–350 | 撰写了早期对话录，如《劝勉篇》（*Protrepticus*）和《论哲学》（*On Philosophy*）——风格更接近柏拉图，但已全部散佚 / Composes early dialogues like *Protrepticus* and *On Philosophy* — more Platonic in style, all now lost | 工具论（Organon）的核心部分成形 / Core parts of the Organon take shape |
| 前347 | 柏拉图去世（享年约80岁）。学园领导权传给柏拉图的侄子斯彪西波，其方向偏向数学化的柏拉图主义。亚里士多德离开学园。/ Plato dies (~age 80). Academy leadership passes to Speusippus, with mathematical Platonist orientation. Aristotle departs. | 思想独立的标志性时刻 / Landmark moment of intellectual independence |

## 游历时期 · 前347–前335 / Travels · 347–335 BCE

| 年份 (约) | 事件 / Event | 学术里程碑 / Intellectual Milestones |
|---|---|---|
| 前347–345 | 前往小亚细亚阿索斯（Assos），与赫尔米亚斯（Hermias of Atarneus）合作建立小型哲学团体。娶赫尔米亚斯的养女皮提亚斯（Pythias）为妻，育有一女。/ Travels to Assos, Asia Minor; collaborates with Hermias to establish a small philosophical community. Marries Hermias's adopted daughter Pythias; they have a daughter. | 接触波斯帝国的政治现实；开始独立研究自然哲学 / Exposure to Persian imperial political realities; begins independent natural philosophy research |
| 前345–343 | 转赴莱斯博斯岛（Lesbos），与塞奥弗拉斯托斯（Theophrastus）合作，进行开创性的海洋生物学研究——系统观察和分类了数百种海洋生物。/ Moves to Lesbos; collaborates with Theophrastus on pioneering marine biological research — systematically observes and classifies hundreds of marine species. | 经验研究深刻塑造了哲学方法论——更加重视观察、分类和归纳。四因说和灵魂论开始成形 / Empirical research profoundly shapes philosophical method — greater emphasis on observation, classification, induction. Four causes and psychology begin taking shape |
| 前343–340 | 受马其顿国王腓力二世（Philip II）之聘，担任十三岁的亚历山大（后来的亚历山大大帝）的导师，为期约三年。据传编写了《论王权》等小册子，并为远征收集各地方志资料。/ Appointed by Philip II of Macedon as tutor to 13-year-old Alexander (later Alexander the Great) for ~3 years. Reportedly composed treatises like *On Kingship* and collected local histories for campaigns. | 获得政治实践经验；加深对多种政体和文化的理解 / Gains practical political experience; deepens understanding of diverse constitutions and cultures |
| 约前345–340 | 《欧德谟伦理学》（*Eudemian Ethics*）成形——伦理思想的较早系统表达 / *Eudemian Ethics* takes shape — earlier systematic expression of ethical thought | 许多主题后来在《尼各马可伦理学》中得到更成熟发展 / Many themes later matured in *Nicomachean Ethics* |
| 约前340 | 返回斯塔吉拉。皮提亚斯去世。与赫尔皮利斯（Herpyllis）结合，生子尼各马可（Nicomachus，《尼各马可伦理学》可能以其命名）。/ Returns to Stagira. Pythias dies. Unites with Herpyllis; they have a son Nicomachus (after whom the *Nicomachean Ethics* may be named). | |

## 吕克昂学园 · 前335–前323 / The Lyceum · 335–323 BCE

| 年份 (约) | 事件 / Event | 学术里程碑 / Intellectual Milestones |
|---|---|---|
| 前335 | 返回雅典，在阿波罗·吕克奥斯神庙附近创立吕克昂学园（Lyceum）。因其师生常在廊柱下边走边谈（peripatein），故称"逍遥学派"（Peripatetics）。/ Returns to Athens; founds the Lyceum near the temple of Apollo Lyceus. Teacher and students walk and converse under colonnades (peripatein), hence "Peripatetics." | 古代世界第一个真正的研究机构——拥有图书馆、自然科学收藏和系统性研究项目 / Ancient world's first true research institution — with library, natural science collections, and systematic research programs |
| 前335–323 | 完成核心著作的讲义定稿。这是一个极其高产的时期——现存的大部分著作都在此期间成形。/ Completes lecture notes for core works. An extraordinarily productive period — most surviving works take shape during this time. | 《尼各马可伦理学》《政治学》《形而上学》《物理学》《灵魂论》《诗学》《修辞学》等核心文本定稿 / Core texts finalized: NE, Politics, Metaphysics, Physics, De Anima, Poetics, Rhetoric |
| 前334 | 亚历山大开始东征（至前323年去世）。亚里士多德与亚历山大保持联系，据传后者从远征中寄送自然科学标本。/ Alexander begins eastern campaigns (until death 323 BCE). Aristotle maintains contact; Alexander reportedly sends natural science specimens from campaigns. | 亚里士多德的哲学体系达到完全成熟 / Aristotle's philosophical system reaches full maturity |
| 前323 | 亚历山大在巴比伦去世（6月）。雅典反马其顿情绪急剧高涨。亚里士多德被指控"不敬神"（asebeia）——如同苏格拉底。他留下名言"不让雅典人第二次对哲学犯罪"（指苏格拉底之死），逃离雅典。/ Alexander dies in Babylon (June). Anti-Macedonian sentiment surges in Athens. Aristotle charged with "impiety" (asebeia) — like Socrates. Declares "I will not let Athens sin twice against philosophy" and flees. | 吕克昂学园由塞奥弗拉斯托斯接管 / Lyceum taken over by Theophrastus |

## 晚年与去世 · 前323–前322 / Final Years and Death · 323–322 BCE

| 年份 | 事件 / Event |
|---|---|
| 前323 | 前往埃维亚岛的哈尔基斯（Chalcis），住在母亲的祖宅中。/ Travels to Chalcis, Euboea, residing in his mother's ancestral home. |
| 前322 | 在哈尔基斯去世，享年约62岁。死因可能为慢性胃病。遗嘱显示对家人、朋友和奴隶（他要求释放部分奴隶）的关怀。/ Dies in Chalcis, age ~62. Cause likely chronic stomach illness. Will shows concern for family, friends, and slaves (requests some be freed). |

## 著作传承大事记 / Key Events in Textual Transmission

| 时间 | 事件 / Event |
|---|---|
| 前322年后 | 手稿和藏书传给塞奥弗拉斯托斯，后转至涅琉斯 / Manuscripts and library pass to Theophrastus, then to Neleus |
| 约前100年 | 手稿在塞普西斯被埋藏于地下，以防帕加马国王掠夺。这一"地下时期"导致部分手稿受潮损坏 / Manuscripts buried in Scepsis to protect from Pergamene kings. This "underground period" causes moisture damage to some manuscripts |
| 约前40年 | 罗德岛的安德罗尼库斯（Andronicus of Rhodes）获得手稿，首次系统编辑亚里士多德著作，确立了现存著作的基本分类和顺序 / Andronicus of Rhodes obtains manuscripts, first systematic editing, establishing basic classification and order of surviving works |
| 1–5世纪 | 新柏拉图主义者（如波菲利的《亚里士多德范畴篇导论》）将亚里士多德整合进柏拉图传统 / Neoplatonists (like Porphyry's *Introduction to Aristotle's Categories*) integrate Aristotle into the Platonic tradition |
| 8–10世纪 | 阿拉伯翻译运动：亚里士多德著作大量翻译为阿拉伯文，阿维森纳和阿威罗伊等伊斯兰哲学家对亚里士多德进行了深刻注释 / Arabic translation movement: massive translation into Arabic; Islamic philosophers Avicenna and Averroes offer profound commentaries |
| 12–13世纪 | 拉丁翻译运动：通过阿拉伯文转译为拉丁文。阿奎那的《亚里士多德注释》和《神学大全》将亚里士多德哲学整合进基督教神学 / Latin translation movement: via Arabic to Latin. Aquinas's commentaries and *Summa Theologica* integrate Aristotelian philosophy into Christian theology |
| 1495 | 阿尔都斯·马努提乌斯（Aldus Manutius）在威尼斯出版了第一部印刷版亚里士多德希腊文全集 / Aldus Manutius publishes first printed Greek Aristotle in Venice |
| 1831 | 柏林科学院出版《亚里士多德全集》（Bekker edition），确立了至今使用的标准页码编码系统（Bekker编号）/ Berlin Academy publishes *Aristotelis Opera* (Bekker edition), establishing the standard page numbering system (Bekker numbers) still used today |

## 思想分期总结 / Periodization Summary

| 阶段 / Phase | 年代 / Dates | 关键词 / Keywords | 核心发展 / Core Development |
|---|---|---|---|
| 早年 / Early | 前384–367 | 斯塔吉拉、医学家庭 / Stagira, medical family | 自然科学的经验方法启蒙 / Empirical method of natural science |
| 学园期 / Academy | 前367–347 | 柏拉图、理念论批判 / Plato, Forms critique | 逻辑学和独立哲学的萌芽 / Germination of logic and independent philosophy |
| 游历期 / Travels | 前347–335 | 生物学、亚历山大 / Biology, Alexander | 经验方法强化，伦理学初步系统化 / Empirical method intensified, ethics preliminarily systematized |
| 吕克昂期 / Lyceum | 前335–323 | 核心著作、逍遥学派 / Core works, Peripatetics | 完整哲学体系的建成 / Complete philosophical system achieved |
| 传承 / Legacy | 前322至今 | 编辑、翻译、注释 / Editing, translation, commentary | 两千年持续影响 / Two millennia of continuous influence |
