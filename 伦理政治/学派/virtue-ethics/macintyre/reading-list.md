# 麦金泰尔阅读指南 · MacIntyre Reading Guide

## 入门 / Introductory

### 第一周：诊断 / Week 1: Diagnosis

1. **《追寻美德》第1-3章** / *After Virtue*, Chs. 1-3
   - 第1章提出了著名的"科学灾难"思想实验 / Ch. 1 presents the famous "scientific catastrophe" thought experiment
   - 第2-3章诊断现代道德话语为情感主义 / Chs. 2-3 diagnose modern moral discourse as emotivist
   - **阅读提示 / Reading tip**: 注意麦金泰尔如何将哲学分析与文化批评相结合 / Note how MacIntyre combines philosophical analysis with cultural criticism

2. **"Is Patriotism a Virtue?"** (1984)
   - 短小精悍的论文，展示美德伦理学如何处理具体政治问题 / Concise essay showing how virtue ethics addresses concrete political questions

3. **《伦理学简史》导论** / *A Short History of Ethics*, Introduction
   - 了解麦金泰尔的历史方法论 / Understand MacIntyre's historical methodology

### 第二周：建构 / Week 2: Construction

4. **《追寻美德》第14-15章** / *After Virtue*, Chs. 14-15
   - 第14章定义"实践"和"美德" / Ch. 14 defines "practice" and "virtue"
   - 第15章发展"叙事统一性"概念 / Ch. 15 develops the concept of "narrative unity"
   - **阅读提示 / Reading tip**: 这两章构成了麦金泰尔解决方案的核心 / These chapters form the core of MacIntyre's constructive project

## 进阶 / Intermediate

### 传统的合理性 / Tradition-Constituted Rationality

5. **《谁之正义？何种合理性？》第18-20章** / *Whose Justice? Which Rationality?*, Chs. 18-20
   - 系统阐述"传统构成的合理性" / Systematic exposition of "tradition-constituted rationality"
   - 包含对"认识论危机"概念的详细分析 / Includes detailed analysis of "epistemological crisis"
   - **阅读提示 / Reading tip**: 建议配合伽达默尔《真理与方法》第二部分阅读 / Best read alongside Gadamer's *Truth and Method*, Part II

6. **《三种对立的道德探究》** / *Three Rival Versions of Moral Enquiry* (全文 / full text)
   - 区分三种道德探究模式 / Distinguishes three modes of moral enquiry
   - 论证传统模式最具哲学说服力 / Argues the traditional mode is most philosophically compelling
   - **阅读提示 / Reading tip**: 注意麦金泰尔对尼采传统的分析 / Note MacIntyre's analysis of the Nietzschean tradition

### 脆弱性与依赖 / Vulnerability and Dependence

7. **《依赖性的理性动物》** / *Dependent Rational Animals* (全文 / full text)
   - 人类脆弱性与承认依赖的美德 / Human vulnerability and virtues of acknowledged dependence
   - 对亚里士多德传统的修正和发展 / Modification and development of the Aristotelian tradition

## 专业 / Advanced

### 核心专著 / Core Monographs

8. **《追寻美德》全文** / *After Virtue* (full text)
   - 特别注意第4-9章对启蒙规划的历史分析 / Pay special attention to Chs. 4-9 on the historical analysis of the Enlightenment project
   - 以及后记中对批评的回应 / And the postscript responding to critics

9. **《谁之正义？何种合理性？》全文** / *Whose Justice? Which Rationality?* (full text)
   - 从荷马到休谟的正义概念谱系 / Genealogy of justice concepts from Homer to Hume

10. **《现代性冲突中的伦理学》** / *Ethics in the Conflicts of Modernity*
    - 麦金泰尔晚期对亚里士多德主义的深化 / MacIntyre's late deepening of Aristotelianism

### 论文集 / Essay Collections

11. **《哲学的任务》** / *The Tasks of Philosophy* (Vol. 1)
    - 包含重要的方法论论文 / Contains important methodological essays
12. **《伦理学与政治学》** / *Ethics and Politics* (Vol. 2)
    - 包含应用伦理学和政治哲学的论文 / Contains essays in applied ethics and political philosophy

## 二手研究 / Secondary Literature

### 入门级导论 / Introductory Guides

- **Horton, John & Mendus, Susan (eds.), *After MacIntyre*** (Polity, 1994)
  - 对《追寻美德》的系统批评与回应 / Systematic critique of and response to *After Virtue*
  - 包含麦金泰尔本人的回应文章 / Includes MacIntyre's own response essay

- **Murphy, Mark C. (ed.), *Alasdair MacIntyre*** (Cambridge, 2003)
  - 全面的学术评估 / Comprehensive scholarly assessment

### 专题研究 / Specialized Studies

- **Lutz, Christopher Stephen, *Tradition in the Ethics of Alasdair MacIntyre*** (Lexington, 2004)
  - 对"传统"概念的深入研究 / In-depth study of the concept of "tradition"

- **Knight, Kelvin, *Aristotelian Philosophy: Ethics and Politics from Aristotle to MacIntyre*** (Polity, 2007)
  - 从亚里士多德到麦金泰尔的思想史 / Intellectual history from Aristotle to MacIntyre

- **Gregory, Peter, *MacIntyre's "After Virtue": A Critical Guide*** (Cambridge, 2024)
  - 最新的研究指南 / Most recent study guide

- **Beadle, Ron & Moore, Geoff, "MacIntyre on Business Ethics"** (in various journals)
  - 麦金泰尔思想在商业伦理中的应用 / Application of MacIntyre's thought in business ethics

1. **龚群《当代社群主义伦理学研究》**
   - 对麦金泰尔及社群主义伦理学的系统研究 / Systematic study of MacIntyre and communitarian ethics
   - 包含详细的思想比较和批评 / Includes detailed intellectual comparison and criticism

2. **万俊人《现代西方伦理学史》**（上下卷）
   - 包含对麦金泰尔的详细评介 / Contains detailed evaluation of MacIntyre
   - 在更广阔的学术背景下定位麦金泰尔 / Positions MacIntyre in a broader scholarly context

3. **李义天《麦金太尔的美德伦理学》**
   - 专题研究 / Specialized monograph
   - 对麦金泰尔核心概念的深入分析 / In-depth analysis of MacIntyre's core concepts

## 推荐阅读顺序 / Suggested Reading Sequence

**快速入门（1-2周） / Quick Start (1-2 weeks)**:
→ 《追寻美德》Chs. 1-3 → Chs. 14-15 → "Is Patriotism a Virtue?"

**标准路径（4-6周） / Standard Path (4-6 weeks)**:
→ 《追寻美德》全文 → 《谁之正义？》Chs. 18-20 → 《三种对立的道德探究》→ 《依赖性的理性动物》

**深度研究（一学期） / Deep Study (one semester)**:
→ 按年表顺序阅读全部专著 → 两卷论文集 → 核心二手文献
→ Read all monographs in chronological order → Two essay volumes → Core secondary literature
