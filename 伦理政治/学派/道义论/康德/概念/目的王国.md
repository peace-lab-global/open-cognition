# 目的王国 · Kingdom of Ends

## 概念概述 / Concept Overview

目的王国（Reich der Zwecke）是康德伦理学中最具理想色彩的概念，指一个由所有理性存在者通过共同的客观法则（道德法则）联合而成的系统整体。在这个理想的道德共同体中，每一个成员既是立法者又是服从者——他们作为自律的存在者共同制定道德法则，同时又自愿服从这些法则。目的王国不是对现实的描述，而是一个实践理性的理念（Idee），是道德行动的终极目标和规范标准。

The Kingdom of Ends (Reich der Zwecke) is the most idealistic concept in Kantian ethics, referring to a systematic whole formed by all rational beings united through shared objective laws (the moral law). In this ideal moral community, every member is simultaneously legislator and subject—as autonomous beings they collectively legislate the moral law while voluntarily submitting to it. The Kingdom of Ends is not a description of reality but an idea (Idee) of practical reason, serving as the ultimate goal and normative standard for moral action.

康德将目的王国与他所谓的"自然王国"（Reich der Natur）对立起来。在自然王国中，事物按照因果法则运行；在目的王国中，理性存在者按照道德法则行动。目的王国的理念要求我们不仅在个体层面遵循道德法则，而且在行动时将整个道德共同体——包括所有理性存在者——纳入考量。

Kant opposes the Kingdom of Ends to what he calls the "Kingdom of Nature" (Reich der Natur). In the Kingdom of Nature, things operate according to causal laws; in the Kingdom of Ends, rational beings act according to moral laws. The idea of the Kingdom of Ends requires us not only to follow the moral law at the individual level but to consider the entire moral community—including all rational beings—when we act.

## 理论内涵 / Theoretical Content

### 系统联合与成员身份 / Systematic Union and Membership

目的王国的核心特征是"系统性"——它不是理性存在者的简单集合，而是一个通过客观法则有机联合的整体。每一个理性存在者都是目的王国的"成员"（Glied），这意味着他们不仅是法则的服从者，更是法则的共同制定者。作为立法者，每个成员的意志必须被尊重——任何道德法则都必须能够被所有成员一致同意。这一思想深刻影响了后世的社会契约论和民主理论。

The core feature of the Kingdom of Ends is its "systematic" nature—it is not a mere aggregate of rational beings but an organically unified whole through objective laws. Every rational being is a "member" (Glied) of the Kingdom of Ends, meaning they are not merely subjects of the law but co-legislators of it. As legislators, each member's will must be respected—any moral law must be capable of unanimous agreement by all members. This idea profoundly influenced later social contract theory and democratic theory.

### 目的与手段的系统秩序 / Systematic Order of Ends and Means

在目的王国中，事物被区分为两类：具有相对价值（价格）的事物可以作为手段使用；具有绝对价值（尊严）的理性存在者必须始终同时被当作目的。目的王国的理念要求建立一个秩序，在其中每个目的（每个理性存在者）的实现都与其他所有目的的实现相容——形成"目的的和谐"。这一和谐并非预设的，而是通过每个成员遵守道德法则来实现的。

In the Kingdom of Ends, things are divided into two categories: things with relative value (price) can be used as means; rational beings with absolute value (dignity) must always be treated simultaneously as ends. The idea of the Kingdom of Ends demands the establishment of an order in which the realization of each end (each rational being) is compatible with the realization of all other ends—forming a "harmony of ends." This harmony is not presupposed but achieved through each member's observance of the moral law.

### 作为实践理念的目的王国 / The Kingdom of Ends as Practical Idea

康德强调，目的王国"当然只是一个理想"（zwar nur ein Ideal），但它并非空洞的幻想。作为实践理性的理念，它发挥着"范导性"（regulativ）功能：引导我们在每一个具体行动中考量自己的行为是否能够成为一个目的王国中的普遍法则。即使现实中这个王国永远不会完全实现，我们仍然有义务朝这个方向行动。

Kant emphasizes that the Kingdom of Ends is "certainly only an ideal" (zwar nur ein Ideal), but it is not an empty fantasy. As an idea of practical reason, it serves a "regulative" function: guiding us in every concrete action to consider whether our conduct could serve as a universal law within a Kingdom of Ends. Even if this kingdom is never fully realized in reality, we are still obligated to act in that direction.

## 思想史脉络 / Intellectual History

目的王国的概念融合了多种思想传统。莱布尼茨的"上帝之城"（City of God）概念——一个由所有心灵（esprits）组成的完美共和国——是重要的思想来源。基督教神学中"圣徒共融"（communio sanctorum）的观念也为其提供了宗教维度的背景。然而，康德的创新在于将这个理想去除了神学前提——目的王国不需要上帝作为其建立者，它完全基于理性存在者的自律。

The concept of the Kingdom of Ends synthesizes multiple intellectual traditions. Leibniz's "City of God"—a perfect republic composed of all minds (esprits)—was an important source. The Christian theological notion of "communion of saints" (communio sanctorum) also provided a religious-dimension background. However, Kant's innovation was to strip this ideal of its theological presupposition—the Kingdom of Ends does not need God as its founder; it is based entirely on the autonomy of rational beings.

科尔斯戈德在《创造目的王国》（1996）中对这一概念进行了当代最重要的重新阐释。她论证道，目的王国不是等待被发现的现成秩序，而是需要被"创造"的——每一次道德行动都是在"创造"目的王国的一步。这种实践导向的解读赋予了目的王国概念更强的现实动力。

Korsgaard provided the most important contemporary reinterpretation in Creating the Kingdom of Ends (1996). She argued that the Kingdom of Ends is not a ready-made order waiting to be discovered but needs to be "created"—every moral action is a step toward "creating" the Kingdom of Ends. This practice-oriented reading gives the concept greater practical momentum.

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

目的王国的理念在当代政治哲学中发挥着核心作用。罗尔斯明确将自己的"良序社会"（well-ordered society）概念视为目的王国理念的当代版本——在良序社会中，所有公民在公平的条件下共同制定和遵守正义原则。在全球正义领域，目的王国的理念支持这样一种观点：国际制度应当以促进所有人的尊严和自主为目标，而非仅服务于强国利益。在商谈伦理中，哈贝马斯的"理想言谈情境"可以被理解为目的王国在交往领域的对应物。

The idea of the Kingdom of Ends plays a central role in contemporary political philosophy. Rawls explicitly treated his concept of a "well-ordered society" as a contemporary version of the Kingdom of Ends—in a well-ordered society, all citizens collectively make and follow principles of justice under fair conditions. In global justice, the Kingdom of Ends supports the view that international institutions should aim to promote the dignity and autonomy of all persons, not merely serve the interests of powerful states. In discourse ethics, Habermas's "ideal speech situation" can be understood as the Kingdom of Ends' counterpart in the communicative domain.

## 批评与回应 / Critiques and Responses

**乌托邦主义批评 / Utopianism Critique**: 批评者认为目的王国过于理想化，忽视了现实社会中权力、利益和冲突的不可消除性。回应：康德本人明确承认目的王国是"理想"，其价值在于提供规范标准，而非描述可实现的社会蓝图。理想的价值恰恰在于它与现实的距离，使现实始终受到批判性审视。

Critics argue that the Kingdom of Ends is overly idealistic, neglecting the ineradicability of power, interest, and conflict in real societies. Response: Kant himself explicitly acknowledged that the Kingdom of Ends is an "ideal"; its value lies in providing a normative standard, not describing a realizable social blueprint. The value of the ideal lies precisely in its distance from reality, subjecting reality to perpetual critical scrutiny.

**抽象性批评 / Abstraction Critique**: 黑格尔批评目的王国忽视了具体社会制度和历史传统的重要性。回应：当代康德主义者认为目的王国的理念并不排斥制度建设，而是为制度提供道德方向——好的制度是促进而非阻碍人的自律和尊严的制度。

Hegel criticized the Kingdom of Ends for neglecting the importance of concrete social institutions and historical traditions. Response: Contemporary Kantians hold that the idea of the Kingdom of Ends does not exclude institutional construction but provides moral direction for institutions—good institutions are those that promote rather than hinder human autonomy and dignity.

## 跨文化回响 / Cross-cultural Echoes

目的王国的理念与儒家的"大同"理想有深层的呼应。《礼记·礼运》描述的"大道之行也，天下为公"的大同社会，与目的王国一样描绘了一个所有成员各得其所、和谐共存的理想共同体。然而两者的基础不同：大同社会基于礼乐秩序和伦理关系的和谐，目的王国基于理性主体的自律和普遍法则。在大乘佛教中，"净土"的概念——一个所有有情众生都获得觉悟和解脱的理想世界——也与目的王国的理念形成了有趣的平行。

The idea of the Kingdom of Ends resonates deeply with the Confucian ideal of "Great Unity" (大同). The description in the Record of Rites (Liji) of a society where "the Great Way prevails and the world is shared by all" depicts, like the Kingdom of Ends, an ideal community where all members flourish in harmonious coexistence. However, their foundations differ: Great Unity is based on ritual order and harmonious ethical relationships, while the Kingdom of Ends is based on the autonomy of rational subjects and universal laws. In Mahāyāna Buddhism, the concept of "Pure Land"—an ideal world where all sentient beings attain awakening and liberation—also forms an interesting parallel with the idea of the Kingdom of Ends.
