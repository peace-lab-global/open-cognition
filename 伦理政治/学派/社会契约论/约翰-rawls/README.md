# 约翰·罗尔斯 · John Rawls

> **"正义是社会制度的第一美德，正如真理是思想体系的第一美德。"**
> *"Justice is the first virtue of social institutions, as truth is of systems of thought."*
> — *A Theory of Justice* (1971), §1

## 基本信息 / Basic Information

约翰·博德利·罗尔斯（John Bordley Rawls，1921年2月21日—2002年11月24日），美国政治哲学家，哈佛大学哲学系教授（1962–1991），被公认为20世纪最具影响力的政治哲学家。他出生于马里兰州巴尔的摩一个法律与公共事务家庭——父亲威廉·李·罗尔斯是著名宪法律师，母亲安娜·阿贝尔·斯坦普是女性参政运动的积极参与者。这一家庭背景深刻塑造了他对正义、宪政与公共生活的终身关怀。

John Bordley Rawls (February 21, 1921 – November 24, 2002) was an American political philosopher and professor at Harvard University's Department of Philosophy (1962–1991), widely regarded as the most influential political philosopher of the twentieth century. Born in Baltimore, Maryland, into a family deeply engaged with law and public affairs — his father William Lee Rawls was a prominent constitutional lawyer, and his mother Anna Abell Stump was an active participant in the women's suffrage movement — this upbringing profoundly shaped his lifelong concern with justice, constitutionalism, and public life.

罗尔斯1943年以最优成绩从普林斯顿大学毕业，随后在二战太平洋战区服役（新几内亚、菲律宾、日本占领军）。战争经历——特别是好友阵亡与广岛原子弹遗址的震撼——使他彻底丧失了青年时期的基督教信仰，转向世俗道德哲学。1950年在普林斯顿完成博士论文《伦理知识研究》，1952–1953年赴牛津大学访学期间结识以赛亚·柏林与H.L.A.哈特，深受后期维特根斯坦与日常语言学派影响。此后执教于康奈尔、麻省理工，1962年转入哈佛大学直至退休。1999年获美国国家人文奖章。

Rawls graduated summa cum laude from Princeton in 1943, then served in the Pacific theater during World War II (New Guinea, Philippines, occupation of Japan). The war experience — particularly the death of close friends and witnessing the aftermath of the Hiroshima atomic bombing — led him to abandon his youthful Christian faith and turn toward secular moral philosophy. He completed his doctoral dissertation *A Study of the Grounds of Ethical Knowledge* at Princeton in 1950, and during a visiting fellowship at Oxford (1952–1953) he encountered Isaiah Berlin and H.L.A. Hart, coming under the influence of later Wittgensteinian thought and ordinary language philosophy. He subsequently taught at Cornell, MIT, and from 1962 at Harvard University until his retirement. He received the National Humanities Medal in 1999.

## 核心命题 / Core Theses

**1. 正义二原则 · Two Principles of Justice**
社会的基本结构应满足两个原则：(1) **平等自由原则**——每个人享有与他人相容的最广泛的基本自由体系；(2) **差别原则**——社会与经济不平等须满足：a. 在公平的机会平等条件下向所有人开放；b. 对最不利的社会成员最为有利。第一原则永远优先于第二原则（自由的优先性）。

The basic structure of society should satisfy two principles: (1) the **equal liberty principle** — each person has an equal right to the most extensive scheme of basic liberties compatible with a similar scheme for others; (2) the **difference principle** — social and economic inequalities must satisfy: (a) be attached to positions open to all under fair equality of opportunity; and (b) be to the greatest benefit of the least advantaged members of society. The first principle always takes priority over the second (the priority of liberty).

**2. 无知之幕 · Veil of Ignorance**
正义原则的证成依赖一个思想实验——**原初状态**：各方在"无知之幕"背后选择社会的基本规则，不知道自己将来的阶层、天赋、性别、种族、价值观念。信息屏蔽确保选择结果不偏袒任何特定群体，从而产生公平的正义原则。

The justification of principles of justice relies on a thought experiment — the **original position**: parties behind a "veil of ignorance" choose the basic rules of society without knowing their future social class, natural talents, gender, race, or conception of the good. The information barrier ensures that the resulting principles do not favor any particular group, thereby producing fair principles of justice.

**3. 作为公平的正义 · Justice as Fairness**
正义不是最大多数人的最大幸福（功利主义），也不是自然秩序的体现（亚里士多德传统），而是**自由平等的理性人在公平条件下所同意的原则**。"公平"是正义的程序性基础——正义原则的正当性来源于产生它们的程序之公平性。

Justice is neither the greatest happiness of the greatest number (utilitarianism) nor the reflection of a natural order (Aristotelian tradition), but rather **the principles that free and equal rational persons would agree to under fair conditions**. "Fairness" is the procedural foundation of justice — the legitimacy of justice principles derives from the fairness of the procedure that generates them.

**4. 政治自由主义 · Political Liberalism**
在合理的多元主义事实下，正义原则不应建立在任何特定的"整全性学说"（comprehensive doctrine）之上，而应成为各种合理学说之间的**重叠共识**（overlapping consensus）的聚焦点。政治正义是"政治的而非形而上学的"。

Given the fact of reasonable pluralism, principles of justice should not rest on any particular "comprehensive doctrine" but should serve as the focal point of an **overlapping consensus** among diverse reasonable doctrines. Political justice is "political, not metaphysical."

**5. 反思平衡 · Reflective Equilibrium**
正义理论的证成方法是：在我们深思熟虑的判断（considered judgments）与抽象原则之间不断往返调节，直到达到两者之间的**融贯**——既非纯粹演绎，也非纯粹归纳，而是一种动态的融贯主义方法。

The justificatory method of justice theory involves continually adjusting between our considered judgments and abstract principles until achieving **coherence** between them — neither pure deduction nor pure induction, but a dynamic coherentist method.

**6. 万民法 · Law of Peoples**
正义论的国际扩展——自由人民与合宜人民之间可以达成国际层面的正义原则，包括尊重人权、援助不利社会、战争行为的限制等。全球正义不等于全球分配正义，而是人民间的正当关系。

The international extension of justice theory — liberal peoples and decent peoples can agree upon international principles of justice, including respect for human rights, assistance to burdened societies, and constraints on the conduct of war. Global justice is not equivalent to global distributive justice, but concerns rightful relations among peoples.

## 思想脉络 / Intellectual Development

### 早期 / Early Period (1921–1958)

罗尔斯的早期生涯充满塑造其思想方向的关键经历。普林斯顿时期受诺曼·马尔科姆（Norman Malcolm）影响接触维特根斯坦哲学，同时在弗兰克·奈特（Frank Knight）和沃尔特·斯泰斯（Walter Stace）指导下探索伦理学的基础问题。二战服役经历是其人生的转折点：好友迪肯·彼得森在菲律宾战斗中阵亡，罗尔斯本人险些被派往同一战场；参观广岛原子弹遗址使他深刻质疑神正论问题——为什么上帝允许如此巨大的恶？这一宗教危机最终推动他从神学转向世俗道德哲学，寻找不依赖宗教预设的正义基础。

Rawls's early career was marked by formative experiences that shaped his intellectual direction. At Princeton, he encountered Wittgensteinian philosophy through Norman Malcolm and explored foundational questions in ethics under Frank Knight and Walter Stace. His World War II service proved a turning point: his close friend Deacon Peterson was killed in combat in the Philippines — Rawls himself narrowly avoided the same battlefield — and visiting the Hiroshima atomic bomb site led him to question theodicy profoundly: why would God permit such enormous evil? This religious crisis ultimately drove his shift from theology to secular moral philosophy, seeking a foundation for justice independent of religious presuppositions.

1950年代是他的方法论探索期。博士论文试图为伦理知识建立认识论基础，但并未找到满意的方案。牛津访学是关键转折：以赛亚·柏林关于"两种自由概念"的讲座使他意识到政治价值冲突的深刻性；H.L.A.哈特的法哲学展示了分析哲学处理规范性问题的力量。1958年发表的《作为公平的正义》论文标志着核心框架的首次亮相——尽管此时论证尚不完备，但原初状态和正义原则的基本结构已经成形。

The 1950s were a period of methodological exploration. His doctoral dissertation attempted to establish an epistemological foundation for ethical knowledge but did not arrive at a fully satisfactory solution. The Oxford visit proved pivotal: Isaiah Berlin's lectures on "Two Concepts of Liberty" made him acutely aware of the depth of political value conflicts; H.L.A. Hart's legal philosophy demonstrated the power of analytic philosophy for addressing normative questions. The 1958 paper "Justice as Fairness" marked the first appearance of the core framework — although the argumentation was not yet fully developed, the basic structure of the original position and principles of justice was already taking shape.

### 中期 / Middle Period (1958–1971)

这一时期是罗尔斯思想的系统建构期。在哈佛的十余年间，他以近乎苦行僧式的严谨打磨正义理论的每一个环节。1960年代发表的系列论文——《分配的正义》（1967）、《宪政自由与正义概念》（1963）——逐步完善了差别原则的经济学论证和基本自由的优先性论证。1969年，《正义论》手稿开始在学界流传，诺齐克、诺曼·丹尼尔斯等人在哈佛读书会上逐章讨论，提出了大量批评性意见，其中许多被吸收进最终版本。

This period represents the systematic construction phase of Rawls's thought. During more than a decade at Harvard, he refined every element of justice theory with near-monastic rigor. The series of papers published in the 1960s — "Distributive Justice" (1967), "Constitutional Liberty and the Concept of Justice" (1963) — progressively refined the economic argumentation of the difference principle and the justification for the priority of basic liberties. By 1969, the manuscript of *A Theory of Justice* was circulating among scholars; Nozick, Norman Daniels, and others discussed it chapter by chapter in Harvard reading groups, offering numerous critical comments, many of which were incorporated into the final version.

1971年《正义论》出版，立即在英语世界的政治哲学界引发地震。该书被誉为"复活了政治哲学"——在逻辑实证主义和行为主义主导的学术界，罗尔斯以严密的分析哲学方法证明规范性政治理论不仅可能，而且必要。该书对功利主义传统的系统批判、对康德道义论的程序化重构、以及对经济正义的创新论证，使之成为跨哲学、法学、经济学和政治学的里程碑式著作。

The publication of *A Theory of Justice* in 1971 immediately triggered an earthquake in the political philosophy of the English-speaking world. The book was hailed as having "revived political philosophy" — in an academic landscape dominated by logical positivism and behavioralism, Rawls demonstrated through rigorous analytic methods that normative political theory was not only possible but necessary. Its systematic critique of the utilitarian tradition, its procedural reconstruction of Kantian deontology, and its innovative arguments for economic justice made it a milestone work spanning philosophy, law, economics, and political science.

### 晚期 / Late Period (1971–2002)

《正义论》出版后，罗尔斯面对来自多个方向的批评，展开了长达三十年的理论回应与重构。诺齐克（1974）从自由至上主义立场质疑再分配的正当性；桑德尔（1982）从社群主义视角批评"无负荷的自我"概念；森（1980）以能力进路挑战基本善指标的充分性。这些批评促使罗尔斯深入反思《正义论》的理论基础，最终实现了从"整全性自由主义"向"政治自由主义"的重大转向。

After the publication of *A Theory of Justice*, Rawls faced criticisms from multiple directions and spent three decades responding to and reconstructing his theory. Nozick (1974) challenged the legitimacy of redistribution from a libertarian standpoint; Sandel (1982) criticized the "unencumbered self" concept from a communitarian perspective; Sen (1980) challenged the adequacy of the primary goods index with the capability approach. These criticisms led Rawls to deeply reflect on the theoretical foundations of *A Theory of Justice*, ultimately achieving a major shift from "comprehensive liberalism" to "political liberalism."

1985年发表的《作为公平的正义：政治的而非形而上学的》标志着转向的正式开始——正义原则不再被呈现为一种道德真理，而是作为自由民主社会中各种合理整全性学说之间的**重叠共识**的聚焦点。1993年《政治自由主义》出版，以"合理的多元主义事实"、"重叠共识"和"公共理性"三大概念重构了整个理论框架。1995年与哈贝马斯在《哲学杂志》上的著名论战进一步深化了公共理性理论。1997年修正公共理性概念，允许宗教语言在满足一定条件后进入公共讨论。1999年《万民法》将正义理论扩展到国际领域。2001年与艾琳·凯利合著的《作为公平的正义：一种重申》是最终的修订性陈述。

The 1985 paper "Justice as Fairness: Political not Metaphysical" marked the formal beginning of the turn — justice principles were no longer presented as moral truths, but as the focal point of an **overlapping consensus** among diverse reasonable comprehensive doctrines in a liberal democratic society. *Political Liberalism* (1993) reconstructed the entire theoretical framework around three key concepts: "the fact of reasonable pluralism," "overlapping consensus," and "public reason." The famous 1995 exchange with Habermas in the *Journal of Philosophy* further deepened the public reason theory. In 1997, he revised the concept of public reason to allow religious language into public discussion under certain conditions. *The Law of Peoples* (1999) extended justice theory to the international domain. *Justice as Fairness: A Restatement* (2001), co-authored with Erin Kelly, served as the final revised statement.

## 重要概念 / Key Concepts

1. **原初状态 / Original Position** — 选择正义原则的假想起始情境，替代传统社会契约论中的"自然状态"。[详见 →](concepts/original-position.md)
2. **无知之幕 / Veil of Ignorance** — 原初状态中屏蔽身份信息的思想装置，确保选择不偏袒任何特定群体。[详见 →](concepts/veil-of-ignorance.md)
3. **作为公平的正义 / Justice as Fairness** — 正义原则来源于公平的程序，而非独立的道德实在或功利计算。[详见 →](concepts/justice-as-fairness.md)
4. **差别原则 / Difference Principle** — 社会与经济不平等只有在对最不利者最为有利时才被允许。[详见 →](concepts/difference-principle.md)
5. **重叠共识 / Overlapping Consensus** — 多元社会中不同整全性学说对政治正义原则的汇聚认同。[详见 →](concepts/overlapping-consensus.md)
6. **反思平衡 / Reflective Equilibrium** — 在深思熟虑的判断与抽象原则之间不断往返调节以达融贯的方法。
7. **基本善 / Primary Goods** — 权利与自由、机会与权力、收入与财富、自尊的社会基础——理性人无论其善的观念如何都需要的社会条件。
8. **公共理性 / Public Reason** — 在宪政根本与基本正义问题上，公民应诉诸其他公民可以合理接受的推理方式。
9. **最大最小规则 / Maximin Rule** — 在原初状态中选择使最坏结果最优化的决策规则。
10. **合理多元主义 / Reasonable Pluralism** — 自由制度下各种合理但互不相容的整全性学说长期共存的必然事实。

## 思想坐标 / Intellectual Coordinates

- **承接 / Inherits**:
  - 洛克（Locke）的社会契约论 → 自然权利与同意理论
  - 康德（Kant）的道义论 → 自律、人是目的、定言命令的程序化重构
  - 卢梭（Rousseau）的公意理论 → 普遍意志与社会合作的理想
  - 密尔（Mill）的自由理论 → 个人自由的价值（尽管批判其功利主义基础）
- **批判 / Critiques**:
  - 功利主义（边沁 Bentham、密尔 Mill、西季威克 Sidgwick）→ 对个体权利的系统性忽视
  - 直觉主义 → 缺乏系统化的原则优先排序
  - 至善论（perfectionism）→ 将特定善观念强加于全体公民
- **对立 / Opposes**:
  - 诺齐克（Nozick）→ 持有正义 vs 分配正义；最小国家 vs 财产所有民主制
  - 哈桑伊（Harsanyi）→ 最大最小规则 vs 期望效用最大化
- **影响 / Influences**:
  - 德沃金（Dworkin）→ 资源平等、认真对待权利
  - 哈贝马斯（Habermas）→ 审议民主与公共领域理论
  - 森（Sen）→ 能力进路（批判性继承）
  - 斯坎伦（Scanlon）→ 契约主义道德理论
  - 博格（Pogge）→ 全球正义理论
  - 巴里（Barry）→ 正义即公平的中立性

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

1. **医疗资源分配 / Healthcare Resource Allocation** — 差别原则指导"谁是最少受惠者"的界定与资源优先权的分配。诺曼·丹尼尔斯（Norman Daniels）在《公正的医疗保健》（1985）中将罗尔斯框架系统应用于医疗伦理。
   The difference principle guides the identification of "who is least advantaged" and the allocation of resource priorities. Norman Daniels systematically applied the Rawlsian framework to healthcare ethics in *Just Health Care* (1985).

2. **累进税制与再分配 / Progressive Taxation and Redistribution** — 差别原则为再分配政策提供道义论证：高税率只有在改善最不利者处境时才被证成，而非为了最大化总体财富。
   The difference principle provides a deontological argument for redistributive policies: high taxation is justified only when it improves the situation of the least advantaged, not to maximize aggregate wealth.

3. **教育公平 / Educational Equity** — 公平的机会平等原则要求补偿性教育投入——不仅要消除形式上的歧视，还要补偿社会出身的不利影响。
   The fair equality of opportunity principle requires compensatory educational investment — not merely eliminating formal discrimination, but compensating for the disadvantages of social background.

4. **AI伦理与算法公平 / AI Ethics and Algorithmic Fairness** — 无知之幕成为设计公正算法的思想工具：如果算法设计者不知道自己将成为哪一类被影响者，会如何设计决策系统？
   The veil of ignorance serves as a thought tool for designing fair algorithms: if algorithm designers did not know which category of affected persons they would become, how would they design decision systems?

5. **全球正义与国际援助 / Global Justice and International Aid** — 《万民法》为国际援助与发展伦理提供框架——自由人民有义务援助"负担沉重的社会"，但义务的性质不同于国内分配正义。
   *The Law of Peoples* provides a framework for international aid and development ethics — liberal peoples have a duty to assist "burdened societies," but the nature of this duty differs from domestic distributive justice.

6. **宪政设计与审议民主 / Constitutional Design and Deliberative Democracy** — 公共理性理论影响宪法裁判与审议民主理论——法官和公民在裁决根本政治问题时应诉诸共享的推理方式。
   Public reason theory influences constitutional adjudication and deliberative democracy theory — judges and citizens should appeal to shared modes of reasoning when deciding fundamental political questions.

7. **气候正义 / Climate Justice** — 代际正义原则（来自《正义论》的"正义的储存原则"）为气候变化伦理提供哲学基础——当代人对后代人负有何种义务？
   The principle of just savings (from *A Theory of Justice*) provides a philosophical foundation for climate change ethics — what obligations do current generations owe to future generations?

## 常见误读 / Common Misreadings

1. ❌ **"无知之幕导致平均主义" / "The veil of ignorance leads to egalitarianism"**
   ✅ 差别原则**允许**不平等，只要其对最不利者最为有利。激励机制与企业家精神在差别原则下被容许——关键是这种不平等必须可证明改善了最差群体的处境。
   The difference principle **permits** inequalities as long as they work to the greatest benefit of the least advantaged. Incentive mechanisms and entrepreneurial spirit are allowed under the difference principle — the key is that such inequalities must demonstrably improve the situation of the worst-off group.

2. ❌ **"罗尔斯忽视文化与社群" / "Rawls ignores culture and community"**
   ✅ 《政治自由主义》正是对这一批评的回应。罗尔斯承认合理的多元主义事实，并寻求不依赖任何特定整全性学说的政治证成。公共理性概念本身就内含社群维度。
   *Political Liberalism* is precisely a response to this criticism. Rawls acknowledges the fact of reasonable pluralism and seeks political justification that does not depend on any particular comprehensive doctrine. The concept of public reason itself contains a communitarian dimension.

3. ❌ **"原初状态是历史假说" / "The original position is a historical hypothesis"**
   ✅ 罗尔斯明确指出原初状态是**纯粹假设性的思想实验**，不具有历史真实性。其功能是为正义原则提供论证工具，类似于数学中的理想化模型。
   Rawls explicitly states that the original position is a **purely hypothetical thought experiment** with no historical reality. Its function is to provide a justificatory tool for principles of justice, analogous to an idealized model in mathematics.

4. ❌ **"罗尔斯是功利主义的改良版" / "Rawls is a refined version of utilitarianism"**
   ✅ 罗尔斯对功利主义进行了根本性批判——他拒绝人际效用比较、拒绝为了总量牺牲个体、坚持权利的优先性。正义二原则与功利最大化在逻辑上不相容。
   Rawls mounted a fundamental critique of utilitarianism — he rejected interpersonal utility comparisons, rejected sacrificing individuals for aggregate gain, and insisted on the priority of rights. The two principles of justice are logically incompatible with utility maximization.

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

罗尔斯的政治自由主义与东亚思想传统之间存在有意义的对话空间：

Meaningful dialogue exists between Rawls's political liberalism and East Asian intellectual traditions:

- **儒家"仁政"与正义论 / Confucian "Benevolent Governance" and Justice Theory** — 儒家的"仁政"理想与罗尔斯的正义原则都关注制度的道德基础，但儒家倾向于精英贤能的治理模式，而罗尔斯强调公平的机会平等与对最不利者的优先关怀。当代新儒家学者（如杜维明、安靖如）尝试在"重叠共识"框架中安置儒家政治理想。
  Both Confucian "benevolent governance" and Rawlsian justice principles focus on the moral foundations of institutions, but Confucianism tends toward meritocratic governance models, while Rawls emphasizes fair equality of opportunity and priority concern for the least advantaged. Contemporary New Confucian scholars (such as Tu Weiming and Stephen Angle) have attempted to situate Confucian political ideals within the framework of "overlapping consensus."

- **佛教"无缘大慈"与无知之幕 / Buddhist "Boundless Compassion" and the Veil of Ignorance** — 佛教的慈悲伦理要求超越自我中心的分别心，这与无知之幕屏蔽自我信息的逻辑存在结构性相似。无知之幕使人无法偏袒自己，佛教的空性智慧则从根本上消解了"我执"。
  Buddhist compassion ethics demands transcending egocentric discrimination, which bears structural similarity to the veil of ignorance's logic of blocking self-referential information. The veil prevents self-favoritism, while Buddhist wisdom of emptiness fundamentally dissolves "ego-attachment."

- **道家"无为而治"与最小干预 / Daoist "Wu Wei" Governance and Minimal Intervention** — 道家主张统治者应当减少干预、顺应自然，这与罗尔斯对政府权力的谨慎态度有呼应之处，但两者的哲学基础截然不同：道家诉诸自然秩序，罗尔斯诉诸理性建构。
  Daoism advocates that rulers should minimize intervention and follow nature's course, which resonates with Rawls's cautious attitude toward government power, but their philosophical foundations are entirely different: Daoism appeals to natural order, while Rawls appeals to rational construction.
