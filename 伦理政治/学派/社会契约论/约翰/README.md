# 约翰·洛克 · John Locke

## 基本信息 / Basic Information

约翰·洛克（John Locke, 1632年8月29日—1704年10月28日）是英国哲学家、政治理论家和医生，被公认为**古典自由主义之父**和**经验主义认识论的奠基者**。他出生于英格兰萨默塞特郡林顿（Wrington）的一个清教家庭，父亲老约翰·洛克是一名乡村律师，在英国内战中为议会军效力。这一家庭背景深刻塑造了洛克日后对王权绝对主义的批判立场。

John Locke (29 August 1632 – 28 October 1704) was an English philosopher, political theorist, and physician, widely regarded as the **father of classical liberalism** and the **founder of empiricist epistemology**. Born in Wrington, Somerset, to a Puritan family, his father — also named John Locke — was a country lawyer who served in the Parliamentary army during the English Civil War. This family background profoundly shaped Locke's later critique of royal absolutism.

洛克于1652年进入牛津大学基督教堂学院（Christ Church, Oxford），先后学习亚里士多德经院哲学和新兴的自然科学，1656年获学士学位，1658年获硕士学位。在牛津期间，他结识了罗伯特·波义耳（Robert Boyle）和罗伯特·胡克（Robert Hooke）等科学家，深受实验方法和经验主义精神的熏陶。他还研习医学，虽未正式执业，但医学训练强化了他对观察与经验的重视。1667年，洛克成为第一代沙夫茨伯里伯爵（Earl of Shaftesbury）的家庭医生和政治顾问，由此深入英国政治的核心。此后他经历了政治动荡、流亡荷兰，最终在1688年光荣革命后返回英国，于1689年一年内出版了三部划时代著作——《政府论》、《人类理解论》和《论宗教宽容》——奠定了他在西方思想史上不可撼动的地位。

Locke entered Christ Church, Oxford in 1652, studying Aristotelian scholastic 哲学 alongside the emerging natural sciences. He received his BA in 1656 and MA in 1658. At Oxford he encountered Robert Boyle and Robert Hooke, absorbing the spirit of experimental method and empiricism. He also studied medicine, and though he never formally practiced, his medical training reinforced his commitment to observation and experience. In 1667, Locke became personal physician and political advisor to the first Earl of Shaftesbury, entering the heart of English politics. After political turmoil and exile in the Netherlands, he returned to England following the Glorious Revolution of 1688, publishing three epoch-making works in a single year (1689) — *Two Treatises of Government*, *An Essay Concerning Human Understanding*, and *A Letter Concerning Toleration* — securing his immovable place in the history of Western thought.

## 核心命题 / Core Theses

### 1. 自然权利 · Natural Rights

人在自然状态中拥有**不可让渡**的三项自然权利：**生命、自由与财产**（life, liberty, and estate）。不同于霍布斯所描绘的"一切人反对一切人"的战争状态，洛克的自然状态受**自然法**约束——理性教导所有平等独立之人不得侵害他人的自然权利。自然权利既是政治权力的边界，也是政治权力的目的。

Humans in the state of nature possess three **inalienable** natural rights: **life, liberty, and estate**. Unlike Hobbes's war of "every man against every man," Locke's state of nature is governed by the **law of nature** — reason teaches all equal and independent persons not to harm others in their natural rights. Natural rights serve as both the boundary and the purpose of political power.

### 2. 财产权的劳动论 · Labor Theory of Property

财产权起源于**劳动**——当一个人将自己的劳动掺入自然物（mixing one's labour with nature），使之脱离自然的共有状态时，该物即成为他的私有财产。但这一占有受两个条件约束：（a）**充足条件**——留给他人"足够且同样好"的资源；（b）**腐败条件**——占有的数量不得超过自己能够使用的限度，不得浪费。货币的出现松动了这两个限制，使不平等积累成为可能，但这源于人们的默示同意。

Property originates in **labour** — when a person mixes their labour with natural objects, removing them from the common state of nature, those objects become private property. This appropriation is constrained by two provisos: (a) the **sufficiency proviso** — leaving "enough and as good" for others; (b) the **spoilage proviso** — not accumulating beyond what one can use before it spoils. The introduction of money loosened both provisos, enabling unequal accumulation, but this arose from tacit consent.

### 3. 基于同意的政府 · Government by Consent

政府的一切正当权力来自被治理者的**同意**（consent of the governed），其职责仅限于保护人民的自然权利。人们进入政治社会时，仅将执行自然法的权力**信托**（trust）给政府，而非绝对让渡。政府若违背信托——侵犯生命、自由或财产权——人民有权**解散**它并建立新政府。这一"革命权"理论是洛克与霍布斯最根本的分歧。

All legitimate political power derives from the **consent of the governed**, and its purpose is limited to protecting natural rights. Upon entering political society, individuals merely **entrust** the executive power of the law of nature to government, rather than absolutely alienating it. When government breaches this trust — by violating life, liberty, or property — the people have the right to **dissolve** it and establish a new one. This theory of the "right of revolution" marks the most fundamental divergence between Locke and Hobbes.

### 4. 白板论 · Tabula Rasa

心灵初生时如同一块**白板**（tabula rasa / white paper），没有任何先天观念或天赋原则。一切知识最终来源于**经验**——包括**感觉**（sensation，外部经验）和**反省**（reflection，内部经验）。这是对笛卡尔和经院哲学"天赋观念论"的系统性批判，为英国经验主义传统奠定了基础。

The mind at birth is like **white paper** (tabula rasa), void of all innate ideas or principles. All knowledge ultimately derives from **experience** — both **sensation** (external experience) and **reflection** (internal experience). This constituted a systematic critique of the "innate ideas" doctrine of Descartes and scholastic philosophy, laying the foundation for the British empiricist tradition.

### 5. 宗教宽容 · Religious Toleration

国家不应干涉个人的宗教信仰与实践——政府的职责限于保护**世俗权利**（生命、自由、财产），灵魂救赎属于教会的领域，不在国家管辖范围之内。洛克提出了三个论证：职能论证（国家目的不包括救赎）、效力论证（强制无法产生真信仰）和知识论证（无人能确知何为唯一正确的宗教道路）。但洛克的宽容有其边界——他将天主教徒和无神论者排除在外。

The state should not interfere with individual religious belief or practice — government's role is limited to protecting **civil interests** (life, liberty, property), while the care of souls belongs to the domain of the church. Locke offered three arguments: the **jurisdictional argument** (salvation is not the state's purpose), the **efficacy argument** (coercion cannot produce genuine belief), and the **epistemic argument** (no one can know with certainty which religious path is uniquely true). Yet Locke's toleration had limits — he excluded Catholics and atheists from its scope.

### 6. 分权与法治 · Separation of Powers and Rule of Law

洛克提出了**立法权**与**执行权**的分离，并附加了**对外权**（federative power，处理战争与外交的权力）。立法权是最高权力，但它本身受制于自然法和公共利益。法律必须事先公布、普遍适用，而非针对个案的临时命令。这一思想为后来孟德斯鸠的三权分立理论铺平了道路，也影响了美国宪法的制定者。

Locke proposed the separation of **legislative** and **executive** powers, adding a **federative power** (handling war and diplomacy). The legislative power is supreme, but it is itself bound by natural law and the public good. Laws must be promulgated in advance and apply generally, rather than being ad hoc decrees for particular cases. This thought paved the way for Montesquieu's later theory of the separation of powers and influenced the framers of the American Constitution.

## 思想脉络 / Intellectual Development

### 早期 / Early Period (1632–1667)

洛克的早期生涯是一个从经院哲学走向经验科学的转变过程。在牛津，他接受了标准的亚里士多德逻辑学训练，但很快被波义耳的化学实验和笛卡尔的新哲学所吸引。1660年代，他开始撰写关于自然法的论文（*Essays on the Law of Nature*, 1663–1664），在其中已显露出经验主义倾向和对道德知识基础的追问。这些论文生前未出版，但表明洛克在三十岁前后已开始构建自己的思想体系。他参与了阿什利·库珀（后来的沙夫茨伯里伯爵）的一场关键手术（1668年成功切除肝脏囊肿），由此赢得了这位重要政治人物的信任，进入了一个全新的世界——不仅是医学的，更是政治的。

Locke's early career was a transition from scholastic 哲学 to empirical science. At Oxford he received standard training in Aristotelian logic but was soon drawn to Boyle's chemical experiments and Descartes's new 哲学. In the 1660s he wrote his *Essays on the Law of Nature* (1663–1664), which already displayed empiricist leanings and an inquiry into the foundations of moral knowledge. Though unpublished during his lifetime, these essays show that Locke was constructing his own philosophical system by his early thirties. His successful surgical intervention on Ashley Cooper (the future Earl of Shaftesbury) in 1668 won him the trust of this pivotal political figure, opening an entirely new world — not merely medical, but political.

### 中期 / Middle Period (1667–1689)

这是洛克思想走向成熟的关键阶段。作为沙夫茨伯里的政治顾问，他直接参与了殖民地政策制定（包括争议极大的《卡罗来纳基本宪法》，1669年）。政治实践迫使他思考财产权、政府合法性和个人自由等根本问题。1671年起，他开始撰写《人类理解论》的草稿，探讨人类知识的起源和边界。与此同时，英国国内政治日趋紧张：沙夫茨伯里因反对查理二世的天主教继承政策而失势，洛克作为其圈子成员面临严重的政治风险。1683年沙夫茨伯里在荷兰去世后，洛克也选择流亡。在荷兰的五年（1683–1689）是他创作最丰盛的时期：他完成了《人类理解论》和《政府论》的主要部分，也撰写了《论宗教宽容》的拉丁文初稿。1688年光荣革命推翻了詹姆斯二世，洛克于次年返回英国，携带着即将震动世界的三部手稿。

This was the crucial period of Locke's intellectual maturation. As Shaftesbury's political advisor, he participated directly in colonial policy-making (including the deeply controversial *Fundamental Constitutions of Carolina*, 1669). Political practice compelled him to confront fundamental questions of property, governmental legitimacy, and individual liberty. From 1671 he began drafting *An Essay Concerning Human Understanding*, exploring the origins and limits of human knowledge. Meanwhile, English domestic politics grew increasingly tense: Shaftesbury fell from grace for opposing Charles II's Catholic succession policies, and Locke, as a member of his circle, faced grave political danger. After Shaftesbury's death in the Netherlands in 1683, Locke chose exile. His five years in Holland (1683–1689) were his most productive: he completed the bulk of the *Essay* and the *Two Treatises*, and drafted the original Latin version of the *Letter Concerning Toleration*. The Glorious Revolution of 1688 overthrew James II, and Locke returned to England the following year, carrying three manuscripts that would shake the world.

### 晚期 / Late Period (1689–1704)

1689年是洛克一生的"奇迹之年"：三部著作——《政府论》、《人类理解论》和《论宗教宽容》——在同一年出版（虽然《政府论》实际在1689年末才面世）。这三部作品分别在政治哲学、认识论和宗教哲学领域产生了划时代的影响。洛克在余生中继续笔耕不辍：1693年出版《教育漫话》（*Some Thoughts Concerning Education*），系统阐述了绅士教育的理念，强调品格培养优先于知识灌输，对后世教育理论影响深远。1695年出版《基督教的合理性》（*The Reasonableness of Christianity*），试图在理性主义与正统基督教之间寻求平衡。洛克晚年居住在埃塞克斯郡的奥特斯庄园（Oates），由友人达默里斯·库德沃斯·马沙姆（Damaris Cudworth Masham）照顾，1704年10月28日在友人陪伴下安静辞世，享年七十二岁。他终身未婚，但其思想遗产深刻塑造了此后三个世纪的政治秩序和知识传统。

1689 was Locke's *annus mirabilis*: three works — *Two Treatises*, *Essay Concerning Human Understanding*, and *Letter Concerning Toleration* — published in a single year (though the *Two Treatises* appeared late in 1689). These works revolutionized political philosophy, epistemology, and the 哲学 of 宗教 respectively. Locke continued writing prolifically: *Some Thoughts Concerning Education* (1693) systematically articulated a theory of gentleman's education, emphasizing character formation over rote learning and profoundly influencing subsequent educational theory. *The Reasonableness of Christianity* (1695) sought a balance between rationalism and orthodox Christianity. Locke spent his final years at Oates in Essex, cared for by his friend Damaris Cudworth Masham, and died peacefully on 28 October 1704 at the age of seventy-two. Though he never married, his intellectual legacy profoundly shaped the political order and knowledge traditions of the following three centuries.

## 重要概念 / Key Concepts

- **[自然权利 Natural Rights](concepts/natural-rights.md)** — 生命、自由与财产三项不可让渡的权利，构成政府权力的边界与目的 / Three inalienable rights — life, liberty, and estate — constituting the boundary and purpose of governmental power.

- **[白板论 Tabula Rasa](concepts/tabula-rasa.md)** — 心灵初始如白板，一切知识源于感觉与反省两种经验 / The mind at birth as a blank slate, with all knowledge deriving from sensation and reflection.

- **[财产权劳动论 Labor Theory of Property](concepts/property-labor.md)** — 劳动掺入自然物产生财产权，受充足条件与腐败条件约束 / Property arising from mixing labour with nature, constrained by sufficiency and spoilage provisos.

- **[基于同意的政府 Government by Consent](concepts/government-consent.md)** — 政治权力的正当性来自被治理者的同意，政府是人民信托的受托人 / Political legitimacy derives from the consent of the governed; government as trustee of the people.

- **[宗教宽容 Toleration](concepts/toleration.md)** — 国家不干涉宗教信仰，政教分离，但宽容有其边界 / State non-interference in religion, church-state separation, though toleration has limits.

- **革命权 Right of Revolution** — 当政府违背信托、侵犯自然权利时，人民有权解散并重建政府 / When government breaches trust and violates natural rights, the people may dissolve and reconstitute it.

- **分权思想 Separation of Powers** — 立法权、执行权与对外权的分离，法治优于人治 / Separation of legislative, executive, and federative powers; rule of law over rule of men.

- **默示同意 Tacit Consent** — 占有或使用一国领土即构成对该国政府的默示同意 / Possession or use of a country's territory constitutes tacit consent to its government.

- **自然状态 State of Nature** — 一种自由平等但受自然法约束的前政治状态，非霍布斯式战争状态 / A pre-political state of freedom and equality governed by natural law, not a Hobbesian state of war.

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

- **宪法与人权 Constitutional and Human Rights** — 洛克的自然权利论是美国《权利法案》和联合国《世界人权宣言》的哲学源头。现代宪法中不可剥夺的基本权利直接继承自洛克传统。 / Locke's natural rights theory is the philosophical source of the U.S. Bill of Rights and the UN Universal Declaration of Human Rights. Inalienable fundamental rights in modern constitutions descend directly from the Lockean tradition.

- **知识产权 Intellectual Property** — 洛克的劳动财产权论被广泛引用于论证知识产权的正当性：创作者将智力劳动"掺入"信息，从而产生对作品的权利。但这同样面临"充足条件"的限制。 / Locke's labour theory of property is widely cited to justify intellectual property: creators "mix" intellectual labour with information, generating rights over their works. But this too faces the "sufficiency proviso" constraint.

- **数据权利与数字财产权 Data Rights and Digital Property** — 在数字经济中，个人数据是否属于洛克的"财产"？用户对平台投入的劳动是否产生财产权？这些是洛克理论在21世纪最前沿的应用。 / In the digital economy, does personal data qualify as Lockean "property"? Does user labour invested in platforms generate property rights? These are the most cutting-edge applications of Locke's theory in the 21st century.

- **有限政府与宪政 Limited Government and Constitutionalism** — 洛克关于政府权力受限、可撤回的理念构成了现代宪政主义的核心。对政府越权的司法审查制度直接体现了洛克的信托理论。 / Locke's vision of limited, revocable governmental power constitutes the core of modern constitutionalism. Judicial review of governmental overreach directly embodies Locke's trust theory.

- **国际人权干预 International Human Rights Intervention** — 洛克的"诉诸上天"（appeal to Heaven）概念被某些学者解读为国际人道主义干预的早期哲学依据——当一国政府系统性侵犯人民权利时，国际社会是否有权干预？ / Locke's "appeal to Heaven" has been interpreted by some scholars as an early philosophical basis for international humanitarian intervention — when a government systematically violates its people's rights, does the international community have the right to intervene?

- **环境伦理 Environmental Ethics** — 洛克的"腐败条件"和"充足条件"为环境资源的可持续使用提供了哲学论证：过度开采即违反洛克式财产权的内在限制。 / Locke's spoilage and sufficiency provisos provide a philosophical argument for sustainable use of environmental resources: over-extraction violates the internal limits of Lockean property rights.

## 常见误读 / Common Misreadings

- ❌ **"洛克是无限制的资本主义辩护者"** → ✅ 洛克的财产权有"充足条件"和"腐败条件"的双重限制。货币的出现虽然松动了这些限制，但洛克本人从未论证无限积累的道德正当性。马克思后来对洛克理论的资本主义解读是一种历史性的挪用，而非洛克本人的立场。 / Locke's property theory carries the double constraint of the sufficiency and spoilage provisos. While the introduction of money loosened these limits, Locke himself never argued for the moral legitimacy of unlimited accumulation. Marx's later capitalist reading of Locke was a historical appropriation, not Locke's own position.

- ❌ **"洛克的自然状态是一个和平的乌托邦"** → ✅ 洛克承认自然状态存在严重的**不便**（inconveniences）——缺乏确定的法律、公正的法官和有效的执行力。它虽然不像霍布斯描绘的那样是普遍的战争状态，但其中的不稳定因素足以构成人们进入政治社会的充分理由。 / Locke acknowledged serious **inconveniences** in the state of nature — the absence of settled law, impartial judges, and effective enforcement. While not the universal state of war Hobbes depicted, its instabilities were sufficient grounds for entering political society.

- ❌ **"洛克的白板论否认一切先天结构"** → ✅ 洛克否认的是先天的**观念内容**（innate ideas），而非先天的**认知能力**（innate faculties）。他承认心灵具有反省、组合和抽象等先天能力，这些能力是经验得以转化为知识的必要前提。 / Locke denied innate **idea-content** (innate ideas), not innate **cognitive capacities** (innate faculties). He acknowledged that the mind possesses innate powers of reflection, combination, and abstraction — prerequisites for converting experience into knowledge.

- ❌ **"洛克的'人人生而平等'意味着结果平等"** → ✅ 洛克的平等是指**道德地位的平等**——没有人天然地拥有管辖他人的权力。但他明确承认人们在才能、勤劳程度和财产上的不平等是自然且正当的，货币的出现使经济不平等成为不可避免的事实。 / Locke's equality refers to **moral equality of status** — no one naturally possesses authority over another. But he explicitly acknowledged that inequalities of talent, industry, and property are natural and legitimate; the introduction of money makes economic inequality inevitable.

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

洛克的自然权利论可以与儒家"仁"的传统进行有趣的比较。两者都承认人具有某种不可侵犯的道德地位，但其论证路径截然不同：洛克从**自然法与理性**出发推导出权利，儒家从**关系性的人伦**出发推导出义务。洛克的权利是**对抗性**的（个人对国家），儒家的仁是**关系性**的（君臣、父子、夫妇之间的相互义务）。值得注意的是，孟子的"民贵君轻"思想与洛克的政府信托理论有着惊人的相似之处——两者都认为统治者的合法性取决于其对被统治者福祉的保障，暴君可以被推翻。

Locke's theory of natural rights invites interesting comparison with the Confucian tradition of *ren* (benevolence). Both acknowledge that humans possess a certain inviolable moral status, but their argumentative paths diverge sharply: Locke derives rights from **natural law and reason**, while Confucianism derives duties from **relational ethics** (the bonds of ruler-minister, father-son, husband-wife). Lockean rights are **adversarial** (individual versus state), whereas Confucian benevolence is **relational** (mutual obligations within hierarchies). Remarkably, Mencius's doctrine that "the people are the most important, the sovereign is the least" bears a striking resemblance to Locke's trust theory of government — both hold that the ruler's legitimacy depends on securing the welfare of the governed, and that tyrants may be overthrown.

在财产权问题上，洛克的劳动占有论与道家"天地不仁"的自然观形成对比：洛克认为劳动可以正当化对自然的占有，道家则倾向于认为自然不应被私人占有和支配。而佛学的"无我"概念更从根本上质疑了洛克财产权所依赖的"自我所有权"前提——如果没有一个固定的"自我"，那么"自我拥有劳动"的论证基础又在哪里？

On property, Locke's labour-appropriation theory contrasts with the Daoist view of nature as fundamentally impersonal ("Heaven and Earth are not benevolent"): Locke holds that labour can legitimize appropriation of nature, while Daoism tends to resist private ownership and domination of the natural world. The Buddhist concept of *anatman* (no-self) raises an even more fundamental challenge to the "self-ownership" premise underlying Lockean property — if there is no fixed self, where does the argumentative foundation for "the self owning its labour" stand?
