# 霍布斯阅读指南 · Hobbes Reading Guide

## 阅读路径总览 / Reading Path Overview

本指南按三个层次组织——入门、进阶和专业——为不同背景的读者提供系统化的阅读建议。霍布斯的核心文本集中在《利维坦》第一和第二部分，建议所有读者从这里开始。

This guide is organized in three tiers — introductory, intermediate, and advanced — providing systematic reading suggestions for readers of different backgrounds. Hobbes's core texts are concentrated in Parts I and II of *Leviathan*, and all readers are advised to begin here.

## 一、入门级 / Introductory (3–5 hours)

### 核心章节 / Core Chapters
| 顺序 / Order | 章节 / Chapter | 主题 / Topic | 预计时间 / Est. Time |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 《利维坦》Ch. 13 | 自然状态 / State of Nature | 30 min |
| 2 | 《利维坦》Ch. 14 | 自然法与契约 / Natural Laws and Contracts | 45 min |
| 3 | 《利维坦》Ch. 17 | 国家的生成 / Generation of Commonwealth | 30 min |
| 4 | 《利维坦》Ch. 18 | 主权者的权利 / Rights of Sovereigns | 30 min |

### 导读推荐 / Recommended Introductions

- **Skinner, Quentin** *Hobbes: A Very Short Introduction* (Oxford, 2000) — 最佳英文入门读物。简明精到，100余页概括了霍布斯思想的精髓。The best English-language introduction. Concise and precise, summarizing the essence of Hobbes's thought in just over 100 pages.

- **Tuck, Richard** *Hobbes* (Oxford Past Masters, 1989) — 注重霍布斯思想的智识语境。Emphasizes the intellectual context of Hobbes's thought.

- **顾肃**《西方政治哲学史》中的霍布斯章节 — 中文读者的最佳起点。The best starting point for Chinese-language readers.

### 入门后的下一步 / Next Steps After Introduction
读完上述4章和一本导读后，你应当能够回答以下问题：After reading these 4 chapters and an introduction, you should be able to answer:
- 为什么霍布斯认为自然状态必然是战争状态？Why does Hobbes think the state of nature is necessarily a state of war?
- 社会契约的内容是什么？谁是契约的当事人？What is the content of the social contract? Who are the parties?
- 为什么主权者的权力必须是绝对的？Why must the sovereign's power be absolute?

### 重要二手研究 / Key Secondary Literature

- **Oakeshott, Michael** *Hobbes on Civil Association* (1975) — 最佳英文导读论文集。Oakeshott将霍布斯解读为"公民联合"理论家而非简单的专制辩护者。The best collection of introductory essays in English. Oakeshott reads Hobbes as a theorist of "civil association" rather than a simple defender of despotism.

- **Hampton, Jean** *Hobbes and the Social Contract Tradition* (1986) — 将霍布斯的社会契约论置于更广阔的思想传统中分析，论证清晰，适合有哲学基础的读者。Places Hobbesian social contract theory in a broader intellectual tradition; clear argumentation, suitable for readers with philosophical background.

- **Kavka, Gregory** *Hobbesian Moral and Political Theory* (1986) — 以分析哲学和博弈论的方法解读霍布斯，对"自然状态困境"的博弈论分析尤为精彩。Reads Hobbes through analytic philosophy and game theory; the game-theoretic analysis of the "state of nature dilemma" is especially brilliant.

- **Strauss, Leo** *The Political Philosophy of Hobbes: Its Basis and Its Genesis* (1936/1952) — 施特劳斯学派的经典解读，认为霍布斯是现代性的奠基者。Classic Straussian reading arguing that Hobbes is the founder of modernity.

- **Macpherson, C.B.** *The Political Theory of Possessive Individualism* (1962) — 马克思主义解读经典，将霍布斯解读为早期资本主义的意识形态辩护者。Classic Marxist reading interpreting Hobbes as an ideological defender of early capitalism.

## 三、专业级 / Advanced (30+ hours)

### 完整原典 / Complete Primary Texts
1. 《利维坦》全书（推荐 Macpherson 编 Penguin 版或 Malcolm 编 Clarendon 版）/ *Leviathan* complete (Macpherson ed. Penguin or Malcolm ed. Clarendon)
2. 《论公民》（*De Cive*, 1642）— Richard Tuck 英译 / English translation by Richard Tuck
3. 《法律原理》（*The Elements of Law*, 1640）— Gaskin 编 Oxford 版 / Gaskin ed. Oxford
4. 《比希莫特》（*Behemoth*, 1668）— Tönnies/Goldsmith 编 / Tönnies/Goldsmith ed.

### 重要批评与回应 / Key Critiques and Responses
- **Locke, John** *Two Treatises of Government* (1689), 尤其是第二篇 / Especially the Second Treatise — 最重要的洛克式回应 / The most important Lockean response
- **Rousseau, Jean-Jacques** *Discourse on Inequality* (1755) 和 *The Social Contract* (1762) — 对霍布斯自然状态论的批判和人民主权论的建构 / Critique of Hobbesian state of nature and construction of popular sovereignty
- **Hume, David** *A Treatise of Human Nature* (1739) — 对契约论的经验主义批判 / Empiricist critique of contract theory

### 专业研究 / Specialized Scholarship
- **Schmitt, Carl** *The Leviathan in the State Theory of Thomas Hobbes* (1938) — 施密特的决断主义解读 / Schmitt's decisionist reading
- **Skinner, Quentin** *Hobbes and Republican Liberty* (2008) — 斯金纳对霍布斯自由观的语境主义解读 / Skinner's contextualist reading of Hobbesian liberty
- **Malcolm, Noel** *Aspects of Hobbes* (2002) — 重要的学术考证和传记研究 / Important scholarly research and biographical study
- **Martinich, A.P.** *Hobbes: A Biography* (1999) — 最详尽的现代传记 / The most detailed modern biography
- **Lloyd, S.A.** *Ideals as Interests in Hobbes's Leviathan* (1992) — 对霍布斯动机理论的深入分析 / Deep analysis of Hobbesian motivational theory

## 四、中文资源 / Chinese-Language Resources

### 专著 / Monographs
- **陈肖生**《霍布斯的政治哲学》— 中文世界中最为系统的霍布斯研究。The most systematic Hobbes study in the Chinese-speaking world.
- **顾肃**《西方政治哲学史》（中国人民大学出版社）— 霍布斯章节简明扼要。The Hobbes chapter is concise and clear.
- **慈继伟**《改革时代的道德中国》— 对霍布斯式困境在中国改革语境中的讨论。Discusses Hobbesian dilemmas in the context of Chinese reform.

### 中译原典 / Chinese Translations of Primary Texts
- 《利维坦》，黎思复、黎廷弼译，商务印书馆, 1985 — 标准中译本 / Standard Chinese translation
- 《论公民》，徐志栋译，华东师范大学出版社
- 《利维坦》（选译），各类政治哲学选集 / Selections in various political philosophy anthologies
