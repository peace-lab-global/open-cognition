# 霍布斯生平大事记 · Hobbes Biographical Timeline

## 早年与教育 / Early Life and Education (1588–1628)

- **1588** 4月5日生于英格兰威尔特郡马尔姆斯伯里（Westport）。父亲托马斯·霍布斯（Thomas Hobbes Sr.）是当地教区牧师。据传其母因西班牙无敌舰队逼近英格兰而惊惧早产。霍布斯日后自嘲："恐惧与我同日诞生"（*Fear and I were born twins*）。Born April 5 in Westport, near Malmesbury, Wiltshire, England. Father Thomas Hobbes Sr. was the local parish vicar. His mother reportedly went into premature labor upon hearing of the approaching Spanish Armada. Hobbes later quipped: "Fear and I were born twins."

- **1592** 父亲因在教堂门口与另一牧师斗殴而被免职，离家出走，将霍布斯交给叔父弗朗西斯·霍布斯抚养。Father was dismissed for fighting with another clergyman outside the church, fled, and left Hobbes in the care of his uncle Francis Hobbes.

- **1603** 进入牛津大学莫德林学院（Magdalen Hall, later Hertford College），学习经院逻辑和亚里士多德哲学。据霍布斯晚年回忆，他"并不喜欢经院哲学"。Entered Magdalen Hall, Oxford (later Hertford College), studying scholastic logic and Aristotelian philosophy. In his later recollection, he "did not delight in scholastic philosophy."

- **1608** 获学士学位。经推荐成为哈德威克的威廉·卡文迪许（William Cavendish of Hardwick）的家庭教师。卡文迪许家族是德比郡的显赫贵族家庭。Received Bachelor's degree. Recommended as tutor to William Cavendish of Hardwick. The Cavendish family was a prominent Derbyshire noble house.

- **1610–1615** 随卡文迪许游历欧洲大陆（法国、意大利），接触了文艺复兴时期的人文主义学术传统。Traveled with Cavendish across continental Europe (France, Italy), encountering Renaissance humanist scholarly traditions.

- **1620–1625** 与弗朗西斯·培根交往，据传曾担任培根的秘书，协助翻译培根的部分拉丁文著作。Associated with Francis Bacon, reportedly serving as his secretary and assisting in translating some of Bacon's Latin works.

- **1628** 翻译出版修昔底德《伯罗奔尼撒战争史》（*Eight Books of the Peloponnesian Warre*）。这是首次从希腊文直接译入英文的版本。修昔底德的政治现实主义——尤其是"强者行其所能为，弱者忍其所必受"——深刻影响了霍布斯的思想。Translated and published Thucydides' *History of the Peloponnesian War*. The first English translation directly from Greek. Thucydidean political realism — especially "the strong do what they can and the weak suffer what they must" — profoundly influenced Hobbes's thought.

## 思想转向 / Intellectual Turn (1629–1640)

- **1629–1630** 第二次欧陆旅行。据其自述，在日内瓦一家书店偶然翻开欧几里得《几何原本》第一卷第47命题（毕达哥拉斯定理），深受启发——确信演绎方法的优越性。Second continental journey. By his own account, he happened to open Euclid's *Elements* at Book I, Proposition 47 (the Pythagorean theorem) in a Geneva bookshop, and was immediately captivated — convinced of the superiority of the deductive method.

- **1634–1636** 第三次欧陆旅行。在巴黎结识梅森（Marin Mersenne）神父的学术圈子——这是当时欧洲最重要的科学和哲学交流网络。Third continental journey. In Paris, joined the intellectual circle of Father Marin Mersenne — the most important scientific and philosophical communication network in Europe at the time.

- **1636** 在佛罗伦萨拜访年迈的伽利略。机械论世界观基本确立——宇宙是物质的运动，一切现象都可以用运动和力学来解释。Visited the elderly Galileo in Florence. His mechanistic worldview was essentially complete — the universe is matter in motion, and all phenomena can be explained by motion and mechanics.

- **1637** 返回英国。开始构思系统的哲学计划——从物体到人再到国家。Returned to England. Began conceiving a systematic philosophical program — from body to man to state.

- **1640** 完成《法律原理》（*The Elements of Law, Natural and Politic*）手稿——首次阐述自然状态、社会契约和绝对主权概念。同年英国长期议会召开，查理一世与议会冲突加剧。霍布斯因保王立场感到威胁，于11月逃往巴黎。Completed *The Elements of Law* manuscript — first articulation of the state of nature, social contract, and absolute sovereignty. The Long Parliament convened; Charles I's conflict with Parliament intensified. Hobbes, fearing for his royalist position, fled to Paris in November.

## 流亡与创作高峰 / Exile and Creative Peak (1640–1651)

- **1641** 在巴黎重新进入梅森的学术圈。阅读了笛卡尔的《第一哲学沉思集》手稿，撰写了《第三组反驳》（Third Objections），与笛卡尔进行了哲学交锋。Re-entered Mersenne's circle in Paris. Read the manuscript of Descartes' *Meditations on First Philosophy* and wrote the "Third Objections," engaging in philosophical confrontation with Descartes.

- **1642** 出版《论公民》（*De Cive*）——首次系统阐述政治理论。以拉丁文写作，面向欧洲学术读者。确立了他在欧洲知识界的地位。Published *De Cive* — first systematic political theory. Written in Latin for a European scholarly audience. Established his reputation in European intellectual circles.

- **1646–1648** 担任流亡巴黎的威尔士亲王（未来的查理二世）的数学教师。Served as mathematics tutor to the exiled Prince of Wales (future Charles II) in Paris.

- **1647** 患重病，几乎死亡。During this period he fell gravely ill and nearly died.

- **1649** 1月30日查理一世被处决——对霍布斯震动极大，强化了他对政治秩序之必要性的信念。Charles I was executed on January 30 — profoundly shocking Hobbes and reinforcing his conviction of the necessity of political order.

- **1650** 《人性论》（*Human Nature*）与《论政治体》（*De Corpore Politico*）出版——为《法律原理》两个部分的分拆出版，未经霍布斯授权。*Human Nature* and *De Corpore Politico* published — unauthorized split publication of the two parts of *Elements of Law*.

- **1651** **出版《利维坦》（*Leviathan*）**——英语政治哲学史上最重要的著作之一。该书同时激怒了保皇派（因否定君权神授）和教会（因否定教会独立权力）。霍布斯失去保皇派信任，被迫逃回克伦威尔治下的英国，向新政权宣誓效忠。**Published *Leviathan*** — one of the most important works in the history of English political philosophy. The book simultaneously enraged royalists (for denying divine right) and the clergy (for denying ecclesiastical independence). Hobbes lost the royalists' trust and fled back to England under Cromwell, swearing allegiance to the new regime.

## 晚年 / Later Years (1651–1679)

- **1655** 出版《论物体》（*De Corpore*）——唯物主义形而上学的系统阐述，包含对亚里士多德物理学的批判。Published *De Corpore* — systematic materialist metaphysics including a critique of Aristotelian physics.

- **1655–1660** 与约翰·沃利斯（John Wallis）和塞斯·沃德（Seth Ward）展开激烈的数学论战。霍布斯坚持自己可以化圆为方，但始终未能成功，且遭受了数学界的嘲笑。Engaged in acrimonious mathematical disputes with John Wallis and Seth Ward. Hobbes insisted he could square the circle but never succeeded, suffering ridicule from the mathematical community.

- **1658** 出版《论人》（*De Homine*）——讨论光学和感知理论，完成了哲学三部曲。Published *De Homine* — on optics and perception, completing the philosophical trilogy.

- **1660** 斯图亚特王朝复辟。查理二世登基后认出昔日的数学教师，授予霍布斯年金（100英镑），使他免于贫困。The Stuart Restoration. Charles II recognized his former mathematics tutor upon ascending the throne and granted Hobbes a pension (£100), saving him from poverty.

- **1665–1666** 伦敦大瘟疫和伦敦大火。霍布斯避居乡间。The Great Plague and Great Fire of London. Hobbes retreated to the countryside.

- **1666** 议会动议调查《利维坦》是否构成异端。霍布斯焚烧了部分手稿以自保。Parliament moved to investigate *Leviathan* for heresy. Hobbes burned some of his papers for self-protection.

- **1668** 完成《比希莫特》（*Behemoth*）——以对话形式分析英国内战的宗教-政治根源。查理二世禁止其出版。完成了拉丁文版《利维坦》。Completed *Behemoth* — analyzing the religio-political roots of the Civil War in dialogue form. Charles II suppressed its publication. Completed the Latin edition of *Leviathan*.

- **1670–1675** 翻译荷马的《伊利亚特》（1673）和《奥德赛》（1675），在86岁高龄完成了最后的文学工作。Translated Homer's *Iliad* (1673) and *Odyssey* (1675), completing his final literary work at the age of 86.

- **1679** 12月4日在德比郡哈德威克庄园去世，享年91岁。葬于圣约翰浸信会教堂（St. John the Baptist's Church），Ault Hucknall。他的墓碑铭文写道："一个众所周知的善良之人"（*vir probus*）。Died December 4 at Hardwick Hall, Derbyshire, aged 91. Buried at St. John the Baptist's Church, Ault Hucknall. His tombstone inscription reads: *vir probus* — "a known good man."

## 历史同代人 / Historical Contemporaries

- **前辈 / Predecessors**：培根（1561–1626）、伽利略（1564–1642）、格劳秀斯（1583–1645）/ Bacon, Galileo, Grotius
- **同时代 / Contemporaries**：笛卡尔（1596–1650）、帕斯卡（1623–1662）、斯宾诺莎（1632–1677）/ Descartes, Pascal, Spinoza
- **晚辈 / Successors**：洛克（1632–1704）、牛顿（1643–1727）、莱布尼茨（1646–1716）/ Locke, Newton, Leibniz
