# 妇女的屈从 · The Subjection of Women

## 概念概述 / Concept Overview

《妇女的屈从》（1869）是密尔晚年最具社会批判力的著作，也是西方哲学史上第一部由男性哲学家撰写的系统论证性别平等的专著。密尔在书中论证，妇女在社会中的从属地位并非基于自然差异，而是基于人为的法律和习俗——这种从属关系既违反了功利原则（因为它不必要地损害了一半人类的幸福），也违反了正义原则（因为它系统性地剥夺了妇女的平等权利）。

*The Subjection of Women* (1869) is Mill's most socially critical work in later life and the first systematic argument for gender equality by a male philosopher in Western philosophical history. Mill argued that women's subordinate social position rests not on natural differences but on artificial laws and customs—that this subordination violates both the utility principle (unnecessarily diminishing half of humanity's happiness) and the justice principle (systematically depriving women of equal rights).

这本书的写作深受密尔的妻子哈丽雅特·泰勒·密尔的影响。哈丽雅特本人在1851年撰写了一篇关于妇女选举权的论文，密尔在《妇女的屈从》中多处引用和发挥了她的观点。这部著作在当时英国社会引起了巨大争议——密尔的议会同事们认为他的立场过于激进——但它在后来的女性主义运动中获得了越来越高的评价。

The book was deeply influenced by Mill's wife, Harriet Taylor Mill. Harriet herself had written an essay on women's suffrage in 1851, and Mill drew upon and extended her views throughout *The Subjection of Women*. The work provoked enormous controversy in Victorian Britain—Mill's parliamentary colleagues found his position too radical—but it has received increasing recognition in subsequent feminist movements.

## 理论内涵 / Theoretical Content

密尔的论证可以分解为三个层次。第一层是经验性的否定论证：密尔指出，我们实际上无法知道妇女的"自然"能力是什么，因为妇女从来没有被允许在平等的条件下展示她们的能力。现行制度下的性别差异反映的是权力和机会的差异，而非自然禀赋的差异。他写道："现在被称作妇女的'天性'的东西……在很大程度上是人为的——在某些方面是强制压抑的结果，在另一些方面是过度刺激的结果。"

Mill's argument decomposes into three levels. The first is an empirical negative argument: Mill observed that we cannot actually know what women's "natural" capacities are, since women have never been permitted to demonstrate them under equal conditions. Gender differences under current institutions reflect differences in power and opportunity, not natural endowment. He wrote: "What is now called the nature of women... is an eminently artificial thing—in some points, the result of forced repression; in others, of unnatural stimulation."

第二层是功利主义的正面论证：密尔论证说，解放妇女将大大增加人类的总体幸福。妇女获得教育和职业机会将提高社会的总体生产力；平等的婚姻关系将比主从关系更有助于夫妻双方的幸福；妇女参与公共生活将改善政治决策的质量。

The second level is a positive utilitarian argument: Mill argued that liberating women would greatly increase overall human happiness. Women's access to education and career opportunities would raise social productivity; egalitarian marriages would better serve both spouses' happiness than master-subordinate relationships; women's participation in public life would improve political decision-making quality.

第三层是正义论证：密尔论证说，性别歧视本身就是一种不正义——它违反了"每个人应得到其应得之物"的正义原则。妇女作为人类的一员，理应享有与男性平等的法律权利、政治权利和社会机会。

The third level is a justice argument: Mill argued that gender discrimination is itself an injustice—it violates the principle that "each person should receive their due." As members of humanity, women are entitled to equal legal rights, political rights, and social opportunities with men.

密尔特别批判了维多利亚时代的婚姻法律，该法律使已婚妇女在法律上成为其丈夫的附属品——她们的财产归丈夫所有，她们无权独立签订合同，她们在遭受家庭暴力时几乎没有法律救济。密尔将这些法律与奴隶制进行类比，认为婚姻中的法律不平等比奴隶制更恶劣——因为奴隶至少理论上可以争取解放，而已婚妇女在法律上永远处于从属地位。

Mill particularly criticized Victorian marriage law, which made married women legal appendages of their husbands—their property belonged to their husbands, they could not independently contract, and they had almost no legal recourse against domestic violence. Mill analogized these laws to slavery, arguing that legal inequality in marriage was worse than slavery—at least slaves could theoretically争取 liberation, while married women were legally perpetually subordinate.

## 思想史脉络 / Intellectual History

密尔的性别平等思想有其先驱者。玛丽·沃斯通克拉夫特（Mary Wollstonecraft）在1792年的《女权辩护》中已系统论证了妇女的理性能力和教育权利。密尔的父亲詹姆斯·密尔在1820年代的《政府论》中对妇女选举权持保留态度，而约翰·斯图亚特·密尔则在这一问题上比其父亲更加激进。哈丽雅特·泰勒对密尔思想的影响尤为关键——她不仅是密尔的灵感来源，更是其论证的实质合作者。

Mill's gender equality thought had predecessors. Mary Wollstonecraft's *A Vindication of the Rights of Woman* (1792) had systematically argued for women's rational capacity and educational rights. James Mill expressed reservations about women's suffrage in his 1820s essay on government, while John Stuart Mill was far more radical on this question. Harriet Taylor's influence was particularly crucial—she was not merely Mill's inspiration but a substantive collaborator in his arguments.

在密尔之后，《妇女的屈从》在二十世纪初的妇女参政运动中被重新发现。二十世纪七十年代的女性主义第二次浪潮再次将此书视为重要的理论资源。然而，一些当代女性主义者也批评密尔的论证过于局限于自由主义框架，忽视了父权制度的结构性和文化性维度。

After Mill, *The Subjection of Women* was rediscovered in the early twentieth-century suffrage movement. The second-wave feminism of the 1970s again treated the book as an important theoretical resource. However, some contemporary feminists have criticized Mill's argument as too confined to the liberal framework, neglecting structural and cultural dimensions of patriarchy.

## 关键文本 / Key Passages

> "The principle which regulates the existing social relations between the two sexes—the legal subordination of one sex to the other—is wrong in itself, and now one of the chief hindrances to human improvement."
>
> ——密尔，《妇女的屈从》第一章

这是全书的开场宣言。密尔直接将性别从属定性为"本身就是错误的"——这在维多利亚时代的男性知识分子中极为罕见。

This is the book's opening declaration. Mill directly characterizes gender subordination as "wrong in itself"—extremely rare among Victorian male intellectuals.

> "I deny that any one knows, or can know, the nature of the two sexes, as long as they have only been placed in their present relation to one another."
>
> ——密尔，《妇女的屈从》第一章

这一认识论论证至今仍是性别平等论争中的核心论点：在不平等的权力关系中，我们无法获得关于被压迫群体能力的可靠知识。

This epistemological argument remains central to gender equality debates: under unequal power relations, we cannot obtain reliable knowledge about the capacities of the oppressed group.

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

密尔的论证框架在当代的应用包括：同工同酬立法（基于平等权利的论证）；反家庭暴力法律改革（基于保护个人免受强制的论证）；妇女参政配额制度（基于矫正历史不平等的论证）；以及对职场"玻璃天花板"现象的分析（基于密尔对人为障碍阻碍能力发挥的论证）。

Contemporary applications include: equal pay legislation (based on equal rights arguments); domestic violence law reform (based on protection from coercion arguments); women's political participation quotas (based on correcting historical inequality arguments); and analysis of workplace "glass ceiling" phenomena (based on Mill's arguments about artificial barriers impeding capacity development).

## 批评与回应 / Critiques and Responses

**批评一**：密尔的论证过于依赖自由主义的个人主义框架，忽视了性别压迫的结构性特征。回应：尽管密尔主要关注法律和政治层面的平等，他对社会习俗和经济结构的批判也显示了对结构性不平等的关注。
*Critique 1*: Mill's argument relies too heavily on liberal individualism, neglecting structural features of gender oppression. *Response*: Though Mill focused primarily on legal and political equality, his critique of social customs and economic structures also shows attention to structural inequality.

**批评二**：密尔低估了性别角色差异的生物学基础。回应：当代生物学和性别研究的发展支持了密尔的核心论点——性别差异很大程度上是社会建构的，而非生物学决定的。
*Critique 2*: Mill underestimated the biological basis of gender role differences. *Response*: Contemporary biology and gender studies support Mill's core thesis—that gender differences are largely socially constructed rather than biologically determined.

## 跨文化回响 / Cross-cultural Echoes

在中国近现代思想中，秋瑾、何香凝等女性革命家对妇女解放的呼吁与密尔的论证存在遥远的共鸣。鲁迅在《我之节烈观》中对传统贞操观的批判，在精神气质上接近密尔对维多利亚时代双重标准的批判。当代中国女性主义运动中，密尔关于法律平等和教育机会的论证仍然具有直接的启发意义。

In modern Chinese thought, the calls for women's liberation by female revolutionaries such as Qiu Jin and He Xiangning distantly echo Mill's arguments. Lu Xun's critique of traditional chastity values in "My Views on Chastity and Virtue" spiritually parallels Mill's critique of Victorian double standards. In contemporary Chinese feminist movements, Mill's arguments on legal equality and educational opportunity retain direct inspirational significance.

在日本，福泽谕吉在《劝学篇》中对妇女教育的倡导受到了密尔思想的间接影响。在印度，密尔的性别平等思想对殖民时期和后殖民时期的妇女改革运动产生了影响。

In Japan, Fukuzawa Yukichi's advocacy for women's education in *An Encouragement of Learning* was indirectly influenced by Mill's thought. In India, Mill's gender equality ideas influenced colonial and post-colonial women's reform movements.
