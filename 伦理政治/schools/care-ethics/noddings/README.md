# 内尔·诺丁斯 · Nel Noddings

## 基本信息 / Basic Information

内尔·诺丁斯（Nel Noddings，1929–2022）是美国教育哲学家和伦理学家，关怀伦理学（Ethics of Care）的奠基人物之一。她出生于新泽西州纽瓦克，早年在蒙特克莱尔州立学院获得数学与教育学士学位，随后在斯坦福大学获得教育哲学博士学位。她曾在公立学校任教多年，担任过中学数学教师和校长，这些一线教育经验深刻塑造了她日后对伦理学和道德教育的思考方式。

Nel Noddings (1929–2022) was an American philosopher of education and ethicist, one of the founding figures of the Ethics of Care. Born in Newark, New Jersey, she earned her bachelor's degree in mathematics and education from Montclair State College and later completed her Ph.D. in philosophy of education at Stanford University. She spent many years teaching in public schools and serving as a high school principal — frontline experiences that profoundly shaped her later thinking about ethics and moral education.

1984年出版的《关怀：一种女性主义伦理学与道德教育》（*Caring: A Feminine Approach to Ethics and Moral Education*）标志着关怀伦理学作为一个独立哲学传统的正式诞生。诺丁斯在此书中系统地提出了以"关怀关系"（caring relation）为核心的伦理学框架，挑战了以康德道义论和功利主义为代表的主流伦理学传统。她认为，道德生活的根源不在于抽象的理性原则或普遍规则，而在于人与人之间具体的关怀实践——关怀者与被关怀者之间的相遇、接纳与回应。

The publication of *Caring: A Feminine Approach to Ethics and Moral Education* in 1984 marked the formal birth of the Ethics of Care as an independent philosophical tradition. In this work, Noddings systematically developed an ethical framework centered on the "caring relation," challenging the dominant traditions of Kantian deontology and utilitarianism. She argued that the foundation of moral life lies not in abstract rational principles or universal rules, but in concrete caring practices between persons — the encounter, reception, and response between the one-caring and the cared-for.

诺丁斯在斯坦福大学教育学院担任教授多年，培养了一代又一代教育哲学研究者。她的学术生涯横跨近四十年，出版了十余部专著，涉及伦理学、教育哲学、女性主义哲学、和平教育等多个领域。她的思想对护理伦理学、教育实践、女性主义国际关系理论和社会政策研究产生了深远影响。2022年，诺丁斯在加利福尼亚去世，享年九十三岁。

Noddings served as a professor at Stanford University's School of Education for many years, mentoring generations of scholars in philosophy of education. Her academic career spanned nearly four decades and produced over a dozen monographs covering ethics, philosophy of education, feminist philosophy, peace education, and related fields. Her thought has profoundly influenced nursing ethics, educational practice, feminist international relations theory, and social policy research. She passed away in California in 2022 at the age of ninety-three.

## 核心命题 / Core Theses

**1. 关怀关系是伦理学的基础。** 道德生活的基本单位不是孤立的道德主体，而是关怀者与被关怀者之间的关系。伦理学应当从关系出发，而非从原则出发。
**The caring relation is the foundation of ethics.** The basic unit of moral life is not the isolated moral agent but the relation between the one-caring and the cared-for. Ethics must begin from relations, not from principles.

**2. 关怀优先于正义。** 正义伦理是重要的，但它应当以关怀为基础。没有关怀关系的支撑，正义原则将变得冷漠和机械。
**Caring takes priority over justice.** The ethics of justice is important but must be grounded in caring. Without the support of caring relations, principles of justice become cold and mechanical.

**3. 自然关怀是伦理关怀的原型。** 我们在自然关怀（如对亲人、朋友的本能关爱）中获得的经验，构成了我们理解伦理关怀的基础和标准。
**Natural caring is the prototype of ethical caring.** The experience we acquire in natural caring — the instinctive love for family and friends — forms the foundation and standard for understanding ethical caring.

**4. 动机替换是关怀的核心机制。** 真正的关怀涉及"动机替换"——关怀者的动机能量流向被关怀者的需要，暂时搁置自身的关切。
**Motivational displacement is the core mechanism of caring.** Genuine caring involves "motivational displacement" — the carer's motivational energy flows toward the needs of the cared-for, temporarily setting aside one's own concerns.

**5. 教育应当以关怀为中心组织。** 学校不应仅仅追求学术成就和标准化考试，而应以关怀关系为核心重新组织课程和教学实践。
**Education should be organized around caring.** Schools should not merely pursue academic achievement and standardized testing but should reorganize curriculum and teaching practice around caring relations.

**6. 关怀必须延伸到公共领域。** 关怀伦理不仅仅是私人领域的事务，它应当影响社会政策、国际关系和全球正义的讨论。
**Caring must extend to the public sphere.** Care ethics is not merely a private-domain affair; it should inform social policy, international relations, and discussions of global justice.

## 思想脉络 / Intellectual Development

### 早期 / Early Period (1929–1970s)

诺丁斯的早期生活和教育经历为她的思想奠定了了一个独特的基础。作为一名数学教师和中学校长，她在日常教育实践中直面传统伦理学的局限：康德式的义务论和功利主义的后果论在面对真实的课堂情境时显得僵化而无力。她注意到，教育中最具道德意义的时刻往往不是应用抽象原则的时刻，而是教师与学生之间产生真实联系的时刻——一个教师放下手中的教案去倾听一个困惑学生的声音，或者一个学生在感受到被关心之后开始对学业产生兴趣。

Noddings' early life and educational experiences laid a distinctive foundation for her thought. As a mathematics teacher and high school principal, she confronted the limitations of traditional ethics in daily educational practice: Kantian deontology and utilitarian consequentialism appeared rigid and impotent when faced with real classroom situations. She observed that the most morally significant moments in education were often not moments of applying abstract principles, but moments when genuine connection arose between teacher and student — a teacher setting aside lesson plans to listen to a confused student, or a student beginning to take interest in academic work after feeling cared for.

在蒙特克莱尔州立学院和随后的学术深造中，她深入研读了杜威的教育哲学、马丁·布伯的关系哲学以及弗洛姆的人本主义精神分析。这些思想资源使她开始形成一种关系性的伦理学视野：道德生活不是孤立的个体根据规则做出决定的过程，而是在关系中生成和维系的意义实践。

At Montclair State College and in subsequent graduate studies, she immersed herself in Dewey's philosophy of education, Martin Buber's relational philosophy, and Erich Fromm's humanistic psychoanalysis. These intellectual resources enabled her to begin forming a relational ethical vision: moral life is not a process in which isolated individuals make decisions according to rules, but a meaningful practice generated and sustained within relations.

### 中期 / Middle Period (1984–1999)

1984年《关怀》一书的出版是诺丁斯学术生涯的转折点，也是关怀伦理学作为一个独立哲学传统正式诞生的标志。在这部开创性著作中，她系统地阐述了关怀伦理学的核心框架：关怀关系的结构（关怀者与被关怀者的互动）、动机替换的概念、接纳（engrossment）与确认（confirmation）的机制、自然关怀与伦理关怀的区分。

The publication of *Caring* in 1984 was a turning point in Noddings' academic career and the formal birth of the Ethics of Care as an independent philosophical tradition. In this groundbreaking work, she systematically articulated the core framework of care ethics: the structure of the caring relation (the interaction between the one-caring and the cared-for), the concept of motivational displacement, the mechanisms of engrossment and confirmation, and the distinction between natural caring and ethical caring.

这一时期她还深入探讨了女性主义视角下的恶（*Women and Evil*, 1989）和母性思维（*The Maternal Factor*, 1990）。她提出了一个大胆的观点：传统神学和哲学将"恶"理解为善的缺失或理性的失控，但从女性经验出发，恶可以被重新理解为关怀关系的破坏——分离、抛弃和冷漠。这一观点在学术界引起了广泛讨论和争议。

During this period she also explored feminist perspectives on evil (*Women and Evil*, 1989) and maternal thinking (*The Maternal Factor*, 1990). She advanced the bold thesis that traditional theology and philosophy understood "evil" as the absence of good or the failure of reason, but from women's experience evil could be reconceived as the destruction of caring relations — separation, abandonment, and indifference. This argument provoked widespread discussion and controversy in academic circles.

### 晚期 / Late Period (2000–2022)

进入21世纪后，诺丁斯将关怀伦理学的应用范围大幅扩展。在《从家开始：关怀与社会政策》（*Starting at Home*, 2002）中，她论证了家庭中的关怀经验如何为社会政策提供规范性标准——好的社会政策应当能够支持而非破坏家庭和社区的关怀实践。在《幸福与教育》（*Happiness and Education*, 2003）中，她提出了一个具有挑衅性的命题：教育的终极目的应当是幸福，而幸福的核心构成要素是关怀关系。

In the twenty-first century, Noddings dramatically expanded the scope of care ethics applications. In *Starting at Home: Caring and Social Policy* (2002), she argued that caring experience in the home provides normative standards for social policy — good social policy should support rather than undermine the caring practices of families and communities. In *Happiness and Education* (2003), she advanced the provocative thesis that the ultimate purpose of education should be happiness, and that the core constituents of happiness are caring relations.

她对美国教育改革的标准化趋势持尖锐批评态度，在《当学校改革被标准化时》（*When School Reform Is Standardized*, 2005）中指出，"不让一个孩子掉队"法案所推动的标准化考试和统一课程恰恰破坏了关怀关系的条件——教师被迫将注意力从学生的个体需要转向考试分数。晚年的她越来越关注和平教育与全球正义问题，在与劳里·布鲁克斯合著的《女性主义视角下的和平与社会正义》（*A Feminist Perspective on Peace and Social Justice*, 2017）中，将关怀伦理扩展到国际关系和全球治理领域。

She was sharply critical of the standardization trend in American education reform, arguing in *When School Reform Is Standardized* (2005) that the standardized testing and uniform curricula promoted by the "No Child Left Behind" Act precisely undermined the conditions for caring relations — teachers were forced to shift their attention from individual student needs to test scores. In her later years, she became increasingly concerned with peace education and global justice, extending care ethics to international relations and global governance in *A Feminist Perspective on Peace and Social Justice* (2017), co-authored with Laurie Brooks.

## 重要概念 / Key Concepts

1. **关怀关系 / Caring Relation** — 关怀者与被关怀者之间的双向互动关系，是伦理学的基本单位。详见 [caring-relation](concepts/caring-relation.md)。
   The bidirectional interactive relation between the one-caring and the cared-for; the basic unit of ethics. See [caring-relation](concepts/caring-relation.md).

2. **关怀伦理学 / Ethics of Care** — 以关怀关系为核心的规范伦理学传统，挑战以正义和原则为中心的主流伦理学。详见 [ethics-of-care](concepts/ethics-of-care.md)。
   A normative ethical tradition centered on the caring relation, challenging mainstream ethics focused on justice and principles. See [ethics-of-care](concepts/ethics-of-care.md).

3. **母性思维 / Maternal Thinking** — 从母亲养育经验中产生的一种独特的道德认知方式，强调保护、培育和回应性。详见 [maternal-thinking](concepts/maternal-thinking.md)。
   A distinctive mode of moral cognition arising from maternal experience, emphasizing protection, nurture, and responsiveness. See [maternal-thinking](concepts/maternal-thinking.md).

4. **关怀教育 / Caring Education** — 以关怀关系为核心组织学校课程和教学实践的教育哲学。详见 [caring-education](concepts/caring-education.md)。
   An educational philosophy that organizes school curriculum and teaching practice around caring relations. See [caring-education](concepts/caring-education.md).

5. **自然关怀 / Natural Caring** — 出于本能和情感的自发关怀（如对亲人的爱），是伦理关怀的基础和原型。详见 [natural-caring](concepts/natural-caring.md)。
   Spontaneous caring arising from instinct and emotion (such as love for family members); the foundation and prototype of ethical caring. See [natural-caring](concepts/natural-caring.md).

6. **动机替换 / Motivational Displacement** — 关怀者的动机能量从自身流向被关怀者的需要，是关怀关系的核心心理机制。
   The flow of the carer's motivational energy from self toward the needs of the cared-for; the core psychological mechanism of the caring relation.

7. **接纳 / Engrossment** — 关怀者对被关怀者的开放、接纳和全身心关注，不同于同情，而是一种"用他人的眼睛看世界"的状态。
   The carer's openness, reception, and full attention to the cared-for — distinct from sympathy, a state of "seeing the world through the other's eyes."

8. **确认 / Confirmation** — 关怀者不仅看到被关怀者的现状，还看到并肯定其潜在的最佳自我，促进其成长。
   The carer sees not only the current state of the cared-for but also affirms and promotes their best possible self.

9. **关系自我 / Relational Self** — 自我不是在孤立中形成的，而是在关怀关系网络中被构成和维系的。
   The self is not formed in isolation but constituted and sustained within a network of caring relations.

10. **伦理理想 / Ethical Ideal** — 关怀作为一种伦理理想，要求我们回忆和珍视自然关怀的经验，并主动将其延伸到陌生人和社会制度中。
    Caring as an ethical ideal requires us to remember and cherish the experience of natural caring and actively extend it to strangers and social institutions.

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

1. **护理与医疗保健伦理 / Nursing and Healthcare Ethics**
   诺丁斯的关怀关系理论已成为护理伦理学的核心框架之一，强调护患关系不仅是技术性的，更是道德性的。
   Noddings' caring relation theory has become a core framework in nursing ethics, emphasizing that the nurse-patient relationship is not merely technical but fundamentally moral.

2. **教育实践与学校改革 / Educational Practice and School Reform**
   全球许多教育工作者将诺丁斯的关怀教育理论应用于课堂实践，发展出"关怀型课堂"模式。
   Educators worldwide have applied Noddings' caring education theory to classroom practice, developing "caring classroom" models.

3. **女性主义政治哲学与公共政策 / Feminist Political Philosophy and Public Policy**
   关怀伦理学被用于批判福利政策、移民政策和工作场所政策中的去人性化倾向。
   Care ethics has been used to critique dehumanizing tendencies in welfare, immigration, and workplace policies.

4. **社会工作与社区建设 / Social Work and Community Building**
   关怀伦理为社会工作提供了一种以关系为本的实践哲学，超越技术化的个案管理模式。
   Care ethics provides social work with a relation-centered practice philosophy that transcends technified case management models.

5. **人工智能与科技伦理 / AI and Technology Ethics**
   在人工智能伦理讨论中，关怀伦理提供了一种不同于原则主义（如阿西洛马原则）的关系性视角，强调技术设计中的人本关怀。
   In AI ethics discussions, care ethics offers a relational perspective distinct from principlism (e.g., the Asilomar Principles), emphasizing humanistic care in technology design.

6. **环境伦理与生态关怀 / Environmental Ethics and Ecological Caring**
   关怀伦理从人际关怀扩展到人与自然的关怀关系，为深层生态学和气候正义提供情感基础。
   Care ethics extends interpersonal caring to human-nature caring relations, providing an emotional foundation for deep ecology and climate justice.

7. **组织领导与管理伦理 / Organizational Leadership and Management Ethics**
   "关怀型领导"概念已被管理学界广泛采纳，强调领导者对员工的真实关怀而非工具化管理。
   The concept of "caring leadership" has been widely adopted in management studies, emphasizing leaders' genuine care for employees rather than instrumentalized management.

8. **全球正义与和平教育 / Global Justice and Peace Education**
   诺丁斯晚年将关怀伦理扩展到国际关系领域，为跨国团结和和平建设提供伦理基础。
   In her later years, Noddings extended care ethics to international relations, providing an ethical foundation for transnational solidarity and peace-building.

## 常见误读 / Common Misreadings

**误读 1 / Misreading 1:**
❌ 关怀伦理学只关注私人领域，与公共事务无关。
❌ Care ethics only concerns the private sphere and has nothing to do with public affairs.
✅ 诺丁斯在《从家开始》中明确论证，关怀伦理应当为社会政策和公共制度提供规范性标准。
✅ Noddings explicitly argues in *Starting at Home* that care ethics should provide normative standards for social policy and public institutions.

**误读 2 / Misreading 2:**
❌ 关怀伦理学是一种情感主义，将道德等同于主观感受。
❌ Care ethics is a form of emotivism, reducing morality to subjective feelings.
✅ 关怀伦理学是关系性理论——情感在关怀中确实重要，但它是关系中的情感（如接纳和回应），而非主观随意的情绪。
✅ Care ethics is a relational theory — emotion is indeed important in caring, but it is emotion within relations (such as engrossment and response), not subjective arbitrary sentiment.

**误读 3 / Misreading 3:**
❌ 诺丁斯的理论本质上是本质主义的，将关怀能力绑定于女性生物学。
❌ Noddings' theory is essentially essentialist, binding caring capacity to female biology.
✅ 诺丁斯明确区分了"女性主义路径"（feminine approach）和"女性路径"（female approach）。关怀是一种人类能力，不是女性独有的特质；她使用"女性"一词是因为在传统社会中关怀实践主要由女性承担。
✅ Noddings explicitly distinguishes between a "feminine approach" and a "female approach." Caring is a human capacity, not a trait exclusive to women; she uses "feminine" because caring practices have traditionally been primarily undertaken by women.

**误读 4 / Misreading 4:**
❌ 关怀伦理学与正义伦理学是对立的，二者不可调和。
❌ Care ethics and justice ethics are opposed and irreconcilable.
✅ 诺丁斯认为关怀与正义是互补的——正义应当以关怀为基础，而关怀在公共领域中需要正义的制度化保障。
✅ Noddings considers care and justice to be complementary — justice should be grounded in caring, while caring in the public sphere requires the institutional safeguards of justice.

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

诺丁斯的关怀伦理学与中国儒家传统有着深层的亲和性。儒家以"仁"为核心的伦理思想同样强调关系在道德生活中的首要地位——"仁者爱人"（《论语》），道德不是遵守抽象规则，而是在具体的人际关系中实现的爱与关怀。

Noddings' care ethics shares a deep affinity with the Chinese Confucian tradition. Confucian ethics, centered on *ren* (仁, benevolence), similarly emphasizes the primacy of relations in moral life — "the benevolent person loves others" (*Analerta*), and morality is not about following abstract rules but realizing love and care within concrete interpersonal relations.

儒家的"差等之爱"与诺丁斯的"自然关怀"概念有着相似的结构：关怀从最亲密的关系（家庭）开始，向外扩展到更广泛的社会关系。但二者也有重要差异：儒家更强调等级秩序中的角色义务（父子、君臣），而诺丁斯更强调关怀关系的平等性和双向性。

The Confucian "graded love" (*ai you cha deng*) and Noddings' concept of "natural caring" share a similar structure: caring begins from the most intimate relations (family) and extends outward to broader social relations. Yet there are important differences: Confucianism emphasizes role obligations within hierarchical orders (father-son, ruler-minister), while Noddings emphasizes the equality and bidirectionality of caring relations.

在佛教传统中，"慈悲"（karuna）概念同样强调对他者苦难的感同身受和回应性行动，这与诺丁斯的"接纳"和"动机替换"概念有着深刻的呼应。日本佛教的"间柄"（aidagara，关系性）概念也与关系自我的观念相近。

In the Buddhist tradition, the concept of *karuna* (compassion) similarly emphasizes empathetic feeling and responsive action toward others' suffering, deeply resonating with Noddings' concepts of engrossment and motivational displacement. The Japanese Buddhist concept of *aidagara* (betweenness/relationality) also parallels the notion of the relational self.
