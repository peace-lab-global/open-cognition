# 定言命令 · Categorical Imperative

## 概念概述 / Concept Overview

定言命令（kategorischer Imperativ）是康德伦理学的核心概念，指一种无条件的、对一切理性存在者普遍有效的道德命令。与假言命令（hypothetischer Imperativ）不同——后者以"如果你想要X，就做Y"的形式出现——定言命令不以任何经验性的欲望、目的或偏好为条件，而是直接要求："你应当做Y"。它是纯粹实践理性的基本法则，表达了道德义务的绝对性和无条件性。

The categorical imperative (kategorischer Imperativ) is the core concept of Kantian ethics: an unconditional moral command universally valid for all rational beings. Unlike hypothetical imperatives—which take the form "if you want X, do Y"—the categorical imperative is conditioned by no empirical desire, purpose, or preference; it simply commands: "you ought to do Y." It is the fundamental law of pure practical reason, expressing the absoluteness and unconditionality of moral obligation.

康德在《道德形而上学奠基》（1785）中提出了定言命令的三种主要公式：普遍法则公式（Universal Law Formula）、人性公式（Humanity Formula，又称目的公式）和自律公式（Autonomy Formula）。这三种公式并非三个不同的命令，而是同一个道德法则从不同角度的表述，逐渐增加内容的丰富性。

In the Groundwork of the Metaphysics of Morals (1785), Kant presents three main formulas of the categorical imperative: the Universal Law Formula, the Humanity Formula (also called the End Formula), and the Autonomy Formula. These are not three different commands but three articulations of the same moral law from different angles, with increasing richness of content.

## 理论内涵 / Theoretical Content

### 普遍法则公式 / Universal Law Formula

"只按照你能同时愿意它成为普遍法则的那个准则去行动。"（Act only on that maxim through which you can at the same time will that it become a universal law.）

这一公式提供了一个形式化的道德检验程序：在采取任何行动之前，问自己——我的行为准则（Maxime）能否被一致地设想为一条普遍的自然法则？如果答案是否定的（即该准则在被普遍化时产生矛盾），那么这一行为在道德上就是不允许的。康德区分了两种矛盾：逻辑矛盾（contradiction in conception），即准则被普遍化后在概念上自相矛盾，对应"完全义务"；意志矛盾（contradiction in the will），即虽然逻辑上可以设想，但你无法理性地愿意它成为普遍法则，对应"不完全义务"。

This formula provides a formalized moral test procedure: before taking any action, ask whether your maxim can be consistently conceived as a universal law of nature. If the answer is negative—if universalizing the maxim generates a contradiction—then the action is morally impermissible. Kant distinguishes two types of contradiction: contradiction in conception (the maxim becomes self-contradictory when universalized, corresponding to "perfect duties") and contradiction in the will (logically conceivable but impossible to rationally will as universal law, corresponding to "imperfect duties").

### 人性公式 / Humanity Formula

"如此行动，使你无论对自己还是对他人的人性，始终同时当作目的，绝不仅仅当作手段。"（Act in such a way that you treat humanity, whether in your own person or in the person of any other, always at the same time as an end, never merely as a means.）

人性公式为定言命令注入了实质内容：理性存在者具有绝对的、不可让渡的内在价值（Würde，尊严），不同于事物所具有的相对价值（Preis，价格）。这一公式禁止将人仅仅工具化，要求在所有行为中承认并尊重每个人作为目的本身的地位。

The Humanity Formula gives the categorical imperative substantive content: rational beings possess an absolute, inalienable inner value (Würde, dignity), distinct from the relative value (Preis, price) that things possess. This formula prohibits merely instrumentalizing persons, requiring that in all actions we acknowledge and respect each person's status as an end in themselves.

### 自律公式 / Autonomy Formula

"每一个理性存在者的意志都是普遍立法的意志。"（The will of every rational being is a will that legislates universally.）

自律公式揭示了定言命令的最终根据：道德法则不是从外部强加的，而是理性存在者自己制定的。道德主体同时是道德法则的制定者和服从者，这种"自我立法"的能力就是自律（Autonomie），也是人的尊严的最终基础。

The Autonomy Formula reveals the ultimate ground of the categorical imperative: the moral law is not externally imposed but self-given by rational beings. The moral agent is simultaneously the legislator and subject of the moral law; this capacity for "self-legislation" is autonomy (Autonomie) and the ultimate foundation of human dignity.

## 思想史脉络 / Intellectual History

定言命令概念的提出是伦理学史上的革命性事件。在康德之前，道德哲学的两大主流传统是亚里士多德的美德伦理学和休谟的道德情感论。前者将道德建立在人的本性（ergon，功能）之上，后者将道德建立在道德感（moral sentiment）之上。康德的定言命令概念开辟了一条全新的路径：道德法则既不依赖于人的自然本性，也不依赖于感性情感，而是根源于纯粹实践理性本身。

The introduction of the categorical imperative was a revolutionary event in the history of ethics. Before Kant, the two dominant traditions were Aristotelian virtue ethics, grounding morality in human nature (ergon), and Humean moral sentimentalism, grounding morality in moral sentiment. Kant's categorical imperative opened an entirely new path: the moral law depends neither on human nature nor on sensible feeling, but is rooted in pure practical reason itself.

在后世的发展中，黑格尔批评定言命令过于"形式主义"，缺乏具体的伦理内容；叔本华则嘲讽它是"神学道德的伪装"。然而，二十世纪下半叶以来，经过罗尔斯、科尔斯戈德、奥尼尔等哲学家的重新阐释，定言命令理论焕发了新的生命力，成为当代规范伦理学的核心范式之一。

In subsequent developments, Hegel criticized the categorical imperative as excessively "formalistic," lacking concrete ethical content; Schopenhauer mocked it as "disguised theological morality." However, since the late twentieth century, through reinterpretations by Rawls, Korsgaard, O'Neill and others, the categorical imperative theory has been revitalized as one of the core paradigms of contemporary normative ethics.

## 关键文本 / Key Passages

> "在世界之内，甚至一般地在世界之外，除了善良意志，不可能设想任何东西能够被无限制地视为善的。"
> —《道德形而上学奠基》，前言

> "There is nothing it is possible to take as good without limitation, in the world or even out of it, except a good will."
> —*Groundwork of the Metaphysics of Morals*, Preface

这段开篇确立了善良意志的至高地位，为定言命令的提出奠定了基础——如果唯一无条件善的东西是善良意志，那么道德法则必然是关于意志的法则，而非关于行为后果的法则。

This opening establishes the supreme status of the good will, laying the foundation for the categorical imperative—if the only unconditionally good thing is a good will, then the moral law must be a law about the will, not about consequences of actions.

> "定言命令只有一条，即：只按你能同时愿意它成为普遍法则的那个准则去行动。"
> —《道德形而上学奠基》，第二章

> "There is only a single categorical imperative, namely: Act only on that maxim through which you can at the same time will that it become a universal law."
> —*Groundwork*, Chapter II

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

定言命令的普遍化测试在当代伦理实践中有广泛应用。在商业伦理中，它被用来检验欺骗性广告（如果所有商家都虚假宣传，广告制度本身将崩溃）和剥削性劳动实践（如果所有人都被仅当作工具，人的尊严概念将不复存在）。在科技伦理中，它为数据隐私保护提供了哲学基础——将用户的个人数据仅当作商业资源，就是将人仅仅当作手段。在生命伦理中，"人是目的"的原则为反对人体实验中的非自愿参与和器官商品化提供了坚实的理论支撑。

The universalization test of the categorical imperative has wide application in contemporary ethical practice. In business ethics, it is used to test deceptive advertising (if all merchants falsely advertised, the institution of advertising itself would collapse) and exploitative labor practices (if all persons were treated merely as tools, the concept of human dignity would cease to exist). In technology ethics, it provides a philosophical foundation for data privacy protection—treating users' personal data merely as commercial resources is treating persons merely as means. In bioethics, the "humanity as end" principle provides robust theoretical support against involuntary participation in human experiments and the commodification of organs.

## 批评与回应 / Critiques and Responses

**黑格尔的形式主义批评 / Hegel's Formalism Critique**: 黑格尔认为定言命令的普遍化测试是空洞的形式主义，无法产生具体的道德义务。回应：科尔斯戈德等当代康德主义者指出，康德在《道德形而上学》中确实将定言命令应用于具体的义务体系，形式主义批评忽视了康德伦理学的完整体系。

Hegel argued that the universalization test is empty formalism unable to generate concrete moral duties. Response: Contemporary Kantians like Korsgaard note that Kant does apply the categorical imperative to concrete systems of duties in the Metaphysics of Morals; the formalism critique neglects the full system of Kantian ethics.

**冲突义务问题 / Conflicting Duties Problem**: 当两条义务相互冲突时（如不说谎与保护无辜者），定言命令似乎无法提供明确的指导。回应：康德主义者区分了义务的优先级和具体情境中的义务适用，认为冲突可以通过更精确地界定准则来解决。

When two duties conflict (e.g., not lying vs. protecting innocents), the categorical imperative seems unable to provide clear guidance. Response: Kantians distinguish priority of duties from application in specific contexts, holding that conflicts can be resolved through more precise specification of maxims.

## 跨文化回响 / Cross-cultural Echoes

儒家的"恕道"——"己所不欲，勿施于人"——常被视为定言命令的东方对应物。然而两者存在关键差异：恕道是基于情感类比的推己及人原则，定言命令是基于纯粹理性的普遍化逻辑测试。恕道诉诸"我不愿意"的主观感受，定言命令诉诸"能否被一致地普遍化"的客观理性标准。尽管如此，两者在实践层面常常导向类似的道德判断，为东西方伦理对话提供了重要的桥梁。

The Confucian "way of reciprocity" (shu/恕)—"do not do unto others what you would not have them do unto you"—is often regarded as an Eastern counterpart to the categorical imperative. However, key differences exist: shu is a principle of extending from self to others based on emotional analogy, while the categorical imperative is a universalization logic test based on pure reason. Shu appeals to the subjective feeling of "I would not want this," while the categorical imperative appeals to the objective rational standard of "whether it can be consistently universalized." Nonetheless, both often converge on similar moral judgments in practice, providing an important bridge for East-West ethical dialogue.
