# 身份与暴力 · Identity and Violence

## 概念概述 / Concept Overview

"身份与暴力"是森在2006年出版的同名著作中系统阐述的理论。这一理论的核心论点是：人类拥有多重身份（multiple identities），任何将人简化为单一身份——如宗教、民族、文明或种族——的做法不仅是错误的，而且是暴力的重要根源。森将这种简化称为"身份的幻象"（illusion of identity）或"命运幻象"（illusion of destiny），认为它构成了当代许多暴力冲突的智识基础。

"Identity and Violence" is a theory Sen systematically articulated in his 2006 book of the same name. Its core argument is: human beings possess multiple identities, and any reduction of a person to a single identity — such as religion, ethnicity, civilization, or race — is not only wrong but an important source of violence. Sen calls this reduction the "illusion of identity" or "illusion of destiny," arguing it constitutes the intellectual foundation of much contemporary violent conflict.

森的这一理论既是对亨廷顿（Samuel Huntington）"文明冲突论"的直接回应，也是对全球范围内日益兴起的身份政治（identity politics）和文明论（civilizational discourse）的深刻批评。森主张，承认身份的多元性与选择性——即每个人都有权在多种身份归属中做出选择和排序——是和平共处与全球对话的前提。

Sen's theory is both a direct response to Samuel Huntington's "clash of civilizations" thesis and a profound critique of the global rise of identity politics and civilizational discourse. Sen argues that acknowledging the plurality and choice in identity — that every person has the right to choose and prioritize among multiple identity affiliations — is a precondition for peaceful coexistence and global dialogue.

## 理论内涵 / Theoretical Content

森的身份理论建立在几个关键论点之上：

Sen's identity theory is built on several key arguments:

**第一，身份的多元性 / First, Identity Plurality**
每个人都同时拥有多重身份。一个孟加拉人可能同时是穆斯林、孟加拉语使用者、印度公民、社会主义者、女性主义者、板球爱好者和诗人。这些身份并不互相排斥，而是在不同语境中呈现不同的重要性。否认这种多元性就是将人"缩小"（miniaturize）为单一维度。

Every person simultaneously possesses multiple identities. A Bengali might simultaneously be Muslim, a Bengali speaker, an Indian citizen, a socialist, a feminist, a cricket enthusiast, and a poet. These identities are not mutually exclusive but assume different importance in different contexts. Denying this plurality is to "miniaturize" a person into a single dimension.

**第二，身份的选择性 / Second, Identity Choice**
森强调，人们在多重身份之间具有选择与排序的能动性（agency）。虽然我们不能完全选择自己的身份归属（如出生时的国籍或种族），但我们可以在相当程度上选择哪些身份对我们更重要、如何在不同身份之间分配忠诚。这种选择自由是个人自主性的重要组成部分。

Sen emphasizes that people have agency in choosing and prioritizing among multiple identities. While we cannot entirely choose our identity affiliations (such as nationality or ethnicity at birth), we can to a considerable extent choose which identities matter more to us and how to distribute loyalty among different identities. This freedom of choice is an important component of individual autonomy.

**第三，身份单一化与暴力的因果关系 / Third, Identity Reduction and Violence**
森论证了身份单一化与暴力之间的因果联系：当人们被说服——或被强迫——接受单一身份为"真正的"或"最重要的"身份时，他们更容易将"他者"视为敌人。"我们 vs 他们"的二元对立只有在身份被简化为单一维度时才能如此尖锐。文明冲突论恰恰通过将人归类为"西方人"、"穆斯林"、"印度教徒"等单一类别来强化这种对立。

Sen argues for a causal link between identity reduction and violence: when people are persuaded — or forced — to accept a single identity as the "true" or "most important" one, they more easily view the "other" as an enemy. The "us vs them" binary can only be so sharp when identity is simplified to a single dimension. The clash of civilizations thesis reinforces precisely this opposition by classifying people into single categories like "Westerners," "Muslims," "Hindus."

**第四，跨文化对话的可能性 / Fourth, Possibility of Cross-cultural Dialogue**
身份的多元性不仅防止暴力，还为跨文化对话创造条件。当人们意识到自己与"他者"共享某些身份维度（如职业身份、兴趣身份、价值观身份）时，对话与理解的桥梁就建立起来了。森称之为"跨身份的团结"（cross-identity solidarity）。

Identity plurality not only prevents violence but creates conditions for cross-cultural dialogue. When people realize they share certain identity dimensions with the "other" (such as professional identity, interest identity, value identity), bridges for dialogue and understanding are built. Sen calls this "cross-identity solidarity."

## 思想史脉络 / Intellectual History

森的身份理论有其深刻的个人与思想史根源。他出生于孟加拉的一个印度教家庭，成长于穆斯林占多数的东孟加拉（后成为东巴基斯坦，再后成为孟加拉国），在泰戈尔的维斯瓦-巴拉蒂大学接受教育——泰戈尔本人就是跨文化对话的先驱。1947年印巴分治期间，森亲眼目睹了宗教身份被单一化和暴力化的过程：一名穆斯林日工因被仅仅视为"印度教徒"而在森的校园中被暴徒刺杀。这一事件成为森身份理论的个人经验基础。

Sen's identity theory has deep personal and intellectual-historical roots. Born into a Hindu family in Bengal, he grew up in Muslim-majority East Bengal (later East Pakistan, later Bangladesh), and was educated at Tagore's Visva-Bharati University — Tagore himself being a pioneer of cross-cultural dialogue. During the 1947 India-Pakistan partition, Sen witnessed firsthand the process of religious identity being reduced and violentized: a Muslim day laborer was stabbed to death on Sen's campus simply for being identified as "Hindu." This event became the personal experiential foundation of Sen's identity theory.

在思想史上，森的身份理论继承了泰戈尔的人文主义、亚当·斯密的"同情"伦理、以赛亚·伯林（Isaiah Berlin）的"价值多元论"以及爱德华·萨义德（Edward Said）对"东方主义"的批评。同时，它与阿皮亚（Kwame Anthony Appiah）的"世界主义"（cosmopolitanism）形成了密切的对话关系。

Intellectually, Sen's identity theory inherits Tagore's humanism, Adam Smith's "sympathy" ethics, Isaiah Berlin's "value pluralism," and Edward Said's critique of "Orientalism." It also forms close dialogue with Kwame Anthony Appiah's "cosmopolitanism."

>
> "Reducing a person to their religious or civilizational identity is not only an intellectual error but a political choice — one that makes the world easier to divide into opposing camps."
> — Sen, *Identity and Violence* (2006)

>
> "The violence I witnessed as a child in Dhaka was not a clash between 'Hindu civilization' and 'Islamic civilization' — it was a specific person killed because he was stripped of all other identities."
> — Sen, *Identity and Violence* (2006)

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

在当代全球政治中，身份单一化的趋势愈发明显：极端主义组织（如ISIS）通过宗教身份动员暴力；民粹主义运动通过民族或种族身份建构"人民"与"他者"的对立；某些国家的政治话语将移民简化为"外国人"或"穆斯林"身份。森的理论为分析和抵抗这些趋势提供了概念工具。

In contemporary global politics, the trend of identity reduction is increasingly apparent: extremist organizations (like ISIS) mobilize violence through religious identity; populist movements construct "people vs other" oppositions through national or ethnic identity; political discourse in some countries reduces immigrants to "foreigner" or "Muslim" identity. Sen's theory provides conceptual tools for analyzing and resisting these trends.

在多元文化教育中，身份多元性概念被用于设计促进跨文化理解的教育课程。在社交媒体治理中，分析算法如何强化身份单一化（通过推荐系统创造的"信息茧房"）成为重要的研究方向。

In multicultural education, the identity plurality concept is used to design curricula promoting cross-cultural understanding. In social media governance, analyzing how algorithms reinforce identity reduction (through "filter bubbles" created by recommendation systems) has become an important research direction.

## 批评与回应 / Critiques and Responses

**批评一：忽视权力维度 / Critique 1: Ignoring Power Dimensions**
批评者指出，身份的"选择性"在不同权力位置的人之间是不平等的：一个少数族裔成员可能无法自由选择不被种族化。森承认权力不平等限制了身份选择的空间，但坚持认为即使在受限条件下，承认身份的多元性仍然比接受单一化叙事更有解放力。

Critics note that identity "choice" is unequal among people in different power positions: a minority group member may not freely choose not to be racialized. Sen acknowledges that power inequality constrains the space for identity choice, but maintains that even under constrained conditions, acknowledging identity plurality remains more liberating than accepting reductionist narratives.

**批评二：低估集体身份的动员力量 / Critique 2: Underestimating Collective Identity's Mobilizing Power**
一些社会运动理论家认为，森低估了集体身份在正义斗争中的积极作用——如民权运动中的种族身份、女权运动中的性别身份。森回应说，他不反对在特定语境中强调某一身份维度的策略性需要，但反对将任何身份视为固定不变的"本质"。

Some social movement theorists argue Sen underestimates collective identity's positive role in justice struggles — such as racial identity in civil rights movements, gender identity in feminist movements. Sen responds that he does not oppose strategically emphasizing a particular identity dimension in specific contexts, but opposes treating any identity as a fixed "essence."

## 跨文化回响 / Cross-cultural Echoes

森的身份多元性理论与中国传统的"和而不同"（harmony in diversity）理念有深层对话空间。《论语》中"君子和而不同，小人同而不和"的教导与森反对身份单一化、主张多元共存的理念相呼应。庄子的"齐物论"中对多元视角的肯定也与森对价值多元性的哲学辩护有相似之处。

Sen's identity plurality theory has deep dialogue space with the Chinese traditional concept of "harmony in diversity" (和而不同). The Analects' teaching that "the noble person seeks harmony but not uniformity; the petty person seeks uniformity but not harmony" echoes Sen's opposition to identity reduction and advocacy of plural coexistence. Zhuangzi's "Equalizing of Things" (齐物论) affirmation of multiple perspectives also parallels Sen's philosophical defense of value pluralism.

在当代中国的民族政策讨论中，森的理论也提供了有价值的视角：如何在承认各民族身份的同时，培育超越单一民族身份的公民认同与跨民族团结，是一个森式问题。

In contemporary Chinese ethnic policy discussions, Sen's theory offers valuable perspectives: how to cultivate civic identity and cross-ethnic solidarity that transcend single ethnic identity while acknowledging ethnic identities is a distinctly Senian question.
