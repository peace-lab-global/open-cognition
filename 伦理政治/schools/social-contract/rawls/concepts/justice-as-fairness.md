# 作为公平的正义 · Justice as Fairness

## 概念概述 / Concept Overview

"作为公平的正义"是罗尔斯正义理论的总名称，也是他自1958年首次使用以来始终坚守的标题。它传达了一个根本性主张：**正义的本质不在于某种独立的道德真理或自然秩序，而在于自由平等的理性人在公平条件下所同意的原则**。"公平"（fairness）是正义（justice）的程序性基础——原则之所以是正义的，是因为它们产生于一个公平的程序。

"Justice as Fairness" is the overarching name for Rawls's theory of justice, a title he consistently maintained since first using it in 1958. It conveys a fundamental claim: **the essence of justice lies not in some independent moral truth or natural order, but in the principles that free and equal rational persons would agree to under fair conditions**. "Fairness" is the procedural foundation of justice — principles are just because they emerge from a fair procedure.

这一名称本身就是一个哲学立场的宣言。传统的正义理论要么诉诸自然法（亚里士多德、阿奎那），要么诉诸效用最大化（功利主义），要么诉诸神学命令。罗尔斯的进路是**契约论**和**建构主义**的——正义原则不是被"发现"的，而是在公平程序中被"建构"出来的。这不是说道德是任意的，而是说道德原则的有效性来源于产生它们的程序之公平性。

This name itself is a declaration of philosophical stance. Traditional justice theories either appeal to natural law (Aristotle, Aquinas), utility maximization (utilitarianism), or divine command. Rawls's approach is **contractarian** and **constructivist** — principles of justice are not "discovered" but "constructed" through a fair procedure. This does not mean morality is arbitrary, but that the validity of moral principles derives from the fairness of the procedure that generates them.

## 理论内涵 / Theoretical Content

### 三个核心要素 / Three Core Elements

"作为公平的正义"包含三个相互支撑的要素（Justice as Fairness contains three mutually supporting elements）：

**1. 契约论框架 / Contractarian Framework**
正义原则是理性人之间达成的协议，而非由权威强加或由自然给定的。但罗尔斯的契约不是历史性的——它不是关于"国家是如何产生的"解释，而是关于"什么原则是正义的"论证工具。

Justice principles are agreements reached among rational persons, neither imposed by authority nor given by nature. But Rawls's contract is not historical — it is not an explanation of "how the state arose" but a justificatory tool for "what principles are just."

**2. 公平的程序 / Fair Procedure**
原初状态的设计确保程序的公平性：无知之幕屏蔽偏见信息、各方地位对称、选择基于理性推理而非权力或威胁。公平的程序产生公平的结果。

The design of the original position ensures procedural fairness: the veil of ignorance blocks biased information, parties occupy symmetrical positions, and choices are based on rational reasoning rather than power or threats. Fair procedures produce fair outcomes.

**3. 康德式建构主义 / Kantian Constructivism**
正义原则不是独立存在的道德事实（道德实在论），也不是主观偏好的表达（情感主义），而是理性主体在公平条件下**建构**出来的。这一立场既拒绝了道德实在论的形而上学负担，也避免了道德相对主义。

Justice principles are neither independently existing moral facts (moral realism) nor expressions of subjective preference (emotivism), but are **constructed** by rational agents under fair conditions. This position rejects both the metaphysical burden of moral realism and moral relativism.

### 与功利主义的根本区别 / Fundamental Difference from Utilitarianism

| 维度 / Dimension | 功利主义 / Utilitarianism | 作为公平的正义 / Justice as Fairness |
|---|---|---|
| 基础 / Foundation | 效用最大化 / Utility maximization | 公平程序 / Fair procedure |
| 个人 / Individual | 可被牺牲于总量 / Sacrificable for aggregate | 不可侵犯的权利 / Inviolable rights |
| 方法 / Method | 单一原则（最大幸福）/ Single principle | 多原则（词典序排列）/ Multiple lexically ordered |
| 证成 / Justification | 经验后果 / Empirical consequences | 程序公平 / Procedural fairness |
| 人的概念 / Conception of person | 效用容器 / Utility containers | 自由平等的道德主体 / Free and equal moral agents |

罗尔斯对功利主义的核心批评是：它将适用于个人的理性选择原则（一个人在不同时间之间权衡得失是合理的）不适当地扩展到了社会层面——**社会不是一个"超级个人"**，不能为了某些人的利益而牺牲其他人的权利。

Rawls's core criticism of utilitarianism is that it improperly extends a rational choice principle applicable to individuals (it is rational for one person to weigh gains against losses across different times) to the social level — **society is not a "super-person"** that may sacrifice some people's rights for the benefit of others.

### 正义原则的最终表述 / Final Statement of the Principles

**第一原则（平等自由原则）/ First Principle (Equal Liberty)**：
每个人对与所有人所拥有的最广泛平等的基本自由体系相容的类似自由体系都应有一种平等的权利。
Each person has an equal right to a fully adequate scheme of equal basic liberties compatible with a similar scheme for all.

**第二原则 / Second Principle**：
社会与经济不平等应满足两个条件：
Social and economic inequalities must satisfy two conditions:
- (a) 公平的机会平等原则 — 在公平的机会平等条件下，与向所有人开放的职位和地位相联系。
  Fair equality of opportunity — attached to offices and positions open to all under conditions of fair equality of opportunity.
- (b) 差别原则 — 对最不利的社会成员最为有利。
  Difference principle — to the greatest benefit of the least advantaged members of society.

**词典序优先规则 / Lexical Priority Rules**：第一原则优先于第二原则（自由的优先性）；公平的机会平等优先于差别原则。
The first principle takes priority over the second (priority of liberty); fair equality of opportunity takes priority over the difference principle.

## 思想史脉络 / Intellectual History

"作为公平的正义"这一概念的思想根源可以追溯到三个传统：

The intellectual roots of "Justice as Fairness" can be traced to three traditions:

**社会契约论 / Social Contract Tradition**：霍布斯、洛克、卢梭确立了"同意"作为政治义务基础的观念。罗尔斯继承了这一传统，但将其从"政治服从"问题转向了"分配正义"问题——契约不再解释国家的起源，而是证成社会基本结构的正义原则。

Hobbes, Locke, and Rousseau established the idea of "consent" as the foundation of political obligation. Rawls inherited this tradition but redirected it from the problem of "political obedience" to "distributive justice" — the contract no longer explains the origin of the state but justifies the principles of justice for the basic structure of society.

**康德道义论 / Kantian Deontology**：康德的"自律"概念——理性人为自己立法——为罗尔斯提供了"建构主义"的哲学基础。正义原则不是被发现的客观道德事实，而是理性主体在公平条件下自主建构的产物。

Kant's concept of "autonomy" — rational agents legislating for themselves — provided Rawls with the philosophical foundation for "constructivism." Justice principles are not objective moral facts to be discovered, but products autonomously constructed by rational agents under fair conditions.

**美国实用主义 / American Pragmatism**：杜威的哲学方法——拒绝绝对确定性、强调问题解决与实验性探究——影响了罗尔斯的反思平衡方法。正义理论不是从第一原理演绎出来的，而是在判断与原则之间不断调节的产物。

Dewey's philosophical method — rejecting absolute certainty, emphasizing problem-solving and experimental inquiry — influenced Rawls's reflective equilibrium method. Justice theory is not deduced from first principles but emerges from continual adjustment between judgments and principles.

## 关键文本 / Key Passages

> "正义是社会制度的第一美德，正如真理是思想体系的第一美德。一种理论，无论它多么精致和简洁，如果它不真，就必须被拒绝或修正；同样，法律和制度，无论它们多么有效率和有条理，如果它们不正义，就必须被改革或废除。"
>
> "Justice is the first virtue of social institutions, as truth is of systems of thought. A theory however elegant and economical must be rejected or revised if it is untrue; likewise laws and institutions no matter how efficient and well-arranged must be reformed or abolished if they are unjust."
> — *A Theory of Justice* (1971), §1

> "在作为公平的正义中，平等的原初状态对应于传统社会契约理论中的自然状态……这种原初状态当然不是要被认为是一种实际的历史状态……它被理解为一种纯粹假设性的情境。"
>
> "In justice as fairness the original position of equality corresponds to the state of nature in the traditional theory of the social contract... This original position is not, of course, thought of as an actual historical state of affairs... It is understood as a purely hypothetical situation."
> — *A Theory of Justice* (1971), §3

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

- **制度改革评估 / Institutional Reform Assessment** — "作为公平的正义"提供了一个评价社会制度的标准：一个制度是否正义，取决于它是否会被处于公平位置的人所同意。这超越了纯粹的效率评估（GDP增长）和纯粹的平等评估（基尼系数），转向了程序与结果的综合考量。
  "Justice as Fairness" provides a standard for evaluating social institutions: whether an institution is just depends on whether it would be agreed to by persons in a fair position. This transcends purely efficiency-based assessment (GDP growth) and purely equality-based assessment (Gini coefficient), moving toward integrated consideration of procedure and outcomes.

- **宪法设计 / Constitutional Design** — 正义原则的词典序优先性对宪法设计有直接指导意义：基本自由（言论、信仰、集会）不可因经济利益而被牺牲，这在宪法裁判中体现为基本权利的"绝对保护"原则。
  The lexical priority of justice principles has direct implications for constitutional design: basic liberties (speech, conscience, assembly) cannot be sacrificed for economic gain, reflected in constitutional adjudication as the principle of "absolute protection" for fundamental rights.

- **全球正义辩论 / Global Justice Debate** — "作为公平的正义"是否应该全球化？世界主义者（如博格）主张正义原则应适用于全球基本结构；罗尔斯本人则认为国际正义的原则主体是"人民"而非个人。这一争论至今仍是全球伦理学的核心问题。
  Should "Justice as Fairness" be globalized? Cosmopolitans (such as Pogge) argue that justice principles should apply to the global basic structure; Rawls himself held that the subjects of international justice are "peoples" rather than individuals. This debate remains a central question in global ethics.

## 批评与回应 / Critiques and Responses

- **批评 / Critique**（诺齐克 Nozick）：公平的程序不等于公平的结果——如果初始持有正当且转让正当，任何分配模式都是正义的。差别原则要求持续的再分配干预，侵犯了个人财产权。
  Fair procedures do not equal fair outcomes — if initial holdings are just and transfers are just, any distribution pattern is just. The difference principle requires continuous redistributive intervention, violating individual property rights.
  → **回应 / Response**：罗尔斯区分了"纯粹程序的正义"与"背景制度的正义"——差别原则不是逐案干预，而是设定基本结构的规则，在此规则内个人自由交换是允许的。
  Rawls distinguishes "purely procedural justice" from "background institutional justice" — the difference principle is not case-by-case intervention but setting rules for the basic structure within which individual free exchange is permitted.

- **批评 / Critique**（女性主义 Feminist）：作为公平的正义忽视了家庭内部的正义问题——原初状态中的"家长"代表模式掩盖了性别不平等。
  Justice as Fairness ignores justice within the family — the "head of household" representation in the original position obscures gender inequality.
  → **回应 / Response**：后期罗尔斯（《再论公共理性》，1997）明确将家庭纳入社会基本结构，承认性别正义是基本正义问题。
  The later Rawls ("The Idea of Public Reason Revisited," 1997) explicitly incorporated the family into the basic structure of society, acknowledging gender justice as a fundamental justice issue.

- **批评 / Critique**（马克思主义 Marxist）：作为公平的正义仍停留在资本主义分配框架内，未触及生产资料所有制的根本问题。
  Justice as Fairness remains within the capitalist distributional framework without addressing the fundamental question of ownership of the means of production.
  → **回应 / Response**：罗尔斯后期提出的"财产所有民主制"（property-owning democracy）恰恰试图通过广泛的财产分散来防止经济权力的集中。
  Rawls's later proposal of "property-owning democracy" precisely attempts to prevent concentration of economic power through widespread dispersal of property.

## 跨文化回响 / Cross-cultural Echoes

- **儒家"义利之辨" / Confucian "Yi-Li Distinction"** — 儒家将"义"（正当性）置于"利"（功利）之上，与罗尔斯对功利主义的批判在精神上一致——正义不能还原为利益计算。但儒家更强调道德修养与德行，而罗尔斯聚焦于制度设计。
  Confucianism places "yi" (righteousness) above "li" (profit), which is spiritually consistent with Rawls's critique of utilitarianism — justice cannot be reduced to interest calculation. But Confucianism emphasizes moral cultivation and virtue, while Rawls focuses on institutional design.

- **印度"达摩"概念 / Indian "Dharma" Concept** — 达摩（Dharma）包含正义、义务、正当行为的多重含义，与"作为公平的正义"共享"正当性优先于效用"的基本直觉。阿马蒂亚·森正是从印度哲学传统出发对罗尔斯进行批判性对话。
  Dharma encompasses justice, duty, and right conduct, sharing with "Justice as Fairness" the basic intuition that "legitimacy takes priority over utility." Amartya Sen engaged in critical dialogue with Rawls precisely from the Indian philosophical tradition.

- **中国"天下为公" / Chinese "Tianxia Wei Gong"** — "天下为公"的理想——社会资源应为全体人民而非特定群体服务——与罗尔斯的正义原则在目标上高度契合，但中国传统的实现路径更倾向于道德教化而非程序建构。
  The ideal of "Tianxia Wei Gong" (the world belongs to all) — that social resources should serve all people rather than particular groups — aligns closely with Rawlsian justice principles in its goals, but the Chinese traditional path toward realization leans more toward moral education than procedural construction.
