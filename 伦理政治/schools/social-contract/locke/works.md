## 核心著作详述 / Detailed Accounts of Core Works

### 《政府论》 *Two Treatises of Government* (1689)

《政府论》分为上下两篇。**上篇**（First Treatise）逐章反驳罗伯特·菲尔默（Robert Filmer）的《家长制》（*Patriarcha*, 1680），驳斥了君权神授和家长制论证——洛克论证，即使亚当拥有对世界的原始主权，也无法确定其合法继承人，因此菲尔默的整个论证是无效的。**下篇**（Second Treatise）正面阐述了洛克的替代理论：从自然状态、自然权利和财产权出发，经由社会契约和同意，推导出有限政府和革命权。下篇是洛克政治哲学的精华，也是整个西方政治思想史上最重要的文献之一。

The *Two Treatises* comprises two parts. The **First Treatise** systematically refutes Robert Filmer's *Patriarcha* (1680), demolishing the divine right and patriarchalist arguments — Locke demonstrated that even if Adam possessed original sovereignty over the world, his legitimate successors cannot be determined, rendering Filmer's entire argument void. The **Second Treatise** positively articulates Locke's alternative theory: from the state of nature, natural rights, and property, through social contract and consent, to limited government and the right of revolution. The Second Treatise is the essence of Locke's political philosophy and one of the most important documents in the history of Western political thought.

**标准学术版**：Peter Laslett 编辑，Cambridge University Press, 1960（1988年第二版修订）。Laslett 的导论证明了《政府论》实际撰写于1679–1681年间（而非如洛克所声称的是为1688年光荣革命辩护），这一发现根本性地改变了对该书的理解。 / **Standard scholarly edition**: Ed. Peter Laslett, Cambridge University Press, 1960 (2nd ed. revised 1988). Laslett's introduction demonstrated that the *Two Treatises* was actually written in 1679–1681 (not, as Locke claimed, to justify the Glorious Revolution of 1688), a discovery that fundamentally changed understanding of the work.

### 《人类理解论》 *An Essay Concerning Human Understanding* (1689)

这是洛克耗时最长、修改最多的著作——从1671年开始撰写草稿，历经十八年反复修订，于1689年首次出版，此后又出了四个修订版（1694、1695、1700、1706）。全书分为四卷：**第一卷**批判天赋观念论；**第二卷**阐述观念的来源和类型；**第三卷**讨论语言和抽象观念；**第四卷**探讨知识的性质、范围和确定性。《人类理解论》是经验主义认识论的奠基之作，也是现代心灵哲学的先驱。

This was Locke's longest-gestated and most-revised work — begun in draft form in 1671, revised over eighteen years, first published in 1689, with four subsequent revised editions (1694, 1695, 1700, 1706). The work comprises four books: **Book I** critiques innate ideas; **Book II** elaborates the sources and types of ideas; **Book III** discusses language and abstract ideas; **Book IV** explores the nature, extent, and certainty of knowledge. The *Essay* is the foundational work of empiricist epistemology and a precursor of modern philosophy of mind.

**标准学术版**：Peter H. Nidditch 编辑，Oxford: Clarendon Press, 1975。 / **Standard scholarly edition**: Ed. Peter H. Nidditch, Oxford: Clarendon Press, 1975.

### 《论宗教宽容》 *A Letter Concerning Toleration* (1689)

该书最初以拉丁文撰写（*Epistola de Tolerantia*），1685–1686年间在荷兰完成，1689年在荷兰 Gouda 出版拉丁文版，同年由 William Popple 译为英文在伦敦出版。洛克在世时匿名出版。该书以书信形式写成，论证了宗教宽容的三个基础（职能、效力、知识论证），并划定了宽容的边界。此后洛克与牛津学者 Jonas Proast 就宽容问题展开了三轮论战（1690、1692、1704），进一步发展和深化了他的宽容理论。

Originally written in Latin (*Epistola de Tolerantia*), completed in the Netherlands in 1685–1686, published in Latin at Gouda in 1689, and translated into English by William Popple the same year in London. Published anonymously during Locke's lifetime. Written in epistolary form, it argues for toleration on three grounds (jurisdiction, efficacy, epistemology) and delineates toleration's limits. Locke subsequently engaged in three rounds of debate with Oxford scholar Jonas Proast (1690, 1692, 1704), further developing and deepening his toleration theory.
