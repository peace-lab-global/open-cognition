# 马尔库塞著作年表 · Herbert Marcuse Works Bibliography

## 主要著作 / Major Works

| 年份 / Year | 原文标题 / Original Title | 中译 / Chinese Translation | 出版信息 / Publisher | 意义 / Significance |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1922 | *Der deutsche Künstlerroman* | 《德国艺术家小说》 | Freiburg im Breisgau | 博士论文，研究德国艺术家小说中的美学与存在主题 / Doctoral dissertation on aesthetics and existence in the German artist novel |
| 1928 | "Beiträge zu einer Phänomenologie des historischen Materialismus" | 《论历史唯物主义的现象学》 | *Philosophische Hefte* | 早期尝试将海德格尔现象学与马克思主义结合 / Early attempt to synthesize Heidegger's phenomenology with Marxism |
| 1932 | *Hegels Ontologie und die Theorie der Geschichtlichkeit* | 《黑格尔的本体论与历史性理论》 | Frankfurt: Klostermann | 早期最重要的哲学著作，通过黑格尔发展"具体的哲学" / Most important early philosophical work, developing "concrete philosophy" through Hegel |
| 1933 | "Zur Grundlegung des historischen Materialismus" | 《论历史唯物主义的基础》 | *Zeitschrift für Sozialforschung* | 对马克思《1844年经济学哲学手稿》的早期阐释 / Early interpretation of Marx's *Economic and Philosophic Manuscripts of 1844* |
| 1936 | "Der Begriff der Essenz" | 《本质的概念》 | *Zeitschrift für Sozialforschung* | 对本质主义的哲学批判 / Philosophical critique of essentialism |
| 1937 | "The Affirmative Character of Culture" | 《文化的肯定性质》 | *Zeitschrift für Sozialforschung* | 分析文化如何在保存幸福承诺的同时维护不自由 / Analyzes how culture preserves the promise of happiness while maintaining unfreedom |
| 1941 | *Reason and Revolution: Hegel and the Rise of Social Theory* | 《理性与革命：黑格尔与社会理论的兴起》 | New York: Oxford UP | 黑格尔辩证法的社会理论阐释，批判实证主义 / Social-theoretical interpretation of Hegelian dialectics, critique of positivism |
| 1955 | *Eros and Civilization: A Philosophical Inquiry into Freud* | 《爱欲与文明：对弗洛伊德思想的哲学探讨》 | Boston: Beacon Press | 弗洛伊德与马克思的综合，压抑性脱升华理论的奠基之作 / Synthesis of Freud and Marx, foundational work for repressive desublimation theory |
| 1958 | *Soviet Marxism: A Critical Analysis* | 《苏联马克思主义：批判性分析》 | New York: Columbia UP | 对苏联官方意识形态的批判性分析 / Critical analysis of Soviet official ideology |
| 1964 | *One-Dimensional Man: Studies in the Ideology of Advanced Industrial Society* | 《单向度的人：发达工业社会意识形态研究》 | Boston: Beacon Press | 最具影响力的著作，全面阐述单向度社会理论 / Most influential work, comprehensive articulation of one-dimensional society theory |
| 1965 | "Repressive Tolerance" | 《压抑性宽容》 | in *A Critique of Pure Tolerance* (Beacon Press) | 对自由主义宽容概念的激进批判 / Radical critique of the liberal concept of tolerance |
| 1969 | *An Essay on Liberation* | 《解放论》 | Boston: Beacon Press | 解放理论的多维度展开，新感性概念 / Multi-dimensional elaboration of liberation theory, new sensibility concept |
| 1972 | *Counterrevolution and Revolt* | 《反革命与反叛》 | Boston: Beacon Press | 运动退潮后的反思，分析保守力量的反扑 / Reflection after the ebb of movements, analysis of conservative counterattack |
| 1978 | *The Aesthetic Dimension: Toward a Critique of Marxist Aesthetics* | 《审美维度：走向马克思主义美学批判》 | Boston: Beacon Press | 最后一部重要著作，论证审美形式的颠覆力量 / Last major work, arguing for the subversive power of aesthetic form |

## 版本说明 / Edition Notes

马尔库塞著作的主要英文出版商为Boston的Beacon Press，该出版社出版了大部分原始英文版。德文原版由Suhrkamp Verlag（Frankfurt）出版。Routledge出版了六卷本《马尔库塞文集》（Collected Papers），是目前最完整的英文版文集。中译本主要由上海译文出版社和三联书店出版。

Marcuse's major English-language publisher was Beacon Press (Boston), which published most of the original English editions. German originals were published by Suhrkamp Verlag (Frankfurt). Routledge published the six-volume *Collected Papers of Herbert Marcuse*, currently the most complete English-language edition. Chinese translations are primarily published by Shanghai Translation Publishing House and SDX Joint Publishing Company.
