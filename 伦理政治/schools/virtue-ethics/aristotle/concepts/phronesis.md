# 实践智慧 · Phronesis

## 概念概述 / Concept Overview

实践智慧（phronēsis, φρόνησις）是亚里士多德伦理学中最核心的理智美德，指一种关于**在具体、可变的情境中如何正确行动**的卓越判断力。它不同于理论知识（epistēmē），后者关注永恒不变的普遍真理；也不同于技艺（technē），后者关注制造或生产某物。实践智慧关注的是**行动本身**——如何在此时此地、对此人此事做出正确的道德判断和选择。

Practical wisdom (phronēsis, φρόνησις) is the most central intellectual virtue in Aristotle's ethics, referring to an excellent capacity for **judging how to act rightly in concrete, variable situations**. It differs from theoretical knowledge (epistēmē), which concerns eternal universal truths; and from craft (technē), which concerns making or producing something. Practical wisdom concerns **action itself** — how to make correct moral judgments and choices here and now, for this person and this matter.

亚里士多德认为，实践智慧不能通过教科书或抽象规则来教授——它只能通过**经验**和**美德品格**的共同培养而逐渐获得。一个年轻人可以是出色的数学家（理论知识），但不可能具有实践智慧，因为实践智慧需要对生活特殊情况的熟悉。实践智慧是美德品格的"眼睛"——没有它，所谓的"美德"只是盲目的冲动；没有美德品格，实践智慧也无从谈起——因为邪恶的欲望会扭曲人对"善"的感知。

Aristotle holds that practical wisdom cannot be taught through textbooks or abstract rules — it can only be gradually acquired through **experience** and the **cultivation of virtuous character** together. A young person can be an excellent mathematician (theoretical knowledge) but cannot have practical wisdom, because practical wisdom requires familiarity with life's particularities. Practical wisdom is the "eye" of virtuous character — without it, so-called "virtue" is merely blind impulse; without virtuous character, practical wisdom is impossible — because vicious desires distort one's perception of "the good."

## 理论内涵 / Theoretical Content

### 五种理智状态 / The Five Intellectual States

亚里士多德在《尼各马可伦理学》第六卷区分了灵魂达到真理的五种状态：

Aristotle distinguishes five states by which the soul attains truth in *Nicomachean Ethics* VI:

| 类型 / Type | 对象 / Object | 特征 / Feature |
|---|---|---|
| 技艺 (technē) | 可制造之物 / Things to be made | 带有正确理性的生产品质 / Productive quality with right reason |
| 理论知识 (epistēmē) | 不变之必然之物 / Unchanging necessities | 可教授、可演绎证明 / Teachable, demonstrable |
| 实践智慧 (phronēsis) | 可变之行动之事 / Variable matters of action | 关于人类善的审慎判断能力 / Deliberative capacity about human goods |
| 直觉理性 (nous) | 第一原理 / First principles | 对不可证明之起点的直接把握 / Direct grasp of indemonstrable starting points |
| 智慧 (sophia) | 最高贵之物 / Most honorable things | nous + epistēmē 的结合 / Combination of nous + epistēmē |

实践智慧位于这一谱系的独特位置：它处理的是**可变的人类事务**，而非不变的数学或形而上学真理。正因如此，它不能像数学那样被精确地教授，而需要在实践中磨练。

Practical wisdom occupies a unique position in this spectrum: it deals with **variable human affairs**, not unchanging mathematical or metaphysical truths. For this reason, it cannot be taught precisely like mathematics but must be honed through practice.

### 实践智慧与美德的互依性 / Interdependence of Phronesis and Virtue

亚里士多德提出了一个著名的互依性命题：**没有实践智慧就没有真正的美德，没有美德就没有实践智慧**（EN VI.12–13）。这看似循环，但亚里士多德的深意在于：

Aristotle proposes a famous interdependence thesis: **without practical wisdom there is no true virtue, and without virtue there is no practical wisdom** (EN VI.12–13). This seems circular, but Aristotle's deeper point is:

- **自然美德**（natural virtue）——如天生勇敢或天生善良的气质——如果没有实践智慧的指引，可能走向极端甚至有害。天生勇敢者可能变为鲁莽者。/ **Natural virtue** — such as innate courage or innate kindness — without practical wisdom's guidance, may go to extremes or even become harmful. The naturally brave may become reckless.

- **实践智慧**本身预设了正确的目的——即美德品格所确立的对善的正确感受。一个品格败坏的人即使具有精明的计算能力，那也不是实践智慧，而是**狡诈**（deinotēs）。/ **Practical wisdom** itself presupposes correct ends — the right feelings about the good established by virtuous character. A person of corrupt character who has shrewd calculative ability does not have practical wisdom but **cleverness** (deinotēs).

### 审慎判断的过程 / The Process of Deliberation

实践智慧的核心运作是**审慎判断**（bouleusis）——在面对可变的人类事务时，权衡各种因素以找到最佳行动方案的过程。审慎判断不是从普遍原则到特殊情况的简单演绎（那是理论知识的应用），而是一种**在特殊中感知普遍**的能力——在具体情境中看出什么是公正的、勇敢的或节制的。亚里士多德将这种感知能力比作"最后的直觉"（nous tes eschatou）——在经过充分的审慎思考后，行动者直接"看到"了正确的行动方案。

The core operation of practical wisdom is **deliberation** (bouleusis) — the process of weighing various factors to find the best course of action when facing variable human affairs. Deliberation is not simple deduction from universal principles to particular cases (that is the application of theoretical knowledge) but a capacity for **perceiving the universal in the particular** — seeing what is just, courageous, or temperate in a concrete situation. Aristotle compares this perceptual capacity to "ultimate intuition" (nous tes eschatou) — after sufficient deliberation, the agent directly "sees" the right course of action.

## 思想史脉络 / Intellectual History

实践智慧的概念在哲学史上经历了复杂的命运。在亚里士多德之后，斯多葛学派虽然继承了部分实践智慧思想，但更强调按照普遍理性法则行动。中世纪的阿奎那将 phronēsis 翻译为拉丁语 prudentia（审慎），并将其列为四大枢德（cardinal virtues）之首——但 prudential 在现代英语中已退化为"小心翼翼的"含义，丢失了亚里士多德赋予的丰富内涵。

The concept of practical wisdom had a complex fate in philosophical history. After Aristotle, the Stoics inherited some practical wisdom thought but emphasized acting according to universal rational laws. Medieval Aquinas translated phronēsis as Latin prudentia (prudence), placing it first among the four cardinal virtues — but "prudential" in modern English has degraded to mean "cautious," losing Aristotle's rich meaning.

康德伦理学从根本上排斥了实践智慧的角色——道德行动应该由普遍法则（绝对命令）来指导，而非依赖情境性的判断。功利主义同样以"最大化幸福"的计算取代了实践智慧的判断。直到20世纪，伽达默尔（Gadamer）在诠释学中重新发现了 phronēsis 作为理解模式的重要性，而美德伦理学复兴运动则使实践智慧重新成为伦理学的核心概念。

Kantian ethics fundamentally excluded practical wisdom's role — moral action should be guided by universal law (categorical imperative), not situational judgment. Utilitarianism similarly replaced practical wisdom's judgment with "maximize happiness" calculations. Not until the 20th century did Gadamer rediscover phronēsis as a mode of understanding in hermeneutics, while the virtue ethics revival made practical wisdom a central ethical concept again.

## 关键文本 / Key Passages

> "实践智慧是关于人类善的、带有正确理性的、真实的品质状态，它在行动领域运作。"
> — 《尼各马可伦理学》VI.5, 1140b20–21

> "Practical wisdom is a true and reasoned state of capacity to act with regard to the human good, operating in the sphere of action."
> — *Nicomachean Ethics* VI.5, 1140b20–21

> "实践智慧不只关乎普遍之物，它还必须认识特殊之物；因为它是实践的，而实践关乎特殊之物。"
> — 《尼各马可伦理学》VI.7, 1141b14–16

> "Practical wisdom is not concerned with universals only; it must also recognize particulars; for it is practical, and practice concerns particulars."
> — *Nicomachean Ethics* VI.7, 1141b14–16

> "年轻人可以成为数学家或几何学家……但他们似乎不能成为具有实践智慧的人。原因在于实践智慧也关乎特殊之物，而特殊之物需要通过经验来熟悉，年轻人缺乏经验。"
> — 《尼各马可伦理学》VI.8, 1142a11–16

> "Young men can become mathematicians or geometers... but they cannot seemingly become practically wise. The reason is that practical wisdom is concerned with particulars as well, and particulars become familiar through experience, which a young man lacks."
> — *Nicomachean Ethics* VI.8, 1142a11–16

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

**临床伦理**: 现代医学伦理学越来越认识到，好的医疗决策不能仅靠抽象的伦理原则（如自主性、不伤害），还需要医生在具体情境中运用实践智慧——权衡患者的特殊需求、家庭背景、文化因素等。/ **Clinical Ethics**: Modern medical ethics increasingly recognizes that good medical decisions cannot rely solely on abstract principles (autonomy, non-maleficence) but require physicians to exercise practical wisdom — weighing patients' particular needs, family backgrounds, and cultural factors.

**法学**: 亚里士多德的 phronesis 概念为司法裁量权提供了哲学基础——法律条文无法预见所有情况，法官需要在具体案件中运用实践智慧来实现正义。这正是亚里士多德"衡平法"（epieikeia）概念的核心。/ **Law**: Aristotle's phronesis provides the philosophical basis for judicial discretion — legal codes cannot foresee all situations; judges need practical wisdom to achieve justice in particular cases. This is the core of Aristotle's concept of equity (epieikeia).

**教育**: 教师的专业判断（professional judgment）本质上是一种实践智慧——教学方法不能机械套用，而需要根据学生、课堂、文化等具体因素灵活调整。/ **Education**: Teachers' professional judgment is essentially practical wisdom — teaching methods cannot be mechanically applied but must flexibly adapt to students, classrooms, and cultural factors.

**人工智能伦理**: phronesis 的核心争论之一是——AI系统能否具有实践智慧？如果实践智慧本质上依赖于具身经验和情感感知，那么纯粹基于算法的系统可能永远无法取代人类的道德判断。/ **AI Ethics**: A core debate about phronesis is whether AI systems can possess practical wisdom. If practical wisdom essentially depends on embodied experience and emotional perception, purely algorithmic systems may never replace human moral judgment.

## 批评与回应 / Critiques and Responses

**批评1：非理性主义** / **Critique 1: Irrationalism**
实践智慧不可还原为规则——这是否意味着亚里士多德为道德非理性主义开了后门？如果道德判断不能被理性地论证，它如何避免成为主观直觉？/ That practical wisdom is irreducible to rules — does this mean Aristotle opened the door to moral irrationalism? If moral judgment cannot be rationally argued, how does it avoid becoming subjective intuition?

**回应**: 实践智慧并非非理性的——它涉及推理和论证，只是这种推理不是演绎式的，而是**实践推理**（practical reasoning），需要考虑多种可变因素。亚里士多德明确区分了实践智慧和纯粹的直觉或预感。/ **Response**: Practical wisdom is not irrational — it involves reasoning and argumentation, just not deductive reasoning but **practical reasoning** that considers multiple variable factors. Aristotle explicitly distinguishes practical wisdom from mere intuition or hunches.

**批评2：精英主义** / **Critique 2: Elitism**
如果实践智慧只能通过长期的经验和美德培养获得，这是否意味着大多数人无法做出正确的道德判断？/ If practical wisdom requires long experience and virtue cultivation, does this mean most people cannot make correct moral judgments?

**回应**: 亚里士多德确实认为完全的实践智慧是罕见的，但他也承认大多数人可以通过听从有实践智慧者的建议来做出正确行动。此外，实践智慧是一个**程度性**概念——人们可以在不同程度上发展这一能力。/ **Response**: Aristotle does hold that complete practical wisdom is rare, but he acknowledges most people can act correctly by following the advice of practically wise persons. Moreover, practical wisdom is a **graduated** concept — people can develop this capacity to varying degrees.

## 跨文化回响 / Cross-cultural Echoes

儒家传统中的"**权**"（quán）概念与 phronesis 高度相似。孔子说："可与共学，未可与适道；可与适道，未可与立；可与立，未可与权。"（《论语·子罕》）——"权"（权衡变通）是道德修养的最高阶段，超越了规则遵守。孟子同样强调"权"——"男女授受不亲，礼也；嫂溺，援之以手者，权也。"在紧急情况下，必须超越常规礼仪做出判断。

The Confucian concept of "**quan**" (quán, weighing/adapting) closely parallels phronesis. Confucius said: "One may study together with someone, yet not proceed with them to the Way; one may proceed with them to the Way, yet not take a stand with them; one may take a stand with them, yet not exercise quan with them" (*Analects* 9.30) — quan is the highest stage of moral cultivation, beyond rule-following. Mencius similarly emphasizes quan: "That men and women should not touch in giving and receiving is a rule of propriety; but if your sister-in-law is drowning, you must rescue her with your hand — this is quan."

佛教中的"方便"（upaya）概念也与实践智慧有结构性相似——在具体情境中灵活运用教义以利益众生，而非僵化地遵守戒律条文。

The Buddhist concept of "skillful means" (upaya) also shares structural similarities with practical wisdom — flexibly applying teachings in concrete situations to benefit beings, rather than rigidly adhering to precept formulations.
