# 亚里士多德著作总览 · Aristotle: Complete Works Bibliography

## 总览 / Overview

亚里士多德的著作涵盖几乎所有已知的知识领域，是古代世界最系统、最广泛的思想遗产。流传至今的著作大多为其在吕克昂学园（Lyceum）的讲义或讲稿（esoteric writings / akroamatika），而非面向公众出版的对话录（exoteric writings，已全部散佚，仅有断片留存）。现存著作约有三十余种，其编辑和传承本身就构成了一段引人入胜的思想史。

Aristotle's works span virtually all known fields of knowledge, constituting the most systematic and comprehensive intellectual legacy of the ancient world. The surviving works are mostly lecture notes from the Lyceum (esoteric writings / akroamatika), rather than published dialogues (exoteric writings, entirely lost except for fragments). Approximately thirty works survive, whose editing and transmission constitute a fascinating intellectual history in themselves.

## 二、《尼各马可伦理学》详细结构 / Detailed Structure of the *Nicomachean Ethics*

### 第一卷：幸福 / Book I: Happiness
- 第1章：一切行动都指向某种善（1094a–1095a）/ Ch.1: All actions aim at some good
- 第7章：**功能论证**（ergon argument）— 人的功能与人的善（1097b–1098a）/ Ch.7: The **ergon argument** — human function and human good

### 第二卷：德性一般 / Book II: Virtue in General
- 第1章：德性的习得——习惯化（ethismos）/ Ch.1: Acquisition of virtue — habituation
- 第2–6章：德性作为中道（mesotes）/ Ch.2–6: Virtue as the mean
- 第7–9章：具体德性的初步讨论 / Ch.7–9: Preliminary discussion of specific virtues

### 第三卷：自愿与选择 / Book III: Voluntariness and Choice
- 第1–5章：自愿（hekousion）、选择（prohairesis）与责任 / Ch.1–5: Voluntary action, choice, and responsibility
- 第6–12章：具体德性（勇敢 andreia、节制 sophrosyne）/ Ch.6–12: Specific virtues (courage, temperance)

### 第四卷：品格德性续论 / Book IV: Character Virtues Continued
- 慷慨（eleutheriotes）、大方（megaloprepeia）、大度（megalopsychia）、温和（praotes）等 / Generosity, magnificence, magnanimity, mildness, etc.

### 第五卷：正义 / Book V: Justice
- 分配正义（dianemetikon）、矫正正义（diorthotikon）、政治正义 / Distributive, corrective, and political justice

### 第六卷：理智德性 / Book VI: Intellectual Virtues
- 第1–7章：五种理智状态（技艺、理论知识、实践智慧、直觉理性、智慧）/ Ch.1–7: Five intellectual states
- 第8–13章：**实践智慧**（phronesis）的核心论述 / Ch.8–13: Core discussion of **practical wisdom**

### 第七卷：自制与快乐 / Book VII: Self-Control and Pleasure
- 意志软弱（akrasia）问题 — 人为何明知故犯 / The problem of weakness of will — why people act against better judgment

### 第八–九卷：友谊 / Books VIII–IX: Friendship
- 三种友谊：基于功利、快乐和美德 / Three types of friendship: utility, pleasure, and virtue
- 完美友谊（teleia philia）——基于美德的友谊 / Perfect friendship (teleia philia) — friendship based on virtue

### 第十卷：快乐与幸福 / Book X: Pleasure and Happiness
- 快乐（hedone）的本质和地位 / The nature and role of pleasure
- 沉思生活（theoria）作为最高幸福 / Contemplative life (theoria) as highest happiness

## 七、已散佚的著作 / Lost Works

亚里士多德的公开对话录（exoteric writings）已全部散佚，仅存断片。古代作家（如西塞罗）称赞其文风优美，与现存讲义的简练风格形成鲜明对比。

Aristotle's published dialogues (exoteric writings) are entirely lost, surviving only in fragments. Ancient writers (like Cicero) praised their literary elegance, contrasting sharply with the concise style of surviving lecture notes.

| 书名 / Title | 主题 / Subject |
|---|---|

## 九、著作传承史 / Transmission History

1. **吕克昂时期**（前335–前322）：讲义在学园内部使用 / Lyceum period: lecture notes used internally
2. **塞普西斯时期**：亚里士多德的藏书和手稿传给学生塞奥弗拉斯托斯，后转至涅琉斯（Neleus）/ Scepsis period: library and manuscripts passed to Theophrastus, then to Neleus
3. **地下埋藏**（约前1世纪前）：据传手稿在塞普西斯被埋藏于地下以防帕加马国王掠夺 / Buried: manuscripts allegedly buried in Scepsis to protect from Pergamene kings
4. **安德罗尼库斯编辑**（约前40年）：罗德岛的安德罗尼库斯（Andronicus of Rhodes）首次系统编辑亚里士多德著作，确立了现存著作的基本顺序 / Andronicus edition (c. 40 BCE): first systematic editing, establishing the basic order of surviving works
5. **阿拉伯翻译运动**（8–10世纪）：亚里士多德著作被大量翻译为阿拉伯文，深刻影响了伊斯兰哲学 / Arabic translation movement (8th–10th centuries): massive translation into Arabic, profoundly influencing Islamic philosophy
6. **拉丁翻译与经院哲学**（12–13世纪）：通过阿拉伯文转译为拉丁文，成为经院哲学（尤其是阿奎那）的核心文本 / Latin translations and scholasticism (12th–13th centuries): translated via Arabic to Latin, becoming core texts for scholastic philosophy (especially Aquinas)
