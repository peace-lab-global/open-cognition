# 自我所有权 · Self-Ownership

## 概念概述 / Concept Overview

自我所有权（Self-Ownership）是诺齐克整个政治哲学体系的形而上学基石。这一原则主张：每个人对自己的身体、才能、劳动及其成果拥有完整且排他性的所有权。正如一个人可以正当地拥有外部物品，一个人首先正当地拥有自己——自己是自己的主人，不是任何他人或集体的财产。

Self-Ownership is the metaphysical cornerstone of Nozick's entire political philosophy. This principle holds that each person has complete and exclusive ownership of their own body, talents, labor, and the fruits thereof. Just as a person may legitimately own external objects, a person first and foremost legitimately owns themselves — one is one's own master, not the property of any other person or collective.

自我所有权原则的规范性力量在于它为诺齐克的其他核心主张提供了统一的道德基础：（1）它解释了为什么个人权利是对行为的边际约束——因为你拥有自己，他人不能把你当作工具来使用；（2）它解释了为什么强制性再分配在道德上等同于强迫劳动——因为拿走你的劳动成果就是部分地拥有了你；（3）它为财产权的洛克式理论提供了前提——因为你拥有自己的劳动，当你的劳动与无主物混合时，你就获得了对该物的财产权。

The normative power of self-ownership lies in its providing a unified moral foundation for Nozick's other core claims: (1) it explains why individual rights are side constraints on action — because you own yourself, others cannot use you as a means; (2) it explains why coercive redistribution is morally equivalent to forced labor — because taking the fruits of your labor is partially owning you; (3) it provides the premise for the Lockean theory of property rights — because you own your labor, when your labor mixes with unowned things, you acquire property rights over them.

## 理论内涵 / Theoretical Content

### 自我所有权的含义 / The Meaning of Self-Ownership

自我所有权并非一个单一的权利，而是一束权利（a bundle of rights），包括：（a）对自己身体的控制权——决定如何使用自己的身体；（b）排除他人使用的权利——他人未经同意不得使用你的身体或劳动；（c）转让权——你可以通过自愿协议转让对自己身体或劳动的使用权；（d）对劳动成果的所有权——你拥有自己劳动所创造的一切。

Self-ownership is not a single right but a bundle of rights, including: (a) control rights over one's body — deciding how to use one's body; (b) the right to exclude others — no one may use your body or labor without your consent; (c) transfer rights — you may transfer rights to use your body or labor through voluntary agreement; (d) ownership of the fruits of labor — you own everything your labor creates.

### 与康德式尊严的关系 / The Relationship to Kantian Dignity

诺齐克将自我所有权与康德"人是目的"（persons as ends）的道德原则联系起来。他认为，尊重一个人的自我所有权就是尊重他作为自主的理性存在的尊严。将一个人当作可以随意使用的资源来对待，就是将他仅仅当作手段，而非当作目的本身。这一康德式的论证为自我所有权提供了比纯粹的利益计算更深层的道德基础。

Nozick connects self-ownership with Kant's principle that persons must be treated as "ends in themselves." He argues that respecting a person's self-ownership is respecting their dignity as an autonomous rational being. Treating a person as a resource to be used at will is treating them merely as a means, not as an end. This Kantian argument provides a deeper moral foundation for self-ownership than mere interest calculation.

### 税收即强迫劳动 / Taxation as Forced Labor

诺齐克最具争议性的论断之一是：通过所得税对劳动成果进行强制征收，在道德上等同于强迫劳动。如果一个人40%的劳动时间所创造的财富被税收拿走，那么他在这40%的时间里实际上是在为他人工作——这与强迫劳动没有本质区别。诺齐克由此论证，福利国家通过税收进行的再分配是对自我所有权的系统性侵犯。

One of Nozick's most controversial claims is that compulsory expropriation of the fruits of labor through income tax is morally equivalent to forced labor. If 40% of the wealth created by a person's labor time is taken through taxation, then during that 40% of their time they are effectively working for others — which is not essentially different from forced labor. Nozick argues from this that welfare-state redistribution through taxation is a systematic violation of self-ownership.

## 思想史脉络 / Intellectual History

自我所有权概念的思想史根源深植于西方自由主义传统。约翰·洛克在《政府论》中写道："每一个人对他自己的人身享有一种所有权，除他以外任何人都没有这种权利。"洛克的这一断言被视为自我所有权原则的经典表述。

The intellectual roots of self-ownership are deeply embedded in the Western liberal tradition. John Locke wrote in the *Second Treatise*: "Every man has a property in his own person. This nobody has any right to but himself." This assertion is regarded as the classical formulation of the self-ownership principle.

然而，自我所有权概念的历史更为复杂。在17世纪的英国，自我所有权的概念与反对奴隶制的论争密切相关——如果一个人拥有自己，那么他就不能被他人正当地拥有。这一反奴隶制的论证后来成为废奴运动的重要思想资源。有趣的是，马克思在某些语境中也接受了自我所有权的前提——他论证资本家剥削工人就是不正当地占有了工人的劳动成果，这恰恰以工人拥有自己的劳动为前提。

However, the history of self-ownership is more complex. In 17th-century England, the concept was closely tied to anti-slavery arguments — if a person owns themselves, they cannot be legitimately owned by another. This anti-slavery argument later became an important intellectual resource for the abolitionist movement. Interestingly, Marx in some contexts accepted the premise of self-ownership — his argument that capitalists exploit workers by unjustly appropriating the fruits of their labor presupposes that workers own their own labor.

G.A. 柯亨在《自我所有权、自由与平等》（1995）中对自我所有权进行了最系统的马克思主义批判。柯亨指出，自我所有权原则虽然看似有利于自由，但实际上可能导致严重的不平等——因为如果每个人都完全拥有自己的才能，那么天赋更高或运气更好的人就有权获得不成比例的回报。

G.A. Cohen's *Self-Ownership, Freedom, and Equality* (1995) offered the most systematic Marxist critique of self-ownership. Cohen argued that while the self-ownership principle appears to favor freedom, it actually may lead to severe inequality — because if each person fully owns their talents, those with greater talent or better luck are entitled to disproportionate rewards.

## 关键文本 / Key Passages

> "个人拥有权利，有些事情是任何他人或群体都不能对他做的。这些权利是如此强大和深远，以至于它们决定了国家及其官员可以做什么的范围。"
> "Individuals have rights, and there are things no person or group may do to them. So strong and far-reaching are these rights that they raise the question of what, if anything, the state and its officials may do."

这段引文虽然没有直接使用"自我所有权"这一术语，但其背后的逻辑正是自我所有权原则的体现——个人权利的不可侵犯性根植于每个人对自己的所有权。

Though this passage does not use the term "self-ownership" directly, the underlying logic is precisely the self-ownership principle — the inviolability of individual rights is rooted in each person's ownership of themselves.

> "对劳动的征税等于强迫人们为他人工作。…… seizure of results of labor is institutionalized seizure of some hours of persons' labor."
> "Taxation of earnings from labor is on a par with forced labor. ... Seizure of results of labor is institutionalized seizure of some hours of persons' labor."

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

自我所有权原则在当代生物伦理辩论中具有重要应用。如果个人完全拥有自己的身体，那么：个人是否有权出售自己的器官？是否有权选择安乐死？是否有权使用任何药物？这些问题的回答取决于对自我所有权范围的不同理解。

The self-ownership principle has important applications in contemporary bioethical debates. If individuals fully own their bodies, then: do individuals have the right to sell their organs? The right to choose euthanasia? The right to use any drug? Answers to these questions depend on different understandings of the scope of self-ownership.

在女权主义哲学中，自我所有权概念引发了复杂而重要的讨论。身体的自我所有权是否为堕胎权提供了基础？一些女权主义哲学家论证，女性对自己身体的所有权是生育自主权的道德基础；另一些则质疑自我所有权概念是否足以涵盖怀孕和生殖的复杂性。

In feminist philosophy, self-ownership has sparked complex and important discussions. Does bodily self-ownership provide a foundation for abortion rights? Some feminist philosophers argue that women's ownership of their own bodies is the moral basis for reproductive autonomy; others question whether the concept of self-ownership can adequately encompass the complexities of pregnancy and reproduction.

## 批评与回应 / Critiques and Responses

**批评一：自我所有权的形而上学基础 / The Metaphysical Basis of Self-Ownership**
自我所有权预设了一个独立的、界限分明的"自我"概念。佛教的"无我"（anatta）教义和后现代的身份理论都质疑这一预设。如果"自我"不是一个固定的实体，而是一个不断流动和建构的过程，那么"自我所有权"的概念就失去了稳固的基础。
Self-ownership presupposes an independent, clearly bounded concept of "self." Buddhism's *anatta* doctrine and postmodern identity theories challenge this presupposition. If the "self" is not a fixed entity but a constantly flowing and constructed process, the concept of "self-ownership" loses its stable foundation.

**批评二：天赋的道德任意性 / The Moral Arbitrariness of Talents**
罗尔斯论证，个人的天赋才能——智力、体力、创造力——在道德上是任意的（morally arbitrary），是"自然彩票"的产物，而非个人应得的。如果才能本身不应被视为个人"拥有"的东西，那么从才能中获得的成果也不应当完全归个人所有。
Rawls argues that personal talents — intelligence, physical strength, creativity — are morally arbitrary, products of the "natural lottery," not things a person deserves. If talents themselves should not be considered things a person "owns," then the fruits of those talents should not belong entirely to the individual.

**批评三：社会合作的贡献 / The Contribution of Social Cooperation**
个人的才能发展和劳动成果总是依赖于社会合作——教育、基础设施、法律制度和文化传统。自我所有权将劳动成果完全归属于个人，忽视了社会对才能发展和价值创造的根本性贡献。
The development of personal talents and the fruits of labor always depend on social cooperation — education, infrastructure, legal systems, and cultural traditions. Self-ownership attributes the fruits of labor entirely to the individual, neglecting society's fundamental contribution to talent development and value creation.

## 跨文化回响 / Cross-cultural Echoes

在儒家传统中，个人的身体被视为父母的馈赠——"身体发肤，受之父母，不敢毁伤"（《孝经》）。这意味着个人对自己身体的"所有权"并非绝对的，而是受到孝道义务的约束。这种关系性的身体观与诺齐克原子式的自我所有权形成了根本对比。

In the Confucian tradition, one's body is regarded as a gift from one's parents — "our bodies — to every hair and bit of skin — are received from our parents, and we must not presume to injure or wound them" (*Classic of Filial Piety*). This means that personal "ownership" of one's body is not absolute but constrained by filial obligations. This relational view of the body stands in fundamental contrast to Nozick's atomistic self-ownership.

在印度教传统中，"阿特曼"（atman，真我）概念提供了一种不同的自我理解：真正的"自我"不是身体或心智，而是永恒不变的灵魂。身体只是灵魂暂时的居所，而非个人"拥有"的财产。这种灵性的自我观为身体自主的概念提供了一个截然不同的形而上学背景。

In Hindu tradition, the concept of *atman* (true self) offers a different self-understanding: the true "self" is not the body or mind but the eternal, unchanging soul. The body is merely a temporary dwelling of the soul, not property that one "owns." This spiritual view of the self provides a radically different metaphysical backdrop for the concept of bodily autonomy.
