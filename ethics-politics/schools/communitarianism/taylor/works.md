## 著作版本与翻译 / Editions and Translations

泰勒的著作被翻译成超过25种语言。其中最重要的翻译包括：德语（Suhrkamp出版社系列）、法语（Cerf/PUF出版社系列）、日语（岩波书店系列）。值得注意的是，*Sources of the Self* 和 *A Secular Age* 各有多个语言的多版次翻译，反映了这两部著作的全球影响力。

Taylor's works have been translated into more than 25 languages. The most significant translation series include: German (Suhrkamp), French (Cerf/PUF), and Japanese (Iwanami Shoten). Notably, *Sources of the Self* and *A Secular Age* each have multiple editions in several languages, reflecting their global influence.

## 著作主题分组 / Thematic Grouping of Works

### 行动哲学与解释学 / Philosophy of Action and Hermeneutics
泰勒的早期著作（1964–1985）主要关注行动哲学和解释学方法论。这一时期的核心著作包括 *The Explanation of Behaviour*（1964）和两卷本 *Philosophical Papers*（1985）。这些著作确立了泰勒作为解释学传统在英语世界代言人的地位，同时也展示了他对自然科学主义方法论的持续批判。Taylor's early works (1964–1985) primarily focus on philosophy of action and hermeneutic methodology. Core works of this period include *The Explanation of Behaviour* (1964) and the two-volume *Philosophical Papers* (1985), establishing Taylor as a spokesman for the hermeneutic tradition in the Anglophone world while demonstrating his sustained critique of naturalistic methodology.

### 道德哲学与自我认同 / Moral Philosophy and Self-Identity
从 *Sources of the Self*（1989）到 *The Ethics of Authenticity*（1992），泰勒的道德哲学著作构成了其思想体系的核心。这些著作追溯了现代自我认同的道德谱系，发展了"强评价"和"道德框架"等关键概念。From *Sources of the Self* (1989) to *The Ethics of Authenticity* (1992), Taylor's moral philosophy works form the core of his thought system, tracing the moral genealogy of modern self-identity and developing key concepts such as "strong evaluation" and "moral frameworks."

### 政治理论与多元文化主义 / Political Theory and Multiculturalism
泰勒的政治理论著作以"承认的政治"为中心，涵盖 *Multiculturalism*（1994）和大量论文。这些著作直接影响了当代认同政治和多元文化主义的理论讨论。Taylor's political theory works center on the "politics of recognition," encompassing *Multiculturalism* (1994) and numerous essays that directly influenced contemporary identity politics and multiculturalism discussions.

### 世俗化与宗教研究 / Secularization and Religious Studies
从 *A Catholic Modernity?*（1999）到 *A Secular Age*（2007），泰勒的宗教研究著作重塑了世俗化理论的范式。*A Secular Age* 尤其被视为21世纪宗教哲学的里程碑式著作。From *A Catholic Modernity?* (1999) to *A Secular Age* (2007), Taylor's religious studies works reshaped the secularization theory paradigm, with *A Secular Age* in particular regarded as a milestone in 21st-century philosophy of religion.

### 语言哲学与表达主义 / Philosophy of Language and Expressivism
*The Language Animal*（2016）和 *Cosmic Connections*（2020）标志着泰勒回归其哲学起点。这两部著作系统发展了构成性语言观和表达主义美学理论，将泰勒的思想体系推向更为完整的形态。*The Language Animal* (2016) and *Cosmic Connections* (2020) mark Taylor's return to his philosophical origins, systematically developing constitutive language theory and expressivist aesthetic theory, bringing Taylor's thought system to a more complete form.

## 著作接受史 / Reception History

泰勒著作的接受经历了几个明显的阶段。1960–70年代，泰勒主要被视为黑格尔研究者和行动哲学家，在英美哲学界的知名度有限。1980年代，随着"自由主义 vs 社群主义"论争的兴起，泰勒成为社群主义阵营的核心人物，其政治哲学论文获得了广泛关注。1989年 *Sources of the Self* 的出版使泰勒确立了道德哲学巨匠的地位。2007年 *A Secular Age* 的出版则将泰勒的影响扩展到宗教学、社会学和文化研究领域。值得注意的是，泰勒的著作在欧陆哲学界的接受相对较晚——其著作的德语和法语翻译大多在原著出版十年后才出现。

Taylor's reception has gone through several distinct phases. In the 1960s–70s, Taylor was primarily seen as a Hegel scholar and philosopher of action with limited visibility in Anglophone philosophy. In the 1980s, with the rise of the "liberalism vs communitarianism" debate, Taylor became a central figure in the communitarian camp, with his political philosophy essays receiving broad attention. The 1989 publication of *Sources of the Self* established Taylor as a major moral philosopher. The 2007 publication of *A Secular Age* extended Taylor's influence into religious studies, sociology, and cultural studies. Notably, Taylor's reception in Continental philosophy was relatively delayed — German and French translations of his works mostly appeared a decade or more after the originals.
