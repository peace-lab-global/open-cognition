# 自我的根源 · Sources of the Self

## 概念概述 / Concept Overview

"自我的根源"既是泰勒1989年出版的巨著《自我的根源：现代认同的形成》（*Sources of the Self: The Making of the Modern Identity*）的书名，也是泰勒道德哲学的核心概念。泰勒论证，现代西方人的自我认同不是凭空产生的，而是由三个深层的道德来源（moral sources）共同塑造的：（1）基督教的内在化（Christian inwardness），从奥古斯丁的"内在的人"（homo interior）到路德的"因信称义"；（2）笛卡尔的分解式自我（Cartesian punctual self），即理性主体从世界中抽离的能力；（3）浪漫主义的表达主义（Romantic expressivism），即通过创造性表达来揭示和实现自我的冲动。

"Sources of the Self" is both the title of Taylor's 1989 magnum opus (*Sources of the Self: The Making of the Modern Identity*) and the core concept of his moral philosophy. Taylor argues that modern Western self-identity is not produced from nothing but is shaped by three deep moral sources: (1) Christian inwardness, from Augustine's "inner man" (homo interior) to Luther's "justification by faith"; (2) the Cartesian punctual self, the capacity of the rational subject to disengage from the world; (3) Romantic expressivism, the impulse to reveal and realize the self through creative expression.

这三个来源并非相互排斥，而是以复杂的张力共存于现代自我之中——现代人的道德生活正是在这三种力量的交互作用中展开。

These three sources are not mutually exclusive but coexist in complex tension within the modern self — modern moral life unfolds precisely in the interaction of these three forces.

## 理论内涵 / Theoretical Content

### 方法论：道德谱系学 / Methodology: Moral Genealogy

泰勒的方法论是一种"道德谱系学"（moral genealogy）——不同于尼采式的解构性谱系，泰勒的谱系学旨在揭示现代道德承诺的深层来源，而非揭露其虚伪。泰勒追问的不是"现代人应该怎么做"（规范伦理学），而是"现代人的道德承诺从何而来"（道德考古学）。

Taylor's methodology is a "moral genealogy" — unlike Nietzschean deconstructive genealogy, Taylor's genealogy aims to reveal the deep sources of modern moral commitments, not to expose their hypocrisy. Taylor asks not "what should modern people do" (normative ethics) but "where do modern moral commitments come from" (moral archaeology).

这种方法与麦金太尔（MacIntyre）的《追寻美德》（*After Virtue*）有相似之处——两者都试图通过思想史来理解当代道德困境。但泰勒的谱系学更为同情式（sympathetic）——他不是在宣布现代道德的破产，而是在为现代道德的深层资源做辩护。

This approach parallels MacIntyre's *After Virtue* — both seek to understand contemporary moral dilemmas through intellectual history. But Taylor's genealogy is more sympathetic — he is not declaring modern morality bankrupt but defending its deep resources.

### 三重道德来源 / Three Moral Sources

**第一来源：基督教内在化 / First Source: Christian Inwardness**
奥古斯丁将道德的焦点从外部行为转向内在意向——"罪"（sin）不在于做了什么，而在于内心的"转向"（turning）。这种"转向内在"（inward turn）为现代自我认同的"内在深度"（inner depth）概念奠定了基础。路德的"因信称义"进一步将救赎的根源内在化——不是教会的圣礼，而是个人的信仰构成了救赎。Augustine shifted moral focus from external behavior to inner intention — "sin" lies not in what one does but in the inner "turning" of the heart. This "inward turn" laid the foundation for modern self-identity's "inner depth." Luther's "justification by faith" further internalized the source of salvation — not the Church's sacraments but personal faith constitutes salvation.

**第二来源：笛卡尔分解式自我 / Second Source: Cartesian Punctual Self**
笛卡尔的"我思故我在"（cogito）将自我理解为一个可以从中抽离一切经验的思维点——这个"分解式自我"（punctual self）能够从世界、身体和传统中抽离出来，以理性的眼光重新审视一切。这种"分解"（disengagement）能力是现代自由和自律概念的基础——但也导致了自我与世界的疏离。Descartes' "cogito" conceives the self as a point of thought that can disengage from all experience — this "punctual self" can withdraw from the world, body, and tradition to re-examine everything rationally. This "disengagement" capacity underlies modern freedom and autonomy — but also produces self-world alienation.

**第三来源：浪漫主义表达主义 / Third Source: Romantic Expressivism**
浪漫主义对笛卡尔传统提出了挑战——赫尔德认为，自我不是一个可以脱离身体的思维点，而是一个通过表达（语言、艺术、行动）来揭示和实现自身的存在。表达不只是传递已有的思想，而是构成新意义的创造性行为。这种表达主义是对启蒙理性主义的"纠偏"——它恢复了情感、想象力和自然在自我理解中的地位。Romanticism challenged the Cartesian tradition — Herder argued that the self is not a thinking point detachable from the body, but a being that reveals and realizes itself through expression (language, art, action). Expression is not merely transmitting pre-existing thoughts but a creative act constituting new meaning. This expressivism "corrects" Enlightenment rationalism — restoring emotion, imagination, and nature to self-understanding.

### 现代自我的张力结构 / The Tension Structure of the Modern Self

泰勒论证，现代自我认同的核心特征不是统一性，而是张力——三种道德来源之间的紧张构成了现代人的道德困境。例如，笛卡尔式的"分解"与浪漫主义式的"表达"之间的张力——我们既要从世界中抽离以批判性地反思，又要通过表达与世界融为一体。又如，基督教内在化遗产中对"善"的超越性追求与笛卡尔式的世俗理性主义之间的张力。

Taylor argues that modern self-identity's core feature is not unity but tension — the strain between three moral sources constitutes modern moral dilemmas. For example, the tension between Cartesian "disengagement" and Romantic "expression" — we must both withdraw from the world for critical reflection and merge with the world through expression. Or the tension between Christianity's transcendent pursuit of the good and Cartesian secular rationalism.

## 思想史脉络 / Intellectual History

《自我的根源》在思想史上的位置极为重要。它回应了20世纪80年代英语哲学界的核心争论——"自由主义 vs 社群主义"之争。桑德尔（Michael Sandel）在《自由主义与正义的局限》（*Liberalism and the Limits of Justice*, 1982）中批评了罗尔斯的"无负荷自我"（unencumbered self），麦金太尔在《追寻美德》（*After Virtue*, 1981）中宣告了启蒙道德方案的失败。泰勒的贡献在于提供了最详细、最有同情心的思想史叙事——展示现代自我认同不是"错误"，而是有其深层道德根源的复杂成就。

*Sources of the Self* occupies a crucial position in intellectual history. It responds to the core debate in 1980s Anglophone philosophy — the "liberalism vs communitarianism" debate. Sandel criticized Rawls' "unencumbered self" in *Liberalism and the Limits of Justice* (1982), and MacIntyre declared the Enlightenment moral project a failure in *After Virtue* (1981). Taylor's contribution was providing the most detailed and sympathetic intellectual historical narrative — showing that modern self-identity is not a "mistake" but a complex achievement with deep moral sources.

## 关键文本 / Key Passages

> "To know who I am is a species of knowing where I stand. My identity is defined by the commitments and identifications which provide the frame or horizon within which I can try to determine from case to case what is good, or valuable, or what ought to be done."
> — Taylor, *Sources of the Self* (1989), p. 27

> "知道我是谁，就是知道我站在何处。我的认同由那些承诺和认同所界定，它们提供了一个框架或视野，使我能够在具体情境中判定什么是善的、有价值的、什么是应该做的。"

> "We become full human agents, capable of understanding ourselves, and hence of defining our identity, through our acquisition of rich human languages of expression."
> — Taylor, *Sources of the Self* (1989), p. 35

> "我们通过习得丰富的表达语言，成为完整的人类行动者，能够理解自身，从而界定自己的认同。"

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

"自我的根源"概念在当代的应用主要体现在三个方面。第一，在心理治疗中，泰勒的三重来源框架帮助治疗师理解来访者的道德困境不仅仅是个人问题，而是嵌入在现代性的深层结构之中——例如，"自我实现"（浪漫主义）与"自律理性"（笛卡尔式）之间的冲突。第二，在文化研究中，泰勒的框架为分析当代文化的"精神分裂"（如消费主义的笛卡尔式计算与社交媒体的浪漫主义表达之间的张力）提供了理论工具。第三，在跨文化比较中，"自我的根源"概念可以被扩展——儒家传统的"自我的根源"是什么？伊斯兰文明呢？这为比较哲学提供了方法论基础。

The "sources of the self" concept has three main contemporary applications. First, in psychotherapy, Taylor's three-source framework helps therapists understand that clients' moral dilemmas are not merely personal problems but embedded in modernity's deep structures — for example, the conflict between "self-actualization" (Romantic) and "rational autonomy" (Cartesian). Second, in cultural studies, Taylor's framework provides a theoretical tool for analyzing contemporary culture's "schizophrenia" (e.g., tension between consumerism's Cartesian calculation and social media's Romantic expression). Third, in cross-cultural comparison, the "sources of the self" concept can be extended — what are the "sources of the self" in the Confucian tradition? In Islamic civilization? This provides a methodological foundation for comparative philosophy.

## 批评与回应 / Critiques and Responses

**批评一：西方中心主义 / Critique 1: Western-centrism**
后殖民批评者指出，泰勒的三重来源完全是西方的——基督教、笛卡尔、浪漫主义——忽视了非西方传统对自我认同的贡献。Postcolonial critics note that Taylor's three sources are entirely Western — Christianity, Descartes, Romanticism — neglecting non-Western traditions' contributions to self-identity.

**回应 / Response:** 泰勒本人承认这一局限，并在后续著作中（如《世俗时代》对"多元宗教"的讨论）试图拓展视野。但泰勒的方法论——深入特定传统来揭示道德来源——本身就预设了每种传统都有自己的"自我的根源"需要被追溯。Taylor himself acknowledges this limitation and attempts to broaden his vision in later works (such as *A Secular Age*'s discussion of "multiple religiosities"). But Taylor's methodology — delving into specific traditions to reveal moral sources — itself presupposes that every tradition has its own "sources of the self" to be traced.

**批评二：叙事的理想化 / Critique 2: Narrative Idealization**
一些批评者认为，泰勒的谱系叙事过于整洁——历史比泰勒讲述的故事更为混乱和偶然。Some critics argue that Taylor's genealogical narrative is overly tidy — history is messier and more contingent than Taylor's story suggests.

**回应 / Response:** 泰勒在《自我的根源》的序言中明确承认他的叙事是"选择性的"（selective）和"重构性的"（reconstructive）——他的目的不是写出完整的历史，而是揭示那些至今仍然活跃在我们道德生活中的深层力量。Taylor explicitly acknowledges in the preface that his narrative is "selective" and "reconstructive" — his aim is not to write complete history but to reveal deep forces still active in our moral lives.

## 跨文化回响 / Cross-cultural Echoes

泰勒的"自我的根源"概念为中西比较提供了独特的方法论框架。在儒家传统中，"自我的根源"可以被追溯到：（1）周礼的外在规范性（li，ritual propriety）——类似于基督教的制度化道德；（2）孟子的"性善论"——类似于基督教的内在化；（3）宋明理学的"心性论"——类似于浪漫主义的表达主义。这种比较不是简单的平行关系，而是揭示了不同文明中"自我认同的道德来源"的结构相似性和实质差异。

Taylor's "sources of the self" concept provides a unique methodological framework for Chinese-Western comparison. In the Confucian tradition, the "sources of the self" could be traced to: (1) the Zhou ritual propriety (li) of external normativity — analogous to Christianity's institutionalized morality; (2) Mencius's "goodness of human nature" — analogous to Christian inwardness; (3) Song-Ming Neo-Confucianism's "mind-nature theory" — analogous to Romantic expressivism. Such comparison is not simple parallelism but reveals both structural similarity and substantive difference in the "moral sources of self-identity" across civilizations.
