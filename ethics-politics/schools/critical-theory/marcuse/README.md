# 赫伯特·马尔库塞 · Herbert Marcuse

## 基本信息 / Basic Information

赫伯特·马尔库塞（Herbert Marcuse, 1898–1979）是德裔美国哲学家、社会理论家，法兰克福学派第一代核心成员之一，被誉为"新左派之父"。他出生于柏林一个富裕的犹太家庭，早年在弗莱堡大学师从马丁·海德格尔（Martin Heidegger），于1922年获得哲学博士学位，博士论文研究德国艺术家小说（*Der deutsche Künstlerroman*）。1930年代初，他加入法兰克福社会研究所（Institut für Sozialforschung），与马克斯·霍克海默（Max Horkheimer）、西奥多·阿多诺（Theodor Adorno）等人共同奠定了批判理论（Kritische Theorie）的基础。

Herbert Marcuse (1898–1979) was a German-American philosopher and social theorist, one of the core first-generation members of the Frankfurt School, and widely regarded as the "father of the New Left." Born into a prosperous Jewish family in Berlin, he studied under Martin Heidegger at the University of Freiburg, earning his doctorate in 1922 with a dissertation on the German artist novel (*Der deutsche Künstlerroman*). In the early 1930s, he joined the Institut für Sozialforschung (Institute for Social Research) in Frankfurt, working alongside Max Horkheimer, Theodor Adorno, and others to lay the foundations of Critical Theory (*Kritische Theorie*).

纳粹上台后，马尔库塞于1934年流亡美国，先后在哥伦比亚大学、哈佛大学、布兰代斯大学和加州大学圣迭戈分校任教。他在美国期间深入分析发达工业社会的新型控制形式，将马克思的政治经济学批判与弗洛伊德的精神分析理论创造性地结合起来。1960年代，他的著作《单向度的人》（*One-Dimensional Man*, 1964）成为全球学生运动和新左派运动的理论纲领，他本人也被视为那个时代最具影响力的公共知识分子之一。

After the Nazis came to power, Marcuse fled to the United States in 1934, teaching at Columbia University, Harvard University, Brandeis University, and the University of California, San Diego. During his American period, he deeply analyzed new forms of control in advanced industrial society, creatively synthesizing Marx's critique of political economy with Freud's psychoanalytic theory. In the 1960s, his book *One-Dimensional Man* (1964) became the theoretical program of the global student movement and the New Left, and Marcuse himself was regarded as one of the most influential public intellectuals of the era.

## 核心命题 / Core Theses

**1. 单向度社会 / One-Dimensional Society**
发达工业社会通过技术理性与消费主义的双重机制，消除了否定性、批判性和超越性的维度，将一切对立力量整合进体系内部，创造出一个"单向度"的封闭社会——在这个社会中，人们甚至无法想象另一种生活方式。
Advanced industrial society, through the dual mechanisms of technological rationality and consumerism, eliminates the dimensions of negation, critique, and transcendence, integrating all oppositional forces into the system, creating a closed "one-dimensional" society — in which people cannot even imagine an alternative way of life.

**2. 虚假需求与真实需求 / False Needs and True Needs**
资本主义体系通过广告、大众媒体和消费文化制造并内化了一套"虚假需求"体系，使人们误将体系的需要当作自身的需要，从而丧失了对自由的真正追求。与之相对，"真实需求"指向人的全面发展和解放。
The capitalist system generates and internalizes a system of "false needs" through advertising, mass media, and consumer culture, causing people to mistake the system's needs for their own, thereby forfeiting the genuine pursuit of freedom. By contrast, "true needs" point toward the comprehensive development and liberation of human beings.

**3. 压抑性脱升华 / Repressive Desublimation**
现代社会表面上给予人们更多的性自由和文化自由，但这种"脱升华"实际上是压抑机制的新形式：它通过将本能能量引导至消费渠道来维持社会控制，使人们在虚假的满足中放弃了根本性的反抗。
Modern society appears to grant greater sexual and cultural freedom, but this "desublimation" is in fact a new form of repression: it maintains social control by channeling instinctual energy into consumption, causing people to abandon fundamental resistance amid false satisfactions.

**4. 大拒绝 / The Great Refusal**
面对单向度社会的全面整合，唯一真正的政治行动是"大拒绝"——对现存秩序及其一切合法化意识形态的彻底否定。这种拒绝不是夺取国家权力的革命，而是对生活形式本身的根本性变革。
In the face of one-dimensional society's total integration, the only authentic political act is the "Great Refusal" — a thoroughgoing negation of the existing order and all its legitimating ideologies. This refusal is not a revolution to seize state power, but a fundamental transformation of the form of life itself.

**5. 技术理性即政治理性 / Technological Rationality as Political Rationality**
技术理性并非中立的工具理性，它本身就是一种政治理性：技术的组织方式内含着统治的逻辑，技术的进步同时就是统治的进步。在发达工业社会中，技术合理性成为最有效的社会控制形式。
Technological rationality is not neutral instrumental reason; it is itself a form of political rationality: the organization of technology contains an inherent logic of domination, and technological progress is simultaneously the progress of domination. In advanced industrial society, technological rationality becomes the most effective form of social control.

**6. 审美维度与解放 / The Aesthetic Dimension and Liberation**
在马尔库塞晚期思想中，艺术和审美经验保存了被压抑的感性维度，成为反抗单向度社会的最后阵地。审美想象能够突破工具理性的封锁，为一种新的感性——"新感性"（new sensibility）——开辟道路，而新感性是真正解放的前提。
In Marcuse's later thought, art and aesthetic experience preserve the repressed dimension of sensibility, becoming the last bastion of resistance against one-dimensional society. Aesthetic imagination can break through the blockade of instrumental rationality, opening the way for a "new sensibility" — which is the precondition for genuine liberation.

## 思想脉络 / Intellectual Development

### 早期 / Early Period (1920s–1940s)

马尔库塞的哲学起点深受海德格尔存在论的影响。他在弗莱堡的博士论文（1922）虽然处理的是文学主题，但已经显露出对"历史性"（Historizität）问题的关注。1932年出版的《黑格尔的本体论与历史性理论》（*Hegels Ontologie und die Theorie der Geschichtlichkeit*）是他早期最重要的著作，该书试图通过重新阐释黑格尔来发展一种"具体的哲学"，将历史性理解为人类存在的根本特征。

Marcuse's philosophical starting point was deeply influenced by Heidegger's ontology. His Freiburg dissertation (1922), though literary in subject, already revealed a concern with the problem of "historicity" (*Historizität*). His 1932 work *Hegel's Ontology and the Theory of Historicity* (*Hegels Ontologie und die Theorie der Geschichtlichkeit*) was his most important early publication, attempting to develop a "concrete philosophy" through a reinterpretation of Hegel, understanding historicity as a fundamental characteristic of human existence.

1933年加入法兰克福社会研究所后，马尔库塞迅速投入批判理论的集体事业。他在研究所的机关刊物《社会研究杂志》上发表了多篇重要论文，包括《论具体哲学的基础》（1934）和《权威与家庭研究》（1936）。流亡美国后，他参与了研究所关于"权威主义国家"的研究项目，并在美国战略服务局（OSS）工作（1942–1945），分析纳粹德国的社会结构。这一时期，他深入研究了黑格尔辩证法，为后来将辩证法与当代社会批判结合奠定了基础。

After joining the Frankfurt Institute for Social Research in 1933, Marcuse quickly immersed himself in the collective project of Critical Theory. He published several important essays in the Institute's journal *Zeitschrift für Sozialforschung*, including "On the Foundations of Concrete Philosophy" (1934) and "Studies on Authority and Family" (1936). After emigrating to the United States, he participated in the Institute's research project on the "authoritarian state" and worked for the Office of Strategic Services (OSS, 1942–1945), analyzing the social structure of Nazi Germany. During this period, he studied Hegelian dialectics intensively, laying the groundwork for his later synthesis of dialectics and contemporary social critique.

### 中期 / Middle Period (1950s–1960s)

1950年代是马尔库塞思想转型的关键时期。1955年出版的《爱欲与文明》（*Eros and Civilization*）标志着他试图将弗洛伊德的精神分析与马克思主义进行系统综合。马尔库塞区分了"基本压抑"（basic repression）和"剩余压抑"（surplus-repression）：前者是文明本身所必需的对本能的限制，后者则是特定统治秩序所强加的额外压抑。他认为，在现代技术条件下，剩余压抑已经变得不必要——技术本可以将人类从苦役中解放出来，但资本主义的生产关系却使技术成为新的压抑工具。

The 1950s were a pivotal period of intellectual transformation for Marcuse. *Eros and Civilization* (1955) marked his attempt at a systematic synthesis of Freudian psychoanalysis and Marxism. Marcuse distinguished between "basic repression" and "surplus-repression": the former being the instinctual limitation necessary for civilization itself, the latter the additional repression imposed by specific orders of domination. He argued that under modern technological conditions, surplus-repression had become unnecessary — technology could liberate humanity from drudgery, yet capitalist production relations turned technology into a new instrument of repression.

1958年的《苏联马克思主义》（*Soviet Marxism*）对苏联意识形态进行了批判性分析，揭示了官方马克思主义如何沦为统治的合法性工具。1964年的《单向度的人》（*One-Dimensional Man*）则是他最具影响力的著作，全面阐述了发达工业社会如何通过技术理性和消费主义消除否定性思维，将工人阶级整合进资本主义体系。这部著作使他成为1960年代全球学生运动的精神导师——从巴黎五月风暴到美国反战运动，从德国学生运动到日本全共斗，马尔库塞的思想无处不在。

*Soviet Marxism* (1958) offered a critical analysis of Soviet ideology, revealing how official Marxism had degenerated into a legitimating instrument of domination. *One-Dimensional Man* (1964) was his most influential work, comprehensively articulating how advanced industrial society eliminates negative thinking through technological rationality and consumerism, integrating the working class into the capitalist system. This book made him the intellectual mentor of the global student movement of the 1960s — from the Paris May events to the American anti-war movement, from the German student movement to Japan's Zenkyoto, Marcuse's ideas were everywhere.

### 晚期 / Late Period (1970s)

1968年之后，马尔库塞面对学生运动的退潮和右翼反扑，继续深化其解放理论。1969年的《解放论》（*An Essay on Liberation*）试图从语言学、生物学和美学等多个维度勾勒解放的具体内涵。他提出了"新感性"（new sensibility）的概念——一种不同于工具理性主导的感知方式，能够感受事物的美和生命的价值。1972年的《反革命与反叛》（*Counterrevolution and Revolt*）分析了当时保守力量的反扑和社会运动的困境。

After 1968, facing the ebb of the student movement and right-wing backlash, Marcuse continued to deepen his theory of liberation. *An Essay on Liberation* (1969) attempted to outline the concrete content of liberation from multiple dimensions — linguistic, biological, and aesthetic. He proposed the concept of a "new sensibility" — a mode of perception different from that dominated by instrumental rationality, capable of sensing the beauty of things and the value of life. *Counterrevolution and Revolt* (1972) analyzed the conservative counterattack and the predicament of social movements.

他最后一部重要著作《审美维度》（*The Aesthetic Dimension*, 1978）回归美学问题，论证艺术的"审美形式"（aesthetic form）具有内在的颠覆力量——不是通过直接的政治内容，而是通过对感知方式本身的变革。马尔库塞于1979年在柏林访问期间去世，享年81岁。他的遗产至今仍在批判理论、文化研究、政治哲学和美学等领域发挥深远影响。

His last major work, *The Aesthetic Dimension* (1978), returned to aesthetic questions, arguing that art's "aesthetic form" possesses an inherently subversive power — not through direct political content, but through the transformation of perception itself. Marcuse died in 1979 during a visit to Berlin, at the age of 81. His legacy continues to exert profound influence in critical theory, cultural studies, political philosophy, and aesthetics to this day.

## 重要概念 / Key Concepts

1. **单向度的人 / One-Dimensional Man** — 发达工业社会的主体形态：丧失否定性思维、被整合进消费体系的个体。详见 [单向度的人](concepts/one-dimensional-man.md)。
   The subject-form of advanced industrial society: individuals who have lost negative thinking and been integrated into the consumer system. See [One-Dimensional Man](concepts/one-dimensional-man.md).

2. **压抑性脱升华 / Repressive Desublimation** — 表面自由掩盖深层压抑的社会机制。详见 [压抑性脱升华](concepts/repressive-desublimation.md)。
   The social mechanism by which surface freedom masks deep repression. See [Repressive Desublimation](concepts/repressive-desublimation.md).

3. **大拒绝 / The Great Refusal** — 对现存秩序的总体性否定。详见 [大拒绝](concepts/great-refusal.md)。
   The total negation of the existing order. See [The Great Refusal](concepts/great-refusal.md).

4. **技术理性 / Technological Rationality** — 技术组织内含的政治统治逻辑。详见 [技术理性](concepts/technological-rationality.md)。
   The political logic of domination inherent in technological organization. See [Technological Rationality](concepts/technological-rationality.md).

5. **解放 / Liberation** — 超越单向度社会的可能性条件。详见 [解放](concepts/liberation.md)。
   The conditions of possibility for transcending one-dimensional society. See [Liberation](concepts/liberation.md).

6. **虚假需求 / False Needs** — 资本主义体系制造并强加于个体的消费需求，使人误以为这些需求是自己的真实欲望，从而甘愿被体系控制。
   Consumer needs manufactured and imposed by the capitalist system, causing individuals to mistake these needs for their authentic desires, willingly submitting to systemic control.

7. **剩余压抑 / Surplus-Repression** — 超出文明基本需要之外的额外压抑，由特定统治秩序（如资本主义绩效原则）所强加。
   Additional repression beyond what is necessary for civilization, imposed by specific orders of domination (such as the capitalist performance principle).

8. **新感性 / New Sensibility** — 一种解放了的感知方式，能够超越工具理性的束缚，以审美态度感受世界。
   A liberated mode of perception that transcends the constraints of instrumental rationality, experiencing the world with an aesthetic attitude.

9. **审美形式 / Aesthetic Form** — 艺术作品的形式结构本身所蕴含的颠覆性力量，它独立于内容而改变感知方式。
   The subversive power inherent in the formal structure of artworks, independently transforming perception regardless of content.

10. **绩效原则 / Performance Principle** — 资本主义社会特有的现实原则形式，要求个体不断竞争、生产和消费。
    The historically specific form of the reality principle in capitalist society, demanding continuous competition, production, and consumption from individuals.

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

1. **数字平台批判 / Digital Platform Critique**
   马尔库塞对技术理性的批判为分析当代数字平台（社交媒体、算法推荐、大数据监控）提供了框架：算法不仅塑造我们的消费选择，还塑造我们的欲望和认知方式本身，这正是"单向度"在数字时代的极端表现。
   Marcuse's critique of technological rationality provides a framework for analyzing contemporary digital platforms (social media, algorithmic recommendation, big data surveillance): algorithms shape not only our consumption choices but our desires and modes of cognition themselves — the extreme manifestation of "one-dimensionality" in the digital age.

2. **消费主义批判与反消费运动 / Consumerism Critique and Anti-Consumer Movements**
   "虚假需求"的概念直接启发了当代反消费主义运动、极简主义生活方式和对"计划性报废"（planned obsolescence）的批判。
   The concept of "false needs" directly inspired contemporary anti-consumerist movements, minimalist lifestyles, and critiques of planned obsolescence.

3. **新自由主义批判 / Neoliberalism Critique**
   马尔库塞对"压抑性宽容"的分析为批判新自由主义提供了工具：新自由主义表面上容忍多元性，但通过市场化逻辑将一切差异收编为商品。
   Marcuse's analysis of "repressive tolerance" provides tools for critiquing neoliberalism: neoliberalism superficially tolerates diversity but subsumes all difference into commodities through market logic.

4. **人工智能伦理 / AI Ethics**
   技术理性概念的当代延伸：人工智能系统的设计与部署并非价值中立，其背后隐含着效率最大化、可预测性和控制的技术理性逻辑。
   A contemporary extension of technological rationality: the design and deployment of AI systems is not value-neutral; behind them lies the technological-rational logic of efficiency maximization, predictability, and control.

5. **文化工业2.0 / Culture Industry 2.0**
   流媒体平台、网红经济和用户生成内容构成了文化工业的新形态：看似民主化的内容生产实际上加深了单向度性——每个人都是生产者，却没有人是真正的批判者。
   Streaming platforms, influencer economies, and user-generated content constitute new forms of the culture industry: seemingly democratized content production actually deepens one-dimensionality — everyone is a producer, yet no one is truly a critic.

6. **生态危机与增长批判 / Ecological Crisis and Growth Critique**
   马尔库塞对"绩效原则"的批判为生态马克思主义提供了理论基础：资本主义对无限增长的追求与生态系统的有限性根本矛盾。
   Marcuse's critique of the "performance principle" provides theoretical foundations for eco-Marxism: capitalism's pursuit of infinite growth is fundamentally contradictory with the finitude of ecological systems.

7. **社会运动理论 / Social Movement Theory**
   "大拒绝"的概念影响了当代社会运动理论，特别是占领运动（Occupy）、"黑人的命也是命"（BLM）和气候罢工等运动中对体制性拒绝的探索。
   The concept of the "Great Refusal" has influenced contemporary social movement theory, particularly explorations of systemic refusal in movements like Occupy, Black Lives Matter, and climate strikes.

8. **心理政治学 / Psychopolitics**
   马尔库塞将弗洛伊德与马克思主义结合的方法影响了当代心理政治学研究——韩炳哲（Byung-Chul Han）的"倦怠社会"概念直接继承了马尔库塞对"绩效原则"的批判。
   Marcuse's synthesis of Freud and Marxism has influenced contemporary psychopolitical studies — Byung-Chul Han's concept of the "burnout society" directly inherits Marcuse's critique of the "performance principle."

## 常见误读 / Common Misreadings

❌ **误读1 / Misreading 1**: 马尔库塞反对一切技术进步，是一个技术恐惧者。
Marcuse opposes all technological progress and is a technophobe.

✅ **纠正 / Correction**: 马尔库塞批判的不是技术本身，而是技术在资本主义关系中所承担的统治功能。他明确区分了技术的"工具性"和技术的"政治性"，认为同样的技术可以在不同的社会关系下服务于解放。
Marcuse critiques not technology itself, but the function of domination that technology assumes within capitalist relations. He explicitly distinguishes between technology's "instrumentality" and its "politicality," arguing that the same technology can serve liberation under different social relations.

❌ **误读2 / Misreading 2**: 马尔库塞的"压抑性宽容"论是极权主义的，主张取消言论自由。
Marcuse's "repressive tolerance" thesis is totalitarian, advocating the abolition of free speech.

✅ **纠正 / Correction**: 马尔库塞批判的是自由主义宽容的虚假性——在权力严重不平等的社会中，形式上的宽容实际上维护了强势群体的支配地位。他的目的是揭示宽容的局限性，而非取消自由。
Marcuse critiques the falseness of liberal tolerance — in a society of severe power inequality, formal tolerance actually maintains the dominance of powerful groups. His aim is to expose the limitations of tolerance, not to abolish freedom.

❌ **误读3 / Misreading 3**: 马尔库塞的"大拒绝"是一种消极的、破坏性的虚无主义。
Marcuse's "Great Refusal" is a passive, destructive nihilism.

✅ **纠正 / Correction**: "大拒绝"是一种积极的否定——它不仅拒绝现存秩序，还指向一种完全不同的生活方式和社会形式。马尔库塞强调，否定本身就包含着肯定的维度：对不自由的否定就是对自由的肯定。
The "Great Refusal" is an active negation — it refuses the existing order while pointing toward an entirely different form of life and society. Marcuse emphasizes that negation itself contains an affirmative dimension: the negation of unfreedom is the affirmation of freedom.

❌ **误读4 / Misreading 4**: 马尔库塞认为工人阶级已经完全被收编，不再是革命主体。
Marcuse believes the working class has been completely co-opted and is no longer a revolutionary subject.

✅ **纠正 / Correction**: 马尔库塞确实指出发达国家工人阶级在物质上被整合进了消费社会，但他并未完全排除工人阶级的革命潜能。他关注的是新的革命主体——边缘群体、学生、第三世界被压迫者——作为"来自外部的力量"。这是一个诊断性的观察，而非本体论的宣判。
Marcuse indeed noted that the working class in developed countries was materially integrated into consumer society, but he did not entirely exclude its revolutionary potential. He focused on new revolutionary subjects — marginalized groups, students, the oppressed of the Third World — as "forces from outside." This was a diagnostic observation, not an ontological verdict.

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

马尔库塞的思想与东方思想传统存在多处有趣的对话可能。首先，他对"新感性"的构想与道家的"无为"思想有共鸣之处：两者都追求一种超越工具理性控制的、更自然的存在方式。庄子对"机心"（机械心智）的批判与马尔库塞对技术理性的批判形成跨文化呼应——两者都警惕技术化思维对生命本真性的侵蚀。

Marcuse's thought opens interesting dialogues with Eastern intellectual traditions. First, his vision of a "new sensibility" resonates with the Daoist concept of *wu wei* (non-action): both pursue a mode of existence beyond the control of instrumental rationality. Zhuangzi's critique of the "mechanical mind" (*ji xin*) echoes Marcuse's critique of technological rationality across cultures — both warning against the erosion of life's authenticity by technologized thinking.

其次，马尔库塞对"虚假需求"的分析可以与佛教的"无明"（avidyā）概念进行比较：两者都指向一种深层的认知错误——将虚假当作真实，将苦当作乐。佛教的"解脱"（moksha）概念与马尔库塞的"解放"概念虽然来源不同，但都涉及从深层束缚中的觉醒。

Second, Marcuse's analysis of "false needs" can be compared with the Buddhist concept of *avidyā* (ignorance): both point to a deep cognitive error — mistaking the false for the true, suffering for pleasure. While the Buddhist concept of *moksha* (liberation) and Marcuse's "liberation" have different sources, both involve awakening from deep bondage.

第三，马尔库塞对审美经验的重视与中国古典美学中的"意境"论有相通之处：两者都认为审美经验具有超越日常功利认知的力量。李泽厚在《美学四讲》中对马克思美学与马尔库塞思想的比较研究，开启了中西美学批判的对话空间。

Third, Marcuse's emphasis on aesthetic experience connects with the Chinese classical aesthetic theory of *yi jing* (artistic conception): both hold that aesthetic experience has the power to transcend everyday utilitarian cognition. Li Zehou's comparative study of Marxist aesthetics and Marcuse's thought in *Four Lectures on Aesthetics* opened a dialogical space for East-West aesthetic critique.
