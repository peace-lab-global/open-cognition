# 大拒绝 · The Great Refusal

## 概念概述 / Concept Overview

"大拒绝"（The Great Refusal）是马尔库塞解放理论的核心概念，指对发达工业社会的现存秩序、价值体系和生活方式的总体性否定。这个概念源自阿多诺的"否定辩证法"，但在马尔库塞那里获得了更明确的政治含义。"大拒绝"不是一种具体的革命策略或政治纲领，而是一种根本性的态度——拒绝接受"事情就是这样"的现实原则，拒绝承认现存秩序的唯一性和不可超越性。

"The Great Refusal" is the core concept of Marcuse's liberation theory, referring to the total negation of the existing order, value system, and way of life in advanced industrial society. The concept derives from Adorno's "negative dialectics" but acquires more explicit political meaning in Marcuse's hands. The "Great Refusal" is not a specific revolutionary strategy or political program, but a fundamental attitude — refusing to accept the reality principle that "this is how things are," refusing to acknowledge the uniqueness and insurmountability of the existing order.

马尔库塞特别强调，"大拒绝"的主体不再是传统的工人阶级——因为发达工业社会的工人阶级已经被消费主义所整合——而是"底层的、被驱逐的、被剥削的、被压迫的"群体：第三世界的被殖民者、种族少数群体、失业者、学生知识分子和边缘化的社会力量。这些群体"从外部"对体系构成威胁，因为他们的生活经验使他们无法被体系的物质满足所收买。

Marcuse particularly emphasizes that the subject of the "Great Refusal" is no longer the traditional working class — because the working class in advanced industrial society has been integrated by consumerism — but "those at the bottom, the outcasts, the exploited, the oppressed": the colonized of the Third World, racial minorities, the unemployed, student intellectuals, and marginalized social forces. These groups threaten the system "from outside" because their lived experience prevents them from being bought off by the system's material satisfactions.

## 理论内涵 / Theoretical Content

### 否定性的政治 / The Politics of Negativity

"大拒绝"的哲学基础是辩证法的"否定性"原则。在黑格尔和马克思的辩证法中，"否定"不是简单的破坏或毁灭，而是对现存事物的批判性超越——否定包含着肯定的维度：对不自由的否定就是对自由的肯定。马尔库塞认为，单向度社会的根本危险在于消除了否定性：当一切对立面都被整合进体系时，批判的维度就消失了。"大拒绝"就是要恢复这种否定性——重新打开被封闭的可能性空间。

The philosophical foundation of the "Great Refusal" is the dialectical principle of "negativity." In Hegelian and Marxist dialectics, "negation" is not mere destruction or annihilation, but critical transcendence of what exists — negation contains an affirmative dimension: the negation of unfreedom is the affirmation of freedom. Marcuse argues that the fundamental danger of one-dimensional society is the elimination of negativity: when all oppositions are integrated into the system, the critical dimension disappears. The "Great Refusal" aims to restore this negativity — to reopen the closed space of possibilities.

### 拒绝与想象 / Refusal and Imagination

"大拒绝"与审美想象力密切相关。马尔库塞认为，在单向度社会中，只有艺术和审美经验还保存着"另一种现实"的可能性。审美想象力能够超越现实原则的限制，描绘一种不同于现状的生活方式。因此，"大拒绝"不仅是政治行为，也是想象力的行为——拒绝接受现实的唯一性，想象并追求一种更自由、更美好的社会。

The "Great Refusal" is closely linked to aesthetic imagination. Marcuse argues that in one-dimensional society, only art and aesthetic experience still preserve the possibility of "another reality." Aesthetic imagination can transcend the limits of the reality principle, envisioning a way of life different from the status quo. Therefore, the "Great Refusal" is not only a political act but an act of imagination — refusing to accept the uniqueness of reality, imagining and pursuing a freer, more beautiful society.

### 拒绝的多元形式 / Plural Forms of Refusal

"大拒绝"可以采取多种形式：从学生的校园抗议到第三世界的民族解放运动，从先锋艺术的审美颠覆到日常生活中的"退出"行为。马尔库塞并不认为"大拒绝"必须采取暴力革命的形式——事实上，他认为在单向度社会中，和平的、文化的、审美形式的拒绝可能比暴力革命更具颠覆性。

The "Great Refusal" can take multiple forms: from student campus protests to Third World national liberation movements, from avant-garde art's aesthetic subversion to "exit" behaviors in daily life. Marcuse does not believe the "Great Refusal" must take the form of violent revolution — in fact, he believes that in one-dimensional society, peaceful, cultural, and aesthetic forms of refusal may be more subversive than violent revolution.

## 思想史脉络 / Intellectual History

"大拒绝"的概念直接继承了阿多诺的"否定辩证法"（Negative Dialektik）。阿多诺在《否定的辩证法》（1966）中提出，真正的辩证法不是走向"否定之否定"的肯定，而是停留在否定本身——拒绝将矛盾调和为虚假的统一。马尔库塞将这种哲学立场政治化，使其成为社会变革的纲领。

The concept of the "Great Refusal" directly inherits Adorno's "negative dialectics" (*Negative Dialektik*). Adorno argued in *Negative Dialectics* (1966) that genuine dialectics does not move toward the affirmation of "negation of negation" but remains at negation itself — refusing to reconcile contradictions into false unity. Marcuse politicizes this philosophical position, turning it into a program for social change.

这个概念也与本雅明（Walter Benjamin）的"弥赛亚式断裂"（messianic rupture）概念有关：两者都指向一种对历史连续性的彻底中断。同时，"大拒绝"也与法国无政府工团主义传统（如索雷尔的"总罢工"神话）和甘地的非暴力不合作运动有思想上的亲缘关系。

This concept also relates to Walter Benjamin's "messianic rupture": both point to a radical interruption of historical continuity. At the same time, the "Great Refusal" has intellectual affinities with the French anarcho-syndicalist tradition (such as Sorel's "general strike" myth) and Gandhi's nonviolent non-cooperation movement.

## 关键文本 / Key Passages

> "在压抑性社会的总体性中，拒绝成为唯一的政治行动——对现存一切的彻底否定。"
> "In the totality of repressive society, refusal becomes the only political act — the thoroughgoing negation of all that exists."

> "大拒绝的力量不在于它能提供替代方案，而在于它能够打开替代方案的空间。"
> "The power of the Great Refusal lies not in its ability to provide alternatives, but in its ability to open the space for alternatives."

马尔库塞在《解放论》中写道：
Marcuse writes in *An Essay on Liberation*:

> "拒绝不仅是消极的——它是对新现实的渴望……否定本身就包含着肯定：对不自由的否定就是对自由的渴望。"
> "Refusal is not merely negative — it is a longing for a new reality … Negation itself contains affirmation: the negation of unfreedom is the longing for freedom."

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

"大拒绝"的概念在当代社会运动中获得了新的生命。2011年的"占领华尔街"（Occupy Wall Street）运动没有提出具体的政治纲领，而是以"我们是99%"的口号表达了对金融资本主义的总体拒绝——这正是马尔库塞意义上的"大拒绝"：不是说"我们要这样做"，而是说"我们不接受这个"。

The concept of the "Great Refusal" has gained new life in contemporary social movements. The 2011 "Occupy Wall Street" movement proposed no specific political program but expressed a general refusal of financial capitalism with the slogan "We are the 99%" — precisely Marcuse's "Great Refusal": not saying "we want to do this," but saying "we don't accept this."

中国的"躺平"运动也可以被理解为一种"消极的大拒绝"：年轻人拒绝参与"内卷"的竞争游戏，拒绝接受"996"的工作伦理，拒绝将人生价值等同于经济成就。虽然这种拒绝缺乏明确的政治纲领，但它表达了年轻一代对单向度社会的深刻不满。

China's "lying flat" (*tang ping*) movement can also be understood as a "passive Great Refusal": young people refuse to participate in the competitive game of "involution," reject the "996" work ethic, and refuse to equate life's value with economic achievement. While this refusal lacks a clear political program, it expresses young people's deep dissatisfaction with one-dimensional society.

## 批评与回应 / Critiques and Responses

**批评 / Critique**: 哈贝马斯认为"大拒绝"过于抽象和消极，无法为实际的政治行动提供指导。他主张通过"交往理性"和制度内的民主改革来实现社会变革。
Habermas argues the "Great Refusal" is too abstract and negative to provide guidance for actual political action. He advocates social change through "communicative rationality" and democratic reform within institutions.

**回应 / Response**: 马尔库塞可能回应说，在单向度社会中，制度内的改革恰恰是最不可能的——因为制度本身就是问题的一部分。"大拒绝"的价值在于它保持了批判的纯粹性，不被体制逻辑所污染。
Marcuse might respond that in one-dimensional society, reform within institutions is precisely what is most impossible — because the institutions themselves are part of the problem. The value of the "Great Refusal" lies in maintaining the purity of critique, uncontaminated by institutional logic.

**批评 / Critique**: 一些马克思主义者认为"大拒绝"忽视了经济基础的决定性作用，过分强调文化和意识形态层面的反抗。
Some Marxists argue the "Great Refusal" neglects the determining role of the economic base, overemphasizing cultural and ideological resistance.

## 跨文化回响 / Cross-cultural Echoes

"大拒绝"与中国思想传统中的"不合作"精神有多重呼应。竹林七贤的"越名教而任自然"、陶渊明的"归去来兮"，都体现了对既存秩序的审美化拒绝——拒绝参与权力的游戏，转而追求一种更真实的生活方式。这种"退隐"在马尔库塞看来可能既是"大拒绝"的表现，也可能是逃避——关键在于退隐是否保持着对现存秩序的否定性意识。

The "Great Refusal" resonates with the spirit of "non-cooperation" in Chinese intellectual traditions. The Seven Sages of the Bamboo Grove's "transcending names and teachings to follow nature," Tao Yuanming's "Return, O Return," all embody an aestheticized refusal of the established order — refusing to participate in the game of power, turning instead to a more authentic way of life. Such "withdrawal" may be both a manifestation of the "Great Refusal" and an escape in Marcuse's view — the key is whether withdrawal maintains a negative consciousness of the existing order.

印度的甘地式"不合作运动"（Satyagraha）也体现了"大拒绝"的逻辑：不是通过暴力推翻殖民政权，而是通过拒绝参与殖民秩序来瓦解其合法性基础。

India's Gandhian non-cooperation movement (*Satyagraha*) also embodies the logic of the "Great Refusal": not overthrowing colonial power through violence, but dissolving its legitimacy foundation by refusing to participate in the colonial order.
