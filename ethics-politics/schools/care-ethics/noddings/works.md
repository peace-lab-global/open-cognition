## 版本与翻译说明 / Notes on Editions and Translations

诺丁斯的著作已被翻译成多种语言，包括中文、日文、韩文、西班牙文、葡萄牙文和德文。《关怀》一书已有多个版本：1984年初版，1995年修订版（增加了新的序言），2003年二十周年纪念版（增加了作者反思）。中译本以1995年修订版为底本。

Noddings' works have been translated into multiple languages, including Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Spanish, Portuguese, and German. *Caring* has appeared in several editions: the 1984 first edition, the 1995 revised edition (with a new preface), and the 2003 twentieth-anniversary edition (with author's reflections). The Chinese translation is based on the 1995 revised edition.

*Philosophy of Education* 作为教育哲学教材，已多次修订再版，最新版本为2015年的第四版。该书被全球数百所大学采用为教育哲学课程的标准教材。

*Philosophy of Education*, as a philosophy of education textbook, has been revised and reprinted multiple times, with the most recent fourth edition appearing in 2015. It has been adopted by hundreds of universities worldwide as the standard textbook for philosophy of education courses.
