## 入门建议 / Suggested Entry Points

**最佳入门 / Best Starting Point**: 《追寻美德》第1-3章（诊断）和第14-15章（建构）
**进阶阅读 / Intermediate**: 《谁之正义？何种合理性？》第18-20章（传统构成的合理性）
**深度阅读 / Advanced**: 《三种对立的道德探究》全文 + 所选论文集

The best entry point is *After Virtue* Chapters 1-3 (diagnosis) and 14-15 (construction). For intermediate reading, see *Whose Justice? Which Rationality?* Chapters 18-20 (tradition-constituted rationality). For advanced study, read *Three Rival Versions* in full plus the selected essay volumes.
