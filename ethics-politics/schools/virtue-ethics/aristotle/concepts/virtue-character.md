# 美德品格 · Virtue-Character (Arete & Hexis)

## 概念概述 / Concept Overview

在亚里士多德的伦理学中，"美德"（arete, ἀρετή）不是一种孤立的善行或道德规则，而是一种深层的、稳定的**品格状态**（hexis, ἕξις）。品格状态不同于感情（pathos）——感情是被动的、不自主的感受（如愤怒、恐惧、欲望）；也不同于能力（dynamis）——能力是做某事的潜在可能性。品格状态是**通过反复实践而获得的、关于感受和行动的稳定的卓越品质**——它塑造了一个人感知世界、回应情境和做出选择的方式。

In Aristotle's ethics, "virtue" (arete, ἀρετή) is not an isolated good deed or moral rule, but a deep, stable **character state** (hexis, ἕξις). A character state differs from feelings (pathos) — which are passive, involuntary experiences (anger, fear, desire); and from capacities (dynamis) — which are potential abilities to do something. A character state is **a stable excellence of feeling and action acquired through repeated practice** — it shapes how a person perceives the world, responds to situations, and makes choices.

这一概念的核心洞见是：**道德生活的关键不在于知道什么规则，而在于成为什么样的人**。一个勇敢的人不仅做勇敢的事，而且在面对危险时**自然地**感受到适度的恐惧和信心，做出正确的判断，并**以此为乐**。品格美德的这种"整体性"——涉及认知、情感和意志的协调统一——使亚里士多德的伦理学与现代规则导向的道德理论形成鲜明对比。

The core insight is: **the key to moral life lies not in knowing rules but in becoming a certain kind of person**. A courageous person not only does courageous things but **naturally** feels the right degree of fear and confidence in danger, judges correctly, and **takes pleasure in doing so**. This "holism" of character virtue — involving coordinated unity of cognition, emotion, and will — sharply contrasts Aristotle's ethics with modern rule-oriented moral theories.

## 理论内涵 / Theoretical Content

### 品格状态的三重特征 / Three Features of Hexis

亚里士多德在《尼各马可伦理学》第二至三卷中揭示了品格状态（hexis）的三个关键特征：

Aristotle reveals three key features of character states (hexis) in *Nicomachean Ethics* Books II–III:

**1. 通过习惯化获得 / Acquired Through Habituation**

"我们通过做公正的事成为公正的人，通过做节制的事成为节制的人，通过做勇敢的事成为勇敢的人。"（EN II.1, 1103a34–b2）美德不是天生的——虽然人天生具有获得美德的**能力**，但这种能力需要通过反复实践才能实现。这正如学习建筑或弹琴：不是先懂了理论再做，而是**在做中学**（learning by doing）。

"We become just by doing just actions, temperate by doing temperate actions, brave by doing brave actions." (EN II.1, 1103a34–b2) Virtue is not innate — though humans naturally have the **capacity** for virtue, this capacity must be actualized through repeated practice. Just like learning architecture or music: one does not first understand the theory then practice, but **learns by doing**.

**2. 涉及感受和行动的正确方向 / Involves Correct Direction of Feelings and Actions**

美德不只是外在行为的正确——更重要的是内在**感受**的正确。勇敢的人不是"虽然害怕但强迫自己前进"的人（那更接近自制/enkrateia），而是**在面对危险时感受到适度的恐惧和信心**的人。亚里士多德强调，真正有美德的人在做美德之事时**感到快乐**——如果一个人做正义之事却感到痛苦，他还没有真正获得正义的美德。

Virtue is not merely correct external behavior — more importantly, it involves correct internal **feelings**. The courageous person is not one who "is afraid but forces himself forward" (that is closer to self-control/enkrateia) but one who **feels the appropriate degree of fear and confidence** in danger. Aristotle emphasizes that the truly virtuous person **takes pleasure** in virtuous action — if one does just things but feels pain, one has not yet truly acquired the virtue of justice.

**3. 是一种稳固的、持久的品质 / Is a Firm, Enduring Quality**

品格状态不是一次性的行为，也不是暂时的情绪状态。一个人偶尔做了一件勇敢的事不等于具有勇敢的美德——美德是一种**稳固的**（bebaios）和**不变的**（akinetos）品质，它构成了一个人的**品格**（character），使其在广泛的、多样的情境中稳定地表现出相应的行为模式。

A character state is neither a one-time action nor a temporary emotional state. Doing one brave thing occasionally does not make one courageous — virtue is a **firm** (bebaios) and **unchanging** (akinetos) quality that constitutes one's **character**, enabling stable behavioral patterns across diverse and varied situations.

### 品格美德与理智美德 / Character Virtues and Intellectual Virtues

亚里士多德区分了两种美德：

Aristotle distinguishes two kinds of virtue:

- **品格美德**（ethike arete）：如勇敢、节制、慷慨、公正、大度——主要通过**习惯化**获得，涉及灵魂的非理性部分（欲望和情感）听从理性的引导。/ **Character virtues** (ethike arete): courage, temperance, generosity, justice, magnanimity — acquired mainly through **habituation**, involving the soul's non-rational part (desires and emotions) obeying reason's guidance.

- **理智美德**（dianoetike arete）：如智慧（sophia）、实践智慧（phronesis）、理解力（sunesis）——主要通过**教导**获得，涉及灵魂理性部分的完善。/ **Intellectual virtues** (dianoetike arete): wisdom (sophia), practical wisdom (phronesis), understanding (sunesis) — acquired mainly through **teaching**, involving the perfection of the soul's rational part.

两种美德不可分离：品格美德需要实践智慧的指引来确定正确的行动方向，实践智慧需要品格美德来确立正确的目的。这一互依性命题是亚里士多德伦理学的核心架构之一。

The two kinds are inseparable: character virtues need practical wisdom's guidance to determine right action, and practical wisdom needs character virtues to establish right ends. This interdependence thesis is a central architectural principle of Aristotle's ethics.

### 自愿与选择 / Voluntariness and Choice

美德作为品格状态，本质上涉及**选择**（prohairesis）。亚里士多德在《尼各马可伦理学》第三卷中详细分析了"自愿"（hekousion）与"非自愿"（akousion）的区分——美德和恶德都预设了行动者的自愿性。一个在不知情的情况下伤害他人的人不具有恶德，一个被强制做"正确之事"的人也不具有美德。选择（prohairesis）是自愿行动的一种特殊形式——它不仅是自发的，而且是**经过审慎判断**（meta logou）后的决定。

Virtue as a character state essentially involves **choice** (prohairesis). Aristotle analyzes the distinction between "voluntary" (hekousion) and "involuntary" (akousion) in EN III — both virtue and vice presuppose the agent's voluntariness. One who harms unknowingly lacks vice, and one forced to do "right" lacks virtue. Choice (prohairesis) is a special form of voluntary action — not merely spontaneous but **deliberated** (meta logou).

## 思想史脉络 / Intellectual History

亚里士多德的品格概念深刻影响了西方道德心理学。中世纪阿奎那将 hexis 翻译为拉丁语 habitus，并将其整合进基督教神学——注入了神学美德（信、望、爱）的概念。启蒙运动后，康德以"善良意志"（good will）取代了品格概念——道德价值不取决于品格状态，而取决于行动是否出于义务。功利主义则完全忽视了品格维度，只关注行动后果。

Aristotle's character concept deeply influenced Western moral psychology. Medieval Aquinas translated hexis as Latin habitus, integrating it into Christian theology — infused with theological virtues (faith, hope, love). After the Enlightenment, Kant replaced the character concept with "good will" — moral worth depends not on character states but on whether action is from duty. Utilitarianism entirely neglected the character dimension, focusing only on action consequences.

20世纪下半叶，安斯科姆（Anscombe, 1958）在《现代道德哲学》中尖锐批评了现代伦理学忽视品格的倾向，呼吁回归亚里士多德式的品格伦理学。赫斯特豪斯（Hursthouse, 1999）在此基础上发展出系统的"新亚里士多德美德伦理学"，将品格美德重新置于伦理学的中心。当代道德心理学（如 Walter Mischel 的品格争论）和情境主义心理学（situationism）的挑战——主张情境而非品格决定行为——则成为亚里士多德品格概念面临的新考验。

In the late 20th century, Anscombe (1958) sharply criticized modern ethics' neglect of character in "Modern Moral Philosophy," calling for a return to Aristotelian character ethics. Hursthouse (1999) developed systematic "neo-Aristotelian virtue ethics," placing character virtue back at ethics' center. Challenges from contemporary moral psychology (Walter Mischel's character debate) and situationist psychology — arguing situations rather than character determine behavior — have become new tests for Aristotle's character concept.

## 关键文本 / Key Passages

> "美德既不是技艺也不是感情……美德是一种品格状态（hexis）。"
> — 《尼各马可伦理学》II.5, 1105b36–1106a1

> "Virtue is neither an art nor a passion... virtue is a state of character (hexis)."
> — *Nicomachean Ethics* II.5, 1105b36–1106a1

> "立法者通过使公民养成习惯来使他们变好——这是每一位立法者的意图；如果他们不能很好地做到这一点，他们就失败了。正是为此，好习惯和坏习惯才使法律有区别。"
> — EN II.1, 1103b3–6

> "Legislators make citizens good by forming habits in them — this is the wish of every legislator; if they fail to do so well, they miss their mark. It is for this reason that good habits and bad habits make laws differ."
> — EN II.1, 1103b3–6

> "一个做公正之事的人并不因此就是公正的，除非他以公正之人做事的方式来做——即出于选择，且为着公正之事本身。"
> — EN II.4, 1105a28–b1

> "A person who does just things is not thereby just, unless they do them in the way just people do — namely, from choice, and for the sake of the just things themselves."
> — EN II.4, 1105a28–b1

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

**品格教育运动**: 21世纪的品格教育运动（Character Education Movement）直接借鉴了亚里士多德的品格概念——教育的目标不仅是传授知识和技能，更是培养学生的美德品格。英国的"Jubilee Centre for Character and Virtues"（伯明翰大学）是这一运动的重要学术中心。/ **Character Education Movement**: The 21st-century Character Education Movement directly draws on Aristotle's character concept — education aims not merely at transmitting knowledge and skills but cultivating students' virtuous character. The UK's "Jubilee Centre for Character and Virtues" (University of Birmingham) is a major academic center.

**组织文化**: 当代企业管理越来越重视"组织品格"（organizational character）——一个企业的文化不仅体现在规章制度中，更体现在员工的**习惯性行为模式**中。这正是亚里士多德品格概念在组织层面的应用。/ **Organizational Culture**: Contemporary business management increasingly values "organizational character" — a company's culture is embodied not merely in regulations but in employees' **habitual behavioral patterns**. This applies Aristotle's character concept at the organizational level.

**道德心理学的情境主义挑战**: 社会心理学家（如 John Doris, 2002）援引米尔格拉姆实验和斯坦福监狱实验等证据，主张情境而非品格决定行为，因此亚里士多德式的"稳固品格状态"可能是一种幻觉。当代亚里士多德主义者（如 Julia Annas, 2003）回应称，品格是**发展性的**——完全的美德是理想，大多数人的品格处于**发展过程中**。/ **Situationist Challenge in Moral Psychology**: Social psychologists (like John Doris, 2002) cite Milgram experiments and Stanford Prison experiments to argue that situations rather than character determine behavior, so Aristotelian "firm character states" may be illusory. Contemporary Aristotelians (like Julia Annas, 2003) respond that character is **developmental** — complete virtue is an ideal; most people's character is **in development**.

## 批评与回应 / Critiques and Responses

**批评: 品格的"运气"问题** / **Critique: The "luck" problem of character**
一个人的品格在很大程度上取决于其成长环境、家庭背景和教育机会——这些都是"道德运气"（moral luck）的产物。如果品格主要由运气决定，那么基于品格的道德评价是否公平？/ One's character largely depends on upbringing, family background, and educational opportunities — all products of "moral luck." If character is largely determined by luck, is character-based moral evaluation fair?

**回应**: 亚里士多德承认外在条件对品格培养的影响（好的法律和教育环境很重要），但他坚持成年人在获得品格之后仍然对自己的行为和品格的维护**负有责任**——"一个人是自己品格的共同原因"（synaitios, EN III.5）。/ **Response**: Aristotle acknowledges external conditions' impact on character cultivation (good laws and education matter), but insists that adults, having acquired character, remain **responsible** for their actions and for maintaining their character — "a person is co-responsible for their character" (synaitios, EN III.5).

## 跨文化回响 / Cross-cultural Echoes

儒家的"**性**"（xing, 性）与"**习**"（xi, 习）的讨论与亚里士多德的品格概念高度相关。孔子说："性相近也，习相远也"（《论语·阳货》）——人的天性相似，但后天的习（habituation）使人差异巨大。这与亚里士多德"美德不是天生的，而是通过习惯化获得"的核心主张几乎完全对应。荀子的"性恶论"与亚里士多德更为接近——荀子主张人性本恶（好利、好声色），需要通过"礼义"（ritual and righteousness）的教化来塑造美德品格，这正是亚里士多德式"习惯化"的中国版本。

The Confucian discussion of "**nature**" (xing) and "**habit**" (xi) closely parallels Aristotle's character concept. Confucius said: "By nature, people are similar; by habit, they become far apart" (*Analects* 17.2) — humans are similar by nature, but habituation creates vast differences. This almost exactly corresponds to Aristotle's core claim that "virtue is not innate but acquired through habituation." Xunzi's "evil nature" thesis is even closer to Aristotle — Xunzi holds that human nature is inclined toward selfishness and sensory pleasure, requiring cultivation through "ritual and righteousness" (li yi) to shape virtuous character, which is essentially the Chinese version of Aristotelian "habituation."
