# 一切人反对一切人的战争 · Bellum Omnium Contra Omnes

## 概念概述 / Concept Overview

"Bellum omnium contra omnes"（一切人反对一切人的战争）是霍布斯对自然状态最凝练的概括——在缺乏公共权力的条件下，人与人之间不可避免地陷入普遍的冲突关系。这一拉丁短语已成为西方政治思想中最具辨识度的命题之一，其影响远超政治哲学，渗透到社会学、国际关系、博弈论和演化生物学等领域。

"Bellum omnium contra omnes" (the war of all against all) is Hobbes's most concise characterization of the state of nature — in the absence of public power, humans inevitably fall into a condition of universal mutual conflict. This Latin phrase has become one of the most recognizable propositions in Western political thought, its influence extending far beyond political philosophy into sociology, international relations, game theory, and evolutionary biology.

需要特别强调的是，霍布斯所说的"战争"并非指持续的实际战斗，而是指一种**持续的战争意愿和倾向**——即在没有公共权威约束的条件下，人们随时准备使用暴力来实现目标或保护自己。这种结构性的暴力可能性使得一切合作、生产和文化活动变得不可能。

It is crucial to emphasize that Hobbes's "war" does not mean constant actual fighting, but rather a **persistent disposition and willingness to fight** — a readiness to use violence to achieve ends or protect oneself in the absence of restraining public authority. This structural possibility of violence renders all cooperation, production, and cultural activity impossible.

## 理论内涵 / Theoretical Content

### 战争的三重逻辑 / The Triple Logic of War

bellum omnium 不是偶然的，而是由人性的三重动力**必然地**产生的：Bellum omnium is not accidental but **necessarily** generated by a triple dynamic of human nature:

**第一重：竞争驱动的战争 / First: Competition-driven war**
人们为争夺有限资源（食物、土地、配偶）而攻击他人。这是最直观的冲突形式，类似于动物界的资源竞争。People attack others to compete for limited resources (food, land, mates). This is the most intuitive form of conflict, analogous to resource competition in the animal kingdom.

**第二重：猜疑驱动的战争 / Second: Diffidence-driven war**
即使没有物质冲突，理性的人也会出于安全考量而**先发制人**地攻击潜在威胁者。这构成了"霍布斯陷阱"——A害怕B，所以A先发制人攻击B；B同样害怕A，所以B也有先发制人的动机。结果是相互恐惧的螺旋升级。Even without material conflict, rational individuals will attack potential threats **preemptively** out of security concerns. This constitutes the "Hobbesian trap" — A fears B, so A strikes preemptively; B equally fears A, so B has the same preemptive motive. The result is an escalating spiral of mutual fear.

**第三重：虚荣驱动的战争 / Third: Glory-driven war**
人们为了名誉、地位和尊严而冲突。虚荣是最难化解的冲突根源——因为名誉是**位置性商品**（positional good），不可能人人平等享有。People conflict over reputation, status, and dignity. Glory is the hardest source of conflict to resolve — because reputation is a **positional good** that cannot be equally shared by all.

### 战争状态 vs 实际战斗 / State of War vs Actual Battle

霍布斯的精确区分至关重要：Hobbes's precise distinction is crucial:

- **战争状态（State of War）** ≠ 时刻在战斗 / ≠ Constant fighting
- **战争状态（State of War）** = 战斗的**意愿**持续存在 + 没有公共权威阻止暴力 / = The **disposition** to fight persists + no public authority to prevent violence
- 类比：坏天气不等于一直下雨，而是指下雨的**倾向**持续存在 / Analogy: foul weather is not constant rain but the persistent **tendency** toward rain

这意味着即使两个理性人之间暂时没有发生暴力冲突，只要他们处于没有公共权力的条件下，他们就**处于战争状态**——因为暴力的可能性始终悬在头顶，使得信任和长期合作不可能。

This means that even if two rational individuals are not currently engaged in violence, as long as they exist without public power, they **are in a state of war** — because the possibility of violence hangs perpetually overhead, making trust and long-term cooperation impossible.

### 战争状态的结构性后果 / Structural Consequences of the State of War

在没有公共权力的条件下，以下一切都不可能：In the absence of public power, all of the following become impossible:

- **产业 / Industry** — 因为劳动果实不确定（随时可能被掠夺）/ Because the fruits of labor are uncertain (liable to be plundered at any time)
- **农业 / Agriculture** — 因为播种后无法确保收获 / Because one cannot ensure harvest after sowing
- **航海与贸易 / Navigation and trade** — 因为缺乏安全保障 / Because of lack of security guarantees
- **知识与艺术 / Knowledge and arts** — 因为生存压力使一切精神活动成为奢侈 / Because survival pressure makes all intellectual activity a luxury
- **社会 / Society** — 因为信任和合作不可能 / Because trust and cooperation are impossible
- 最可怕的：**持续的恐惧和暴死的危险 / The worst: continual fear and danger of violent death**

## 思想史脉络 / Intellectual History

bellum omnium 的思想先驱可追溯到修昔底德对科西拉革命的描述——在那里，一切社会纽带在党争中瓦解。罗马诗人卢克莱修描述了前文明时代的暴力生存。但霍布斯首次将"一切人反对一切人的战争"提升为**严格的概念工具**，用于推演政治秩序的必要性。

Intellectual precursors of bellum omnium can be traced to Thucydides' description of the Corcyrean revolution — where all social bonds dissolved in factional strife. The Roman poet Lucretius described violent existence in the pre-civil era. But Hobbes was the first to elevate the "war of all against all" into a **rigorous conceptual tool** for deducing the necessity of political order.

这一概念在思想史中引发了持久的争论。洛克坚持认为自然状态不必然等同于战争状态；卢梭认为自然人的孤独和自怜使其不可能发动普遍战争；当代人类学家质疑霍布斯的前政治想象是否符合人类早期社会的实际。然而，20世纪的两次世界大战、冷战核威慑和当代失败国家的经验不断为bellum omnium提供新的验证。

This concept has provoked enduring debate in intellectual history. Locke insisted the state of nature need not equal a state of war; Rousseau held that natural man's solitude and pity made universal war impossible; contemporary anthropologists question whether Hobbes's pre-political imagination matches the reality of early human societies. Yet 20th-century world wars, Cold War nuclear deterrence, and the experience of contemporary failed states continually provide new validation for bellum omnium.

## 关键文本 / Key Passages

> "Hereby it is manifest, that during the time men live without a common power to keep them all in awe, they are in that condition which is called war; and such a war, as is of every man, against every man."
> — *Leviathan*, Ch. 13

> "In such condition, there is no place for industry; because the fruit thereof is uncertain: and consequently no culture of the earth; no navigation, nor use of the commodities that may be imported by sea; no commodious building; no instruments of moving, and removing such things as require much force; no knowledge of the face of the earth; no account of time; no arts; no letters; no society; and which is worst of all, continual fear, and danger of violent death; and the life of man, solitary, poor, nasty, brutish, and short."
> — *Leviathan*, Ch. 13

> "For WARRE, consisteth not in Battell onely, or the act of fighting; but in a tract of time, wherein the Will to contend by Battell is sufficiently known."
> — *Leviathan*, Ch. 13

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

- **国际关系 / International Relations** — 国际体系的无政府状态是最接近bellum omnium的当代实例。国家间缺乏最高权威，安全困境（security dilemma）直接对应霍布斯的猜疑驱动冲突。The anarchic structure of the international system is the closest contemporary instance of bellum omnium. States lack a supreme authority, and the security dilemma directly corresponds to Hobbesian diffidence-driven conflict.

- **博弈论 / Game Theory** — 囚徒困境和多次博弈中的背叛激励在数学上形式化了bellum omnium的核心逻辑：个体理性导致集体非理性。The Prisoner's Dilemma and defection incentives in iterated games mathematically formalize the core logic of bellum omnium: individual rationality produces collective irrationality.

- **演化生物学 / Evolutionary Biology** — 动物种群内的资源竞争和领地冲突是自然界的bellum omnium。演化博弈论中的"鹰-鸽博弈"（Hawk-Dove game）模拟了霍布斯式冲突的演化动力学。Resource competition and territorial conflict within animal populations represent bellum omnium in nature. The "Hawk-Dove game" in evolutionary game theory models the evolutionary dynamics of Hobbesian conflict.

- **网络空间 / Cyberspace** — 匿名性、缺乏中央权威和攻击成本低廉使互联网成为数字版的bellum omnium——网络犯罪、网络战和信息战是其当代表现形式。Anonymity, lack of central authority, and low attack costs make the internet a digital version of bellum omnium — cybercrime, cyberwarfare, and information warfare are its contemporary manifestations.

- **组织管理 / Organizational Management** — 缺乏有效治理结构的组织内部会退化为bellum omnium——部门间的权力斗争和资源争夺。Organizations lacking effective governance structures degenerate into bellum omnium — interdepartmental power struggles and resource competition.

## 批评与回应 / Critiques and Responses

- **人类学批评 / Anthropological Critique**：早期人类社会并非"一切人反对一切人"——部落社会有复杂的合作机制、亲属制度和互惠规范。霍布斯将近代欧洲的个人主义投射到了全人类。Early human societies were not "all against all" — tribal societies had complex mechanisms of cooperation, kinship systems, and reciprocity norms. Hobbes projected early modern European individualism onto all of humanity.

- **卢梭式批评 / Rousseauian Critique**：自然人的基本情感是自怜（pitié），而非攻击性。战争状态不是自然的产物，而是文明（尤其是私有制）的产物。Natural man's fundamental sentiment is pity (*pitié*), not aggression. The state of war is not a product of nature but of civilization (especially private property).

- **女性主义批评 / Feminist Critique**：bellum omnium假设了孤立的男性战士形象，忽视了家庭、生育和照护关系在人类合作中的基础作用。Bellum omnium assumes the image of isolated male warriors, ignoring the foundational role of family, reproduction, and care relations in human cooperation.

- **回应 / Response**：霍布斯的支持者指出，bellum omnium是分析工具而非历史描述——其目的是通过反事实推理论证国家的必要性。即使在合作存在的条件下，猜疑驱动的冲突升级仍然是结构性风险。Hobbes's defenders argue that bellum omnium is an analytical tool, not a historical description — its purpose is to argue for the necessity of the state through counterfactual reasoning. Even where cooperation exists, diffidence-driven conflict escalation remains a structural risk.

## 跨文化回响 / Cross-cultural Echoes

佛教的"六道轮回"中的阿修罗道（asura realm）描绘了一个永恒争斗的存在状态——阿修罗因嫉妒、争斗和傲慢而永远处于战争之中，这与bellum omnium的结构性冲突有深刻的共鸣。不同的是，佛教将这一状态归因于业力和无明，而非理性计算的困境。

The Asura realm in the Buddhist "Six Realms of Rebirth" depicts a state of existence defined by perpetual conflict — Asuras, driven by jealousy, strife, and pride, are forever at war, which resonates profoundly with the structural conflict of bellum omnium. The difference is that Buddhism attributes this condition to karma and ignorance, not to the dilemmas of rational calculation.

印度政治思想中的"matsyanyaya"（大鱼吃小鱼法则）同样描述了无权威状态下的弱肉强食。《摩奴法典》和《政事论》（*Arthashastra*）都论证了国王（*danda*，惩罚之杖）对维持秩序的必要性，这与霍布斯以主权者终结bellum omnium的逻辑高度一致。

The Indian political concept of *matsyanyaya* (the law of the fish — big fish eat small fish) likewise describes the condition of the strong devouring the weak without authority. Both the *Manusmriti* and the *Arthashastra* argue for the necessity of the king (*danda*, the rod of punishment) to maintain order, which is highly consistent with Hobbes's logic of the sovereign ending bellum omnium.
