# 罗尔斯作品总览 · Rawls Works Bibliography

## 总览 / Overview

罗尔斯一生著作精简，以少数几部系统性著作构建了完整的正义理论体系。与同时代学者不同，他不追求产量而追求论证的严密与完整。他的每一部核心著作都经历了多年甚至数十年的打磨——《正义论》从1958年第一篇论文到1971年出版历经13年，《政治自由主义》从1985年讲座到1993年出版也历经8年。这种近乎苦行的写作方式使之成为20世纪学术产量最低但影响力最大的哲学家之一。

Rawls's lifetime output was remarkably concise, constructing a complete system of justice theory through a small number of systematic works. Unlike contemporaneous scholars, he pursued not quantity but rigor and completeness of argumentation. Each of his core works underwent years or even decades of refinement — *A Theory of Justice* took 13 years from the 1958 paper to its 1971 publication; *Political Liberalism* took 8 years from the 1985 lectures to its 1993 publication. This near-ascetic writing style made him one of the least prolific yet most influential philosophers of the twentieth century.

## 二、《正义论》结构 / Structure of *A Theory of Justice*

《正义论》分为三部分，共九章，600余页。其结构严谨，从基本原则到制度应用再到道德心理学，构成完整的理论体系。

*A Theory of Justice* is divided into three parts, nine chapters, over 600 pages. Its structure is rigorous, moving from basic principles to institutional application to moral psychology, forming a complete theoretical system.

### 第一部分：理论 / Part I: Theory
- **第1章：作为公平的正义 / Chapter 1: Justice as Fairness** — 核心框架的概述，提出正义二原则、原初状态与无知之幕
  Overview of the core framework, presenting the two principles, original position, and veil of ignorance
- **第2章：正义的原则 / Chapter 2: The Principles of Justice** — 详细阐述平等自由原则、差别原则、公平的机会平等
  Detailed exposition of equal liberty, the difference principle, fair equality of opportunity
- **第3章：原初状态 / Chapter 3: The Original Position** — 论证原初状态的设计理由、最大最小规则与正义原则的推导
  Arguments for the original position's design, the maximin rule, and derivation of justice principles
- **第4章：平等自由 / Chapter 4: Equal Liberty** — 良心自由、宽容、法治与基本自由的优先性
  Liberty of conscience, toleration, rule of law, and priority of basic liberties

### 第二部分：制度 / Part II: Institutions
- **第5章：分配的份额 / Chapter 5: Distributive Shares** — 经济正义的制度安排、代际正义（正义的储存原则）
  Institutional arrangements for economic justice, intergenerational justice (just savings principle)
- **第6章：义务与职责 / Chapter 6: Duty and Obligation** — 自然义务、公平原则与公民不服从
  Natural duties, the principle of fairness, and civil disobedience
- **第7章：善的概念 / Chapter 7: Goodness as Rationality** — 基本善、自尊、亚里士多德原则、善的弱理论
  Primary goods, self-respect, the Aristotelian Principle, thin theory of the good

### 第三部分：目的 / Part III: Ends
- **第8章：正义感 / Chapter 8: The Sense of Justice** — 道德心理学、道德发展的三个阶段
  Moral psychology, three stages of moral development
- **第9章：正义的善 / Chapter 9: The Goodness of Justice** — 稳定性论证、正义感与社会团结的理性基础
  Stability argument, rational basis of justice and social solidarity

## 六、关键版本信息 / Key Edition Information

### *A Theory of Justice*
- **1971 初版 / First Edition**: Harvard University Press — 原始版本，包含部分后来被修正的表述
- **1999 修订版 / Revised Edition**: Harvard University Press — 罗尔斯本人进行了重要修订，特别是在第3章中对最大最小规则的论证进行了加强
- 中译本以1999修订版为底本

### *Political Liberalism*
- **1993 初版 / First Edition**: Columbia University Press — 包含八次杜威讲座的修订稿
- **2005 扩展版 / Expanded Edition**: Columbia University Press — 增加了"The Idea of Public Reason Revisited"等重要论文
