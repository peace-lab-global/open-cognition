# 无知之幕 · Veil of Ignorance

## 概念概述 / Concept Overview

无知之幕是罗尔斯**原初状态**中的核心信息屏蔽装置——在选择社会基本规则时，各方**不知道**自己的社会地位、天赋、性别、种族、宗教信仰与人生计划，从而确保所选原则不偏袒任何特定群体。这一思想装置是20世纪政治哲学中最具原创性的概念之一，它将康德定言命令的普遍化测试转化为一个可操作的程序设计。

The Veil of Ignorance is the core information-blocking device within Rawls's **original position** — when choosing the basic rules of society, the parties **do not know** their social status, natural talents, gender, race, religious beliefs, or life plans, thereby ensuring that the chosen principles do not favor any particular group. This thought device is one of the most original concepts in twentieth-century political philosophy, transforming Kant's universalization test of the categorical imperative into an operational procedural design.

无知之幕的哲学功能在于将"公平"这一抽象道德理想转化为具体的程序约束。通过屏蔽那些可能导致偏见的个人信息，它迫使选择者从所有人的立场出发进行推理——你不是在为"你自己"选择规则，而是在为"任何可能处于任何位置的人"选择规则。这种立场的中立性正是正义的本质要求。

The philosophical function of the veil of ignorance lies in converting the abstract moral ideal of "fairness" into concrete procedural constraints. By blocking personal information that could lead to bias, it forces choosers to reason from everyone's standpoint — you are not choosing rules "for yourself," but choosing rules "for anyone who might occupy any position." This neutrality of standpoint is precisely what justice essentially requires.

## 理论内涵 / Theoretical Content

### 遮蔽的信息 / What Is Veiled

无知之幕遮蔽以下特定信息（The veil blocks the following specific information）：

- **社会身份 / Social identity**：阶层、财富、种族、性别、民族（class, wealth, race, gender, ethnicity）
- **自然禀赋 / Natural endowments**：智力、体力、才能、健康状况（intelligence, physical strength, talents, health status）
- **善的观念 / Conception of the good**：宗教信仰、人生目标、价值偏好（religious beliefs, life goals, value preferences）
- **社会状况 / Social circumstances**：文明程度、世代、人口规模（level of civilization, generation, population size）
- **心理特征 / Psychological traits**：风险偏好、乐观/悲观倾向（risk preferences, optimism/pessimism tendencies）

### 保留的信息 / What Is Retained

各方**知道**的一般事实（The parties **know** these general facts）：

- 人类社会的基本规律——经济学、心理学、政治学的一般法则（General laws of human society — economics, psychology, political science）
- 基本善的存在——权利、自由、机会、收入、自尊的社会基础（The existence of primary goods — rights, liberties, opportunities, income, social bases of self-respect）
- 社会合作的必要性与"中等程度的匮乏"（The necessity of social cooperation and "moderate scarcity"）
- 正义的环境——人们有冲突的利益但也有合作的可能（The circumstances of justice — people have conflicting interests but also the possibility of cooperation）

### 最大最小规则 / The Maximin Rule

无知之幕下的理性选择者使用**最大最小规则**（maximin rule）而非期望效用最大化：

| 方案 / Option | 最优者收益 / Best-off | 最差者收益 / Worst-off | 选择 / Choice |
|---|---|---|---|
| A: 功利主义 / Utilitarianism | 100 | -20 | 风险过高 / Too risky |
| B: 正义二原则 / Two Principles | 80 | 30 | **最大最小 / Maximin** |
| C: 严格平等 / Strict Equality | 50 | 50 | 牺牲效率 / Sacrifices efficiency |

罗尔斯论证：由于你不知道自己将是谁，而且选择是一次性的、不可逆的、影响一生基本利益的，你会优先确保**最坏情况下的最大收益**。这不是"保守"，而是在极端不确定性下的理性反应。

Rawls argues: since you do not know who you will be, and the choice is one-time, irreversible, and affects your fundamental life interests, you will prioritize ensuring **the greatest benefit in the worst case**. This is not "conservative," but a rational response under extreme uncertainty.

## 思想史脉络 / Intellectual History

无知之幕在思想史上有多重先驱。康德的定言命令要求"你只能意愿你行动的准则成为普遍法则"——这实质上要求行为者超越自身特殊立场进行普遍化思考。霍布斯和洛克的"自然状态"也是一种信息屏蔽的雏形——在自然状态中，没有人拥有压倒性的力量优势，这迫使所有人考虑缔结社会契约。

The veil of ignorance has multiple precursors in intellectual history. Kant's categorical imperative demands that "you should will that the maxim of your action become a universal law" — this essentially requires agents to universalize their thinking beyond their particular standpoint. Hobbes's and Locke's "state of nature" is also a proto-form of information blocking — in the state of nature, no one possesses overwhelming power advantage, which forces all to consider entering a social contract.

但罗尔斯的关键创新在于将信息屏蔽推向**彻底化**：不仅屏蔽关于力量的信息（如霍布斯），而是屏蔽**所有**可能导致偏见的特殊信息。这使得原初状态成为一个真正中立的论证场景——任何能够被用来为自己争取特权的知识都被排除。

But Rawls's key innovation lies in pushing information blocking to its **thoroughgoing conclusion**: not merely blocking information about power (as Hobbes does), but blocking **all** special information that could lead to bias. This makes the original position a genuinely neutral argumentative setting — any knowledge that could be used to claim privileges for oneself is excluded.

20世纪的类似思想实验还包括经济学家约翰·海萨尼（John Harsanyi）的"无知之幕"——但海萨尼从类似设置出发推导出的是**平均功利主义**而非罗尔斯的正义二原则。这一分歧成为政治哲学与福利经济学的核心争论之一。

Twentieth-century analogues also include economist John Harsanyi's "veil of ignorance" — but Harsanyi derived **average utilitarianism** rather than Rawls's two principles from a similar setup. This divergence has become one of the central debates in political philosophy and welfare economics.

## 关键文本 / Key Passages

> "在原初状态中，各方不知道他们在社会中的位置——他们的阶级地位或社会身份，他们在自然天赋和能力分配中的运气，他们的善的观念，他们的心理倾向的特征……他们在不偏不倚的条件下进行选择。"
>
> "In the original position, the parties do not know their place in society — their class position or social status, their fortune in the distribution of natural assets and abilities, their conception of the good, the particulars of their psychological propensities... They choose under conditions that are impartial."
> — *A Theory of Justice* (1971), §24

> "无知之幕的概念……是一个纯粹假设性的情境的特征……任何人在任何时候都可以进入原初状态，只要他遵循适当的限制。"
>
> "The concept of the veil of ignorance... characterizes a purely hypothetical situation... Anyone can enter the original position at any time, so long as he follows the appropriate restrictions."
> — *A Theory of Justice* (1971), §24

分析：罗尔斯强调无知之幕不是一个历史事件，而是一个**可反复进入的论证程序**。每个公民都可以在日常生活中进行这一思想实验——"如果我不知道自己将处于什么位置，我会选择什么样的规则？"这使无知之幕成为民主审议的思想工具。

Analysis: Rawls emphasizes that the veil of ignorance is not a historical event but a **repeatable justificatory procedure**. Every citizen can perform this thought experiment in everyday life — "If I did not know what position I would occupy, what kind of rules would I choose?" This makes the veil of ignorance a thought tool for democratic deliberation.

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

- **算法公平 / Algorithmic Fairness** — 设计AI算法时，设计者应假设自己可能成为任何被影响的群体成员。这为"公平机器学习"提供了规范性标准。
  When designing AI algorithms, designers should assume they could become any member of the affected groups. This provides normative standards for "fair machine learning."

- **立法审议 / Legislative Deliberation** — 立法者在制定政策时应想象自己可能处于任何社会位置，从而避免为特定利益集团量身定做的法律。
  Legislators should imagine themselves potentially occupying any social position when crafting policy, thereby avoiding legislation tailored to specific interest groups.

- **企业治理 / Corporate Governance** — 薪酬结构设计应考虑"如果我是最低薪员工，我能否接受这一薪酬差距？"
  Compensation structure design should consider: "If I were the lowest-paid employee, could I accept this pay gap?"

- **全球气候协议 / Global Climate Agreements** — 各国在谈判中应假设不知道自己的地理位置与发展水平，从而达成更公平的气候责任分担。
  Nations in negotiations should assume they do not know their own geographic location or development level, thereby reaching fairer climate responsibility sharing.

## 批评与回应 / Critiques and Responses

- **批评 / Critique**：过于抽象，脱离真实决策情境（Too abstract, detached from real decision-making contexts）
  → **回应 / Response**：罗尔斯明确说它是"纯粹假设性的"论证装置，其功能不是模拟真实决策，而是为正义原则提供论证基础。
  Rawls explicitly states it is a "purely hypothetical" justificatory device whose function is not to simulate real decisions but to provide a justificatory foundation for principles of justice.

- **批评 / Critique**（哈桑伊 Harsanyi）：无知之幕下应使用期望效用最大化，结果是平均功利主义（Should use expected utility maximization, yielding average utilitarianism）
  → **回应 / Response**：在涉及一生基本利益的"一次性、不可逆"决策中，期望效用计算不合理——你不会因为轮盘赌的期望收益为正就在枪口下赌命。
  In "one-time, irreversible" decisions affecting fundamental life interests, expected utility calculation is unreasonable — you would not bet your life on Russian roulette just because the expected payoff is positive.

- **批评 / Critique**（女性主义 Feminist）：无知之幕遮蔽了性别经验，忽视了关怀伦理（Veils gendered experience, ignores ethics of care）
  → **回应 / Response**：后期罗尔斯在《政治自由主义》中将性别正义纳入基本正义问题，公共理性概念包容关怀视角。
  The later Rawls incorporated gender justice into fundamental justice questions in *Political Liberalism*, and the concept of public reason accommodates care perspectives.

- **批评 / Critique**：隐含的个体主义忽视社群（Implicit individualism ignores community）
  → **回应 / Response**：《政治自由主义》中增加了"公共理性"的社群维度——公民在公共讨论中并非孤立的个体，而是相互尊重的共同体成员。
  *Political Liberalism* adds a communitarian dimension of "public reason" — citizens in public discussion are not isolated individuals but members of a community of mutual respect.

## 跨文化回响 / Cross-cultural Echoes

- **儒家"恕道" / Confucian "Shu" (Reciprocity)** — "己所不欲，勿施于人"与无知之幕共享一种角色互换的推理逻辑：在制定规则时，你应当考虑自己可能成为规则的承受者而非制定者。
  "Do not do unto others what you would not have them do unto you" shares with the veil of ignorance a role-reversal reasoning logic: when making rules, you should consider that you might be the recipient rather than the maker of those rules.

- **佛教"无我" / Buddhist "Anatta" (Non-self)** — 佛教的空性智慧消解了对"自我"的执着，这与无知之幕遮蔽自我信息的功能在结构上相似——两者都试图消除偏见以达至公正判断。
  Buddhist wisdom of emptiness dissolves attachment to "self," which is structurally similar to the veil of ignorance's function of blocking self-referential information — both attempt to eliminate bias to achieve impartial judgment.

- **墨子"兼爱" / Mozi's "Universal Love"** — 墨子主张不分亲疏远近地平等关怀所有人，这种超越特殊关系的普遍主义与无知之幕排除特殊知识的逻辑相呼应。
  Mozi advocated caring equally for all people regardless of proximity, and this universalism transcending special relationships echoes the veil of ignorance's logic of excluding special knowledge.
