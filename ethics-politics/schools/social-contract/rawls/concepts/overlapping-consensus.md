# 重叠共识 · Overlapping Consensus

## 概念概述 / Concept Overview

重叠共识是罗尔斯在《政治自由主义》（1993）中提出的核心理念——在一个存在合理多元主义的社会中，不同的宗教、哲学和道德学说虽然各自坚持不同的整全性世界观，但可以在**政治正义**的层面上汇聚于共同的原则。每种学说从自身的内部资源出发，都能为政治正义原则找到理由。共识不是"最小公分母"的妥协，而是**深层理由的汇聚**。

Overlapping Consensus is the core idea proposed by Rawls in *Political Liberalism* (1993) — in a society characterized by reasonable pluralism, different religious, philosophical, and moral doctrines, while each maintaining distinct comprehensive worldviews, can converge upon common principles at the level of **political justice**. Each doctrine, drawing from its own internal resources, can find reasons to endorse political justice principles. The consensus is not a "lowest common denominator" compromise, but a **convergence of deep reasons**.

这一概念标志着罗尔斯思想从《正义论》到《政治自由主义》的重大转向。《正义论》假设了社会对正义原则的普遍接受，但未充分解释在一个多元社会中这种普遍接受如何可能。重叠共识回答了这一问题：正义原则不需要建立在任何特定的哲学或宗教基础之上——它们可以成为各种不同的合理学说从各自不同角度共同支持的聚焦点。

This concept marks the major turn in Rawls's thought from *A Theory of Justice* to *Political Liberalism*. *A Theory of Justice* assumed widespread social acceptance of justice principles but did not fully explain how such acceptance is possible in a pluralistic society. Overlapping consensus answers this question: justice principles need not rest on any particular philosophical or religious foundation — they can serve as the focal point supported by diverse reasonable doctrines from their respective different angles.

## 理论内涵 / Theoretical Content

### 问题的产生 / How the Problem Arises

《正义论》（1971）假设了社会对正义原则的普遍接受。但现实中，合理的多元主义（reasonable pluralism）是自由制度的**必然结果**而非暂时缺陷。自由的良心自由和思想自由必然产生各种互不相容但各自合理的整全性学说。问题变成：持不同整全性学说的公民，如何共同接受一套政治正义原则？

*A Theory of Justice* (1971) assumed widespread social acceptance of justice principles. But in reality, reasonable pluralism is an **inevitable result** of free institutions rather than a temporary defect. Free conscience and freedom of thought inevitably produce diverse comprehensive doctrines that are mutually incompatible yet each individually reasonable. The question becomes: how can citizens holding different comprehensive doctrines jointly accept a set of political justice principles?

### 重叠共识的结构 / The Structure of Overlapping Consensus

```
        天主教整全性学说 / Catholic Comprehensive Doctrine
              ↓
   自由主义哲学 ──→ [正义二原则] ←── 康德伦理学
   Liberal Philosophy → [Two Principles] ← Kantian Ethics
              ↑          ↑
        伊斯兰现代派   儒家传统
   Islamic Modernism    Confucian Tradition
```

每种学说从**自身的内部资源**出发，都能为政治正义原则找到理由：
Each doctrine, from **its own internal resources**, can find reasons for political justice principles:

- **天主教徒**可能从自然法传统和社会正义教导中找到支持正义二原则的理由
  Catholics may find reasons supporting the two principles from natural law tradition and social justice teachings
- **康德主义者**从自律和人是目的的原则中推导出正义原则
  Kantians derive justice principles from autonomy and the principle that persons are ends
- **功利主义者**（温和的）可能论证正义二原则在长期中最有利于总体福利
  Utilitarians (moderate ones) may argue that the two principles best promote overall welfare in the long run
- **儒家学者**可能从仁政和天下为公的传统中找到支持公平机会和关怀弱者的理由
  Confucian scholars may find reasons supporting fair opportunity and care for the vulnerable in traditions of benevolent governance

### 与"权宜之计"的区别 / Distinction from Modus Vivendi

| 要素 / Element | 权宜之计 / Modus Vivendi | 重叠共识 / Overlapping Consensus |
|---|---|---|
| 基础 / Foundation | 利益平衡 / Balance of interests | 道德理由 / Moral reasons |
| 稳定性 / Stability | 条件改变即瓦解 / Collapses when conditions change | 基于深层承诺 / Based on deep commitments |
| 动机 / Motivation | 工具性的 / Instrumental | 道德性的 / Moral |
| 例子 / Example | 冷战中的核威慑平衡 / Cold War nuclear deterrence | 宪法原则的多元支持 / Plural support for constitutional principles |

### 合理的多元主义 / Reasonable Pluralism

罗尔斯区分**合理的**（reasonable）与**不合理的**（unreasonable）学说：
Rawls distinguishes **reasonable** from **unreasonable** doctrines:

- **合理学说 / Reasonable doctrines**：承认判断的负担（burdens of judgment），接受多元事实，尊重他人的合理观点。
  Acknowledge the burdens of judgment, accept the fact of pluralism, respect others' reasonable views.
- **不合理学说 / Unreasonable doctrines**：拒绝多元事实，主张强制统一（如原教旨主义、极权主义意识形态）。
  Reject the fact of pluralism, advocate enforced uniformity (e.g., fundamentalism, totalitarian ideologies).

重叠共识**不包括**不合理学说——它们被"遏制"（contained）而非被纳入共识。这是罗尔斯理论中最具争议的设计之一：它预设了对不合理学说的排斥，但这种排斥本身的正当性需要论证。

Overlapping consensus does **not include** unreasonable doctrines — they are "contained" rather than incorporated into the consensus. This is one of the most controversial design choices in Rawls's theory: it presupposes the exclusion of unreasonable doctrines, but the justification for this exclusion itself requires argumentation.

### 公共理性 / Public Reason

重叠共识的实践场所是**公共理性**——在涉及宪法根本（constitutional essentials）和基本正义问题的公共讨论中，公民应诉诸其他公民可以合理接受的推理方式，而非仅诉诸自己的整全性学说。

The practical arena for overlapping consensus is **public reason** — in public discussion concerning constitutional essentials and matters of basic justice, citizens should appeal to modes of reasoning that other citizens can reasonably accept, rather than solely appealing to their own comprehensive doctrines.

**但公共理性不禁止宗教语言 / But public reason does not prohibit religious language**——只要宗教论证能在适当时候被翻译为公共理性的语言，就可以进入公共讨论。这一"但书条款"（proviso）是罗尔斯1997年修正的重要成果。
As long as religious arguments can be translated into the language of public reason at an appropriate time, they may enter public discussion. This "proviso" was an important refinement Rawls made in 1997.

## 思想史脉络 / Intellectual History

重叠共识概念的提出有着深刻的思想史背景。宗教改革后的宗教战争表明，试图在整全性学说层面达成共识只会导致冲突和迫害。美国的宪政实践——特别是第一修正案对宗教自由的保护——展示了一种不同的可能性：公民可以在政治原则层面达成共识，同时在终极信仰层面保持分歧。

The proposal of the overlapping consensus concept has deep intellectual historical context. The religious wars following the Reformation demonstrated that attempting to achieve consensus at the level of comprehensive doctrine only leads to conflict and persecution. American constitutional practice — particularly the First Amendment's protection of religious freedom — demonstrated a different possibility: citizens can reach consensus on political principles while maintaining disagreement on ultimate beliefs.

约翰·洛克在《论宗教宽容》（1689）中已经区分了政治权力与宗教权威的不同领域。罗尔斯将这一区分深化为"政治的"与"整全性的"之间的区分——不仅宗教，任何整全性学说（包括世俗哲学如功利主义和康德伦理学）都不应成为政治正义的基础。

John Locke in *A Letter Concerning Toleration* (1689) already distinguished the domains of political power and religious authority. Rawls deepened this distinction into one between "the political" and "the comprehensive" — not only religion, but any comprehensive doctrine (including secular philosophies such as utilitarianism and Kantian ethics) should not serve as the foundation of political justice.

## 关键文本 / Key Passages

> "重叠共识不仅仅是一种关于接受某些权威或参与某些制度安排的共识……它是在一种道德观念上达成的共识，而这一道德观念本身需要由某种道德概念来支撑。"
>
> "An overlapping consensus is not merely a consensus on accepting certain authorities or on participation in certain institutional arrangements... It is a consensus on a moral conception, and this moral conception needs to be supported by a moral concept."
> — *Political Liberalism* (1993), Lecture IV

> "公共理性的理想……要求公民能够在彼此之间就根本政治问题以他们可以合理期望其他公民依据他们作为自由平等公民的共同人类理性可以接受的理由来加以解释和辩护。"
>
> "The ideal of public reason... requires that citizens be able to justify to one another on fundamental political questions in terms each could reasonably expect that others might endorse as consistent with their shared human reason as free and equal citizens."
> — "The Idea of Public Reason Revisited" (1997)

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

- **多元文化社会的治理 / Governance of Multicultural Societies** — 如何在不强加特定文化价值观的前提下维持社会正义？重叠共识提供了一个框架：不要求公民放弃自己的文化认同，而是要求他们从各自的传统中找到支持共同政治原则的理由。
  How to maintain social justice without imposing specific cultural values? Overlapping consensus provides a framework: it does not require citizens to abandon their cultural identity, but asks them to find reasons within their own traditions for supporting common political principles.

- **宗教自由与国家法律 / Religious Freedom and State Law** — 宗教学校、头巾禁令、同性婚姻等争议中，重叠共识要求：国家不能以特定宗教教义为立法基础，但可以容纳各宗教传统从自身出发对公共正义原则的支持。
  In controversies over religious schools, headscarf bans, same-sex marriage, overlapping consensus requires: the state cannot base legislation on particular religious doctrines, but can accommodate each religious tradition's support for public justice principles from within its own framework.

- **全球治理 / Global Governance** — 不同文明传统（西方自由主义、儒家传统、伊斯兰传统、印度传统）如何在全球正义问题上达成共识？重叠共识的国际版本不要求各国采纳相同的政治哲学，而是寻求不同传统对基本人权和发展正义的汇聚支持。
  How can different civilizational traditions (Western liberalism, Confucian tradition, Islamic tradition, Indian tradition) reach consensus on global justice? An international version of overlapping consensus does not require nations to adopt the same political philosophy, but seeks convergent support from different traditions for basic human rights and development justice.

- **宪法裁判 / Constitutional Adjudication** — 法官在裁决涉及基本正义的案件时，应诉诸公共理性——即所有合理公民都能接受的理由——而非个人的道德或宗教信念。
  When adjudicating cases involving basic justice, judges should appeal to public reason — reasons that all reasonable citizens can accept — rather than personal moral or religious convictions.

## 批评与回应 / Critiques and Responses

- **批评 / Critique**（哈贝马斯 Habermas）：重叠共识过于保守——它预设了各学说的"给定"不变性，忽视了通过公共审议**改变**人们偏好的可能性。
  Overlapping consensus is too conservative — it presupposes the "given" immutability of doctrines, neglecting the possibility of **changing** people's preferences through public deliberation.
  → **回应 / Response**：罗尔斯承认重叠共识是动态的——学说本身可以在参与共识的过程中发生内部变化，而且公共理性本身就是一种审议实践。
  Rawls acknowledges that overlapping consensus is dynamic — doctrines themselves can undergo internal changes through participation in the consensus, and public reason is itself a deliberative practice.

- **批评 / Critique**（桑德尔 Sandel）：重叠共识预设了政治可以与道德/宗教分离，这本身就是一个有争议的哲学立场——许多宗教传统恰恰主张其信仰覆盖全部生活。
  Overlapping consensus presupposes that politics can be separated from morality/religion, which is itself a controversial philosophical position — many religious traditions precisely claim that their faith covers all of life.
  → **回应 / Response**：罗尔斯不否认这一张力，但他论证说在合理的多元主义条件下，这是维持自由社会的唯一可行途径——不分离就意味着某种学说对另一种学说的强制。
  Rawls does not deny this tension, but argues that under conditions of reasonable pluralism, this is the only viable path to maintaining a free society — non-separation means one doctrine's coercion of another.

- **批评 / Critique**（沃尔泽 Walzer）：正义原则不能脱离特定社群的共享理解——不存在超越所有文化的普遍正义原则。
  Justice principles cannot be detached from particular communities' shared understandings — there are no universal justice principles transcending all cultures.
  → **回应 / Response**：罗尔斯同意正义原则不是"形而上学的"——它们是在特定政治文化（自由民主）中产生的，但它们的合理可接受性可以超越单一文化。
  Rawls agrees that justice principles are not "metaphysical" — they arise within a particular political culture (liberal democracy), but their reasonable acceptability can transcend a single culture.

## 跨文化回响 / Cross-cultural Echoes

- **儒家"和而不同" / Confucian "Harmony in Diversity"** — "君子和而不同，小人同而不和"（《论语·子路》）——这正是重叠共识的精神：不追求思想上的统一（同），而是在差异中寻求和谐的合作（和）。
  "The gentleman harmonizes but does not uniformize; the petty person uniformizes but does not harmonize" (*Analects*, Zi Lu) — this is precisely the spirit of overlapping consensus: not pursuing intellectual uniformity (tong), but seeking harmonious cooperation amid diversity (he).

- **佛教"方便法门" / Buddhist "Skillful Means" (Upaya)** — 佛教承认不同根器的众生需要不同的教化方式（方便法门），但最终都指向同一解脱目标。这与重叠共识的结构类似——不同的整全性学说从各自的角度通向共同的政治正义。
  Buddhism acknowledges that beings of different capacities need different teaching methods (skillful means), but all ultimately point toward the same goal of liberation. This parallels the structure of overlapping consensus — different comprehensive doctrines lead from their respective angles to common political justice.

- **伊斯兰"舒拉"（协商）/ Islamic "Shura" (Consultation)** — 伊斯兰传统中的舒拉原则要求通过协商达成共识，这为伊斯兰现代派参与重叠共识提供了内部资源。
  The Shura principle in Islamic tradition requires achieving consensus through consultation, providing internal resources for Islamic modernists to participate in overlapping consensus.
