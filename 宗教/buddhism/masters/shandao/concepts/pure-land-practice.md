# 净土修行 Pure Land Practice

> "往生之事，不在行者之功夫，而在弥陀之本愿。"——善导思想要旨
> "The matter of rebirth depends not on the practitioner's own attainment, but on Amitābha's Original Vow." — The essential purport of Shandao's thought

---

## 定义 Definition

净土修行是指以往生阿弥陀佛极乐世界（Sukhāvatī）为目标的佛教修行体系。在善导的诠释中，净土修行的核心不是积累功德或修习禅定，而是"信受弥陀救度、专称弥陀佛名、愿生弥陀净土"——以信心、称名、愿生三要素构成一个完整的修行路径。这一路径被善导称为"易行道"，与依靠自力断惑证真的"难行道"形成对比。

Pure Land practice refers to the Buddhist practice system aimed at rebirth in Amitābha's Pure Land of Ultimate Bliss (Sukhāvatī). In Shandao's interpretation, the core of Pure Land practice is not the accumulation of merit or the cultivation of meditation, but rather "accepting Amitābha's salvation, exclusively reciting Amitābha's Name, and aspiring to be born in Amitābha's Pure Land" — a complete path composed of three elements: faith, recitation, and aspiration. Shandao called this the "Easy Path," in contrast to the "Difficult Path" of relying on one's own power to eradicate delusions and realize truth.

## 净土的意涵 The Meaning of the Pure Land

### 极乐世界的描述 Description of Sukhāvatī

根据净土三部经（《无量寿经》《观无量寿佛经》《阿弥陀经》）的描述，极乐世界是阿弥陀佛在因地修行时发四十八大愿、经无量劫修行所成就的清净国土。其地平坦宽广，由七宝（金、银、琉璃、玻璃、砗磲、赤珠、玛瑙）庄严，没有三恶道（地狱、饿鬼、畜生），没有老病死苦。国土中有七宝池、八功德水、种种奇妙杂色之鸟昼夜出和雅音，演说佛法。往生者皆具三十二相，寿命无量，直至成佛。

According to the three Pure Land sūtras (the Larger Sukhāvatīvyūha Sūtra, the Contemplation Sūtra, and the Smaller Sukhāvatīvyūha Sūtra), Sukhāvatī is the pure land accomplished through Amitābha's forty-eight great vows and his practice over immeasurable kalpas. Its ground is flat and vast, adorned with seven treasures (gold, silver, lapis lazuli, crystal, tridacna shell, red pearl, agate). It is free from the three evil destinies (hell, hungry ghosts, animals) and from the sufferings of aging, illness, and death. The land features pools of seven treasures, waters with eight merits, and wondrous multicolored birds that sing harmonious Dharma teachings day and night. All beings reborn there possess the thirty-two marks of a great being and enjoy immeasurable lifespans until they attain Buddhahood.

### 净土的象征意义 Symbolic Meaning

善导虽然强调极乐世界的真实存在（凡夫入报——凡夫可以真实地往生真实的报土），但极乐世界的描述同时也蕴含深刻的象征意义。七宝庄严象征佛的无量功德；光明无量象征佛的智慧遍照一切；寿命无量象征佛的法身常住不灭；国土中没有苦难象征涅槃的究竟安乐。

While Shandao emphasized the real existence of Sukhāvatī (ordinary beings genuinely entering the true reward land), the descriptions of the Pure Land simultaneously carry profound symbolic meaning. The seven-treasure adornments symbolize the Buddha's immeasurable merit; the boundless light symbolizes the Buddha's wisdom pervading all; the boundless lifespan symbolizes the Buddha's eternal Dharmakāya; the absence of suffering symbolizes the ultimate bliss of nirvāṇa.

## 往生的条件 Conditions for Rebirth

### 善导的"三心"说 Shandao's "Three Minds"

善导在《观经四帖疏》中提出，往生净土需要具备"三心"：

In his Commentary on the Contemplation Sūtra, Shandao proposed that rebirth requires the "Three Minds" (sān xīn):

1. **至诚心**（Sincere Mind）：真实无伪的信心，不是表面的信仰，而是内心深处对弥陀救度的全然信任。善导强调"至诚"即"真实"，反对虚假的形式主义。

   **Sincere Mind** (zhìchéng xīn): Genuine, authentic faith — not superficial belief, but a wholehearted, deep trust in Amitābha's salvation. Shandao emphasized that "sincere" means "real," opposing hypocritical formalism.

2. **深心**（Deep Mind）：深刻的信心，包含两层含义——深信自己是"罪恶生死凡夫"（旷劫以来流转生死），以及深信阿弥陀佛的本愿力能够救度自己。这种"机法两种深信"（对根机和法门的深信）是善导思想的重要特征。

   **Deep Mind** (shēn xīn): Profound faith with two dimensions — deep conviction that one is "a sinful ordinary being流转ing in birth and death since beginningless kalpas," and deep conviction that Amitābha's vow-power can save oneself. This "twofold deep faith regarding the person and the Dharma" is a distinctive feature of Shandao's thought.

3. **回向发愿心**（Mind of Merit-Transfer and Aspiration）：将自己的一切善根功德回向净土，发愿往生极乐世界。这不是将自己修行所得的功德"转移"到净土，而是将自己与佛的功德融为一体，以净土为目标。

   **Mind of Merit-Transfer and Aspiration** (huíxiàng fāyuàn xīn): Dedicating all one's roots of goodness and merit toward the Pure Land, vowing to be reborn in Sukhāvatī. This is not "transferring" one's earned merit to the Pure Land, but rather merging oneself with the Buddha's merit, taking the Pure Land as one's goal.

### 具足三心即得往生 Rebirth upon Possessing the Three Minds

善导明确主张："具三心者，必得往生。"三心具足的标志就是"专称弥陀佛名"——以信心称念佛名、愿生净土。三心与称名不是分离的：信心是称名的内在基础，称名是信心的外在表现，愿生则是两者的方向。

Shandao stated explicitly: "Those who possess the Three Minds will assuredly attain rebirth." The sign of possessing the Three Minds is "exclusively reciting Amitābha's Name" — reciting with faith and aspiring to the Pure Land. The Three Minds and recitation are not separate: faith is the inner foundation of recitation, recitation is the outer expression of faith, and aspiration is the direction of both.

## 修行的日常生活 Daily Life of Practice

### 行住坐卧皆念佛 Nianfo in All Postures

善导倡导的净土修行不是脱离日常的苦修，而是融入生活的每一个时刻。"行住坐卧，不问时节久近，念念不舍"意味着念佛不限于佛堂之中，而是贯穿于劳作、饮食、睡眠之间。这种"生活化的修行"是净土法门能够深入民间的重要原因。

The Pure Land practice Shandao advocated is not asceticism removed from daily life, but practice integrated into every moment. "Walking, standing, sitting, or lying down, regardless of duration, never ceasing" means that nianfo is not confined to the meditation hall, but permeates work, meals, and sleep. This "life-integrated practice" is a major reason why the Pure Land Dharma gate penetrated so deeply among the common people.

### 净土礼仪 Pure Land Liturgy

善导编撰了多种净土礼仪文本，包括《往生礼赞》和《法事赞》，为净土行者提供了系统的日常修行框架。这些礼仪包括：朝暮礼拜阿弥陀佛、诵念净土经典、唱赞佛偈、回向发愿等。这些仪轨至今仍是汉传佛教净土修行的重要组成部分。

Shandao compiled various Pure Land liturgical texts, including the Hymns of Rebirth Praise and Liturgical Hymns, providing practitioners with a systematic framework for daily practice. These liturgies include: morning and evening prostrations to Amitābha, chanting of Pure Land sūtras, singing hymns of praise, and merit-transfer with aspiration. These ritual forms remain an important component of Chinese Buddhist Pure Land practice to this day.

## 往生的品位 Grades of Rebirth

### 九品往生 The Nine Grades

《观经》提出往生净土有"九品"（上上、上中、上下、中上、中中、中下、下上、下中、下下），根据修行者的根机和修行深浅而有差别。善导对此进行了革命性的诠释：他认为九品往生者都是凡夫，差别在于修行的精勤程度，而非修行者的阶位高低。更重要的是，不论哪一品，往生的根本原因都是阿弥陀佛的本愿力，而非行者自身的修行功德。

The Contemplation Sūtra proposes "nine grades" of rebirth (from the highest of the highest grade to the lowest of the lowest grade), varying according to the practitioner's capacity and depth of practice. Shandao offered a revolutionary interpretation: he argued that beings of all nine grades are ordinary beings, and the differences lie in the diligence of practice, not in the practitioners' spiritual rank. Most importantly, regardless of grade, the fundamental cause of rebirth is Amitābha's vow-power, not the practitioner's own merit.

### 凡夫入报的平等性 The Egalitarianism of Ordinary Beings Entering the Reward Land

善导的"凡夫入报"说打破了传统佛教中圣者与凡夫之间的壁垒。在他的体系中，一位精进念佛的居士与一位高深的禅师在往生面前是平等的——他们同样依赖佛的他力，同样往生同一报土。这种平等性是净土法门能够吸引广大信众的根本原因之一。

Shandao's doctrine of "ordinary beings entering the reward land" broke down the barriers between saints and ordinary beings in traditional Buddhism. In his system, a devoted lay nianfo practitioner and a profound Chan meditation master stand equal before rebirth — both rely equally on the Buddha's Other-Power, both are reborn in the same reward land. This egalitarianism is one of the fundamental reasons why the Pure Land Dharma gate attracted such vast numbers of adherents.

## 净土修行与其他宗派的比较 Pure Land Practice Compared with Other Schools

善导将净土修行定位为"易行道"，与其他宗派的"难行道"形成对比。天台宗主张通过止观禅定证入实相；华严宗强调法界圆融的哲学观照；禅宗追求直指人心见性成佛。这些路径都需要极高的根器和长期的精进修持。净土法门则提供了一条"凡夫可行"的道路——不需要断除烦恼，不需要深入禅定，只需要信心和称名。

Shandao positioned Pure Land practice as the "Easy Path," contrasting with other schools' "Difficult Path." Tiantai advocates realizing ultimate reality through calming-and-insight meditation; Huayan emphasizes philosophical contemplation of the interpenetration of all phenomena; Chan pursues direct pointing to the mind to see one's nature and become Buddha. These paths all require exceptionally high capacities and prolonged diligent practice. The Pure Land gate offers a "path accessible to ordinary beings" — requiring neither the eradication of afflictions nor profound meditation, only faith and recitation.

## 关键引用 Key Quotations

- "一切凡夫，不问罪福多少、时节久近，但能上尽百年，下至一日七日，一心专念弥陀名号，定得往生。" ——善导《观经四帖疏》
- "All ordinary beings, regardless of how much sin or merit they have, or how long they practice — whether for a full hundred years above, or down to a single day or seven days below — if they can single-mindedly and exclusively recite Amitābha's Name, they will assuredly attain rebirth." — Shandao, Commentary on the Contemplation Sūtra
