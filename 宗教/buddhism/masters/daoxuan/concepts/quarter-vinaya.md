# 四分通大乘 · The Dharmaguptaka Vinaya as a Mahāyāna Path

> "律为声闻之轨，而道通菩萨之行。"
> "The Vinaya is the norm for Śrāvakas, yet its path extends to the practice of Bodhisattvas."

---

## 一、问题的起源 · Origin of the Problem

汉传佛教面临一个根本的理论困境：它在教理上自视为**大乘法门**（Mahāyāna），但其僧团所依循的戒律却来自**声闻律藏**——具体而言，是法藏部（Dharmaguptaka）的《四分律》。这一矛盾在南北朝至初唐时期引发了广泛的讨论。

Chinese Buddhism faced a fundamental theoretical dilemma: doctrinally it identified itself as **Mahāyāna**, yet the monastic discipline followed by its saṅgha derived from a **Śrāvaka Vinaya** — specifically, the *Dharmaguptaka Vinaya* (四分律). This contradiction provoked extensive discussion from the Northern and Southern Dynasties through the early Tang period.

核心问题是：如果《四分律》属于声闻律，那么大乘僧团依之受戒和持戒，是否在理论上自相矛盾？汉传佛教的戒律实践是否具有大乘的合法性？

The core question: if the *Dharmaguptaka Vinaya* belongs to the Śrāvaka canon, is it theoretically contradictory for a Mahāyāna saṅgha to receive and observe its precepts? Does Chinese Buddhist precept practice possess Mahāyāna legitimacy?

---

## 二、道宣之前的讨论 · Pre-Daoxuan Discussion

### 2.1 慧光（468–537）的初步尝试 · Huiguang's Initial Attempt

地论师慧光最早提出《四分律》可以通向大乘的观点。他认为《四分律》中包含了某些大乘思想的因子，因此不应被简单地归为小乘。但慧光的论证尚不充分系统。

The Dilun master Huiguang was the first to propose that the *Dharmaguptaka Vinaya* could extend to the Mahāyāna. He argued that the Vinaya contained certain proto-Mahāyāna elements and should not be simply classified as Hīnayāna. However, his argument was not yet fully systematized.

### 2.2 法砺（569–635）的保守立场 · Fali's Conservative Position

相部宗创始人法砺持较为保守的立场，认为《四分律》本质上属于声闻乘，不应被强行解释为大乘。他主张大乘僧团应当另外依据菩萨戒（如《梵网经》菩萨戒）来补充声闻戒的不足。

Fali, founder of the Xiangbu school, held a more conservative position, maintaining that the *Dharmaguptaka Vinaya* essentially belongs to the Śrāvaka vehicle and should not be forced into a Mahāyāna interpretation. He advocated that a Mahāyāna saṅgha should additionally rely on the Bodhisattva precepts (such as those in the *Brahmajāla Sūtra*) to supplement the shortcomings of the Śrāvaka precepts.

## 三、道宣的五重论证 · Daoxuan's Five Arguments

道宣在《行事钞》中系统地提出了**五个论证**（"五义"或"五重"），证明《四分律》可以通向大乘：

In his *Xingshi Chao* (行事钞), Daoxuan systematically advanced **five arguments** (五义) demonstrating that the *Dharmaguptaka Vinaya* can extend to the Mahāyāna:

### 3.1 第一义：沓婆回心 · First Argument: The Conversion of Dabba

《四分律》中记载了沓婆摩罗子（Dabba Mallaputta）的故事。沓婆原是声闻阿罗汉，后来发心回向大乘。道宣认为，这表明《四分律》本身就包含了声闻行者回心向大的可能性。

The *Dharmaguptaka Vinaya* records the story of Dabba Mallaputta, originally a Śrāvaka arhat who later aroused the aspiration to turn toward the Mahāyāna. Daoxuan argued that this demonstrates the Vinaya's own acknowledgment of the possibility that Śrāvaka practitioners can redirect themselves toward the Mahāyāna.

### 3.2 第二义：施生成佛 · Second Argument: Dāna Leading to Buddhahood

《四分律》中多处提到布施等修行可以导向成佛。道宣认为，如果一部纯粹的小乘律典，不应该出现导向成佛的说法；这说明《四分律》的精神实质上与大乘相通。

The *Dharmaguptaka Vinaya* contains multiple references to dāna (giving) and other practices leading to Buddhahood. Daoxuan argued that a purely Hīnayāna Vinaya would not contain such references to the path to Buddhahood; this indicates that the spiritual substance of the *Dharmaguptaka Vinaya* is in fact consonant with the Mahāyāna.

### 3.3 第三义：戒体通大乘 · Third Argument: Precept-Substance Extends to Mahāyāna

这是道宣最具独创性的论证。他认为，受戒时如果行者发了菩提心（bodhicitta），那么通过羯磨仪式所获得的**戒体**本身就具有了大乘的性质。戒体的性质取决于受戒者的发心，而非仅仅取决于戒律文本的形式归属。

This is Daoxuan's most original argument. He held that if the practitioner arouses *bodhicitta* during ordination, then the **precept-substance** (戒体) acquired through the karma ritual itself possesses a Mahāyāna character. The nature of the precept-substance depends on the ordinand's aspiration, not merely on the formal sectarian classification of the Vinaya text.

### 3.4 第四义：识了尘境界 · Fourth Argument: Consciousness Apprehends Objects

《四分律》中某些关于心识与认知过程的讨论，与唯识学的观点相呼应。道宣认为这表明《四分律》中隐含了大乘唯识学的因子。

Certain discussions in the *Dharmaguptaka Vinaya* regarding consciousness and cognitive processes echo Yogācāra views. Daoxuan argued that this indicates latent Yogācāra elements within the Vinaya.

### 3.5 第五义：舍财用轻 · Fifth Argument: Light Punishment for Property Offenses

《四分律》对某些涉及财物的犯戒行为处罚较轻，这在大乘佛教的慈悲精神下可以得到更好的解释——大乘佛教更强调内心的动机而非外在的行为结果。

The *Dharmaguptaka Vinaya* imposes lighter penalties for certain property-related offenses, which can be better explained under the Mahāyāna spirit of compassion — Mahāyāna emphasizes internal motivation over external behavioral outcomes.

## 四、理论意义 · Theoretical Significance

### 4.1 解决了汉传佛教的核心困境 · Resolving a Core Dilemma

道宣的"四分通大乘"论证，为汉传佛教的戒律实践提供了**大乘的合法性基础**。从此，汉传僧团可以安心地依循《四分律》受戒持戒，而不必担心自己的戒律实践与大乘信仰之间的矛盾。

Daoxuan's argumentation provided the **Mahāyāna legitimacy foundation** for Chinese Buddhist precept practice. Henceforth, the Chinese saṅgha could confidently receive and observe precepts according to the *Dharmaguptaka Vinaya* without fearing a contradiction between its precept practice and its Mahāyāna faith.

### 4.2 戒律诠释学的方法论 · Methodology of Vinaya Hermeneutics

道宣的方法展示了一种精密的**戒律诠释学**：他并非否认《四分律》的声闻来源，而是通过重新诠释其精神实质，使其适应大乘佛教的需要。这种方法论对当代宗教文本的诠释仍有启发意义。

Daoxuan's approach demonstrates a sophisticated **Vinaya hermeneutics**: he did not deny the Śrāvaka origins of the *Dharmaguptaka Vinaya* but re-interpreted its spiritual substance to suit the needs of Mahāyāna Buddhism. This methodology remains instructive for contemporary religious textual interpretation.

### 4.3 声闻与大乘的桥梁 · Bridge Between Śrāvaka and Mahāyāna

"四分通大乘"的理论实质上在声闻戒律和大乘菩萨道之间建立了一座**桥梁**，使得两者可以在同一个修行体系中共存和互补，而不是彼此排斥。

The thesis effectively builds a **bridge** between Śrāvaka Vinaya and the Bodhisattva path, enabling the two to coexist and complement each other within a single practice framework rather than being mutually exclusive.

## 六、当代讨论 · Contemporary Discussion

在现代学术界，"四分通大乘"的理论仍然引发着讨论：

In modern scholarship, the Dharmaguptaka-as-Mahāyāna thesis continues to provoke discussion:

- **平川彰**（Hirakawa Akira）从文献学角度研究了《四分律》的部派归属，确认其属于法藏部（Dharmaguptaka），但对道宣的大乘化诠释持保留态度。
  **Hirakawa Akira** studied the sectarian affiliation of the *Dharmaguptaka Vinaya* from a philological perspective, confirming its Dharmaguptaka origin but reserving judgment on Daoxuan's Mahāyāna interpretation.

- **印顺法师**认为道宣的论证虽然有创意，但有些论证牵强，尤其是从"沓婆回心"推出整部律典通大乘的逻辑值得商榷。
  **Yin Shun** considered Daoxuan's arguments creative but some strained — particularly the logic of inferring that the entire Vinaya extends to Mahāyāna from the Dabba story is debatable.

- **当代汉传佛教实践**：在实际层面，汉传佛教僧团仍然依循《四分律》受戒，同时受持菩萨戒，两者并行不悖。道宣的理论在实践中得到了验证。
  **Contemporary East Asian practice**: In practice, the Chinese Buddhist saṅgha continues to receive ordination according to the *Dharmaguptaka Vinaya* while simultaneously receiving Bodhisattva precepts — the two practiced in parallel. Daoxuan's theory has been validated in practice.

---

## 七、与菩萨戒的关系 · Relationship to Bodhisattva Precepts

需要指出的是，道宣的"四分通大乘"理论并不排斥菩萨戒。相反，他主张声闻戒（别解脱戒）和菩萨戒应当**同时受持**：

It should be noted that Daoxuan's thesis does not exclude the Bodhisattva precepts. Rather, he advocated that the Śrāvaka precepts (prātimokṣa) and the Bodhisattva precepts should be **received and observed simultaneously**:

- **声闻戒**（别解脱戒）：侧重于外在行为的规范，是修行的基础。
  **Śrāvaka precepts** (prātimokṣa): Emphasize external behavioral norms as the foundation of practice.

- **菩萨戒**：侧重于内在心念的净化和利他行的实践，是修行的提升。
  **Bodhisattva precepts**: Emphasize inner mental purification and altruistic practice as the elevation of practice.

两者相辅相成，共同构成汉传佛教完整的戒律体系。

The two are complementary, together constituting the complete Vinaya system of East Asian Buddhism.
