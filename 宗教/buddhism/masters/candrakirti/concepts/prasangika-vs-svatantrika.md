# 应成 vs 自续 / Prasangika vs Svatantrika

## 定义 / Definition

**应成派 (Prasangika)** 与**自续派 (Svatantrika)** 是中观学派内部两大传统。分歧的起源可追溯到佛护 (Buddhapalita) 与清辨 (Bhaviveka) 对龙树《中论》第一章注释的方法论争论。月称在《明句论》中明确站在佛护一边，系统批判清辨的自立因方法，确立了应成派的独立身份。在藏传佛教中，宗喀巴进一步深化了这一区分，认为两派的分歧不仅是方法论的，更是对空性理解深度的根本差异。

**Prasangika** ("Consequentialist") and **Svatantrika** ("Autonomist") are the two major traditions within Madhyamaka. The divide traces back to the methodological dispute between Buddhapalita and Bhaviveka over the interpretation of Nagarjuna's *Mulamadhyamakakarika* Chapter 1. Candrakirti, in his *Prasannapada*, explicitly sided with Buddhapalita and systematically critiqued Bhaviveka's autonomous-reasoning method. In Tibetan Buddhism, Tsongkhapa deepened this distinction, arguing the divide reflects not merely methodology but fundamentally different depths of emptiness realization.

## 梵藏语义 / Sanskrit-Tibetan Semantics

- **Prasaṅgika** (应成派): 以归谬法 (prasaṅga) 为方法论特征的学派
- **Svātantrika** (自续派): 使用自立因 (svatantra-hetu) 的学派
- **dbu ma rang rgyud pa** (藏文): 中观自续派
- **dbu ma thal 'gyur ba** (藏文): 中观应成派
- **thal 'gyur** (应成): 字面意思"必然推出"——即归谬

## 历史起源 / Historical Origins

### 佛护与清辨的争论 The Buddhapalita-Bhaviveka Debate

龙树《中论》第一章"观因缘品"分析因果关系的四种可能（自生、他生、共生、无因生），全部否定。注释者们面对一个方法论选择：

- **佛护 Buddhapalita** (约 470-540): 只用归谬法——展示"自生"等主张的内在矛盾，不自立任何替代性因果理论
- **清辨 Bhaviveka** (约 500-570): 认为仅用归谬不够——中观行者需要以自立因 (svatantra-hetu) 正面论证"诸法无自性"

**Buddhapalita used only reductio: showing the internal contradictions of positions like "self-production" without asserting an alternative theory of causation. Bhaviveka argued this was insufficient — the Madhyamika must positively establish "all phenomena lack inherent existence" through autonomous reasoning (svatantra-hetu).**

### 月称的介入 Candrakirti's Intervention

月称在《明句论》中详细回应了清辨对佛护的批评，为佛护的纯归谬方法辩护。他论证说：

1. 清辨使用自立因，暗含了对认识量 (pramana) 和所量 (prameya) 的自性承许
2. 中观行者不应有自己独立的宗义 (paksa)，否则就与实有论者无异
3. 归谬法足以令具缘者通达空性，不需要额外的自立论证

**Candrakirti defended Buddhapalita's pure-reductio method: (1) Bhaviveka's use of autonomous reasoning implicitly accepts inherent existence of epistemic instruments (pramana) and objects (prameya); (2) the Madhyamika should hold no independent thesis (paksa); (3) reductio alone suffices for those with the capacity to realize emptiness.**

## 系统性对比 / Systematic Comparison

## 宗喀巴的深化 / Tsongkhapa's Deepening

宗喀巴对月称的应成-自续区分进行了革命性的深化。他指出，两派最根本的分歧在于**名言中是否承许"以自相成立" (svalaksana-siddha)**：

- **自续派**: 认为在世俗名言中，事物以某种"自相" (svalaksana) 成立——虽然胜义中无自性，但名言中事物有其固有的特征
- **应成派**: 即使在世俗名言中，事物也完全不以"自相"成立——一切纯粹是假名安立 (prajnapti-matra)

这意味着：自续派的空性不够彻底——他们在名言中保留了一种微细的自性承许。

**Tsongkhapa identifies the root difference: whether one accepts existence by way of own-character (svalaksana-siddha) at the conventional level. Svatantrika holds that conventionally, things exist by some "own-character" (svalaksana). Prasangika holds that even conventionally, nothing exists by way of own-character — everything is mere conceptual designation (prajnapti-matra). Svatantrika's emptiness is thus less thorough.**

## 自续派的内部分化 / Svatantrika Subdivisions

在藏传分类中，自续派又分为两支：

### 经部行中观自续派 Sautrantika-Svatantrika-Madhyamaka

- 代表：清辨 Bhaviveka
- 特征：名言中承许外境存在（类似经量部的认识论），但胜义中无自性

### 瑜伽行中观自续派 Yogacara-Svatantrika-Madhyamaka

- 代表：寂护 Santaraksita、莲花戒 Kamalasila
- 特征：名言中承许唯识（外境不存在），胜义中无自性
- 将唯识的认识论整合入中观的本体论

**Svatantrika subdivides into: Sautrantika-Svatantrika (Bhaviveka — accepts external objects conventionally) and Yogacara-Svatantrika (Santaraksita — accepts mind-only conventionally). The latter integrates Yogacara epistemology into Madhyamaka ontology.**

## 当代学术争论 / Contemporary Scholarly Debates

### "应成-自续区分是藏传佛教的发明吗？"

当代西方佛教学者 (如 Dreyfus, McClintock, Eckel) 就这一问题展开了激烈讨论：

- **支持"藏传发明说"**: 在印度文献中，没有明确的"应成派"和"自续派"的自我标识——这一分类是藏传佛教（特别是宗喀巴传统）的回溯性建构
- **反对"藏传发明说"**: 虽然术语可能是后起的，但方法论分歧在印度文献中有明确的文本依据——月称《明句论》中对清辨的批判就是明证

**Western scholars debate whether the Prasangika-Svatantrika distinction is a Tibetan invention. Some argue it is a retrospective construction by Tibetan Buddhism (especially the Tsongkhapa tradition); others counter that while the terminology may be later, the methodological divide has clear textual basis in Indian sources.**

### 哲学意义的评估

无论历史起源如何，应成-自续区分在哲学上是极其富有成果的 (philosophically fruitful)——它迫使思考者精确界定：

1. 批判的边界在哪里？
2. 否定是否需要正面立场来支撑？
3. "空"到什么程度才算彻底？
