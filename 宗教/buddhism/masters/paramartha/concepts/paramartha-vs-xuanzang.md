# 真谛vs玄奘系唯识 / Paramārtha vs. Xuánzàng Yogācāra (真谛vs玄奘系唯识)

> "旧译新译，各有宗承；八识九识，理路迥异。"

---

## 概述 / Overview

真谛系唯识与玄奘系唯识是汉传佛教中唯识学的两条主要传承路线。两者虽然都以无著—世亲的瑜伽行派为共同源头，但在识论、种姓论、如来藏观等核心问题上存在深刻分歧。这些分歧不仅反映了翻译和诠释的差异，更代表了印度唯识学内部不同流派的理论分歧。

Paramārtha's and Xuánzàng's Yogācāra systems represent the two principal lineages of Chinese Yogācāra. Although both trace their origins to the Asaṅga–Vasubandhu tradition, they diverge profoundly on core issues: consciousness theory, lineage doctrine, and the status of tathāgata-garbha. These divergences reflect not merely translational or interpretive differences but genuine theoretical schisms within Indian Yogācāra itself.

---

## 历史背景 / Historical Background

### 两条传承线 / Two Lineages

唯识学在印度的发展并非铁板一块。在世亲之后，唯识学内部出现了不同的诠释传统：

Yogācāra in India was not monolithic. After Vasubandhu, different interpretive traditions emerged:

- **安慧系**（Sthiramati, c.475–555）：倾向于将唯识学与如来藏思想融合，强调"心性本净"。这一系可能影响了真谛的唯识学。
  **Sthiramati lineage** (c.475–555): Tended toward integrating Yogācāra with tathāgata-garbha thought, emphasizing "natural luminosity of mind." This tradition likely influenced Paramārtha.

- **护法系**（Dharmapāla, c.530–561）：强调唯识学的"纯粹性"，严格区分有为法（识）与无为法（真如），不融合如来藏思想。护法的弟子戒贤（Śīlabhadra）是玄奘在印度的老师。
  **Dharmapāla lineage** (c.530–561): Emphasized Yogācāra's "purity," strictly distinguishing conditioned phenomena (consciousness) from unconditioned reality (suchness), without integrating tathāgata-garbha. Dharmapāla's disciple Śīlabhadra was Xuánzàng's teacher in India.

真谛于546年来华，玄奘于645年归国。两人相差约一个世纪，所传的唯识学代表了印度唯识学发展的不同阶段和不同流派。

Paramārtha arrived in China in 546; Xuánzàng returned in 645. Separated by roughly a century, they transmitted Yogācāra systems representing different stages and lineages of Indian Yogācāra development.

## 核心分歧 / Core Divergences

### 一、识数之争：八识 vs 九识 / Consciousness Count: Eight vs. Nine

| 维度 | 真谛系 | 玄奘系 |
|------|--------|--------|
| 识数 | 九识（前五+第六+第七+第八+第九阿摩罗识） | 八识（前五+第六+第七+第八） |
| 第九识 | 阿摩罗识（无垢识），是转依后的清净识 | 不承认独立的第九识，认为清净状态是第八识转为大圆镜智 |

**真谛的立场**：阿摩罗识（amalavijñāna）是转识成智后的清净识，有独立的存在论地位。将清净识单列出来，强调了觉悟不只是杂染的"消灭"，而是有积极的清净内容。

**Paramārtha's position**: The amala-vijñāna is the purified consciousness after transformation, with independent ontological status. Listing it separately emphasizes that awakening is not merely the "destruction" of defilement but has positive pure content.

**玄奘的立场**：不需要另立第九识。第八阿赖耶识在转依后成为"大圆镜智"（mahādarśa-jñāna），这已经足够描述清净状态。"阿摩罗识"只是第八识转依后的别名，不是独立的识体。

**Xuánzàng's position**: No need for a ninth consciousness. The eighth ālaya transforms into "great mirror wisdom" after transformation, which suffices to describe the pure state. "Amala-vijñāna" is merely an alternate name for the transformed eighth, not an independent consciousness.

### 二、阿黎耶 vs 阿赖耶 / Ālīyé vs. Ālàyé

| 维度 | 真谛（阿黎耶识） | 玄奘（阿赖耶识） |
|------|------------------|------------------|
| 翻译 | "黎耶"强调"藏"义（repository） | "赖耶"强调"执藏"义（clinging-storehouse） |
| 性质 | 中性的种子储存处 | 被第七识执着为"我"的问题性存在 |
| 与如来藏的关系 | "不一不异"——可以融合 | 严格区分——不可混淆 |
| 解性 | 本具"解性"（觉悟的可能性） | 不含先天的觉悟根据（无漏种子非普遍存在） |

**真谛**：阿黎耶识是一个中性的功能概念——它是种子的"仓库"，本身不预设染净。更重要的是，阿黎耶识中先天包含"解性"（清净种子），这使得觉悟具有内在的可能性。

**Paramārtha**: The ālaya is a neutral functional concept—a "warehouse" of seeds that does not intrinsically presuppose purity or defilement. Crucially, it innately contains "awakening-nature" (pure seeds), making awakening internally possible.

**玄奘**：阿赖耶识的得名正是因为第七识执着它为"自我"——这个名字本身就暗示了一个需要被超越的问题状态。阿赖耶识中虽然也有无漏种子，但这些种子不是所有有情都具有的（无种姓有情可能没有）。

**Xuánzàng**: The ālaya is so named because the seventh consciousness clings to it as "self"—the name itself implies a problematic state requiring transcendence. While it contains undefiled seeds, these are not universally possessed (beings without lineage may lack them).

### 三、种姓论：一性皆成 vs 五种姓 / Lineage Theory: Universal vs. Five-fold

| 维度 | 真谛系 | 玄奘系 |
|------|--------|--------|
| 主张 | 一性皆成（一切有情皆能成佛） | 五种姓（五类有情各有不同果位） |
| 一阐提 | 最终也能成佛 | 永远不能成佛（或至少不能成佛） |
| 理论依据 | 解性黎耶——阿黎耶识中本具解性 | 无漏种子的有无——部分有情缺乏无漏种子 |
| 经典依据 | 《法华经》《涅槃经》等 | 《解深密经》《瑜伽师地论》等 |

这是两个体系之间最具争议性的分歧。玄奘在印度学习时，曾就这个问题请教戒贤。据《大唐西域记》记载，玄奘倾向于"一性皆成"，但戒贤以护法系的"五种姓"说回应。玄奘回国后，忠实传授了护法—戒贤的"五种姓"说，引发了中国佛教界的激烈争论。

This is the most contentious divergence. When Xuánzàng studied in India, he reportedly raised this question with Śīlabhadra. According to the *Great Tang Records on the Western Regions*, Xuánzàng leaned toward universal Buddhahood, but Śīlabhadra upheld the Dharmapāla "five-lineage" position. Upon returning to China, Xuánzàng faithfully transmitted this doctrine, sparking fierce controversy.

### 四、如来藏的定位 / The Status of Tathāgata-garbha

| 维度 | 真谛系 | 玄奘系 |
|------|--------|--------|
| 态度 | 积极融合——如来藏与阿黎耶识"不一不异" | 保持距离——如来藏是真如的别名，不是独立的实体 |
| 定位 | 如来藏是唯识学的有机组成部分 | 如来藏是"方便说"，需要被"转"入唯识框架 |
| 风险 | 可能滑向"真常唯心论" | 可能过于"干燥"，缺乏修行的内在动力 |

**真谛**：如来藏思想与唯识学不是对立的，而是互补的。如来藏描述了阿黎耶识的"深层本质"——当杂染被净化后，阿黎耶识的真实面目就是如来藏。

**Paramārtha**: Tathāgata-garbha and Yogācāra are complementary, not opposed. Tathāgata-garbha describes the ālaya's "deep essence"—when defilements are purified, the ālaya's true nature is tathāgata-garbha.

**玄奘**：如来藏只是"真如"（tathatā）的另一个名字，属于"无为法"。它不是一个独立的实体或"真心"。将如来藏视为一个积极的实体，有落入"梵我论"（ātman-vāda）的危险。

**Xuánzàng**: Tathāgata-garbha is merely another name for "suchness" (*tathatā*), belonging to "unconditioned phenomena." It is not an independent entity or "true mind." Treating it as a positive entity risks falling into *ātman-vāda* (self-doctrine).

### 五、三性的诠释 / Interpretation of the Three Natures

| 维度 | 真谛系 | 玄奘系 |
|------|--------|--------|
| 依他起与圆成实 | 倾向于融合——依他起的本质即圆成实 | 严格区分——依他起是有为法，圆成实是无为法 |
| 遍计所执 | 纯粹的虚妄 | 纯粹的虚妄 |
| 重点 | 三性最终归于一性（圆成实） | 三性各有自性，不可混淆 |

## 哲学分析 / Philosophical Analysis

### 根本分歧："性相"关系 / The Fundamental Divergence: Nature and Characteristics

上述所有分歧可以归结为一个根本的哲学问题：**法性**（dharmatā，诸法的真实本性）与**法相**（dharma-lakṣaṇa，诸法的现象特征）之间的关系是什么？

All the above divergences can be reduced to one fundamental philosophical question: **What is the relationship between *dharmatā* (the true nature of phenomena) and *dharma-lakṣaṇa* (the phenomenal characteristics of phenomena)?**

- **真谛系**倾向于"性相融合"：法相的深层本质就是法性。识（法相）的究竟实相就是真如（法性）。因此，阿黎耶识（识）与如来藏（真如）可以"不一不异"。
  **Paramārtha** tends toward "integration of nature and characteristics": the deep essence of phenomenal characteristics is their true nature. Consciousness's ultimate reality is suchness. Therefore, ālaya (consciousness) and tathāgata-garbha (suchness) can be "neither identical nor different."

- **玄奘系**坚持"性相分离"：法性（真如、无为法）与法相（识、有为法）是两个不同的层面，不可混淆。真如是"不变的"，识是"生灭的"。将二者混同，会导致"真常唯心论"的错误。
  **Xuánzàng** insists on "separation of nature and characteristics": suchness (unconditioned) and consciousness (conditioned) are distinct levels that must not be conflated. Suchness is "unchanging"; consciousness is "arising and ceasing." Conflating them leads to the error of "true-permanent idealism."

### 两种思维范式 / Two Paradigms

从更抽象的层面看，两个体系代表了两种不同的哲学思维范式：

At a more abstract level, the two systems represent different philosophical paradigms:

**真谛系：综合的、辩证的范式。** 倾向于在对立中寻求统一，在差异中发现深层的一致性。这种范式更适合处理"超越对立"的宗教体验。

**Paramārtha: Synthetic, dialectical paradigm.** Tends to seek unity within opposition, discovering deep coherence beneath apparent difference. This paradigm better accommodates religious experience that "transcends opposition."

**玄奘系：分析的、精确的范式。** 倾向于严格区分不同的概念层次，追求理论的精确性和内在一致性。这种范式更适合学术研究和逻辑论证。

**Xuánzàng: Analytic, precisionist paradigm.** Tends to strictly distinguish conceptual levels, pursuing theoretical precision and internal consistency. This paradigm better serves academic study and logical argumentation.

## 历史影响 / Historical Impact

### 摄论宗的兴衰 / Rise and Fall of the Shèlùn School

真谛创立的摄论宗在隋代达到鼎盛，但在唐代随法相宗的兴起而衰落。法相宗借助唐太宗和唐高宗的皇权支持，以及玄奘巨大的个人声望，迅速成为唯识学的"正统"传承。摄论宗被边缘化为"旧译"传统。

The Shèlùn School founded by Paramārtha flourished during the Suí dynasty but declined in the Táng as the Fǎxiàng School rose. Backed by imperial patronage under Táng Tàizōng and Táng Gāozōng, and by Xuánzàng's immense personal prestige, Fǎxiàng rapidly became the "orthodox" Yogācāra lineage, marginalizing Shèlùn as the "old translation" tradition.

### 间接影响 / Indirect Influence

尽管摄论宗作为独立宗派消亡了，但其思想遗产通过其他渠道延续：

Although the Shèlùn School ceased as an independent institution, its intellectual legacy persisted through other channels:

1. **天台宗**：智者大师的"一念三千"学说吸收了摄论宗的心识分析。
   **Tiāntái**: Zhìzhě's "three thousand realms in a single thought-moment" absorbed Shèlùn consciousness analysis.

2. **华严宗**：法藏的"性起"学说与摄论宗的如来藏思想有深刻的联系。
   **Huáyán**: Fǎzàng's "nature-arising" doctrine connects deeply with Shèlùn tathāgata-garbha thought.

3. **禅宗**：禅宗的"即心即佛"思想与摄论宗的"一性皆成"和解性黎耶有思想上的共鸣。
   **Chán**: The "mind itself is Buddha" teaching resonates with Shèlùn's universal Buddhahood and awakening-nature.

## 当代学术评价 / Contemporary Scholarly Assessment

当代佛教学者对两个体系的评价趋于平衡和多元：

Contemporary Buddhist scholars offer balanced and pluralistic assessments:

- **不再简单地以"对错"衡量**：过去的中国佛教传统倾向于以玄奘系为"正确"标准来评判真谛系。当代学者更倾向于将两者视为唯识学内部的合理分歧。
  **Beyond "right vs. wrong"**: Earlier Chinese Buddhist tradition tended to judge Paramārtha by Xuánzàng's standards. Contemporary scholars tend to view both as legitimate internal divergences within Yogācāra.

- **重视真谛的独立价值**：真谛的唯识学不是"不完整的玄奘唯识"，而是代表了印度唯识学的另一条重要传承线。
  **Valuing Paramārtha independently**: Paramārtha's Yogācāra is not "incomplete Xuánzàng Yogācāra" but represents another important Indian Yogācāra lineage.

- **比较研究的价值**：两个体系的比较为理解印度唯识学的多元面貌提供了宝贵的窗口。
  **Value of comparative study**: Comparing the two systems provides a valuable window into the pluralistic landscape of Indian Yogācāra.
