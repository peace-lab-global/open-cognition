# 求法精神 The Spirit of Dharma-Seeking

## 定义 / Definition

**求法精神**是指中国佛教僧人为寻求佛法原典、补全教义缺失，而不惜远涉万里、冒生死危险赴印度（或西域）取经的精神传统。法显是这一传统的真正开创者——他以 65 岁高龄出发，历经 14 年，穿越沙漠、翻越雪山、渡过大海，同行十余人中唯有他一人完成全程并携经归国。

**The spirit of Dharma-seeking** refers to the tradition of Chinese Buddhist monks who, driven by the desire to obtain original Buddhist scriptures and complete doctrinal gaps, undertook perilous journeys of thousands of miles to India (or the Western Regions) at the risk of their lives. Faxian was the true pioneer of this tradition — departing at age 65, spending 14 years crossing deserts, traversing snow mountains, and sailing seas. Of the dozen or more companions who set out together, only he completed the entire journey and returned with scriptures.

## 法显的出发 / Faxian's Departure

### 65 岁的决断

公元 399 年（东晋隆安三年），法显已年届 65 岁。在那个平均寿命远低于今天的时代，65 岁已是高龄。法显从 3 岁起出家，一生浸润于佛学，深知中国佛教在律藏方面的严重缺失。他的出发不是年轻人的冒险冲动，而是一位老僧深思熟虑后的坚定抉择。

In 399 CE, Faxian was already 65 years old. In an era when average life expectancy was far lower than today, 65 was advanced age indeed. Ordained since the age of 3, Faxian had spent his entire life immersed in Buddhist learning and was acutely aware of the severe deficiency in China's Vinaya canon. His departure was not a young man's impulsive adventure but the resolute decision of an elderly monk after careful deliberation.

### 同行者

法显并非独自出发。与他同行的还有慧景、道整、慧应、慧嵬等十余位僧人。然而，这条漫长而危险的道路逐渐淘汰了几乎所有人：

Faxian did not travel alone. He set out with over ten fellow monks including Huijing, Daozheng, Huiying, and Huiwei. However, the long and perilous road gradually eliminated nearly all of them:

- **慧景 Huijing**: 在翻越小雪山时因体力不支而亡 / Died while crossing the Lesser Snow Mountains from physical exhaustion
- **道整 Daozheng**: 到达印度后决定留居不归 / Reached India and decided to remain permanently
- **慧应 Huiying**: 在途中去世 / Died en route
- **慧嵬 Huiwei**: 中途折返 / Turned back midway
- **其他同伴**: 或因疾病、或因畏难、或因其他原因相继离散 / Dispersed due to illness, fear, or other reasons

最终，只有法显一人完成了从出发到归国的全程。

In the end, only Faxian completed the full journey from departure to return.

## 旅途中的艰难 / Hardships Along the Way

### 沙漠与西域

从长安出发后，法显经河西走廊进入西域。穿越塔克拉玛干沙漠边缘的旅途极为艰苦——缺水、酷热、沙暴是常态。法显在《佛国记》中记载了沙河（沙漠地带）的恐怖："沙河中多有恶鬼热风……遇则皆死，全无遗骸。"

After departing Chang'an, Faxian traveled through the Hexi Corridor into the Western Regions. The journey along the edges of the Taklamakan Desert was extremely harsh — water scarcity, extreme heat, and sandstorms were constant. In his *Record*, Faxian described the terrors of the沙河 (Sand River / desert zone): "In the沙河 there are many evil spirits and hot winds... those who encounter them all die, leaving no remains."

### 翻越葱岭

法显翻越了帕米尔高原（葱岭），这段行程是整个旅途中最危险的陆路部分之一。高海拔、严寒、陡峭的山路使许多旅行者丧命。法显记载："岭冬种夏收，无日不风雪……冰雪经旬，寒风切骨。"

Faxian crossed the Pamir Plateau (Congling / Onion Range), one of the most dangerous overland segments of the entire journey. High altitude, extreme cold, and precipitous mountain paths claimed many travelers' lives. Faxian recorded: "On the range, one plants in winter and harvests in summer; no day passes without wind and snow... ice and snow for weeks on end, the cold wind cutting to the bone."

### 印度的漫长学习

到达印度后，法显花了约十年时间周游各地、学习梵文、抄写经典。他访问了佛教的诸多圣地——佛陀诞生地蓝毗尼、成道处菩提伽耶、初转法轮处鹿野苑、涅槃处拘尸那迦——以及那烂陀、华氏城等学术重镇。

After reaching India, Faxian spent approximately ten years traveling throughout the subcontinent, studying Sanskrit, and copying scriptures. He visited many of Buddhism's holiest sites — Lumbini (birthplace), Bodh Gaya (enlightenment), Sarnath (first sermon), and Kushinagar (parinirvana) — as well as major academic centers like Nalanda and Pataliputra.

### 海上的风暴

法显从斯里兰卡乘商船归国时，遭遇了猛烈的海上风暴。船上的商人们惊恐万状，有人要把法显推下船以减轻重量（或因为迷信）。一位居士挺身而出保护了法显。船在风暴中漂流多日，最终偏离航线到达爪哇。

When Faxian departed Sri Lanka aboard a merchant vessel, the ship encountered a violent storm at sea. The merchants aboard were terrified; some wanted to throw Faxian overboard to lighten the ship (or out of superstition). A lay Buddhist passenger stepped forward to protect him. The ship drifted for many days in the storm, eventually being blown off course to Java.

## 精神动力的分析 / Analysis of Spiritual Motivation

### 宗教信念

法显的精神力量首先来自深厚的佛教信仰。在佛教传统中，"法"(Dharma) 是三宝（佛、法、僧）之一，是佛教存续的根基。法显视补全律藏为延续佛法慧命的使命——这不仅是个人的学术追求，更是对整个佛教社群的责任。

Faxian's spiritual strength derived first from deep Buddhist faith. In Buddhist tradition, the Dharma is one of the Three Jewels (Buddha, Dharma, Sangha) and the foundation of Buddhism's survival. Faxian regarded completing the Vinaya canon as a mission to sustain the Dharma's living transmission — not merely a personal academic pursuit but a responsibility to the entire Buddhist community.

### 对知识完整性的追求

法显的行为体现了中国佛教的一种深层文化心理：对知识**完整性**的执着追求。在法显看来，中国佛教缺少完整的律藏，就如同一个有机体缺少了关键的器官——僧团无法在缺乏完整戒律的情况下健康发展。这种对"完整"的追求驱动了一代又一代的求法僧。

Faxian's actions embodied a deep cultural 心理学 within Chinese Buddhism: an unwavering pursuit of **knowledge completeness**. In Faxian's view, Chinese Buddhism lacking a complete Vinaya was like an organism missing a vital organ — the monastic community could not develop healthily without complete monastic discipline. This pursuit of "completeness" drove generation after generation of Dharma-seeking monks.

### 大乘菩萨精神

法显的求法行为与大乘佛教的菩萨道精神密切相关。菩萨为了利益众生不惜一切，法显为了中国佛教的完善而不顾个人安危，体现了"为法忘躯"的大乘精神。他在《佛国记》中多次表达对佛法传播的关切，而非对个人修行的关注。

Faxian's Dharma-seeking was closely related to the Mahayana bodhisattva spirit. Just as a bodhisattva spares no effort for the benefit of all beings, Faxian disregarded personal safety for the improvement of Chinese Buddhism, embodying the Mahayana ideal of "forgetting one's body for the Dharma." Throughout his *Record*, he repeatedly expressed concern for the propagation of Buddhism rather than for personal cultivation.

## 当代意义 / Contemporary Significance

### 学术执着

法显的求法精神对当代学术界仍有启示：对知识完整性的追求往往需要超越舒适区，甚至承担巨大的个人代价。在当今信息过载但深度不足的时代，法显那种"为求真知不惜万里"的精神尤为珍贵。

Faxian's spirit of Dharma-seeking remains relevant to contemporary academia: the pursuit of knowledge completeness often requires transcending one's comfort zone and even bearing significant personal cost. In today's age of information overload but insufficient depth, Faxian's spirit of "traveling ten thousand miles for authentic knowledge" is especially precious.

### 跨文化勇气

法显以一位年迈的中国僧人之身，独自行走于完全陌生的文化和地理环境中，学习新语言，与不同文化背景的人交流。这种跨文化勇气在今天全球化的语境中仍然值得敬佩。

Faxian, as an elderly Chinese monk, traveled alone through completely unfamiliar cultural and geographical environments, learned new languages, and communicated with people of diverse cultural backgrounds. This cross-cultural courage remains admirable in today's globalized context.
