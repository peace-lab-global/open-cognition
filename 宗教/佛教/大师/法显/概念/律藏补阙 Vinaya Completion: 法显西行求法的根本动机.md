# 律藏补阙 Vinaya Completion

## 定义 / Definition

**律藏补阙**是法显西行求法的根本动机——补全中国佛教缺失的完整律藏（戒律典籍，Vinaya）。在法显时代，中国虽已有大量经藏和论藏传入，但完整的广律（Vinaya-vibhanga / 律分别）极度匮乏，导致中国僧团在受戒、安居、布萨等核心僧事活动上缺乏权威规范。法显"慨律藏残阙"，遂以 65 岁高龄立志亲赴印度寻求。

**Vinaya completion** was the fundamental motivation behind Faxian's westward journey — to fill the critical gaps in the Chinese Buddhist Vinaya canon. In Faxian's era, while substantial Sutra and Sastra collections had reached China, complete monastic codes (broad Vinaya / 广律) were severely lacking, leaving Chinese monastic communities without authoritative guidance for core monastic affairs including ordination, rainy-season retreat, and posadha ceremonies.

## 背景：中国律藏的缺失 / Background: The Vinaya Deficit in China

### 佛教传入初期的律学状况

佛教约在公元 1 世纪传入中国，但在随后的三百年间，翻译工作的重心主要在经藏（sutra）和论藏（sastra/abhidharma）上。完整的广律——即包含比丘戒、比丘尼戒的详细条文、每条戒的制戒因缘、以及僧团运作的完整规范——的翻译严重滞后。

Buddhism reached China around the 1st century CE, but for the following three centuries, translation efforts focused primarily on Sutras and Sastras. The translation of complete broad Vinaya texts — containing detailed regulations for monks and nuns, the circumstances behind each rule's establishment, and comprehensive guidelines for monastic community operations — lagged far behind.

### 道安的呼吁

法显的精神先驱道安（312-385）是第一位系统整理佛典目录的中国学者。他在编纂《综理众经目录》时痛感律藏的缺失，多次呼吁派遣僧人赴印度和西域寻求完整律典。道安的呼吁直接影响了法显一代僧人的求法志向。

Dao'an (312-385), Faxian's spiritual predecessor, was the first Chinese scholar to systematically catalogue Buddhist texts. While compiling his catalogue, he keenly felt the absence of complete Vinaya texts and repeatedly called for monks to be sent to India and the Western Regions to obtain them. Dao'an's advocacy directly influenced the Dharma-seeking aspirations of Faxian's generation.

### 已有的不完整律学文献

在法显出发前，中国已有的律学文献主要是零散的戒本（pratimoksa / 波罗提木叉）和戒律的摘要，而非完整的广律。这些文献虽然提供了基本的戒条清单，但缺少每条戒的详细释义、制戒因缘和判例分析，无法满足僧团日常运作的需要。

Before Faxian's departure, the Vinaya materials available in China consisted mainly of scattered pratimoksa texts and Vinaya summaries rather than complete broad Vinaya codes. While these provided basic lists of precepts, they lacked detailed commentary on each rule, the circumstances of their establishment, and case-law analysis necessary for day-to-day monastic governance.

## 法显的律典寻求之旅 / Faxian's Vinaya-Seeking Journey

### 在北印度的发现

法显在北印度（包括犍陀罗、乌苌等地）发现当地主要流行的是有部律（Sarvastivada-vinaya）。他在乌苌国（Uddiyana，今巴基斯坦斯瓦特河谷）获得了部分有部律典，但并不完整。法显意识到，要获得最完整的律藏，必须前往中印度——佛教的发源地。

In northern India (including Gandhara and Uddiyana), Faxian found that the Sarvastivada-vinaya was predominant. He obtained partial Sarvastivada Vinaya texts in Uddiyana (modern Swat Valley, Pakistan), but found them incomplete. Realizing that the most complete Vinaya collections would be found in central India — Buddhism's birthplace — he pressed onward.

### 在华氏城的突破

法显最终在摩竭提国的华氏城（Pataliputra，今巴特那）取得了重大突破。他在那里获得了完整的**大众部广律（Mahasanghika-vinaya / 摩诃僧祇律）**——这是大众部所传的完整律藏，包含比丘和比丘尼戒的详细条文。此外，他还获得了**有部的《萨婆多律》**抄本。

Faxian achieved his major breakthrough at Pataliputra (modern Patna) in Magadha. There he obtained the complete **Mahasanghika-vinaya** (摩诃僧祇律) — the full monastic code of the Mahasanghika school, containing detailed regulations for monks and nuns. He also acquired manuscripts of the **Sarvastivada Vinaya** (萨婆多律).

### 在斯里兰卡的补充

法显在斯里兰卡（师子国）停留两年期间，又获得了**弥沙塞部的律典（Mahisasaka-vinaya）**以及其他部派的律学文献。这些补充性的发现进一步丰富了他所携归的律藏收藏。

During his two-year stay in Sri Lanka (the "Lion Country" / 师子国), Faxian additionally obtained the **Mahisasaka-vinaya** (弥沙塞律) and other Vinaya 文学 from various schools. These supplementary discoveries further enriched the Vinaya collection he would bring back to China.

## 翻译成果 / Translation Achievements

### 《摩诃僧祇律》四十卷

法显归国后，在建业（今南京）与印度僧人佛陀跋陀罗（Buddhabhadra）合作，将携归的《摩诃僧祇律》翻译为汉文，共 40 卷。这是中国佛教史上最重要的律典翻译之一。

After returning to China, Faxian collaborated with the Indian monk Buddhabhadra at Jianye (modern Nanjing) to translate the *Mahasanghika-vinaya* into Chinese, producing a 40-fascicle work. This stands as one of the most important Vinaya translations in Chinese Buddhist history.

**《摩诃僧祇律》的内容 / Content of the Mahasanghika-vinaya:**

- 比丘戒（约 218 条）及其详细释义
- 比丘尼戒（约 290 条）及其详细释义
- 每条戒的制戒因缘（佛陀为何制定此戒）
- 僧团运作的规范（布萨、安居、自恣等）
- 各种僧事活动的判例和处理方式

### 《萨婆多部钞律》

法显还翻译了有部的《萨婆多部钞律》，但此译本后来失传。不过它在法显时代曾一度流通，对中国早期律学研究产生了影响。

Faxian also translated the Sarvastivada *Vinaya*, but this translation was subsequently lost. However, it circulated during Faxian's era and influenced early Chinese Vinaya studies.

## 律藏补阙的历史意义 / Historical Significance

### 填补中国佛教的结构性空缺

法显带回并翻译的律典，第一次为中国僧团提供了完整的广律文本。在此之前，中国僧团在很多僧事活动上不得不依赖零散的戒本和口传惯例。《摩诃僧祇律》的翻译使中国佛教在制度建设上迈出了关键一步。

The Vinaya texts Faxian brought back and translated provided Chinese monastic communities with complete broad Vinaya texts for the first time. Previously, Chinese monks had to rely on scattered pratimoksa texts and oral traditions for many monastic affairs. The translation of the *Mahasanghika-vinaya* marked a critical step forward in Chinese Buddhist institutional development.

### 为后世律学奠基

虽然道宣（596-667）后来以《四分律》（Dharmaguptaka-vinaya）为基础创立了南山律宗，使《四分律》成为中国佛教的主流律典，但法显翻译的《摩诃僧祇律》始终被视为重要的参考文本。不同部派广律的并存，使中国律学具有了比较研究的学术视野。

Although Daoxuan (596-667) later founded the Nanshan Vinaya school based on the *Dharmaguptaka-vinaya* (四分律), making it the mainstream Vinaya in Chinese Buddhism, Faxian's *Mahasanghika-vinaya* has always been valued as an important reference text. The coexistence of Vinaya codes from different schools gave Chinese Vinaya studies a comparative scholarly perspective.

### 对中国佛教制度的长远影响

完整的律藏对中国佛教的影响是深远的：它规范了受戒程序、僧团组织、寺院管理、僧人行为准则等方方面面。没有完整的律藏，中国佛教的制度化建设将无从谈起。法显的贡献在于为这一制度化进程提供了不可或缺的文本基础。

The impact of complete Vinaya on Chinese Buddhism was far-reaching: it standardized ordination procedures, monastic community organization, temple management, and codes of conduct for monastics. Without complete Vinaya, the institutionalization of Chinese Buddhism would have been impossible. Faxian's contribution lay in providing the indispensable textual foundation for this institutionalization process.

## 与其他律典翻译的比较 / Comparison with Other Vinaya Translations

| 律典 Vinaya | 部派 School | 翻译者 Translator | 年代 Date | 地位 Status |
|-------------|-------------|-------------------|-----------|-------------|
| 摩诃僧祇律 | 大众部 Mahasanghika | 法显 + 佛陀跋陀罗 | 416-418 CE | 法显携归，重要参考 |
| 四分律 | 法藏部 Dharmaguptaka | 佛陀耶舍 + 竺佛念 | 410-412 CE | 后世主流，南山律宗根本 |
| 五分律 | 化地部 Mahisasaka | 佛陀什 + 竺道生 | 423-424 CE | 法显携归梵本，后由他人翻译 |
| 十诵律 | 有部 Sarvastivada | 弗若多罗 + 鸠摩罗什 | 404-405 CE | 最早翻译的广律 |
| 根本说一切有部律 | 有部 Mulasarvastivada | 义净 | 703-713 CE | 最详尽的有部律 |

法显的贡献在于：他不仅自己翻译了《摩诃僧祇律》，还带回了《弥沙塞律》的梵文原本，后来由佛陀什和竺道生翻译为《五分律》。一部求法之旅，为中国律藏贡献了至少两部完整的广律文本。

Faxian's contribution was twofold: he not only translated the *Mahasanghika-vinaya* himself but also brought back the Sanskrit original of the *Mahisasaka-vinaya*, which was later translated by Buddhajiva and Zhu Daosheng as the *Wu Fen Lu* (五分律). A single pilgrimage journey contributed at least two complete broad Vinaya texts to the Chinese Vinaya canon.
