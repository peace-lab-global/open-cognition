# 大手印 Mahamudra (Great Seal)

> "心性本空，空而明，明而无碍——这就是大手印。"
>
> "Mind's nature is empty, empty yet luminous, luminous yet unobstructed — this is Mahamudra."

## 概述 Overview

大手印（梵文：Mahāmudrā，藏文：ཕྱག་རྒྱ་ཆེན་པོ་，phyag rgya chen po），字面义为"伟大的印章"或"伟大的手印"，是藏传佛教噶举派最核心的修行法门。它指向一切现象的究竟实相——心性的本来面目。在密勒日巴的传承中，大手印不是理论探讨的对象，而是必须通过直接体验来证悟的真理。

Mahamudra (Sanskrit: Mahāmudrā; Tibetan: ཕྱག་རྒྱ་ཆེན་པོ་, phyag rgya chen po), literally "Great Seal" or "Great Gesture," is the most central practice of the Kagyu school of Tibetan Buddhism. It points to the ultimate nature of all phenomena — the innate face of mind's nature. In Milarepa's lineage, Mahamudra is not a subject for theoretical discussion but a truth that must be realized through direct experience.

"大"（mahā）表示这一实相超越一切限定，无所不包；"手印"（mudrā）表示它是诸佛的印记，是实相的终极认证。合而言之，大手印就是一切存在的根本印记——无可超越、无可增减的究竟实相。

"Great" (mahā) indicates that this reality transcends all limitations and encompasses everything; "seal" (mudrā) signifies it as the stamp of all Buddhas, the ultimate authentication of reality. Together, Mahamudra is the fundamental seal of all existence — the ultimate reality beyond which nothing surpasses, to which nothing can be added.

## 大手印的四种分类 Four Types of Mahamudra

噶举传统将大手印分为四个层次：

The Kagyu tradition classifies Mahamudra into four levels:

### 1. 经部大手印 Sutra Mahamudra

基于显教经典的修持方式，通过闻思修三慧来理解空性。行者首先通过学习中观、唯识等哲学体系来建立正见，然后通过止观双运来证悟空性。这是最接近印度大乘佛教传统的方法。

Based on sutra-level teachings, this approach cultivates understanding of emptiness through study, reflection, and meditation. The practitioner first establishes correct view through studying Madhyamaka and Yogacara philosophical systems, then realizes emptiness through the union of shamatha and vipashyana. This is the approach closest to Indian Mahayana Buddhist tradition.

### 2. 密续大手印 Tantra Mahamudra

基于无上瑜伽密续的修持，涉及本尊观想、咒语念诵和气脉明点的修习。行者通过生起次第和圆满次第来体验心性的本来面目。这一层次需要接受灌顶并严守密乘戒律。

Based on unsurpassed yoga tantra, involving deity visualization, mantra recitation, and practices of channels, winds, and drops. The practitioner experiences mind's nature through the generation and completion stages. This level requires empowerment and strict observance of tantric commitments.

### 3. 本质大手印 Essence Mahamudra

这是大手印最精要的形式，也被称为"直指心性"（pointing-out instruction）。上师直接指出弟子心性的本来面目，弟子在当下安住于这一指认中。密勒日巴的大手印教法主要属于这一层次——超越一切形式和概念，直接契入心性的明空不二。

The most essential form of Mahamudra, also known as "pointing-out instruction." The guru directly points out the innate nature of mind, and the student rests in that recognition in the present moment. Milarepa's Mahamudra teaching primarily belongs to this level — transcending all forms and concepts, directly entering the unity of clarity and emptiness.

### 4. 无上大手印 Supreme Mahamudra

四种大手印的圆满统合，行者完全证悟心性，一切行为和体验都成为大手印的展现。密勒日巴在其晚年达到了这一境界——行住坐卧、吃喝拉撒无不是大手印。

The perfect integration of all four types, where the practitioner has fully realized mind's nature and all activities and experiences become the display of Mahamudra. Milarepa attained this state in his later years — every action, whether walking, sitting, eating, or sleeping, was Mahamudra.

## 密勒日巴的大手印教授 Milarepa's Mahamudra Instructions

密勒日巴对大手印的教授有几个显著特点：

Milarepa's Mahamudra instructions have several distinctive characteristics:

### 不造作 Non-fabrication

> "不修整的，就是修；不执取的，就是见。"
>
> "Non-meditation is meditation; non-grasping is the view."

密勒日巴反复强调，大手印的修持不是"做"什么，而是"不做"什么。心性本来就是圆满的，任何刻意的造作——包括试图"修行"的念头本身——都会遮蔽心性的本来面目。修行者需要放下一切企图，包括放下"放下"这个企图本身。

Milarepa repeatedly emphasized that Mahamudra practice is not about "doing" something but about "not doing." Mind's nature is already complete; any deliberate fabrication — including the very thought of trying to "meditate" — obscures its innate face. The practitioner must let go of all efforts, including the effort to let go.

### 妄念即法身 Thoughts as Dharmakaya

> "当妄念生起时，不要追随它，不要压抑它。看着它的来处、住处和去处——你会发现它无处可去。妄念的本质就是法身。"
>
> "When thoughts arise, do not follow them, do not suppress them. Look at where they come from, where they stay, where they go — you will find they go nowhere. The essence of thoughts is Dharmakaya."

这是密勒日巴最具革命性的教授之一。在传统佛教中，妄念通常被视为修行的障碍；但在大手印中，妄念本身就是心性的展现，当行者能够不加造作地看着妄念生灭时，妄念就自我解脱了。

This is one of Milarepa's most revolutionary teachings. In traditional Buddhism, discursive thoughts are usually seen as obstacles to practice; but in Mahamudra, thoughts themselves are the display of mind's nature. When the practitioner can watch thoughts arise and dissolve without fabrication, thoughts self-liberate.

### 六识自解脱 Self-liberation of the Six Consciousnesses

密勒日巴教授：眼、耳、鼻、舌、身、意六识的生起，不需要被排斥也不需要被追逐。当行者安住于大手印时，六识自然运作，如同镜中映象——清晰而无执着。

Milarepa taught that the arising of the six consciousnesses (eye, ear, nose, tongue, body, mind) need not be rejected nor pursued. When the practitioner rests in Mahamudra, the six consciousnesses function naturally, like reflections in a mirror — vivid yet without grasping.

## 修持次第 Stages of Practice

噶举传统将大手印修持分为四个阶段（四瑜伽）：

The Kagyu tradition divides Mahamudra practice into four stages (Four Yogas):

1. **专一瑜伽 (One-pointedness)**：培养心安住于所缘境的能力，达到止（shamatha）的稳定。
   Cultivating the ability of mind to rest on its object, achieving the stability of shamatha.

2. **离戏瑜伽 (Simplicity)**：超越一切概念建构，直接体验心性的空性。
   Transcending all conceptual constructs, directly experiencing the emptiness of mind's nature.

3. **一味瑜伽 (One Taste)**：体验到一切现象——轮回与涅槃、烦恼与智慧——具有相同的本质。
   Experiencing all phenomena — samsara and nirvana, afflictions and wisdom — as having the same essence.

4. **无修瑜伽 (Non-meditation)**：超越修与不修的二元对立，完全融入心性的本来面目。
   Transcending the duality of meditation and non-meditation, fully merging with mind's innate nature.

密勒日巴的道歌中，四瑜伽经常以隐喻出现：如牧童看牛（专一）、虚空无云（离戏）、大海一味（一味）、无有修者（无修）。

In Milarepa's songs, the Four Yogas frequently appear as metaphors: a shepherd watching cattle (one-pointedness), sky without clouds (simplicity), ocean of one taste (one taste), no meditator (non-meditation).

## 与其他传承的比较 Comparison with Other Traditions

大手印与禅宗的"直指人心，见性成佛"有深层的呼应。两者都强调超越文字概念、直接体验心性。不同的是，大手印传统保留了更系统的修持次第（四瑜伽），并与密续的气脉修持紧密结合。

Mahamudra resonates deeply with Chan/Zen's "pointing directly to the human mind, seeing nature and becoming Buddha." Both emphasize transcending words and concepts to directly experience mind's nature. The difference is that the Mahamudra tradition preserves a more systematic practice framework (the Four Yogas) and is closely integrated with tantric channel-and-wind practices.

与南传佛教的毗婆舍那（Vipassana）相比，大手印不强调对无常、苦、无我的逐步分析，而是直接指向能分析的心本身。

Compared with Theravada Vipassana, Mahamudra does not emphasize gradual analysis of impermanence, suffering, and non-self, but points directly to the analyzing mind itself.

## 与密勒日巴其他概念的关系 Relation to Milarepa's Other Concepts

- **拙火 Tummo** 为大手印提供身体层面的基础——通过气脉修持净化粗重的身心，使心性更容易显现。
  Tummo provides a somatic foundation — purifying gross body-mind through channel practices so that mind's nature manifests more readily.

- **道歌 Spiritual Songs** 是大手印教学的载体——密勒日巴用诗歌的直觉力量传达概念语言无法表达的真理。
  Spiritual songs are the vehicle for Mahamudra teaching — poetry's intuitive power conveys truths beyond conceptual language.

- **苦行转化 Ascetic Transformation** 是大手印修持的生活方式表达——通过极端简朴来切断对世俗的执着。
  Ascetic transformation is the lifestyle expression of Mahamudra — cutting attachment through radical simplicity.
