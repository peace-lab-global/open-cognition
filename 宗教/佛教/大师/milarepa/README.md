# 密勒日巴 Milarepa (1052–1135)

> "我无寺院无田地，无有眷属无侍从；我行苦修住山洞，一切众生之福田。"
>
> "I have no monastery, no fields, no retinue, no servants; I practice austerities and dwell in caves — a field of merit for all sentient beings."

## 概述 Overview

密勒日巴（藏文：མི་ལ་རས་པ，威利转写：mi la ras pa）是西藏历史上最伟大的瑜伽士、诗人和精神导师之一。作为噶举派（Kagyu）第二代祖师，他的一生是佛教史上最震撼的转化叙事——从一个因复仇而杀人的黑咒术士，到在单一生命周期内证悟成佛的圣者。他的教法核心围绕大手印（Mahamudra）、拙火（Tummo）和那洛六法（Six Yogas of Naropa）展开，通过道歌（spiritual songs）这一独特形式传播深奥的密法义理。

Milarepa (Tibetan: མི་ལ་རས་པ, Wylie: mi la ras pa) is one of the greatest yogis, poets, and spiritual teachers in Tibetan history. As the second patriarch of the Kagyu lineage, his life presents one of the most dramatic transformation narratives in Buddhist history — from a practitioner of black magic who killed in revenge, to a saint who attained Buddhahood in a single lifetime. His teachings center on Mahamudra (Great Seal), Tummo (inner heat), and the Six Yogas of Naropa, transmitted through the unique medium of spiritual songs (道歌).

- **出生地 / Birthplace**: 西藏贡塘 Gungthang（今吉隆县）
- **时期 / Era**: 11-12 世纪，藏传佛教后弘期
- **身份 / Role**: 瑜伽士、诗人、密法传承者
- **传承 / Lineage**: 帝洛巴 Tilopa → 那洛巴 Naropa → 玛尔巴 Marpa → 密勒日巴 → 冈波巴 Gampopa
- **核心经典 / Core Text**: 《密勒日巴道歌集》Mi la ras pa'i mgur 'bum

## 历史背景 Historical Context

密勒日巴生活在西藏后弘期（phyi dar），这是佛教在经历朗达玛灭佛之后重新复兴的时代。十一世纪的西藏正处于佛教翻译运动的高峰期，大量印度密教经典被翻译为藏文。玛尔巴译师（Marpa Lotsawa, 1012–1097）曾三次赴印度、四次赴尼泊尔求学，将那不洛巴（Naropa）和梅只巴（Maitripa）的密法传承带回西藏。

Milarepa lived during the Later Diffusion period (phyi dar) of Tibetan Buddhism, an era of revival after the persecution under King Langdarma. Eleventh-century Tibet was at the height of the translation movement, with vast numbers of Indian tantric texts being rendered into Tibetan. Marpa the Translator (1012–1097) traveled to India three times and Nepal four times, bringing back the tantric lineages of Naropa and Maitripa.

这一时期的噶举传承特别强调实修而非学术，强调口耳相传的密法指导而非文字教条。密勒日巴正是这一传统的巅峰体现：他终生未建寺院、未立制度，而是以最纯粹的苦行瑜伽士形象行走于喜马拉雅山脉的洞穴之间。

The Kagyu lineage of this period emphasized practice over scholarship, and oral transmission over textual authority. Milarepa was the supreme embodiment of this tradition: he never built monasteries or established institutions, but walked as a pure yogi among the caves of the Himalayas.

## 生平要略 Biographical Outline

### 早年：罪与复仇 Early Years: Sin and Revenge

密勒日巴本名"闻喜"（Thopa Ga），生于贡塘（Gungthang）地区的一个富裕家庭。父亲去世后，叔父和姑母侵吞了家产，母子三人沦为奴仆。母亲悲愤难忍，送他去学习黑咒术复仇。密勒日巴学到了强大的降雹术和诛法，降下冰雹和蝎子咒摧毁了叔父的房屋，杀死了三十余人。

Born Thopa Ga ("Delightful to Hear") into a wealthy family in the Gungthang region, Milarepa saw his inheritance seized by his uncle and aunt after his father's death. His mother, consumed by grief and rage, sent him to study black magic for revenge. Milarepa mastered powerful hail-casting and destructive mantras, collapsing his uncle's house and killing over thirty people.

深重的罪恶感笼罩了他的灵魂。他意识到自己已造下了堕入下三道的业因，必须寻求究竟的解脱。

The weight of guilt overwhelmed his soul. He recognized that he had created the karmic causes for rebirth in the lower realms, and needed to seek ultimate liberation.

### 求法：与玛尔巴的师徒关系 Seeking Dharma: The Guru-Disciple Bond

密勒日巴先从上师荣敦拉嘎（Rongton Lhaga）学习大圆满法，但未能相应。荣敦拉嘎告诉他，唯有玛尔巴译师才能度化他。于是密勒日巴长途跋涉，历尽艰辛来到玛尔巴座前。

Milarepa first studied Dzogchen under the master Rongton Lhaga, but could not make it work for him. Rongton told him that only Marpa the Translator could save him. So Milarepa undertook an arduous journey to find Marpa.

玛尔巴为了净化密勒日巴的恶业，命他建造房屋、拆除、再建造，反复折磨。密勒日巴背上长满脓疮，几近绝望，但始终不退信心。最终玛尔巴认定他业障已净，授予全部密法灌顶，并传以大手印和那洛六法的核心教授。

To purify Milarepa's negative karma, Marpa subjected him to grueling trials — building houses, tearing them down, and rebuilding. Milarepa's back was covered with sores; he nearly despaired but never lost faith. Finally, Marpa declared his obscurations purified, bestowed the complete tantric empowerments, and transmitted the core instructions of Mahamudra and the Six Yogas of Naropa.

### 苦修与证悟 Austerity and Realization

离开玛尔巴后，密勒日巴独自在山洞中苦修数十年。他仅以荨麻为食，身体因缺乏营养而变绿（这也是唐卡中他常呈绿色的原因）。他穿着单薄的棉衣，在喜马拉雅的严寒中修拙火定（tummo），以内在热能抵御酷寒。

After leaving Marpa, Milarepa practiced in solitary mountain caves for decades. He subsisted solely on nettles, his body turning green from malnutrition (hence his green depiction in thangka paintings). Wearing only thin cotton cloth, he practiced tummo meditation in the freezing Himalayan cold, sustaining himself with inner heat.

在这一时期，他经历了一系列考验：妹妹琵达（Peta）和未婚妻则色（Dzese）来访劝他放弃苦行；猎人发现他衣衫褴褛而嘲笑他；非人鬼神前来干扰。面对一切境遇，密勒日巴均以歌咏回应，将每个遭遇转化为修行指导。

During this period, he underwent a series of trials: his sister Peta and fiancee Dzese visited to persuade him to abandon his austerities; hunters mocked his ragged appearance; spirits came to disturb him. To every encounter, Milarepa responded with songs, transforming each situation into a teaching.

### 晚期：弘法与传承 Later Years: Teaching and Lineage

证悟后，密勒日巴以道歌教化各方信众，从普通牧民到学者僧侣，他以不同层次的教法应对不同根器的弟子。他最重要的弟子冈波巴（Gampopa, 1079–1153）将噶举传承制度化，建立了寺院体系，从而确保了噶举派在西藏的广泛传播。

After realization, Milarepa taught through songs to all manner of people — from common herders to scholarly monks — adapting his instructions to the capacity of each disciple. His most important student, Gampopa (1079–1153), institutionalized the Kagyu lineage by establishing monasteries, ensuring the widespread propagation of the Kagyu tradition across Tibet.

## 核心命题 Core Propositions

### 1. 即身成佛 Buddhahood in One Lifetime

密勒日巴以自身经历证明：即使曾造下极重恶业（杀生、咒术），通过真诚的忏悔、对上师的绝对信心和精进的密法修持，仍可在一生中证得佛果。这是金刚乘最核心的承诺。

Milarepa proved through his own life that complete Buddhahood can be attained in a single lifetime, regardless of past karma, through tantric practice under a qualified guru — the most central promise of Vajrayana.

### 2. 大手印 Mahamudra（见 [concepts/mahamudra.md](concepts/mahamudra.md)）

大手印是密勒日巴教法的基石。这一法门直接指向心性的本来面目——超越概念、超越二元对立的明空不二。密勒日巴的大手印教授强调：不造作、不修整、安住于本然状态。

Mahamudra ("Great Seal") is the cornerstone of Milarepa's teaching. This practice points directly to the innate nature of mind — the unity of clarity and emptiness that transcends concepts and duality. Milarepa's Mahamudra instructions emphasize: non-fabrication, non-meditation, and resting in the natural state.

### 3. 拙火 Tummo（见 [concepts/tummo.md](concepts/tummo.md)）

拙火是那洛六法之首，通过观修气脉明点来生起内在热能。密勒日巴的拙火修行尤为著名——他在雪山之巅仅以单衣抵御严寒，展示了密法修行的强大力量。

Tummo (inner heat) is the first of the Six Yogas of Naropa, generating inner heat through visualization of channels, winds, and drops. Milarepa's tummo practice is legendary — withstanding the freezing cold of snow-clad peaks in thin cotton cloth, demonstrating the power of tantric practice.

### 4. 道歌 Spiritual Songs（见 [concepts/spiritual-songs.md](concepts/spiritual-songs.md)）

密勒日巴以歌传法，其十万首道歌（《密勒日巴道歌集》）既是深奥的修行指导，也是优美的文学作品。道歌传统成为噶举派最具标志性的教学方式。

Milarepa taught through songs; his hundred thousand songs (collected in *The Hundred Thousand Songs of Milarepa*) serve as both profound practice instructions and exquisite literature. The song tradition became the most distinctive pedagogical method of the Kagyu lineage.

### 5. 苦行转化 Ascetic Transformation（见 [concepts/ascetic-transformation.md](concepts/ascetic-transformation.md)）

密勒日巴的一生展示了通过极端苦行实现精神转化的可能性。他的苦行并非自虐，而是一种精密的修行方法——通过断除对物质享受的执着，将身心推向极限，从而突破凡夫的局限认知。

Milarepa's life demonstrates the possibility of spiritual transformation through extreme austerity. His asceticism was not self-mortification but a refined practice method — pushing body and mind to their limits by relinquishing attachment to material comfort, thereby breaking through ordinary cognitive limitations.

### 6. 那洛六法 Six Yogas of Naropa（见 [concepts/six-yogas-naropa.md](concepts/six-yogas-naropa.md)）

那洛六法是噶举派最核心的密法体系，包含拙火、幻身、光明、梦瑜伽、迁识（颇瓦）和中阴教法。密勒日巴通过玛尔巴完整接受了这一传承，并在实修中将其发挥到极致。

The Six Yogas of Naropa constitute the core tantric system of the Kagyu tradition, comprising tummo, illusory body, clear light, dream yoga, consciousness transference (phowa), and bardo teachings. Milarepa received this lineage in full from Marpa and brought it to its fullest expression through practice.

## 思想特质 Intellectual Character

密勒日巴的思想具有鲜明的特征：

Milarepa's thought displays distinctive characteristics:

- **反智主义倾向 Anti-intellectualist tendency**：他警惕概念思维对修行的干扰，常批评学究式的佛学研究。He warned against conceptual thinking's interference with practice, often criticizing scholastic Buddhism.
- **经验主义 Empiricism**：一切教法必须通过个人实证来检验，而非依赖文字权威。All teachings must be verified through personal realization, not textual authority.
- **实用主义 Pragmatism**：教法应直指解脱，不纠缠于哲学争论。Teachings should point directly to liberation, without entanglement in philosophical disputes.
- **普世关怀 Universal compassion**：虽然修行方法极为严格，但他的终极目标是利益一切众生。Though his methods were rigorous, his ultimate aim was the benefit of all beings.

## 文化影响 Cultural Impact

密勒日巴的影响远超宗教领域：

Milarepa's influence extends far beyond religion:

**文学 Literature**：《密勒日巴道歌集》被公认为藏文文学的巅峰之作，可与但丁的《神曲》和鲁米的诗歌相媲美。张澄基教授（Garma C.C. Chang）的英文翻译使其成为世界文学遗产的一部分。

*The Hundred Thousand Songs of Milarepa* is recognized as a pinnacle of Tibetan literature, comparable to Dante's *Divine Comedy* and Rumi's poetry. Professor Garma C.C. Chang's English translation made it part of world literary heritage.

**艺术 Art**：密勒日巴是唐卡绘画中常见的题材，通常被描绘为右手捂耳倾听、身呈绿色、端坐于山洞中的瑜伽士形象。他的右手捂耳象征着倾听内心的声音。

Milarepa is a common subject in thangka painting, typically depicted with his right hand cupped to his ear (listening), his body green, seated in a mountain cave. The hand-to-ear gesture symbolizes listening to the inner voice.

**电影 Cinema**：意大利导演利利亚纳·卡瓦尼（Liliana Cavani）1974年拍摄了《密勒日巴》电影；不丹导演钦哲诺布（Dzongsar Khyentse Rinpoche）亦有相关作品。

Italian director Liliana Cavani made a film about Milarepa in 1974; Bhutanese director Dzongsar Khyentse Rinpoche has also created related works.

**心理学 Psychology**：密勒日巴从黑暗到光明的转化叙事，为现代心理学中的创伤后成长（post-traumatic growth）提供了古老的范例。

Milarepa's narrative of transformation from darkness to light provides an ancient paradigm for post-traumatic growth in modern psychology.

**生态思想 Ecological thought**：极端简朴的生活方式在当代环境危机中具有启示意义——幸福不需要以物质消耗来衡量。

His radically simple lifestyle offers insight in the era of ecological crisis — happiness need not be measured by material consumption.

## 传承与影响 Lineage and Legacy

密勒日巴的弟子冈波巴 Gampopa 将噶举派从师徒口传的小规模传承发展为大规模寺院制度，建立了四大八小支派。其中帕竹噶举、噶玛噶举等支派在西藏政治和宗教生活中发挥了深远影响。

Gampopa transformed the Kagyu from a small oral lineage into a large-scale monastic institution, establishing four major and eight minor sub-schools. Among these, the Phagdru Kagyu and Karma Kagyu exerted profound influence on Tibetan political and religious life.

密勒日巴的道歌传统影响了后世藏传佛教的文学、音乐、绘画（唐卡）等多个领域。他的生平故事是西藏最广泛流传的精神叙事之一。

Milarepa's song tradition influenced later Tibetan Buddhist literature, music, thangka painting, and other fields. His life story remains one of the most widely circulated spiritual narratives in Tibet.

## 常见误读 Common Misconceptions

| 误读 Misconception | 辨析 Clarification |
|---|---|
| "密勒日巴是神话人物" / "Milarepa is mythical" | 有坚实的历史基础，其传记作者桑杰坚赞基于口传传统编写 |
| "苦行等于自虐" / "Austerity = self-mortification" | 苦行是方法而非目的，是精密的密法修行体系 |
| "道歌只是诗歌" / "Songs are mere poetry" | 每首道歌都包含实修指导，是活的教学工具 |
| "即身成佛太容易" / "Buddhahood in one life is easy" | 需要极端的奉献和苦修，绝非轻易可达 |
| "密勒日巴排斥学术" / "Milarepa rejected learning" | 他反对的是将学术替代实修，而非知识本身 |
