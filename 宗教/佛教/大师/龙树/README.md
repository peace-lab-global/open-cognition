# 龙树 Nagarjuna

> "众因缘生法，我说即是空，亦为是假名，亦是中道义。"
> —《中论》观四谛品 (MMK 24.18)
>
> "Whatever is dependently co-arisen, that is explained to be emptiness. That, being a dependent designation, is itself the middle way."
> — *Mulamadhyamakakarika* 24.18 (Garfield trans.)

---

## 基本信息 / Basic Information

**龙树 Nagarjuna**（约 150–250 CE），南印度婆罗门出身，后出家为僧，被尊为**中观学派 (Madhyamaka) 创始人**和大乘佛教"八宗共祖"。在佛教思想史上，龙树的地位仅次于佛陀本人——他系统地将般若经中的"空"思想转化为严密的哲学论证体系，奠定了整个大乘佛教的理论基础。

Nagarjuna (c. 150–250 CE), born into a Brahmin family in South India, is revered as the **founder of the Madhyamaka school** and the "patriarch shared by eight schools" of Mahayana Buddhism. In Buddhist intellectual history, his stature is second only to the Buddha himself — he systematically transformed the "emptiness" teachings of the Prajnaparamita sutras into a rigorous philosophical argumentation system that laid the theoretical foundation for all of Mahayana Buddhism.

- **出生地 / Birthplace**: 南印度（Vidarbha 地区或 Krishna 河谷，学界有争议）/ South India (Vidarbha region or Krishna River valley — scholarly debate continues)
- **年代 / Era**: 约 2–3 世纪 CE，萨陀伐诃那王朝 (Satavahana) 时期
- **身份 / Role**: 中观学派创始人、哲学家、逻辑学家 / Founder of Madhyamaka, philosopher, logician
- **传承 / Lineage**: 汉传佛教八宗共尊为祖师；藏传佛教视其为最重要的印度论师 / Revered as patriarch by all eight schools of Chinese Buddhism; regarded as the most important Indian treatise author in Tibetan Buddhism
- **核心经典 / Core Text**: 《中论》*Mulamadhyamakakarika* (MMK)

## 核心命题 / Core Propositions

### 1. 缘起即空 / Dependent Origination Is Emptiness (Pratityasamutpada = Sunyata)

一切法因缘生，故无自性 (nihsabhava)；无自性，故空 (sunya)。空不是虚无，而是对独立自存之实体性的否定。缘起与空是同义语——正因为事物是依缘而起的，所以它们不具有固定不变的本质。

Everything that arises dependently lacks inherent existence (svabhava). Emptiness is not nihilism but the logical consequence of dependent origination. Because things arise in dependence upon conditions, they cannot possess fixed, immutable essences. Emptiness and dependent origination are synonymous — this is Nagarjuna's most revolutionary equation.

详见 [缘起性空](concepts/dependent-origination.md) | See [Dependent Origination](concepts/dependent-origination.md)

### 2. 二谛不可分 / The Inseparability of Two Truths (Dve Satye)

世俗谛 (samvrti-satya) 与胜义谛 (paramartha-satya) 是同一实相的两种言说层次。不理解二谛的关系，就无法理解佛法。龙树的关键洞见是：不依世俗谛，不得胜义谛——概念化认知与非概念化洞察之间不是断裂，而是连续。

Conventional truth and ultimate truth are not two separate realities but two registers of discourse about the same reality. Without relying on the conventional, the ultimate cannot be taught. Nagarjuna's key insight: conceptual cognition and non-conceptual insight form a continuum, not a rupture.

详见 [二谛](concepts/two-truths.md) | See [Two Truths](concepts/two-truths.md)

### 3. 四句破 / Tetralemma (Catuskoti)

对任何本质主义命题以四种逻辑可能性——有、无、亦有亦无、非有非无——全部否定。这不是反逻辑，而是以逻辑自身展示逻辑的极限，迫使认知超越概念框架。

The tetralemma systematically negates all four logical positions regarding any metaphysical proposition — existence, non-existence, both, and neither. This is not anti-logic but logic's self-transcendence: when all conceptual possibilities are exhausted and negated, cognition is forced beyond its own frameworks.

详见 [四句破](concepts/catuskoti-tetralemma.md) | See [Tetralemma](concepts/catuskoti-tetralemma.md)

### 4. 八不中道 / Eight Negations of the Middle Way (Asta-nisedha)

不生不灭、不常不断、不一不异、不来不去——八组极端概念的双重否定，构成中道的否定性表达。这不是折衷主义，而是对所有概念固化的系统瓦解。

Neither arising nor ceasing, neither permanent nor annihilated, neither identical nor different, neither coming nor going — these eight double negations constitute the negative expression of the Middle Way. Not compromise but systematic deconstruction of all conceptual fixation.

详见 [中道](concepts/middle-way.md) | See [Middle Way](concepts/middle-way.md)

### 5. 戏论寂灭 / Cessation of Conceptual Proliferation (Prapanca-upasama)

一切概念投射 (prapanca) 的止息是涅槃的本质特征。涅槃不是一个"地方"或"状态"，而是概念造作的止息。

The cessation of all conceptual proliferation (prapanca) is the hallmark of nirvana — not a transcendent realm but the quieting of the mind's compulsive reification.

### 6. 空亦复空 / Emptiness of Emptiness (Sunyata-sunyata)

空性本身也是空的——"空"不是一个终极实体或替代性本体论。这一递归否定确保中观不会退化为另一种本质主义。

Emptiness itself is empty — "emptiness" is not an ultimate substance or substitute ontology. This recursive negation ensures that Madhyamaka cannot degenerate into yet another essentialism.

## 思想脉络 / Intellectual Context

### 前驱 / Predecessors

- **般若经传统 (Prajnaparamita)**: 龙树的空观是对般若经核心思想的哲学系统化 / Nagarjuna's emptiness 哲学 systematizes the core insights of the Prajnaparamita sutras
- **阿含经 (Agama)**: 缘起思想的原始来源 / The original source of dependent origination thought
- **阿毗达磨 (Abhidharma)**: 部派佛教对法的分析传统——龙树继承其方法论但反对其本体论承诺 / Nagarjuna inherits its analytical methodology but rejects its ontological commitments
- **说一切有部 (Sarvastivada)**: 主张"三世实有"——龙树的主要批判对象 / The thesis that "all three times truly exist" — Nagarjuna's primary critical target

### 后继 / Successors

- **阿耶提婆 Aryadeva** (约 3 世纪): 首席弟子，《四百论》作者 / Chief disciple, author of *Catuhsataka*
- **佛护 Buddhapalita** (约 470–540): 开创应成派 (Prasangika) / Founded the Prasangika commentary tradition
- **清辨 Bhaviveka** (约 500–570): 开创自续派 (Svatantrika) / Founded the Svatantrika commentary tradition
- **月称 Candrakirti** (约 600–650): 应成派集大成者 / Culmination of the Prasangika tradition
- **宗喀巴 Tsongkhapa** (1357–1419): 藏传中观应成派复兴者 / Reviver of Prasangika Madhyamaka in Tibetan Buddhism
- **智顗 Zhiyi** (538–597): 天台宗创始人 / Founder of Tiantai school, built Chinese Buddhist 哲学 on Madhyamaka foundations

## 重要概念 / Key Concepts

1. **空性 Sunyata** — 无自性，非虚无；缘起法的本质特征 / Absence of inherent existence; not nihilism but the essential character of dependently originated phenomena
2. **二谛 Dve Satye** — 世俗谛与胜义谛的不可分割关系 / The inseparable relationship between conventional and ultimate truth
3. **四句破 Catuskoti** — 四句全否的逻辑穷尽法 / Exhaustive logical negation of all four possible positions
4. **戏论 Prapanca** — 概念投射之网，认知固化的根源 / The web of conceptual projection, root of cognitive fixation
5. **假名 Prajnapti** — 缘起法的命名是约定俗成，非指实有 / Designation of dependently originated phenomena is conventional, not referential
6. **中道 Madhyamaka** — 离有无二边的否定性道路 / The negative path that departs from both existence and non-existence extremes
7. **八不 Asta-nisedha** — 八组极端概念的双重否定 / Double negation of eight extreme conceptual pairs
8. **自性 Svabhava** — 龙树批判的核心靶标：独立自存的本质 / The core target of Nagarjuna's critique: independently existing essence
9. **缘起 Pratityasamutpada** — 相依相待的生起，空性的正面表达 / Dependent co-arising, the positive expression of emptiness
10. **涅槃 Nirvana** — 戏论的寂灭，非超越性实体 / Cessation of conceptual proliferation, not a transcendent substance

## 思想坐标 / Intellectual Positioning

### 哲学定位 / Philosophical Orientation

- **反本质主义 Anti-essentialism**: 对"自性 svabhava"的全面批判，是哲学史上最彻底的反本质主义运动之一 / The most thorough anti-essentialist movement in the history of 哲学
- **批判理性主义 Critical rationalism**: 以逻辑破逻辑——不是否定理性，而是展示理性的自我消解 / Logic against logic — not denying reason but revealing reason's self-dissolution
- **中道哲学 Middle Way philosophy**: 不是折衷主义，而是对所有二元概念框架的系统超越 / Not compromise but systematic transcendence of all dualistic frameworks
- **语言哲学 Philosophy of language**: 对指称理论和意义理论的深刻批判 / Profound critique of reference theory and meaning theory

### 与佛教内部的关系 / Relationship within Buddhism

- **vs 有部 Sarvastivada**: 反对"三世实有、法体恒有" / Rejects "all three times truly exist"
- **vs 经量部 Sautrantika**: 继承方法，超越实在论 / Inherits methodology, transcends realism
- **vs 唯识 Yogacara**: 后世争论"恶取空"，月称为中观辩护 / Later debates over "nihilistic emptiness"
- **vs 如来藏 Tathagatagarbha**: 空性与"真常"之间的张力 / Tension between emptiness and "true permanence"

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

### 哲学 / Philosophy

与维特根斯坦 (Wittgenstein)、海德格尔 (Heidegger)、德里达 (Derrida)、罗蒂 (Rorty) 形成深度对话。龙树对语言界限的洞察与维特根斯坦"对于不可说的，必须保持沉默"形成跨文化平行。德里达的解构主义与中观的互读已成为比较哲学的经典议题。

Nagarjuna enters deep dialogue with Wittgenstein, Heidegger, Derrida, and Rorty. His insight into the limits of language parallels Wittgenstein's "Whereof one cannot speak, thereof one must be silent." The Derrida-Madhyamaka cross-reading has become a classic topic in comparative 哲学.

### 认知科学 / Cognitive Science

- **反物化 Anti-reification**: 认知对象不是固定的"东西"，而是关系性的显现 / Cognitive objects are not fixed "things" but relational manifestations
- **认知灵活性 Cognitive flexibility**: 四句破训练认知超越二元框架的能力 / The tetralemma trains the ability to transcend binary frameworks

### AI 哲学 / Philosophy of AI

- **本质主义批判**: AI 是否有"本质"——龙树的框架拒绝这一问题本身 / Does AI have an "essence"? Nagarjuna's framework rejects the question itself
- **表征问题 Representation**: 假名 (prajnapti) 的分析对 AI 符号主义提出质疑 / Analysis of designation challenges AI symbolism

### 心理学 / Psychology

- **自我解构 Deconstruction of self**: 无我论证与叙事自我理论的对话 / No-self arguments in dialogue with narrative self theories
- **认知行为疗法 CBT**: 四句破与认知重构技术的结构性相似 / Structural parallels between tetralemma and cognitive restructuring

## 常见误读 / Common Misreadings

### 1. "龙树 = 虚无主义者" / "Nagarjuna = Nihilist"

**误读 / Misreading**: 龙树否定一切，所以他是虚无主义者。/ Nagarjuna negates everything, so he is a nihilist.

**正解 / Correction**: 龙树明确批判虚无主义 (断见 uccheda-drsti)。空性是无自性，不是无物。《中论》24.11 警告："若人见有空，诸佛所不化。"将空性理解为虚无，恰恰是龙树批判的"恶取空"。

Nagarjuna explicitly criticizes nihilism (uccheda-drsti). Emptiness means lack of inherent existence, not non-existence. MMK 24.11 warns: "If one sees emptiness [as nihilism], even the Buddhas cannot help." Understanding emptiness as nothingness is precisely the "wrong grasp of emptiness" that Nagarjuna condemns.

### 2. "空 = 什么都不存在" / "Emptiness = Nothing exists"

**误读 / Misreading**: "一切皆空"意味着什么都不存在。/ "Everything is empty" means nothing exists.

**正解 / Correction**: 空 (sunya) 是否定自性 (svabhava)，不是否定缘起现象。"以有空义故，一切法得成"——正因为空，缘起才能运作。空是存在的可能性条件，不是存在的否定。

Emptiness negates svabhava, not dependently originated phenomena. "Because of the meaning of emptiness, all dharmas are established" — emptiness is the condition of possibility for existence, not its negation.

### 3. "四句破 = 反逻辑" / "Tetralemma = Anti-logic"

**误读 / Misreading**: 四句全否意味着龙树否定逻辑本身。/ The negation of all four positions means Nagarjuna rejects logic itself.

**正解 / Correction**: 四句破是严格运用逻辑推理的结果——当所有逻辑可能都被穷尽且都被否定，这不是反逻辑，而是逻辑的自我超越。类似哥德尔不完备定理展示的是形式系统的内在限制。

The tetralemma is the result of rigorous logical reasoning — when all logical possibilities are exhausted and negated, this is not anti-logic but logic's self-transcendence. Analogous to how Godel's incompleteness theorems reveal the inherent limits of formal systems.

### 4. "龙树否认因果" / "Nagarjuna denies causation"

**误读 / Misreading**: 龙树在《中论》第一章否定因果关系。/ Nagarjuna denies causation in the first chapter of MMK.

**正解 / Correction**: 龙树否定的是自性因果 (具有独立实体的因果关系)，而非缘起因果 (相依相待的因果过程)。他是在批判对因果关系的实体化理解，而非否定因果律本身。

Nagarjuna denies causation-with-svabhava (causal relations between independently existing entities), not dependently originated causation. He critiques the reification of causal relations, not the law of causation itself.
