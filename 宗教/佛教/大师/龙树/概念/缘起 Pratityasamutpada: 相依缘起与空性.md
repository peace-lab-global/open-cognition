# 缘起 Pratityasamutpada / Dependent Origination

> "此有故彼有，此生故彼生；此无故彼无，此灭故彼灭。"——《杂阿含经》
>
> "When this is, that is; this arising, that arises; when this is not, that is not; this ceasing, that ceases." — Samyutta Nikaya

---

## 概念概述 / Concept Overview

**缘起 (Pratityasamutpada)** 是佛教最核心的哲学概念之一，意为"依缘而起"或"相依而生"——一切现象都依赖于条件和关系而生起、存在和消亡，没有任何事物具有独立自存的实体性。龙树将缘起与空性等同：缘起即空，空即缘起。这一等式是他对整个佛教哲学最深刻的贡献。

**Dependent Origination (Pratityasamutpada)** is one of Buddhism's most central philosophical concepts, meaning "arising in dependence upon conditions" — all phenomena arise, persist, and cease in dependence upon conditions and relations; nothing possesses independent, self-sufficient substantiality. Nagarjuna equated dependent origination with emptiness: dependent origination is emptiness, emptiness is dependent origination. This equation is his most profound contribution to Buddhist 哲学.

梵文语义分析 / Sanskrit semantic analysis:
- **pratitya** (不变词): 依赖于、基于 / depending on, based on
- **samutpada** (名词): 共同生起、缘起 / co-arising, dependent arising
- 合起来：**依赖于条件的共同生起** / Together: **co-arising in dependence upon conditions**

## 哲学分析 / Philosophical Analysis

### 龙树的缘起论 / Nagarjuna's Theory of Dependent Origination

龙树对缘起的诠释具有革命性。在早期佛教中，缘起主要指十二因缘 (dvadasanga-pratityasamutpada) 的因果链：无明→行→识→名色→六入→触→受→爱→取→有→生→老死。龙树将缘起从具体的因果序列扩展为普遍的哲学原理——一切法都是缘起的，因此一切法都是空的。

Nagarjuna's interpretation of dependent origination is revolutionary. In early Buddhism, dependent origination primarily referred to the twelve-linked causal chain (dvadasanga-pratityasamutpada): ignorance → formations → consciousness → name-and-form → six sense bases → contact → feeling → craving → grasping → becoming → birth → aging-and-death. Nagarjuna extended dependent origination from a specific causal sequence to a universal philosophical principle — all dharmas are dependently originated, therefore all dharmas are empty.

核心公式 (MMK 24.18) / Core formula (MMK 24.18):

**缘起 = 空 = 假名 = 中道**
**Dependent Origination = Emptiness = Designation = Middle Way**

这一等式的含义 / Implications of this equation:
1. 缘起的事物不具有自性 (因为依赖他缘) / Dependently originated things lack svabhava (because they depend on other conditions)
2. 无自性就是空 (空不是独立的概念，而是缘起法的特征) / No svabhava is emptiness (emptiness is not an independent concept but a characteristic of dependently originated phenomena)
3. 空的事物只有假名 (正因为空，名称才能被使用) / Empty things have only conventional names (precisely because empty, names can be used)
4. 理解这一点就是中道 (不执有也不执无) / Understanding this is the Middle Way (grasping neither existence nor non-existence)

### 缘起的因果分析 / Causal Analysis of Dependent Origination

龙树在《中论》第一章以四句破分析因果关系，论证自性化的因果不可能成立：

In the first chapter of MMK, Nagarjuna analyzes causation through the tetralemma, demonstrating that causation-with-svabhava is impossible:

- **因中有果论 (Satkaryavada)**: 数论派立场——果已潜存于因中。龙树反驳：如果果已经存在，"生"就没有意义 / Sankhya position — effect pre-exists in cause. Nagarjuna's refutation: if the effect already exists, "arising" is meaningless
- **因中无果论 (Asatkaryavada)**: 胜论派立场——果是全新的创造。龙树反驳：如果果与因完全不同，因果关系无法建立 / Vaisesika position — effect is entirely new creation. Nagarjuna's refutation: if the effect is wholly different from the cause, the causal relation cannot be established
- **亦有亦无论**: 两种立场的结合，继承两者的缺陷 / Combination of both positions, inheriting both defects
- **非有非无论**: 无因论——否定了因果秩序本身 / No-cause theory — negates the causal order itself

龙树的结论：因果关系的自性化理解是不可能的，但缘起的因果过程是有效的。关键在于不将因果关系实体化。

Nagarjuna's conclusion: reified understanding of causation is impossible, but the dependently originated causal process is valid. The key is not to reify the causal relation.

### 缘起的三个层次 / Three Levels of Dependent Origination

后世中观学者 (特别是宗喀巴) 区分了缘起的三个层次：

Later Madhyamaka scholars (especially Tsongkhapa) distinguished three levels of dependent origination:

1. **因果缘起 Causal dependence**: 果依赖于因——这是最基本的层次 / Effects depend on causes — the most basic level
2. **部分缘起 Mereological dependence**: 整体依赖于部分——事物由部分组成，不具有独立的整体性 / Wholes depend on parts — things are composed of parts, lacking independent wholeness
3. **名言缘起 Conceptual dependence**: 事物依赖于概念的指称——事物的"存在"依赖于命名和认知活动 / Things depend on conceptual designation — the "existence" of things depends on naming and cognitive activity

## 历史发展 / Historical Development

### 早期佛教 / Early Buddhism

缘起思想是佛陀证悟的核心内容。传说佛陀在菩提树下观十二因缘而证悟。早期经典的缘起主要关注苦的起源和止息。

Dependent origination was the core content of the Buddha's awakening. Tradition holds that the Buddha realized enlightenment under the Bodhi tree by contemplating the twelve links. In early scriptures, dependent origination primarily concerned the origin and cessation of suffering.

### 部派佛教 / Abhidharma Buddhism

说一切有部将缘起分析为六种因 (hetu) 和四种缘 (pratyaya) 的系统，试图穷尽因果关系的所有类型。龙树继承了这种分析方法的精密性，但反对其本体论预设。

The Sarvastivada analyzed dependent origination into six causes (hetu) and four conditions (pratyaya), attempting to exhaust all types of causal relations. Nagarjuna inherited the precision of this analytical method but rejected its ontological presuppositions.

### 东亚佛教的发展 / Development in East Asian Buddhism

- **华严宗**: 法藏发展出"法界缘起"——一切法互即互入，如因陀罗网 / Huayan: Fazang developed "dharma-realm dependent origination" — all dharmas mutually interpenetrate, like Indra's net
- **天台宗**: 智顗提出"一念三千"——一念之中包含三千世界的缘起 / Tiantai: Zhiyi proposed "three thousand realms in a single thought-moment"
- **禅宗**: "即心即佛"——将缘起内化于心性的当下显现 / Chan: "Mind itself is Buddha" — internalizing dependent origination as the present manifestation of mind-nature

## 修行应用 / Practice Applications

- **观因缘 Meditation on conditions**: 观察一切经验都是缘起的——消解对经验的执着和厌恶 / Observe that all experience is dependently originated — dissolving attachment and aversion toward experience
- **慈悲修行 Compassion practice**: 了知众生的苦都是缘起的，因此可以被止息——这给予修行以希望和动力 / Knowing that all beings' suffering is dependently originated and therefore can be ceased — giving hope and motivation to practice
- **无我修行 No-self practice**: 通过分析自我的缘起性，消解对自我的实体化认知 / Dissolving the reification of self through analyzing the dependent origination of selfhood

## 当代对话 / Contemporary Dialogue

- **系统论 Systems theory**: 系统理论强调要素之间的相互依存——与缘起论的结构性平行 / Systems theory emphasizes mutual dependence among elements — structurally parallel to dependent origination
- **生态学 Ecology**: 深层生态学 (deep ecology) 的关系本体论——存在是关系性的，不是实体性的 / Deep ecology's relational ontology — existence is relational, not substantial
- **量子力学 Quantum mechanics**: 量子纠缠 (entanglement) 和关系性解释——粒子的属性只在关系中确定 / Quantum entanglement and relational interpretation — particle properties are determinate only in relation
- **过程哲学 Process philosophy**: 怀特海 (Whitehead) 的过程本体论——实在不是实体，而是事件和关系 / Whitehead's process ontology — reality is not substance but events and relations
- **网络科学 Network science**: 节点的属性由网络关系决定——缘起论的数学化表达 / Node properties are determined by network relations — a mathematical expression of dependent origination
