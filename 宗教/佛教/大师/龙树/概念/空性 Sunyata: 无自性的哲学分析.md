# 空性 Sunyata / Emptiness

> "以有空义故，一切法得成；若无空义者，一切则不成。"——《中论》24.14
>
> "By virtue of emptiness, all dharmas are established; if emptiness were not the case, nothing could be established." — MMK 24.14

---

## 概念概述 / Concept Overview

**空性 (sunyata)** 是龙树哲学的核心概念，意指一切法 (dharma) 无自性 (nihsvabhava)——不具有独立、固定、不变的本质。空不是"无物"(nothingness)，而是对自性 (svabhava) 的否定。

**Emptiness (sunyata)** is the central concept of Nagarjuna's philosophy, meaning that all phenomena (dharmas) lack inherent existence (nihsvabhava) — they do not possess independent, fixed, immutable essences. Emptiness is not "nothingness" but the negation of svabhava (inherent existence).

在印度哲学语境中，svabhava 指不依赖他缘、不变化、不可分解的实体性本质。龙树论证：如果法有自性，则不能生灭变化——而这与经验世界矛盾。因此一切法无自性。

In Indian philosophical context, svabhava refers to a self-existing essence that does not depend on other conditions, does not change, and cannot be decomposed. Nagarjuna argues: if dharmas possessed svabhava, they could not arise, change, or cease — which contradicts the empirical world. Therefore all dharmas are nihsvabhava.

## 哲学分析 / Philosophical Analysis

### 梵文语义分析 / Sanskrit Semantic Analysis

- **sunya** (形容词): 空的、缺乏的 / empty, lacking
- **sunyata** (名词): 空性、缺乏性 / emptiness, lack
- **svabhava** (被否定的对象): 自性——独立自存的本质 / inherent existence — independently existing essence
- **nihsvabhava**: 无自性——空性的正面表达 / without svabhava — the positive expression of emptiness

### 《中论》中的核心论证 / Key Arguments in MMK

**1. 缘起论证 / Argument from Dependent Origination (MMK 24.18–19)**

"众因缘生法，我说即是空"——这是龙树最精炼的公式：缘起 = 空。如果事物是缘起的，就不具有自性；如果事物有自性，就不可能是缘起的。缘起与空是同义语。

"Whatever is dependently co-arisen, that is explained to be emptiness." This is Nagarjuna's most concise formula: dependent origination = emptiness. If things are dependently originated, they lack svabhava; if things had svabhava, they could not be dependently originated. The two are synonymous.

**2. 因果论证 / Argument from Causation (MMK Chapter 1)**

龙树在《中论》首章"观因缘品"中分析因果关系的四种可能——因中有果、因中无果、因中亦有亦无果、因中非有非无果——全部否定。这不是否定因果本身，而是否定对因果的自性化理解。

In the opening chapter, Nagarjuna analyzes four possibilities of causation — effect exists in cause, effect does not exist in cause, both, neither — and negates all four. This does not deny causation but denies the reification of causal relations.

**3. 运动论证 / Argument from Motion (MMK Chapter 2)**

"已去无有去，未去亦无去，离已去未去，去时亦无去"——通过分析"去"(运动) 的概念，龙树展示了对运动之自性化理解的内在矛盾。

"What has already been traversed is not being traversed; what has not yet been traversed is not being traversed; apart from what has been and has not been traversed, the present traversal is also not being traversed." Through analyzing the concept of motion, Nagarjuna reveals the internal contradictions of reifying motion.

### 空性的递归性 / The Recursive Nature of Emptiness

龙树最深刻的洞见之一是**空亦复空 (sunyata-sunyata)**——空性本身也是空的。这防止了"空"被实体化为某种终极本质。如果空性有自性，那空性就成为了新的形而上学实体，违背了中观的根本精神。

One of Nagarjuna's most profound insights is **the emptiness of emptiness (sunyata-sunyata)** — emptiness itself is empty. This prevents "emptiness" from being reified into an ultimate substance. If emptiness had svabhava, it would become a new metaphysical entity, betraying the fundamental spirit of Madhyamaka.

这一递归否定与当代解构主义形成深度共鸣：德里达的延异 (différance) 同样拒绝成为新的先验所指。

This recursive negation resonates deeply with contemporary deconstructionism: Derrida's différance likewise refuses to become a new transcendental signified.

## 历史发展 / Historical Development

### 前史 / Pre-history

空性概念在龙树之前已有雏形。般若经 (Prajnaparamita sutras) 中已提出"一切法空"的思想，但缺乏系统的哲学论证。龙树的贡献是将这一直觉性的洞见转化为严格的逻辑论证。

The concept of emptiness had precedents before Nagarjuna. The Prajnaparamita sutras already proposed that "all dharmas are empty," but lacked systematic philosophical argumentation. Nagarjuna's contribution was transforming this intuitive insight into rigorous logical proof.

### 后世发展 / Later Development

- **佛护 (Buddhapalita)** 和 **月称 (Candrakirti)** 的应成派 (Prasangika) 传统：坚持空性的彻底性，认为中观不应提出任何正面主张 / Maintained the thoroughness of emptiness, arguing Madhyamaka should not posit any thesis
- **清辨 (Bhaviveka)** 的自续派 (Svatantrika) 传统：承认世俗层面的自性，但在胜义层面坚持空性 / Accepted conventional svabhava while maintaining ultimate emptiness
- **宗喀巴 (Tsongkhapa)** 的中观应成派复兴：对空性的"应成派"解读进行了精确化 / Refined the Prasangika interpretation of emptiness

## 空与假名的关系 / Emptiness and Conventional Designation

"亦为是假名"——空性不否定世俗假名。正因为空，假名才能成立。事物虽然没有自性，但可以在世俗层面被命名、被使用、被认知。这是中观"二谛"框架的基础。

"That, being a dependent designation, is itself the middle way." Emptiness does not negate conventional designation. Precisely because things are empty, they can be designated. Things, though lacking svabhava, can be named, used, and cognized at the conventional level. This is the foundation of the Madhyamaka "two truths" framework.

## 修行应用 / Practice Applications

空性不仅是一个哲学概念，更是佛教修行的核心工具：

Emptiness is not merely a philosophical concept but a central tool of Buddhist practice:

- **观空 Meditation on emptiness**: 通过系统分析一切法无自性，消解对自我和世界的执着 / Through systematic analysis of the lack of svabhava in all phenomena, dissolving attachment to self and world
- **破我执 Dismantling self-grasping**: 空性直接对治"我执"——对自我的实体化认知 / Emptiness directly counters "self-grasping" — the reification of self
- **悲智双运 Union of compassion and wisdom**: 了知空性而生大悲——正因为一切法空，众生之苦才更加令人悲悯 / Knowing emptiness gives rise to great compassion — precisely because all phenomena are empty, the suffering of beings is all the more poignant

## 当代对话 / Contemporary Dialogue

- **海德格尔 (Heidegger)**: "存在"(Sein) 与"存在者"(Seiendes) 的本体论差异——类似于空性与缘起法的区分 / Ontological difference between Being and beings — analogous to the distinction between emptiness and dependently originated phenomena
- **德里达 (Derrida)**: 延异 (différance) 作为非在场的痕迹——与空性的递归否定平行 / Différance as the trace of non-presence — parallel to the recursive negation of emptiness
- **量子力学 (Quantum mechanics)**: 关系性解释——粒子的属性只在测量关系中显现，不具有独立的"自性" / Relational interpretation — particle properties manifest only in measurement relations, lacking independent "svabhava"
- **实用主义 (Pragmatism)**: 罗蒂的反基础主义——没有终极的"镜式本质" / Rorty's anti-foundationalism — no ultimate "mirror of nature"

## 常见误解 / Common Misunderstandings

1. **"空 = 虚无" / "Emptiness = Nihilism"**: 这是最普遍的误解。龙树明确说"以有空义故，一切法得成"——空是存在的可能性条件 / The most widespread misunderstanding. Nagarjuna explicitly states that emptiness is the condition of possibility for all phenomena
2. **"空 = 不存在" / "Emptiness = Non-existence"**: 空否定的不是存在，而是自性存在的方式 / Emptiness negates not existence but the mode of existence-as-svabhava
3. **"空性是一种终极实在" / "Emptiness is an ultimate reality"**: 空亦复空——空性本身不具有本体论地位 / Emptiness of emptiness — emptiness itself has no ontological status
