# 兼修立场 Kenshū Richiba / Combined Practice Stance

## 定义 / Definition

**兼修立场**是荣西在其弘法实践中所贯彻的方法论立场：禅宗、天台宗、密教（真言宗）三宗不是互相排斥的对立体系，而是可以融合并修、各取所长的互补传统。这一立场在建仁寺"三宗兼学"的制度设计中得到了最充分的体现。

**Kenshū Richiba** ("Combined Practice Stance") is the methodological position Yōsai maintained throughout his career: Zen, Tendai, and esoteric Buddhism (Shingon) are not mutually exclusive opposing systems but complementary traditions that can be integrated and practiced together, each contributing its strengths. This stance found its fullest institutional expression in Kennin-ji's "three-school combined study" system.

## 历史背景 / Historical Background

### 日本佛教的宗派格局 / The Sectarian Landscape of Japanese Buddhism

在荣西之前，日本佛教的主导格局是：

Before Yōsai, Japanese Buddhism was dominated by:

- **天台宗 Tendai**：以比叡山延历寺为本山，教理最为完备，涵盖法华思想、净土念佛和密教元素。
  Tendai: headquartered at Enryaku-ji on Mount Hiei, with the most comprehensive doctrinal system, encompassing Lotus Sūtra thought, Pure Land nembutsu, and esoteric elements.

- **真言宗 Shingon**：以高野山为本山，空海创立，专注密教仪轨和即身成佛的修行。
  Shingon: headquartered at Mount Kōya, founded by Kūkai, focused on esoteric ritual and the practice of "becoming Buddha in this body."

- **南都六宗 Nanto Rokushū**：奈良的六个学术宗派（三论、法相、华严等），以学术研究为主。
  Nanto Rokushū: six academic schools in Nara (Sanron, Hossō, Kegon, etc.), primarily scholarly.

在这一格局中，禅宗是完全缺席的。当荣西试图引入禅宗时，他面对的不是空白地带，而是一个已有完备体系的成熟佛教生态。

In this landscape, Zen was entirely absent. When Yōsai sought to introduce Zen, he faced not a blank slate but a mature Buddhist ecosystem with established comprehensive systems.

### "兼修"与"纯粹"的张力 / The Tension Between "Combined" and "Pure"

荣西之后的日本禅宗史呈现出一条清晰的线索——从"兼修禅"走向"纯粹禅"：

Japanese Zen history after Yōsai reveals a clear trajectory — from "combined Zen" to "pure Zen":

| 阶段 Stage | 代表人物 Figure | 特征 Character |
|---|---|---|
| 兼修禅 Combined Zen | 荣西 Yōsai | 禅 + 天台 + 密教 / Zen + Tendai + Mikkyō |
| 过渡期 Transition | 圆尔辨圆 Enni Ben'en | 仍以兼修为主但禅的比重增大 / Still combined but Zen-dominant |
| 纯粹禅 Pure Zen | 道元 Dōgen | 排斥一切兼修，只修坐禅 / Rejects all combination; sitting only |

道元对"兼修禅"持严厉批判态度，认为兼修是对禅的"污染"。但荣西的兼修立场有其深刻的历史和逻辑理由。

Dōgen sharply criticized "combined Zen," regarding combination as a "contamination" of Zen. But Yōsai's combined-practice stance had deep historical and logical justifications.

## 三宗的分工 / The Division of Labor Among Three Schools

荣西对三宗的融合不是无原则的混合，而是有清晰的分工：

Yōsai's integration was not indiscriminate mixing but a clear division of labor:

### 禅宗的角色 / Zen's Role

- **核心修行**：禅（特别是看话禅）是解脱的根本方法。
  Core practice: Zen (especially Kanna Zen) is the fundamental method for liberation.

- **见性开悟**：通过公案参究实现"明心见性"。
  Seeing nature and awakening: achieving "seeing one's original nature" through kōan introspection.

- **禅林制度**：以宋代禅寺为模范，建立严格的修行共同体。
  Monastic system: establishing rigorous practice communities modeled on Song-dynasty Chan monasteries.

### 天台宗的角色 / Tendai's Role

- **教理体系**：天台宗的判教体系（五时八教）为全部佛教教义提供系统的理论框架。
  Doctrinal framework: Tendai's classification system (Five Periods, Eight Teachings) provides a systematic theoretical framework for all Buddhist doctrine.

- **止观修行**：天台止观（奢摩他与毗婆舍那）为禅修提供理论基础和技术补充。
  Calming and insight: Tendai *śamatha-vipaśyanā* provides theoretical foundations and technical supplements for meditation.

- **法华思想**：《法华经》"一乘"思想为大乘佛教的统一性提供哲学依据。
  Lotus thought: the *Lotus Sūtra*'s "One Vehicle" doctrine provides philosophical basis for Mahāyāna unity.

### 密教的角色 / Esoteric Buddhism's Role

- **仪轨加持**：密教的护摩、加持祈祷等仪轨为国家和个人的现世利益提供宗教手段。
  Ritual empowerment: esoteric rituals such as *homa* fire ceremonies and empowerment prayers provide religious means for worldly benefits of the state and individuals.

- **即身成佛**：密教"即身成佛"的修行理论与禅宗"见性成佛"形成有趣的对话。
  Buddhahood in this body: the esoteric theory of "becoming Buddha in this body" creates an interesting dialogue with Zen's "seeing nature and becoming Buddha."

- **国家祈祷**：密教的"镇护国家"传统为禅宗在日本的政治合法性提供支持。
  State protection: esoteric Buddhism's "guarding the nation" tradition supports Zen's political legitimacy in Japan.

## 建仁寺的制度体现 / Institutional Expression at Kennin-ji

建仁寺（1202年创立）是荣西兼修思想的制度结晶：

Kennin-ji (founded 1202) is the institutional crystallization of Yōsai's combined-practice thought:

- **三宗兼学**：寺内同时设置禅宗、天台宗、密教的修行空间和教学体系。
  Three-school combined study: the temple simultaneously maintained Zen, Tendai, and esoteric practice spaces and teaching programs.

- **日常修行**：僧人的日程中包括坐禅（禅宗）、诵经（天台）、修法（密教）三种修行。
  Daily practice: the monks' schedule included sitting meditation (Zen), sutra chanting (Tendai), and esoteric ritual (Mikkyō).

- **开放入学**：不限于某一宗派出身的僧人，吸引了大量比叡山和南都的学僧前来学习。
  Open enrollment: not restricted to monks from a single school, attracting many scholar-monks from Mount Hiei and the Nara temples.

## 理论论证 / Theoretical Justification

荣西为兼修立场提供了多层理论论证：

Yōsai offered multi-layered theoretical justification for combined practice:

### 判教论证 / Doctrinal Classification Argument

荣西运用天台宗的判教方法，将禅宗定位为"顿教"（顿悟法门），与天台的"圆教"和密教的"密教"并列，而非对立。三宗各有其对应的根机（修行者的资质），可以互补而非排斥。

Using Tendai's doctrinal classification method, Yōsai positioned Zen as the "sudden teaching" (sudden awakening method), parallel to — not opposed to — Tendai's "perfect teaching" and esoteric Buddhism's "secret teaching." The three schools each correspond to different capacities of practitioners and complement rather than exclude one another.

### 实践论证 / Practical Argument

不同的修行者有不同的需要：

Different practitioners have different needs:

- 利根（高资质）者适合禅宗的直指人心
  Sharp faculties suit Zen's direct pointing to the mind

- 中根者适合天台教观的系统学习
  Medium faculties suit Tendai's systematic doctrinal study

- 钝根（需要更多辅助）者适合密教的仪轨加持
  Duller faculties suit esoteric ritual and empowerment

三宗并行，使得不同资质的修行者都能找到适合的法门。

Three schools in parallel ensure practitioners of all capacities find suitable methods.

### 历史论证 / Historical Argument

荣西指出，中国宋代的大禅师——如大慧宗杲、虚庵怀敞——并非排斥其他教义，而是博通经论之后专修禅宗。兼修不但不违背禅宗精神，反而是中国禅林的正统做法。

Yōsai noted that great Song-dynasty Chan masters — such as Dahui Zonggao and Kian Ejō — did not exclude other teachings but mastered the scriptures before specializing in Zen. Combined practice does not violate Zen's spirit but represents the orthodox approach of Chinese Chan forests.

## 与道元的对比 / Contrast with Dōgen

荣西的兼修立场与道元的纯粹禅形成鲜明对比：

Yōsai's combined-practice stance stands in sharp contrast to Dōgen's pure Zen:

| 维度 Dimension | 荣西 Yōsai | 道元 Dōgen |
|---|---|---|
| 立场 Stance | 兼修 Combined | 纯粹 Pure |
| 对其他宗派的态度 Attitude to others | 包容融合 Inclusive integration | 批判排斥 Critical rejection |
| 修行方法 Method | 禅 + 天台 + 密教 | 只管打坐 Shikantaza only |
| 政治策略 Political strategy | 妥协与调和 Compromise and accommodation | 独立与退隐 Independence and withdrawal |
| 寺院制度 Monastic system | 三宗兼学 Three-school combined | 纯粹禅堂 Pure Zen hall |

但需要注意：这种对比不应简单化为"荣西不如道元纯粹"。荣西的兼修是一种**务实的传播策略**——在禅宗尚未被日本社会接受的环境中，兼修是使禅宗站稳脚跟的必要手段。没有荣西的兼修铺路，道元的纯粹禅可能根本没有生存空间。

But note: this contrast should not be simplified to "Yōsai was less pure than Dōgen." Yōsai's combination was a **pragmatic transmission strategy** — in an environment where Zen was not yet accepted by Japanese society, combination was necessary for Zen to establish a foothold. Without Yōsai's combined-practice groundwork, Dōgen's pure Zen might never have found space to exist.

## 当代意义 / Contemporary Significance

- **方法论多元论**：荣西的兼修立场预示了当代哲学中的方法论多元论——不同方法适用于不同问题和语境，不存在唯一"正确"的方法。
  Methodological pluralism: Yōsai's combined-practice stance anticipates contemporary methodological pluralism — different methods suit different problems and contexts; no single "correct" method exists.

- **跨传统对话**：在全球化时代，荣西的三宗融合经验为跨宗教、跨文化对话提供了历史范例。
  Cross-tradition dialogue: in a globalized era, Yōsai's three-school integration provides a historical model for interreligious and intercultural dialogue.

- **制度创新**：建仁寺的"三宗兼学"制度是宗教组织创新的典型案例——如何在保持传统认同的同时容纳新的元素。
  Institutional innovation: Kennin-ji's "three-school combined study" is a classic case of religious organizational innovation — how to accommodate new elements while maintaining traditional identity.
