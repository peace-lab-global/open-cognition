# 信愿行 Faith, Vow, and Practice

## 定义 / Definition

**信愿行**是净土宗修行的三个必要条件，合称"净土三资粮"。印光大师在继承善导大师等前人思想的基础上，对信愿行做了精辟的阐发和独特的排序强调：**信愿为先，行持为要**。三者缺一不可，但信愿的真实性比念佛的数量更为根本。

**Faith, Vow, and Practice (xin-yuan-xing)** are the three necessary conditions for Pure Land practice, collectively called the "Three Provisions for the Pure Land." Building on predecessors like Shandao, Master Yinguang offered precise analysis and distinctive emphasis: **Faith and Vow take priority; Practice provides the substance.** All three are indispensable, but the authenticity of faith and aspiration matters more than the quantity of recitation.

## 概念解析 / Conceptual Analysis

### 信 Faith (xin)

印光所说的"信"不是盲信，而是基于理解的深信。具体包含三个层面：

Yinguang's "faith" is not blind belief but deep trust rooted in understanding. It encompasses three dimensions:

1. **信自**：相信自己本具佛性，有成佛的可能性 / Faith in oneself — trusting that one possesses Buddha-nature and the potential for Buddhahood
2. **信他**：相信阿弥陀佛的四十八大愿真实不虚，相信释迦牟尼佛所说的净土法门真实可信 / Faith in others — trusting that Amitabha Buddha's forty-eight great vows are real and that Shakyamuni Buddha's Pure Land teachings are trustworthy
3. **信因信果**：相信念佛是因，往生是果，如同种瓜得瓜、种豆得豆 / Faith in cause and effect — trusting that recitation is the cause and rebirth is the effect, just as planting melons yields melons

印光特别强调信与愿的不可分割性："有信无愿，非真信也。有愿无信，非真愿也。"——真正的信心必然包含往生的愿望，真正的愿望必然建立在信心的基础上。

Yinguang especially stressed the inseparability of faith and vow: "Faith without vow is not true faith. Vow without faith is not true vow." Genuine faith necessarily includes the aspiration for rebirth; genuine aspiration necessarily rests on the foundation of faith.

### 愿 Vow (yuan)

"愿"是发愿往生西方极乐世界。印光将愿分为两个面向：

"Vow" means the aspiration to be reborn in the Western Pure Land of Ultimate Bliss. Yinguang divided this aspiration into two orientations:

- **厌离娑婆**：认识到娑婆世界（我们所处的世界）的本质是苦，不再贪恋世间的名利、情感、地位 / Renunciation of samsara — recognizing that this world's nature is suffering, no longer clinging to worldly fame, profit, emotion, or status
- **欣求极乐**：向往西方极乐世界的清净、安乐、不退转，渴望在阿弥陀佛的加持下快速成佛 / Aspiration for the Pure Land — yearning for the purity, bliss, and irreversibility of the Western Pure Land, desiring swift Buddhahood under Amitabha's grace

印光指出，愿的真实性不在于口头上说"我要往生"，而在于内心是否真正放下了对世间的执着。一个嘴上说愿往生、心里却放不下世间名利的人，其愿是虚假的。

Yinguang pointed out that the authenticity of one's vow lies not in saying "I want to be reborn there" but in whether one's heart has truly released attachment to this world. One who speaks of rebirth while clinging to worldly fame and profit has a false vow.

### 行 Practice (xing)

"行"是具体的修行方法，在净土宗中主要指**持名念佛**——称念"南无阿弥陀佛"（Namo Amituofo）。印光的念佛方法以**简单易行**著称：

"Practice" refers to the concrete method of cultivation, which in Pure Land Buddhism primarily means **reciting the Buddha's name** — chanting "Namo Amituofo" (Namo Amitabha Buddha). Yinguang's approach to recitation is noted for its **simplicity and accessibility**:

- **不拘形式**：行住坐卧都可以念佛，不需要特定的姿势或场所 / No fixed form — one can recite while walking, standing, sitting, or lying down
- **计数或不计数**：初学可以计数（每天念多少声），纯熟后可以不计数 / Counting optional — beginners may count recitations; the proficient need not
- **重在摄心**：念佛的关键不是嘴巴念，而是心念——口中念得清清楚楚，耳中听得清清楚楚，心中记得清清楚楚 / Focus the mind — the key is not vocal recitation but mental recitation: clear in the mouth, clear in the ear, clear in the mind

## 印光的独特贡献 / Yinguang's Unique Contribution

### 信愿为决定性因素 / Faith and Vow as Decisive Factors

印光最独特的贡献在于强调：**往生的决定性因素是信愿的有无，而非念佛功夫的深浅**。他引用蕅益大师的话："得生与否，全由信愿之有无；品位高下，全由持名之深浅。"——能不能往生，看信愿真不真；往生后的品位高低，看念佛功夫深不深。

Yinguang's most distinctive contribution is his emphasis that **the decisive factor for rebirth is whether one has genuine faith and vow, not the depth of one's recitation practice**. He quoted Master Ouyi: "Whether one is reborn depends entirely on the presence or absence of faith and vow; the grade of rebirth depends entirely on the depth of one's recitation practice."

这一观点的革命性意义在于：它降低了净土修行的门槛——即使念佛功夫不深，只要有真信切愿，就有往生的可能。这使净土法门对普通人（非专业修行人）具有极大的吸引力。

The revolutionary significance of this view lies in lowering the threshold for Pure Land practice — even with modest recitation skill, genuine faith and sincere aspiration make rebirth possible. This makes the Pure Land path enormously attractive to ordinary people who are not professional practitioners.

### 对治修行中的偏差 / Correcting Practice Deviations

印光在《文钞》中反复批评几种修行偏差：

In his Collected Writings, Yinguang repeatedly criticized several practice deviations:

1. **有行无信愿**：每天念很多佛号，但内心没有真实的信心和愿望——这是"有口无心"，效果甚微 / Practice without faith and vow — reciting many Buddha-names daily without genuine faith or aspiration is "lips without heart," yielding minimal results
2. **重功夫轻信愿**：过度追求念佛的"功夫"（如一心不乱），忽视信愿的培养——本末倒置 / Prioritizing skill over faith — obsessing over "concentration" while neglecting faith and vow is putting the cart before the horse
3. **好高骛远**：看不起简单的念佛，追求高深的禅宗或密宗法门——印光认为这是对净土法门的信心不足 / Reaching too high — dismissing simple recitation in favor of "advanced" Chan or Esoteric practices shows insufficient faith in the Pure Land path

## 与其他概念的关联 / Connections to Other Concepts

### 与敦伦尽分的关系 / Relationship to Fulfilling Duties

敦伦尽分是信愿行的**前置条件**。一个在世间伦理中不负责任的人，不可能真正生起往生的愿望。印光说：做人做得好，念佛才能念得好。

Fulfilling duties is the **prerequisite** for Faith-Vow-Practice. One who is irresponsible in worldly ethics cannot genuinely develop aspiration for rebirth. As Yinguang taught: only those who live well as human beings can practice Buddha-recitation well.

### 与因果教育的关系 / Relationship to Karma Education

深信因果是生起真实信愿的**认知基础**。不理解因果，就不会真正厌离娑婆（因为不知道世间是苦因所感）；不理解因果，就不会真正欣求极乐（因为不知道净土是善因所感）。

Deep belief in karma is the **cognitive foundation** for genuine faith and vow. Without understanding karma, one cannot truly renounce samsara (not knowing the world arises from afflictive causes) nor truly aspire to the Pure Land (not knowing it arises from virtuous causes).

## 当代意义 / Contemporary Significance

### 信心在现代心理学中的对应 / Faith and Modern Psychology

现代积极心理学中的"自我效能感"(self-efficacy) 和"希望理论"(hope theory) 与印光的"信愿"概念有深层共鸣。班杜拉 (Bandura) 的自我效能理论认为，对自己能力的信心是行动的前提——这与印光"信为道元功德母"的观点异曲同工。

Modern positive psychology's concepts of self-efficacy and hope theory resonate deeply with Yinguang's "faith and vow." Bandura's self-efficacy theory holds that confidence in one's capacity is the prerequisite for action — echoing Yinguang's teaching that "faith is the source of the path and the mother of all merit."

### 简化修行的现代价值 / Modern Value of Simplified Practice

在快节奏的现代生活中，印光"简单易行"的念佛方法具有特殊的实用价值——不需要大量时间、不需要专门场所、不需要复杂仪轨，任何人都可以在日常生活中修行。

In fast-paced modern life, Yinguang's simple, accessible recitation method holds special practical value — requiring no large time commitments, no special venues, no complex rituals, anyone can practice within daily life.

## 常见误读 / Common Misreadings

- **❌ "信愿行 = 只要信就够了 / Just having faith is enough"** → 信愿行三者缺一不可。印光强调信愿为先，但从未说行不重要。 / All three are indispensable. Yinguang emphasized faith and vow as primary but never said practice is unimportant.

- **❌ "念佛 = 数量越多越好 / More recitation is always better"** → 印光强调念佛重在摄心，不在数量。有口无心地念一万声，不如诚心诚意地念十声。 / Yinguang stressed that recitation's value lies in mental focus, not quantity. Ten thousand distracted recitations are inferior to ten sincere ones.

- **❌ "净土 = 逃避现实 / Pure Land is escapism"** → 印光的净土修行恰恰要求在现实生活中尽责（敦伦尽分），不是逃避。 / Yinguang's Pure Land practice demands responsible engagement with real life (fulfilling duties), not escape from it.
