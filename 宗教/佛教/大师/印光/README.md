# 印光 Yinguang

> "敦伦尽分，闲邪存诚，诸恶莫作，众善奉行，信愿念佛，求生西方。"
> — 印光大师

## 基本信息 / Basic Information

**印光大师 Yinguang**（1861-1940），陕西郃阳人，俗姓赵，名绍伊，法名圣量，字印光，别号常惭愧僧。被尊为**净土宗第十三祖**——中国净土宗传承中的最后一位祖师，也是近代中国佛教复兴运动最具影响力的精神导师。

- **出生地 / Birthplace**: 陕西省同州府郃阳县（今合阳县）
- **年代 / Era**: 1861-1940，横跨晚清、民国两个时代
- **身份 / Role**: 净土宗第十三祖、佛教教育家、书信导师
- **传承 / Lineage**: 继承善导大师净土思想，融合儒家伦理与佛教修行
- **核心著作 / Core Text**: 《印光法师文钞》（正编/续编/三编）

Born Zhao Shaoyi in 1861 in Heyang County, Shaanxi Province, Master Yinguang (Dharma name: Shengliang) became the **Thirteenth Patriarch of the Pure Land School** — the final patriarch in the Chinese Pure Land lineage. He is widely regarded as the most influential Buddhist teacher of modern China, whose epistolary guidance shaped the spiritual lives of millions.

## 核心命题 / Core Propositions

### 1. 敦伦尽分 Fulfill One's Duties

修行的起点不是高深的禅定或哲学思辨，而是在日常生活中尽到自己的伦理责任——做父亲就像父亲，做子女就像子女，做丈夫就像丈夫，做妻子就像妻子。"敦伦"是敦睦人伦，"尽分"是尽自己的本分。这是儒家伦理与佛教修行的交汇点。

**The starting point of practice is not lofty meditation or philosophical speculation, but fulfilling one's ethical duties in daily life — as parent, child, spouse, citizen. "Fulfilling duties" is where Confucian ethics meets Buddhist cultivation.**

详见 [敦伦尽分](concepts/fulfill-duties.md)。

### 2. 信愿行 Faith, Vow, and Practice

往生净土的三个必要条件构成一个完整的修行框架：**信**——深信阿弥陀佛的愿力和西方净土的真实存在；**愿**——发愿往生净土，厌离娑婆世界的痛苦；**行**——以持名念佛为日常功课。三者缺一不可，而信愿为先。

**Faith, Vow, and Practice are the three requisites for rebirth in the Pure Land. Faith means trusting Amitabha's vows; Vow means aspiring to be reborn there; Practice means reciting the Buddha's name. All three are necessary, with Faith and Vow taking priority.**

详见 [信愿行](concepts/faith-vow-practice.md)。

### 3. 常惭愧 Constant Humility

印光自称"常惭愧僧"——一生以惭愧心自居，不以高僧大德自诩。惭愧不是自卑，而是一种清醒的自我认知：知道自己是凡夫，知道烦恼未断，因此不敢懈怠、不敢骄傲。惭愧心是对治我慢（arrogance）最有效的良药。

**Yinguang called himself the "Constant Ashamed Monk" — maintaining humility throughout his life. Shame is not self-deprecation but clear-eyed self-awareness: knowing one is still an ordinary being with unresolved afflictions, and thus never becoming complacent or arrogant.**

详见 [常惭愧](concepts/constant-humility.md)。

### 4. 因果教育 Karma Education

"深信因果"是印光一切教诲的基石。不信因果，则一切修行无从谈起。印光特别强调因果不是宿命论——因果是动态的、可以通过当下的努力改变的。他大力推广《了凡四训》《感应篇》等因果教育读本，认为这是挽救世道人心的根本。

**Deep belief in cause and effect (karma) is the cornerstone of all Yinguang's teachings. Karma is not fatalism — it is dynamic and transformable through present effort. He vigorously promoted karma education texts as the foundation for moral and spiritual transformation.**

详见 [因果教育](concepts/karma-education.md)。

### 5. 儒佛合一 Confucian-Buddhist Synthesis

印光提出了清晰的儒佛关系框架：儒家是做人的基础，佛教是解脱的归宿。二者不是混淆，而是分层融合——"敦伦尽分"属于儒家伦理层面，"信愿念佛"属于佛教解脱层面。没有前者，后者是空中楼阁；没有后者，前者是不究竟的。

**Confucianism provides the ethical foundation for being human; Buddhism provides the ultimate liberation. They are not conflated but integrated in layers — without ethical foundations, spiritual liberation is impossible; without liberation, ethics remain incomplete.**

详见 [儒佛合一](concepts/confucian-buddhist-synthesis.md)。

## 思想脉络 / Intellectual Context

### 早年：儒学根基 / Early Years: Confucian Foundation

印光出生于陕西农家，少年时读儒书，走科举之路。后因病（眼疾）读佛经，对佛法生起信心，遂于21岁出家。早年的儒学教育深刻影响了他的佛学思想——他一生强调"敦伦尽分"，正是儒学根基的体现。

Yinguang was born into a farming family in Shaanxi. As a youth he studied Confucian classics and prepared for the imperial examinations. After an eye illness led him to read Buddhist sutras, he developed faith and ordained at age 21. His early Confucian education profoundly shaped his Buddhist thought — his lifelong emphasis on "fulfilling duties" directly reflects this foundation.

### 中期：普陀山闭关 / Middle Period: Putuo Mountain Retreat

出家后，印光在浙江普陀山法雨寺闭关修行长达三十年（约1887-1917）。期间精修净土念佛法门，博览大藏经，不问世事，不以高僧自居。这三十年的潜心修行为他后来的弘法奠定了深厚的学养和证量基础。

After ordination, Yinguang entered a thirty-year retreat (c. 1887-1917) at Fayu Temple on Mount Putuo. During this period he devoted himself to Pure Land recitation practice, studied the entire Buddhist canon, and avoided public attention. These three decades of intensive cultivation laid the deep scholarly and experiential foundation for his later teaching.

### 晚期：书信弘法 / Later Period: Epistolary Teaching

约60岁后，印光的名声渐起，各地信众纷纷来信求教。他以书信方式一一回复，针对不同根器、不同处境的信众给出具体指导。这些书信后被编为《印光法师文钞》，成为近代最重要的佛教文献之一。印光不建道场、不收徒弟、不做住持，以书信度化百万人，是佛教弘法方式的独特范例。

Around age 60, Yinguang's reputation grew, and laypeople across China began writing to him for guidance. He responded individually to each letter, offering specific advice tailored to the questioner's capacity and circumstances. These letters were later compiled as *The Collected Writings of Master Yinguang* — one of the most important Buddhist documents of the modern era. Notably, he never established temples, never accepted formal disciples, and never served as abbot, yet guided millions through letters alone — a unique model of Buddhist outreach.

## 关键影响 / Key Influence

### 对近代佛教的影响 / Impact on Modern Buddhism

印光被尊为净土宗第十三祖，是中国净土宗传承的最后一位祖师。他的影响极为深远：

- **弘一法师**：近代最伟大的律宗高僧，以印光为师，多次请求皈依，最终在印光指导下精进修行
- **太虚大师**：近代佛教改革领袖，虽走不同路线，但对印光极为尊敬
- **净土宗复兴**：印光是近代净土宗复兴的核心人物，使净土法门在知识阶层中获得广泛认可
- **佛教世俗化**：印光的"敦伦尽分"理念使佛教修行融入日常生活，降低了修行门槛

Yinguang is recognized as the Thirteenth (final) Patriarch of the Chinese Pure Land School. His influence was profound: he inspired Master Hongyi (the greatest Vinaya master of the era), earned the respect of Master Taixu (the leading Buddhist reformer), spearheaded the modern Pure Land revival, and made Buddhist practice accessible to laypeople through his "fulfilling duties" approach.

### 弘法风格 / Teaching Style

印光的弘法风格以**平实、恳切、不玄妙**著称。他反对高谈阔论、故弄玄虚的"口头禅"，强调老实念佛、脚踏实地修行。他常说"一分诚敬得一分利益，十分诚敬得十分利益"——诚敬心是获得法益的关键。

His teaching style was characterized by **plainness, earnestness, and rejection of mystical pretension**. He opposed empty philosophical talk, insisting instead on sincere, grounded practice. His famous saying: "One portion of sincerity yields one portion of benefit; ten portions of sincerity yield ten portions of benefit."

## 常见误读 / Common Misreadings

- **❌ "印光 = 保守派 / Yinguang was a conservative"** → 印光立足传统，但面对现代社会进行了务实的适应。他的书信弘法方式本身就是创新。 / He was rooted in tradition but pragmatically adapted to modernity. His epistolary teaching method was itself innovative.

- **❌ "儒佛合一 = 混淆 / Confucian-Buddhist synthesis is confusion"** → 印光的儒佛合一是分层融合：儒为做人基础，佛为解脱归宿，各有定位。 / It is a layered integration: Confucianism for ethical foundations, Buddhism for ultimate liberation, each with a clear role.

- **❌ "文钞 = 书信集不深 / The Collected Writings are just shallow letters"** → 《印光法师文钞》是深入浅出的修行指南，涵盖了净土教理、修行方法、伦理教育等全方位内容。 / The Collected Writings are a comprehensive, accessible guide covering Pure Land doctrine, practice methods, and ethical education.

- **❌ "念佛 = 不思考 / Buddha-recitation means no thinking"** → 印光强调信愿为先，念佛需要理解教理、深信因果，不是盲修瞎练。 / Yinguang emphasized that recitation requires understanding doctrine and believing in karma — it is never blind practice.
