# 三密 The Three Mysteries (三密 · Sanmitsu)

> "以印契印身，以真言印口，以三摩地印意。"
> "Seal the body with mudrā, seal the speech with mantra, seal the mind with samādhi."

## 定义 · Definition

**三密**（さんみつ，sanmitsu）是空海密教修行的核心架构，指**身密**（身体的奥秘）、**口密**（语言的奥秘）和**意密**（意识的奥秘）三个维度。空海认为，这三者不是分离的修行领域，而是法身佛大日如来（Mahāvairocana）之存在的三个不可分割的面向。修行者通过三密修行，使自己的身口意与大日如来的身口意"相应"，从而实现"即身成佛"。

The **Three Mysteries** (sanmitsu) constitute the core framework of Kūkai's esoteric practice, referring to the **Mystery of Body**, **Mystery of Speech**, and **Mystery of Mind**. Kūkai taught that these three are not separate domains of practice but three inseparable dimensions of Dharma Body Buddha Mahāvairocana's existence. Through the Three Mysteries practice, the practitioner's body, speech, and mind "correspond" with those of Mahāvairocana, thereby achieving "Buddhahood in this very body."

## 三密的哲学基础 · Philosophical Foundation

### 大日如来的三密 · Mahāvairocana's Three Mysteries

空海密教与显教的根本差异在于对佛陀的理解。显教以历史上的释迦牟尼佛为本尊，而密教以**大日如来**（梵文：Mahāvairocana，意为"大光明遍照"）为法身佛，即宇宙终极实相本身的人格化显现。大日如来的三密遍满整个宇宙：

The fundamental difference between Kūkai's Esoteric Buddhism and exoteric Buddhism lies in the understanding of Buddhahood. Exoteric Buddhism takes the historical Śākyamuni as its principal Buddha, while Esoteric Buddhism takes **Mahāvairocana** (Sanskrit: "Great Illuminator") as the Dharma Body Buddha — the personified manifestation of cosmic ultimate reality itself. Mahāvairocana's Three Mysteries pervade the entire cosmos:

- **身密**：宇宙中一切物质形态——山川、星辰、人体——都是大日如来的身体显现。
  **Body Mystery**: All material forms in the universe — mountains, rivers, stars, human bodies — are manifestations of Mahāvairocana's body.
- **口密**：一切声音——风声、水声、鸟鸣、人语——都是大日如来的语言宣说。
  **Speech Mystery**: All sounds — wind, water, birdsong, human speech — are Mahāvairocana's Dharma proclamation.
- **意密**：一切心识活动——思维、情感、觉知——都是大日如来的意识流动。
  **Mind Mystery**: All mental activities — thought, emotion, awareness — are the flow of Mahāvairocana's consciousness.

### 众生本具三密 · All Beings Possess the Three Mysteries

空海的重要洞见是：不仅佛具有三密，一切众生本具三密。问题不在于众生缺少什么，而在于众生不知道自己拥有什么。三密修行的本质不是"获取"佛性，而是"揭示"本来具足的佛性。

Kūkai's crucial insight is that not only Buddhas but all sentient beings inherently possess the Three Mysteries. The problem is not that beings lack something but that they are unaware of what they already possess. The essence of Three Mysteries practice is not "acquiring" Buddha-nature but "revealing" the inherently present Buddha-nature.

## 三密修行 · The Practice of Three Mysteries

### 身密 · Mystery of Body: Mudrā (印相)

身密的修行主要通过**印相**（mudrā，いんぞう）来实现。印相是特定的手印或身体姿势，每一个手印都与特定的佛或菩萨的誓愿和力量相应。

The practice of the Body Mystery is primarily realized through **mudrā** (印相). Mudrās are specific hand gestures or bodily postures, each corresponding to the vow and power of a particular Buddha or Bodhisattva.

最重要的手印之一是**智拳印**（chiken-in），这是大日如来的根本手印，象征着智慧与方便的合一。修行者结此印时，其身体即与大日如来的身体相应。

One of the most important mudrās is the **Wisdom Fist Mudrā** (chiken-in), the fundamental mudrā of Mahāvairocana, symbolizing the union of wisdom and skillful means. When the practitioner forms this mudrā, their body corresponds with Mahāvairocana's body.

此外，身体的其他行为——行走、坐姿、呼吸——也都被纳入身密修行的范畴。空海认为，整个身体可以成为修行的工具，而非修行的障碍。

Furthermore, other bodily actions — walking, sitting posture, breathing — are all incorporated into the scope of Body Mystery practice. Kūkai taught that the entire body can become a vehicle for practice rather than an obstacle to it.

### 口密 · Mystery of Speech: Mantra (真言)

口密的修行主要通过**真言**（mantra，しんごん）来实现。真言是梵文种子字（bīja）或短句的音译，被认为蕴含着佛菩萨的本质力量。

The practice of the Speech Mystery is primarily realized through **mantra** (真言). Mantras are transliterations of Sanskrit seed syllables (bīja) or short phrases believed to contain the essential power of Buddhas and Bodhisattvas.

最著名的真言之一是大日如来的种子字**"嗡"**（Oṃ / Auṃ），以及大日如来心咒 **"嗡 阿毗罗吽 欠 娑婆诃"**（Oṃ a vi ra hūṃ khaṃ svāhā）。空海在《声字实相义》中论证，真言不是象征性的符号，而是实相本身的声学显现——诵念真言就是直接调动宇宙的实相力量。

Among the most famous mantras is Mahāvairocana's seed syllable **"Oṃ"** (Auṃ) and the Mahāvairocana Heart Mantra **"Oṃ a vi ra hūṃ khaṃ svāhā."** Kūkai argued in *Shōji Jissō Gi* that mantras are not symbolic signs but acoustic manifestations of reality itself — reciting mantras directly mobilizes the power of cosmic reality.

真言的发音被认为至关重要：音准的偏差会影响修行效果。这一立场使密教在语音学和声学方面积累了丰富的知识传统。

The pronunciation of mantras is considered crucial: deviations in pronunciation affect practice efficacy. This stance led Esoteric Buddhism to accumulate rich knowledge traditions in phonetics and acoustics.

### 意密 · Mystery of Mind: Samādhi (三摩地/观想)

意密的修行主要通过**观想**（visualization / contemplation）来实现。修行者在冥想中观想曼荼罗、种子字、佛菩萨形象等，将心识集中于法身佛的境界。

The practice of the Mind Mystery is primarily realized through **visualization** (观想). The practitioner visualizes maṇḍalas, seed syllables, and Buddha-Bodhisattva forms in meditation, concentrating the mind on the realm of the Dharma Body Buddha.

最重要的观想法之一是**月轮观**（gachirin-kan）：修行者观想心中有一轮明月，月轮中有种子字，种子字放光遍照法界。这一观想象征着众生本具的清净佛性，如同明月被云雾遮蔽，修行只是驱散云雾、显露月光。

One of the most important visualization practices is the **Moon Disk Contemplation** (gachirin-kan): the practitioner visualizes a bright moon disk within the heart, containing a seed syllable that radiates light throughout the dharmadhātu. This visualization symbolizes the inherently pure Buddha-nature within all beings, like a bright moon obscured by clouds — practice merely dispels the clouds to reveal the moonlight.

## 三密加持 · Sanmitsu Kaji (Empowerment through Three Mysteries)

空海提出的**三密加持**（さんみつかじ）概念是其密教思想的关键机制。"加持"（kaji）由"加"（佛的力量加被于修行者）和"持"（修行者持受此力量）两部分组成。空海用"如日轮照水"的比喻来解释：如同太阳的光照射在水面上，水面反映太阳的光辉——佛的三密（日轮）加被于众生的三密（水面），众生的三密反映佛的三密，二者不二不一。

The concept of **Sanmitsu Kaji** (empowerment through the Three Mysteries) is the crucial mechanism of Kūkai's esoteric thought. "Kaji" comprises "ka" (the Buddha's power bestowed upon the practitioner) and "ji" (the practitioner's reception and retention of this power). Kūkai used the metaphor of "sunlight shining on water": just as sunlight illuminates the water's surface and the water reflects the sun's radiance — the Buddha's Three Mysteries (sun) are bestowed upon the practitioner's Three Mysteries (water), and the practitioner's Three Mysteries reflect the Buddha's, the two being neither identical nor separate.

这一机制解决了密教的核心悖论：如果众生本具佛性，为何还需要修行？空海的回答是：佛性如种子，三密修行如浇水施肥——种子本就具有长成大树的潜能，但需要适当条件才能显现。

This mechanism resolves a core esoteric paradox: if beings inherently possess Buddha-nature, why is practice necessary? Kūkai's answer: Buddha-nature is like a seed, and Three Mysteries practice is like watering and fertilizing — the seed inherently possesses the potential to become a great tree, but requires appropriate conditions to manifest.

## 三密与现代身心学 · Three Mysteries and Modern Somatics

空海的三密体系在若干方面预见了现代身心学的洞见：

Kūkai's Three Mysteries framework anticipates modern somatic insights in several respects:

- **具身认知**：身密修行承认身体不仅是心灵的容器，更是认知和觉悟的直接参与者。
  **Embodied Cognition**: Body Mystery practice acknowledges that the body is not merely a container for the mind but a direct participant in cognition and awakening.
- **言语行为理论**：口密修行与奥斯汀（J.L. Austin）的"言语行为"理论相通——真言不仅是描述现实，更是在执行现实。
  **Speech Act Theory**: Speech Mystery practice resonates with J.L. Austin's speech act theory — mantras do not merely describe reality but perform it.
- **冥想神经科学**：意密的观想修行与现代神经科学关于冥想对大脑可塑性影响的研究相吻合。
  **Meditation Neuroscience**: Mind Mystery visualization practice aligns with modern neuroscience 研究 on meditation's effects on brain plasticity.
