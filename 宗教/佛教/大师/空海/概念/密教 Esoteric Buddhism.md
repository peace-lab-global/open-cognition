# 密教 Esoteric Buddhism

> "密者，秘奥之义，非隐藏之义。"
> "The 'esoteric' means the profound and ineffable, not the concealed."

## 定义 · Definition

密教（梵文：गुह्य Mantrayāna / Vajrayāna；中文：密教 / 密宗；日文：みっきょう Mikkyō）是佛教中与"显教"（exoteric Buddhism）相对的一个传统。其核心主张是：佛陀最深奥的证悟经验无法仅通过公开的经典文字传达，而必须通过师徒之间直接的灌顶（abhiṣeka）和口传（kuden）来传递。

Esoteric Buddhism (Sanskrit: Guhyamantrayāna / Vajrayāna; Chinese: Mìjiào / Mìzōng; Japanese: Mikkyō) is a Buddhist tradition contrasted with "exoteric Buddhism." Its central claim is that the Buddha's most profound realization cannot be conveyed through publicly available scriptural texts alone but must be transmitted directly from master to disciple through initiation (abhiṣeka) and oral transmission (kuden).

"密"并非意味着将知识对大众隐藏，而是指这些教法的深奥性超越了概念性语言的表达能力。正如空海所言，密教的"密"是"自证之境"，只有亲证者方能完全理解。

"Esoteric" does not mean hiding knowledge from the masses; rather, the profundity of these teachings transcends the expressive capacity of conceptual language. As Kūkai stated, the "esoteric" of Esoteric Buddhism is a "self-realized realm" fully comprehensible only to those who have directly experienced it.

## 历史发展 · Historical Development

### 印度起源 · Indian Origins

密教的根源可追溯到公元三至七世纪的印度，当时大乘佛教内部发展出了一系列以"陀罗尼"（dhāraṇī，总持咒语）和"曼荼罗"（maṇḍala，坛城）为核心的修行法门。重要的印度密教经典包括：

The roots of Esoteric Buddhism trace to third- to seventh-century India, when Mahāyāna Buddhism developed practices centered on dhāraṇī (memory-retention formulas) and maṇḍala (sacred diagrams). Key Indian esoteric texts include:

- **《大日经》**（Mahāvairocana Sūtra）：约七世纪成书，是密教的基础经典，阐述胎藏界曼荼罗和"三密"修行。
  *Mahāvairocana Sūtra*: Compiled around the seventh century, this foundational text expounds the Garbhadhātu Maṇḍala and the "three mysteries" practice.
- **《金刚顶经》**（Vajraśekhara Sūtra）：阐述金刚界曼荼罗和"五相成身观"等高阶修行。
  *Vajraśekhara Sūtra*: Expounds the Vajradhātu Maṇḍala and advanced practices such as the "five-phase self-realization."
- **《苏悉地经》**（Susiddhi Sūtra）：密教仪轨和成就法的综合手册。
  *Susiddhi Sūtra*: A comprehensive manual of esoteric rituals and accomplishment methods.

### 中国传播 · Transmission to China

密教于唐代（618–907）传入中国，形成了所谓"唐密"。三位关键人物是：

Esoteric Buddhism was transmitted to Tang-dynasty China (618–907), forming what is known as "Tang Esotericism." Three pivotal figures were:

1. **善无畏**（Śubhakarasiṃha, 637–735）：将《大日经》带入中国并翻译。
   **Śubhakarasiṃha** (637–735): Brought and translated the *Mahāvairocana Sūtra* to China.
2. **金刚智**（Vajrabodhi, 671–741）：传入《金刚顶经》系统的教法。
   **Vajrabodhi** (671–741): Transmitted the *Vajraśekhara Sūtra* system.
3. **不空**（Amoghavajra, 705–774）：集两家之大成，成为唐代最受尊崇的密教大师。
   **Amoghavajra** (705–774): Synthesized both lineages and became the most revered esoteric master of the Tang dynasty.

惠果（746–805）作为不空的弟子，在长安青龙寺融合两部大法，并将之传给空海，完成了密教从中国到日本的传承。

Huiguo (746–805), a disciple of Amoghavajra, integrated the two great mandala systems at Qinglong Temple in Chang'an and transmitted them to Kūkai, completing the transmission of Esoteric Buddhism from China to Japan.

### 日本发展 · Development in Japan

空海于 806 年将密教带回日本后，建立了独立的真言宗，形成了"东密"（Tōmitsu）传统。与此同时，最澄将密教融入天台宗，形成了"台密"（Taimitsu）传统。两者之间的对话和竞争贯穿了日本佛教史。

After Kūkai brought Esoteric Buddhism back to Japan in 806, he established the independent Shingon school, forming the "Tōmitsu" (Eastern Esotericism) tradition. Meanwhile, Saichō integrated esoteric elements into Tendai Buddhism, forming the "Taimitsu" tradition. The dialogue and competition between these two traditions thread through the history of Japanese Buddhism.

值得注意的是，中国本土的唐密在唐末因政治动荡而几近消亡，而空海在日本的传承却绵延不绝，至今已超过一千二百年。这使得日本的东密成为研究印度和中国密教的最重要活传统。

Significantly, Tang Esotericism in China nearly disappeared during the political upheavals of the late Tang dynasty, while Kūkai's Japanese lineage continued unbroken for over twelve centuries. This makes Japanese Tōmitsu the most important living tradition for the study of Indian and Chinese Esoteric Buddhism.

## 核心教义 · Core Doctrines

### 法身说法 · The Dharma Body Preaches

显教认为佛的教法是由历史上的释迦牟尼佛宣说的，而密教主张真正的教法源头是**法身佛大日如来**（Dharmakāya Mahāvairocana）。大日如来不是历史人物，而是宇宙的终极实相本身，其"说法"不是通过语言，而是通过整个宇宙的显现。空海在《声字实相义》中论证：宇宙本身就是一部无字的经典。

Exoteric Buddhism holds that the Buddha's teachings were proclaimed by the historical Śākyamuni Buddha, while Esoteric Buddhism asserts that the true source of the Dharma is **Dharma Body Buddha Mahāvairocana**. Mahāvairocana is not a historical figure but the ultimate reality of the cosmos itself, whose "preaching" occurs not through language but through the manifestation of the entire universe. Kūkai argued in *Shōji Jissō Gi* that the cosmos itself is a wordless scripture.

### 即事而真 · Phenomena Are the Absolute

密教不认为现象世界与绝对真理之间存在鸿沟。每一个具体的事物——一朵花、一声鸟鸣、一个手势——都是大日如来的显现。这与华严宗"事事无碍"的思想相通，但密教更进一步，将其落实为具体的修行实践。

Esoteric Buddhism denies any chasm between the phenomenal world and absolute truth. Every concrete thing — a flower, a bird's song, a gesture — is a manifestation of Mahāvairocana. This resonates with Huayan Buddhism's "non-obstruction of phenomena," but Esoteric Buddhism goes further by embodying this insight in concrete practice.

### 灌顶与传承 · Initiation and Lineage

密教强调**阿阇梨**（ācārya，导师）的重要性。修行者必须经过正式的灌顶仪式，获得阿阇梨的授权，才能修习密法。这一传承链条从大日如来开始，经印度祖师、中国祖师，直到空海及其后继者，构成了真言宗法脉的根本保障。

Esoteric Buddhism emphasizes the importance of the **ācārya** (master/teacher). Practitioners must undergo formal initiation (abhiṣeka) and receive authorization from an ācārya before practicing esoteric methods. This lineage chain, beginning with Mahāvairocana, through Indian patriarchs, Chinese patriarchs, to Kūkai and his successors, constitutes the fundamental guarantee of the Shingon Dharma transmission.

## 与当代思想的对话 · Dialogue with Contemporary Thought

密教的若干核心概念与当代哲学和科学形成了有意义的对话：

Several core esoteric concepts engage meaningfully with contemporary 哲学 and science:

- **具身认知**（Embodied Cognition）：密教强调身、口、意不可分割的修行，与当代认知科学对"具身性"的重视相呼应。
  **Embodied Cognition**: Esoteric Buddhism's emphasis on inseparable body-speech-mind practice resonates with cognitive science's focus on embodied cognition.
- **过程哲学**（Process Philosophy）：大日如来的"说法"不是静态的教条，而是宇宙本身的动态显现，类似于怀特海的过程思想。
  **Process Philosophy**: Mahāvairocana's "preaching" is not static doctrine but the dynamic manifestation of the cosmos itself, paralleling Whitehead's process thought.
- **符号学**（Semiotics）：密教对"声字实相"的探讨预见了当代符号学对符号与实在关系的追问。
  **Semiotics**: The esoteric exploration of "sound, letter, and reality" anticipates contemporary semiotic inquiries into the relationship between signs and reality.
