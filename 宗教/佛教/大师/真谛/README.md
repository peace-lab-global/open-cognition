# 真谛 Paramārtha

> "阿黎耶识与如来藏，不一不异。"
> "The ālaya-vijñāna and tathāgata-garbha are neither identical nor different."

---

## 概述 / Overview

真谛（499–569 CE），梵名 Paramārtha（音译"波罗末陀"），又名拘那罗陀（Kulanātha），西印度优禅尼国（Ujjain）人，婆罗门种姓。他是南北朝时期最重要的佛经翻译家之一，也是中国唯识学的先驱。真谛在中国译出《摄大乘论》（*Mahāyānasaṃgraha*）、《摄大乘论释》（*Mahāyānasaṃgraha-bhāṣya*）、《俱舍论》（*Abhidharmakośa*）等数十部经论，创立了**摄论宗**——在玄奘法相唯识宗成立之前，这是中国唯识学的主要传承。

Paramārtha (499–569 CE), also known as Kulanātha, was a Brahmin from Ujjain in western India and one of the most important Buddhist translators of the Southern and Northern Dynasties period. He rendered into Chinese dozens of foundational treatises, including Asaṅga's *Mahāyānasaṃgraha*, Vasubandhu's commentary on it, and the *Abhidharmakośa*. He established the **Shèlùn School** (攝論宗), which constituted the principal lineage of Chinese Yogācāra before Xuánzàng's Fǎxiàng School arose in the Tang dynasty.

真谛的唯识学与后来玄奘系唯识有显著而深刻的差异。他主张**九识说**（在八识之上增设第九"阿摩罗识"）、将 ālaya 译为**阿黎耶识**（强调"藏"义而非"执藏"义）、并提出**解性黎耶**的概念（阿黎耶识中含觉悟的可能性）。这些思想代表了印度唯识学中不同于护法（Dharmapāla）—玄奘传承的另一条重要路线，可能与安慧（Sthiramati）一系的诠释更为接近。

Paramārtha's Yogācāra differs profoundly from the system later transmitted by Xuánzàng. He posited **nine consciousnesses** (adding a ninth, the *amala-vijñāna* or "immaculate consciousness"), translated *ālaya* as **阿黎耶** (emphasizing "repository" rather than "clinging-storehouse"), and introduced the concept of **jiěxìng líyé** (解性黎耶)—the "awakening-nature" within the ālaya. These positions represent an alternative trajectory within Indian Yogācāra, one that diverges from the Dharmapāla–Xuánzàng line and may align more closely with the interpretive tradition of Sthiramati.

## 生平 / Biography

### 印度时期 / Years in India (499–c.546)

真谛生于西印度优禅尼国（今印度中央邦乌贾因），这是一个历史悠久的婆罗门文化中心。他出身婆罗门种姓，自幼受过良好的传统教育，后皈依佛教并深入唯识学的修学。在印度，他继承了无著（Asaṅga）与世亲（Vasubandhu）的瑜伽行派传统，并广泛研习经论。据《续高僧传》记载，真谛"博通内外，尤精大乘"，在印度佛教界已有相当声望。

Born in Ujjain—a longstanding center of Brahmanical learning in western India—Paramārtha received a thorough traditional education before converting to Buddhism and immersing himself in Yogācāra studies. He inherited the lineage of Asaṅga and Vasubandhu and was widely read in both Buddhist and non-Buddhist 文学. According to the *Continued Biographies of Eminent Monks* (*Xù Gāosēng Zhuàn*), he was "conversant with inner and outer learning, and especially proficient in the Mahāyāna."

### 来华历程 / Journey to China (c.546)

公元546年（梁武帝中大同元年），真谛经海路抵达中国南海郡（今广州）。他应梁武帝之邀北上建康（今南京），但途中遭遇侯景之乱（548–552），建康陷落，梁朝崩溃。真谛被迫辗转流离于广州、始兴、南康等地，在极其动荡的时局中坚持译经事业。

In 546 CE, Paramārtha arrived by sea at Nánhǎi Commandery (modern Guǎngzhōu). Invited by Emperor Wǔ of Liáng, he set out for the capital Jiànkāng (modern Nánjīng), but the devastating Rebellion of Hóu Jǐng (548–552) shattered the Liáng dynasty. Paramārtha was forced into a peripatetic existence across southern China—Guǎngzhōu, Shǐxīng, Nánkāng—translating tirelessly amid political chaos.

### 译经生涯 / Translation Career (548–569)

真谛的译经生涯大致可分为三个阶段：

Paramārtha's translation career can be divided into roughly three phases:

**第一阶段（548–552）：建康时期。** 真谛初到中国，在梁武帝支持下于建康开始译经。但侯景之乱打断了这一时期，大量译稿散佚。

**Phase One (548–552): The Jiànkāng Period.** Initial translation work under imperial patronage, disrupted by the Hóu Jǐng Rebellion with significant losses of manuscripts.

**第二阶段（552–562）：流离时期。** 真谛辗转南方各地，在广州、豫章（今南昌）等地继续译经。这一时期他完成了《摄大乘论》及《释》的翻译（563年于广州），奠定了摄论宗的理论基础。同时译出《俱舍论》（559–563），这是世亲有部哲学巨著的首个汉译本。

**Phase Two (552–562): The Wandering Period.** Moving between Guǎngzhōu, Yùzhāng (modern Nánchāng), and other southern cities, Paramārtha completed his landmark translation of the *Mahāyānasaṃgraha* and its commentary (563, Guǎngzhōu), and the first Chinese rendering of the *Abhidharmakośa* (559–563).

**第三阶段（562–569）：广州晚期。** 真谛晚年定居广州，在制旨寺等处继续翻译和讲学。他圆寂于569年（陈宣帝太建元年），享年约七十岁。

**Phase Three (562–569): Late Guǎngzhōu Period.** Settling in Guǎngzhōu, he continued translating and teaching at Zhìzhǐ Temple until his death around 569 CE at approximately seventy years of age.

## 核心思想 / Core Doctrines

真谛的思想体系可以从以下几个关键命题来把握：

Paramārtha's doctrinal system can be grasped through several key theses:

### 一、九识说 / The Nine Consciousnesses

在唯识学传统的八识体系（前五识、第六意识、第七末那识、第八阿赖耶识）之上，真谛增设了第九识——**阿摩罗识**（amalavijñāna，无垢识）。这一识不是独立于八识之外的另一个识体，而是转识成智后的清净识——当阿赖耶识中的杂染种子被彻底净化时，识不再是"藏"（ālaya）而成为"无垢"（amala）。

Above the standard eight consciousnesses, Paramārtha posited a ninth—the **amala-vijñāna** (immaculate consciousness). This is not a separate ontological entity but the purified state of consciousness after transformation: when the defiled seeds in the ālaya are completely exhausted, consciousness ceases to be a "storehouse" (ālaya) and becomes "immaculate" (amala).

九识说的哲学意义在于：它为"转识成智"（vijñāna-parāvṛtti）提供了一个积极的存在论基础。在玄奘系唯识中，转依（āśraya-parāvṛtti）主要是否定性的——杂染灭尽即菩提。而真谛的九识说则暗示：有一个超越染净对立的清净本体，作为转依的最终依据。这与如来藏思想的对话是显而易见的。

The philosophical significance lies in providing a positive ontological grounding for "consciousness-transformation" (*vijñāna-parāvṛtti*). In Xuánzàng's system, transformation is largely negative—the cessation of defilement is enlightenment. Paramārtha's ninth consciousness suggests a pure substrate transcending the duality of defiled and pure, serving as the ultimate basis for transformation. The dialogue with *tathāgata-garbha* thought is evident.

### 二、阿黎耶识 / Ālaya According to Paramārtha

真谛将梵文 *ālaya-vijñāna* 译为"阿黎耶识"，而非玄奘所用的"阿赖耶识"。这一翻译差异并非仅仅是音译选择的不同，而是反映了对 *ālaya* 概念的不同理解：

Paramārtha rendered *ālaya-vijñāna* as **阿黎耶識** rather than Xuánzàng's **阿賴耶識**. This translational difference reflects a substantive divergence in understanding:

- **阿黎耶**（真谛）：强调 *ālaya* 的"藏"义——含藏一切种子的总库，是中性的功能性描述。
- **阿赖耶**（玄奘）：强调"执藏"义——第七识执着第八识为"我"，故 *ālaya* 带有被执取的消极含义。

真谛的阿黎耶识概念更为宽泛和中性：它是种子的储存处，是一切法生起的所依，但不必然与第七识的执着相关联。这使得阿黎耶识可以更自然地与如来藏概念对话——一个不含杂染种子的"纯净"阿黎耶，与如来藏的"自性清净心"之间的距离大大缩短。

Paramārtha's ālaya is broader and more neutral—a repository of seeds, the basis for the arising of all phenomena, not necessarily implicated by the seventh consciousness's clinging. This allows a more natural dialogue with *tathāgata-garbha*: a "pure" ālaya, free from defiled seeds, comes much closer to the "inherently pure mind" of Buddha-nature doctrine.

→ See [Ālaya in Paramārtha's System](concepts/alaya-paramartha.md)

### 三、解性黎耶 / The Awakening-Nature of the Ālaya

真谛思想中最具原创性的概念或许是**解性黎耶**——阿黎耶识中本具的觉悟之性（"解性"）。这一概念主张：即使在杂染的阿黎耶识中，也包含着觉悟的种子或可能性。这不是后天添加的，而是阿黎耶识本身固有的。

Perhaps the most original concept in Paramārtha's thought is **jiěxìng líyé** (解性黎耶)—the "awakening-nature" inherent in the ālaya. This thesis holds that even within the defiled ālaya-consciousness, there exists an innate capacity for awakening. This is not adventitious but intrinsic to the ālaya itself.

解性黎耶的概念在唯识学与如来藏思想之间架起了一座桥梁。在传统唯识学中，一切有情是否都能成佛是一个争论不休的问题（五种姓说 vs 一性皆成说）。真谛的解性黎耶明确支持后者：既然阿黎耶识中本具解性，那么一切有情皆有觉悟的可能性，五种姓的区分只是方便说而非究竟义。

The concept bridges Yogācāra and *tathāgata-garbha* thought. It unequivocally supports universal Buddhahood (*ekayāna*): since awakening-nature is intrinsic to the ālaya, all sentient beings possess the possibility of enlightenment, and the doctrine of five lineages (*gotra*) is merely provisional, not definitive.

## 摄论宗 / The Shèlùn School

真谛所创立的摄论宗以《摄大乘论》为核心经典，是中国佛教中最早系统传授唯识学的宗派。摄论宗的主要传承者包括法泰、智恺、道岳、慧旷等人。该宗在隋代（581–618）达到鼎盛，但随玄奘（602–664）回国后法相唯识宗的兴起而逐渐衰落，最终在唐代被并入法相宗。

The Shèlùn School (攝論宗), founded by Paramārtha and centered on the *Mahāyānasaṃgraha*, was the first Chinese Buddhist school to systematically transmit Yogācāra doctrine. Its principal successors included Fǎtài, Zhìkǎi, Dàoyuè, and Huìkuàng. The school flourished during the Suí dynasty (581–618) but gradually declined after Xuánzàng's return from India and the rise of the Fǎxiàng School, ultimately being absorbed into the latter during the Táng period.

尽管摄论宗作为一个独立宗派已经消失，但它的思想遗产深刻地影响了中国佛教的后续发展。特别是其"一性皆成"的主张与如来藏思想的融合，成为天台宗、华严宗和禅宗的重要理论资源。

Although the Shèlùn School no longer exists as an independent institution, its intellectual legacy profoundly shaped subsequent Chinese Buddhism. In particular, its universalist soteriology and integration with *tathāgata-garbha* thought became important theoretical resources for the Tiāntái, Huáyán, and Chán schools.

## 真谛与玄奘 / Paramārtha and Xuánzàng

真谛系唯识与玄奘系唯识之间的差异，是汉传佛教思想史上最重要的理论分歧之一。两者代表了印度唯识学的两条不同传承路线：

The divergence between Paramārtha's and Xuánzàng's Yogācāra systems represents one of the most significant theoretical schisms in Chinese Buddhist intellectual history, reflecting two distinct lineages within Indian Yogācāra:

| 维度 / Dimension | 真谛系 / Paramārtha's System | 玄奘系 / Xuánzàng's System |
|---|---|---|
| 识数 / Number of consciousnesses | 九识（含阿摩罗识）/ Nine (incl. amala-vijñāna) | 八识 / Eight |
| 核心识 / Core consciousness | 阿黎耶识（藏义）/ Ālaya as "repository" | 阿赖耶识（执藏义）/ Ālaya as "clinging-storehouse" |
| 种姓论 / Lineage theory | 一性皆成 / Universal Buddhahood | 五种姓 / Five lineages (gotra) |
| 如来藏 / Tathāgata-garbha | 融合 / Integrated | 保持距离 / Maintains distance |
| 印度源头 / Indian source | 可能近安慧系 / Possibly closer to Sthiramati | 护法—戒贤系 / Dharmapāla–Śīlabhadra lineage |
| 历史传承 / Historical lineage | 摄论宗（后并入法相宗）/ Shèlùn School (absorbed) | 法相唯识宗 / Fǎxiàng School |

这些差异不仅仅是术语上的分歧，而是反映了唯识学内部关于"心性本净"（*citta-prakṛti-prabhāsvaratā*）这一根本问题的不同回答。玄奘系坚持"性相分离"——法性（真如）与法相（识的流转）是两个不同的层面，不可混淆。而真谛系则倾向于"性相融合"——在识的深层结构中寻找觉悟的根据。

These differences are not merely terminological but reflect fundamentally divergent answers to the question of "the natural luminosity of mind" (*citta-prakṛti-prabhāsvaratā*). Xuánzàng's system insists on the separation of nature (*dharmatā*) and characteristics (*dharma-lakṣaṇa*); Paramārtha's tends toward their integration, seeking the ground of awakening within the deep structure of consciousness itself.

## 《大乘起信论》归属争议 / The *Awakening of Faith* Attribution Debate

《大乘起信论》（*Mahāyāna-śraddhotpāda-śāstra*）的归属问题是佛教研究中最持久的学术争论之一。传统上将此论归于马鸣菩萨（Aśvaghoṣa）造、真谛译，但自日本学者望月信亨（1922）以来，大量学者质疑这一归属：

The attribution of the *Awakening of Faith in Mahāyāna* is among the most enduring debates in Buddhist studies. Traditionally ascribed to Aśvaghoṣa and translated by Paramārtha, this attribution has been challenged since Mochizuki Shinkō's 1922 study:

- **印度原著派**：认为《起信论》确为印度论师所造，真谛所译。论中的"一心二门"结构（心真如门、心生灭门）和如来藏思想有印度渊源。
- **Pro-Indian authorship**: The "one mind, two gates" structure and *tathāgata-garbha* thought have Indian antecedents.
- **中国撰述派**：认为《起信论》是中国学者的撰述，托名翻译。其思想体系（特别是"本觉"概念）与中国佛教的后续发展（华严宗、禅宗）高度一致，而与印度唯识学有明显张力。
- **Pro-Chinese composition**: The thought system (especially the "original awakening" concept) aligns closely with later Chinese Buddhist developments and shows significant tension with Indian Yogācāra.

无论最终结论如何，这部论典的思想深度和历史影响都是无可置疑的。

Regardless of the ultimate verdict, the philosophical depth and historical influence of this treatise remain indisputable.

## 思想遗产 / Intellectual Legacy

真谛的思想遗产可以从三个维度来评价：

Paramārtha's intellectual legacy can be assessed from three dimensions:

**一、学术史价值。** 真谛的唯识学保留了印度唯识学中一条不同于护法—玄奘系的独立传承。通过比较真谛与玄奘的差异，学者可以重构印度唯识学内部的多元面貌，避免将玄奘所传等同于唯识学的全部。

**Academic-historical value.** Paramārtha's Yogācāra preserves an independent lineage distinct from the Dharmapāla–Xuánzàng line, enabling scholars to reconstruct the pluralistic landscape of Indian Yogācāra rather than reducing it to a single transmission.

**二、哲学贡献。** 真谛在唯识学与如来藏思想之间架设的桥梁，开创了一条"唯识如来藏"的思想路线。这条路线深刻影响了中国佛教的核心教义——一切有情皆有佛性、皆可成佛。

**Philosophical contribution.** The bridge Paramārtha built between Yogācāra and *tathāgata-garbha* inaugurated a "Yogācāra-tathāgata-garbha" synthesis that profoundly shaped the core Chinese Buddhist doctrine that all sentient beings possess Buddha-nature.

**三、当代意义。** 在当代佛学研究中，真谛的唯识学为比较哲学提供了丰富的素材。九识说与如来藏的融合模式，为解决"意识的结构"与"觉悟的可能性"之间的关系提供了独特的思想资源。

**Contemporary relevance.** In modern Buddhist studies, Paramārtha's system provides rich material for comparative philosophy, offering unique resources for addressing the relationship between "the structure of consciousness" and "the possibility of awakening."

## 常见误读 / Common Misreadings

1. ❌ **"真谛的唯识 = 玄奘的唯识"** → 二者有根本差异。真谛代表的是印度唯识学的一条独立传承线，不是玄奘唯识的"错误版本"。
   **"Paramārtha's Yogācāra = Xuánzàng's Yogācāra"** → They differ fundamentally. Paramārtha represents an independent Indian Yogācāra lineage, not an "erroneous version" of Xuánzàng's system.

2. ❌ **"九识说 = 如来藏"** → 九识说是在唯识框架内吸纳如来藏要素，而非简单地等同。阿摩罗识是"转依"后的清净识，而非一个独立的如来藏实体。
   **"Nine consciousnesses = tathāgata-garbha"** → The doctrine integrates *tathāgata-garbha* elements within a Yogācāra framework rather than simply equating them.

3. ❌ **"《大乘起信论》= 印度原著"** → 其归属至今争议未决。
   **"The *Awakening of Faith* = an Indian original"** → Its attribution remains unresolved.

4. ❌ **"摄论宗 = 法相宗"** → 摄论宗是独立的宗派，有自己独特的教义体系，虽然后来被并入法相宗，但二者的理论立场有显著差异。
   **"Shèlùn School = Fǎxiàng School"** → The Shèlùn School was independent with its own doctrinal system, and although later absorbed, the two held significantly different theoretical positions.
