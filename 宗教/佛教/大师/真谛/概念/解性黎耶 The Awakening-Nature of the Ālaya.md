# 解性黎耶 / The Awakening-Nature of the Ālaya (解性黎耶)

> "阿黎耶识中，自有解性，不从他得。"

---

## 定义 / Definition

**解性黎耶**是真谛唯识学中最具原创性的概念之一。"解性"（jiěxìng）指觉悟的能力或可能性，"黎耶"是阿黎耶识的简称。**解性黎耶**即"阿黎耶识中本具的觉悟之性"——即使在杂染的阿黎耶识中，也先天包含着觉悟的种子或潜能。这不是后天修行添加的，而是阿黎耶识本身固有的结构特征。

**Jiěxìng líyé** (解性黎耶) is among the most original concepts in Paramārtha's Yogācāra. *Jiěxìng* means "awakening-capacity" or "soteriological potential"; *líyé* is short for ālaya-vijñāna. **Jiěxìng líyé** thus means "the awakening-nature inherent in the ālaya"—even within the defiled ālaya-consciousness, there exists an innate capacity for awakening. This is not something added through subsequent practice but an intrinsic structural feature of the ālaya itself.

---

## 概念分析 / Conceptual Analysis

### "解性"的含义 / The Meaning of "Awakening-Nature"

"解性"一词在真谛的语境中有多层含义：

The term *jiěxìng* carries multiple layers of meaning in Paramārtha's context:

1. **觉解之性**：理解真理的能力——即理解诸法实相（空性、真如）的先天能力。
   **Capacity for understanding truth**: The innate ability to comprehend the true nature of phenomena (emptiness, suchness).

2. **解脱之性**：从烦恼中解脱的可能性——即不被烦恼永远束缚的先天自由。
   **Capacity for liberation**: The innate possibility of being freed from afflictions—not being permanently bound by them.

3. **觉悟之性**：成佛的可能性——即圆满觉悟（阿耨多罗三藐三菩提）的先天根据。
   **Capacity for Buddhahood**: The innate ground for perfect awakening (*anuttara-samyak-saṃbodhi*).

这三层含义从浅到深，构成了一个完整的觉悟论体系。

These three layers, from surface to depth, constitute a complete soteriological system.

### "黎耶"的结构 / The Structure of the Ālaya

在真谛的诠释中，阿黎耶识不是单一的、同质的存在，而是包含了不同层次的复合结构：

In Paramārtha's interpretation, the ālaya-vijñāna is not a simple, homogeneous entity but a composite structure with different layers:

- **杂染层面**：含藏由烦恼和业力熏习的杂染种子，这些种子产生轮回的现象世界。
  **Defiled layer**: Contains seeds perfumed by afflictions and karma, producing the phenomenal world of saṃsāra.

- **清净层面**（解性）：先天具足的清净本性，不受杂染所污染，是觉悟的内在根据。
  **Pure layer** (awakening-nature): An innately pure nature, undefiled by adventitious defilements, serving as the inner ground of awakening.

这两个层面的关系是"不一不异"的：它们不是同一个东西（因为杂染和清净的性质相反），但也不是完全不同的两个东西（因为它们共同构成阿黎耶识的整体）。

The relationship between these layers is "neither identical nor different": they are not the same (since defiled and pure are opposed in nature), but they are also not entirely separate (since they jointly constitute the whole ālaya).

## 哲学意义 / Philosophical Significance

### 一、解决"觉悟如何可能"的问题 / Resolving "How Is Awakening Possible?"

唯识学面临的一个根本问题是：如果一切有情都被杂染种子所束缚，那么觉悟是如何可能的？杂染的种子只能产生杂染的现行，杂染的现行只能熏习杂染的种子——这似乎是一个封闭的循环，觉悟无从介入。

A fundamental problem facing Yogācāra is: if all sentient beings are bound by defiled seeds, how is awakening possible? Defiled seeds produce only defiled manifestations; defiled manifestations perfume only defiled seeds. This appears to be a closed cycle with no entry point for awakening.

真谛的解性黎耶概念为这个问题提供了一个优雅的解答：阿黎耶识中**先天**就包含清净的解性，因此觉悟不是从"外部"注入的，而是从阿黎耶识的**内部**生发的。就像一个被泥土包裹的种子，虽然表面被泥土覆盖，但内部的生命力（解性）始终存在，一旦条件成熟（修行），它就会发芽、生长，最终突破泥土的覆盖。

Paramārtha's concept provides an elegant solution: the ālaya **innately** contains awakening-nature, so awakening is not injected from "outside" but emerges from **within** the ālaya itself. Like a seed wrapped in mud—though covered on the surface, its inner vitality (awakening-nature) persists, and when conditions mature (through practice), it sprouts and eventually breaks through the mud.

### 二、支持"一性皆成" / Supporting Universal Buddhahood

在印度唯识学中，关于一切有情是否都能成佛，存在严重分歧：

Within Indian Yogācāra, there was serious disagreement about whether all sentient beings can attain Buddhahood:

- **五种姓说**（玄奘系）：有情分为五种种姓——声闻种姓、独觉种姓、菩萨种姓、不定种姓、无种姓（一阐提，icchantika）。其中无种姓的有情永远不能成佛。
  **Five-lineage theory** (Xuánzàng): Beings are divided into five lineages—śrāvaka, pratyekabuddha, bodhisattva, indeterminate, and those without lineage (*icchantika*). The last category can never attain Buddhahood.

- **一性皆成说**（真谛系）：一切有情皆有佛性，皆能成佛。种姓的差别只是修行阶段的差别，不是本质的差别。
  **Universal Buddhahood** (Paramārtha): All beings possess Buddha-nature and can attain Buddhahood. Lineage differences are merely stages of cultivation, not differences in essence.

解性黎耶的概念明确支持"一性皆成"的立场：既然一切有情的阿黎耶识中都本具解性，那么一切有情都有成佛的可能性。五种姓的区分只是方便说——是根据当前修行状态而做的暂时分类，不是对有情本质的终极判断。

The awakening-nature concept unequivocally supports universal Buddhahood: since all beings' ālaya-consciousness innately possesses awakening-nature, all have the possibility of Buddhahood. The five-lineage distinction is merely provisional—based on current cultivation status, not an ultimate judgment on beings' essence.

### 三、唯识与如来藏的桥梁 / Bridge Between Yogācāra and Tathāgata-garbha

解性黎耶的概念在唯识学与如来藏思想之间架起了一座关键的桥梁。

The awakening-nature concept serves as a crucial bridge between Yogācāra and *tathāgata-garbha* thought.

**从唯识学的角度看**：解性黎耶保持了唯识学"万法唯识"的基本原则——觉悟的根据在识的内部，不需要预设一个超越识的独立实体（如"真我"或"梵"）。

**From the Yogācāra perspective**: The concept preserves the principle that "all phenomena are consciousness-only"—the ground of awakening lies within consciousness, without requiring a transcendent entity (such as "true self" or "Brahman").

**从如来藏的角度看**：解性黎耶承认了一切有情本具成佛可能性的核心主张——这与众生皆有"如来藏"的说法实质上是一致的。

**From the tathāgata-garbha perspective**: The concept acknowledges the core claim that all beings possess the possibility of Buddhahood—substantially equivalent to saying all beings have "tathāgata-garbha."

真谛的贡献在于：他用唯识学的概念框架（阿黎耶识、种子、熏习）来表达如来藏的核心主张，使得两种看似不同的思想传统得以在一个统一的理论框架内对话。

Paramārtha's contribution lies in expressing tathāgata-garbha's central claims using Yogācāra's conceptual framework (ālaya, seeds, perfuming), enabling two seemingly different intellectual traditions to dialogue within a unified theoretical framework.

## 与相关概念的比较 / Comparisons with Related Concepts

### 与"本觉"的比较 / Comparison with "Original Awakening"

中国佛教后来发展出"本觉"（original awakening）的概念，特别是在《大乘起信论》和华严宗中。"本觉"主张众生本来觉悟，只是被无明所遮蔽。

Chinese Buddhism later developed the concept of "original awakening" (*benjue*), particularly in the *Awakening of Faith* and Huáyán school, holding that beings are originally awakened, merely obscured by ignorance.

解性黎耶与本觉有相似之处，但也有重要区别：

The awakening-nature concept shares similarities with "original awakening" but has important differences:

- **相似**：都主张觉悟的可能性是先天的，不是后天的。
  **Similarity**: Both hold that the possibility of awakening is innate, not acquired.

- **区别**：解性黎耶仍然在唯识的框架内运作，承认杂染种子的现实力量；"本觉"则倾向于更为激进地否定杂染的实在性。
  **Difference**: Awakening-nature operates within Yogācāra's framework, acknowledging the real power of defiled seeds; "original awakening" tends toward a more radical denial of defilement's substantiality.

### 与"无漏种子"的比较 / Comparison with "Undefiled Seeds"

在玄奘系唯识中，也有"无漏种子"（anāsrava-bīja）的概念——阿赖耶识中也存在清净的无漏种子，这些种子是修行的依据。但玄奘系对无漏种子的理解与真谛的解性黎耶有根本区别：

Xuánzàng's system also includes "undefiled seeds" (*anāsrava-bīja*)—pure seeds within the ālaya that serve as the basis for cultivation. But this differs fundamentally from Paramārtha's awakening-nature:

- **玄奘系**：无漏种子是"法尔本有"或"新熏"的，且并非一切有情都具有。无种姓的有情可能根本没有无漏种子。
  **Xuánzàng**: Undefiled seeds are either "primordially present" or "newly perfumed," and not all beings possess them. *Icchantika* beings may lack them entirely.

- **真谛系**：解性黎耶是一切有情阿黎耶识中普遍具足的结构特征，不是某些有情独有而其他有情没有的。
  **Paramārtha**: Awakening-nature is a universal structural feature of all beings' ālaya, not possessed by some and lacking in others.

## 学术争论 / Scholarly Debates

关于解性黎耶概念的来源和性质，学界有不同看法：

Scholars differ on the origin and nature of the awakening-nature concept:

1. **印度渊源说**：部分学者认为解性黎耶的思想可以在印度唯识文献中找到根据，如世亲的《佛性论》和《摄大乘论释》中的相关论述。
   **Indian origin**: Some find grounds in Indian Yogācāra texts, such as relevant passages in Vasubandhu's *Buddha-dhātu-śāstra* and his commentary on the *Mahāyānasaṃgraha*.

2. **中国创发说**：另一些学者认为解性黎耶是真谛在中国语境下的创造性诠释，是唯识学与中国本土思想（特别是儒家的"性善"论）对话的产物。
   **Chinese innovation**: Others view it as Paramārtha's creative interpretation in the Chinese context, a product of dialogue between Yogācāra and indigenous Chinese thought (particularly the Confucian doctrine of innate human goodness).

3. **综合说**：最可能的是，真谛在印度传承的基础上进行了独特的诠释和发展，使解性黎耶成为一个既有印度渊源又有中国特色的概念。
   **Synthesis**: Most likely, Paramārtha built on Indian foundations while developing a unique interpretation, making awakening-nature a concept with both Indian roots and Chinese characteristics.

## 当代意义 / Contemporary Significance

解性黎耶的概念在当代哲学和心理学中仍具有启发意义：

The awakening-nature concept remains philosophically and psychologically suggestive today:

- **意识研究**：在意识的深层结构中是否存在某种先天的"觉知"或"反思"能力？这与当代认知科学关于"元认知"（metacognition）的研究有潜在的对话空间。
  **Consciousness studies**: Does the deep structure of consciousness contain some innate "awareness" or "reflective" capacity? This potentially dialogues with cognitive science 研究 on metacognition.

- **人本主义心理学**：人是否先天具有"自我实现"的倾向？这与马斯洛、罗杰斯等人本主义心理学家的主张有共鸣之处。
  **Humanistic psychology**: Do humans have an innate tendency toward "self-actualization"? This resonates with claims by Maslow, Rogers, and other humanistic psychologists.

- **教育哲学**：如果学习者先天具有"解性"（理解和觉悟的能力），那么教育的角色就不是"灌输"知识，而是"唤醒"内在的潜能。
  **Educational philosophy**: If learners possess innate "awakening-nature," education's role is not to "inculcate" knowledge but to "awaken" inner potential.
