# 九识说 / The Nine Consciousnesses (九識說)

> "转八识成四智，束四智成三身，更有第九阿摩罗识为清净本体。"

---

## 定义 / Definition

九识说是真谛唯识学的核心教义之一，主张在唯识学传统所立的八识之外，另立第九识——**阿摩罗识**（梵文 *amalavijñāna*，意为"无垢识"或"清净识"）。这一学说与玄奘系唯识的"八识说"形成了根本性的理论分歧，是区分真谛系与玄奘系唯识的最显著标志。

The Nine Consciousnesses doctrine is one of the core teachings of Paramārtha's Yogācāra. It posits a ninth consciousness—the **amala-vijñāna** ("immaculate" or "pure consciousness")—beyond the traditional eight. This doctrine forms the most conspicuous theoretical divergence from Xuánzàng's eight-consciousness system and serves as the clearest marker distinguishing Paramārtha's lineage.

---

## 八识与第九识 / The Eight and the Ninth

### 传统八识 / The Traditional Eight

唯识学的基本心识分析将意识活动分为八个层次：

The basic Yogācāra analysis divides conscious activity into eight levels:

1. **前五识**（眼、耳、鼻、舌、身识）——对应五种感官认知 / The five sensory consciousnesses (visual, auditory, olfactory, gustatory, tactile)
2. **第六识**（意识，*mano-vijñāna*）——思维、推理、概念化 / The sixth consciousness: thinking, reasoning, conceptualization
3. **第七识**（末那识，*manas*）——自我执着，恒审思量第八识为"我" / The seventh consciousness: ego-clinging, perpetually grasping the eighth as "self"
4. **第八识**（阿黎耶/阿赖耶识，*ālaya-vijñāna*）——种子的储存处，一切法的所依 / The eighth consciousness: repository of seeds, the basis of all phenomena

### 第九阿摩罗识 / The Ninth: Amala-vijñāna

真谛在八识之上增设第九识——**阿摩罗识**。*amala* 在梵文中意为"无垢"、"清净"、"无染"。阿摩罗识不是八识之外的另一个独立的识体，而是**转识成智**（*vijñāna-parāvṛtti*）之后的清净识状态。

Paramārtha adds a ninth—the **amala-vijñāna**. *Amala* in Sanskrit means "immaculate," "pure," "undefiled." The amala-vijñāna is not an ontologically separate entity beyond the eight, but rather the **purified state of consciousness after transformation** (*vijñāna-parāvṛtti*).

当真谛说"九识"时，他的意思是：

When Paramārtha speaks of "nine consciousnesses," he means:

- 前八识是**有漏识**——与烦恼（kleśa）相应的、产生轮回的意识活动
- 第九识是**无漏识**——转依（āśraya-parāvṛtti）后的清净意识

- The first eight are **defiled consciousnesses** (*sāsrava-vijñāna*)—conscious activities associated with afflictions (kleśa) that produce saṃsāra
- The ninth is the **undefiled consciousness** (*anāsrava-vijñāna*)—pure consciousness after fundamental transformation (āśraya-parāvṛtti)

## 哲学意义 / Philosophical Significance

### 一、转依的积极基础 / Positive Ground of Transformation

在玄奘系唯识中，"转依"（āśraya-parāvṛtti）主要被描述为一个否定性的过程：杂染种子灭尽，烦恼不起，即为菩提。换句话说，菩提不是"得到"了什么，而是"去掉"了杂染。这类似于"云散见日"的比喻——太阳（真如）一直都在，只是被云（烦恼）遮蔽了。

In Xuánzàng's system, "transformation of the basis" is primarily described negatively: when defiled seeds are exhausted and afflictions cease, that is enlightenment. Bodhi is not something "gained" but something "revealed" when defilements are removed—analogous to clouds dispersing to reveal the ever-present sun (suchness).

真谛的九识说则为转依提供了一个**积极的存在论基础**。阿摩罗识作为一个独立的识类被列出，意味着清净状态不只是"杂染的缺席"，而是有其自身的积极内容。转依不只是"灭"，而是"转"——从有漏识转为无漏识，从阿赖耶识转为阿摩罗识。

Paramārtha's ninth consciousness provides a **positive ontological foundation** for transformation. By listing amala-vijñāna as a distinct category, the pure state is not merely "the absence of defilement" but possesses its own positive content. Transformation is not merely "cessation" but "conversion"—from defiled consciousness to pure consciousness, from ālaya to amala.

### 二、与如来藏的对话 / Dialogue with Tathāgata-garbha

九识说与如来藏思想之间存在明显的亲和力。如来藏（tathāgata-garbha）学说主张一切有情本具成佛的可能性——"如来藏自性清净心"。阿摩罗识的概念可以被理解为如来藏思想在唯识框架内的表达：第九识就是那个"自性清净"的维度，尽管它被杂染的前八识所遮蔽。

The ninth consciousness doctrine shows clear affinity with *tathāgata-garbha* thought, which holds that all sentient beings inherently possess the possibility of Buddhahood—the "inherently pure mind of the tathāgata-garbha." The amala-vijñāna can be understood as the Yogācāra expression of this idea: the ninth consciousness is the "inherently pure" dimension, though obscured by the defiled first eight.

但真谛并没有简单地将九识等同于如来藏。在他的体系中，阿摩罗识仍然是一个**识**（vijñāna），而非一个独立的**实体**（ātman 或 brahman）。这保持了唯识学"唯识无境"的基本原则——即使是最清净的意识，也不是一个独立于缘起法之外的实体。

Yet Paramārtha did not simply equate the ninth consciousness with tathāgata-garbha. In his system, amala-vijñāna remains a **consciousness** (vijñāna), not an independent **entity** (ātman or brahman). This preserves the fundamental Yogācāra principle of "consciousness-only without external objects"—even the purest consciousness is not an entity independent of dependent origination.

### 三、修行论的含意 / Soteriological Implications

九识说对修行理论有深刻的影响。如果存在第九阿摩罗识，那么修行的目标就不仅是"灭"烦恼，而是"转化"识的整体结构。这为菩萨道的积极修行提供了更强的理论基础：成佛不是消极地"什么都不做"，而是积极地实现意识结构的根本转化。

The ninth consciousness has profound soteriological implications. If the amala-vijñāna exists, the goal of practice is not merely to "extinguish" afflictions but to "transform" the entire structure of consciousness. This provides a stronger theoretical basis for the active cultivation of the bodhisattva path: Buddhahood is not a passive "doing nothing" but the active realization of a fundamental transformation of consciousness.

## 学术争论 / Scholarly Debates

### 九识说的来源 / Sources of the Doctrine

关于九识说的来源，学界有不同看法：

Scholars differ on the origins of the nine-consciousness doctrine:

1. **印度渊源说**：部分学者认为九识说在印度唯识学中有其依据。《楞伽经》（*Laṅkāvatāra-sūtra*）中提到"九识"，《密严经》也有类似表述。真谛可能继承了印度某一支唯识传承中对这些经典的独特诠释。
   **Indian origin**: Some scholars find antecedents in Indian Yogācāra. The *Laṅkāvatāra-sūtra* mentions "nine consciousnesses," and the *Ghanavyūha-sūtra* contains similar formulations.

2. **中国发展说**：另一些学者认为九识说是真谛在中国的独特发展，是唯识学与如来藏思想在中国语境下融合的产物。
   **Chinese development**: Others view the doctrine as Paramārtha's unique development in China, a product of the encounter between Yogācāra and *tathāgata-garbha* thought in the Chinese context.

3. **折中说**：认为九识说有印度经典依据，但真谛对其进行了独特的诠释和发展。
   **Eclectic view**: The doctrine has Indian textual bases but received unique interpretation and development from Paramārtha.

### 玄奘系的回应 / Xuánzàng School's Response

玄奘系唯识不承认第九识的独立地位。窥基在《成唯识论述记》中明确批评九识说，认为阿摩罗识只是第八识转依后的清净状态，不需要另立一识。在玄奘系看来，"转识成智"后，第八识转为"大圆镜智"，这已经足够描述清净状态，不需要额外的第九识。

Xuánzàng's Yogācāra does not recognize the ninth consciousness as independent. Kuījī explicitly criticized the doctrine in his *Commentary on the Vijñaptimātratā-siddhi*, arguing that amala-vijñāna is merely the purified state of the eighth consciousness after transformation and does not require a separate category. In this system, the eighth consciousness transforms into the "great mirror wisdom" (*mahādarśa-jñāna*), which suffices to describe the pure state.

## 当代评价 / Contemporary Assessment

当代佛教学者对九识说的评价趋于多元：

Contemporary Buddhist scholars offer diverse assessments:

- **印顺法师**认为九识说有其经典依据，但需要审慎理解，不应简单地将其等同于"真常唯心论"。
- **Venerable Yìnshùn** acknowledged textual bases for the doctrine but urged careful interpretation against simplistic equation with "true-permanent idealism."

- **牟宗三**在《佛性与般若》中详细分析了九识说的哲学意义，认为它代表了唯识学与如来藏思想融合的一种有价值的尝试。
- **Móu Zōngsān** analyzed the doctrine's philosophical significance in *Buddha-nature and Prajñā*, viewing it as a valuable attempt to synthesize Yogācāra and *tathāgata-garbha*.

- **横山纮一**等日本学者对九识说的印度渊源进行了详细的文献学研究，为理解这一概念提供了重要的学术基础。
- **Yokoyama Kōitsu** and other Japanese scholars conducted detailed philological 研究 on the doctrine's Indian antecedents.
