# 阿黎耶识 / Ālaya-vijñāna in Paramārtha's System (阿黎耶識)

> "黎耶，此云藏识，能藏一切种子故。"

---

## 定义 / Definition

**阿黎耶识**是真谛对梵文 *ālaya-vijñāna* 的翻译。在玄奘的翻译系统中，同一概念被译为"阿赖耶识"。这两个翻译的差异不仅仅是音译选择的不同，而是反映了对 *ālaya* 这一概念的两种不同理解路径。

**Ālīyé shí** (阿黎耶识) is Paramārtha's Chinese rendering of the Sanskrit *ālaya-vijñāna*. In Xuánzàng's translation system, the same concept is rendered as **Ālàyé shí** (阿赖耶识). This difference is not merely translational but reflects two distinct interpretive approaches to the concept of *ālaya*.

---

## 词源与翻译 / Etymology and Translation

### 梵文 *ālaya* 的多重含义 / Multiple Meanings of Sanskrit *ālaya*

梵文 *ālaya* 一词有多重含义：

The Sanskrit word *ālaya* carries multiple meanings:

1. **藏**（repository, storehouse）——含藏、收纳的意思 / "Store" or "repository"—the sense of containing and preserving
2. **住处**（abode, dwelling）——安住、停留的意思 / "Abode" or "dwelling"—the sense of resting or persisting
3. **执藏**（clinging-storehouse）——被执着为"自我"的意思 / "Clinging-storehouse"—the sense of being grasped as "self"

这三个含义在唯识学文献中都有出现，但不同的论师对哪一个含义是 *ālaya* 的"主要"含义有不同看法。

All three senses appear in Yogācāra literature, but different masters disagreed on which constitutes the "primary" meaning.

### 真谛的翻译选择 / Paramārtha's Translational Choice

真谛将 *ālaya* 音译为"阿黎耶"，并在释义时主要采用"藏"（repository）的含义。在他的诠释中，阿黎耶识是"能藏一切种子"的识——它是一切种子（bīja）的储存处，是一切有为法生起的所依（āśraya）。

Paramārtha transliterated *ālaya* as "阿黎耶" and primarily adopted the "repository" sense in his interpretation. In his reading, the ālaya-vijñāna is the consciousness that "stores all seeds"—it is the repository of all seeds (*bīja*) and the basis (*āśraya*) for the arising of all conditioned phenomena.

这一翻译选择的意义在于：它将阿黎耶识定位为一个**功能性**的概念——它是"仓库"，是种子的存放处，本身是中性的。就像仓库本身不关心存放的是粮食还是毒药，阿黎耶识作为种子的储存处，本身不带染净的价值判断。

This translational choice is significant: it positions the ālaya as a **functional** concept—a "storehouse" that is itself neutral. Just as a warehouse is indifferent to whether it stores grain or poison, the ālaya as a seed-repository carries no intrinsic value judgment regarding purity or defilement.

### 玄奘的翻译选择 / Xuánzàng's Translational Choice

相比之下，玄奘将 *ālaya* 音译为"阿赖耶"，并在释义时主要采用"执藏"的含义。在玄奘的体系中，*ālaya* 的得名主要是因为第七末那识（manas）恒常执着第八识为"自我"——第八识是"被执藏"的，因此称为"阿赖耶"（执藏）。

By contrast, Xuánzàng transliterated *ālaya* as "阿赖耶" and primarily adopted the "clinging-storehouse" sense. In his system, *ālaya* is so named primarily because the seventh consciousness (*manas*) perpetually grasps the eighth as "self"—the eighth consciousness is "clung to as a storehouse" and therefore called "ālaya" (clinging-storehouse).

这一翻译选择的意义在于：它将阿赖耶识定位为一个**问题性**的概念——它之所以叫做"阿赖耶"，正是因为它被执着，被错误地当作"自我"。这个名字本身就暗示了需要被超越的状态。

This choice positions the ālaya as a **problematic** concept—it is called "ālaya" precisely because it is clung to, erroneously taken as "self." The name itself implies a state that needs to be transcended.

## 真谛的阿黎耶识概念 / Paramārtha's Concept of Ālaya

### 种子与现行 / Seeds and Manifestations

在真谛的体系中，阿黎耶识的核心功能是储存**种子**（bīja）。种子是过去行为（业，karma）留下的潜势力，它们在因缘成熟时会"现行"——产生具体的心理活动和外部现象。同时，现行的活动又会"熏习"新的种子，回存到阿黎耶识中。这就是唯识学著名的"种子生现行，现行熏种子"的循环。

In Paramārtha's system, the ālaya's core function is storing **seeds** (*bīja*). Seeds are the latent potentials left by past actions (karma); when conditions mature, they "manifest" (*samudācara*) as specific mental activities and external phenomena. These manifest activities in turn "perfume" (*vāsanā*) new seeds back into the ālaya. This is the well-known Yogācāra cycle: "seeds produce manifestations; manifestations perfume seeds."

真谛对这一循环的理解与玄奘基本一致，但他的诠释有一个重要不同：真谛更强调阿黎耶识中不仅有杂染种子，也有清净种子——这就是"解性黎耶"概念的来源。

Paramārtha's understanding of this cycle largely agrees with Xuánzàng's, but with one crucial difference: he emphasizes that the ālaya contains not only defiled seeds but also pure seeds—the source of the "awakening-nature ālaya" concept.

### 阿黎耶识与如来藏 / Ālaya and Tathāgata-garbha

真谛体系中最具特色的观点之一是：**阿黎耶识与如来藏"不一不异"**。

One of the most distinctive positions in Paramārtha's system is that **the ālaya-vijñāna and tathāgata-garbha are "neither identical nor different."**

- **不一**（not identical）：阿黎耶识含有杂染种子，产生轮回；如来藏是"自性清净心"，是成佛的根据。从现象层面看，二者不同。
- **Not identical**: The ālaya contains defiled seeds and produces saṃsāra; tathāgata-garbha is the "inherently pure mind," the ground of Buddhahood. Phenomenally, they differ.

- **不异**（not different）：阿黎耶识的本质（解性）与如来藏是同一的。当杂染种子被净化后，阿黎耶识的"真实面目"就是如来藏。从本体层面看，二者不二。
- **Not different**: The essence of the ālaya (its awakening-nature) and tathāgata-garbha are the same. When defiled seeds are purified, the "true face" of the ālaya is tathāgata-garbha. Ontologically, they are non-dual.

这一立场在玄奘系唯识中是不被接受的。玄奘系严格区分"有为法"（阿赖耶识，属于有为法）和"无为法"（真如，属于无为法），认为二者不可混淆。但真谛认为这种区分是方便说，究竟而言，有为法的深层本质就是无为法。

This position is rejected in Xuánzàng's system, which strictly distinguishes conditioned phenomena (the ālaya, as conditioned) from unconditioned reality (suchness, *tathatā*), insisting they must not be conflated. Paramārtha, however, holds that this distinction is provisional; ultimately, the deep nature of the conditioned is the unconditioned.

## 阿黎耶识的三义 / Three Aspects of the Ālaya

根据真谛的诠释传统，阿黎耶识可以从三个层面来理解：

According to Paramārtha's interpretive tradition, the ālaya can be understood from three aspects:

1. **能藏**（the storing function）：阿黎耶识能含藏一切种子。这是其最基本的功能。
   **The storing function**: The ālaya's capacity to contain all seeds—its most basic function.

2. **所藏**（the stored contents）：阿黎耶识所含藏的种子本身。种子有生灭，但含藏的功能不生不灭。
   **The stored contents**: The seeds themselves, which arise and cease, though the storing function neither arises nor ceases.

3. **执藏**（the clung-to aspect）：第七末那识执着阿黎耶识为"自我"。真谛承认这一层面，但不将其视为阿黎耶识的本质特征。
   **The clung-to aspect**: The seventh consciousness grasps the ālaya as "self." Paramārtha acknowledges this dimension but does not treat it as the ālaya's essential characteristic.

正是由于真谛将"执藏"（第三义）视为阿黎耶识的非本质特征，他才可能设想一个不含杂染、不被执着的"清净阿黎耶"——这就是阿摩罗识（第九识）。

Precisely because Paramārtha treats "clinging-storehouse" (the third aspect) as non-essential, he can conceive of a "pure ālaya" free from defilement and without clinging—the amala-vijñāna (ninth consciousness).

## 与如来藏经典的关系 / Relationship to Tathāgata-garbha Texts

真谛的阿黎耶识概念与几部重要的如来藏经典有密切关系：

Paramārtha's ālaya concept is closely related to several important *tathāgata-garbha* texts:

- **《楞伽经》**（*Laṅkāvatāra-sūtra*）：经中提出"如来藏藏识"的概念，将如来藏与藏识（阿黎耶识）联系起来。真谛对此经有深入研究。
  The *Laṅkāvatāra-sūtra* introduces the concept of "tathāgata-garbha-storehouse-consciousness," linking tathāgata-garbha and ālaya.

- **《大乘密严经》**（*Ghanavyūha-sūtra*）：经中说"阿赖耶识即是如来藏"，直接将二者等同。
  The *Ghanavyūha-sūtra* directly identifies ālaya-vijñāna with tathāgata-garbha.

- **《胜鬘经》**（*Śrīmālā-sūtra*）：提出"如来藏智是空性智"，为如来藏与空性的融合提供了理论基础。
  The *Śrīmālā-sūtra* provides a theoretical basis for integrating tathāgata-garbha with emptiness.

## 当代评价 / Contemporary Assessment

当代学者对真谛的阿黎耶识概念有不同的评价：

Contemporary scholars offer varied assessments:

- **比较语言学角度**：真谛的"阿黎耶"可能更接近 *ālaya* 在早期唯识文献中的原始含义（"住处"、"含藏"），而玄奘的"阿赖耶"（"执藏"）是护法系诠释的产物。
  **Philological perspective**: Paramārtha's "阿黎耶" may be closer to the original sense of *ālaya* in early Yogācāra texts ("abode," "repository"), while Xuánzàng's "阿赖耶" ("clinging-storehouse") may be a product of the Dharmapāla school's interpretation.

- **哲学角度**：真谛将阿黎耶识与如来藏融合的做法，虽然在理论上存在张力，但为思考"意识的深层结构"与"觉悟的可能性"之间的关系提供了有价值的思想资源。
  **Philosophical perspective**: Paramārtha's integration of ālaya and tathāgata-garbha, despite theoretical tensions, provides valuable resources for thinking about the relationship between the deep structure of consciousness and the possibility of awakening.
