# 真谛译著与著作 / Works and Translations of Paramārtha (真谛译著与著作)

> 真谛一生译经约五十部，三百余卷。以下按重要性排列。
> Paramārtha translated approximately fifty texts totaling over three hundred fascicles. Listed below by significance.

---

## 核心论典 / Core Treatises

### 《摄大乘论》三卷 / *Mahāyānasaṃgraha* (3 fascicles)

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 原著 | 无著（Asaṅga）造 |
| 翻译年代 | 563年，广州 |
| 大正藏编号 | T.1592 |
| 内容 | 唯识学的纲要性著作，系统阐述大乘佛教的核心理论，包括阿赖耶识、三性、六度、十地等。 |
| 地位 | 摄论宗的根本论典，中国唯识学的奠基之作。 |

The foundational Yogācāra compendium by Asaṅga, systematically presenting core Mahāyāna doctrines including ālaya-vijñāna, the three natures, the six perfections, and the ten bodhisattva stages. Translated in 563 CE at Guǎngzhōu. The root text of the Shèlùn School and the founding work of Chinese Yogācāra.

### 《摄大乘论释》十五卷 / *Mahāyānasaṃgraha-bhāṣya* (15 fascicles)

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 原著 | 世亲（Vasubandhu）释 |
| 翻译年代 | 563年，广州 |
| 大正藏编号 | T.1594 |
| 内容 | 世亲对无著《摄大乘论》的详细注释，是理解《摄论》的最重要参考资料。 |
| 地位 | 摄论宗的理论依据之一。真谛在翻译中加入了大量自己的诠释性按语。 |

Vasubandhu's detailed commentary on Asaṅga's *Mahāyānasaṃgraha*. Translated alongside the root text in 563 CE. Contains extensive interpretive notes by Paramārtha himself, making it a key source for understanding his distinctive Yogācāra interpretation.

### 《俱舍论》二十二卷 / *Abhidharmakośa* (22 fascicles)

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 原著 | 世亲（Vasubandhu）造 |
| 翻译年代 | 559–563年 |
| 大正藏编号 | T.1559 |
| 内容 | 说一切有部（Sarvāstivāda）阿毗达磨哲学的集大成之作，系统论述有部的宇宙论、心理学和修行论。 |
| 地位 | 首个汉译本。后被玄奘于651–654年重译（《阿毗达磨俱舍论》三十卷，T.1558），真谛译本逐渐被取代。但真谛译本保留了重要的早期翻译传统。 |

Vasubandhu's masterwork of Sarvāstivāda Abhidharma philosophy. The first Chinese translation, later superseded by Xuánzàng's 30-fascicle version (651–654 CE, T.1558). Despite being replaced, Paramārtha's version preserves an important early translation tradition.

## 重要论典 / Important Treatises

### 《佛性论》四卷 / *Buddha-dhātu-śāstra* (4 fascicles)

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 原著 | 世亲（Vasubandhu）造（归属有争议） |
| 翻译年代 | 约560年 |
| 大正藏编号 | T.1610 |
| 内容 | 系统论述佛性（buddha-dhātu）思想，主张一切有情皆有佛性。论中详细分析了佛性的三种含义：自性住佛性、引出佛性、至得果佛性。 |
| 地位 | 中国佛性思想的重要文献，与真谛的"解性黎耶"概念密切相关。 |

A systematic treatise on Buddha-nature (*buddha-dhātu*), arguing that all sentient beings possess it. Analyzes three dimensions of Buddha-nature: naturally abiding, drawn forth, and attained at fruition. A key text for Chinese Buddha-nature thought, closely related to Paramārtha's "awakening-nature ālaya" concept.

### 《中边分别论》二卷 / *Madhyāntavibhāga* (2 fascicles)

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 原著 | 弥勒（Maitreya）颂，世亲（Vasubandhu）释 |
| 翻译年代 | 558年 |
| 大正藏编号 | T.1599 |
| 内容 | 唯识学的根本论典之一，论述中道与二边（有、无）的分别，以及三性、三无性的学说。 |
| 地位 | 后被玄奘重译为《辨中边论》（T.1600）。真谛译本是了解早期唯识学的重要资料。 |

A fundamental Yogācāra treatise by Maitreya (verses) and Vasubandhu (commentary), discussing the middle way and two extremes, as well as the three natures and three non-natures. Later retranslated by Xuánzàng as *Biàn Zhōngbiān Lùn* (T.1600).

### 《三无性论》一卷 / *Triniḥsvabhāvatā* (1 fascicle)

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 原著 | 无著（Asaṅga）造（归属有争议） |
| 翻译年代 | 约558年 |
| 大正藏编号 | T.1598 |
| 内容 | 论述三无性（相无性、生无性、胜义无性）与三性的关系。 |
| 地位 | 唯识学"三性三无性"学说的专门论著。 |

A specialized treatise on the three non-natures (non-nature of characteristics, of production, and of ultimate meaning) and their relationship to the three natures.

## 争议性译作 / Disputed Translations

### 《大乘起信论》一卷 / *Mahāyāna-śraddhotpāda-śāstra* (1 fascicle)

| 项目 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 原著 | 传统归于马鸣（Aśvaghoṣa），归属争议未决 |
| 翻译年代 | 约550年代（？） |
| 大正藏编号 | T.1666 |
| 内容 | 提出"一心二门"（心真如门、心生灭门）的结构，系统论述如来藏思想，包括"本觉"、"不觉"、"始觉"等核心概念。 |
| 争议 | 自望月信亨（1922）以来，大量学者质疑此论是否为印度原著。主要争论：(1) 是翻译还是中国撰述？(2) 如果是翻译，原著者是马鸣还是他人？(3) 如果是中国撰述，是否以真谛的讲学为基础？ |
| 影响 | 无论归属如何，此论是中国佛教史上影响最深远的论典之一，直接影响了华严宗、禅宗和天台宗的核心教义。 |

Proposes the "one mind, two gates" structure (gate of suchness, gate of arising-and-ceasing), systematically presenting tathāgata-garbha thought including "original awakening," "non-awakening," and "incipient awakening." Attribution has been debated since Mochizuki Shinkō (1922): is it an Indian original, a Chinese composition, or a Chinese elaboration based on Paramārtha's lectures? Regardless, it is among the most influential treatises in Chinese Buddhist history, directly shaping Huáyán, Chán, and Tiāntái core doctrines.

## 翻译特色 / Translation Characteristics

### 音译 vs 意译 / Transliteration vs. Free Translation

真谛的翻译风格介于音译和意译之间：

Paramārtha's translation style falls between transliteration and free translation:

- **音译方面**：他保留了大量梵文音译术语，如"阿黎耶"（而非玄奘的"阿赖耶"）。
  He retained numerous Sanskrit transliterations, e.g., "阿黎耶" rather than Xuánzàng's "阿赖耶."

- **意译方面**：他创造了大量富有诠释性的汉语术语，如"解性黎耶"、"阿摩罗识"等。
  He coined numerous interpretively rich Chinese terms, e.g., "解性黎耶," "阿摩罗识."

### 翻译方法 / Translation Method

据《续高僧传》记载，真谛的翻译方法是"口宣梵文，笔受为汉"——他口头宣读梵文原文，由中国助手笔录为汉文。在翻译过程中，真谛经常加入讲解和诠释，这些内容有时被直接融入译文。

According to the *Continued Biographies of Eminent Monks*, Paramārtha's method was to orally recite the Sanskrit while Chinese assistants transcribed it into Chinese. During translation, he frequently inserted explanations and interpretations, sometimes incorporating them directly into the translated text.

这种方法导致真谛的译本具有"翻译+注释"的混合特征，既是原典的翻译，也是真谛自身理解的表达。这也是为什么真谛译本在学术研究中有特殊价值——它不仅传达了印度原典的内容，也传达了一位重要唯识论师对原典的独特理解。

This method gives Paramārtha's translations a hybrid "translation + commentary" character—they convey both the Indian original and Paramārtha's distinctive understanding. This is why his translations hold special scholarly value: they transmit not only the Indian text but also an important Yogācāra master's unique interpretation.
