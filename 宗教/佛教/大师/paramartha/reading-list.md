# 真谛研究阅读清单 / Reading List for Paramārtha Studies (真谛研究阅读清单)

> 按主题分类，从入门到专题研究排列。
> Organized by theme, from introductory to specialized research.

---

## 入门读物 / Introductory Readings

### 通论 / General Overviews

1. **Paul Williams**, *Mahāyāna Buddhism: The Doctrinal Foundations* (2nd ed., Routledge, 2009)
   - 第七章"瑜伽行派"是英文世界最好的唯识学入门之一，涵盖了真谛与玄奘的差异。
   - Chapter 7 on Yogācāra is among the best English-language introductions, covering the Paramārtha–Xuánzàng divergence.

2. **横山纮一**（Yokoyama Kōitsu），《唯识思想入门》（*Introduction to Yogācāra Thought*），大藏出版，1997
   - 日文唯识学入门经典，对真谛系与玄奘系有清晰的比较。
   - A classic Japanese introduction to Yogācāra with clear comparison of Paramārtha's and Xuánzàng's systems.

3. **释恒清**，《佛性思想研究》（*A Study of Buddha-nature Thought*），台湾大学博士论文，1989
   - 对佛性思想（包括真谛的"解性黎耶"概念）的系统研究。
   - Systematic study of Buddha-nature thought including Paramārtha's "awakening-nature ālaya" concept.

4. **印顺法师**，《摄大乘论讲记》（*Lectures on the Mahāyānasaṃgraha*），正闻出版社
   - 以现代学术视角讲解《摄大乘论》，是理解真谛唯识学的最佳中文入门之一。
   - Modern academic exposition of the *Mahāyānasaṃgraha*; one of the best Chinese-language introductions to Paramārtha's Yogācāra.

## 专题研究 / Specialized Studies

### 真谛传记与历史 / Biography and History

5. **释道宣**，《续高僧传》卷一"真谛传"（*Continued Biographies of Eminent Monks*, fascicle 1, "Biography of Paramārtha"）
   - 最原始的真谛传记资料。T.2060。
   - The earliest biographical source on Paramārtha. T.2060.

6. **镰田茂雄**（Kamata Shigeo），《中国佛教史》第三卷"南北朝佛教史"（*History of Chinese Buddhism*, Vol. 3），东京大学出版会
   - 将真谛置于南北朝佛教史的广阔背景中考察。
   - Situates Paramārtha within the broad context of Southern and Northern Dynasties Buddhist history.

7. **曹仕邦**，《中国佛教史学史》（*Historiography of Chinese Buddhism*），法鼓文化
   - 关于佛教翻译史和真谛在其中的位置。
   - On the history of Buddhist translation and Paramārtha's place within it.

### 唯识学比较研究 / Comparative Yogācāra Studies

8. **牟宗三**，《佛性与般若》（*Buddha-nature and Prajñā*），学生书局，1977
   - 对中国佛教思想史的哲学分析，包含对真谛系唯识的详细讨论。牟宗三从新儒学的角度分析唯识与如来藏的融合问题。
   - Philosophical analysis of Chinese Buddhist intellectual history with detailed discussion of Paramārtha's Yogācāra. Approaches the Yogācāra–tathāgata-garbha synthesis from a Neo-Confucian perspective.

9. **宇井伯寿**（Ui Hakuju），《印度哲学研究》（*Studies in Indian Philosophy*），岩波书店
   - 对印度唯识学各流派的详细比较，为理解真谛与玄奘的差异提供了印度学背景。
   - Detailed comparison of Indian Yogācāra schools, providing Indological background for understanding the Paramārtha–Xuánzàng divergence.

10. **长尾雅人**（Nagao Gadjin），*Madhyamaka and Yogācāra*（SUNY Press, 1991）
    - 对瑜伽行派思想的深入研究，讨论了唯识学内部的不同诠释传统。
    - In-depth study of Yogācāra thought, discussing different interpretive traditions within the school.

11. **Lusthaus, Dan**, *Buddhist Phenomenology: A Philosophical Investigation of Yogācāra Buddhism and the Ch'eng Wei-shih Lun*（Routledge, 2002）
    - 对唯识学哲学（特别是《成唯识论》）的深入研究，包含对真谛系与玄奘系差异的讨论。
    - Philosophical investigation of Yogācāra (especially the *Chéng Wéishí Lùn*), including discussion of Paramārtha–Xuánzàng differences.

### 九识说与如来藏 / Nine Consciousnesses and Tathāgata-garbha

12. **释恒清**，"真谛的九识说"（"Paramārtha's Doctrine of Nine Consciousnesses"），收录于相关佛学论文集
    - 对九识说的专题研究，分析了其印度渊源和中国发展。
    - Specialized study of the nine-consciousness doctrine, analyzing its Indian origins and Chinese development.

13. **高崎直道**（Takasaki Jikidō），《如来藏思想》（*Tathāgata-garbha Thought*），春秋社
    - 如来藏思想的经典研究，涉及真谛对如来藏的独特诠释。
    - Classic study of tathāgata-garbha thought, touching on Paramārtha's distinctive interpretation.

14. **Zimmermann, Michael**, *A Buddha Within: The Tathāgatagarbhasūtra*（Biblotheca Philologica et Philosophica Buddhica, 2002）
    - 对如来藏经典的英文研究，为理解真谛融合唯识与如来藏的思想背景提供了重要参考。
    - English-language study of tathāgata-garbha sūtras, providing important context for understanding Paramārtha's synthesis of Yogācāra and tathāgata-garbha.

### 《大乘起信论》研究 / Studies on the *Awakening of Faith*

15. **望月信亨**（Mochizuki Shinkō），"《大乘起信论》支那撰述说"（"On the Chinese Authorship of the *Awakening of Faith*"），1922
    - 开创性的学术论文，首次系统论证《起信论》为中国撰述而非印度翻译。
    - Groundbreaking paper first systematically arguing for Chinese rather than Indian authorship of the *Awakening of Faith*.

16. **印顺法师**，《大乘起信论讲记》（*Lectures on the Awakening of Faith*），正闻出版社
    - 对《起信论》的详细讲解，涉及归属争议和思想分析。
    - Detailed exposition of the *Awakening of Faith*, addressing the attribution debate and thought analysis.

17. **Hakeda, Yoshito S.** (trans.), *The Awakening of Faith: Attributed to Aśvaghoṣa*（Columbia University Press, 1967）
    - 英文翻译和导论，是西方学者研究《起信论》的基本参考。
    - English translation and introduction; a fundamental reference for Western scholarship on the *Awakening of Faith*.

18. **Grosnick, William H.**, "The Categories of T'i, Hsiang and Yung: Evidence that Paramārtha Composed the Awakening of Faith," *Journal of the International Association of Buddhist Studies* 1 (1989)
    - 论证《起信论》为真谛所撰（而非翻译）的重要英文论文。
    - Important English-language paper arguing that Paramārtha composed (rather than translated) the *Awakening of Faith*.

## 英文研究 / English-Language Scholarship

31. **Griffiths, Paul J.**, *The Idea of Pure Consciousness*（Penn State University Press, 1997）
    - 关于"纯粹意识"概念的比较哲学研究，涉及真谛的阿摩罗识概念。
    - Comparative philosophical study of "pure consciousness," touching on Paramārtha's amala-vijñāna concept.

32. **King, Richard**, *Early Advaita Vedānta and Buddhism*（SUNY Press, 1995）
    - 讨论唯识学与吠檀多思想的关系，涉及真谛的如来藏诠释。
    - Discusses the relationship between Yogācāra and Vedānta, touching on Paramārtha's tathāgata-garbha interpretation.

33. **Rahula, Walpola**, "The Problem of the 9th Consciousness," in *Zen and the Taming of the Bull*（Gordon Fraser, 1978）
    - 对九识说的专题英文研究。
    - English-language study specifically on the nine-consciousness doctrine.
