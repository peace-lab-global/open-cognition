# 坛经 Tanjing / Platform Sutra

## 定义 / Definition

**《六祖大师法宝坛经》**，简称《坛经》(Tánjīng)，是慧能在大梵寺等处说法的记录，由弟子法海编集。它是中国佛教史上唯一由汉人所说而被尊称为"经" (sutra) 的文本——在佛教传统中，"经"原本只用于佛陀亲说的教法，这一破例标志着慧能在中国佛教中的至高地位。

The **Platform Sutra of the Sixth Patriarch** (Tanjing) records Huineng's teachings at Dafan Temple and other locations, compiled by his disciple Fahai. It is the only text in Chinese Buddhist history composed by a Chinese person to be honored with the title "sutra" — traditionally reserved for the Buddha's own words. This exceptional honor marks Huineng's supreme status in Chinese Buddhism.

## 文本历史 / Textual History

### 版本流传 / Version History

《坛经》现存多个版本，反映了数百年间的传抄、编辑和增补过程：

The Platform Sutra survives in multiple versions, reflecting centuries of copying, editing, and supplementation:

1. **敦煌本 (Dunhuang manuscript, 约 780–800 CE)**: 最古老版本，约一万两千字，1900 年在敦煌莫高窟发现。语言质朴，较少后期加工，被认为最接近慧能原始教导。/ The oldest version, approximately 12,000 characters, discovered in the Dunhuang caves in 1900. Its language is unadorned with minimal later additions, considered closest to Huineng's original teachings.

2. **惠昕本 (Huixin edition, 约 967 CE)**: 宋代改编本，增加了部分内容。/ A Song dynasty revision with some additions.

3. **德异本 (Deyi edition, 1290 CE)**: 元代版本，增加了更多的传记材料和教导。/ A Yuan dynasty version with expanded biographical materials and teachings.

4. **宗宝本 (Zongbao edition, 1291 CE)**: 最通行的版本，约两万字，收入《大藏经》。内容比敦煌本多出约一倍，增加了大量后期禅宗材料。/ The most widely circulated version, approximately 20,000 characters, included in the Chinese Buddhist Canon. About twice the length of the Dunhuang manuscript, with substantial later Chan materials added.

### 学术争议 / Scholarly Debates

关于《坛经》的文本批评是禅宗研究的核心课题之一。主要争论包括：敦煌本是否真的最接近原始版本？宗宝本增加的材料是"失真"还是合理的传承发展？慧能是否真的是《坛经》的作者，还是后人以其名义创作？

Textual criticism of the Platform Sutra is a central concern in Chan studies. Key debates include: Is the Dunhuang version truly closest to the original? Are the additions in the Zongbao edition "corruptions" or legitimate developments in transmission? Was Huineng truly the author, or did later figures compose in his name?

## 核心内容 / Core Content

### 主要章节结构 / Major Structural Sections

1. **行由品 (Autobiography)**: 慧能的生平故事——从贫寒樵夫到六祖的传奇历程，包括听《金刚经》开悟、黄梅求法、得传衣钵、隐遁十五年等关键事件。/ Huineng's life story — from impoverished woodcutter to Sixth Patriarch, including his awakening upon hearing the Diamond Sutra, study under Hongren, secret transmission of the robe, and fifteen years of hiding.

2. **般若品 (Prajna/Wisdom)**: 对摩诃般若波罗蜜多的阐释，将般若智慧与自性结合。/ Interpretation of Maha-Prajna-Paramita, uniting transcendent wisdom with self-nature.

3. **疑问品 (Questions and Answers)**: 对弟子和官员提问的回答，涵盖功德与福德的区别、在家修行等实际问题。/ Responses to questions from disciples and officials, covering practical issues like the difference between merit and virtue, and lay practice.

4. **定慧品 (Meditation and Wisdom)**: 禅定与般若不可分割的论述——"定是慧体，慧是定用"。/ The inseparability of meditation and wisdom — "Meditation is the substance of wisdom; wisdom is the function of meditation."

5. **坐禅品 (Sitting Meditation)**: 对坐禅的革命性的重新定义——不拘形式，重在心不执取。/ Revolutionary redefinition of sitting meditation — form is not essential; what matters is non-grasping of mind.

6. **忏悔品 (Repentance)**: "无相忏悔"——不是仪式性的忏悔，而是心念上的转变。/ "Formless repentance" — not ritual confession but transformation at the level of mind.

7. **机缘品 (Encounters)**: 与弟子们的对话记录，展示慧能因材施教的风格。/ Records of dialogues with disciples, demonstrating Huineng's skillful teaching adapted to each student.

8. **付嘱品 (Final Instructions)**: 慧能圆寂前的最后教导和三十六对法。/ Huineng's final instructions before his passing, including the thirty-six pairs of opposites.

### 关键偈颂 / Key Verses

慧能与神秀的偈颂对照是《坛经》最著名的段落，浓缩了南顿北渐的全部差异：

The contrasting verses of Huineng and Shenxiu form the most famous passage of the Platform Sutra, condensing the entire Southern-Northern divide:

**神秀 Shenxiu:**
> 身是菩提树，心如明镜台。时时勤拂拭，勿使惹尘埃。
> "The body is the Bodhi tree, the mind like a bright mirror stand. Constantly wipe it clean — let no dust collect."

**慧能 Huineng:**
> 菩提本无树，明镜亦非台。本来无一物，何处惹尘埃。
> "Bodhi originally has no tree; the bright mirror is also not a stand. Originally there is not a single thing — where can dust collect?"

## 历史意义 / Historical Significance

### 佛教中国化的里程碑 / Milestone of Buddhism' Sinicization

《坛经》标志着印度佛教在中国文化土壤中的彻底转化。它用中国的语言、中国的思维方式、中国的生活场景来表达佛教最核心的洞见——使佛教不再是"外来宗教"，而成为中国文化的有机组成部分。

The Platform Sutra marks the thoroughgoing transformation of Indian Buddhism in Chinese cultural soil. It uses Chinese language, Chinese thought-patterns, and Chinese life-settings to express Buddhism's core insights — making Buddhism no longer a "foreign religion" but an organic part of Chinese culture.

### 对后世禅宗的影响 / Influence on Later Chan

《坛经》奠定了后世禅宗发展的理论基础：
- **五家七宗**: 临济、曹洞、沩仰、云门、法眼——全部源自慧能的法脉
- **公案传统**: 《坛经》中的师徒对话预示了后来公案 (koan) 传统的发展
- **禅宗文学**: 《坛经》的文体影响了此后所有禅宗语录的风格

The Platform Sutra laid the theoretical foundation for all subsequent Chan development:
- **Five Houses**: Linji, Caodong, Weiyang, Yunmen, Fayan — all derive from Huineng's lineage
- **Koan tradition**: The master-disciple dialogues in the Platform Sutra prefigured the later koan tradition
- **Chan literature**: The Platform Sutra's literary style influenced all subsequent Chan discourse records

## 常见误解 / Common Misunderstandings

1. **"《坛经》是慧能亲笔所写"**: 《坛经》是弟子法海编集的口述记录，且在流传过程中经历了大量编辑
2. **"敦煌本 = 唯一真实版本"**: 敦煌本最古老，但不一定最"原始"——它自身也是传抄产物
3. **"《坛经》是禅宗唯一的经典"**: 禅宗虽以《坛经》为核心，但也重视《金刚经》《楞伽经》《维摩经》等

1. **"The Platform Sutra was written by Huineng himself"**: It was compiled by his disciple Fahai from oral teachings and underwent extensive editing
2. **"The Dunhuang version = the only authentic version"**: The oldest but not necessarily the most "original" — it is itself a product of transmission
3. **"The Platform Sutra is Chan's only scripture"**: Though central, Chan also values the Diamond Sutra, Lankavatara Sutra, Vimalakirti Sutra, etc.

## 跨文化对话 / Cross-Cultural Dialogue

- **苏格拉底对话录 Socratic Dialogues**: 与《坛经》同为大师口述、弟子编辑的经典文本形式 / Both are classic texts of a master's oral teachings compiled by disciples
- **福音书 Gospels**: 宗教创始人口述传统的记录，经历了编辑和选择过程 / Records of oral traditions of religious founders, subject to editorial processes
- **口述传统研究 Orality Studies**: Walter Ong 的口述文化理论有助于理解《坛经》从口述到书面的转化 / Walter Ong's orality studies help understand the Platform Sutra's transformation from oral to written
