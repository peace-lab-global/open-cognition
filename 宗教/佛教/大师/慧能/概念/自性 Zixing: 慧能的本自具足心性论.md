# 自性 Zixing / Self-Nature

## 定义 / Definition

**自性 (zìxìng)** 是慧能心性论的核心概念，指每个人本自具足的佛性、本来清净的心性。慧能在悟道时连叹五个"何期"来表达对自性的惊叹："何期自性本自清净，何期自性本不生灭，何期自性本自具足，何期自性本无动摇，何期自性能生万法。"

**Self-nature (zixing)** is the core concept of Huineng's mind-nature theory, referring to the Buddha-nature inherently complete in every person — the originally pure mind. Upon his awakening, Huineng exclaimed five times in wonder at self-nature: "How unexpected! The self-nature is originally pure, originally unborn and undying, originally complete, originally unwavering, and capable of giving rise to all phenomena."

## 核心内涵 / Core Content

### 五个特质 / Five Characteristics

慧能为自性赋予了五个根本特质：

1. **本自清净 (Originally pure)**: 自性不被烦恼所污染，如同明镜虽蒙尘但镜体不变 / Self-nature is unstained by afflictions, like a mirror whose substance is unchanged even when dust-covered
2. **本不生灭 (Originally unborn and undying)**: 自性超越生灭变化，是永恒的认知基底 / Self-nature transcends arising and ceasing — the eternal cognitive ground
3. **本自具足 (Originally complete)**: 自性不缺少任何东西，佛性不是需要获取的 / Self-nature lacks nothing; Buddha-nature need not be acquired
4. **本无动摇 (Originally unwavering)**: 自性不被外境动摇，如大地不被风雨所动 / Self-nature is unmoved by external conditions, like the earth unmoved by wind and rain
5. **能生万法 (Capable of giving rise to all phenomena)**: 一切法从自性中显现，自性是万法的根源 / All phenomena manifest from self-nature — it is the source of all dharmas

### 从"获取"到"发现" / From Acquisition to Discovery

慧能对佛教修行模式进行了根本性转变：佛性不是需要从外部获取的，而是需要"认出"的。这将修行从"获取模式" (acquisition model) 转化为"发现模式" (discovery model)。如同一个人一直戴着的眼镜却在到处寻找眼镜——问题不是获得，而是认出已经拥有的。

Huineng fundamentally transformed the Buddhist practice model: Buddha-nature is not something to acquire externally but something to "recognize." This shifts practice from an acquisition model to a discovery model. Like a person searching everywhere for the glasses already on their face — the problem is not obtaining but recognizing what is already possessed.

### 与如来藏思想的关系 / Relationship to Tathagatagarbha Thought

慧能的"自性"概念与印度如来藏 (tathagatagarbha) 思想有深层关联。如来藏思想主张一切众生皆有佛性，如金矿中含金。但慧能进一步发展了这一思想：他不仅说佛性在众生中，更说众生本身就是佛——只是不自知而已。

Huineng's "self-nature" is deeply connected to the Indian tathagatagarbha (Buddha-womb) thought, which holds that all beings possess Buddha-nature like gold within ore. But Huineng went further: he not only said Buddha-nature is in beings, but that beings already are Buddha — they simply do not know it.

## 哲学分析 / Philosophical Analysis

### 本有与始有之争 / The Innate vs Acquired Debate

慧能的"自性本自具足"立场属于"本有" (innate) 一派——佛性是先天的、本有的。这与"始有" (acquired) 立场——佛性需要通过修行逐渐成就——形成佛教内部的重要争论。慧能采取了最激进的"本有"立场，但他并不否认修行在"发现"佛性中的作用。

Huineng's "self-nature is originally complete" position belongs to the "innate" (benyou) camp — Buddha-nature is congenital, original. This contrasts with the "acquired" (shiyou) position — Buddha-nature must be gradually developed through practice. Huineng took the most radical "innate" stance, though he did not deny the role of practice in "discovering" Buddha-nature.

### 与龙树"空性"的张力 / Tension with Nagarjuna's Emptiness

龙树 (Nagarjuna) 批判一切"自性" (svabhava)——包括佛性的自性。慧能的"自性"似乎与龙树的反本质主义形成张力。但深层分析表明，慧能的"自性"恰恰是"空性"的正面表述——当慧能说自性"本自清净""本无动摇"时，他描述的不是一个实体，而是空性的体验性面向。慧能自己说"自性本空"。

Nagarjuna critiques all "self-nature" (svabhava) — including the self-nature of Buddha-nature. Huineng's "zixing" appears to create tension with Nagarjuna's anti-essentialism. But deeper analysis suggests Huineng's "self-nature" is the positive expression of "emptiness" — when Huineng describes self-nature as "originally pure" and "unwavering," he describes not an entity but the experiential dimension of emptiness. Huineng himself said "self-nature is originally empty."

### 民主性的基础 / Foundation of Egalitarianism

"自性本自具足"为慧能的民主化主张提供了理论基础。如果佛性人人本具，那么出身、教育、性别、阶级都不构成觉悟的障碍。"獦獠亦有佛性"——慧能对弘忍的这句回答，是中国佛教史上最有力的平等宣言。

"Self-nature is originally complete" provides the theoretical foundation for Huineng's democratizing claims. If Buddha-nature is inherent in all, then origin, education, gender, and class are no barriers to awakening. "Even a barbarian has Buddha-nature" — Huineng's reply to Hongren is the most powerful egalitarian declaration in Chinese Buddhist history.

## 实践含义 / Practical Implications

### 修行方式的转变 / Transformation of Practice

自性论改变了修行的方向：从向外寻求转为向内体认。慧能批评向外求佛的做法："东方人造罪念佛求生西方，西方人造罪念佛求生何国？"修行不是去某个地方或成为某种人，而是认出当下的自己本来就是的。

Self-nature theory redirects practice: from seeking outwardly to recognizing inwardly. Huineng criticized seeking Buddha externally: "People in the East, committing sins, recite Buddha's name seeking rebirth in the West. People in the West, committing sins — where do they seek rebirth?" Practice is not going somewhere or becoming someone, but recognizing what you already are.

### 自信与自度 / Self-Confidence and Self-Liberation

慧能强调"自性自度"——没有佛菩萨能替你觉悟，觉悟必须由自己完成。"迷时师度，悟了自度"——在迷惑时可以依靠老师指引，但真正的觉悟只能自己实现。这种自主性赋予了修行者极大的自信和力量。

Huineng emphasized "self-nature self-liberates" — no Buddha or bodhisattva can awaken you; awakening must be accomplished by yourself. "When deluded, the teacher guides; when awakened, you guide yourself." This autonomy grants practitioners immense confidence and empowerment.

## 常见误解 / Common Misunderstandings

1. **"自性 = 灵魂/永恒自我"**: 自性不是灵魂 (atman) 或永恒实体；慧能说"自性本空"
2. **"自性本具 = 不需修行"**: 虽然本具，但被烦恼覆盖，仍需通过修行"发现"
3. **"自性 = 泛神论"**: 慧能不能被简化为泛神论；自性是体验性的而非本体论命题

1. **"Self-nature = soul/eternal self"**: Self-nature is not an atman or eternal entity; Huineng said "self-nature is originally empty"
2. **"Inherently complete = no practice needed"**: Though inherent, it is obscured by afflictions and must still be "discovered"
3. **"Self-nature = pantheism"**: Huineng cannot be reduced to pantheism; self-nature is experiential, not an ontological proposition

## 跨文化对话 / Cross-Cultural Dialogue

- **海德格尔 Heidegger**: "本真存在" (Eigentlichkeit) 与"自性"都指向回到本来面目的存在方式 / Both "authenticity" and "zixing" point to returning to one's original way of being
- **孟子 Mencius**: 性善论与自性本净的结构性平行 / Structural parallel between innate goodness and originally pure self-nature
- **荣格 Jung**: "自性" (Self) 作为心灵整体的原型与慧能自性作为完整心性的概念有共鸣 / Jung's "Self" archetype as psychic totality resonates with Huineng's self-nature as complete mind
- **卢梭 Rousseau**: 人的自然善性被文明扭曲——与自性本净被烦恼覆盖的叙事平行 / Natural goodness distorted by civilization parallels originally pure nature obscured by afflictions
