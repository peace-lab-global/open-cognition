# 宗喀巴 Tsongkhapa (1357–1419)

> **"一切善法之根本，在于善知识之摄受。"**
> *The root of all virtue is the guidance of a qualified spiritual teacher.*

宗喀巴（bLo-bzang grags-pa），出生于安多宗喀地区（今青海省湟中县），是藏传佛教格鲁派（dGe-lugs-pa，意为"善规"）的创立者。他自幼出家，十六岁赴卫藏游学，遍访萨迦、噶举、宁玛、噶当诸派大师，广学显密教法。面对十四世纪西藏佛教戒律松弛、密法滥用的乱象，宗喀巴发起了一场深刻的宗教改革运动——恢复比丘戒的严格性，以显教为密教的基础，以中观应成派（Prāsaṅgika-Madhyamaka）为最高哲学见地。

Tsongkhapa (bLo-bzang grags-pa) was born in the Tsongkha region of Amdo (present-day Huangzhong County, Qinghai Province). He is the founder of the Gelug school (dGe-lugs-pa, "Virtuous Tradition") of Tibetan Buddhism. Ordained as a child, he traveled to Central Tibet at sixteen to study extensively with masters of the Sakya, Kagyu, Nyingma, and Kadam traditions, mastering both sutra and tantra. Confronted with the widespread laxity of monastic discipline and misuse of tantric practices in fourteenth-century Tibet, Tsongkhapa initiated a profound religious reform—restoring strict vinaya observance, establishing sutra as the foundation for tantra, and upholding Prāsaṅgika-Madhyamaka as the highest philosophical view.

1409年，宗喀巴在拉萨创建甘丹寺（dGa'-ldan），标志着格鲁派正式成立。其弟子根敦主巴（dGe-'dun grub-pa）后来被追认为第一世达赖喇嘛，克主杰（mKhas-grub rje）被追认为第一世班禅额尔德尼。格鲁派日后发展为藏传佛教中规模最大、影响最深远的宗派，并深刻塑造了西藏的政治与文化格局。

In 1409, Tsongkhapa founded Ganden Monastery (dGa'-ldan) near Lhasa, marking the formal establishment of the Gelug school. His disciple Gendun Drupa (dGe-'dun grub-pa) was later recognized as the First Dalai Lama, and Khedrup Je (mKhas-grub rje) as the First Panchen Lama. The Gelug school grew to become the largest and most influential tradition in Tibetan Buddhism, profoundly shaping Tibet's political and cultural landscape.

## 核心命题 / Core Propositions

### 1. 三士道 / The Three Scopes of Practice (Tri-scope Path)

修行者依发心之不同分为三个层次：**下士道**（追求来世善趣，以因果正见为基础）、**中士道**（追求个人解脱，以出离心为动力）、**上士道**（追求无上菩提，以菩提心为根本）。三士道不是三种不同的修行者，而是同一修行者的三个渐进阶段——每一阶段都包含并超越前一阶段。

Practitioners are classified by their motivation into three scopes: the **initial scope** (seeking better future lives, grounded in right view of karma), the **middle scope** (seeking personal liberation, driven by renunciation), and the **great scope** (seeking full Buddhahood, rooted in bodhicitta). These are not three types of practitioners but three progressive stages of the same individual—each stage encompassing and transcending the previous one.

### 2. 中观应成见 / Prāsaṅgika-Madhyamaka View

宗喀巴以月称（Candrakīrti）的应成派为最高哲学见地。应成见的核心方法是"只破不立"——不建立任何自性有的正面命题，只通过归谬法（prasaṅga）揭示一切实有见解的内在矛盾。宗喀巴强调，正确理解空性（śūnyatā）是解脱的关键，而对空性的错误理解——无论是断灭见还是常见——都是危险的。

Tsongkhapa upheld Candrakīrti's Prāsaṅgika school as the highest philosophical view. The core method of Prāsaṅgika is "refutation without assertion"—establishing no positive thesis of inherent existence, but only exposing the internal contradictions of all essentialist positions through reductio ad absurdum (prasaṅga). Tsongkhapa emphasized that correct understanding of emptiness (śūnyatā) is the key to liberation, and that misreading emptiness—whether as nihilism or eternalism—is perilous.

### 3. 戒律复兴 / Vinaya Revival

面对十四世纪西藏僧侣饮酒、娶妻、参与世俗权力斗争的乱象，宗喀巴发起严格的戒律复兴运动。他主张恢复印度比丘戒（bhikṣu-saṃvara）的完整性，强调戒律是一切修行的基础——没有戒律，定与慧都无从建立。

Facing the widespread laxity of fourteenth-century Tibetan monasticism—monks drinking alcohol, marrying, and engaging in secular power struggles—Tsongkhapa launched a rigorous vinaya revival. He advocated restoring the integrity of the Indian bhikṣu-saṃvara (full monastic ordination), emphasizing that ethical discipline is the foundation of all practice: without discipline, neither concentration nor wisdom can be established.

### 4. 显密双修 / Integration of Sutra and Tantra

宗喀巴主张显教为基、密教为果。修行者必须先通过显教的学习建立正见（尤其是空性见与菩提心），然后才能进入密法的修持。密法不是独立于显教之外的捷径，而是建立在显教基础上的加速道。这一立场有效地遏制了当时密法被滥用的风气。

Tsongkhapa taught that sutra is the foundation and tantra is the fruition. Practitioners must first establish right view (especially the view of emptiness and bodhicitta) through sutra study before entering tantric practice. Tantra is not a shortcut independent of sutra but an accelerated path built upon the sutra foundation. This position effectively curbed the then-prevalent misuse of tantric practices.

### 5. 菩提心为修行核心 / Bodhicitta as the Heart of Practice

在宗喀巴的体系中，菩提心（bodhicitta）——为利益一切众生而追求无上正觉的发心——是一切修行中最核心的要素。没有菩提心，即使空性见再深刻，也只能成为声闻或缘觉的解脱，不能成就佛果。菩提心的修持以"七因果教授"和"自他交换"（tonglen）为主要方法。

In Tsongkhapa's system, bodhicitta—the aspiration to attain full enlightenment for the benefit of all sentient beings—is the most essential element of all practice. Without bodhicitta, no matter how profound one's view of emptiness, one can only achieve the liberation of a śrāvaka or pratyekabuddha, not Buddhahood. The cultivation of bodhicitta employs primarily the "sevenfold cause-and-effect instruction" and "exchanging self and others" (tonglen).

### 6. 三主要道 / The Three Principal Aspects of the Path

宗喀巴在《三主要道论》中将整个佛法修行浓缩为三个核心要素：**出离心**（renunciation，从轮回的执取中退出）、**菩提心**（bodhicitta，从自利转向利他）、**空性见**（right view of emptiness，破除自性见）。三者缺一不可，构成认知与精神转化的完整框架。

In his *Three Principal Aspects of the Path*, Tsongkhapa condensed the entirety of Buddhist practice into three core elements: **renunciation** (withdrawing from cyclic clinging), **bodhicitta** (redirecting from self-interest to altruism), and **right view of emptiness** (deconstructing inherent existence). None can be omitted; together they form the complete framework for cognitive and spiritual transformation.

## 思想脉络 / Intellectual Development

### 早年广学期 (1357–1388) / Early Period of Extensive Study

宗喀巴三岁受近事戒，七岁受沙弥戒，十六岁（1372年）赴卫藏求学。他先后师从萨迦派的仁达瓦（Red-mda'-ba gZhon-nu blo-gros）学习中观与因明，从噶当派的绛生·曲杰扎西学习道次第，从噶举派的法王·噶玛巴学习大手印，并广泛研习宁玛派的大圆满教法。这一时期的广学为他日后整合诸派奠定了坚实基础。

Tsongkhapa received his lay vows at age three and novice ordination at seven. At sixteen (1372), he journeyed to Central Tibet for advanced study. He studied Madhyamaka and epistemology under the Sakya master Rendawa (Red-mda'-ba gZhon-nu blo-gros), lamrim under the Kadam master Jangchub Chokyi Tashi, Mahāmudrā under the Kagyu Karmapa, and extensively studied Nyingma Dzogchen teachings. This period of broad study laid the foundation for his later integration of all traditions.

### 中期改革期 (1388–1402) / Middle Period of Reform

约1388年起，宗喀巴开始对西藏佛教的现状深感忧虑。他看到许多僧侣以"密法"为借口放纵欲望，戒律几乎名存实亡。在文殊菩萨的"启示"与自身的理性反思中，宗喀巴开始推动戒律复兴，同时深入修持中观见地与密法。1398年前后，他在禅修中获得了空性的直接证悟，这一经验深刻影响了他的教学。

From around 1388, Tsongkhapa grew deeply concerned about the state of Tibetan Buddhism. He observed many monks using "tantra" as a pretext for indulgence, with vinaya observance nearly extinguished. Through both visionary experiences of Mañjuśrī and his own rational reflection, Tsongkhapa began to promote vinaya revival while deepening his practice of Madhyamaka and tantra. Around 1398, he attained direct realization of emptiness in meditation—an experience that profoundly shaped his teaching.

### 晚期建宗期 (1402–1419) / Late Period of Institution Building

1402年，宗喀巴完成了划时代的巨著《菩提道次第广论》（Lam-rim chen-mo），系统阐述了从凡夫到成佛的完整修行路径。1406年完成了《密宗道次第广论》（sNgag-rim chen-mo）。1409年在拉萨创建甘丹寺，同年发起大愿法会（sMon-lam chen-mo），此后每年举办。宗喀巴于1419年圆寂于甘丹寺，享年六十二岁。

In 1402, Tsongkhapa completed his epoch-making magnum opus, *The Great Treatise on the Stages of the Path to Enlightenment* (Lam-rim chen-mo), systematically expounding the complete path from ordinary being to Buddhahood. In 1406, he completed *The Great Treatise on the Stages of Mantra* (sNgag-rim chen-mo). In 1409, he founded Ganden Monastery near Lhasa and inaugurated the Great Prayer Festival (sMon-lam chen-mo), which became an annual event. Tsongkhapa passed away at Ganden in 1419, at the age of sixty-two.

## 重要概念 / Key Concepts

### 1. 三士道 Tri-scope Path (skyes-bu gsum)
修行的三个渐进层次：下士道（追求善趣）、中士道（追求解脱）、上士道（追求菩提）。每一层包含并超越前一层。
Three progressive levels: initial (seeking good future lives), middle (seeking liberation), great (seeking enlightenment). Each encompasses and transcends the previous.

### 2. 出离心 Renunciation (nges-'byung)
对轮回之乐的彻底厌离，是修行的根本动力。不是逃避现实，而是对存在本质的深刻洞察。
Thorough disillusionment with cyclic existence; the fundamental motivation for practice. Not escapism but profound insight into the nature of existence.

### 3. 菩提心 Bodhicitta (byang-chub kyi sems)
为利益一切众生而追求无上正觉的发心。分为愿菩提心（aspiration）与行菩提心（engagement）。是一切大乘法门的核心。
The aspiration to attain full enlightenment for the benefit of all beings. Divided into aspirational bodhicitta and engaged bodhicitta. The core of all Mahāyāna practice.

### 4. 空性见 View of Emptiness (stong-pa-nyid)
一切法无自性（niḥsvabhāva），但不落入断灭见。宗喀巴特别强调"缘起有"与"自性空"的不二——正因为无自性，缘起才成为可能。
All phenomena lack inherent existence (niḥsvabhāva) without falling into nihilism. Tsongkhapa especially emphasized the non-duality of "dependent arising" and "emptiness"—it is precisely because things lack inherent existence that dependent arising is possible.

### 5. 应成见 Prāsaṅgika View (thal-'gyur-ba)
以月称为代表的中观最高见地。只通过归谬法揭示对方论点的内在矛盾，自身不建立任何"自性有"的命题。这是一种纯粹的解构方法。
The highest Madhyamaka view, represented by Candrakīrti. Exposing only the internal contradictions of opponents' positions through reductio ad absurdum, without asserting any thesis of inherent existence. A purely deconstructive method.

### 6. 止观双运 Union of Calm Abiding and Insight (zhi-lhag zung-'brel)
定（śamatha）与慧（vipaśyanā）的不可分离。宗喀巴认为仅有止而无观不能断烦恼根，仅有观而无止则如风中烛火。二者必须双运。
The inseparability of calm abiding (śamatha) and insight (vipaśyanā). Tsongkhapa taught that concentration without insight cannot uproot afflictions, while insight without concentration is like a candle in a gale. Both must be united.

### 7. 七因果教授 Sevenfold Cause-and-Effect Instruction
修持菩提心的主要方法：知母→念恩→报恩→慈心→悲心→增上意乐→菩提心。通过七个因果相连的心理步骤，逐步培养无造作的利他心。
The primary method for cultivating bodhicitta: recognizing all beings as mothers → remembering their kindness → wishing to repay it → love → compassion → exceptional resolve → bodhicitta. Seven causally linked mental steps progressively cultivating genuine altruism.

### 8. 自他交换 Exchanging Self and Others (bdag-gzhan brje-ba / tonglen)
将自己与他人交换位置：吸气时接纳他人的痛苦，呼气时给予自身的快乐与功德。这是佛教最具体的认知行为重构练习之一。
Exchanging one's own position with others': inhaling to take on others' suffering, exhaling to give one's own happiness and merit. One of Buddhism's most concrete cognitive-behavioral restructuring exercises.

### 9. 六大庄严与二胜 Six Ornaments and Two Supreme Ones
宗喀巴所依据的印度论师谱系：龙树、提婆、无著、世亲、陈那、法称（六大庄严），加上功德光与释迦光（二胜）。构成格鲁派教育的核心传承。
The Indian master lineage Tsongkhapa relied upon: Nāgārjuna, Āryadeva, Asaṅga, Vasubandhu, Dignāga, Dharmakīrti (Six Ornaments), plus Guṇaprabha and Śākyaprabha (Two Supreme Ones). The core lineage of Gelug education.

### 10. 密乘四部 Four Classes of Tantra
事部（Kriyā）、行部（Caryā）、瑜伽部（Yoga）、无上瑜伽部（Anuttarayoga）。宗喀巴在《密宗道次第广论》中系统阐述了四部密法的次第与关系。
Action (Kriyā), Performance (Caryā), Yoga, and Unsurpassed Yoga (Anuttarayoga). Tsongkhapa systematically expounded the stages and relationships among the four tantric classes in his *Great Treatise on the Stages of Mantra*.

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

**藏传教育 Tibetan Monastic Education**：格鲁派寺院学院（如哲蚌寺、色拉寺、甘丹寺）至今沿用宗喀巴建立的课程体系——以《现观庄严论》《入中论》《释量论》《俱舍论》《戒律论》五部大论为核心，学僧需经过十五至二十年的系统学习方可获得格西（dge-bshes）学位。

The Gelug monastic colleges (such as Drepung, Sera, and Ganden) continue to follow the curriculum Tsongkhapa established—centered on the five great treatises (*Abhisamayālaṃkāra*, *Madhyamakāvatāra*, *Pramāṇavārttika*, *Abhidharmakośa*, and *Vinayasūtra*)—requiring fifteen to twenty years of systematic study for the geshe degree.

**跨宗教对话 Interreligious Dialogue**：第十四世达赖喇嘛——格鲁派最高精神领袖——将宗喀巴的理性精神带入与现代科学的对话。从量子物理到神经科学，达赖以宗喀巴的中观方法论为框架，探索佛教认知科学与现代科学的交汇点。

The Fourteenth Dalai Lama—the Gelug tradition's highest spiritual authority—has brought Tsongkhapa's rational spirit into dialogue with modern science. From quantum physics to neuroscience, the Dalai Lama uses Tsongkhapa's Madhyamaka methodology as a framework to explore the intersections of Buddhist cognitive science and modern science.

**心理学与心理治疗 Psychology and Psychotherapy**：菩提心修持（尤其是自他交换 tonglen）与正念冥想已广泛进入西方心理治疗领域。宗喀巴的系统化心训练方法（Lojong）为认知行为疗法（CBT）提供了古老的东方参照。

Bodhicitta cultivation (especially tonglen) and mindfulness meditation have widely entered Western psychotherapy. Tsongkhapa's systematized mind-training methods (Lojong) provide an ancient Eastern counterpart to Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT).

**政治与地缘 Politics and Geopolitics**：格鲁派与西藏政教合一的传统深刻影响了近现代西藏的历史走向。理解宗喀巴的思想遗产，是理解当代西藏问题不可或缺的文化维度。

The Gelug tradition's integration with Tibetan governance profoundly influenced modern Tibetan history. Understanding Tsongkhapa's intellectual legacy is an indispensable cultural dimension for understanding contemporary Tibetan issues.

## 常见误读 / Common Misreadings

### 误读 1："格鲁派 = 保守僵化" / Misreading 1: "Gelug = Conservative and Rigid"

宗喀巴的改革在当时是激进的——他恢复了已被废弃数百年的戒律标准，重新定义了密法的修持前提。格鲁派的"严谨"不是保守，而是对当时佛教腐败的革命性回应。将格鲁派的学术严谨等同于思想僵化，是对其改革本质的误解。

Tsongkhapa's reform was radical in its time—he restored vinaya standards that had been abandoned for centuries and redefined the prerequisites for tantric practice. The Gelug "rigor" was not conservatism but a revolutionary response to Buddhist corruption of the era. Equating Gelug academic rigor with intellectual rigidity misunderstands its reformist essence.

### 误读 2："宗喀巴只讲戒律，不懂哲学" / Misreading 2: "Tsongkhapa Only Cared about Discipline, Not Philosophy"

宗喀巴是中观哲学史上最深刻的思想家之一。他对空性的阐释——尤其是"缘起与空性的不二"——被西方学者（如 Jeffrey Hopkins、Guy Newland、Jay Garfield）认为是中观哲学发展的高峰。他的《入中论善显密意疏》至今仍是中观研究的必读文献。

Tsongkhapa is among the most profound thinkers in the history of Madhyamaka 哲学. His interpretation of emptiness—especially the non-duality of dependent arising and emptiness—is regarded by Western scholars (such as Jeffrey Hopkins, Guy Newland, and Jay Garfield) as a pinnacle of Madhyamaka development. His *Illumination of the Thought* remains essential reading for Madhyamaka studies.

### 误读 3："格鲁派排斥其他宗派" / Misreading 3: "Gelug Excludes Other Schools"

宗喀巴的思想恰恰是整合性的。他从萨迦派学中观，从噶当派学道次第，从噶举派学大手印，从宁玛派了解大圆满——然后将这些教法纳入一个统一的框架。格鲁派的"严格"是关于修行次第的严格，不是宗派排斥。

Tsongkhapa's thought was precisely integrative. He studied Madhyamaka from Sakya, lamrim from Kadam, Mahāmudrā from Kagyu, and Dzogchen from Nyingma—then incorporated these teachings into a unified framework. Gelug "strictness" concerns the strictness of practice stages, not sectarian exclusion.

### 误读 4："密法是格鲁派的'秘密'或'高级'内容" / Misreading 4: "Tantra is the Gelug's 'Secret' or 'Advanced' Content"

在宗喀巴的体系中，密法确实是高阶修持，但它是公开的、有前提的——修行者必须先完成显教的准备（出离心、菩提心、空性见）才能进入密法。密法不是被隐藏的秘密，而是需要充分准备的修持阶段。宗喀巴写作《密宗道次第广论》的目的之一，正是要让密法修持变得透明和规范化。

In Tsongkhapa's system, tantra is indeed advanced practice, but it is open and conditional—practitioners must first complete sutra preparation (renunciation, bodhicitta, emptiness view) before entering tantra. Tantra is not a hidden secret but a practice stage requiring thorough preparation. One purpose of writing the *Great Treatise on the Stages of Mantra* was precisely to make tantric practice transparent and standardized.

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

宗喀巴的思想与西方哲学传统有着深刻的对话空间：

Tsongkhapa's thought offers rich possibilities for dialogue with Western philosophical traditions:

**与康德伦理学的对话 Dialogue with Kantian Ethics**：宗喀巴的戒律伦理与康德的义务论伦理学有着结构性相似——二者都强调行为的道德品质不取决于后果，而取决于动机与原则。但宗喀巴通过菩提心超越了康德的形式主义，将伦理建立在同情（karuṇā）而非纯粹理性之上。

Tsongkhapa's vinaya ethics shares structural similarities with Kantian deontology—both emphasize that the moral quality of actions depends on motivation and principle, not consequences. Yet Tsongkhapa transcends Kantian formalism through bodhicitta, grounding ethics in compassion (karuṇā) rather than pure reason alone.

**与解构主义的对话 Dialogue with Deconstruction**：应成派的归谬法与德里达（Derrida）的解构方法有着惊人的相似——二者都拒绝建立任何正面命题，只通过揭示文本/概念的内在矛盾来消解本质主义。但宗喀巴的解构有明确的伦理-解脱论目标，而非纯粹的文本游戏。

The Prāsaṅgika method of reductio ad absurdum bears striking similarities to Derridean deconstruction—both refuse to establish any positive thesis, dissolving essentialism only by exposing internal contradictions of texts/concepts. Yet Tsongkhapa's deconstruction has a clear ethical-soteriological aim, not mere textual play.

**与认知科学的对话 Dialogue with Cognitive Science**：宗喀巴的心训练方法（Lojong）——尤其是自他交换——已被现代认知科学所关注。Richard Davidson 等神经科学家对长期禅修者的研究表明，系统化的注意力训练与慈悲冥想可以显著改变大脑结构与功能。宗喀巴的体系提供了一个详尽的"认知工程"手册。

Tsongkhapa's mind-training methods (Lojong)—especially tonglen—have attracted attention from modern cognitive science. Research by neuroscientists like Richard Davidson on long-term meditators demonstrates that systematic attention training and compassion meditation can significantly alter brain structure and function. Tsongkhapa's system provides a detailed "cognitive engineering" manual.
