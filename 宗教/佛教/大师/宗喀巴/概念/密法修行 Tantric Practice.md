# 密法修行 / Tantric Practice: The Vajrayāna Path in Tsongkhapa's System

## 概念概述 / Overview

密法（密乘、金刚乘 Vajrayāna）是宗喀巴修行体系的最高阶段——但不是独立于显教之外的体系，而是建立在显教基础之上的"加速道"。宗喀巴通过《密宗道次第广论》（sNgag-rim chen-mo）对整个密法体系进行了系统化整理，明确了四部密续的次第、前提条件、修持方法和最终目标。

Tantra (Vajrayāna, the "Diamond Vehicle") is the highest stage in Tsongkhapa's practice system—but not a system independent of sutra. Rather, it is an "accelerated path" built upon the sutra foundation. Through his *Great Treatise on the Stages of Mantra* (sNgag-rim chen-mo), Tsongkhapa systematized the entire tantric system, clarifying the stages of the four classes of tantra, their prerequisites, practice methods, and ultimate goals.

## 显密关系 / The Sutra-Tantra Relationship

宗喀巴关于显密关系的核心主张：

Tsongkhapa's core thesis on the sutra-tantra relationship:

**显教是基础，密法是果** / **Sutra is the foundation, tantra is the fruition**.

修行者必须首先在显教中建立三个条件，才能进入密法：
A practitioner must first establish three conditions through sutra before entering tantra:

1. **出离心** Renunciation：对轮回的真实厌离
2. **菩提心** Bodhicitta：为利益一切众生而成佛的发心
3. **空性见** Emptiness View：对无自性的正确理解

没有这三个基础就修密法，宗喀巴比喻为"在冰面上建高楼"——基础不牢，终必倒塌。他严厉批评当时那些以"密法"为借口跳过基础修行的僧侣。

Without these three foundations, practicing tantra is, in Tsongkhapa's metaphor, "building a tower on ice"—the foundation is unstable and collapse inevitable. He sharply criticized monks of his era who used "tantra" as an excuse to skip foundational practice.

## 密乘四部 / The Four Classes of Tantra

### 事部 / Action Tantra (Kriyā-tantra)

**重点**：外在的净化仪式与行为。
**Focus**: External purification rituals and conduct.

事部是最基础的密法层次，强调外在的洁净——包括沐浴、素食、持咒、观想本尊在前方的"主仆关系"（修行者与本尊是分开的）。修行者视本尊为主人，自己为仆人，通过祈求获得加持。

Action Tantra is the most basic tantric level, emphasizing external cleanliness—including bathing, vegetarian diet, mantra recitation, and visualizing the deity in front (a "master-servant relationship" where practitioner and deity are separate). The practitioner views the deity as master and oneself as servant, receiving blessings through supplication.

### 行部 / Performance Tantra (Caryā-tantra)

**重点**：外在行为与内在禅修的平衡。
**Focus**: Balance between external conduct and internal meditation.

行部在事部的基础上增加了更多的内在禅修成分。修行者与本尊的关系从"主仆"转变为"朋友"——更加平等和亲密。

Performance Tantra adds more internal meditation components to the Action Tantra base. The practitioner-deity relationship shifts from "master-servant" to "friend"—more equal and intimate.

### 瑜伽部 / Yoga Tantra

**重点**：内在禅修为主，外在行为为辅。
**Focus**: Primarily internal meditation, with external conduct as secondary.

瑜伽部进一步内化了修行——修行者开始将自己观想为本尊（"自生本尊"），而非仅仅在本尊前方观想。这标志着从"向外祈求"到"向内转化"的转变。

Yoga Tantra further internalizes practice—the practitioner begins visualizing oneself as the deity ("self-generation") rather than only visualizing the deity in front. This marks the shift from "external supplication" to "internal transformation."

### 无上瑜伽部 / Unsurpassed Yoga Tantra (Anuttarayoga-tantra)

**重点**：直接操纵心识的最微细层面。
**Focus**: Directly manipulating the subtlest levels of consciousness.

无上瑜伽部是密法的最高层次，也是宗喀巴最为重视的密法。它分为两个阶段：

Unsurpassed Yoga Tantra is the highest tantric level and the one Tsongkhapa valued most. It is divided into two stages:

#### 生起次第 / Generation Stage (bskyed-rim / utpatti-krama)

修行者通过复杂的观想，将自己完全转化为本尊——观想自己的身体、语言、意念都成为本尊的身、语、意。这不仅是想象力的练习，而是一种深层的**认知重构**——打破凡夫的庸常自我认同（"我是一个有限的、有缺陷的众生"），建立佛慢（"我本具佛的一切功德"）。

Through elaborate visualization, the practitioner completely transforms into the deity—visualizing one's body, speech, and mind becoming the deity's body, speech, and mind. This is not mere imagination but a deep **cognitive restructuring**—breaking the ordinary self-identity ("I am a limited, flawed being") and establishing divine pride ("I inherently possess all Buddha qualities").

#### 圆满次第 / Completion Stage (rdzogs-rim / niṣpanna-krama)

在生起次第的基础上，修行者通过操纵身体的微细能量系统（脉 nāḍī、气 prāṇa、明点 bindu），使粗大心识消融，显露最微细的"光明心"（'od-gsal / clear light）。在光明心中直接证悟空性，即是成佛。

Building on the generation stage, the practitioner manipulates the body's subtle energy system (channels / nāḍī, winds / prāṇa, drops / bindu) to dissolve gross consciousness and reveal the subtlest "clear light" mind ('od-gsal). Directly realizing emptiness within clear light is Buddhahood itself.

宗喀巴特别重视的圆满次第修法包括"那洛六法"（Nā-ro chos-drug）：拙火（gtum-mo）、幻身（sgyu-lus）、梦境（rmi-lam）、光明（'od-gsal）、中阴（bar-do）、迁识（'pho-ba）。

Completion-stage practices Tsongkhapa especially valued include the "Six Dharmas of Nāropa": inner heat (gtum-mo), illusory body (sgyu-lus), dream yoga (rmi-lam), clear light ('od-gsal), bardo (bar-do), and consciousness transference ('pho-ba).

## 宗喀巴的密法改革 / Tsongkhapa's Tantric Reform

宗喀巴对当时密法修持的批评与改革：

Tsongkhapa's critiques and reforms of contemporary tantric practice:

### 1. 反对"以欲为道"的滥用 / Against Misuse of "Desire as Path"

无上瑜伽部的最高修法包括"双运"（yab-yum，佛父佛母的结合），其目的是在最强烈的感官经验中证悟空性。但宗喀巴指出，这只有在修行者已经完全掌控了微细能量系统之后才能实际进行——在此之前，只能以观想方式进行。许多僧侣以此为借口进行性行为，宗喀巴严厉禁止这种行为。

The highest practices of Anuttarayoga Tantra include "union" (yab-yum, the joining of male and female consorts), aimed at realizing emptiness within the most intense sensory experience. Tsongkhapa taught that this can only be physically practiced after the practitioner has fully mastered the subtle energy system—before then, it should be practiced only in visualization. Many monks used this as a pretext for sexual conduct; Tsongkhapa strictly prohibited this.

### 2. 戒律为密法的前提 / Vinaya as Prerequisite for Tantra

宗喀巴强调，比丘戒（bhikṣu-saṃvara）是一切密法三昧耶戒（samaya）的基础。一个不守基本戒律的人，不可能有效地修持密法。

Tsongkhapa emphasized that the bhikṣu-saṃvara (monastic ordination) is the foundation of all tantric samaya vows. One who does not maintain basic discipline cannot effectively practice tantra.

### 3. 系统化密法教育 / Systematizing Tantric Education

在宗喀巴之前，密法往往以师徒口传的方式秘密传授，缺乏统一的课程标准。宗喀巴的《密宗道次第广论》首次将整个密法体系——从四部密续的分类到具体的修持方法——进行了百科全书式的系统阐述，使密法修持变得透明和可教学。

Before Tsongkhapa, tantra was often transmitted orally and secretly between teacher and student, lacking a unified curriculum. Tsongkhapa's *Great Treatise on the Stages of Mantra* was the first encyclopedic systematization of the entire tantric system—from the classification of four tantras to specific practice methods—making tantric practice transparent and teachable.

## 格鲁派密法修持特色 / Distinctive Features of Gelug Tantric Practice

| 特色 Feature | 说明 Description |
|---|---|
| 先显后密 Sutra before Tantra | 至少完成十五至二十年的显教学习后才进入密法 |
| 三律仪并重 Three Vows | 别解脱戒 + 菩萨戒 + 密乘三昧耶戒 |
| 本尊修持 Deity Practice | 以密集金刚（Guhyasamāja）、胜乐金刚（Cakrasaṃvara）、大威德金刚（Yamāntaka）为三大本尊 |
| 学术与实修并重 Study and Practice | 密法修持必须建立在扎实的哲学理解之上 |
| 密法在寺院中的位置 Tantra in Monasteries | 上密院（rGyud-smad）与下密院（rGyud-stod）为格鲁派专门的密法修学机构 |

## 认知意义 / Cognitive Significance

密法从认知科学的角度可以理解为一种**全面的认知转化工程**：

From a cognitive science perspective, tantra can be understood as a **comprehensive cognitive transformation engineering**:

- **生起次第 = 身份重构** Generation Stage = Identity Reconstruction：通过将自己观想为本尊，打破固定的自我模型（self-model），建立一种全新的认知身份。
- **圆满次第 = 神经-意识操纵** Completion Stage = Neuro-consciousness Manipulation：通过操纵身体的微细能量系统，直接影响意识状态——这与现代神经科学对自主神经系统的研究相关。
- **密法的"加速"效果 Tantric Acceleration**：如果显教需要三大阿僧祇劫才能成佛，密法号称可以在一生中证悟——其"加速"机制是通过同时运用身、语、意三个层面的全面转化，而非仅在意念层面进行改变。
