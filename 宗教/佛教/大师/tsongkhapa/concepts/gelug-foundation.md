# 格鲁派基础 / Gelug Foundation: The Virtuous Tradition

## 概念概述 / Overview

格鲁派（dGe-lugs-pa，意为"善规"或"善道"）是宗喀巴于1409年创立的藏传佛教宗派，以黄色僧帽为标志，汉地俗称"黄教"。格鲁派是藏传佛教四大主要宗派（宁玛、萨迦、噶举、格鲁）中最晚成立的，也是规模最大、影响最深远的。它不仅是一个宗教宗派，更是一个庞大的教育与制度体系。

The Gelug school (dGe-lugs-pa, meaning "Virtuous Tradition") was founded by Tsongkhapa in 1409. Marked by yellow monastic hats, it is known in Chinese as "黄教" (Yellow Hat school). Of the four major Tibetan Buddhist schools (Nyingma, Sakya, Kagyu, Gelug), Gelug was the last to be established yet became the largest and most influential. It is not merely a religious sect but a vast educational and institutional system.

## 创立背景 / Historical Context

### 十四世纪西藏佛教的状况 / State of Tibetan Buddhism in the 14th Century

宗喀巴创立格鲁派的时代背景：

The historical context of Tsongkhapa's founding of the Gelug school:

- **戒律松弛** Laxity of Discipline：许多僧侣不守基本戒律，饮酒、娶妻、追求世俗权力。
- **密法滥用** Misuse of Tantra：以"密法"为借口进行不当行为，尤其是性行为的滥化。
- **学术衰退** Academic Decline：系统的佛学教育衰落，许多僧侣对基本教义缺乏理解。
- **宗派对立** Sectarian Division：各宗派之间缺乏有效的整合框架。

### 宗喀巴的改革措施 / Tsongkhapa's Reform Measures

- **恢复比丘戒** Restoring Bhikṣu Ordination：严格恢复印度比丘戒的完整性，要求僧侣严守二百五十三条戒律。
- **建立学术标准** Establishing Academic Standards：制定以五部大论为核心的系统课程，要求长期严格的学术训练。
- **规范密法修持** Standardizing Tantric Practice：明确密法修持的前提条件与方法，禁止不具资格者修持高阶密法。
- **创建寺院体系** Building the Monastic System：建立甘丹寺（1409）作为格鲁派的中心，后发展为三大寺（甘丹、哲蚌、色拉）。

## 格鲁派的核心特色 / Core Features of the Gelug School

### 1. 学修并重 / Integration of Study and Practice

格鲁派最显著的特色是**学术与实修的严格整合**。与某些强调"直指心性"的宗派不同，格鲁派认为没有扎实的哲学理解，修行就是盲目的。格鲁派的学僧必须经过十五至二十五年的系统学习，掌握五部大论，通过严格的辩经考试，方可获得格西（dge-bshes）学位。

The Gelug school's most distinctive feature is the **rigorous integration of academic study and meditative practice**. Unlike some schools emphasizing "direct pointing to mind," Gelug holds that practice without solid philosophical understanding is blind. Gelug scholars must undergo fifteen to twenty-five years of systematic study, mastering the five great treatises, and passing rigorous debate examinations before earning the geshe (dge-bshes) degree.

### 2. 辩经传统 / Debate Tradition

辩经（rtsod-grwa）是格鲁派教育的核心方法。学僧通过正反双方的辩论来深化对教义的理解——攻方（提问者）试图发现守方（回答者）论证中的矛盾，守方必须在严格的逻辑框架内回应。这一方法源自印度那烂陀大学的传统，经由宗喀巴及其弟子发扬光大。

Debate (rtsod-grwa) is the core method of Gelug education. Scholars deepen their understanding through adversarial debate—the challenger attempts to find contradictions in the defender's reasoning, while the defender must respond within strict logical frameworks. This method originated at India's Nālandā University and was carried forward by Tsongkhapa and his disciples.

### 3. 五部大论 / The Five Great Treatises

格鲁派寺院学院的核心课程：

The core curriculum of Gelug monastic colleges:

| 论著 Treatise | 作者 Author | 主题 Subject | 学习年限 Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| 《现观庄严论》 *Abhisamayālaṃkāra* | 弥勒（传） Maitreya | 般若波罗蜜多的隐义 Hidden meaning of Prajñāpāramitā | 4–6年 years |
| 《入中论》 *Madhyamakāvatāra* | 月称 Candrakīrti | 中观哲学 Madhyamaka philosophy | 2–3年 years |

| 《戒律论》 *Vinayasūtra* | 功德光 Guṇaprabha | 佛教戒律 Buddhist monastic discipline | 2–3年 years |

### 4. 三大寺 / The Three Great Monasteries

| 寺院 Monastery | 创建 Founded | 特色 Features |
|---|---|---|
| 甘丹寺 dGa'-ldan | 1409（宗喀巴亲建） | 格鲁派祖寺，宗喀巴法座所在 |
| 哲蚌寺 'Bras-spungs | 1416（绛央曲杰建） | 曾是世界最大寺院，僧侣逾万 |

此后，扎什伦布寺（bKra-shis lhun-po，1447年一世达赖建）成为班禅喇嘛的驻锡地，也是格鲁派重要寺院之一。

Later, Tashilhunpo Monastery (bKra-shis lhun-po, founded 1447 by the First Dalai Lama) became the seat of the Panchen Lama and another major Gelug institution.

## 传承体系 / Lineage System

### 两大活佛传承 / Two Major Tulku Lineages

- **达赖喇嘛传承** Dalai Lama Lineage：始于根敦主巴（dGe-'dun grub-pa, 1391–1474），被追认为一世达赖。现任第十四世达赖喇嘛（b. 1935）是格鲁派的最高精神领袖。
- **班禅额尔德尼传承** Panchen Lama Lineage：始于克主杰（mKhas-grub rje, 1385–1438），被追认为一世班禅。班禅传统上被视为阿弥陀佛的化身。

### 甘丹赤巴 / Ganden Tripa

甘丹赤巴（dGa'-ldan Khri-pa）是格鲁派名义上的最高领袖——宗喀巴法座的持有者。与达赖喇嘛的活佛转世制度不同，甘丹赤巴是通过学术成就选出的——通常由在辩经和学术方面最杰出的格西担任。

The Ganden Tripa (dGa'-ldan Khri-pa) is the nominal head of the Gelug school—the holder of Tsongkhapa's throne. Unlike the Dalai Lama's reincarnation system, the Ganden Tripa is selected through academic merit—typically the most distinguished geshe in debate and scholarship.

## 格鲁派与其他宗派的关系 / Gelug Relations with Other Schools

### 整合而非排斥 / Integration, Not Exclusion

尽管格鲁派常被误解为"排斥他派"，宗喀巴的思想本质是整合性的：

Although the Gelug school is often misunderstood as "excluding other schools," Tsongkhapa's thought is essentially integrative:

- **从萨迦派**继承：仁达瓦的中观教学，尤其是月称的注释传统
- **From Sakya**: Rendawa's Madhyamaka teaching, especially the Candrakīrti commentary tradition
- **从噶当派**继承：阿底峡的菩提道次第体系与心训练（Lojong）传统
- **From Kadam**: Atiśa's lamrim system and mind-training (Lojong) tradition
- **从噶举派**继承：大手印的禅修方法（特别是那洛六法）
- **From Kagyu**: Mahāmudrā meditation methods (especially the Six Dharmas of Nāropa)
- **从宁玛派**了解：大圆满的教义（虽未采纳为其核心体系）
- **From Nyingma**: Awareness of Dzogchen teachings (though not adopted as core)

### 格鲁派的"严谨" Gelug "Rigor"

格鲁派的严谨不是教条主义的僵化，而是对修行次第的严格坚持——这恰恰是宗喀巴改革精神的体现：

Gelug rigor is not dogmatic rigidity but strict adherence to practice stages—precisely embodying Tsongkhapa's reform spirit:

1. 先学后修 Study before practice
2. 先显后密 Sutra before tantra
3. 先戒后定 Discipline before concentration
4. 先定后慧 Concentration before wisdom

## 当代发展 / Contemporary Developments

- **流亡后的重建** Post-exile Rebuilding：1959年后，格鲁派三大寺在印度南部重建（卡纳塔克邦），保持了完整的教育体系。
- **与科学的对话** Dialogue with Science：在第十四世达赖喇嘛的推动下，格鲁派寺院开始引入科学教育（物理、生物、神经科学）。
- **西方传播** Western Transmission：格鲁派是藏传佛教在西方传播最广泛的宗派之一，建立了众多禅修中心与研究机构。
- **女性出家众** Female Ordination：当代格鲁派内部正在讨论恢复比丘尼戒（bhikṣuṇī-saṃvara）的问题。

## 认知意义 / Cognitive Significance

格鲁派可以被理解为一个**认知训练的大型制度框架**：

The Gelug school can be understood as a **large-scale institutional framework for cognitive training**:

- **辩经 = 批判性思维训练** Debate = Critical Thinking Training：在严格的逻辑框架内进行对抗性论证。
- **五部大论 = 多学科认知课程** Five Treatises = Multidisciplinary Cognitive Curriculum：涵盖哲学、逻辑学、心理学、伦理学和修行方法论。
- **格西考试 = 认知能力的标准化评估** Geshe Examination = Standardized Assessment of Cognitive Competence：通过公开的辩经考试验证认知转化是否真实发生。
