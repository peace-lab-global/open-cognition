# 五蕴 / The Five Aggregates

> "色如聚沫，受如水泡，想如阳焰，行如芭蕉，识如幻术。"
> "Form is like a lump of foam, feeling like a bubble, perception like a mirage, formations like a plantain trunk, consciousness like a magic trick."
> — *Pheṇapiṇḍūpama Sutta*, SN 22.95

---

## 一句话定义 / One-Line Definition

**五蕴是乔达摩对"人"的存在分析——将所谓的"自我"解构为五个不断变化的心理-物理过程（色、受、想、行、识），论证其中不存在恒常、独立、统一的实体自我。**

**The five aggregates are Gautama's existential analysis of the "person" — deconstructing the so-called "self" into five constantly changing psycho-physical processes (form, feeling, perception, formations, consciousness), arguing that no permanent, independent, unified substantial self exists within them.**

## 概述 / Overview

五蕴（pañcakkhandha / pañcaskandha）是乔达摩用来分析"人是什么"的基本框架。在古印度思想中，婆罗门教主张存在一个恒常的"阿特曼"（ātman，真我），是个体轮回和认识梵（Brahman）的主体。乔达摩的五蕴分析从根本上否定了这一实体自我的存在：当我们将"人"分解为色、受、想、行、识五个过程时，在任何一层中都找不到那个恒常的、独立的"我"。

The five aggregates are Gautama's basic framework for analysing "what a person is." In ancient Indian thought, Brahmanism posited a permanent "Ātman" (true self) as the subject of individual rebirth and the knower of Brahman. Gautama's five-aggregate analysis fundamentally denied the existence of this substantial self: when we decompose the "person" into form, feeling, perception, formations, and consciousness, no permanent, independent "I" can be found at any level.

## 五蕴详析 / Detailed Analysis

### 1. 色蕴（Rūpa） / Form

色蕴涵盖一切物质性存在：四大（mahābhūta）——地（坚固性）、水（粘着性）、火（温度性）、风（运动性）——以及由四大所造的物质现象（upādā-rūpa），包括五种感官器官（眼、耳、鼻、舌、身）和五种感官对象（色、声、香、味、触）。

Form encompasses all material existence: the four great elements (mahābhūta) — earth (solidity), water (cohesion), fire (temperature), air (movement) — and the material phenomena derived from them (upādā-rūpa), including the five sense organs (eye, ear, nose, tongue, body) and five sense objects (visible form, sound, smell, taste, tangible).

### 2. 受蕴（Vedanā） / Feeling

受蕴是对经验的情感色调的感受——不是"情绪"（emotion），而是更基本的"感受基调"（hedonic tone）。分为三类：

Feeling is the affective tone of experience — not "emotion" in the modern sense, but the more basic "hedonic tone." Threefold classification:

- **乐受**（sukha vedanā）：愉悦的感受
- **苦受**（dukkha vedanā）：不愉悦的感受
- **不苦不乐受**（adukkhamasukha vedanā）：中性的感受

受蕴是缘起链中关键的转化节点：在"触→受→爱"的序列中，受是渴爱生起的直接条件。正念修行中对受的觉知（受念处）被设计为截断从感受到渴爱的自动反应链。

Feeling is a critical transformation node in the chain of dependent origination: in the sequence "contact → feeling → craving," feeling is the direct condition for the arising of craving. Mindfulness practice's awareness of feeling (vedanānupassanā) is designed to interrupt the automatic reactive chain from feeling to craving.

### 3. 想蕴（Saññā / Saṃjñā） / Perception

想蕴是认知的"标签化"功能——对感官输入进行识别、分类和命名。它是"这是蓝色的""那是男人的声音"等概念化认知的基础。想蕴的运作方式是通过过去的经验和学习所建立的"概念库"（saññā-vipallāsa）来对当前经验进行归类。

Perception is the "labelling" function of cognition — identifying, classifying, and naming sensory input. It is the basis of conceptualised cognition such as "this is blue" or "that is a man's voice." Perception operates through a "concept bank" (saññā-vipallāsa) established by past experience and learning, classifying present experience accordingly.

### 4. 行蕴（Saṅkhāra / Saṃskāra） / Formations

行蕴是五蕴中**最复杂**和**最广泛**的蕴，涵盖一切有意图的心理造作——包括意志（cetanā）、思维、判断、决定、习惯倾向、情感反应等。在阿毗达磨中，行蕴包含了52种心所（cetasika），除受和想之外的所有心理因素都属于行蕴。

Formations is the **most complex** and **most extensive** aggregate, encompassing all intentional mental activities — including volition (cetanā), thinking, judging, deciding, habitual tendencies, emotional reactions, etc. In the Abhidhamma, formations encompasses 52 mental factors (cetasikas) — all psychological factors except feeling and perception.

行蕴之所以特别重要，是因为它是**业**（kamma / karma）的所在。乔达摩明确说："我说意志就是业。有意志，故通过身语意造业。"（AN 6.63）行蕴中的意志活动是创造业力的直接来源。

Formations is particularly important because it is the locus of **kamma** (karma). Gautama explicitly stated: "Volition, I say, is kamma. Having willed, one acts through body, speech, and mind." (AN 6.63) The volitional activities within formations are the direct source of karmic creation.

### 5. 识蕴（Viññāṇa / Vijñāna） / Consciousness

识蕴是纯粹的觉知——对感官对象的意识。分为六类：眼识、耳识、鼻识、舌识、身识、意识。识蕴不是"自我"或"灵魂"，而是依赖于感官和对象而生起的条件性过程——"无眼无色则无眼识"。

Consciousness is bare awareness — consciousness of sense objects. Sixfold: eye-consciousness, ear-consciousness, nose-consciousness, tongue-consciousness, body-consciousness, mind-consciousness. Consciousness is not a "self" or "soul" but a conditional process dependent on sense organ and object — "without eye and visible form, there is no eye-consciousness."

## 五蕴与无我 / Aggregates and Non-Self

五蕴分析的最终目的是论证**无我**（anattā）。乔达摩在《无我相经》（*Anattalakkhaṇa Sutta*, SN 22.59）中的论证结构是：

The ultimate purpose of the five-aggregate analysis is to demonstrate **non-self** (anattā). Gautama's argumentative structure in the *Anattalakkhaṇa Sutta* (SN 22.59) is:

1. 五蕴中的每一蕴都是**无常**的（anicca）
2. 凡是无常的都是**苦**的（dukkha）
3. 凡是无常、苦、变化法的东西，能否合理地说"这是我的，这是我，这是我的自我"？
4. 答案显然是否定的

结论：五蕴中的每一蕴都应当以正慧如实观照为"此非我所（netaṃ mama），此非我（nesohamasmi），此非我之自我（na meso attā）。"

Conclusion: each of the five aggregates should be regarded with right wisdom as "This is not mine, this I am not, this is not my self."

## 与当代心灵哲学的对话 / Dialogue with Contemporary Philosophy of Mind

五蕴分析与当代心灵哲学形成了精确的结构平行：

| 五蕴 | 当代心灵哲学对应 | 关系 |
|------|-----------------|------|
| 色蕴 | 物理主义/身体 | 物质基础 |
| 受蕴 | 感受质（qualia） | 情感色调 |
| 想蕴 | 概念/范畴化 | 认知标签 |
| 行蕴 | 意志/意向性 | 心理造作 |
| 识蕴 | 意识/觉知 | 条件性觉知 |

Thomas Metzinger 在 *Being No One* (2003) 中提出的"自我模型"（self-model）理论——自我不是实体而是一个透明的表征过程——与五蕴无我分析形成了精确的当代呼应。Antonio Damasio 的"自传性自我"理论——自我是由大脑不断叙事建构的产物——同样与五蕴分析高度一致。

Thomas Metzinger's "self-model" theory in *Being No One* (2003) — that the self is not an entity but a transparent representational process — forms a precise contemporary echo of the five-aggregate non-self analysis. Antonio Damasio's "autobiographical self" theory — that the self is a narrative construct continuously built by the brain — similarly aligns closely with the aggregate analysis.

## 常见误读 / Common Misunderstandings

### 误读一："五蕴就是身心二元论"

**纠正**：五蕴不是"身"（色）和"心"（受想行识）的二元划分。色蕴不仅包括身体，也包括外部物质世界；而受想行识四蕴之间的关系也不是简单的"心"——它们是不同的心理过程，各有其独特的功能和运作方式。五蕴分析的精确性远超简单的身心二元论。

### 误读二："行蕴 = 思想"

**纠正**：行蕴（saṅkhāra）不是一个简单的"思想"概念。它涵盖了从意志、决策到习惯倾向、情绪反应的一切心理造作。将行蕴简化为"思想"是对这一最复杂蕴的严重简化。行蕴中的核心因素——意志（cetanā）——才是业（kamma）的所在。
