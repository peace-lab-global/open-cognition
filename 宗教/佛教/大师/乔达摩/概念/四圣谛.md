# 四圣谛 / The Four Noble Truths

> "诸比丘，何为四圣谛？苦圣谛、苦集圣谛、苦灭圣谛、苦灭道圣谛。"
> "And what, monks, are the Four Noble Truths? The noble truth of suffering, the noble truth of the origin of suffering, the noble truth of the cessation of suffering, the noble truth of the way leading to the cessation of suffering."
> — *Dhammacakkappavattana Sutta*, SN 56.11

---

## 一句话定义 / One-Line Definition

**四圣谛是乔达摩对苦的存在论诊断与解脱论处方——苦（duḥkha）、集（samudāya）、灭（nirodha）、道（mārga）——构成佛教全部教义的总纲和逻辑骨架。**

**The Four Noble Truths are Gautama's ontological diagnosis and soteriological prescription regarding suffering — suffering (duḥkha), origination (samudāya), cessation (nirodha), path (mārga) — constituting the master-framework and logical skeleton of all Buddhist doctrine.**

## 概述 / Overview

四圣谛（cattāri ariyasaccāni）是乔达摩在鹿野苑初转法轮时首次系统宣说的核心教法，被视为佛教教义史的"第一声"。它在佛教中的地位类似于几何学的公理——整个佛教思想体系都围绕这四个命题展开。四圣谛的独特之处在于它不是一个神学命题（不依赖神的启示），而是一个经验性的诊断-治疗框架，模仿了古印度医学的四步诊断法：确认病症、找出病因、判断可治愈性、开具处方。

The Four Noble Truths were Gautama's first systematic teaching, delivered at the Deer Park in his inaugural discourse, and are regarded as the "first utterance" of Buddhist doctrinal history. Their status in Buddhism is analogous to axioms in geometry — the entire Buddhist doctrinal system unfolds around these four propositions. The uniqueness of the Four Noble Truths lies in their being not a theological claim (independent of divine revelation) but an empirical diagnostic-therapeutic framework, mirroring the four-step diagnostic method of ancient Indian medicine: confirm the disease, identify the cause, assess curability, prescribe treatment.

## 四谛详析 / Detailed Analysis

### 一、苦谛（Dukkha-sacca） / The Truth of Suffering

苦谛不是"一切都是痛苦的"这种简单化的悲观论断，而是对存在结构的精确分析。乔达摩在《转法轮经》中列举了苦的多种形态：

The truth of suffering is not the simplistic pessimistic assertion that "everything is painful," but a precise analysis of the structure of existence. In the *Dhammacakkappavattana Sutta*, Gautama enumerated multiple forms of suffering:

"生是苦，老是苦，病是苦，死是苦；怨憎会是苦，爱别离是苦，求不得是苦；总之，五取蕴是苦。"

"Birth is suffering, aging is suffering, illness is suffering, death is suffering; association with the disagreeable is suffering, separation from the agreeable is suffering, not getting what one wants is suffering; in brief, the five aggregates subject to clinging are suffering."

最后一句——"五取蕴是苦"（pañcupādānakkhandhā dukkhā）——是苦谛的哲学核心。它不是说"五蕴本身是苦的"，而是说**被执取（upādāna）所渗透的五蕴**构成了苦。这为理解苦的本质提供了精确的分析框架：苦不在事物本身，而在对事物的执取。

The final statement — "the five aggregates subject to clinging are suffering" (pañcupādānakkhandhā dukkhā) — is the philosophical core of the truth of suffering. It does not say "the five aggregates themselves are suffering" but that **the five aggregates permeated by clinging (upādāna)** constitute suffering. This provides a precise analytical framework for understanding suffering's nature: suffering lies not in things themselves, but in the clinging to things.

→ 详见 [苦](苦.md) 对三苦（苦苦、坏苦、行苦）的详细分析。

### 二、集谛（Samudāya-sacca） / The Truth of Origination

集谛诊断了苦的根源——**渴爱**（taṇhā / tṛṣṇā）："导致再生、伴随喜乐与贪欲、到处寻求满足的渴爱——即欲爱（kāma-taṇhā）、有爱（bhava-taṇhā）、无有爱（vibhava-taṇhā）。"

The truth of origination diagnoses suffering's root — **craving** (taṇhā / tṛṣṇā): "It is craving that leads to rebirth, accompanied by delight and lust, seeking delight here and there — namely, sensual craving (kāma-taṇhā), craving for existence (bhava-taṇhā), and craving for non-existence (vibhava-taṇhā)."

三种渴爱的区分具有深刻的哲学含义：
- **欲爱**（kāma-taṇhā）：对感官享受的渴望——最显而易见的执着形式
- **有爱**（bhava-taṇhā）：对存在和成为的渴望——驱动轮回的深层动力
- **无有爱**（vibhava-taṇhā）：对断灭和不存在的渴望——表现为自毁倾向或虚无主义

The distinction among the three types of craving has profound philosophical implications: **sensual craving** is the most obvious form of attachment; **craving for existence** is the deep drive perpetuating rebirth; **craving for non-existence** manifests as self-destructive tendencies or nihilism.

需要注意的是，乔达摩将渴爱——而非某种外在的恶力或神的惩罚——定位为苦的根本原因。这一内在化的病因学使得解脱成为可以通过自身努力实现的目标。

Importantly, Gautama identified craving — not some external evil force or divine punishment — as the root cause of suffering. This internalised etiology makes liberation an achievable goal through one's own effort.

### 三、灭谛（Nirodā-sacca） / The Truth of Cessation

灭谛是四圣谛中最具解放性的命题：苦**可以被彻底终止**。"对那种渴爱的无余离贪、灭尽、舍弃、放下、解脱、无执。"（SN 56.11）

The truth of cessation is the most liberating proposition of the Four Noble Truths: suffering **can be completely terminated**. "The remainderless fading away and cessation of that same craving, the giving up and relinquishing of it, freedom from it, non-reliance on it." (SN 56.11)

灭谛的关键哲学贡献在于它断言了**可治愈性**：苦不是存在的必然属性，而是可以被消除的条件性产物。这一立场既反对宿命论（苦不可避免），也反对虚无主义（灭苦即消灭一切存在）。灭谛所指向的状态就是涅槃（nibbāna）——渴爱的彻底熄灭。

The key philosophical contribution of the truth of cessation is its assertion of **curability**: suffering is not an inevitable property of existence but a conditional product that can be eliminated. This position opposes both fatalism (suffering is inevitable) and nihilism (cessation of suffering means annihilation of all existence). The state pointed to by the truth of cessation is Nirvana (nibbāna) — the complete extinguishing of craving.

→ 详见 [涅槃](涅槃.md)。

### 四、道谛（Magga-sacca） / The Truth of the Path

道谛指明了通往灭苦的系统修行路径——**八正道**（ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo），被组织为三学：

The truth of the path points to the systematic practice path leading to cessation — the **Noble Eightfold Path** (ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo), organised into three trainings:

| 学 | 巴利 | 道支 | 英文 |
|----|------|------|------|
| 慧学 (Paññā) | paññā | 正见、正思维 | Right View, Right Intention |
| 戒学 (Sīla) | sīla | 正语、正业、正命 | Right Speech, Right Action, Right Livelihood |
| 定学 (Samādhi) | samādhi | 正精进、正念、正定 | Right Effort, Right Mindfulness, Right Concentration |

→ 详见 [八正道](八正道.md)。

## 四谛的逻辑结构 / Logical Structure of the Four Truths

四圣谛不是四个并列的命题，而是一个严密的**因果链条**和**治疗程序**：

The Four Noble Truths are not four parallel propositions but a rigorous **causal chain** and **therapeutic programme**:

| 步骤 | 诊断 | 治疗 | 功能 |
|------|------|------|------|
| 苦谛 | 确认病症 | — | 应遍知（pariññeyya） |
| 集谛 | 找出病因 | — | 应断除（pahātabba） |
| 灭谛 | — | 确认治愈 | 应证得（sacchikātabba） |
| 道谛 | — | 开具处方 | 应修习（bhāvetabba） |

每一个圣谛都有一个"应做之事"（kicca）：苦应被完全认识（遍知），集应被断除，灭应被亲证，道应被修习。四圣谛的完全了悟意味着四种应做之事全部完成——这就是阿罗汉的状态。

Each truth has a "task to be done" (kicca): suffering should be fully understood, origination should be abandoned, cessation should be realised, and the path should be developed. Complete penetration of the Four Noble Truths means all four tasks are fulfilled — this is the state of the arahant.

## 部派分歧与学术争议 / Sectarian Disputes and Scholarly Debate

### 现观次第的争论

部派佛教时期，各派对四圣谛的"现观"（abhisamaya，直觉性证悟）的次第存在根本分歧：

During the sectarian period, schools fundamentally disagreed on the sequence of "penetration" (abhisamaya, intuitive realisation) of the Four Noble Truths:

- **说一切有部**（Sarvāstivāda）：主张**渐次现观**——按苦→集→灭→道的顺序逐一证悟，需要十五刹那
- **上座部**（Theravāda）：主张**一时现观**——在一个心路过程中同时证悟四谛，如四束灯同时照亮

This sectarian disagreement has parallels in the later Chan debates over sudden vs gradual enlightenment.

### "苦"的范围争议

学术界对苦谛的范围也有争议：乔达摩是在做一种普遍性的存在论断言（"一切存在皆苦"），还是在做一种条件性的诊断（"在渴爱驱动下的存在皆苦"）？后一种解读得到越来越多学者的支持，因为它与灭谛（苦可以被消除）更加逻辑一致。

Scholars debate whether Gautama made a universal ontological claim ("all existence is suffering") or a conditional diagnosis ("existence driven by craving is suffering"). The latter reading has gained increasing scholarly support, as it is more logically consistent with the truth of cessation (suffering can be eliminated).

## 常见误读 / Common Misunderstandings

### 误读一："四圣谛是消极的"

**纠正**：四圣谛的第一谛是苦，但第三谛是灭——苦可以被彻底消除。整个框架的指向是解脱而非绝望。将四圣谛等同于悲观主义是只看了前半部分而忽略了后半部分。

### 误读二："佛教说要消灭所有欲望"

**纠正**：乔达摩要消灭的是**渴爱**（taṇhā）——一种不健康的、强迫性的、以无明为基础的执着。佛教并不反对所有形式的愿望——事实上，追求觉悟的愿望（chanda，善法欲）被视为修行的必要动力。区分健康的意向（chanda）和不健康的渴爱（taṇhā）是理解佛教伦理的关键。

### 误读三："灭谛就是死后上天堂"

**纠正**：灭谛所指向的涅槃不是任何意义上的"天堂"或"彼岸世界"。涅槃是渴爱的彻底熄灭，它可以在今生中证得（有余涅槃），而非死后才能达到的状态。将涅槃等同于天堂是对佛教解脱论的基督教化误读。
