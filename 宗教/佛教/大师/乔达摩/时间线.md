# 乔达摩年表 / Gautama Buddha Timeline

> **年代争议 / Dating Controversy**：传统南传上座部年代学将乔达摩的生卒年定为约公元前624–前544年。然而，现代学术界普遍接受的"修正年代"（corrected long chronology）约为公元前563–前483年；另有以Richard Gombrich为代表的"短年代"（short chronology）主张约公元前480–前400年。本表采用传统学术界最广泛使用的约公元前563–前483年。
> **Dating Controversy**: Traditional Theravāda chronology places Gautama's dates at c. 624–544 BCE. However, the "corrected long chronology" widely accepted in modern scholarship places his dates at c. 563–483 BCE; an alternative "short chronology" advocated by Richard Gombrich and others places them at c. 480–400 BCE. This timeline uses the most widely cited scholarly dating of c. 563–483 BCE.

## 求道时期 / Period of Spiritual Seeking

| 年代 | 事件 | 来源 / Source |
|------|------|--------------|
| 约前534–前533年 | 师从阿罗逻·迦蓝（Āḷāra Kālāma），修习无所有处定（ākiñcaññāyatana）。虽达到师父的水平，但发现此定不能导向解脱。 | MN 26 |
| c. 534–533 BCE | Studied under Āḷāra Kālāma, attaining the base of nothingness (ākiñcaññāyatana). Though reaching the master's level, found this attainment did not lead to liberation. | |
| 约前533–前532年 | 师从郁陀迦·罗摩子（Uddaka Rāmaputta），修习非想非非想处定（nevasaññānāsaññāyatana）。同样发现不足。 | MN 26 |
| c. 533–532 BCE | Studied under Uddaka Rāmaputta, attaining the base of neither-perception-nor-non-perception. Similarly found it insufficient. | |
| 约前532–前528年 | 在尼连禅河（Nerañjarā）畔进行六年极端苦行。日食一麻一麦，身体极度消瘦，但未获得任何超人的智慧或觉悟。 | MN 36 *Mahāsaccaka Sutta*; MN 12 |
| c. 532–528 BCE | Practised extreme asceticism for six years on the banks of the Nerañjarā. Eating one grain of sesame and one grain of rice per day, emaciating the body to the extreme, yet gaining no superhuman knowledge or awakening. | |
| 约前528年 | 放弃极端苦行，接受牧女苏嘉达（Sujātā）的乳粥供养。五位苦行同伴因此离去。 | MN 36; *Mahāvagga* |
| c. 528 BCE | Abandoned extreme asceticism, accepted milk-rice offering from Sujātā. The five ascetic companions departed. | |

## 觉悟与初转法轮 / Awakening and First Teaching

| 年代 | 事件 | 来源 / Source |
|------|------|--------------|
| 约前528年（35岁） | 在伽耶（今菩提伽耶 Bodh Gayā）的菩提树（Aśvattha / Ficus religiosa）下静坐冥想。夜间依次证得三明（tevijja）：宿命明、天眼明、漏尽明。于黎明时分证得无上正等正觉。 | MN 36; DN 14; Vin *Mahāvagga* |
| c. 528 BCE (age 35) | Seated in meditation under the Bodhi tree at Gayā (present-day Bodh Gayā). During the night, attained the Three Knowledges (tevijja): knowledge of past lives, divine eye, and knowledge of the destruction of the taints. Attained unsurpassed perfect awakening at dawn. | |
| 约前528年（觉悟后七周） | 在菩提树附近度过七周。梵天（Brahmā Sahampati）请佛转法轮。 | Vin *Mahāvagga*; SN 6.1 |
| c. 528 BCE (seven weeks after awakening) | Spent seven weeks near the Bodhi tree. Brahmā Sahampati invited the Buddha to teach. | |
| 约前528年 | 在波罗奈斯（Bārāṇasī）鹿野苑（Isipatana/Mṛgadāva）向五位旧同伴宣说《转法轮经》（*Dhammacakkappavattana Sutta*, SN 56.11），首次阐述四圣谛与中道。憍陈如（Koṇḍañña）成为第一位证得法眼净（dhammacakkhu）的弟子。佛教僧伽（Saṃgha）由此诞生。 | SN 56.11; Vin *Mahāvagga* |
| c. 528 BCE | At the Deer Park in Isipatana near Bārāṇasī, delivered the *Dhammacakkappavattana Sutta* (SN 56.11) to the five former companions, expounding the Four Noble Truths and Middle Way for the first time. Koṇḍañña became the first disciple to attain the "eye of Dharma" (dhammacakkhu). The Buddhist Saṃgha was born. | |

## 弘法时期 / Teaching Period

| 年代 | 事件 | 来源 / Source |
|------|------|--------------|
| 约前528–前527年 | 宣说《无我相经》（*Anattalakkhaṇa Sutta*, SN 22.59），五位弟子全部证得阿罗汉果。僧伽初具规模。 | SN 22.59; Vin *Mahāvagga* |
| c. 528–527 BCE | Delivered the *Anattalakkhaṇa Sutta* (SN 22.59); all five disciples attained arahantship. The Saṃgha took initial shape. | |
| 约前527年 | 在王舍城（Rājagaha）度化舍利弗（Sāriputta）和目犍连（Moggallāna），二人成为佛陀最重要的两大弟子。频毗沙罗王（Bimbisāra）皈依，赠竹林精舍（Veḷuvana）。 | Vin *Mahāvagga* |
| c. 527 BCE | Converted Sāriputta and Moggallāna in Rājagaha; they became the Buddha's two chief disciples. King Bimbisāra converted and donated the Veḷuvana (Bamboo Grove) monastery. | |
| 约前527–前490年 | 四十五年间行走于摩揭陀（Magadha）、憍萨罗（Kosala）、吠舍离（Vesālī）等十六大国之间，每年雨季安居（vassa），其余时间游行教化。 | Vin; DN 16 |
| c. 527–490 BCE | For forty-five years, walked among the sixteen great kingdoms including Magadha, Kosala, and Vesālī, observing the annual rain-retreat (vassa) and wandering to teach during the rest of the year. | |
| 约前510年 | 给孤独长者（Anāthapiṇḍika）在舍卫城（Sāvatthī）购建祇园精舍（Jetavana），成为佛陀最常居住的安居处。大量经典在此宣讲。 | Vin *Cullavagga* |
| c. 510 BCE | The householder Anāthapiṇḍika purchased and built the Jetavana monastery in Sāvatthī, which became the Buddha's most frequented residence. A large number of discourses were delivered here. | |
| 约前500年 | 回迦毗罗卫城探亲，为父亲和族人说法。子罗睺罗出家。此后多次回乡。 | Vin *Mahāvagga*; Sn |
| c. 500 BCE | Returned to Kapilavastu to visit family, teaching his father and clansmen. Son Rāhula ordained. Returned home multiple times thereafter. | |
| 约前500–前490年 | 重要弟子陆续加入：阿难（Ānanda，佛陀的侍者）、优波离（Upāli，律学专家）、阿那律（Anuruddha）等。 | Vin; AN |
| c. 500–490 BCE | Important disciples joined successively: Ānanda (the Buddha's attendant), Upāli (Vinaya expert), Anuruddha, and others. | |
| 约前490年 | 舍利弗和目犍连相继去世。佛陀教导"以自己为岛屿"（attadīpā viharatha）。 | SN 47.13-14; DN 16 |
| c. 490 BCE | Sāriputta and Moggallāna passed away in succession. The Buddha taught "Dwell as islands unto yourselves." | |

## 般涅槃 / Parinibbāna

| 年代 | 事件 | 来源 / Source |
|------|------|--------------|
| 约前483年（80岁） | 最后一次雨季安居于吠舍离（Vesālī）附近的贝卢瓦村（Veḷuva）。 | DN 16 |
| c. 483 BCE (age 80) | Final rain-retreat at the village of Veḷuva near Vesālī. | |
| 约前483年 | 从吠舍离出发，经过波吒厘村（Pāṭaligāma）、拘利村（Koṭigāma）、那陀迦（Nādikā），最终到达波婆城（Pāvā）。在铁匠纯陀（Cunda）家中应供后身体不适。 | DN 16 |
| c. 483 BCE | Departed from Vesālī, passing through Pāṭaligāma, Koṭigāma, Nādika, and finally reaching Pāvā. Fell ill after accepting a meal at the smith Cunda's house. | |
| 约前483年 | 抵达拘尸那迦（Kusinārā），在沙罗双树（yamaka-sāla）间右胁而卧。对弟子做最后教诲："一切有为法皆是灭法，当自精进，不放逸"（vayadhammā saṅkhārā, appamādena sampādetha）。般涅槃（parinibbāna）。 | DN 16 *Mahāparinibbāna Sutta* |
| c. 483 BCE | Arrived at Kusinārā, lying on his right side between twin sāla trees. Delivered his final instruction: "All conditioned things are subject to decay. Strive on with diligence." Attained parinibbāna. | |
| 约前483年（涅槃后不久） | 大迦叶（Mahākassapa）主持第一次经典结集（Paṭhama Saṅgīti），五百阿罗汉在王舍城集结经藏和律藏。阿难诵出经藏，优波离诵出律藏。 | Vin *Cullavagga* XI |
| c. 483 BCE (shortly after parinibbāna) | Mahākassapa presided over the First Council; five hundred arahants assembled the Sutta and Vinaya collections at Rājagaha. Ānanda recited the Suttas; Upāli recited the Vinaya. | |
