# 道元著作列表 / Works of Dōgen

## 主要著作 / Major Works

### 《正法眼藏》 *Shōbōgenzō* (1231-1253)

道元最重要的著作，共95卷（另有多卷本系统）。涵盖了道元思想的全部维度：存在论（有时、佛性）、修行论（坐禅、修证一等）、语言哲学（现成公案）、伦理学（因果、道德规范）。每一卷都是一个独立的哲学论文，但共同构成了一个完整的思想体系。

Dōgen's most important work, comprising 95 fascicles (with multiple textual traditions). Covers the full spectrum of his thought: ontology (*uji*, Buddha-nature), soteriology (zazen, practice-enlightenment identity), philosophy of language (*genjōkōan*), ethics (causality, moral norms). Each fascicle is an independent philosophical essay, together forming a complete system.

**重要卷帙 / Key fascicles**:
- 《现成公案》 *Genjōkōan* — 全卷的纲领性文本 / Programmatic text for the whole work
- 《有时》 *Uji* — 时间-存在论 / Being-time ontology
- 《佛性》 *Bushō* — 佛性的重新诠释 / Reinterpretation of Buddha-nature
- 《坐禅箴》 *Zazen-shin* — 坐禅的心法 / The mind-method of zazen
- 《因果》 *Shinjin Gaku* — 深信因果 / Deep faith in causality
- 《山水经》 *Sansuikyō* — 自然的哲学 / Philosophy of nature

### 《普劝坐禅仪》 *Fukan Zazengi* (1227)

道元从宋归日后撰写的第一部重要文本。是坐禅的通用指导手册，涵盖坐姿、呼吸、心态、环境等方面。文字精炼，至今仍是曹洞宗寺院每日诵读的文本。

The first major text Dōgen wrote after returning from Song China. A universal manual for zazen covering posture, breathing, attitude, and environment. Concisely written, it remains a daily recitation text in Sōtō monasteries today.

### 《永平清规》 *Eihei Shingi* (1246)

永平寺的寺院生活规范，包括日常作息、饮食规范、礼仪制度等。体现了道元将哲学贯彻于日常生活的努力——每一刻的每一个行为都是修行。

Monastic regulations for Eihei-ji, including daily schedule, dietary norms, ceremonial protocols. Embodies Dōgen's effort to embody philosophy in daily life — every action in every moment is practice.

### 《办道法》 *Bendōhō* (1246)

修行的入门指导，解释修行的心态、方法和目标。"办道"意为"致力于佛道"——全身心地投入修行生活。

Introductory guidance for practice, explaining the attitude, methods, and aims of training. *Bendō* means "devoting oneself to the Buddha-Way" — wholehearted engagement with the practice life.

## 文本传承 / Textual Transmission

道元著作的文本传承复杂。《正法眼藏》存在多个版本系统：75卷本（最早的编辑）、12卷本（晚期编辑）、95卷本（通行本）。学者们对不同版本的编年、真伪和思想发展仍有争论。

The textual transmission of Dōgen's works is complex. *Shōbōgenzō* exists in multiple versions: the 75-fascicle edition (earliest compilation), the 12-fascicle edition (later compilation), and the 95-fascicle edition (standard). Scholars continue to debate the chronology, authenticity, and intellectual development reflected in different versions.
