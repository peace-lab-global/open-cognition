# 佛性 Busho (Buddha-Nature in Dōgen)

> "一切众生悉有佛性"——《涅槃经》
> 道元读为："悉有（一切存在）即佛性。"
>
> "All sentient beings possess Buddha-nature." — *Nirvana Sutra*
> Dōgen reads it as: "All being (*shitsu-u*) is Buddha-nature."

## 概念概述 / Overview

"佛性"（bushō）是佛教思想中最核心也最具争议的概念之一。传统理解是：一切众生内在具足成佛的潜能——一种类似于"种子"的存在。道元对佛性的诠释彻底颠覆了这一理解：佛性不是众生内在的某种"东西"，而是存在本身。

*Buddha-nature* (*bushō*) is one of the most central and contested concepts in Buddhist thought. The traditional understanding holds that all sentient beings possess an inner potential for awakening — a kind of "seed." Dōgen's interpretation radically overturns this: Buddha-nature is not something "inside" beings; it is existence itself.

## 历史争论 / Historical Debate

"佛性"概念在佛教史上经历了复杂的演变：

The concept of Buddha-nature underwent complex evolution in Buddhist history:

- **印度佛教 Indian Buddhism**：《涅槃经》提出"一切众生悉有佛性"，但关于"一阐提"（断善根者）是否有佛性存在争论。
  The *Nirvana Sutra* proposes "all sentient beings possess Buddha-nature," but debates persisted about whether *icchantikas* (those who have severed their roots of goodness) possess it.
- **中国佛教 Chinese Buddhism**：天台宗、华严宗发展了"一切皆有佛性"的普遍论，禅宗（特别是六祖慧能）将佛性与"自性"等同。但关于"无情"是否有佛性仍有争论——南阳慧忠主张"无情说法"，而大珠慧海则持保留态度。
  Tiantai and Huayan schools developed the universalist claim "all possess Buddha-nature." Chan Buddhism (especially the Sixth Patriarch Huineng) equated Buddha-nature with "self-nature." But debates continued about whether insentient beings possess Buddha-nature — Nanyang Huizhong argued "insentient beings preach the dharma," while Dazhu Huahai remained reserved.
- **日本佛教 Japanese Buddhism**：天台宗的"本觉思想"将佛性理解为众生内在的、现成的觉醒状态，但这导致了修行必要性的危机。
  Tendai *hongaku shisō* understood Buddha-nature as an innate, ready-made awakened state within beings, but this created a crisis regarding the necessity of practice.

道元在这一复杂的思想史背景下，提出了他独特的佛性诠释。

Dōgen proposed his distinctive interpretation of Buddha-nature against this complex intellectual-historical background.

## 核心论证 / Core Argument

### 语法翻转 / Grammatical Reversal

道元对佛性的独特诠释始于对《涅槃经》名句"一切众生悉有佛性"的语法重读。传统读法将"悉有"（shitsu-u）理解为"全都拥有"——所有众生都拥有佛性。道元将"悉有"重新读为名词——"一切存在"，于是这句话变成了："一切存在即是佛性"（all being is Buddha-nature）。

Dōgen's distinctive interpretation begins with a grammatical re-reading of the *Nirvana Sutra*'s famous phrase. The conventional reading takes *shitsu-u* as "all possess" — all sentient beings possess Buddha-nature. Dōgen re-reads *shitsu-u* as a noun — "all being" — transforming the sentence into: "All being is Buddha-nature."

这一语法翻转的哲学后果是深远的：

The philosophical consequences of this grammatical reversal are profound:

- **佛性不是属性，而是存在本身**：佛性不是众生拥有的某种品质，而是存在的根本方式。
  Buddha-nature is not an attribute beings possess but the fundamental mode of existence.
- **无情也有佛性**：不仅是有情众生，草木瓦石、山河大地都是佛性。
  Not only sentient beings but grass, trees, tiles, stones, mountains, and rivers are all Buddha-nature.
- **佛性与时间**：佛性不是永恒不变的实体，而是每一刻的完整展现——与"有时"概念相通。
  Buddha-nature is not a permanent substance but the complete manifestation of each moment — connecting to *uji*.

### 佛性的时间性 / The Temporality of Buddha-Nature

在《佛性》卷中，道元进一步提出"佛性"的时间性维度。他引用百丈怀海的话："佛性不是固定的，它随着时间而变化。"道元将此推向极致：佛性不是永恒的"本质"，而是每一刻的生灭——佛性就是时间性的存在本身。

In the *Bushō* fascicle, Dōgen pushes further into the temporal dimension of Buddha-nature. Citing Baizhang Huaihai: "Buddha-nature is not fixed; it changes with time." Dōgen takes this to the extreme: Buddha-nature is not an eternal "essence" but the arising and perishing of each moment — Buddha-nature is temporal existence itself.

### 无情佛性 / Insentient Buddha-Nature

传统佛教争论"无情"（草木、矿物等）是否有佛性。道元的立场是彻底的：一切存在——有情与无情——都是佛性。"草木成佛"不是隐喻，而是存在论事实。

Traditional Buddhism debated whether insentient beings (plants, minerals, etc.) possess Buddha-nature. Dōgen's stance is absolute: all existence — sentient and insentient — is Buddha-nature. "Grass and trees becoming Buddha" is not metaphor but ontological fact.

## 与传统的张力 / Tension with Tradition

道元的佛性诠释与多种传统理解形成了张力：

Dōgen's Buddha-nature interpretation creates tension with several traditional understandings:

| 传统理解 / Traditional | 道元的立场 / Dōgen's stance |
|---|---|
| 佛性是内在潜能 / Inner potential | 佛性是存在本身 / Existence itself |
| 佛性是永恒本质 / Eternal essence | 佛性是时间性的 / Temporal |
| 仅众生有佛性 / Only sentient beings | 万物皆佛性 / All phenomena |
| 佛性是修行目标 / Practice goal | 佛性是修行基础 / Practice ground |

## 佛性与修行 / Buddha-Nature and Practice

道元的佛性论不是纯理论——它直接关联到修行实践。如果佛性是存在本身（而非某种内在属性），那么修行就不是"发现"佛性的过程，而是佛性自身的活动。这正是"修证一等"的存在论基础：

Dōgen's Buddha-nature theory is not purely theoretical — it connects directly to practice. If Buddha-nature is existence itself (rather than an inner attribute), then practice is not a process of "discovering" Buddha-nature but Buddha-nature's own activity. This is the ontological foundation of *shushō-ittō*:

- **坐禅即佛性的表达**：坐禅不是修行者在"追求"佛性，而是佛性通过修行者的身体在表达自身。
  Zazen is not the practitioner "pursuing" Buddha-nature but Buddha-nature expressing itself through the practitioner's body.
- **日常生活即佛性**：不仅坐禅，日常生活中的每一个行为——吃饭、打扫、行住坐卧——都是佛性的表现。这解释了道元为何如此重视《永平清规》中的日常规范。
  Not only zazen but every action in daily life — eating, cleaning, walking, standing, sitting, lying — is Buddha-nature's expression. This explains why Dōgen placed such importance on the daily regulations in *Eihei Shingi*.
- **万物说法**：如果一切存在都是佛性，那么一切存在都在"说法"——山河大地、草木瓦石都在展现真理。修行者的任务不是去"获取"真理，而是倾听万物的说法。
  If all existence is Buddha-nature, then all existence is "preaching the dharma" — mountains, rivers, grass, trees, tiles, stones all manifest truth. The practitioner's task is not to "acquire" truth but to listen to all things' preaching.

## 常见误读 / Common Misreadings

- **"佛性 = 灵魂/自我"**：道元的佛性不是印度教的"阿特曼"(atman)或基督教的"灵魂"。佛性不是一个永恒的、不变的实体——恰恰相反，道元将佛性与无常等同。
  Dōgen's Buddha-nature is not the Hindu *atman* or the Christian "soul." Buddha-nature is not an eternal, unchanging substance — quite the opposite, Dōgen equates Buddha-nature with impermanence.
- **"一切皆佛性 = 泛神论"**：道元的立场不是泛神论（pantheism），因为泛神论预设了一个超越的"神"与世界的等同。道元的佛性没有超越性/内在性的二元区分——佛性就是存在本身。
  Dōgen's stance is not pantheism, because pantheism presupposes the identity of a transcendent "God" with the world. Dōgen's Buddha-nature has no transcendent/immanent binary — Buddha-nature is existence itself.
- **"佛性是可以'拥有'的东西"**：道元明确反对将佛性理解为某种可以被"拥有"的属性或品质。佛性不是你有或没有的东西——你就是佛性，佛性就是你。
  Dōgen explicitly opposes understanding Buddha-nature as an attribute or quality that can be "possessed." Buddha-nature is not something you have or lack — you are Buddha-nature, Buddha-nature is you.

## 哲学意义 / Philosophical Significance

道元的佛性论具有深刻的哲学意义：

- **反本质主义 Anti-essentialism**：佛性不是隐藏的"真实自我"，而是现象世界的动态全体。
  Buddha-nature is not a hidden "true self" but the dynamic totality of the phenomenal world.
- **生态哲学 Ecology**：万物皆佛性的主张为深层生态学和生态整体论提供了存在论基础。
  The claim that all phenomena are Buddha-nature provides ontological grounding for deep ecology and ecological holism.
- **非人类中心 Non-anthropocentrism**：将佛性从有情众生推广到一切存在，打破了人类中心主义的框架。
  Extending Buddha-nature beyond sentient beings breaks the framework of anthropocentrism.
