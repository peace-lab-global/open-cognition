## 专题研究 / Topical Studies

### 坐禅研究 / Zazen Studies

- Carl Bielefeldt, *Dōgen's Manuals of Zen Meditation* (UC Press, 1988)
- Shunryū Suzuki, *Zen Mind, Beginner's Mind* (Weatherhill, 1970) — 虽非专门研究道元，但深受其影响 / Not specifically on Dōgen but deeply influenced

### 语言与诠释 / Language and Hermeneutics

- Steven Heine, *Dōgen and the Kōan Tradition* (Wisdom, 2014)
- Graham Parkes (ed.), *Dōgen and the Philosophy of Language* — 道元语言哲学的专题研究 / Topical study of Dōgen's philosophy of language

### 生态与环境 / Ecology and Environment

- David Barnhill (ed.), *Buddhism and Ecology* (Harvard CSWR, 1997) — 包含道元相关论文 / Includes Dōgen-related essays

## 在线资源 / Online Resources

- **Sōtō Zen Text Project** (Stanford) — 道元著作的学术翻译与研究 / Academic translations and studies
- **The Zen Site** — 多种道元文本的在线版本 / Online versions of various Dōgen texts
- **Bukkyō Dendō Kyōkai (BDK)** — 道元著作的免费 PDF 版本 / Free PDF versions of Dōgen's works

## 阅读建议 / Reading Suggestions

### 初学者路径 / Beginner's Path
1. Shohaku Okumura, *Realizing Genjōkōan*
2. 木村清孝《道元禅师的生涯与思想》
3. *Moon in a Dewdrop* (选读 / selected reading)
4. *Fukan Zazengi* 全文 / full text

### 进阶路径 / Advanced Path
1. Kim Hee-Jin, *Dōgen Kigen: Mystical Realist*
2. *Shōbōgenzō* 全译本 (Tanahashi) — 按主题选读 / by theme
3. Steven Heine, *The Philosophy of Dōgen*
4. 田中久《道元》

### 比较哲学路径 / Comparative Philosophy Path
1. Joan Stambaugh, *Existential and Ontological Dimensions of Time*
2. Steven Heine, *The Philosophy of Dōgen*
3. Jason Wirth, *The Conspiracy of Life* — 道元与怀特海 / Dōgen and Whitehead
