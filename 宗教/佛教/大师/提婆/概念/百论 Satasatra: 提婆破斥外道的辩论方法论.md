# 百论 Satasatra / The Hundred Treatise

## 定义 / Definition

**《百论》(Satasatra)** 是提婆最重要的辩论性著作，共 20 品，以对话体形式记录提婆与外道（非佛教哲学家）的辩论。此论仅存鸠摩罗什 (Kumarajiva, 404 CE) 的汉译本，无梵文原本或藏译传世。《百论》是汉传佛教**三论宗**的"三论"之一（另外两部是龙树的《中论》和《十二门论》）。

**The Satasatra (Hundred Treatise)** is Aryadeva's most important dialectical work, comprising 20 chapters in dialogue form, recording debates between Aryadeva and non-Buddhist philosophers. It survives only in Kumarajiva's Chinese translation (404 CE), with no extant Sanskrit original or Tibetan translation. The *Satasatra* is one of the "Three Treatises" (sanlun) of the Chinese Sanlun school, alongside Nagarjuna's *Mulamadhyamakakarika* and *Dvadasamukha-sastra*.

## 文本结构与内容 / Textual Structure and Content

《百论》的结构是一场精心设计的辩论序列，每一品针对外道的一个核心主张展开破斥：

The structure of the *Satasatra* is a carefully designed sequence of debates, each chapter targeting a core doctrine of non-Buddhist schools:

| 品 Chapter | 主题 Topic | 破斥对象 Target | 核心论证 Key Argument |
|------------|-----------|-----------------|----------------------|
| 1. 舍罪福品 | 善恶业的自性 | 外道业论 | 若业有自性，则不可消除 |
| 2. 破神品 | 灵魂/神我的存在 | 数论派、胜论派 | 神我与五蕴的关系论证 |
| 3. 破一品 | 万物的统一性 | 吠檀多 | "一"与"多"的逻辑矛盾 |
| 4. 破异品 | 万物的差异性 | 胜论派 | 差异的自性化矛盾 |
| 5. 破情品 | 感官知觉的实有性 | 正理派 | 感官认识的缘起性 |
| 6. 破尘品 | 外在对象的实有性 | 胜论派 | 极微 (paramanu) 的逻辑矛盾 |
| 7-10 | 破有/无/生/灭 | 各派 | 存在与非存在的双遣 |
| 11-20 | 破因果、时间、空间等 | 各派 | 缘起论对实有论 |

## 辩论方法论 / Debate Methodology

### 归谬法 Prasanga 的系统运用

提婆在《百论》中系统地运用了**归谬法 (prasanga)**——即"应成论证"。这一方法的核心特征是：

Aryadeva systematically employs **prasanga** (reductio ad absurdum) — "consequence-reasoning" — in the *Satasatra*. The core features of this method:

1. **不立自宗 (no positive thesis)**: 提婆不提出自己的正面主张，只指出对手立场的逻辑后果
2. **以子之矛 (using the opponent's own logic)**: 从对手自己的前提出发，推导出矛盾
3. **穷尽可能 (exhausting possibilities)**: 逐一检验所有逻辑可能性，全部否定
4. **对话结构 (dialogical structure)**: 保持辩论的现场感，让读者跟随论证过程

**No positive thesis**: Aryadeva does not assert his own position but only exposes the logical consequences of the opponent's stance. **Using the opponent's own logic**: Starting from the opponent's premises, he derives contradictions. **Exhausting possibilities**: Every logical possibility is examined and negated. **Dialogical structure**: The live-debate format allows readers to follow the argumentation process.

### 对话体的哲学意义 / Philosophical Significance of Dialogue Form

《百论》采用对话体不是偶然的选择。在印度哲学传统中，辩论 (vada) 是知识生产的核心方式——哲学观点必须在公共辩论中经受检验。提婆的对话体体现了一种认识论承诺：真理不是独白的产物，而是对话中涌现的。

The dialogical form of the *Satasatra* is not accidental. In the Indian philosophical tradition, debate (vada) is the core mode of knowledge production — philosophical positions must withstand public scrutiny. Aryadeva's dialogical form embodies an epistemological commitment: truth emerges from dialogue, not monologue.

## 核心破斥案例 / Key Refutation Cases

### 破数论派的因中有果论 / Refuting Samkhya's Satkaryavada

数论派主张"因中有果"(satkaryavada)——果在因中已经存在，变化只是潜在状态的显现。提婆的反驳：如果果已经存在于因中，那么"产生"就是多余的——一个已经存在的东西不需要再次产生。如果果不存在于因中，那就不能说"因中有果"。这一论证彻底瓦解了数论派因果理论的基础。

The Samkhya school holds that the effect pre-exists in the cause (satkaryavada) — change is merely the manifestation of a latent state. Aryadeva's rebuttal: if the effect already exists in the cause, then "production" is redundant — what already exists does not need to be produced again. If the effect does not exist in the cause, one cannot claim "the effect is in the cause." This argument completely undermines the foundation of Samkhya causation theory.

### 破胜论派的极微实有论 / Refuting Vaisesika's Atomic Realism

胜论派主张世界由不可分割的极微 (paramanu) 构成，极微是实有的。提婆的反驳：极微如果有方分（占据空间的方向），就可以进一步分割，故非"极微"；极微如果无方分，就无法组合成宏观物体（没有方向性的东西如何构成有方向性的物体？）。这一"方分论证"后来成为佛教批判原子论的经典论证。

The Vaisesika school holds that the world is composed of indivisible atoms (paramanu), which are substantially real. Aryadeva's rebuttal: if atoms have spatial extension (directions), they can be further divided and are therefore not "atomic"; if atoms lack spatial extension, they cannot combine to form macroscopic objects (how can directionless entities compose directional objects?). This "extension argument" became a classic Buddhist refutation of atomism.

### 破正理派的灵魂实有论 / Refuting Nyaya's Soul Realism

正理派主张"我"(atman) 是实有的实体，是知觉、情感和意志的主体。提婆的反驳：如果"我"是遍在的实体，那么一切知觉应该同时发生（因为遍在的"我"同时接触一切对象）；如果"我"不是遍在的，就需要解释它如何在不同经验之间移动。无论哪种情况，灵魂实有论都无法自圆其说。

The Nyaya school holds that the "self" (atman) is a real entity — the subject of perception, emotion, and volition. Aryadeva's rebuttal: if the "self" is an omnipresent entity, all perceptions should occur simultaneously (since the omnipresent self contacts all objects at once); if the "self" is not omnipresent, one must explain how it moves between different experiences. Either way, soul-realism cannot be made coherent.

## 《百论》在三论宗的地位 / Status in the Sanlun School

在汉传佛教三论宗中，《百论》具有独特地位：

In the Chinese Sanlun school, the *Satasatra* holds a distinctive position:

- **吉藏 Jizang (549-623)**: 三论宗集大成者，在《三论玄义》中将《百论》定位为"破外道"的专门著作——与《中论》"破内学"、《十二门论"通破内外"形成分工
- **功能定位**: 《百论》的主要功能是"破邪"——破斥佛教外部的实有论；而《中论》的功能是"显正"——正面阐发空义
- **方法论意义**: 三论宗认为《百论》展示了中观学派最纯粹的"破而不立"方法

## 文本学问题 / Textual Issues

### 仅存汉译的困境 / The Chinese-Only Problem

《百论》仅存鸠摩罗什的汉译本，这一事实带来了多重问题：

The *Satasatra* survives only in Kumarajiva's Chinese translation, which creates multiple problems:

1. **无法回译**: 没有梵文原本对照，无法确定汉译的精确度
2. **术语翻译**: 鸠摩罗什的翻译以意译为主，部分梵文术语的对应关系不确定
3. **结构问题**: 20 品的结构是否为原貌，还是鸠摩罗什或其团队的编辑
4. **归属争议**: 有学者质疑此论是否确为提婆所著，还是后人编著

### 与《四百论》的关系 / Relationship with the Catuhsataka

学界对《百论》与《四百论》的关系有不同看法：

Scholars hold different views on the relationship between the *Satasatra* and the *Catuhsataka*:

- **传统观点**: 二者是提婆的两部独立著作
- **Tillemans 观点**: 《百论》可能是《四百论》某个版本的改编或缩编
- **平川彰观点**: 二者代表提婆思想的不同阶段——《百论》较早（侧重破外道），《四百论》较晚（侧重菩萨道修证）

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

### 辩论理论 / Debate Theory

《百论》是研究古代印度辩论术 (vada-vidhi) 的重要文献。提婆的辩论方法展示了如何将逻辑分析与修辞策略结合——不仅要在逻辑上击败对手，还要在辩论现场说服观众。这对当代论证理论 (argumentation theory) 和批判性思维教学都有启发。

The *Satasatra* is an important document for studying ancient Indian debate techniques (vada-vidhi). Aryadeva's methods demonstrate how to combine logical analysis with rhetorical strategy — defeating opponents logically while also persuading the audience. This has implications for contemporary argumentation theory and critical thinking pedagogy.

### 跨宗教对话 / Interreligious Dialogue

提婆在《百论》中展示了一种批判但尊重对手的辩论风格——他针对的是观点，而非个人。这为当代跨宗教和跨哲学传统的对话提供了方法论典范。

Aryadeva demonstrates in the *Satasatra* a critical yet respectful debate style — targeting ideas, not persons. This provides a methodological model for contemporary interreligious and cross-philosophical dialogue.
