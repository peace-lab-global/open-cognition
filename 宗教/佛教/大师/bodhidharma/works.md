# 菩提达摩相关著作 / Works Related to Bodhidharma

本页面系统整理与菩提达摩相关的原典、托名著作及重要研究文献。

This page systematically organizes primary texts, attributed works, and important scholarly literature related to Bodhidharma.

---

## 一、达摩亲撰或近真文献 / Works Authored or Close to Authentic

### 《二入四行论》 / *Two Entries and Four Practices*

- **真伪 Authenticity**：学界公认最接近达摩本人的文献，敦煌写本为其最早版本。
  Scholarly consensus holds this as the text closest to Bodhidharma himself; the Dunhuang manuscripts are its earliest versions.
- **内容 Content**：系统阐述"理入"（壁观安心）与"行入"（报怨行、随缘行、无所求行、称法行）两大修行路径。
  Systematically expounds the two cultivation paths of "entry through principle" (wall-gazing to pacify the mind) and "entry through practice" (four practices: accepting karmic retribution, adapting to conditions, non-striving, and practicing in accord with Dharma).
- **版本 Versions**：
  - 敦煌本（斯坦因本、伯希和本） / Dunhuang manuscripts (Stein and Pelliot collections)
  - 《续高僧传》卷十六引用 / Quoted in *Continued Biographies of Eminent Monks*, vol. 16
  - 《景德传灯录》收录 / Included in *Jingde Record of the Transmission of the Lamp*
- **现代翻译 Modern Translations**：
  - Red Pine (Bill Porter), *The Zen Teaching of Bodhidharma* (1989)
  - 铃木大拙英译 / D.T. Suzuki, English translation in *Manual of Zen Buddhism*

### 《略辨大乘入道四行》 / *Brief Discernment of the Four Practices for Entering the Mahayana Path*

- **真伪 Authenticity**：为《二入四行论》的弟子记录版本，由昙林（达摩弟子）笔录整理。
  A version of the *Two Entries and Four Practices* recorded by the disciple Tanlin.
- **内容 Content**：与《二入四行论》内容基本一致，但增加了昙林的序言和注解。
  Content is essentially the same as *Two Entries and Four Practices* but adds Tanlin's preface and annotations.

## 二、托名著作（真伪有争议） / Attributed Works (Authenticity Disputed)

### 《达摩血脉论》 / *Bloodline Treatise of Bodhidharma*

- **真伪 Authenticity**：可能为晚唐或宋初禅僧托名之作，非达摩亲撰。但其思想与达摩禅高度一致。
  Likely a work attributed by late Tang or early Song Chan monks, not written by Bodhidharma himself. However, its thought is highly consistent with Bodhidharma's Chan.
- **内容 Content**：阐述"即心是佛"的核心思想，强调见性成佛不需要向外求法。语言直接有力，具有鲜明的禅宗风格。
  Expounds the core thought of "this mind is Buddha," emphasizing that seeing one's nature and becoming Buddha requires no external seeking. The language is direct and powerful, with a distinctly Chan style.
- **影响 Influence**：对临济宗影响极大，临济义玄的"无心道人"思想与此论有深层关联。
  Enormous influence on the Linji school; Linji Yixuan's thought of the "no-mind person of the Way" is deeply connected to this treatise.

### 《达摩悟性论》 / *Treatise on Awakening the Nature*

- **真伪 Authenticity**：托名可能性极大，学术界普遍认为是后人伪托。
  Very likely attributed; scholars generally consider it a later fabrication.
- **内容 Content**：讨论心性与觉悟的关系，融合如来藏思想与般若空观。
  Discusses the relationship between mind-nature and awakening, synthesizing Tathagatagarbha thought with Prajnaparamita emptiness.

### 《少室六门集》 / *Shaoshi Six Gates Collection*

- **真伪 Authenticity**：部分可信。六篇中某些章节可能保留了达摩的教学内容，但整体编辑成集的时间较晚。
  Partially credible. Some sections among the six essays may preserve Bodhidharma's teaching content, but the collection as a whole was compiled at a later date.
- **内容 Content**：包含六篇禅法论述，涵盖心灯、破相、二种入、安心、悟性、血脉等主题。
  Contains six essays on Chan Dharma, covering themes such as the mind-lamp, breaking appearances, two entries, pacifying the mind, awakening the nature, and the bloodline.
- **六门 The Six Gates**：
  1. 心经颂 / Heart Sutra Verse
  2. 破相论 / Treatise on Breaking Appearances
  3. 二种入 / Two Entries
  4. 安心法门 / Dharma Gate of Pacifying the Mind
  5. 悟性论 / Treatise on Awakening the Nature
  6. 血脉论 / Bloodline Treatise

### 《达摩大师绝观论》 / *Treatise on Cutting Off Contemplation*

- **真伪 Authenticity**：托名之作。
  Attributed work.
- **内容 Content**：讨论止息观照的方法，与壁观修行有关联。
  Discusses methods of stopping contemplation, related to wall-gazing practice.

### 《达摩大师安心法门》 / *Dharma Gate of Pacifying the Mind*

- **真伪 Authenticity**：托名之作。
  Attributed work.
- **内容 Content**：围绕达摩与慧可的"安心"公案展开，讨论如何使心安住。
  Expands on the Bodhidharma-Huike "pacifying the mind" koan, discussing how to allow the mind to abide.

## 三、达摩传心经典 / Scripture of Mind-Transmission

### 《楞伽经》四卷本 / *Lankavatara Sutra* (Four-Fascicle Version)

- **译者 Translator**：求那跋陀罗（Gunabhadra），443 年译出。
  Translated by Gunabhadra in 443 CE.
- **与达摩的关系 Relation to Bodhidharma**：达摩以此经四卷传给慧可，作为"藉教悟宗"的核心经典。达摩说："我观汉地，唯有此经。仁者依行，自得度世。"
  Bodhidharma transmitted this sutra in four fascicles to Huike as the core scripture for "using teachings to awaken to the source." Bodhidharma said: "I have observed the land of Han, and only this sutra [is suitable]. If the benevolent ones practice according to it, they will naturally cross to the other shore."
- **核心思想 Core Thought**：如来藏思想、唯识学、五法三自性。
  Tathagatagarbha thought, Yogacara philosophy, five dharmas and three self-natures.

## 四、记载达摩事迹的早期史料 / Early Historical Sources Recording Bodhidharma

### 《洛阳伽蓝记》 / *Record of Buddhist Monasteries in Luoyang*

- **作者 Author**：杨衒之（Yang Xuanzhi），约 547 年成书。
  By Yang Xuanzhi, completed c. 547 CE.
- **相关内容 Relevant Content**：提到"菩提达摩"为"波斯国胡人"，在洛阳永宁寺的活动。这是关于达摩的最早文献记录之一。
  Mentions "Bodhidharma" as a "Persian foreigner" and his activities at the Yongning Temple in Luoyang. One of the earliest documentary records of Bodhidharma.

### 《续高僧传》 / *Continued Biographies of Eminent Monks*

- **作者 Author**：道宣（Daoxuan），约 645 年成书。
  By Daoxuan, completed c. 645 CE.
- **相关内容 Relevant Content**：卷十六有达摩的传记，是学术界公认最可靠的早期达摩史料。记载了达摩的生平、"二人四行"论及传法慧可等事迹。
  Volume 16 contains Bodhidharma's biography, recognized by scholars as the most reliable early source. Records his life, the "Two Entries and Four Practices," and his Dharma transmission to Huike.

### 《景德传灯录》 / *Jingde Record of the Transmission of the Lamp*

- **作者 Author**：道原（Daoyuan），1004 年成书。
  By Daoyuan, completed 1004 CE.
- **相关内容 Relevant Content**：详细记载了达摩的传说故事，包括与梁武帝的对话、面壁九年、传法慧可、只履西归等。虽然成书较晚，但收录了大量早期传说。
  Records in detail Bodhidharma's legendary stories, including the dialogue with Emperor Wu of Liang, nine years facing the wall, Dharma transmission to Huike, and returning west with one shoe. Although compiled later, it preserves many early legends.
