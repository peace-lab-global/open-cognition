# 应成法 Prasanga / The Consequentialist Method

## 定义 / Definition

**应成法 (prasaṅga)** 是月称中观哲学的核心方法论——通过揭示对手立场的内在逻辑矛盾 (归谬, reductio ad absurdum) 来瓦解其实体性执着，而自身不建立任何正面主张。"应成"一词字面意思是"必然推出的结果"——即从对手自己的前提出发，推出对手无法接受的荒谬结论。

**Prasanga** (consequence/reductio) is the core methodological principle of Candrakirti's Madhyamaka — exposing the internal logical contradictions of the opponent's position without asserting any independent thesis. The term literally means "necessary consequence" — drawing out absurd conclusions from the opponent's own premises.

## 梵文语义 / Sanskrit Semantics

- **prasaṅga** (梵文): 必然的后果、逻辑推论、归谬
- **prasaṅgika** (应成派): 以应成法为方法论特征的学派
- **prasaṅga-vakya**: 归谬语句——"如果按你所说，则必然导致……"
- **svatantra** (自立): 与应成相对——自立因、自立论证

## 应成法的哲学原理 / Philosophical Principles

### 1. 破而不立 Refutation Without Assertion

月称在《明句论》(Prasannapada) 中明确声明：中观行者不自立任何宗义 (paksa)。这不是因为中观没有"观点"，而是因为任何正面主张都会暗含对所主张之对象的自性承许。中观的任务是消解一切自性执，包括对"中观"本身的自性执。

**Candrakirti explicitly states in the Prasannapada that the Madhyamika asserts no thesis (paksa). This is not because Madhyamaka has no "view" but because any positive assertion implicitly accepts the inherent existence of its object. The Madhyamika's task is to dissolve all grasping at inherent existence, including grasping at "Madhyamaka" itself.**

### 2. 以子之矛攻子之盾 Using the Opponent's Own Logic

应成法的精妙之处在于：它不引入中观自己的前提，而是从对手自己承认的前提出发。"你说诸法有自性——好，让我们看看有自性的诸法会怎样。"然后从对手的前提推出矛盾——例如，如果有自性，则不能变化；但经验显示一切在变化，所以自性论自相矛盾。

**The brilliance of prasanga lies in using the opponent's own premises. "You say phenomena have inherent existence — let us see what follows." From the opponent's own premises, contradictions are derived: if things had inherent existence, they could not change; yet experience shows everything changes, so the svabhava theory is self-contradictory.**

### 3. 四句破的应成运用 Catuskoti via Prasanga

龙树的四句破 (catuskoti) 在月称手中获得了更严格的方法论自觉。月称将四句破理解为归谬论证的四种形式——穷尽所有逻辑可能性后逐一展示其内在矛盾。

## 与自立因的对比 / Contrast with Svatantra-hetu

| 维度 Dimension | 应成因 Prasanga | 自立因 Svatantra-hetu |
|---|---|---|
| 前提来源 Source of premises | 来自对手 From opponent | 来自自宗 From own system |
| 结论性质 Nature of conclusion | 只否定对手 Only negation | 肯定自宗 Affirmation of own view |
| 对自性的态度 Attitude to svabhava | 完全不承许 No acceptance at all | 名言中暗含承许 Implicit at conventional level |
| 认识论立场 Epistemic stance | 不承许独立有效的认识量 No independently valid pramana | 承许名言中的认识量 Accepts pramana conventionally |
| 代表人物 Representatives | 佛护、月称 Buddhapalita, Candrakirti | 清辨、寂护 Bhaviveka, Santaraksita |

## 应成法的逻辑结构 / Logical Structure

一个典型的应成论证包含以下步骤：

1. **识别对手前提 Identify opponent's premise**: "你承许 X 具有自性 (svabhava)"
2. **推出逻辑后果 Derive consequence**: "如果有自性，则 X 不能 Y (变化/生灭/与他物关联)"
3. **展示矛盾 Expose contradiction**: "但经验显示 X 确实 Y"
4. **结论 Conclusion**: "所以 X 不具有自性"——但中观不自立"X 无自性"为宗义，只是迫使对手放弃"X 有自性"

**A typical prasanga argument: (1) Identify opponent's premise ("You assert X has svabhava"); (2) Derive consequence ("If svabhava, X cannot change/perish/relate"); (3) Expose contradiction ("Yet experience shows X does change"); (4) Force abandonment of the premise — without asserting the negation as one's own thesis.**

## 应成法的哲学深度 / Philosophical Depth

### 超越形而上学 Beyond Metaphysics

应成法的终极目标不是建立一种新的形而上学（哪怕是"空的形而上学"），而是彻底消解一切形而上学执着。月称警告：如果把"空性"当作一种正面主张来持有，那恰恰是对空性的最大误解——"若人见有空，诸佛所不化"(MMK 24.11)。

**The ultimate aim of prasanga is not to establish a new metaphysics (even a "metaphysics of emptiness") but to dissolve all metaphysical fixation. Candrakirti warns: holding emptiness as a positive thesis is the greatest misunderstanding of emptiness.**

### 与维特根斯坦的平行 Parallel with Wittgenstein

月称的应成法与维特根斯坦后期哲学形成惊人的结构平行。维特根斯坦说"哲学不做任何断言，只是把一切摆在那里"(PI §124-126)——这与月称"破而不立"的立场异曲同工。两者都将哲学视为一种治疗活动 (therapeutic activity)，而非理论建构。

**Candrakirti's prasanga method parallels Wittgenstein's later 哲学 strikingly. Wittgenstein says "philosophy asserts nothing, it only puts everything before us" (PI §124-126) — resonating with Candrakirti's "refute without asserting." Both see 哲学 as therapeutic activity, not theory-building.**

## 应成法的局限性争论 / Debates on Prasanga's Limits

### 自续派的批评 Svatantrika Critique

清辨和后来的寂护批评说：如果中观行者不自立任何主张，那如何区分正确与错误？如何指导修行？如果一切只是归谬，中观是否会沦为一种纯破坏性的虚无？

**Bhaviveka and later Santaraksita object: if the Madhyamika asserts nothing, how can one distinguish right from wrong or guide practice? If everything is mere reductio, does Madhyamaka degenerate into purely destructive nihilism?**

### 月称的回应 Candrakirti's Response

月称的回答是：中观在世俗名言层面完整承认"世间共许"(loka-prasiddhi) 的一切——因果、业报、修道次第。应成法破的是胜义层面的自性执，不是世俗层面的功能性存在。所以应成派不是虚无主义，而是在更深的层次上理解缘起。

**Candrakirti responds: Madhyamaka fully accepts everything the world agrees upon (loka-prasiddhi) at the conventional level — causation, karma, the path. Prasanga targets inherent existence at the ultimate level, not functional existence at the conventional level. Prasangika is not nihilism but a deeper understanding of dependent origination.**
