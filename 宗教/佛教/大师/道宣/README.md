# 道宣 Dàoxuān (596–667)

> "戒为无上菩提本，长养一切诸善根。"
> "Precepts are the root of unsurpassed bodhi; they nurture all wholesome roots."

---

## 一、生平概略 · Biographical Overview

道宣（596–667），俗姓钱，丹徒（今江苏镇江）人，一说长城（今浙江长兴）人。唐代高僧，中国佛教**律宗**（南山宗）的创立者，被后世尊为"南山律师""律宗祖师"。他一生精研戒律，以《四分律》（Dharmaguptaka Vinaya）为基础，融合大乘佛教思想，建立了一套完整的律学体系——**南山律**，深刻影响了此后一千三百余年汉传佛教的戒律实践。

Daoxuan (596–667), surnamed Qian (钱) before ordination, was a native of Dantu (modern Zhenjiang, Jiangsu), though some sources identify his birthplace as Changcheng (modern Changxing, Zhejiang). A towering figure of the Tang dynasty, he is recognized as the founder of the **Nanshan Vinaya School** (南山律宗) within Chinese Buddhism and venerated as the "Vinaya Master of Nanshan." His lifelong dedication to the study and systematization of monastic discipline — rooted in the *Dharmaguptaka Vinaya* (四分律) yet enriched by Mahāyāna thought — produced a comprehensive framework known as **Nanshan Vinaya** (南山律) that has shaped the practice of monastic discipline in East Asian Buddhism for over thirteen centuries.

道宣少年出家，十五岁依止日严寺智頵法师学习佛法，后师从大禅定寺智首律师系统学习律学。智首是当时律学的权威，道宣在其门下精研律藏数年，奠定了日后律学事业的基础。据《续高僧传》记载，道宣"持律精严，行仪轨范"，年轻时就以戒行清净著称。

Daoxuan entered monastic life as a youth. At fifteen he began studying under Master Zhiyun (智頵) at Riyan Monastery, and later became a disciple of the eminent Vinaya master Zhishou (智首) at the Great Meditation Monastery (大禅定寺). Zhishou was the foremost Vinaya authority of the era, and under his tutelage Daoxuan immersed himself in the Vinaya canon for several years, laying the intellectual and practical foundation for his future work. According to the *Continued Biographies of Eminent Monks* (续高僧传), Daoxuan was noted even in youth for his "strict adherence to the Vinaya and exemplary conduct."

## 二、终南山时期 · The Nanshan (Zhongnan Mountains) Period

约贞观年间（627–649），道宣移居终南山丰德寺，此后又住净业寺。终南山位于长安（今西安）以南，自古即为修行者隐居之所。道宣以此为基地，潜心著述，完成了律学三大部——即所谓"南山三大部"：

During the Zhenguan era (approximately 627–649), Daoxuan relocated to Fengde Monastery on Mount Zhongnan (终南山), south of the capital Chang'an (modern Xi'an), and later resided at Jingye Monastery. Mount Zhongnan had long been a retreat for contemplatives. Here Daoxuan devoted himself to writing and completed his monumental **"Three Great Works of Nanshan"** (南山三大部):

1. **《四分律删繁补阙行事钞》**（简称《行事钞》）——律学的百科全书式著作，系统整理受戒、持戒、忏悔等僧团生活的全部规范。
   *Sifenlü Shanfan Buque Xingshi Chao* (*Procedural Manual of the Dharmaguptaka Vinaya, Abridged and Supplemented*) — an encyclopedic compendium systematizing every aspect of monastic life, from ordination rituals to daily conduct and penance procedures.

2. **《四分律删补随机羯磨》**（简称《羯磨疏》）——关于僧团羯磨（会议法）的详细注释。
   *Sifenlü Shanbu Suiji Jiemo* (*Commentary on Karma Procedures*) — a detailed commentary on saṅgha-karma (communal decision-making rituals).

3. **《四分律含注戒本疏》**（简称《戒本疏》）——对戒条本文的逐条注释。
   *Sifenlü Hanzhu Jieben Shu* (*Annotated Commentary on the Prātimokṣa*) — a clause-by-clause commentary on the monastic precepts.

这三部著作奠定了南山律宗的理论基础，使道宣成为中国佛教律学史上最重要的系统建构者。

These three works established the doctrinal foundation of the Nanshan Vinaya School and made Daoxuan the single most important systematizer in the history of Chinese Buddhist Vinaya studies.

## 三、核心思想体系 · Core Doctrinal System

### 3.1 戒体论 · Theory of Precept-Substance (Jieti)

道宣律学的核心概念是**戒体**（jiè tǐ）。所谓戒体，是指受戒时通过如法的羯磨仪式，在受戒者心中所形成的一种"防非止恶"的功能体。道宣认为，戒体既非色法（物质性存在），亦非心法（心理活动），而是一种**"非色非心"的功能性存在**——它是通过受戒仪式而在受戒者相续中建立的一种**防恶止非的力量**。

At the heart of Daoxuan's Vinaya theory is the concept of **precept-substance** (戒体, *jiè tǐ*). This refers to the "anti-transgression force" that arises in the mind-stream of the ordinand during a properly conducted karma ritual (羯磨). Daoxuan argued that the precept-substance is **neither a material phenomenon (色法) nor a mental phenomenon (心法)** — it is a *functional entity* (非色非心) that constitutes an enduring power to prevent wrongdoing, established in the ordinand's continuum through the ritual of ordination.

这一理论的关键在于：道宣以唯识学的"种子说"来解释戒体。他认为，受戒仪式中的身口意三业活动，在阿赖耶识（第八识）中熏习了善种子，这些善种子就是戒体。因此戒体具有持续的功能性，能够自发地防范恶行，而不仅仅依赖外在的约束。

Crucially, Daoxuan drew on the Yogācāra (唯识) theory of "seeds" (种子, *bīja*) to explain the mechanism: the bodily, verbal, and mental activities during ordination plant wholesome seeds in the *ālaya-vijñāna* (storehouse consciousness), and these seeds *constitute* the precept-substance. Thus the precept-substance possesses ongoing efficacy — it spontaneously prevents transgressions rather than relying merely on external constraint.

### 3.2 四分通大乘 · The Dharmaguptaka Vinaya as a Mahāyāna Path

在道宣之前，中国律学界对《四分律》的定位存在争议：它究竟属于声闻乘（小乘）还是可通大乘？道宣提出了**"四分通大乘"**的创造性主张，认为《四分律》虽然在形式上属于声闻律藏，但其精神实质可以通向大乘菩萨道。

Before Daoxuan, the status of the *Dharmaguptaka Vinaya* (四分律) was disputed among Chinese Vinaya scholars: did it belong to the Śrāvakayāna ("Hīnayāna") alone, or could it also serve the Mahāyāna path? Daoxuan proposed the innovative thesis of **"the Dharmaguptaka Vinaya as贯通 Mahāyāna"** (四分通大乘), arguing that while the Vinaya is formally part of the Śrāvaka canon, its spiritual substance can extend to the Bodhisattva path.

他提出了五个论证（"五义"）来支持这一观点，其中最重要的是：受戒时所发的菩提心和慈悲心，使声闻律的持戒行为具有了大乘的性质。这一主张解决了中国佛教的一个核心理论困境：汉传佛教自视为大乘法门，但其所依循的戒律却来自声闻律藏。道宣的解答使得汉传佛教的戒律实践获得了大乘的合法性。

He advanced five arguments (五义) to support this thesis, the most important being that the *bodhicitta* (bodhi mind) and compassion aroused during ordination invest Śrāvaka-level precept-keeping with Mahāyāna significance. This resolved a core theoretical dilemma in Chinese Buddhism: the tradition considered itself Mahāyāna, yet its monastic discipline derived from a Śrāvaka Vinaya. Daoxuan's solution legitimized the precept practice of East Asian Buddhism within the Mahāyāna framework.

### 3.3 化制二教 · The Two Teachings: Doctrinal and Regulatory

道宣提出了**化制二教**的判教理论，将佛陀的一代教法分为两大类：

Daoxuan proposed the hermeneutical framework of the **Two Teachings — Doctrinal (化教) and Regulatory (制教)** — to classify the Buddha's entire teaching:

- **化教**（教理之教）：佛陀为引导众生悟入真理而说的教理，如四谛、十二因缘、六度等。这类教法侧重于**理解与觉悟**，适用于所有修行者（包括在家和出家）。
  **Doctrinal Teaching** (化教): The soteriological doctrines taught to guide beings toward awakening — the Four Noble Truths, Dependent Origination, the Six Pāramitās, etc. These teachings emphasize *understanding and realization* and apply to all practitioners, lay and monastic alike.

- **制教**（戒律之教）：佛陀为规范僧团行为而制定的戒律条文，如比丘二百五十戒、比丘尼三百四十八戒等。这类教法侧重于**行为规范**，主要适用于出家僧众。
  **Regulatory Teaching** (制教): The behavioral regulations established by the Buddha to govern monastic conduct — the 250 precepts for monks (bhikṣu), the 348 precepts for nuns (bhikṣuṇī), etc. These emphasize *behavioral norms* and apply primarily to ordained monastics.

道宣强调，化教与制教**不可偏废**：仅有教理而无戒律，则修行失去基础；仅有戒律而无教理，则戒律沦为形式主义。两者相辅相成，共同构成完整的佛教修行体系。

Daoxuan insisted that the two teachings must **not be separated**: doctrine without discipline loses its practical foundation; discipline without doctrine degenerates into empty formalism. The two are complementary and together constitute a complete system of Buddhist practice.

### 3.4 南山律 · The Nanshan Vinaya Tradition

"南山律"（Nanshan Vinaya）是以道宣为祖师的律学传统的总称。它之所以得名"南山"，是因为道宣长期住持终南山。南山律的核心特征是：

The **Nanshan Vinaya** (南山律) designates the Vinaya tradition founded by Daoxuan, so named because of his long residence on Mount Zhongnan (终南山). Its defining characteristics are:

1. 以《四分律》为根本依据
2. 融合唯识学的戒体理论
3. 主张声闻律可通大乘
4. 强调化教与制教并重
5. 建立了完整的律学注释体系

1. Taking the *Dharmaguptaka Vinaya* (四分律) as its foundational text
2. Integrating Yogācāra seed-theory into the account of precept-substance
3. Asserting that Śrāvaka Vinaya can extend to the Mahāyāna
4. Emphasizing the complementarity of doctrinal and regulatory teachings
5. Establishing a complete commentary tradition on the Vinaya

自道宣以后，南山律逐渐取代了相部律（法砺）和东塔律（怀素），成为中国律学的唯一正统传承。唐代以后，历代律师几乎都以南山律为标准。近代弘一法师（李叔同）更是以弘传南山律为己任，使南山律在现代社会得以延续。

After Daoxuan, the Nanshan Vinaya gradually supplanted the Xiangbu school (founded by Fali 法砺) and the Dongta school (founded by Huaisu 怀素) to become the sole orthodox Vinaya tradition in China. From the Tang dynasty onward, virtually all subsequent Vinaya masters took the Nanshan tradition as their standard. In modern times, Master Hongyi (弘一法师, the artist Li Shutong) dedicated himself to reviving and propagating the Nanshan Vinaya, ensuring its continuity into the modern era.

### 3.5 戒相 · Precept-Manifestation (Jiexiang)

**戒相**是道宣律学中关于戒条具体表现形式的概念。每一戒条都有其特定的犯缘（构成犯戒的条件）、开遮（允许与禁止的情况）以及轻重等级。道宣通过精细的戒相分析，使得戒律的适用更加精确和可操作。

**Precept-manifestation** (戒相, *jiè xiàng*) refers to the concrete expression and operationalization of each precept in Daoxuan's Vinaya system. Each precept has its specific conditions of transgression (犯缘), its allowances and restrictions (开遮), and its grading of severity. Through meticulous analysis of precept-manifestations, Daoxuan made the application of the Vinaya more precise and practical.

## 四、史学贡献 · Historiographical Contributions

除了律学成就，道宣还是一位杰出的佛教史学家和文献学家。他的主要史学著作包括：

Beyond his Vinaya scholarship, Daoxuan was an accomplished Buddhist historian and bibliographer. His principal historiographical works include:

- **《续高僧传》**（三十卷）：续慧皎《高僧传》，收录自梁至唐初的高僧传记，是研究南北朝至初唐佛教史的核心文献。
  *Continued Biographies of Eminent Monks* (30 fascicles): A continuation of Huijiao's *Biographies of Eminent Monks*, covering eminent monks from the Liang dynasty through the early Tang — a primary source for the study of Chinese Buddhism from the Southern Dynasties through the early Tang.

- **《广弘明集》**（三十卷）：佛教护教文献汇编，收录了自汉至唐的佛教论辩文章，涉及儒释道三教争论、因果报应、神灭论等重要议题。
  *Expanded Collection on the Propagation of the Light* (30 fascicles): An anthology of Buddhist apologetic 文学 spanning the Han through Tang dynasties, covering debates on Buddhism vis-à-vis Confucianism and Daoism, karma, the mortality of the soul, and other major intellectual issues.

- **《释迦方志》**（二卷）：关于印度佛教地理和历史的著作。
  *Gazetteer of Śākyamuni's Lands* (2 fascicles): A geographical and historical account of Buddhist India.

- **《大唐内典录》**（十卷）：唐代佛教经录。
  *Catalogue of the Inner Canon of the Great Tang* (10 fascicles): A bibliographic catalogue of Buddhist texts of the Tang dynasty.

这些著作显示了道宣作为学者的广阔视野：他不仅是一位戒律专家，还是一位佛教文化的全方位守护者和记录者。

These works reveal the breadth of Daoxuan's scholarly vision: he was not merely a Vinaya specialist, but a comprehensive guardian and chronicler of Buddhist culture.

## 五、历史影响与遗产 · Historical Influence and Legacy

### 对中国佛教的影响 · Impact on Chinese Buddhism

道宣创立的南山律宗，深刻塑造了汉传佛教的戒律传统。自唐代以降，汉传佛教的受戒仪轨、僧团管理、戒律解释，几乎都以南山律为标准。宋代的允堪、元照，明代的如馨、见月，清代的读体，近代的弘一法师等历代律师，都在南山律的框架内发展律学。

The Nanshan Vinaya School profoundly shaped the Vinaya tradition of East Asian Buddhism. From the Tang dynasty onward, ordination rituals, saṅgha governance, and Vinaya interpretation in Chinese Buddhism have almost universally followed the Nanshan standard. Successive Vinaya masters — Yunakan and Yuanzhao of the Song, Ruxin and Jianyue of the Ming, Duti of the Qing, and Hongyi of the modern era — all worked within the Nanshan framework.

### 对东亚佛教的影响 · Impact on East Asian Buddhism

南山律通过唐代的文化交流传播至日本和朝鲜。日本奈良时代的鉴真和尚（688–763）即为南山律的传承者，他将南山律传至日本，创立了日本律宗。在日本，南山律的影响延续至今。

Nanshan Vinaya spread to Japan and Korea through Tang-era cultural exchange. The Chinese monk Jianzhen (鉴真, 688–763), a transmitter of the Nanshan tradition, brought it to Japan during the Nara period and founded the Japanese Ritsu (律) school. In Japan, the influence of Nanshan Vinaya endures to this day.

### 当代意义 · Contemporary Significance

在当代，道宣律学的意义已经超越了纯粹的宗派传承：

In the contemporary era, the significance of Daoxuan's Vinaya scholarship extends beyond sectarian transmission:

- **宗教伦理学**：戒体论提供了一种独特的道德内在化理论，可与康德的道德律令、亚里士多德的德性伦理学进行对话。
  **Religious Ethics**: The theory of precept-substance offers a distinctive account of moral internalization, open to dialogue with Kantian moral imperatives and Aristotelian virtue ethics.

- **法学思想**：戒律体系中的规范分类、量刑原则、程序正义等概念，与世俗法学体系存在有意义的对照。
  **Legal Thought**: The Vinaya system's normative classifications, sentencing principles, and procedural justice concepts invite meaningful comparison with secular legal systems.

- **僧团治理**：南山律为现代汉传佛教的僧团管理提供了传统的制度资源。
  **Saṅgha Governance**: Nanshan Vinaya provides traditional institutional resources for the management of modern Chinese Buddhist monastic communities.

## 七、学术方法论 · Scholarly Methodology

道宣的学术方法论具有鲜明的特色，可以概括为以下几个方面：

Daoxuan's scholarly methodology is distinctive and can be summarized in several aspects:

### 7.1 综合融贯 · Comprehensive Integration

道宣不是简单地注释律藏文本，而是将不同来源的律学知识——包括有部、经量部、大众部等不同部派的律藏和论藏——整合为一个统一的体系。他在《行事钞》中广泛引用了数十种佛教文献，展现了一位百科全书式学者的素养。

Daoxuan did not simply annotate Vinaya texts; he integrated Vinaya knowledge from diverse sources — including the Vinaya and Abhidharma canons of different sects such as Sarvāstivāda, Sautrāntika, and Mahāsāṅghika — into a unified system. In the *Xingshi Chao*, he extensively cited dozens of Buddhist texts, demonstrating the erudition of an encyclopedic scholar.

### 7.2 以理判文 · Reasoning Over Text

道宣在处理律藏文本时，不拘泥于文字的表面意思，而是通过理性的分析来把握文本的深层含义。当不同律藏之间存在矛盾时，他不是简单地选择其中之一，而是分析其背后的逻辑，寻求更高层次的综合。

When handling Vinaya texts, Daoxuan was not bound by the surface meaning of words but grasped deeper significance through rational analysis. When contradictions arose between different Vinaya canons, he did not simply choose one over the other but analyzed the underlying logic and sought a higher-level synthesis.

### 7.3 因时制宜 · Adaptation to Circumstances

道宣深知戒律的制定有其特定的历史因缘（"随犯随制"），因此在解释戒律时，他注意区分戒律的**永恒原则**与**时代性规定**。这使得南山律能够在保持原则性的同时具有足够的灵活性，适应不同时代和地域的需要。

Daoxuan understood that precepts were formulated in specific historical circumstances ("promulgated as offenses arose"). In interpreting the Vinaya, he carefully distinguished between **eternal principles** and **time-bound regulations**. This enabled Nanshan Vinaya to maintain principled consistency while retaining sufficient flexibility to adapt to different eras and regions.

### 7.4 跨学科视野 · Interdisciplinary Vision

道宣的学术视野远超纯粹的律学领域。他同时从事史学编纂（《续高僧传》）、护教论辩（《广弘明集》）、地理考证（《释迦方志》）和经录编纂（《大唐内典录》），展现了一位综合性学者的格局。这种跨学科视野使他能够从多个维度理解戒律在佛教整体中的位置和意义。

Daoxuan's scholarly vision extended far beyond pure Vinaya studies. He simultaneously engaged in historiographical compilation (*Continued Biographies*), apologetic argumentation (*Expanded Collection*), geographical 研究 (*Gazetteer of Śākyamuni's Lands*), and bibliographic work (*Catalogue of the Inner Canon*), embodying the breadth of a comprehensive scholar. This interdisciplinary vision enabled him to understand the position and significance of Vinaya within the totality of Buddhism from multiple dimensions.

| **Xuanzang** (602–664) | Faxiang/Yogācāra | Translation and Yogācāra propagation | Contemporary: Daoxuan borrowed Yogācāra for precept-substance theory |
| **Fazang** (643–712) | Huayan | Dharma-realm dependent origination | Successor: likewise known for systematization |
| **Huineng** (638–713) | Chan | Sudden enlightenment | Contemporary: a contrast — sudden awakening vs gradual cultivation |

值得注意的是，道宣与玄奘是同时代人。玄奘将唯识学系统传入中国，而道宣则将唯识学的种子理论运用于戒律学，建立了独特的戒体论。这种跨领域的理论借用，展示了道宣作为思想家的创造性。

Notably, Daoxuan and Xuanzang were contemporaries. Xuanzang systematically transmitted Yogācāra to China, and Daoxuan applied Yogācāra seed theory to Vinaya studies, establishing the distinctive precept-substance theory. This cross-domain theoretical borrowing demonstrates Daoxuan's creativity as a thinker.

## 九、常见误读与澄清 · Common Misreadings

1. **"道宣只关心戒律"** → 道宣是戒律学家、史学家和护教者的三位一体。他的《广弘明集》和《续高僧传》表明其视野远超纯律学领域。
   "Daoxuan only cared about Vinaya" → He was a Vinaya scholar, historian, and apologist. His *Guang Hongming Ji* and *Xu Gaoseng Zhuan* show a vision far exceeding pure Vinaya studies.

2. **"戒体是一个物质实体"** → 道宣明确主张戒体"非色非心"，是一种功能性存在而非实体性存在，以唯识种子说为其形上学基础。
   "The precept-substance is a material entity" → Daoxuan explicitly held it is "neither form nor mind" — a functional rather than substantial reality grounded in Yogācāra seed theory.

3. **"律宗是保守派"** → 南山律实际上是一种系统化、理性化的戒律诠释学。道宣融合大乘、引入唯识学，是一种创造性的理论革新。
   "The Vinaya school is conservative" → Nanshan Vinaya is in fact a systematized, rationalized hermeneutics of discipline. Daoxuan's integration of Mahāyāna and Yogācāra was a creative theoretical innovation.

4. **"四分通大乘意味着混淆大小乘"** → 道宣并非抹杀声闻与大乘的区别，而是论证声闻律的持戒实践在大乘发心的引导下可以成为菩萨行的一部分。
   "The Mahāyāna-thesis conflates Hīnayāna and Mahāyāna" → Daoxuan did not erase the distinction; he argued that Śrāvaka-level precept practice, guided by Mahāyāna aspiration, can become part of bodhisattva practice.

5. **"道宣的戒律学与现代无关"** → 南山律关于道德内在化、制度弹性、程序正义等问题的思考，对当代伦理学、法学和组织管理学仍有启发意义。
   "Daoxuan's Vinaya is irrelevant to modernity" → Nanshan Vinaya's reflections on moral internalization, institutional elasticity, and procedural justice remain instructive for contemporary ethics, jurisprudence, and organizational studies.
