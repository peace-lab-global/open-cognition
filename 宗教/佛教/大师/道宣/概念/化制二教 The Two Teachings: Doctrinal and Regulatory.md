# 化制二教 · The Two Teachings: Doctrinal and Regulatory (Huà-Jiào & Zhì-Jiào)

> "化教明理，制教立行。理行相资，方为具足。"
> "The Doctrinal Teaching illuminates principle; the Regulatory Teaching establishes conduct. Only when principle and conduct support each other is practice complete."

---

## 一、概念定义 · Definition

**化制二教**是道宣提出的佛教判教理论，将佛陀的一切教法分为两大类：

The **Two Teachings** (化制二教) is a hermeneutical framework proposed by Daoxuan that classifies all of the Buddha's teachings into two categories:

- **化教**（huà jiào, Doctrinal Teaching）：佛陀为引导众生悟入真理而宣说的教理，包括四谛、十二因缘、六度、中观、唯识等一切义理。
  **Doctrinal Teaching** (化教): The soteriological doctrines proclaimed by the Buddha to guide beings toward awakening — the Four Noble Truths, Dependent Origination, the Six Pāramitās, Madhyamaka, Yogācāra, and all doctrinal expositions.

- **制教**（zhì jiào, Regulatory Teaching）：佛陀为规范僧团行为而制定的戒律条文，包括比丘戒、比丘尼戒、沙弥戒等一切律制。
  **Regulatory Teaching** (制教): The behavioral regulations established by the Buddha to govern monastic conduct — the precepts for monks (bhikṣu), nuns (bhikṣuṇī), novices (śrāmaṇera), and all Vinaya institutions.

---

## 二、化教详解 · The Doctrinal Teaching in Detail

### 2.1 化教的内涵 · Content of the Doctrinal Teaching

化教涵盖了佛教的所有**教理体系**，其核心功能是引导修行者理解宇宙人生的真理，从而获得觉悟。化教的内容包括：

The Doctrinal Teaching encompasses all **doctrinal systems** of Buddhism. Its primary function is to guide practitioners toward understanding the truth of existence and thereby attain awakening. Its content includes:

- **四谛**（苦、集、灭、道）：佛教的基础教理
  **Four Noble Truths** (suffering, origin, cessation, path): Buddhism's foundational doctrine

- **十二因缘**：生死轮回的因果法则
  **Twelve Links of Dependent Origination**: The causal law governing saṃsāra

- **三学**（戒、定、慧）中的定学和慧学部分
  The **meditation** (samādhi) and **wisdom** (prajñā) portions of the Threefold Training

- **六度**（布施、持戒、忍辱、精进、禅定、般若）
  **Six Pāramitās** (giving, morality, patience, effort, meditation, wisdom)

- **中观、唯识、如来藏**等大乘义理体系
  **Madhyamaka, Yogācāra, Tathāgatagarbha** and other Mahāyāna philosophical systems

### 2.2 化教的适用范围 · Scope of the Doctrinal Teaching

化教适用于**一切修行者**，无论是在家还是出家，无论声闻还是菩萨。道宣强调，化教的目的是开启智慧、引导觉悟，这是所有佛教修行者的共同目标。

The Doctrinal Teaching applies to **all practitioners** — lay and ordained, Śrāvaka and Bodhisattva alike. Daoxuan emphasized that the purpose of doctrinal teaching is to open wisdom and guide awakening, a goal common to all Buddhist practitioners.

---

## 三、制教详解 · The Regulatory Teaching in Detail

### 3.1 制教的内涵 · Content of the Regulatory Teaching

制教专指佛陀为出家僧团制定的**行为规范**，其核心功能是维护僧团的清净和秩序。制教的内容包括：

The Regulatory Teaching refers specifically to the **behavioral norms** established by the Buddha for the monastic community. Its primary function is to maintain the purity and order of the saṅgha. Its content includes:

- **波罗夷**（pārājika）：最严重的犯戒行为，导致逐出僧团
  **Pārājika**: The gravest offenses, resulting in expulsion from the saṅgha

- **僧残**（saṅghāvaśeṣa）：严重犯戒，需要僧团处分
  **Saṅghāvaśeṣa**: Serious offenses requiring saṅgha disciplinary action

- **波逸提**（pāyantika/pācittika）：中等程度的犯戒
  **Pācittika**: Offenses of moderate severity

- **提舍尼**（pratideśanīya）：需要忏悔的犯戒
  **Pratideśanīya**: Offenses requiring confession

- **突吉罗**（duṣkṛta）：轻微的犯戒
  **Duṣkṛta**: Minor offenses

- **众学法**（śaikṣa-dharma）：日常行为准则
  **Śaikṣa-dharma**: Rules of daily conduct

### 3.2 制教的适用范围 · Scope of the Regulatory Teaching

制教主要适用于**出家僧众**。道宣指出，制教的制定有其特定的因缘背景——佛陀根据僧团中发生的具体事件而随机制定戒条（"随犯随制"），因此每一条戒都有其特定的历史情境。

The Regulatory Teaching applies primarily to **ordained monastics**. Daoxuan noted that the regulations were established in response to specific circumstances — the Buddha formulated rules as situations arose ("随犯随制"), so each precept has its particular historical context.

## 四、两者的关系 · The Relationship Between the Two

### 4.1 不可偏废 · Neither Can Be Neglected

道宣最核心的主张是：化教与制教**不可偏废**。

Daoxuan's central insistence is that the two teachings **must not be neglected in favor of one another**:

- **有化无制**：仅有教理而无戒律，则修行失去基础，如同"无根之木"。僧团没有规范，修行者的行为失去约束，教理再好也无法落实。
  **Doctrine without regulation**: Teachings without discipline lose their foundation, like "a tree without roots." Without norms for the saṅgha, practitioners' conduct lacks constraint, and no matter how excellent the doctrine, it cannot be implemented.

- **有制无化**：仅有戒律而无教理，则戒律沦为形式主义。修行者只知道遵守条文而不理解其精神，持戒变成了机械的行为。
  **Regulation without doctrine**: Discipline without teachings degenerates into formalism. Practitioners merely follow rules without understanding their spirit, and precept-keeping becomes mechanical behavior.

### 4.2 相辅相成 · Mutually Complementary

化教与制教的关系是**相辅相成**的：

The relationship between the two is **mutually complementary**:

| 维度 | 化教 | 制教 |
|------|------|------|
| **功能** | 开启智慧，引导觉悟 | 规范行为，维护清净 |
| **对象** | 一切修行者 | 主要为出家僧众 |
| **内容** | 教理义学 | 戒律条文 |
| **目的** | 理解真理 | 规范行为 |
| **性质** | 理论性 | 实践性 |

| Dimension | Doctrinal | Regulatory |
|-----------|-----------|------------|
| **Function** | Open wisdom, guide awakening | Regulate conduct, maintain purity |
| **Audience** | All practitioners | Primarily ordained monastics |
| **Content** | Doctrinal exposition | Vinaya rules |
| **Purpose** | Understanding truth | Norming behavior |
| **Nature** | Theoretical | Practical |

### 4.3 在修行体系中的位置 · Position in the Practice System

在佛教的三学（戒、定、慧）框架中：

Within the framework of the Threefold Training (戒、定、慧):

- **戒学**属于**制教**范畴——规范身口二业的行为
  **Morality** (śīla) belongs to the **Regulatory Teaching** — governing bodily and verbal conduct

- **定学和慧学**属于**化教**范畴——引导心的修炼和觉悟
  **Meditation** (samādhi) and **wisdom** (prajñā) belong to the **Doctrinal Teaching** — guiding mental cultivation and awakening

道宣通过化制二教的框架，强调了戒律（制教）与教理（化教）在修行体系中的**同等重要地位**。

Through the Two Teachings framework, Daoxuan emphasized the **equal importance** of discipline (Regulatory Teaching) and doctrine (Doctrinal Teaching) within the practice system.

## 五、与其他判教体系的比较 · Comparison with Other Hermeneutical Systems

### 5.1 与天台判教的比较 · Compared to Tiantai Hermeneutics

天台宗的智顗提出了复杂的"五时八教"判教体系，主要对教理（化教的内容）进行分类。道宣的化制二教则从另一个维度——教理与戒律的关系——进行判教。两者不矛盾，而是互补的：天台的判教侧重教理的深浅层次，道宣的判教侧重教理与戒律的功能区分。

Zhiyi of the Tiantai school proposed the complex "Five Periods and Eight Teachings" hermeneutical system, primarily classifying doctrinal content. Daoxuan's Two Teachings framework operates on a different dimension — the relationship between doctrine and discipline. The two are not contradictory but complementary: Tiantai classification focuses on the depth and levels of doctrine, while Daoxuan's focuses on the functional distinction between doctrine and discipline.

### 5.2 与华严判教的比较 · Compared to Huayan Hermeneutics

华严宗法藏提出了"五教十宗"的判教体系，同样侧重于教理的分类。道宣的贡献在于：**他是唯一系统性地将戒律纳入判教框架的思想家**。

Fazang of the Huayan school proposed the "Five Teachings and Ten Schools" framework, also focused on doctrinal classification. Daoxuan's distinctive contribution: **he is the only thinker to systematically integrate Vinaya into a hermeneutical framework**.

## 六、当代意义 · Contemporary Significance

### 6.1 对佛教教育的启示 · Implications for Buddhist Education

化制二教的框架对当代佛教教育仍有重要启示：佛学院的教育不应仅侧重教理（化教），也不应仅侧重戒律实践（制教），而应两者并重。

The Two Teachings framework offers important guidance for contemporary Buddhist education: Buddhist academy curricula should not focus exclusively on doctrine (Doctrinal Teaching) or exclusively on disciplinary practice (Regulatory Teaching), but must emphasize both equally.

### 6.2 对僧团管理的启示 · Implications for Saṅgha Management

在僧团管理层面，化制二教提醒我们：制度建设（制教）和精神引导（化教）不可偏废。仅有严格的规章制度而无精神内涵，僧团将变成官僚机构；仅有精神教导而无制度保障，僧团将失去秩序。

At the level of saṅgha governance, the Two Teachings remind us that institutional development (Regulatory Teaching) and spiritual guidance (Doctrinal Teaching) must not be neglected. Strict regulations without spiritual substance turn the saṅgha into a bureaucracy; spiritual instruction without institutional safeguards lead the saṅgha to lose order.

### 6.3 对世俗组织的启示 · Implications for Secular Organizations

化制二教的框架可以类比到现代组织管理中"文化建设"与"制度建设"的关系：

The Two Teachings framework can be analogized to the relationship between "culture-building" and "institution-building" in modern organizational management:

- **化教** ≈ 企业文化、价值观教育、愿景使命
  **Doctrinal Teaching** ≈ Corporate culture, values education, vision and mission

- **制教** ≈ 规章制度、行为准则、合规管理
  **Regulatory Teaching** ≈ Rules and regulations, codes of conduct, compliance management

两者相辅相成的原则，对任何类型的组织管理都具有普遍意义。

The principle of their mutual complementarity has universal relevance for organizational governance of any kind.

## 七、常见误读 · Common Misreadings

1. **"化教高于制教"** → 道宣明确反对这种等级化理解。两者是功能不同而非层次不同。
   "The Doctrinal Teaching is superior to the Regulatory" → Daoxuan explicitly rejected this hierarchical reading. The two differ in function, not in level.

2. **"制教只适用于声闻乘"** → 虽然制教的内容来自声闻律，但道宣通过"四分通大乘"论证了其在菩萨道中的有效性。
   "The Regulatory Teaching applies only to the Śrāvaka vehicle" → Although its content derives from Śrāvaka Vinaya, Daoxuan's Dharmaguptaka-as-Mahāyāna thesis established its validity for the Bodhisattva path.

3. **"化制二教是僵硬的二分法"** → 道宣强调的是两者的互动关系，而非僵硬的对立。戒律的持守需要教理的理解来指导，教理的学习需要戒律的实践来落实。
   "The Two Teachings are a rigid dichotomy" → Daoxuan emphasized their dynamic interaction, not rigid opposition. Precept observance requires doctrinal understanding for guidance; doctrinal study requires disciplinary practice for implementation.
