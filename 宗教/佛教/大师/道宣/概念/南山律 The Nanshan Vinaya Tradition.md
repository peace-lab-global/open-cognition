# 南山律 · The Nanshan Vinaya Tradition (Nánshān Lǜ)

> "南山一宗，传持千载，律学正统，尽在斯矣。"
> "The single Nanshan school, transmitted for a thousand years — the orthodox lineage of Vinaya studies resides entirely herein."

---

## 一、概念定义 · Definition

**南山律**（Nánshān Lǜ）是以道宣为祖师、以终南山为基地发展起来的律学传统的总称。它是中国佛教**律宗**的核心传承，也是此后一千三百余年汉传佛教戒律学的唯一正统。"南山"之名，源于道宣长期驻锡终南山丰德寺和净业寺。

The **Nanshan Vinaya** (Nánshān Lǜ) designates the Vinaya tradition that developed around Daoxuan as patriarch, centered on Mount Zhongnan (终南山). It is the core lineage of the Chinese Buddhist **Vinaya School** (律宗) and became, for over thirteen centuries thereafter, the sole orthodox tradition of Vinaya studies in East Asian Buddhism. The name "Nanshan" (Southern Mountain) derives from Daoxuan's long residence at Fengde and Jingye Monasteries on Mount Zhongnan.

---

## 二、历史形成 · Historical Formation

### 2.1 道宣之前的律学 · Vinaya Studies Before Daoxuan

在道宣之前，中国佛教的律学研究已经经历了数百年的发展。自鸠摩罗什（Kumārajīva）和佛陀耶舍（Buddhayaśas）翻译律藏以来，中国出现了三大律学流派：

Before Daoxuan, Chinese Buddhist Vinaya studies had already developed over several centuries. Since the translation of the Vinaya canon by Kumārajīva and Buddhayaśas, three major Vinaya currents emerged in China:

1. **《十诵律》**（Sarvāstivāda Vinaya）——早期最流行的律典
   **Sarvāstivāda Vinaya** (十诵律) — the most popular Vinaya text in the early period

2. **《四分律》**（Dharmaguptaka Vinaya）——后来逐渐取代《十诵律》
   **Dharmaguptaka Vinaya** (四分律) — gradually superseded the Sarvāstivāda Vinaya

3. **《五分律》**（Mahīśāsaka Vinaya）——影响较小
   **Mahīśāsaka Vinaya** (五分律) — less influential

北魏时期的慧光（468–537）是《四分律》研究的重要先驱，他首先提出《四分律》可通大乘的观点，为道宣后来系统化这一理论奠定了基础。

Huiguang (468–537) of the Northern Wei was a crucial pioneer in *Dharmaguptaka Vinaya* studies; he was the first to propose that the *Dharmaguptaka Vinaya* could extend to the Mahāyāna, laying the groundwork for Daoxuan's later systematization.

### 2.2 三足鼎立：唐初的三大律宗 · Three Schools of the Early Tang

道宣所处时代，律学界形成了三大流派并立的格局：

During Daoxuan's era, the Vinaya scholarly world was characterized by three competing schools:

| 宗派 | 创始人 | 基地 | 戒体观 | 核心立场 |
|------|--------|------|--------|----------|
| **南山宗** | 道宣（596–667） | 终南山 | 非色非心（唯识种子说） | 四分通大乘 |
| **相部宗** | 法砺（569–635） | 邺城 | 非色非心（成实论） | 四分属声闻 |
| **东塔宗** | 怀素（625–698） | 长安 | 色法 | 批判南山与相部 |

| School | Founder | Base | Precept-Substance View | Core Position |
|--------|---------|------|------------------------|---------------|
| **Nanshan** | Daoxuan (596–667) | Mt. Zhongnan | Neither form nor mind (Yogācāra seeds) | Dharmaguptaka extends to Mahāyāna |
| **Xiangbu** | Fali (569–635) | Yecheng | Neither form nor mind (Satyasiddhi) | Dharmaguptaka belongs to Śrāvaka |
| **Dongta** | Huaisu (625–698) | Chang'an | Material dharma | Critiques both Nanshan and Xiangbu |

### 2.3 南山宗的胜出 · The Triumph of the Nanshan School

经过唐代数百年的争论，南山宗最终胜出，成为中国律学的唯一正统。其胜出的原因包括：

After centuries of debate during the Tang dynasty, the Nanshan school ultimately prevailed as the sole orthodox Vinaya tradition in China. The reasons include:

1. **理论的完备性**：南山宗以唯识学为基础，建立了一套完整的戒体理论，既避免了有部的"色法"说之粗，也避免了简单心理化解释之浅。
   **Theoretical completeness**: The Nanshan school established a comprehensive precept-substance theory grounded in Yogācāra, avoiding both the crudeness of the Sarvāstivādin "material dharma" view and the shallowness of simplistic mentalist interpretations.

2. **大乘的兼容性**：南山宗的"四分通大乘"理论解决了汉传佛教的核心困境——大乘法门与声闻戒律之间的矛盾。
   **Mahāyāna compatibility**: The Dharmaguptaka-as-Mahāyāna thesis resolved the core dilemma of Chinese Buddhism — the tension between Mahāyāna doctrine and Śrāvaka Vinaya.

3. **体系的完整性**：道宣的"南山三大部"提供了前所未有的律学百科全书，使后世律师有了一个系统化的参考框架。
   **Systematic completeness**: Daoxuan's "Three Great Works of Nanshan" provided an unprecedented Vinaya encyclopedia, giving subsequent Vinaya masters a systematic reference framework.

4. **实践的可操作性**：南山律不仅理论精深，而且在僧团实践中具有很强的可操作性。
   **Practical operability**: Nanshan Vinaya was not only theoretically profound but also highly practical in saṅgha application.

## 三、核心教义 · Core Doctrines

南山律的核心教义可以概括为以下几点：

The core doctrines of the Nanshan Vinaya can be summarized as follows:

### 3.1 以《四分律》为根本 · Dharmaguptaka Vinaya as Foundation

南山律以法藏部的《四分律》为根本依据，但通过创造性的诠释使其适应大乘佛教的需要。

The Nanshan tradition takes the Dharmaguptaka *Sifenlü* (四分律) as its foundational text, but through creative interpretation adapts it to the needs of Mahāyāna Buddhism.

### 3.2 戒体论 · Precept-Substance Theory

以唯识学的种子说解释戒体的本质——"非色非心"的功能性存在。这是南山律区别于其他两宗的根本特征。

Explaining the nature of precept-substance through Yogācāra seed theory — a "neither form nor mind" functional reality. This is the fundamental characteristic distinguishing Nanshan from the other two schools.

→ 详见 [[precept-substance|戒体论]]

### 3.3 化制二教 · Two Teachings Framework

将佛陀教法分为化教（教理）和制教（戒律），强调两者不可偏废。

Classifying the Buddha's teachings into Doctrinal (教理) and Regulatory (戒律), insisting that neither can be neglected.

→ 详见 [[teaching-regulation|化制二教]]

### 3.4 四分通大乘 · Dharmaguptaka as Mahāyāna

论证声闻律可以通向大乘菩萨道，为汉传佛教的戒律实践提供大乘合法性。

Demonstrating that Śrāvaka Vinaya can extend to the Bodhisattva path, providing Mahāyāna legitimacy for Chinese Buddhist precept practice.

→ 详见 [[quarter-vinaya|四分通大乘]]

## 四、传承谱系 · Lineage

### 4.1 唐代传承 · Tang Dynasty Transmission

道宣 → 周秀（道宣弟子） → 后续律师

Daoxuan → Zhouxiu (Daoxuan's disciple) → subsequent Vinaya masters

### 4.2 宋代复兴 · Song Dynasty Revival

- **允堪**（1005–1061）：宋代南山律的重要传承者，著有《行事钞会正记》。
  **Yunkan** (1005–1061): An important Song-era transmitter of Nanshan Vinaya, author of *Commentary on the Xingshi Chao*.

- **元照**（1048–1116）：宋代律学的集大成者，著有《行事钞资持记》，被后世视为南山律的正统注释。
  **Yuanzhao** (1048–1116): The great synthesizer of Song-era Vinaya studies, author of the *Zichi Ji* commentary on the *Xingshi Chao*, regarded by later generations as the orthodox commentary on Nanshan Vinaya.

### 4.3 明清传承 · Ming-Qing Transmission

- **如馨**（1541–1615）：明代律宗复兴者，在南京古林寺传戒。
  **Ruxin** (1541–1615): Ming dynasty Vinaya revivalist, transmitted precepts at Gulin Monastery in Nanjing.

- **读体**（1601–1679）：明末清初律学大师，著有《毗尼止持会集》等重要著作。
  **Duti** (1601–1679): Late Ming/early Qing Vinaya master, author of important works including *Vinaya Compendium on Cessation and Observance*.

- **见月**（1601–1679）：与读体同时代的律学大师，在宝华山传戒。
  **Jianyue** (1601–1679): A contemporary Vinaya master of Duti, transmitted precepts at Baohua Mountain.

### 4.4 近代复兴 · Modern Revival

- **弘一法师**（1880–1942）：原名李叔同，近代最重要的律学大师。他以弘传南山律为己任，著有《四分律比丘戒相表记》《南山律在家备览》等，使南山律在现代社会得以延续。
  **Master Hongyi** (1880–1942): Originally named Li Shutong, the most important modern Vinaya master. He dedicated himself to reviving and propagating Nanshan Vinaya, authoring works such as *Tabular Presentation of the Bhikṣu Precepts* and *Nanshan Vinaya Handbook for Laypersons*, ensuring the tradition's continuity into the modern era.

## 五、对东亚佛教的影响 · Impact on East Asian Buddhism

### 5.1 日本 · Japan

唐代高僧**鉴真**（688–763）历经六次东渡的艰辛历程，终于将南山律传至日本。他在奈良建立了唐招提寺，创立了日本律宗（Ritsu school），对日本佛教的戒律制度产生了深远影响。日本佛教的受戒制度至今仍在很大程度上依循南山律的传统。

The Tang dynasty high monk **Jianzhen** (688–763, known as Ganjin in Japanese) endured six arduous attempts to cross the sea before finally transmitting Nanshan Vinaya to Japan. He established Tōshōdai-ji in Nara and founded the Japanese Ritsu school, profoundly influencing the ordination system of Japanese Buddhism. The ordination system of Japanese Buddhism still largely follows the Nanshan tradition today.

### 5.2 朝鲜半岛 · Korean Peninsula

南山律通过唐代的文化交流传至新罗，对朝鲜半岛佛教的戒律传统也产生了重要影响。

Nanshan Vinaya reached Silla through Tang-era cultural exchange and significantly influenced the Vinaya tradition of Korean Buddhism.

### 5.3 越南 · Vietnam

汉传佛教的戒律传统（以南山律为主体）也传播至越南，成为越南佛教戒律制度的基础。

The Chinese Buddhist Vinaya tradition (centered on Nanshan Vinaya) also spread to Vietnam, becoming the foundation of the Vietnamese Buddhist Vinaya system.

---

## 六、当代传承 · Contemporary Transmission

### 6.1 汉传佛教 · Chinese Buddhism

当代汉传佛教的受戒仪轨和僧团管理，仍然在很大程度上依循南山律的传统。三坛大戒（沙弥戒、比丘戒、菩萨戒）的传戒仪式，其基本框架可以追溯到道宣的律学体系。

Contemporary Chinese Buddhist ordination rituals and saṅgha management still largely follow the Nanshan tradition. The framework of the Three Platform Great Precepts (novice precepts, bhikṣu precepts, bodhisattva precepts) can be traced back to Daoxuan's Vinaya system.

### 6.2 台湾佛教 · Taiwanese Buddhism

台湾佛教界对南山律的传承尤为重视。当代多位法师致力于南山律的研究和弘传，包括日常法师、果清律师等。

The Taiwanese Buddhist community places particular emphasis on the Nanshan tradition. Several contemporary masters have dedicated themselves to Nanshan Vinaya research and propagation.

### 6.3 学术研究 · Academic Research

在国际学术界，南山律研究也日益受到重视。日本学者（如佐藤达玄）、中国学者（如劳政武、王建光）和西方学者都在从不同角度研究南山律的历史和理论。

In international scholarship, Nanshan Vinaya studies have gained increasing attention. Japanese scholars (such as Sato Tatugen), Chinese scholars (such as Lao Zhengwu and Wang Jianguang), and Western scholars are all studying Nanshan Vinaya's history and theory from various perspectives.

## 七、南山律的独特贡献 · Unique Contributions of Nanshan Vinaya

1. **第一次系统化了中国律学**：道宣是第一个将散乱的律学研究整合为完整体系的思想家。
   **First systematization of Chinese Vinaya studies**: Daoxuan was the first thinker to integrate scattered Vinaya research into a comprehensive system.

2. **实现了声闻律的大乘化**：通过"四分通大乘"的论证，使声闻律获得了大乘的合法性。
   **Achieved the Mahāyāna-ization of Śrāvaka Vinaya**: Through the Dharmaguptaka-as-Mahāyāna thesis, Śrāvaka Vinaya gained Mahāyāna legitimacy.

3. **建立了戒体论的唯识学基础**：以唯识种子说解释戒体，提供了一个既非唯物也非唯心的中间路径。
   **Established the Yogācāra foundation of precept-substance theory**: Using seed theory to explain precept-substance, providing a middle path between materialism and mentalism.

4. **影响了整个东亚佛教的戒律传统**：南山律不仅影响了中国，还影响了日本、朝鲜和越南的佛教戒律制度。
   **Influenced the Vinaya tradition of all East Asian Buddhism**: Nanshan Vinaya influenced not only China but also the Buddhist Vinaya systems of Japan, Korea, and Vietnam.
