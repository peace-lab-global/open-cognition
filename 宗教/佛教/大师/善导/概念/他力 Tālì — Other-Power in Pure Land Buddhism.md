# 他力 Other-Power

> "一切善恶凡夫得生者，莫不皆乘阿弥陀佛大愿业力为增上缘也。"——善导《观经四帖疏》
> "All beings, whether good or evil, who attain rebirth do so without exception by riding upon Amitābha Buddha's great vow-karmic power as the dominant condition." — Shandao

---

## 定义 Definition

他力（tālì）是善导净土思想中最核心的概念之一，指阿弥陀佛以其本愿力主动救度一切众生的力量。与"自力"（zìlì，修行者依靠自身的戒定慧修行来断惑证真）相对，他力强调的是：往生成佛的根本力量不是来自修行者自身，而是来自阿弥陀佛在因地修行时所发的四十八大愿——尤其是第十八愿（本愿）——所积累的无量功德。这一概念彻底改变了佛教修行的基本范式：从"以自力修行达到觉悟"转变为"以信心领受佛的救度"。

Other-Power (tālì) is one of the most central concepts in Shandao's Pure Land thought. It refers to the power by which Amitābha Buddha actively saves all beings through his Original Vow-power. Contrasted with "self-power" (zìlì — the practitioner's reliance on one's own practice of precepts, meditation, and wisdom to eradicate delusions and realize truth), Other-Power emphasizes that the fundamental power for rebirth and Buddhahood does not come from the practitioner, but from the immeasurable merit accumulated through Amitābha's forty-eight great vows — especially the Eighteenth Vow (the Original Vow) — made during his causal practice. This concept radically transformed the basic paradigm of Buddhist practice: from "attaining awakening through one's own effort" to "receiving the Buddha's salvation through faith."

## 他力的经典依据 Scriptural Basis

### 阿弥陀佛四十八愿 Amitābha's Forty-Eight Vows

他力思想的根本经典依据是《无量寿经》（Larger Sukhāvatīvyūha Sūtra）中记载的阿弥陀佛四十八愿。其中，与善导思想关系最为密切的是：

The fundamental scriptural basis for Other-Power thought is the forty-eight vows of Amitābha recorded in the Larger Sukhāvatīvyūha Sūtra. Among these, the vows most closely related to Shandao's thought are:

**第十八愿（本愿 / The Eighteenth Vow, the Original Vow）：**
"设我得佛，十方众生，至心信乐，欲生我国，乃至十念，若不生者，不取正觉。"
"If, when I attain Buddhahood, the beings of the ten directions who sincerely and joyfully entrust themselves to me, wish to be born in my land, and recite my Name even ten times, should not be born there, may I not attain perfect Enlightenment."

善导将此愿解读为整个净土法门的核心。阿弥陀佛已经成佛，这意味着他的誓言已经实现——因此，一切至心信乐、愿生净土、称念佛名的众生，往生的保证已经由佛的本愿力确立。

Shandao interpreted this vow as the heart of the entire Pure Land Dharma gate. Amitābha has already attained Buddhahood, meaning his pledge has been fulfilled — therefore, the assurance of rebirth for all beings who sincerely entrust, aspire to the Pure Land, and recite the Name has already been established by the Buddha's vow-power.

**第十一愿（必至灭度愿 / The Eleventh Vow, the Vow of Assured Nirvāṇa）：**
"设我得佛，国中天人不住定聚、必至灭度者，不取正觉。"
"If, when I attain Buddhahood, the devas and humans in my land do not dwell in the stage of the truly settled and assuredly attain nirvāṇa, may I not attain perfect Enlightenment."

这一愿保证：往生净土者不会退转，必将成佛。这使得净土不仅是暂时的安乐处，而是通向究竟成佛的保证道路。

This vow guarantees that those reborn in the Pure Land will never regress and will assuredly attain Buddhahood. This makes the Pure Land not merely a temporary haven of bliss, but an assured path to ultimate Buddhahood.

## 他力与自力的关系 Other-Power and Self-Power

### 自力的局限 The Limits of Self-Power

善导并不否定自力修行的价值，但他认为在"末法时期"（Dharma-Ending Age），众生根机低劣，烦恼深重，单凭自力修行几乎不可能断惑证真。他在《观经四帖疏》中描述众生为"罪恶生死凡夫，旷劫以来常没常流转"——从无始以来就在生死轮回中流转，无法自拔。在这种状况下，唯有依靠佛的他力才能获得解脱。

Shandao did not deny the value of self-power practice, but he held that in the "Dharma-Ending Age," beings have inferior capacities and deep afflictions, making it virtually impossible to eradicate delusions through self-power alone. In his Commentary, he describes beings as "sinful ordinary beings in birth and death, sinking and revolving since beginningless kalpas" — trapped in saṃsāra from time without beginning, unable to extricate themselves. In this condition, only reliance on the Buddha's Other-Power can bring liberation.

### 他力不是否定修行 Other-Power Does Not Negate Practice

一个常见的误解是：他力思想意味着完全放弃修行。善导明确反对这种理解。他力不是"不修行"，而是修行的基础和动力来源不同——修行的动力不是来自自己的意志力，而是来自佛的加持力。称名念佛本身就是修行的体现，但这种修行的功效不是由行者自身产生的，而是由佛的本愿力赋予的。

A common misunderstanding is that Other-Power thought means abandoning practice entirely. Shandao explicitly opposed this interpretation. Other-Power is not "non-practice," but rather a different foundation and source of motivation for practice — the driving force comes not from one's own willpower but from the Buddha's empowering grace. Verbal nianfo recitation is itself the manifestation of practice, but its efficacy is not produced by the practitioner — it is bestowed by the Buddha's vow-power.

### 信心即是他力的领受 Faith as the Reception of Other-Power

在善导的体系中，"信心"（至心信乐）是他力发生作用的关键环节。信心不是行者自己"制造"的，而是佛的他力在行者心中引发的回应。善导称之为"佛力所使"——是被佛的力量所推动而产生的信心。这一观点在日本净土真宗中被亲鸾发展为"信心本愿"——认为信心本身就是佛赐予的，不是行者的主观选择。

In Shandao's system, "faith" (sincere and joyful entrusting) is the crucial link through which Other-Power takes effect. Faith is not something the practitioner "manufactures" by themselves, but a response evoked in the practitioner's mind by the Buddha's Other-Power. Shandao calls this "moved by the Buddha's power" — faith arising through being propelled by the Buddha's force. This view was developed by Shinran in Japanese Jōdo Shinshū into the "Vow of Faith" — holding that faith itself is bestowed by the Buddha, not a subjective choice of the practitioner.

## 他力的思想谱系 Intellectual Genealogy of Other-Power

### 昙鸾的开创 Tanluan's Pioneering Work

他力思想的中国先驱是昙鸾（476–542）。昙鸾在《往生论注》中引用龙树《十住毗婆沙论》的"难易二道"说，提出在末法时代，众生应该选择"易行道"——即依靠阿弥陀佛的本愿力往生净土，而非选择"难行道"——依靠自力在五浊恶世中断惑证真。昙鸾用"水路"（易行道，如乘船顺流而下）和"陆路"（难行道，如徒步跋涉）的比喻来说明两种修行路径的差别。

The Chinese pioneer of Other-Power thought was Tanluan (476–542). In his Commentary on the Treatise on the Pure Land, Tanluan cited Nāgārjuna's Treatise on the Ten Stages on the "Easy and Difficult Paths," proposing that in the Dharma-Ending Age, beings should choose the "Easy Path" — relying on Amitābha's vow-power for rebirth — rather than the "Difficult Path" of relying on self-power amid the five turbidities. Tanluan used the metaphor of a "water route" (Easy Path, like riding a boat downstream) versus a "land route" (Difficult Path, like trekking on foot) to illustrate the difference.

### 道绰的发展 Daochuo's Development

道绰（562–645）继承昙鸾的他力思想，在《安乐集》中进一步论证了末法时代他力念佛的必要性。道绰提出"时教相应"的原则——修行方法必须与时代特征相适应。在正法和像法时代，众生根机较高，自力修行可行；在末法时代，唯有他力念佛才是当机之法。

Daochuo (562–645) inherited Tanluan's Other-Power thought and further argued for the necessity of Other-Power nianfo in the Dharma-Ending Age in his Collection on the Land of Bliss. Daochuo proposed the principle of "teaching corresponding to the age" — practice methods must match the characteristics of the era. In the ages of True Dharma and Semblance Dharma, beings had higher capacities and self-power practice was viable; in the Dharma-Ending Age, only Other-Power nianfo is the appropriate teaching.

### 善导的集大成 Shandao's Culmination

善导在昙鸾、道绰的基础上，将他力思想发展为一个完整的理论体系。他的贡献在于：（1）以"望佛本愿"为诠释原则，系统论证了称名念佛是他力的具体体现；（2）以"凡夫入报"为结论，确立了他力救度的终极范围——不仅是化土，而是真实的报土；（3）以"正行杂行"为判分标准，清晰界定了哪些修行是他力的正道，哪些是自力的杂行。

Building upon the foundations of Tanluan and Daochuo, Shandao developed Other-Power thought into a complete theoretical system. His contributions include: (1) establishing "looking to the Buddha's Original Vow" as the hermeneutical principle, systematically demonstrating that verbal nianfo is the concrete expression of Other-Power; (2) concluding with "ordinary beings entering the reward land," establishing the ultimate scope of Other-Power salvation — not merely the transformation land, but the true reward land; (3) using the "primary and miscellaneous practices" classification to clearly define which practices follow the Other-Power path and which are self-power diversions.

## 他力与比较宗教学 Other-Power in Comparative Religion

善导的他力思想与多个宗教传统中的"恩典"概念形成了有意义的对话：

Shandao's Other-Power thought enters meaningful dialogue with "grace" concepts in multiple religious traditions:

- **基督教 · 因信称义**（Justification by Faith）：保罗和马丁·路德强调人不能靠善行得救，唯有凭信心领受上帝的恩典。这与善导的"他力信心"说有着显著的结构性相似——两者都否定了自力救赎的可能性，都强调信心的核心地位。
- **Christianity · Justification by Faith**: Paul and Martin Luther emphasized that humans cannot be saved by good works, only by receiving God's grace through faith. This bears a striking structural similarity to Shandao's "Other-Power faith" — both deny the possibility of self-power salvation and emphasize the centrality of faith.

- **伊斯兰教苏菲派 · 神恩**（Sufi Divine Grace / Tawfīq）：苏菲传统认为，灵性进步最终依赖于真主的恩典（tawfīq），而非修行者自身的努力。修行者需要做的是清除障碍、开放心灵，让神恩流入。
- **Sufi Islam · Divine Grace (Tawfīq)**: The Sufi tradition holds that spiritual progress ultimately depends on God's grace (tawfīq), not the practitioner's own effort. The practitioner's task is to clear obstacles and open the heart to allow divine grace to flow in.

- **印度教毗湿奴派 · 普拉沙达**（Hindu Vaiṣṇava Prasāda）：毗湿奴派强调通过完全皈依（prapatti / śaraṇāgati）领受毗湿奴的恩典（prasāda），与善导的"信受弥陀救度"形成平行。
- **Hindu Vaiṣṇava Prasāda**: The Vaiṣṇava tradition emphasizes receiving Viṣṇu's grace (prasāda) through complete surrender (prapatti / śaraṇāgati), paralleling Shandao's "accepting Amitābha's salvation."
