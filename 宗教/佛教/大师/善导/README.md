# 善导大师 Shandao (613–681)

> "一心专念弥陀名号，行住坐卧，不问时节久近，念念不舍。"
> "Single-mindedly recite the Name of Amitābha exclusively — walking, standing, sitting, or lying down, regardless of how long or short the time, never ceasing even for a single thought."
> — 善导《观经四帖疏》

---

## 引言 Introduction

善导（Shàndǎo, 613–681）是中国唐代高僧，被尊为净土宗的实际创立者。他以革命性的诠释——往生净土不需要高深的禅定或广博的学问，只需至诚称念"南无阿弥陀佛"——将佛教从精英修行的象牙塔中解放出来，使之成为普通百姓触手可及的信仰道路。在中国佛教史上，善导的影响力无出其右：据载他在长安弘法期间，城中"屠夫改业、肉铺关门"，百万人受其感化而念佛。他的思想经由法然、亲鸾传入日本，催生了日本净土宗和净土真宗，至今仍是东亚佛教最深厚的精神遗产之一。

Shandao (613–681) was an eminent monk of China's Tang dynasty, widely honored as the de facto founder of the Pure Land school. Through a revolutionary interpretation — that rebirth in the Pure Land requires neither profound meditative absorption nor encyclopedic learning, but only the sincere recitation of "Namo Amituofo" — he liberated Buddhism from the ivory tower of elite monasticism and made it an accessible path for ordinary people. His influence on Chinese Buddhist history is unparalleled: tradition records that during his teaching years in Chang'an, "butchers changed their trade and meat shops closed," with a million people converted to nianfo recitation. His thought traveled to Japan through Hōnen and Shinran, giving rise to the Jōdo-shū and Jōdo Shinshū schools, and remains one of East Asia's deepest spiritual legacies.

## 生平 Biography

### 早年出家 Early Life and Ordination

善导生于613年，山东临淄人（一说安徽泗县人）。少年出家，初习《法华经》和《维摩经》。一日于寺院藏经中偶读《观无量寿佛经》（《观经》），深感净土法门之殊胜，遂发愿往生西方极乐世界。他开始修习观想念佛，日夜观想阿弥陀佛的庄严相好和极乐世界的种种清净。

Shandao was born in 613 in Linzi, Shandong (some sources cite Sixian, Anhui). He entered monastic life at a young age, initially studying the Lotus Sūtra and the Vimalakīrti Sūtra. One day, while browsing the monastery's scriptural collection, he encountered the Sūtra on the Contemplation of the Buddha of Immeasurable Life (the Contemplation Sūtra), and was deeply moved by the magnificence of the Pure Land Dharma gate. He vowed to be reborn in the Western Pure Land of Ultimate Bliss (Sukhāvatī) and began practicing contemplative nianfo — visualizing the庄严 attributes of Amitābha Buddha and the purity of his land day and night.

### 师从道绰 Studying under Daochuo

约641年，善导北上并州（今太原），参谒净土大德道绰（562–645）。道绰是昙鸾（476–542）思想的继承者，已建立起系统的他力念佛理论。在道绰门下，善导精进修学，深得"他力本愿"思想的精髓。道绰圆寂后，善导继承其法脉，并进一步发展了称名念佛的理论体系。

Around 641, Shandao traveled north to Bingzhou (modern Taiyuan) to study under the Pure Land master Daochuo (562–645). Daochuo was the inheritor of Tanluan's (476–542) thought and had already established a systematic theory of Other-Power nianfo. Under Daochuo's guidance, Shandao studied diligently and deeply grasped the essence of the "Other-Power Original Vow" teaching. After Daochuo's passing, Shandao inherited his Dharma lineage and further developed the theoretical framework of verbal nianfo recitation.

### 长安弘法 Teaching in Chang'an

善导晚年驻锡长安光明寺（一说慈恩寺），在此大规模弘扬净土法门。据《佛祖统纪》记载，善导在长安三十余年，"每入室长跪唱佛，非力竭不休"——每次进入佛堂便长跪称念佛号，不到精疲力竭不肯停歇。他甚至"出即为人说净土法"，逢人便劝念佛。传说他念佛一声，口中即出一道光明，因此被尊称为"光明和尚"。在他的感化下，长安城中念佛之风大盛，甚至出现了"家家弥陀佛"的盛况。

In his later years, Shandao resided at Guangming Temple (some sources say Ci'en Temple) in Chang'an, where he promoted the Pure Land teaching on a massive scale. According to the Fozu Tongji (Comprehensive Chronicle of the Buddhas and Patriarchs), during his thirty-plus years in Chang'an, "each time he entered the hall he would kneel and chant the Buddha's name, not stopping until his strength was exhausted." Outside the monastery, he would teach the Pure Land Dharma to anyone he met. Legend holds that each time he recited the Buddha's name, a ray of light issued from his mouth, earning him the honorific title "Monk of Light" (Guangming Heshang). Under his influence, nianfo practice swept Chang'an, and it was said that "every household recited Amitābha."

## 核心思想 Core Teachings

善导的净土思想可以概括为四大命题，它们彼此关联，构成一个完整的救度体系：

Shandao's Pure Land thought can be summarized in four major propositions, which are interrelated and constitute a complete system of salvation:

### 一、称名念佛 Verbal Nianfo Recitation

善导在《观经四帖疏》中提出，《观经》所教授的十六种观想法门中，前十三观属于"定善"（禅定之善），后三观（九品往生）属于"散善"（散心之善）。但佛陀真正要传达的核心信息是：上辈、中辈、下辈三类往生者，其"正定业"都是"称名念佛"——口称"南无阿弥陀佛"。

In his Commentary on the Contemplation Sūtra (Guān Jīng Sì Tiē Shū), Shandao argued that among the sixteen contemplation methods taught in the Sūtra, the first thirteen belong to "settled good" (meditative practice), while the final three (the nine grades of rebirth) belong to "scattered good" (practice with a scattered mind). But the Buddha's truly essential message, Shandao insisted, is that for all three grades of rebirth — superior, middling, and inferior — the "rightly established practice" (zhèngdìng yè) is verbal nianfo: the vocal recitation of "Namo Amituofo."

这一诠释的革命性在于：它否定了观想念佛的优先地位，将最简单的口称佛号提升为最高修行。任何人——不论学问高低、根器利钝——都可以通过称名念佛获得往生的保证。

The revolutionary nature of this interpretation lies in its displacement of contemplative nianfo from its privileged position, elevating the simplest vocal recitation to the highest practice. Anyone — regardless of educational attainment or spiritual capacity — can obtain the assurance of rebirth through verbal nianfo.

### 二、他力本愿 Other-Power and the Original Vow

善导思想的基石是阿弥陀佛的第十八愿（本愿）："设我得佛，十方众生，至心信乐，欲生我国，乃至十念，若不生者，不取正觉。"（如果我成佛时，十方众生至诚信心、欢喜信乐、愿生我国，乃至称念十声佛号，若不能往生，我就不取正觉。）

The cornerstone of Shandao's thought is Amitābha Buddha's Eighteenth Vow (the Original Vow): "If, when I attain Buddhahood, the beings of the ten directions who sincerely and joyfully entrust themselves to me, wish to be born in my land, and recite my Name even ten times, should not be born there, may I not attain perfect Enlightenment."

善导将此解读为：往生的力量完全来自阿弥陀佛的本愿力（他力），而非修行者自身的功德（自力）。修行者的任务不是积累功德，而是"至心信乐"——以真实的信心领受佛的救度。这与中国传统佛教中"自修自证"的主流路径形成了根本性的转向。

Shandao interpreted this to mean that the power for rebirth comes entirely from Amitābha's vow-power (Other-Power, tālì), not from the practitioner's own merit (self-power, zìlì). The practitioner's task is not to accumulate merit, but to "sincerely and joyfully entrust" — to receive the Buddha's salvation with genuine faith. This represented a fundamental departure from the mainstream Chinese Buddhist path of "self-cultivation and self-realization."

### 三、凡夫入报 Rebirth of Ordinary Beings in the True Reward Land

在善导之前的净土学者（如隋代慧远、吉藏等）多认为：凡夫只能往生阿弥陀佛的"化土"（临时化现的方便国土），只有高阶菩萨才能往生"报土"（佛因修行功德而感召的真实国土）。善导大胆地否定了这一区分，主张阿弥陀佛的极乐世界就是报土，而凡夫凭借佛的本愿力可以直接往生报土。

Pure Land scholars before Shandao (such as Huiyuan and Jizang of the Sui dynasty) generally held that ordinary beings could only be reborn in Amitābha's "transformation land" (a provisional, expedient Buddha-land), and that only advanced bodhisattvas could be reborn in the "reward land" (the true Buddha-land arising from the Buddha's accumulated merit). Shandao boldly rejected this distinction, asserting that Amitābha's Pure Land is itself the reward land, and that ordinary beings can be reborn directly into it through the power of the Buddha's Original Vow.

这一主张具有深刻的平等意义：它宣告净土法门不是低级方便，而是与最高佛果直接相通的究竟法门。

This assertion carries profound egalitarian significance: it declares that the Pure Land Dharma gate is not a lower expedient, but an ultimate teaching directly connected to the highest Buddhahood.

### 四、正行与杂行 Primary and Miscellaneous Practices

善导在《观经四帖疏》中将往生净土的修行分为"正行"与"杂行"两大类。正行是专门指向阿弥陀佛和极乐世界的修行，分为五种：

Shandao divided Pure Land practices into "primary practices" (zhèngxíng) and "miscellaneous practices" (záxíng). Primary practices are those specifically directed toward Amitābha and the Pure Land, and are divided into five:

1. **读诵正行** — 专门读诵净土三部经
2. **观察正行** — 专门观想极乐世界的庄严
3. **礼拜正行** — 专门礼拜阿弥陀佛
4. **称名正行** — 专门称念"南无阿弥陀佛"
5. **赞叹供养正行** — 专门赞叹供养阿弥陀佛

In these five, the fourth — verbal nianfo — occupies a special position as the "rightly established practice" (正定业), while the other four are "auxiliary practices" (助业). All other Buddhist practices (studying non-Pure Land sūtras, practicing non-Pure Land meditations, etc.) are classified as "miscellaneous practices" and are considered secondary to the primary practices.

其中第四种——称名——居于"正定业"的核心地位，其余四种为"助业"。其他一切佛教修行（研读非净土经典、修习非净土禅定等）都被归为"杂行"，居于次要地位。

## 历史影响 Historical Impact

### 中国 China

善导在世时，净土信仰已在民间广泛传播。其弟子怀感、净业等继续弘法。中唐以后，善导的著作成为净土学者的必读文献。宋代永明延寿（904–975）虽主张"禅净双修"，但深受善导称名思想的影响。明清时期，莲池祩宏（1535–1615）和印光（1861–1940）等大师均以善导为楷模，推动称名念佛成为汉传佛教最普遍的修行方式。

During Shandao's lifetime, Pure Land faith had already spread widely among common people. His disciples Huaigan, Jingye, and others continued to propagate the teaching. From the mid-Tang onward, Shandao's works became essential reading for Pure Land scholars. The Song dynasty master Yongming Yanshou (904–975), though advocating "dual cultivation of Chan and Pure Land," was deeply influenced by Shandao's verbal nianfo thought. In the Ming-Qing period, masters such as Lianchi Zhuhong (1535–1615) and Yinguang (1861–1940) took Shandao as their model, promoting verbal nianfo as the most widespread practice in Chinese Buddhism.

### 日本 Japan

善导思想对日本佛教的影响更为深远。法然（1133–1212）读善导《观经四帖疏》中"一心专念弥陀名号"一段而大悟，创立日本净土宗，奉善导为"净土宗高祖"。法然的弟子亲鸾（1173–1263）进一步发展他力思想，创立净土真宗，将善导的"他力本愿"推至极致。至今日本净土宗和净土真宗信徒超过两千万人，善导被尊为"弥陀化身"。

Shandao's influence on Japanese Buddhism was even more profound. Hōnen (1133–1212) experienced great awakening upon reading the passage "single-mindedly recite the Name of Amitābha exclusively" in Shandao's Commentary, and founded the Japanese Jōdo-shū (Pure Land School), honoring Shandao as the "High Patriarch of the Pure Land School." Hōnen's disciple Shinran (1173–1263) further developed the Other-Power teaching and founded Jōdo Shinshū (True Pure Land School), pushing Shandao's "Other-Power Original Vow" to its ultimate conclusion. Today, adherents of Japanese Pure Land schools number over twenty million, and Shandao is revered as an "incarnation of Amitābha."

## 当代意义 Contemporary Significance

善导的思想在当代仍然具有深刻的启示意义。从宗教社会学的角度看，善导是宗教大众化的典范——他证明了深刻的精神传统可以在保持教义深度的同时降低实践门槛。从心理学的角度看，"他力信心"思想与当代心理治疗中的"自我接纳"和"放下控制"的理念不谋而合。从比较宗教学的角度看，善导的"他力本愿"与基督教的"因信称义"和伊斯兰教苏菲派的"神恩"概念形成了有趣的对话空间。

Shandao's thought continues to offer profound insights today. From the perspective of religious sociology, he exemplifies the democratization of 宗教 — proving that a deep spiritual tradition can lower its practical threshold without sacrificing doctrinal depth. From a psychological perspective, the "faith in Other-Power" resonates with contemporary therapeutic concepts of self-acceptance and letting go of control. From the standpoint of comparative religion, Shandao's "Other-Power Original Vow" opens fascinating dialogue with Christianity's "justification by faith" and the Sufi concept of divine grace.

### 临终关怀与生死学 End-of-Life Care and Thanatology

善导的往生思想为当代临终关怀提供了独特的精神资源。在他的体系中，死亡不是终结，而是"往生"——从苦难的娑婆世界过渡到极乐净土的门槛。临终时，阿弥陀佛和观音、大势至两位菩萨会亲自前来迎接（"临终来迎"）。这一信仰为面对死亡的人提供了巨大的心理安慰——死亡不再是需要恐惧的黑暗深渊，而是通向光明的门户。

Shandao's rebirth teaching provides unique spiritual resources for contemporary end-of-life care. In his system, death is not an ending but a "passing over" (wǎngshēng) — a threshold from the suffering Sahā world to the Pure Land of Ultimate Bliss. At the moment of death, Amitābha Buddha and the bodhisattvas Avalokiteśvara and Mahāsthāmaprāpta come personally to welcome the practitioner ("deathbed welcome," línzhōng lái yíng). This belief offers immense psychological comfort to those facing death — death is no longer a dark abyss to be feared, but a gateway to light.

在现代安宁疗护（hospice care）实践中，净土宗的临终助念——即在临终者身边集体念佛——已被证明能够有效减轻患者对死亡的焦虑，帮助他们在平静和信任中离世。这一实践直接源自善导所弘扬的净土传统。

In modern hospice care practice, Pure Land deathbed nianfo support — collective nianfo recitation at the bedside of the dying — has been shown to effectively reduce patients' death anxiety and help them pass away in peace and trust. This practice derives directly from the Pure Land tradition that Shandao promoted.

## 善导的诠释方法 Shandao's Hermeneutical Method

善导之所以能够在净土思想史上取得如此突破性的成就，与其独特的诠释方法密不可分。他在《观经四帖疏》中运用了多种诠释策略：

Shandao's breakthrough achievements in the history of Pure Land thought were inseparable from his distinctive hermeneutical method. In his Commentary on the Contemplation Sūtra, he employed multiple interpretive strategies:

**"望佛本愿"原则 (The Principle of "Looking to the Buddha's Original Vow")**：这是善导最核心的诠释方法。他不从文字表面来理解经典，而是追问"佛陀讲这段经文的真正意图是什么"，然后以阿弥陀佛的第十八愿作为回答这个问题的钥匙。这种方法使他能够穿透《观经》中繁复的观想描述，直达佛陀的"密意"——称名念佛。

**The "Looking to the Buddha's Original Vow" Principle**: This is Shandao's most central hermeneutical method. Rather than interpreting the sūtra at the surface level of the text, he asked "what was the Buddha's true intent in teaching this sūtra?" and then used Amitābha's Eighteenth Vow as the key to answering that question. This method enabled him to penetrate the elaborate visualization descriptions in the Contemplation Sūtra and reach the Buddha's "hidden meaning" — verbal nianfo recitation.

**"要门"与"弘愿"的区分 (Distinction between the "Essential Gate" and the "Expansive Vow")**：善导认为，《观经》中存在着两条线索——"要门"（指观想修行，即定善和散善）和"弘愿"（指阿弥陀佛的本愿，即称名念佛）。前者是佛陀为了适应不同根机的众生而开设的方便之门，后者才是佛陀真正要传达的终极教义。

**Distinction between the "Essential Gate" and the "Expansive Vow"**: Shandao held that two threads exist within the Contemplation Sūtra — the "Essential Gate" (referring to contemplative practice, i.e., settled and scattered good) and the "Expansive Vow" (referring to Amitābha's Original Vow, i.e., verbal nianfo). The former is an expedient gate opened by the Buddha to accommodate beings of different capacities; the latter is the ultimate teaching the Buddha truly intended to convey.

**以经证经 (Using Sūtra to Prove Sūtra)**：善导在论证自己的观点时，广泛引用净土三部经和其他大乘经典，以经典自身的权威来支持自己的诠释。这种严谨的论证方法使他的思想具有很高的说服力。

**Using Sūtra to Prove Sūtra**: In arguing his positions, Shandao extensively cited the three Pure Land sūtras and other Mahāyāna scriptures, using the authority of the sūtras themselves to support his interpretation. This rigorous argumentative method gave his thought considerable persuasive power.

## 善导与同时代思想家 Shandao and His Contemporaries

善导生活在初唐（613–681），这一时期是中国佛教的黄金时代，多种宗派同时繁荣。将善导置于同时代思想家的语境中，有助于理解他的独特贡献：

Shandao lived during the early Tang dynasty (613–681), a golden age of Chinese Buddhism when multiple schools flourished simultaneously. Placing Shandao in the context of his contemporaries helps illuminate his unique contributions:

- **慧能（638–713）**：禅宗六祖，与善导几乎同一时代。慧能通过"直指人心、见性成佛"使禅宗大众化；善导通过"称名念佛、他力本愿"使净土宗大众化。两者都是佛教的大众化改革者，但路径截然不同——慧能依赖自力的顿悟，善导依赖他力的信心。
- **Huineng (638–713)**: The Sixth Patriarch of Chan, nearly contemporary with Shandao. Huineng democratized Chan through "direct pointing to the mind, seeing one's nature and becoming Buddha"; Shandao democratized Pure Land through "verbal nianfo and Other-Power Original Vow." Both were populist reformers of Buddhism, but with radically different paths — Huineng relied on self-power sudden awakening, Shandao on Other-Power faith.

- **玄奘（602–664）**：伟大的译经师和唯识学大师，代表了中国佛教中最精深的义学传统。玄奘的唯识学需要极高的智力和长期的学术训练；善导的称名念佛则面向最广大的普通信众。两者代表了佛教修行中"精英路线"与"大众路线"的两极。
- **Xuanzang (602–664)**: The great translator and Yogācāra master, representing the most sophisticated scholastic tradition in Chinese Buddhism. Xuanzang's Yogācāra required exceptional intelligence and prolonged academic training; Shandao's verbal nianfo was oriented toward the broadest possible community of ordinary believers. The two represent the poles of "elite path" and "popular path" in Buddhist practice.

- **智顗（538–597）和吉藏（549–623）**：天台宗和三轮宗的大师，代表了高度系统化的哲学佛教。善导虽然同样具有深厚的学养（从其《观经四帖疏》可见），但他选择的不是哲学建构，而是实践简化——将复杂的修行体系浓缩为一句佛号。
- **Zhiyi (538–597) and Jizang (549–623)**: Masters of Tiantai and Sanlun schools, representing highly systematized philosophical Buddhism. While Shandao was equally well-learned (as his Commentary demonstrates), he chose not philosophical construction but practical simplification — distilling a complex practice system into a single recitation.

## 善导思想的哲学深度 Philosophical Depth of Shandao's Thought

善导的思想常被视为"简单的信仰主义"，但深入分析可以揭示其丰富的哲学内涵：

Shandao's thought is often dismissed as "simple fideism," but deeper analysis reveals its rich philosophical dimensions:

**本体论层面 Ontological Dimension**：善导的"凡夫入报"说涉及佛教本体论的核心问题——什么是"真实"？化土是方便示现的"幻化"存在，报土是因缘和合的"真实"存在。善导主张凡夫可以进入真实报土，这意味着他赋予了凡夫一种超越其自身条件的本体论地位——通过佛的他力，凡夫可以"跳跃"到通常只有圣者才能达到的存在层次。

**Ontological Dimension**: Shandao's doctrine of "ordinary beings entering the reward land" engages the core ontological question of Buddhism — what is "real"? The transformation land is a provisional, illusory manifestation; the reward land is a "real" existence arising from causes and conditions. Shandao's assertion that ordinary beings can enter the true reward land means he accords ordinary beings an ontological status transcending their own conditions — through the Buddha's Other-Power, they can "leap" to a level of existence normally attainable only by saints.

**认识论层面 Epistemological Dimension**：善导对"信心"的理解包含深刻的认识论内涵。"至心信乐"不是盲目的信仰，而是一种特殊的认知方式——通过信心来"认识"佛的真实存在和救度力量。这种"信心即认知"的观点与当代宗教认识论中"信仰作为一种认知模式"（faith as a mode of cognition）的讨论相呼应。

**Epistemological Dimension**: Shandao's understanding of "faith" carries profound epistemological implications. "Sincere and joyful entrusting" is not blind belief, but a special mode of cognition — "knowing" the Buddha's real existence and saving power through faith. This view of "faith as cognition" resonates with contemporary religious epistemology discussions of "faith as a mode of cognition."

**伦理学层面 Ethical Dimension**：善导的他力思想并非否定道德行为的价值，而是重新定位了道德行为的根基。在他的体系中，善行不是换取往生的"筹码"，而是信心和感恩的自然表达。这与康德"义务伦理学"中"为义务而义务"的思路有有趣的平行——善行不是出于功利计算，而是出于内在的道德必然性。

**Ethical Dimension**: Shandao's Other-Power thought does not negate the value of moral conduct but repositions its foundation. In his system, good deeds are not "chips" to exchange for rebirth, but natural expressions of faith and gratitude. This bears an interesting parallel with Kant's "deontological ethics" — moral action not out of utilitarian calculation, but out of inner moral necessity.

## 常见误读 Common Misconceptions

- **误读**："善导的念佛是愚民佛教。" **事实**：善导简化的是修行门槛，而非教理深度。他的《观经四帖疏》展现了极高的诠释学水平。
- **Misconception**: "Shandao's nianfo is a Buddhism for the ignorant." **Reality**: What Shandao simplified was the threshold of practice, not doctrinal depth. His Commentary on the Contemplation Sūtra demonstrates an extremely high level of hermeneutical sophistication.

- **误读**："他力意味着不需要修行。" **事实**：善导强调的是在信心基础上的称名念佛，而非放弃一切努力。"念念不舍"本身就是持续的精进。
- **Misconception**: "Other-Power means no practice is needed." **Reality**: Shandao emphasized nianfo recitation grounded in faith, not the abandonment of all effort. "Never ceasing even for a single thought" is itself sustained diligence.

- **误读**："凡夫入报是廉价救赎。" **事实**：善导要求的是"至心信乐"——真实的信心，而非口头的形式主义。
- **Misconception**: "Ordinary beings entering the reward land is cheap salvation." **Reality**: Shandao requires "sincere and joyful faith" — genuine trust, not mere lip service.
