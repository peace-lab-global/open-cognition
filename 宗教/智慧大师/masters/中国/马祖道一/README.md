# 马祖道一 Mazu Daoyi (709-788)

> "即心是佛，无心是道。"——马祖道一
>
> "Mind is Buddha; no-mind is the Way." — Mazu Daoyi

---

## 基本信息 / Basic Information

**马祖道一**（709年—788年），唐代禅宗高僧，洪州宗创始人，中国禅宗史上最具革命性影响的大师之一。俗姓马，汉州什邡（今四川什邡）人，世人尊称"马祖"。幼年出家，先后师从资州处寂禅师与南岳怀让禅师，于怀让座下开悟得法。后于江西洪州开元寺（今南昌佑民寺）弘法三十余年，培养弟子百余人，开"洪州宗"一派，深刻改变了中国禅宗的发展方向。其"即心是佛"、"平常心是道"、"非心非佛"等思想，不仅是对六祖慧能顿悟禅法的继承与发扬，更为后世临济宗、沩仰宗等五家七宗奠定了思想基础。马祖与其弟子百丈怀海并称"马祖建丛林，百丈立清规"，对中国佛教寺院制度的形成具有开创性贡献。

**Mazu Daoyi** (709–788) was a towering Chan master of the Tang Dynasty, founder of the Hongzhou School, and one of the most revolutionary figures in the history of Chinese Chan Buddhism. Surnamed Ma, born in Shifang, Hanzhou (present-day Shifang, Sichuan), he was popularly known as "Mazu" (Patriarch Ma). Ordained as a youth, he studied under Masters Chuji of Zizhou and Huairang of Nanyue, attaining awakening under the latter. He spent over thirty years teaching at Kaiyuan Monastery in Hongzhou, Jiangxi (present-day Youmin Monastery in Nanchang), training over a hundred disciples and establishing the Hongzhou School, which profoundly redirected the course of Chinese Chan. His teachings — "Mind is Buddha," "Ordinary Mind is the Way," "Neither Mind nor Buddha" — not only inherited and expanded the sudden-awakening legacy of the Sixth Patriarch Huineng, but laid the philosophical groundwork for the later Linji and Weiyang schools and the entire Five Houses and Seven Traditions. Together with his disciple Baizhang Huaihai, Mazu is credited with "Mazu established the monasteries, Baizhang laid down the regulations" — a foundational contribution to Chinese Buddhist institutional life.

## 核心命题 / Core Propositions

1. **即心是佛 Mind is Buddha**：当下之心即是佛，无需向外寻求。认识本心，当下成佛。
   The present mind itself is Buddha; there is no need to seek outside. Recognize your own mind and you are Buddha right now.

2. **平常心是道 Ordinary Mind is the Way**：不造作、不取舍、不断常的平常心，本身就是道。
   The ordinary mind — without artifice, without grasping or rejecting, without断 (severance) or常 (permanence) — is itself the Way.

3. **非心非佛 Neither Mind nor Buddha**：超越"即心是佛"的概念执着，扫除一切名相，直契实相。
   Transcend the conceptual attachment to "Mind is Buddha"; sweep away all names and forms to directly encounter reality.

4. **随处任真 Natural Authenticity Everywhere**：在任何时间、任何地点，保持本来面目，随缘而行，任运自在。
   At any time, in any place, maintain your original face; follow conditions and act naturally, dwelling in spontaneous freedom.

5. **洪州宗 Hongzhou School**：以"触类是道"为宗风——一切行为举止、言语动作，无不是佛性的显现。
   The Hongzhou School takes "all activities are the Way" as its hallmark — every gesture, word, and action is the manifestation of Buddha-nature.

## 思想脉络 / Intellectual Trajectory

### 早年：四川出家与律学基础 / Early Years: Ordination in Sichuan and Vinaya Studies

马祖道一出生于唐中宗景龙三年（709年），汉州什邡人。幼年即于罗汉寺出家，法名道一。少年时期，他广泛学习佛教律藏与经论，奠定了扎实的佛学基础。二十岁前后，他在资州（今四川资中）从处寂禅师（一说为新罗国王子无相禅师）受具足戒，并开始接触禅修实践。这一时期四川地区的佛教环境以经教与律学为主，马祖虽受传统教育，但已显露出对心性问题的深切关注。

Mazu Daoyi was born in 709 CE in Shifang, Hanzhou. He was ordained young at Luohan Monastery and given the dharma name Daoyi. In his youth he studied broadly across Vinaya and sutra literature, laying a solid foundation in Buddhist learning. Around age twenty he received full ordination from Master Chuji of Zizhou (some sources say from the Korean prince-monk Wuxiang) and began engaging in meditation practice. The Buddhist environment in Sichuan at this time emphasized scriptural study and vinaya, but even within this traditional education, Daoyi showed a deep preoccupation with questions of mind-nature.

### 中年：南岳参学与开悟 / Middle Years: Study at Nanyue and Awakening

约开元年间（713-741），马祖离开四川，南下参访南岳怀让禅师。《景德传灯录》记载了著名的"磨砖作镜"公案：马祖独居庵中，日日坐禅，怀让以砖磨石，马祖问其故，怀让曰"磨作镜"。马祖惊问"磨砖岂得成镜？"怀让反问"坐禅岂得成佛？"此一棒喝令马祖豁然开悟，认识到成佛不在于形式上的坐禅，而在于心性的直接觉悟。此后马祖侍奉怀让十余年，深得心法，密受心印。

Around the Kaiyuan period (713–741), Mazu left Sichuan and traveled south to study with Master Huairang at Nanyue. The *Jingde Chuandeng Lu* records the famous "polishing a brick to make a mirror" encounter: Mazu was living alone in a hermitage, sitting in meditation day after day. Huairang began polishing a brick on a stone in front of his hut. When Mazu asked why, Huairang replied, "I'm making a mirror." Mazu exclaimed, "How can polishing a brick make a mirror?" Huairang countered, "How can sitting in meditation make you a Buddha?" This shock jolted Mazu into sudden awakening — the realization that Buddhahood lies not in the formal posture of sitting but in the direct realization of mind-nature. Mazu then attended Huairang for over a decade, deeply absorbing the dharma and receiving mind-to-mind transmission.

### 晚期：洪州弘法与宗派建立 / Later Years: Teaching at Hongzhou and School Building

天宝年间（约742年），马祖来到江西洪州，先住建昌（今江西永修），后移住开元寺（今南昌佑民寺）。在此弘法三十余年，声名远播，四方学者云集。据载马祖座下有入室弟子一百三十九人，各为一方宗主。这一时期他提出了"即心是佛"、"平常心是道"等核心命题，并以独特的接引方式——喝、打、竖拂、画圆等——开创了洪州宗活泼泼辣的教学风格。大历八年（773年），马祖受到唐代宗的崇敬，获赐"大寂禅师"号。贞元四年（788年）二月一日，马祖圆寂，世寿八十。弟子们建塔于石门山（今江西靖安宝峰寺）。

During the Tianbao period (c. 742), Mazu arrived in Hongzhou, Jiangxi. He first stayed at Jianchang (present-day Yongxiu, Jiangxi) and later moved to Kaiyuan Monastery (present-day Youmin Monastery, Nanchang). For over thirty years he taught there, his fame spreading far and wide, drawing students from all directions. Records indicate 139 inner-chamber disciples, each of whom became a regional dharma leader. During this period he articulated his core teachings — "Mind is Buddha," "Ordinary Mind is the Way" — and pioneered the Hongzhou School's lively, direct teaching style through shouting, striking, raising the whisk, drawing circles, and other spontaneous methods. In 773 Emperor Daizong bestowed upon him the title "Chan Master Daji" (Great Stillness). Mazu passed away on the first day of the second month of 788, at age eighty. His disciples built a stupa at Shimen Mountain (present-day Baofeng Monastery, Jing'an, Jiangxi).

## 重要概念 / Key Concepts

- **即心是佛 Mind is Buddha** — 当下之心即是佛，一切具足，不假外求。详见 [即心是佛](concepts/mind-is-buddha.md)。
  The present mind itself is Buddha, complete in every way, needing nothing from outside.

- **平常心是道 Ordinary Mind is the Way** — 不造作、不取舍、不断常，平常心即是道。详见 [平常心是道](concepts/ordinary-mind-way.md)。
  Without artifice, without grasping or rejecting, the ordinary mind is itself the Way.

- **非心非佛 Neither Mind nor Buddha** — 超越一切概念执着，扫除"心"与"佛"的名相。详见 [非心非佛](concepts/neither-mind-nor-buddha.md)。
  Transcend all conceptual attachment; sweep away the names "mind" and "Buddha."

- **随处任真 Natural Authenticity** — 在任何时间地点保持本来面目。详见 [随处任真](concepts/natural-authenticity.md)。
  Maintain your original face in every time and place.

- **洪州宗 Hongzhou School** — 马祖开创的禅宗宗派，以"触类是道"为宗风。详见 [洪州宗](concepts/hongzhou-school.md)。
  The Chan school founded by Mazu, taking "all activities are the Way" as its hallmark.

## 思想坐标 / Intellectual Coordinates

马祖道一在中国禅宗思想史上占据关键的枢纽位置：

Mazu Daoyi occupies a pivotal hinge position in Chinese Chan intellectual history:

- **承接六祖 Inheriting the Sixth Patriarch**：直接继承慧能"直指人心，见性成佛"的顿悟传统，将其从理论层面推向生活实践的层面。
  Directly inheriting Huineng's "pointing directly at the human mind, seeing nature and becoming Buddha" tradition, pushing it from theory into the dimension of lived practice.

- **开创洪州 Founding Hongzhou**：以洪州为根据地，建立了具有独特宗风和完整传承体系的禅宗宗派。
  Establishing at Hongzhou a Chan school with a distinctive ethos and complete lineage system.

- **影响五家 Influencing the Five Houses**：马祖弟子百丈怀海（沩仰宗远祖）、黄檗希运（临济宗远祖）分别开创了后世禅宗最重要的两大宗派。
  Mazu's disciples Baizhang Huaihai (remote ancestor of Weiyang) and Huangbo Xiyun (remote ancestor of Linji) each founded two of the most important later Chan houses.

- **批判教条 Critiquing Dogma**：激烈批判依赖经典文字、执着坐禅形式、追求外在佛果的倾向，主张回归当下之心。
  Vigorously critiquing reliance on scriptural language, attachment to meditation form, and pursuit of external Buddhahood, advocating a return to the present mind.

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

- **禅修指导 Meditation Guidance**：平常心是道的理念适用于现代人的禅修实践——不追求特殊境界，在日常中保持觉知。
  The principle of "Ordinary Mind is the Way" applies to modern meditation — not seeking special states, maintaining awareness in everyday life.

- **心理治疗 Psychotherapy**：即心是佛的思想与现代心理学中的自我接纳、自我肯定具有深层共鸣。
  "Mind is Buddha" resonates deeply with self-acceptance and self-affirmation in modern 心理学.

- **生活禅 Everyday Zen**：随处任真的理念深刻影响了当代"生活禅"运动——禅不在山上，在柴米油盐中。
  Natural authenticity has profoundly influenced the contemporary "Everyday Zen" movement — Zen is not on the mountain but in the kitchen.

- **领导力与管理 Leadership & Management**：禅宗的直接、简洁、不拘形式的风格影响了现代管理理论与实践。
  Chan's direct, concise, non-formulaic style has influenced modern management theory and practice.

- **教育学 Education**：马祖独特的接引方式——因材施教、当头棒喝——对启发式教育具有深刻启示。
  Mazu's unique pedagogical methods — teaching according to capacity, direct shocking — offer profound insights for heuristic education.

## 常见误读 / Common Misreadings

### 1. "马祖反对坐禅" / "Mazu opposed sitting meditation"
马祖并非反对坐禅本身，而是反对执着于坐禅的形式。"磨砖作镜"的公案批判的是将坐禅等同于成佛的谬见，而非坐禅行为本身。
Mazu did not oppose sitting meditation itself but opposed attachment to its form. The "polishing a brick" kōan criticizes the error of equating sitting with Buddhahood, not the act of sitting itself.

### 2. "平常心是放任" / "Ordinary Mind means indulgence"
平常心不是随波逐流、放纵欲望，而是"不造作、不取舍"——在不执着中保持觉知，于日常生活中自然行道。
Ordinary mind is not drifting with the current or indulging desires. It is "without artifice, without grasping or rejecting" — maintaining awareness within non-attachment, naturally walking the Way in daily life.

### 3. "即心是佛是唯心主义" / "Mind is Buddha is idealism"
即心是佛不是一种形而上学的唯心论，而是指向直接经验的教学方便——让学人回归自身，不要向外驰求。
"Mind is Buddha" is not a metaphysical idealism but a pedagogical expedient pointing to direct experience — directing students to return to themselves rather than seeking externally.

### 4. "非心非佛否定一切" / "Neither Mind nor Buddha denies everything"
非心非佛不是虚无主义的否定，而是对"即心是佛"这一概念本身的超越——当"即心是佛"变成新的执着时，必须以"非心非佛"来扫除。
"Neither Mind nor Buddha" is not nihilistic negation but the transcendence of the concept "Mind is Buddha" itself — when "Mind is Buddha" becomes a new attachment, "Neither Mind nor Buddha" must sweep it away.
