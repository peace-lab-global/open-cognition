# 老子 · Laozi

> **"道可道，非常道；名可名，非常名。"**
> *The Dao that can be told is not the eternal Dao; the name that can be named is not the eternal name.*

## 基本信息 / Biographical Note

老子，传为春秋末期楚国苦县厉乡曲仁里（今河南鹿邑）人，姓李名耳，字聃（dān）。道家哲学的奠基者，中国思想史上最具深远影响的人物之一。传统记载其生卒年约为公元前601年至前531年，但自司马迁《史记·老子韩非列传》以来，关于老子的历史身份即存在争议——司马迁本人已列举三种可能人选：老聃、老莱子（楚人，与孔子同时）、太史儋（周太史，孔子后百余年）。

Laozi (traditionally c. 601–531 BCE) is the foundational figure of Daoist 哲学 and one of the most profoundly influential thinkers in Chinese intellectual history. According to tradition, he was born in Qurenli village, Li township, Ku County in the state of Chu (present-day Luyi, Henan). His surname was Li, his given name Er, and his courtesy name Dan. His historical identity has been debated since Sima Qian's *Records of the Grand Historian*, which already listed three possible candidates. Modern scholarship tends to view the *Daodejing* as a gradually compiled text, reaching its final form in the mid-to-late Warring States period, rather than the work of a single author.

老子曾任周朝守藏室史（相当于国家图书馆馆长），博览群书，深谙周礼与上古典籍。目睹周室衰微、礼崩乐坏，老子开始对文明异化进行深刻反思，最终形成了以"道"为核心的哲学体系。传说孔子曾问礼于老子，老子以"去子之骄气与多欲，态色与淫志"回应，批评儒家仁义礼智的局限性。

Laozi served as the Keeper of the Archives (守藏室史) of the Zhou dynasty — effectively the director of the national library. He was deeply versed in the Zhou rites and ancient texts. Witnessing the decline of the Zhou court and the collapse of ritual order, Laozi began a profound reflection on the alienation of civilization, ultimately developing a philosophical system centered on the concept of Dao. According to legend, Confucius once visited Laozi to inquire about ritual propriety; Laozi responded by urging him to "rid yourself of your arrogant air and your many desires, your ingratiating manners and your excessive ambitions" — a direct critique of the limitations of Confucian benevolence and righteousness.

文本方面，1973年马王堆帛书甲乙本的出土和1993年郭店楚简本的发现，彻底改变了老子研究的面貌。郭店本可追溯至公元前4世纪，是目前所见最早的《老子》文本，其篇幅约为今本五分之二，章序与今本大不相同，揭示了文本在漫长流传中的层累过程。

The discovery of Mawangdui silk manuscripts (1973) and Guodian bamboo slips (1993) revolutionized Laozi studies. The Guodian text, dating to the 4th century BCE, is the earliest known version of the *Laozi*. Its length is roughly two-fifths of the received text, and its chapter order differs substantially, revealing the gradual accretion of the text over centuries.

## 一句话定位 / One-line Positioning

老子是**中国古代第一位系统追问宇宙本源与人类行动之关系的哲学家**——以五千言构建了以"道"为核心、以"无为"为方法、以"自然"为价值、以"反"为运动规律的完整哲学体系，深刻影响了此后两千五百年的东亚文明和世界哲学。

Laozi is **the first Chinese thinker to systematically interrogate the relationship between the origin of the cosmos and human action** — constructing in five thousand characters a complete philosophical system centered on Dao as origin, wu-wei as method, ziran as value, and reversal as the law of movement, profoundly shaping East Asian civilization and world 哲学 for twenty-five centuries.

## 核心命题 / Core Theses

### 1. 道为万物本源（Dao as the Origin of All Things）

"道可道，非常道；名可名，非常名。"（第1章）——道是超越言说的宇宙本体和运行规律。道不是一个"东西"（entity），而是使一切东西成为可能的那个"无名"。"有物混成，先天地生"（第25章），道先于天地而存在，是万物之所从出的终极根源。详见 [道 / Dao](concepts/dao-the-way.md)。

"The Dao that can be told is not the eternal Dao" (Ch. 1) — Dao transcends language and conceptual grasp. It is not an entity but the nameless ground that makes all entities possible. "There was something formless yet complete, that existed before heaven and earth" (Ch. 25). See [Dao / The Way](concepts/dao-the-way.md).

### 2. 无为而无不为（Wu-wei: Non-action yet Nothing is Left Undone）

"道常无为而无不为。"（第37章）——最高的行动方式是不强为、不妄为，顺应事物的自然趋势，反而能成就一切。无为不是消极怠惰，而是以最小干预达到最大效果的最高行动哲学。详见 [无为 / Wu-wei](concepts/wuwei.md)。

"The Dao is ever non-active, yet nothing is left undone" (Ch. 37) — The highest form of action is non-interference with natural tendencies. Wu-wei is not passivity but the most efficient 哲学 of action. See [Wu-wei](concepts/wuwei.md).

### 3. 反者道之动（Reversal is the Movement of Dao）

"反者道之动，弱者道之用。"（第40章）——道的运动方式是反向的：物极必反，刚强走向毁灭，柔弱走向生机。"天下之至柔，驰骋天下之至坚"（第43章）。这是老子辩证法的核心。详见 [反者道之动 / Reversal](concepts/reversal.md)。

"Reversal is the movement of the Dao; weakness is the function of the Dao" (Ch. 40) — Things turn into their opposites at their extremes. The softest thing in the world rides roughshod over the hardest (Ch. 43). This is the core of Laozi's dialectics. See [Reversal](concepts/reversal.md).

### 4. 道法自然（Dao Follows Its Own Nature）

"人法地，地法天，天法道，道法自然。"（第25章）——自然是最高价值，不是指自然界（nature），而是"自己如此"（self-so）的状态。万物应顺其本性，不被外力扭曲。详见 [自然 / Ziran](concepts/ziran.md)。

"Humans follow the earth, the earth follows heaven, heaven follows Dao, Dao follows its own nature" (Ch. 25) — Ziran (self-so) is the highest value: not "nature" in the environmental sense, but the state of being "so of itself." See [Ziran](concepts/ziran.md).

### 5. 道生万物（Dao Generates All Things）

"道生一，一生二，二生三，三生万物。"（第42章）——道不是造物主，而是万物自生的本源。"有"生于"无"，但"无"不是虚无，而是无限的可能性。万物"负阴而抱阳，冲气以为和"。

"Dao gives birth to One, One to Two, Two to Three, Three to the ten thousand things" (Ch. 42) — Dao is not a creator deity but the spontaneous ground from which all things emerge. "Being" arises from "non-being," but "non-being" is not nihilistic void — it is infinite potentiality.

### 6. 知止不殆（Know When to Stop）

"知足不辱，知止不殆，可以长久。"（第44章）——最大的智慧是知道限度。"祸莫大于不知足，咎莫大于欲得"（第46章）。贪得无厌是灾祸之源，知足知止是保全之道。

"Know contentment and you will not be disgraced; know when to stop and you will not be imperiled" (Ch. 44) — The greatest wisdom lies in knowing limits. No calamity is greater than not knowing when one has enough.

### 7. 小国寡民（Small State, Few People）

"小国寡民……甘其食，美其服，安其居，乐其俗。"（第80章）——政治理想不是扩张和征服，而是让人民安居乐业、返璞归真。"太上，不知有之"（第17章）——最好的统治者让人民感觉不到他的存在。

"A small state with few people... they find their food sweet, their clothes fine, their homes peaceful, their customs joyful" (Ch. 80) — The political ideal is not expansion but simple, self-sufficient community. "The best ruler is one whose existence is barely known" (Ch. 17).

## 思想脉络 / Intellectual Context

### 道经（1–37章）：形上学与宇宙论 / Dao Jing: Metaphysics and Cosmology

道经建立了老子思想的形上学框架。核心范畴是"道"——先天地生、独立不改、周行不殆。道与"有""无"的关系是本体论的核心："无，名天地之始；有，名万物之母。"有与无不是对立，而是"同出而异名"——道的一体两面。道既超越又内在于万物，"大道泛兮，其可左右"（第34章）。

The Dao Jing establishes the metaphysical framework of Laozi's thought. The central category is Dao — born before heaven and earth, independent and unchanging, circulating without cease. The relationship between Dao and "being" (有) / "non-being" (无) forms the ontological core: "The nameless is the beginning of heaven and earth; the named is the mother of the ten thousand things." Being and non-being are not opposites but "emerge from the same source with different names" — two faces of the one Dao.

### 德经（38–81章）：伦理与政治 / De Jing: Ethics and Politics

德经将道的原理应用于人间秩序。核心概念是"德"——道在万物中的具体显现。最高的德是"上德不德"——不刻意追求德行的自然之德。政治上主张"无为而治"：最好的统治者让人民感觉不到他的存在（"太上，不知有之"）。详见 [德 / De-Virtue](concepts/de-virtue.md)。

The De Jing applies the principles of Dao to human order. The central concept is De (virtue/power) — the concrete manifestation of Dao in all things. The highest virtue is "superior virtue that does not consider itself virtue" — natural virtue that is not deliberately pursued. Politically, it advocates "governing through non-action." See [De / Virtue](concepts/de-virtue.md).

## 思想坐标 / Position in Intellectual History

老子与孔子并列为中国哲学的两大奠基者，但其思想路径截然不同。孔子重礼乐人伦，以仁义为核心；老子以道为核心，批判儒家的人为造作——"失道而后德，失德而后仁，失仁而后义，失义而后礼"（第38章）。老子认为仁义礼智是道丧失之后的替代品，文明越繁复，离道越远。

Laozi stands alongside Confucius as one of the two foundational pillars of Chinese philosophy, yet their paths diverge fundamentally. Confucius emphasized ritual propriety and human relationships, centering on benevolence (ren) and righteousness (yi). Laozi centered on Dao, critiquing Confucian artifice: "When the Dao is lost, virtue appears; when virtue is lost, benevolence appears; when benevolence is lost, righteousness appears; when righteousness is lost, ritual appears" (Ch. 38). For Laozi, benevolence, righteousness, and ritual are substitutes for what has been lost — the more elaborate civilization becomes, the further it departs from Dao.

老子直接影响了庄子，经庄子发展为更激进的精神自由哲学。汉代黄老之学将老子政治化，成为文景之治的治国理念；魏晋玄学（王弼、何晏）将老子本体论化；道教将老子宗教化，尊为太上老君。在东亚思想史上，老子与佛教的相遇催生了禅宗——中国化的佛教。

Laozi directly influenced Zhuangzi, who radicalized the tradition into a 哲学 of spiritual freedom. The Huang-Lao school of the early Han politicized Laozi's thought, making it the governing 哲学 of the Wen-Jing era. Wei-Jin metaphysics (Wang Bi, He Yan) ontologized Laozi. Religious Daoism deified him as Taishang Laojun (the Most High Lord Lao). In East Asian intellectual history, the encounter of Daoism with Buddhism produced Chan (Zen) Buddhism.

## 当代应用 / Contemporary Applications

- **生态哲学 (Ecology)**："道法自然"为深层生态学（deep ecology）提供东方思想资源——人不是自然的主宰，而是自然的一部分
- **管理哲学 (Management)**：无为而治被视为"仆人式领导"（servant leadership）的东方原型——最好的领导者让团队自我组织
- **心理治疗 (Psychotherapy)**：罗杰斯（Carl Rogers）的"来访者中心"疗法与无为哲学结构相通——治疗师不指导，而是创造让来访者"自化"的条件
- **物理学 (Physics)**：玻尔（Niels Bohr）的互补原理与"有无相生"的辩证思维相呼应；玻尔获封爵士时选择的族徽图案即为太极图
- **武术 (Martial Arts)**：太极拳以"以柔克刚""舍己从人"为核心理念，直接源于老子思想
- **中医 (Traditional Chinese Medicine)**：阴阳五行理论深受老子辩证法影响——"万物负阴而抱阳"
- **设计哲学 (Design)**："大巧若拙"对极简主义设计的影响——最好的设计是不刻意展示设计的设计
- **政治哲学 (Political Philosophy)**：对过度干预的批判，"治大国若烹小鲜"——小政府理念的古代原型

## 常见误读 / Common Misunderstandings

1. **"无为就是什么都不做" / "Wu-wei means doing nothing"** — 纠正：无为是"不妄为"——不强加人为秩序于自然之上；庖丁解牛式的顺势而为才是无为的真义。Wu-wei is "not acting recklessly" — it means not imposing artificial order upon natural tendencies. The cook Ding's effortless butchery is the paradigm of wu-wei.

2. **"道就是上帝" / "Dao is God"** — 纠正：道不是人格神，不创造也不主宰；道是"万物自化"的非人格本源。"道生之"不是"创造"（creatio），而是"使之自然生成"。Dao is not a personal deity; it does not create or govern. It is the impersonal ground from which all things spontaneously arise.

3. **"老子是消极避世的" / "Laozi is a pessimistic escapist"** — 纠正：老子有强烈的政治关怀和社会批判。"小国寡民"是对大国兼并的反向理想，不是否定一切社会组织。老子恰恰是最积极的行动哲学——"无为而无不为"是以最小干预达到最大效果。Laozi has deep political engagement and social critique. "Small state, few people" is a counter-ideal to imperial expansion, not a rejection of all social organization.

4. **"老子反对一切文明" / "Laozi opposes all civilization"** — 纠正：老子批判的是文明的异化——当礼乐制度变成虚伪的外壳、当智巧变成欺诈的工具——而非文明本身。"甘其食，美其服，安其居，乐其俗"描绘的是一种简朴而充实的文明生活。Laozi critiques the alienation of civilization — when rituals become empty shells, when cleverness becomes a tool for fraud — not civilization itself.

## 东西方对话 / East-West Dialogue

### 老子 × 海德格尔（Laozi x Heidegger）

海德格尔晚年深受老子影响，曾尝试与台湾学者萧师毅合译《道德经》。

- **道与存在 (Dao and Being)**：两者都追问"使一切存在成为可能"的那个"东西"——但道是"无名"的，存在（Sein）是"被遗忘"的。Both interrogate "that which makes all beings possible" — but Dao is "nameless," while Being (Sein) is "forgotten."
- **无与虚无 (Wu and Nichts)**：老子的"无"不是虚无，而是万物之母——海德格尔的"无"也是存在的"面纱"。Laozi's "wu" is not nihilistic void but the mother of all things — Heidegger's "Nothing" is the "veil" of Being.
- **技术与自然 (Technology and Nature)**：两者都批判技术文明对自然的遮蔽——老子用"朴"，海德格尔用"Gestell"（座架）。Both critique technological civilization's obscuring of nature — Laozi uses "pu" (uncarved block), Heidegger uses "Gestell" (enframing).

### 老子 × 赫拉克利特（Laozi x Heraclitus）

同为轴心时代的辩证法大师：
- **道与逻各斯 (Dao and Logos)**：赫拉克利特的"逻各斯"（Logos）是万物运行的理性法则，与"道"作为万物法则的维度平行——但逻各斯可以被"听"和"理解"，而道"不可名"
- **对立统一 (Unity of Opposites)**：赫拉克利特说"对立的事物是统一的"，老子说"有无相生，难易相成"——两者都看到了对立面的互相依存和转化
- **万物皆流 (Panta Rhei)**：赫拉克利特"你不能两次踏入同一条河流"与老子"反者道之动"——两者都洞察到变化的根本性
